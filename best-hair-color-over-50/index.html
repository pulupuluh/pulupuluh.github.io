Best Hair Color Over 50

Written by Petter Sep 09, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're over 50 and looking to change up your hair color, you're not alone! Many women and men in their 50s and beyond want to try something new and fresh with their hair. However, it can be daunting to find the right hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. In this article, we'll explore the best hair color options for those over 50.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to lose its natural color, becoming dull and gray. Many people opt to dye their hair to maintain their youthful look and boost their confidence. However, it's important to choose a hair color that flatters your skin tone and doesn't look too harsh or fake.

How to Choose the Best Hair Color Over 50

Choosing the right hair color can be tricky, especially if you're not sure what will look best on you. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hair color over 50:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is an essential factor to consider when choosing a hair color. If you have a warm skin tone, opt for warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, or chestnut brown. If you have a cool skin tone, choose cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or burgundy.

Think About Your Natural Hair Color

When choosing a new hair color, it's essential to consider your natural hair color. If you have light hair, you can go for a lighter shade or highlights. If you have dark hair, you can opt for a darker shade or lowlights.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another crucial factor to consider when choosing a hair color. If you have an active lifestyle and spend a lot of time outdoors, opt for a low-maintenance hair color that won't fade easily. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, you can go for a more high-maintenance hair color.

Step by Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair Over 50

Once you've chosen the best hair color for you, it's time to color your hair! Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Preparation

Before coloring your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wear an old shirt or towel around your shoulders to protect your clothes from hair dye. Mix the hair dye according to the instructions on the box.

Step 2: Application

Apply the hair dye to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a comb to distribute the hair dye evenly. Leave the hair dye on for the recommended amount of time.

Step 3: Rinse and Condition

After the recommended time, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear. Apply the conditioner that comes with the hair dye and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color Over 50

After coloring your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy and shine. Here are some tips:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color-treated hair. These products are formulated to protect your hair color from fading and keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause your hair color to fade faster. Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners as much as possible.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can cause your hair color to fade faster. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

FAQs

What is the best hair color for over 50?

The best hair color for over 50 depends on your skin tone and natural hair color. Warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, and chestnut brown are great for those with warm skin tones, while cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, and burgundy are ideal for those with cool skin tones.

How often should I color my hair over 50?

It's best to wait at least 4-6 weeks between hair colorings to avoid damaging your hair. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and the hair color you choose.

Can I color my hair at home?

Yes, you can color your hair at home using a hair dye kit. However, it's essential to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test before applying the hair dye to your entire head.

Hair Care for Colored Hair over 50

Colored hair needs extra care to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips for taking care of your colored hair over 50:

Use a Color-Protectant Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color-treated hair. These products are formulated to protect your hair color from fading and keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy. Use a hair mask or deep conditioner that's formulated for colored hair.

Avoid Hot Tools

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause your hair color to fade faster. Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners as much as possible.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can cause your hair color to fade faster. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

In conclusion, choosing the best hair color over 50 can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance and knowledge, it's possible to find a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. Follow our tips and tricks for coloring and maintaining your hair, and you'll have healthy, vibrant hair that looks and feels great!
