How Do I Know If Hairstyle Will Suit Me Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Feb 24, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit you. With so many options available, it's easy to get overwhelmed and make the wrong choice. However, there are some tips and tricks that can help you determine what hairstyle will suit you best. In this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know about choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape and personality.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Consider Your Hair Type
  • Matching Your Personality
  • Popular Haircut Styles
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Determining Your Face Shape

One of the most important things to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your face shape. Your face shape can help you determine which hairstyles will suit you best. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. Here's how to determine your face shape:

  • Round: Your face is as wide as it is long, with full cheeks and a round chin.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a slightly curved jawline.
  • Square: Your face is as wide as it is long, with a strong jawline and forehead.
  • Heart: Your face is wider at the forehead and temples, with a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones, with a narrow forehead and chin.
  • Long: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a straight jawline.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, like a pixie cut or a long bob.

Consider Your Hair Type

In addition to your face shape, you should also consider your hair type when choosing a hairstyle. Your hair type can affect how a hairstyle looks on you and how easy it is to maintain. Here are the four main hair types:

  • Straight: Your hair is naturally straight and does not have a lot of body.
  • Wavy: Your hair has a natural wave to it and may be prone to frizz.
  • Curly: Your hair is naturally curly and may be prone to frizz and dryness.
  • Kinky: Your hair is tightly coiled and may be prone to dryness and breakage.

Depending on your hair type, you may want to opt for a hairstyle that works with your natural texture. For example, if you have naturally curly hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that require a lot of heat styling, as this can damage your hair. Instead, opt for a hairstyle that allows your curls to shine, like a shoulder-length bob or a layered cut.

Matching Your Personality

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your personality. Your hairstyle can be an expression of who you are, so it's important to choose a style that reflects your personality. Here are some examples:

  • Bold and daring: If you're bold and daring, you may want to opt for a short pixie cut or a funky asymmetrical bob.
  • Classic and elegant: If you're classic and elegant, you may want to opt for a sleek, shoulder-length bob or a chic chignon.
  • Bohemian and free-spirited: If you're bohemian and free-spirited, you may want to opt for long, loose waves or a messy topknot.

Of course, these are just examples, and the hairstyle that suits you best ultimately depends on your personal style and preferences. The key is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Popular Haircut Styles

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, let's take a look at some popular haircut styles:

  • Bob: A classic bob is a timeless hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. It can be worn straight or curly, and can be cut at various lengths.
  • Pixie cut: A pixie cut is a short, edgy hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways. It's a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style.
  • Long layers: Long layers are a popular choice for those who want to add movement and volume to their hair. They're also a great option for those with thick hair.
  • Bangs: Bangs can be a great way to switch up your look without making a drastic change. There are many different types of bangs, from blunt to wispy.

Remember, these are just a few examples, and there are many other haircut styles to choose from. The key is to find a style that works for you and makes you feel confident.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use the right products: Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type to keep it healthy and strong.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can damage your hair, so try to limit your use of hot tools like curling irons and straighteners.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair: When spending time in the sun, wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to prevent damage.

FAQs

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me?

A: Consider your face shape, hair type, and personality when choosing a hairstyle. It's also a good idea to consult with a hairstylist, who can offer personalized advice based on your features.

Q: What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

A: If you don't like your new hairstyle, don't panic! Hair grows back, and you can always try a different style. Talk to your hairstylist about what you don't like, and they may be able to make adjustments to better suit your preferences.

Q: How do I take care of my new hairstyle?

A: Use the right products for your hair type, avoid heat styling as much as possible, and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.


Choosing the right hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair type, and personality, you can find a hairstyle that complements your features and makes you feel confident. Remember, it's okay to experiment and try new things - after all, it's just hair!
