Shampoo

When it comes to washing thick frizzy hair, you want to choose a shampoo that will both cleanse and hydrate. Look for products that contain natural oils like argan or coconut oil, which will help to smooth and tame frizz. Our top pick is the OGX Moroccan Argan Oil Shampoo. This nourishing formula is infused with argan oil, which helps to hydrate and smooth hair, leaving it soft and manageable. Plus, it's sulfate-free, so it won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

Conditioner

After shampooing, be sure to use a conditioner that will moisturize and detangle your hair. We recommend the Shea Moisture Raw Shea Butter Restorative Conditioner. This rich formula is packed with shea butter, which helps to nourish and repair damaged hair. It's also free from sulfates, parabens, and other harmful ingredients, making it a safe and natural choice for your hair.

Hair Mask

A weekly hair mask is essential for keeping thick frizzy hair in check. The Briogeo Don't Despair, Repair! Deep Conditioning Mask is our top pick. This award-winning mask is infused with a blend of nourishing oils and vitamins that work together to strengthen and hydrate hair. It also helps to reduce breakage and improve elasticity, leaving your hair looking and feeling healthier than ever.

Serum

A serum can help to tame frizz and add shine to your hair. The John Frieda Frizz Ease Extra Strength Serum is a cult favorite for a reason. This lightweight formula is infused with silk proteins, which help to smooth and add shine to your hair. It also contains a blend of natural oils, including coconut and jojoba, which help to nourish and protect your hair from damage.

Hair Oil

A hair oil can work wonders for thick frizzy hair, helping to nourish and calm unruly strands. The Ouai Hair Oil is a favorite among hairdressers and beauty editors. This lightweight oil is infused with a blend of nourishing oils, including jojoba and sweet almond, which help to smooth and protect hair. It also contains a UV filter to protect hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

Styling Products

When it comes to styling thick frizzy hair, you want to choose products that will help to smooth and tame your locks without weighing them down. The Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser's Invisible Oil Heat/UV Protective Primer is a great option. This lightweight spray is infused with a blend of natural oils, including coconut and argan, which help to nourish and protect your hair. It also contains a UV filter to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Tools

The right tools can make all the difference when it comes to managing thick frizzy hair. We recommend investing in a high-quality hair dryer, like the Dyson Supersonic Hair Dryer. This innovative dryer is designed to dry your hair quickly and efficiently, while also minimizing heat damage. It also comes with a range of attachments, including a smoothing nozzle, which can help to tame frizz.

Tips for Managing Thick Frizzy Hair

Managing thick frizzy hair can be a challenge, but there are a few tips and tricks that can help. Here are some of our top tips:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Try to let your hair air-dry as much as possible, as heat styling can exacerbate frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do.

FAQs

What causes frizzy hair?

Frizzy hair is often caused by a lack of moisture in the hair shaft. This can be due to genetics, environmental factors, or heat styling. When the hair becomes dried out, the cuticles become rough and raised, causing the hair to look frizzy and unruly.

Can you prevent frizzy hair?

While you can't always prevent frizzy hair, there are steps you can take to minimize it. These include using a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, using a weekly hair mask, avoiding heat styling as much as possible, and using a silk or satin pillowcase.

What is the best way to style thick frizzy hair?

The best way to style thick frizzy hair is to use products that will help to smooth and tame your locks, without weighing them down. This may include a serum, hair oil, or styling cream. Be sure to avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can exacerbate frizz and damage your hair.

Hair Care Routine for Thick Frizzy Hair

Here's a simple hair care routine for managing thick frizzy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a hydrating shampoo, like the OGX Moroccan Argan Oil Shampoo.
  2. Apply a conditioner, like the Shea Moisture Raw Shea Butter Restorative Conditioner, and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing.
  3. Once a week, use a hair mask, like the Briogeo Don't Despair, Repair! Deep Conditioning Mask, to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  4. Apply a serum, like the John Frieda Frizz Ease Extra Strength Serum, to damp hair to help tame frizz and add shine.
  5. Use a hair oil, like the Ouai Hair Oil, to nourish and protect your hair from damage.
  6. Style your hair using a lightweight styling product, like the Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser's Invisible Oil Heat/UV Protective Primer, and avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.

By following this simple routine and using the right products, you can achieve smooth, manageable locks that you'll love.
