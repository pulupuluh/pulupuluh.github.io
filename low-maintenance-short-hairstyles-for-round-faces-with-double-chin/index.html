Low Maintenance Short Hairstyles For Round Faces With Double Chin

Written by Daniel Apr 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face with a double chin, finding the perfect hairstyle can be challenging. You want a style that will flatter your face shape and highlight your best features, but you also want something low maintenance that won't require a lot of time or effort to maintain. Fortunately, there are plenty of short hairstyles that are perfect for round faces with double chins. In this article, we'll share some of our favorite low maintenance styles that are sure to look great on you.

Description

A round face with a double chin typically has a fullness to the cheeks and jawline. The goal of a good hairstyle for this face shape is to create a sense of balance and proportion by elongating the face and drawing attention to the eyes and cheekbones. Short hairstyles can be a great choice for this face shape, as they can help to create the illusion of length and add definition to the face.

How to

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle for a round face with a double chin is to consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have thin hair, a layered bob or pixie cut can help to create volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or shaggy layers can help to thin out your hair and make it more manageable.

Next, think about your styling routine and how much time you want to spend on your hair each day. If you're looking for a truly low maintenance style, consider a wash-and-go cut like a short, textured crop or a messy pixie cut. These styles require minimal styling and can be easily tousled with your fingers for a casual, effortless look.

Step by Step

1. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel-dry your hair, then apply a styling product like mousse or gel to add volume and texture.

3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, lifting at the roots to create volume.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement.

5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too tight, as these can make your face look rounder.

- If you have a double chin, avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or severe, as these can emphasize the chin area.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

Q: Will a short hairstyle make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. The key is to choose a style that creates balance and proportion by elongating the face and drawing attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

Q: Do I need to blow dry my hair every day?

A: No, you can allow your hair to air dry for a more casual, relaxed look. However, blow drying can help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently. Regular trims can also help to maintain the shape of your style and prevent split ends. With the right care and styling, your short hairstyle can be both low maintenance and flattering for your face shape.
