How To Do Big Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 26, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your straight, flat hair and looking for a change? Big wavy hair might just be the answer! This trendy hairstyle can add volume and dimension to your hair, giving you a glamorous and effortless look. In this article, we'll walk you through how to achieve big wavy hair in just a few easy steps.

Description

Big wavy hair is a hairstyle that features loose and voluminous waves that add texture and movement to your hair. This look is perfect for anyone who wants to add some dimension and body to their straight or flat hair.

How to

Here's how you can achieve big wavy hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair gently and apply a heat protectant spray to avoid damage from heat styling tools.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or elastics. The number of sections you create will depend on the thickness and length of your hair. Generally, the more sections you create, the tighter and more defined your waves will be.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a curling wand or a curling iron and wrap each section of your hair around the barrel, holding it for a few seconds before releasing it. Remember to curl your hair away from your face to create a more natural look.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Once you've curled all the sections of your hair, use a wide-toothed comb to gently comb through your hair. This will help to loosen up the curls and create more volume.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your big wavy hair look by spraying a light hold hairspray all over your hair. This will help to hold the waves in place and prevent them from falling flat throughout the day.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when doing big wavy hair:

  • Use a curling wand or iron that is at least 1 inch in diameter to create loose waves.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly or too close to the roots to avoid a "prom" look.
  • Experiment with the size of the sections you create to achieve different types of waves.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any product buildup that can weigh down your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about big wavy hair:

How long will big wavy hair last?

Big wavy hair can last for up to 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and the products you use to style it. You can prolong the life of your waves by sleeping with your hair in loose braids or using a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.

Can I do big wavy hair on short hair?

Yes! You can still achieve big wavy hair on short hair by using a smaller curling wand or iron and creating more sections.

Do I need to use hairspray?

While hairspray is not necessary, it can help to hold the waves in place and prevent them from falling flat throughout the day.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage from heat styling tools. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and health.

And there you have it! With these simple steps and tips, you can achieve big wavy hair in no time. Don't be afraid to experiment with different types of waves and find the look that works best for you.
