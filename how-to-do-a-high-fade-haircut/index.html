How to Do a High Fade Haircut

A high fade haircut is a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving more length on the top. It's a versatile style that can be worn by men of all ages and hair types.

To do a high fade haircut, you'll need a good quality clipper with different guard sizes, a comb, and a pair of scissors. If you're not confident in your barbering skills, it's always a good idea to seek professional help.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve a perfect high fade haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair, but leave it slightly damp for easier cutting.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard Size

Select the guard size you want to use for the sides and back of your head. A number 1 or 2 guard will give you a shorter fade, while a number 3 or 4 guard will give you a longer fade.

Step 3: Start Cutting the Sides and Back

Using the selected guard size, start cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head. Work your way up from the bottom, using an upward motion to blend the hair from the shorter length to the longer length on top.

Step 4: Create the Fade

Change to a shorter guard size and continue cutting the hair, starting from the bottom again and working your way up. Use an upward motion to create a fade that gradually blends the shorter hair on the sides and back with the longer hair on top.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head to the desired length. You can leave it longer for a more dramatic effect or shorter for a more subtle look.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and style it as desired. You can use a comb, your fingers, or a brush to create your desired look.

Tips for a Perfect High Fade Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high fade haircut:

  • Start with a higher guard size and gradually work your way down to create a more natural-looking fade.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers and create a more even cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process. A high fade haircut requires patience and precision.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a high fade haircut easy to maintain?

Yes, a high fade haircut is easy to maintain, especially if you keep the top of your hair shorter. Simply use a small amount of styling product to create your desired look.

What hair types are suitable for a high fade haircut?

A high fade haircut can be worn by men with all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair. However, it looks best on hair that is thick and full.

How often should I get a high fade haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the fade grows out. On average, a high fade haircut should be maintained every 2-4 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care Advice

After getting a high fade haircut, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as it can damage the hair and cause split ends.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and advice, you're now ready to try a high fade haircut. Remember to take your time, be patient, and most importantly, have fun with it!
