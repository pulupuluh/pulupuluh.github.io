Cool Short Haircuts For Round Faces A Guide To The Best Styles

Written by Oliver Jul 25, 2023 · 11 min read

Welcome to our guide on cool short haircuts for round faces! If you're looking for a new hairstyle but have a round face shape, it can be hard to know where to start. But fear not – we've put together a list of the best short haircuts for round faces, along with tips on how to style and care for your new 'do. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Description

Round faces are characterized by their soft, curved lines and lack of prominent angles. If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a short haircut that helps to elongate your face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and jawline. The key is to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for round faces, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Hair Texture

If you have fine hair, you'll want to avoid short haircuts that are too choppy or textured, as this can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, opt for a blunt bob or a sleek pixie cut. If you have thick hair, you can go for more layered or textured cuts to help thin out your hair and create movement.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your short haircut is also important when it comes to flattering a round face shape. Generally, you'll want to go for a haircut that falls at or just below your chin, as this helps to elongate your face. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as these can make your face appear rounder.

Consider Your Face Shape

While round faces share certain characteristics, there are still subtle differences between different face shapes. Take a look at your face in the mirror and try to identify any other features that might influence your choice of short haircut. For example, if you have a round face and a high forehead, you might want to consider a short haircut with bangs to help balance out your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Once you've chosen your perfect short haircut, it's time to learn how to style it! Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add body. If you have thick hair, use a smoothing shampoo and conditioner to help tame frizz.

Step 2: Towel-Dry Your Hair

Gently towel-dry your hair, being careful not to rub too hard or create too much friction, which can damage your hair.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply any styling products you like, such as mousse, gel, or styling cream, to help add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections, starting at the back and working your way forward. Use the brush to help create volume and movement in your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Now that you've got your new short haircut, here are a few tips to help you maintain it:

Visit Your Stylist Regularly

To keep your short haircut looking fresh and stylish, you'll need to visit your stylist for regular trims. Depending on how fast your hair grows, this might be every 4-6 weeks.

Invest in Quality Hair Products

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. This might include shampoo, conditioner, styling products, and hair treatments.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you like to use heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to help protect your hair from damage.

FAQs

What are the best short haircuts for round faces?

Some of the best short haircuts for round faces include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob (long bob). These hairstyles help to elongate your face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and jawline.

Can I wear bangs with a short haircut for round faces?

Yes! Bangs can be a great way to balance out your features and add some dimension to your short haircut. Consider a side-swept fringe or a blunt bang to suit your face shape.

Do I need to use styling products on my short haircut?

While you don't necessarily need to use styling products on your short haircut, they can help to add texture, volume, and hold to your hair. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and style.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to help keep your short haircut looking its best:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

How often you wash your hair will depend on your hair type and lifestyle, but generally, you should aim to wash it every 2-3 days. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

Avoid Over-Styling

While styling your hair can be fun, be careful not to overdo it. Too much heat styling or chemical treatments can damage your hair and make it harder to style in the long run.

Eat a Healthy Diet

What you eat can have a big impact on the health of your hair. Make sure to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, healthy fats, and vitamins and minerals like biotin and vitamin C.

And there you have it – our guide to the best cool short haircuts for round faces! We hope you found this article helpful and that you're feeling inspired to try out a new hairstyle. Remember, the key is to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and stylish.
