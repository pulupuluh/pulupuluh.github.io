Does Short Hair Make Your Face Look Shorter

Written by Daniel Jun 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair has always been a popular hairstyle choice among women. Not only does it require less maintenance, but it also gives a chic and sophisticated look. However, many women are hesitant to try short hair because they fear it may make their face look shorter. In this article, we'll explore this topic and answer the question - does short hair make your face look shorter?

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair can indeed make your face look shorter, but it all depends on the style and your face shape. Short haircuts with blunt ends and no layers tend to make the face look wider and shorter. On the other hand, short haircuts with layers and volume can add height to the face, giving it a longer appearance.

How To

If you're considering a short haircut but are worried about it making your face look shorter, there are a few things you can do to avoid this. The first step is to determine your face shape. Different face shapes suit different hairstyles, so it's important to know your face shape before choosing a haircut. Once you know your face shape, speak to a hairstylist who can recommend short hairstyles that suit your face shape and features.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Research short hairstyles that suit your face shape
Step 3: Speak to a hairstylist who can recommend a suitable haircut
Step 4: Choose a short haircut with layers and volume to add height to your face
Step 5: Maintain your short haircut with regular trims to avoid it looking unkempt

Tips

- Avoid blunt ends and no layers in short haircuts if you have a round face
- Choose short haircuts with layers and volume if you have a square or heart-shaped face
- Consider a pixie cut if you have an oval face as it can accentuate your facial features
- Experiment with different short hairstyles to find the one that suits you best

FAQ

Q: Can short hair make my face look longer?

A: Yes, short haircuts with layers and volume can add height to your face, making it look longer.

Q: Is short hair easier to maintain than long hair?

A: Yes, short hair requires less maintenance than long hair as it doesn't tangle as easily and takes less time to wash and style.

Q: Will a bob haircut make my face look shorter?

A: It depends on the style of the bob. A bob with blunt ends and no layers can make the face look wider and shorter, while a layered bob can add height to the face.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape and avoid split ends. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using hot styling tools too frequently as they can damage your hair. Instead, opt for air drying or using a diffuser when blow-drying. Lastly, consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

In conclusion, short hair can make your face look shorter or longer, depending on the style and your face shape. Do your research, speak to a hairstylist, and experiment with different short hairstyles to find the one that suits you best. With the right haircut and hair care routine, short hair can give you a chic and sophisticated look.
