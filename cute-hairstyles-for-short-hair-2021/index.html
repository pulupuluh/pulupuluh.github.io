Cute Hairstyles For Short Hair 2021 Step By Step Guide And Tips

If you have short hair, there's no need to worry about styling options. Short hair can be versatile, cute, and easy to maintain. In this blog post, we will show you some of the best cute hairstyles for short hair 2021. We have included a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look. So, let's get started!

How to Style Short Hair

Before we dive into the cute hairstyles for short hair 2021, let's go over some general tips for styling short hair. First, make sure your hair is clean and dry before styling. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools to prevent damage to your hair. Secondly, determine your hair type and texture before choosing a hairstyle. Lastly, don't be afraid to experiment with accessories like headbands, clips, and scarfs to add a pop of color or texture to your hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic hairstyle that will never go out of style. It's perfect for short hair and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. Here's how to achieve the perfect bob cut:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts, one at the crown, one at each temple, and one at the nape of your neck.
  3. Starting at the nape of your neck, use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to your desired length.
  4. Gradually cut the hair at the crown and temples to match the length at the nape of your neck.
  5. Style your hair using a round brush and a blow dryer or a straightening iron for a sleek finish.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a popular hairstyle for short hair that is low maintenance and easy to style. Here's how to achieve the perfect pixie cut:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts, one at the crown, one at each temple, and one at the nape of your neck.
  3. Starting at the nape of your neck, use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to your desired length.
  4. Gradually cut the hair at the crown and temples to match the length at the nape of your neck.
  5. Style your hair using a texturizing spray and your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

3. Bob with Bangs

The bob with bangs is a cute hairstyle that can add a youthful and playful look to your appearance. Here's how to achieve the perfect bob with bangs:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts, one at the crown, one at each temple, and one at the nape of your neck.
  3. Starting at the nape of your neck, use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to your desired length.
  4. Gradually cut the hair at the crown and temples to match the length at the nape of your neck.
  5. Create a side part and cut your bangs to your desired length.
  6. Style your hair using a round brush and a blow dryer or a straightening iron.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your cute hairstyle for short hair 2021:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your hairstyle.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Q: Can I still style my short hair if it's too short?

A: Yes, you can still style your short hair even if it's too short. Try using accessories like headbands or clips to add some texture to your hair.

Q: Is it possible to get a cute hairstyle for short hair if I have curly hair?

A: Absolutely! There are many cute hairstyles for short hair that work well with curly hair, such as a curly bob or a messy pixie cut.

Hair Care Advice

Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too many hair products on your hair as they can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

We hope you found these cute hairstyles for short hair 2021 helpful. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair. If you have any questions or comments, feel free to leave them below.
