Can You Cut the Back of Your Own Hair? Tips and How-To

Are you tired of constantly going to the salon to get your hair trimmed or styled? Have you ever wondered if you could cut the back of your own hair? Well, the answer is yes, you can! With the right tools and technique, you can easily trim the back of your hair at home. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut the back of your own hair, along with some helpful tips and frequently asked questions.

How to Cut the Back of Your Own Hair

Cutting the back of your own hair may seem like a daunting task, but it's actually quite simple if you follow these steps:

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. It's also helpful to have a hair clip to hold sections of your hair in place.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's best to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see the areas that need to be trimmed. Use your comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into two parts - the top and the bottom. Clip the top section away from the bottom section using your hair clip.

Step 4: Trim the Bottom Section

Using your handheld mirror, hold it up to the back of your head so you can see the bottom section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold your scissors at a slight angle and trim the ends of your hair. Repeat this process for all sections of the bottom half of your hair.

Step 5: Trim the Top Section

Once you have finished trimming the bottom section, unclip the top section of your hair. Use your handheld mirror to see the back of your head and trim the top section of your hair in the same way. Be sure to blend the top and bottom sections of your hair together for a seamless look.

Step 6: Check Your Work

After you have finished trimming your hair, use your handheld mirror to check the back of your head. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your hair looks even and balanced.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting the back of your own hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Take breaks if you need to rest your arms or hands.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Be patient and take your time to avoid mistakes.

FAQ

Can I cut the back of my own hair with a razor?

We do not recommend using a razor to cut the back of your own hair. Razors can easily lead to uneven cuts and damage to your hair. Stick to using scissors for the best results.

How often should I cut the back of my own hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair growth. However, you can trim the back of your own hair as often as you feel necessary to maintain your desired hairstyle.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain healthy hair growth. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

Overall, cutting the back of your own hair is a simple and cost-effective way to maintain your hairstyle at home. With the right tools, technique, and patience, you can achieve salon-worthy results from the comfort of your own home.
