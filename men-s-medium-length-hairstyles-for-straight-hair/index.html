Men s Medium Length Hairstyles For Straight Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Apr 19, 2023 · 7 min read

Medium length hair for men has been a popular trend for a while now, and it's not going away anytime soon. It's versatile, easy to manage, and suits almost every face shape. If you have straight hair and want to try out some new hairstyles, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about men's medium length hairstyles for straight hair, including how to style them, tips, and hair care.

Description

Medium length hair for men is typically between 2 and 6 inches long, and it falls just above or below the shoulders. Straight hair is the most common hair type, and it's relatively easy to style. Men's medium length hairstyles for straight hair can range from messy to sleek and everything in between. Whether you want a casual look for everyday wear or a more polished style for a special occasion, there's a medium length hairstyle for you.

How to

Styling medium length hair for men is simple and straightforward. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair clean, healthy, and manageable.

Step 2: Towel Dry

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Don't rub your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Choose a styling product that works well with straight hair, such as a pomade or wax. Apply a small amount to your fingertips and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired. You can create a messy, textured look by tousling your hair with your fingers, or you can create a sleek, polished look by using a comb to smooth your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your medium length hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles – it's only hair, and it will grow back
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's medium length hairstyles for straight hair:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often. If you have dry hair or a more sedentary lifestyle, you may be able to wash your hair less often.

Q: Can I still style my hair if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair if you have thinning hair. Just be gentle when handling your hair, and use styling products that add volume and texture.

Q: How can I make my hair look thicker?

A: You can make your hair look thicker by using volumizing shampoos and conditioners, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, and using styling products that add volume, such as mousse or sea salt spray.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you take care of your medium length hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushing can cause breakage
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends

There you have it – everything you need to know about men's medium length hairstyles for straight hair. With the right styling products, tools, and techniques, you can achieve a variety of looks that suit your style and personality. Remember to take care of your hair, and don't be afraid to try something new!
