How to Feather Cut Hair at Home

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's quickly go over what feather cutting is. Feather cutting is a technique that involves cutting the hair at an angle to create layers that blend seamlessly together. This technique is great for adding volume and texture to the hair.

Now that you know what feather cutting is, let's get into how to do it at home.

What You'll Need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to feather cut your hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it completely. You want your hair to be dry and tangle-free before you start cutting.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Using the comb, part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts, left and right. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting with the left section, take a small section of hair from the back and hold it between your fingers. Hold the hair at a 45-degree angle and cut it with the scissors. Repeat this process for the rest of the left section.

Once you're done with the left section, move on to the right section and repeat the process.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut both sections, it's time to blend the layers. Take the comb and comb through your hair to blend the layers together.

Step 6: Check for Unevenness

Check your hair for any unevenness. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair to even it out.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired.

Tips for a Perfect Feather Cut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when feather cutting your hair:

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid split ends
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting off too much
  • Use a spray bottle to keep your hair damp and easy to work with
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it may lead to uneven cutting
  • Take your time and be patient, especially if it's your first time

FAQs

How often should I feather cut my hair?

It depends on your hair growth and the length of your hair. On average, you can feather cut your hair every 3-4 months.

Can I feather cut my own hair?

Yes, you can. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

What hair types work best for feather cutting?

Feather cutting works well for all hair types, from straight to curly.

Hair Care After Feather Cutting

After feather cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the feather cut

That's it! You now know how to feather cut your hair at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully for the best results.
