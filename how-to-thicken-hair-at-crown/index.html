How To Thicken Hair At Crown Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Apr 21, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are struggling with thinning hair at the crown of your head, you are not alone. This is a common problem that affects many people, both men and women. Luckily, there are several ways to thicken hair at crown and boost your confidence. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks to help you achieve fuller, thicker hair.

Description

The crown of your head is the area at the top, where your hair growth sprouts in different directions. It is a common area for hair loss and thinning. Thinning hair at the crown can be caused by many factors, including genetics, aging, hormonal changes, poor nutrition, stress and hairstyles that pull the hair tightly. However, there are several things you can do to thicken hair at the crown and regain your fullness and confidence.

How to Thicken Hair at Crown

If you are wondering how to thicken hair at crown, here are some tips and tricks you can try:

1. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp is an excellent way to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth. Use your fingers to massage your scalp gently for a few minutes every day. You can also use essential oils like peppermint, rosemary, or lavender to massage your scalp.

2. Use Hair Thickening Products

There are many hair thickening products available on the market that can help you achieve fuller, thicker hair. Look for products that contain biotin, keratin, collagen, or other hair-strengthening ingredients. Use them regularly as directed to see results.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Your diet plays a crucial role in the health of your hair. Eat a balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair growth and strength. Foods like eggs, nuts, leafy greens, and fish are excellent for hair health.

4. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like braids, ponytails, and buns can cause hair breakage and thinning. Avoid tight hairstyles or wear them loosely to prevent damaging your hair.

5. Try Hair Growth Supplements

Hair growth supplements like biotin, saw palmetto, or folic acid can help promote hair growth and thickness. However, consult your doctor before taking any supplements to avoid any side effects.

Step by Step

If you want to thicken hair at crown, here are the steps you can follow:

Step 1: Massage Your Scalp

Use your fingers to massage your scalp gently for a few minutes every day. You can also use essential oils like peppermint, rosemary, or lavender to massage your scalp.

Step 2: Use Hair Thickening Products

Look for hair thickening products that contain biotin, keratin, collagen, or other hair-strengthening ingredients. Use them regularly as directed to see results.

Step 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair growth and strength. Foods like eggs, nuts, leafy greens, and fish are excellent for hair health.

Step 4: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles or wear them loosely to prevent damaging your hair.

Step 5: Try Hair Growth Supplements

Consult your doctor before taking any supplements to avoid any side effects. Hair growth supplements like biotin, saw palmetto, or folic acid can help promote hair growth and thickness.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you thicken hair at crown:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can prevent split ends and breakage, which can lead to thinning hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from Sun Damage

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to prevent sun damage to your hair, which can cause hair thinning and breakage.

FAQ

1. Can stress cause thinning hair at the crown?

Yes, stress can cause hair loss and thinning, including at the crown of your head. Try to manage your stress levels through relaxation techniques like yoga or meditation.

2. Is hair thinning at the crown permanent?

No, hair thinning at the crown is not always permanent. With the right treatment and care, you can regain your hair's fullness and thickness.

3. Can I use hair thickening products every day?

Yes, you can use hair thickening products every day as directed. However, avoid overusing them, as it can cause product buildup and weigh down your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, thick hair. Here are some hair care tips for thickening hair at crown:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is sulfate-free and pH-balanced to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner that is designed for your hair type to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently, starting from the ends and working your way up to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.

4. Air Dry Your Hair

Air dry your hair whenever possible to avoid heat damage from blow dryers and other styling tools.

In conclusion, thinning hair at the crown is a common problem, but it is not permanent. By following these tips and tricks and taking good care of your hair, you can thicken hair at crown and regain your confidence.
