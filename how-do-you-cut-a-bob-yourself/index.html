How To Cut A Bob Yourself Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of going to the salon for a simple haircut? Or maybe you're looking to save some money? Whatever the reason may be, cutting a bob yourself is a great option. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to cut a bob yourself. Let's get started!

How to Cut a Bob Yourself

Cutting a bob yourself may seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to cut a bob yourself:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.
  2. Section your hair. Use a comb to create a middle parting and then divide your hair into two sections, one on each side of your head. Secure each section with a hair tie.
  3. Start cutting. Take one section of hair and use your scissors to cut it to your desired length. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Blend the layers. Take a small section of hair from the back and cut it to the same length as the sides. This will create a blended look.
  5. Cut the back. Unclip the back section of your hair and comb it straight down. Cut it to your desired length.
  6. Check for symmetry. Use your mirror to check that both sides are even.
  7. Add layers (optional). If you want to add layers, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle.
  8. Trim the ends. Use your scissors to trim any uneven ends.
  9. Use clippers (optional). If you want a shorter bob, use clippers to trim the back and sides.
  10. Style your new bob! Use a hair straightener or curling iron to create your desired look.

Congratulations, you've just cut a bob yourself!

Tips for Cutting a Bob Yourself

  • Start with small cuts. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp. Dull scissors can damage your hair.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair appears longer and can result in a shorter cut than intended.
  • Use hair clips to keep the sections of hair separate.
  • Take breaks. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so take breaks when you need to.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut a bob with curly hair?

Yes, you can cut a bob with curly hair. However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so don't cut it too short.

Should I use clippers to cut a bob?

Clippers are optional when cutting a bob. They can be useful if you want a shorter bob or if you're having trouble cutting the back of your hair.

How often should I cut my bob?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your bob.

Hair Care After Cutting a Bob

Now that you've cut your bob, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain the color and health of your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your bob.

Now that you know how to cut a bob yourself, you can save time and money by doing it at home. Just remember to take your time, start with small cuts, and use the right tools. Happy cutting!
