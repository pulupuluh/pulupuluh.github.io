How To Cut Layers For A Round Face

Written by Luffy Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering how to cut layers that will flatter your features. Fortunately, there are several strategies and techniques that can help you achieve the perfect look. In this article, we'll discuss some tips and tricks for cutting layers for a round face, as well as some hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Layers for a Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Layers for a Round Face
  • FAQ

How to Cut Layers for a Round Face

The key to cutting layers for a round face is to create height and length in the hair. This can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced appearance. Here are some tips for achieving this look:

  • Opt for longer layers that start below the chin. This will help to create length in the face and balance out the roundness.
  • Consider adding face-framing layers around the front of the face. This can help to draw attention away from the roundness and create a more angular appearance.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheekbones. This can make the face look wider and rounder.
  • Try a side-swept fringe to create a more angular look.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension in the hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for cutting layers for a round face:

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair. Make sure it is completely dry before you begin cutting.
  2. Section the hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back.
  3. Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use scissors to cut the hair at a slight angle, creating a longer layer that starts below the chin.
  4. Continue cutting small sections of hair in this manner until you reach the top of the head.
  5. Move on to the side sections and repeat the same process, cutting longer layers that start below the chin.
  6. Finally, move on to the top section and cut longer layers that start at the crown of the head and angle down towards the front.
  7. Once you've finished cutting, style the hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Layers for a Round Face

Here are some additional tips for cutting layers for a round face:

  • Consider consulting with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the best cut and style for your face shape.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to make small cuts and gradually build up the layers.
  • Take breaks and step back to assess your progress. This can help you avoid over-cutting and creating an uneven look.
  • Experiment with different styles and cuts to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting layers for a round face:

  • Can I still have short hair with a round face? Absolutely! The key is to create height and volume in the hair to balance out the roundness of the face.
  • What should I avoid when cutting layers for a round face? Blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheekbones can make the face look wider and rounder. It's also best to avoid layers that start at the cheekbones or jawline.
  • How often should I trim my hair? It's recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Layered Hair

Once you've cut your layers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining layered hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle.

By following these tips and techniques, you can cut layers that flatter a round face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure about how to cut your hair, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
