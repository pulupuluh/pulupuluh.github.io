Black Celebrities With Short Blonde Hair A Trendy Look

Written by Bardi May 10, 2023 · 6 min read

If you are looking for a bold and daring hairstyle, you might want to consider black celebrities with short blonde hair. This unique hair color and style combination has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it’s easy to see why. Short blonde hair can help black women stand out and make a statement. In this article, we will explore the world of black celebrities with short blonde hair, how to get the look, and some tips for maintaining it.

Table of Contents:

  • Black Celebrities with Short Blonde Hair
  • How to Get the Look
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Blonde Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Short Blonde Hair

Black Celebrities with Short Blonde Hair

One of the most iconic black celebrities with short blonde hair is Beyoncé. She has rocked everything from a platinum blonde bob to a honey blonde pixie cut. Other famous black women who have tried the look include Rihanna, Nicki Minaj, and Keke Palmer.

It’s not just singers and actresses who have experimented with this trend. Athletes like Serena Williams and models like Chanel Iman have also been spotted with short blonde hair.

How to Get the Look

The first step to getting the perfect short blonde hair is to find a stylist who specializes in this type of hair. They will be able to help you choose the right shade of blonde and cut that will suit your face shape and skin tone.

You should also be prepared for the upkeep that comes with short blonde hair. This includes regular salon visits for touch-ups and trims, as well as using special products to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting short blonde hair:

  1. Find a stylist who specializes in short blonde hair
  2. Choose the right shade of blonde for your skin tone
  3. Decide on a cut that will suit your face shape
  4. Be prepared for regular salon visits for touch-ups and trims
  5. Use special products to keep your hair healthy and vibrant

Tips for Maintaining Short Blonde Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining short blonde hair:

  • Use a purple shampoo to prevent brassy tones
  • Avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant

FAQ

What shade of blonde should I choose?

The shade of blonde you choose will depend on your skin tone. Warm skin tones look best with honey or caramel blonde, while cool skin tones look best with platinum or ash blonde.

How often should I get my hair touched up?

You should get your hair touched up every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color and shape of your cut.

Can I still wear extensions with short blonde hair?

Absolutely! You can still wear extensions with short blonde hair. Just make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and cut.

Hair Care for Short Blonde Hair

Here are some tips for caring for short blonde hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage
  • Avoid excessive heat styling to prevent breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a weekly deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and strong
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or UV protectant

Short blonde hair is a bold and daring hairstyle that can help black women stand out from the crowd. With the right stylist and care, you can achieve this trendy look and feel confident and beautiful.
