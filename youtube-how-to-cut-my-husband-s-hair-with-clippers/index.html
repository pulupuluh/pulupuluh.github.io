Youtube How To Cut My Husband s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 02, 2023 · 7 min read
Are you tired of spending money on your husband's haircuts? Do you want to learn how to cut his hair yourself? With the help of YouTube, cutting your husband's hair with clippers is easier than you think. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your husband's hair with clippers, along with tips on how to care for his hair afterwards.

Description

Cutting your husband's hair with clippers can be a daunting task, especially if you have never done it before. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. In this tutorial, we will be using a set of clippers, scissors, and a comb to create a classic short back and sides haircut.

How To

Before you begin, make sure that your husband's hair is clean and dry. It's also important to choose the right length guard for the clippers, depending on how short your husband wants his hair. Once you have all your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with the Sides

Begin by using the clippers with the chosen length guard to trim the sides of your husband's hair. Start at the bottom of the hairline, and work your way up to the temple. Use the comb to guide the clippers and create an even length.

Step 2: Move to the Back

Next, move to the back of your husband's head and use the same technique to trim the hair. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the crown.

Step 3: Trim the Top

Once the sides and back are trimmed, it's time to move on to the top of the head. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length, and then use the clippers to blend the top with the sides and back.

Step 4: Clean Up the Edges

Use the clippers without a guard to clean up the edges around the hairline, sideburns, and neck. Be careful not to cut too close to the skin.

Tips

- Make sure that your husband is sitting still and looking straight ahead while you cut his hair. - Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure that the haircut is even. - If you're unsure about the length of the guard to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down. - Clean your tools after each use to prevent the spread of bacteria.

FAQ

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my husband's hair?
A: While you can use regular scissors, it's best to use hair cutting scissors, as they are sharper and more precise. Q: Can I cut my husband's hair wet or dry?
A: It's best to cut hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly. Q: How often should I cut my husband's hair?
A: This depends on how quickly his hair grows and how short he wants it. A good rule of thumb is to cut it every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your husband's hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips: - Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for his hair type. - Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers or straighteners too often, as this can damage the hair. - Trim the ends of the hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends. - Encourage your husband to eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water, as this can promote healthy hair growth.

Conclusion

Cutting your husband's hair with clippers can seem intimidating at first, but with the help of YouTube and our step-by-step guide, it's easier than you think. By following our tips and taking care of his hair afterwards, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. So why not give it a try? Your husband will thank you for it!
