How To Make Big Southern Hair Bows

Written by Oliver Jul 10, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re looking to add a touch of southern charm to your hairdo, then big southern hair bows are the way to go. These oversized bows are a staple of southern style and can be worn for any occasion, whether it’s a wedding, a church service, or a day out with friends.

Description

Big southern hair bows are typically made from ribbon or fabric and can be as large or as small as you want them to be. They’re often adorned with embellishments like pearls, rhinestones, or feathers to make them even more eye-catching.

How to Make Big Southern Hair Bows

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make your own big southern hair bows:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

To make a big southern hair bow, you’ll need:

  • Ribbon or fabric
  • Scissors
  • Hot glue gun
  • Embellishments (optional)

Step 2: Cut Your Ribbon or Fabric

Cut your ribbon or fabric to the desired length. For a large bow, you’ll want your ribbon to be at least 2 inches wide and 36 inches long. For a smaller bow, you can use narrower ribbon and cut it to a shorter length.

Step 3: Fold Your Ribbon or Fabric

Take one end of your ribbon or fabric and fold it over to create a loop. Pinch the loop in the middle to create a crease, then repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Tie Your Ribbon or Fabric

Take a small piece of ribbon or string and tie it tightly around the middle of your folded ribbon or fabric. This will create the center of your bow and hold it in place.

Step 5: Fluff Your Bow

Starting from the center of your bow, carefully fluff out each loop until you have a full, fluffy bow. You may need to adjust the loops to make them even.

Step 6: Add Embellishments (Optional)

If you want to add some extra sparkle to your bow, you can glue on embellishments like pearls, rhinestones, or feathers. Just be careful not to overdo it – a big southern hair bow is already a statement piece!

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when making your big southern hair bows:

  • Use a hot glue gun to secure your bow in place – regular glue may not be strong enough.
  • Experiment with different fabrics and ribbon to create different looks.
  • Don’t be afraid to make your bow extra large – big southern hair bows are all about making a statement.

FAQ

Q: How do I wear a big southern hair bow?

A: Big southern hair bows can be worn in a variety of ways. You can clip them onto a ponytail, wear them on the side of your head, or attach them to a headband.

Q: How do I store my big southern hair bow?

A: To keep your bow looking its best, store it in a box or a drawer where it won’t get crushed or flattened.

Hair Care

When wearing a big southern hair bow, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to make your own big southern hair bows and rock this classic southern style with confidence.
