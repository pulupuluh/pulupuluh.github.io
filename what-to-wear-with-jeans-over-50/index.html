What To Wear With Jeans Over 50 A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 22, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're over 50, you may be wondering what to wear with jeans. Jeans are a classic wardrobe staple that never goes out of style, but it can be challenging to find the right outfit to match them with. In this guide, we'll give you tips on how to style your jeans, what to wear with them, and some frequently asked questions.

What to Wear with Jeans over 50

Jeans are versatile and can be paired with a variety of items. Here are some outfit ideas to get you started:

1. Blazer and Heels

A blazer and heels can dress up your jeans for a more formal occasion. Choose a blazer in a neutral color such as black, gray, or navy, and pair it with black or nude heels. You can wear a blouse or a simple top underneath the blazer.

2. Sweater and Sneakers

A sweater and sneakers make for a comfortable and casual outfit. You can wear a sweater in a solid color or with a pattern, and pair it with white sneakers or a pair of Converse. This outfit is perfect for running errands or a day out with friends.

3. Tunic and Leggings

A tunic and leggings are a great way to dress up your jeans while still being comfortable. Choose a tunic in a bold color or pattern, and pair it with black leggings and black ankle boots. This outfit is perfect for a night out or a dinner party.

4. Denim on Denim

Denim on denim is a fun and trendy outfit that works well with jeans. Choose a denim jacket or shirt and pair it with jeans in a different shade. You can wear a simple white t-shirt underneath and a pair of ankle boots or sneakers.

How to Style Your Jeans

Here are some tips on how to style your jeans:

1. Choose the Right Fit

Make sure your jeans fit well and are comfortable. They should be snug around the waist but not too tight, and the length should be just right. If you're unsure about the fit, try on different sizes and styles to find the perfect pair.

2. Dress Them Up or Down

Jeans can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. You can wear them with heels and a blazer for a formal event or with sneakers and a t-shirt for a casual outing.

3. Accessorize

Add accessories to your jeans to complete your outfit. You can wear a statement necklace, a scarf, or a belt to add some interest.

4. Choose the Right Shoes

The right shoes can make or break your outfit. Choose shoes that match the occasion and your outfit. You can wear heels for a formal event, sneakers for a casual outing, or ankle boots for a night out.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your jeans:

1. Choose Your Jeans

Pick a pair of jeans that fit well and are comfortable.

2. Choose Your Outfit

Decide on the occasion and choose your outfit accordingly. You can dress up your jeans with a blazer and heels, or dress them down with a t-shirt and sneakers.

3. Add Accessories

Add accessories to complete your outfit. You can wear a statement necklace, a scarf, or a belt.

4. Choose Your Shoes

Pick the right shoes for the occasion and your outfit. You can wear heels, sneakers, or ankle boots.

Tips

Here are some additional tips:

1. Experiment with Different Styles

Try on different styles of jeans to find the perfect fit for your body type.

2. Don't Be Afraid of Color

Don't be afraid to wear bright colors or patterns. They can add some interest to your outfit.

3. Keep it Simple

A simple outfit can be just as stylish as a more complicated one. Stick to basic pieces and add some interest with accessories.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions:

1. Can I Wear Jeans to a Formal Event?

Yes, you can wear jeans to a formal event if you pair them with a blazer and heels.

2. What Shoes Should I Wear with Jeans?

You can wear heels, sneakers, or ankle boots with jeans depending on the occasion and your outfit.

3. Can I Wear Leggings with Jeans?

Yes, you can wear leggings with jeans. Pair them with a tunic and ankle boots for a stylish outfit.

Hair Care

When styling your jeans, don't forget about your hair. A simple hairstyle can complete your outfit. Here are some tips:

1. Keep it Simple

A simple hairstyle like a ponytail or a bun can be just as stylish as a more complicated one.

2. Add Some Interest

You can add some interest to your hairstyle with a braid or a hair accessory.

3. Consider Your Outfit

Choose a hairstyle that matches your outfit and the occasion.

Now that you know what to wear with jeans over 50, you can create stylish outfits that are both comfortable and chic. Remember to choose the right fit, accessorize, and pick the right shoes for the occasion. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and colors, and keep your hair simple and chic.
