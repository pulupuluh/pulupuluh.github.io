Is Synthetic Or Human Hair Extensions Better

Written by Petter Aug 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are looking to add length or volume to your hair, hair extensions are a great way to do so. However, with so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right type of hair extensions for your hair. One of the biggest decisions you will need to make is whether to choose synthetic or human hair extensions. In this article, we will discuss which one is better and why.

Description

Synthetic hair extensions are made from synthetic fibers such as polyester, acrylic, and PVC, while human hair extensions are made from real human hair. Synthetic hair extensions are usually cheaper than human hair extensions, but they do not look as natural and do not last as long. Human hair extensions, on the other hand, are more expensive but look and feel like real hair, and can be styled and treated just like your natural hair.

How to Choose the Right Hair Extensions

When choosing hair extensions, there are several factors to consider. Firstly, you need to decide whether you want synthetic or human hair extensions. If you are looking for a temporary solution or do not want to spend too much money, synthetic hair extensions are a good option. However, if you want a more natural look and are willing to invest in high-quality hair, human hair extensions are the way to go.

Other factors to consider include the length and texture of the hair, the color of the hair, and the type of attachment method used. It is important to choose hair extensions that match your natural hair texture and color to avoid any noticeable differences.

Step by Step Guide on Applying Hair Extensions

Applying hair extensions can be a bit tricky, but with some practice, it can be done at home. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to apply hair extensions:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly.
  2. Section your hair where you want to apply the extensions.
  3. Take one extension and apply a small amount of hair glue or tape to the weft.
  4. Place the extension close to your scalp and press it firmly in place.
  5. Repeat the process until all the extensions are applied.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Hair Extensions

To ensure that your hair extensions last as long as possible, it is important to take good care of them. Here are some tips for maintaining hair extensions:

  • Brush your hair extensions regularly to prevent tangling and matting.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools on your extensions as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair extensions.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products on your extensions.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long do hair extensions last?

Human hair extensions can last up to 6 months with proper care, while synthetic hair extensions typically last between 2-4 months.

2. Can I dye my hair extensions?

Yes, you can dye human hair extensions just like your natural hair. However, it is not recommended to dye synthetic hair extensions as they can be damaged.

3. Can I swim with hair extensions?

Yes, you can swim with hair extensions, but it is recommended to wear a swim cap to protect them from chlorine and saltwater.

Hair Care Tips for Hair Extensions

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair extensions. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair extensions:

  • Keep your hair extensions clean by washing them regularly.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your extensions.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heated styling tools.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair extensions.
  • Trim your hair extensions regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, whether to choose synthetic or human hair extensions depends on your personal preference and budget. While synthetic hair extensions are cheaper, they do not look as natural or last as long as human hair extensions. Human hair extensions are more expensive, but they look and feel like real hair and can be styled and treated just like your natural hair. With proper care and maintenance, hair extensions can be a great way to add length and volume to your hair.
