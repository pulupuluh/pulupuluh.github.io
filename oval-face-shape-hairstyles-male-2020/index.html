Oval Face Shape Hairstyles For Men In 2023

Written by Robby Mar 04, 2023 Â· 11 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect hairstyle that suits your personality, style, and face shape. In this article, we'll discuss the top oval face shape hairstyles for men in 2023, along with tips on how to style them and maintain them.

How to Determine if You Have an Oval Face Shape

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's essential to determine if you have an oval face shape. An oval-shaped face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded hairline and jawline. Your forehead and jawline are slightly narrower than your cheekbones, and your face is symmetrical. If you're still unsure, take a ruler and measure the length and width of your face. If your face length is 1.5 times your face width, you have an oval face shape.

Top Oval Face Shape Hairstyles for Men in 2023

1. Short Textured Crop

The short textured crop is a modern and stylish hairstyle that works well for oval face shapes. The textured layers add volume and dimension to your hair, while the short length keeps it low maintenance. You can style this haircut with a side part, messy fringe, or slicked back look, depending on your preference.

2. Classic Side Part

The classic side part is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. It works well for oval face shapes, as it adds definition to your face and creates a sharp, sophisticated look. You can pair this haircut with a well-groomed beard or go clean-shaven for a more professional look.

3. Medium Length Waves

If you have wavy or curly hair, the medium-length waves hairstyle is perfect for you. This hairstyle adds volume and texture to your hair, creating a relaxed, beachy vibe. You can style this haircut with a side part or a messy, tousled look for a more laid-back appearance.

4. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic and low-maintenance hairstyle that works well for oval face shapes. It highlights your facial features and creates a clean, sharp look. You can opt for a short buzz cut or a longer one, depending on your preference.

5. Slicked Back Undercut

The slicked-back undercut is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that works well for oval face shapes. The undercut creates a contrast between the short sides and the longer top, while the slicked-back look adds a touch of sophistication. You can pair this haircut with a well-groomed beard or go clean-shaven for a more dramatic look.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Your Oval Face Shape Hairstyle

Once you've chosen your hairstyle, it's essential to know how to style it correctly. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your oval face shape hairstyle:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

2. Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a hair gel, wax, or pomade, to your hair. Start from the roots and work your way up to the tips, ensuring every strand is coated evenly.

3. Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair according to your preference. You can create a side part, slicked-back look, messy fringe, or any other style you like.

4. Finish with Hair Spray

Finish your hairstyle with a light mist of hair spray to keep it in place throughout the day. Use a high-quality hair spray that doesn't leave residue or build-up on your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Oval Face Shape Hairstyle

To keep your hairstyle looking fresh and stylish, here are some tips to follow:

1. Visit Your Barber Regularly

Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut's shape and length. This will ensure your hairstyle looks sharp and well-groomed.

2. Use High-Quality Hair Products

Use high-quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type and style. This will ensure your hair looks healthy, shiny, and well-styled.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hairdryers, flat irons, or curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize the damage.

FAQ About Oval Face Shape Hairstyles for Men in 2023

1. Can I pull off any hairstyle with an oval face shape?

Yes, oval face shapes are considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, it's essential to choose a hairstyle that suits your personality, style, and face shape.

2. What is the best haircut for oval face shapes?

The best haircut for oval face shapes depends on your preference and style. However, some popular hairstyles include the short textured crop, classic side part, medium-length waves, buzz cut, and slicked-back undercut.

3. How do I style my oval face shape hairstyle?

To style your oval face shape hairstyle, wash your hair, apply styling product, style your hair according to your preference, and finish with hair spray.

4. How often should I visit my barber to maintain my oval face shape hairstyle?

You should visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your oval face shape hairstyle's shape and length.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Oval Face Shapes

Here are some hair care tips for men with oval face shapes:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. This will keep your hair clean, healthy, and well-conditioned.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight for extended periods, as it can damage your hair and scalp. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated and flush out toxins from your body.

In conclusion, choosing the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape can be daunting, but with our guide, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits your personality and style. Remember to follow our tips on how to style and maintain your hairstyle, and you'll have a fresh and stylish look that will turn heads everywhere you go.
