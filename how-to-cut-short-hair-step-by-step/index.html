How To Cut Short Hair Step By Step A Guide For Beginners

Written by Christine Apr 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting short hair can be intimidating if you've never done it before, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and flattering haircut at home. In this step-by-step guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting short hair, from sectioning and trimming to styling and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • Sectioning Your Hair
  • Trimming Your Hair
  • Blow Drying and Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Cutting Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

Sectioning Your Hair

The first step in cutting short hair is to section off your hair into manageable sections. Use clips or hair ties to divide your hair into four or more sections, depending on the thickness of your hair. Start by dividing your hair down the middle and then from ear to ear.

Once you have your hair sectioned off, wet your hair thoroughly. This will make it easier to see the lines and angles you want to cut, and it will also help prevent split ends and breakage.

Trimming Your Hair

Start by trimming the hair at the nape of your neck. This area should be the shortest part of your hair, so use your clippers or scissors to trim it to the desired length. Work your way up the back of your head, using a comb to lift each section of hair as you go.

Next, move on to the sides of your head. Use your comb to lift each section of hair straight out from your head, and then trim the hair to the desired length. Be sure to keep the length consistent on both sides.

Finally, trim the hair on the top of your head. Use your comb to lift each section of hair straight up from your head, and then trim the hair to the desired length. You can also use texturizing scissors to add volume and texture to the top of your hair.

Blow Drying and Styling Your Hair

Once you've finished trimming your hair, it's time to blow dry and style it. Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair, and use a flat iron or curling iron to create curls or waves. Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. Using dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve a clean, even cut.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting short hair requires precision and attention to detail, so don't rush the process.
  • Use a mirror to check your work. Make sure both sides of your hair are even and that you haven't missed any spots.
  • Consider getting a second opinion. If you're not confident in your cutting skills, ask a friend or family member to help you or visit a professional stylist.

FAQs

How often should I cut my short hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, it's a good idea to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Can I cut my short hair with clippers?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut your hair, but be sure to use the right guards and attachments for the length you want. Clippers are best for cutting the hair at the nape of your neck and around your ears.

What's the best way to style short hair?

The best way to style short hair depends on your hair type and personal style. Experiment with different products and tools to find the ones that work best for you. Some popular styling options for short hair include blow drying with a round brush, using a flat iron or curling iron to create curls or waves, and using pomade or wax to add texture and definition.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and using the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering short haircut that suits your personal style and lifestyle. Happy cutting!
