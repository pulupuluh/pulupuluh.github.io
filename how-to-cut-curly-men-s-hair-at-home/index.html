How To Cut Curly Men s Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts for your curly locks? With the right tools and techniques, cutting curly men's hair at home can be a breeze. This guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve a fresh look from the comfort of your own home.

How To

The first step in cutting curly men's hair at home is to gather the necessary tools. You will need sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clippers if you prefer a shorter cut.

Next, it's important to keep in mind that curly hair should be cut dry. Unlike straight hair, curly hair can appear shorter when wet, so cutting it while dry will allow for a more accurate cut.

Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: the top, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to hold each section in place as you work.

Begin with the top section and comb through it thoroughly. Use your scissors to cut small sections at a time, starting with the longest hairs and working your way towards the shorter hairs.

When cutting the sides and back, use the same technique of cutting small sections at a time. Make sure to blend the sides into the top section for a seamless look.

If you prefer a shorter cut, use hair clippers to achieve your desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach your desired length.

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Tools

Before starting, make sure you have sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clippers (if desired).

Step 2: Section Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to hold each section in place as you work.

Step 3: Comb Through Hair

Comb through each section thoroughly to ensure all knots and tangles are removed.

Step 4: Cut Top Section

Starting with the top section, cut small sections at a time, starting with the longest hairs and working your way towards the shorter hairs.

Step 5: Cut Sides and Back

Using the same technique, cut small sections of the sides and back, blending them into the top section for a seamless look.

Step 6: Use Clippers (Optional)

If you prefer a shorter cut, use hair clippers to achieve your desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach your desired length.

Tips

Cutting curly men's hair at home can be tricky, but these tips will help make the process easier:

  • Start with small sections to avoid taking off too much hair
  • Work slowly and carefully to achieve a precise cut
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help or seek out tutorials online
  • Invest in quality tools to ensure a clean cut

FAQ

Can I cut my curly hair wet?

No, cutting curly hair while wet can result in a shorter cut than desired. It's best to cut curly hair while dry.

Do I need hair clippers?

No, hair clippers are optional, but can be helpful for achieving a shorter cut.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to cut curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy locks and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your curly hair at home, it's important to take care of your locks to maintain a healthy appearance. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair to keep your locks nourished and moisturized.

Avoid using heat styling tools and instead embrace your natural curls with a diffuser. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and prevent frizz.

Cutting curly men's hair at home may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a fresh look from the comfort of your own home. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to achieve healthy, stylish locks.
