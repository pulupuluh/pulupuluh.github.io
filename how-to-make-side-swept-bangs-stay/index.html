How To Make Side Swept Bangs Stay A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Side swept bangs are a trendy and fashionable hairstyle that can give a fresh and youthful look to your face. However, many people struggle with keeping them in place throughout the day. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make side swept bangs stay, as well as some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you maintain this style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Side Swept Bangs Stay
  • Tips for Keeping Your Side Swept Bangs in Place
  • FAQs About Side Swept Bangs
  • Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

How to Make Side Swept Bangs Stay

Follow these steps to help your side swept bangs stay in place all day long:

Step 1: Start with Clean and Dry Hair

Before you style your side swept bangs, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help prevent any excess oil or dirt from weighing down your bangs and causing them to fall flat. If your hair is oily, consider using a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil before styling your bangs.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or hairspray, to your bangs before blow-drying them. This will help add volume and hold to your hair, making it easier to style and keeping your bangs in place.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Bangs

Use a round brush and a blow dryer to blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go. This will help set the style and keep your bangs in place.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron

Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or flyaways in your bangs. This will help create a sleek and polished look and keep your bangs in place.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, spray your bangs with hairspray to help hold them in place. Choose a hairspray that provides a strong hold without making your hair feel crunchy or stiff.

Tips for Keeping Your Side Swept Bangs in Place

Here are some additional tips to help your side swept bangs stay in place:

  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, as this can cause them to become oily and fall flat.
  • Consider using bobby pins or hair clips to secure your bangs in place if they start to fall out of position.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your bangs from becoming tangled or frizzy while you sleep.
  • Don't overuse styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make your bangs fall flat.

FAQs About Side Swept Bangs

Here are some frequently asked questions about side swept bangs:

Q: How long should my side swept bangs be?

A: The length of your side swept bangs depends on your face shape and personal preference. Generally, they should be long enough to cover your forehead but not so long that they obstruct your vision.

Q: Can I style my side swept bangs with a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron to add some texture and wave to your side swept bangs. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray before styling to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my side swept bangs?

A: It's recommended to trim your side swept bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from becoming too long and heavy.

Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

Here are some general hair care tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful side swept bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of excess oil and dirt.
  • Use a conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve beautiful and long-lasting side swept bangs that will elevate your style and give you a fresh and youthful look.
