How To Hide Bangs On A Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're someone who loves to wear wigs, you may have encountered the challenge of hiding bangs on a wig. While some people love the look of bangs, others prefer to have a more versatile hairstyle that allows them to experiment with different looks. Whatever your reason for wanting to hide bangs on a wig, we've got you covered with this easy-to-follow guide.

Description

When you purchase a wig, it may come with bangs that you don't want or that don't suit your face shape. Hiding the bangs can be tricky, but with a few simple steps, you can achieve a seamless, natural look that will allow you to style your wig in any way you want.

How to Hide Bangs on a Wig

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step to hiding bangs on a wig is to gather all the tools you'll need. You'll need a wig comb, bobby pins, and hairspray. You may also want to have a wig cap on hand to help keep your hair in place.

Step 2: Comb Your Wig

Using your wig comb, gently comb the hair on your wig to remove any tangles or knots. Be careful not to tug too hard on the hair as this can cause damage to the wig.

Step 3: Part Your Wig

Using your wig comb, create a part in the hair where you want your bangs to be. Use the comb to smooth out any bumps or lumps in the hair.

Step 4: Pin Your Bangs Back

Using bobby pins, gently pin your bangs back behind the part you created. Make sure to use enough pins to hold the hair securely in place. You may also want to use hairspray to help keep the hair in place.

Step 5: Style Your Wig

Now that your bangs are hidden, you're free to style your wig however you like. You can create a sleek, straight look, add curls or waves, or even braid your wig for a fun, bohemian vibe.

Tips

  • When purchasing a wig, look for one that has a lace front or monofilament top. These types of wigs allow for more versatility in styling and make it easier to hide bangs.
  • Be gentle when combing your wig to avoid damaging the hair fibers.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Can I cut the bangs on my wig?

Yes, you can cut the bangs on your wig if you prefer a different style. However, be careful when cutting the hair to avoid cutting too much or creating uneven layers.

What type of wig should I look for to hide bangs?

Look for a wig with a lace front or monofilament top. These types of wigs allow for more versatility in styling and make it easier to hide bangs.

How do I care for my wig?

When caring for your wig, be gentle when combing or styling the hair to avoid damaging the fibers. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and soft. Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape.

Hair Care

When wearing a wig, it's important to take care of your natural hair to avoid damage or breakage. Wear a wig cap to help protect your hair from rubbing against the wig. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using heat styling tools on your natural hair, as this can cause damage. If you're experiencing hair loss or thinning, talk to your doctor or a hair care professional for advice on how to care for your hair.
