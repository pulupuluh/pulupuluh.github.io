How To Keep Hair Set Without Gel Tips And Tricks

Written by William May 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're tired of using gel to keep your hair set, you're not alone. Many people find gel to be too heavy, sticky, or simply ineffective. Luckily, there are plenty of other ways to keep your hair in place without resorting to gel. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for keeping your hair set without gel. Whether you have short hair, long hair, curly hair, or straight hair, we've got you covered.

  2. Step-by-Step Method
  3. Tips for Keeping Hair Set Without Gel

How to Keep Hair Set Without Gel

There are many ways to keep your hair set without gel. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Use a hair spray

Hair spray is a great alternative to gel, as it can help keep your hair in place without leaving a sticky residue. Look for a hairspray that is labeled as "firm hold" or "strong hold," as these will provide the most staying power. Spray the hairspray onto your hair from a distance of about 10-12 inches, being sure to cover all of your hair evenly.

2. Try a mousse

Mousse is another great alternative to gel, as it provides hold without leaving a sticky residue. Apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to damp hair, then blow dry your hair with a round brush for added volume and hold.

3. Use a hair wax

Hair wax is a good option for shorter hair, as it provides hold and texture without weighing down your hair. Warm up a small amount of hair wax in your palms, then apply it to your hair, working it through from roots to ends.

4. Try a hair pomade

Hair pomade is similar to hair wax, but it provides a shinier finish. It's a good option for longer hair, as it can help keep your hair in place without making it look stiff or crunchy. Apply a small amount of hair pomade to your hair, then use a comb to style it as desired.

Step-by-Step Method

Here's a step-by-step method for keeping your hair set without gel:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup that may prevent your hair from staying in place.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a styling product of your choice (hairspray, mousse, hair wax, or hair pomade).
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush for added volume and hold.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  5. Finish with a final spritz of hairspray to lock your style in place.

Tips for Keeping Hair Set Without Gel

Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair set without gel:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying your hair to prevent damage.
  • Don't apply too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and style.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause it to lose its shape and volume.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping hair set without gel:

Can I use hairspray and mousse together?

Yes, you can use hairspray and mousse together to achieve maximum hold and volume. Apply the mousse to damp hair, then blow dry with a round brush. Once your hair is dry, spray with hairspray to lock your style in place.

Can I use hair wax on long hair?

Yes, you can use hair wax on long hair, but it may not provide enough hold on its own. Consider using a hair pomade or hairspray in addition to the hair wax for added hold.

Hair Care Tips

Keeping your hair set without gel is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools (such as curling irons and straighteners) too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair set without using gel. Whether you prefer hairspray, mousse, hair wax, or hair pomade, there's a product out there that will work for you. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.
