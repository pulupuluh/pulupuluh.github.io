Why Does My Hair Look Better Up Than Down

Written by Robby Sep 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

In today's world, hair is an important aspect of an individual's appearance. People spend a lot of time and money on hair care, styling, and grooming. But, there are times when you may feel that your hair looks better when it's up than when it's down. So, what's the reason behind this? Let's find out.

Why Does Hair Look Better Up?

Hair looks better up due to various reasons, such as:

  • Face Shape: The way your hair falls and frames your face depends on your face shape. Certain hairstyles may not suit your face shape, making it look less flattering. When you tie your hair up, you can create a more flattering and polished look.
  • Hair Texture: Individuals with thick and curly hair may find it difficult to manage their hair when it's down. Tying it up makes it easier to manage and maintain. It also gives a more polished and professional look.
  • Activity: When you're engaged in physical activities such as exercise, cooking, or cleaning, tying your hair up helps to keep it out of your face and prevents it from getting tangled or damaged.
  • Weather: In hot and humid weather, hair can become frizzy and unmanageable. Tying it up helps to keep it in place and prevents it from becoming a distraction.

How to Tie Your Hair Up?

Tying your hair up can be done in various ways, such as:

  1. Ponytail: Gather your hair at the back of your head and tie it with a hair elastic. You can either leave the ponytail as it is or wrap a strand of hair around the elastic to hide it.
  2. Bun: Gather your hair at the crown of your head and twist it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins or a hair elastic. You can also create a messy bun by pulling out a few strands of hair to frame your face.
  3. Braid: Divide your hair into three sections and weave it into a braid. Tie the end with a hair elastic. You can create different types of braids, such as a fishtail braid or a French braid, for a more intricate look.

Step by Step Guide:

Here's a step by step guide on how to tie your hair up:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair at the desired location (e.g., crown of your head for a bun or at the back of your head for a ponytail).
  3. Secure your hair with a hair elastic or bobby pins.
  4. If you want to hide the hair elastic, take a strand of hair from the ponytail or bun and wrap it around the elastic. Secure it with a bobby pin.
  5. Adjust your hair to create the desired look. You can pull out a few strands of hair to frame your face or create a messy look.

Tips for Tying Your Hair Up:

Here are some tips for tying your hair up:

  • Avoid tying your hair up too tight as it can cause hair breakage and damage.
  • Use hair elastics that are gentle on your hair and don't cause any damage.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to create a unique look.
  • Consider using hair accessories such as headbands, clips, or scarfs to add some style to your hair.

FAQs:

Q. Is it bad to tie your hair up every day?

A. Tying your hair up every day can cause hair breakage and damage. It's best to alternate between wearing your hair up and down to prevent damage.

Q. Can tying your hair up cause hair loss?

A. Tying your hair up too tight or using hair elastics that are too tight can cause hair breakage and damage, which can lead to hair loss over time.

Hair Care Tips:

To keep your hair healthy and looking good, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free from dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, hair looks better up than down due to various reasons such as face shape, hair texture, activity, and weather. Tying your hair up can be done in various ways such as a ponytail, bun, or braid. Follow these tips and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking good.
