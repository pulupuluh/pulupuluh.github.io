How To Style Short Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jun 18, 2023 · 10 min read

Short thick curly hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can make your curls work for you. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to style short thick curly hair, step by step, and provide tips and advice along the way. Whether you’re looking for a casual, everyday look or something more formal, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Thick Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Thick Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Short Thick Curly Hair

How to Style Short Thick Curly Hair

The first step in styling short thick curly hair is to find the right haircut. You want a cut that will enhance your natural curls and work with your hair’s thickness. A layered cut can help to add volume and definition to your curls, while a blunt cut can create a sleek, polished look.

Once you have the right cut, it’s time to start styling. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, conditioned hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and defined.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to your damp hair. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.

Step 3: Diffuse your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp, and use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and add volume.

Step 4: Define your curls

Use a curling iron or wand to define any sections of hair that need a little extra help. Wrap small sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron or wand, and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 5: Finish with a hair spray

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place and prevent frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step by step guide to styling short thick curly hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and defined. Rinse your hair thoroughly, and gently squeeze out any excess water.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair, focusing on the ends. This will help to keep your curls moisturized and prevent breakage. You can also apply a curl-enhancing product at this stage.

Step 3: Detangle your hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be gentle to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Part your hair

Part your hair in the desired style. You can create a deep side part, a middle part, or a zig-zag part.

Step 5: Diffuse your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp, and use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and add volume.

Step 6: Define your curls

Use a curling iron or wand to define any sections of hair that need a little extra help. Wrap small sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron or wand, and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 7: Finish with a hair spray

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Styling Short Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling short thick curly hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, which can cause frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can break your curls and cause frizz.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a flat iron or straightener, as they can damage your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my short thick curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer to dry my short thick curly hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. Just be sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my short thick curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair, and apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.

Hair Care for Short Thick Curly Hair

In addition to styling, it’s important to take care of your short thick curly hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a flat iron or straightener, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your short thick curly hair with confidence and ease. Experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you.
