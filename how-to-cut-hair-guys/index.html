How To Cut Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 27, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking to save some money and give yourself or a friend a fresh haircut, you've come to the right place. Cutting hair for guys can be intimidating, but with the right tools and a bit of practice, anyone can do it. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of how to cut hair guys and provide some tips and tricks for achieving a professional-looking cut.

How to Cut Hair Guys: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a good pair of hair cutting scissors, clippers, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to invest in a cape or towel to protect clothing.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair. Shampoo and condition the hair as usual, then allow it to air dry or use a blow dryer to completely dry the hair.

Step 3: Determine Length and Style

Before you start cutting, decide on the length and style you want to achieve. Use a comb to section the hair and create a guide for your cut.

Step 4: Trim the Sides and Back with Clippers

Start by using clippers to trim the sides and back of the head. Use a shorter guard for a shorter cut and a longer guard for a longer cut. Work from the bottom up and use a comb to guide the clippers.

Step 5: Cut the Top with Scissors

Once you've finished trimming the sides and back, use scissors to cut the top. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Use the guide you created earlier to ensure an even cut.

Step 6: Blend the Top and Sides

Use scissors to blend the top and sides together. Hold the hair at a 45-degree angle and cut small sections at a time until the top and sides blend seamlessly.

Step 7: Trim the Bangs

If you want to have bangs, use scissors to trim them to the desired length. Use a comb to create a guide and cut straight across.

Step 8: Clean Up the Neckline and Ears

Use clippers or scissors to clean up the neckline and around the ears. Use a smaller guard for a closer cut and a larger guard for a longer cut.

Step 9: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, use a mirror to check for evenness. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure a polished, professional-looking cut.

Step 10: Style and Finish

Style the hair as desired using gel, pomade, or other styling products. Finish with a quick spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Cutting Hair Guys

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one for a more gradual fade.

- Use a spray bottle to keep the hair damp while cutting.

- Take your time and don't rush the process.

- Don't cut too much at once; it's easier to take off more hair than to add it back.

FAQ: How to Cut Hair Guys

How often should guys get their hair cut?

It depends on the length and style of the hair. Generally, guys should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep the hair healthy.

Can I use regular scissors to cut hair?

No, regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can cause uneven and jagged cuts. Use hair cutting scissors specifically designed for the purpose.

Is it better to cut hair wet or dry?

It's generally easier to cut hair when it's wet, but it can also be cut when it's dry. Wet hair is more manageable and easier to section and comb, but dry hair allows for better precision when cutting.

Hair Care for Guys

Proper hair care is important for maintaining a healthy head of hair. Guys should shampoo and condition regularly and use a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to keep the hair moisturized. Avoid using too much heat and styling products, as they can damage the hair and cause breakage. Regular trims can also help keep the hair healthy and prevent split ends.
