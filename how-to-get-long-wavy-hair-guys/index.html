How To Get Long Wavy Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Introduction

Long hair has always been associated with women, but that doesn't mean men can't rock long hair too! Long wavy hair is a popular hairstyle for guys who want to stand out from the crowd. Getting long wavy hair as a guy can seem daunting, but with the right steps and care, it can be achieved. In this blog post, we'll go over how to get long wavy hair guys, step-by-step.

How to Get Long Wavy Hair Guys

First and foremost, it's important to note that getting long wavy hair as a guy takes time and patience. It won't happen overnight, but by following these steps, you'll be on your way to achieving the long wavy hair look you desire.

Step 1: Grow out your hair

The first step to getting long wavy hair is to let your hair grow out. It's recommended to grow your hair out to at least shoulder length before attempting to style it into waves. This can take anywhere from six months to a year depending on how fast your hair grows.

Step 2: Use a curl enhancing product

Once your hair has grown out, it's important to use a curl enhancing product to help create waves. Look for products that are specifically designed to enhance curls, such as curl creams or mousses. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Use a diffuser

To enhance your hair's natural waves, use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair. A diffuser helps to distribute heat evenly throughout your hair, creating natural-looking waves. Set your blow dryer on a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser.

Step 4: Braid your hair

Another way to enhance your hair's natural waves is to braid it before bed. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section before going to sleep. In the morning, take out the braids and run your fingers through your hair to create loose waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of how to get long wavy hair guys, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look:

Step 1:

Let your hair grow out to at least shoulder length. This can take anywhere from six months to a year depending on how fast your hair grows.

Step 2:

Use a curl enhancing product to help create waves. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3:

Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your hair's natural waves. Set your blow dryer on a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser.

Step 4:

Braid your hair before bed to enhance your hair's natural waves. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section before going to sleep. In the morning, take out the braids and run your fingers through your hair to create loose waves.

Tips for Maintaining Long Wavy Hair Guys

Now that you've achieved long wavy hair, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining long wavy hair guys:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straightening irons or curling wands, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQs

Q: Can I still get long wavy hair if my hair is naturally straight?

A: Yes, you can still achieve long wavy hair even if your hair is naturally straight. Follow the steps outlined in this blog post, and use a curl enhancing product to create waves.

Q: How often should I wash my long wavy hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care Tips for Long Wavy Hair Guys

Long wavy hair requires proper care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for long wavy hair guys:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for curly or wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straightening irons or curling wands, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these steps and tips, you'll be on your way to achieving long wavy hair guys. Remember, it takes time and patience, but the end result is worth it!
