Do Short Hairstyles Suit Long Faces

Written by Christine Apr 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Description | How to | Step by step | Tips | FAQ | Hair Care

Description

Long faces are characterized by a narrow, elongated shape that can be difficult to style. Many women with long faces believe that they are limited to certain hairstyles, but this is not the case. Short hairstyles can be a great option for women with long faces, but it is important to choose the right style to complement your features.

In this article, we will explore whether short hairstyles suit long faces, how to choose a style that flatters your features, and tips for maintaining your new look.

How to

Choosing a short hairstyle for a long face can be a daunting task, but there are a few guidelines that can help you make the right choice:

Consider your hair texture

Short hairstyles work best on hair that is fine or medium in texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a longer style that will help to balance out your face shape.

Look for volume

Hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head can help to balance out a long face. Consider a style with layers, curls, or waves that will add body to your hair.

Avoid blunt cuts

Straight, blunt cuts can make a long face appear even longer. Instead, opt for a style with soft layers that will frame your face and add dimension.

Step by step

Once you have chosen a short hairstyle that suits your long face, it is important to maintain your new look:

Choose the right products

Use products that are designed for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and manageable. If you have fine hair, look for products that add volume and thickness. If you have curly hair, look for products that define and control your curls.

Stay on top of trims

Short hairstyles require regular trims to maintain their shape. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you choose a short hairstyle that flatters your long face:

Consider your lifestyle

Choose a style that complements your lifestyle. If you are a busy mom or have a demanding job, choose a low-maintenance style that is easy to maintain.

Experiment with accessories

Accessorize your short hairstyle with headbands, barrettes, or other accessories that can add interest and complement your features.

FAQ

What short hairstyles suit long faces?

Short hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head, such as layered cuts or styles with curls, waves, or texture, can help to balance out a long face.

Can I still wear a pixie cut if I have a long face?

Yes, you can still wear a pixie cut if you have a long face. Look for a style with volume at the crown of the head, and avoid blunt cuts that can make your face appear longer.

Hair Care

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips to help you care for your new style:

Wash your hair regularly

Short hair can get greasy quickly, so wash your hair every other day or as needed to keep it looking fresh.

Use a heat protectant

If you style your hair with heat tools, use a heat protectant to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Get regular trims

Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your style looking fresh and maintain its shape.

In conclusion, short hairstyles can be a great option for women with long faces, as long as you choose a style that complements your features. Follow these guidelines and tips to choose a short hairstyle that flatters your face shape and maintain your new look with ease.
