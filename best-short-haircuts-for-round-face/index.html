Best Short Haircuts For Round Face In 2023

Written by Robby Jun 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect haircut can be challenging. You want to choose a style that flatters your face shape and accentuates your best features. In this article, we'll discuss the best short haircuts for round face in 2023. We'll cover everything from how to choose the right cut to tips for maintaining healthy hair.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a round face, it's essential to select a style that elongates your face and draws attention to your best features. Here are some tips for choosing the right haircut:

  • Opt for haircuts that have layers and volume at the crown of your head.
  • Avoid haircuts that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.
  • Consider side-swept bangs, which can help elongate your face.
  • Choose haircuts that have texture and movement, such as a shag or pixie cut.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for round faces. This style features short, choppy layers that add texture and movement to your hair. The side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes.

To maintain this style, you'll need to visit your stylist every four to six weeks for a trim. Use a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to add volume and texture to your hair.

The Bob

The bob is another classic short haircut that works well for round faces. This style features a chin-length cut that can be straight or curly. A layered bob can add volume and movement to your hair, while a blunt bob can create a more polished look.

To maintain this style, you'll need to visit your stylist every six to eight weeks for a trim. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair. You can also use a flat iron to create a sleek, straight look.

The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is a versatile haircut that works well for round faces. This style features a longer cut that falls just above or below the shoulders. A textured lob can add volume and movement to your hair, while a blunt lob can create a more polished look.

To maintain this style, you'll need to visit your stylist every eight to ten weeks for a trim. Use a curling iron or a straightener to add texture and shape to your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to create a beachy, tousled look.

The Shag

The shag is a layered haircut that works well for round faces. This style features choppy layers and a textured, messy look. The shag can add volume and movement to your hair and can be styled in a variety of ways.

To maintain this style, you'll need to visit your stylist every six to eight weeks for a trim. Use a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a curling iron to create loose, beachy waves.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for round faces:

Q: Can I wear bangs with a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a round face. Side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a pixie cut with a round face. This style can add texture and movement to your hair and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best short haircuts for round faces in 2023 and how to maintain healthy hair, you can confidently choose a style that flatters your face shape and accentuates your best features.
