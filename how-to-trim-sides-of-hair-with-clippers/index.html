How To Trim Sides Of Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Trimming the sides of your hair with clippers can be an easy and convenient way to maintain your hairstyle at home. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a clean and professional-looking haircut without having to visit a barber or hair salon. In this guide, we'll show you how to trim the sides of your hair with clippers in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

How to Trim Sides of Hair with Clippers

Before we get started, here are the tools you'll need:

  • Clippers with adjustable guards
  • Comb
  • Scissors
  • Mirror
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you start trimming. Then, put on your haircutting cape or towel to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard

Clippers come with different guards that determine the length of the hair they cut. For the sides of your hair, you'll want to choose a guard that's shorter than the one you use for the top of your head. A #2 or #3 guard is a good place to start.

Step 3: Start Trimming

Comb your hair in the direction it grows, and then use your clippers to trim the sides of your head. Start at the bottom of your hairline and work your way up. Keep the clippers flat against your head, and move them in an upward motion. Repeat this process until you've trimmed all the hair on the sides of your head.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've trimmed the sides of your hair, you'll want to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on top. To do this, switch to a longer guard and trim the hair around your temples and ears. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that stick out.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure you haven't missed any spots. You can also ask someone to check for you.

Tips for Trimming Sides of Hair with Clippers

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and make sure you're trimming evenly.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair as you trim. This will help you cut evenly and prevent uneven spots.
  • If you're not sure how short to cut your hair, start with a longer guard and work your way down. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.

FAQ

Can I Trim My Hair with Clippers Without a Guard?

It's not recommended to trim your hair without a guard. Clippers without guards can cut your hair too short and cause uneven spots.

How Often Should I Trim the Sides of My Hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your hairstyle. As a general rule, you should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Can I Use the Same Clippers for My Hair and Beard?

Yes, you can use the same clippers for your hair and beard, but make sure you clean them thoroughly between uses to prevent bacteria buildup.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can trim the sides of your hair with clippers like a pro. Remember to take your time, choose the right guard, and blend your hair for a polished look. Happy trimming!
