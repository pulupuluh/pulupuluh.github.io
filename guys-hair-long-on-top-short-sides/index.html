Guys Hair Long On Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that's both trendy and practical, you might want to consider the guys hair long on top short sides haircut. This style has been popular for a few years now, and it's not hard to see why: it's easy to maintain, it looks great on almost everyone, and it can be styled in many different ways. In this article, we'll show you how to get this haircut, how to style it, and how to take care of it. Let's get started!

Description

The guys hair long on top short sides haircut is characterized by long hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it's usually between 2 and 4 inches. The sides and back are typically faded or tapered, meaning that the hair gradually gets shorter from the top to the bottom. This creates a clean and polished look that's perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

How to Get the Haircut

If you're interested in getting the guys hair long on top short sides haircut, you should visit a hairstylist or barber who is experienced in this style. Be sure to bring a picture of the hairstyle you want, so that your stylist can get a better idea of what you're looking for. During the haircut, your stylist will use clippers and scissors to create the fade or taper on the sides and back, and will leave the hair on top longer. The entire process should take about 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity of the cut.

Step by Step Guide to Style

Styling the guys hair long on top short sides haircut is easy and fun. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup, and will leave your hair clean and soft.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Use a clean towel to gently pat your hair dry. Don't rub your hair vigorously, as this can damage the hair and cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply a styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a pomade, wax, or gel, to your hair. Work the product through your hair with your fingers, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 4: Style your hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair. You can create a side part, a slick back, a messy fringe, or any other style that you like. Be creative and have fun!

Tips for Maintaining the Haircut

To keep your guys hair long on top short sides haircut looking great, follow these tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist or barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or flat irons, too often, as this can damage your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the guys hair long on top short sides haircut:

Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair.

Can I style this haircut in different ways?

Yes, this haircut is very versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different products and techniques to find a style that you like.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days, but this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care

To keep your guys hair long on top short sides haircut looking healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry out your scalp
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can promote hair growth and strength
  • Get enough sleep and exercise, as this can improve your overall health and wellbeing, which in turn can benefit your hair

With these tips and techniques, you can rock the guys hair long on top short sides haircut with confidence and style. Have fun experimenting with different looks, and don't be afraid to try something new!
