Oblong Face with Short Hair

If you have an oblong face, it can be tough to find the right hairstyle that flatters your face. However, short hair can be a great option for those with oblong faces. In this article, we'll discuss how to style short hair for oblong faces, tips, and hair care advice.

Description

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow chin and forehead. Short hair can help balance out the length of the face and make it appear wider.

How to Style Short Hair for Oblong Faces

When styling short hair for oblong faces, it's important to add volume and width to the sides of the face. Here are some tips:

1. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut can help add volume and movement to the hair, creating the illusion of width. Ask your stylist to cut layers that start at the cheekbones or jawline.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help break up the length of the face and create the illusion of a wider forehead. Ask your stylist to cut the bangs at an angle, starting from the forehead and ending at the cheekbones.

3. Waves or Curls

Adding waves or curls to short hair can also help create volume and width. Use a curling iron or a straightener to create loose waves or curls.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair for oblong faces:

1. Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots to add volume.

2. Create waves or curls

Use a curling iron or a straightener to create waves or curls in your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.

3. Add texture

To add texture, use a texturizing spray or pomade. Apply it to the ends of your hair and use your fingers to create a tousled look.

4. Finish with hairspray

Finish off your style with a light-hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling short hair for oblong faces:

1. Avoid straight, blunt cuts

Straight, blunt cuts can make an oblong face appear even longer. Instead, opt for layered cuts or side-swept bangs.

2. Use volume-boosting products

Products like volumizing mousse or spray can help add volume to flat hair.

3. Consider highlights

Adding highlights can help add dimension to the hair and create the illusion of width.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear even longer, such as straight, blunt cuts or center parts.

Can I wear my hair in a bob if I have an oblong face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a bob. However, make sure it's a layered bob that adds volume and width to the sides of the face.

Do I have to use heat styling tools to create volume?

No, you can also use volumizing products like mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny, make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking fresh.

In conclusion, if you have an oblong face, short hair can be a great option. Just remember to add volume and width to the sides of the face, and avoid hairstyles that make your face appear longer. With the right styling and hair care, you can rock a short hairdo with confidence.
