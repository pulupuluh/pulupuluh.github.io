Mens Short Hairstyle Fade The Ultimate Guide For Relaxed Style

Written by Oliver Mar 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, mens short hairstyle fade is the perfect choice. This haircut features a gradual taper from short to even shorter hair, resulting in a clean and professional look that works for any occasion. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the mens short hairstyle fade, including how to get it, style it, and care for it.

  2. How to Get Mens Short Hairstyle Fade
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Style Mens Short Hairstyle Fade

  6. Hair Care for Mens Short Hairstyle Fade

Description

The mens short hairstyle fade is a popular haircut that involves tapering the hair from short to even shorter lengths. The hair is usually longer on top and gradually gets shorter towards the neck and sides. This type of haircut is versatile and works for any hair type and face shape. It's also easy to maintain and style, making it a great choice for busy men who still want to look their best.

How to Get Mens Short Hairstyle Fade

To get a mens short hairstyle fade, you'll need to visit a professional barber or stylist. They will start by cutting your hair to the desired length on top, usually around 1-2 inches. They will then gradually taper the hair towards the sides and back using clippers or scissors. The length of the taper can vary depending on your preference, but most fades start at a #1 or #2 clipper setting and gradually get shorter towards the bottom.

It's important to communicate with your barber or stylist about the type of fade you want. You can choose from a low, medium, or high fade, depending on how much contrast you want between the top and bottom of your hair. A low fade starts just above the ears, a medium fade starts halfway up the sides, and a high fade starts at the temples.

Step-by-Step Guide to Style Mens Short Hairstyle Fade

Styling mens short hairstyle fade is relatively easy and straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product, such as wax or pomade, to your fingertips.
  3. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the top and sides.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

The great thing about mens short hairstyle fade is that it's versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can slick your hair back for a more formal look, or leave it messy for a more casual style.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Short Hairstyle Fade

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens short hairstyle fade:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your fade looking fresh.
  • Use a quality hair product that works for your hair type.
  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup of product and oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

FAQs

1. Is mens short hairstyle fade suitable for all hair types?

Yes, mens short hairstyle fade works for all hair types, including curly, straight, and wavy hair.

2. How often should I get a haircut for mens short hairstyle fade?

You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your mens short hairstyle fade.

3. Can I style mens short hairstyle fade with a hairdryer?

Yes, you can use a hairdryer to style your mens short hairstyle fade. Use a low heat setting and a round brush to create volume and texture.

Hair Care for Mens Short Hairstyle Fade

To keep your mens short hairstyle fade looking its best, you should follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every other day.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

By following these tips and maintaining a regular hair care routine, you can keep your mens short hairstyle fade looking sharp and stylish.
