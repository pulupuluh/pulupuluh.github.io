How To Know Your Face Shape Male Online A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 03, 2023 · 8 min read

Knowing your face shape is crucial in determining the best hairstyle, beard style, and even glasses that suit your face. However, not everyone has the time or resources to go to a professional stylist for a face shape analysis. Luckily, with the rise of technology and the internet, you can now easily determine your face shape male online. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to know your face shape male online.

Table of Content:

  1. Step 1: Measure Your Face
  2. Step 2: Identify Your Face Shape
  3. Step 3: Confirm Your Face Shape
  4. Tips for Determining Your Face Shape Male Online
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Each Face Shape

Step 1: Measure Your Face

The first step in determining your face shape male online is to measure your face. You will need a measuring tape or ruler and a mirror. Measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure across your forehead at the widest point, which is usually just below your hairline.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones at the widest point.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jawbone starts to angle upwards.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Write down each measurement and keep them handy for the next step.

Step 2: Identify Your Face Shape

Now that you have your measurements, it's time to identify your face shape. There are six main face shapes for males:

  • Oval
  • Square
  • Round
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Compare your measurements to the characteristics of each face shape:

  • Oval: Your face length is larger than your forehead and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Square: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Round: Your face length is similar to your cheekbone and forehead measurements, and your jawline is soft.
  • Heart: Your forehead is the widest part of your face, and your cheekbones taper down to a narrow jawline.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrow and approximately the same width.
  • Triangle: Your jawline is wider than your forehead and cheekbones.

Based on the characteristics, determine which face shape best matches your measurements.

Step 3: Confirm Your Face Shape

Once you have identified your face shape, it's important to confirm it to ensure accuracy. You can do this by taking a photo of yourself facing the camera straight on with your hair pulled back. Print out the photo and draw lines around your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline based on your measurements. The lines should form the shape of your identified face shape. If it doesn't, you may need to re-measure or re-evaluate based on the photo.

Tips for Determining Your Face Shape Male Online

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when determining your face shape male online:

  • Make sure your hair is pulled back and out of the way so it doesn't interfere with your measurements.
  • Use a flexible measuring tape or ruler to ensure accuracy.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you with the measurements.
  • Use natural lighting when taking your confirmation photo.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about determining face shape male online:

Can I determine my face shape without measuring?

It's possible to estimate your face shape based on your facial features, but measuring is the most accurate method.

Can face shape change over time?

While your bone structure doesn't change, your face shape can appear different due to weight gain or loss, aging, or changes in hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Each Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to determine the best hairstyles for you. Here are some general tips:

  • Oval: Most hairstyles work well, but avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead.
  • Square: Short, textured hairstyles work well, and avoid hairstyles that are too flat or sleek.
  • Round: Keep hair short on the sides and longer on top to elongate the face, and avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides.
  • Heart: Side-swept bangs and longer hairstyles work well, and avoid short, cropped hairstyles.
  • Diamond: Short, textured hairstyles work well, and avoid styles that add height to the crown.
  • Triangle: Longer hairstyles with texture work well, and avoid hairstyles that accentuate the jawline.

Knowing your face shape male online is a quick and easy process that can help you determine the best hairstyles, beard styles, and glasses for your face. Use our step-by-step guide and tips to confidently identify your face shape and rock your best look.
