What Wedding Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Written by Christine Mar 20, 2023 · 8 min read

Choosing the perfect wedding hairstyle can be overwhelming, especially when you have to consider factors like your dress, the theme of your wedding, and your hair type. In this article, we’ll help you figure out which wedding hairstyle will suit you best. Keep reading to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Wedding Hairstyle
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Every Bride

Description

The perfect wedding hairstyle is one that compliments your dress, your face shape, and your personal style. It’s important to choose a hairstyle that you feel comfortable in and that will make you feel confident on your big day. Whether you have long hair, short hair, curly hair, or straight hair, there’s a wedding hairstyle out there that will suit you perfectly. The key is to find the right one for your individual needs.

How to Choose the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle

Choosing the perfect wedding hairstyle can be a daunting task, but it doesn’t have to be. Here are a few tips to help you choose the perfect wedding hairstyle:

Consider Your Dress

The first thing you should consider when choosing your wedding hairstyle is your dress. If you have a strapless dress, you may want to opt for an updo to show off your neckline. If your dress has a lot of intricate details, you may want to choose a simple hairstyle that won’t compete with your dress.

Think About Your Face Shape

Your face shape is another important factor to consider when choosing your wedding hairstyle. If you have a round face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds height to your crown. If you have a square face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that softens your features.

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is also an important factor to consider when choosing your wedding hairstyle. If you have curly hair, you may want to opt for a hairstyle that embraces your natural curls. If you have straight hair, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds some volume and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle

Once you’ve chosen your wedding hairstyle, it’s important to know how to achieve it. Here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect wedding hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. It’s important to use products that are suited for your hair type.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a diffuser to enhance your curls.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair according to your chosen wedding hairstyle. If you’re doing an updo, start by pulling your hair into a ponytail and then twisting it into a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your wedding hairstyle with a spritz of hairspray to hold it in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wedding Hairstyle

Here are a few tips for maintaining your wedding hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day
  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair throughout the day
  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent dryness

FAQ

What wedding hairstyle will suit me if I have short hair?

If you have short hair, you may want to opt for a pixie cut or a bob. You can also add some volume with some curling irons or by using a volumizing mousse.

What wedding hairstyle will suit me if I have long hair?

If you have long hair, you have a lot of options to choose from. You can opt for an elegant updo, loose waves, or a braided hairstyle.

What wedding hairstyle will suit me if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, you may want to embrace your natural curls and opt for a hairstyle that enhances them. You can also choose a pulled-back style or an updo that shows off your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Every Bride

Here are a few hair care tips for every bride:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth

Choosing the perfect wedding hairstyle can seem overwhelming, but with these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect hairstyle to complement your dress, face shape, and hair type. Remember to take good care of your hair in the months leading up to your wedding to ensure that it’s healthy and ready to be styled on your big day!
