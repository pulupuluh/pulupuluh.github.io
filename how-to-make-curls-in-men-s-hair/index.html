How To Make Curls In Men s Hair A Complete Guide In 2023

Curly hair has been a trend for quite some time now, and it's not just for women. Men can also rock the curly hair look, and it's easier than you think. In this guide, we'll show you how to make curls in men's hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

How to Make Curls in Men's Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's essential to understand that not all hair types can achieve curls. Men with straight hair may have a harder time getting curls, but it's not impossible.

The easiest way to achieve curls is by using a curling iron or wand. However, this method can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly. Another way is to use hair rollers or flexi rods, which are gentler on your hair and can be used overnight for a more defined curl.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make curls in men's hair:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. The cleaner your hair, the better your curls will hold.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair. This will prevent your hair from getting damaged due to the high temperature.

3. Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections, depending on how many curls you want. The more sections you have, the tighter your curls will be.

4. Use a Curling Iron or Wand

Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, starting at the root and ending at the tip. Hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat for all sections of hair.

5. Use Hair Rollers or Flexi Rods

If you want a gentler option, use hair rollers or flexi rods instead of a curling iron. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the roller or rod, then secure with a clip. Leave them in overnight or for a few hours, depending on how tight you want your curls.

6. Apply Hair Spray

Once you've achieved your desired curls, apply a hair spray to hold them in place. You can also use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Making Curls in Men's Hair

Here are some tips to help you make curls in men's hair:

  • Use products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Don't use too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Use hair rollers or flexi rods for a gentler option.
  • Apply a hair spray or serum to hold your curls in place and add shine.

FAQs

Can men with straight hair achieve curls?

Yes, men with straight hair can achieve curls, but it may take more effort and the use of hair rollers or flexi rods.

Can I use a curling iron or wand every day?

No, using a curling iron or wand every day can damage your hair. It's best to limit the use to once or twice a week and use heat protectant products.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Curls

Men with curly hair need to take extra care of their hair to prevent frizz and dryness. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.

With these tips and techniques, you can make curls in men's hair and rock the curly hair trend. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
