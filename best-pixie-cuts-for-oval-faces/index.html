Best Pixie Cuts For Oval Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 26, 2023 Â· 11 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. And if you're looking to take the plunge with a pixie cut, you'll find that there are plenty of options that will flatter your features. In this guide, we'll take a look at the best pixie cuts for oval faces, along with tips on how to style and care for your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Best Pixie Cut for Your Oval Face
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Pixie Cut
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut
  4. FAQs About Pixie Cuts for Oval Faces
  5. Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

How to Choose the Best Pixie Cut for Your Oval Face

When it comes to choosing the best pixie cut for your oval face, you'll want to consider your hair texture, personal style, and the length you're comfortable with. Here are some popular pixie cut options that work well with oval faces:

1. Classic Pixie Cut

The classic pixie cut is a timeless option that never goes out of style. It features short, cropped layers that are tapered at the back and sides, with longer layers on top. This cut works well with straight, wavy, or curly hair, and it's easy to maintain with regular trims.

2. Side-Swept Pixie Cut

The side-swept pixie cut is a great option for oval faces because it adds asymmetry to your features. This cut features longer layers on one side of the head, swept across the forehead and tucked behind the ear on the other side. It works well with straight or wavy hair, and you can add texture with a little bit of styling product.

3. Layered Pixie Cut

If you want a little more volume and movement in your pixie cut, the layered pixie cut is a great option. This cut features choppy, textured layers throughout the hair, with longer layers on top. It works well with all hair textures, and you can style it with a little bit of mousse or texturizing spray.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Pixie Cut

Styling a pixie cut is all about finding the right products and techniques for your hair type. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your pixie cut:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair using a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair gently, and then let it air dry or blow dry it on low heat.

2. Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, depending on your hair type. For fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray. For thicker hair, use a styling cream or pomade.

3. Use a round brush to add volume

Use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots and add volume to the layers. Blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the back and working your way up to the crown of your head.

4. Add texture

Use a small amount of texturizing spray or wax to add texture and definition to your hair. Apply the product to your fingertips and run it through your hair, concentrating on the ends and the crown.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish your pixie cut with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place. Choose a flexible hold hairspray that won't make your hair stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Pixie cuts are low-maintenance, but there are a few things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

1. Schedule regular trims

Even though pixie cuts are short, they still require regular trims to keep the shape and style looking fresh. Schedule a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair in top condition.

2. Protect your hair from heat damage

Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using hot tools like curling irons or flat irons. This will help prevent heat damage and keep your hair healthy.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to nourish and hydrate your hair. This will help prevent dryness and breakage, and keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

FAQs About Pixie Cuts for Oval Faces

1. Can I pull off a pixie cut if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Pixie cuts work well with all hair textures, including curly hair. Just make sure to choose a cut that flatters your face shape and hair texture, and work with a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair.

2. Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

No, not necessarily. In fact, a well-chosen pixie cut can actually elongate your face and make it appear more oval. Just make sure to choose a cut that flatters your features, and work with a stylist who can help you find the right style for your face shape.

3. How can I add volume to my pixie cut?

There are a few ways to add volume to a pixie cut. First, try blow drying your hair with a round brush to lift the roots and add volume to the layers. You can also use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add more body to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts are easy to care for, but there are a few things you can do to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are gentle and nourishing, and avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent dryness and breakage.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle. Use a hat or scarf to protect your hair when you're spending time outside, or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

3. Avoid over-styling your hair

While it's tempting to experiment with different styles and products, over-styling your hair can lead to damage and breakage. Stick to simple, low-maintenance styles, and avoid using hot tools or harsh chemicals too often.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect pixie cut for your oval face shape and keep your hair looking healthy and fabulous. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products, and have fun with your new 'do!
