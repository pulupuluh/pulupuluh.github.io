Does Short Haircuts Make Your Face Look Thinner

Written by Petter Sep 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short haircuts are a popular trend, but do they make your face look thinner? This is a common question among those who want to change their look but are unsure about the impact of short hair on their face shape. In this article, we will answer this question and provide you with tips on how to choose the best short haircut for your face shape.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts can make your face look thinner if you choose the right style for your face shape. A short haircut can create an illusion of length and height, which can make your face appear slimmer. However, if you choose the wrong style, it can have the opposite effect and make your face look wider and rounder.

How to

The first step to finding the right short haircut for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six different face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has its own unique characteristics, and certain hairstyles can flatter or accentuate those features.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck because most short haircuts will flatter your face shape. The best short haircut for an oval face shape is a pixie cut or a bob. Both of these styles will accentuate your cheekbones and jawline.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you should avoid short haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face. The best short haircut for a round face shape is a layered bob or a lob (long bob). These styles will add length to your face and make it appear slimmer.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you should choose a short haircut that softens your jawline. The best short haircut for a square face shape is a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs. These styles will soften your facial features and make your face appear more feminine.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you should choose a short haircut that balances out your facial features. The best short haircut for a heart-shaped face is a side-swept pixie cut or a bob with layers. These styles will accentuate your cheekbones and soften your chin.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you should choose a short haircut that adds volume to your hair at the crown of your head. The best short haircut for a diamond-shaped face is a textured pixie cut or a layered bob. These styles will add volume to the top of your head and balance out your facial features.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you should choose a short haircut that adds width to your face. The best short haircut for an oblong face shape is a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs. These styles will add width to your face and make it appear more proportional.

Step by step

Here are the steps to choose the best short haircut for your face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Research short haircuts that flatter your face shape.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which short haircut will work best for you.
  4. Consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a short haircut.
  5. Maintain your short haircut with regular trims and styling.

Tips

  • Use Pinterest or Instagram to find short haircut inspiration.
  • Bring pictures of short haircuts you like to your hairstylist.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your short haircut to add dimension.
  • Invest in quality hair products to maintain your short haircut.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look too masculine?

A: No, a short haircut can be feminine and flattering if you choose the right style for your face shape and hair texture.

Q: Can I still style my short haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your short haircut in different ways by using different hair products and styling tools.

Q: Will a short haircut require a lot of maintenance?

A: Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape, but they can be low-maintenance if you choose a style that works with your hair texture and lifestyle.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking its best, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. Use a leave-in conditioner to add moisture and protect your hair from heat damage. Style your short haircut with a lightweight hair product, such as a mousse or a texturizing spray, to add volume and hold without weighing down your hair.

Overall, short haircuts can make your face look thinner if you choose the right style for your face shape. Use these tips and tricks to find the perfect short haircut for you and maintain it with ease.
