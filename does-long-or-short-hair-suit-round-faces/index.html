Does Long Or Short Hair Suit Round Faces

Written by Oliver May 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

People with round faces often find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that complements their facial features. Choosing between long and short hair can be daunting, especially if you're unsure of what works best for your face shape. In this article, we'll explore whether long or short hair suits round faces and provide tips on how to choose the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Description

Round faces have soft, curved lines and are typically wider than they are long. People with round faces tend to have fuller cheeks, a rounded chin, and a shorter forehead. When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, the goal is to create the illusion of length and slimness. Long hair can make a round face appear longer and more slender, while short hair can accentuate the roundness of the face.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, the goal is to create balance and proportion. The right hairstyle can help elongate the face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Here are some things to keep in mind when deciding between long and short hair:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness.
  • Think about the shape of your face and what features you want to accentuate.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your lifestyle and personality.
  • Consult with your hairstylist for their professional opinion.

Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Follow these steps to choose the perfect hairstyle for your round face:

  1. Determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all similar in width, you have a round face.
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have fine hair, long layers can help add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can help reduce bulk.
  3. Think about the features you want to accentuate. If you have beautiful eyes or high cheekbones, consider a hairstyle that draws attention to those features.
  4. Choose a hairstyle that complements your lifestyle and personality. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance hairstyle like a messy bun or ponytail may be ideal.
  5. Consult with your hairstylist. Your hairstylist can recommend the best hairstyle based on your face shape, hair type, and personal preferences.

Tips for Styling Your Hair with a Round Face

Here are some styling tips to help you look your best with a round face:

  • Part your hair to the side to create the illusion of length.
  • Try loose waves or curls to add volume and movement.
  • Avoid blunt bangs or hairstyles that end at the chin, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create the illusion of length and volume.
  • Accessorize with earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from your face.

FAQs about Choosing Hairstyles for a Round Face

Q: Does long hair make a round face look slimmer?

A: Yes, long hair can make a round face appear longer and more slender. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and features.

Q: Can short hair work for round faces?

A: Yes, short hair can work for round faces, but it's important to choose a hairstyle that creates balance and proportion. A pixie cut or blunt bob can help elongate the face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Q: Should I avoid hairstyles with volume?

A: No, hairstyles with volume can help create the illusion of length and slimness. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and features.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have a round face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Invest in a good hairbrush to help add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and length of your hairstyle.
  • Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of maintenance, as they can be time-consuming and difficult to maintain.

In conclusion, both long and short hair can work for round faces. The key is to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and creates balance and proportion. By following these tips and consulting with your hairstylist, you can find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
