What Is An Undercut Hairstyle

Written by Bardi Mar 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

An undercut hairstyle is a popular men's haircut that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. The hair on the sides and back is typically shaved or cut very short, creating a clean and sharp look. The longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, messy, or curly. This style has become increasingly popular in recent years and is often seen on celebrities, athletes, and fashion-forward men.

How to Get an Undercut Hairstyle

If you're interested in getting an undercut hairstyle, the first step is to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who can create the look you want. Here are the basic steps involved in getting an undercut:

Step 1: Choose Your Lengths

Decide how short you want your hair on the sides and back, and how long you want it on top. This will depend on your personal style and the shape of your face. You can show your barber a picture of the style you want, or ask for their advice on what will work best for you.

Step 2: Trim the Sides and Back

The barber will use clippers or scissors to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. This can be done in a variety of lengths, depending on your preference. Some men prefer a very short buzz cut, while others prefer a slightly longer length.

Step 3: Cut the Top

The barber will then cut the hair on top of your head to the desired length. This can be done with scissors or clippers, depending on the style you want.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

The barber will blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top, creating a seamless transition between the two lengths. This is an important step to ensure that the haircut looks polished and professional.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair on top in a variety of ways. Some men prefer a slicked-back style, while others prefer a messy, textured look. You can use hair products like pomade or wax to help hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining an Undercut Hairstyle

Here are some tips for keeping your undercut looking sharp:

  • Get regular trims to keep the sides and back looking neat
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage the hair and make it look dull

FAQ About Undercut Hairstyles

Here are some common questions about undercut hairstyles:

What face shapes look good with an undercut?

An undercut can work well on a variety of face shapes, but it's best suited for those with square or oval faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid an undercut, as it can make your face look even rounder.

How often should I get my undercut trimmed?

It's a good idea to get your undercut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking sharp. This will also help prevent the hair on the sides and back from growing out too much.

Can women have undercut hairstyles?

Absolutely! Undercut hairstyles can look great on women as well, and can be styled in a variety of ways. Some women prefer a short undercut, while others prefer a longer length on top.

Hair Care for Undercut Hairstyles

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair when you have an undercut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage the hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot styling tools
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause breakage and split ends

With the right care and maintenance, an undercut hairstyle can look sharp and stylish for weeks on end. Whether you prefer a short or long undercut, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
