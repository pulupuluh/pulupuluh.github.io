How To Check Which Haircut Will Suit Me A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 27, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of getting a haircut that doesn’t flatter your face shape or hair type? If you’re looking for a new hairstyle but aren’t sure where to start, don’t worry – we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll show you how to check which haircut will suit you in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Check Which Haircut Will Suit Me

Before you head to the salon, it’s important to determine which haircut will suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Consider your face shape. Different haircuts flatter different face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, a layered haircut with volume at the top can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a long bob or soft layers can soften your features.
  • Think about your hair texture. If you have thick, curly hair, a blunt cut may be too heavy and difficult to manage. In this case, layers or a textured cut may be a better option. On the other hand, if you have fine, straight hair, a blunt cut can add volume and thickness.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule and don’t have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance cut may be a better option. Conversely, if you enjoy experimenting with different styles and have the time to style your hair, a more intricate cut may be a good choice.
  • Look to celebrities for inspiration. If you have a favorite celebrity with a similar face shape and hair type, look at their hairstyles for inspiration. Keep in mind that their hair may be styled by professionals and may not be practical for everyday wear.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have a general idea of what to consider when choosing a haircut, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you find the perfect style:

  1. Start by identifying your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining your face in the mirror. There are several face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond.
  2. Research haircuts that flatter your face shape. You can do this by looking at pictures online or in magazines, or by consulting with a hairstylist.
  3. Consider your hair type and texture. Think about how much time and effort you’re willing to put into styling your hair, and choose a cut that’s manageable for you.
  4. Bring pictures to your hairstylist. Once you’ve narrowed down your options, bring pictures of the hairstyles you like to your hairstylist. They can help you determine which cut will work best for your face shape and hair type.
  5. Be open to suggestions. Your hairstylist may have suggestions for slight modifications to your chosen cut that will better suit your face shape and hair type.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right haircut:

  • Consider your hairline. If you have a receding hairline, a short, textured cut may be a better option than a long, layered cut.
  • Think about your hair’s natural part. If your hair naturally parts in the middle, a straight, blunt cut may be more flattering than a side-swept style.
  • Experiment with different lengths. If you’re not sure which length will look best on you, try a shorter cut first. You can always grow your hair out if you don’t like it.
  • Consider your skin tone. Certain hair colors and highlights can complement your skin tone and enhance your features.

FAQs

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, most people should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep their hair looking healthy and well-maintained.

How can I maintain my haircut?

Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can help maintain the health and appearance of your haircut. It’s also important to use the right hair products for your hair type and to avoid excessive heat styling.

What if I don’t like my new haircut?

If you’re not happy with your new haircut, don’t panic. Hair grows back! Talk to your hairstylist about what you don’t like and see if they can make any adjustments. If you’re still not satisfied, consider trying a different style or hairstylist.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling by using a heat protectant spray and limiting your use of hot tools.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips and steps, you should be able to find a haircut that flatters your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Remember to be open to suggestions from your hairstylist and to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
