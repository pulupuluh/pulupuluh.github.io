Best Mid Length Hairstyles For Over 40

Written by Robby Apr 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair changes along with our bodies. For many women over 40, mid-length hairstyles are the perfect balance between too short and too long. They are versatile, easy to care for, and can be styled in many different ways. In this article, we will discuss the best mid-length hairstyles for women over 40.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Mid-length hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. They are a great option for women over 40 who want a hairstyle that is not too short or too long. Mid-length hairstyles can be worn straight, wavy, or curly, and can be styled with bangs or without. They are also easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways for different occasions.

How to

When choosing a mid-length hairstyle, it is important to consider your face shape and hair type. Some styles may work better for certain face shapes or hair types than others. It is also important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair.

Face Shape

Women with oval faces can wear almost any mid-length hairstyle, while women with round faces should avoid styles that add volume to the sides of their faces. Women with square faces should avoid blunt cuts and instead opt for styles with layers and soft edges. Women with heart-shaped faces should avoid styles that add volume to the top of their heads.

Hair Type

Women with thick hair may prefer a mid-length hairstyle that is layered to add texture and reduce bulk. Women with fine hair may prefer a mid-length hairstyle with blunt ends to add volume. Women with curly hair may prefer a mid-length hairstyle that is layered to help define their curls.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling some popular mid-length hairstyles:

The Lob

1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush.
3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
4. Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair for shine.
5. Style your hair with a center or side part.

The Shag

1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush.
3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
4. Apply a small amount of hair wax to the ends of your hair for texture.
5. Style your hair with layers and choppy ends.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your mid-length hairstyle:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair healthy and styled.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent it from becoming dry and damaged.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet to prevent breakage.

FAQ

What is a mid-length hairstyle?

A mid-length hairstyle is a hairstyle that falls between the chin and shoulders.

What face shape is best for a mid-length hairstyle?

Women with oval faces can wear almost any mid-length hairstyle, while women with round faces should avoid styles that add volume to the sides of their faces. Women with square faces should avoid blunt cuts and instead opt for styles with layers and soft edges. Women with heart-shaped faces should avoid styles that add volume to the top of their heads.

What hair type is best for a mid-length hairstyle?

Women with thick hair may prefer a mid-length hairstyle that is layered to add texture and reduce bulk. Women with fine hair may prefer a mid-length hairstyle with blunt ends to add volume. Women with curly hair may prefer a mid-length hairstyle that is layered to help define their curls.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent it from becoming dry and damaged.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair every day.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly when styling it.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes foods rich in vitamins and minerals that are good for your hair.

In conclusion, there are many different mid-length hairstyles that are perfect for women over 40. Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a style that works for you. With proper care and maintenance, your mid-length hairstyle will look great for years to come.
