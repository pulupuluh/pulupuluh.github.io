Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces Over 50 A Guide

Written by Daniel May 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you over the age of 50 and struggling to find the perfect short hairstyle for your round or chubby face? Look no further! In this guide, we will provide you with tips, tricks, and a variety of short hairstyles suitable for fat faces over 50. Let’s get started!

Description

As we age, our faces tend to lose volume, and our skin becomes less elastic, resulting in a rounder and fuller face. Women with round or chubby faces often struggle to find a flattering hairstyle that complements their features. Short haircuts can make your face look slimmer, give you a more youthful appearance, and require less maintenance.

How to

The first step is to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, you can opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head and elongate your face. A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs are excellent options. If you have a chubby face, you can go for a chin-length bob or a short shaggy haircut that frames your face and adds volume to the crown.

Step by Step

1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which short hairstyle is best suited for your face shape and hair type.

2. Choose a haircut that flatters your features and highlights your best facial features.

3. Consider adding layers or bangs to your haircut to create movement and texture.

4. Experiment with different hair styling products to achieve your desired look.

Tips

1. Avoid blunt cuts or hairstyles that end at your chin, as they can make your face look wider.

2. Go for asymmetrical hairstyles that create an illusion of a slimmer face.

3. Add highlights or low lights to your hair to create dimension and depth.

4. Use volumizing products to add volume to the crown of your head.

FAQ

Q: Are pixie cuts suitable for fat faces over 50?

A: Yes, pixie cuts with side-swept bangs can elongate your face and add volume to the crown of your head.

Q: Can I add highlights to my short hair?

A: Yes, adding highlights or low lights to your hair can create dimension and depth, making your face look slimmer.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs proper care. Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type and apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis and use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair.

There you have it – our guide to short hairstyles for fat faces over 50. Remember, the key is to choose a haircut that flatters your features and highlights your best facial features. With the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best at any age!
