How To Cut Your Own Hair Messy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, cutting your own hair messy can be a great way to achieve a trendy and effortless look. While it may seem daunting, with the right tools and techniques, you can successfully give yourself a messy haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to cut your own hair messy.

How To

Before you get started, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It's also important to work in a well-lit area, preferably in front of a mirror.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair so that it's clean and free of any tangles. If you have curly hair, it's best to cut it while it's dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Clip each section to keep them separate.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting with the back section, use your scissors to cut small sections of hair at a time. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle, moving your fingers in a zig-zag motion to create a messy look. Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Step 4: Trim the Ends

Once you've cut your hair into a messy style, take small sections and trim any uneven ends with your scissors. Be careful not to take off too much at once.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting, style your hair as desired. You can use a texturizing spray or pomade to enhance the messy look.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid taking off too much hair.
  • Use your fingers to twist and pull your hair while cutting to create a more natural and messy look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different cutting techniques and angles to achieve the perfect messy style.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair messy if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut curly hair messy. It's best to cut it while it's dry to avoid any shrinkage.

How often should I cut my hair messy?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain a healthy and fresh look.

Can I cut my own hair if it's long?

Yes, you can still cut your own hair if it's long. However, it may be more difficult to achieve a messy look on longer hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair messy, it's important to take good care of it to maintain its health and shine. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these simple steps and tips, you can successfully cut your own hair messy and achieve a trendy and effortless look. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
