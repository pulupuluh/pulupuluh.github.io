How To Do A Scissor Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending a lot of money on haircuts? Maybe it's time to learn how to do a scissor cut by yourself. It's a simple technique that can save you time and money. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of doing a scissor cut in a relaxed, easy-to-understand language.

How to Do a Scissor Cut

A scissor cut involves cutting hair with a pair of scissors. It's a basic technique that is widely used by hairstylists. Here's how to do a scissor cut in a few simple steps.

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair and Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You'll also need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into manageable sections. Start by dividing your hair in half down the middle, then create sections on each side.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut off the desired length. Repeat this process for each section of hair until you've cut all the hair.

Step 4: Check the Length

After cutting all the hair, check the length by running your fingers through it. If there are any uneven spots, trim them with the scissors.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you're happy with the length, style your hair as desired. You can use a hairbrush or your fingers to create your desired look.

Tips for a Great Scissor Cut

Here are some tips to ensure you get a great scissor cut.

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Don't cut too much hair in one go.
  • Take your time to ensure you get an even cut.
  • Check the length as you go.
  • Be patient and don't rush the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about scissor cuts.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Can I use any scissors to cut my hair?

No, you should use hairdressing scissors, which are designed for cutting hair. Using regular scissors can lead to split ends and uneven cuts.

Can I cut my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your own curly hair. However, it's important to be careful and take your time. Curly hair tends to bounce up when it dries, so make sure you cut it a bit longer than you want it to be.

Hair Care after Scissor Cut

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care after a scissor cut.

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim the ends of your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to do a scissor cut, you can save time and money by cutting your own hair. Follow these simple steps and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
