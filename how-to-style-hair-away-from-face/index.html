How To Style Hair Away From Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're tired of having your hair constantly in your face, then it's time to learn how to style it away. Not only will this help you feel more comfortable, but it can also give you a fresh new look. In this guide, we'll show you step-by-step how to style your hair away from your face, as well as some tips and tricks to make it easier.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Hair Away From Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Hair Away From Face
  • FAQs

How to Style Hair Away From Face

There are many ways to style your hair away from your face, but some of the most popular methods include:

  • Ponytail
  • Bun
  • Braid
  • Half-Up, Half-Down
  • Clip or Pin Back

These methods work for a variety of hair lengths and textures, so experiment with each to find the one that works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair away from your face:

1. Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to work with.

2. Decide on a Style

Choose the method you want to use to style your hair. If you're not sure, try a few different methods to see which one looks best on you.

3. Gather Your Hair

Gather your hair into a ponytail, bun, braid, or half-up, half-down style. Use a clip or elastic to secure it in place.

4. Smooth Any Flyaways

Use a small amount of hairspray or styling product to smooth any flyaways or frizz.

5. Secure with Accessories

Use a clip, pin, or headband to secure your hair in place and keep it away from your face.

Tips for Styling Hair Away From Face

Here are some tips to help make styling your hair away from your face easier:

  • Use bobby pins or hairpins to secure hair in place.
  • Try using a hairband or headband to keep hair back.
  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color for a more seamless look.
  • Use a light hairspray to keep hair in place without making it stiff or crunchy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.

FAQs

Q: Can I still style my hair away from my face if I have short hair?

A: Yes! You can still use a clip or headband to pull your hair back, or try a braid or half-up, half-down style.

Q: How can I keep my hair from falling out of the ponytail or bun?

A: Make sure to use a hair tie or elastic that is strong enough to hold your hair in place. You can also try using bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Hair Care Tips

Styling your hair away from your face can be a great way to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair moisturized.
  • Trim hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or styling products, as this can weigh hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep hair healthy from the inside out.

Now that you know how to style your hair away from your face, try out some of these methods and see which one works best for you. With a little experimentation and practice, you'll be able to create a variety of stylish looks that keep your hair out of your face and looking great.
