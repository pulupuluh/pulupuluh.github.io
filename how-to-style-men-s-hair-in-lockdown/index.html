How To Style Men s Hair In Lockdown A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Feb 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

With the ongoing pandemic, many men have been forced to adjust their grooming routines. With salons and barbershops closed, it can be challenging to maintain a stylish hairstyle at home. However, with a little creativity and the right tools, it's possible to style your hair and look your best during lockdown.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Men's Hair in Lockdown
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Style Men's Hair in Lockdown

One of the most important things to keep in mind when styling your hair during lockdown is to keep it simple. You don't need to be a professional hairstylist to achieve a great look. All you need is a few basic tools and some practice.

Before you start styling, it's essential to know what type of hair you have. This will help you choose the right products and styling techniques. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, you'll need to use different products than someone with thin, straight hair.

Tools You'll Need

Here are the basic tools you'll need to style your hair:

  • Comb
  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Hair gel or wax
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your hair during lockdown:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Use a comb to part your hair in the desired direction.
  4. Use clippers or scissors to trim the sides and back of your hair.
  5. Apply hair gel or wax to the top of your hair and style it as desired.
  6. Use hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your hair:

  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help or advice from friends or family members.
  • Use quality hair products to achieve the best results.
  • Consider investing in a good pair of clippers or scissors for at-home haircuts.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's hair during lockdown:

Can I cut my own hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your own hair at home, but it's important to be careful and use the right tools. Consider watching tutorials or asking for advice before attempting a haircut.

What's the best product to style men's hair?

There's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as the best product for styling men's hair depends on the individual's hair type and desired style. Consider experimenting with different products to find what works best for you.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's generally recommended to wash your hair every other day or every two to three days. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and personal preference.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, stylish hair during lockdown. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Consider using hair masks or other treatments to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish, groomed look during lockdown without leaving your home. Remember to keep it simple, experiment with different styles, and take good care of your hair to maintain a healthy, attractive appearance.
