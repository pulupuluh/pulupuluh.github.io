How To Style Shoulder Length Curly Layered Hair

Are you looking for ways to style your shoulder-length curly layered hair? Look no further! In this article, we'll provide you with tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Shoulder length curly layered hair is a versatile and beautiful hairstyle that can be styled in numerous ways. The layers add volume and texture while the curls add bounce and movement. Whether you're going for a casual look or a formal event, shoulder length curly layered hair can be styled to suit any occasion.

How to Style Shoulder Length Curly Layered Hair

There are many ways to style shoulder length curly layered hair. You can wear it down, swept to the side, in a ponytail, or in an updo. Here are some popular styles:

1. Loose Waves

To achieve loose waves, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance the curls. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to curl random sections of your hair. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

2. Half-Up, Half-Down

For a half-up, half-down style, gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the rest of your hair down. You can add curls or waves to the down section for added texture.

3. Messy Bun

To create a messy bun, gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. Twist your hair around the hair tie and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands to create a messy, relaxed look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style shoulder length curly layered hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for curly hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's wet.

Step 2: Apply Curl-Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 3: Diffuse Your Hair

Attach a diffuser attachment to your blow dryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting. Use your hands to scrunch your hair as you dry it to enhance the curls.

Step 4: Add Volume

To add volume to your hair, flip your head upside down and use a pick or a wide-tooth comb to tease the roots of your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair in your desired style. You can wear it down, half-up, half-down, or in an updo.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Curly Layered Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your shoulder length curly layered hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and reduce frizz
  • Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls
  • Tease the roots of your hair to add volume
  • Use bobby pins to secure your updo
  • Leave some strands of hair out for a relaxed, messy look

FAQs

What products should I use for curly hair?

You should use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

Curly hair should be washed about once or twice a week. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Curly Layered Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your shoulder length curly layered hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's wet
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

By following these tips and using the right products, you can easily style and care for your shoulder length curly layered hair. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
