How To Cut Men s Hair Short Sides Longer Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read
If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, the short sides longer top hairstyle is a great option. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks trendy and fashionable. In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's hair short sides longer top in a relaxed and easy-to-follow language.

Description

The short sides longer top hairstyle is a classic look that has been around for years. The sides of the hair are cut short while the top is left longer, creating a contrast in length that is both stylish and versatile. This hairstyle can be worn in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, messy, or with a side part. It's a perfect look for any occasion, from a casual day out to a formal event.

How to Cut Men's Hair Short Sides Longer Top

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair short sides longer top:

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section the hair by separating the top from the sides. Use a comb to create a parting line from the temple to the back of the head.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Use the clippers to cut the sides of the hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard, depending on the desired length.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use the scissors to cut the top of the hair. Start at the front of the head and work your way to the back. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair by using the scissors to cut the hair where the sides and the top meet. Use a comb to blend the hair together for a seamless look.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Style the hair using your preferred method, such as slicking it back or adding a side part.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard when cutting the sides and work your way down to a shorter guard to avoid cutting too much hair. - Use a mirror to check the back of your head to ensure an even cut. - Cut the hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises when it dries. - Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or a professional if you're unsure about cutting your own hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get my hair cut if I have a short sides longer top hairstyle? A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, a haircut every 4-6 weeks is recommended. Q: Can I still style my hair if I have a short sides longer top haircut? A: Yes, absolutely! This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many ways, such as slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

Hair Care

To maintain your short sides longer top hairstyle, it's important to keep your hair clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type, and avoid using too much product. Trim the hair around the sides and the back every 2-3 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut. In conclusion, the short sides longer top hairstyle is a trendy and versatile haircut that is perfect for any occasion. By following this step-by-step guide, you can easily cut your own hair or ask for help from a friend or a professional. With proper maintenance and care, you can rock this hairstyle for a long time.
