How To Make Curtain Bangs Look Nice A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're thinking about getting bangs, one of the most popular styles right now is curtain bangs. These soft, wispy bangs can frame your face and add a touch of glamour to any hairstyle. However, if you're not careful, curtain bangs can quickly become a messy, unflattering mess. In this guide, we'll show you how to make curtain bangs look nice and stylish.

Description

Curtain bangs are a timeless and elegant style that frames your face with a soft, wispy fringe. They're called "curtain" bangs because they're parted down the middle, just like a pair of curtains. Curtain bangs can be styled in many ways, from straight and sleek to tousled and messy.

How to Make Curtain Bangs Look Nice

Making curtain bangs look nice isn't difficult, but it does require a bit of effort and attention to detail. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Curtain bangs look best when they're clean and dry. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair, making sure to style your bangs in the direction you want them to go. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, use a straightener to smooth out your bangs.

Step 2: Part your bangs in the middle

Curtain bangs are all about the part, so make sure your part is in the middle. Use a fine-tooth comb to create a straight part down the middle of your bangs.

Step 3: Style your bangs

The great thing about curtain bangs is that they can be styled in many ways. Here are a few options:

  • Straight and sleek: Use a straightener to create a smooth, polished look.
  • Tousled and messy: Use a texturizing spray to create a messy, beachy look.
  • Pinned back: Use bobby pins to pin your bangs back and create a half-up, half-down look.

Step 4: Trim your bangs regularly

To keep your curtain bangs looking nice and neat, make sure to trim them regularly. If you're not comfortable trimming them yourself, visit a hair salon to have them trimmed by a professional.

Tips for Making Curtain Bangs Look Nice

Here are a few additional tips for making curtain bangs look nice:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using a straightener or curling iron.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and textures.
  • If you're not sure about getting curtain bangs, try clip-in bangs first to see how they look.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

How long do curtain bangs take to grow out?

It can take anywhere from 3 to 6 months for curtain bangs to grow out, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Can I style curtain bangs with curly hair?

Yes! Curtain bangs look great with curly hair. Just make sure to use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls.

Do curtain bangs work with all face shapes?

Yes, curtain bangs can work with all face shapes. Just make sure to adjust the length and thickness of your bangs to flatter your face.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Keeping your hair healthy and shiny is key to making your curtain bangs look nice. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this is less damaging than a brush.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your curtain bangs look stylish and chic. Whether you're going for a sleek and polished look or a tousled and messy style, curtain bangs are a versatile and trendy option for any hair type.
