Shoulder Length Hairstyles For A Round Face The Ultimate Guide

If you have a round face and are looking for the perfect shoulder length hairstyle, you've come to the right place! In this guide, we'll be covering everything you need to know about finding the perfect hairstyle to flatter your round face shape. From how to determine your face shape, to tips and tricks for styling your hair, we've got you covered. So let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your round face is to determine your face shape. To do this, you'll need to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, you can compare them to the following guidelines:

  • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width, you have a round face shape.
  • If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline, you have a heart-shaped face.
  • If your cheekbones are wider than your forehead and jawline, you have a square face.
  • If your jawline is wider than your forehead and cheekbones, you have a diamond face.

If you're still unsure about your face shape, you can try using a face shape app or consult with a hairstylist for advice.

Shoulder Length Hairstyles for a Round Face

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to find the perfect shoulder length hairstyle to flatter your round face. Here are some great options:

1. Long Bob

The long bob, or "lob," is a great option for those with round faces. This hairstyle hits right around shoulder length and features subtle layers to add texture and movement. To add even more dimension, consider adding highlights or lowlights.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add interest to a shoulder length hairstyle for a round face. The diagonal line of the bangs helps to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

3. Soft Waves

Soft waves are a great option for those with round faces. The gentle curves help to elongate the face shape and add volume and movement to the hair.

4. Layered Shoulder Length Cut

A layered shoulder length cut is a great way to add volume and texture to a round face. The layers should be long and subtle, with the shortest layer hitting at the chin.

Tips and Tricks for Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Once you've found the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your round face, it's important to know how to style it to best flatter your features. Here are some tips and tricks:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.
  • Use a curling iron to create soft waves or curls.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Experiment with different part positions to see which one flatters your face shape the most.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that adds height and length to the face, such as a long bob or layered shoulder length cut.

What hairstyles should I avoid with a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or a blunt cut that ends at the jawline.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your shoulder length hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect shoulder length hairstyle to flatter your round face shape. Whether you opt for a long bob, side-swept bangs, or soft waves, remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Happy styling!
