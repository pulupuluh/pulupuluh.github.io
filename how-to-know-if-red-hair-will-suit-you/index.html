How To Know If Red Hair Will Suit You A Guide For 2023

Are you considering dyeing your hair red, but not sure if it will suit you? Red hair can be a bold and beautiful choice, but it's important to make sure it complements your skin tone and features. In this guide, we'll show you how to know if red hair will suit you in 2023.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Know if Red Hair Will Suit You
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Shade
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Red Hair

How to Know if Red Hair Will Suit You

There are a few factors to consider when deciding if red hair will suit you:

Skin Tone:

Red hair can look great on a variety of skin tones, but it's important to choose the right shade. If you have cool undertones, a blue-based red like auburn or burgundy may be the best choice. If you have warm undertones, a copper or golden red may be more flattering.

Eye Color:

Red hair can bring out the beauty in different eye colors. Blue and green eyes can pop against a rich red hue, while brown eyes can be complemented with a warm, coppery tone.

Natural Hair Color:

If you have naturally dark hair, a dramatic red can be a stunning change. However, if you have lighter or already red-toned hair, a subtler shade may be a better choice.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you decide if red hair will suit you:

  1. Consider your skin tone: Are you cool-toned or warm-toned?
  2. Think about your eye color: What shades will complement your eyes?
  3. Look at your natural hair color: Will a dramatic change suit you, or should you opt for a subtler shade?
  4. Consult with a professional stylist: They can help you choose the right shade and provide expert advice on hair care and maintenance.
  5. Try a temporary color: If you're not ready to commit to a permanent change, try a temporary color or highlights to see how you like the look.

Tips for Choosing the Right Shade

Here are some tips to help you choose the right shade of red:

  • Use online tools to "try on" different shades of red before committing to a color.
  • Consider the season: Warmer reds may be more flattering in the fall and winter, while brighter reds can be perfect for summer.
  • Choose a shade that complements your wardrobe: If you wear a lot of cool colors, a cooler-toned red may be more flattering.
  • Take your lifestyle into account: If you have a busy schedule, a low-maintenance shade may be a better choice.

FAQs

Can anyone pull off red hair?

While red hair can look great on many people, it's important to choose the right shade for your skin tone and features. Consulting with a professional stylist can help you make the best choice.

Is red hair high-maintenance?

Red hair can require more maintenance than other colors, as it tends to fade faster. Regular touch-ups and using color-safe products can help preserve your color.

What if I don't like the way red hair looks on me?

If you're not happy with your red hair, you can always dye it back to your natural color or try a different shade. A professional stylist can help you make the best choice.

Hair Care Tips for Red Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your red hair:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your color.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can cause your color to fade faster.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Consider getting regular touch-ups to keep your color looking vibrant.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently decide if red hair will suit you in 2023. Whether you opt for a bold and bright shade or a subtle copper hue, red hair can be a stunning and unique choice.
