Medium Length Haircuts Over 50 A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Style

Written by Eveline Mar 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on medium length haircuts over 50. As we age, our hair changes and so do our preferences. Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, but we're here to help you find the perfect one. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to choose the right style to hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Medium Length Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Your Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Length Haircut

How to Choose the Right Medium Length Haircut

When choosing a medium length haircut over 50, there are a few factors to consider. First, think about your face shape. Some styles may be more flattering than others depending on your facial features. Second, consider your hair texture. Some haircuts may work better with fine or thick hair. Finally, think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day.

Here are a few popular medium length haircuts to consider:

Shoulder-Length Layers

Shoulder-length layers are a great option for those with fine or thin hair. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, making it look fuller. This style is also easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Long Bob

The long bob, or "lob," is a trendy and versatile haircut that works well on most face shapes. It's a great option for those who want a shorter style without going too drastic. The long bob can be styled straight, wavy, or curly depending on your mood.

Blunt Cut

The blunt cut is a classic style that never goes out of fashion. It's a great option for those with thick hair as it helps to remove some of the bulk. This style looks best when straightened and can be worn with or without bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Your Haircut

Now that you've chosen your perfect medium length haircut, it's time to get it done! Here's a step-by-step guide to help you prepare for your salon appointment:

  1. Bring a few photos of your desired style to show your stylist.
  2. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  3. Communicate clearly with your stylist about what you want.
  4. Relax and enjoy the process!

Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Length Haircut

Once you've got your new haircut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your medium length haircut:

  • Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs About Medium Length Haircuts Over 50

Here are a few common questions about medium length haircuts over 50:

Q: Will a medium length haircut make me look younger?

A: While a great haircut can certainly make you look and feel younger, it's not a guarantee. The right style will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences.

Q: How often should I get my medium length haircut trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a medium length haircut?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of ways to style medium length hair up, including ponytails, buns, and braids.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Haircuts

To keep your medium length haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect medium length haircut over 50 that suits your personal style and preferences. Remember to communicate clearly with your stylist and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
