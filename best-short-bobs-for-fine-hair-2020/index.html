Best Short Bobs For Fine Hair 2020

Written by Eveline Apr 14, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will give your fine hair the volume and texture it needs? Look no further than the short bob! In 2020, there were plenty of short bob hairstyles that were perfect for fine hair. In this article, we will discuss the best short bobs for fine hair in 2020, as well as some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle.

Description

A short bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit any face shape and hair texture. For those with fine hair, a short bob can add volume and texture, making hair appear thicker and fuller. In 2020, there were many variations of the short bob that were perfect for fine hair, from blunt bobs to choppy bobs.

How to

Getting a short bob for fine hair is easy. First, find a reputable hairstylist who has experience cutting hair for your hair type. Then, discuss your hair goals with your stylist and ask for their recommendations. They may suggest a blunt bob for a sleek look, or a choppy bob for a more textured look. Once you've decided on a style, sit back and let your stylist work their magic!

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash your hair

Before getting your hair cut, make sure it's clean and free of any styling products. This will make it easier for your stylist to cut and style your hair.

Step 2: Consult with your stylist

Discuss your hair goals with your stylist and ask for their recommendations. They may suggest a blunt bob for a sleek look, or a choppy bob for a more textured look.

Step 3: Sit back and let your stylist work their magic

Your stylist will cut and style your hair according to your preferences. Sit back, relax, and enjoy your new hairstyle!

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new short bob:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair extra volume and texture.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your bob's shape and keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening, to create different looks.

FAQ

Q: Will a short bob make my fine hair look thinner?

Not necessarily. A well-executed short bob can actually make fine hair appear thicker and fuller by adding volume and texture.

Q: How often should I get my short bob trimmed?

It's best to get your short bob trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and keep your hair healthy.

Q: Can I still style my short bob even if I have fine hair?

Absolutely! There are many styling techniques you can use to create different looks with your short bob, such as curling or straightening.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, shiny, and voluminous short bob. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your bob's shape and keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know the best short bobs for fine hair in 2020, as well as some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out this classic and versatile haircut!
