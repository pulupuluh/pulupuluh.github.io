Easy Care Short Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Aprilia May 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know that styling it can often be a challenge. However, with the right haircut, you can make your hair more manageable and easy to style. In this article, we will discuss some easy care short hairstyles for thick wavy hair that you can try out.

Description

Thick wavy hair can be difficult to style, especially if it is long. It can be heavy and hard to manage, and it can take a long time to dry and style. That's why many women with thick wavy hair opt for shorter hairstyles that are more manageable and easy to style.

How to

If you're considering a short hairstyle for your thick wavy hair, there are a few things you need to keep in mind. First, you'll want to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture. Second, you'll want to consider the amount of time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair. Third, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that you feel comfortable with and that makes you feel confident.

Step by Step

Here are some easy care short hairstyles for thick wavy hair that you can try out:

Tips

1. Consider getting layers to add volume and movement to your hair.

2. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves.

3. Use a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or a texturizing spray, to define your waves and add texture.

4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a brush can cause frizz and breakage.

5. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Which short hairstyles are best for thick wavy hair?

A: Some of the best short hairstyles for thick wavy hair include a bob, a lob, a pixie cut, and a shag.

Q: How can I make my thick wavy hair more manageable?

A: You can make your hair more manageable by getting regular trims, using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair, using lightweight styling products, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your thick wavy hair, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are formulated for thick or curly hair, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, and use a heat protectant spray if you do use them. Finally, get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
