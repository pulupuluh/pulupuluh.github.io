How To Style Men s Long Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jun 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long wavy hair can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you style it. It can make you look effortlessly cool and stylish, or it can make you look like you just rolled out of bed. The key to pulling off long wavy hair is to know how to style it properly. In this article, we'll show you how to style men's long wavy hair like a pro.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Men's Long Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Wavy Hair
  • FAQs About Long Wavy Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Wavy Hair

How to Style Men's Long Wavy Hair

The first step to styling men's long wavy hair is to know what kind of wavy hair you have. Is it loose waves or tight curls? Is it thick or thin? Knowing your hair type will help you choose the right styling products and techniques.

When it comes to styling long wavy hair, there are a few different looks you can go for. Some of the most popular styles include:

  • Beachy waves
  • Surfer hair
  • Slicked-back waves
  • Curly fringe

Each of these styles requires a different approach to styling, so it's important to know what you're going for before you start.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling men's long wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair. This could be a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your hair type and the style you're going for.
  3. Use a diffuser on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your natural waves and prevent frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create your desired style.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair wax to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Long Wavy Hair

Here are a few tips for styling men's long wavy hair:

  • Don't wash your hair too often. Wavy hair tends to be dry, so washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help prevent breakage and frizz.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and the style you're going for.
  • Don't be afraid to use a little heat. A diffuser on your hair dryer or a curling iron can help enhance your natural waves.

FAQs About Long Wavy Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's long wavy hair:

Q: How often should I wash my long wavy hair?

A: It's best to wash wavy hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: What styling products should I use for long wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you're going for. Mousse, gel, and cream are all good options for wavy hair.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my long wavy hair?

A: Use a diffuser on your hair dryer, avoid washing your hair too often, and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Long Wavy Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for men's long wavy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair, as this can dry it out.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can style men's long wavy hair like a pro. Whether you're going for beachy waves or a slicked-back look, the key is to work with your natural texture and find the right products and techniques that work for you.
