How To Make Wavy Hair Naturally Curly

Written by Eveline May 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of having wavy hair that just won't curl? Do you long for bouncy, curly locks without resorting to harsh styling tools or chemicals? Well, you're in luck! With a few simple tricks and techniques, you can transform your wavy hair into beautiful, natural curls. In this article, we'll show you how to make wavy hair naturally curly using only natural methods and products. So, let's get started!

  • How to Make Wavy Hair Naturally Curly

Description

Wavy hair can be beautiful, but sometimes you just want more defined curls. The good news is that you don't have to resort to harsh chemicals or hot styling tools to get the look you want. With a few natural techniques, you can enhance your hair's natural texture and create beautiful, bouncy curls. Whether you have naturally wavy hair or want to create curls from straight hair, these tips will help you achieve your desired look.

How to Make Wavy Hair Naturally Curly

The first step to making wavy hair naturally curly is to understand your hair type. Wavy hair is typically more prone to frizz and can be easily weighed down by heavy products. To enhance your natural waves and create more defined curls, you'll need to use lightweight products that won't weigh down your hair. Here are some tips for making wavy hair naturally curly:

1. Use a Curl-Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by using a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy or curly hair and that contain ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, or argan oil. These ingredients help to moisturize your hair and protect it from frizz.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help define your curls. Use a lightweight, curl-enhancing product that won't weigh down your hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Scrunch Your Hair

To enhance your natural waves and create more defined curls, try scrunching your hair. After applying your leave-in conditioner, gently scrunch your hair with your hands. This will help to enhance your natural waves and create more defined curls.

4. Use a Diffuser Attachment

If you're using a hairdryer, use a diffuser attachment to help enhance your curls. This attachment helps to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Start by using the hairdryer on low heat and low speed, and gently diffuse your hair from the roots to the ends.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make wavy hair naturally curly:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a lightweight, curl-enhancing leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Gently scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your natural waves and create more defined curls.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser Attachment

If you're using a hairdryer, use a diffuser attachment to help enhance your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you've achieved your desired curls, it's important to maintain them. Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Swap out your cotton pillowcase for a silk one. Silk pillowcases are gentler on your hair and help to prevent frizz.

2. Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Avoid brushing your hair, as this can disrupt your curls and create frizz. Instead, use your fingers to gently detangle your hair.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and create frizz.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my straight hair curly?

A: Yes, you can use the same techniques to make straight hair curly. Start by applying a curl-enhancing product to your hair, then use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to create curls.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's best to wash curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and bouncy. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

2. Avoid Sulfates and Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using shampoos and styling products that contain sulfates or harsh chemicals, as these can dry out your hair and create frizz.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective product.

With these tips and techniques, you can make wavy hair naturally curly without resorting to harsh chemicals or hot styling tools. Remember to use lightweight, curl-enhancing products and to maintain your curls with gentle care. Happy styling!
