How To Make Your Face Look More Oval

Written by Bardi Aug 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you may be looking for ways to make it look more oval. An oval face is considered to be the most attractive face shape, and it is also the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles and makeup. In this article, we will give you tips on how to make your face look more oval using makeup, hairstyles, and other techniques.

Table of Contents

  2. Step by Step

How to Make Your Face Look More Oval

There are several ways to make your face look more oval:

  • Use makeup to contour your face
  • Try a new hairstyle
  • Wear glasses that flatter your face shape
  • Use accessories to elongate your face

1. Use makeup to contour your face

Contouring is a technique that uses makeup to create shadows and highlights on your face, giving it a more defined shape. To make your face look more oval, you should focus on contouring your cheeks and jawline.

Start by applying a foundation that matches your skin tone. Then, use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to create shadows on the sides of your forehead, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline. Blend the contouring powder or cream well, so it looks natural.

Next, use a highlighter that is one or two shades lighter than your skin tone to create highlights on the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, and your cheekbones. Blend the highlighter well, so it looks natural.

2. Try a new hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make your face look more oval. If you have a round face, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will make your face look rounder. Instead, try hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head or that have layers that frame your face.

You can also try a side part or a deep side part, as this will create the illusion of an oval face shape. Another option is to wear your hair in an updo, as this will elongate your face.

3. Wear glasses that flatter your face shape

If you wear glasses, choose a pair that flatters your face shape. Oval-shaped glasses are ideal for round faces, as they balance out the roundness of your face. You can also try glasses with angular frames or cat-eye glasses, as these will create the illusion of an oval face shape.

4. Use accessories to elongate your face

Accessories can also help you make your face look more oval. Long earrings or necklaces can elongate your face, while wide-brimmed hats or headbands can create the illusion of an oval face shape.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to make your face look more oval:

  1. Apply foundation that matches your skin tone
  2. Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to create shadows on the sides of your forehead, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline
  3. Use a highlighter that is one or two shades lighter than your skin tone to create highlights on the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, and your cheekbones
  4. Blend the contouring powder or cream and the highlighter well, so it looks natural
  5. Try a new hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head or that has layers that frame your face
  6. Wear glasses that flatter your face shape, such as oval-shaped glasses or angular frames
  7. Use accessories to elongate your face, such as long earrings or necklaces, or wide-brimmed hats or headbands

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your face look more oval:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Avoid glasses with round frames
  • Choose accessories that elongate your face
  • Use contouring and highlighting to create shadows and highlights on your face
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and glasses to find the ones that flatter your face shape

FAQ

What is an oval face shape?

An oval face shape is considered to be the most attractive face shape. It is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the jawline, and a face length that is longer than the width of the face.

What hairstyles flatter a round face?

Hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head or that have layers that frame the face flatter a round face. A side part or a deep side part can also create the illusion of an oval face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Condition your hair after washing
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can make your face look more oval and feel confident in your appearance.
