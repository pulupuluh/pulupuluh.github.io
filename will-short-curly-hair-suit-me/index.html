Will Short Curly Hair Suit Me A Guide To Rocking The Curls

Written by Eveline Feb 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're considering chopping off your locks and going for a short curly hairstyle, you might be wondering if it will suit you. Short curly hair is a bold and striking look that can be both edgy and feminine. But, like any hairstyle, it's important to consider if it will work with your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this guide, we'll answer all your questions about whether short curly hair will suit you, and give you tips for rocking the perfect curly 'do.

Description: What is Short Curly Hair?

Short curly hair is any hairstyle that features curls or waves that fall above the shoulders. This can include bobs, pixie cuts, and other short hairstyles. Short curly hair can be natural or achieved through styling techniques like curling irons or rollers. This type of hairstyle is popular for its low-maintenance and effortless look, as well as its versatility.

How to: Determine If Short Curly Hair Will Suit You

When considering a short curly hairstyle, there are a few things to think about to determine if it will suit you:

Face Shape

Short curly hair can work with a variety of face shapes, but some styles may be more flattering than others. For example, if you have a round face, a short bob with layers can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a curly pixie cut can soften your features. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best style for your face shape.

Hair Texture

Short curly hair works best with natural curls or waves. If you have straight hair, you can achieve a curly look with styling techniques, but it may require more maintenance. If you have very tight or coily curls, you might want to consider a longer length to avoid the dreaded "triangle" shape.

Personal Style

Your personal style is an important factor to consider when choosing a short curly hairstyle. If you prefer a more classic or polished look, a sleek bob with loose waves may be more your style. If you're more edgy or adventurous, a curly pixie cut or asymmetrical bob might be right up your alley.

Step by Step: How to Style Short Curly Hair

Once you've decided that short curly hair is right for you, it's important to know how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair. Use a conditioner that's designed for curly hair to help define your curls.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a curl-defining styling product to your damp hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or cream designed for curly hair.

Step 3: Diffuse or Air-Dry

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, or simply air-dry. Avoid using a brush or comb, as this can disrupt your curls.

Step 4: Define Curls

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to define your curls. You can also use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need a little extra definition.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Rocking Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips for rocking short curly hair:

  • Invest in a good quality conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your natural oils and make your curls dry and frizzy
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to avoid disrupting your curls
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type
  • Accessorize with headbands, scarves, or statement earrings to add some extra flair to your look

FAQ: Common Questions About Short Curly Hair

Can I still straighten my hair if I have a short curly hairstyle?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have a short curly hairstyle. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid over-styling, as this can damage your hair.

Will short curly hair make my face look wider?

Short curly hair can actually help elongate your face, rather than making it look wider. Just be sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape.

How often should I trim my short curly hair?

It's important to trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Short curly hair requires a little extra TLC to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your natural oils and make your curls dry and frizzy
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

In conclusion, short curly hair can be a bold and striking look that works with a variety of face shapes and personal styles. By following these tips and taking good care of your curls, you can rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence.
