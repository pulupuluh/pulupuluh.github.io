How To Curl The Left Side Of Your Hair With A Wand

Written by Bardi Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you want to add some volume and texture to your hair, curling is a great way to do it. Using a wand is one of the easiest ways to curl your hair, but if you're not careful, you can end up with a lopsided look. In this blog post, we'll show you how to curl the left side of your hair with a wand, step by step.

Description

Curling the left side of your hair with a wand can be a bit tricky since the wand is designed to be held in your right hand. However, with a little practice and the right technique, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls on both sides of your head.

How to

Step 1: Prepping your hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean, dry, and brushed out. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the wand's heat. You can also apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add some extra texture and hold to your hair.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using a comb, divide your hair into two sections, with one section on the left and the other on the right. Clip the right section out of the way so you can focus on curling the left side.

Step 3: Heat up your wand

Turn on your wand and let it heat up to the desired temperature. For fine hair, use a lower temperature setting, and for thicker hair, use a higher temperature setting. Make sure to use a wand with a clip or glove to protect your hands from the heat.

Step 4: Start curling

Take a small section of hair from the left side of your head and wrap it around the wand, starting at the root and working your way down. Hold the hair in place for a few seconds, then release it from the wand. Repeat this process for the rest of your hair on the left side.

Step 5: Repeat on the right side

Once you've finished curling the left side of your hair, unclip the right side and repeat the same process. Remember to hold the wand in your right hand and curl the hair away from your face.

Tips

Here are some tips to make sure you get the best results when curling the left side of your hair with a wand:

  • Practice makes perfect. Don't worry if your curls don't look perfect at first. The more you practice, the better you'll get.
  • Use a smaller wand for tighter curls and a larger wand for looser curls.
  • Don't hold the hair on the wand for too long, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold your curls in place.
  • Experiment with different techniques, such as twisting the hair before wrapping it around the wand, to create different types of curls.

FAQ

Can you curl your hair with a wand if it's short?

Yes, you can curl short hair with a wand. Use a smaller wand and take smaller sections of hair to create tighter curls.

How long will the curls last?

How long your curls last will depend on your hair type, the products you use, and how well you maintain your curls. Generally, curls will last for a few hours to a full day.

Do I need to use a heat protectant spray?

Yes, it's always a good idea to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair from the wand's heat.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to curl the left side of your hair with a wand, you can experiment with different styles and techniques to create the perfect look for you.
