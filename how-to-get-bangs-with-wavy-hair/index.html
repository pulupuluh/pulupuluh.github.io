How To Get Bangs With Wavy Hair

Written by Oliver Apr 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but worried about how they will look with your wavy hair? Don't worry, with the right technique and hair care, you can rock a bang with wavy locks. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of how to get bangs with wavy hair, including tips and hair care.

Description

Bangs are a great way to change up your look and add some flair to your hairstyle. However, if you have wavy hair, getting bangs can be a bit tricky. Wavy hair tends to have a mind of its own and can go in all directions. But fear not, with some effort and the right techniques, you can get the perfect bangs for your wavy hair.

How to

The first step in getting bangs with wavy hair is to determine the type of bangs that will suit your face shape. There are different types of bangs, such as side-swept, blunt, or choppy. Once you have decided on the type of bangs, it's time to cut them. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, gently towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp. Next, blow-dry your hair until it's about 80% dry. This will make it easier to cut your bangs.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by parting it down the middle and then from ear to ear. Clip the rest of your hair away from your face, leaving only the section you want to cut as your bangs.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Start cutting your bangs from the middle and work your way outwards. Use small, sharp scissors and start with small snips. Remember, you can always cut more, so start with a little and work your way up. If you want side-swept bangs, cut them at an angle, following the curve of your eyebrow. If you want blunt bangs, cut them straight across.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once you have cut your bangs, it's time to style them. Use a round brush and a blow-dryer to style your bangs. If you want to add some volume, use a volumizing spray or mousse. If you want to tame your waves, use a smoothing cream or serum.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect bangs for your wavy hair:

  • Make sure your hair is dry before cutting your bangs.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Start with small snips and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's cut.
  • When styling your bangs, use a round brush and blow-dryer for best results.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Consider getting your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting bangs with wavy hair:

Can I get bangs with curly hair?

Yes, you can get bangs with curly hair. However, curly hair tends to shrink up, so make sure to cut your bangs when your hair is dry and stretched out. You can also consider getting longer bangs that blend in with the rest of your hair.

How do I know if bangs will suit my face shape?

There are different types of bangs that suit different face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, side-swept bangs can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, blunt bangs can soften your features. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best type of bangs for your face shape.

What if I don't like my bangs?

If you don't like your bangs, don't panic. Bangs grow out quickly, so you can either wait for them to grow out or have them trimmed to a different style.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your wavy hair and bangs looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing your hair when it's wet.

With these tips and techniques, you can get the perfect bangs for your wavy hair. Remember, getting bangs is a big change, so make sure you're ready for the commitment. But don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
