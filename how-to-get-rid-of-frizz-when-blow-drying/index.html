How To Get Rid Of Frizz When Blow Drying Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank May 07, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizz every time you blow dry your hair? Frizz can be a frustrating problem to deal with, but don't worry – it's not impossible to get rid of. In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to get rid of frizz when blow drying your hair.

Description

Frizz is a common problem for people with all hair types, but it's especially common for those with curly or wavy hair. When you blow dry your hair, the hot air can cause the cuticles to lift, resulting in frizz. Additionally, the lack of moisture in the air can cause your hair to become dry and frizzy.

There are several ways to combat frizz when blow drying your hair, including using the right products, using the right technique, and taking care of your hair in between washes.

How to Get Rid of Frizz When Blow Drying - Step by Step

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to getting rid of frizz when blow drying your hair is to start with clean hair. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. If you have curly or wavy hair, consider using a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start blow drying your hair, make sure to apply a heat protectant. This will help protect your hair from the heat of the blow dryer and prevent damage. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Use the Right Technique

The key to getting rid of frizz when blow drying your hair is to use the right technique. Start by using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and then use a round brush to smooth out your hair as you blow dry it. Be sure to keep the blow dryer moving constantly to prevent overheating any one section of your hair.

Step 4: Finish with a Cool Shot

When you're finished blow drying your hair, finish with a cool shot of air. This will help to seal the cuticles and prevent frizz.

Tips for Getting Rid of Frizz When Blow Drying

Here are some additional tips for getting rid of frizz when blow drying your hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer if you have curly or wavy hair
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair before blow drying, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze excess water out of your hair before blow drying
  • Avoid using high heat settings on your blow dryer, as this can cause damage and frizz
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while blow drying, as this can cause frizz

FAQ

Q: Can I use a leave-in conditioner to help prevent frizz?
A: Yes, a leave-in conditioner can be a great way to help prevent frizz. Apply it evenly throughout your hair before blow drying.

Q: How often should I blow dry my hair?
A: It's best to limit blow drying to once or twice a week to prevent damage and dryness.

Hair Care Tips for Preventing Frizz

In addition to using the right products and techniques when blow drying your hair, there are several hair care tips that can help prevent frizz:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent friction and frizz while you sleep
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage and frizz

By following these tips and tricks, you can say goodbye to frizz and hello to smooth, healthy-looking hair.
