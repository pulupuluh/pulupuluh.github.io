How Long Do Large Knotless Braids Last Tips And Hair Care

Written by Petter Jun 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering getting large knotless braids but wondering how long they will last? You're not alone! Knotless braids have become increasingly popular over the years, and for good reason. They're a protective style that can last for weeks, even months, with proper care. In this article, we'll discuss how long you can expect your large knotless braids to last and provide some tips on how to care for them.

Table of Contents

  • Introduction
  • How To Get Large Knotless Braids
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Large Knotless Braids
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Large Knotless Braids

Introduction

Large knotless braids are a great option for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that will last for weeks. Unlike traditional braids, there are no knots at the base of each braid, which makes them less likely to cause breakage or damage to your hair.

How To Get Large Knotless Braids

The first step to getting large knotless braids is to find a stylist who specializes in this technique. They will be able to help you choose the right type of hair for your braids and provide guidance on the best length and width for your individual needs.

Step By Step Guide

The process of getting large knotless braids is similar to getting traditional braids, but without the knots. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, detangled hair.
  2. Section your hair into small parts.
  3. Take a small section of extension hair and attach it to your natural hair using the feed-in method.
  4. Begin braiding, making sure to keep the tension even throughout the braid.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the desired length, then secure the braid with a hair tie.
  6. Repeat the process until all sections of hair have been braided.
  7. Trim any excess extension hair if necessary.

Tips for Maintaining Large Knotless Braids

Here are some tips to help you maintain your large knotless braids and keep them looking great for as long as possible:

  • Wash your braids regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Keep your scalp moisturized by applying a lightweight oil or leave-in conditioner to your scalp every few days.
  • Avoid excessive pulling or tugging on your braids, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin scarf or pillowcase to protect your braids while you sleep.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about large knotless braids:

How long do large knotless braids last?

With proper care, large knotless braids can last for up to 8 weeks.

Can you swim with large knotless braids?

Yes, you can swim with large knotless braids, but it's important to protect them by wearing a swim cap or keeping your head above water.

Do large knotless braids cause breakage?

No, large knotless braids are less likely to cause breakage than traditional braids because there are no knots at the base of each braid.

Hair Care for Large Knotless Braids

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair while wearing large knotless braids. Here are some tips:

  • Keep your scalp clean and moisturized by washing your braids regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Apply a lightweight oil or leave-in conditioner to your scalp every few days to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid excessive pulling or tugging on your braids, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin scarf or pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep.

With these tips and proper care, your large knotless braids can last for weeks, if not months. Enjoy your new hairstyle with confidence and style!
