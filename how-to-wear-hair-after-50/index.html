How To Wear Hair After 50 Tips Tricks And Hairstyles

Written by Eveline May 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're over 50 and wondering how to wear your hair, you're not alone. Many women find that as they age, their hair changes texture, becomes thinner, or loses its natural color. But that doesn't mean you can't have stylish, vibrant hair that makes you feel confident and beautiful. In this guide, we'll share some tips, tricks, and hairstyles that will help you look and feel your best.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear Hair After 50
  2. Step-By-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Your Hair
  4. FAQs

How to Wear Hair After 50

There are many different ways to wear your hair after 50, depending on your personal style, face shape, and hair texture. Here are some popular hairstyles to consider:

1. Short and Sassy

A short, layered haircut can be both fun and practical. It's easy to maintain and can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. Plus, it can help you look younger and more energetic.

2. Shoulder-Length Waves

Shoulder-length waves are a classic hairstyle that can be worn by women of all ages. They're versatile, easy to style, and can help soften your features. You can add layers or highlights to give your hair more dimension.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold, daring hairstyle that can be very flattering on women over 50. It's low-maintenance and can make you look more confident and sophisticated. You can add bangs or highlights to make it more personalized.

Step-By-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair after 50:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  3. Use a heat protectant before blow-drying or using hot tools.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift.
  5. Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or straighten your hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to help hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips for styling your hair after 50:

  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to give your hair more dimension.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or root lifter to add volume to your hair.
  • Try a new hair color, such as a bold red or soft blonde, to give your hair a fresh look.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and hydrated.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some flair to your style.

FAQs

Q: I have thinning hair. What can I do to make it look fuller?

A: Try a layered haircut or add some volumizing products to your hair. You can also consider using a hair topper or wig to add some extra volume and coverage.

Q: Can I still wear long hair after 50?

A: Absolutely! There's no age limit on long hair. Just make sure to keep it healthy and well-maintained.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, you should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and avoid split ends.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to wear hair after 50, it's time to experiment and find a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Whether you opt for a short and sassy cut or long, flowing locks, remember to take good care of your hair and have fun with your style!
