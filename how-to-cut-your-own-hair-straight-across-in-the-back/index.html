How To Cut Your Own Hair Straight Across In The Back

Written by Bardi Sep 03, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to trim your hair straight across in the back, you might be hesitant to go to a salon or barber. Luckily, with the right tools and technique, you can easily cut your own hair at home. In this post, we'll go through step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect straight cut, as well as some helpful tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Own Hair Straight Across in the Back
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Helpful Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

Step-by-Step Instructions

To start, you'll need a few tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you begin. Make sure it's completely dry, as wet hair can be difficult to cut evenly.

2. Divide your hair into sections

Use a comb to divide your hair into sections. Start by creating a middle part, then bring the hair forward over your shoulders. Divide the hair into two sections, one on each side of your head.

3. Cut the bottom layer

Take one of the sections and gather it all together at the nape of your neck. Use a hair tie or clip to secure the rest of your hair out of the way. This will be your bottom layer. Take your scissors and cut straight across, using the bottom of your hair as a guide. Make sure your scissors are sharp and cut slowly to avoid any mistakes.

4. Cut the top layer

Release the top layer of hair and bring it down over the bottom layer. Use the bottom layer as a guide and cut straight across. Be sure to cut the top layer evenly with the bottom layer.

5. Check for evenness

Use a mirror to check the back of your hair for evenness. If you notice any uneven spots, trim them carefully with your scissors.

Helpful Tips

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with a few helpful tips, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking cut:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. They'll make the process much easier and ensure a straight cut.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time. It's better to take longer and get a good result than rush and make a mistake.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you're struggling to reach the back of your head or can't see well in the mirror, ask a friend or family member to assist you.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about cutting your own hair:

Can I cut my hair wet?

It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be deceivingly longer and harder to cut evenly.

How often should I cut my hair?

Most people should aim to trim their hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

What do I do if I make a mistake?

If you accidentally cut too much off, don't panic. You can always trim a little more to even it out, or visit a salon for a professional fix.

Hair Care Advice

To maintain healthy hair after your DIY cut, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these easy-to-follow steps and helpful tips, you'll be able to cut your own hair straight across in the back and achieve a professional-looking result. Just remember to take your time and use a steady hand!
