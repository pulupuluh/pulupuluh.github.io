How to Change Your Hairstyle

Changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Let's Go is actually very simple. All you need to do is visit the Hair Salon in any major city. Once there, you'll be able to choose from a variety of different hairstyles to try out.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Let's Go:

  1. Go to any major city in the game.
  2. Find the Hair Salon.
  3. Enter the Hair Salon and talk to the stylist.
  4. Select the hairstyle you want to try out.
  5. Confirm your selection.
  6. Your character's hairstyle will be changed immediately.

It's that easy! Just be sure to save your game afterward so that your new hairstyle is saved.

Tips for Choosing a New Hairstyle

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a bit overwhelming, especially with so many options to choose from. Here are a few tips to help you make the right choice:

  • Consider your character's overall look and style.
  • Think about the type of Pokemon you typically use and try to choose a hairstyle that complements that type.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new! You can always change your hairstyle again later if you don't like it.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Let's Go:

  • Can I change my hairstyle multiple times? Yes, you can visit the Hair Salon as many times as you like to try out different hairstyles.
  • Do I have to pay to change my hairstyle? Yes, there is a fee for changing your hairstyle. The cost varies depending on the style you choose.
  • Will my new hairstyle affect my gameplay in any way? No, your hairstyle has no effect on your gameplay.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've changed your hairstyle, it's important to take care of it! Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Try not to pull your hair too tightly when styling it, as this can cause breakage and damage.

With these tips, you'll be able to keep your new hairstyle looking great for as long as you want. Have fun exploring all the different options and finding the perfect look for your character!
