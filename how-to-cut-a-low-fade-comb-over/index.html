How To Cut A Low Fade Comb Over A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, a low fade comb over might just be the perfect choice for you. This type of haircut features short sides that gradually fade into longer hair on top, styled into a sleek comb over. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to cut a low fade comb over, along with tips and tricks to make it look its best.

How to Cut a Low Fade Comb Over

Cutting a low fade comb over requires some skill and precision, so it's important to take your time and follow these steps carefully. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair gel or pomade

Step 1: Determine the Length

Before you start cutting, you'll need to decide how long you want the hair on top to be. Use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length, keeping in mind that you'll be styling it into a comb over later.

Step 2: Start the Fade

Using the clippers, start cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head. Begin with a longer guard, such as a #4 or #5, and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard, such as a #2 or #3. Make sure to blend the hair evenly so that there are no harsh lines.

Step 3: Blend the Sides

Next, use the scissors to blend the sides and back into the longer hair on top. This will create a smooth transition and prevent any noticeable lines.

Step 4: Create the Part

Use the comb to create a part on the side of the head, where the hair on top will be combed over.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to the hair on top, and use the comb to style it into a sleek comb over.

Tips for a Perfect Low Fade Comb Over

While cutting a low fade comb over can be challenging, there are a few tips and tricks that can help make it look its best. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair requires precision and attention to detail.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.
  • Keep your tools clean and well-maintained for the best results.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Can I cut a low fade comb over at home?

Yes, you can cut a low fade comb over at home. However, it's important to have the right tools and take your time to ensure a precise cut.

How often should I get a low fade comb over?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

What hair products should I use for a low fade comb over?

Hair gel or pomade works well for a low fade comb over, as it provides hold and a sleek, shiny finish. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

Hair Care for a Low Fade Comb Over

To keep your low fade comb over looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and style of your low fade comb over.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut a perfect low fade comb over and keep it looking great. Remember to take your time, be patient, and experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
