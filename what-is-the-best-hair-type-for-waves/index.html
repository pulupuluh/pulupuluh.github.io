What Is The Best Hair Type For Waves

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Wavy hair is a popular hairstyle for both men and women. Wavy hair can add volume and texture to any hair type. However, not all hair types are suitable for creating waves. In this article, we will discuss what is the best hair type for waves and how to achieve this look.

Description

Wavy hair is a hairstyle that has a slight curve or bend in the hair. This style is different from curly hair, which has a tight, spiraled pattern. Wavy hair can be achieved with the use of styling products or by using heat tools like a curling iron. However, the success of creating waves depends on the hair type.

How to Achieve Waves

If you have the best hair type for waves, achieving this hairstyle is easy. Here are the steps to create waves in your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will add texture and volume to your hair, making it easier to create waves.
  2. Apply a styling product like a mousse or a sea salt spray to your damp hair. This will give your hair the hold it needs to maintain the wave pattern.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your natural waves and add volume to your hair.
  4. If you want to create more defined waves, use a curling iron with a large barrel. Wrap sections of your hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Finish your look with a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

What is the Best Hair Type for Waves?

The best hair type for waves is thick, coarse hair. This hair type has the volume and texture needed to hold the wave pattern. Fine, thin hair may not have the texture or volume needed to create waves.

However, it is possible to create waves in fine, thin hair with the use of styling products and heat tools. Using a volumizing mousse and a curling iron with a smaller barrel can help to create waves in fine hair.

Tips for Creating Waves

Here are some tips to help you create waves in your hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools like a curling iron.
  • Start with clean, dry hair to ensure the wave pattern holds.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Can I create waves in short hair?

Yes, you can create waves in short hair using a curling iron with a smaller barrel or by using a texturizing spray.

How long will the waves last?

The longevity of the waves depends on your hair type and the products you use. Using a hairspray can help the waves last longer.

Can I create waves in naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can create waves in naturally curly hair using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer or by using a curling iron.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair can be prone to frizz and dryness. Here are some hair care tips for wavy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to combat dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb on wet hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too frequently, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Now that you know what is the best hair type for waves, you can achieve this popular hairstyle with ease. Whether you have thick, coarse hair or fine, thin hair, there are ways to create waves that will suit your hair type. Follow the tips and tricks in this article to create beautiful, wavy hair.
