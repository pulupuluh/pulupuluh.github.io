Best Clothing Brands For Over 60 A Guide To Looking Stylish And Comfortable

Written by Robby Jul 05, 2023 · 8 min read

As we age, our fashion choices tend to shift towards more comfortable and practical clothing. However, that doesn't mean we can't still look stylish and put-together! In this guide, we'll explore the best clothing brands for over 60, with a focus on comfort, quality, and style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose Clothing for Over 60
  • Tips for Shopping for Clothing as an Over 60
  • Best Clothing Brands for Over 60

How to Choose Clothing for Over 60

When it comes to choosing clothing as an over 60, comfort and practicality should be top priorities. However, that doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style! Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose fabrics that are soft, breathable, and easy to care for, such as cotton, linen, and jersey.
  • Opt for looser fits that allow for ease of movement and won't cling to the body.
  • Look for pieces that can be dressed up or down, such as a versatile cardigan or a pair of comfortable but stylish pants.
  • Avoid clothing with complicated closures or details that can be difficult to manage, such as buttons or zippers.

Tips for Shopping for Clothing as an Over 60

Shopping for clothing can be daunting, but it doesn't have to be! Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Find brands that cater specifically to the over 60 demographic, as they will likely have more options that are tailored to your needs.
  • Shop online to avoid the hassle of crowded stores and long lines.
  • Read reviews from other customers to get an idea of the fit and quality of the clothing.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and experiment with your look!

Best Clothing Brands for Over 60

Now that we've covered some tips for choosing and shopping for clothing, let's take a look at some of the best clothing brands for over 60:

1. Eileen Fisher

Eileen Fisher is a brand that focuses on sustainable and ethical fashion, with a wide range of comfortable and stylish pieces that are perfect for over 60s. They offer a variety of sizes and cuts, and their clothing is made from high-quality, eco-friendly materials.

2. Chico's

Chico's is a brand that caters specifically to women over 60, with a focus on comfort, versatility, and style. They offer a range of pieces that can be mixed and matched to create different looks, and their clothing is designed to flatter a variety of body types.

3. JJill

JJill is a brand that offers comfortable and easy-to-wear clothing for women of all ages, with a focus on natural fibers and relaxed fits. They offer a range of sizes and styles, and their clothing is perfect for everyday wear.

4. Soft Surroundings

Soft Surroundings is a brand that offers comfortable and stylish clothing, as well as home decor and beauty products. Their clothing is designed to be versatile and easy to wear, with a focus on soft fabrics and relaxed fits.

5. Lands' End

Lands' End is a brand that offers a wide range of clothing for all ages, with a focus on quality and comfort. They offer a variety of sizes and styles, and their clothing is designed to be both practical and stylish.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about clothing for over 60s:

What should I look for in clothing as an over 60?

You should look for clothing that is comfortable, practical, and easy to care for. Opt for fabrics that are soft and breathable, and choose looser fits that allow for ease of movement.

What are some good clothing brands for over 60s?

Some good clothing brands for over 60s include Eileen Fisher, Chico's, JJill, Soft Surroundings, and Lands' End.

Where can I shop for clothing as an over 60?

You can shop for clothing as an over 60 both online and in stores. Look for brands that cater specifically to your demographic, and read reviews from other customers to get an idea of the fit and quality of the clothing.

Hair Care Tips for Over 60s

In addition to choosing comfortable and stylish clothing, it's important to take care of your hair as an over 60. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

Conclusion

Choosing clothing as an over 60 doesn't have to be difficult or stressful. By focusing on comfort, quality, and style, and shopping from brands that cater specifically to your demographic, you can look and feel your best at any age. Don't be afraid to experiment with new styles and embrace your personal sense of fashion!
