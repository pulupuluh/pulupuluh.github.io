Best Haircut For Round Face Over 60 Tips Styles And Hair Care

Written by William Aug 22, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're over 60 and have a round face, it's always a good idea to choose a haircut that flatters your features. A good haircut can make you look younger, feel more confident, and highlight your best features. But with so many haircuts to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the right one. In this article, we'll share some tips, styles, and hair care tips to help you find the best haircut for round face over 60.

Table of Contents

  2. Best Haircut Styles for Round Face Over 60
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your Best Haircut
  4. Tips to Style and Maintain Your Haircut
  5. FAQs About Haircuts for Round Face Over 60
  6. Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Round Face Over 60

When it comes to choosing a haircut, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and rounded chin. You want to choose a haircut that will elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Another thing to consider is your hair texture. As we age, our hair texture often changes, becoming thinner, drier, or coarser. You want to choose a haircut that works with your hair texture and doesn't require a lot of styling.

Finally, consider your lifestyle. If you're low-maintenance and don't want to spend a lot of time styling your hair, choose a haircut that is easy to maintain and doesn't require a lot of upkeep.

Best Haircut Styles for Round Face Over 60

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a haircut, let's take a look at some of the best styles for round face over 60.

Layered Bob

The layered bob is a classic haircut that works well for round faces. It's a low-maintenance style that is easy to maintain and doesn't require a lot of styling. The layers help to add volume and movement to your hair, giving it a more youthful look. If you have thin hair, ask your stylist to add some layers to create the illusion of thickness.

Long Layers

If you have long hair, consider adding some long layers to create movement and volume. The layers should start at your chin and go all the way down to the ends of your hair. This style is perfect for those who want to keep their hair long but still want to add some dimension to their hair.

Shoulder-Length Waves

If you want to add some texture and volume to your hair, consider shoulder-length waves. This style works well for those with thin hair, as the waves add volume and texture. To create waves, use a curling iron or a curling wand and wrap small sections of your hair around the barrel. Finish with a volumizing hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your Best Haircut

Once you've chosen the right haircut for your face shape and hair type, it's time to achieve the look. Follow these steps to achieve your best haircut:

  1. Find a reputable stylist who has experience working with your hair type and face shape.
  2. Bring a photo of the haircut you want to show your stylist.
  3. Communicate with your stylist about your hair goals and any concerns you may have.
  4. Trust your stylist's expertise and let them work their magic.
  5. Ask your stylist for tips on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

Tips to Style and Maintain Your Haircut

Now that you have your new haircut, here are some tips to help you style and maintain your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to protect your hair from damage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Haircuts for Round Face Over 60

What is the best haircut for a round face over 60?

The best haircut for a round face over 60 is one that elongates your face and draws attention away from your cheeks. Consider a layered bob, long layers, or shoulder-length waves.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair upside down, or using hot rollers or a curling iron to create volume.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

In addition to choosing the right haircut and styling your hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Congratulations! You now have all the tips and tricks you need to find the best haircut for round face over 60. Remember to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair type, and to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
