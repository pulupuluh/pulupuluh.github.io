Medium Length Layered Hairstyles For Thick Hair Over 50

Written by Luffy Mar 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and practical hairstyle that suits your thick hair? Then, you are in the right place. In this article, we will discuss medium length layered hairstyles for thick hair over 50. These hairstyles are not only easy to maintain but also help to add volume and texture to your hair. Let's dive in!

Description

Medium length layered hairstyles for thick hair over 50 are versatile and trendy. They are perfect for women who want to add some movement and dimension to their hair without sacrificing length. The layers help to create a flattering shape around the face and add volume to the hair. These hairstyles are suitable for any occasion, whether it's for a casual day out or a formal event.

How To

The following are the steps to achieve medium length layered hairstyles for thick hair over 50:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Use a towel to dry your hair until it's damp.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Use a round brush and a hairdryer to blow-dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Make sure to lift the hair at the roots to add volume.

Step 4: Curl your hair

Use a curling iron to curl your hair. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until all your hair is curled.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish by spraying hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

The following are some tips to help you maintain your medium length layered hairstyles for thick hair over 50:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy

FAQ

Q. Are medium length layered hairstyles suitable for thick hair over 50?

A. Yes, medium length layered hairstyles are perfect for thick hair over 50. They help to add volume and texture to the hair.

Q. How often should I trim my hair?

A. It's recommended to trim your hair every 6 to 8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. The following are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals

Now that you have learned about medium length layered hairstyles for thick hair over 50, it's time to try them out. Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
