How To Wear A Lace Front Wig Everyday Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Mar 18, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Wearing a lace front wig can be a great way to switch up your look or cover up hair loss. However, if it's your first time wearing one, you may be wondering how to wear a lace front wig everyday without it looking fake or feeling uncomfortable. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to wearing a lace front wig and provide tips and tricks to help you achieve a natural look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Lace Front Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Wear a Lace Front Wig

A lace front wig is a type of wig that has a thin, sheer lace panel at the front of the wig that extends from ear to ear. The hair strands are hand-sewn onto the lace panel, giving the wig a more natural-looking hairline. Here's a quick overview of how to wear a lace front wig:

  1. Prepare your hair by braiding it or flattening it against your head with a wig cap.
  2. Put on the wig cap and adjust it so that it fits snugly on your head.
  3. Put the wig on by holding it by the ear tabs and sliding it on from the front of your head.
  4. Adjust the wig so that it sits comfortably on your head and the ear tabs are aligned with your natural ear position.
  5. Trim any excess lace at the front of the wig, being careful not to cut any of the hair strands.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now let's dive into each step in more detail:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before putting on the wig, you'll want to prepare your hair so that it lies flat against your head. Depending on the length of your hair, you can either braid it or use a wig cap to flatten it. Here's how to prepare your hair:

  • If your hair is long, braid it into several small braids.
  • If your hair is short, use a wig cap to flatten it against your head.
  • Make sure your hair is completely flat against your head with no bumps or lumps.

Step 2: Put on the wig cap

A wig cap is a stretchy cap that fits over your head to keep your hair in place and provide a smooth surface for the wig to rest on. Here's how to put on a wig cap:

  1. Place the wig cap on your head.
  2. Adjust the cap so that it fits snugly on your head, but not too tight.
  3. Tuck any loose hairs under the wig cap.

Step 3: Put on the wig

Now it's time to put on the lace front wig. Here's how to do it:

  1. Hold the wig by the ear tabs and place it at the front of your head.
  2. Slide the wig on from the front of your head to the back.
  3. Adjust the wig so that it sits comfortably on your head and the ear tabs are aligned with your natural ear position.
  4. Use the adjustable straps at the back of the wig to tighten or loosen the fit as needed.

Step 4: Trim the lace

Most lace front wigs come with excess lace at the front that needs to be trimmed. Here's how to do it:

  1. Cut off any excess lace at the front of the wig, being careful not to cut any of the hair strands.
  2. Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut along the natural hairline, following the curve of your forehead.
  3. Be sure to leave a small amount of lace at the front to blend with your skin.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you wear a lace front wig everyday:

  • Choose a wig that matches your natural hair color and texture for a more natural look.
  • Use wig glue or tape to secure the wig to your head for a more secure fit.
  • Style the wig with heat tools or hair products to achieve your desired look.
  • Wash and condition the wig regularly to keep it looking fresh.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.

FAQ

How long can I wear a lace front wig?

You can wear a lace front wig for up to 6 weeks at a time, but it's recommended to take it off and give your scalp a break every few days.

Can I sleep in a lace front wig?

No, it's not recommended to sleep in a lace front wig as it can cause tangling and matting of the hair.

Can I swim or shower with a lace front wig?

No, it's not recommended to swim or shower with a lace front wig as it can damage the wig and cause tangling and matting of the hair.

Hair Care

To keep your lace front wig looking its best, it's important to wash and condition it regularly. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gently brush the wig to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Fill a sink or basin with lukewarm water and add a small amount of wig shampoo.
  3. Place the wig in the water and gently swish it around for a few minutes.
  4. Rinse the wig thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  5. Apply a small amount of wig conditioner and leave it on for a few minutes.
  6. Rinse the wig thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  7. Gently pat the wig dry with a towel and let it air dry on a wig stand or mannequin head.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear a lace front wig everyday with confidence and style. Remember to take care of your wig and give your scalp a break every few days to keep your hair healthy and happy.
