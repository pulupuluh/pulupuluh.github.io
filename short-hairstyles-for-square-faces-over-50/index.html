Short Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 50

Written by Daniel Aug 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a square face and are over 50, you may be wondering what short hairstyles would look best on you. Square faces typically have a strong jawline and wide forehead, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements these features. In this article, we'll provide tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice for short hairstyles for square faces over 50.

Description

Short hairstyles are a great way to change up your look and show off your features. For those with square faces over 50, it's important to choose a hairstyle that accentuates your jawline and forehead. The right cut can also help to soften your features and create a more youthful appearance.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

When choosing a short hairstyle for a square face over 50, it's important to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

If you have thick, coarse hair, a short layered cut can help to add volume and movement. For those with fine hair, a short bob or pixie cut can help to create the illusion of thickness.

2. Think About Your Lifestyle

If you're an active person who likes to exercise or spend time outdoors, a low-maintenance cut may be best. On the other hand, if you enjoy styling your hair and trying new looks, a more layered or textured cut may be a better fit.

3. Consider Your Personal Style

Your hairstyle should reflect your personal style and make you feel confident and comfortable. If you prefer a classic look, a sleek bob or pixie cut may be best. For those who like to experiment with bold colors or edgy cuts, a textured or asymmetrical style may be a better fit.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Short Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some popular short hairstyles for square faces over 50:

1. Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic hairstyle that can work well on a square face. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Starting at the nape of your neck, cut your hair to the desired length. Work your way up to the crown of your head, cutting each section to the same length.
  4. For a more textured look, use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk from the ends.
  5. Style your hair with a round brush and blow dryer, adding volume at the crown of your head.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, low-maintenance hairstyle that can be a great choice for those with square faces over 50. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using scissors, cut your hair to the desired length. Keep in mind that a pixie cut is typically shorter on the sides and longer on top.
  3. For a more textured look, use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk from the ends.
  4. Style your hair with a styling cream or wax, using your fingers to create texture and volume.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Short hair tends to get greasy faster than longer hair, so it's important to wash it regularly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Trim Your Hair Every 4-6 Weeks

To keep your short hairstyle looking fresh and polished, it's important to get regular trims. This will help to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you use heat styling tools like a blow dryer or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

1. What short hairstyles are best for square faces over 50?

Short hairstyles that are layered or textured can help to soften a square face and create a more youthful appearance. Some popular styles include the bob cut, pixie cut, and layered shag.

2. Can I still color my hair with a short hairstyle?

Absolutely! Short hair can be a great canvas for experimenting with different hair colors. Just be sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Products

Choose hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. This will help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Get Regular Trims

To keep your short hairstyle looking fresh and polished, it's important to get regular trims. This will help to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length.

In conclusion, short hairstyles can be a great way to change up your look and show off your features. If you have a square face and are over 50, consider a bob cut, pixie cut, or layered shag to complement your features and create a more youthful appearance. Use the right products, protect your hair from the sun, and get regular trims to keep your short hairstyle looking its best.
