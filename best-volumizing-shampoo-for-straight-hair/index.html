Best Volumizing Shampoo For Straight Hair Get Fuller Bouncier Locks

Written by Frank May 28, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have straight hair that's prone to falling flat, you know how frustrating it can be to achieve volume and body. While there are many styling products that can help, starting with the right shampoo can make all the difference. In this article, we'll share our top picks for the best volumizing shampoo for straight hair, along with tips for making the most of your hair type.

What is Volumizing Shampoo?

Volumizing shampoo is formulated to add fullness and body to hair, making it look thicker and more voluminous. These shampoos typically contain ingredients that help to lift the hair cuticle, such as protein or polymers, and may also include ingredients that help to absorb oils and other substances that can weigh hair down.

How to Choose the Best Volumizing Shampoo for Straight Hair

When choosing a volumizing shampoo for straight hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, look for a shampoo that is specifically formulated for your hair type. This will ensure that you get the best results without damaging your hair.

It's also important to consider the ingredients in the shampoo. Look for ingredients like keratin, collagen, or biotin, which can help to strengthen hair and add volume. Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, which can strip hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to breakage.

Finally, consider your hair's specific needs. If your hair is dry or damaged, look for a shampoo that is moisturizing and nourishing. If you have oily hair, look for a shampoo that is lightweight and won't weigh your hair down.

Our Top Picks for the Best Volumizing Shampoo for Straight Hair

1. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Shampoo

Bumble and Bumble Thickening Shampoo is a cult favorite for good reason. This shampoo contains a blend of ingredients, including wheat protein and panthenol, that work to add volume and thickness to hair. It also helps to smooth frizz and flyaways, leaving hair looking healthy and shiny.

2. OGX Biotin & Collagen Shampoo

OGX Biotin & Collagen Shampoo is a budget-friendly option that still delivers great results. This shampoo is formulated with biotin and collagen, which help to strengthen hair and add volume. It also contains hydrolyzed wheat protein, which helps to smooth and soften hair.

3. Living Proof Full Shampoo

Living Proof Full Shampoo is another great option for adding volume to straight hair. This shampoo contains a patented molecule called PBAE, which helps to create fullness and body. It also contains a blend of sulfate-free cleansers that gently cleanse hair without stripping it of its natural oils.

How to Use Volumizing Shampoo for Straight Hair

To get the best results from your volumizing shampoo, it's important to use it correctly. Start by wetting your hair thoroughly, then apply a small amount of shampoo to your scalp. Massage the shampoo into your scalp using your fingertips, then work it through the rest of your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly, then follow up with a conditioner if desired.

Tips for Maximizing Volume on Straight Hair

In addition to using the right shampoo, there are a few other things you can do to maximize volume on straight hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create lift at the roots
  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse before blow drying
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh hair down
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add texture and movement
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair after washing

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between volumizing shampoo and regular shampoo?

Volumizing shampoo is formulated to add body and fullness to hair, while regular shampoo is simply designed to cleanse hair. Volumizing shampoo typically contains ingredients that help to lift the hair cuticle, making hair appear thicker and more voluminous.

Can volumizing shampoo damage my hair?

While volumizing shampoo is designed to add body and fullness to hair, it should not damage your hair when used correctly. However, it's important to choose a shampoo that is formulated for your hair type, as using the wrong type of shampoo can lead to dryness, breakage, and other damage.

Conclusion

Adding volume to straight hair can be a challenge, but with the right shampoo and styling techniques, you can achieve fuller, bouncier locks. Our top picks for the best volumizing shampoo for straight hair are Bumble and Bumble Thickening Shampoo, OGX Biotin & Collagen Shampoo, and Living Proof Full Shampoo. Remember to choose a shampoo that is specifically formulated for your hair type, and consider incorporating some of our tips for maximizing volume.
