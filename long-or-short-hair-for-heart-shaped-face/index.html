Long Or Short Hair For Heart Shaped Face

Written by Luffy Jun 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you might be wondering whether long or short hair would suit you better. In this article, we'll explore the pros and cons of both styles, as well as offer some tips and tricks for maintaining and styling your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a pointed chin. This face shape is often considered the most feminine, and can be quite versatile when it comes to hair styles.

How to

When deciding between long or short hair for a heart shaped face, there are a few things to consider. First and foremost, the length of your hair should be in proportion to your face. If you have a small face, shorter hair may be more flattering, while longer hair can balance out a larger face.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine or thin hair, shorter styles may be better as they can help create the illusion of volume. On the other hand, if you have thick or curly hair, longer styles can help weigh down your hair and prevent it from looking too puffy.

Lastly, consider your personal style and lifestyle. Shorter hair can be low-maintenance and easy to style, while longer hair may require more upkeep and styling time.

Step by Step

If you've decided to go for a shorter style, a pixie cut or bob can be a great option for a heart shaped face. For a longer style, consider layers or waves to add dimension and soften the angles of your face.

When styling shorter hair, use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and definition. For longer hair, try using a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or sleek, straight hair.

Tips

When styling your hair, consider parting it to the side rather than down the middle. This can help balance out the width of your forehead and cheekbones.

Another tip is to avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back, as they can emphasize the point of your chin and make your face look more angular.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a heart shaped face?

A: Absolutely! Soft, side-swept bangs can be a great way to balance out a wider forehead.

Q: I have curly hair - should I avoid short styles?

A: Not necessarily. A short, curly bob can be very flattering for a heart shaped face.

Hair Care

No matter what length or style you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too many styling products that can weigh down your hair or make it look greasy.

Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Whether you opt for long or short hair, there are plenty of options that can suit a heart shaped face. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when making your decision, and don't be afraid to try something new!
