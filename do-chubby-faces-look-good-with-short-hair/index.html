Do Chubby Faces Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Petter Jul 24, 2023 · 6 min read

Short hair has always been popular among women. It is easy to maintain, looks chic, and can be styled in several different ways. However, the question remains – do chubby faces look good with short hair? This article will discuss whether short hair is a good option for women with rounder faces or not.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Chubby faces have a certain charm to them, but they can also make it difficult to find the right hairstyle. Short hair can be a great option for women with rounder faces, as it can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. However, not all short hairstyles are created equal. Some styles can actually make your face look even more round or chubby.

How to

When it comes to choosing the right short hairstyle for a chubby face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider the length of the hairstyle
  • Think about the volume of the hairstyle
  • Look for hairstyles that frame the face
  • Consider adding layers to your hair

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a short hairstyle for a chubby face:

  1. Start by determining the shape of your face. Chubby faces can be round, oval, or heart-shaped. Knowing your face shape can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.
  2. Consider the length of the hairstyle. Short hairstyles are generally considered to be anything above the shoulders. However, you don't want your hair to be too short or it can make your face look even rounder.
  3. Think about the volume of the hairstyle. You want a hairstyle that has some volume, but not too much. Too much volume can make your face look wider.
  4. Look for hairstyles that frame the face. Side-swept bangs, layers, and angled cuts can all help to frame the face and create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  5. Consider adding layers to your hair. Layers can help to add texture and movement to your hair, which can help to create a more slimming effect.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a short hairstyle for a chubby face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or straight
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension
  • Try a side part instead of a center part
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for chubby faces:

Q: Can short hair make my face look even rounder?

A: Yes, if the hairstyle is too short or too blunt, it can actually make your face look rounder. It's important to choose a hairstyle with some volume and texture to help create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Q: What are some good short hairstyles for chubby faces?

A: Some good short hairstyles for chubby faces include bob cuts, layered cuts, angled cuts, and side-swept bangs.

Hair Care

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

In conclusion, short hair can look great on women with rounder faces, but it's important to choose the right style to flatter your features. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can find a short hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
