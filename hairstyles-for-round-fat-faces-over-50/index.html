Hairstyles For Round Fat Faces Over 50 Tips And Ideas

Written by Oliver Aug 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our face shape and features change. Many women over 50 may have a round and fuller face, which can make finding the perfect hairstyle a bit challenging. However, there are plenty of options available to flatter and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for round fat faces over 50, along with tips and tricks to help you achieve your desired look.

How to Determine if You Have a Round Face?

Before we dive into hairstyles, it's crucial to determine if you have a round face shape. Here are some simple steps to help you identify your face shape:

  • Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  • If all three measurements are similar, then you have a round face shape.
  • Your face may also appear fuller, and your chin may be less pronounced.

Hairstyles for Round Fat Faces Over 50

Now that you've identified your face shape let's explore some hairstyles that can flatter round fat faces over 50:

1. Short Bob

A short bob can be an excellent choice for those with a round face shape. This hairstyle can create the illusion of a longer face and draw attention to your cheekbones.

Tips: Consider adding side-swept bangs or layers to add dimension and frame your face.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a chic and trendy hairstyle that can make you look youthful and stylish. This look can highlight your cheekbones and draw attention away from your chin.

Tips: Consider adding texture or layers to add volume and dimension to your hair.

3. Layered Shoulder-Length Cut

A layered shoulder-length cut is a versatile hairstyle that can flatter round faces of any age. This style can create the illusion of a slimmer face and add texture to your hair.

Tips: Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

FAQ

1. Can I wear bangs with a round face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a round face. However, consider adding side-swept bangs or layered bangs to add dimension and frame your face. Avoid blunt straight bangs that can make your face appear rounder.

2. Should I avoid long hair with a round face?

No, you don't have to avoid long hair with a round face. However, consider adding layers or texture to your hair to elongate your face and create dimension.

Hair Care Tips for Round Fat Faces Over 50

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently that can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  • Consider using hair masks or oils to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.

Conclusion

Hairstyles for round fat faces over 50 can be challenging, but there are plenty of options available to flatter and enhance your natural beauty. From short bobs to layered shoulder-length cuts, these styles can create the illusion of a slimmer face and highlight your best features. Remember to take good care of your hair and choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
