Best Hair Style For Round Full Face A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the best hair style for your round full face? Don't worry, you are not alone. Many people with round full faces face the same issue. But the good news is, there are plenty of hair styles that can flatter your face shape and enhance your features. In this article, we will guide you through the best hair styles for round full faces, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round full face typically has a wider forehead, fuller cheeks, and a round chin. The best hair styles for this face shape are those that create the illusion of length and height, while also adding volume and texture to the hair.

Opt for hair styles that are layered, textured, and have length around the face. Hairstyles that are too short or too long can make the face appear rounder. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs that can make the face look fuller.

How to

The first step in achieving the best hair style for your round full face is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to recommend the best hair styles based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Once you have found the right hair style, you can achieve it at home with the right tools and products. Use a round brush to add volume and texture to the hair, and a hair dryer to set the style. Finish with a texturizing spray or serum to add definition and hold.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the best hair style for your round full face:

  1. Consult with a professional hairstylist to find the right hair style for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Wash and condition your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  3. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  4. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and definition to your hair.
  6. Finish with a texturizing spray or serum to add hold and definition.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining the best hair style for your round full face:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of your hair.
  • Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Add highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Accessorize your hair with hair clips or headbands to add interest and style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair style for round full face:

What is the best hair length for a round full face?

The best hair length for a round full face is typically shoulder length or longer. This length creates the illusion of length and height, while also adding volume and texture to the hair.

What hair styles should I avoid if I have a round full face?

Avoid hair styles that are too short or too long, as they can make the face appear rounder. Also avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs that can make the face look fuller.

How can I add volume to my hair?

Use a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair while blow drying. You can also use a texturizing spray or serum to add definition and hold.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving the best hair style for your round full face. Use products that are suitable for your hair type and avoid over-styling and heat damage. Get regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of your hair, and use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

Now that you know the best hair style for round full face, go ahead and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. With the right hair style and care, you can enhance your features and feel confident and beautiful.
