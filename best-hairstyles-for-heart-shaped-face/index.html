Best Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face

Written by Bardi Jul 11, 2023 Â· 2 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best hairstyles for this face shape. Whether you have short, medium, or long hair, there is a style that will flatter your features. Read on to find out more.

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the forehead and chin while accentuating the cheekbones. This can be achieved by creating volume at the crown of the head, adding layers to the sides, and avoiding styles that are too heavy at the bottom.

How to

When deciding on a hairstyle for your heart shaped face, it is important to consider the length and texture of your hair. Short haircuts like a pixie cut or a bob can work well for this face shape, as they draw attention to the cheekbones and create a more balanced look. Medium length hair can be styled with layers or waves to add volume to the sides. Long hair can also work for a heart shaped face, as long as it is styled with layers and volume at the crown of the head.

Short Haircuts

For short haircuts, consider a pixie cut with side-swept bangs or a bob with layers. These styles will draw attention to the cheekbones and create a more balanced look. Avoid styles that are too heavy at the bottom, as they can make the chin appear narrower.

Medium Length Hair

If you have medium length hair, consider adding layers or waves to create volume at the sides of your face. This will balance out the forehead and chin while accentuating your cheekbones. Avoid styles that are too flat at the crown of the head, as they can make the forehead appear wider.

Long Hair

For long hair, consider adding layers and volume at the crown of the head. This will create balance while still allowing you to enjoy the length of your hair. Avoid styles that are too heavy at the bottom, as they can make the chin appear narrower.

Step by Step

Here are some step by step instructions for styling your hair based on your face shape:

Short Haircuts

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to add texture and hold.
  3. If you have a pixie cut, use a flat iron to create side-swept bangs.
  4. If you have a bob, use a curling iron to add layers and volume.

Medium Length Hair

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to add volume to the sides.
  3. Use a curling iron to create loose waves.

Long Hair

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing spray to add volume at the crown of the head.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair based on your face shape:

  • Consider adding bangs to balance out a wider forehead.
  • Avoid styles that are too heavy at the bottom, as they can make the chin appear narrower.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a heart shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too heavy at the bottom, as they can make the chin appear narrower. Also avoid styles that are too flat at the crown of the head, as they can make the forehead appear wider.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a heart shaped face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part if you have a heart shaped face. However, it is recommended that you add some volume at the crown of the head to balance out the forehead and chin.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it is important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

With these tips and the best hairstyles for a heart shaped face, you can feel confident and beautiful every day.
