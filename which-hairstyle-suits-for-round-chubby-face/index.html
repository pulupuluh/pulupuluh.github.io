Which Hairstyle Suits For Round Chubby Face

Written by Frank May 28, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have a round and chubby face, choosing the right hairstyle can be challenging. The hairstyle you choose can either accentuate your features or make your face look even rounder. However, with the right hairstyle and cut, you can create an illusion of a slimmer face. In this article, we will discuss different hairstyles that suit round chubby faces.

Description

Round faces have a circular shape with a rounded chin and cheeks. Chubby faces have a fuller look with less defined features. The combination of these two features can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that flatters your face. However, there are many hairstyles that can help you enhance your features and make your face look slimmer.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

When choosing a hairstyle for a round chubby face, you should consider the following:

  • The length of your hair
  • The shape of your face
  • The texture of your hair
  • Your personal style

Step by Step: Hairstyles for Round Chubby Face

1. Long Layers

Long layers are ideal for round chubby faces as they create an illusion of length and slimness. Layers also add volume to your hair, which balances the roundness of your face. You can wear it straight or wavy, depending on your preference.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that suits almost all face shapes. However, for a round chubby face, you should opt for a longer bob that ends just below your chin. This style will create a slimming effect and accentuate your jawline.

3. Side Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for round chubby faces as they create an angled look that slims down your face. Bangs also draw attention to your eyes, making them the focal point of your face.

4. Pixie Cut

While a pixie cut may seem intimidating, it's an excellent hairstyle for round chubby faces. This haircut adds height to your hair, creating a slimmer look. You can also add texture with layers or bangs to enhance your features.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your round chubby face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Keep your hair length above your shoulders to avoid adding width to your face.
  • Choose hairstyles with layers to create a slimming effect.
  • Experiment with different styles and cuts to find what suits you best.

FAQ

1. Are bangs suitable for round chubby faces?

Yes, side-swept bangs are ideal for round chubby faces as they create an angled look that slims down your face.

2. Are short haircuts suitable for round chubby faces?

Yes, short haircuts like a pixie cut or a bob that ends just below your chin are ideal for round chubby faces as they create a slimming effect.

3. Should I avoid adding volume to my hair?

Yes, you should avoid adding volume to the sides of your face as it can make your face look even rounder.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it's essential to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after every wash to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

Choosing the right hairstyle for a round chubby face can be challenging, but with the right cut and style, you can enhance your features and create a slimmer look. Follow our tips and experiment with different hairstyles to find what suits you best.
