How To Style Your Hair To Look Like A Guy

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle and want to opt for a more masculine look, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be giving you step-by-step instructions on how to style your hair to look like a guy. From buzz cuts to textured crops, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

How to:

1. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is the ultimate low-maintenance hairstyle for guys. It's simple, easy to style, and requires little maintenance. To achieve this look, all you need is a pair of clippers and a steady hand. Start by setting your clippers to your desired length, and then work your way around your head, cutting your hair evenly. Once you're done, use a comb to get rid of any stray hairs.

2. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a popular hairstyle among guys. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in many different ways. To achieve this look, start by asking your barber for a short crop with textured layers. Then, use a styling product like pomade or wax to add texture and definition to your hair. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair.

3. Classic Side Part

The classic side part is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, start by parting your hair on one side. Then, use a comb to slick back the hair on the other side. You can also use a styling product like gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

4. Slicked Back

The slicked back hairstyle is a classic look that's perfect for any occasion. To achieve this look, start by applying a generous amount of pomade or gel to your hair. Then, use a comb to slick back your hair, making sure to smooth out any bumps or flyaways. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair.

Step by Step:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

The first step in styling your hair like a guy is to determine your hair type. Is it thick, thin, curly, or straight? This will help you choose the right haircut and styling product for your hair.

Step 2: Choose Your Haircut

Once you've determined your hair type, it's time to choose your haircut. Do you want a buzz cut, a textured crop, or something else? Make sure to choose a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 3: Wash Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure to wash it with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup from your hair.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

Once your hair is clean and dry, it's time to apply styling product. Depending on your hair type and the style you're going for, you may want to use gel, pomade, wax, or another styling product. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Use a comb, brush, or your fingers to style your hair into your desired look. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or flyaways, and use a blow dryer to add volume if needed.

Tips:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Invest in a good-quality styling product to achieve the best results.
  • Consider visiting a barber or hairstylist for a professional haircut.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for advice from your friends or family.

FAQ:

Q: Can I style my hair like a guy if I have long hair?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair like a guy if you have long hair. You may want to consider getting a haircut to achieve a shorter style, or experiment with different ways to tie back your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's generally recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage.

Hair Care:

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and strong. Make sure to use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Additionally, consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know how to style your hair to look like a guy, it's time to experiment with different looks and find the one that suits you best. Remember to have fun and be creative!
