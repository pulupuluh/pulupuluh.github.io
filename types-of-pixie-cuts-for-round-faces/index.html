Types Of Pixie Cuts For Round Faces

Written by Robby Apr 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with long hair that just won't cooperate? Pixie cuts may be the perfect solution for you! But if you have a round face, it can be challenging to find the right style that flatters your features. Don't worry, we have compiled a list of the best types of pixie cuts for round faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Pixie cuts are a chic and trendy hairstyle that can transform your look instantly. This short haircut is perfect for those who want to showcase their face and neck. However, not all pixie cuts are suitable for round faces. The goal is to choose a style that elongates your face and enhances your features.

How to Choose the Right Pixie Cut

When selecting a pixie cut, you need to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. For round faces, it's best to opt for styles that have height on top and length on the sides. Here are some types of pixie cuts that work well for round faces:

Asymmetrical Pixie Cut

The asymmetrical pixie cut is a modern and edgy style that can add dimension to round faces. The longer side of the cut can create the illusion of a longer face, while the shorter side can highlight your cheekbones. This style is perfect for those who want to experiment with bold hairstyles.

Side-Swept Pixie Cut

The side-swept pixie cut is a classic style that can soften round faces. This cut involves sweeping your hair to one side to create an angled look. This style can also add volume to the crown of your head, making your face appear longer.

Long Pixie Cut

The long pixie cut is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your face shape. This cut involves keeping your hair longer on top and shorter on the sides. The length on top can be styled in various ways, such as adding volume or creating a messy look. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a pixie cut:

  1. Start with dry, detangled hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, left side, right side, and back.
  3. Using a clipper or scissors, cut the back section of your hair to your desired length.
  4. Move to the sides and cut to the same length as the back.
  5. For the top section, use scissors to cut your hair to your desired length and style.
  6. Blend the sides and top using scissors or clippers.
  7. Use a comb and scissors to create layers and texture.
  8. Style your hair using a blow dryer or styling products.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie cut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair in shape.
  • Use styling products, such as mousse or wax, to add volume and texture.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider using a color treatment to enhance your cut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts:

Can I still wear a pixie cut if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Pixie cuts can look great on curly hair. Just make sure to choose a style that works with your natural texture.

Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

No, if you choose the right style, a pixie cut can actually elongate your face and enhance your features.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful pixie cuts:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling wands, too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know the different types of pixie cuts for round faces, it's time to find the perfect style for you. Embrace your new look and enjoy the freedom of short hair!
