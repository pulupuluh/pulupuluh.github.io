How To Wear A Cathedral Veil With Hair Down

Written by Eveline Aug 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you planning to wear a cathedral veil on your wedding day but want to wear your hair down? It is a stunning look that many brides opt for, but it can be tricky to achieve. In this article, we will guide you through the process step by step to ensure that you look absolutely breathtaking on your big day. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Cathedral Veil with Hair Down
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Cathedral Veil with Hair Down
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Choose Your Veil

The first step is to choose the right veil. Cathedral veils come in various lengths, so make sure to choose one that is long enough to reach the floor. You should also consider the style and material of the veil to make sure that it complements your wedding dress.

Step 2: Choose Your Hairstyle

Next, choose your hairstyle. If you want to wear your hair down, make sure to choose a style that complements the veil. Loose waves, curls, or a half-up, half-down style are all great options.

Step 3: Secure the Veil

Once you have chosen your veil and hairstyle, it's time to secure the veil. You can do this by placing the veil on top of your head and securing it with bobby pins near the crown of your head. Make sure that the veil is securely in place.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now that your veil is in place, it's time to style your hair. If you're wearing loose waves or curls, use a curling iron to create your desired look. If you're wearing a half-up, half-down style, use bobby pins to secure the top half of your hair in place.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your look with hairspray to ensure that your hair and veil stay in place throughout the day. Use a lightweight hairspray to avoid weighing down your hair.

Tips for Wearing a Cathedral Veil with Hair Down

  • Consider adding a hair accessory, such as a flower crown or hair comb, to complement your veil.
  • Practice wearing your veil and hair down before your wedding day to ensure that you are comfortable with the look.
  • Choose a veil with a comb attached to make it easier to secure to your hair.
  • Have a backup plan in case your hair or veil doesn't cooperate on your wedding day.

FAQ

Can I wear a cathedral veil with any hairstyle?

While a cathedral veil can be worn with many different hairstyles, it is important to choose a hairstyle that complements the veil. Loose waves, curls, and half-up, half-down styles are all great options.

How do I keep my veil in place with hair down?

To keep your veil in place with hair down, use bobby pins to secure the veil near the crown of your head. Make sure that the veil is securely in place before moving on to styling your hair.

Hair Care Tips

  • Start with clean, dry hair to ensure that your style lasts throughout the day.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a lightweight hairspray to avoid weighing down your hair.

With these tips and step by step guide, you can confidently wear a cathedral veil with your hair down on your wedding day. Remember to practice beforehand and have a backup plan in case anything goes wrong. Congratulations and best of luck on your special day!
