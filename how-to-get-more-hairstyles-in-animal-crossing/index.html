Animal Crossing is a popular video game where players can create their own character and live in a virtual world. One of the things that players can customize is their character's hairstyle. If you're looking to get more hairstyles in Animal Crossing, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you get more hairstyles in the game.

How to

1. Visit Harriet's Salon

Harriet's Salon is a hair salon in the game where players can change their hairstyle. To unlock more hairstyles, you'll need to visit Harriet's Salon multiple times. Each time you visit, you'll unlock more hairstyles. Be sure to visit the salon often to see what new hairstyles are available.

2. Use Amiibo Cards

If you have Amiibo cards, you can use them to unlock more hairstyles in the game. Simply scan the card at the town hall and you'll unlock new hairstyles. If you don't have any Amiibo cards, you can purchase them online or at a video game store.

3. Complete Challenges

Animal Crossing has many challenges for players to complete. Some of these challenges will reward you with new hairstyles. Be sure to check the challenges often to see what rewards are available.

4. Use QR Codes

Players can create and share their own QR codes for custom designs, including hairstyles. You can search for QR codes online or on social media sites like Twitter and Reddit. Once you find a QR code for a hairstyle you like, simply scan it with your in-game phone and the hairstyle will be added to your character's options.

Step by Step

Step 1: Visit Harriet's Salon

Go to Harriet's Salon and talk to her. She will give you a quick tutorial on how to change your hairstyle. You can choose from the hairstyles that are currently available, or you can unlock more hairstyles by visiting the salon multiple times.

Step 2: Use Amiibo Cards

Step 3: Complete Challenges

Complete challenges in the game to unlock new hairstyles. Be sure to check the challenges often to see what rewards are available.

Step 4: Use QR Codes

Search for QR codes online or on social media sites like Twitter and Reddit. Once you find a QR code for a hairstyle you like, simply scan it with your in-game phone and the hairstyle will be added to your character's options.

Tips

- Visit Harriet's Salon often to unlock more hairstyles.
- Check the challenges often to see what rewards are available.
- Use QR codes to find new hairstyles.
- Use Amiibo cards to unlock new hairstyles.
- Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q: How many hairstyles are there in Animal Crossing?

A: There are over 30 hairstyles available in the game, and more can be unlocked by visiting Harriet's Salon multiple times.

Q: Can I create my own hairstyle in Animal Crossing?

A: Unfortunately, you cannot create your own hairstyles in the game. However, you can use QR codes to find custom hairstyles created by other players.

Q: Can I change my character's hairstyle at any time?

A: Yes, you can change your character's hairstyle at any time by visiting Harriet's Salon.

Hair Care

Once you've unlocked a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing, you'll want to take care of it. Here are some tips to keep your virtual hair looking its best:

- If you're going for a specific look, be sure to dress your character accordingly.
- Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your character best.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to unlock and enjoy a wide range of hairstyles in Animal Crossing. Have fun experimenting with different looks and customizing your character's appearance!
