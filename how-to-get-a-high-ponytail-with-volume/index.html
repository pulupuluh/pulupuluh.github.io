How To Get A High Ponytail With Volume

Written by Bardi Jun 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your flat and lifeless ponytail? Do you want to add some volume and height to your hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you on how to get a high ponytail with volume. We will cover the basics, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care to achieve the perfect high ponytail with volume. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Basics
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Basics

Before we dive into the step-by-step instructions, let's cover some basics of getting a high ponytail with volume. First, you will need to have long hair or hair extensions to achieve the desired length. Second, you will need some tools such as a brush, hair tie, hair spray, and hairpins. Third, you can use hair products such as volumizing mousse or dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair. Lastly, you can customize your high ponytail with volume by adding braids, twists, or curls to your hair.

How To

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Then, apply a volumizing mousse or dry shampoo to your roots to add texture and volume to your hair. Next, flip your head upside down and gather all your hair at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure your ponytail in place. Make sure to pull your hair tight to create a sleek and polished look.

Step-by-Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions to get a high ponytail with volume:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or dry shampoo to your roots to add texture and volume to your hair.
  3. Flip your head upside down and gather all your hair at the crown of your head.
  4. Use a hair tie to secure your ponytail in place.
  5. Make sure to pull your hair tight to create a sleek and polished look.
  6. Use hairpins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  7. Spray your ponytail with hairspray to hold the style in place.
  8. Customize your high ponytail with volume by adding braids, twists, or curls to your hair.
  9. Finish off your look with some hair accessories such as a scrunchie or headband.
  10. Voila! You have achieved a high ponytail with volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail with volume:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Tease your hair at the crown of your head to add more volume.
  • Apply hairspray to your hairbrush and then brush your ponytail to distribute the product evenly.
  • Use bobby pins to create the illusion of a fuller ponytail by pinning sections of your hair to the underside of your ponytail.
  • For a more dramatic look, you can use a hairpiece or hair extensions to add length and volume to your ponytail.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a high ponytail with volume:

Can I get a high ponytail with volume if I have short hair?

No, you will need to have long hair or hair extensions to achieve the desired length for a high ponytail with volume.

What hair products should I use to add volume to my hair?

You can use volumizing mousse, dry shampoo, or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

How do I keep my high ponytail with volume in place?

You can use a hair tie, hairpins, and hairspray to hold your high ponytail with volume in place.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair and prevent damage, it is essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly when styling or wearing a ponytail.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to get a high ponytail with volume, you can rock this hairstyle for any occasion. Whether you're going to work, school, or a party, a high ponytail with volume is a versatile and chic hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful locks. Happy styling!
