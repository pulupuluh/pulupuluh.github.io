Does No Facial Hair Make You Look Younger

Written by Robby May 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're reading this, you're probably wondering if shaving your facial hair will make you look younger. The answer is not so simple, but we're here to give you the lowdown on whether or not going clean-shaven will erase a few years from your appearance.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Facial hair has been a popular trend for years now, and many men have embraced it as a way to showcase their masculinity. However, some men prefer the clean-shaven, baby-faced look. But does this look actually make them look younger?

The truth is, it depends on your face shape and the amount of facial hair you had before. For some men, shaving off their beard or mustache can make them look younger because it can change the appearance of their face. For others, it may not make much of a difference.

How To

If you're interested in seeing if going clean-shaven will make you look younger, there are a few steps you can take:

1. Grow out your facial hair

Before you can shave your facial hair, you need to grow it out. This will give you a better idea of what you'll look like without it.

2. Take a before picture

Take a picture of yourself with your full beard or mustache before you shave it off. This will give you something to compare your new look to.

3. Shave it off

Once you've grown out your facial hair and taken a before picture, it's time to shave it off. Use a good quality razor and shaving cream to prevent irritation.

4. Take an after picture

Take another picture of yourself after you've shaved off your facial hair. Compare it to your before picture to see if you look younger or not.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to shaving off your facial hair:

1. Trim your facial hair

Use a beard trimmer to trim your facial hair down to a manageable length.

2. Wash your face

Wash your face with warm water and a mild cleanser to remove any dirt or oil.

3. Apply shaving cream

Apply a thick layer of shaving cream to your face to help lubricate your skin and prevent razor burn.

4. Shave with the grain

Shave with the grain of your hair to prevent ingrown hairs and irritation.

5. Rinse your face

Rinse your face with cool water to close your pores and prevent irritation.

6. Apply aftershave

Apply aftershave or a moisturizer to your face to soothe any irritation and keep your skin hydrated.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when shaving off your facial hair:

  • Use a sharp razor to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs.
  • Take your time when shaving to prevent cuts and nicks.
  • Moisturize your skin regularly to prevent dryness and irritation.
  • Experiment with different styles of facial hair to see what works best for your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Will shaving off my beard make me look younger?

A: It depends on your face shape and the amount of facial hair you had before. For some men, shaving off their beard or mustache can make them look younger because it can change the appearance of their face. For others, it may not make much of a difference.

Q: Will shaving off my beard prevent wrinkles?

A: Shaving off your beard will not prevent wrinkles, but keeping your skin moisturized and protected from the sun can help prevent premature aging.

Q: How often should I shave my face?

A: How often you shave your face depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your clean-shaven look.

Hair Care

Whether you decide to go clean-shaven or keep your beard, it's important to take care of your facial hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a mild cleanser to remove dirt and oil.
  • Condition your beard to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Trim your beard regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length.
  • Use a beard oil to keep your beard moisturized and prevent irritation.

So, does no facial hair make you look younger? It really depends on the individual, but it's worth experimenting with to see if it makes a difference for you. Just remember to take care of your facial hair and skin to keep yourself looking your best.
