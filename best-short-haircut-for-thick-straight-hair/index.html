Best Short Haircut For Thick Straight Hair In 2023

Written by Eveline Mar 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of constantly dealing with thick and straight hair? Do you want to make a change and try out a short haircut? Look no further than this guide for the best short haircut for thick straight hair in 2023. In this article, we will provide a description of the haircut, step-by-step instructions on how to achieve it, tips for maintaining the style, and answers to frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining the Style
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Description:

The best short haircut for thick straight hair in 2023 is the bob with blunt bangs. This haircut is perfect for those who want to add some edge to their style while still keeping it low maintenance. The bob with blunt bangs is a classic and versatile look that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

One of the great things about this haircut is that it works well with all face shapes and hair types. However, it is especially flattering on those with thick and straight hair as it helps to add some dimension and movement to the hair.

How to Achieve the Haircut:

The bob with blunt bangs is best achieved by visiting a professional hairstylist. They will be able to customize the haircut to your specific face shape and hair type. However, if you are feeling adventurous and want to try it out at home, follow the step-by-step instructions below.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section the hair into four parts: top, left, right, and back.
  3. Begin cutting the hair in the back section, starting at the nape of the neck and working your way up.
  4. Move on to the left and right sections, cutting the hair to match the back section.
  5. Finally, cut the top section to match the rest of the hair.
  6. Trim the ends to create a blunt look.
  7. Create blunt bangs by sectioning off the front of the hair and cutting straight across.
  8. Style the hair as desired using a flat iron or curling iron.

Tips for Maintaining the Style:

To keep your bob with blunt bangs looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Visit a hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep the ends looking blunt.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner designed for thick and straight hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands or clips to add some extra flair to the style.

Frequently Asked Questions:

What face shapes does this haircut work best on?

The bob with blunt bangs works well on all face shapes.

Can this haircut be styled differently for different occasions?

Yes, this haircut can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Use a curling iron for a more formal look or keep it straight for a casual day out.

What hair types does this haircut work best on?

This haircut works best on those with thick and straight hair.

Hair Care Tips:

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

And there you have it, the best short haircut for thick straight hair in 2023. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your hair looking stylish and healthy all year long!
