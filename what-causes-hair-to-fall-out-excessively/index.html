What Causes Hair To Fall Out Excessively

Written by Frank May 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our blog where we will discuss what causes hair to fall out excessively. Hair loss is a common problem that affects both men and women, and it can be a cause of concern for many. There are many reasons why hair fall occurs, and it is important to understand the cause so that you can take appropriate steps to prevent it. In this article, we will discuss the common causes of hair loss and how you can prevent it.

Causes of Excessive Hair Fall

1. Genetics

Genetics plays a significant role in hair loss. If your parents or grandparents have a history of baldness, you are more likely to experience hair loss. This type of hair loss is known as androgenetic alopecia, and it is caused by the hormone dihydrotestosterone (DHT).

2. Hormonal Imbalance

Hormonal imbalances can also cause hair loss. Changes in hormone levels during pregnancy, menopause, and thyroid disorders can lead to hair fall. In men, excess testosterone can convert to DHT, leading to hair loss.

3. Medications

Some medications can cause hair loss as a side effect. Chemotherapy drugs, blood thinners, and antidepressants are known to cause hair fall. If you are experiencing hair loss after starting a new medication, consult your doctor.

4. Stress

Stress can cause hair loss by disrupting the hair growth cycle. Telogen effluvium is a condition caused by stress, which leads to excessive hair fall. Managing stress levels can help prevent hair loss.

5. Nutritional Deficiencies

Nutritional deficiencies can also cause hair loss. Deficiencies in iron, zinc, biotin, and vitamin D can lead to hair fall. Eating a balanced diet and taking supplements can help prevent hair loss due to nutritional deficiencies.

How to Prevent Hair Loss

1. Maintain a Healthy Diet

Eating a balanced diet that includes proteins, vitamins, and minerals can help prevent hair loss. Include foods like eggs, nuts, leafy greens, and fish in your diet.

2. Manage Stress

Stress can cause hair loss, so it is important to manage stress levels. Practice yoga, meditation, or other relaxation techniques to reduce stress.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails can pull on the hair, leading to hair breakage and hair fall. Avoid tight hairstyles and use a soft scrunchie instead of an elastic band.

4. Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Avoid using harsh hair care products like shampoos and conditioners that contain sulfates and parabens. Use gentle hair care products that are free of chemicals and sulfates.

FAQs

Q. Can hair loss be reversed?

A. Hair loss can be reversed in some cases. If the hair follicles are not damaged, they can regrow hair. However, if the follicles are damaged, hair regrowth may not be possible.

Q. Can hair loss be prevented?

A. Yes, hair loss can be prevented by maintaining a healthy diet, managing stress levels, and avoiding harsh hair care products.

Q. How much hair fall is normal?

A. It is normal to lose 50 to 100 hairs per day. However, if you are losing more than that, it may be a cause for concern.

Conclusion

Hair loss can be a cause of concern, but there are many ways to prevent it. Understanding the causes and taking appropriate steps can help prevent hair fall. Maintain a healthy diet, manage stress levels, and use gentle hair care products to prevent hair loss. If you are experiencing excessive hair fall, consult your doctor.
