Women s Short Haircuts Over 60 How To Choose The Perfect Style

Written by Aprilia Apr 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a woman over 60, you may be looking for a haircut that's easy to maintain and stylish at the same time. Short haircuts are a great option, as they require minimal upkeep and can make you look younger and more vibrant. In this article, we'll discuss the different types of short haircuts that are popular among women over 60, how to choose the perfect style, and how to take care of your new haircut.

Types of Short Haircuts for Women Over 60

There are many different types of short haircuts that are popular among women over 60. Some of the most common ones include:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, layered haircut that's perfect for women who want a low-maintenance style. It's also a great option if you have fine hair, as it can add volume and texture to your hair.

Bob

The bob is a classic haircut that never goes out of style. There are many different types of bobs, including the blunt bob, the asymmetrical bob, and the layered bob. This haircut is perfect for women who want a versatile style that can be dressed up or down.

Shag

The shag is a layered haircut that's perfect for women who want a textured, messy look. This haircut is great for women who have thick hair or want to add volume to their hair.

How to Choose the Perfect Style

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect style:

Consider Your Face Shape

If you have a round face, a layered bob or pixie cut can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a shag or asymmetrical bob can soften your features.

Consider Your Hair Texture

If you have fine hair, a pixie cut or layered bob can add volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, a shag or blunt bob can help thin out your hair.

Consider Your Lifestyle

If you're an active person, a low-maintenance haircut like a pixie cut or bob may be the best option for you. If you're more of a glamour girl, a shag or asymmetrical bob can be dressed up for special occasions.

Step by Step Guide: Getting a Short Haircut

If you've decided to get a short haircut, here's a step by step guide to help you prepare for your appointment:

Step 1: Research

Look for inspiration photos online or in magazines. Bring these photos to your appointment to show your stylist what you like.

Step 2: Consultation

Talk to your stylist about your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. They can help you choose the perfect style for you.

Step 3: Wash and Dry

Wash and dry your hair before your appointment. This will help your stylist see your natural texture and make the best haircut decisions.

Step 4: Cut

Let your stylist work their magic! They will cut your hair to the desired length and shape.

Step 5: Style

Your stylist will style your hair to show off your new cut. They can also show you different ways to style your hair at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts require minimal upkeep, but here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for your hair type. For example, if you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

Experiment with Styling

Play around with different styling techniques to find the ones that work best for you.

FAQ

Q: Are short haircuts suitable for women over 60?

A: Yes, short haircuts are a great option for women over 60. They require minimal upkeep and can make you look younger and more vibrant.

Q: What is the best short haircut for women over 60?

A: The best short haircut for women over 60 depends on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Talk to your stylist to find the perfect style for you.

Q: How do I style my short haircut?

A: Your stylist can show you different ways to style your hair at home. Experiment with different techniques to find the ones that work best for you.

Conclusion

Short haircuts are a great option for women over 60 who want a low-maintenance style that's easy to maintain and stylish at the same time. When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Talk to your stylist to find the perfect style for you, and don't forget to experiment with different styling techniques to keep your hair looking its best.
