How To Fix Messed Up Doll Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Having a doll with beautiful hair can be a source of pride and joy for kids and collectors alike. However, it's not uncommon for doll hair to get tangled, frizzy, or just plain messy over time. If you're wondering how to fix messed up doll hair, don't worry! With the right tools and techniques, you can restore your doll's locks to their former glory.

  • How to Fix Messed Up Doll Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Doll Hair

How to Fix Messed Up Doll Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some of the tools you'll need to fix your doll's hair:

  • A wide-tooth comb
  • A small spray bottle filled with water
  • A hairbrush with soft bristles
  • A detangling spray or fabric softener
  • A small pair of scissors (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide:

Now that you have your tools ready, let's get started on fixing your doll's hair:

  1. Start by gently combing through your doll's hair with a wide-tooth comb. This will help to remove any tangles or knots without causing further damage.
  2. Next, dampen the hair with water using the spray bottle. Be careful not to saturate the hair too much, as this can cause it to become frizzy.
  3. If the hair is still tangled, you can use a detangling spray or fabric softener to help loosen the knots. Spray a small amount onto the hair and carefully comb through with a soft-bristled brush.
  4. If there are any stubborn knots that won't come out, you can carefully snip them out with a pair of small scissors. Be very careful not to cut too much hair or accidentally cut the doll's scalp.
  5. Once the hair is detangled and smooth, you can style it as desired. You can use hair clips, ribbons, or other accessories to add some flair to your doll's look.

Tips for Maintaining Doll Hair:

Now that your doll's hair is looking beautiful again, here are some tips to help maintain it:

  • Store your doll in a cool, dry place to prevent the hair from becoming frizzy or damaged.
  • Use a fabric softener or detangling spray regularly to keep the hair soft and easy to manage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on doll hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • If the hair becomes tangled or messy again, repeat the steps above to fix it.

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing messed up doll hair:

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on doll hair?
A: It's best to avoid using regular shampoo and conditioner on doll hair, as these products can be too harsh and may damage the hair. Q: Can I use hot water to fix tangled doll hair?
A: No, you should avoid using hot water on doll hair as it can cause damage and breakage. Stick to using cool or lukewarm water.

Doll Hair Care:

Proper doll hair care is important to prevent it from becoming tangled, frizzy, or damaged. Here are some tips:

  • Brush the hair regularly with a soft-bristled brush to prevent tangles.
  • Store your doll in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on doll hair.
  • Use a fabric softener or detangling spray to keep the hair soft and easy to manage.

With these tips and techniques, you can fix messed up doll hair and keep it looking beautiful for years to come. Happy styling!
