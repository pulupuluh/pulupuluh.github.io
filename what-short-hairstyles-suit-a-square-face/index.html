What Short Hairstyles Suit A Square Face

Description

Are you looking for a new short hairstyle that will flatter your square face? Square faces have a strong jawline, forehead, and cheekbones that are all roughly the same width. When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to find a cut that will soften your features and create balance. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short hairstyles for square faces that will make you look and feel your best.

How to Choose Short Hairstyles for Square Faces

When choosing a short hairstyle for a square face, it's important to consider the following:

  • Length: Short hairstyles that are chin-length or above are ideal for square faces.
  • Layers: Layers can help soften the angles of a square face.
  • Side-swept bangs: Bangs that are swept to the side can help create balance and soften a strong jawline.
  • Volume: Short hairstyles with volume on top can help elongate a square face.

Short Hairstyles to Avoid for Square Faces

There are also some short hairstyles that you should avoid if you have a square face:

  • Straight bangs: Straight bangs can make a square face appear even more angular.
  • Short bob: A short bob can accentuate a strong jawline.

Step by Step Guide for Styling Short Hair for Square Faces

Follow these steps for styling short hair for square faces:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to add volume and hold.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create soft waves.
  5. If you have bangs, blow dry them to the side to create a soft, sweeping effect.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Square Faces

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and body to your hair.

FAQs

What is the best short hairstyle for a square face?

The best short hairstyles for square faces are chin-length or above, with layers, side-swept bangs, and volume on top.

What short hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face?

Avoid short hairstyles with straight bangs and short bobs if you have a square face.

Hair Care for Short Hair and Square Faces

When caring for short hair and a square face, it's important to use products that will add volume and body to your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

With the right short hairstyle and hair care routine, you can flatter your square face and look and feel your best.
