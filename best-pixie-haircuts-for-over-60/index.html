Best Pixie Haircuts For Over 60 How To Choose And Style

Written by Oliver Mar 11, 2023 Â· 13 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and modern haircut that suits your age and personality, a pixie cut is worth considering. Pixie haircuts are versatile, easy to style, and can enhance your natural features. However, finding the best pixie haircut for over 60 can be challenging, as you want a style that flatters your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. In this article, we'll share some tips, ideas, and inspiration for the best pixie haircuts for over 60.

How to Choose the Best Pixie Haircut for Over 60

Before you book an appointment with your hairstylist, there are a few things to consider when choosing the best pixie haircut for over 60. Here are some factors to keep in mind:

Face Shape

The first thing to consider is your face shape, as it can affect how a pixie cut looks on you. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for a longer pixie with side-swept bangs to elongate your features. If you have a square face, a textured pixie with layers can soften your jawline. If you have an oval face, you're lucky, as most pixie cuts can suit you.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also influence the type of pixie cut that works best for you. If you have fine hair, you may want to try a layered pixie with volume at the crown to add fullness. If you have thick hair, a choppy pixie with textured layers can reduce the weight and add movement. If you have curly or wavy hair, a short curly pixie can showcase your curls and frame your face.

Lifestyle

Lastly, your lifestyle can impact your choice of pixie cut. If you're low-maintenance and prefer a wash-and-go style, a short and simple pixie can be perfect for you. If you're more adventurous and love experimenting with styling products, a messy and edgy pixie can be a fun option. If you're somewhere in between, a classic and elegant pixie with a side part can be versatile and chic.

Best Pixie Haircuts for Over 60

Now that you know how to choose the best pixie haircut for over 60, let's explore some of the most popular and flattering styles. Here are ten ideas to inspire you:

1. Classic Pixie

The classic pixie is a timeless and elegant haircut that can suit any age or face shape. It features short and even layers all around the head, with a tapered nape and sideburns. You can add a side part, bangs, or a touch of volume to customize the look.

2. Layered Pixie

The layered pixie is a versatile and voluminous haircut that works well for fine or thinning hair. It features choppy layers that add texture and movement, with longer pieces on top and shorter pieces on the sides and back. You can style it messy or sleek, depending on your mood.

3. Edgy Pixie

The edgy pixie is a bold and daring haircut that can showcase your personality and confidence. It features asymmetrical or shaved sides, longer layers on top, and jagged edges. You can add highlights, spikes, or a funky color to make it stand out.

4. Side-Swept Pixie

The side-swept pixie is a soft and feminine haircut that can flatter a round or square face. It features longer layers on one side, swept across the forehead, and shorter layers on the other side. You can add layers at the crown to create volume and movement.

5. Short Curly Pixie

The short curly pixie is a playful and charming haircut that can embrace your natural curls. It features short layers all around the head, with defined curls on top and tapered sides and back. You can add a side part or bangs to frame your face.

6. Textured Pixie

The textured pixie is a modern and chic haircut that can add dimension and interest to your hair. It features layers of different lengths and angles, with razored ends and wispy strands. You can style it messy or tousled, using a texturizing spray or paste.

7. Tousled Pixie

The tousled pixie is a relaxed and carefree haircut that can suit a casual and bohemian style. It features choppy layers with a messy and tousled look, using a salt spray or a curl cream. You can add a headband, a scarf, or a hat to accessorize it.

8. Feathered Pixie

The feathered pixie is a light and airy haircut that can add softness and movement to your hair. It features feathered layers that frame your face and blend seamlessly with your hairline. You can add highlights or lowlights to enhance the texture.

9. Spiky Pixie

The spiky pixie is a playful and energetic haircut that can bring out your inner rebel. It features short and textured layers that can be spiked up using a gel or a wax. You can add a side part or a fringe to soften the look.

10. Layered Bangs Pixie

The layered bangs pixie is a trendy and youthful haircut that can flatter any face shape. It features short layers all around the head, with longer layers that form bangs on the forehead. You can add texture and volume to the crown to create a tousled look.

How to Style and Maintain Your Pixie Haircut

Now that you have chosen your best pixie haircut for over 60, it's time to learn how to style and maintain it. Here are some tips to help you:

Styling Tips

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add fullness and lift at the roots.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a straightening iron or a curling wand to add texture and waves.
  • Apply a styling wax or a pomade to define the layers and add shine.
  • Experiment with different partings, accessories, and hair colors to refresh your look.

Maintenance Tips

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to trim the ends and maintain the shape.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools or chemicals that can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment or a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and humidity using a hat or a scarf.

FAQ

Can I wear a pixie haircut if I'm over 60?

Absolutely! Age is just a number, and a pixie haircut can be a flattering and stylish choice for any woman, regardless of her age. Just make sure to choose a pixie cut that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, and to maintain it regularly.

Which pixie haircut is best for fine hair?

A layered pixie with volume at the crown is ideal for fine hair, as it can add fullness and texture. You can also try a short and simple pixie with side-swept bangs or a textured pixie with choppy layers.

How often should I trim my pixie haircut?

You should visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to trim the ends and maintain the shape of your pixie haircut. This will prevent split ends, breakage, and frizz, and keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Can I style my pixie haircut differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can experiment with different styling techniques and products to create different looks for different occasions. For example, you can style your pixie haircut sleek and smooth for a formal event, messy and tousled for a casual outing, or spiky and edgy for a night out.

Hair Care

Finally, to keep your hair healthy and beautiful, it's important to follow a hair care routine that suits your hair type and needs. Here are some general tips to help you:

  • Eat a balanced and nutritious diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to hydrate your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid smoking, alcohol, and stress, as they can affect your hair growth and quality.
  • Use hair products that are gentle, natural, and free of harsh chemicals.
