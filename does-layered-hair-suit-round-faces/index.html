Does Layered Hair Suit Round Faces

Written by Oliver Jul 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Layered hair is a popular hairstyle that has been around for many years. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by people of all ages and hair types. However, one question that many people ask is whether layered hair suits round faces. If you have a round face and are considering getting a layered haircut, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss whether layered hair suits round faces, how to get the perfect layered haircut, tips for maintaining your hair, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Layered hair is a style that involves cutting the hair into different lengths. This creates layers that add volume and texture to the hair. Layered hair is a popular style because it is versatile and can be worn by people of all ages and hair types. However, if you have a round face, you may be wondering if layered hair is the right choice for you.

How to

The first step in getting a layered haircut is to find a good hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience cutting layered hair and who understands your hair type and face shape. When you go in for your appointment, bring pictures of layered haircuts that you like. This will help your stylist understand the look that you are going for.

Step by Step

The process of getting a layered haircut will vary depending on your hair type and the style that you want. However, here are some general steps that your hairstylist may follow:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Section your hair into different sections.
  3. Cut the hair into different lengths.
  4. Blend the layers together.
  5. Style your hair.

Tips

If you have a round face and are considering getting a layered haircut, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Ask your hairstylist to create layers that frame your face.
  • Avoid getting layers that are too short or choppy.
  • Consider getting side-swept bangs to create a slimming effect.
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Does layered hair suit all face shapes?

A: Layered hair can suit all face shapes, but the style of the layers may vary depending on the face shape.

Q: How often do I need to get my layered haircut trimmed?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the layers lose their shape. Generally, you should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your layered haircut looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, layered hair can suit round faces if the layers are cut in a way that flatters the face shape. If you are considering getting a layered haircut, be sure to find a good hairstylist who understands your hair type and face shape. Follow these tips for maintaining your hair, and you will have a hairstyle that looks great and is easy to maintain.
