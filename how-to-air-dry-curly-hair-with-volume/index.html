How To Air Dry Curly Hair With Volume Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Apr 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to get the perfect amount of volume and definition without using heat styling tools. One great way to achieve this look is by air drying your hair. Air drying allows your curls to form naturally, resulting in a more natural and effortless style. However, getting volume with air drying can be tricky. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you air dry your curly hair with volume.

How to Air Dry Curly Hair with Volume: Step by Step

Follow these steps to air dry your curly hair with volume:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Hair

Start with clean hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to keep your curls healthy and hydrated. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water to help seal the cuticles and enhance shine.

Step 2: Apply Products

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair after washing. This will help to define your curls and keep them hydrated. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Flip Your Hair

Flip your hair upside down and gently scrunch your curls with your hands to encourage volume. You can also use a microfiber towel or an old T-shirt to scrunch your curls and remove excess water.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Let your hair air dry naturally. Avoid touching your hair too much to prevent frizz. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on low heat if you're in a rush.

Step 5: Fluff Your Curls

Once your hair is completely dry, flip your head back up and use your fingers to fluff your curls. This will help to add volume and create a more natural look. You can also use a pick or a wide-tooth comb to gently lift your roots and create more volume.

Tips for Air Drying Curly Hair with Volume

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when air drying your curly hair:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Choose products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that are hydrating and nourishing, and avoid products that contain sulfates, silicones, and alcohol, as these can dry out your curls and cause frizz.

Tip 2: Don't Touch Your Hair Too Much

Touching your hair too much can cause frizz and disrupt the natural curl pattern. Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible while it's drying. You can also use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.

Tip 3: Use a Diffuser

If you're in a rush, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on low heat. This will help to enhance your curls and create more volume.

FAQ: Air Drying Curly Hair with Volume

Q: How long does it take to air dry curly hair?

A: It can take anywhere from 2-4 hours to air dry curly hair, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer to speed up the drying process?

A: Yes, you can use a hair dryer on low heat with a diffuser attachment to speed up the drying process. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using high heat, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Q: How often should I air dry my curly hair?

A: You can air dry your curly hair as often as you like, but it's important to keep your hair hydrated and moisturized to prevent damage and breakage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help to nourish and hydrate your curls. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair at Night

Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep. You can also use a silk or satin scarf to protect your hair.

Air drying your curly hair with volume is a great way to achieve a natural and effortless look. By following these tips and tricks, you can get the perfect amount of volume and definition without using heat styling tools. Remember to choose the right products, avoid touching your hair too much, and use a diffuser if you're in a rush. With the right care and attention, your curly hair can look healthy and beautiful every day.
