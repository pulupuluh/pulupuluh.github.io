How To Relax Male Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Mar 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a man with naturally curly or kinky hair, you may be looking for ways to relax your hair for a sleeker, straighter look. Relaxing your hair can help you achieve the style you want, but it's important to do it safely and correctly. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to relax male hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Relax Male Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Relaxing Male Hair
  • FAQs About Relaxing Male Hair
  • Hair Care After Relaxing

Description

Relaxing male hair is the process of chemically altering the hair's natural texture to make it straighter. This is typically done using a relaxer, which is a cream or lotion that contains chemicals that break down the hair's protein bonds. Relaxing your hair can help you achieve a smoother, more manageable style that requires less daily maintenance.

How to Relax Male Hair

Before you begin the relaxing process, it's important to understand that it can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly. It's best to have a professional stylist perform the relaxer treatment, but if you choose to do it yourself, be sure to follow the instructions carefully and take all necessary safety precautions.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the basic steps for relaxing male hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a protective base to your scalp to prevent chemical burns.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and apply the relaxer cream or lotion to each section, starting at the roots and working your way out to the ends.
  4. Leave the relaxer on for the recommended amount of time, as specified in the instructions.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove all of the relaxer.
  6. Apply a neutralizing shampoo to stop the chemical process and restore the hair's natural pH balance.
  7. Rinse your hair again with warm water and apply a deep conditioner to moisturize and nourish your hair.
  8. Rinse the deep conditioner out and style your hair as desired.

Tips for Relaxing Male Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when relaxing your hair:

  • Always follow the instructions carefully and take all necessary safety precautions.
  • Don't leave the relaxer on for longer than recommended, as this can cause damage to your hair and scalp.
  • Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly after applying the relaxer, as any residue left behind can cause irritation or damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools immediately after relaxing your hair, as this can cause further damage.
  • Consider getting a touch-up treatment every few months to maintain your desired style.

FAQs About Relaxing Male Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about relaxing male hair:

  • Is it safe to relax male hair?
  • What should I do if I experience irritation or discomfort during the relaxing process?
  • How often should I get a touch-up treatment?
  • Can I still use regular shampoo and conditioner after relaxing my hair?

Hair Care After Relaxing

After relaxing your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for post-relaxer hair care:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioner regularly to keep hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add extra moisture and shine.
  • Get regular trims to keep hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to relax male hair, you can achieve the sleek, straight style you've been dreaming of. Just be sure to take all necessary safety precautions and take care of your hair properly afterwards. With a little bit of effort, you can have healthy, beautiful, and manageable hair.
