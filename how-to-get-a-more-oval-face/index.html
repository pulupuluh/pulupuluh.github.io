How to Get a More Oval Face: Tips, Tricks, and Hair Care

If you're looking to get a more oval face, there are several steps you can take to achieve this look. From simple beauty tricks to hair care tips, we've got you covered. Here's how to get a more oval face:

How to Get a More Oval Face

Step 1: Contouring

One of the easiest ways to get a more oval face is through contouring. This makeup technique involves using a darker shade of makeup to create shadows and highlight certain areas of the face. By doing this, you can create the illusion of a more defined jawline, slimmer cheeks, and a more oval face shape.

Step 2: Facial Exercises

Facial exercises are another effective way to get a more oval face. By strengthening and toning the muscles in your face, you can create a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Some popular facial exercises include the fish face, cheek lift, and jaw release.

Step 3: Hair Care

Your hair can also play a role in creating a more oval face shape. Consider getting a haircut that flatters your face shape, such as a layered bob or long layers. You can also use styling techniques, such as adding volume at the crown of your head, to create a more oval look.

Tips for Getting a More Oval Face

Tip 1: Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can make your face appear shorter and more round. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or no bangs at all to create a more elongated look.

Tip 2: Choose the Right Glasses

If you wear glasses, choose a pair that flatters your face shape. Oval or square frames can help create a more oval look, while round frames can make your face appear rounder.

Tip 3: Emphasize Your Eyes

By emphasizing your eyes with makeup, you can draw attention away from the rounder areas of your face. Consider using eyeliner or eyeshadow to create a winged or cat-eye look.

FAQ

Q: Can losing weight help me get a more oval face?

A: Yes. Losing weight can help reduce the amount of fat in your face, creating a more defined and oval shape.

Q: Are there any non-surgical procedures that can help me get a more oval face?

A: Yes. Non-surgical procedures, such as Botox and dermal fillers, can help create a more defined jawline and cheekbones.

Hair Care for a More Oval Face

Tips for Styling Your Hair

When it comes to hair care for a more oval face, there are several tips you can follow:

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape, such as a layered bob or long layers.
  • Avoid heavy bangs that can make your face appear shorter and more round.
  • Add volume at the crown of your head to create a more elongated look.
  • Consider using hair accessories, such as headbands or barrettes, to create a more oval shape.

Tips for Hair Care

Proper hair care is also important for creating a more oval face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get a more oval face that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
