Short Hair For Over 60 Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you over 60 with a round face and looking for a new hairstyle? Look no further than short hair! Short haircuts are not only stylish but also practical and low-maintenance. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to choose the perfect short hairstyle for your round face, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Over 60 Round Face
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut

  5. Hair Care Advice for Short Hair

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Over 60 Round Face

Choosing the right hairstyle is all about balancing your facial features. For round faces, it's important to add height and volume to the crown of the head while elongating the face. Here are some short hairstyles that work well for round faces:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, layered haircut that's perfect for round faces. It adds height to the crown of the head and elongates the face. You can also add side-swept bangs to further elongate the face.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a short haircut that typically falls between the chin and the shoulders. For round faces, it's best to opt for a longer bob that's slightly angled to elongate the face.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. It works well for round faces because it adds height to the crown of the head and elongates the face. You can also add side-swept bangs to further elongate the face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut

Now that you've chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your round face, it's time to get it cut! Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Find a Good Hairstylist

Find a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts. They'll be able to give you the best advice on what hairstyle will work best for your face shape.

Step 2: Show Your Hairstylist a Picture

Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to your hairstylist. This will give them a better idea of what you're looking for.

Step 3: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair before your haircut appointment. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to cut your hair.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Dry Naturally

Let your hair dry naturally before your haircut appointment. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to see how your hair falls naturally.

Step 5: Trust Your Hairstylist

Trust your hairstylist to give you the perfect short haircut. They know what they're doing!

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair

Short hair is low-maintenance, but there are still some things you can do to keep it looking its best:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for short hair. These products will help keep your hair looking its best.

Tip 2: Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your short haircut looking neat and tidy.

Tip 3: Experiment with Styling

Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you. You can use a flat iron, curling iron, or even a hair dryer to achieve different looks.

FAQs About Short Hair for Over 60 Round Face

Q: Is short hair easy to maintain?

A: Yes, short hair is low-maintenance and easy to maintain.

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

A: No, a pixie cut can actually elongate a round face by adding height to the crown of the head.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs with short hair. Side-swept bangs work best for round faces.

Hair Care Advice for Short Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for short hair:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

Tip 2: Use Conditioner

Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

With these tips and advice, you're now ready to rock a stylish and low-maintenance short haircut that flatters your round face. Remember to trust your hairstylist and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you!
