Best Medium Length Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, thick, wavy hair? Finding the right haircut can make all the difference. Medium length haircuts are a great option for those with thick wavy hair. In this article, we will discuss the best medium length haircuts for thick wavy hair, how to style them, and tips for maintaining healthy locks.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium length haircuts are versatile and can work with most face shapes. They are also easy to style and can be dressed up or down. For those with thick wavy hair, a medium length haircut can help to manage the volume and create a more polished look.

How to

Before getting a medium length haircut, it is important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A stylist can help you determine which cut will work best for you. When styling, use products that are designed for thick wavy hair, such as a curl-enhancing cream or a volumizing mousse.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, damp hair.
2. Apply a styling product to enhance curls or add volume.
3. Blow dry hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance curls.
4. Use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition.
5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

- Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair healthy.
- Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage.
- Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
- Embrace your natural texture and work with it instead of against it.

FAQ

Q: What is the best medium length haircut for thick wavy hair?
A: A layered haircut with long layers is a great option for those with thick wavy hair.

Q: What is the best way to style thick wavy hair?
A: Use products that are designed for thick wavy hair and embrace your natural texture.

Hair Care

When caring for thick wavy hair, it is important to use products that are designed for your hair type. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping natural oils from your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair healthy. Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage. Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, medium length haircuts are a great option for those with thick wavy hair. They are versatile and easy to style. When choosing a haircut, consider your face shape and hair texture. Use products that are designed for thick wavy hair and embrace your natural texture. With the right haircut and care, you can have healthy, beautiful locks.
