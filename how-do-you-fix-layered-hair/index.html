How To Fix Layered Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Layered hair is a popular hairstyle that can give volume and movement to your locks. However, sometimes the layers can become uneven, making it difficult to style. If you're wondering how to fix layered hair, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to fix layered hair, along with tips and FAQs to help you achieve a flawless hairstyle.

How to Fix Layered Hair

Fixing layered hair requires patience and a little bit of skill. Here's how to fix layered hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in fixing layered hair is to wash your hair. Make sure you use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. After washing, gently towel-dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the front, the sides, and the back. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 3: Trim the Layers

To fix uneven layers, start by trimming the longest layers first. Use hair-cutting scissors and cut small sections at a time, making sure to blend the layers as you go. Be careful not to cut too much hair, as it can lead to a choppy and uneven look.

Step 4: Check the Length

After trimming the layers, check the length to make sure it's even. If you spot any unevenness, trim it until you achieve the desired length.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've fixed the layers, style your hair as usual. You can blow-dry your hair or let it air-dry. Use a hair straightener or curling iron to add some texture and volume to your locks.

Tips for Fixing Layered Hair

Here are some tips to help you fix layered hair:

  • Trim your hair when it's dry to avoid any shrinkage.
  • Use hair-cutting scissors instead of regular scissors.
  • Start by trimming a small section of hair and work your way up.
  • Blend the layers as you go to avoid a choppy look.

FAQs About Fixing Layered Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing layered hair:

Can I fix my layered hair at home?

Yes, you can fix your layered hair at home. However, if you're unsure about how to do it, it's best to consult a hairstylist.

How often should I trim my layered hair?

You should trim your layered hair every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I fix my layered hair without cutting it?

If you're looking to fix your layered hair without cutting it, you can try using hair products that add volume and texture to your locks. You can also try blow-drying your hair to add volume.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Hair

Here are some hair care tips for layered hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools.

Conclusion

Fixing layered hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a flawless look. Follow our step-by-step guide, tips, and FAQs to fix your layered hair and maintain its shape and volume. Don't forget to take care of your hair by using the right products and tools.
