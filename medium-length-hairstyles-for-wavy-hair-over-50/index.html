Medium Length Hairstyles For Wavy Hair Over 50

Written by Eveline Feb 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is both stylish and easy to manage? As we age, our hair changes and it can be difficult to find a style that suits our changing hair texture and face shape. If you have wavy hair over 50, medium length hairstyles can be a great option. In this article, we will discuss some of the best medium length hairstyles for wavy hair over 50, along with tips and tricks for styling and caring for your hair.

Description

Medium length hairstyles are a great option for women over 50 who want a versatile look that is easy to maintain. With wavy hair, you can add volume and texture to your hair, creating a soft and feminine style. Medium length hairstyles can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style, whether you prefer a layered look or a blunt cut.

How to

If you are considering a medium length hairstyle for your wavy hair, there are several factors to consider. First, think about your face shape and choose a style that flatters your features. A layered cut can add volume and dimension to your hair, while a blunt cut can create a sleek and sophisticated look.

You should also consider your hair texture and choose a style that works with your natural waves. If you have thick, coarse hair, a layered cut can help to thin out your hair and make it more manageable. If you have fine, limp hair, a blunt cut can create the illusion of thickness and volume.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for wavy hair. This will help to define your waves and reduce frizz.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.

3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.

4. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves throughout your hair.

5. Finish with a hairspray or styling cream to hold your waves in place.

Tips

- Experiment with different styles and cuts to find the one that works best for your hair texture and face shape.

- Use products that are designed for wavy hair to enhance your natural texture.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your strands and cause frizz.

- Get regular trims to maintain the health and shape of your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a medium length hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with a medium length hairstyle. Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It is recommended to wash wavy hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

To keep your wavy hair looking its best, it is important to practice good hair care habits. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair to enhance your natural texture and reduce frizz. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before styling. Get regular trims to maintain the health and shape of your hair, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your strands.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a beautiful and stylish medium length hairstyle for your wavy hair over 50. Whether you prefer a layered cut or a blunt bob, there is a style that will work for you. Embrace your natural texture and enjoy your new look!
