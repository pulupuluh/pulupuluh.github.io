Will Short Hair Make My Head Look Bigger A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Mar 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering cutting your hair short but worried that it will make your head look bigger? This is a common concern for many people, but the truth is, it's not that simple. In this guide, we'll explore the answer to this question and provide you with some tips on how to choose the best short hairstyle for you.

Description

Short hair has been a popular hairstyle for decades, and it's easy to see why. It's low-maintenance, versatile, and can give you a fresh, new look. However, many people are hesitant to cut their hair short because of the fear that it will make their head look bigger. This is a valid concern as the size and shape of your head can affect your overall appearance.

How to

The good news is that there's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. The size and shape of your head, as well as your facial features, will determine whether or not short hair will make your head look bigger. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Step by step

  1. Consider your face shape: If you have a round face, a short hairstyle with volume on top can help elongate your face and balance out the proportions. If you have a long face, a shorter bob or pixie cut can help shorten your face and make it appear wider.
  2. Think about your hair texture: If you have thick, coarse hair, a short hairstyle with layers can help reduce bulk and make your head appear smaller. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can add volume and make your head look fuller.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist: A professional hairstylist can help you determine the best short hairstyle for you based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

  • Avoid blunt, straight cuts that can make your head look boxy and square.
  • Consider adding layers or texture to your hair to create movement and dimension.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a side part or bangs, to find what works best for you.
  • Accessorize with hats, headbands, or scarves to add some variety to your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair and head size:

Will a short pixie cut make my head look bigger?

Not necessarily. A pixie cut can actually make your head look smaller if it's layered and textured. However, if you have a round face, a shorter pixie cut may not be the most flattering option.

What short hairstyle is best for a square face?

A short bob with layers and volume on top can soften the angles of a square face and create a more balanced look.

Can I still wear my hair up with a short hairstyle?

Yes! You can still wear your hair up with a short hairstyle. Just experiment with different styles, such as a messy bun or a half-up, half-down look.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to keep it clean and fresh.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Invest in a high-quality styling product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to create different styles.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your short hair.

Conclusion

So, will short hair make your head look bigger? The answer is, it depends. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a short hairstyle. Consult with a hairstylist to help you find the perfect look for you. With the right cut and styling, short hair can be a flattering and stylish option for any head size.
