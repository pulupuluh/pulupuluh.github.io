What Sunglasses Look Good On Heart Shaped Faces Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 23, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, finding the perfect sunglasses can be a challenge. With a wider forehead and narrower chin, it's important to find sunglasses that balance out your features. In this article, we'll go over what sunglasses look good on heart shaped faces and provide some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect pair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Sunglasses for Heart Shaped Faces
  • Tips and Tricks for Finding the Perfect Pair
  • Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into what sunglasses look good on heart shaped faces, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the shape of your face. Is it round, square, oval, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped?

If you have a heart shaped face, you'll notice that your forehead is wider and your chin is narrower. Your cheeks may also be slightly wider than your chin.

Best Sunglasses for Heart Shaped Faces

When it comes to sunglasses for heart shaped faces, the key is to choose frames that balance out your features. Here are some styles to consider:

Cat Eye Sunglasses

Cat eye sunglasses are perfect for heart shaped faces because they draw attention to the top of your face and balance out your chin. Look for frames that are wider at the top and taper towards the bottom.

Round Sunglasses

Round sunglasses are another great option for heart shaped faces. Look for frames that are slightly wider than your forehead and have a gentle curve.

Aviator Sunglasses

Aviator sunglasses are a classic style that look great on heart shaped faces. Look for frames that are wider at the top and have a slight curve inwards towards the bottom.

Wayfarer Sunglasses

Wayfarer sunglasses are a timeless style that work well on heart shaped faces. Look for frames that are slightly wider than your forehead and have a straight top.

Tips and Tricks for Finding the Perfect Pair

Now that you know what styles to look for, here are some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect pair of sunglasses:

  • Choose frames that are wider than your forehead to balance out your features.
  • Avoid frames that are too narrow or too small, as they can make your forehead appear wider.
  • Look for frames with a slight curve that follow the natural shape of your face.
  • Try on different styles and shapes to see what looks best on you.
  • Consider the color of the frames and lenses, as well as the level of UV protection.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces

When it comes to hair care for heart shaped faces, the goal is to create balance between your forehead and chin. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on top, as they can make your forehead appear even wider.
  • Consider a side part or bangs to help balance out your features.
  • Try a layered haircut to add volume around your chin and jawline.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your chin appear more narrow.

FAQs

What color frames should I choose?

When it comes to color, it's all about personal preference. However, neutral colors like black, brown, and tortoise shell tend to be the most versatile.

What level of UV protection do I need?

It's important to choose sunglasses with at least 99% UV protection to protect your eyes from harmful UV rays.

Can I wear oversized sunglasses?

Yes, oversized sunglasses can look great on heart shaped faces. Just make sure to choose frames with a slight curve that follow the natural shape of your face.

What about mirrored lenses?

Mirrored lenses can be a fun and trendy option, but make sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone and the color of your frames.

Now that you know what sunglasses look good on heart shaped faces, it's time to start shopping! Remember to choose frames that balance out your features and complement your personal style.
