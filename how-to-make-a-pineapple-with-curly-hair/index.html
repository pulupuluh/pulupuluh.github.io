How To Make A Pineapple With Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful and unique, it can also be difficult to manage and style. One of the best and easiest ways to style your curly hair is by making a pineapple. A pineapple is a bun-like hairstyle that sits on top of your head, resembling the shape of a pineapple. In this post, we will guide you through the steps of making a pineapple with curly hair.

Table of Content

  • How to Make a Pineapple with Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making a Pineapple with Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Make a Pineapple with Curly Hair

The first step in making a pineapple with curly hair is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a hairbrush or comb, a hair tie, and bobby pins (optional). Once you have all the tools, you can start with the following steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by brushing or combing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Flip your hair upside down and gather all your curls at the crown of your head.
  3. Secure your hair with a hair tie. Make sure the hair tie is not too tight, as it can cause tension and breakage in your curls.
  4. Fluff your curls to create volume and shape the pineapple to your liking.
  5. If you have any loose curls or frizz, you can use bobby pins to secure them in place.
  6. To complete the look, you can add a headband or scarf to accessorize your pineapple.

Tips for Making a Pineapple with Curly Hair

Making a pineapple with curly hair is a simple and easy hairstyle that can be done in just a few minutes. However, there are some tips that you can follow to make the process even easier:

  • Use a hair tie that does not have any metal parts, as it can damage your curls.
  • Do not brush or comb your hair after making the pineapple, as it can cause frizz and breakage.
  • If you have shorter hair, you can use a hair clip instead of a hair tie to secure your curls.
  • For a more defined pineapple, apply a curl cream or gel before making the hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different pineapple shapes and sizes to find the one that suits you the best.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a pineapple with curly hair:

Q: Can I make a pineapple with wet hair?
A: Yes, you can make a pineapple with wet hair. However, it may take longer to dry and set in place. Q: How long can I keep my hair in a pineapple?
A: You can keep your hair in a pineapple for as long as you want. It is a comfortable and easy hairstyle that can be worn both during the day and at night. Q: Will making a pineapple damage my curls?
A: No, making a pineapple will not damage your curls. However, if you tie your hair too tightly, it can cause tension and breakage in your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Making a pineapple with curly hair is just one of the many ways to style your beautiful curls. To keep your curls healthy and strong, here are some hair care tips that you can follow:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to provide extra moisture to your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause damage and breakage in your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these hair care tips and making a pineapple with curly hair, you can achieve beautiful and healthy curls that will turn heads everywhere you go. So go ahead and give it a try!
