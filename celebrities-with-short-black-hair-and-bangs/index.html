Celebrities With Short Black Hair And Bangs A Trendy Hairstyle For 2023

Written by William May 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair and bangs are back in style, and many celebrities are rocking this trendy look. If you're looking for a fresh and edgy hairstyle, then you should definitely consider short black hair with bangs. In this article, we will be discussing the top celebrities with this hairstyle, how to achieve the look, tips and tricks, and hair care advice.

Celebrities with Short Black Hair and Bangs

Short black hair with bangs has become increasingly popular, and many celebrities have jumped on board with this trend. Here are some of the top celebrities who are rocking this look:

Kendall Jenner

Kendall Jenner is known for her striking looks, and her short black hair with bangs is no exception. She has been seen sporting this hairstyle on numerous occasions, and it suits her perfectly.

Rihanna

Rihanna is a trendsetter in the world of fashion and beauty, and her short black hair with bangs is no exception. This hairstyle complements her strong features and adds a touch of edginess to her look.

Lupita Nyong'o

Lupita Nyong'o has proven that short hair can be just as versatile as long hair. Her short black hair with bangs is both chic and sophisticated, and it perfectly frames her face.

Zendaya

Zendaya is known for her bold and daring style, and her short black hair with bangs is no exception. This hairstyle adds an extra element of coolness to her already fierce look.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're considering getting short black hair with bangs, here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Find a reputable hairstylist who has experience with this hairstyle.
  2. Show them pictures of the celebrities mentioned above to give them an idea of what you're looking for.
  3. Discuss the length and style of the bangs with your hairstylist.
  4. Make sure to maintain your hair with regular trims to keep the style looking fresh.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for maintaining your short black hair with bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener or curling iron.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short black hair with bangs:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can be adapted to suit all face shapes. Your hairstylist will be able to advise you on the best style for your face shape.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail if you have short hair with bangs. You may need to use some bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Is it easy to maintain short hair with bangs?

Short hair with bangs does require regular maintenance, but it can be easy to maintain with the right hair products and styling tools.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some tips for keeping your short black hair with bangs healthy and strong:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal parts, as they can damage your hair.

In conclusion, short black hair with bangs is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that is perfect for those looking for a fresh new look. By following the tips and tricks mentioned in this article, you can easily achieve this hairstyle and maintain it with ease.
