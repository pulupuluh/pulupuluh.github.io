Should Curly Hair Be Cut Short

Written by Bardi Jun 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it adds a unique texture to your look that can make you stand out from the crowd. On the other hand, it can be difficult to tame and maintain, especially if it's long. This leads many people to ask the question: should curly hair be cut short? In this article, we'll explore the pros and cons of short curly hair and give you some tips on how to care for it.

Pros of Short Curly Hair

One of the biggest advantages of having short curly hair is that it's easier to manage. You won't have to spend as much time washing, conditioning, and styling your hair. Plus, shorter hair tends to look healthier and more voluminous than longer hair.

Another benefit of short curly hair is that it can make you look younger and more vibrant. This is especially true if you have fine, thin hair, which can appear limp and lifeless when it's long. By cutting your hair short, you'll give it more body and bounce, which can make you look more energetic and youthful.

Cons of Short Curly Hair

While there are many advantages to cutting your curly hair short, there are also some drawbacks to consider. One of the biggest disadvantages is that short hair can be more difficult to style. You may need to spend more time blow-drying, straightening, or curling your hair to achieve the look you want.

Another downside to short curly hair is that it can be more prone to frizz and flyaways. This is because shorter hair is more susceptible to environmental factors like humidity and wind. If you live in a humid climate or spend a lot of time outdoors, you may want to consider keeping your hair longer to avoid these issues.

How to Cut Short Curly Hair

If you've decided that you want to cut your curly hair short, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, it's important to find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair. Curly hair requires a different cutting technique than straight hair, so you'll want to make sure that your stylist knows what they're doing.

When it comes to the actual cut, there are many different styles to choose from. Some popular options include a pixie cut, a bob, or a layered cut. The best style for you will depend on your hair type, face shape, and personal preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Curly Hair

If you want to cut your curly hair short at home, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual, and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, back, and two sides.
  3. Starting with the back section, use scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Begin at the nape of your neck and work your way up, cutting small sections at a time.
  4. Move on to the side sections, cutting them to the same length as the back section.
  5. Finally, cut the top section to the desired length, making sure to blend it with the sides and back.
  6. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Once you've cut your curly hair short, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you maintain your new look:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Invest in a good quality hair serum or oil to keep your hair moisturized and frizz-free.
  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Q: Will cutting my curly hair short make it less curly?

A: No, cutting your hair won't change its natural texture. However, shorter hair may appear more curly because it has more bounce and body.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I cut it short?

A: Yes, you can still put your hair up in a ponytail or bun if it's short. However, you may need to use bobby pins or hair clips to secure it in place.

Q: Should I cut my curly hair short if I have a round face?

A: It depends on the style you choose. A pixie cut or a bob can be flattering for round faces, but you may want to avoid a blunt cut that ends at your chin.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Overall, cutting your curly hair short can be a great way to simplify your hair care routine and give yourself a fresh, youthful look. Just be sure to choose a style that works for your hair type and face shape, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
