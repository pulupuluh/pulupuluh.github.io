What To Eat To Stop Severe Hair Fall

Written by William Aug 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you experiencing severe hair fall and don't know what to do about it? Well, you are not alone! Hair fall is a common issue that affects both men and women. However, the good news is that there is something you can do about it. One of the most effective ways to stop severe hair fall is by eating the right foods. In this article, we will discuss what to eat to stop severe hair fall and other related tips that will help you achieve healthy and strong hair.

How To Stop Severe Hair Fall

Before we dive into what to eat to stop severe hair fall, it's essential to understand what causes hair fall. Hair fall can be caused by several factors, including genetics, stress, hormonal imbalances, and nutrient deficiencies. Therefore, to stop severe hair fall, you need to address these underlying causes. Here are some tips to help you stop severe hair fall:

  • Manage stress levels
  • Get enough sleep
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation
  • Eat a balanced diet

What To Eat To Stop Severe Hair Fall

Eating a balanced diet is one of the most effective ways to stop severe hair fall. Here are some of the foods you should consider adding to your diet to prevent hair fall:

1. Protein-rich foods

Protein is essential for healthy hair growth. Therefore, it's crucial to include protein-rich foods in your diet. Some of the best sources of protein include eggs, fish, chicken, beans, and lentils. These foods are also rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.

2. Omega-3 fatty acids

Omega-3 fatty acids are essential for healthy hair growth. They help nourish hair follicles and promote healthy hair growth. Some of the best sources of omega-3 fatty acids include fatty fish like salmon, mackerel, and sardines, as well as chia seeds, flaxseeds, and walnuts.

3. Iron-rich foods

Iron is essential for healthy hair growth as it helps transport oxygen to the hair follicles. Some of the best sources of iron include lean red meat, spinach, lentils, and fortified cereals.

4. Vitamin C-rich foods

Vitamin C is essential for healthy hair growth as it helps produce collagen, which is a vital component of hair. Some of the best sources of vitamin C include citrus fruits, berries, kiwi, and tomatoes.

5. Zinc-rich foods

Zinc is essential for healthy hair growth as it helps regulate the oil glands in the scalp. Some of the best sources of zinc include oysters, beef, pumpkin seeds, and lentils.

Step By Step Guide

If you're not sure how to incorporate these foods into your diet, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start your day with a protein-rich breakfast. You can have eggs, Greek yogurt, or a protein smoothie.
  2. Include a source of omega-3 fatty acids in your lunch. You can have grilled salmon or a salad with chia seeds or flaxseeds.
  3. Snack on fruits and nuts throughout the day to get a dose of vitamins and minerals.
  4. Include iron-rich foods in your dinner. You can have lean red meat, spinach, or lentils.
  5. Take a zinc supplement if you're not getting enough zinc from your diet.

Tips To Prevent Severe Hair Fall

Aside from eating the right foods, here are some additional tips to help prevent severe hair fall:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

FAQs

1. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress can cause hair fall. When you're stressed, your body produces cortisol, which can affect your hair growth cycle and cause hair fall.

2. Can diet affect hair fall?

Yes, a poor diet can lead to nutrient deficiencies that can cause hair fall. Therefore, it's essential to eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.

3. Can hair fall be reversed?

Yes, hair fall can be reversed by addressing the underlying causes and eating a balanced diet that's rich in nutrients that promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care Tips To Prevent Severe Hair Fall

Aside from eating the right foods, it's essential to take care of your hair to prevent severe hair fall. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

In conclusion, eating a balanced diet that's rich in nutrients that promote healthy hair growth is one of the most effective ways to stop severe hair fall. Incorporate protein-rich foods, omega-3 fatty acids, iron-rich foods, vitamin C-rich foods, and zinc-rich foods into your diet to achieve healthy and strong hair. Don't forget to take care of your hair by using sulfate-free shampoo, avoiding heat styling tools, and massaging your scalp regularly. By following these tips, you can prevent severe hair fall and achieve healthy and strong hair.
