How To Get Semi Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you've always dreamed of having semi curly hair, you're not alone. Many people with straight hair are envious of those with natural curls, but luckily, it's possible to achieve a semi curly look even if your hair is naturally straight. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps you need to take to get semi curly hair, including how to style your hair and how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Semi Curly Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Semi Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Semi Curly Hair

How to Get Semi Curly Hair

There are a few different ways to get semi curly hair, but the most common methods are through heat styling or using hair products. Here are some of the most popular options:

  • Using a curling iron or wand to create curls
  • Using hot rollers to create curls
  • Using a perm to permanently curl your hair
  • Using styling products like mousse or curl cream to enhance natural waves or create curls

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting semi curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is damp or wet, it will be harder to style and may not hold curl as well.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips or elastics.
  4. Using a curling iron or wand, wrap each section of hair around the barrel and hold for 10-15 seconds. Release the curl and repeat with the rest of your hair.
  5. If using hot rollers, wrap small sections of hair around the rollers and secure with clips. Leave in for 10-15 minutes, then release the rollers and comb through with your fingers.
  6. If using a perm, visit a professional stylist who can help you choose the right type of perm for your hair and apply it safely.
  7. If using styling products, apply them to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage natural waves or curls.
  8. Finish with a light hold hairspray to help your curls last longer.

Tips for Maintaining Semi Curly Hair

Once you've achieved semi curly hair, there are a few things you can do to help maintain your curls:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of natural oils that help keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, comb through your hair with a wide-toothed comb when it's wet, and use your fingers to style.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit heat styling to once or twice a week.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can I get semi curly hair without using heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can use styling products like mousse or curl cream to enhance natural waves or create curls without heat. However, these methods may not create as defined curls as heat styling tools.

Q: How long will my semi curly hair last?

A: The length of time your curls will last depends on the method you used to create them and how well you maintain them. Heat styling curls may last a day or two, while a perm will last several months.

Q: Can I still straighten my semi curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your semi curly hair using a flat iron or straightening brush. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray or cream to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips for Semi Curly Hair

Here are a few additional hair care tips to help keep your semi curly hair looking its best:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using hair ties that can cause breakage or damage. Instead, use scrunchies or hair bands made from soft materials.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine or salt water, as this can cause dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

By following these tips and using the right products and styling tools, you can achieve beautiful, semi curly hair that will make you feel confident and stylish.
