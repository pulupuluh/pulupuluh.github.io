How to Change Face Shape Naturally | Tips, Steps, and FAQ

Are you tired of your current face shape and want to change it without undergoing any surgery? You're not alone. Many people feel self-conscious about their face shape and want to change it to feel more confident. In this article, we'll share some tips and steps on how to change face shape naturally in 2023.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Change Face Shape Naturally
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Change Face Shape Naturally
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Face Shape Change

Description

Changing your face shape naturally involves certain lifestyle changes and habits that can help you achieve your desired look. It requires patience and dedication, but the results are worth it. By following the tips and steps we'll share in this article, you can change your face shape gradually and naturally.

How to Change Face Shape Naturally

The first step to changing your face shape naturally is to identify your current face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. Once you've identified your face shape, you can start making changes to enhance or soften certain features.

One way to change your face shape naturally is through facial exercises. These exercises target specific muscles in your face and can help tone and define them. Some popular facial exercises include cheek lifts, jawline exercises, and forehead exercises. You can find many tutorials online on how to do these exercises.

Another way to change your face shape naturally is through makeup. By using certain contouring techniques, you can create the illusion of a different face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you can use contouring to make your face appear more oval. There are many tutorials online on how to do makeup contouring for different face shapes.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to change your face shape naturally:

  1. Identify your current face shape
  2. Research facial exercises for your specific face shape
  3. Practice facial exercises daily
  4. Research makeup contouring techniques for your specific face shape
  5. Practice makeup contouring
  6. Adjust your hairstyle to complement your new face shape
  7. Be patient and consistent with your efforts

Tips to Change Face Shape Naturally

Here are some additional tips to change your face shape naturally:

  • Eat a balanced and healthy diet
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Get enough sleep to give your body time to repair and rejuvenate
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Use sunscreen to protect your skin from sun damage

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing face shape naturally:

Can face exercises really change your face shape?

Yes, facial exercises can help tone and define specific muscles in your face, which can alter your face shape over time.

How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

It can take several weeks or even months to see noticeable results from facial exercises. Consistency is key.

Can contouring really change your face shape?

Contouring can create the illusion of a different face shape, but it doesn't actually change your face shape permanently.

Hair Care for Face Shape Change

Your hairstyle can also play a role in changing your face shape naturally. Here are some tips for adjusting your hairstyle to complement your new face shape:

  • For a round face, try a long and layered haircut
  • For a square face, try a soft and layered haircut
  • For a heart-shaped face, try a side-swept bang or layered haircut
  • For a long face, try a blunt cut or a chin-length bob

Changing your face shape naturally takes time and effort, but it's possible. By following the tips and steps we've shared in this article, you can achieve your desired face shape and feel more confident in your appearance.
