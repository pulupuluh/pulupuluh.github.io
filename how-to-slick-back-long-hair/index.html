How To Slick Back Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have long hair, you may feel like it's impossible to achieve a sleek, polished look without spending hours in front of the mirror. However, with the right technique and products, slicking back your long hair can be quick and easy. In this article, we'll show you how to slick back long hair in relaxed, easy-to-understand language. We'll cover everything from the basics to advanced tips, so you can achieve the perfect slicked-back look every time.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Slick Back Long Hair

Slicking back long hair can be done with a variety of products, depending on your hair type and personal preference. Here are some options:

  • Pomade: A pomade is a water-based styling product that provides a firm hold without making your hair stiff or crunchy. It's perfect for creating a sleek, shiny look.
  • Gel: A hair gel is a thicker, stickier product that can provide a stronger hold than pomade. However, it can also make your hair feel stiff or crunchy.
  • Hairspray: Hairspray is a finishing product that can be used to lock in your style and add shine.

When selecting a product, consider your hair type and the look you're going for. If you have thick, coarse hair, you may want to opt for a gel or a stronger hold pomade. If you have fine hair, a lighter hold pomade may be a better option.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to slick back your long hair? Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, wet hair. This will help ensure that your style stays in place.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your hands and rub them together.
  3. Starting at the front of your hairline, use your fingers to comb the product back through your hair. Be sure to distribute it evenly.
  4. Using a comb, smooth your hair back and away from your face. If you want a more dramatic look, you can create a side part at this point.
  5. Continue combing your hair back until you reach the nape of your neck.
  6. Use your hands to smooth down any flyaways or stray hairs.
  7. If desired, apply a small amount of hairspray to lock in your style and add shine.
  8. Enjoy your sleek, polished look!

Tips for Slicking Back Long Hair

If you want to take your slicked-back look to the next level, try these tips:

  • Experiment with different products until you find one that works well for your hair type and desired look.
  • Use a comb to create a precise, polished look.
  • If your hair tends to frizz or curl, apply a smoothing serum before slicking it back.
  • If you want a more natural look, try using a lighter hold product and leaving some texture in your hair.
  • Consider adding some layers or texture to your hair to make it easier to slick back.

FAQs

How long does slicked-back hair last?

The longevity of your slicked-back style will depend on your hair type, the product you use, and your activity level. Generally, a well-executed slicked-back style can last for several hours or even all day.

Can I slick back my hair if it's thin or fine?

Yes! However, you may want to opt for a lighter hold product to avoid weighing down your hair. You can also try adding some volume to your hair by blow-drying it before slicking it back.

Can I slick back my hair if it's curly?

Yes! However, you may need to use a stronger hold product to keep your curls in place. You can also try straightening your hair before slicking it back.

Hair Care Tips for Slicked-Back Hair

If you're planning on slicking back your hair regularly, it's important to take care of your hair to avoid damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your hair to avoid pulling or breaking it.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily achieve the perfect slicked-back look with your long hair. Whether you're heading to a formal event or just want to switch up your everyday style, slicked-back hair is a chic and sophisticated option.
