What Hairstyle Suits Round Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Feb 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't seem to flatter your round face shape? Fret not, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss what hairstyles suit round face shape, along with tips, step-by-step guide, FAQs, and hair care tips to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Round Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Round Face Shape Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Round Face Shape Hair

Description

Round face shapes have a soft and circular shape with fuller cheeks and a rounded chin. The aim of choosing the right hairstyle for round face shape is to create an illusion of length and angles to balance out the roundness. A well-chosen hairstyle can make a significant difference and enhance your facial features.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Round Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle for round face shape, consider the following:

  • Length: Longer hairstyles can create the illusion of length, making your face appear slimmer. However, avoid hairstyles that are too long and flat, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and texture, creating angles that balance out the roundness of your face. However, avoid short layers that add volume to the sides of your face, making it look wider.
  • Bangs: Bangs can create the illusion of a longer face, especially if they are side-swept. However, avoid blunt bangs that cut across your forehead, making your face look wider.
  • Curls: Soft curls can add volume and texture, creating angles that balance out the roundness of your face. However, avoid tight curls that add volume to the sides of your face, making it look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right hairstyle for round face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape: Look in the mirror and determine if your face shape is round.
  2. Research hairstyles: Look for hairstyles that suit round face shape, such as long layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and soft curls.
  3. Consult a hairstylist: A professional hairstylist can help you choose the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
  4. Try it out: Once you have chosen a hairstyle, try it out before committing to it. Use virtual hairstyle apps or try on wigs to see how it looks on you.
  5. Maintain it: Once you have found the perfect hairstyle, maintain it with regular trims and hair care routines.

Tips for Styling Round Face Shape Hair

Here are some tips for styling round face shape hair:

  • Avoid straight and flat hairstyles that make your face look wider.
  • Try hairstyles with side parts that create angles and length.
  • Use volumizing products to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round face shape:

Q: Can short hair work for round face shape?

A: Yes, short hair can work for round face shape, as long as it has layers and volume to create angles.

Q: Are blunt bangs suitable for round face shape?

A: No, blunt bangs can make your face look wider. Opt for side-swept bangs instead.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail, as long as it's high and has volume to create length.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face Shape Hair

Here are some hair care tips for round face shape hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your hairstyle.

With these tips, you can choose the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape and enhance your natural beauty. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair, and always maintain it with proper hair care.
