Best Size Curling Iron For Beach Waves Everything You Need To Know In 2023

Written by William Feb 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a way to achieve those gorgeous beach waves without actually going to the beach? If so, using a curling iron is the perfect solution. However, not all curling irons are suitable for creating beachy waves. In this post, we'll be discussing the best size curling iron for beach waves, as well as some tips on how to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Beach Waves
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfect Beach Waves
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Beach Waves

Description:

Beach waves are a popular hairstyle that can be achieved with a curling iron. This style is perfect for those who want a relaxed, effortless look. The key to achieving beach waves is to use the right size curling iron. A curling iron with a barrel size of 1 inch or larger is ideal for creating beach waves.

How to Achieve Beach Waves:

Before we dive into the best size curling iron for beach waves, let's first discuss how to achieve the look. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Take a small section of hair and clamp it with the curling iron.
  5. Hold for a few seconds and release.
  6. Repeat until all sections of hair are curled.
  7. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.
  8. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Now that you know the basic steps, let's dive into a more detailed step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. It's best to start with clean, dry hair to achieve the best results.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant Spray

Before using a curling iron, it's important to protect your hair from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Choose the Right Curling Iron

Choose a curling iron with a barrel size of 1 inch or larger. This will create loose, beachy waves. If you have shorter hair, you can use a smaller barrel size.

Step 5: Clamp and Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp it with the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat until all sections of hair are curled.

Step 6: Loosen Your Curls

Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a more relaxed look.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Perfect Beach Waves:

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect beach waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel to reduce heat damage and create smoother curls.
  • Wrap your hair around the curling iron, rather than clamping it, to create a more natural look.
  • Don't curl all of your hair in the same direction. Alternate the direction of the curls to create a more natural look.
  • Use a sea salt spray to enhance the beachy texture.
  • Don't overdo it with hairspray. A light hold spray will keep your curls in place without making them look stiff.

FAQs:

What is the best size curling iron for beach waves?

A curling iron with a barrel size of 1 inch or larger is best for creating beach waves.

Can you create beach waves with a straightener?

Yes, you can create beach waves with a straightener, but it may be more difficult than using a curling iron.

How do you make beach waves last longer?

To make beach waves last longer, use a sea salt spray and avoid touching your hair too much.

Hair Care for Beach Waves:

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair after creating beach waves. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the best size curling iron for beach waves, it's time to try it out for yourself. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect beachy waves that will make you feel like you're on vacation all year round.
