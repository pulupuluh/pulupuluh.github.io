How To Spike My Hair Without Gel A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Spiking your hair can give you an edgy and stylish look, but using gel can be damaging to your hair in the long run. Fortunately, there are other ways to get the spike without the gel. In this article, we’ll show you how to spike your hair without gel, with a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Spike Your Hair Without Gel
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Spiking Your Hair Without Gel
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Spike Your Hair Without Gel

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s take a look at some of the methods you can use to spike your hair without gel:

  • Hairspray
  • Pomade
  • Mousse
  • Wax

Each of these methods has its pros and cons, so you’ll need to experiment to find out which one works best for you. In this guide, we’ll be using hairspray.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s how to spike your hair without gel:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade to your hair to give it some texture.
  3. Blow-dry your hair, using your fingers to create volume and texture.
  4. Take a section of hair, and spray it with hairspray from the roots to the ends.
  5. Use a comb to tease the hair, creating volume and height.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until you’ve spiked all the sections of hair you want to.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the spikes in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to hairspray. You don’t want to use too much, or your hair will look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Spiking Your Hair Without Gel

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a small amount of pomade to give your hair some texture.
  • Blow-dry your hair with your fingers to create volume and texture.
  • Tease your hair gently, taking care not to damage it.
  • Use hairspray sparingly, and hold the can several inches away from your head.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Can I spike my hair without using any products?

Yes, you can, but it will be harder to create and hold the spike without any product to help. You can try blow-drying your hair with your fingers to create some volume, and then using a comb to tease the hair into a spike.

What type of hairspray is best for spiking hair?

Look for a strong hold hairspray that won’t make your hair too stiff or sticky. You can also try a volumizing hairspray to add some extra lift.

Will spiking my hair damage it?

If you use the right products and techniques, spiking your hair shouldn’t cause any damage. Just be careful not to pull or tug on your hair too hard when teasing it.

Hair Care Tips

Spiking your hair can be fun, but it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray if you do.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair in good condition.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling or tugging.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and strong, even when spiking it without gel.
