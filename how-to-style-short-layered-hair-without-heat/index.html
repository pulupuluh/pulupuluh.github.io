How To Style Short Layered Hair Without Heat

Written by Robby Mar 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have short layered hair, you may think that styling options are limited. However, there are many ways to style your hair without using heat tools such as curling irons or straighteners. Not only does this save time and reduce damage to your hair, but it also allows you to experiment with different hairstyles that you may not have considered before.

Description

Short layered hair can be styled in many ways without heat. From braids to twists to updos, there are many options to choose from. These styles are ideal for those who want to avoid heat damage, have busy schedules, or simply prefer a more natural look.

How to Style Short Layered Hair Without Heat

Here are some simple steps to follow to style your short layered hair without heat:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and condition your hair as usual, then allow it to air dry completely. If you need to speed up the drying process, you can use a microfiber towel to gently pat your hair dry.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a leave-in conditioner or a styling cream. This will help to add moisture and hold to your hair, making it easier to style without heat.

Step 3: Try braids or twists

Braids and twists are easy and versatile hairstyles that work well on short layered hair. You can create a variety of different looks depending on the size and style of the braids or twists. Here are some options to consider:

  • French braids
  • Dutch braids
  • Fishtail braids
  • Twist outs
  • Bantu knots

Step 4: Experiment with updos

Updos are another great option for short layered hair. You can create a sleek and polished look with a low bun, or go for a more relaxed style with a messy bun or top knot. Here are some updo styles to try:

  • Low bun
  • Messy bun
  • Top knot
  • Half up, half down

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your short layered hair without heat:

  • Use bobby pins or hair ties to secure your style in place
  • Experiment with different partings to create new looks
  • Accessorize with headbands, scarves, or hair clips to add interest to your style
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

FAQ

Q: Can I still use heat occasionally?

A: Yes, you can still use heat tools occasionally if you want to switch up your style. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat every day.

Q: Will these styles work on all hair types?

A: Yes, these styles can work on all hair types, although some may require more styling product or a different technique to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding harsh chemicals or heat tools whenever possible.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your short layered hair without heat and achieve a variety of different looks. Whether you prefer braids, twists, or updos, there are many options to choose from that will help you look and feel your best.
