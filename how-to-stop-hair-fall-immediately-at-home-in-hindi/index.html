How To Stop Hair Fall Immediately At Home In Hindi

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of seeing strands of hair on your pillow, in your brush, and in the shower drain? Hair fall is a common problem faced by both men and women. But don't worry, there are several remedies available that can help you stop hair fall immediately at home. In this blog post, we will discuss some effective ways to stop hair fall in Hindi.

How to Stop Hair Fall Immediately at Home in Hindi

If you want to stop hair fall immediately at home, you need to follow a proper hair care regime. Here are some tips that can help:

Step 1: Massage your scalp

Massaging your scalp improves blood circulation and strengthens the roots of your hair. Use warm oil like coconut, almond, or olive oil to massage your scalp. Massage your scalp in circular motions for at least 5-10 minutes. This will help to reduce hair fall instantly.

Step 2: Use onion juice

Onion juice is rich in sulfur, which is known to promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. Grind an onion and extract its juice. Apply the juice to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo. Repeat this once a week for better results.

Step 3: Apply Aloe vera

Aloe vera is a natural remedy for hair fall. It contains enzymes that promote hair growth and reduce scalp inflammation. Apply fresh aloe vera gel to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes. Rinse your hair with lukewarm water. Repeat this twice a week for best results.

Step 4: Use egg white

Egg whites are rich in proteins that help to strengthen hair follicles and prevent hair fall. Beat 1-2 egg whites and apply them to your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes and rinse with a mild shampoo. Repeat this once a week for better results.

Step 5: Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet can do wonders for your hair. Include foods rich in protein, iron, and vitamins in your diet. Eat foods like spinach, eggs, nuts, and fruits to promote hair growth and reduce hair fall.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips that can help prevent hair fall:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Do not comb wet hair
  • Avoid using hair styling products
  • Do not tie your hair too tightly
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos

FAQ

Q. How much hair fall is normal?

It is normal to lose 50-100 strands of hair per day. Anything more than that can be a cause for concern.

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress is a common cause of hair fall. Try to reduce stress levels by practicing yoga, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.

Q. Is hair fall permanent?

No, hair fall is not permanent. With proper care and treatment, you can prevent hair fall and promote hair growth.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to prevent hair fall. Follow these tips to maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun

In conclusion, hair fall is a common problem faced by many people. But with the right care and treatment, you can prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. Follow the tips mentioned in this blog post to stop hair fall immediately at home in Hindi.
