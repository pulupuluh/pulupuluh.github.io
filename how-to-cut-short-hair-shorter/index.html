How To Cut Short Hair Shorter A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to freshen up your short hairstyle? Cutting your hair shorter can be a great way to switch up your look without making a drastic change. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to cut short hair shorter in a relaxed, easy-to-understand language. We'll cover the tools you need, the steps to take, and some tips for achieving the perfect cut. Let's get started!

Tools You'll Need

Before you get started, make sure you have the right tools on hand. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see your hair's natural texture and make it easier to cut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use your sectioning clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting with the back section, use your scissors to cut off any excess length. Work your way up to the top section, cutting small sections at a time.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Once you've cut the top section, move on to the sides. Use your clippers (if you have them) to blend the sides with the back. If you don't have clippers, use your scissors to cut the sides to the desired length.

Step 5: Check Your Work

When you're finished cutting, use your mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your hair is even and the desired length.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair Shorter

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short haircut:

  • Always start with small sections and work your way up to larger ones.
  • Use your fingers to hold your hair in place while cutting. This will help you control the length and shape.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you're not confident in your cutting skills, ask a friend or family member to assist you.
  • Invest in quality hair cutting scissors. They'll make a big difference in the final result.

FAQs

Can I cut my own short hair?

Yes, you can cut your own short hair. Just make sure you have the right tools and take your time.

Should I cut my hair when it's wet or dry?

You should always cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can be misleading and make it harder to achieve the desired length.

How often should I cut my short hair?

Short hair grows faster than longer hair, so you'll need to cut it more frequently. Aim to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length.

Hair Care After Cutting

Now that you've cut your hair shorter, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Conclusion

Cutting short hair shorter can be a great way to switch up your look and freshen up your style. By following this step-by-step guide and using the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect short haircut. Remember to take your time, start with small sections, and check your work as you go. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at cutting your own hair in no time!
