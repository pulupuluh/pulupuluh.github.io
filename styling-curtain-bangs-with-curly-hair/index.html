Styling Curtain Bangs With Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jun 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair and are thinking about getting curtain bangs, you may be wondering how to style them. Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of glam to any look. But, styling them with curly hair can be tricky. In this article, we will give you tips and tricks on how to style curtain bangs with curly hair to make your hair look amazing.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Style Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair

The first step in styling curtain bangs with curly hair is to find the right cut. You want to make sure that your bangs are long enough to be parted down the middle and styled to the side. Once you have the right cut, you can start styling your curtain bangs.

One of the best ways to style curly curtain bangs is to blow dry them with a diffuser. This will help to create a more defined curl pattern and give your bangs more volume. You can also use a curling iron or wand to curl your bangs and create a more polished look.

Another great way to style your curtain bangs is to use a hair straightener. This will help to smooth out any frizz and give your bangs a sleek look. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent any damage to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style curtain bangs with curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Blot your hair dry with a microfiber towel, being careful not to rub your hair too vigorously.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls.
  4. Blow dry your hair with a diffuser attachment, focusing on your bangs to create more volume.
  5. Use a curling iron or wand to curl your bangs, if desired.
  6. Use a hair straightener to smooth out any frizz and give your bangs a sleek look.
  7. Finish with a hair spray or other hair styling product to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for styling curtain bangs with curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create more volume in your curls.
  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener to smooth out any frizz.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Try using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling curtain bangs with curly hair:

Can I style my curtain bangs without using heat?

Yes, you can style your curtain bangs without using heat. You can try using a hair mousse or gel to help define your curls and give your bangs more volume.

Should I cut my bangs myself or go to a salon?

We recommend going to a salon to get your curtain bangs cut. A professional stylist will be able to give you the best cut for your face shape and hair type.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair looking its best, it is important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes using products designed for curly hair, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends. Additionally, you should avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage and damage to your curls.

Styling curtain bangs with curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and tools, you can create a look that is both stylish and easy to maintain. Follow our tips and tricks, and you'll have gorgeous, curly curtain bangs in no time.
