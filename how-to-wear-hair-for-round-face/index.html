How To Wear Hair For Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Apr 03, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect hairstyle that flatters your round face? Look no further, because we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll share some of our top tips and tricks on how to wear hair for round face shapes. From haircuts to styling techniques, we’ve got everything you need to know to enhance your natural beauty. So, let’s get started!

Description

Round faces have a softness and roundness to them that can be accentuated or minimized with the right hairstyle. Choosing the right hairstyle can make a huge difference in how you look and feel. A flattering hairstyle can help elongate your face, create the illusion of angles, and draw attention to your best features.

How to Wear Hair for Round Face: Step by Step

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start experimenting with different hairstyles, it’s important to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take a look at the shape of your face. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a round face shape.

Step 2: Choose the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make all the difference in how your hair looks and how it flatters your face shape. Here are some of our top haircut recommendations for round faces:

  • Long layers
  • Angled bob
  • Side swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical cut
  • Long bob with face-framing layers

These haircuts help create the illusion of length and angles, which can balance out a round face shape.

Step 3: Experiment with Different Partings

The way you part your hair can also affect how it flatters your face shape. A middle part can make a round face look even rounder, while a side part can create the illusion of angles. Experiment with different partings to find what works best for you.

Step 4: Add Volume to the Crown

Adding volume to the crown of your head can help elongate your face and create the illusion of height. You can achieve this by blow-drying your hair upside down or using a volumizing spray or mousse.

Tips for Wearing Hair for Round Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt bangs
  • Opt for soft, textured layers
  • Try a side-swept fringe
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as a bob that ends at your jawline or a blunt cut that falls at your cheekbones
  • Experiment with updos and half-up styles to create the illusion of height

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that adds length and angles to balance out the roundness. Long layers, angled bobs, and face-framing layers are all great options.

Can I wear blunt bangs with a round face?

Blunt bangs can be tricky with a round face shape, as they can make your face look even rounder. If you want to try bangs, opt for a soft, side-swept fringe instead.

What styling products should I use to add volume to the crown?

You can add volume to the crown of your head by using a volumizing spray or mousse. Blow-drying your hair upside down can also help create volume.

Hair Care for Round Face

It’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote hair health

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle to flatter your round face. Remember to experiment with different cuts, styles, and partings to find what works best for you. With a little bit of effort and experimentation, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your own skin.
