Hairdos For Shoulder Length Straight Hair

Written by Luffy Apr 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In 2023, shoulder length hair is still a popular choice for women. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and looks great on almost everyone. If you have straight hair, there are plenty of hairdos you can try to switch things up. In this article, we'll go over some of the best hairdos for shoulder length straight hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length hair is hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. It's a popular choice for women because it's easy to style and maintain. Straight hair is hair that falls straight down without any waves or curls. There are many hairdos you can try if you have shoulder length straight hair, including braids, buns, and ponytails.

How to

If you want to try a new hairdo for your shoulder length straight hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, consider the occasion. Some hairdos are better suited for casual events, while others are more formal. Second, think about your face shape. Some hairdos look better on certain face shapes than others. Third, consider your hair type. Some hairdos work better on thick hair, while others work better on thin hair.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some popular hairdos for shoulder length straight hair:

Messy Bun

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face for a messy look.

Fishtail Braid

  1. Start by dividing your hair into two sections.
  2. Take a small piece of hair from the outside of one section and cross it over to the other section.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Continue crossing small pieces of hair over until you reach the end.
  5. Secure the braid with a hair tie.
  6. Pull out a few pieces of hair for a messy look.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling your shoulder length straight hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage.
  • Experiment with different hairdos to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to add some hair accessories, like a headband or hair clip.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairdos for shoulder length straight hair:

What hairdos are best for shoulder length straight hair?

Some popular hairdos for shoulder length straight hair include messy buns, fishtail braids, and low ponytails.

How can I add volume to my shoulder length straight hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow drying your hair upside down, and using a round brush when blow drying.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your shoulder length straight hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.

With these tips and hairdos, you can keep your shoulder length straight hair looking great all year round. Try out different styles and see what works best for you!
