Best Hair Style For Long Face Over 50

Written by Frank Jul 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair and facial features change, and we may find ourselves searching for the best hair style that suits our face shape and age. If you have a long face and are over 50, you may be wondering what the best hair style is for you. In this article, we will discuss the top hair styles that will flatter your long face and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Description

A long face is characterized by its length and narrowness. The best hair styles for a long face are those that add width and fullness to the face, while also balancing out the length. As we age, our hair may become thinner and lose its volume, so it's important to choose a hair style that adds volume and texture to the hair.

How To

The first step in choosing the best hair style for your long face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring your face from the hairline to the chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone. If these measurements are similar, you have a long face shape.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hair styles that will flatter your features. Here are some tips:

Step 1: Consider a Shorter Length

A shorter hair length can help balance out a long face by adding width and fullness. A shoulder-length bob or a pixie cut can be a great option, especially if you have thinning hair. Shorter hair styles also require less maintenance and can be easier to style.

Step 2: Add Layers and Texture

Adding layers and texture to your hair can also help balance out a long face. Layers can add volume and movement to the hair, giving the illusion of width. Texture can also help add fullness and body to the hair, making it look thicker.

Step 3: Consider Bangs

Adding bangs can also help balance out a long face. Side-swept bangs can create a diagonal line that breaks up the length of the face, while blunt bangs can add width and fullness to the forehead area.

Tips

When choosing a hair style, it's important to consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have thinning hair, a shorter hair style with layers and texture can help add fullness and body. If you have thick hair, a longer hair style with layers can help reduce bulk and add movement.

It's also important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair. Shorter hair styles require less maintenance, while longer hair styles may require more time and effort to style.

FAQ

Q: What is the best hair color for a long face over 50?

A: The best hair color for a long face over 50 is one that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural features. Cool tones like ash blonde, platinum, and silver can help brighten the complexion, while warm tones like golden blonde, caramel, and chestnut can add warmth and depth to the hair.

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have a long face, but it's important to add layers and texture to the hair to balance out the length. Side-swept bangs can also help break up the length of the face.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hair style. To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf, and use a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to keep your locks looking shiny and healthy.

By following these tips and choosing the best hair style for your long face over 50, you can look and feel your best at any age.
