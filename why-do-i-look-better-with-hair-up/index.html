Why Do I Look Better With Hair Up

Written by Frank Apr 06, 2023 · 7 min read

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Many women wonder why they look better with their hair up. There are several reasons why this is the case. For one, when your hair is up, it can elongate your neck and make you appear taller. Additionally, having your hair up can highlight your facial features, such as your cheekbones and jawline. Overall, putting your hair up can be a great way to enhance your appearance and look your best.

How To

If you're not used to wearing your hair up, it can be a bit daunting to figure out how to do it properly. Here are some tips:

Choose the Right Style

There are many different ways to wear your hair up, so it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture. Some popular options include ponytails, buns, and braids. Experiment with different styles to see what works best for you.

Prep Your Hair

Before putting your hair up, it's important to prep it properly. This can include brushing it out, applying a bit of styling product, and ensuring that it's free of tangles and knots.

Secure Your Style

Once you've chosen a style and prepped your hair, it's time to secure it in place. Use bobby pins, hair ties, or other hair accessories to keep your hair up and in place throughout the day.

Step By Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to putting your hair up:

  1. Brush your hair out with a wide-tooth comb or brush.
  2. Apply a bit of styling product, such as hairspray or mousse, to help hold your style in place.
  3. Gather your hair together at the back of your head or the nape of your neck.
  4. Secure your hair with a hair tie or elastic band.
  5. Twist your hair into a bun or other desired style.
  6. Secure your hair with bobby pins or other hair accessories.
  7. Spray your hair with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for wearing your hair up:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and barrettes, to add some extra flair to your style.
  • If you have thick or heavy hair, consider using a hair donut or other hair accessory to help create a more voluminous bun.
  • If you're wearing your hair up for a special occasion, consider getting it done professionally to ensure that it looks its best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing your hair up:

Will putting my hair up damage it?

As long as you're gentle when putting your hair up and don't pull too tightly, wearing your hair up shouldn't cause any damage.

Can I wear my hair up if it's short?

Yes! There are many ways to wear short hair up, such as with a half-up style or a braided updo.

How often should I wear my hair up?

It's generally fine to wear your hair up on a daily basis, as long as you're not pulling it too tightly or causing any damage.

Hair Care

When wearing your hair up, it's important to take care of it properly to avoid any damage or breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly or using hair accessories that are too tight.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as from blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment or hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Overall, wearing your hair up can be a great way to enhance your appearance and look your best. Whether you're going for a casual look or dressing up for a special occasion, there are many different ways to wear your hair up and show off your beautiful features.
