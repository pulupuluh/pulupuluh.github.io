Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long Back The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a stylish and edgy haircut, the mens haircut shaved sides long back might be just what you need. This haircut features shaved sides and a longer back, creating a unique and eye-catching look. In this article, we'll take a closer look at this popular hairstyle, including how to get it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

How to Get a Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long Back

If you're interested in getting a mens haircut shaved sides long back, the first step is to find a skilled barber who can help you achieve the look you want. Make sure to bring in a few pictures to show your barber exactly what you're looking for.

During your appointment, your barber will likely use clippers to shave the sides of your head, leaving the hair on top and in the back longer. This creates a contrast between the short sides and the longer hair on top and in the back.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a mens haircut shaved sides long back:

  1. Find a skilled barber
  2. Show them pictures of the haircut you want
  3. Sit back and let them do their magic
  4. Ask for any necessary touch-ups

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have your new haircut, you'll want to take good care of it to keep it looking sharp. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a soft brush or comb to style your hair
  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups to keep your haircut looking fresh
  • Consider using hair products like pomade or wax to style your hair and keep it in place

FAQ

Is the mens haircut shaved sides long back suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can work for a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How long does it take to get a mens haircut shaved sides long back?

The length of time it takes to get this haircut will depend on a variety of factors, including the skill of your barber and the thickness of your hair. However, most appointments take between 30 minutes to an hour.

Can I style my hair in different ways with this haircut?

Yes, you can style your hair in a variety of ways with this haircut. Some popular options include slicking it back, spiking it up, or leaving it messy and tousled.

Hair Care for Shaved Sides Long Back Haircut

When it comes to hair care for the mens haircut shaved sides long back, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. This means using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are designed to nourish your hair and prevent damage.

Additionally, you may want to consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration. This can help keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, even with a shaved sides long back haircut.

In conclusion, the mens haircut shaved sides long back is a stylish and bold choice for those looking to switch up their look. With the right care and maintenance, this haircut can look great for months to come.
