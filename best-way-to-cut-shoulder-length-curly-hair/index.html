The Best Way To Cut Shoulder Length Curly Hair In 2023

Written by Christine Jul 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your shoulder length curly hair getting out of control? Cutting your hair can seem like a daunting task, especially if you have curly hair. But with the right techniques and tools, you can easily cut your curly hair at home. In this article, we'll discuss the best way to cut shoulder length curly hair to ensure you achieve the perfect cut every time.

How to Cut Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Cutting curly hair can be challenging because it tends to shrink when dry. That's why it's essential to cut your hair when it's wet. Here's how to cut your shoulder length curly hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the ends

Starting with one section, use hair cutting scissors to cut the ends of your hair. Cut a small amount at a time, and avoid cutting too much. Work your way around your head, cutting the ends of each section.

Step 4: Cut the layers

Once you've cut the ends, it's time to cut the layers. This step is optional, but it can add volume and shape to your hair. Use the point cutting technique to cut the layers. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut into the hair at an angle. This technique will create soft, textured layers.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair, and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz.

Tips for Cutting Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair.
  • Start by cutting a small amount of hair. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's cut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's dry. Curly hair tends to shrink when dry, so cutting it wet will give you a more accurate length.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head. It can be challenging to see the back of your head, so use a handheld mirror to check your progress.

FAQ

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to cut your curly hair every 8-12 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

What's the best way to cut curly hair?

The best way to cut curly hair is to cut it when it's wet and use hair cutting scissors. It's also important to cut small amounts at a time and avoid cutting too much hair.

Can I cut my curly hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your curly hair at home. Just make sure you have the right tools and techniques to achieve the perfect cut.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair is essential to maintain its health and prevent damage. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best way to cut shoulder length curly hair, you can achieve the perfect cut at home. Remember to take your time and cut small amounts at a time to avoid any mistakes. With the right tools and techniques, you'll have beautiful, healthy curls in no time.
