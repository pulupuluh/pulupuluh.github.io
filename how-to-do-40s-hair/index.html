How To Do 40S Hair A Relaxed Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 09, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a fan of vintage style, you may be interested in learning how to do 40s hair. The 1940s were a time of classic glamour and sophistication, and the hairstyles of the era reflected that. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve a 40s-inspired look, from Victory rolls to pin curls. Let's get started!

How To:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling, it's important to prep your hair. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair with products that work well with your hair type. Then, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, working in sections to create volume and smoothness.

Step 2: Create Victory Rolls

One of the most iconic 40s hairstyles is the Victory roll. To create this look, take a small section of hair from the front of your head and roll it backwards, securing it with bobby pins. Repeat on the other side. You can also create larger Victory rolls by rolling sections of hair from the sides of your head and pinning them in place.

Step 3: Pin Curls

Another popular 40s hairstyle is pin curls. To create pin curls, take a section of hair and twist it tightly. Then, wrap the twisted section around your finger to create a loop, and pin it in place with a bobby pin. Repeat this process all over your head, creating as many pin curls as you like. Leave the curls in place until they've completely cooled, then remove the pins and brush out your hair to create soft waves.

Step 4: Finger Waves

Finger waves are a classic 40s hairstyle that can be achieved with a little bit of practice. To create finger waves, section off a small portion of hair and use a comb to create an S-shaped wave. Pin the wave in place with bobby pins, then move on to the next section of hair. Repeat this process all over your head, then leave the waves in place until they've completely cooled. Remove the pins and brush out your hair to create smooth, defined waves.

Tips:

  • Use a hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Practice makes perfect – don't worry if your first attempt isn't perfect.
  • Accessorize with vintage-inspired hair accessories like hair clips, headbands, or scarves.

FAQ:

What products should I use to create 40s hair?

Use a heat protectant spray, hairspray, and a styling mousse or gel to create volume and hold.

Can I create 40s hair with short hair?

Yes! Victory rolls and pin curls can be achieved on shorter hair by using smaller sections of hair.

How long does it take to create 40s hair?

The time it takes to create 40s hair depends on the style you choose and your level of experience. It could take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour.

Hair Care:

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to use products that are appropriate for your hair type. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a classic 40s hairstyle that looks great and makes you feel confident. So why not give it a try?
