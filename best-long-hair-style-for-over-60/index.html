Best Long Hair Style For Over 60

Written by Eveline Jun 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair is not just for the young, but can be a flattering and elegant look for women over 60. In this article, we will explore the best long hair style for women over 60, including how to achieve the look, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The best long hair style for women over 60 is a layered cut with soft waves or curls. This style is flattering for all face shapes and can be customized to suit individual preferences. The layers create movement and volume, while the waves or curls add texture and dimension to the hair.

How to

The best way to achieve the layered cut with soft waves or curls is to visit a professional hairstylist. They can assess your hair type, face shape, and preferences to create a customized style that works best for you. You can also bring in pictures of styles that you like to give the hairstylist an idea of what you are looking for.

Step by step

Once you have the layered cut with soft waves or curls, you can style your hair at home using the following steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add fullness to your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape. Use a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural curls or waves.
  4. Section your hair and use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves or curls. Alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long hair style:

  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, or scarves, to add interest to your style.

FAQ

1. Will long hair make me look older?

No, long hair can be a flattering and youthful look for women of any age. The key is to choose a style that works best for your face shape and hair type.

2. Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or bun?

Yes, long hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including ponytails, buns, braids, and updos. These styles can be customized to suit your preferences and occasion.

3. Is it necessary to use heat styling tools to create soft waves or curls?

No, you can achieve soft waves or curls without using heat styling tools by braiding your hair or using foam rollers overnight. This will create a natural-looking texture and volume to your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and beautiful long hair, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently remove tangles and knots from your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and using the best long hair style for women over 60, you can achieve a flattering and elegant look that suits your individual style and personality.
