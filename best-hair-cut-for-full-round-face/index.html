Best Hair Cut For Full Round Face In 2023 How To Tips And Care

Written by Oliver May 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a full round face, you might find it challenging to choose the perfect hair cut that flatters your features. Fortunately, there are plenty of haircuts that can complement your face shape, enhance your features, and make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we'll guide you through the best hair cut for full round face, with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Choose the Best Hair Cut for Full Round Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair Cut
  4. Tips to Maintain Your Hair Cut
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Full Round Face

Description

A full round face is characterized by a wide forehead, prominent cheeks, and a rounded chin. The best hair cut for full round face should balance out the width of your face, create the illusion of length, and draw attention away from the fullness of your cheeks. Some of the most flattering haircuts for full round face include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob cut
  • Pixie cut
  • Asymmetrical cut

How to Choose the Best Hair Cut for Full Round Face

When choosing the best hair cut for full round face, consider the following factors:

  • Length: Long hair can elongate your face and balance out the width, while short hair can make your face appear wider. Choose a length that flatters your face shape and features.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, creating the illusion of length and drawing attention away from your cheeks. Opt for long, face-framing layers that start below the chin.
  • Bangs: Side-swept bangs can soften your features and create a diagonal line that slims down your face. Avoid full, blunt bangs that can make your face appear wider.
  • Curls: Soft, loose curls can add volume and texture to your hair, creating the illusion of length and slenderness. Avoid tight curls that can accentuate the fullness of your cheeks.
  • Asymmetry: An asymmetrical cut, with longer hair on one side and shorter hair on the other, can create a diagonal line that slims down your face and adds interest to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair Cut

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, starting at the roots and pulling the brush down to the ends. For added volume, flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair in sections.
  4. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose curls or waves. For a more natural look, curl random sections of hair in different directions.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to your hair to add volume and texture.
  6. Run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a tousled, effortless look.
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips to Maintain Your Hair Cut

Here are some tips to maintain your hair cut and keep it looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to keep your look fresh and interesting.

FAQs

What is the best hair length for full round face?

The best hair length for full round face depends on your personal preferences and features. Long hair can elongate your face and balance out the width, while short hair can make your face appear wider. Choose a length that flatters your face shape and features.

What kind of bangs are best for full round face?

Side-swept bangs are best for full round face, as they can soften your features and create a diagonal line that slims down your face. Avoid full, blunt bangs that can make your face appear wider.

What is the best hair cut for full round face with curly hair?

The best hair cut for full round face with curly hair is a layered cut that adds volume and movement to your hair. Opt for long, face-framing layers that start below the chin and avoid tight curls that can accentuate the fullness of your cheeks.

Hair Care for Full Round Face

Here are some hair care tips for full round face:

  • Avoid using heavy, greasy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently brush your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the best hair cut for full round face that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect look for you, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
