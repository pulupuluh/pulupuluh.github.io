Cute Short Hair For Heart Shaped Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you're in luck! This face shape is known for its delicate features and defined jawline, which make it the perfect canvas for cute short haircuts. Whether you're looking to chop off your locks for a fresh new look or want to switch up your current short hairstyle, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll take you through everything you need to know about cute short hair for heart-shaped faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones with a narrower chin. The goal of a cute short hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the wider top half of the face with the narrower bottom half. This can be achieved with a variety of short haircuts, such as pixie cuts, bobs, and shags.

How to

When choosing a cute short haircut for a heart-shaped face, it's important to consider the length, texture, and layers. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Length

Short haircuts that fall between the chin and cheekbone are perfect for heart-shaped faces. This length helps to balance out the wider forehead and cheekbones with the narrower chin. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look unbalanced.

Texture

Textured short haircuts work well for heart-shaped faces, as they add volume and movement to the hair. This can be achieved with choppy layers, feathered ends, or a messy, tousled look. Avoid smooth, sleek styles, as they can make your face look even wider.

Layers

Layered short haircuts also work well for heart-shaped faces, as they add volume and dimension to the hair. Layers can be added throughout the hair, or just at the crown for added height. Avoid blunt, one-length cuts, as they can make your face look too round.

Step by step

Now that you know what to look for in a cute short haircut for a heart-shaped face, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Choose a haircut that falls between the chin and cheekbone.
  2. Ask your hairstylist for choppy layers or textured ends.
  3. Consider adding layers throughout the hair or just at the crown.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to angle the ends of your hair towards your chin.
  5. Style your hair with a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your cute short hair for a heart-shaped face:

  • Use a round brush to add volume to your hair at the crown.
  • Avoid straightening your hair, as this can make your face look wider.
  • Consider adding bangs to your haircut, as this can help balance out your forehead.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can heart-shaped faces pull off a pixie cut?

A: Yes! A pixie cut can work well for heart-shaped faces, as long as it's not too short. Opt for a longer, textured pixie with choppy layers to add volume and dimension to your hair.

Q: Should I avoid bobs if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Not necessarily. Bobs can work well for heart-shaped faces, as long as they're the right length and have the right amount of layers. Ask your hairstylist for a chin-length bob with choppy layers to balance out your face shape.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for keeping your cute short hair for heart-shaped face healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you're ready to rock a cute short haircut for your heart-shaped face. Remember to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style, and don't be afraid to experiment with different textures and layers. Happy styling!
