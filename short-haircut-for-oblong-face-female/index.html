Short Haircut For Oblong Face Female A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver May 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oblong face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. Your face shape is longer than it is wide, making it important to choose a haircut that adds width and volume to your face. One of the best options for oblong face females is a short haircut. In this article, we'll provide you with a complete guide on how to choose and maintain a short haircut for oblong face female.

Description

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow jawline and forehead. This face shape is often referred to as rectangular or elongated. The goal of a short haircut for oblong face female is to add width and volume to the face, making it appear more balanced and proportional.

How to Choose a Short Haircut for Oblong Face Female

When choosing a short haircut for oblong face female, there are a few key factors to consider:

1. Length

Short haircuts come in a variety of lengths, from pixie cuts to bobs. For oblong face females, it's best to choose a length that hits above or at the chin. This length will help to add width to the face, making it appear more balanced.

2. Layers

Layers can add volume and movement to a short haircut, which is important for oblong face females. Opt for a layered bob or pixie cut to add volume to the sides of the face.

3. Bangs

Bangs are a great way to add width to the forehead, which can help to balance out an oblong face. Consider a side-swept or wispy bang for a short haircut.

Step by Step Guide: How to Get a Short Haircut for Oblong Face Female

Step 1: Choose Your Hairstyle

Start by choosing a short hairstyle that works for your face shape. Consider the length, layers, and bangs, as well as your personal style and preferences.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

Before you get your haircut, schedule a consultation with your stylist. They can help you choose the best haircut for your face shape and provide you with styling tips and advice.

Step 3: Get Your Haircut

Once you've chosen your hairstyle and consulted with your stylist, it's time to get your haircut. Relax and let your stylist work their magic!

Tips for Maintaining a Short Haircut for Oblong Face Female

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

1. Trim Regularly

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape and style. Schedule a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

2. Use the Right Products

Use styling products that are designed for your hair type and texture. Talk to your stylist about the best products for your hair.

3. Experiment with Styling

Short haircuts offer a lot of styling options, from sleek and polished to messy and textured. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for oblong face female?

The best short haircut for oblong face female is one that adds width and volume to the face. Consider a layered bob or pixie cut with side-swept or wispy bangs.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

Short haircuts require regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and style.

Hair Care for Short Haircut for Oblong Face Female

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting a short haircut:

1. Wash and Condition Regularly

Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and clean.

2. Use a Heat Protectant

Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

Conclusion

A short haircut is a great option for oblong face females. By choosing the right length, layers, and bangs, you can add width and volume to your face, making it appear more balanced and proportional. Follow our tips for maintaining and caring for your short haircut, and you'll be sure to look and feel your best.
