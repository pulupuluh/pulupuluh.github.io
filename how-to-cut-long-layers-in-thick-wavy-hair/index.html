How To Cut Long Layers In Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the right haircut that complements your natural texture. One of the most popular haircuts for wavy hair is long layers, which add dimension and movement to your hair. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of cutting long layers in thick wavy hair, step by step.

Description

Long layers are a versatile haircut that works well with thick wavy hair. They help to remove some of the weight from your hair, making it more manageable and easier to style. Long layers can also enhance your natural waves, giving your hair more movement and dimension. This haircut is perfect for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair while keeping the length.

How to Cut Long Layers in Thick Wavy Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure that it is clean and dry. You will also need a pair of sharp scissors and a comb. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Divide your hair into sections

Start by dividing your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use a comb to create a central parting and then divide your hair from ear to ear.

Step 2: Cut the first layer

Take a small section of hair from the back of your head and comb it straight down. Hold the section between your index and middle fingers, and cut the ends with scissors. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can result in an uneven haircut.

Step 3: Continue cutting the layers

Work your way up the back of your head, cutting small sections of hair at a time. Make sure that each layer is slightly shorter than the previous one, to create a gradual tapering effect.

Step 4: Move to the front

Once you have finished cutting the back, move to the front sections. Use the same technique to cut the layers, working your way from the bottom to the top.

Step 5: Blend the layers

Once you have finished cutting all the layers, use a comb to blend them together. This will help to create a smooth and seamless transition between the layers.

Step 6: Check the length

After you have finished cutting your hair, check the length to make sure that it is even. If you notice any unevenness, use your scissors to trim the ends until they are even.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect long layers in thick wavy hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean and precise cut.
  • Start by cutting a small section of hair and gradually work your way up.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can result in an uneven haircut.
  • Blend the layers together to create a seamless transition.
  • Check the length to make sure that it is even.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for thick wavy hair?

Long layers are one of the best haircuts for thick wavy hair. They help to remove some of the weight from your hair, making it more manageable and easier to style. Long layers can also enhance your natural waves, giving your hair more movement and dimension. This haircut is perfect for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair while keeping the length.

How often should I cut my hair if I have thick wavy hair?

If you have thick wavy hair, you should aim to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks. Regular haircuts can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your thick wavy hair healthy and looking its best, it's essential to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips and getting regular haircuts, you can keep your thick wavy hair looking healthy and beautiful.
