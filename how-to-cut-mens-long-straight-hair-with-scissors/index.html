How To Cut Men s Long Straight Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Feb 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to cut your own or someone else's long straight hair, using scissors can be a great way to achieve a clean and precise cut. In this guide, we'll go over the steps and tips to help you achieve a great cut every time.

Description

Long straight hair can be challenging to cut, especially if you're not familiar with the process. Scissors are a great tool to use for this type of hair because they allow for precision and control. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut men's long straight hair with scissors in a way that is easy to follow and understand.

How to Cut Men's Long Straight Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Prepare the hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots, and section it into manageable parts.

Step 2: Decide on the length

Determine how much you want to cut off and use your fingers to measure the length. This will help you achieve a more even cut throughout the hair.

Step 3: Start cutting

Using your scissors, start cutting the hair at the desired length. Cut in small sections, about 1/4 inch at a time, to ensure a neat and even cut.

Step 4: Check for symmetry

After you've cut one section, check to see if it's symmetrical with the rest of the hair. Adjust your cutting as needed to ensure a balanced and even cut.

Step 5: Continue cutting

Repeat steps 3-4 until you've cut all sections of the hair.

Tips

- Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
- Cut hair in small sections to ensure precision.
- Check for symmetry throughout the cutting process.
- Take your time and be patient to achieve the desired result.

FAQ

Can I use regular household scissors to cut hair?

It's not recommended to use regular household scissors to cut hair because they can be dull and not give you a clean cut. Use hair cutting scissors for the best results.

How often should I cut my long straight hair?

It's recommended to cut long straight hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your long straight hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Use conditioner to keep it soft and moisturized, and avoid using heat tools too frequently to prevent damage.

In conclusion, cutting men's long straight hair with scissors can be a great way to achieve a neat and precise cut. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a great cut every time, and remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
