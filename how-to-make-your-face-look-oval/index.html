How To Make Your Face Look Oval Tips Steps And Hair Care

Do you dream of having an oval face shape? This shape is considered the most ideal among all because it creates balance and symmetry in facial features. While you can't change your natural face shape, there are ways to create the illusion of an oval shape. In this blog post, we'll show you how to make your face look oval through makeup, hair styling, and other techniques.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Face Look Oval
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting an Oval Face Shape

How to Make Your Face Look Oval

There are different ways to make your face look oval, and you can choose one or a combination of them depending on your preference and situation. Here are some of the most effective techniques:

1. Contouring

Contouring is the art of creating shadows and highlights on your face to sculpt and define your features. By using a darker shade of foundation or powder on the sides of your face and a lighter shade on the center, you can create the illusion of an oval shape. Focus on the temples, cheekbones, and jawline to make them appear more angular and balanced.

2. Highlighting

Highlighting is the opposite of contouring, as it involves adding light to the high points of your face to bring them forward. By using a shimmery or dewy product on your cheekbones, bridge of the nose, and cupid's bow, you can draw attention to the center of your face and create the illusion of length.

3. Bangs

Bangs can be a game-changer for your face shape, as they can cover a high forehead or shorten a long face. For an oval face shape, go for side-swept bangs that frame your face and create softness. Avoid blunt or heavy bangs that can make your face look rounder.

4. Hairstyles

Hairstyles can also play a role in making your face look oval. Go for hairstyles that add volume on top and keep the sides sleek or tucked behind the ears. This will create the illusion of height and balance, which is what an oval face shape is all about. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides or cover your face, as they can make it look rounder.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your face look oval using makeup:

Step 1: Cleanse and Moisturize

Start with a clean and moisturized face to ensure that your makeup will apply smoothly and last longer. Use a gentle cleanser and a lightweight moisturizer that suits your skin type.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Apply a foundation that matches your skin tone all over your face, using a sponge or a brush. Make sure to blend it well and avoid harsh lines or patches.

Step 3: Contour

Take a darker shade of foundation or powder and apply it on the sides of your face, starting from the temples and going down to the jawline. Blend it well using a brush or a sponge, making sure to create a smooth gradient.

Step 4: Highlight

Take a shimmery or dewy product and apply it on the high points of your face, such as the cheekbones, bridge of the nose, and cupid's bow. Blend it well using a brush or a sponge, making sure to create a natural glow.

Step 5: Set with Powder

Take a translucent powder and apply it all over your face, using a brush or a puff. This will set your makeup and prevent it from creasing or fading throughout the day.

Tips for Getting an Oval Face Shape

Here are some additional tips for getting an oval face shape:

  • Wear earrings that create length, such as drop earrings or hoops
  • Use sunglasses that have a rectangular or angular shape
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides or cover your face
  • Use a lip color that is darker on the corners and lighter in the center
  • Keep your eyebrows groomed and arched to create a lift

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I really make my face look oval?

A: While you can't change your natural face shape, you can create the illusion of an oval shape through makeup, hair styling, and other techniques.

Q: What face shape is considered the most ideal?

A: An oval face shape is considered the most ideal among all because it creates balance and symmetry in facial features.

Q: Do I need to contour and highlight every day?

A: It depends on your preference and situation. Contouring and highlighting can take some time and effort, so you can choose to do it on special occasions or when you want to enhance your features.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

Here are some hair care tips for oval face shape:

  • Keep your hair healthy and shiny by using a nourishing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides or cover your face
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, such as a curling iron or a straightener
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and control frizz

With these tips and techniques, you can make your face look oval and enjoy a more balanced and symmetrical appearance. Remember to embrace your natural features and have fun experimenting with different looks and styles.
