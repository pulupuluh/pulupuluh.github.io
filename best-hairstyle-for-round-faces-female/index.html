Best Hairstyles For Round Faces Female

Written by Robby Aug 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't suit your round face? Don't worry! We have got you covered. In this blog post, we will discuss the best hairstyles for round faces female. These hairstyles will not only flatter your face shape but also enhance your overall look. Let's dive in!

Description

Round faces have a wider forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The aim is to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and creates an illusion of an oval face. The right hairstyle can make all the difference and accentuate your features. It's important to find a style that suits your personality and lifestyle.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

Before we jump into the best hairstyles, let's discuss how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

It's important to determine your face shape before choosing a hairstyle. Stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner. You can also take a picture and trace it on paper. This will help you determine your face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Once you know your face shape, research hairstyles that flatter your face. Look for celebrities or models who have a similar face shape and see what hairstyles they sport. You can also consult with a hairstylist.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle before choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy schedule, you need a hairstyle that's easy to maintain. If you love experimenting, you can go for a bold hairstyle.

Best Hairstyles for Round Faces Female

Here are the best hairstyles for round faces female:

1. Long Layers

Long layers create an illusion of length and are perfect for round faces. Layers add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller. You can also experiment with side-swept bangs.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. A chin-length bob with layers can make your face look slimmer. You can also go for an asymmetrical bob or a lob (long bob).

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that can make you stand out. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that's perfect for busy women. You can also add layers or bangs to your pixie cut.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for round faces. They create an illusion of length and add volume to your hair. You can also go for wispy bangs or curtain bangs.

5. High Ponytail

A high ponytail can elongate your face and make it look slimmer. You can also add volume to your ponytail by teasing your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Avoid Blunt Cuts

Avoid blunt cuts as they can make your face look rounder. Instead, go for layered cuts.

2. Add Volume

Add volume to your hair to create an illusion of length. You can use a volumizing spray or mousse.

3. Avoid Center Parting

Avoid center parting as it can make your face look rounder. Instead, go for side parting.

FAQ

1. Can I go for a short hairstyle if I have a round face?

Yes, you can go for a short hairstyle if you have a round face. Just make sure it's a layered cut and not a blunt cut.

2. Do bangs suit round faces?

Yes, bangs suit round faces. Side-swept bangs, wispy bangs, and curtain bangs are perfect for round faces.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

1. Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Don't shampoo your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Hair Oil

Use a hair oil to nourish your hair and scalp. Massage your scalp with oil once a week.

3. Hair Masks

Use a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair. You can make a DIY hair mask with ingredients like honey, yogurt, and avocado.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for round faces female, it's time to try them out. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and look your best.
