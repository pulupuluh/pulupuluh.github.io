What Is A Short Back And Sides Haircut

Written by Frank Apr 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our blog post about the popular hairstyle known as the short back and sides. This classic haircut has been around for decades and it's still a popular choice for men of all ages. In this article, we'll explain what a short back and sides haircut is, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The short back and sides is a men's haircut that features shorter hair on the sides and back of the head, while the hair on the top of the head is left longer. The length of the hair on the sides and back can vary depending on your preference, but it's typically around 1/2 inch to 2 inches long. The length of the hair on the top can also vary, but it's usually 2 to 4 inches long.

This hairstyle is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. It's a great choice for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that looks stylish and professional.

How to Get a Short Back and Sides Haircut

If you're interested in getting a short back and sides haircut, the first step is to find a barber or hair stylist who is experienced in this style. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members, or you can search for a barber online.

Once you've found a barber, you should bring a picture of the style you want or be prepared to describe it in detail. The barber will then use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length. They may also use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head.

It's important to communicate with your barber throughout the process to make sure you're happy with the length and style of your haircut.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short back and sides haircut:

  1. Find a barber or hair stylist who is experienced in this style.
  2. Bring a picture of the style you want or be prepared to describe it in detail.
  3. Sit in the barber's chair and communicate your preferences.
  4. The barber will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length.
  5. They may also use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head.
  6. Communicate with your barber throughout the process to ensure you're happy with the length and style of your haircut.
  7. Finish with hair styling product if desired.

Tips

Here are some tips for taking care of your short back and sides haircut:

  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the length of your hair.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or gel to keep it in place throughout the day.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short back and sides haircut:

How long does a short back and sides haircut last?

A short back and sides haircut can last up to 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Can I style my short back and sides haircut in different ways?

Yes, a short back and sides haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways depending on your preference.

Is a short back and sides haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a short back and sides haircut can be customized to suit any face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for maintaining a healthy and stylish short back and sides haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Overall, the short back and sides haircut is a classic and versatile style that looks great on men of all ages. Follow these tips and you'll be sure to maintain a stylish and healthy haircut.
