How To Grow Out Hair From Pixie Cut Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 10, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’ve recently cut your hair into a pixie cut and have realized that you miss your long locks, you’re not alone. Many people go through the same process of growing out their hair from a short cut. While it may seem daunting, with the right tips and tricks, you can successfully grow out your hair from a pixie cut. In this article, we’ll be discussing the step-by-step process, tips, and hair care practices to help you achieve your desired hair length.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing Out Hair from a Pixie Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Practices for Growing Out Hair from a Pixie Cut

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Be patient. Growing out your hair will take time and it’s important to be patient throughout the process.

Step 2: Get regular trims. While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims will help your hair grow faster and prevent split ends.

Step 3: Experiment with different hairstyles. As your hair grows, experiment with different hairstyles to keep your look fresh and new.

Step 4: Use hair growth products. There are many hair growth products on the market that can help stimulate hair growth and promote healthy hair.

Step 5: Consider hair extensions. If you’re struggling with growing out your hair, consider getting hair extensions to add length and volume.

Tips for Growing Out Hair from a Pixie Cut

Tip 1: Embrace the awkward phase. Growing out your hair can be awkward, but embrace it and try new hairstyles to make the most of it.

Tip 2: Use heat protectant. When styling your hair with heat tools, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

Tip 3: Use a wide-tooth comb. When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage.

Tip 4: Avoid tight hairstyles. Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension on the hair and potentially cause breakage.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to grow out hair from a pixie cut?

A: It can take up to a year or more to fully grow out hair from a pixie cut.

Q: Can hair growth products really help stimulate hair growth?

A: Yes, hair growth products can help stimulate hair growth and promote healthy hair.

Q: Is it okay to use hair extensions while growing out hair from a pixie cut?

A: Yes, hair extensions can help add length and volume to your hair while you’re growing it out.

Hair Care Practices for Growing Out Hair from a Pixie Cut

Practice 1: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Practice 2: Avoid using hot tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.

Practice 3: Use a hair mask once a month to nourish your hair and promote healthy growth.

Practice 4: Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood flow and stimulate hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to successfully grow out your hair from a pixie cut. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair along the way.
