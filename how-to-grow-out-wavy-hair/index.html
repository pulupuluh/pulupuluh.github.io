How To Grow Out Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Apr 29, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Welcome to our comprehensive guide on how to grow out wavy hair! Whether you're tired of your current hairstyle or simply want to change things up, growing out your hair can be a long and sometimes frustrating process. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll provide you with all the tips and tricks you need to grow out your wavy hair and achieve the luscious locks you've always dreamed of.

How to grow out wavy hair

Growing out wavy hair can be a bit of a challenge, especially if you're used to having shorter hair. Here are some tips to help you along the way:

1. Be patient

The first thing you need to know about growing out wavy hair is that it takes time. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month, so it could take several months or even years to reach your desired length. The key is to be patient and not give up.

2. Get regular trims

While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow faster. Trimming the ends of your hair every six to eight weeks can prevent split ends from forming, which can cause your hair to break and become shorter.

3. Use the right products

Using the right products can make a big difference when it comes to growing out your wavy hair. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for your hair type and use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

4. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can be extremely damaging to your hair, so it's best to avoid it as much as possible. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting as low as possible.

5. Experiment with different styles

While you're growing out your wavy hair, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. Braids, buns, and updos can all help you get through the awkward in-between stages and keep your hair looking stylish.

Step by step guide to growing out wavy hair

If you're ready to take the plunge and grow out your wavy hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you along the way:

Step 1: Decide on your desired length

The first step in growing out your wavy hair is to decide on your desired length. This will help you determine how long it will take to reach your goal and keep you motivated along the way.

Step 2: Get a trim

Before you start growing out your hair, it's a good idea to get a trim. This will help prevent split ends from forming and make your hair look healthier overall.

Step 3: Start taking care of your hair

Now that you're growing out your hair, it's important to take good care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type and use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.

Step 4: Avoid heat styling

As we mentioned earlier, heat styling can be very damaging to your hair. Try to avoid heat styling as much as possible and opt for air-drying instead.

Step 5: Experiment with different styles

As your hair grows out, try different styles to keep things interesting. Braids, buns, and updos are all great options that can help you get through the awkward in-between stages.

Tips for growing out wavy hair

Here are some additional tips to help you grow out your wavy hair:

1. Be gentle when brushing

Wavy hair can be prone to tangles, so it's important to be gentle when brushing. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for wavy hair to avoid damaging your hair.

2. Consider taking supplements

There are certain supplements, such as biotin and folic acid, that can help promote hair growth. Talk to your doctor to see if these supplements are right for you.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, such as ponytails and braids, can put pressure on your hair and cause it to break. Try to avoid these styles as much as possible.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out wavy hair:

How long does it take to grow out wavy hair?

On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. So, depending on your desired length, it could take several months or even years to grow out your wavy hair.

Can you speed up hair growth?

While there's no magic pill to make your hair grow faster, there are certain things you can do to help promote hair growth. Eating a healthy diet, taking supplements, and avoiding heat styling can all help your hair grow faster.

What are some good hairstyles for growing out wavy hair?

Braids, buns, and updos are all great options for growing out wavy hair. These styles can help you get through the awkward in-between stages and keep your hair looking stylish.

Hair care for growing out wavy hair

When it comes to growing out wavy hair, taking good care of your hair is key. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can be very harsh on your hair, so it's best to use a sulfate-free shampoo. This will help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your hair hydrated and prevent damage.

3. Avoid washing your hair every day

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils, which can cause it to become dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days instead.

And that's it! With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to growing out your wavy hair and achieving the luscious locks you've always dreamed of.
