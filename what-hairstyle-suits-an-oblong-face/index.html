What Hairstyle Suits An Oblong Face

Written by Christine Feb 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Read on to learn more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a straight hairline and defined jawline. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face is to create the illusion of a wider face and soften the angles of the jawline. The right hairstyle can make your face look more balanced and proportionate.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face, consider the following:

Length

Long hair can elongate an oblong face even further, so it's best to opt for a shorter style. A chin-length bob or a layered shoulder-length cut can add volume to the sides of your face and create the illusion of a wider face.

Bangs

Bangs can work wonders for an oblong face. Side-swept bangs can soften the angles of the jawline, while blunt bangs can make your face appear wider. Avoid straight-across bangs, as they can make your face look longer.

Layers

Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, creating the illusion of a wider face. However, avoid adding layers at the top of your head, as this can make your face look even longer.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right hairstyle for your oblong face:

  1. Measure the length and width of your face to determine your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that flatter an oblong face.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which hairstyle suits your face shape and hair type.
  4. Consider adding bangs or layers to create the illusion of a wider face.
  5. Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the crown of your head, as this can make your face look longer.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the crown of your head, as this can make your face look longer. Also, avoid long, straight hair, as this can elongate your face even further.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have an oblong face?

Absolutely! A low ponytail or a messy bun can be a great option for an oblong face. Just make sure to add some volume to the sides of your face to create the illusion of a wider face.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face. Remember to be confident and have fun with your hair!
