Which Hairstyle Suits My Face Male

Written by Oliver Jul 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you confused about which hairstyle suits your face male? Don't worry! We've got you covered. In this article, we will guide you on choosing the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your overall look.

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially for men. There are several factors to consider, such as face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Your hairstyle can make or break your overall look, so it's essential to choose one that suits your face shape.

There are several face shapes, including oval, square, round, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. Each of these face shapes has specific hairstyles that complement them. For example, if you have an oval face shape, you can opt for a short or long hairstyle, as this face shape can carry off most haircuts.

How To Choose The Right Hairstyle

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have your measurements, you can determine your face shape using the following guidelines:

  • Oval: The length of your face is about one and a half times the width
  • Square: The width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost the same
  • Round: The width of your face is almost the same as the length
  • Heart-shaped: The width of your forehead is wider than your jawline
  • Diamond-shaped: The cheekbones are the widest part of your face

Step By Step Guide

Once you have determined your face shape, follow these steps to choose the right hairstyle:

  1. Research hairstyles that complement your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair type and lifestyle before choosing a hairstyle.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for you.
  4. Ask for recommendations on hair products to maintain your hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your problem areas, such as a receding hairline or a double chin.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider your personality when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and suits your lifestyle.

FAQ

Q. Can I change my hairstyle if I don't like it?

A. Yes, you can always change your hairstyle if you're not happy with it. However, it's best to consult with a hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair.

Q. Do I need to use hair products to maintain my hairstyle?

A. Yes, hair products such as hair gel, wax, and hairspray can help maintain your hairstyle and keep it in place throughout the day.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking great. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know which hairstyle suits your face male, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get a new look. Remember to take your time and choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your overall look.
