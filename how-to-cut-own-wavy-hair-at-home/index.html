How To Cut Own Wavy Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have wavy hair. But with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting your own wavy hair at home, from start to finish.

  • How to Cut Own Wavy Hair at Home

Description

Wavy hair is known for its unpredictable nature, making it difficult to cut at times. However, with the right tools, technique, and a little bit of patience, you can cut your own wavy hair at home. This can save you time and money, while also giving you the freedom to experiment with different haircuts and styles.

How to Cut Own Wavy Hair at Home

Before you start cutting your own wavy hair at home, it's important to gather the right tools. You'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Mirror

Once you have your tools, you can start the process of cutting your own wavy hair at home.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel dry it until it's damp. This will make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Using your comb, work through your hair to remove any tangles. Make sure your hair is smooth and free of knots before you start cutting.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into several sections and clip them up. This will make it easier to cut each section individually.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Take one section of hair at a time and start cutting. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut off the desired length. Be sure to cut straight across, and not at an angle.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you've cut each section, release it from the clip and check your progress in the mirror. You can always go back and trim more if needed.

Step 6: Blend Your Layers

If you have layers in your hair, blend them by holding a section of hair between your fingers and cutting it at an angle. This will create a seamless, natural-looking blend.

Step 7: Finish Up

When you're finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can blow dry it or let it air dry.

Tips for Cutting Own Wavy Hair at Home

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to bigger cuts. You can always trim more, but you can't undo a bad haircut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it can be difficult to see how much you're cutting.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so don't push yourself too hard.

FAQs

Can you cut wavy hair when it's dry?

Yes, you can cut wavy hair when it's dry, but make sure it's well-combed and free of tangles. Dry hair can also be more prone to split ends, so be careful not to over-cut.

How often should I cut my wavy hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care after Cutting Own Wavy Hair at Home

After cutting your own wavy hair at home, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and shape. This includes:

  • Regularly washing and conditioning your hair
  • Using a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Using a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools
  • Trimming split ends regularly to prevent further damage

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your own wavy hair at home and achieve a beautiful, salon-worthy haircut.
