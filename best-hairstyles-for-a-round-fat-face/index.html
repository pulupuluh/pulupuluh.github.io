Best Hairstyles For A Round Fat Face In 2023

Written by Eveline Jun 20, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round, fat face? Don’t worry, you’re not alone. It can be tough to find a hairstyle that complements your features and flatters your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round, fat face can be a challenge when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. The key is to find a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer, more angular shape. This can be achieved through a variety of different hairstyles, including long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for a round, fat face, it’s important to consider your hair type and texture as well as your personal style. A consultation with a hairstylist can help you determine which styles will work best for you. Here are some general guidelines to keep in mind:

1. Length

Longer hairstyles tend to be more flattering for round faces, as they help to create the illusion of length. Try a long layered cut, or add some face-framing layers to a longer cut to elongate your face.

2. Bangs

Side-swept bangs can be a great option for a round face, as they help to create a diagonal line across the face, which can help to create the illusion of length. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the face look rounder.

3. Layers

Layers can be a great way to add dimension and movement to your hair. Opt for longer layers that start at the chin or below to create a more elongated look. Avoid short, choppy layers that can add volume to the sides of the face.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating some of the best hairstyles for a round, fat face:

1. Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off your hair into three parts: the top section, the middle section, and the bottom section.
  3. Starting with the bottom section, take a small section of hair and hold it straight up.
  4. Cut the ends of the hair in a slight V-shape.
  5. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way up to the middle section.
  6. Cut the middle section in the same way, working your way up to the top section.
  7. Cut the top section in the same way, blending the layers into the rest of the hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair at the front of your head, from your eyebrows to your temples.
  3. Comb the section of hair straight down.
  4. Hold the hair at a slight angle and cut the ends in a diagonal line.
  5. Continue cutting small sections of hair, making sure to maintain the diagonal line.
  6. Once you’ve finished cutting, comb the bangs to the side and style as desired.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hairstyle for a round, fat face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, as this can make the face look rounder.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.
  • If you have a double chin, avoid hairstyles that are too short or too tight around the neck.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for a round, fat face?

The best hairstyles for a round, fat face are those that elongate the face and create the illusion of a slimmer, more angular shape. This can be achieved through a variety of different hairstyles, including long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a round, fat face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, as this can make the face look rounder. Also, avoid blunt bangs and short, choppy layers.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can flatter your round, fat face and feel confident and beautiful.
