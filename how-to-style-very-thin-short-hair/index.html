How To Style Very Thin Short Hair

Written by Christine Apr 17, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have very thin short hair, you might feel like you’re limited in your styling options. But fear not! With the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of stylish looks that will flatter your hair type. In this article, we’ll go over how to style very thin short hair, from how to prep your hair to the best styling products to use.

Description

Very thin short hair can be challenging to style, as it lacks volume and texture. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create volume and texture that will make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. The key is to use the right products and styling tools, and to avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

How to Style Very Thin Short Hair

Here are some tips for styling very thin short hair:

1. Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray or mousse to add even more volume.

2. Use the Right Styling Tools

When styling very thin short hair, it’s important to use the right tools. Use a small round brush to add volume and curl to your hair. You can also use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and volume to your hair.

3. Add Volume with Styling Products

Use volumizing products to add volume and texture to your hair. You can use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to add even more texture.

4. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy products such as pomades or waxes, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Instead, use lightweight products such as mousses or sprays.

5. Experiment with Different Styles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles! Try a messy bob or a layered pixie cut. You can also try adding bangs or highlights to your hair to create a new look.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling very thin short hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a small round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume. Use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 3: Add Texture

Use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and volume to your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to add even more texture.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers or a comb, style your hair into the desired shape. You can add a side part or bangs to create a new look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling very thin short hair:

  • Use a volumizing powder to add extra volume to your roots.
  • Don’t be afraid to use hair accessories such as headbands or clips to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Avoid using heavy products such as pomades or waxes, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

What are the best products for styling very thin short hair?

The best products for styling very thin short hair are volumizing sprays, mousses, and texturizing sprays. These products add volume and texture to your hair without weighing it down.

How often should I wash my very thin short hair?

It depends on your hair type, but generally you should wash your hair every two to three days. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it look even thinner.

How can I add volume to my very thin short hair?

You can add volume to your very thin short hair by using volumizing products, blow drying your hair with a small round brush, and using a curling iron or flat iron to add texture.

Hair Care

To keep your very thin short hair looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot styling tools too often. Try to let your hair air dry whenever possible, and use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your very thin short hair in a variety of ways that will flatter your hair type and make you feel confident and stylish!
