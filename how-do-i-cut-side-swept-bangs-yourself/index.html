How Do I Cut Side Swept Bangs Yourself

Written by Bardi Feb 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your boring hairstyle and want to add some flair to it? Cutting side swept bangs is a great way to switch up your look without making a drastic change. In this post, we will guide you on how to cut side swept bangs yourself in a relaxed English language. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Side Swept Bangs Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Side Swept Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Side swept bangs are a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can enhance your features and give your face a softer look. This hairstyle is suitable for all face shapes and hair types. Side swept bangs can be cut at any length, from short to long, and can be styled in different ways to complement your overall look.

How to Cut Side Swept Bangs Yourself

Cutting side swept bangs yourself can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and technique, it can be a breeze. Follow these simple steps to cut your own side swept bangs:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash and dry your hair. Make sure it is completely dry before cutting.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut for your bangs. Use a comb to create a triangle shape from the top of your head to the outer edges of your eyebrows.
  3. Clip the rest of your hair away from the section you want to cut.
  4. Hold the section of hair you want to cut between your index and middle fingers. Use the tip of the middle finger as a guide for how much to cut.
  5. Start cutting from the middle of your eyebrows and cut towards the outer edges, following the angle of the triangle shape. Be careful not to cut too much at once.
  6. Once you have cut one side, match the length of the other side by using the first side as a guide.
  7. Blend the bangs with the rest of your hair by cutting diagonally towards the outer edges of your eyebrows.
  8. Style your bangs by blow-drying them with a round brush or flat iron.

Tips for Cutting Side Swept Bangs

Here are some additional tips to make cutting side swept bangs a success:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Cut your bangs when your hair is dry to avoid cutting too much.
  • Start by cutting a smaller amount, then gradually cut more if needed.
  • Keep your fingers straight while cutting to avoid cutting at an angle.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or overwhelmed.

FAQs

1. Can I cut side swept bangs with curly hair?

Yes, you can cut side swept bangs with curly hair. However, it is recommended to cut your bangs when your hair is straight to avoid cutting too much.

2. How often should I trim my side swept bangs?

It is recommended to trim your side swept bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

3. Can I cut my bangs with kitchen scissors?

No, it is not recommended to cut your bangs with kitchen scissors. Use sharp hair scissors to avoid split ends and achieve a clean cut.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you know how to cut side swept bangs yourself, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Cutting side swept bangs yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look that suits your face shape and hair type. Remember to take your time and be patient. Happy cutting!
