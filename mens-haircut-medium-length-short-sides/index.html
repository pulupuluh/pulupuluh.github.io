Mens Haircut Medium Length Short Sides The Trendy Style For 2023

Written by Petter Jun 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, then the mens haircut medium length short sides is the perfect choice for you. This style features longer hair on the top with shorter sides, creating a sharp and sophisticated look. This article will provide you with all the information you need to know about this trendy hairstyle, including how to get it, style it, and care for it.

Description

The mens haircut medium length short sides is a versatile style that can be tailored to suit any face shape and hair type. This style typically features hair that is longer on the top, about 2-3 inches in length, and shorter on the sides, usually about 1 inch. The hair is usually tapered, creating a clean and sharp look. This style can be styled in many different ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Medium Length Short Sides

The mens haircut medium length short sides is a popular style that can be achieved by following these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

To achieve the mens haircut medium length short sides, you will need to choose the right haircut. We recommend going to a professional hairstylist to get the perfect cut, as they will be able to tailor the style to suit your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you get your haircut, make sure to wash and condition your hair. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to cut your hair and will also ensure that your hair looks its best.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have your haircut, you can begin styling your hair. We recommend using a high-quality styling product, such as pomade or wax, to achieve the desired look. Apply the product to your hair and style as desired.

Tips for Styling the Mens Haircut Medium Length Short Sides

Here are some tips for styling the mens haircut medium length short sides:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to achieve the desired look
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of your hair
  • Consider getting a fade or undercut to add more dimension to your style

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shape is best suited for the mens haircut medium length short sides?

The mens haircut medium length short sides can be tailored to suit any face shape, but it is especially flattering for those with oval or square-shaped faces.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, the mens haircut medium length short sides is a versatile style that can be styled in many different ways to suit different occasions. Whether you want a slicked-back look for a formal event or a messy, textured look for a casual day out, this style can be tailored to suit your needs.

How often should I visit my hairstylist to maintain this style?

We recommend visiting your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair. This will ensure that your hair looks its best at all times.

Hair Care Tips for the Mens Haircut Medium Length Short Sides

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, we recommend following these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can cause damage and breakage

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect mens haircut medium length short sides and keep your hair looking healthy and stylish. So why not try this trendy style for yourself and see how it can transform your look?
