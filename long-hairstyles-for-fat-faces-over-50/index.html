Long Hairstyles For Fat Faces Over 50

Written by Daniel Jun 17, 2023 · 8 min read

As we age, our faces tend to lose volume and become rounder. This can make it challenging to find hairstyles that flatter our features, especially if we have a few extra pounds. However, with the right cut and style, you can still look fabulous and confident. In this article, we’ll share some of the best long hairstyles for fat faces over 50, along with tips on how to care for your hair and how to style it to perfection.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products to Try

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a cut that will elongate your face and draw attention to your best features. Second, you want to avoid styles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make it appear even rounder.

For women over 50 with a fat face, long hairstyles are an excellent choice. They help to create the illusion of length and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your preferences. Here are some of our favorite long hairstyles for fat faces over 50:

1. Layered Cut

A layered cut is an excellent choice for women with a fat face as it helps to add movement and dimension to your hair. Layers can be cut at different lengths to frame your face and draw attention to your best features. You can wear your hair straight or add some loose waves for a soft and feminine look.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. They help to create a diagonal line that elongates your face and gives it a slimmer appearance. You can wear your bangs straight or add some texture for a more playful look.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a popular choice for women of all ages as they add a relaxed and carefree vibe to your look. To create this style, use a curling iron or wand to add some loose waves to your hair. You can wear your waves loose or add a half-up, half-down style for a more polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen your perfect long hairstyle, it’s essential to know how to style it to perfection. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed to nourish and hydrate your hair. If you have color-treated hair, choose products that are formulated to protect your color.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After you’ve washed and conditioned your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and protect it from heat damage.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment to help define your curls.

Step 4: Style

Once your hair is dry, style it according to your preference. You can wear it straight, add some loose waves or create a half-up, half-down style.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Use products that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have a fat face?

A: Yes, absolutely! Long hairstyles are an excellent choice for women with a fat face as they help to create the illusion of length and draw attention to your best features.

Q: What are some good hairstyles for women over 50 with a fat face?

A: Some of our favorite hairstyles for women over 50 with a fat face include layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and beachy waves.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair every day. However, if you have dry hair or color-treated hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care Products to Try

If you’re looking for some hair care products to help you maintain healthy and shiny hair, here are some of our top recommendations:

  • Shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Hair oil
  • Heat protectant spray

With the right cut, style, and care, you can look and feel confident at any age. Try out some of these long hairstyles for fat faces over 50 and discover the perfect look for you.
