How To Curl Natural Hair With Cantu Products

Written by Frank Jun 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cantu is a popular brand that offers a wide range of hair care products specifically designed for natural hair. Their products contain natural ingredients that help to moisturize and nourish your hair, making it easier to style and manage. If you're looking to curl your natural hair, Cantu products can help you achieve the perfect look. In this article, we'll show you how to curl natural hair with Cantu products.

Description

Curling natural hair can be a bit challenging, especially if you don't have the right products. Cantu offers a range of products that are perfect for natural hair, including their Coconut Curling Cream, Define & Shine Custard, and Moisturizing Curl Activator Cream. These products are designed to enhance your curls and provide your hair with the moisture it needs to stay healthy and strong.

How to Curl Natural Hair with Cantu Products

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in curling your natural hair with Cantu products is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo that is specifically designed for natural hair, such as the Cantu Sulfate-Free Cleansing Cream Shampoo.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help nourish your hair and make it easier to detangle. The Cantu Leave-In Conditioning Repair Cream is a great option for natural hair.

Step 3: Apply the Curling Cream

Next, apply the Cantu Coconut Curling Cream to your hair. This cream will help define your curls and provide your hair with the moisture it needs to stay healthy.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and give them a more defined look.

Step 5: Apply the Define & Shine Custard

Once your hair is dry, apply the Cantu Define & Shine Custard to your curls. This custard will help define your curls and give them a shiny, healthy look.

Step 6: Scrunch Your Hair

Use your hands to scrunch your hair and enhance your curls. This will help give your hair a more natural, voluminous look.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying any products
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls
  • Use a satin scarf or pillowcase to protect your curls while sleeping
  • Experiment with different Cantu products to find the ones that work best for your hair

FAQ

What is the best Cantu product for curling natural hair?

The Cantu Coconut Curling Cream is a great option for curling natural hair. It helps define your curls and provides your hair with the moisture it needs to stay healthy.

Can I use Cantu products on relaxed hair?

Yes, Cantu products can be used on relaxed hair. However, you may want to use products that are specifically designed for relaxed hair, such as the Cantu Shea Butter Leave-In Conditioning Repair Cream.

Hair Care

When curling your natural hair with Cantu products, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a leave-in conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. With the right products and care, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls that will make you feel confident and stylish.
