Who Doesn t Suit Blonde Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 08, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're considering going blonde, you're not alone. It's a popular hair color that's been around for decades. However, not everyone can pull off this look. In this article, we will be discussing who doesn't suit blonde hair and why.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Blonde hair can look stunning on some people, but not everyone is suited to this color. Some people may look washed out or simply not feel like themselves with blonde hair. It's important to know who doesn't suit blonde hair before making a drastic change to your appearance.

How to

So, how can you tell if you're one of the people who doesn't suit blonde hair? Here are a few things to consider:

  • Skin tone: If you have a cool skin tone, blonde hair can be incredibly flattering. However, if you have a warm skin tone, blonde hair may not look as good on you.
  • Natural hair color: If you have very dark hair, going blonde can be a difficult transition. It may take multiple sessions to achieve the desired shade, and it can be damaging to your hair.
  • Eye color: Blonde hair can bring out the best in blue or green eyes, but it may not have the same effect on brown or hazel eyes.

Step by step

Here are the steps to determine if you're someone who doesn't suit blonde hair:

  1. Figure out your skin tone. Are you cool or warm? If you're not sure, look at the veins in your wrist. If they look blue, you have cool undertones. If they look green, you have warm undertones.
  2. Consider your natural hair color. If you have very dark hair, going blonde may not be the best choice for you.
  3. Look at your eye color. Blonde hair can bring out the best in blue or green eyes, but it may not have the same effect on brown or hazel eyes.
  4. Consult with a hair stylist. They can give you an honest opinion on whether or not blonde hair will suit you.

Tips

If you're still unsure whether or not blonde hair is for you, here are a few tips:

  • Try a wig or temporary hair color to see if you like the look before committing to a permanent change.
  • Consider highlights or lowlights instead of going fully blonde.
  • Experiment with different shades of blonde to find the one that's most flattering for you.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about who doesn't suit blonde hair:

Q: Can warm skin tones ever pull off blonde hair?

A: Yes, but they need to choose the right shade of blonde. Honey or golden blondes can work well on warm skin tones.

Q: Can people with brown eyes ever look good with blonde hair?

A: Yes, but they need to choose the right shade of blonde. Darker shades of blonde, like caramel or honey, can look stunning on people with brown eyes.

Hair Care

If you do decide to go blonde, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a purple shampoo to keep your blonde looking fresh and bright.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

In conclusion, not everyone is suited to blonde hair. It's important to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color before making a drastic change to your appearance. If you're still unsure, consult with a hair stylist and consider trying a wig or temporary hair color before committing to a permanent change. And if you do decide to go blonde, make sure to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
