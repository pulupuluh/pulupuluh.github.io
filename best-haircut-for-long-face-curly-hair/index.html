Best Haircut For Long Face Curly Hair

Written by Christine Apr 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a long face with curly hair, you might find it challenging to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered with the best haircut options for long face curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also make it challenging to style and manage. When it comes to long face shapes, it's essential to find a haircut that can balance out your features and complement your curls.

How to

Before you head to the salon, it's essential to understand your face shape and hair type. A long face shape has more length than width and can benefit from haircuts that add width to the sides. When it comes to curly hair, it's best to opt for haircuts that enhance your natural texture and shape.

Step by Step

Here are the best haircut options for long face curly hair:

1. Layers

Layers can add volume and movement to your curly hair, making it look fuller and bouncier. Opt for long layers that start at your chin and gradually blend into the rest of your hair. This will help create a balanced look and add width to the sides of your face.

2. Long Bob

A long bob or lob is a versatile haircut that works well for long face shapes. It can be worn straight or curly, and the length falls just above your shoulders, creating a balanced look. If you have thick curly hair, a long bob can help lighten the weight and make it more manageable.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help soften your features and add width to the sides of your face. Opt for long, side-swept bangs that blend into the rest of your hair to create a seamless look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut for long face curly hair:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that can make your face appear longer.
  • Opt for haircuts that add volume and width to the sides of your face.
  • Embrace your natural curls and texture.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What other hairstyles work for long face curly hair?

Other hairstyles that work well for long face curly hair include a curly shag, a layered pixie, and a curly half-up, half-down style.

How often should I get a haircut for long face curly hair?

It's best to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and maintain its shape.

Hair Care

To keep your long face curly hair healthy and manageable, it's essential to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your curls when they're dry to prevent frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance your curls and keep them hydrated.
  • Air-dry your hair whenever possible to prevent heat damage. If you need to use a blow dryer, use a diffuser attachment to create defined curls.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock your long face curly hair with confidence. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and embrace your natural texture.
