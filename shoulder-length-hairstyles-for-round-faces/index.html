Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Petter Aug 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Round faces have charm and character, but they can also pose a challenge when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. Shoulder length hairstyles for round faces are an excellent choice because they create a slimming effect and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. In this article, we'll explore some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for round faces, how to style them, and tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length hairstyles for round faces are perfect because they create the illusion of a slimmer face. They draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, creating a focal point that takes attention away from the roundness of the face. These hairstyles are also versatile and can be styled in many ways, whether you prefer your hair straight, wavy, or curly.

How to

The first step in choosing a shoulder length hairstyle for a round face is to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of length and height. This can be achieved by adding layers to your hair and creating volume at the crown. Another important factor to consider is the texture of your hair. If you have thin hair, you may want to consider a shoulder length bob with layers to create volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a shoulder length shag with choppy layers to thin out your hair.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair

2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair

3. Section your hair into manageable sections

4. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the crown to create volume

5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair

6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles with blunt cuts or heavy bangs, as they can make your face look rounder.

- Experiment with different partings to find the one that flatters your face shape best.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension in your hair.

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create the illusion of thickness and volume.

FAQ

What shoulder length hairstyle is best for a round face?

The best shoulder length hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height and length, such as a shoulder length shag with choppy layers or a shoulder length bob with layers.

Can I wear bangs with a round face?

Yes, but it's important to choose the right style. Soft, side-swept bangs can help to create a slimming effect, while heavy bangs can make your face look rounder.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle, so it's important to take care of your hair. This includes using a good shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, and using a heat protectant when you do use them. You should also avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage. Finally, make sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

In conclusion, shoulder length hairstyles for round faces are an excellent choice because they create a slimming effect and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a flattering and versatile look that complements your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.
