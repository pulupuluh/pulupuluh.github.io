Hairstyles For Long Face Shape Black Female

Written by Bardi Jul 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a black female with a long face shape and wondering what hairstyle will suit you best? Look no further, as we've gathered some of the best hairstyles for long face shapes that will complement your features and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Having a long face shape can be challenging when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. But with the right cut and style, you can accentuate your best features and create a stunning look.

How To

The key to finding the right hairstyle for a long face shape is to create balance by adding width to the sides of your face. This can be achieved through layers, bangs, and volume. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can make your face appear even longer.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating some of the best hairstyles for long face shape black females:

1. Layered Bob

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight using a round brush.
  3. Cut your hair into a layered bob, with the layers starting at the chin and going down to the ends of your hair.
  4. Add some texture to your hair using a curling iron or flat iron.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight.
  3. Cut your hair into a long bob, with side-swept bangs.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out your hair.
  5. Finish with a shine serum.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a long face shape:

  • Choose a style that adds volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight.
  • Consider adding layers or bangs to create balance.
  • Use styling products that add texture and volume to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for long face shape black females:

What is the best hairstyle for a long face shape?

The best hairstyle for a long face shape is one that adds width to the sides of your face, such as a layered bob or side-swept bangs.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long face shape?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a long face shape, but it's best to add some texture and volume to create balance.

Should I avoid hairstyles that are too long if I have a long face shape?

Yes, it's best to avoid hairstyles that are too long if you have a long face shape, as they can make your face appear even longer.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

With these tips and hairstyles for long face shape black females, you can create a stunning look that complements your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
