How To Know What Hair Color Suits Me Best

If you're planning to change your hair color, there are a few things to consider before making a decision. You want to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and overall look. In this article, we will discuss how to know what hair color suits you best, step by step.

Description

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure which shade will suit you best. Your hair color can affect your overall look, as well as your confidence and self-esteem. The right hair color can make you look younger, healthier, and more vibrant.

How to Know What Hair Color Suits Me Best

Knowing what hair color suits you best is not as simple as choosing a color you like. Your hair color should complement your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Here are some tips to help you find the right hair color:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair color. If you have warm undertones, such as yellow or gold, you should choose warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey brown, or copper. If you have cool undertones, such as pink or blue, you should choose cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy.

Look at Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help you choose the right hair color. If you have blue or green eyes, you should choose hair colors with warm undertones to make your eyes pop. If you have brown or hazel eyes, you can choose either warm or cool hair colors.

Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have dark hair, it may be easier to stick with darker shades. If you have light hair, you may want to choose lighter shades. However, you can also choose a hair color that is completely different from your natural hair color.

Try on Wigs or Use Virtual Hair Color App

If you're still not sure which hair color suits you best, you can try on wigs or use a virtual hair color app. This will allow you to see what you would look like with different hair colors without committing to anything.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hair color:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones.

Step 2: Determine Your Eye Color

Look at your eyes in natural light. If you have blue or green eyes, you have cool tones. If you have brown or hazel eyes, you have warm tones.

Step 3: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

If you have dark hair, stick with darker shades. If you have light hair, choose lighter shades. If you have grey hair, choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

Step 4: Choose a Hair Color

Based on your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, choose a hair color that complements your overall look.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle and personality when choosing a hair color.
  • Ask your hair stylist for advice on choosing the right hair color.
  • Choose a hair color that complements your wardrobe.
  • Choose a hair color that is appropriate for your age.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, but you should choose a hair dye that is specifically designed for sensitive skin.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I'm pregnant?

A: It is generally safe to dye your hair when pregnant, but you should consult with your doctor first.

Q: How often should I color my hair?

A: It depends on the type of hair color you use and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you should color your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After Coloring

After coloring your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know how to choose the right hair color, you can confidently make a decision that will enhance your overall look. Remember to consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color when choosing a hair color, and take good care of your hair after coloring.
