Is There A Way To Make Straight Hair Permanently Curly

Written by Luffy Mar 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to try something new? Have you ever wondered if there is a way to make your straight hair permanently curly? This is a question that many people ask, and the answer is yes, there is a way to make your hair permanently curly. In this article, we will discuss how to make straight hair permanently curly, the steps involved, tips, and hair care.

Description

Straight hair is beautiful, but sometimes it can be boring. If you want to switch things up and add some curls to your hair, there are several ways to do it. However, not all methods are permanent. Many people resort to using heat styling tools or chemical treatments to curl their hair, but these methods can be damaging to your hair and may not last very long.

How to Make Straight Hair Permanently Curly

If you want to make your straight hair permanently curly, there are two main methods you can use: a perm or a keratin treatment. A perm involves using chemicals to break down the hair's natural bonds and then reforming them into a curly pattern. A keratin treatment, on the other hand, involves using a protein to smooth out the hair and then curling it using heat. Both methods have their pros and cons, and it's essential to choose the one that's right for your hair type and desired outcome.

Perms

Perms have been around for decades and are a popular way to make straight hair curly permanently. The process involves using chemicals, such as ammonium thioglycolate or sodium hydroxide, to break down the hair's natural bonds. Once the bonds are broken, the hair is wrapped around rods, and a neutralizer is used to reform the bonds in a curly pattern. Perms can last for several months, but they require a bit of maintenance to keep the curls looking their best.

Keratin Treatments

Keratin treatments are a newer method for making straight hair permanently curly. They involve using a protein called keratin to smooth out the hair and then curling it using heat. The process is less damaging than a perm and can last for several months. Keratin treatments are also great for taming frizz and making hair more manageable. However, they are more expensive than a perm and may not work as well on extremely curly or thick hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you've decided to make your straight hair permanently curly, here is a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Choose Your Method

Decide whether you want to get a perm or a keratin treatment. Consider your hair type, budget, and desired outcome when making your decision.

Step 2: Find a Professional

Look for a professional hairstylist who specializes in the method you've chosen. Make sure they have experience and good reviews.

Step 3: Consultation

Schedule a consultation with your hairstylist to discuss your hair type, the process, and any concerns you may have. This is also a good time to ask about the cost and maintenance involved.

Step 4: Prep Your Hair

Before your appointment, make sure your hair is clean, dry, and free of any styling products.

Step 5: Get Your Perm or Keratin Treatment

During your appointment, your hairstylist will either apply the perm solution or keratin treatment to your hair, depending on the method you've chosen. They will then curl your hair using rods or heat.

Step 6: Maintenance

Follow your hairstylist's instructions for maintaining your new curls. This may include using special shampoos and conditioners, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular touch-ups.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make your straight hair permanently curly:

  • Choose a method that works best for your hair type and desired outcome.
  • Find a professional hairstylist with experience in the method you've chosen.
  • Invest in quality hair care products to keep your curls looking their best.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Get regular touch-ups to maintain your curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making straight hair permanently curly:

Q: How long does a perm or keratin treatment last?

A: Perms can last for several months, while keratin treatments can last up to six months.

Q: Will a perm or keratin treatment damage my hair?

A: Both methods can be damaging if not done correctly. It's essential to choose a professional hairstylist with experience in the method you've chosen.

Q: Can I still straighten my hair after getting a perm or keratin treatment?

A: Yes, but it's best to avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your curls.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain your new curls. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Invest in quality hair care products designed for curly hair.
  • Get regular touch-ups to maintain your curls.

In conclusion, making straight hair permanently curly is possible with a perm or keratin treatment. Both methods have their pros and cons, and it's essential to choose the one that's right for your hair type and desired outcome. Finding a professional hairstylist with experience in the method you've chosen is key to achieving the best results. Follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your new curls looking their best!
