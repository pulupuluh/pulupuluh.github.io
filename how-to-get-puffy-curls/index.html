How To Get Puffy Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 26, 2023 · 6 min read

If you want to add some volume and bounce to your hair, puffy curls are a great option. Achieving this hairstyle may seem daunting, but with the right techniques and tools, you can easily create the look you desire. In this article, we’ll go over how to get puffy curls, including tips and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Puffy Curls
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Achieving Puffy Curls
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Puffy Curls

How to Get Puffy Curls

Puffy curls are essentially defined curls with added volume. To achieve this look, you’ll need to use a combination of heat styling tools, products, and techniques.

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • A curling iron or wand
  • Hair clips
  • Hairspray or styling cream
  • Round brush
  • Blow dryer

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to create puffy curls:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant product to your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using hair clips.
  3. Take a section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron or wand. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  4. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.
  5. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume to your curls. Place the brush under a section of hair and blow dry it while holding it up and away from your head.
  6. Once all sections are curled and volumized, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to gently separate the curls.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray or styling cream to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Achieving Puffy Curls

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect puffy curls:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a larger barrel size to create bigger curls.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or spray before blow drying your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down for added volume.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls.
  • Don’t overdo it with the hairspray or styling cream – too much product can weigh down your curls.

FAQs

How long will puffy curls last?

Puffy curls can last for several hours to a day, depending on your hair type and the weather conditions. To help your curls last longer, use a quality hairspray or styling cream and avoid touching your hair too much.

What products should I use for puffy curls?

For puffy curls, you’ll want to use a heat protectant, volumizing product, and a light hold hairspray or styling cream. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Can I achieve puffy curls on short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve puffy curls on shorter hair. Use a smaller curling iron or wand and focus on adding volume at the roots.

Hair Care for Puffy Curls

To maintain healthy, puffy curls, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant product when styling.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet – use a wide-tooth comb instead.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve beautiful, puffy curls that will turn heads. Experiment with different products and tools to find the perfect combination that works for you. Happy styling!
