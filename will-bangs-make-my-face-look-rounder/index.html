Will Bangs Make My Face Look Rounder

Description

Do you have a round face and are considering getting bangs but are afraid that it might make your face look rounder? You are not alone. Many people with round faces are hesitant to get bangs because they fear that it will accentuate their roundness. The good news is that there are different types of bangs that can flatter round faces and make them look slimmer.

How to Determine if Bangs Will Make Your Face Look Rounder

Before getting bangs, it is important to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking at the shape of your face in the mirror. If your face is as wide as it is long and you have a round chin, then you have a round face.

Next, you can use a measuring tape to measure the width of your face from cheekbone to cheekbone. If the measurement is almost the same as the length of your face from your hairline to your chin, then your face is round.

If you have determined that you have a round face, the next step is to choose the right type of bangs that will flatter your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide in Choosing Bangs for Round Faces

1. Consider the length of your bangs

When choosing bangs for round faces, it is important to consider the length of your bangs. Shorter bangs tend to make your face look rounder, while longer bangs can elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Opt for longer bangs that fall below your eyebrows.

2. Go for side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs can create the illusion of a slimmer face. They draw attention away from the roundness of your face and instead highlight your eyes. Make sure that the bangs are not too short and that they blend well with the rest of your hair.

3. Avoid blunt bangs

Blunt bangs can emphasize the roundness of your face. They tend to make your face look fuller and wider. If you want to have bangs, opt for wispy or textured bangs that can soften your face and make it look slimmer.

Tips on Styling Bangs for Round Faces

1. Blow dry your bangs to the side

Blow drying your bangs to the side can create volume and movement. It can also make your face look longer and slimmer. Use a round brush when blow drying and finish off with a blast of cool air to set the style.

2. Avoid center-parted bangs

Center-parted bangs can make your face look wider. If you want to part your bangs, go for a side part instead. It can create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Frequently Asked Questions about Bangs and Round Faces

Q: Will bangs make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the type of bangs you choose. Side-swept bangs that fall below your eyebrows can elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Blunt bangs tend to emphasize the roundness of your face.

Q: Can I still have bangs if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still have bangs if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose the right type of bangs that will flatter your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Bangs on Round Faces

1. Trim your bangs regularly

To maintain the shape and style of your bangs, make sure to trim them regularly. You can do this yourself or go to a professional hairstylist.

2. Use a dry shampoo

If you have oily hair, use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh and clean. It can also add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Avoid touching your bangs

Avoid touching your bangs too much as it can transfer oil from your hands to your hair. It can also ruin the shape and style of your bangs.

In conclusion, bangs can be a great addition to your hairstyle even if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose the right type of bangs that will flatter your face shape and maintain them properly to keep them looking their best.
