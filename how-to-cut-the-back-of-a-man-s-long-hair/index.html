How To Cut The Back Of A Man s Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair can be a great style for men, but maintaining it can be a challenge. One of the most difficult parts of cutting long hair is getting the back just right. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut the back of a man's long hair step-by-step, with some tips and frequently asked questions to help you get the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut the Back of a Man's Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQ

How to Cut the Back of a Man's Long Hair

Cutting the back of a man's long hair can be a challenge, but with a little practice and the right tools, you can get great results. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and combing the hair. Wet hair is easier to work with, and combing it will help you see any tangles or knots that need to be removed.
  2. Section off the hair. Use a comb to create a horizontal part at the back of the head, from one ear to the other. Clip the hair above the part out of the way.
  3. Begin cutting. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of the parted section and comb it straight down. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.
  4. Move up the section. Comb another small section of hair down and use the first section as a guide to cut the next one. Continue moving up the section until you reach the top.
  5. Repeat on the other side. Release the clipped hair and repeat the process on the other side of the head.
  6. Blend the sides. Once both sides are cut, use scissors or clippers to blend the sides into the back. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a comb to guide you.
  7. Check for evenness. Use a mirror to check for any uneven areas and make any necessary adjustments.
  8. Finish with styling. Once the back is cut to your liking, style the hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair

Cutting long hair can be tricky, but with these tips, you can get great results:

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage the hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Work in small sections. Cutting too much hair at once can lead to uneven results.
  • Take breaks. Cutting long hair can be tiring, so take breaks as needed.
  • Be patient. Cutting long hair takes time and practice, so don't rush it.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting the back of a man's long hair:

Q: How often should I cut my long hair?

A: It depends on the individual, but generally every 6-8 weeks is a good time frame to keep the hair healthy and looking its best.

Q: Can I cut my own long hair?

A: Yes, but it can be difficult to cut the back evenly without help. Consider asking a friend or family member to assist you.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: Don't panic! If you make a mistake, try to fix it by cutting a little more hair or blending it in with the surrounding hair. If all else fails, seek the help of a professional stylist.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've cut the back of your long hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for long hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too frequently, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid pulling the hair back tightly, as this can lead to breakage.

By following these tips and using the step-by-step guide above, you can cut the back of a man's long hair with confidence and get great results.
