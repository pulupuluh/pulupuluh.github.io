How To Know Which Hairstyle Suits My Face

Written by Christine May 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles but not getting the desired look? It's time to figure out which hairstyle suits your face shape. In this article, we'll guide you on how to know which hairstyle suits your face and provide tips on hair care.

Table of Content

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • Tips for Hair Care
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Here are the common face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

To determine your face shape, you can use a measuring tape or follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

Alternatively, you can use an online face shape detector or consult a hairstylist.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your features. Here's how:

Round Face

If you have a round face shape, choose a hairstyle that adds length and avoids width. You can try:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • High ponytails

Oval Face

If you have an oval face shape, you're lucky as most hairstyles suit you. You can try:

  • Bobs
  • Long hair
  • Updos

Square Face

If you have a square face shape, choose a hairstyle that softens your features and adds roundness. You can try:

  • Soft waves
  • Side parts
  • Layered bobs

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, choose a hairstyle that balances your forehead and jawline. You can try:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob with volume at the bottom
  • Updos

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond face shape, choose a hairstyle that adds width at the forehead and chin. You can try:

  • Full fringes
  • Layered bobs
  • Side parts

Tips for Hair Care

Choosing the right hairstyle is important, but so is taking care of your hair. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use a conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes vitamins and minerals for healthy hair.

FAQs

Q. Can I change my face shape with a hairstyle?

A. No, you can't change your face shape with a hairstyle, but you can enhance your features with the right haircut.

Q. What if I have a combination of face shapes?

A. If you have a combination of face shapes, choose a hairstyle that flatters the dominant face shape.

Q. Do I need to consult a hairstylist?

A. It's recommended to consult a hairstylist for personalized advice on choosing the right hairstyle.

Choosing the right hairstyle can boost your confidence and complement your features. Remember to take care of your hair and consult a hairstylist if needed. With these tips, you can achieve your desired look.
