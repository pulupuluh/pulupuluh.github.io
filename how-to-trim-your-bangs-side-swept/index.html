How To Trim Your Bangs Side Swept A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your bangs always getting in your eyes? One solution is to trim them side swept. Not only will it keep them out of your face, but it can also give you a chic and fashionable look. Here is a step by step guide on how to trim your bangs side swept.

Description

Trimming your bangs may seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, you can easily achieve the perfect side swept look. This guide will provide you with the necessary steps to trim your bangs side swept and give you confidence in your new look.

How to Trim Your Bangs Side Swept

Trimming your bangs side swept is a simple process that can be done at home. Here is what you will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Small mirror

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect side swept look:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it is completely dry before you begin trimming your bangs.

Step 2: Section Your Bangs

Using a comb, section off your bangs from the rest of your hair. Clip back the rest of your hair so it doesn't get in the way.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want your bangs to be. Keep in mind that it's always better to start longer and gradually trim them shorter.

Step 4: Start Trimming

Using your sharp scissors, start trimming your bangs. Hold the scissors at a slight angle and cut small sections at a time. Remember to keep them longer on the outer edges so they sweep to the side.

Step 5: Check the Length

After each cut, use your comb to check the length of your bangs. If they are still too long, continue to trim them until you get the desired length.

Step 6: Blend Them In

Once you have the desired length, use your scissors to blend them in with the rest of your hair. This will give them a more natural look.

Step 7: Style Your Bangs

Use your comb to style your bangs to the side. You can use a straightener or curling iron to add some extra style.

Step 8: Check Your Work

Use a small mirror to check the back of your head and make sure your bangs are even.

Tips

Here are some tips to ensure that you get the perfect side swept bangs:

  • Start with longer bangs and gradually trim them shorter.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid any jagged edges.
  • Trim your bangs when they are dry to avoid any shrinkage.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time to ensure that you get the perfect length.

FAQ

Q: Can I trim my bangs wet?

A: It's not recommended to trim your bangs wet because they will shrink when they dry. This can result in them being shorter than you intended.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and maintained.

Q: Can I trim my bangs myself?

A: Yes, you can trim your bangs yourself if you have the right tools and technique. Just make sure to take your time and follow the steps carefully.

Hair Care

After trimming your bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

Trimming your bangs side swept is a simple process that can give you a fresh and stylish look. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect side swept bangs.
