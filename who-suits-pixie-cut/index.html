Who Suits Pixie Cut A Guide To The Perfect Short Haircut

Written by Bardi Aug 29, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking for a trendy and easy-to-maintain haircut, a pixie cut might be the perfect choice. However, many people wonder if they would look good with this short hairstyle. In this article, we’ll discuss who suits pixie cut, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Table of Contents

  • Who Suits Pixie Cut
  • How to Get Pixie Cut
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Who Suits Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically leaves less than two inches of hair on the scalp. It’s a bold and edgy look that suits many face shapes and hair textures. However, it may not be the best choice for everyone.

If you have a round face, a pixie cut can help balance out your features and make your face appear slimmer. On the other hand, if you have a square or rectangular face, a pixie cut may make your face appear even more angular.

If you have thin or fine hair, a pixie cut can add volume and texture to your hair. However, if you have thick or curly hair, you may need to spend more time styling your pixie cut to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Ultimately, the best way to determine if a pixie cut is right for you is to consult with a hairstylist. They can assess your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle to help you make an informed decision.

How to Get Pixie Cut

Getting a pixie cut is a relatively simple process, but it’s important to choose a qualified hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair. Here’s what you can expect during the process:

Consultation

Your hairstylist will first ask you about your hair goals and assess your hair texture and face shape. They may also show you pictures of different pixie cut styles to help you choose the right one for you.

Cutting

Your hairstylist will use scissors and/or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length. They may also use a razor to add texture and movement to your hair.

Styling

Once your hair is cut, your hairstylist will style it using a blow dryer, styling products, and possibly a flat iron or curling iron. They may also show you how to style your pixie cut at home.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a pixie cut:

  1. Choose a qualified hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best pixie cut style for you.
  3. Sit in the chair and let your hairstylist cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Style your pixie cut using a blow dryer, styling products, and possibly a flat iron or curling iron.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you rock your pixie cut:

  • Invest in high-quality styling products to keep your pixie cut looking sleek and shiny.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain your pixie cut’s shape and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts:

Do I need to have a certain face shape to rock a pixie cut?

No, but some face shapes may be more suited to certain pixie cut styles. Consult with a hairstylist to find the right style for you.

What if I don’t like my pixie cut?

Hair grows back! If you’re not happy with your pixie cut, talk to your hairstylist about options for growing it out or changing the style.

Will a pixie cut require a lot of maintenance?

It depends on your hair texture and the style of your pixie cut. Some styles may require more styling and upkeep than others.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your pixie cut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and make your hair look frizzy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Overall, a pixie cut can be a fun and stylish way to switch up your look. By choosing the right style for your face shape and hair texture, and taking good care of your hair, you can rock your pixie cut with confidence!
