Mens Hairstyles Fade Long On Top How To Get The Look In 2023

Written by Robby May 31, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish men's haircut, the fade long on top is a great option to consider. This trendy hairstyle has been popular for several years, and it's not showing any signs of slowing down anytime soon. In this blog post, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about mens hairstyles fade long on top, including how to get the look, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get the Look
  3. Step-by-Step Instructions

  5. FAQs

Description

Mens hairstyles fade long on top are characterized by a gradual tapering of hair length from the sides and back of the head to the top. The hair on top of the head is left longer, usually around 2 to 4 inches, and can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. The fade can be high, mid, or low, depending on your preference. This hairstyle is popular among men of all ages and can be customized to suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

How to Get the Look

To get the mens hairstyles fade long on top, you'll need to visit a skilled barber or hairstylist who has experience with this type of cut. It's essential to communicate your desired length, fade, and style to ensure you get the look you want. Bring a picture of the hairstyle you like to show your stylist, and they can advise you on whether it's suitable for your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are the steps to get the mens hairstyles fade long on top:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Section your hair and use clippers to create the fade on the sides and back of your head. You can choose a high, mid, or low fade based on your preference.
  3. Blend the fade using scissors or clippers to create a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  4. Trim the hair on top to the desired length, usually around 2 to 4 inches. You can use scissors or clippers, depending on the style you want.
  5. Style your hair using your preferred product, such as pomade, wax, or gel. You can slick it back, create a messy look, or add volume and texture.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting mens hairstyles fade long on top:

  • Choose a fade that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Regularly visit your barber or hairstylist for touch-ups to maintain the look.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the one that works best for you.
  • Consider getting a beard trim or shave to complement your hairstyle.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens hairstyles fade long on top:

1. Is the fade long on top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the mens hairstyles fade long on top can be customized to suit any hair type, including straight, wavy, curly, and coiled.

2. How often should I get my hair cut to maintain the look?

You should visit your barber or hairstylist every 4 to 6 weeks for touch-ups to maintain the fade and length on top.

3. Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

Yes, the hair on top can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, textured, or voluminous.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting the mens hairstyles fade long on top:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

In conclusion, mens hairstyles fade long on top are a trendy and versatile option for men who want a stylish and modern haircut. With the right cut, styling, and hair care, you can achieve a look that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Don't be afraid to experiment with different fades, styles, and products to find the one that works best for you.
