How Long Does It Take To Grow Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair is beautiful and unique, but it can be challenging to grow and maintain. If you're wondering how long it takes to grow shoulder length curly hair, the answer is not straightforward. Some factors can influence hair growth, such as genetics, age, health, and lifestyle. Generally, it takes around two to three years for curly hair to reach shoulder length, but it can vary depending on the individual's hair type and care routine.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a popular choice for many people because it's versatile and easy to style. However, growing curly hair to this length can be a long journey, and it requires patience and commitment. Curly hair tends to shrink, and it can be challenging to determine the actual length of the hair. To achieve shoulder length curly hair, you need to take proper care of your hair and have a healthy lifestyle.

How to Grow Shoulder Length Curly Hair?

If you want to grow your curly hair to shoulder length, you need to follow a few steps to help your hair grow healthier and stronger:

Step 1: Start with a Healthy Scalp

A healthy scalp is essential for hair growth. You can achieve a healthy scalp by eating a balanced diet, drinking enough water, and using natural hair products. Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair and scalp.

Step 2: Protect Your Hair

Curly hair is fragile and prone to breakage. To protect your hair, you need to avoid tight hairstyles that can pull your hair and cause damage. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage while sleeping.

Step 3: Moisturize Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be dry, and it needs regular moisture to stay healthy. Use a deep conditioner once a week and a leave-in conditioner daily to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using products that contain alcohol, sulfates, and silicones that can dry out your hair.

Step 4: Trim Your Hair Regularly

To prevent split ends and breakage, you need to trim your hair regularly. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and promote growth.

Tips for Growing Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you grow your curly hair to shoulder length:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water and opt for lukewarm water when washing your hair.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt instead.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection.

FAQ About Growing Shoulder Length Curly Hair

1. How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair once or twice a week to prevent it from drying out. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become frizzy.

2. How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

To prevent frizz, you should avoid using hot water, towel-drying your hair, and using hair products that contain alcohol and sulfates. Use a leave-in conditioner and a curl-defining product to help define your curls and prevent frizz.

3. Can I use hair extensions to achieve shoulder length curly hair?

Yes, you can use hair extensions to achieve shoulder length curly hair. However, you need to choose extensions that match your hair texture and color to achieve a natural look.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for shoulder length curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol and sulfates, as they can dry out your hair and cause damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection.

In conclusion, growing shoulder length curly hair can take some time and effort, but with proper care and patience, you can achieve your desired length. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this article to help your hair grow healthier and stronger. Remember, healthy hair starts with a healthy scalp and a healthy lifestyle.
