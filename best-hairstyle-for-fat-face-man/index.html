Best Hairstyle For Fat Face Man How To Choose The Right Cut

Written by Luffy Aug 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As a man with a round or chubby face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a cut that flatters your features and helps you look your best. But with so many options out there, it can be hard to know where to start.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Best Hairstyles for Fat Face Men
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step in choosing the best hairstyle for your face shape is to determine your face shape. In general, men with round or chubby faces should look for cuts that add height and length to the face. Avoid styles that add width or volume to the sides of the face.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. Thick, curly hair may require a different cut than thin, straight hair. Talk to your stylist about your hair type and how it can affect your hairstyle options.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. Are you a busy professional who needs a low-maintenance cut? Or do you have more time to style your hair each day? Think about how much time and effort you're willing to put into your hair before making a decision.

Best Hairstyles for Fat Face Men

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the best options for men with round or chubby faces.

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can work well for men with fat faces, as they can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. A buzz cut, crew cut, or undercut are all good options. Just be sure to avoid styles that are too short on the sides, as this can make the face look wider.

Medium-Length Haircuts

If you prefer a bit more length, a medium-length cut can also work well. A textured crop or messy fringe can help to add height and volume to the top of the head, balancing out a round face. Avoid styles that are too slick or flat, as this can make the face look wider.

Long Haircuts

Men with round faces can also wear longer hairstyles, but it's important to keep the hair layered and textured. A shaggy, layered cut can help to create movement and volume, while also elongating the face. Avoid styles that are too straight or sleek, as this can make the face look wider.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle, it's important to know how to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you keep your haircut looking its best:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Invest in quality hair products that work well with your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a fat face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as a bowl cut or a style with heavy bangs. Also avoid styles that are too short on the sides, as this can make the face look wider.

Can men with round faces wear long hairstyles?

Yes, men with round faces can wear longer hairstyles, but it's important to keep the hair layered and textured to avoid adding width to the face.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's generally recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy.

Hair Care for Fat Face Men

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle and maintaining it properly, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to help detangle and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to support healthy hair growth.

With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, men with fat faces can look and feel their best. Experiment with different cuts and styles to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice.
