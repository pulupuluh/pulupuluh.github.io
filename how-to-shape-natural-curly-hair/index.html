How To Shape Natural Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Having natural curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have gorgeous, bouncy curls that can turn heads wherever you go. On the other hand, it can be tough to figure out how to style and shape those curls to look their best. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of shaping your natural curly hair, step-by-step. We'll also provide some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your curls, and answer some frequently asked questions about curly hair. Let's get started!

  • Step-by-Step

  • FAQ

How to Shape Natural Curly Hair

The key to shaping natural curly hair is to work with the natural texture of your hair, rather than against it. Here are the basic steps:

1. Start with clean, wet hair.

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and gently towel dry your hair so it's damp but not soaking wet.

2. Apply a styling product.

Choose a styling product that works well with your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, try a lightweight mousse or gel. If you have thicker hair, a cream or butter might work better. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, using your fingers or a wide-tooth comb.

3. Use a diffuser.

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and set it to a low heat setting. Gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser, lifting your curls up towards your scalp. This will help to enhance your natural curl pattern and add volume.

4. Shape your curls.

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers or a pick to gently shape your curls. You can also use a curling iron or wand to define individual curls, if desired. Be gentle and avoid using too much heat, as this can damage your hair over time.

Tips for Shaping Natural Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your natural curls:

  • Invest in a high-quality diffuser attachment for your blow dryer.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze excess water out of your hair, rather than rubbing it with a regular towel.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I shape my natural curly hair without heat?

A: Yes! You can use a variety of styling techniques to shape your natural curls without heat. Try using a curl cream or butter, or experiment with finger coiling or twist-outs.

Q: How often should I wash my natural curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture, as well as your personal preference. Some people with curly hair find that they can go longer between washes, while others prefer to wash their hair more frequently. As a general rule, try to wash your hair no more than once every 2-3 days, as over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I straighten my natural curly hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to be careful when using heat styling tools on curly hair, as it can be more prone to damage. Always use a heat protectant spray, and try to limit your use of heat styling tools to once or twice a week.

Hair Care for Natural Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to help you keep your natural curly hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help hydrate your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to help keep it healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or sulfates on your hair, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that you'll love showing off. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
