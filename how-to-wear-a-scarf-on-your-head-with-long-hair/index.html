How To Wear A Scarf On Your Head With Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Scarves are a versatile accessory that can be styled in many different ways. One popular way to wear a scarf is on your head, especially if you have long hair. In this article, we will show you how to wear a scarf on your head with long hair. We will cover everything from how to tie the scarf to hair care tips to keep your hair healthy.

How to Wear a Scarf on Your Head with Long Hair

Wearing a scarf on your head with long hair can be both stylish and practical. Here are some ways to wear a scarf on your head with long hair:

  • As a headband
  • As a turban
  • As a bandana
  • As a hair tie

Each of these styles can be achieved with a few simple steps. Keep reading to learn how.

Step-by-Step Guide

As a Headband

To wear a scarf as a headband, follow these steps:

  1. Fold the scarf in half diagonally to create a triangle shape.
  2. Place the scarf on the top of your head with the point in the back.
  3. Tie the two ends of the scarf at the nape of your neck.
  4. Tuck any loose ends of the scarf under the knot.

As a Turban

To wear a scarf as a turban, follow these steps:

  1. Fold the scarf in half diagonally to create a triangle shape.
  2. Place the scarf on the top of your head with the point in the front.
  3. Take the two ends of the scarf and cross them at the back of your head.
  4. Bring the ends back to the front of your head and tie a knot.
  5. Tuck any loose ends of the scarf under the knot.

As a Bandana

To wear a scarf as a bandana, follow these steps:

  1. Fold the scarf in half diagonally to create a triangle shape.
  2. Place the scarf on the top of your head with the point in the back.
  3. Tie the two ends of the scarf in a knot at the nape of your neck.

As a Hair Tie

To wear a scarf as a hair tie, follow these steps:

  1. Tie your hair in a ponytail or bun.
  2. Wrap the scarf around the base of the ponytail or bun.
  3. Tie the two ends of the scarf in a knot to secure it in place.

Tips for Wearing a Scarf on Your Head with Long Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing a scarf on your head with long hair:

  • Choose a scarf made from a lightweight fabric to avoid adding too much bulk to your head.
  • Experiment with different scarf styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the scarf in place if needed.
  • Accessorize your scarf with earrings or sunglasses for a trendy look.

FAQs

What type of scarf should I use?

It is best to use a scarf made from a lightweight fabric, such as cotton or silk, to avoid adding too much bulk to your head.

How can I keep the scarf from slipping off my head?

You can use bobby pins to secure the scarf in place if needed.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a scarf on your head can be a great way to protect your hair from the sun and wind. However, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid wearing scarves too tight to prevent tension on your hairline.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from damage.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can wear a scarf on your head with long hair and keep your hair healthy at the same time.

Now that you know how to wear a scarf on your head with long hair, you can try out different styles and find the one that works best for you. Whether you choose to wear a scarf as a headband, turban, bandana, or hair tie, you can add a stylish touch to any outfit.
