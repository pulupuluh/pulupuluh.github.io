Haircuts For Round Faces 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia May 31, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of scrolling through Pinterest and Instagram, trying to find the perfect haircut for your round face? Look no further, because we have compiled a list of the best haircuts for round faces in 2021. From short to long, curly to straight, we’ve got you covered. In this article, you’ll learn how to choose the best haircut for your face shape, how to style it, and how to take care of it. Let’s get started!

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a soft jawline. The goal of a haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and elongate the face. The right haircut can make all the difference, and we’ve rounded up the best ones for you.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Your Face Shape

When choosing a haircut for a round face, it’s important to consider the length and texture of your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can work well for round faces, as long as they are layered and asymmetrical. The layers will add dimension and movement to your hair, while the asymmetry will create the illusion of a slimmer face. Pixie cuts, bobs, and lobs are all great options.

Medium Haircuts

Medium haircuts are also a good choice for round faces. Shoulder-length hair with layers that start below the chin can create a slimming effect. A long bob, also known as a lob, is a popular choice for those with a round face.

Long Haircuts

Long hair can be tricky for round faces, but it is possible to make it work. The key is to add layers and volume around the crown of your head. Loose waves or curls can also help to elongate the face.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know which haircut will work best for your round face, it’s time to learn how to style it. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Short Haircuts

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair.

3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the roots.

4. Use a flat iron or curling wand to add texture and volume.

5. Finish with a light hairspray.

Medium Haircuts

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair.

3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the ends.

4. Use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves.

5. Finish with a light hairspray.

Long Haircuts

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair.

3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the roots.

4. Use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves or curls.

5. Finish with a light hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut for your round face, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

- Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

- Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a middle part with a round face?

A: Yes, a middle part can work with a round face, but it’s best to add some volume and texture to your hair to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Q: Are bangs a good choice for a round face?

A: Yes, bangs can work well for a round face, as long as they are layered and not too blunt. Side-swept bangs are a good option.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it’s important to use products that are suited to your hair type. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

- Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Now that you know everything there is to know about haircuts for round faces in 2021, it’s time to schedule your next salon appointment. Remember, the right haircut can make all the difference, so choose wisely!
