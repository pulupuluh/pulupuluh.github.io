Mens Curly Hair Long On Top Short On Sides A Guide To Styling And Care

If you're a man with curly hair, you know that it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits your texture. But fear not, because the mens curly hair long on top short on sides is a trendy and versatile cut that can work for any curly-haired man. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps on how to achieve this look, tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions about curly hair.

How to Style Mens Curly Hair Long on Top Short on Sides

The mens curly hair long on top short on sides is a classic and modern style that can work for any occasion. Here are the steps to achieve this look:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-defining product to your hair, such as a curl cream or gel.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a hair clip to clip the sides of your hair up, leaving the top section loose.
  5. Use a curling iron or wand to enhance the curls on the top section of your hair. You can also use a round brush to create volume and shape.
  6. Once you're happy with the style, remove the hair clip and use your fingers to gently separate the curls on the sides of your hair.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or a lightweight oil to add shine and hold.

Remember that everyone's hair is different, so you may need to experiment with different products and styling techniques to find what works best for you.

Products to Use

When styling mens curly hair long on top short on sides, it's essential to use products that enhance your curls and provide hold without weighing them down. Here are some products to consider:

  • Curl cream or gel
  • Diffuser attachment for your hairdryer
  • Curling iron or wand
  • Round brush
  • Hairspray or lightweight oil

Tips for Maintaining Mens Curly Hair Long on Top Short on Sides

Maintaining mens curly hair long on top short on sides can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, it can be done. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid towel-drying your hair vigorously. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze the water out.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Mens Curly Hair Long on Top Short on Sides

1. What face shape suits mens curly hair long on top short on sides?

The mens curly hair long on top short on sides can work for any face shape, but it's especially flattering for those with square or oval faces.

2. How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash your curly hair two to three times a week to avoid drying it out.

3. Can I wear this hairstyle for formal events?

Yes, the mens curly hair long on top short on sides can be dressed up or down, making it suitable for any occasion.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a different kind of care than straight hair. Here are some tips to keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Avoid brushing your curly hair, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Remember, with the right products and care, mens curly hair long on top short on sides can be a stylish and manageable hairstyle for any curly-haired man.
