How To Slick My Hair Back Without Gel A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 06, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re looking for a sleek and polished hairstyle, slicking your hair back is a great option. However, many people don’t like the feel or look of gel in their hair. The good news is that you can still achieve this look without using gel. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to slick your hair back without gel.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick My Hair Back Without Gel
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

How to Slick My Hair Back Without Gel

There are a few products you can use to slick your hair back without gel:

  • Pomade
  • Wax
  • Clay
  • Hair spray

Each of these products has its own pros and cons, so it’s important to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to slick your hair back without gel:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type.
  2. Apply a small amount of your chosen product to your fingertips.
  3. Starting at the crown of your head, use your fingers to comb your hair back. Use a comb to smooth down any flyaways.
  4. Continue combing your hair back until you reach the nape of your neck.
  5. Use a comb to create a part if desired.
  6. Finish by using a hair spray to hold your hair in place.

Remember to use a small amount of product to start with. You can always add more if needed.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Use a product that works well for your hair type. If you have fine hair, you may want to use a lighter product like a pomade. If you have thick hair, a wax or clay may work better.
  • Don’t use too much product. A little goes a long way.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that looks best on you.
  • Use a comb to smooth down any flyaways.
  • Use a hair spray with a strong hold to keep your hair in place all day.

FAQs

Can I slick my hair back without any product?

Technically, yes. However, using a product will help hold your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

Can I slick my hair back if it’s short?

Yes, you can. Use a little bit of product and a comb to slick your hair back.

What can I do if my hair keeps falling out of place?

Try using a stronger hold product or reapplying hair spray throughout the day.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

Slicking your hair back can put extra strain on your hair, so it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product or applying it too frequently.
  • Try not to pull your hair back too tightly as this can cause breakage.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your hair to avoid damaging it.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and polished look without using gel. Experiment with different products and styles to find the one that works best for you.
