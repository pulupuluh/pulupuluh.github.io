Description

A high top fade is a hairstyle that is characterized by a long, straight top hair that stands upright and a short faded hair on the back and sides. This hairstyle has been popular among African-American men since the 1980s and has made a comeback in recent years. However, some people wonder if a high top fade is professional enough for the workplace or other formal occasions.

How to

If you are interested in getting a high top fade, there are a few things you need to consider. First, you need to find a good barber who is experienced in cutting this hairstyle. Second, you need to have the right hair texture and length. Third, you need to maintain your hair regularly to keep the style looking fresh and neat.

Step by step

Here are the steps to get a high top fade:

  1. Grow your hair to at least 2-3 inches on top.
  2. Visit a barber who specializes in high top fades.
  3. Show the barber a picture of the style you want.
  4. The barber will use clippers to fade the sides and back of your head.
  5. The barber will cut and shape the top of your hair to create the high top.
  6. The barber will add some hair product to create the desired texture and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your high top fade looking professional:

  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat or chemicals on your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about high top fades:

Is a high top fade suitable for the workplace?

Yes, a high top fade can be professional if it is well-maintained and styled appropriately. However, it may not be suitable for all workplaces, so you should check with your employer or HR department before getting this hairstyle.

Can I get a high top fade if I have curly hair?

Yes, a high top fade can be done on curly hair, but it may require more work to achieve the desired shape and texture.

How often do I need to get a high top fade?

You should get a high top fade trimmed every 2-4 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Hair Care

To keep your high top fade looking its best, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This includes:

  • Washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Using a leave-in conditioner or oil to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoiding excessive heat styling or chemical treatments.
  • Trimming your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length.

In conclusion, a high top fade can be professional if it is well-maintained and styled appropriately. To get this hairstyle, you need to find a good barber who is experienced in cutting this style, have the right hair texture and length, and maintain your hair regularly. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can rock a high top fade with confidence.
