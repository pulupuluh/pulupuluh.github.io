How To Apply Product To Wet Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to hair care, applying the right product to wet hair is crucial for achieving the desired style and maintaining healthy hair. Whether you're looking to add volume, control frizz, or enhance your natural curls, applying product to wet hair is the first step in achieving your desired look. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of how to apply product to wet hair, including tips and tricks for maximum results.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Choose the Right Product
  • Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair
  • Step 3: Section Your Hair
  • Step 4: Apply the Product
  • Step 5: Comb or Scrunch Your Hair
  • Tips for Applying Product to Wet Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Choose the Right Product

Before you start applying product to your wet hair, it's important to choose the right product for your hair type and desired style. Depending on your hair type, you may need a different type of product. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to use a lightweight volumizing mousse, while if you have thick, curly hair, you may prefer a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a towel to gently blot and squeeze out excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz. You want your hair to be damp, but not dripping wet, before applying product.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to apply the product evenly throughout your hair. Start with the bottom sections and work your way up, applying product to each section as you go.

Step 4: Apply the Product

Take a small amount of product (about the size of a quarter) and apply it to the palm of your hand. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly. Then, starting at the roots, work the product through your hair, using your fingers or a comb to distribute it evenly. Be sure to apply the product all the way to the ends of your hair.

Step 5: Comb or Scrunch Your Hair

After applying the product, you can either comb your hair for a sleeker look or scrunch it for more volume and texture. If you're going for a curly look, scrunching is the way to go. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disturbing the curls.

Tips for Applying Product to Wet Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when applying product to wet hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying product
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed
  • Avoid applying product directly to your scalp, as this can cause buildup
  • For best results, use products from the same brand or line, as they are designed to work together

FAQs

Here are some common questions about applying product to wet hair:

  • Q: Can I apply product to dry hair?
  • A: While it's possible to apply product to dry hair, it's generally easier to distribute evenly when applied to wet hair.
  • Q: How much product should I use?
  • A: Start with a small amount and add more as needed. Using too much product can weigh down your hair and cause buildup.
  • Q: How often should I apply product to my hair?
  • A: This will depend on your hair type and the product you are using. Some products are designed to be used daily, while others are best used once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to applying product to wet hair, there are other things you can do to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using hot tools (like curling irons and flat irons) too often, as they can cause damage and breakage
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage

By following these tips and applying product to wet hair, you can achieve the perfect style and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
