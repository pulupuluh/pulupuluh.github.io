Description

Changing your hair color can be a big decision, but it can also be a fun way to switch up your look and express your individuality. When someone you know changes their hair color, it's important to show them support and compliment their new style. In this article, we'll provide tips on how to compliment new hair color in a relaxed English language.

How to Compliment New Hair Color

When complimenting someone on their new hair color, it's important to be sincere and specific. Here are some tips:

1. Be genuine

When complimenting someone on their new hair color, make sure you are genuine. Nobody likes a fake compliment, so make sure your words are sincere and heartfelt.

2. Be specific

Instead of just saying "I like your hair", be specific about what you like. For example, "Your new blonde highlights really bring out your eyes" or "That shade of red looks amazing against your skin tone".

3. Show enthusiasm

When complimenting someone on their new hair color, show enthusiasm and excitement. This will make the person feel good about their decision to change their hair color and boost their confidence.

Step by Step

If you're not sure how to compliment someone on their new hair color, follow these steps:

1. Approach the person

Approach the person and start a conversation with them.

2. Notice the change

Notice the change in their hair color and comment on it. For example, "I love your new hair color, it looks great on you"

3. Be specific

Be specific about what you like about their hair color. For example, "That shade of blue really brings out your eyes" or "The lighter highlights make your hair look so shiny"

4. Show enthusiasm

Show enthusiasm and excitement about their new hair color. For example, "Wow, your new hair color looks amazing! I love it!"

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to compliment new hair color:

1. Ask questions

If you're not sure what to say, ask questions about their new hair color. For example, "What made you decide to change your hair color?" or "Who did your hair? They did an amazing job!"

2. Use positive language

Use positive language when complimenting someone on their new hair color. For example, "I love your new hair color" instead of "Your old hair color wasn't bad, but this one is better".

3. Be respectful

Be respectful of the person's decision to change their hair color, even if it's not your personal preference. Everyone has different tastes and preferences.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to compliment new hair color:

1. What if I don't like their new hair color?

It's okay if you don't like their new hair color. You can still be respectful and find something positive to say. For example, "I can tell you put a lot of thought into your new hair color" or "It's a bold choice, and it looks great on you".

2. Is it okay to touch someone's new hair color?

No, it's not okay to touch someone's new hair color without their permission. It could ruin their hairstyle or damage their hair.

3. How often should I compliment someone on their new hair color?

It's up to you how often you compliment someone on their new hair color. If you see them frequently, you could mention it once or twice. If you only see them occasionally, you could compliment them every time you see them.

Hair Care

Changing your hair color can be fun, but it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips:

1. Use color-safe products

Use hair care products that are specifically designed for color-treated hair. This will help preserve the color and keep it looking fresh.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun can cause hair color to fade, so protect your hair by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective hair product.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage hair and cause color to fade. Try to avoid using them as much as possible or use a heat protectant product.


Complimenting someone on their new hair color is a great way to show support and boost their confidence. By being sincere, specific, and enthusiastic, you can make someone feel great about their new look. Remember to also take care of your own hair to maintain its health and vibrancy.
