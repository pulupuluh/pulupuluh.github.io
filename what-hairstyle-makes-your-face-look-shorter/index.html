What Hairstyle Makes Your Face Look Shorter

Description

Having a face that appears longer than it actually is can be frustrating when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. Fortunately, there are various hairstyles that can help to create the illusion of a shorter face. In this article, we will discuss what hairstyle makes your face look shorter and provide you with some useful tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle.

How To

When it comes to finding a hairstyle that will make your face look shorter, the key is to create volume and width around your face. This can be achieved through various techniques, such as adding layers, using a side part, and creating curls or waves.

Adding Layers

Adding layers to your hair is a great way to create volume and movement around your face. Layers can help to soften the edges of your face and draw attention away from its length. Opt for long layers if you have long hair or short layers if you have short hair.

Using a Side Part

A side part is a simple yet effective way to make your face look shorter. By creating a diagonal line across your hair, you can break up the length of your face and create the illusion of width. Experiment with different parting positions to find the one that suits you best.

Creating Curls or Waves

Creating curls or waves in your hair can also help to make your face look shorter. The texture and movement created by curls and waves can help to distract from the length of your face and draw attention to other areas. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves or tight curls.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve a hairstyle that will make your face look shorter:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  4. If you have long hair, add long layers to help create movement around your face.
  5. Create a side part to break up the length of your face.
  6. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves or tight curls.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat, as they can accentuate the length of your face.
  • Consider getting bangs to help create the illusion of a shorter face.
  • If you have a round face, avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your head.
  • If you have a square face, consider hairstyles that soften your jawline.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a long face?

The best haircut for a long face is one that adds volume and width around the face. This can be achieved through various techniques, such as adding layers, using a side part, and creating curls or waves.

Will bangs make my face look shorter?

Yes, bangs can help to create the illusion of a shorter face. Opt for bangs that hit just above your eyebrows to draw attention to your eyes and help balance out the length of your face.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential when it comes to achieving the perfect hairstyle. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a hairstyle that will make your face look shorter and enhance your natural beauty. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
