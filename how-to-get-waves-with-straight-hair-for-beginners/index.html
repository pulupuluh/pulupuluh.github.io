How To Get Waves With Straight Hair For Beginners

Written by Daniel Jun 30, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to switch things up with some waves? Look no further! In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to get waves with straight hair for beginners. Here’s what we’ll cover:

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Waves are a popular hairstyle that many people strive to achieve. However, if you have straight hair, this can be a challenge. With the right technique and products, you can achieve the perfect waves.

How to

The first step in getting waves with straight hair is to choose the right products. You will need a texturizing spray, a hairbrush, and a hair straightener.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it.

2. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair, focusing on the roots.

3. Use a hairbrush to work the spray into your hair.

4. Divide your hair into sections, using hair clips to keep them separated.

5. Take a section of hair and twist it tightly.

6. Use a hair straightener to run over the twisted section a few times.

7. Release the twist and repeat steps 5-6 on the remaining sections.

8. Once you have finished all the sections, use your fingers to comb through your hair and create the desired wave pattern.

Tips

Here are some tips to ensure you get the best results when getting waves with straight hair:

  • Use a texturizing spray that is specifically designed for straight hair.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before using the hair straightener.
  • Twist your hair tightly to create a more defined wave pattern.
  • If you have trouble getting the waves to hold, try using a hairspray to keep them in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a curling iron instead of a hair straightener?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron to create waves. However, a hair straightener will give you a more defined wave pattern.

Q: How long will the waves last?

A: The waves should last for several hours, but if you use a hairspray, they can last all day.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair after getting waves. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after getting the waves.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

By following these tips, you can achieve the perfect waves with straight hair.

So, there you have it – a step-by-step guide on how to get waves with straight hair for beginners. With the right products, technique, and care, you can achieve the perfect waves and switch up your hairstyle.
