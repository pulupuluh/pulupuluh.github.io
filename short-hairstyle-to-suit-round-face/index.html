Short Hairstyle To Suit Round Face

Written by William Jun 14, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face, you may find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this blog post, we will discuss short hairstyles that are perfect for round faces. We've also included tips on how to style your hair and take care of it to maintain its healthy look. So, let's get started!

  2. How to

Description

Short hairstyles are perfect for round faces as they help to create an illusion of length and slim down the face. The key is to find a hairstyle that has volume on top and is tapered at the bottom. This will help to elongate the face and balance out the features.

Another important factor to consider is the texture of your hair. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a layered haircut that adds volume and movement. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to go for a blunt bob or a pixie cut that helps to tame the volume.

How to

When it comes to styling short hair for round faces, there are a few things you need to keep in mind. Firstly, avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head, such as a layered pixie cut or a short bob with choppy layers.

Secondly, consider adding texture to your hair to create more definition and movement. You can do this by using a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray that adds volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a curling iron or a straightener to create loose waves or curls that add dimension to your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for round faces:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray to add volume and texture.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.
  4. Use a curling iron or a straightener to create loose waves or curls that add dimension to your hair.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short hair for round faces:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider adding layers to create volume and movement.
  • Use a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.
  • Use a curling iron or a straightener to create loose waves or curls that add dimension to your hair.
  • Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

What kind of short hairstyles are best for round faces?

Short hairstyles that add volume at the crown of your head and taper at the bottom are perfect for round faces. Some examples include layered pixie cuts, short bobs with choppy layers, and asymmetrical haircuts.

What kind of hair texture works best for short hairstyles for round faces?

Short hairstyles work best for fine to medium hair texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a blunt bob or a pixie cut that helps to tame the volume.

How do I style short hair for round faces?

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray to add volume and texture. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head. Use a curling iron or a straightener to create loose waves or curls that add dimension to your hair. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Hair Care

To maintain your short hairstyle and keep it looking healthy and shiny, it's important to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, short hairstyles are perfect for round faces as they help to create an illusion of length and slim down the face. The key is to find a hairstyle that has volume on top and is tapered at the bottom. Remember to consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle and follow a hair care routine to maintain your hair's healthy look. Happy styling!
