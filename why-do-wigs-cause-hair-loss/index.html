Why Do Wigs Cause Hair Loss

Written by Oliver Mar 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Welcome to our blog post where we will discuss why wigs can cause hair loss. If you are someone who wears a wig or is considering wearing one, it is important to understand the potential risks associated with them. While wigs may seem like a convenient and easy solution for hair loss or styling, they can actually lead to further hair damage and loss if not used properly. In this article, we will explain the reasons why wigs can cause hair loss and provide tips for proper wig use and hair care to prevent any damage.

What Causes Hair Loss When Wearing Wigs?

Wearing wigs can cause traction alopecia, which is a type of hair loss that is caused by constant pulling or tension on the hair. This is especially common when wearing wigs that are too tight or are not properly secured to the head. The constant pulling can damage the hair follicles, leading to hair breakage and eventually hair loss.

In addition, wearing wigs can lead to scalp irritation and inflammation. This can further damage the hair follicles and lead to hair loss. Wigs can also trap moisture and sweat, which can create a breeding ground for bacteria and fungi. These can cause scalp infections, which can also contribute to hair loss.

How to Prevent Hair Loss When Wearing Wigs

If you want to wear wigs without risking hair loss, there are several things you can do to prevent damage to your hair and scalp:

Choose the Right Wig

Make sure you choose a wig that fits properly and is not too tight. A wig that is too tight can cause scalp irritation and hair loss. It is also important to choose a wig made of high-quality materials that will not cause allergies or irritations.

Secure the Wig Properly

Make sure your wig is properly secured to your head. This will prevent it from slipping and sliding, which can cause tension and hair breakage. You can use wig clips, bobby pins, or a wig cap to help secure your wig in place.

Take Breaks from Wearing Wigs

It is important to take breaks from wearing wigs to give your scalp a chance to breathe. Wearing wigs every day for extended periods of time can cause damage to your hair and scalp. Try to alternate between wearing wigs and letting your natural hair breathe.

Clean Your Scalp and Wig Regularly

Regularly cleaning your scalp and wig can prevent bacteria and fungi from growing and causing scalp infections. It is important to follow the cleaning instructions that come with your wig to avoid damaging it. You should also use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your scalp and wig.

FAQs About Wigs and Hair Loss

Can wearing wigs cause hair loss?

Yes, wearing wigs that are too tight or not properly secured can cause traction alopecia, which is a type of hair loss caused by constant pulling or tension on the hair.

What type of wigs are best for preventing hair loss?

Wigs made of high-quality materials that fit properly and are not too tight are best for preventing hair loss. It is also important to choose wigs that are breathable and allow air to circulate around the scalp.

What should I do if I experience hair loss from wearing wigs?

If you experience hair loss from wearing wigs, it is important to stop wearing them and seek medical advice. Your doctor may recommend treatments such as topical medications or hair transplants to help restore your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

In addition to proper wig use, there are several hair care tips you can follow to prevent hair loss:

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles such as braids, buns, and ponytails can cause traction alopecia. Try to avoid these styles or wear them loosely to prevent damage to your hair.

Protect Your Hair From Heat

Excessive heat can damage your hair and contribute to hair loss. Try to avoid using hot styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons too frequently.

Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. Make sure you are getting enough protein, iron, and B vitamins in your diet.

By following these tips and taking proper care of your hair and scalp, you can prevent hair loss when wearing wigs and maintain healthy hair.
