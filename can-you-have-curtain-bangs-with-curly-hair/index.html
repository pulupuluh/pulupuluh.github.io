Can You Have Curtain Bangs With Curly Hair

Written by Robby Mar 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you might be wondering if you can have curtain bangs. The good news is, yes, you can! Curtain bangs are a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can be adapted to suit any hair type, including curly hair. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve curtain bangs with curly hair, as well as some tips and tricks for maintaining and styling them.

How to Achieve Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair

The first step to achieving curtain bangs with curly hair is to find a skilled stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. It's important to get a consultation beforehand to discuss your hair type and desired look. Your stylist should be able to recommend the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair texture.

Once you've found a stylist you trust, it's time to discuss the length and shape of your curtain bangs. For curly hair, it's best to go for longer, wispy bangs that are longer at the sides than in the middle. This will create a more natural look and prevent your bangs from looking too heavy or bulky.

When it comes to styling your curtain bangs, it's important to use products that will enhance your curls and add definition. A curl-enhancing cream or gel can help to define your curls and prevent frizz. To style your bangs, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry them gently and avoid disrupting your curls.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair

Step 1: Consultation

Find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair and discuss your desired look.

Step 2: Cut

Get your curtain bangs cut by a skilled stylist who can create a shape that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Step 3: Style

Apply a curl-enhancing cream or gel to your curls and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to style your bangs.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair

To maintain your curtain bangs with curly hair, it's important to keep them trimmed regularly to prevent them from getting too long or heavy. It's also important to use products that will enhance your curls and prevent frizz. You can also experiment with different styling techniques, such as twisting your bangs to create more definition.

FAQ

Can I Get Curtain Bangs with Tight Curls?

Yes, you can get curtain bangs with tight curls. It's important to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair and can create a shape that suits your hair texture.

How Often Should I Trim My Curtain Bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long or heavy.

What Products Should I Use to Style My Curtain Bangs?

You should use a curl-enhancing cream or gel to define your curls and prevent frizz. It's also a good idea to use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your bangs gently and avoid disrupting your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

To keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a few key hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair while it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Conclusion

If you have curly hair, you can definitely have curtain bangs! With the right cut, styling techniques, and products, you can achieve a trendy and versatile look that suits your hair type and face shape. Remember to find a skilled stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair, and to maintain your bangs with regular trims and proper hair care.
