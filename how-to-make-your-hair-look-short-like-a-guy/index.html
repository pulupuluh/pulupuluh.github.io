How To Make Your Hair Look Short Like A Guy

Written by Daniel May 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and want to try something new? Look no further than the short haircuts typically associated with men. Not only can they be stylish and low-maintenance, but they can also be a fun way to switch up your look. In this article, we’ll discuss how to make your hair look short like a guy, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Choose Your Cut
  • Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair
  • Step 3: Dry Your Hair
  • Step 4: Style Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

Step 1: Choose Your Cut

The first step in making your hair look short like a guy is choosing the right cut. Some popular options include the buzz cut, crew cut, and undercut. Talk to your stylist about which cut might work best for your face shape and hair type. Keep in mind that shorter cuts may require more frequent salon visits to maintain the shape.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Once you have your new cut, it’s important to properly wash and condition your hair to keep it looking healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won’t weigh down your hair. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently than someone with dry hair.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz. Next, use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a diffuser attachment to help define your curls.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now comes the fun part – styling your new short hair. Depending on the cut you chose, you may not need to do much styling at all. For a more polished look, consider using a styling product like gel, pomade, or wax. Start with a small amount of product and work it through your hair, using your fingers to style it into place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you have curly hair, consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls.
  • Be prepared to visit the salon more frequently to maintain the shape of your cut.

FAQs

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a short cut?

Depending on the length of your hair, you may still be able to wear it in a small ponytail or braid. However, keep in mind that shorter cuts may not allow for as much versatility in styling.

Will a short cut make my face look rounder?

The right short cut can actually help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Talk to your stylist about which cut might work best for your face shape.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Once you’ve achieved your desired short cut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy. This may include regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut, using a heat protectant before using hot tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. With the right care, your short hair can look stylish and chic for years to come.
