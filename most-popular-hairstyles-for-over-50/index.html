Most Popular Hairstyles For Over 50

Written by Christine Apr 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle to freshen up your look? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the most popular hairstyles for over 50, including how to achieve them, tips for maintenance, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents:

  • Short hair
  • Bob Haircut
  • Layered Hair
  • Pixie Haircut
  • Updo Hair
  • Hair Care

Short Hair

Short hair is a popular choice for women over 50 as it is low maintenance and can make you look younger. A short bob or pixie cut can add volume to thinning hair and create a sleek, modern look. To achieve this style, ask your stylist to cut your hair just above the chin or in a pixie cut. Use a volumizing spray to add body and texture, and a hair wax to define your style.

Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is a classic look that has been popular for decades. A bob can be tailored to suit any face shape and hair type, and can be worn straight or curly. To achieve this style, ask your stylist to cut your hair to just below the chin or in a longer, shoulder-length style. Use a straightening iron or curling wand to create your desired look, and finish with a shine serum or hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Layered Hair

Layered hair is a great choice for women over 50 as it can add volume and movement to thinning hair. Layers can be added to any haircut, whether it is short or long, and can be styled straight or curly. To achieve this style, ask your stylist to add layers throughout your hair, starting at the crown and working their way down. Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add body, and a hairspray to keep your style in place.

Pixie Haircut

The pixie haircut is a bold and daring choice for women over 50, but it can create a youthful and modern look. A pixie cut is short and choppy, with layers throughout the hair. To achieve this style, ask your stylist to cut your hair short and choppy, with layers throughout. Use a hair wax or pomade to define your style, and a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Updo Hair

An updo is a great choice for special occasions, and can be achieved with any hair length. A low bun or chignon can create an elegant and sophisticated look, while a messy updo can add a touch of glamour. To achieve this style, start by curling or straightening your hair. Then, gather it into a low bun or chignon, securing with bobby pins. Use a hairspray to keep your hair in place, and add accessories such as a hair clip or headband to complete your look.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a heat protectant spray when using a straightening iron or curling wand, and avoid using hot tools on wet hair. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and manageable.

Tips:

  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add body to thinning hair
  • Use a hair wax or pomade to define short styles
  • Use a hairspray to keep your style in place
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and manageable

FAQ:

  1. What is the best hairstyle for women over 50?

    2. The best hairstyle for women over 50 depends on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Short hair, layered hair, and bob haircuts are popular choices.

  2. What is a good haircut for thinning hair?

    3. A short bob or pixie cut can add volume to thinning hair, while layered hair can create movement.

  3. How often should I trim my hair?

    4. It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

  4. How can I make my hair shiny?

    5. Use a shine serum or hairspray to add shine to your hair, and avoid using too much heat or harsh chemicals.

In conclusion, there are many popular hairstyles for women over 50, including short hair, bob haircuts, layered hair, pixie haircuts, and updos. Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look for you!
