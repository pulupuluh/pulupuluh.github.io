Hairstyle For Rectangle Face Shape Male A Comprehensive Guide

If you have a rectangle face shape, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on hairstyle for rectangle face shape male. We will discuss everything from how to determine your face shape to the best hairstyles for your face shape. So, let's get started!

What is a Rectangle Face Shape?

A rectangle face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and a long face. The length of the face is more than the width, and the cheekbones are not very prominent. If you have a rectangle face shape, you can balance the proportions of your face by choosing the right hairstyle.

How to Determine Your Face Shape?

Before we discuss the best hairstyle for rectangle face shape male, it's essential to determine your face shape. Here's how you can do it:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a measuring tape to measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when measuring your face:

  • Measure from the widest point of your forehead to the widest point of your jawline to determine the width of your face.
  • Measure from the tip of your hairline to the bottom of your chin to determine the length of your face.

Best Hairstyles for Rectangle Face Shape Male

Now that you know your face shape let's discuss the best hairstyles for rectangle face shape male:

1. Short and Textured Hair

Short and textured hair is an excellent choice for rectangle face shape male as it adds volume to the top of the head and creates an illusion of a wider forehead. This hairstyle also draws attention away from the length of the face and towards the eyes and cheekbones.

2. Side Parting

Side parting is a classic hairstyle that works well for rectangle face shape male. It adds volume to the top of the head and creates an illusion of a wider forehead. You can also add some texture to the hair to create a more modern look.

3. Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a low maintenance hairstyle that works well for rectangle face shape male. It draws attention away from the length of the face and towards the eyes and cheekbones. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a clean and minimalist look.

4. Slicked Back Hair

Slicked back hair is a timeless hairstyle that works well for rectangle face shape male. It adds volume to the top of the head and creates an illusion of a wider forehead. This hairstyle also draws attention away from the length of the face and towards the eyes and cheekbones.

5. Messy Quiff

A messy quiff is a modern hairstyle that works well for rectangle face shape male. It adds volume to the top of the head and creates an illusion of a wider forehead. This hairstyle also draws attention away from the length of the face and towards the eyes and cheekbones.

Hair Care Tips for Rectangle Face Shape Male

Now that you know the best hairstyle for rectangle face shape male let's discuss some hair care tips:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo and conditioner.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Don't wash your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Wash your hair every other day or every three days.

3. Use a Leave-in Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished. This will also make your hair more manageable and easier to style.

4. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools like hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as they can damage your hair. If you must use these tools, use a heat protectant spray.

FAQs

1. What hairstyles should rectangle face shape male avoid?

Rectangle face shape male should avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head as it will make the face look longer. They should also avoid hairstyles with a center parting as it will make the face look narrower.

2. Can rectangle face shape male have long hair?

Yes, rectangle face shape male can have long hair, but they should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head. Long hair that is styled to the side or slicked back works well for rectangle face shape male.

3. What should I do if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, opt for short hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head. You can also use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner and a hair volumizing spray to add volume to your hair.

Conclusion

Rectangle face shape male can choose from a variety of hairstyles that work well for their face shape. Short and textured hair, side parting, buzz cut, slicked back hair, and messy quiff are some of the best hairstyles for rectangle face shape male. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right shampoo and conditioner and avoiding heat styling tools. With these tips, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.
