Best Curly Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to choosing a haircut for curly hair, it can be challenging to find the perfect one that suits your face shape. Heart-shaped faces, in particular, require a specific type of haircut to enhance their features. In this article, we will discuss the best curly haircuts for heart shaped faces, including a step-by-step guide on how to achieve them, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Curly Haircut for Heart Shaped Faces
  3. Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Best Curly Haircut for Heart Shaped Faces
  4. Tips for Maintaining the Best Curly Haircut for Heart Shaped Faces
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Advice for Curly Hair

Description

Heart shaped faces have a wider forehead and narrower jawline, with a pointed chin. The best curly haircuts for this face shape should balance out the proportions and highlight the cheekbones. The ideal curly haircut for heart shaped faces is one that adds volume and texture to the hair without overwhelming the face. It should also draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, while softening the jawline.

How to Choose the Best Curly Haircut for Heart Shaped Faces

The best curly haircut for heart shaped faces is one that complements the natural features of the face. When choosing a haircut, consider the following factors:

  • Face shape: Heart shaped faces require haircuts that balance out the proportions of the face
  • Hair texture: Curly hair requires a different type of haircut than straight hair
  • Hair length: The length of the hair can affect the overall appearance of the face
  • Personal style: Your haircut should reflect your personal style and preferences

Best Curly Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

There are several curly haircuts that work well for heart shaped faces:

  • Long layers
  • Shoulder-length curls
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Curly bob
  • Asymmetrical cut

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Best Curly Haircut for Heart Shaped Faces

Follow these steps to achieve the best curly haircut for heart shaped faces:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair
  2. Determine the best length and style for your face shape
  3. Decide on the type of layers, if any
  4. Choose a part that flatters your face shape
  5. Opt for side-swept bangs to soften the forehead
  6. Add volume and texture to the hair with a diffuser or curling iron
  7. Use styling products that enhance and define your curls

Tips for Maintaining the Best Curly Haircut for Heart Shaped Faces

Here are some tips for maintaining the best curly haircut for heart shaped faces:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid brushing curly hair, as it can cause frizz and breakage
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep curls hydrated
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible, and use a heat protectant if needed
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut

FAQ

What is the best curly haircut for heart shaped faces?

The best curly haircut for heart shaped faces is one that balances out the proportions of the face, highlights the cheekbones, and softens the jawline. Long layers, shoulder-length curls, side-swept bangs, curly bob, and asymmetrical cut are all great options.

Can I wear my curly hair short with a heart shaped face?

Yes, you can wear your curly hair short with a heart shaped face. However, it's important to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and adds volume and texture to the hair without overwhelming the face.

Hair Care Advice for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires specific care to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle hair when wet
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can cause frizz and dryness
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment every week to keep curls hydrated
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, alcohol, or silicones
  • Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry hair, as regular towels can cause frizz

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls that flatter your heart shaped face. Use the tips and advice in this article to find the best curly haircut for your face shape and keep your curls looking their best.
