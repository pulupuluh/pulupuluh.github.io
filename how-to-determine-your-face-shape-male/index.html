Description

Knowing your face shape is important when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle, glasses, and even beard style. However, determining your face shape can be a bit tricky, especially if you are a male. This article will guide you on how to determine your face shape male, step by step. It also includes some tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips for each face shape.

How To Determine Your Face Shape Male

Determining your face shape is all about measuring your facial features and analyzing them. There are seven different face shapes, including oval, square, rectangle, round, heart, diamond, and triangle. Here are the steps to determine your face shape male:

Step 1: Measure Your Forehead

Using a measuring tape, measure the distance between your eyebrows and your hairline at the widest point of your forehead. Write down the measurement.

Step 2: Measure Your Cheekbones

Measure the distance between the two cheekbones, just below the eyes. Write down the measurement.

Step 3: Measure Your Jawline

Measure the distance from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jawbone begins to angle upwards. Write down the measurement.

Step 4: Measure Your Face Length

Measure the distance from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin. Write down the measurement.

Step 5: Analyze Your Measurements

Now that you have your measurements, analyze them to determine your face shape. Here are the measurements for each face shape:

  • Oval: Your face length is greater than the width of your cheekbones, and your forehead is wider than your jawline.
  • Square: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Rectangle: Your face length is the greatest measurement, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Round: Your cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement, and your forehead and jawline are also similar in width.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, and your chin is narrow and pointed.
  • Diamond: Your face length is the greatest measurement, and your cheekbones are wider than your forehead and jawline, which are about the same width.
  • Triangle: Your jawline is wider than your cheekbones, which are wider than your forehead.

Tips

Once you have determined your face shape, here are some tips:

  • Oval: Most hairstyles and glasses will look good on you.
  • Square: Choose hairstyles with more volume on top and round glasses.
  • Rectangle: Choose hairstyles with volume on the sides and avoid glasses with sharp angles.
  • Round: Choose hairstyles with volume on top and angular glasses.
  • Heart: Choose hairstyles with volume on top and avoid glasses with sharp angles.
  • Diamond: Choose hairstyles with volume on the sides and avoid glasses with sharp angles.
  • Triangle: Choose hairstyles with volume on top and glasses with a straight top line.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about determining your face shape male:

What if my measurements are all similar?

If your measurements are all similar, then you have a square face shape.

What if my forehead is wider than my cheekbones?

If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, then you have a heart face shape.

What if my jawline is wider than my forehead?

If your jawline is wider than your forehead, then you have a triangle face shape.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, each face shape has different needs. Here are some tips:

  • Oval: Most hairstyles will look good on you, so experiment with different styles.
  • Square: Choose hairstyles with more volume on top to soften your angular features.
  • Rectangle: Choose hairstyles with volume on the sides to add width to your face.
  • Round: Choose hairstyles with volume on top and avoid styles that are too short on the sides.
  • Heart: Choose hairstyles with volume on top to balance out your narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Choose hairstyles with volume on the sides to add width to your face.
  • Triangle: Choose hairstyles with volume on top to balance out your wide jawline.

Now that you know how to determine your face shape male, you can choose the right hairstyle, glasses, and even beard style that will complement your features. Remember, there are no strict rules when it comes to fashion and grooming, so experiment with different styles and have fun!
