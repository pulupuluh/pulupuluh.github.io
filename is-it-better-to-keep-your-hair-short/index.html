Is It Better To Keep Your Hair Short

Written by Oliver Apr 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are someone who is always on the go and wants to make sure that your hair looks good at all times, then you may be asking yourself, "is it better to keep your hair short?" This is a valid question, and the answer may vary depending on your lifestyle and personal preferences. In this article, we will discuss the pros and cons of keeping your hair short, how to maintain short hair, and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Pros and Cons of Keeping Your Hair Short
  • How to Maintain Short Hair
  • Tips for Short Hair Care
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Pros and Cons of Keeping Your Hair Short

There are many advantages and disadvantages to having short hair. Here are some of the pros and cons:

Pros:

  • Low Maintenance: Short hair requires less maintenance and can be styled quickly and easily.
  • Less Damage: Short hair is less likely to sustain damage from heat styling tools and environmental factors.
  • Great for Active Lifestyles: Short hair is perfect for those who lead an active lifestyle and don't have time for long hair maintenance.
  • Can Enhance Facial Features: Short hair can help to enhance your facial features and make your face appear slimmer.

Cons:

  • Less Versatile: Short hair may limit your styling options compared to longer hair.
  • Requires Frequent Trims: Short hair requires more frequent trims to maintain the shape and style.
  • May Not Suit Everyone: Short hair may not suit everyone's face shape or personal style.

Ultimately, whether you should keep your hair short or not depends on your lifestyle, face shape, and personal preference.

How to Maintain Short Hair

Maintaining short hair is relatively easy, but there are a few things you need to keep in mind:

  • Regular Trims: To maintain the shape and style of your short hair, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks.
  • Use Quality Hair Products: Use high-quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Blow-Dry with a Round Brush: To add volume and texture to your short hair, use a round brush when blow-drying.
  • Avoid Heat Styling: Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips for Short Hair Care

Here are some additional tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

  • Wash Your Hair Regularly: Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid Over-Brushing: Brush your hair gently to avoid damaging it.
  • Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect Your Hair from the Sun: Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: It depends on the individual. Short hair can make some people look older, while it can make others look younger.

Q: Is short hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Short hair can be suitable for most face shapes, but it's important to choose a style that complements your features.

Q: Can I still style my short hair?

A: Yes, there are many styling options for short hair, such as adding texture with a sea salt spray or using a pomade to create a sleek look.

In conclusion, whether you should keep your hair short or not depends on your personal preference and lifestyle. If you're considering a shorter hairstyle, make sure to consider the pros and cons, and consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect style for you.
