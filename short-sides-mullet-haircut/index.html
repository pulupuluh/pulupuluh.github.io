Short Sides Mullet Haircut A Bold And Trendy Look

Written by Oliver Mar 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you ready to take your hairstyle to the next level? The short sides mullet haircut may be just what you need. This bold and trendy look has been making a comeback in recent years, and it's not hard to see why. In this article, we'll go through everything you need to know about this daring cut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The short sides mullet haircut is a modern take on the classic mullet. It features short hair on the sides and longer hair in the back. The length of the hair can vary, but typically it falls somewhere between the shoulders and the middle of the back. This cut is all about contrast, with the short sides creating a clean and sharp look while the longer back adds a touch of edginess.

How to

If you're thinking about getting a short sides mullet haircut, the first step is finding a skilled stylist who knows how to execute the look. From there, the process is relatively simple. The stylist will start by trimming the sides of your hair to the desired length. Then, they'll move on to the back, leaving the hair longer and gradually layering it to create texture and movement.

It's important to communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and style you want. Bring in pictures or examples to help them understand your vision. Once the cut is complete, you can style it in a variety of ways.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step breakdown of the process for getting a short sides mullet haircut:

  1. Find a skilled stylist who knows how to execute the look.
  2. Communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and style you want.
  3. The stylist will start by trimming the sides of your hair to the desired length.
  4. Next, they'll move on to the back, leaving the hair longer and gradually layering it to create texture and movement.
  5. Once the cut is complete, you can style it in a variety of ways.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind if you're considering a short sides mullet haircut:

  • Make sure you're comfortable with the attention that comes with this bold look.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type before committing to the cut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you.
  • Be prepared for regular trims to maintain the shape of the cut.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about the short sides mullet haircut:

Is the short sides mullet haircut suitable for all hair types?

While this cut can work on a variety of hair types, it may not be the best choice for very fine or thinning hair.

Can women rock a short sides mullet haircut?

Absolutely! This cut can be just as edgy and stylish on women as it is on men.

What styling products should I use for a short sides mullet haircut?

This will depend on your hair type and the look you're going for. Experiment with different products like pomades, waxes, and gels to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your short sides mullet looking its best, there are a few key hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to detangle and style your hair.
  • Regularly trim the sides and back of your hair to maintain the shape of the cut.

With the right care and styling, a short sides mullet haircut can be a bold and trendy look that's sure to turn heads. Whether you're a man or a woman, this cut is all about confidence and individuality. Are you ready to take the plunge?
