How To Get Messy Hair Look Guys A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both stylish and low-maintenance, the messy hair look is a great option. It's perfect for guys who want to achieve a carefree, effortless look that's still put-together. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to get messy hair look guys in easy steps, tips, and hair care.

How to Get Messy Hair Look Guys

The messy hair look is all about creating a relaxed, undone style that looks effortless. Here are some tips to achieve the perfect messy hair look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. Avoid using conditioner as it can weigh down your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Use a matte hair product, such as a clay or pomade. This will give your hair a natural, textured look without making it look greasy or shiny.
  • Work the product through your hair using your fingers, focusing on the roots and ends. Don't worry too much about getting it perfect â the messier, the better!
  • Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to add volume and texture to your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair as you dry it to create a messy, textured look.
  • Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the style in place without making it look stiff or crunchy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get messy hair look guys:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a matte hair product, such as a clay or pomade, to your hair.
  3. Work the product through your hair using your fingers, focusing on the roots and ends.
  4. Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to add volume and texture to your hair.
  5. Use your fingers to tousle your hair as you dry it to create a messy, textured look.
  6. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Hair Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your messy hair look:

  • Don't wash your hair every day. This can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it harder to style. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days instead.
  • Avoid using too much product. This can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Invest in a good quality hair product that's specifically designed for creating a messy hair look.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair instead of a brush. This will prevent your hair from looking too polished or neat.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a messy hair look:

Can I achieve a messy hair look with short hair?

Absolutely! The messy hair look works well with all hair lengths. Just make sure to use a hair product that's appropriate for your hair type and length.

What if my hair is naturally straight?

If you have naturally straight hair, you may need to use more product to create texture and hold. Try using a sea salt spray or a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

How do I avoid my hair looking too messy?

To avoid your hair looking too messy or unkempt, make sure to use a light hairspray to hold the style in place. You can also use your fingers to style your hair and create a more polished look.

Hair Care for Messy Hair Look

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, shiny hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often â aim to wash it every 2-3 days instead.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from becoming dry or damaged.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you do use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect messy hair look. Remember, the key to this style is to embrace the imperfections and go for a relaxed, effortless look. Good luck!
