Description of Heart Shaped Face

A heart shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. This face shape is often referred to as an inverted triangle, as the widest part of the face is at the forehead and the narrowest part is at the chin.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. Look for the following characteristics:

  • Round: Soft, curved lines with no sharp angles
  • Square: Angular jawline and forehead
  • Oval: Balanced proportions with a slightly curved jawline
  • Heart: Broad forehead and cheekbones with a narrow chin
  • Diamond: Narrow forehead and jawline with wide cheekbones

Good Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to find the perfect hairstyle. The goal is to balance out the width of the forehead and create volume at the bottom of the face. Here are some great options:

  • Long Layers: Soft, face-framing layers that hit at the cheekbones can help balance out a heart shaped face. Avoid layers that start at the chin or jawline, as they can accentuate the narrowness of the chin.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Bangs that sweep to the side can help create the illusion of a wider forehead, balancing out the face shape. Avoid straight, blunt bangs that cut across the forehead.
  • Bob: A chin length bob can help balance out a heart shaped face by adding volume at the bottom. Opt for a slightly angled bob that is longer in the front and shorter in the back.
  • Updo: A high updo or top knot can help elongate the face and balance out the forehead. Avoid low, tight buns that can accentuate the narrowness of the chin.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a volumizing product at the roots to add fullness to the top of the head.
  • Avoid center parts, as they can accentuate the width of the forehead.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying to create volume and movement.
  • Add waves or curls to the ends of the hair to add volume at the bottom of the face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for heart shaped faces:

  • Can I wear my hair straight? Yes, but avoid straight, blunt cuts that can accentuate the narrowness of the chin. Soft layers or a slight wave can help balance out the face shape.
  • Should I avoid short haircuts? Not necessarily. A chin length bob or pixie cut can be flattering on a heart shaped face, as long as it adds volume at the bottom of the face.
  • What about highlights? Highlights can be a great way to add dimension and movement to the hair. Opt for highlights that start at the mid-length of the hair and avoid highlights that start at the roots.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and hairstyle options, you'll be able to find the perfect look for your heart shaped face. Remember, the goal is to balance out the width of the forehead and add volume at the bottom of the face. Happy styling!
