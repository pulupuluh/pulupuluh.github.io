How To Use Thinning Shears On A Man A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thinning shears are a great tool for men who want to achieve a more natural and textured look. Whether you want to reduce bulk, add definition, or create layers, thinning shears can help you achieve your desired look. In this guide, we'll show you how to use thinning shears on a man, step by step.

Description

Thinning shears are scissors with teeth on one or both blades. These teeth are used to remove hair from specific areas of the head, creating a more natural and textured look. Thinning shears are great for men who have thick or coarse hair, as they can help to reduce bulk and make the hair more manageable.

How to Use Thinning Shears on a Man: Step by Step

Step 1: Clean and Dry Hair

Before you start using thinning shears on your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will help you to see the natural texture of your hair and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into smaller pieces. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any areas.

Step 3: Hold the Thinning Shears Correctly

Hold the thinning shears with the teeth facing upwards. This will ensure that the blades are cutting the hair at an angle, rather than straight across. This will create a more natural and textured look.

Step 4: Cut Small Sections of Hair

Start by cutting small sections of hair, rather than large chunks. This will help you to achieve a more even and natural look. Use the thinning shears to remove hair from specific areas of the head, such as the sides or the top.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

As you cut, regularly check your progress in the mirror. This will help you to ensure that you're getting an even cut and that you're achieving the desired look.

Step 6: Repeat the Process

Continue to cut small sections of hair and check your progress as you go. Repeat the process until you've achieved your desired look.

Tips for Using Thinning Shears on a Man

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Regularly check your progress in the mirror to ensure an even cut.
  • Use the thinning shears sparingly, as they can remove a lot of hair quickly.
  • Experiment with different techniques, such as point cutting or sliding cutting, to achieve different textures and looks.

FAQ

Can thinning shears be used on any type of hair?

Thinning shears are best suited for thick or coarse hair. If you have thin or fine hair, thinning shears may not be the best tool for you.

How often should I use thinning shears on my hair?

It's best to use thinning shears sparingly, as they can remove a lot of hair quickly. If you use them too often, you may end up with uneven or patchy hair.

Can I use thinning shears on my beard?

Yes, you can use thinning shears on your beard. However, it's important to be careful and to use them sparingly, as you don't want to remove too much hair.

Hair Care After Using Thinning Shears

After using thinning shears on your hair, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as much as possible.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.

Using thinning shears on a man can be a great way to achieve a natural and textured look. By following these steps and tips, you can use thinning shears to create your desired hairstyle. Just be sure to take care of your hair afterwards to ensure that it stays healthy and strong.
