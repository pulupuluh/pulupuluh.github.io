How To Style Soft Wavy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your soft wavy hair looking frizzy and unkempt? Fear not, because with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a gorgeous, beachy wave that will turn heads. In this guide, we will walk you through how to style soft wavy hair, step by step. We will also provide some useful tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you perfect your wavy hair look. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Soft Wavy Hair

The first step to styling soft wavy hair is to choose the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy hair, such as mousse or salt spray. These products will help define your waves and provide hold without weighing down your hair.

Next, it's important to properly prep your hair before styling. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then gently towel dry your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz. Instead, gently scrunch your hair with the towel to remove excess water.

Once your hair is towel-dried, apply a styling product to your hair. For soft waves, we recommend using a mousse or salt spray. Apply the product to your hair, then use your fingers to scrunch your hair from the ends up to the roots. This will help define your waves and create texture.

After applying product, you can either air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. If you choose to air dry your hair, avoid touching your hair until it is completely dry, as this can cause frizz.

If you choose to use a hair dryer, attach a diffuser to the end of the dryer. This will help distribute the heat more evenly and prevent your hair from becoming too hot. Use the diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching your hair as you go to help define your waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling soft wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair by gently scrunching it with a towel.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or salt spray, to your hair.
  4. Scrunch your hair from the ends up to the roots to define your waves.
  5. Air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  6. Scrunch your hair as you go to help define your waves.

Useful Tips

Here are some useful tips to help you achieve the perfect soft wavy hair:

  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair once it is dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from becoming tangled overnight.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve soft wavy hair if my hair is naturally straight?

A: Yes, you can still achieve soft wavy hair even if your hair is naturally straight. Use a salt spray or mousse to add texture and define your waves. You can also braid your hair when it is damp and leave it in overnight to create natural waves.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others may be able to go several days between washes. Experiment with different washing schedules to find what works best for your hair.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from becoming frizzy?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid touching your hair too much and use a gentle shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or oil to help smooth your hair and prevent frizz.

Hair Care

To keep your soft wavy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve soft, beautiful waves that will make you feel confident and stylish. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type and personal style. Happy styling!
