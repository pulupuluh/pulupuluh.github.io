How Long Does It Take To Grow Dreadlocks

Written by Oliver Jul 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Dreadlocks are one of the most popular hairstyles in the world. They are a great way to express yourself, and they can be styled in a variety of ways. However, one of the most common questions that people have is how long does it take to grow dreadlocks? The answer to this question is not straightforward, as it depends on a variety of factors. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about growing dreadlocks and how long it takes to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Grow Dreadlocks
  2. Step by Step Guide to Growing Dreadlocks
  3. Tips for Growing Dreadlocks Faster
  4. Frequently Asked Questions about Growing Dreadlocks
  5. Hair Care for Dreadlocks

How to Grow Dreadlocks

Growing dreadlocks is a process that requires time and patience. The first step is to decide on the type of dreadlocks you want. There are several different types of dreadlocks, including backcombed, twist and rip, and freeform. Once you have decided on the type of dreadlocks, you need to grow your hair to the desired length. This can take anywhere from a few months to a few years, depending on your hair type and how fast it grows.

It is important to note that growing dreadlocks is a commitment. You will need to dedicate time to maintain them and keep them clean. You will also need to be patient, as it can take several months for your hair to lock up and form dreadlocks.

Step by Step Guide to Growing Dreadlocks

If you are ready to grow your own dreadlocks, here is a step-by-step guide to get you started:

Step 1: Stop Brushing Your Hair

The first step in growing dreadlocks is to stop brushing your hair. This will allow your hair to start locking up and forming knots. You can still comb or finger detangle your hair, but avoid using a brush.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair Less Often

Washing your hair less often will help your hair to lock up faster. However, it is important to keep your scalp clean to prevent any buildup of oils or dirt. You can use a residue-free shampoo or a natural alternative like apple cider vinegar to clean your scalp.

Step 3: Separate Your Hair

As your hair starts to lock up, it is important to separate the sections to prevent them from growing together. You can do this by using a crochet hook or your fingers to separate the sections.

Step 4: Be Patient

Growing dreadlocks takes time, so it is important to be patient. It can take anywhere from six months to two years for your hair to fully lock up and form dreadlocks.

Tips for Growing Dreadlocks Faster

If you are looking to speed up the process of growing dreadlocks, here are some tips to help:

Tip 1: Keep Your Hair Healthy

Healthy hair grows faster, so it is important to take care of your hair. This includes eating a healthy diet, drinking plenty of water, and using natural hair products.

Tip 2: Use a Dreadlock Accelerator

There are several dreadlock accelerators on the market that can help your hair to lock up faster. These products contain enzymes and proteins that break down the oils and dirt in your hair, making it easier for your hair to lock up.

Tip 3: Use the Right Tools

Using the right tools can make a big difference in how fast your hair locks up. A crochet hook can help to separate the sections of your hair, while a dread sponge can help to create tighter, more defined dreadlocks.

Frequently Asked Questions about Growing Dreadlocks

How long does it take to grow dreadlocks?

The time it takes to grow dreadlocks depends on several factors, including your hair type, the type of dreadlocks you want, and how fast your hair grows. It can take anywhere from six months to two years for your hair to fully lock up and form dreadlocks.

Can you grow dreadlocks with short hair?

Yes, you can grow dreadlocks with short hair. However, it may take longer for your hair to lock up and form dreadlocks.

Do dreadlocks smell?

Dreadlocks can develop a smell if they are not properly maintained. It is important to wash your hair regularly and keep your scalp clean to prevent any buildup of oils or dirt.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks

Once you have grown your dreadlocks, it is important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for hair care for dreadlocks:

Tip 1: Keep Your Scalp Clean

It is important to keep your scalp clean to prevent any buildup of oils or dirt. You can use a residue-free shampoo or a natural alternative like apple cider vinegar to clean your scalp.

Tip 2: Avoid Using Heavy Products

Heavy products can weigh down your hair and make it harder for your hair to lock up. Use natural oils like coconut oil or jojoba oil to keep your hair moisturized.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair at Night

Protecting your hair at night can prevent any tangling or matting of your dreadlocks. You can use a satin or silk pillowcase, or wear a bonnet or headscarf to protect your hair while you sleep.

Now that you know how long it takes to grow dreadlocks and how to take care of them, you can confidently start your journey to achieving the perfect look. Remember to be patient, and enjoy the process of growing your own unique style.
