Men s Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides A Guide To The Relaxed Look

Written by Christine Jul 26, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle, a men's haircut long on top short on sides might be perfect for you. This versatile style can be adapted to suit different hair types and face shapes, and it's easy to style for a casual or formal look. In this article, we'll show you how to get the long on top short on sides haircut, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description of Men's Haircuts Long on Top Short on Sides

Men's haircuts long on top short on sides are characterized by a longer length on the top of the head and shorter sides and back. The length on the top can vary from a few inches to shoulder-length, depending on your preference and hair type. The sides and back are usually tapered or faded, creating a gradual transition from long to short hair. This haircut can be styled in many ways, such as slicked back, messy, curly, or straight.

How to Get a Men's Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides

If you're going to a barber or stylist, you can ask for a men's haircut long on top short on sides by showing them a picture of the style you want. Be specific about the length you want on the top and the type of fade or taper you prefer on the sides and back. If you're cutting your own hair, you'll need clippers, scissors, and a comb. Start by trimming the sides and back with a #2 or #3 clipper guard, then blend the hair into the longer hair on top with scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Men's Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides

Styling a men's haircut long on top short on sides is easy and simple. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Towel-Dry Your Hair

Begin by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water, but don't rub your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a pomade, wax, or gel. Use a small amount, starting from the back of your head and working your way forward. Distribute the product evenly with your fingers or a comb.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair according to your preference. You can slick it back, create a side part, comb it over, or leave it messy. Use a blow dryer if you want more volume or a sleeker look. Finish with a hairspray if you want extra hold.

Tips for Maintaining a Men's Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides

Here are some tips for maintaining a men's haircut long on top short on sides:

  • Trim the sides and back every 2-3 weeks to maintain the shape and length.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or styling with hot tools.
  • Consider getting regular haircuts from a professional stylist to keep your hair in top condition.

FAQ About Men's Haircuts Long on Top Short on Sides

What face shape is best for a men's haircut long on top short on sides?

A men's haircut long on top short on sides can suit different face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for men with oval or square faces. It can also balance out a long or narrow face by adding volume on top.

What hair types are best for a men's haircut long on top short on sides?

A men's haircut long on top short on sides can work for different hair types, such as straight, wavy, curly, or thick hair. However, it may not be suitable for men with very fine or thinning hair, as it can emphasize the lack of volume and texture.

Can I style a men's haircut long on top short on sides for a formal occasion?

Yes, a men's haircut long on top short on sides can be styled for a formal occasion by slicking back the hair and using a stronger hold product, such as a pomade or gel. You can also create a side part or a neat comb-over for a classic look.

Hair Care for Men's Haircuts Long on Top Short on Sides

To keep your men's haircut long on top short on sides looking its best, you should follow a hair care routine that suits your hair type and lifestyle. This may include washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a styling product that suits your hair texture and preference, and protecting your hair from environmental stressors and heat damage. You should also avoid over-styling or over-processing your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
