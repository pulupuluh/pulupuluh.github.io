Hairstyle For Oblong Face Female 2018 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oblong face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll walk you through the best hairstyles for oblong face females in 2018. From short to long, curly to straight, we've got all the tips and tricks you need to look your best. So let's get started!

Description

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a prominent forehead and a narrow chin. The best hairstyles for oblong face females are those that add width and volume to the sides of the face, while drawing attention away from the forehead.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face, it's important to consider your hair texture, length, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you find your perfect look:

Short Hairstyles

If you have short hair, try adding layers and texture to create volume on the sides of your face. A pixie cut with side-swept bangs can also be flattering for oblong face females. Avoid blunt cuts that make your face look longer.

Medium-Length Hairstyles

A shoulder-length bob or lob that's cut with layers and waves can add width to an oblong face. You can also try a half-up, half-down style with loose curls or waves to create volume at the sides of your face.

Long Hairstyles

If you have long hair, try adding layers or waves to create volume at the sides of your face. A side-parted style with loose waves or curls can also be flattering. Avoid straight, long hair that makes your face look even longer.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a wavy hairstyle that's perfect for oblong face females:

  1. Wash and condition your hair, then towel dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  4. Section your hair and curl it using a curling iron or wand. Start at the roots and curl away from your face.
  5. Once your hair is curled, run your fingers through it to loosen the waves.
  6. Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to create a tousled look.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face:

  • Try a side part to create asymmetry and draw attention away from your forehead.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle, but avoid blunt bangs that make your face look longer.
  • Avoid sleek, straight styles that make your face look even longer.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands or hair clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oblong face females:

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, but avoid pulling your hair straight back, which can elongate your face. Instead, try a side ponytail or a low, loose bun.

Q: Should I get a short or long hairstyle?

A: It depends on your personal style and hair texture. Generally, medium-length hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face are the most flattering for oblong face females.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight?

A: Yes, but try adding volume at the sides of your face with layers or waves.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day, or as needed.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like curling irons or straighteners, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face in 2018. Remember to choose a style that flatters your face shape and fits your personal style. Happy hairstyling!
