How To Cover Balayage Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have balayage hair and want to switch up your look, covering it can be a bit of a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll take you through the steps on how to cover balayage hair.

Description

Balayage is a popular hair coloring technique that creates a natural, sun-kissed look. It involves hand-painting highlights onto the hair, creating a graduated effect that looks like it has been lightened by the sun. While balayage looks great, it can be difficult to cover up if you decide you want a change.

How To

Here's how to cover balayage hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Color

The first step in covering balayage hair is to choose the right color. You'll need to decide whether you want to go darker, lighter, or stay the same color. If you're not sure, consult with a professional hair colorist to find the best shade for you.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start coloring your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. You should also apply a protective cream around your hairline and ears to avoid staining your skin.

Step 3: Mix the Color

Follow the instructions on the hair color kit to mix the color. Make sure you mix it thoroughly to avoid any uneven patches.

Step 4: Apply the Color

Apply the color to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips. Make sure you saturate your hair evenly with the color. Use a comb to distribute the color evenly and avoid any patches.

Step 5: Wait

Follow the instructions on the hair color kit for how long to leave the color on your hair. This will usually be around 30 minutes. While you're waiting, put on a shower cap to keep the color from dripping.

Step 6: Rinse

After the waiting time is up, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure you remove all the color from your hair.

Step 7: Condition

Apply a conditioner to your hair to keep it soft and silky. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse it out.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cover your balayage hair:

  • Choose a color that is close to your natural hair color to avoid any drastic changes.
  • Use a hair color kit that is specifically designed to cover highlights.
  • If you're not confident in coloring your hair yourself, consult a professional hair colorist.

FAQ

How long will it take to cover balayage hair?

It usually takes around 30 minutes to apply the color and 30 minutes to wait for it to develop.

Will covering balayage hair damage my hair?

Coloring your hair can be damaging, but using a hair color kit that is specifically designed to cover highlights will help minimize any damage.

Can I cover my balayage hair with a temporary hair color?

You can, but temporary hair color won't last as long as permanent hair color, and it may not cover your highlights completely.

Hair Care

After you've covered your balayage hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help preserve the color. Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause the color to fade. Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools, and try to avoid using them too often.

Now that you know how to cover balayage hair, you can experiment with different colors and styles. Remember to take care of your hair properly to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
