Best Cut For Round Fat Face A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Daniel Apr 19, 2023 · 11 min read

Having a round and chubby face can be a blessing and a curse. While it exudes youthfulness and cuteness, it can also make you look heavier and wider than you actually are. With the right haircut, however, you can enhance your features and create a more balanced and slimming effect. In this article, we’ll show you the best cut for round fat face and how to achieve it without sacrificing style and personality.

Description

A round fat face is characterized by a circular shape, soft and full cheeks, and a wide jawline. It lacks angularity and definition, which can make it appear wider and shorter than other face shapes. To counterbalance this, you need a hairstyle that adds height, length, and angles to your face. You also need to avoid styles that emphasize your roundness or make your face look heavier.

How to

Before choosing the best cut for round fat face, you need to consider your hair type, texture, and lifestyle. Not all hairstyles work for all people, so it’s important to consult with a professional hairstylist who can recommend the most suitable style for you. Here are some general tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for round fat face:

1. Add volume on top

Avoid flat, sleek hairstyles that cling to your face and make it look wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume on top, such as layered cuts, pixies, and bobs with layers. This will elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks and jawline.

2. Create angles and layers

Angular and layered hairstyles create the illusion of angles and depth, which can slim down your face and add movement to your hair. Avoid blunt and one-length cuts that make your face look wider and boxier.

3. Part your hair to the side

A side part can create a diagonal line that slims down your face and adds asymmetry to your look. Avoid center parts that divide your face in half and emphasize your roundness.

4. Avoid heavy bangs

While bangs can be cute and stylish, they can also make your face look shorter and rounder. If you want to have bangs, opt for wispy or side-swept bangs that frame your face and soften your features.

Step by step

Here’s how to achieve the best cut for round fat face:

1. Consult with a hairstylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, schedule a consultation with a hairstylist who specializes in round fat face haircuts. Bring photos of hairstyles that you like and be honest about your hair type, texture, and maintenance routine.

2. Choose the right cut

Based on your face shape and hair type, your hairstylist will recommend the best cut for round fat face. This may include layered cuts, asymmetrical bobs, long pixies, or shaggy hairstyles.

3. Add layers and angles

To create depth and movement, your hairstylist will add layers and angles to your haircut. This may involve texturizing, razor cutting, or point cutting techniques.

4. Style your hair

Once you’ve got the right cut, you can style your hair to enhance your features and flatter your face shape. This may involve blow drying, curling, straightening, or using hair products that add volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best cut for round fat face:

1. Embrace your natural texture

If you have curly or wavy hair, don’t be afraid to show off your natural texture. This can add volume and dimension to your hair and make your face look slimmer.

2. Use hair products wisely

Choose hair products that enhance your hair type and texture, but don’t weigh down your hair or create frizz. Use volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, or dry shampoo to add volume and thickness to your hair.

3. Consider face-framing highlights

Adding highlights or lowlights to your hair can create contrast and depth, which can elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Ask your hairstylist for face-framing highlights that accentuate your cheekbones and jawline.

4. Balance your proportions

When choosing a haircut, consider the proportions of your face and body. If you have a round and fat face, you may want to avoid short and tight hairstyles that make your body look heavier. Instead, opt for medium-length or long hairstyles that create balance and harmony.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round fat face?

The best haircut for a round fat face is a layered cut with volume on top and angles on the sides. This can elongate your face and create a slimming effect.

Can I have bangs with a round fat face?

Yes, you can have bangs with a round fat face, but you need to choose the right type of bangs. Avoid heavy and blunt bangs that make your face look shorter and rounder. Instead, opt for wispy or side-swept bangs that frame your face and soften your features.

How do I style my hair after getting a round fat face haircut?

You can style your hair after getting a round fat face haircut by adding volume and texture. Use hair products that add volume and hold, such as mousse, hairspray, or dry shampoo. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create height and movement. You can also curl or straighten your hair to add more texture and dimension.

Hair Care

After getting a round fat face haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.

2. Use heat protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as curling irons or flat irons, use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage and breakage.

3. Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut’s shape and style.

4. Protect your hair from the sun

Use a hat or hair products with SPF to protect your hair from the sun’s UV rays, which can dry out and damage your hair.

With the right cut for round fat face and proper hair care, you can enhance your features and feel confident and beautiful. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
