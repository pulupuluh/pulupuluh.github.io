Best Long Hairstyles For 50 Year Olds

Written by Frank Aug 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're 50 years old, you may be wondering what hairstyles are best suited for your age. With age comes wisdom, and there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you look and feel your best. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best long hairstyles for 50 year olds, including how to style them, tips, and hair care advice.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose its elasticity, which can make it more difficult to find the right hairstyle. However, there are plenty of long hairstyles that can work well for 50 year olds. Whether you have naturally curly hair, straight hair, or something in between, there's a long hairstyle out there that will work for you.

How to

The first step in finding the best long hairstyle for you is to consider your face shape. A long hairstyle can help to elongate a round face, while a layered cut can add volume to a square face. Once you know your face shape, you can start to explore different long hairstyles that will work well for you.

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have naturally curly hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that embraces your curls, such as loose waves or a curly bob. If you have straight hair, you may want to consider a sleek, straight hairstyle that shows off your length.

Step by Step

Once you've found the perfect long hairstyle for you, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some step-by-step instructions for some popular long hairstyles:

Loose Waves: Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's about 80% dry. Then, use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair, focusing on the ends. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Sleek and Straight: Start by washing your hair and blow drying it straight using a round brush. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any remaining waves or curls. Finish with a shine serum to add some extra shine.

Tips

When it comes to long hairstyles for 50 year olds, there are a few tips that can help you look and feel your best:

  • Don't be afraid to embrace your natural hair texture
  • Add layers to your hairstyle to add volume and movement
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair long at 50?

A: Absolutely! There's no age limit on long hair, and there are plenty of long hairstyles that are well-suited for 50 year olds.

Q: What's the best way to care for long hair?

A: To keep your long hair looking and feeling its best, it's important to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoid using hot tools too often, and get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential for keeping it looking and feeling its best. Here are some general hair care tips for 50 year olds:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong

By following these tips, you can keep your long hair looking and feeling its best well into your 50s and beyond.
