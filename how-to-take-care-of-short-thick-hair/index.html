How To Take Care Of Short Thick Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 01, 2023 · 7 min read

In this article, we will discuss the best ways to take care of short thick hair. Short thick hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right care and attention, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Take Care of Short Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Taking Care of Short Thick Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Short Thick Hair

How to Take Care of Short Thick Hair

Short thick hair requires proper care to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

1. Shampoo and Condition Regularly

One of the most important things you can do for your hair is to shampoo and condition it regularly. Use a gentle shampoo that is designed for your hair type, and follow up with a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

2. Use a Deep Conditioner

If your hair is thick and prone to dryness, using a deep conditioner once a week can help keep it healthy and moisturized. This will help prevent breakage and split ends, and keep your hair looking and feeling soft and smooth.

3. Don’t Overuse Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can be damaging to your hair if you use them too often. Try to limit your use of blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons, and always use a heat protectant spray to help minimize damage.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or as often as your stylist recommends.

5. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, especially if it’s exposed to it for long periods of time. Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun’s harmful rays, or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to help protect your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for taking care of short thick hair:

1. Shampoo your hair with a gentle shampoo designed for your hair type.

Massage the shampoo into your scalp, and rinse thoroughly with warm water.

2. Apply conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends.

Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes, and then rinse thoroughly with cool water.

3. Towel dry your hair gently.

Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel, as this can cause damage and breakage.

4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or serum to your hair.

This will help keep your hair moisturized and healthy, and prevent breakage.

5. Style your hair as desired.

Use heat styling tools sparingly, and always use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Tips for Taking Care of Short Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of short thick hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in nutrients that promote hair health, such as protein, vitamins A and C, and omega-3 fatty acids.

FAQs

Q: How often should I shampoo my short thick hair?

A: It’s best to shampoo your hair every 2-3 days, or as often as your hair type requires. If your hair is very oily, you may need to shampoo it more often.

Q: Can I use a deep conditioner on my short thick hair?

A: Yes, using a deep conditioner once a week can help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Q: How can I prevent breakage in my short thick hair?

A: To prevent breakage, be gentle when detangling your hair, avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

Hair Care Products for Short Thick Hair

Here are some hair care products that are great for short thick hair:

  • Gentle shampoo
  • Moisturizing conditioner
  • Deep conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Serum

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can keep your short thick hair looking healthy and beautiful.
