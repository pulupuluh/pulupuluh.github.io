How To Style Skirts With Tights Tips And Tricks

Written by William Aug 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old jeans and leggings? Well, it's time to spice up your wardrobe with skirts and tights! This classic combination not only looks stylish but also keeps you warm during the chilly months. In this article, we'll show you how to style skirts with tights and give you some tips and tricks to make your outfits stand out.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Skirts with Tights
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Skirts with Tights

Skirts and tights go together like peanut butter and jelly. There are many ways to style this classic combination, and it all depends on your personal style and the occasion. Here are some ideas to get you started:

  • Pair a mini skirt with opaque black tights and ankle boots for a chic and edgy look.
  • Wear a midi skirt with sheer tights and pumps for a feminine and elegant outfit.
  • Try a maxi skirt with patterned tights and sneakers for a casual and cool vibe.
  • Rock a leather skirt with fishnet tights and high heels for a daring and bold look.

Remember to play around with textures, colors, and patterns to create a unique and eye-catching outfit.

Step-by-Step Guide

Still unsure how to style skirts with tights? Follow these simple steps:

  1. Choose a skirt that flatters your body type and fits comfortably.
  2. Select tights that complement your skirt and match your shoes.
  3. Accessorize with jewelry, scarves, or hats to add some personality to your outfit.
  4. Finish off with shoes that match the occasion and your personal style.

That's it! You're ready to rock your skirt and tights.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips to make your skirt and tights outfit even more stylish:

  • Experiment with different lengths and styles of skirts and tights to find what works best for you.
  • Layer a sweater or jacket over your skirt for added warmth and dimension.
  • Try wearing colored tights or printed tights to add some fun and personality to your outfit.
  • Invest in high-quality tights that won't easily rip or tear.
  • Choose shoes that complement your skirt and tights, such as ankle boots, pumps, or sneakers.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling skirts with tights:

What shoes should I wear with skirts and tights?

It depends on the occasion and your personal style. For a casual look, try sneakers or ankle boots. For a dressier outfit, opt for pumps or high heels.

What color tights should I wear with a black skirt?

Black tights are a classic choice, but you can also experiment with colored or patterned tights to add some interest to your outfit.

Can I wear a patterned skirt with patterned tights?

Yes, you can! Just make sure that the patterns complement each other and don't clash.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, don't forget to care for your hair when styling skirts with tights. Here are some tips:

  • Wear a hat or headband to protect your hair from the cold and wind.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Try a sleek ponytail or messy bun for a quick and easy hairstyle.
  • Accessorize with hair clips or scrunchies to add some flair to your look.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style skirts with tights like a pro. So go ahead and experiment with different combinations to find your perfect outfit!
