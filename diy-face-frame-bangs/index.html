Diy Face Frame Bangs The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank May 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your boring hairstyle and want to try something new? Then why not give DIY face frame bangs a try? This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some dimension to their hair and frame their face. In this article, we'll show you how to create DIY face frame bangs, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

DIY face frame bangs are a great way to change up your hairstyle without having to drastically cut your hair. This style involves cutting shorter layers of hair around the face to create a "frame" effect. This is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn with any length of hair and is suitable for most face shapes.

How to

The first thing you'll need to do is gather your tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a fine-tooth comb, and a handheld mirror. It's important to use sharp scissors to avoid frayed ends and to get a clean cut.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. It's best to work with clean and dry hair to get the most accurate cut.

2. Use the fine-tooth comb to section off the hair that will become your bangs. This will be the hair that frames your face, so make sure to section it off accordingly.

3. Use the handheld mirror to hold up to the back of your head. This will give you a clear view of the front of your hair and where you'll be cutting.

4. Using your scissors, cut the hair in a straight line, starting from the middle of your forehead and working your way outwards. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much hair.

5. Once you've finished cutting, use the fine-tooth comb to trim any uneven pieces or to adjust the length of your bangs.

6. Style your new face frame bangs to your liking!

Tips

1. Always start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.

2. Use a mirror to check your progress as you cut. This will help you see any areas that need more attention.

3. If you have curly hair, it's best to cut your bangs when your hair is dry. This will give you a more accurate cut.

4. If you're unsure about cutting your own bangs, ask a friend or family member to help you.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with face frame bangs?

A: Yes! Face frame bangs are versatile and can be styled in many ways, including updos.

Q: Will face frame bangs work with my face shape?

A: Yes! Face frame bangs are suitable for most face shapes and can be adjusted to suit your individual needs.

Q: Do I need to use special scissors for cutting my hair?

A: Yes, it's important to use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid frayed ends and to get a clean cut.

Hair Care

Now that you've got your new face frame bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

3. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

4. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to maintain your face frame bangs.

Congratulations! You've now learned how to create your own DIY face frame bangs. Remember to be patient and take your time when cutting your hair, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Have fun and enjoy your new hairstyle!
