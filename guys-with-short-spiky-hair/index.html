Guys With Short Spiky Hair A Trendy Look That Never Goes Out Of Style

Written by Christine Jul 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are looking for a cool and trendy hairstyle, then the short spiky hair is the perfect option for you. This hairstyle is popular among guys of all ages and can add a modern touch to your overall look. In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting and maintaining short spiky hair.

How to Get Short Spiky Hair

The first step in getting short spiky hair is to choose the right haircut. You can either ask your hairstylist for a short and layered cut or experiment with different lengths on your own. Once you have the right cut, you can start styling your hair into spikes.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Spiky Hair

Step 1: Start with clean and dry hair. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and hydrated.

Step 2: Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your hair. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a comb or your fingers to create spikes. Start at the front of your hair and work your way back. Use your fingers to shape and define the spikes.

Step 4: Finish with a hairspray. This will help keep your spikes in place all day long.

Tips for Maintaining Short Spiky Hair

Tip 1: Avoid washing your hair too frequently. This can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

Tip 2: Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for menâs hair. This will help keep your hair healthy and strong.

Tip 3: Get regular haircuts to maintain your short spiky hair. This will help keep your hair looking fresh and stylish.

FAQs about Short Spiky Hair

Q. How often should I wash my short spiky hair?

A. You should wash your short spiky hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Q. Can I style my short spiky hair without gel or wax?

A. Yes, you can style your short spiky hair without gel or wax. However, using a styling product will help keep your spikes in place and add texture to your hair.

Q. Is short spiky hair suitable for all face shapes?

A. Short spiky hair can be suitable for most face shapes. However, it is best to consult with your hairstylist to find the right cut that complements your face shape.

Hair Care for Short Spiky Hair

Short spiky hair requires minimal maintenance, but it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care:

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner

- Avoid using hot styling tools too frequently

- Get regular haircuts to maintain your short spiky hair

- Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf

By following these tips and tricks, you can rock the short spiky hair look with confidence.
