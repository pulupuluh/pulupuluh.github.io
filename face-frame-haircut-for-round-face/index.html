Face Frame Haircut For Round Face

Written by Christine May 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a haircut that flatters your round face? A face frame haircut might be just what you need! This trendy haircut can create the illusion of a slimmer and more angular face by emphasizing your features. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to get a face frame haircut for a round face, along with tips and hair care advice.

Description

A face frame haircut is a layered haircut that accentuates your face. The layers are cut around the face, creating a frame that highlights your features. This haircut can work for any face shape, but it is especially flattering for a round face. By adding layers around the face, it creates the illusion of angles and slims down your face.

How To

If you're interested in getting a face frame haircut, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consultation

Before getting any haircut, it's always a good idea to consult with your hairstylist. They can give you advice on what type of face frame haircut will suit your face shape best.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow-dry it. Make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 4: Cut the Layers

Start with the bottom layer and cut it to the desired length. Then, move on to the next layer, cutting it slightly shorter than the previous layer. Continue this process until you reach the top layer.

Step 5: Cut the Face Frame

For the face frame, take the front sections of your hair and create a diagonal parting. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the cheekbones and ending at the chin. This will create a face-framing effect that emphasizes your features.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

After cutting your hair, style it using a round brush and a blow dryer. Use a straightener or curling iron to create loose curls or waves. Finish with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a face frame haircut:

  • Choose a hairstylist who has experience cutting face frame haircuts.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to enhance the layers and create more dimension.
  • Keep your hair hydrated by using a deep conditioner once a week.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about face frame haircuts:

What is the difference between a layered haircut and a face frame haircut?

A layered haircut involves cutting the hair into layers throughout the head, whereas a face frame haircut focuses on layering the hair around the face to create a framing effect.

Can a face frame haircut work for any hair length?

Yes, a face frame haircut can work for any hair length, whether it's short, medium, or long.

How often should I get a face frame haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your face frame haircut.

Hair Care

After getting a face frame haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and frizz-free.

A face frame haircut is a great way to flatter a round face and emphasize your features. By following these steps, tips, and hair care advice, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.
