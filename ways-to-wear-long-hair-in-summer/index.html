Ways To Wear Long Hair In Summer Keep Your Mane Stylish And Cool

Written by Frank Mar 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Summer is here and long hair can be a bit of a hassle during the hot season. However, there are a lot of ways to keep your hair stylish and cool at the same time. Here are some ideas on ways to wear long hair in summer:

Table of Contents

  • Braids
  • Top Knot
  • Ponytail
  • Beach Waves
  • Accessories
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Braids

Braids are perfect for summer as they keep your hair off your face and neck. There are a lot of braids to choose from, such as the classic three-strand braid, fishtail braid, and Dutch braid. You can also add some accessories like flowers or ribbons to give your braids a summer vibe.

How to:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Choose the type of braid you want to do.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Start braiding from the top and work your way down.
  5. Secure the end with an elastic band.
  6. Add accessories if you like.

Tips:

  • Braids work best on second-day hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Loosen the braid for a more relaxed and bohemian look.

Top Knot

A top knot is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for summer. It is easy to do and keeps your hair off your neck and face, making it a great option for hot and humid days.

How to:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the elastic band.
  3. Secure the knot with bobby pins.

Tips:

  • Use a scrunchie instead of an elastic band to prevent hair breakage.
  • Leave some strands of hair loose for a more relaxed look.
  • Add some hair accessories like a headband or scarf to jazz up your top knot.

Ponytail

A ponytail is a classic hairstyle that can be worn in different ways. It is perfect for summer as it keeps your hair off your neck and face, making it a great option for hot and humid days.

How to:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Choose the height and style of your ponytail.
  3. Gather your hair into a ponytail using an elastic band.
  4. Secure any loose strands with bobby pins.

Tips:

  • Use a scrunchie instead of an elastic band to prevent hair breakage.
  • Loosen the ponytail for a more relaxed and casual look.
  • Add some hair accessories like a headband or scarf to jazz up your ponytail.

Beach Waves

Beach waves are perfect for summer as they give your hair a natural, effortless look. You can achieve beach waves with a curling iron, flat iron, or even braids.

How to:

  1. Start by applying a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to your hair.
  2. Section your hair and start curling or braiding.
  3. Leave the curls or braids for a few hours or overnight.
  4. Unravel the curls or braids and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the waves.

Tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using a curling or flat iron.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair after unraveling the curls or braids.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and tame frizz.

Accessories

Accessories can add some fun and personality to your summer hairstyles. Here are some ideas:

  • Headbands
  • Hair clips
  • Bobby pins with designs
  • Hair scarves
  • Flower crowns

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair in summer?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less frequently. If you swim a lot or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Q: How do I protect my hair from the sun?

A: You can wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF. You can also apply a hair mask or oil to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from getting frizzy in summer?

A: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, avoid using hot tools frequently, and use a hair oil or serum to tame frizz.

Hair Care

Summer can be harsh on your hair, so it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools frequently.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to tame frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or hair product with SPF.
  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.

These are just some ideas on ways to wear long hair in summer. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair this season!
