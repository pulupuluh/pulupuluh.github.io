How To Cut A Curly Bob Hairstyle Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read
If you're thinking about getting a new haircut, why not try a curly bob hairstyle? It's a trendy and low-maintenance style that looks great on different hair types and face shapes. In this article, we will show you how to cut a curly bob hairstyle step by step. We will also provide some tips on hair care and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Cut a Curly Bob Hairstyle

Cutting a curly bob hairstyle may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful result. Here are some things you'll need:
  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle
  • Mirror

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut a curly bob hairstyle:
  1. Wash and condition your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Part your hair in the middle and comb it straight. Then divide it into four sections using hair clips.
  3. Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair and cut it at an angle. Use the length you desire as a guide. Repeat this process for the rest of the hair in that section.
  4. Release the next section of hair and repeat the same process as before. Do this for all sections of hair, working from the back to the front.
  5. Once you're done with cutting, shake your hair and check if it's even. Make any necessary adjustments.
  6. You can also add some layers to your bob if you desire. Take a small section of hair and cut it diagonally. This will add texture and volume to your hair.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting a Curly Bob

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curly bob:
  • Make sure your hair is wet when cutting. This will help you see the natural curls and how they fall.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry if you want a more precise cut.
  • Start cutting your hair longer than your desired length. You can always go shorter, but you can't go longer once you've cut it.
  • Use sharp scissors to get a clean cut.
  • Take small sections of hair when cutting to avoid making mistakes. You can always cut more if needed.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to take your time and cut a little bit at a time.

FAQs

How often should I cut my curly bob?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it gets damaged. On average, you should cut your curly bob every 8-12 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Can I cut my curly bob myself?

You can cut your curly bob yourself, but it's recommended to go to a professional hairstylist. They have the expertise and tools to give you the best results.

Do I need to use any specific products for my curly bob?

Yes, curly hair requires special care and products. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner, curl cream, or gel to define and enhance your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Bobs

To keep your curly bob healthy and beautiful, here are some hair care tips:
  • Don't over-wash your hair. Curly hair is naturally dry, so washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. Don't use a brush, as it can break your hair and cause frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair. If you need to use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your hair's shape.

Conclusion

Cutting a curly bob hairstyle can be fun and easy if you follow the right steps and use the right tools. Remember to take your time, start with small sections of hair, and make sure your hair is wet. Don't forget to use the right hair care products to keep your curly bob healthy and beautiful.
