How To Tame Frizzy Hair Naturally A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Aug 13, 2023 · 9 min read

Frizzy hair can be a real pain to deal with, especially during humid weather. But don't worry, there are natural ways to tame your frizzy hair without having to use any harsh chemicals or expensive hair treatments. In this guide, we'll show you how to tame frizzy hair naturally and provide you with some useful tips and tricks to keep your hair looking smooth and shiny.

How to Tame Frizzy Hair Naturally

Here are some natural ways to tame frizzy hair:

1. Use a Natural Hair Mask

One of the best ways to tame frizzy hair is to use a natural hair mask. You can make a hair mask at home by mixing ingredients like avocado, honey, and coconut oil. Apply the mask to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it off with lukewarm water. This will help to nourish and moisturize your hair, making it less prone to frizz.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb is a great way to detangle your hair without causing any damage. This is important because damaged hair is more likely to be frizzy. Start by combing your hair from the ends and work your way up to the roots.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Leave-in conditioners are great for taming frizzy hair because they help to lock in moisture. Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing it and comb it through with a wide-tooth comb.

4. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce frizz because it causes less friction than a cotton pillowcase. This means that your hair is less likely to tangle and become frizzy while you sleep.

5. Avoid Using Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz. If you must use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Step by Step Guide to Tame Frizzy Hair Naturally

Here is a step by step guide to tame frizzy hair naturally:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it more prone to frizz.

Step 2: Use a Natural Hair Mask

After washing your hair, apply a natural hair mask and leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it off with lukewarm water.

Step 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Once you've rinsed out the hair mask, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Step 4: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair and comb it through with a wide-tooth comb.

Step 5: Dry Your Hair

Gently pat your hair dry with a towel and avoid rubbing it vigorously, as this can cause frizz.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

If you must use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Tips to Tame Frizzy Hair Naturally

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking smooth and shiny:

1. Use a Satin or Silk Scarf

Covering your hair with a satin or silk scarf can help to reduce frizz caused by friction.

2. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, making it more prone to frizz. Try to wash your hair every other day instead of every day.

3. Use a Natural Hairbrush

Using a natural hairbrush made of boar bristles can help to distribute your hair's natural oils, making it less prone to frizz.

4. Avoid Using Hot Water

Hot water can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it more prone to frizz. Try to use lukewarm or cool water instead.

5. Drink Plenty of Water

Staying hydrated is important for healthy hair. Make sure to drink plenty of water throughout the day.

FAQ

Q: Can I use natural oils to tame frizzy hair?

A: Yes, natural oils like coconut oil, argan oil, and jojoba oil can help to tame frizzy hair. Apply a small amount of oil to your hair and comb it through with a wide-tooth comb.

Q: How often should I use a hair mask?

A: You can use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Q: Will cutting my hair help to tame frizz?

A: Yes, getting regular haircuts can help to keep your hair healthy and reduce frizz.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important for keeping it healthy and reducing frizz. Here are some tips for hair care:

1. Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it more prone to frizz. Use a sulfate-free shampoo instead.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to nourish and moisturize your hair.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids can cause breakage and frizz. Avoid wearing your hair in tight hairstyles for long periods of time.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

By following these natural tips and taking good care of your hair, you can tame frizzy hair naturally and keep it looking smooth and shiny.
