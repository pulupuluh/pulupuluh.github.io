Best Haircut For Fine Wavy Hair And Square Face

Written by Luffy Aug 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your fine, wavy hair and square face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for your hair type and face shape, along with tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and stunning.

Description

Fine, wavy hair can be a challenge to style, especially if you have a square face. A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, and your goal is to soften those features with the right haircut. The best haircut for this hair type and face shape is one that adds volume, texture, and movement, while also creating a balance between your facial features.

How To

The best haircut for fine, wavy hair and square face is a layered bob. This style adds volume to your hair while also creating a balance between your facial features. Here's how to achieve this look:

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist

Before getting a layered bob, consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and layers for your hair type and face shape. They will be able to provide expert advice on what will work best for you.

Step 2: Start with a clean slate

Wash your hair thoroughly with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections and start cutting from the bottom layer. Cut your hair to the desired length and add layers to create volume and movement.

Step 4: Style your hair

To style your hair, use a curling wand to create loose waves throughout your hair. Finish with a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stunning:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.
  • Use a curling wand to create loose waves throughout your hair to add texture and movement.
  • Finish with a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a layered bob work for all face shapes?

A: No, a layered bob is best for those with a square face shape. If you have a different face shape, consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and stunning, it is important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

Conclusion

The best haircut for fine, wavy hair and square face is a layered bob. This style adds volume, texture, and movement, while also creating a balance between your facial features. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and layers for your hair type and face shape. Follow these tips and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stunning.
