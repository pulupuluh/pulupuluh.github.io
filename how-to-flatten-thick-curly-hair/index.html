How To Flatten Thick Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Do you struggle with thick curly hair that seems to have a life of its own? Taming those curls can seem impossible, but fear not! In this article, we'll explore tips and tricks for flattening thick curly hair that will have you looking and feeling your best. From how to prepare your hair to the best products to use, we'll cover everything you need to know. So, let's get started!

How to Flatten Thick Curly Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about some general tips for flattening thick curly hair. First and foremost, it's important to have the right products on hand. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they will be more effective in taming your curls. Additionally, consider investing in a hair straightener or flat iron to help create a sleek, straight look.

Another important aspect of flattening thick curly hair is the preparation process. Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. You can blow dry your hair straight or let it air dry. If you're using a blow dryer, be sure to use a diffuser attachment to help prevent frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have some general tips in mind, let's go over a step-by-step guide for flattening thick curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. Be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.
  2. Let your hair air dry or blow dry it straight with a diffuser attachment.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the flat iron.
  4. Section your hair into manageable sections using clips.
  5. Starting at the roots, use your flat iron to straighten each section of hair. Be sure to move slowly and use a low to medium heat setting to avoid damage to your hair.
  6. Once you've straightened all of your hair, apply a smoothing serum or oil to help keep your hair sleek and smooth.
  7. Finish off your look with a light hairspray to help keep your hair in place.

Tips for Flattening Thick Curly Hair

Here are a few additional tips for flattening thick curly hair:

  • Be patient! It may take some practice to get the hang of using a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about flattening thick curly hair:

1. Can I flatten my hair without using a flat iron?

While a flat iron is the most effective tool for flattening thick curly hair, you may be able to achieve a similar look using a hair straightening brush or blow dryer with a round brush attachment.

2. How often should I straighten my hair?

It's best to limit heat styling to once or twice a week to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some general tips for caring for thick curly hair:

  • Use products specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a deep conditioner or hair mask once a week to help moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make your hair more prone to breakage.

With these tips and tricks in mind, you'll be able to flatten your thick curly hair like a pro. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you'll be rewarded with sleek, smooth locks that will turn heads wherever you go!
