Short Haircuts That Make You Look Younger

Written by Petter Jul 19, 2023 Â· 10 min read

As we age, our hair begins to change. It may become thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. One way to combat these changes and look younger is to try a shorter haircut. Short haircuts can give you a fresh look and make you appear more youthful. In this article, we'll cover some of the best short haircuts that make you look younger.

Table of Contents

  • Pixie Cut
  • Bob Cut
  • Lob Cut
  • Shag Cut
  • Faux Hawk Cut

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless classic that has been popular for decades. It's a short, cropped haircut that is perfect for women who want to embrace their natural beauty and look younger. The pixie cut is low maintenance and easy to style. It's also great for women with thinning hair, as it can create the illusion of volume.

To get a pixie cut, you'll need to visit a stylist who specializes in short haircuts. They'll be able to help you find the perfect length and style for your face shape and hair type. Once you have your pixie cut, you can style it with a little bit of product and a blow dryer. You can also add some texture with a texturizing spray or pomade.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another classic haircut that never goes out of style. It's a short to medium length haircut that is perfect for women who want to look younger and more sophisticated. The bob cut can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and wavy.

To get a bob cut, you'll need to choose the length that works best for you. A stylist can help you decide whether a chin-length bob or a shoulder-length bob is best for your face shape and hair type. Once you have your bob cut, you can style it with a straightening iron or a curling iron to create different looks.

Lob Cut

The lob cut is a longer version of the bob cut, and it's perfect for women who want a little more length but still want a short haircut. The lob cut is usually shoulder-length or slightly longer, and it can be styled in a variety of ways.

To get a lob cut, you'll need to choose the length that works best for you. A stylist can help you decide whether a shoulder-length lob or a longer lob is best for your face shape and hair type. Once you have your lob cut, you can style it with a straightening iron or a curling iron to create different looks.

Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered, messy haircut that is perfect for women who want a youthful, carefree look. The shag cut is usually shoulder-length or slightly longer, and it's great for women with thin or fine hair.

To get a shag cut, you'll need to visit a stylist who specializes in layered haircuts. They'll be able to help you find the perfect length and style for your face shape and hair type. Once you have your shag cut, you can style it with a texturizing spray or pomade to create a messy, tousled look.

Faux Hawk Cut

The faux hawk cut is a short, edgy haircut that is perfect for women who want to make a statement. It's a great way to add some personality to your look and make you appear more youthful. The faux hawk cut is usually shorter on the sides and longer on top.

To get a faux hawk cut, you'll need to visit a stylist who specializes in short, edgy haircuts. They'll be able to help you find the perfect length and style for your face shape and hair type. Once you have your faux hawk cut, you can style it with a little bit of product and a blow dryer to create a spiky look.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of which short haircut you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and youthful. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

FAQ

What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

A pixie cut can work for any face shape, but it's especially flattering for women with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Will a bob cut make me look younger?

Yes, a bob cut can make you look younger and more sophisticated. It's a classic haircut that never goes out of style.

Can I style a lob cut in different ways?

Yes, a lob cut can be styled in a variety of ways. You can use a straightening iron or a curling iron to create different looks.

Is a shag cut good for thin or fine hair?

Yes, a shag cut is great for women with thin or fine hair. The layers can create the illusion of volume and make your hair appear fuller.

Will a faux hawk cut work for me?

A faux hawk cut can work for any face shape, but it's especially flattering for women with round or square faces.

In conclusion, there are many short haircuts that can make you look younger and more vibrant. Whether you choose a pixie cut, a bob cut, a lob cut, a shag cut, or a faux hawk cut, make sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and youthful. With the right haircut and some basic hair care, you can look and feel your best at any age.
