How To Make A Pixie Cut Curly A Step By Step Guide

Are you looking to add some curls to your pixie cut? If so, you're in luck! With the right tools and techniques, you can easily transform your straight pixie into a curly masterpiece. In this article, we'll go over how to make a pixie cut curly, including tips, FAQ, and hair care.

How to Make a Pixie Cut Curly

There are several ways to make a pixie cut curly, including using hot tools, braiding your hair, and using rollers. No matter which method you choose, it's important to start with clean, dry hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. 2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water, then apply a curl-enhancing mousse or gel. 3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching it with your hands as you go. 4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to add more defined curls to your hair. 5. Alternatively, you can braid your hair in small sections while it's damp and allow it to air dry overnight. In the morning, remove the braids and use your fingers to separate the curls. 6. You can also use small, foam rollers to curl your hair. Apply a styling product to your hair, then wrap small sections of hair around the rollers. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight, then remove and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

Tips for Making a Pixie Cut Curly

- Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to add more definition to your curls. - Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair before drying to help hold the curls. - Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, as this will help prevent frizz. - If using a curling iron or wand, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. - Experiment with different techniques, such as braiding or using rollers, to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can you make a pixie cut curly without heat? A: Yes, you can braid your hair while it's damp and allow it to air dry overnight for natural-looking curls. Q: What products should I use to make my pixie cut curly? A: Look for products specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, mousse, and gel. Q: How long will my curls last? A: This will depend on your hair type and the technique you use, but curls typically last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Pixie Cuts

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils. - Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. - Avoid brushing your hair, as this can disrupt your curls and cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. - Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage. - Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain your pixie cut. By following these tips and techniques, you can easily make your pixie cut curly and create a fun, playful look. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to embrace your natural curls!
