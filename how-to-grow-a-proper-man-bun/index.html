How To Grow A Proper Man Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your short hair and looking for a new hairstyle? A man bun might be the perfect look for you! However, growing a proper man bun takes time and effort. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to grow a proper man bun. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow a Proper Man Bun
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing a Man Bun
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for a Proper Man Bun

How to Grow a Proper Man Bun

A proper man bun requires long hair, typically shoulder length or longer. Growing a man bun can take anywhere from 6 months to a year, depending on how fast your hair grows. The key to growing a proper man bun is patience and consistency in hair care. Here are some tips to help you grow a proper man bun:

1. Start with a good haircut

Before growing a man bun, start with a good haircut. Ask your barber to give you a trim and shape your hair to suit your face shape. This will make growing your hair out easier and will help you avoid awkward stages.

2. Be patient

As mentioned earlier, growing a proper man bun requires patience. It can be tempting to cut your hair short when it gets frustrating, but resist that urge! Keep in mind that it takes time to grow long hair.

3. Use hair products

Using hair products can help you maintain your hair and promote growth. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. You can also use hair oils and leave-in conditioners to help promote growth.

4. Maintain a healthy diet

A healthy diet is crucial for healthy hair growth. Eat foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, zinc, and biotin. These nutrients help promote hair growth and keep your hair healthy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know how to grow a proper man bun, here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with a good haircut

Before growing a man bun, start with a good haircut. Ask your barber to give you a trim and shape your hair to suit your face shape.

Step 2: Be patient and let your hair grow

Let your hair grow out to shoulder length or longer. Be patient and avoid cutting your hair short.

Step 3: Use hair products to maintain your hair

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. You can also use hair oils and leave-in conditioners to help promote growth.

Step 4: Tie your hair into a bun

Once your hair is long enough, tie it into a bun. Use a hair tie or a scrunchie to secure the bun.

Step 5: Maintain your man bun

Once you have a man bun, maintain it by using hair products and keeping it clean. You can also trim the ends periodically to keep it looking neat.

Tips for Growing a Man Bun

Here are some additional tips for growing a proper man bun:

  • Be patient and avoid cutting your hair short
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth
  • Use hair products to maintain your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat

FAQs

How long does it take to grow a man bun?

It can take anywhere from 6 months to a year to grow a proper man bun, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Do I need to use hair products?

Using hair products can help you maintain your hair and promote growth. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. You can also use hair oils and leave-in conditioners to help promote growth.

Hair Care for a Proper Man Bun

Once you have a man bun, it’s important to maintain a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Use hair oils and leave-in conditioners to promote growth and keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair
  • Trim the ends periodically to keep your man bun looking neat

With these tips and a little patience, you can grow a proper man bun that will turn heads wherever you go!
