Mens Haircuts Long Hair On Top The Ultimate Guide For 2023

If you're looking for a trendy, stylish and versatile haircut, then mens haircuts long hair on top might be the perfect option for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity over the past few years, and it's not hard to see why. Long hair on top allows for various styling options, from slicked back to messy and textured, making it an ideal choice for men who like to switch up their look. In this blog post, we'll explore everything you need to know about mens haircuts long hair on top, from how to get the style to hair care tips and FAQs.

How to Get Mens Haircuts Long Hair on Top

The first step to getting mens haircuts long hair on top is to find a skilled and experienced hairstylist who understands your vision. During the consultation, you can discuss the length and style you want, as well as the best products to use to achieve the look you desire. Keep in mind that mens haircuts long hair on top work best for men with thick, wavy or curly hair, but can also be adapted for men with straight hair.

Types of Mens Haircuts Long Hair on Top

There are several types of mens haircuts long hair on top, including:

  • Slicked back
  • Messy and textured
  • Undercut
  • Pompadour
  • Side part

Each of these styles has its unique features and requires different techniques to achieve. You can discuss these options with your hairstylist to determine which one suits your face shape, lifestyle and personal preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mens Haircuts Long Hair on Top

Once you have your mens haircuts long hair on top, the next step is to learn how to style it correctly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade, wax or gel, to your fingertips.
  3. Distribute the product evenly through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to create your desired style. For a slicked-back look, comb your hair straight back. For a messy and textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to add hold and volume.

Remember, mens haircuts long hair on top work best when you keep the sides and back short or shaved. This contrast creates a balanced, stylish look.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Haircuts Long Hair on Top

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens haircuts long hair on top:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair trimmed and shaped.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products that suit your hair type and style.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hairdryers, straighteners or curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind and pollution by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs About Mens Haircuts Long Hair on Top

1. How long should my hair be for mens haircuts long hair on top?

The length of your hair depends on the style you want to achieve. Generally, mens haircuts long hair on top should be at least 3-4 inches long, but can be longer or shorter depending on your preference.

2. Can I get mens haircuts long hair on top if I have thinning hair?

Mens haircuts long hair on top work best for men with thick or wavy hair, but can also be adapted for men with thinning hair. Your hairstylist can recommend the best style and products to use to create the illusion of volume and thickness.

3. How often should I wash my hair with mens haircuts long hair on top?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircuts Long Hair on Top

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your mens haircuts long hair on top looking healthy and shiny:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol or harsh chemicals, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock mens haircuts long hair on top with confidence and style. Whether you prefer a sleek and sophisticated look or a messy and textured vibe, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
