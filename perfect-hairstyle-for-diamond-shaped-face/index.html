Perfect Hairstyle For Diamond Shaped Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 29, 2023 · 8 min read

A diamond-shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a pointed chin. If you have this facial shape, you’re in luck because there are a variety of hairstyles that will flatter your features. In this article, we’ll share with you the perfect hairstyle for diamond-shaped face and provide you with tips on how to maintain it.

Description

A diamond-shaped face is also known as an “inverted triangle” shape. This facial shape is widest at the cheekbones and gradually narrows towards the forehead and jawline. The pointed chin is the most distinguishing feature of this facial shape.

How To Determine If You Have A Diamond Shaped Face?

There are different ways to determine if you have a diamond-shaped face:

  • Measure your face from the top of your forehead to the tip of your chin
  • Check if the width of your cheekbones is wider than your forehead and jawline
  • Look at your face in the mirror and trace the outline of your face using a lipstick or eyeliner

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you can enhance your features by choosing the right hairstyle.

Perfect Hairstyles for Diamond Shaped Face

The perfect hairstyle for diamond-shaped face is one that balances out the features of your face. Here are some hairstyles that will flatter your facial shape:

1. Long Layers

Long layers will soften the angles of your face and highlight your cheekbones. This hairstyle will also add volume to your hair, giving it a more balanced look.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs will draw attention to your eyes and create a diagonal line that will complement your facial shape. This hairstyle will also balance out the width of your forehead.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut that ends at your jawline will accentuate your chin and create a more balanced look. This hairstyle will also add volume to your hair, making it look fuller.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut will highlight your cheekbones and create a more defined jawline. This hairstyle will also add volume to your hair, giving it a more balanced look.

5. Half-Up, Half-Down Hairstyle

A half-up, half-down hairstyle will draw attention to your cheekbones and create a more balanced look. This hairstyle will also add volume to your hair, making it look fuller.

Step by Step Guide for Perfect Hairstyle for Diamond Shaped Face

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect hairstyle for diamond-shaped face:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, make sure to determine your face shape to ensure that you’re choosing the right one.

2. Consult a Hairstylist

Consult a hairstylist to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for your facial shape. They can also give you tips on how to maintain your hair.

3. Choose a Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that will balance out the features of your face. Consider the length, texture, and volume of your hair.

4. Maintain Your Hair

Use hair products that will keep your hair healthy and shiny. Visit your hairstylist regularly for trimming and maintenance.

Tips for Perfect Hairstyle for Diamond Shaped Face

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle for diamond-shaped face:

  • Choose a hairstyle that balances out the features of your face
  • Consider the length, texture, and volume of your hair
  • Use hair products that will keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trimming and maintenance

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a diamond-shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail or a top knot. These hairstyles will make your face look longer and narrower.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a diamond-shaped face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a diamond-shaped face. Just make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out the width of your cheekbones.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a diamond-shaped face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part if you have a diamond-shaped face. Just make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out the width of your cheekbones.

Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Face

Here are some hair care tips for diamond-shaped face:

  • Use hair products that will keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trimming and maintenance
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation

With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you can enhance the features of your diamond-shaped face. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits you best.
