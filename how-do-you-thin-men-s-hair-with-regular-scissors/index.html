How To Thin Men s Hair With Regular Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thinning men's hair is a common practice that helps to create more volume and texture. While many people opt for special thinning scissors, it is also possible to achieve the same effect with regular scissors. In this guide, we will show you how to thin men's hair with regular scissors in a few easy steps.

What You Will Need

Before getting started, you will need a few basic tools:

  • A comb
  • A pair of sharp regular scissors
  • A mirror
  • A spray bottle filled with water

How to Thin Men's Hair with Regular Scissors

Follow these simple steps to thin men's hair with regular scissors:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free from any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section Hair

Next, use a comb to section your hair into manageable parts. Start with the hair at the crown of your head, and work your way down to the sides and back.

Step 3: Spritz with Water

Lightly spritz each section of hair with water. This will help to soften the hair and make it easier to work with.

Step 4: Hold Hair Between Fingers

Take a small section of hair between your fingers and hold it tightly. Make sure to hold the hair at an angle, so that you are cutting into it diagonally.

Step 5: Cut Hair with Scissors

Using your regular scissors, cut into the hair at an angle. Cut about halfway up the hair shaft, and then release the hair. Repeat this process on each section of hair until you achieve your desired level of thinning.

Step 6: Blend Hair

Once you have thinned out your hair, use your scissors to blend the different sections together. This will help to create a more natural look.

Step 7: Check in the Mirror

Check your hair in the mirror to make sure that it is evenly thinned out. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and touch them up with your scissors.

Tips for Thinning Men's Hair with Regular Scissors

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when thinning men's hair with regular scissors:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up. It's always easier to take more hair off than it is to put it back on.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. It's always better to err on the side of caution and take it slow.
  • Make sure that you are cutting at an angle, rather than straight across. This will help to create a more natural look.

FAQs

Can you thin men's hair with regular scissors?

Yes, it is possible to thin men's hair with regular scissors. Just make sure that your scissors are sharp and that you are cutting at an angle.

What is the best way to thin men's hair?

The best way to thin men's hair is to use thinning scissors. However, if you don't have access to these, you can achieve a similar effect with regular scissors.

How often should you thin men's hair?

This will depend on your individual hair type and style. Some people may need to thin their hair every few weeks, while others may only need to do it once every few months.

Hair Care After Thinning

Thinning your hair can help to create a more voluminous and textured look. However, it's important to take care of your hair after thinning to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair shaft.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.

By following these simple steps, you can achieve a professionally thinned-out look with regular scissors. Just remember to take your time, start slowly, and use these tips to keep your hair looking its best.
