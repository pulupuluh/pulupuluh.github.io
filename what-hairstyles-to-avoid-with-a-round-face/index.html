What Hairstyles To Avoid With A Round Face

Written by Frank Apr 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of getting the wrong hairstyle that makes your face look rounder than it actually is? As someone with a round face, it's essential to choose a hairstyle that complements your features rather than accentuate them. In this article, we'll discuss what hairstyles to avoid with a round face and give you some tips on finding the perfect haircut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Hairstyles to Avoid
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Round Faces

Hairstyles to Avoid

Before we get into what hairstyles to avoid, let's first understand what a round face is. A round face is characterized by having a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a rounded chin. The best hairstyles for a round face are those that create angles and lengthen the face.

Now, let's get into the hairstyles to avoid:

Bangs

Bangs that are straight across can make your face look shorter and wider. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that create a diagonal line across your forehead and draw attention to your eyes.

Bob Haircuts

Bob haircuts that end at the chin can make your face look rounder. Instead, choose a longer bob that ends just below your chin or a shoulder-length cut that creates angles around your face.

Center Parts

A center part can make your face look more symmetrical, but it can also make it look rounder. Instead, try a side part that creates angles around your face.

Curls

Curls that are too tight can make your face look rounder. Instead, choose loose waves or beachy curls that create volume and length.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for a round face, the goal is to create angles and lengthen the face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head to elongate your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that end at your chin or cheekbones.
  • Try hairstyles with layers that create movement around your face.
  • Consider a side part or deep side part to create angles around your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or sleek.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Haircut

Follow these steps to find the perfect haircut for your round face:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that complement your face shape.
  3. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your stylist.
  4. Consult with your stylist to determine the best haircut for you.
  5. Consider trying a wig or hair extensions to see how a new hairstyle would look before committing to a haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your round face, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-styling or using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes! A low ponytail or a high ponytail with some volume at the crown can elongate your face.

Q: What hair color looks best on a round face?

A: Warm hair colors, like caramel or honey blonde, can complement a round face. Avoid hair colors that are too dark or too light.

Hair Care for Round Faces

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Consider using a hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and shiny.

By following these tips and avoiding the hairstyles listed above, you can find the perfect haircut for your round face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
