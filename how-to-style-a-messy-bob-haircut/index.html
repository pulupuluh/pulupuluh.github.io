How To Style A Messy Bob Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 26, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a messy bob haircut, you know how versatile and fashionable it can be. However, you may not know how to style it to get that perfect, effortless look. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks to help you style your messy bob haircut in a way that looks effortless and chic.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style a Messy Bob Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style a Messy Bob Haircut

Before we dive into the styling process, it’s important to note that the best way to style a messy bob haircut is to embrace its natural texture. This means that you should avoid using too much heat or styling products that can weigh down your hair.

With that said, here are the steps to style a messy bob haircut:

Step 1: Start with clean, towel-dried hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it’s clean and towel-dried. This will help you avoid damage from heat styling tools and make your hair easier to work with.

Step 2: Apply a lightweight styling product

Apply a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or sea salt spray, to your hair. This will help enhance your hair’s natural texture and give it some hold.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands. This will help enhance your hair’s natural texture and give it some volume.

Step 4: Add some waves

If you want to add some waves to your messy bob haircut, you can use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose waves. However, make sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 5: Finish with a texturizing spray

Finish off your look with a texturizing spray. This will help give your hair some hold and enhance its natural texture.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your messy bob haircut:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

What kind of haircut is a messy bob?

A messy bob is a short-to-medium length haircut that is typically styled in a messy, tousled way. It’s a popular haircut among women who want a low-maintenance, yet stylish look.

Can I style a messy bob without heat?

Yes, you can style a messy bob without heat. One way to do this is to apply a styling product, such as a mousse or sea salt spray, to your hair and scrunch it with your hands. This will help enhance your hair’s natural texture and give it some hold.

How often should I wash my hair if I have a messy bob?

It’s recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days if you have a messy bob. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your messy bob looking healthy and stylish, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your messy bob haircut in a way that looks effortless and chic. Remember to embrace your hair’s natural texture and have fun experimenting with different styling products and techniques.
