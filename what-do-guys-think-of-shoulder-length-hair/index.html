What Do Guys Think Of Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 16, 2023 · 8 min read

Shoulder length hair is a popular hairstyle among women. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and looks great on different face shapes. But have you ever wondered what guys think of shoulder length hair? In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question and provide tips on how to style and care for shoulder length hair.

Table of Content

  • The Appeal of Shoulder Length Hair to Guys
  • How to Style Shoulder Length Hair
  • Tips for Caring for Shoulder Length Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

The Appeal of Shoulder Length Hair to Guys

Generally, guys find shoulder length hair attractive. It's not too long that it becomes unmanageable, but it's not too short that it can't be styled in different ways. Shoulder length hair frames the face and accentuates the eyes and cheekbones. It also has a feminine appeal that many guys find alluring.

However, like any other hairstyle, shoulder length hair may not suit everyone's taste. Some guys may prefer shorter or longer hair, depending on their personal preference. It's also worth noting that guys may have different opinions on the style, color, and texture of shoulder length hair.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair

One of the best things about shoulder length hair is its versatility. You can style it in many ways, from sleek and straight to voluminous and curly. Here are some styling options to consider:

  • Straight and sleek: Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and apply a shine serum or spray to give it a glossy finish.
  • Beachy waves: Use a curling wand or a hair straightener to create loose waves. Apply a sea salt spray or texturizing spray to add volume and texture.
  • Half up, half down: Gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the rest of your hair down or style it in loose waves.
  • Braids: Create a fishtail or Dutch braid on the side of your head or at the back of your hair.

Tips for Caring for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips on how to keep your shoulder length hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently as they can damage your hair. If you need to use a blow dryer or flat iron, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too many hair products that can weigh down your hair or make it look greasy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair color looks best with shoulder length hair?

The best hair color for shoulder length hair depends on your skin tone and personal preference. Generally, warm tones like caramel, honey, and copper look great on shoulder length hair. If you have a cool skin tone, consider shades like ash blonde, platinum, or cool brown.

Can shoulder length hair make my face look rounder?

Shoulder length hair can frame your face and make it look more oval. However, if you have a round face shape, avoid blunt cuts or heavy bangs that can make your face appear fuller. Instead, opt for layers or side-swept bangs that can create a slimming effect.

How can I add volume to my shoulder length hair?

To add volume to your shoulder length hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Blow dry your hair upside down to create lift at the roots. You can also use a round brush to create volume while blow drying. Another trick is to apply dry shampoo to your roots to absorb excess oil and add texture.

Can I still wear a ponytail with shoulder length hair?

Yes, you can wear a ponytail with shoulder length hair. However, you may need to use a hair tie that's smaller than usual or wrap it around your hair twice to secure it. You can also create a high ponytail or a low ponytail with a side part for a chic look.

Is shoulder length hair easy to maintain?

Shoulder length hair is relatively easy to maintain compared to longer hair. It's not too short that it requires frequent trims, but it's not too long that it becomes difficult to manage. However, you still need to wash and condition your hair regularly, and style it with the right products to keep it healthy and shiny.

The Verdict

Shoulder length hair is a popular hairstyle that guys generally find attractive. It's versatile, feminine, and easy to maintain. With the right styling and hair care products, you can rock this hairstyle and feel confident and beautiful.
