How To Tell What Hair Color Looks Good On You A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jul 02, 2023 · 8 min read

Choosing a hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you are not sure which shade will suit you best. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to pick the right one. However, there are a few simple steps you can take to determine which hair color will look good on you. In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect hair color for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color
  • Step 3: Take Your Personal Style Into Account
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Keeping Your Color Vibrant

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining which hair color will look good on you is to determine your skin tone. Skin tone refers to the natural color of your skin, which can be classified into three categories: cool, warm, and neutral.

If you have cool undertones, your skin will have hints of blue, pink, or red. On the other hand, if you have warm undertones, your skin will have hints of yellow, peach, or gold. If your skin has a mix of both cool and warm undertones or does not have any noticeable undertones, then you have a neutral skin tone.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can choose a hair color that complements it. If you have cool undertones, consider shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, or black. If you have warm undertones, go for shades like golden blonde, honey brown, or copper. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can pull off most hair colors.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining which hair color will look good on you. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop while others can make them look dull. For example, if you have blue eyes, a warm hair color like golden blonde or light brown can make them stand out. If you have brown eyes, a cool hair color like ash blonde or black can create a striking contrast.

Step 3: Take Your Personal Style Into Account

Your personal style also plays a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have a bold and adventurous style, you may want to try a vibrant hair color like purple or blue. If you prefer a more natural look, stick to shades that are close to your natural hair color.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

  • Consider the maintenance required for the hair color you choose. Some shades require more upkeep than others.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual makeover tools to see how different hair colors will look on you.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist to get their opinion on which hair color will look best on you.
  • Take into account the colors of your wardrobe when choosing a hair color.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair colors until you find the perfect one for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, you can still dye your hair if you have sensitive skin. Look for hair dyes that are formulated for sensitive skin or do a patch test before applying the dye to your hair.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: The frequency of touch-ups depends on the hair color you choose and how fast your hair grows. Generally, hair color should be touched up every four to six weeks to maintain its vibrancy.

Hair Care Tips for Keeping Your Color Vibrant

Once you have chosen the perfect hair color, it is important to take care of it to ensure it stays vibrant and healthy-looking. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause your color to fade.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these simple steps and tips, you can find the perfect hair color to suit your skin tone, eye color, and personal style.
