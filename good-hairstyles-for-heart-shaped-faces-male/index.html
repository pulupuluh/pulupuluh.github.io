Good Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces Male

If you have a heart shaped face, you are in luck! This face shape is considered to be one of the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. In this article, we will provide you with some of the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces male in relaxed English language.

Table of Content:

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

A heart shaped face has a wider forehead and cheekbones with a narrower chin. The goal of a good hairstyle for a heart shaped face is to balance out the width of the forehead and cheekbones with the narrower chin. The best hairstyles for this face shape accentuate the jawline and bring attention to the eyes.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for a heart shaped face, it is important to consider the length and texture of your hair. Shorter haircuts tend to work best for this face shape, as they draw attention to the jawline and balance out the wider forehead and cheekbones. However, longer hairstyles can work as well - it's all about finding the right cut and style.

Short Hairstyles:

Short hairstyles tend to work well for heart shaped faces. A classic buzz cut or crew cut can work well to accentuate the jawline. A short, messy textured cut like a French crop or messy fringe can also work well. For those who want to keep some length, a short, layered cut can help balance out the face.

Medium Hairstyles:

For those who prefer medium length hairstyles, a textured quiff or messy pompadour can work well to balance out the face. A side swept fringe can also help to draw attention to the eyes and balance out the wider forehead.

Long Hairstyles:

Long hairstyles can work for heart shaped faces as well. A layered cut with long, side swept bangs can help to balance out the face. A messy, textured style like a messy bun or ponytail can also work well to soften the look of the face.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a haircut for a heart shaped face:

  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best style for your face shape and hair type.
  • Consider the length and texture of your hair when choosing a style.
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your appointment to help your stylist understand your desired look.
  • Maintain your hairstyle with regular trims and styling products to keep it looking its best.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a heart shaped face:

  • Choose a style that accentuates the jawline and brings attention to the eyes.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the forehead or temple area.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a style.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can heart shaped faces pull off bangs?

A: Yes, heart shaped faces can pull off bangs. Side swept bangs can work well to balance out the wider forehead and draw attention to the eyes.

Q: Are there any hairstyles heart shaped faces should avoid?

A: Heart shaped faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the forehead or temple area. This can make the forehead appear wider and throw off the balance of the face.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair with tools like flat irons or blow dryers.
  • Use styling products like hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, a heart shaped face is a versatile face shape that can pull off a variety of hairstyles. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there is a style out there that will work for you. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can find the perfect hairstyle to accentuate your best features and balance out your face shape.
