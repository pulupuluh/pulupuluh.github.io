Short Curly Hair For Round Face 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Aug 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, short curly hair can enhance your features and bring out the best in you. In this post, we will discuss how you can style your short curly hair for round face in 2021. We will cover everything from how to choose the right cut to tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Cut
  • How to Style Your Short Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Conclusion

How to Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a short curly hairstyle for a round face, it's essential to consider the shape of your face. There are several cuts that can work well with a round face, including:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, layered cut that can add volume to your hair and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. This cut works well with curly hair as it can enhance your natural curls.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a versatile cut that can be customized to suit your face shape. For a round face, a chin-length bob with layers can help to elongate your face and add dimension to your hair.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a great option for those who want a unique and edgy look. This cut can be customized to suit your face shape and can add volume to your hair.

How to Style Your Short Curly Hair

Styling short curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful look. Here are some tips on how to style your short curly hair:

Use a Curl Enhancing Product

Using a curl-enhancing product can help to define your curls and keep them looking great all day long. Look for products that contain ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter, as these can help to moisturize your hair and reduce frizz.

Avoid Using a Brush

Brushing your curly hair can lead to frizz and damage. Instead, use your fingers to comb through your hair gently. This will help to preserve your curls and keep them looking great.

Try a Diffuser

A diffuser is a great tool for drying your curly hair. It can help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Simply attach the diffuser to your hairdryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Curly Hair

Short curly hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your short curly hair:

Moisturize Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's essential to moisturize your hair regularly. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil, as these can help to moisturize your hair and reduce frizz.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking great.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and lead to frizz. Try to avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons. Instead, embrace your natural curls and work with them.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your short curly hair. However, it's essential to choose a color that complements your skin tone and eye color. It's also important to use a dye that is specifically designed for curly hair.

Q: How often should I wash my short curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days. Washing it too often can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and frizz.

Q: Can I straighten my short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your short curly hair. However, it's important to use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Conclusion

Short curly hair can be a great option for those with round faces. By choosing the right cut, styling your hair properly, and maintaining it regularly, you can achieve a beautiful and unique look. Remember to embrace your natural curls and work with them to achieve the best results.
