Easy Hairdo For Shoulder Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 30, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old hairstyle every day? Do you want to change your look without spending hours in front of the mirror? If you answered yes to these questions, then you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you a variety of easy hairdos for shoulder length hair that you can do in just a few minutes. Whether you're heading to work, a party, or a casual outing, these hairstyles will make you look and feel fabulous.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in many different ways. From braids to buns, there are plenty of easy hairdos that you can do yourself. These hairstyles are perfect for women who are always on the go and don't have a lot of time to spend on their hair.

How To

The key to creating easy hairdos for shoulder length hair is to keep it simple. You don't need a lot of fancy tools or products to achieve a great look. All you need is a few basic styling tools like a brush, a comb, and some hair ties. Here's how to create some of the most popular easy hairdos for shoulder length hair:

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for when you're in a rush. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with a hair tie.
  5. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

You can also add a headband or some hair accessories to dress up the look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a great way to show off your shoulder length hair while keeping it out of your face. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take a section of hair from the top of your head.
  2. Tie the section into a small ponytail.
  3. Tease the ponytail to create volume.
  4. Let the rest of your hair hang loose.

You can also add some curls or waves to the bottom half of your hair for a more polished look.

3. Braids

Braids are a great way to add texture and interest to shoulder length hair. Here are a few different braid styles you can try:

  • Fishtail braid
  • Dutch braid
  • French braid

There are plenty of tutorials online that can show you how to create these braids step by step. Once you've mastered the technique, you can experiment with different variations and styles.

Step by Step

Now that you know the basics of how to create easy hairdos for shoulder length hair, let's break it down step by step:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. If you're going to be using heat styling tools, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray or serum first.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Decide which hairstyle you want to create. You can choose from any of the styles we've mentioned above, or come up with your own creative look.

Step 3: Gather Your Tools

Make sure you have all the tools you need to create your hairstyle. This may include a brush, a comb, hair ties, bobby pins, and any styling products you want to use.

Step 4: Follow the Instructions

Follow the step-by-step instructions we've provided above for your chosen hairstyle. Take your time and be patient, especially if you're trying a new technique or style.

Step 5: Finish with Hair Spray

Once you've finished your hairstyle, give it a spritz of hairspray to help it hold its shape throughout the day.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your easy hairdos for shoulder length hair:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands, clips, and scarves.
  • Try adding some texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different braids and twists to create unique looks.
  • Invest in a good quality brush and comb to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes. Try to find a schedule that works for you and your hair.

Q: Can I use heat styling tools on my shoulder length hair?

A: Yes, you can use heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons on your shoulder length hair. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray or serum first to prevent damage.

Q: How can I keep my hair looking healthy?

A: To keep your hair looking healthy, make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too many styling products. You can also get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips to help you maintain healthy shoulder length hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Try to air dry your hair instead of using heat styling tools whenever possible.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and easy hairdos for shoulder length hair, you'll never have to worry about a bad hair day again. Whether you're looking for a simple and casual look, or something more polished and sophisticated, there's a hairstyle out there for you. So go ahead, experiment, and have fun!
