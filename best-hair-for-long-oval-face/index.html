Best Hair For Long Oval Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 13, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a long oval face, choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. You want to make sure that your hair flatters your face shape and brings out the best in your features. In this guide, we'll cover some of the best hairstyles for long oval faces, as well as tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hair for Long Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

A long oval face is characterized by a long, narrow shape, with a slightly wider forehead and a narrow chin. The key to finding the best hair for this face shape is to choose styles that add width and volume to the sides, while keeping the length of the hair below the chin. This helps to balance out the proportions of the face and make it appear more symmetrical.

How to Choose the Best Hair for Long Oval Face

When it comes to choosing the best hair for long oval faces, there are a few key factors to keep in mind:

Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. If you have a long oval face, you should look for hairstyles that add width and volume to the sides of your face. This can include layers, curls, and waves.

Hair Type

The next factor to consider is your hair type. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy layers that can make your hair look flat. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for more layers to help reduce bulk and add movement.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also play a role in choosing the best hair for your face shape. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, you may want to choose a low-maintenance style that can be easily styled on the go.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the best styles for long oval faces:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for long oval faces, as they help to add volume and movement to the sides of the face. To style, simply blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the ends to create a soft, bouncy look.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for long oval faces, as they help to break up the length of the face and add width to the forehead. To style, simply blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the bangs to create a soft, face-framing look.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great option for long oval faces, as they help to add volume and texture to the sides of the face. To style, simply use a curling iron to create loose waves, then tousle with your fingers to create a beachy, effortless look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you've chosen the best hairstyle for your long oval face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best:

Use the Right Products

Make sure to use products that are formulated for your hair type. If you have fine hair, you may want to use volumizing products to add body and texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to use smoothing products to reduce frizz and add shine.

Avoid Heat Damage

Try to limit your use of heat styling tools, such as curling irons and flat irons, as they can damage your hair over time. If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential for keeping your hair healthy and preventing split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or as needed to maintain your style.

FAQ

What hair color is best for long oval faces?

When it comes to hair color, there are no hard and fast rules. However, some hair colors that can look particularly flattering on long oval faces include warm, golden tones, as well as rich, dark shades like chocolate brown and black.

Can I wear my hair in a pixie cut if I have a long oval face?

While pixie cuts can be flattering on many face shapes, they may not be the best choice for long oval faces. This is because they can make the face appear even longer. If you do want to try a pixie cut, consider opting for a longer, more layered style to add volume and width to the sides of the face.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Make sure to wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed based on your hair type and lifestyle.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to help nourish and hydrate your hair. This can help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking its best.

Avoid Overstyling

While it can be tempting to style your hair every day, try to avoid overstyling. This can cause damage to your hair over time and make it harder to maintain your style.

By following these tips and choosing the best hair for your long oval face, you can achieve a flattering, stylish look that brings out the best in your features.
