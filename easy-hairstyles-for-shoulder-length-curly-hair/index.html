Easy Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Description

Shoulder length curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look gorgeous and voluminous, it can also be difficult to manage and style. However, with the right techniques and products, you can easily create stunning hairstyles that will enhance your natural curls.

In this article, we will share some easy hairstyles for shoulder length curly hair that you can try at home. Whether you have loose waves or tight coils, these hairstyles will work for you.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let’s talk about some basic techniques that you need to know when styling curly hair.

Firstly, it’s important to use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet. This will prevent breakage and help you avoid painful knots.

Secondly, always apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair before styling. This will help define your curls and keep them moisturized.

Lastly, avoid using a brush on your curls as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.

Step by step

1. Twisted Updo

To create this elegant updo, start by dividing your hair into two sections. Take one section and twist it upwards towards the crown of your head. Secure the twisted section with bobby pins. Repeat on the other side. Then, take the two twisted sections and tuck them into each other, securing with bobby pins. Voila! You have a chic twisted updo.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

This classic hairstyle is perfect for any occasion. Start by taking a section of hair from the front of your head and twisting it upwards. Secure with bobby pins. Repeat on the other side. Then, take a section of hair from the back of your head and tie it into a ponytail. You can leave the rest of your hair down or add some curls for extra volume.

3. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a great option for days when you want to keep your hair out of your face. Start by tying your hair into a high ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun. Secure with bobby pins. Use your fingers to gently pull out some strands from the bun to create a messy look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your shoulder length curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your curls from getting tangled while you sleep.
  • Try not to touch your curls too much throughout the day as this can cause frizz.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my shoulder length curly hair?

A: While it’s possible to straighten curly hair, we recommend embracing your natural curls as they are beautiful and unique.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but generally it’s best to wash curly hair once or twice a week to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Hair Care

Keeping your curly hair healthy and hydrated is essential for achieving beautiful styles. Here are some tips for taking care of your shoulder length curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your curls as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these easy hairstyles and hair care tips, you can rock your shoulder length curly hair with confidence. Remember to embrace your natural curls and have fun experimenting with different styles!
