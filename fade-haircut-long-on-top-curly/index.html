Fade Haircut Long On Top Curly A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curls are one of the most popular hair types, but they can be difficult to manage. If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut for curly hair, a fade haircut long on top curly might be just what you need. In this article, we'll take a look at everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle, including how to get it, tips for styling, and hair care advice to keep your curls looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Fade Haircut Long on Top Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Advice

Description

A fade haircut long on top curly is a modern and stylish hairstyle that features shorter sides and back, gradually fading into longer hair at the top. This style works particularly well for curly hair because it allows the curls to shine while keeping the hair low-maintenance and easy to style. The long hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from loose curls to slicked-back hair or even a messy, textured look.

How to Get a Fade Haircut Long on Top Curly

The first step in getting a fade haircut long on top curly is to find a skilled barber or stylist who has experience working with curly hair. They will be able to help you determine the best fade style and length for your hair type and face shape. When you go in for your haircut, be sure to bring a picture of the style you want to help your barber understand your vision.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a fade haircut long on top curly:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair to make sure it's clean and tangle-free.
  2. Sit down in the barber's chair and discuss the style you want.
  3. The barber will start by using clippers to create a short fade on the sides and back of your head.
  4. They will then use scissors or clippers to trim the hair on top to your desired length.
  5. The barber will then blend the fade into the longer hair on top, creating a seamless transition.
  6. Finally, they will style your hair using a comb, brush, or their fingers to create the desired look.

Tips for Styling

Once you've got your fade haircut long on top curly, it's important to know how to style it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair to keep your locks hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot excess moisture.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to wet hair to help define your curls and keep them in place.
  • You can experiment with different styling products to achieve different looks, such as a matte finish or a shiny, slicked-back style.
  • If you want to add volume to your hair, try blow-drying it upside down using a diffuser attachment.

FAQ

How often should I get a fade haircut long on top curly?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it. As a general rule, you should get a trim every four to six weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Can I still wear my hair curly with a fade?

Absolutely! In fact, the fade haircut long on top curly is designed to show off your natural curls. Just make sure to use the right products and styling techniques to keep your curls looking their best.

Is a fade haircut long on top curly suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this style can work for a variety of face shapes. Your barber or stylist can help you determine the best length and style for your unique features.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your fade haircut long on top curly looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with UV protection.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using too many products, as this can weigh down your hair and cause buildup.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can rock a fade haircut long on top curly that looks stylish and effortless. So go ahead and embrace your natural curls with this trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle!
