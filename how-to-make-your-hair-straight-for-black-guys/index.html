How To Make Your Hair Straight For Black Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Straight hair is always in trend, and many black guys prefer to have straight hair instead of curly. However, getting straight hair for black guys can be challenging without using heat. Relaxers are the most common and effective way to straighten hair for black guys. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to make your hair straight for black guys with relaxed English language.

Description

Black men have naturally curly hair, and getting straight hair can be challenging. However, with proper techniques and products, black guys can achieve straight hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to straighten your hair with a relaxer, the dos and don'ts of hair straightening, and how to maintain your straight hair.

How to Make Your Hair Straight for Black Guys: Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Preparation

Before applying the relaxer, make sure to prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Then, section your hair into four parts and detangle it with a wide-tooth comb.

Step 2: Protect Your Scalp

Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline, ears, and neck to protect your skin from the relaxer.

Step 3: Apply the Relaxer

Mix the relaxer according to the instructions on the package. Then, apply the relaxer to your hair, starting from the back and working your way to the front. Make sure to apply the relaxer evenly and avoid overlapping. Leave the relaxer on for the recommended time.

Step 4: Rinse

After the recommended time, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove the relaxer.

Step 5: Neutralize

Apply the neutralizing shampoo to your hair and massage it gently. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off.

Tips for Straightening Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when straightening your hair:

  • Always follow the instructions on the relaxer package.
  • Don't leave the relaxer on for too long, as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioner after the relaxer to restore moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools immediately after applying the relaxer, as it can cause further damage.
  • Use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools.

FAQ

Can I straighten my hair without using a relaxer?

Yes, you can straighten your hair without using a relaxer. However, it may not be as effective or long-lasting. You can use heat styling tools or try a keratin treatment to straighten your hair.

Can I apply the relaxer myself?

It's best to have a professional hairstylist apply the relaxer, as it can be challenging to apply it evenly and avoid overlapping.

How often can I apply a relaxer?

It's best to wait at least six weeks before applying a relaxer again to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Straight Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your straight hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid friction and breakage.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Protect your hair from heat and sun damage by using a hat or scarf.

Conclusion

Straightening your hair can be a game-changer for black guys looking for a new look. While it may require some effort and care, the results can be worth it. Follow our step-by-step guide, use the right products, and maintain your hair well to achieve and maintain your straight hair.
