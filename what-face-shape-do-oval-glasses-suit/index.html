What Face Shape Do Oval Glasses Suit

Are you wondering if oval glasses will suit your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we'll discuss which face shapes oval glasses are best for, how to choose the right pair, and some tips on styling them.

What Face Shapes Suit Oval Glasses?

Oval glasses are known for their rounded, elongated shape that looks great on many face shapes. However, they are particularly suited for those with square or angular faces. The rounded shape of the glasses helps to soften the angles of the face, creating a more balanced, harmonious look.

That being said, oval glasses can also work well for those with round or oval faces. The key is to choose a pair that complements your facial features and doesn't overwhelm your face.

How to Choose the Right Pair

When choosing oval glasses, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape: As mentioned earlier, oval glasses work best for those with square or angular faces. However, they can also work for round or oval faces with the right pair.
  • Choose the right size: Make sure the glasses fit your face properly - not too big or too small. The frames should sit comfortably on your nose and ears, without sliding down or feeling too tight.
  • Consider your personal style: Choose a pair that reflects your personal style and complements your wardrobe. Do you prefer bold, statement glasses or something more understated?

Tips for Styling Oval Glasses

Now that you've chosen the perfect pair of oval glasses, it's time to style them! Here are a few tips:

  • Match your glasses to your outfit: Choose colors and patterns that complement your outfit. For example, if you're wearing a bold, patterned shirt, opt for a more understated pair of glasses.
  • Accessorize with jewelry: Pair your glasses with some statement earrings or a necklace to draw attention to your face.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles: Try different hairstyles to see which ones complement your glasses. For example, a sleek ponytail or bun can help to showcase your glasses.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about oval glasses:

What face shape do oval glasses suit?

Oval glasses are best suited for those with square or angular faces, but can also work for those with round or oval faces.

What are some popular brands that make oval glasses?

There are many brands that make oval glasses, including Ray-Ban, Warby Parker, and Oakley.

Can I wear oval glasses with a hat?

Absolutely! Oval glasses can be worn with a variety of hats, including baseball caps, fedoras, and beanies.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Glasses

If you wear glasses, it's important to take care of your hair to avoid any discomfort or damage to your frames. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hair products near your glasses: Hair products such as hairspray or gel can get on your glasses and make them difficult to clean. Apply these products away from your face or after you've put your glasses on.
  • Adjust your hairstyle: If your glasses are constantly slipping down your nose, try adjusting your hairstyle. A hairstyle that keeps your hair away from your face can help to keep your glasses in place.
  • Clean your glasses regularly: Regularly clean your glasses with a microfiber cloth to avoid any buildup of dirt, oil, or debris. Avoid using any harsh chemicals or abrasive materials on your frames.

In conclusion, oval glasses can be a great choice for many face shapes. By considering your face shape, choosing the right pair, and styling them properly, you can rock your oval glasses with confidence!
