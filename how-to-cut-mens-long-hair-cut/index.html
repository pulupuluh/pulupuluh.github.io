How To Cut Men s Long Hair Cut A Step By Step Guide In 2023

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a man with long hair, you may be looking to switch up your style or save some money on salon visits. Whatever your reason, cutting your own hair can be a daunting task. But fear not, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut men's long hair cut in relaxed English language, step-by-step. We'll also provide tips, answer frequently asked questions, and offer hair care advice. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Men's Long Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care after Cutting

How to Cut Men's Long Hair Cut

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need a few tools:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Cape or towel to protect clothing
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips

Now that you have your tools, let's get started with our step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and give you a better idea of how it will look once styled.

2. Section your hair using clips or ties. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Use a comb to create straight lines.

3. Use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one. Use a comb to blend the lengths together.

4. Use scissors to cut the top of your hair. Take small sections at a time and cut straight across. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut at an angle for a more natural look.

5. Blend the top and sides together using scissors or clippers. Use the comb to blend any uneven areas.

6. Cut your bangs if you have them. Start with small sections and cut straight across.

7. Clean up any stray hairs around your ears and neckline using clippers or scissors.

8. Style your hair as desired. Use products like gel or pomade to add texture and hold.

Tips for Cutting Men's Long Hair

- Take your time and work in small sections.

- Use a mirror to check your progress.

- Don't cut too much off at once. You can always go back and trim more later.

- Ask a friend or family member for help if you're unsure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut my own hair if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. Just be sure to cut it when it's dry and use a comb to separate the curls.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want it to be. On average, men with long hair should get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Should I cut my hair wet or dry?

A: It's best to cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can appear longer than it actually is, which can result in cutting too much off.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting your hair, be sure to take good care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair. Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Congratulations, you've successfully learned how to cut men's long hair cut. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to cut your hair like a pro. Remember, if you're ever unsure, it's always best to seek help from a professional stylist.
