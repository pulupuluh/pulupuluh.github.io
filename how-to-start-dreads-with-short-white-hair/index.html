How To Start Dreads With Short White Hair

Written by Robby Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Table of Content: Description, How to, Step by step, Tips, FAQ, Hair Care

Description

Dreadlocks are a beautiful and unique hairstyle that have been around for centuries. They can be styled in many different ways and are perfect for people who want to express their individuality. Starting dreadlocks with short white hair can be a bit challenging, but it is definitely possible. In this article, we will guide you through the process of starting dreads with short white hair.

How to

The first step in starting dreads with short white hair is to decide what method you want to use. There are many different methods for starting dreadlocks, including backcombing, twisting, and using a crochet hook. Each method has its pros and cons, so it is important to do your research and decide which one is right for you.

Backcombing

Backcombing is a popular method for starting dreadlocks. To start, section your hair into small squares and backcomb each section from the tips to the roots. This will create a tangled, knotted look that will eventually form into dreadlocks. This method can be time-consuming and can cause damage to your hair, so it is important to be patient and gentle.

Twisting

Twisting is another method for starting dreadlocks. To start, section your hair into small squares and twist each section tightly. This will create a rope-like appearance that will eventually form into dreadlocks. This method is less damaging to your hair than backcombing, but it can take longer for the dreadlocks to form.

Crochet Hook

Using a crochet hook is a method for starting dreadlocks that involves using a special tool to pull your hair into knots. This method is less damaging to your hair than backcombing and can create tight, well-formed dreadlocks. However, it can be difficult to do on your own, so it is recommended that you seek the help of a professional.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to start dreads with short white hair using the backcombing method:

  1. Section your hair into small squares.
  2. Take one section and backcomb from the tips to the roots.
  3. Repeat with each section until your entire head is backcombed.
  4. Use a hair tie to hold each section in place.
  5. Wait for your hair to knot and tangle, which will eventually form into dreadlocks.
  6. After a few weeks, you can start to palm roll your dreadlocks to help them form and tighten.

Tips

Here are some tips for starting dreads with short white hair:

  • Be patient. It can take several weeks or even months for your hair to form into dreadlocks.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a silk or satin scarf.
  • Use a residue-free shampoo to keep your hair clean without damaging your dreadlocks.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol or other harsh chemicals.
  • Seek the help of a professional if you are unsure about how to start your dreadlocks.

FAQ

Can I start dreadlocks with short white hair?

Yes, you can start dreadlocks with short white hair. It may take longer for your hair to form into dreadlocks, but it is definitely possible.

What method should I use to start my dreadlocks?

There are many different methods for starting dreadlocks, including backcombing, twisting, and using a crochet hook. It is important to do your research and decide which method is right for you.

How long does it take for dreadlocks to form?

It can take several weeks or even months for your hair to form into dreadlocks, depending on the method you use and how fast your hair grows.

Hair Care

Once you have started your dreadlocks, it is important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips for caring for your dreadlocks:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using conditioner, as it can cause buildup in your dreadlocks.
  • Use a hair tie to keep your dreadlocks in place while sleeping.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your dreadlocks.
  • Get regular maintenance from a professional to keep your dreadlocks looking their best.

Starting dreadlocks with short white hair can be a bit challenging, but with the right method and proper care, you can have beautiful, unique dreadlocks that express your individuality. Be patient, take care of your hair, and enjoy your new look!
