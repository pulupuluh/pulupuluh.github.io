Step 1: Choose Your Length

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to style it. You can use a hair dryer and a styling product to create a sleek and smooth look, or you can use a texturizing product to add some volume and texture to your hair.
