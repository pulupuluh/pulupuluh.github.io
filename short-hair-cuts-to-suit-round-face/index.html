Short Hair Cuts To Suit Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 27, 2023 Â· 10 min read

A round face shape is characterized by having a wider forehead and cheekbones with a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you might find it challenging to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. Fortunately, there are plenty of short hair cuts to suit round face shapes that can help accentuate your best features and create a slimming effect. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to choose the perfect short hair cut to suit your round face shape.

Description

Short haircuts are perfect for round face shapes as they can help create the illusion of length and balance out the proportions of your face. With the right cut, you can draw attention to your best features and minimize the roundness of your face. Short haircuts can also be low-maintenance and easy to style, making them a popular choice for busy women.

How To Choose The Right Short Hair Cut

When it comes to choosing the right short hair cut for your round face shape, there are a few factors to consider:

Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, round face shapes have wider foreheads and cheekbones with a rounded chin. The goal is to choose a short hair cut that can create the illusion of length and balance out the proportions of your face.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also influence the type of short hair cut that will suit you best. If you have thick hair, you might want to opt for a layered cut that can help thin out your hair and create movement. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can help create the illusion of volume and thickness.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also influence the type of short hair cut that will suit you best. If you have a busy schedule and don't have much time to style your hair, you might want to opt for a low-maintenance cut that can be easily styled with a few products.

Step By Step Guide to Short Hair Cuts for Round Face Shapes

Step 1: Consult with a Hairstylist

The first step in choosing the right short hair cut for your round face shape is to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you determine the best cut based on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

The length of your short hair cut can influence the way your face looks. For round face shapes, it's best to opt for a cut that hits just above the jawline or chin.

Step 3: Consider Layers

If you have thick hair, layers can help thin out your hair and create movement. However, if you have fine hair, too many layers can make your hair look sparse and thin.

Step 4: Opt for Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create the illusion of length and balance out the proportions of your face. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look rounder.

Step 5: Add Volume

If you have fine hair, you can add volume to your short hair cut by using volumizing products such as mousse or dry shampoo. You can also use a round brush to add volume when blow-drying your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair Cut

Short hair cuts can be low-maintenance, but they still require some care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short hair cut:

Regular Trims

Short haircuts require regular trims to keep them looking neat and tidy. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use heat protectant products when using styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons. Too much heat can damage your hair and make it look dull.

FAQs About Short Hair Cuts for Round Face Shapes

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a round face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have a round face shape. However, make sure to choose a cut that has volume on top and avoid cuts that are too short and blunt.

Q: Can I wear blunt bangs if I have a round face shape?

A: Blunt bangs can make your face look rounder, so it's best to avoid them if you have a round face shape. Opt for side-swept bangs instead.

Q: Is it possible to add volume to my short hair cut?

A: Yes, you can add volume to your short hair cut by using volumizing products or using a round brush when blow-drying your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair Cuts

Here are some additional hair care tips for short hair cuts:

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Use a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays. Too much sun exposure can damage your hair and make it look dull.

Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup from styling products. This can help keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. This can help prevent split ends and breakage.

Conclusion

Short hair cuts can be a great option for round face shapes. By choosing the right cut, you can create the illusion of length and balance out the proportions of your face. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing the perfect short hair cut for you. Remember to maintain your short hair cut with regular trims and proper hair care to keep it looking its best.
