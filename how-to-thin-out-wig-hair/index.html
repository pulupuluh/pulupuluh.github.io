How To Thin Out Wig Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling with a wig that has too much hair and looks bulky? Thinning out wig hair can create a more natural look and make the wig much more comfortable to wear. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of thinning out wig hair.

Description

Thinning out wig hair is a process where you remove some of the hair from the wig to make it look more natural and less bulky. This process can be done on both synthetic and human hair wigs, and it is relatively simple to do at home.

How to Thin Out Wig Hair

Before we start, you will need to gather some tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a wig stand or mannequin head. Once you have these tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Comb the Wig

Take your wig and remove any tangles by gently combing it with a wide-toothed comb. This will make it easier to see which areas of the wig need to be thinned out.

Step 2: Section the Wig

Section the wig into small parts and clip them out of the way. This will help you identify which areas of the wig need to be thinned out.

Step 3: Thin Out the Hair

Using your scissors, cut small sections of hair at a time. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut upwards towards the top of the wig. This will create a more natural and gradual thinning effect.

Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can create choppy and uneven results. It is best to thin out the hair gradually and continue to comb through as you go.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you have thinned out the hair, use your comb to blend the sections of hair together. This will create a more natural and seamless look.

Step 5: Style the Wig

After thinning out your wig, you can style it as desired. You may find that the wig is more comfortable to wear and looks more natural than before.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when thinning out wig hair:

  • Start by thinning out small sections of hair, and gradually work your way through the wig.
  • Use sharp scissors to create a clean cut and reduce any fraying.
  • Be patient and take your time, as rushing can lead to uneven results.
  • Try the wig on periodically to see how it looks and if you need to thin out more hair.

FAQ

Can I thin out a synthetic wig?

Yes, you can thin out a synthetic wig using the same process as a human hair wig. However, be careful not to use hot tools or styling products on synthetic wigs, as this can damage the fibers.

How often should I thin out my wig?

It depends on how often you wear the wig and how quickly the hair grows. As a general rule, you may need to thin out your wig every 3-6 months.

Can I thin out my wig too much?

Yes, it is possible to thin out your wig too much, which can create an unnatural and uneven look. It is best to thin out the hair gradually and blend as you go to create a natural look.

Hair Care

To keep your wig looking its best, it is important to take proper care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Use a wig brush or wide-toothed comb to detangle the hair.
  • Wash your wig with a wig shampoo and conditioner every 2-4 weeks.
  • Avoid using hot tools or styling products on synthetic wigs.
  • Protect your wig from sunlight and heat to prevent fading and damage.

Thinning out wig hair is a simple process that can make a big difference in the overall look and feel of your wig. By following these steps and taking proper care of your wig, you can enjoy a more natural and comfortable wig-wearing experience.
