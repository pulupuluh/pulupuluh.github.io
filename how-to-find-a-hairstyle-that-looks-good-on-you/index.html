How To Find A Hairstyle That Looks Good On You

Written by Daniel Jun 29, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of always wearing the same hairstyle or struggling to find one that suits you? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle to find a hairstyle that looks good on them. But the good news is that with a little bit of effort, you can find a style that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect hairstyle for you.

How to

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start looking for hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape will help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. There are four main face shapes: round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror.

If your face is round, your cheeks are the widest part of your face. If your face is oval, your forehead is slightly wider than your jawline. If your face is square, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width. If your face is heart-shaped, your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin is pointed.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair texture. Do you have straight, wavy, or curly hair? Is your hair fine or thick? Different hairstyles work better with different hair textures. For example, if you have fine hair, a short bob may work better for you than long, heavy layers.

3. Look for Inspiration

Once you've determined your face shape and hair texture, it's time to start looking for inspiration. You can find inspiration in magazines, online, or by looking at celebrities with similar face shapes and hair textures as you.

Take note of the styles that catch your eye and consider if they would work for your face shape and hair texture. It's also important to consider your personal style. Do you prefer a classic, sophisticated look, or are you more of a trendsetter?

4. Consult with a Professional

If you're still having trouble finding a hairstyle that works for you, consider consulting with a professional hair stylist. They can offer personalized advice and recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

They may also be able to suggest products or treatments that can help you achieve your desired look. Just be sure to bring in pictures of the styles you like and be open to their suggestions.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, determining your face shape is the first step in finding a hairstyle that suits you. Use the tips we provided to determine your face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Next, consider your hair texture. Is your hair straight, wavy, or curly? Is it fine or thick? Knowing your hair texture will help you choose a hairstyle that works with your hair type.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Start looking for inspiration in magazines, online, or by looking at celebrities with similar face shapes and hair textures as you. Take note of the styles that catch your eye and consider if they would work for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you're still having trouble finding a hairstyle that works for you, consider consulting with a professional hair stylist. They can offer personalized advice and recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Tips

  • Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to see how different styles would look on you before committing to a cut.
  • Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of maintenance if you don't have the time or energy to style your hair regularly.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new or step out of your comfort zone.
  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance style may work best for you.
  • Ask your stylist for tips on how to care for your hair and maintain your new style.

FAQ

What if I have a unique face shape or hair texture?

If you have a unique face shape or hair texture, it may be more difficult to find a hairstyle that suits you. In this case, we recommend consulting with a professional stylist who can offer personalized advice and recommendations.

How often should I get my hair cut to maintain my new style?

This will depend on your individual hair growth rate and how quickly your hair grows out of its shape. On average, we recommend getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you don't like your new hairstyle, don't panic. Hair grows back! Talk to your stylist about what you don't like and see if there are any adjustments that can be made.

Hair Care

Once you've found a hairstyle that works for you, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. This includes washing your hair regularly, using products that work well for your hair type, and protecting your hair from heat damage.

Be sure to ask your stylist for tips on how to care for your new style and invest in high-quality hair care products to keep your hair looking its best.

Finding a hairstyle that looks good on you may take some effort, but the end result is worth it. By following these tips and consulting with a professional stylist, you can find a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
