Will My Hair Grow Back After Gastric Sleeve

Written by Eveline May 27, 2023 · 7 min read

If you are considering gastric sleeve surgery, you may have heard about one of the most common side effects – hair loss. This can be a concerning and stressful issue, especially if you have spent years trying to grow your hair. However, the good news is that in most cases, your hair will grow back after gastric sleeve surgery.

Why Does Hair Loss Occur After Gastric Sleeve Surgery?

Hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, also known as telogen effluvium, occurs due to the body's reaction to the rapid weight loss. When you undergo gastric sleeve surgery, your body goes through significant changes, including a reduction in the amount of food you consume, which leads to a decrease in the number of nutrients your body receives. This can lead to a deficiency in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin, which are essential for healthy hair growth.

How to Prevent Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve Surgery

While hair loss is a common side effect of gastric sleeve surgery, there are several ways to prevent or minimize it:

1. Take Nutritional Supplements

It's essential to take nutritional supplements after gastric sleeve surgery to ensure that your body receives the necessary vitamins and minerals. Your doctor will recommend the appropriate supplements, including biotin, iron, and zinc, which are essential for healthy hair growth.

2. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that includes protein, fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can help prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery. These foods provide essential nutrients that promote healthy hair growth.

3. Avoid Crash Diets

Crash diets or extreme weight loss can cause hair loss, especially if they are not nutritionally balanced. It's essential to lose weight gradually and maintain a healthy weight to prevent hair loss.

FAQ About Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve Surgery

1. How Long Does Hair Loss Last After Gastric Sleeve Surgery?

Hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery usually occurs within the first three to six months after surgery and lasts for about six months to a year. However, in most cases, hair growth resumes after the initial shedding phase.

2. Can Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve Surgery Be Permanent?

In rare cases, hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery can be permanent. This can occur if the body experiences a severe nutrient deficiency for an extended period. However, with proper nutrition and care, most people experience hair regrowth.

3. Is Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve Surgery Common?

Yes, hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is common. Studies have shown that up to 50% of people who undergo gastric sleeve surgery experience hair loss.

How to Take Care of Your Hair After Gastric Sleeve Surgery

After gastric sleeve surgery, it's essential to take care of your hair to promote healthy growth:

1. Be Gentle

Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or a soft brush to detangle your hair gently.

2. Use a Mild Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a mild, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of essential oils. Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair

Wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the sun and wind. Avoid tight hairstyles, such as braids or ponytails, as they can cause breakage.

In Conclusion

While hair loss is a common side effect of gastric sleeve surgery, it's usually temporary, and your hair will grow back with proper nutrition and care. Remember to take your nutritional supplements, eat a balanced diet, and be gentle with your hair to promote healthy growth. If you have any concerns about hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, speak to your doctor.
