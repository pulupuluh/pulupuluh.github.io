Best Curly Hairstyles For Small Faces

If you have a small face and curly hair, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be sharing the best curly hairstyles for small faces. From short to long, we have options for every curly girl out there. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to finding the best curly hairstyles for small faces, it's important to consider the shape of your face. A small face typically means that your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all in proportion with each other. This means that you'll want to choose a hairstyle that complements these features and doesn't overwhelm them.

How to

The first step in choosing the best curly hairstyle for your small face is to determine your face shape. Once you know your face shape, you can then start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features. Some of the best hairstyles for small faces include:

1. Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut can be a great option for small faces with curly hair. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to show off their curls while still keeping their hair short and manageable. It's also a great way to highlight your cheekbones and jawline.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is another great option for small faces with curly hair. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. It's also a great way to balance out your features and create a more symmetrical look.

3. Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, then long layers are a great option for small faces with curly hair. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to show off their curls while still keeping their hair long and flowing. It's also a great way to add volume and movement to your hair.

Step by Step

Now that you know the best curly hairstyles for small faces, let's take a look at how to achieve these looks:

1. Short Pixie Cut

To achieve a short pixie cut, start by cutting your hair short all around. Then, use a curl enhancing product to define your curls and add volume. Finish off the look with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob

To achieve a shoulder-length bob, start by cutting your hair to the desired length. Then, use a curl enhancing product to define your curls and add volume. Finish off the look with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

3. Long Layers

To achieve long layers, start by cutting your hair to the desired length. Then, use a curl enhancing product to define your curls and add volume. Finish off the look with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best curly hairstyle for your small face:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape
  • Use curl enhancing products to define your curls and add volume
  • Consider the length of your hair when choosing a hairstyle
  • Experiment with different styles to find the perfect look for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hairstyles for small faces:

What is the best hairstyle for a small face?

The best hairstyle for a small face is one that complements your features and doesn't overwhelm them. Short pixie cuts, shoulder-length bobs, and long layers are all great options for small faces.

What products should I use to enhance my curls?

You can use a curl enhancing shampoo and conditioner, as well as a curl defining cream or gel to enhance your curls.

How often should I trim my curly hair?

You should trim your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair looking its best, be sure to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. This includes a curl enhancing shampoo and conditioner, as well as a curl defining cream or gel. You should also avoid using excessive heat on your hair, as this can damage your curls. Instead, try air drying your hair or using a diffuser on your blow dryer.

Now that you know the best curly hairstyles for small faces, it's time to try them out for yourself. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Happy styling!
