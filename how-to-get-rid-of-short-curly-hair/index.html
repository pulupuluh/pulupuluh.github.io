How To Get Rid Of Short Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver May 06, 2023 · 10 min read

Short curly hair can be a fun and playful look, but it can also be a struggle to manage. If you're tired of dealing with frizz, tangles, and unmanageable curls, then it's time to learn how to get rid of short curly hair. In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to straighten your hair, from the basics of hair care to the best tools and techniques for achieving sleek, straight locks.

Description

Short curly hair is a common hair type that can be difficult to manage. It often requires a lot of styling and maintenance to keep it looking its best. If you're looking to get rid of your short curly hair, there are several options available to you. You can choose to straighten your hair using heat styling tools or chemical treatments, or you can opt for a more natural approach by using hair care products that can help to tame your curls.

How to Get Rid of Short Curly Hair

If you're ready to say goodbye to your short curly hair, then it's time to start exploring your options for straightening your locks. Here are some of the best ways to get rid of short curly hair:

Heat Styling Tools

One of the most popular ways to straighten short curly hair is by using heat styling tools like straighteners and blow dryers. These tools work by applying heat to your hair, which can help to break down the bonds that make your hair curly. However, it's important to use these tools carefully to avoid damaging your hair. Here are some tips for using heat styling tools:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to help prevent damage to your hair
  • Start with a lower heat setting and gradually increase the temperature as needed
  • Don't leave the tool on one section of hair for too long
  • Use a comb or brush to help guide the tool through your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage

Chemical Treatments

If you're looking for a more permanent solution for your short curly hair, then chemical treatments like keratin treatments and chemical straightening may be the answer. These treatments work by breaking down the bonds in your hair that make it curly, resulting in straighter locks. However, these treatments can be expensive and can also cause damage to your hair if not done properly. It's important to do your research and choose a reputable stylist if you decide to go this route.

Natural Hair Care Products

If you're looking for a more natural approach to getting rid of your short curly hair, then there are several hair care products that can help to tame your curls. Look for products that are designed specifically for curly hair, as these will contain ingredients that can help to soften and smooth your curls. Here are some tips for using natural hair care products:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to help smooth your curls
  • Avoid products that contain sulfates, as these can dry out your hair
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair

Step by Step Guide

If you're ready to start straightening your short curly hair, then follow these steps for a sleek and smooth style:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray
  3. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up
  4. Starting at the back of your head, use a straightener to straighten each section of hair
  5. Once you've straightened all of your hair, apply a hair oil or serum to help smooth your locks

Tips for Getting Rid of Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for achieving a sleek and straight look:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling
  • Try sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz
  • Be patient – it may take some time to find the right products and techniques for your hair

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting rid of short curly hair:

Is it possible to permanently straighten short curly hair?

Yes, it is possible to permanently straighten short curly hair using chemical treatments like keratin treatments and chemical straightening. However, these treatments can be expensive and can also cause damage to your hair if not done properly.

Can I straighten my short curly hair without using heat?

Yes, you can straighten your short curly hair without using heat by using hair care products that can help to tame your curls. Look for products that are designed specifically for curly hair, as these will contain ingredients that can help to soften and smooth your curls.

Hair Care for Straight Hair

Once you've successfully straightened your short curly hair, it's important to take care of your new straight locks. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, straight hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to keep your hair smooth and shiny
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair

Conclusion

If you're ready to get rid of your short curly hair, then there are several options available to you. From heat styling tools to natural hair care products, there are many ways to achieve sleek, straight locks. Just remember to take care of your hair and be patient – it may take some time to find the right products and techniques for your hair type.
