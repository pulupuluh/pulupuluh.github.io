What Haircuts To Avoid For Round Faces In 2023

Written by William Apr 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we all know, haircuts can make or break our overall look. It is important to choose the right haircut to suit our face shape. Round faces, in particular, need haircuts that can create an illusion of length and add angles to the face. In this article, we will discuss what haircuts to avoid for round faces in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • Haircuts to Avoid
  • Best Haircuts for Round Faces
  • How to Style
  • Tips for Round Faces
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Round Faces

Haircuts to Avoid

Round faces tend to have wide cheekbones and a rounded chin. Therefore, haircuts that add volume on the sides or top of the head should be avoided. Here are a few haircuts to avoid:

  • Bobs: Short bobs that end at the chin or cheekbones can make the face look wider. Instead, opt for longer bobs that end at the collarbone or below.
  • Pixie cuts: Pixie cuts can accentuate the roundness of the face. If you must have a pixie cut, add layers on top to create the illusion of height.
  • Straight bangs: Straight bangs that end at the eyebrows can make the face look shorter. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or no bangs at all.
  • One-length cuts: One-length cuts can make the face look wider. Instead, opt for layered haircuts that add angles to the face.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces

Now that we know what haircuts to avoid, let's talk about the best haircuts for round faces:

  • Long layers: Long layers that frame the face can create the illusion of length. Add volume at the crown to create height.
  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs can create angles and make the face look longer.
  • Asymmetrical bobs: Asymmetrical bobs that are shorter on one side can create angles and add length to the face.
  • Shoulder-length cuts: Shoulder-length cuts that are layered can create angles and add volume.

How to Style

Styling your hair can make a huge difference in how it looks. Here are a few tips on how to style your hair for round faces:

  • Add volume: Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume at the crown. This will create height and lengthen the face.
  • Curl your hair: Curls can add angles and make the face look longer. Use a curling iron or rollers to add loose curls to your hair.
  • Side part: A side part can create angles and make the face look longer. Use a comb to create a deep side part.

Tips for Round Faces

Here are a few more tips for round faces:

  • Avoid center parts: Center parts can make the face look wider. Opt for a side part instead.
  • Accessorize: Use accessories like earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from the face.
  • Highlight: Use highlights to create angles and add depth to the hair.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about haircuts for round faces:

  • Can I still have short hair with a round face? Yes, you can still have short hair with a round face. Just make sure to add layers and volume to create height and angles.
  • Are there any haircuts that work for all face shapes? The lob (long bob) is a universally flattering haircut that works for all face shapes.
  • Should I avoid bangs altogether? No, you don't have to avoid bangs altogether. Side-swept bangs can work well for round faces.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Here are a few tips on how to take care of your hair if you have a round face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo: A volumizing shampoo can add volume and create height at the crown.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid heavy products: Heavy products can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Opt for lighter products like mousses and sprays.

Now that you know what haircuts to avoid for round faces in 2023, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to choose a haircut that suits your face shape and personal style.
