What Hair Color Do Guys Like Most On A Girl A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine May 09, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you wondering what hair color do guys like most on a girl? Look no further, as we have gathered all the information you need to know about this topic. From describing the most popular hair colors to providing tips on how to achieve your desired shade, we’ve got you covered. Keep reading to learn more!

Description

According to a survey conducted by L’Oreal, the most popular hair color among men is brown. This is followed by blonde, black, and red. It is important to note that these preferences may vary depending on the individual, but these are the most commonly favored hair colors.

How to Achieve Your Desired Hair Color

If you want to change your hair color to appeal to your male partner or to attract potential suitors, you should consider the following tips:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a significant role in determining which hair color suits you best. If you have warm undertones, you should opt for warm hair colors such as golden blonde, copper, or chestnut. On the other hand, if you have cool undertones, you should go for cool hair colors such as ash blonde, black, or blue-black.

2. Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also influence which hair color looks best on you. For example, if you have blue eyes, blonde or light brown hair will complement them well. If you have brown eyes, black or dark brown hair will enhance their color.

3. Choose a Shade That Matches Your Personality

Your hair color should reflect your personality and style. If you’re outgoing and adventurous, you might want to try a bold hair color such as bright red or platinum blonde. If you’re more reserved, a natural hair color such as brown or black may be more suitable.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

If you’ve decided to change your hair color, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you achieve your desired shade:

1. Choose Your Hair Dye

Choose a hair dye that matches your desired shade and is suitable for your hair type. If you’re unsure, consult a hair colorist for advice.

2. Prep Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly and dry it completely before coloring. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to prevent the dye from staining your skin.

3. Mix and Apply the Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye box to mix the dye. Apply the dye to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the tips. Massage the dye into your hair to ensure even coverage.

4. Wait and Rinse

Wait for the recommended time before rinsing the dye off with warm water. Apply a conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you’ve achieved your desired hair color, you should take steps to maintain it. Here are some tips:

1. Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for color-treated hair. This will help preserve your hair color and prevent fading.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause your color to fade faster. If you must use these tools, use a heat protectant spray and set them at a lower temperature.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun’s UV rays can also cause your hair color to fade. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun.

FAQ

Q: What hair color do guys find most attractive?

A: According to a survey conducted by L’Oreal, the most popular hair color among men is brown. This is followed by blonde, black, and red.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it is recommended that you consult a professional hair colorist if you’re unsure about which shade to choose or if you have damaged or chemically treated hair.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your color fades. Generally, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks and refresh your overall color every 8-10 weeks.

Hair Care

Regardless of your hair color, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining healthy and shiny locks. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Properly

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to cleanse your hair and scalp. Massage your scalp with your fingertips to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.

2. Condition Regularly

Apply a conditioner to your hair after shampooing to keep it moisturized and nourished. Leave the conditioner in your hair for a few minutes before rinsing it off.

3. Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling your hair with heat tools or harsh chemicals can damage it and cause split ends. Try to limit your use of these tools and opt for natural hairstyles such as braids or buns.

In conclusion, while men may have different preferences when it comes to hair color, the most popular shades are brown, blonde, black, and red. To achieve your desired hair color, consider your skin tone, eye color, and personality. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain your color with color-safe products and proper hair care techniques.
