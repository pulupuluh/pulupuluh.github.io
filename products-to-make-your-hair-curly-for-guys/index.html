Products To Make Your Hair Curly For Guys

Written by Bardi Aug 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and wishing for curly locks? You're not alone! Many guys want to add some curl to their hair for a new look. Fortunately, there are products available that can help you achieve those curls you've been dreaming of. In this article, we'll talk about the best products to make your hair curly for guys, how to use them, and some tips for maintaining your new curly hair.

How to Make Your Hair Curly for Guys

There are a few ways to make your hair curly as a guy. You can use a curling iron or hot rollers, but these methods can be time-consuming and may damage your hair. Another option is to use products specifically designed to help your hair curl. These products can range from curl creams to gels to mousses, and they work by enhancing your natural curls or creating new curls.

Products to Make Your Hair Curly for Guys

Here are some of the best products to make your hair curly for guys:

Curl Creams

Curl creams are great for enhancing natural curls and adding definition to your hair. They typically contain moisturizing ingredients to help your curls stay soft and bouncy. Some popular curl creams for guys include:

  • Ouidad Curl Quencher Moisturizing Styling Gel
  • DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler
  • Cantu Shea Butter Coconut Curling Cream

Hair Gels

Gels are great for creating new curls and holding them in place. They typically provide a stronger hold than curl creams, but can also make your hair stiff or crunchy if you use too much. Some popular gels for guys include:

  • Aveda Flax Seed Aloe Strong Hold Sculpting Gel
  • Garnier Fructis Style Curl Shape Defining Spray Gel
  • L'Oreal Paris Studio Line Melting Gel

Hair Mousses

Mousses are light and airy and can help add volume to your hair while defining your curls. They are great for guys with fine hair who want to add some bounce to their curls. Some popular mousses for guys include:

  • Paul Mitchell Sculpting Foam
  • Bumble and bumble Curl Mousse
  • Kenra Volume Mousse Extra

Step-by-Step Guide to Curly Hair for Guys

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your hair curly:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply your chosen curl product to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching it gently as you go.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently separate and fluff your curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair for Guys

Here are some tips for maintaining your new curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair, as this can break up your curls and create frizz.
  • Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage.
  • Refresh your curls in the morning with a spritz of water and a bit of curl cream or gel.

Frequently Asked Questions About Curly Hair for Guys

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair for guys:

Can guys with short hair have curly hair?

Yes! Guys with short hair can definitely have curly hair. In fact, short curly hair can look really stylish and trendy.

How do I prevent my curls from getting frizzy?

Frizz can be a common problem for guys with curly hair. To prevent frizz, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoid brushing your hair, and sleep with a satin or silk pillowcase.

Can I use the same products for curly hair as I do for straight hair?

No, you should use products specifically designed for curly hair. These products are formulated to enhance your curls and keep them looking their best.

Hair Care for Curly Hair for Guys

Caring for curly hair requires a bit of extra attention. Here are some tips for keeping your curls looking their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your curls.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can create frizz.

With the right products and care, you can achieve the curly hair you've always wanted. Give some of these products a try and see which ones work best for you. Happy curling!
