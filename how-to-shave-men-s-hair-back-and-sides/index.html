How To Shave Men s Hair Back And Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Feb 22, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking to freshen up your look or simply need a trim, shaving your hair back and sides is a great way to do it. This style is popular among men of all ages and can be achieved easily with a few simple steps. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to shave your hair back and sides, including some tips for achieving the perfect look and taking care of your hair afterwards.

Table of Contents

  • How to Shave Men’s Hair Back and Sides
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Perfect Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Shaving

How to Shave Men’s Hair Back and Sides

Before getting started, it’s important to note that shaving your hair back and sides can be tricky, especially if you’re doing it for the first time. It’s best to take your time and go slow to avoid any mishaps.

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Electric shaver or clipper
  • Comb or brush
  • Scissors (optional)
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to shave your hair back and sides:

  1. Start by combing or brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. If your hair is longer than desired, use scissors to trim it down a bit.
  3. Choose the right guard for your electric shaver or clipper. A shorter guard will give you a closer shave, while a longer one will leave more hair.
  4. Starting at the bottom of your hairline, use your electric shaver or clipper to shave upwards towards the top of your head.
  5. Repeat this process on the other side of your head, making sure to keep the same length and angle.
  6. Next, move to the back of your head and shave upwards towards the crown.
  7. Continue shaving until you’ve reached the desired length, making sure to blend the back and sides.
  8. Use your mirror to check for any missed spots or uneven areas.
  9. Touch up as needed until you’re happy with the result.
  10. Once you’re finished, wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any loose hairs.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Look

Follow these tips to ensure you get the perfect shave:

  • Go slow and be patient. Rushing can lead to uneven cuts or missed spots.
  • Use a light touch when shaving to avoid irritating your scalp.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one if needed.
  • Use your mirror to check for any missed spots or uneven areas.
  • Consider asking a friend or family member for help, especially if it’s your first time.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaving men’s hair back and sides:

  • How often should I shave my hair back and sides? It depends on your hair growth and personal preference, but typically every 2-4 weeks is recommended.
  • Do I need to use shaving cream or oil? No, you can shave dry with an electric shaver or clipper. However, using shaving cream or oil can help protect your scalp and make for a smoother shave.
  • Can I shave my hair back and sides with a razor? Yes, but it’s more difficult and time-consuming. An electric shaver or clipper is recommended.

Hair Care After Shaving

After shaving your hair back and sides, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect shaved hair back and sides look that will leave you feeling fresh and stylish.
