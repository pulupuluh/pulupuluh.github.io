How To Make Hair Look Messy Girl Tips Steps And Hair Care

If you're looking for a way to get that effortless, messy girl look, then look no further. Here are some tips and steps to help you achieve that perfectly undone hairstyle.

How to Make Hair Look Messy Girl

The key to making your hair look messy girl is to embrace the natural texture of your hair. Messy hair is all about creating volume and texture. Here are some ways to get that look:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls in your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Tease your hair at the roots to add volume and texture.
  • Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, undone look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you create that messy, undone look:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair using your fingers to create volume and texture.
  3. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair and scrunch with your fingers.
  4. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls in your hair.
  5. Tease your hair at the roots to add volume and texture.
  6. Spray a dry shampoo onto your roots to add volume and texture.
  7. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, undone look.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Making Hair Look Messy Girl

Here are some additional tips to help you get that perfectly undone hairstyle:

  • Don't be afraid to use your fingers to style your hair. Messy hair is all about embracing the natural texture of your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and create volume.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create volume and texture.
  • Experiment with different texturizing sprays to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in braids to create natural waves and texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my hair look messy girl without using heat tools?

Yes, you can achieve that messy girl look without using heat tools. You can use braids or twists to create natural waves and texture in your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray and your fingers to create volume and texture.

How often should I wash my hair to achieve the messy girl look?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to maintain the natural oils in your hair. Over washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it difficult to achieve the messy girl look.

Hair Care Tips for Messy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your messy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day. Heat can damage your hair and make it difficult to achieve the messy girl look.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it difficult to achieve volume and texture.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and steps, you can achieve that perfectly messy girl hairstyle. Embrace your natural texture and have fun creating volume and texture in your hair.
