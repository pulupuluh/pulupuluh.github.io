How To Tell What Hairstyle Suits Me A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Maybe you've browsed through countless magazines and Instagram pages but still can't decide on the perfect style for you. Don't worry, you're not alone! Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, but with a few simple steps, you'll be able to figure out what suits you best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you determine which styles will complement your features and highlight your best qualities. To determine your face shape, follow these simple steps:

  1. Pull your hair back and take a look at your face in the mirror.
  2. Use a measuring tape or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Write down each measurement and compare them to determine your face shape.

There are typically six face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start exploring different hairstyle options that will complement your features.

Consider Your Style Options

When choosing a new hairstyle, consider your personal style and the image you want to portray. Do you prefer a bold, edgy look or a more classic, sophisticated style? Your lifestyle and profession may also play a role in the hairstyle you choose.

Some popular hairstyle options include:

  • Short pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Long layers
  • Bangs
  • Updo
  • Braids

Research different styles and find inspiration from celebrities, social media, and magazines. Don't be afraid to try something new and step out of your comfort zone!

Determine Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a new hairstyle is your hair type. Different hairstyles work better with certain hair types, so it's important to choose a style that will work well with your natural hair texture and thickness.

Some common hair types include:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Kinky

Consider your hair type when choosing a new style, and talk to your hairstylist about the best options for your hair.

Consider Professional Help

If you're still unsure about which hairstyle suits you best, consider seeking professional help. A hairstylist can provide expert advice and help you choose a style that complements your features and fits your lifestyle.

When visiting a hairstylist, bring pictures of hairstyles you like and be open to their suggestions. They may recommend a style you never considered that will work well for your face shape and hair type.

Think About Maintenance

When choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to think about the maintenance required to keep it looking its best. Some styles require daily styling and frequent salon visits, while others are low-maintenance and easy to maintain.

Consider how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling and maintaining your hair. If you have a busy lifestyle or prefer a low-maintenance style, choose a hairstyle that requires minimal styling and upkeep.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your skin tone and eye color when choosing a hair color.
  • Take into account any facial features you want to highlight or downplay.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to get an idea of how different styles will look on you.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you.

FAQs About Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hairstyle:

What hairstyle suits a round face?

Hairstyles that elongate the face, such as long layers or a center part, work well with a round face shape.

What hairstyle suits an oval face?

Oval faces can pull off a variety of styles, but long layers and a side part are particularly flattering.

What hairstyle suits a square face?

Soft, layered styles that frame the face, such as a long bob or loose waves, work well with a square face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for your new hairstyle:

  • Invest in quality hair care products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your style.
  • Consider using hair masks and treatments to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to choose the perfect hairstyle that complements your features and fits your personal style. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you!
