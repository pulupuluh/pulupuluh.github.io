Haircuts For Thin Straight Hair Round Face Tips Tricks And Styles

Written by Frank Jul 08, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have thin, straight hair and a round face, finding the right haircut can be challenging. You want a style that complements your facial features, adds volume to your hair, and makes you feel confident. In this article, we’ll share some tips, tricks, and styles you can try to get the perfect haircut for your round face and thin straight hair.

Tips for Choosing a Haircut for Thin Straight Hair Round Face

When choosing a haircut for thin straight hair and a round face, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Opt for a layered cut to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Avoid blunt cuts or bobs that end at your chin level, as they can make your face look rounder
  • Choose a side part or deep side part to create the illusion of a longer face
  • Try a pixie cut or a short bob with side bangs to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

Remember to consult with your hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair. They can help you determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

How to Style Thin Straight Hair Round Face

Styling thin straight hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques, you can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. Here are some tips:

  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying to give your hair more body
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down
  • Use a round brush to create curls or waves for a more textured look
  • Try a messy bun or a half-up half-down style to add volume to your hair

Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Thin Straight Hair Round Face

If you’re considering cutting your own hair at home, follow these steps:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a comb to section your hair into four parts: top, back, and two sides
  • Starting with the back section, comb your hair straight down and trim the ends of your hair
  • Move on to the side sections, combing the hair straight down and cutting the ends at a slight angle to add layers
  • Finish with the top section, combing the hair straight up and cutting the ends at a slight angle to add volume

Remember to cut small sections of hair at a time and to regularly check your progress in a mirror.

Popular Haircut Styles for Thin Straight Hair Round Face

Here are some popular haircut styles that work well for thin straight hair and a round face:

  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Short bob with side bangs
  • Layered long bob (lob)
  • Long layers with face-framing highlights
  • Side-parted wavy lob

Remember to consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut style for your face shape and hair type.

FAQs

Q: Can I still get a blunt cut if I have a round face and thin straight hair?
A: While blunt cuts aren’t the best choice for a round face, you can still get a blunt cut if you like the look. Just make sure to add some layers to your hair to create the illusion of volume and texture.

Q: Should I get bangs if I have a round face and thin straight hair?
A: Bangs can work well for a round face and thin hair, as long as they are side-swept or asymmetrical. Avoid blunt or straight-across bangs that can make your face look rounder.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Straight Hair Round Face

To keep your thin straight hair healthy and voluminous, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools every day and use a heat protectant spray when you do
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Try a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair

Remember to be gentle when brushing or styling your hair to avoid damaging it.

In conclusion, finding the perfect haircut for thin straight hair and a round face can be challenging, but with the right tips, tricks, and styles, you can achieve a confident and flattering look. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
