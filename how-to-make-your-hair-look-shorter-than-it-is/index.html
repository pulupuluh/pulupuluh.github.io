How To Make Your Hair Look Shorter Than It Is

Written by William Apr 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you ever wish you could switch up your hair length without committing to a haircut? You're not alone. Luckily, there are ways to make your hair look shorter than it is without making a drastic change. In this article, we'll explore various techniques and styling tips that can help you achieve the look you desire.

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

Before we dive into the specifics, let's talk about the general idea behind making your hair look shorter. The goal is to create the illusion of less length by adding volume and texture to the hair. By doing so, the hair appears shorter and more layered.

1. Try a Bob

A bob is a classic haircut that is shorter in the back and longer in the front. If you're not ready to commit to the chop, you can create the look of a bob by tucking your hair under and pinning it in place. Use a curling iron to add waves to the front pieces for added texture.

2. Experiment with Braids

Braids are a great way to create the appearance of shorter hair. Try a french braid or fishtail braid that starts at the crown of your head and ends in a ponytail. This will give the illusion of a shorter, layered haircut.

3. Use Hair Accessories

Adding accessories to your hair can help make it appear shorter. Try a headband or hair scarf to create the illusion of a shorter haircut. You can also use bobby pins to create a faux-bob by pinning the hair under at the nape of your neck.

Step by Step

1. Create Texture

Start by creating texture in your hair. Use a volumizing mousse on damp hair and blow-dry using a round brush. This will create volume and movement in the hair.

2. Curl the Ends

Use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair. This will create a layered look and make the hair appear shorter. Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

3. Tuck and Pin

Tuck your hair under and pin it in place at the nape of your neck. Use bobby pins to create a faux-bob by pinning the hair under at the nape of your neck. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use hair accessories to create the illusion of shorter hair.
  • Don't be afraid to use products to create texture and volume in the hair.

FAQ

Can I make my hair look shorter without cutting it?

Yes, you can make your hair look shorter without cutting it by using styling techniques and hair accessories.

What kind of hairstyles should I try?

Try a bob, braids, or a faux-bob created with bobby pins.

What products should I use to create volume and texture?

Use a volumizing mousse on damp hair and blow-dry using a round brush. You can also use a curling iron to add waves and texture to the hair.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using a nourishing shampoo and conditioner. Use a heat protectant when using hot tools to prevent damage to the hair. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

In conclusion, making your hair look shorter than it is can be achieved through styling techniques and using hair accessories. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
