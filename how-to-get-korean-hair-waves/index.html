How To Get Korean Hair Waves A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 21, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking to achieve soft, effortless waves like those of Korean celebrities, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll walk you through how to get Korean hair waves in relaxed English language. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, this hairstyle is perfect for any occasion. So, let's get started!

Description

Korean hair waves are a popular hairstyle that has been trending for years. It's a relaxed hairstyle that gives you soft, bouncy waves without looking too curly or frizzy. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

How to Get Korean Hair Waves

Getting Korean hair waves is easier than you might think. Here's how:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in getting Korean hair waves is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, always apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will prevent your hair from getting damaged and keep it healthy.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry. Use a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish. Make sure to blow-dry your hair in sections to ensure that every strand is dry.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts, the top, and bottom. Secure the top section with a clip or hair tie.

Step 5: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron, curl the bottom section of your hair. Hold the curling iron vertically and wrap a small section of hair around the barrel. Hold it for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process until you've curled all of the hair in the bottom section.

Step 6: Curl the Top Section

Unclip the top section of your hair and repeat the curling process. Make sure to curl the hair away from your face to achieve the Korean hair wave look.

Step 7: Run Your Fingers Through Your Hair

After you've curled your entire head, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create soft waves. You can also use a wide-tooth comb to create a more defined wave.

Step 8: Apply Hairspray

To keep your Korean hair waves in place, apply a light hairspray. This will also add some shine and hold to your hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect Korean hair waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a barrel size of 1 inch or smaller. This will give you tighter and more defined waves.
  • Curl your hair in small sections to ensure that every strand is curled.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly or the waves will look too curly.
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting Korean hair waves:

How long will my Korean hair waves last?

Your Korean hair waves should last for 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Can I achieve Korean hair waves with naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve Korean hair waves with naturally curly hair. Just make sure to blow-dry your hair straight before curling it.

What products should I use to maintain my Korean hair waves?

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. You can also use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.

Now that you know how to get Korean hair waves, it's time to try it out for yourself. With these easy steps and tips, you'll be able to achieve the perfect Korean hair wave look. Good luck!
