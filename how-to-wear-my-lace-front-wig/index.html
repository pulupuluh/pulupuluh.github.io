How To Wear My Lace Front Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you new to wearing lace front wigs? Do you find it daunting to put on a wig for the first time? Fear not! In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to wear a lace front wig with ease. We will also provide you with tips on how to maintain your wig and answer some of the most frequently asked questions about lace front wigs.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Lace Front Wig
  • Tips for Wearing a Lace Front Wig
  • FAQs About Lace Front Wigs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Lace Front Wig

How to Wear a Lace Front Wig

Step 1: Prepare your hair by braiding it or wrapping it around your head. This will create a flat base for the wig to sit on.

Step 2: Put on a wig cap that matches your skin tone. This will help the wig look more natural and blend in with your skin.

Step 3: Cut off the excess lace on the wig. Be careful not to cut too close to the hairline or you may damage the wig.

Step 4: Apply wig adhesive or tape to the hairline of the wig. You can also apply some adhesive to your forehead to keep the wig in place.

Step 5: Place the wig on your head starting from the back and working your way to the front. Make sure the wig is centered on your head and adjust it until it feels secure.

Step 6: Style the wig as desired. You can use a flat iron, curling iron, or hot rollers to create different looks.

Tips for Wearing a Lace Front Wig

1. Use a wig stand to store your wig when you're not wearing it. This will help it maintain its shape and prevent it from tangling.

2. Avoid using products that contain alcohol or oil as they can damage the wig.

3. Don't wear your wig for too long. It's best to give your scalp a break and let it breathe.

4. Invest in a good quality wig. A high-quality wig will last longer and look more natural.

FAQs About Lace Front Wigs

Q: Can I wear a lace front wig if I have a sensitive scalp?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure to use a wig cap and avoid using products that contain alcohol or oil.

Q: How long can I wear my lace front wig?

A: It's best to give your scalp a break and not wear the wig for more than a few hours at a time.

Q: Can I swim with my lace front wig?

A: It's not recommended as chlorine and saltwater can damage the wig.

Hair Care Tips for Your Lace Front Wig

1. Wash your wig regularly using a wig shampoo and conditioner.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig.

3. Let your wig air dry on a wig stand.

4. Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig as they can damage the fibers.

Now that you know how to wear a lace front wig, it's time to go out and rock that new hairstyle! Don't forget to take good care of your wig so it can last for many wears to come.
