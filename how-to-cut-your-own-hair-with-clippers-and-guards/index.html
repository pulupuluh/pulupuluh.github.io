How To Cut Your Own Hair With Clippers And Guards A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 30, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts at the salon or barber shop? If so, you may want to consider cutting your own hair with clippers and guards. This can save you time and money, while also giving you more control over your hairstyle. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair with clippers and guards, as well as some tips for achieving the best results.

Description

Cutting your own hair with clippers and guards is a great way to maintain your hairstyle without having to visit a salon or barber shop. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.

How to

Before you begin, you will need to gather some essential tools, including hair clippers, guards, scissors, and a comb. You may also want to invest in a handheld mirror, so you can see the back of your head. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Guard Size

The first step is to choose the guard size that you want to use. Guards come in different sizes, ranging from 1/8 inch to 1 inch. The smaller the guard, the shorter your hair will be. If you are unsure which guard size to use, start with a larger guard and work your way down until you achieve your desired length.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even result.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin by attaching the guard to your clippers and starting with the sides of your head. Use the clippers to trim your hair in an upward motion, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Be sure to keep the clippers level and move them in a consistent motion to avoid uneven cuts.

Step 4: Move to the Top

Once you have finished the sides, move on to the top of your head. Use the same technique as before, starting at the back and working your way forward. If you want to leave your hair longer on top, you can use a larger guard or no guard at all.

Step 5: Trim the Back

After you have finished the sides and top, it's time to trim the back of your head. This can be a bit trickier, so you may want to use a handheld mirror to help you see what you are doing. Use the clippers to trim your hair in a downward motion, starting from the top and working your way down.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair with the clippers, use scissors to blend the hair and create a more natural look. Start by combing your hair straight up and trimming any longer strands. Then, comb the hair back down and blend the top with the sides using scissors.

Step 7: Clean Up

Finally, clean up any stray hairs around your ears and neck using the clippers or scissors. You can also use a comb to check for any uneven spots and trim them as needed.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your own hair with clippers and guards:

  • Start with a larger guard size and work your way down until you achieve your desired length.
  • Use a handheld mirror to help you see the back of your head.
  • Take your time and use a consistent motion to avoid uneven cuts.
  • Blend the hair with scissors for a more natural look.
  • Clean up any stray hairs around your ears and neck for a polished finish.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with clippers and guards if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still cut your own hair with clippers and guards if you have curly hair. However, you may want to use a larger guard size to account for the natural curl pattern of your hair.

How often should I cut my own hair with clippers and guards?

How often you cut your own hair with clippers and guards will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. As a general rule, most people will need to cut their hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain their desired length.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting your hair with clippers and guards, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking and feeling healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim any split ends regularly to prevent breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your own hair with clippers and guards, saving time and money while achieving a professional-looking result.
