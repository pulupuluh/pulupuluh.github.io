Women s Short Haircuts For Cowlicks

Written by Luffy Aug 28, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're a woman with a cowlick, you know how difficult it can be to find a short haircut that works for you. Cowlicks can make it challenging to style your hair, and they can also make your hair look uneven or messy. Fortunately, there are plenty of short haircuts for cowlicks that can help you look stylish and put-together. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about finding the perfect short haircut for your cowlick.

Description

A cowlick is a section of hair that grows in a different direction than the rest of your hair. Cowlicks can be frustrating to deal with, especially if you're trying to style your hair in a certain way. Women with cowlicks often struggle to find short haircuts that flatter their face shape and work with their hair's natural growth pattern.

How to Find the Right Short Haircut for Your Cowlick

If you have a cowlick, it's important to choose a short haircut that works with your hair's natural growth pattern. Here are some tips to help you find the right haircut:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. Different face shapes look best with different types of haircuts. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a short haircut that adds height to the top of your head to elongate your face.

2. Look for Short Haircuts with Texture

Short haircuts with texture can help to camouflage your cowlick and make your hair look more voluminous. Consider a short haircut with layers or choppy ends to add texture to your hair.

3. Opt for a Side-Parted Hairstyle

A side-parted hairstyle can help to disguise your cowlick and make your hair look more even. Consider a short haircut with a deep side part to help control your cowlick.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Cowlicks

Once you've found the perfect short haircut for your cowlick, it's important to learn how to style it correctly. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short haircuts for cowlicks:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

2. Apply a Styling Product

To help control your cowlick and keep your hair in place, apply a styling product to your hair. A lightweight gel or mousse can help to add texture and control to your hair.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair in the direction of your cowlick. This will help to smooth out your cowlick and make your hair look more even.

4. Finish with Hairspray

To keep your hair in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to control your cowlick and keep your hair looking polished.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Cowlicks

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short haircut for your cowlick:

1. Visit Your Hairstylist Regularly

Regular haircuts can help to keep your hair looking its best and prevent split ends. Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks to maintain your short haircut.

2. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Choose products that are designed for your hair type and use them as directed.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make your cowlick more difficult to control. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools and avoid using them too often.

FAQs

Q: Can I still have bangs if I have a cowlick?

A: Yes, you can still have bangs if you have a cowlick. However, it's important to choose a bang style that works with your cowlick. Consider a side-swept bang or a blunt bang that falls in the direction of your cowlick.

Q: Can I color my hair if I have a cowlick?

A: Yes, you can color your hair if you have a cowlick. However, it's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and natural hair color. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect hair color for you.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have a cowlick?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up if you have a cowlick. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that works with your cowlick. Consider a messy bun or a low ponytail to help control your cowlick.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Cowlicks

Here are some tips to help you care for your hair if you have a short haircut for your cowlick:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb can help to detangle your hair without causing breakage or damage. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through your hair after washing it.

2. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle. Wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's rays can damage your hair and make your cowlick more difficult to control. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

In conclusion, there are plenty of short haircuts for cowlicks that can help you look stylish and put-together. By following these tips and using the right hair products, you can keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
