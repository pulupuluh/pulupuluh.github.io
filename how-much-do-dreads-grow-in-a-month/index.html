How Much Do Dreads Grow In A Month A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank May 30, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you considering getting dreadlocks but wondering how much your hair will grow each month? This is a common question for those who are new to the world of dreadlocks. In this guide, we will answer the question "how much do dreads grow in a month?" and provide you with tips on how to care for your locks.

Table of Contents

  • How Much Do Dreads Grow in a Month?
  • How to Measure Your Dreadlock Growth
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Measuring Your Dreadlock Growth
  • Tips for Promoting Healthy Dreadlock Growth
  • FAQs About Dreadlock Growth
  • Hair Care Tips for Dreadlocks

How Much Do Dreads Grow in a Month?

On average, dreadlocks grow about 0.5-1 inch per month. This is the same rate as regular hair growth. However, there are several factors that can affect the rate of dreadlock growth, such as:

  • The health of your scalp and hair
  • Your diet
  • Your genetics
  • The length and thickness of your hair
  • Your lifestyle and stress levels

It's important to keep these factors in mind when measuring your dreadlock growth. It's also important to note that while your hair may grow at a certain rate, your dreadlocks may not appear to grow as fast due to the way the hair is locked together.

How to Measure Your Dreadlock Growth

Measuring your dreadlock growth may seem daunting, but it's actually quite simple. All you need is a ruler or tape measure and a section of hair to measure. Here's how to do it:

Step-by-Step Guide to Measuring Your Dreadlock Growth

  1. Select a section of hair to measure. This can be a single dreadlock or a group of them.
  2. Hold the section of hair up and straighten it as much as possible.
  3. Place the ruler or tape measure at the base of the section of hair and measure to the tip.
  4. Record the measurement.
  5. Repeat these steps every month to track your dreadlock growth.

Tips for Promoting Healthy Dreadlock Growth

If you want to promote healthy dreadlock growth, there are several things you can do:

  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of protein and vitamins
  • Stay hydrated
  • Massage your scalp to promote blood flow
  • Use natural hair products that are free of harsh chemicals
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind

FAQs About Dreadlock Growth

Here are some frequently asked questions about dreadlock growth:

Can I speed up my dreadlock growth?

While you can't change your hair's natural growth rate, you can promote healthy growth by following the tips above.

Will my dreadlocks grow faster if I have shorter hair?

No, the rate of hair growth is the same regardless of hair length.

Can I make my dreadlocks grow longer?

No, you can't make your hair grow longer than its natural rate. However, following the tips above can help promote healthy growth.

Hair Care Tips for Dreadlocks

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your dreadlocks healthy and looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and prevent buildup
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your locks
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using rubber bands or tight hair ties that can cause breakage

By following these tips and measuring your dreadlock growth regularly, you can keep your locks looking healthy and vibrant.
