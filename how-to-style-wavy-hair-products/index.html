How To Style Wavy Hair Products

Written by Aprilia Jul 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks effortlessly chic, it can be a struggle to style and maintain. Luckily, there are a variety of wavy hair products on the market that can help you achieve the perfect look. In this article, we will guide you through the process of styling wavy hair products so that you can achieve the perfect look every time.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Wavy Hair Products
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Wavy Hair Products
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

How to Style Wavy Hair Products

The first step in styling wavy hair products is to determine your hair type. Wavy hair can range from loose waves to tight, coiled curls. Once you have determined your hair type, you can choose the right products to enhance your natural waves.

Some of the best wavy hair products on the market include:

  • Sea salt spray
  • Curl enhancing cream
  • Texturizing spray
  • Mousse
  • Hair oil

Each of these products can help you achieve a different look, whether you prefer loose waves or defined curls. The key is to experiment with different products until you find the right combination for your hair type.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to styling wavy hair products:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for wavy hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of curl enhancing cream to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help define your natural waves.
  3. If you want more volume, apply a mousse to your roots. Use a volumizing brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume.
  4. Spray a texturizing spray or sea salt spray throughout your hair. This will give your waves a beachy, tousled look.
  5. If your hair is prone to frizz, apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair. This will help smooth out any flyaways.
  6. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your natural waves and prevent frizz.
  7. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define any loose waves or curls. Wrap small sections of hair around the iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your waves in place.

Tips for Styling Wavy Hair Products

Here are some tips for styling wavy hair products:

  • Experiment with different products to find the right combination for your hair type.
  • Don't overdo it with product. Start with a small amount and build up as needed.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • If you have tight curls, avoid brushing your hair when it is dry. This can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and diffuser attachment to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling wavy hair products:

Can I use sea salt spray on dry hair?

Yes, you can use sea salt spray on dry hair to add texture and volume. Just be sure to use a light hand and avoid over-applying.

How often should I wash my wavy hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Wavy hair tends to be on the drier side, so you may only need to wash it once or twice a week. Experiment with different washing schedules to find what works best for you.

Can I use a hair oil on my roots?

No, hair oil should only be applied to the ends of your hair. Applying it to your roots can cause your hair to look greasy.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for wavy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your natural wave pattern.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

By following these tips and using the right wavy hair products, you can achieve gorgeous, effortless waves every time.
