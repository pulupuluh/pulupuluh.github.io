How To Do Easy Updos For Long Hair

Written by Frank Apr 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of wearing your long hair down or in a simple ponytail? Updos are a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some elegance to your look. In this article, we’ll go over some easy updos for long hair that you can do at home.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Do Easy Updos for Long Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Creating Easy Updos for Long Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Do Easy Updos for Long Hair

Before we get started, make sure you have the following items:

  • Bobby pins
  • Comb
  • Hairspray
  • Elastic bands

Now, let’s get into some easy updos for long hair:

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic and easy updo for long hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself until it forms a bun
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place

2. Side Braid

A side braid is a simple yet elegant updo for long hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Part your hair to one side
  2. Gather all of your hair to the opposite side of the part
  3. Divide your hair into three sections
  4. Braid your hair all the way down to the end
  5. Secure the braid with an elastic band
  6. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look
  7. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place

3. Half-Up Bun

The half-up bun is a trendy and easy updo for long hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Take the top half of your hair and gather it into a high ponytail
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself until it forms a bun
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a general step by step guide for creating easy updos for long hair:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair
  2. Decide on the type of updo you want to create
  3. Gather the necessary tools and accessories
  4. Style your hair according to the updo you’ve chosen
  5. Secure your hair with bobby pins or elastic bands
  6. Pull out strands of hair to create a messy look (if desired)
  7. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips for Creating Easy Updos for Long Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating easy updos for long hair:

  • Practice makes perfect - don’t get discouraged if your first attempts don’t turn out perfectly
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable
  • Start with a small amount of hairspray and add more as needed
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories

FAQ

Can I create easy updos for long hair if my hair is thin?

Yes, you can still create easy updos for long hair even if your hair is thin. You may need to use more bobby pins and hairspray to hold the style in place.

How long does it take to create an easy updo for long hair?

It depends on the style you choose and your level of experience. Some updos can be created in just a few minutes while others may take longer.

Hair Care for Long Hair

To keep your long hair healthy and looking its best, here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness

With these tips and easy updos for long hair, you can switch up your hairstyle and add some elegance to your look. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
