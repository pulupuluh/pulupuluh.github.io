Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps on how to make thick straight hair fluffy:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup that can weigh your hair down.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair, focusing on the roots to add volume. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your hair upside down and blow dry it using a large round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. This technique will lift your roots and add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

Using a curling iron, curl your hair away from your face to add volume and bounce to your hair. Make sure to curl your hair in small sections to achieve the best results.

Step 5: Tease Your Hair

Take a section of your hair and tease it at the roots using a teasing comb to add volume. Repeat this step until you reach your desired volume.

Tips to Make Thick Straight Hair Fluffy

Here are some tips to make your thick straight hair fluffy:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to lift your roots and add volume.
  • Use a curling iron to add bounce and volume to your hair.
  • Tease your hair at the roots to add volume.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash my hair to make it fluffy?

You should wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Using dry shampoo in between washes can help you maintain fluffy hair.

Can I use a volumizing spray instead of a mousse?

Yes, you can use a volumizing spray instead of a mousse. Just make sure to apply it to your roots and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

What if I have fine hair?

If you have fine hair, you can still achieve fluffy hair by using lightweight styling products and avoiding heavy products that can weigh your hair down.

Hair Care for Fluffy Hair

To maintain your fluffy hair, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
