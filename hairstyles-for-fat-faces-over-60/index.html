Hairstyles For Fat Faces Over 60 A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Daniel Mar 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our faces tend to lose definition and volume. For those with round or chubby faces, this can be even more noticeable. However, with the right haircut, you can enhance your features and bring out your best self. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to choose hairstyles for fat faces over 60, as well as some popular options to consider.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first thing to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle is to find a cut that flatters your face shape. For those with round or chubby faces, you'll want to look for hairstyles that add height and length to your features. This can be achieved by opting for cuts that are longer on top and shorter on the sides, or by adding layers and texture to your hair. Additionally, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too voluminous, as they can make your face appear wider.

Step-by-Step Guide: Hairstyles for Fat Faces Over 60

Here are some popular hairstyles to consider:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for those with round faces. This short and easy-to-manage cut adds height to your features and draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones. To make it work for your face shape, ask your stylist to add layers and texture to the top of your hair, while keeping the sides and back short.

2. Bob Cut

A classic bob cut can also be flattering for those with round faces. This cut adds length to your features and can be customized to suit your preferences, whether you like it straight, wavy, or curly. To make it work for your face shape, opt for a longer bob that hits just below your chin, and ask your stylist to add layers and texture to the ends.

3. Shoulder-Length Cut

A shoulder-length cut is another great option for those with round faces. This cut adds length to your features without being too overwhelming, and can be styled in many different ways. To make it work for your face shape, opt for a cut that hits just below your shoulders, and ask your stylist to add layers and texture throughout your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and flattering. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about hairstyles for fat faces over 60:

1. Can I still wear bangs if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still wear bangs if you have a round face. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your features. Opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle.

2. Should I color my hair if I have a round face?

Whether or not to color your hair is a personal choice. However, keep in mind that lighter hair colors can make your face appear wider, while darker colors can have a slimming effect. If you're unsure, talk to your stylist about what colors would work best for your face shape.

3. What should I do if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you don't like your new hairstyle, don't panic. Talk to your stylist about what changes you would like to make, and they can help you find a solution that works for you. Remember, it's important to feel confident and comfortable in your hair.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the right hairstyle can make a big difference in how you look and feel. By following these tips and exploring different options, you can find a cut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, bob cut, or shoulder-length cut, remember to maintain your hair and keep it healthy and shiny. With the right hairstyle and a little bit of confidence, you can look and feel your best at any age.
