Short Hairstyles For Oval Face Over 50

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are over 50 and have an oval face, you are lucky enough to have the most versatile face shape. You can rock almost any hairstyle, but short haircuts are the best for you. Short hairstyles can accentuate your features and make you look younger. In this post, we will give you tips on how to choose the perfect short hairstyle for your oval face over 50.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Oval Face over 50

Choosing the perfect short hairstyle for your oval face over 50 can be a daunting task. You need to consider your hair texture, face shape, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect short hairstyle:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture plays a significant role in choosing the perfect short hairstyle. If you have fine hair, go for a short bob or a pixie cut with layers. If you have thick hair, go for a short bob with layers or a graduated bob.

2. Consider Your Face Shape

Even though you have an oval face, there are still some considerations to make. If you have a long oval face, go for a short bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs. If you have a round oval face, go for a layered bob or a pixie cut with volume on top.

3. Consider Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also an essential factor in choosing the perfect short hairstyle. If you are a fan of the edgy look, go for a choppy pixie cut. If you prefer a classic look, go for a sleek bob.

Step by Step Guide for Short Hairstyles for Oval Face over 50

Here is a step by step guide to help you achieve the perfect short hairstyle for your oval face over 50:

Step 1: Consult with Your Hairstylist

The first step is to consult with your hairstylist. They will help you choose the perfect short hairstyle based on your hair texture, face shape, and personal style.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.

Step 3: Apply Hair Gel or Mousse

Apply hair gel or mousse to your hair to add volume and texture.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Use a diffuser attachment to dry the roots and add volume.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair according to your chosen short hairstyle. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture, if needed.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Oval Face over 50

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short hairstyles need regular trimming to maintain their shape. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

2. Use Heat Protectant Products

Use heat protectant products when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Use Hair Masks

Use hair masks once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have a short hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have a short hairstyle. Choose a color that complements your skin tone and personal style.

Q: Can I wear a short hairstyle if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a short hairstyle if you have thin hair. Go for a short bob or a pixie cut with layers to add volume and texture.

Q: Can I wear a short hairstyle if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a short hairstyle if you have curly hair. Go for a layered bob or a curly pixie cut to accentuate your curls.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Oval Face over 50

Short hairstyles require minimal maintenance, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.

2. Use Hair Masks

Use hair masks once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use heat protectant products when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Short hairstyles are perfect for oval faces over 50. They can accentuate your features and make you look younger. Follow the tips and steps above to choose and maintain the perfect short hairstyle for you.
