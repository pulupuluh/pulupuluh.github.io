How To Deva Cut Curly Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a Deva Cut at a salon? Do you want to learn how to cut your own curly hair at home? You're in luck! In this guide, we will walk you through the steps of a Deva Cut and provide tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Let's get started!

Description

A Deva Cut is a specialized haircut for curly hair that involves cutting hair while it is dry and in its natural state. The technique is designed to enhance the natural curl pattern and create a more personalized look.

Deva Cuts are typically expensive and not always available at all salons. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a Deva Cut at home.

How to Deva Cut Curly Hair at Home

Before you begin, it's important to invest in the proper tools for a Deva Cut:

  • Sharp hair cutting shears
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Once you have the necessary tools, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would.
  2. After washing, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Do not rub or twist your hair as this can cause frizz.
  3. Comb your hair with a wide-tooth comb to remove any tangles.
  4. Section your hair into small, manageable sections using hair clips.
  5. Start with the bottom section of your hair and twist it into a small spiral. Hold the spiral at the end and snip off any split ends with hair cutting shears.
  6. Continue to work your way up your head, section by section, twisting and trimming each spiral.
  7. Once you have trimmed all of your curls, shake out your hair to see the final result. You may need to make additional small trims to ensure an even look.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Deva Cutting Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when Deva Cutting your curly hair:

  • Start with small trims and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.
  • Be patient. Deva Cutting can take a lot of time, especially if you have long hair. Take breaks as needed.
  • Do not use regular scissors or razors to cut your hair. These tools can cause split ends and damage to your curls.
  • Invest in a good quality pair of hair cutting shears. This will ensure a clean cut and prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about Deva Cutting curly hair:

  • Can you Deva Cut your hair if it's wet?
  • No, a Deva Cut must be done on dry hair in its natural state to achieve the best results.

  • How often should you Deva Cut your hair?
  • This can vary depending on your hair type and how quickly it grows. On average, it's recommended to get a Deva Cut every 3-4 months.

  • Can you Deva Cut your hair if it's straightened?
  • No, a Deva Cut is designed for curly hair and should not be done on straightened hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair while it's still wet to help define your curls.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangles and frizz.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful Deva Cut at home and maintain healthy, defined curls. Happy cutting!
