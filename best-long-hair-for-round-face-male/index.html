Best Long Hair For Round Face Male How To Choose And Style

Written by Aprilia Jun 19, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have a round face and want to grow your hair long, it’s important to choose the right style that flatters your face shape. While there are no hard and fast rules, there are some general guidelines that can help you find the best long hair for round face male. In this blog post, we’ll share some tips on how to choose and style long hair for round face male, as well as some frequently asked questions and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Long Hair for Round Face Male
  • How to Style Long Hair for Round Face Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Round Face Male
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Round Face Male
  • FAQs About Long Hair for Round Face Male
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair for Round Face Male

How to Choose the Best Long Hair for Round Face Male

When choosing the best long hair for round face male, you want to create balance by adding height and volume. This can be achieved with layers and texture, which can also help elongate the face. Here are some styles to consider:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help create vertical lines, which can elongate the face. They also add volume and movement, which can make the hair appear fuller.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create angles and break up the roundness of the face. They also draw attention to the eyes, which can be a great way to accentuate your best features.

3. Textured Waves

Textured waves can add volume and movement to the hair, which can create the illusion of a longer face. They also add dimension and can make the hair look more interesting.

4. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail can help elongate the face and create the illusion of height. It’s also a simple and classic style that can be dressed up or down.

How to Style Long Hair for Round Face Male

Once you’ve chosen the best long hair for round face male, it’s important to know how to style it to flatter your face shape. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

To add volume to your hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help create the illusion of height and fullness.

2. Blow-Dry with a Round Brush

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create volume and movement. Start at the roots and work your way down, pulling the brush away from your face to create lift.

3. Use a Texturizing Spray

To add texture to your hair, use a texturizing spray or pomade. This will help create dimension and make the hair look more interesting.

4. Experiment with Parting

Changing the way you part your hair can create different looks and help flatter your face shape. Try a deep side part or a middle part to see what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Round Face Male

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling long hair for round face male:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before styling.

2. Apply Volumizing Mousse

Apply a small amount of volumizing mousse to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers.

3. Blow-Dry with a Round Brush

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair, pulling it away from your face to create lift and volume.

4. Use a Texturizing Spray

Spray a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade onto your hair and work it through with your fingers to create texture.

5. Style as Desired

Experiment with different styles, such as a low ponytail or textured waves, to find what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Round Face Male

Here are some tips for maintaining long hair for round face male:

1. Get Regular Trims

To keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

When using heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Avoid Over-Shampooing

Shampooing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils. Try to shampoo every other day or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

FAQs About Long Hair for Round Face Male

Q: Can I wear my hair in a bun or top knot?

A: Yes, a bun or top knot can be a great way to elongate the face and create the illusion of height. Just make sure to add some volume at the crown to balance out the roundness of the face.

Q: What if I have thinning hair?

A: If you have thinning hair, try a layered cut to add volume and movement. Avoid styles that are too long or heavy, as they can make your hair look flat and lifeless.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight?

A: Yes, straight hair can be a great look for round face male. Just make sure to add some volume and texture to avoid a flat, lifeless look.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair for Round Face Male

Here are some hair care tips for long hair for round face male:

1. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-toothed comb to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles, such as braids or ponytails, which can pull on your hair and cause damage.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

When spending time in the sun, wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from damage.

4. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage, as well as keep your hair looking smoother and shinier.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can find the best long hair for round face male and style it to flatter your face shape. Remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments, and protect it from damage with heat protectant sprays and SPF products.
