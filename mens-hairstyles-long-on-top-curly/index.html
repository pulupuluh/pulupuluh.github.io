Mens Hairstyles Long On Top Curly A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Apr 12, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle that suits your curly hair? Mens hairstyles long on top curly are perfect for you. In this guide, we will discuss everything you need to know about mens hairstyles long on top curly. From how to style it to hair care tips, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Curly
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Curly
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips for Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Curly

Description

Mens hairstyles long on top curly are versatile and can work for any occasion. The hairstyle features long hair on the top of the head, while the sides and back are kept short. This style is perfect for men with curly hair as it accentuates their natural curls and adds volume and texture. Mens hairstyles long on top curly are perfect for any face shape and can be customized to suit your personal style.

How to Style Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Curly

The key to styling mens hairstyles long on top curly is to embrace your natural curls. Here’s how to style it:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to enhance your curls.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch your curls and add volume.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re new to styling mens hairstyles long on top curly, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a curl cream or gel.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to comb through your hair and evenly distribute the product.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry each section. Avoid using a regular hairdryer as it can cause frizz.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch your curls and add volume.
  6. If you want a more defined look, use a curling wand or curling iron to define your curls.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Curly

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your mens hairstyles long on top curly:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry as it can cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair to enhance your natural curls and add definition and volume.
  • If you want a more defined look, use a curling wand or curling iron to define your curls.

FAQ

What face shape suits mens hairstyles long on top curly?

Mens hairstyles long on top curly can work for any face shape. However, if you have a round face, avoid adding too much volume on top as it can make your face look rounder.

What is the best product to use for mens hairstyles long on top curly?

The best product to use for mens hairstyles long on top curly is a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel. These products will enhance your natural curls and add definition and volume.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Curly

To keep your mens hairstyles long on top curly looking healthy and vibrant, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry as it can cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking neat and healthy.

In conclusion, mens hairstyles long on top curly are a stylish and trendy hairstyle that suits any occasion. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this guide, you can achieve a stylish and healthy look that accentuates your natural curls. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and techniques to keep it healthy and vibrant.
