How To Make Your Hair Straight Permanently At Home For Guys

Written by Eveline Feb 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with curly or wavy hair every day? Do you want a permanent solution to make your hair straight? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss how to make your hair straight permanently at home for guys.

Description

Having curly or wavy hair can be challenging to manage, especially if you want to have a straight hairdo. While there are several temporary options, such as flat ironing or blow-drying, they only last for a few hours. Therefore, to achieve permanent straight hair, you need to undergo a hair straightening procedure. However, going to a salon can be expensive, and not everyone can afford it. Fortunately, you can straighten your hair at home using natural ingredients and tools.

How to

The following are the steps to make your hair straight permanently at home for guys.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before starting the straightening process, you need to wash your hair thoroughly. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or oil from your hair. Rinse your hair with lukewarm water and pat it dry with a towel.

Step 2: Apply Hair Straightening Solution

Next, apply a hair straightening solution to your hair. You can use coconut milk, aloe vera gel, or milk and honey mixture as a natural hair straightener. Apply the solution to your hair, focusing on the roots and tips. Leave the solution on your hair for 30-40 minutes.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

After applying the hair straightening solution, use a blow dryer to dry your hair completely. Use a comb to straighten your hair while blow-drying. Make sure to direct the airflow downwards to prevent frizz.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron

Once your hair is dry, divide it into small sections and use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Start from the roots and glide the flat iron to the tips. Repeat the process until all sections are straightened.

Step 5: Apply a Hair Mask

After straightening your hair, apply a hair mask to keep it soft and shiny. You can use a homemade hair mask made of avocado, banana, and olive oil. Mix the ingredients and apply the mask to your hair for 30 minutes. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron to prevent hair damage.
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after the straightening process.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly, as it can cause tension and breakage.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a hair straightener cream instead of a natural solution?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener cream. However, make sure to read the instructions carefully and do a patch test before applying it to your hair.

Q: How long does the hair straightening process last?

A: The hair straightening process can last up to six months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of it.

Q: Can I go swimming after the hair straightening process?

A: It is best to avoid swimming for at least 48 hours after the hair straightening process to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain your straight hair, you need to take care of it regularly. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot water. Apply a hair serum to keep your hair smooth and shiny. Avoid using hair styling products that contain alcohol or chemicals that can damage your hair. Also, avoid excessive heat styling, as it can cause hair breakage and split ends.

Now that you know how to make your hair straight permanently at home for guys, you can achieve the hair of your dreams without spending a fortune. Follow these steps, take care of your hair, and enjoy your new straight hairdo!
