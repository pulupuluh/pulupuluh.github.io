How To Style Permed Hair In Ghana A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Permed hair is a popular hairstyle in Ghana, especially among women. It is a hairstyle that involves chemical treatment to create curls or waves in the hair. Many women prefer to perm their hair because it is easy to maintain and can last for several months. However, styling permed hair can be a challenge, especially if you are new to the hairstyle. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to style permed hair in Ghana.

What is Permed Hair?

Permed hair is a hairstyle that involves chemical treatment to create curls or waves in the hair. The chemical used in perming is called a perm solution, which breaks down the bonds in the hair to create a new shape. The hair is then rinsed with a neutralizer, which helps to reset the bonds in the hair to hold the new shape. Permed hair can last for several months, depending on how well it is cared for.

How to Style Permed Hair

Styling permed hair can be a challenge, especially if you are new to the hairstyle. Here are some tips on how to style permed hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently. Avoid using a brush, as it can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair gently.
  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create defined curls or waves in your hair.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your curls or waves in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Permed Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style permed hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water, as it can damage your hair.
  2. Blot your hair dry with a microfiber towel. Avoid rubbing your hair, as it can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  5. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  6. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair gently.
  7. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create defined curls or waves in your hair.
  8. Use a light hold hairspray to keep your curls or waves in place.

Tips for Styling Permed Hair

Here are some tips for styling permed hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as it can cause breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling permed hair:

  1. How often should I wash my permed hair?

    2. You should wash your permed hair once or twice a week with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

  2. Can I straighten my permed hair?

    3. Yes, you can straighten your permed hair, but you should use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

  3. How long does a perm last?

    4. A perm can last for several months, depending on how well it is cared for.

  4. Can I swim with permed hair?

    5. Yes, you can swim with permed hair, but you should wear a swim cap or use a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from chlorine.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

Here are some tips for caring for permed hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as it can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as it can cause breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair.

By following these tips, you can style and care for your permed hair in Ghana. Remember to always use products that are gentle on your hair and avoid using heat styling tools too often to keep your hair healthy.
