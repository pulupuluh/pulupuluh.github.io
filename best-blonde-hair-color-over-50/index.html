Best Blonde Hair Color Over 50 How To Achieve A Youthful Look

Written by Luffy Jun 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a change in your hair color? Going blonde can help you achieve a youthful and vibrant look. However, not all blonde shades are suitable for mature women. In this article, we will guide you on the best blonde hair color over 50 and give you tips on how to maintain it.

How to Choose the Best Blonde Hair Color Over 50

Choosing the right blonde shade can make a huge difference in how you look. Here are some factors to consider when choosing the best blonde hair color over 50:

Skin Tone

Your skin tone is a crucial factor in determining the best blonde shade for you. If you have a warm skin tone, go for golden or honey blonde shades. For cool skin tones, ash blonde shades are more suitable.

Eye Color

Your eye color also plays a role in choosing the best blonde hair color over 50. If you have blue or green eyes, go for cool blonde shades. For brown or hazel eyes, warm blonde shades work well.

Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color also affects how the blonde shade will look on you. If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach your hair before applying the blonde color. If you have lighter hair, you may be able to achieve the blonde shade without bleaching.

Steps to Achieve the Best Blonde Hair Color Over 50

Here are the steps to achieve the best blonde hair color over 50:

Step 1: Choose the Right Shade

Refer to the factors mentioned above to choose the best blonde shade for you.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before applying the blonde color, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have previously colored hair, remove the color using a color remover.

Step 3: Apply the Blonde Color

Follow the instructions on the hair color kit to apply the blonde color. Start from the roots and work your way down to the ends. Leave the color on for the recommended time.

Step 4: Rinse and Condition

Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water until the water runs clear. Apply the conditioner that comes with the hair color kit and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off.

Tips for Maintaining the Best Blonde Hair Color Over 50

To maintain the best blonde hair color over 50, follow these tips:

Use a Color-Safe Shampoo

Use a shampoo that is specifically formulated for color-treated hair to prevent the blonde color from fading.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners as they can damage your hair and cause the blonde color to fade.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

FAQs About the Best Blonde Hair Color Over 50

What blonde shade is best for cool skin tones?

Ash blonde shades are best for cool skin tones.

Do I need to bleach my hair to achieve a blonde shade?

If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach your hair before applying the blonde color. If you have lighter hair, you may be able to achieve the blonde shade without bleaching.

How often should I touch up my blonde hair color?

You should touch up your blonde hair color every 4-6 weeks to maintain the vibrancy of the color.

Hair Care for the Best Blonde Hair Color Over 50

To keep your hair healthy and vibrant, follow these hair care tips:

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, going blonde can be a great way to achieve a youthful and vibrant look. By following the tips and steps mentioned in this article, you can choose the best blonde hair color over 50 and maintain it for a long time.
