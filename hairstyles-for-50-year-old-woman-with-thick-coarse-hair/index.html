Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Woman With Thick Coarse Hair

Written by Aprilia Feb 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As women get older, the texture and thickness of their hair can change. For 50 year old women with thick coarse hair, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right knowledge and guidance, you can find a hairstyle that suits your hair type and complements your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Thick coarse hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it's great to have a lot of hair, it can be difficult to manage and style, especially as you age. For 50 year old women with thick coarse hair, the key is to find a hairstyle that adds volume and movement without overwhelming your face or making you look older than you are.

How to

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your thick coarse hair is to identify your face shape. There are several different face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Once you know your face shape, you can narrow down the hairstyles that will work best for you.

Next, consider your hair texture and thickness. Thick coarse hair can be difficult to manage, but there are many hairstyles that can help you tame your hair and make it look great. Some popular hairstyles for 50 year old women with thick coarse hair include layered cuts, long bobs, and pixie cuts.

Step by Step

If you're looking for a layered cut, start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for thick coarse hair. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and create loose waves or curls. Finish with a flexible hold hairspray.

If you prefer a long bob, start by parting your hair in the middle and blow drying it straight using a paddle brush. Use a flat iron to straighten any kinks or waves. Finish with a shine serum or spray to add shine and reduce frizz.

If you're feeling daring and want to try a pixie cut, start by consulting with your stylist to find the right length and style for your face shape. Be prepared for a dramatic change, but remember that a pixie cut can be a great way to show off your features and add a youthful edge to your look.

Tips

  • Use products designed for thick coarse hair to keep your hair healthy and manageable.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles or experiment with different hair accessories.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQ

Q. What is the best hairstyle for 50 year old women with thick coarse hair?

A. The best hairstyle for you will depend on your face shape and personal style. Some popular options include layered cuts, long bobs, and pixie cuts.

Q. How can I make my thick coarse hair look more manageable?

A. Use products designed for thick coarse hair, such as volumizing mousse or spray, to add volume and control. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and manageable.

Hair Care

To keep your thick coarse hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use products designed for your hair type, such as shampoos and conditioners formulated for thick coarse hair. Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.

When styling your hair, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage. Be sure to visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair looking healthy and well-maintained.

With the right knowledge and care, you can find a hairstyle that suits your thick coarse hair and makes you feel confident and beautiful at 50 and beyond.
