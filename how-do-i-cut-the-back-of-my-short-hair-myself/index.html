How Do I Cut The Back Of My Short Hair Myself

Written by Luffy May 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short hair can be a great style for those who want to look chic and stylish without spending hours on styling. But one of the biggest challenges of having short hair is keeping the back of it trimmed and neat. It can be difficult to reach the back of your head, and going to a salon every few weeks can get expensive. In this article, we will share some tips on how to cut the back of your short hair yourself, so you can save time and money.

Description

Short hair is a popular style for both men and women. While it can be easy to maintain, it can also be difficult to keep the back of it trimmed and neat. Going to a salon every few weeks can be expensive, and it can also be time-consuming. By learning how to cut the back of your short hair yourself, you can save money and time while still looking stylish.

How to Cut the Back of Your Short Hair Yourself

Cutting the back of your short hair can seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be easy. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Get the Right Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you will need to make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, and a hand-held mirror.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Using the comb, create a horizontal parting about an inch above the nape of your neck. Comb the hair below the parting down and clip it out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Using the mirror, look at the back of your head and cut the hair below the parting. Start by cutting a small amount of hair at a time, and be sure to cut straight across. Continue cutting until you have trimmed all the hair below the parting.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Remove the clip and blend the hair you just cut with the hair above it. This can be done by holding the hair between your fingers and cutting into it at an angle. This will create a gradual transition between the shorter hair at the back of your head and the longer hair above it.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Use the mirror to check the back of your head and make sure your hair is even. If you need to make any adjustments, repeat the previous steps until you are satisfied with the length and shape of your hair.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Your Short Hair Yourself

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting the back of your short hair yourself:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always take more off, but you can't put it back on.
  • Be patient. Cutting your hair can take time, especially if you're doing it for the first time.
  • Use the right tools. Sharp scissors are essential for a clean cut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair can shrink up when it dries, which can lead to a shorter cut than you intended.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the back of your short hair yourself:

Can I cut the back of my short hair myself?

Yes, you can cut the back of your short hair yourself. With the right tools and techniques, it can be easy to do.

What tools do I need to cut the back of my short hair myself?

You will need a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, and a hand-held mirror.

How often should I cut the back of my short hair?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. As a general rule, you should aim to trim the back of your short hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Cutting the back of your short hair yourself can save you time and money, but it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for taking care of short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it's wet.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

By following these tips, you can keep your short hair looking great and feeling healthy.
