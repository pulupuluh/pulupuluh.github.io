How To Get Wavy Curly Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Description

Many men dream of having wavy curly hair, but achieving this style can be a challenge. Whether you have naturally straight hair or have been styling your hair in the same way for years, getting wavy curly hair requires some effort and the right products. In this guide, we'll show you how to get wavy curly hair male in a relaxed, easy-to-follow way.

How to

Follow these steps to get wavy curly hair male:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Use lukewarm water to wash your hair, as hot water can also dry out your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair. This will help to hydrate your hair and make it easier to style. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and distribute the conditioner evenly.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to your damp hair. Use a dime-sized amount for short hair and a quarter-sized amount for longer hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the curls to form. Use a diffuser on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Hold the diffuser close to your hair, but don't touch your hair with the diffuser.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Oil

Once your hair is dry, apply a hair oil to your hair to add shine and reduce frizz. Use a dime-sized amount for short hair and a quarter-sized amount for longer hair. Apply the hair oil evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Tips

- Use a microfiber towel or an old cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, which can cause frizz. - Don't touch your hair while it's drying, as this can disrupt the curls. - Sleep on a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and protect your curls.

FAQ

Q: Can I get wavy curly hair if I have naturally straight hair?
A: Yes, you can achieve wavy curly hair even if you have naturally straight hair. It may take some effort and the right products, but it's possible. Q: How often should I wash my hair?
A: It's best to wash your hair every other day or every three days to prevent it from drying out. Q: Can I use a hair straightener on my wavy curly hair?
A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener to straighten your hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Hair Care

To keep your wavy curly hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips: - Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. - Use a leave-in conditioner to add moisture to your hair. - Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as hair straighteners and curling irons. - Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
