How To Make Relaxed Hair Natural Without Cutting

Written by Luffy May 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have relaxed hair but want to transition to your natural hair without cutting off your relaxed hair, there are a few things you need to know. In this article, we'll go over the steps you can take to make your relaxed hair natural without having to chop it all off.

Description

Many women with relaxed hair experience a desire to go natural. It's a big decision, and it can be daunting to think about cutting off all your hair to start from scratch. The good news is that you don't have to. There are ways to make your relaxed hair natural without having to cut it off.

How to Make Relaxed Hair Natural Without Cutting

Step 1: Stop Relaxing Your Hair

The first step to making your relaxed hair natural is to stop relaxing it. This means no more chemical treatments to straighten your hair. If you continue to relax your hair, you will never be able to transition to your natural hair.

Step 2: Moisturize Your Hair

Relaxed hair is often dry, so it's essential to keep it moisturized. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner and apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair every day. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 3: Protect Your Hair

Protecting your hair is crucial when transitioning from relaxed to natural hair. Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair. Wear protective styles, such as braids or twists, to keep your hair healthy and protected.

Step 4: Trim Your Ends

While you don't have to cut off all your relaxed hair, it's essential to trim your ends regularly. This will help to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth. Aim to trim your ends every 6-8 weeks.

Tips

  • Be patient. It takes time to transition from relaxed to natural hair.
  • Use natural hair products that are free of sulfates and parabens.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Can I transition from relaxed to natural hair without cutting my hair?

Yes, you can. However, you will need to be patient and take good care of your hair. It may take some time, but with the right products and techniques, you can make your relaxed hair natural without cutting it.

How often should I trim my ends?

You should aim to trim your ends every 6-8 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Can I still straighten my hair while transitioning to natural hair?

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair. If you want to straighten your hair, try using a heatless method, such as a blowout or a roller set.

Hair Care

Transitioning from relaxed to natural hair takes time and patience. Be sure to take good care of your hair by moisturizing it regularly, protecting it from damage, and trimming your ends every 6-8 weeks. With the right products and techniques, you can make your relaxed hair natural without having to cut it off.
