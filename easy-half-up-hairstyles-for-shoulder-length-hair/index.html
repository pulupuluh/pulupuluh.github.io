Easy Half Up Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Frank May 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old ponytail or messy bun? If you have shoulder length hair, there are plenty of easy half up hairstyles that you can try to switch up your look. In this article, we will share some simple yet stylish half up hairstyles that you can do at home.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in many ways. If you have an event or a special occasion coming up, you may want to try a half up hairstyle. Half up hairstyles are perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of their face but still want to show off their locks.

How to

Here are some easy half up hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

1. Half Up Bun

To create a half up bun, gather the top half of your hair and tie it into a ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out or even for work.

2. Half Up French Twist

To create a half up French twist, gather the top half of your hair and twist it towards the back of your head. Secure the twist with bobby pins and tuck in any loose ends. This hairstyle is perfect for a formal event or a night out.

3. Half Up Braids

To create a half up braided hairstyle, divide the top half of your hair into two sections. Braid each section and secure them with small elastic bands. You can leave the rest of your hair down or style it in loose waves. This hairstyle is perfect for a boho-chic look.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for each half up hairstyle:

1. Half Up Bun

  1. Gather the top half of your hair and tie it into a ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

2. Half Up French Twist

  1. Gather the top half of your hair and twist it towards the back of your head.
  2. Secure the twist with bobby pins.
  3. Tuck in any loose ends.

3. Half Up Braids

  1. Divide the top half of your hair into two sections.
  2. Braid each section and secure them with small elastic bands.
  3. Leave the rest of your hair down or style it in loose waves.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Apply hairspray to the finished hairstyle to hold it in place.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about half up hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

1. Can I do half up hairstyles on curly hair?

Yes, you can do half up hairstyles on curly hair. You may want to straighten the top half of your hair before styling it, or you can embrace your curls and create a curly half up hairstyle.

2. Do I need to use hot tools to create these hairstyles?

No, you don't need to use hot tools to create these hairstyles. You can style your hair using just bobby pins and elastics, or you can use hot tools like curling irons or straighteners to add waves or curls to your hair.

3. How long do these hairstyles take to create?

These hairstyles are relatively quick and easy to create. Depending on the hairstyle, it may take anywhere from 5 to 15 minutes to complete.

Hair Care

When styling your hair, it's important to take care of it to prevent damage. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you have some easy half up hairstyles for shoulder length hair, you can switch up your look without spending a lot of time or money. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion.
