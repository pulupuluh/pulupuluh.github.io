What Is A Low Fade Taper

Written by Petter Sep 06, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you wondering what a low fade taper is? Well, you’ve come to the right place. A low fade taper is a popular men’s hairstyle that is characterized by a gradual decrease in hair length from the top of the head down to the sides and back. In this blog post, we’ll break down everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle.

Description of a Low Fade Taper

A low fade taper is a type of haircut that is achieved by gradually decreasing the length of the hair on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair on top longer. This hairstyle is often confused with a regular taper, but the main difference is that a low fade taper involves a fade that begins lower on the head, typically around the temples.

The low fade taper is a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit different hair types and lengths. It can be worn with a variety of hair lengths and textures, and can be styled in different ways to suit your personal preferences.

How to Get a Low Fade Taper

If you’re interested in getting a low fade taper, the first step is to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who is experienced in cutting this type of hairstyle. To achieve a low fade taper, the hair on the sides and back of the head is gradually trimmed shorter, with the shortest length at the bottom of the fade.

Here are the steps to get a low fade taper:

Step 1: Decide on the Length of the Hair on Top

The first step is to decide on the length of the hair on top. This will depend on your personal preference and hair type. The hair on top should be long enough to style but not too long that it looks unkempt.

Step 2: Choose the Length of the Fade

Next, you’ll need to decide on the length of the fade. A low fade taper typically starts around the temples and gradually fades down to the shortest length at the nape of the neck. The length of the fade can be customized to suit your preferences.

Step 3: Trim the Hair on the Sides and Back

Using clippers or scissors, the hair on the sides and back of the head is gradually trimmed shorter, with the shortest length at the bottom of the fade. The hair is blended between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 4: Style the Hair on Top

Finally, the hair on top is styled according to your preferences. You can use a variety of products such as pomade, wax, or gel to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining a Low Fade Taper

Here are some tips for maintaining a low fade taper:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape of the haircut.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles to keep your look fresh and interesting.

FAQ About Low Fade Taper

What is the difference between a low fade taper and a regular taper?

The main difference between a low fade taper and a regular taper is that the fade in a low fade taper begins lower on the head, typically around the temples. A regular taper involves a gradual decrease in length from the top of the head down to the sides and back.

Can a low fade taper be worn with any hair type?

Yes, a low fade taper can be adapted to suit different hair types and textures. However, the length of the hair on top may need to be adjusted depending on the thickness and texture of your hair.

Hair Care for Low Fade Taper

To keep your low fade taper looking its best, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

In conclusion, a low fade taper is a trendy and versatile men’s hairstyle that can be adapted to suit different hair types and personal preferences. With the right care and maintenance, this hairstyle can keep you looking sharp and stylish.
