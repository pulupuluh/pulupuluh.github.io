Easy Wedding Guest Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Daniel Jun 08, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you attending a wedding soon and wondering what to do with your shoulder length hair? Don't worry, we've got you covered with some easy and elegant hairstyles that will make you stand out as a wedding guest. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs on how to achieve these hairstyles.

Table of Contents

  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Low Bun
  • Side-Swept Curls
  • Braided Crown
  • Fishtail Braid

Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to show off their shoulder length hair while keeping it out of their face. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start by curling your hair using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it into a mini ponytail using an elastic band.
  3. Tease the ponytail lightly to add volume and texture.
  4. Take a small section of hair from each side of your head and twist them towards the back. Secure them with bobby pins just below the mini ponytail.
  5. Finish off the look by adding some hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips:

  • Add some accessories like a flower crown or hair clip to make the hairstyle more wedding-appropriate.
  • If you have bangs, leave them out and style them separately.

FAQ:

  • Can I do this hairstyle without curling my hair?
    Yes, you can. Straight hair will give a sleek and sophisticated look.
  • How long does this hairstyle take to do?
    It should take around 10-15 minutes.

Low Bun

A low bun is a classic hairstyle that works well for weddings. Here's how to create it:

  1. Gather all your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it into a low ponytail using an elastic band.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it curls up into a bun shape. Secure it with bobby pins.
  3. If you have any loose strands of hair, tuck them into the bun and secure them with bobby pins.
  4. Finish off the look with some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

  • Add some hair accessories like a hairpin or a headband to make the hairstyle more elegant.
  • If you have layers, leave some strands out to frame your face.

FAQ:

  • Can I do this hairstyle with dirty hair?
    Yes, you can. In fact, dirty hair will hold the style better.
  • How long does this hairstyle take to do?
    It should take around 5-10 minutes.

Side-Swept Curls

If you want a romantic and feminine look, side-swept curls are the way to go. Here's how to achieve them:

  1. Curl your hair using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  2. Part your hair deeply to one side.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the side with less hair and twist it towards the back. Secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Brush out the curls to create a soft and natural look.
  5. Finish off the look with some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

  • Add some hair accessories like a hair clip or a flower to make the hairstyle more festive.
  • If you have bangs, sweep them to the side and style them separately.

FAQ:

  • Can I do this hairstyle without curling my hair?
    No, you need to curl your hair to achieve this look.
  • How long does this hairstyle take to do?
    It should take around 15-20 minutes.

Braided Crown

A braided crown is a beautiful and sophisticated hairstyle that will make you look like a princess. Here's how to create it:

  1. Part your hair deeply to one side.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the side with more hair and start braiding it towards the back. Keep adding sections of hair as you go along until you reach the other side of your head.
  3. Secure the braid with bobby pins just above your ear.
  4. Repeat the process on the other side of your head.
  5. Finish off the look with some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

  • Add some flowers or beads to the braids to make the hairstyle more festive.
  • If you have layers, tuck them into the braids and secure them with bobby pins.

FAQ:

  • Can I do this hairstyle without a part?
    No, the part is essential for creating the braids.
  • How long does this hairstyle take to do?
    It should take around 20-25 minutes.

Fishtail Braid

A fishtail braid is a trendy and chic hairstyle that will make you stand out as a wedding guest. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Gather all your hair to one side and divide it into two sections.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the outer edge of the left section and cross it over to the right section.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the outer edge of the right section and cross it over to the left section.
  4. Keep repeating steps 2 and 3 until you reach the end of your hair.
  5. Secure the braid with an elastic band.
  6. Loosen the braid slightly to create a messy and undone look.
  7. Finish off the look with some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

  • Add some hair accessories like a hairpin or a ribbon to make the hairstyle more festive.
  • If you have layers, leave some strands out to frame your face.

FAQ:

  • Can I do this hairstyle without a part?
    Yes, you can. A middle part or no part will work just fine.
  • How long does this hairstyle take to do?
    It should take around 10-15 minutes.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair before and after styling it for a wedding. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair a day before the wedding to give it time to adjust to its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before curling or straightening your hair to avoid damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair smooth and shiny.
  • Don't overuse hairspray or other hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • After the wedding, use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup and restore your hair's natural balance.

With these easy wedding guest hairstyles for shoulder length hair, you'll be sure to turn heads at the next wedding you attend. Just remember to take care of your hair and have fun with your styling!
