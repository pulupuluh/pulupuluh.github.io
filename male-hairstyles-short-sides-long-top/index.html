Male Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top A Timeless Trend In 2023

Written by Luffy Apr 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy hairstyle that suits any occasion, then male hairstyles short sides long top are the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been popular for years and is still trending in 2023. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this timeless trend including how to style it, tips, and hair care.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Male Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top

Description

Male hairstyles short sides long top are characterized by having short hair on the sides and a longer length on top. This creates a contrast between the two lengths and allows for a variety of styling options. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. It suits all face shapes and can be adapted to suit any personal style.

How to Style Male Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top

The styling process for male hairstyles short sides long top is relatively simple. First, you need to decide on the length of the hair on the top and the sides. Once you have the desired length, you can style your hair in various ways. One popular way to style this haircut is to slick back the hair on top using a hair gel or pomade. Another popular way is to create a messy, textured look by using a salt spray or a hair wax.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style male hairstyles short sides long top:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture.
  3. Apply a styling product that suits your hair type and the desired look.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a hair dryer to create volume and texture.
  5. Style your hair using your fingers or a comb to create the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Maintaining male hairstyles short sides long top is relatively easy. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair:

  • Trim the sides regularly to maintain the short length.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

FAQs

1. Can male hairstyles short sides long top suit any face shape?

Yes, this hairstyle can suit any face shape. However, you should consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style that suits your face shape.

2. How often should I trim the sides?

You should trim the sides every two to three weeks to maintain the short length.

3. What styling products should I use?

The styling products you use depend on your hair type and the desired look. You can use hair gel, pomade, hair wax, or a salt spray.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and hydrated:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using chemical treatments such as perms or relaxers as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh hair products that contain sulfates, parabens, or alcohol as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

In conclusion, male hairstyles short sides long top are a timeless trend that suits any personal style and occasion. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can maintain this hairstyle and keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Follow the tips and step-by-step guide in this article to achieve the perfect look and make a lasting impression.
