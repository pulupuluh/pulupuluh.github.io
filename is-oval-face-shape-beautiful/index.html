Is Oval Face Shape Beautiful Tips And Tricks For Flattering Your Features

Written by William Mar 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered one of the most versatile and beautiful of all the face shapes. In fact, many celebrities and models have oval faces, which only adds to their stunning beauty. But what makes oval face shapes so beautiful, and how can you make the most of your features? This article will explore these questions and more.

What is an Oval Face Shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by a face that is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded jawline and forehead. The cheekbones are typically the widest part of the face, and the forehead and jawline are roughly the same width. This face shape is often described as "egg-shaped" or "soft-angled."

Why is an Oval Face Shape Considered Beautiful?

There are many reasons why an oval face shape is considered beautiful. For one, it is a very balanced and symmetrical face shape, with no one feature overpowering the others. This symmetry is pleasing to the eye and is often associated with beauty. Additionally, oval face shapes are very versatile when it comes to hairstyles and makeup. Many different styles and looks can flatter an oval face shape, making it a favorite among hairstylists and makeup artists.

How to Flatter Your Oval Face Shape

Hairstyles

When it comes to hairstyles, there are many options that can flatter an oval face shape. One of the keys is to add volume and texture to the hair, which can help balance out the face shape. Layers are also a great option, as they can add movement and dimension to the hair. Here are some specific hairstyle tips for oval face shapes:

  • Long layers that frame the face
  • Side-swept bangs
  • A long bob (aka "lob")
  • A messy updo
  • Soft waves or curls

Makeup

As with hairstyles, there are many makeup looks that can flatter an oval face shape. One of the keys is to enhance the natural symmetry of the face, while also playing up your favorite features. Here are some specific makeup tips for oval face shapes:

  • Use a highlighter on the cheekbones to enhance their natural prominence
  • Apply blush to the apples of the cheeks to add a healthy glow
  • Use eyeshadow to play up your favorite feature (e.g. blue eyes, long lashes, etc.)
  • Apply lipstick or gloss to the lips to add fullness and definition

Fashion

When it comes to fashion, there are a few tips that can help flatter an oval face shape. One is to wear accessories that draw attention to your favorite features (e.g. a statement necklace that enhances your collarbone). Another is to experiment with different necklines and silhouettes to see what works best for your body type. Here are some specific fashion tips for oval face shapes:

  • Wear V-neck or scoop-neck tops to elongate the neck and draw attention to the collarbone
  • Experiment with different jacket styles to find one that flatters your shoulders and arms
  • Wear statement earrings to draw attention to the cheekbones
  • Try a fitted blouse or dress to show off your curves

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't like my oval face shape?

It's important to remember that everyone has unique features that make them beautiful. If you don't love your oval face shape, try experimenting with different hairstyles, makeup looks, and fashion choices to find what makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Can oval face shapes wear short hair?

Absolutely! Oval face shapes are very versatile when it comes to hairstyles, and short hair can look great on an oval face. Just be sure to choose a style that flatters your features and adds volume and texture to the hair.

What are some hairstyles to avoid with an oval face shape?

While oval face shapes can wear many different hairstyles, there are a few to avoid. One is a straight bob that falls at chin-length, as this can make the face appear wider. Another is a center part, as this can elongate the face in a way that is unflattering.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shapes

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips specifically for oval face shapes:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and fullness to the hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to keep the hair looking healthy and prevent split ends
  • Experiment with different hair products (e.g. mousse, hairspray, etc.) to find what works best for your hair type

Overall, an oval face shape is considered one of the most beautiful face shapes out there. By following these tips and tricks, you can make the most of your features and feel confident and beautiful every day.
