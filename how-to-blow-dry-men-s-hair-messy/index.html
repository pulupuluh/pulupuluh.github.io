How To Blow Dry Men s Hair Messy A Relaxed Guide

Written by Christine Aug 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy hairstyle that still looks put together, the messy blowout might be just the ticket. This style is perfect for men who want to look effortlessly cool without spending too much time in front of the mirror. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect messy blowout.

Table of Contents

  • How to Blow Dry Men's Hair Messy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Great Messy Blowout
  • FAQs

How to Blow Dry Men's Hair Messy

The first step to achieving the perfect messy blowout is to start with clean, towel-dried hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you begin, as wet hair can be more difficult to style.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help to prevent damage from the blow dryer's heat. Then, use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and blow dry your hair in sections, starting from the back and working your way forward.

As you blow dry each section, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured look. Continue this process until your hair is completely dry and styled to your liking.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a messy blowout:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and blow dry your hair in sections, starting from the back and working your way forward.
  4. As you blow dry each section, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured look.
  5. Continue this process until your hair is completely dry and styled to your liking.

Tips for a Great Messy Blowout

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy blowout:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the blow dryer's heat.
  • Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  • Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and add volume.
  • Blow dry your hair in sections, starting from the back and working your way forward.
  • Use your fingers to tousle your hair as you blow dry to create a messy, textured look.
  • Finish with a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about blowing out men's hair:

Q: How often should I blow dry my hair?

A: It's best to limit heat styling as much as possible to prevent damage. Try to blow dry your hair no more than a few times a week.

Q: Can I blow dry my hair without a heat protectant?

A: We don't recommend it. Heat protectant sprays help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

Q: What if my hair is too short for a messy blowout?

A: You can still achieve a tousled, textured look with shorter hair. Use a small round brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume, then use your fingers to tousle your hair as you blow dry.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Here are some general hair care tips for men:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too frequently.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when blow drying or using hot tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve a perfectly messy blowout in no time. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
