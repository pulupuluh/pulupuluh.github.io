How To Cut Curly Hair For More Volume Tips Steps And Faqs

Written by Eveline Feb 17, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have curly hair, you might be struggling to achieve more volume. Many people with curly hair find that their hair falls flat and lacks body, which can be frustrating. However, there are ways to cut curly hair to add more volume and create a bouncier, fuller look. In this article, we'll explain how to cut curly hair for more volume, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and frequently asked questions.

How to Cut Curly Hair for More Volume: Description

Cutting curly hair can be challenging, but it's essential to achieving more volume. The right cut can add body and movement to your hair, making it look bouncier and fuller. However, cutting curly hair requires a different approach than cutting straight hair. You need to consider the curl pattern, the thickness of the hair, and the desired length when cutting curly hair. In this article, we'll explain how to cut curly hair for more volume, step-by-step.

How to Cut Curly Hair for More Volume: How to

Before you start cutting your curly hair, it's essential to have the right tools. You'll need a sharp pair of hair cutting scissors, a wide-tooth comb, and clips to section your hair. It's also a good idea to have a mirror and good lighting so that you can see what you're doing. Here are the steps to cut curly hair for more volume:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair. This will help to hydrate your hair and make it easier to detangle. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently squeeze out excess water with a towel.

Step 2: Comb your hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, being gentle to avoid breaking or pulling your hair.

Step 3: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips. Start by clipping the top section of your hair, leaving the bottom section loose.

Step 4: Cut the bottom section

Start by cutting the bottom section of your hair. Take a small section of hair, hold it between your fingers, and cut the ends at an angle. This will create layers and add volume to your hair. Continue cutting small sections of hair, making sure to cut at an angle to create layers.

Step 5: Cut the top section

Once you've finished cutting the bottom section, unclip the top section of your hair. Take a small section of hair, hold it between your fingers, and cut the ends at an angle. Make sure to cut the top section of your hair slightly shorter than the bottom section to create more volume.

Step 6: Check your work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make any final adjustments to ensure that your hair looks even and has the desired volume.

How to Cut Curly Hair for More Volume: Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut curly hair for more volume:

  • Use a sharp pair of hair cutting scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.
  • Cut your hair in small sections to ensure that you create even layers.
  • Cut your hair at an angle to create layers and add volume.
  • Don't cut your hair too short, as this can make it look flat and lifeless.

How to Cut Curly Hair for More Volume: Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting curly hair for more volume:

How often should I cut my curly hair for more volume?

It's recommended to cut curly hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and volume.

Should I cut my curly hair when it's wet or dry?

It's best to cut curly hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.

How can I add more volume to my curly hair without cutting it?

You can add more volume to your curly hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using a diffuser when drying your hair.

How to Cut Curly Hair for More Volume: Hair Care

After you've cut your curly hair for more volume, it's essential to take good care of it to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and reduce volume.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as brushes can break your hair and reduce volume.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and add shine.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.

By following these tips and cutting your curly hair for more volume, you can achieve bouncy, fuller curls that look healthy and vibrant.
