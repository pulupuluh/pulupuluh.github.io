How To Do A Man Bun With Short Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your short curly hair getting in the way? Do you want to try a new hairstyle that’s both stylish and practical? Look no further than the man bun! This versatile hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether you’re heading to the office or hitting the gym. In this article, we’ll show you how to do a man bun with short curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a Man Bun with Short Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for the Perfect Man Bun
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Do a Man Bun with Short Curly Hair

Before we get started, it’s important to note that you’ll need at least 4-5 inches of hair on top of your head to create a man bun. If your hair is too short, you may need to wait a few months before attempting this hairstyle.

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help tame any frizz and make your hair easier to manage.

Step 2: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Be gentle, as curly hair can be prone to breakage.

Step 3: Gather all of your hair at the crown of your head, as if you were creating a ponytail.

Step 4: Twist your hair into a tight coil, making sure to keep the ends tucked in.

Step 5: Secure the coil with an elastic hair tie.

Step 6: If you have any loose strands or flyaways, use bobby pins to secure them in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Still not sure how to create the perfect man bun? Follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Gather all of your hair at the crown of your head.
  4. Twist your hair into a tight coil.
  5. Secure the coil with an elastic hair tie.
  6. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.

Tips for the Perfect Man Bun

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating your man bun:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Be gentle when detangling your hair, as curly hair can be prone to breakage.
  • Twist your hair tightly to create a secure bun that won’t come undone.
  • Use bobby pins to keep any loose strands or flyaways in place.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style.

FAQ

Still have questions about how to do a man bun with short curly hair? Check out these frequently asked questions:

Can I create a man bun with very short hair?

No, you’ll need at least 4-5 inches of hair on top of your head to create a man bun. If your hair is too short, you may need to wait a few months before attempting this hairstyle.

Do I need to use hair products to create a man bun?

No, you can create a man bun without any hair products. However, using a styling cream or gel can help tame any frizz and give your hair a more polished look.

How do I keep my man bun in place?

Use an elastic hair tie to secure your hair in place. If you have any loose strands, use bobby pins to keep them in place.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a little extra TLC to keep it looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb.
  • Avoid using hot tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to create the perfect man bun with short curly hair. Whether you’re headed to the office or the gym, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
