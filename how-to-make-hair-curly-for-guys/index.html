How To Make Hair Curly For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 26, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and looking for a change? Do you want to try out curly hair but don't know where to start? Look no further! In this article, we will teach you how to make hair curly for guys. Whether you have short or long hair, this guide will work for you. Read on to learn more!

Description

Hair is an important part of our appearance, and it can be fun to switch things up every once in a while. One way to do this is by making your hair curly. Curly hair can add volume and texture to your look, and it's a great way to change things up. However, some people might think that curly hair is only for women. This is not true! Men can also rock curly hair, and it can look great on them.

How to Make Hair Curly for Guys

Before we get started, it's important to note that there are different ways to make hair curly. Some methods involve using heat tools, while others use no heat at all. In this guide, we will cover both options.

Method 1: Using Heat Tools

Step 1: Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt or oil from your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will protect your hair from heat damage caused by styling tools.

Step 3: Use a hair dryer to dry your hair. Make sure to use a diffuser attachment to help create curls.

Step 4: Use a curling iron to curl your hair. Start by taking small sections of hair and wrapping them around the curling iron. Hold the hair in place for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Method 2: No Heat

Step 1: Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This could be a curl cream, mousse, or gel.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections and twist each section into a coil.

Step 4: Use bobby pins to secure the coils in place.

Step 5: Leave the coils in place for several hours or overnight.

Step 6: Remove the bobby pins and gently unravel the coils.

Tips

Tip 1: Experiment with different curling methods to find what works best for you.

Tip 2: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.

Tip 3: Don't be afraid to use hair products. They can help enhance your curls and keep them in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my hair curly if it's naturally straight?

A: Yes, you can! There are many ways to make hair curly, even if it's naturally straight.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: This depends on the method you use and your hair type. Generally, curls created with heat tools will last longer than curls created without heat.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're using heat tools or styling products. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

- Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

- Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

- Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can enjoy curly hair without damaging your hair.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to make hair curly for guys, it's time to try it out for yourself! Whether you prefer heat styling or no heat, there's a method that will work for you. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun with your new curly look!
