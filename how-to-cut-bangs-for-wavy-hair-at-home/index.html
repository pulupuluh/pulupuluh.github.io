How To Cut Bangs For Wavy Hair At Home

Written by Oliver Aug 04, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have wavy hair and are considering cutting bangs, you might be wondering how to achieve the perfect look at home. Cutting bangs can be intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve the look you want. In this article, we’ll cover how to cut bangs for wavy hair at home, step-by-step, along with some tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How To Cut Bangs for Wavy Hair at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Bangs for Wavy Hair at Home
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Advice for Wavy Hair

How To Cut Bangs for Wavy Hair at Home

The first step to cutting bangs for wavy hair at home is to make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a fine-tooth comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. It’s also a good idea to have a mirror and some hair clips on hand.

Before you start cutting, decide on the length and style of your bangs. Will they be blunt or layered? Will they be long or short? Take some time to look at pictures of hairstyles with bangs to get an idea of the look you want to achieve.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools and know what style you want to achieve, it’s time to start cutting your bangs. Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use the fine-tooth comb to separate your bangs from the rest of your hair. Clip the rest of your hair back to keep it out of the way.
  2. Spray your bangs lightly with water to make them easier to work with.
  3. Using the comb, comb your bangs straight down over your forehead. Hold the comb at the length you want your bangs to be.
  4. Hold the comb steady with one hand and use the scissors to cut your bangs in a straight line. Start in the center and work your way out to the sides.
  5. If you want layered bangs, take small sections of your bangs and cut them at an angle. Start with the hair closest to your forehead and work your way back.
  6. Once you’ve cut your bangs to the desired length, comb them out and check for any uneven spots. Make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Style your bangs as desired. You can blow dry them straight or let them air dry for a more natural look.

Tips for Cutting Bangs for Wavy Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with small snips. It’s easier to cut more hair later than to fix a mistake.
  • Don’t cut your bangs too short. You can always cut more off later, but you can’t add it back.
  • If you’re unsure about your cutting skills, start with longer bangs and work your way shorter.
  • Remember that wavy hair tends to shrink when it dries, so keep this in mind when cutting your bangs.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting bangs for wavy hair at home:

Can I cut my bangs when they’re wet?

It’s best to cut your bangs when they’re dry, but if you prefer to cut them wet, make sure to cut them longer than you want them to be. Hair tends to shrink when it dries, so cutting them wet can result in shorter bangs than you intended.

What’s the best way to style my wavy hair with bangs?

There are many ways to style wavy hair with bangs. You can blow dry them straight for a sleek look, or let them air dry for a more natural look. You can also use a curling iron or wand to enhance your natural waves.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It’s best to trim your bangs every four to six weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Advice for Wavy Hair

Finally, here are some tips for taking care of your wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to damp hair to keep your waves defined and frizz-free.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz. Air drying your hair is the best option, but if you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut bangs for your wavy hair at home. Remember to take your time, start with small snips, and have fun experimenting with different styles.
