Men s Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top A Trendy Hairstyle For 2023

Written by Robby Sep 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle for men in 2023, then the men's haircut shaved sides long top is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been popular for several years now, and it doesn't seem to be going out of fashion anytime soon. This article will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to achieve this look, as well as tips on how to maintain and take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The men's haircut shaved sides long top is a hairstyle that features shaved or closely trimmed sides and a longer top. This hairstyle is often referred to as an undercut or a disconnected haircut, and it can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. The shaved sides create a sharp contrast with the longer hair on top, which can be styled in a variety of ways.

How to

To achieve the men's haircut shaved sides long top, you will need to visit a professional hairstylist who has experience with this style. You can bring a picture with you to show the hairstylist the look you want to achieve. The hairstylist will then use clippers or scissors to shave or trim the sides of your head, leaving the hair on top longer.

Step by step

Here are the steps to achieve the men's haircut shaved sides long top:

  1. Find a professional hairstylist who has experience with this style.
  2. Bring a picture with you to show the hairstylist the look you want to achieve.
  3. The hairstylist will use clippers or scissors to shave or trim the sides of your head.
  4. The hair on top will be left longer and can be styled in a variety of ways.
  5. You can use styling products such as pomade, wax or gel to create different looks.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style your men's haircut shaved sides long top:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Trim the sides regularly to maintain the sharp contrast with the longer hair on top.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.
  • Try different hairstyles such as slicked back, messy or textured to keep your look fresh and versatile.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut shaved sides long top:

  • Is the men's haircut shaved sides long top suitable for all hair types?
    Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit different hair types.
  • How often do I need to trim the sides?
    It depends on how fast your hair grows, but a good rule of thumb is to trim the sides every 2-3 weeks.
  • Can I style the longer hair on top in different ways?
    Yes, you can experiment with different hairstyles such as slicked back, messy or textured.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair after getting the men's haircut shaved sides long top:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much styling products, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, the men's haircut shaved sides long top is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can suit different face shapes and hair types. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can maintain this look and keep it looking fresh and stylish. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
