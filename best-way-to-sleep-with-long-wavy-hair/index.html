Best Way To Sleep With Long Wavy Hair

Written by Robby Mar 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Long wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it’s beautiful, it can be a hassle to maintain, especially when it comes to sleeping. Tossing and turning can lead to tangles and knots, leaving you with a mess in the morning. But fear not, there are ways to sleep with long wavy hair that can help reduce the damage and keep your locks looking luscious. Here are some tips on the best way to sleep with long wavy hair.

How to Sleep with Long Wavy Hair

Step by Step

Step 1: Brush your hair before bed.

Step 2: Divide your hair into two sections.

Step 3: Braid each section.

Step 4: Secure the ends of the braids with hair ties.

Step 5: Sleep on a silk pillowcase.

Tips

- Use a detangling spray before brushing your hair to avoid breakage.

- Don’t braid your hair too tightly, as this can cause tension and breakage.

- Use a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent tangles.

FAQ

Q: Can I sleep with my hair loose?

A: It’s not recommended to sleep with your hair loose, as it can lead to tangles and knots.

Q: Can I use a regular pillowcase?

A: While you can use a regular pillowcase, it’s recommended to use a silk one to reduce friction and prevent tangles.

Q: How often should I brush my hair?

A: It’s recommended to brush your hair at least once a day, but not more than twice a day to avoid over-brushing and damaging your hair.

Hair Care

Aside from sleeping with your hair properly, it’s important to take care of your long wavy locks to keep them healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips:

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

- Apply a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week.

- Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.

- Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

By following these tips on the best way to sleep with long wavy hair and taking care of your locks, you can wake up with beautiful, healthy hair every day.
