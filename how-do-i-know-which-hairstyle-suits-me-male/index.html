How Do I Know Which Hairstyle Suits Me Male

Written by Bardi Feb 18, 2023 · 8 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, especially if you’re not quite sure what suits you. With so many options available for men, it can be challenging to find the perfect one that complements your facial features, hair type, and lifestyle. In this article, we’ll explore how to determine which hairstyle suits you best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. This will help you understand which hairstyles will complement your features and enhance your overall appearance. There are six main face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, you can use a few simple techniques. One way is to take a photo of yourself facing the camera straight on, with your hair pulled back. Then, trace the outline of your face with a pen or marker. You can then compare the shape to the six main face shapes to determine which one matches the closest.

Matching Your Hairstyle with Your Face Shape

Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement it. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval: Most hairstyles will suit this face shape.
  • Round: Short hairstyles with volume on top work well. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides.
  • Square: Short to medium length hairstyles with volume on top work well. Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the squareness of the face.
  • Heart: Short to medium length hairstyles with volume on top work well. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides.
  • Diamond: Short to medium length hairstyles with volume on top work well. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the temples.
  • Triangle: Medium to long hairstyles with volume on the sides work well. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top.

It’s important to note that these are just general guidelines. You can always experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. Different hairstyles work better with different hair types. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, you may want to consider a shorter hairstyle that is easy to manage. If you have fine, straight hair, you may want to consider a longer hairstyle with layers to add volume.

Lifestyle and Occupation

Your lifestyle and occupation can also influence the type of hairstyle you choose. If you have an active lifestyle or work in a physically demanding job, you may want a shorter, low-maintenance hairstyle. If you work in a more professional setting, you may want a more polished and refined hairstyle.

Personal Preferences

Ultimately, the hairstyle you choose should be based on your personal preferences. You should feel comfortable and confident in your hairstyle, and it should reflect your personal style and taste.

Seeking Professional Advice

If you’re still unsure about which hairstyle suits you best, it’s always a good idea to seek professional advice. A hairstylist can help you determine your face shape and hair type and recommend hairstyles that will suit you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hairstyle and hair type. Shorter hairstyles typically require more frequent haircuts, while longer hairstyles can go longer between cuts. As a general rule, aim to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

What products should I use for my hair?

The products you use will depend on your hair type and hairstyle. Some common products include shampoo, conditioner, styling gel, pomade, and hairspray. It’s important to choose products that are suited to your hair type and avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair after shampooing
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy

By following these tips and choosing a hairstyle that suits you, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and flattering.
