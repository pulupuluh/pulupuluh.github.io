What Sunglasses Look Best On A Heart Shaped Face

Written by Petter Mar 25, 2023 · 7 min read

Summer is just around the corner and it's time to start thinking about sunglasses. With so many different styles to choose from, it can be tough to find the perfect pair. If you have a heart shaped face, it's important to find sunglasses that complement your features. In this article, we'll go over what sunglasses look best on a heart shaped face, as well as some tips for choosing the right pair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Choose Sunglasses for a Heart Shaped Face
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing Sunglasses for a Heart Shaped Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Wearing Sunglasses

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrow chin. The goal when choosing sunglasses for this face shape is to balance out the wider top of the face with the narrower bottom. Look for sunglasses that are wider at the bottom, with rounded edges to soften the face's angles.

How To Choose Sunglasses for a Heart Shaped Face

When shopping for sunglasses, keep these tips in mind:

  • Look for sunglasses with rounded edges to soften the angles of your face.
  • Choose frames that are wider at the bottom than the top to balance out your features.
  • Avoid frames that are too small or too large for your face.
  • Try on a variety of styles to see what looks best on you.

Step By Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the perfect sunglasses for your heart shaped face:

  1. Measure the width of your face at the widest point (usually the forehead).
  2. Choose sunglasses that are wider at the bottom than the top.
  3. Look for frames with rounded edges to soften your features.
  4. Avoid frames that are too small or too large for your face.
  5. Try on a variety of styles to see what looks best on you.
  6. Consider the color of the frames and lenses.
  7. Make sure the sunglasses fit comfortably and securely on your face.
  8. Take your time and don't be afraid to ask for help.

Tips for Choosing Sunglasses for a Heart Shaped Face

Here are some additional tips for choosing sunglasses that flatter a heart shaped face:

  • Look for frames that are wider at the bottom than the top. This will help balance out your features.
  • Choose frames with rounded edges to soften the angles of your face.
  • Stay away from frames that are too small or too large for your face.
  • Consider the color of the frames and lenses. Neutral colors like black, brown, and tortoise shell are always a safe bet, but don't be afraid to try something bold and colorful.
  • Make sure the sunglasses fit comfortably and securely on your face.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. A salesperson or friend can help you choose the perfect pair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing sunglasses for a heart shaped face:

What shape sunglasses look best on a heart shaped face?

Sunglasses with rounded edges and wider bottoms look best on a heart shaped face.

What color sunglasses look best on a heart shaped face?

Neutral colors like black, brown, and tortoise shell are always a safe bet, but don't be afraid to try something bold and colorful.

Can heart shaped faces wear aviator sunglasses?

Yes, heart shaped faces can wear aviator sunglasses, but look for ones with rounded edges and a wider bottom to balance out your features.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Sunglasses

Wearing sunglasses can cause your hair to get tangled or flattened. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great while wearing sunglasses:

  • Wear your hair up in a bun or ponytail to avoid tangling.
  • Use a hair tie or clip to secure your hair away from your face.
  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume to your hair after wearing sunglasses.

Choosing the perfect sunglasses for a heart shaped face doesn't have to be difficult. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect pair of shades to flatter your features and keep you looking great all summer long.
