Men s Hair Long On Top Short On Bottom A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

The men's hair long on top short on bottom style has been around for quite some time now and it doesn't seem to be going away anytime soon. This hairstyle is a perfect combination of the classic and modern looks, giving you a stylish and trendy appearance. In this guide, we will be discussing everything you need to know about the long on top short on bottom hairstyle for men in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The long on top short on bottom hairstyle is characterized by long hair on the top of the head and short hair on the sides and back. This style is versatile and can be worn in different ways, such as a slicked-back look, a messy style, or a pompadour, among others. It can work for any hair type, but it's particularly great for those with thick hair.

How To

Getting the long on top short on bottom hairstyle is relatively easy. All you need to do is leave the hair on top of your head to grow longer while keeping the sides and back short. You can then style the long hair on top in different ways depending on your preference.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the long on top short on bottom hairstyle:

  1. Grow out the hair on the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short.
  2. Visit a barber or hairstylist to get a trim for the sides and back.
  3. Style the long hair on top in a way that suits you best.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting the long on top short on bottom hairstyle:

  • Make sure to communicate clearly with your barber or hairstylist about the length you want for the sides and back.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find one that works best for you.
  • Consider getting a fade or undercut for a more defined look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top short on bottom hairstyle:

Is the long on top short on bottom hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the long on top short on bottom hairstyle can work for any face shape. However, some variations of the style may be more flattering for certain face shapes than others.

How often should I get a trim for the sides and back?

You should get a trim for the sides and back every 2-4 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining your long on top short on bottom hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy.
  • Use a styling product that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Get a regular trim to keep the style looking fresh.

Overall, the long on top short on bottom hairstyle is a timeless and versatile look that can work for any occasion. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can keep this style looking great all year round.
