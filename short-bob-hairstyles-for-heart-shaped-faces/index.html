Short Bob Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Petter Mar 12, 2023 · 6 min read

Description

If you have a heart shaped face, you know that finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to accentuate your features without overwhelming them. Short bob hairstyles for heart shaped faces are a great option because they can frame your face and highlight your cheekbones. In this article, we’ll show you some of the best short bob hairstyles for heart shaped faces and give you tips for maintaining your new look.

How To

1. Choose the right length

The length of your bob is crucial for creating the perfect look for your heart shaped face. You want to go for a length that falls between your chin and shoulders. This length will help to balance out the width of your forehead while highlighting your cheekbones.

2. Add layers

Layers are a great way to add volume and movement to your short bob hairstyle. They can also help to soften the sharp angles of your jawline. Ask your stylist to add layers that start at your chin and work their way down.

3. Consider bangs

Bangs can be a great addition to your short bob hairstyle. They can help to balance out the width of your forehead and highlight your eyes. However, be careful not to go too short with your bangs. You want them to be long enough to sweep to the side.

Step by Step

1. Wash your hair

Before you start styling your short bob, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

2. Apply styling product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or a styling cream. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the tips. Use the brush to create volume and movement in your hair.

4. Style your hair

Once your hair is dry, style it using a flat iron or a curling iron. You can create soft waves or sleek and straight hair.

Tips

- Use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron or a curling iron.


- Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.


- Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and lengths to find the perfect look for your face shape.


- Consult with your stylist before making any major changes to your hair.

FAQ

What is a heart shaped face?

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead and a narrow chin. The cheekbones are usually the widest part of the face.

What are the best short bob hairstyles for heart shaped faces?

The best short bob hairstyles for heart shaped faces are those that fall between your chin and shoulders and have layers that start at your chin and work their way down. Bangs can also be a great addition to your hairstyle.

Hair Care

To maintain your short bob hairstyle, make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
