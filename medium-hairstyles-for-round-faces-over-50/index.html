Medium Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50 A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Apr 25, 2023 · 8 min read

If you are over 50 with a round face and looking for a new hairstyle, you have come to the right place. In this guide, we will discuss some of the best medium hairstyles for round faces over 50. We will cover everything from how to choose the right hairstyle to hair care tips to help you maintain your new look. So, let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it’s important to consider the shape of your face. Round faces have softer features and a wider forehead and cheekbones. The goal is to create balance by adding volume to the top of the head and slimming the sides of the face.

Medium hairstyles work well for round faces because they provide versatility and can be styled in a variety of ways. Some great options include:

  • Layered cuts
  • Long bobs
  • Shoulder-length styles with layers
  • Side-swept bangs

Layered Cuts

Layered cuts are a great option for round faces because they add volume to the top of the head and create movement. The layers should be cut in a way that frames the face, with shorter layers around the crown and longer layers at the bottom.

Long Bobs

A long bob, or lob, is a great option for round faces because it elongates the face and creates a slimming effect. The length should hit just below the chin and be styled with soft waves or curls.

Shoulder-Length Styles with Layers

Shoulder-length styles with layers are a great option for round faces because they create volume and movement while slimming the face. The layers should be cut in a way that frames the face, with shorter layers around the crown and longer layers at the bottom.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces because they create a diagonal line that slims the face. The bangs should be cut in a way that frames the face and hits just below the eyebrows.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Hair

Styling medium hair can be easy with the right tools and techniques. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.
  4. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create soft waves or curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Maintaining your hairstyle is important to ensure that it looks its best. Here are some tips to help you keep your medium hairstyle looking great:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for a round face over 50?

Medium hairstyles with layers, long bobs, shoulder-length styles with layers, and side-swept bangs are all great options for round faces over 50.

Can I wear my medium hair in a ponytail?

Yes, you can wear your medium hair in a ponytail. Just be sure to use a hair tie that won’t damage your hair and avoid pulling your hair too tight.

What is the best way to style medium hair?

The best way to style medium hair is to use a round brush to create volume and then use a curling iron or flat iron to create soft waves or curls. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Hair

Keeping your hair healthy is important to ensure that it looks its best. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your medium hairstyle:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair to prevent damage.

Now that you have all the information you need, it’s time to choose the perfect medium hairstyle for your round face over 50. Remember to choose a style that complements your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful!
