How To Cut Men s Long Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man with long, curly hair, you know that finding a stylist who knows how to cut your locks can be a challenge. Fortunately, with the right tools and techniques, you can learn how to trim your own hair at home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut men's long hair curly, and provide tips to keep your curls looking great.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Men's Long Hair Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Men's Curly Hair

How to Cut Men's Long Hair Curly

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's review some of the tools you'll need to get started:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror

Now, let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help remove any product buildup and make your curls easier to manage. Comb your hair while it's wet to detangle it.

Step 2: Part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your preference. Use hair ties or clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 3: Starting at the back of your head, use the scissors to trim any split ends or damaged hair. Take small sections of hair and cut 1/4 inch at a time. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.

Step 4: Move to the sides of your head, and use the same technique to trim any damaged hair. Be sure to keep the sections of hair separate using hair ties or clips.

Step 5: If you want to use clippers to trim the sides and back of your head, use a guard attachment to ensure an even cut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if needed.

Step 6: Once you've finished trimming all sections of your hair, use the scissors to shape your curls. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle to create layers and texture.

Step 7: Use the wide-tooth comb to style your curls. You can also use a bit of curl cream or gel to define your curls and keep them looking great.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when cutting curly hair:

  • Always cut curly hair when it's dry, as wet hair can shrink and lead to uneven cuts.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends and damage to your hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, consider visiting a professional stylist who specializes in curly hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my own curly hair?

A: Yes, with the right tools and techniques, you can learn how to trim your own curly hair at home.

Q: How often should I trim my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to trim curly hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Q: Should I cut my curly hair when it's wet or dry?

A: Always cut curly hair when it's dry, as wet hair can shrink and lead to uneven cuts.

Hair Care for Men's Curly Hair

Now that you know how to cut men's long hair curly, let's talk about how to keep your curls looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls when wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and keep them looking great.

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your curly hair looking great and save money by trimming your own hair at home. Happy cutting!
