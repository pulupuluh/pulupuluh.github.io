How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors At Home For Beginners

Written by Petter Feb 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a beginner and looking to cut men's hair with scissors at home, you've come to the right place. This guide will provide you with all the necessary steps and tips to help you achieve a professional-looking haircut. Whether you're doing this for yourself, a family member, or a friend, this guide is perfect for beginners.

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you start cutting, you'll need a few things:

  • A pair of scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • A towel
  • A spray bottle of water

Once you have these items ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair thoroughly and towel dry it, so it's not dripping wet. Make sure the hair is combed and free of tangles.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections using a comb. Start by combing the hair straight down the middle of the head, from the forehead to the nape of the neck.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Starting with one side of the head, comb the hair down and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair with scissors, using your fingers as a guide. Repeat this process on the other side of the head.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Comb the hair at the back of the head down and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair with scissors, using your fingers as a guide.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Comb the hair on top of the head forward and hold it between your fingers. Trim the hair with scissors, using your fingers as a guide. Be sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides of the head.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

After cutting, check the hair for evenness. If there are any uneven spots, trim them with scissors.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors at Home

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid uneven cutting.
  • Comb the hair before cutting to avoid tangles.
  • Use a spray bottle to mist the hair with water to help it lay flatter.
  • Always cut the hair when it's dry.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut small sections at a time.

FAQs

How long should the hair be before cutting?

The hair should be at least 1 inch long before cutting. If the hair is shorter, it will be harder to cut and could result in an uneven haircut.

How often should men get their hair cut?

Men should get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain a well-groomed look.

Can I use regular household scissors to cut men's hair?

No, it's not advisable to use regular household scissors to cut men's hair. They won't be sharp enough and could result in an uneven haircut.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting the hair, it's important to wash it again to remove any loose hairs. Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends to keep them from splitting. Avoid using any hair products for at least 24 hours to allow the hair to settle.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with scissors at home for beginners, you can save money by doing it yourself. Remember to use sharp scissors, comb the hair before cutting, and take your time to ensure an even haircut. Good luck!
