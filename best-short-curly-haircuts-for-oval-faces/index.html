How to Choose the Right Short Curly Haircut for Oval Faces

When choosing a short curly haircut for an oval face, it's important to consider the overall shape and proportions of your face. Oval faces are generally symmetrical and well-balanced, with a slightly curved jawline and forehead that's wider than the chin. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short curly haircut:

  • Opt for a cut that adds volume and fullness to the sides of your face, rather than the top. This will help balance out your facial features and prevent your face from looking too long or narrow.
  • Avoid cuts that are too short or cropped close to the head, as these can make your face appear longer and narrower than it actually is.
  • Consider adding layers to your haircut to create movement and texture. This can help soften your facial features and add dimension to your curls.
  • If you're not sure which cut to choose, consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair. They can help you find a cut that flatters your face shape and works with your natural curl pattern.

Best Short Curly Haircuts for Oval Faces

Now that you know what to look for in a short curly haircut for an oval face, let's explore some of the best options:

Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic short haircut that works well with curly hair and oval faces. It's a versatile cut that can be customized to suit your face shape and curl pattern. For oval faces, opt for a bob that falls just below the chin and features layers to add volume and movement to your curls.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and edgy short haircut that can bring out the best in your curls. For oval faces, choose a pixie cut that's slightly longer on top and features textured layers to add volume and dimension to your curls. This will help balance out your facial features and prevent your face from looking too narrow.

Cropped Curls

Cropped curls are a fun and playful short haircut that works well with oval faces and curly hair. This cut features cropped curls all over the head, with the sides and back cut shorter than the top. It's a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks chic and stylish.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Haircuts for Oval Faces

Styling short curly haircuts for oval faces can be a bit tricky, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful and natural-looking style. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your curls:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls and distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Flip your head upside down and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. If you don't have a diffuser, you can use a regular hair dryer on a low heat setting.
  4. Once your hair is about 80% dry, flip your head back up and use your fingers to scrunch your curls upwards. This will help create volume and definition.
  5. Finish off your style with a lightweight hairspray or curl-defining spray to help hold your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Haircuts for Oval Faces

Maintaining short curly haircuts for oval faces requires a bit of effort, but with these tips, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your curls of their natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your curls when they're dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools, as excessive heat can damage your curls and cause them to lose their shape.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Yes, oval faces can pull off short curly haircuts with the right cut and styling techniques. It's important to choose a cut that adds volume and fullness to the sides of your face, rather than the top, to balance out your facial features.

Which short curly haircut is best for oval faces?

The best short curly haircut for an oval face depends on your personal style and curl pattern. Some popular options include the bob cut, pixie cut, and cropped curls.

How do I style short curly haircuts for oval faces?

To style short curly haircuts for oval faces, start with clean, damp hair and apply a curl-defining product. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, then scrunch your curls upwards to create volume and definition.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Haircuts for Oval Faces

Caring for short curly haircuts for oval faces requires a bit of effort, but with these tips, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a gentle sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your curls of their natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your curls when they're dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them sparingly and with a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short curly haircut that complements your oval face shape and brings out the best in your curls. Remember to consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair if you're not sure which cut to choose, and always use products that are specifically formulated for curly hair.
