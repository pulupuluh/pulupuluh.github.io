Short Hair Styles For Round Chubby Faces

Written by Luffy Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that don't seem to work for your round chubby face? Well, worry no more because we've got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing short hair styles that are perfect for round chubby faces.

Description

Having a round chubby face can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. However, with the right haircut and styling, you can enhance your facial features and look stunning. Short haircuts are perfect for round chubby faces as they help to create an illusion of length and add definition to the cheekbones.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for a round chubby face, it's essential to consider the length and the style. Short haircuts that are layered and textured are perfect for adding volume and creating an illusion of length. Here are some short haircuts that work well for round chubby faces:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short haircut that is perfect for round chubby faces. This haircut is ideal for those who want to draw attention away from their cheeks and towards their eyes. The short layers and the textured look create an illusion of length, making the face look slimmer.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is another excellent short haircut for round chubby faces. The bob cut is versatile and can be styled in different ways, making it perfect for those who like to experiment with different hairstyles. The layers and the textured look add volume and create an illusion of length, making the face appear slimmer.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is perfect for those who want to add volume to their hair and create an illusion of length. The layers add texture and movement to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker. This haircut is perfect for those with round chubby faces as it helps to create an illusion of length and add definition to the cheekbones.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style short haircuts for round chubby faces:

Step 1:

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

Step 2:

Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 3:

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create an illusion of length.

Step 4:

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to create loose waves or curls in your hair.

Step 5:

Apply a texturizing spray to add texture and movement to your hair.

Step 6:

Finish off your look by applying a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and movement to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still style my short haircut if I have a round chubby face?

A: Yes, you can still style your short haircut if you have a round chubby face. Short haircuts that are layered and textured are perfect for adding volume and creating an illusion of length.

Q: How often should I visit my hairstylist to maintain my short haircut?

A: It's recommended that you visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your short haircut.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and the wind by wearing a hat or a scarf.

In conclusion, short haircuts are perfect for round chubby faces. When choosing a short haircut, it's essential to consider the length and the style. Short haircuts that are layered and textured are perfect for adding volume and creating an illusion of length. Remember to visit your hairstylist regularly and take care of your hair to maintain your short haircut.
