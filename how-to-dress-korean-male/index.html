How To Dress Korean Male A Relaxed Guide

Written by Christine May 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to dress like a Korean male, you're in luck. Korean fashion is known for its unique and trendy style that's both comfortable and practical. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve the perfect Korean look, from clothing to hair care. Here's a table of contents to help you navigate:

Table of Contents

  • Clothing
  • Accessories
  • Shoes
  • Hair Care
  • FAQ

Clothing

When it comes to dressing like a Korean male, comfort and style go hand in hand. Korean fashion is all about mixing and matching different pieces to create a unique look. Here are some essential clothing items to get you started:

Tops

Korean males often wear loose-fitting tops that are comfortable and easy to move in. T-shirts with graphic prints or bold colors are a staple in Korean fashion. You can also layer a shirt over a T-shirt for a more sophisticated look.

Bottoms

Jeans are a popular choice for Korean males, but you can also opt for chinos or joggers. The fit should be slim but not too tight. Roll up the hem of your pants for a more casual look.

Jackets

A denim jacket or a bomber jacket is a must-have in any Korean male's wardrobe. You can also add a hoodie or a cardigan for extra warmth.

Accessories

Accessories are the perfect way to add a touch of personality to your outfit. Here are some accessories that Korean males often wear:

Hats

A baseball cap or a bucket hat is a popular choice among Korean males. You can also wear a beanie or a beret for a more stylish look.

Sunglasses

A pair of sunglasses not only protects your eyes from the sun but also adds a cool factor to your outfit. Aviators or round-shaped sunglasses are a safe bet.

Backpacks

A backpack is a practical accessory that also adds a pop of color to your outfit. Choose a backpack with a bold print or a bright color to stand out.

Shoes

The right pair of shoes can make or break your outfit. Here are some shoe options that Korean males often wear:

Sneakers

Sneakers are a staple in Korean fashion. White sneakers are a safe choice, but you can also opt for sneakers with bold colors or prints.

Dress Shoes

If you're going for a more formal look, dress shoes are the way to go. Choose a pair of black or brown dress shoes that are comfortable to walk in.

Sandals

During the summer months, sandals are a great option. Choose a pair of simple sandals that are easy to slip on and off.

Hair Care

Your hair is an important part of your overall look. Here are some tips for Korean-inspired hairstyles:

Short Hair

A short, textured haircut is a popular choice among Korean males. Use a hair wax or a pomade to create a messy, bedhead look.

Medium Hair

If you have medium-length hair, you can try a messy, layered haircut. Use a hair dryer and a round brush to add volume to your hair.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, you can try a half-up, half-down hairstyle or a low ponytail. Use a hair oil or a serum to add shine to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Where can I buy Korean fashion?

A: You can buy Korean fashion online from websites such as YesStyle or Kooding. You can also find Korean fashion in select stores in major cities.

Q: What colors should I wear?

A: Korean fashion often incorporates bold and bright colors. Don't be afraid to mix and match different colors and patterns.

Q: Can I wear makeup?

A: Yes, Korean males often wear makeup to enhance their features. You can try using a tinted moisturizer or a BB cream for a natural look.

Q: How can I accessorize without overdoing it?

A: Stick to one or two accessories per outfit. You can wear a hat and a watch, for example, but avoid wearing too many accessories at once.

By following these tips, you can achieve the perfect Korean look that's both stylish and comfortable. Remember to mix and match different pieces to create your own unique style.
