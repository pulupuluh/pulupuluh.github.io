How To Make Your Hair Straight And Smooth Naturally

Written by Eveline Aug 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools and chemicals to straighten your hair? Not only are they damaging to your hair, but they can also be expensive. Luckily, there are natural ways to make your hair straight and smooth without using any harsh chemicals or heat tools. In this article, we will show you how to make your hair straight and smooth naturally.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The key to making your hair straight and smooth naturally is to hydrate your hair and use natural ingredients that can help relax your hair strands. These natural ingredients will not only help straighten your hair but also nourish and moisturize it. Here are some natural ingredients that you can use:

  • Coconut milk
  • Lemon juice
  • Castor oil
  • Aloe vera gel
  • Banana
  • Eggs

Coconut milk

Coconut milk is an excellent natural ingredient that can help straighten your hair. It contains fatty acids that can penetrate your hair shaft and help relax your hair strands. To use coconut milk, simply apply it to your hair, leave it on for 30 minutes, and rinse it off with lukewarm water.

Lemon juice

Lemon juice is another natural ingredient that can help make your hair straight and smooth. It contains citric acid, which can help relax your hair strands. To use lemon juice, mix it with water and apply it to your hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes and rinse it off with lukewarm water.

Castor oil

Castor oil is a natural oil that can help straighten your hair. It contains ricinoleic acid, which can help relax your hair strands. To use castor oil, simply apply it to your hair, leave it on for 30 minutes, and rinse it off with lukewarm water.

Aloe vera gel

Aloe vera gel is an excellent natural ingredient that can help make your hair straight and smooth. It contains enzymes that can help relax your hair strands. To use aloe vera gel, apply it to your hair, leave it on for 30 minutes, and rinse it off with lukewarm water.

Banana

Banana is a natural ingredient that can help make your hair straight and smooth. It contains vitamins and minerals that can help nourish your hair. To use banana, mash it and mix it with coconut milk. Apply the mixture to your hair, leave it on for 30 minutes, and rinse it off with lukewarm water.

Eggs

Eggs are another natural ingredient that can help make your hair straight and smooth. They contain protein that can help nourish your hair. To use eggs, beat them and mix them with olive oil. Apply the mixture to your hair, leave it on for 30 minutes, and rinse it off with lukewarm water.

Step by Step

Now that you know the natural ingredients that you can use to make your hair straight and smooth, here is a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

  1. Choose a natural ingredient that you want to use.
  2. Prepare the natural ingredient according to the instructions above.
  3. Apply the natural ingredient to your hair, making sure that you cover all the strands.
  4. Leave the natural ingredient on your hair for 30 minutes.
  5. Rinse the natural ingredient off your hair with lukewarm water.
  6. Pat your hair dry with a towel.
  7. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.

Tips

  • Do a patch test before using any natural ingredient to make sure that you are not allergic to it.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to avoid any breakage.
  • Be patient. It may take a few applications before you see the results.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair straight and smooth naturally:

Is it safe to use natural ingredients to make my hair straight?

Yes, natural ingredients are safe to use on your hair. They are not harsh like chemicals or heat tools, so they will not damage your hair.

How often should I use natural ingredients to make my hair straight?

You can use natural ingredients once a week to make your hair straight and smooth.

Can I use natural ingredients on my colored hair?

Yes, you can use natural ingredients on your colored hair. They are gentle and will not strip the color from your hair.

Hair Care

In addition to using natural ingredients to make your hair straight and smooth, it is also important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.

By following these tips and using natural ingredients, you can make your hair straight and smooth naturally. Not only will your hair look amazing, but it will also be healthy and nourished.
