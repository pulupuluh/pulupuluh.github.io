What Hair Color Is Best For My Face Shape

Written by Oliver Jun 15, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hair color but not sure what shade will look best on you? The answer might lie in your face shape. By choosing a hair color that complements your face shape, you can enhance your natural beauty and make your features stand out. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of choosing the perfect hair color for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Choose The Best Hair Color For Your Face Shape
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Description

Before we dive into the specifics of each face shape, let's first understand the different types of face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

Each face shape has its unique features, and choosing a hair color that complements those features can enhance your overall look.

How To Choose The Best Hair Color For Your Face Shape

The following are some general guidelines to follow when choosing a hair color based on your face shape:

  • Round Face: Opt for hair colors that create the illusion of length and height to balance out the roundness of your face. Highlights, lowlights, and ombre styles are great options. Avoid hair colors that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Oval Face: Lucky for you, almost any hair color will look great on an oval face. Experiment with warm and cool tones to see what complements your skin tone the best.
  • Square Face: Choose hair colors that soften your angular features. Warm, caramel tones and highlights are perfect for adding dimension and a softer look to your face. Avoid hair colors that are too dark or blunt.
  • Heart Face: Balayage and ombré hair colors are perfect for heart-shaped faces. These styles add depth and dimension to your hair while complementing your facial features. Avoid hair colors that add volume to the top of your head.
  • Diamond Face: Choose hair colors that soften your angular jawline and cheekbones. Soft, warm tones and highlights are ideal for adding a soft, feminine touch to your face. Avoid hair colors that are too dark or blunt.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the best hair color for your face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape
  2. Research hair colors that complement your face shape
  3. Consider your skin tone and natural hair color
  4. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best hair color for you
  5. Take care of your new hair color with proper hair care techniques

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance your new hair color will require
  • Experiment with temporary hair color options before committing to a permanent change
  • Choose hair colors that complement your skin tone and eye color
  • Take inspiration from celebrities with similar face shapes and skin tones

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the best hair color for your face shape:

  • Can I dye my hair a completely different color if it doesn't match my face shape? Of course! These guidelines are simply suggestions to help you choose a hair color that complements your facial features. Ultimately, the decision is up to you.
  • Will changing my hair color affect the shape of my face? No, changing your hair color will not physically alter the shape of your face.
  • What if I have a combination of face shapes? In this case, choose a hair color that complements the dominant features of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Once you've chosen the perfect hair color for your face shape, it's essential to maintain it properly. Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your new hair color:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently
  • Use heat protectant when styling with heat tools
  • Avoid exposing your hair to too much sun or chlorine
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair

By following these tips and guidelines, you can choose a hair color that enhances your natural beauty and complements your unique features. Remember to consult with a professional stylist and take care of your new hair color to keep it looking its best.
