Does Shoulder Length Hair Suit Square Face

Written by Robby Aug 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Shoulder length hair is a popular hairstyle that can flatter any face shape. But does it suit square faces? If you have a square face and are considering getting shoulder length hair, here's everything you need to know.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

A square face has a strong jawline, a straight forehead, and angular features. If you have a square face, you may want to soften your features with a hairstyle that adds curves and volume to your face. Shoulder length hair can do just that.

How to:

Shoulder length hair can be a great option for square faces, but it's important to choose the right cut and style. Here's how to get the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your square face shape:

1. Consider Layers:

Adding layers to your shoulder length hair can soften your face and add volume. Ask your hairstylist for long layers that start below your chin and frame your face.

2. Side-Swept Bangs:

Side-swept bangs can soften your forehead and add curves to your face. Ask your hairstylist for long, side-swept bangs that start at your temples and blend into your layers.

3. Volume:

Adding volume to your hair can add curves to your face shape. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair at the roots. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.

Step by Step:

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for square faces:

Step 1:

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.

Step 2:

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair at the roots.

Step 3:

Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume.

Step 4:

Ask your hairstylist for long layers that start below your chin and frame your face.

Step 5:

Ask your hairstylist for long, side-swept bangs that start at your temples and blend into your layers.

Tips:

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a shoulder length hairstyle for square faces:

1. Avoid Blunt Cuts:

Avoid blunt cuts that can make your face look even more angular. Instead, opt for layers and soft waves.

2. Embrace Your Natural Texture:

Embrace your natural texture and work with it. If you have naturally curly hair, don't straighten it. Instead, enhance your curls with a curl-enhancing product.

3. Keep Your Hair Healthy:

Healthy hair is key to any hairstyle. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ:

Q. Will shoulder length hair make my face look wider?

A. No, shoulder length hair can actually make your face look slimmer if you add volume and layers.

Q. Can I still wear my hair up with shoulder length hair?

A. Yes, you can still wear your hair up with shoulder length hair. Try a messy bun or a half-up, half-down hairstyle.

Hair Care:

To keep your shoulder length hair looking great, here are a few hair care tips:

1. Use a Heat Protectant:

Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage.

2. Don't Overwash:

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils. Try to wash your hair every other day or every two days.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly:

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, shoulder length hair can be a great option for square faces. Just remember to choose the right cut and style that adds curves and volume to your face. Follow the tips and hair care advice above to keep your hair looking great.
