Short Hairstyles For Fine Straight Hair Over 60 Square Face

Written by Robby May 27, 2023 · 10 min read

If you're over 60 and have a square face shape with fine straight hair, you might be looking for a new hairstyle that flatters your features. Short haircuts can be a great option for this, as they can make your face appear more lifted and youthful. In this article, we'll go over some of the best short hairstyles for fine straight hair over 60 square face, and give you some tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, with a width that is roughly equal to the length of the face. Fine straight hair can be challenging to style, as it tends to lack volume and texture. However, with the right cut and styling products, you can create a flattering and modern look that suits your face shape and hair type.

How to

The first step in getting a great short hairstyle is to find a skilled hairstylist who understands your needs and preferences. You can bring some pictures of hairstyles you like, and discuss what would work best for your face shape and hair type. Some of the most popular short hairstyles for square faces over 60 include:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and layered haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. It's a low-maintenance style that can be dressed up or down, and it can make your face appear more lifted and elongated. To style a pixie cut, you can use a volumizing mousse or spray, and blow-dry your hair with a round brush. You can also add some texture with a wax or pomade, and tousle your hair with your fingers.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic and versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to different lengths and angles. It can frame your face and highlight your best features, and it can add some volume and movement to your hair. To style a bob cut, you can use a smoothing serum or cream, and blow-dry your hair with a paddle brush. You can also curl the ends with a curling iron or wand, and spritz some hairspray for hold.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a haircut that features different lengths and textures, and can create a soft and feminine look. It can add some volume and depth to your hair, and it can blend any unevenness in your hairline or jawline. To style a layered cut, you can use a texturizing spray or powder, and scrunch your hair with your hands. You can also use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls.

Step by Step

Here are some general steps you can follow to create a short hairstyle for fine straight hair over 60 square face:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a gentle and moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, and apply a leave-in conditioner or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round or paddle brush, using a low to medium heat setting. Lift the roots and smooth the ends for volume and shine.
  4. Use a styling product such as mousse, gel, or wax to add texture or hold to your hair. Apply it evenly and sparingly, starting from the roots and working your way down.
  5. Use a flat iron, curling iron, or wand to create waves, curls, or bends in your hair. Hold the tool for a few seconds, and release it gently.
  6. Spray some hairspray or finishing spray to set your style and add shine. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it greasy or stiff.

Tips

Here are some tips you can follow to care for your short fine straight hair over 60 square face:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a soft brush to detangle your hair gently, and avoid pulling or tugging.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it brittle or frizzy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and maintain its shape and health.
  • Eat a balanced and nutritious diet, and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated and healthy.
  • Wear a hat or a scarf to protect your hair from the sun, wind, and cold weather.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for fine straight hair over 60 square face:

Q: What should I consider when choosing a short hairstyle for my face shape?

A: You should consider your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. A short hairstyle can make your face appear more lifted and youthful, but it should also suit your hair type and maintenance routine.

Q: Can I add some color or highlights to my short hair?

A: Yes, you can add some dimension and depth to your short hair with some highlights or lowlights. Talk to your hairstylist about the best color options for your skin tone and hair type.

Q: How often should I wash my short fine straight hair?

A: You should wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle and sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and dull.

Hair Care

Finally, here are some hair care products you can use to maintain your short fine straight hair over 60 square face:

  • Shampoo and conditioner for fine or thin hair, such as Pantene Pro-V Sheer Volume Shampoo and Conditioner.
  • Leave-in conditioner or detangler, such as It's a 10 Miracle Leave-In Product or TRESemmé Keratin Smooth Heat Defense Spray.
  • Styling product, such as L'Oreal Paris Studio Line Clean Gel or Bumble and bumble Sumotech.
  • Hairspray or finishing spray, such as Kenra Volume Spray 25 or Moroccanoil Luminous Hairspray Strong.

With these tips and products, you can rock a stylish and flattering short hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type. Remember to experiment with different cuts and styles, and to have fun with your hair!
