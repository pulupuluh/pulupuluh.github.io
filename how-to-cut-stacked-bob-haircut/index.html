How To Cut Stacked Bob Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a chic and modern hairstyle, the stacked bob is a great option. It's a trendy cut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. Whether you have curly, straight, or wavy hair, a stacked bob can enhance your look and give you a fresh new style. In this post, we'll show you how to cut a stacked bob haircut step-by-step so that you can achieve the perfect look.

Description of Stacked Bob Haircut

A stacked bob haircut is a short, layered haircut that is cut at the back of the head to create a graduated effect. The hair is cut shorter at the back and gradually gets longer towards the front. This creates a stacked, layered look that gives the hair volume and movement. The length of the bob can vary from chin-length to shoulder-length, depending on your preference.

How to Cut Stacked Bob Haircut

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Comb the hair to remove any tangles and divide it into sections. You can use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 2: Cut the Back Section

Start by cutting the back section of the hair. Take a vertical section of hair at the center of the back of the head and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length. Repeat this on both sides of the center section, working your way down.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the sides of the head. Take a horizontal section of hair at the temple and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length. Repeat this on both sides, working your way towards the back of the head.

Step 4: Cut the Front

Finally, cut the front section of the hair. Take a horizontal section of hair at the front of the head, just above the eyebrows. Comb it straight and hold it between your fingers. Cut it to the desired length, making sure to keep the hair longer towards the front. Repeat this on both sides, working your way towards the back of the head.

Tips for Cutting Stacked Bob Haircut

Tip 1: Use sharp hairdressing scissors to get a clean cut.

Tip 2: Cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid mistakes.

Tip 3: Keep the hair wet as you cut to make it easier to work with.

Tip 4: Use a mirror to check the length and shape of the cut.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut a stacked bob by myself?

A: It's not recommended to cut a stacked bob by yourself, as it requires precision and skill. It's best to go to a professional hairstylist for the best results.

Q: How often should I get my stacked bob haircut?

A: It depends on your hair growth rate and how quickly the style loses its shape. Generally, a stacked bob should be trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape.

Hair Care for Stacked Bob Haircut

After cutting your stacked bob, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture. Avoid using too much heat styling tools, as it can damage the hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and avoid split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a perfect stacked bob haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and stylish.
