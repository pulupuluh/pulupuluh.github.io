How Long Will My Hair Grow With Box Braids

Written by Luffy May 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Box braids have been a popular protective hairstyle for years now, and many women turn to them as a way to help their hair grow. If you're wondering how long your hair will grow while wearing box braids, you're not alone. In this article, we'll explore the answer to this question and provide you with some tips on how to care for your hair while wearing box braids.

Description

Box braids are a type of protective hairstyle that involves sectioning the hair into small, square-shaped parts and braiding each section. This style is popular because it can be customized to suit different hair types and lengths, and it's a low-maintenance option for those who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair. The braids themselves can be made from synthetic or natural hair extensions, and they can be styled in a variety of ways.

How To

If you're interested in getting box braids, the first step is to find a stylist who specializes in this type of hairstyle. They'll be able to help you choose the right type of extensions and braid pattern for your hair. Once your hair is braided, you'll need to take care of it properly to ensure that it stays healthy and grows as much as possible.

Step 1: Moisturize Your Scalp

One of the most important things you can do for your hair while wearing box braids is to keep your scalp moisturized. You can do this by using a light oil or moisturizer on your scalp every few days. This will help to prevent dryness and promote healthy hair growth.

Step 2: Protect Your Braids at Night

When you go to bed at night, it's important to protect your braids by wearing a silk or satin scarf or bonnet. This will help to prevent frizz and keep your braids looking neat and tidy.

Step 3: Wash Your Hair Regularly

While it's important to keep your scalp moisturized, it's also important to keep your hair clean. You can wash your hair while wearing box braids by using a gentle shampoo and focusing on your scalp. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips for promoting healthy hair growth while wearing box braids:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated from the inside out.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair, as these can weigh down your braids and make them uncomfortable.
  • Avoid pulling your braids too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

FAQ

How long can I keep box braids in?

You can keep box braids in for anywhere from 4-8 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how well you care for your braids.

Will box braids make my hair grow faster?

Box braids themselves won't make your hair grow faster, but they can help to protect your hair from damage and breakage, which can allow your hair to grow longer over time.

Can I wear box braids if I have relaxed hair?

Yes, you can wear box braids if you have relaxed hair. Just be sure to take extra care to protect your hair and scalp, as relaxed hair can be more prone to breakage and damage.

Hair Care

To care for your hair while wearing box braids, be sure to follow the steps outlined above and avoid any practices that could cause damage to your hair. With proper care, your hair should be able to grow to its full potential while wearing box braids.
