Mid Length Hairstyles For Fine Hair Over 50 A Guide

Written by William Aug 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're over 50 and have fine hair, you may be wondering what hairstyles would work best for you. Luckily, there are many mid length hairstyles that can give you a fresh and youthful look. In this guide, we'll go over some of the best mid length hairstyles for fine hair over 50, along with tips for hair care and styling.

Description

Mid length hairstyles are great for fine hair because they can add volume and texture. They also provide versatility in terms of styling options, making them a popular choice for women over 50. Whether you prefer a sleek and straight look, loose curls, or tousled waves, there's a mid length hairstyle that can work for you.

How To

When choosing a mid length hairstyle, consider your face shape and hair texture. Oval faces work well with almost any mid length hairstyle, while square faces look best with layered cuts that soften the jawline. If you have fine hair, it's important to avoid heavy layers that can make your hair look thinner. Instead, opt for light layers that will add movement and volume without weighing down your hair.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume.
4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose curls or straighten your hair.
5. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mid length hairstyle:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in high-quality styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron, to achieve the best results.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a mid length hairstyle?
A: Yes, you can! Mid length hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in many different ways, including updos and ponytails.

Q: What is the best way to add volume to fine hair?
A: Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, along with a root-lifting spray or mousse, can help add volume to fine hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?
A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have fine hair, you may need to wash it more frequently to prevent it from looking greasy. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, so it's important to find a balance that works for you.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and beautiful mid length hairstyle. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

With these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect mid length hairstyle for your fine hair over 50. Whether you prefer a classic bob, layered cut, or tousled waves, there's a style that can work for you. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
