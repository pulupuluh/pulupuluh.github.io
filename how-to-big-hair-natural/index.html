How To Big Hair Natural A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your flat, lifeless hair and looking to add some volume and bounce? Look no further than this step-by-step guide on how to big hair natural. We'll go over everything from hair care to styling techniques to help you achieve the voluminous locks of your dreams.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. How to Big Hair Natural
  3. Tips for Maintaining Big Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions

Hair Care

Before we dive into how to big hair natural, it's important to establish a solid hair care routine. This includes using the right products for your hair type, washing and conditioning regularly, and avoiding excessive heat styling. Here are some tips:

Choose the Right Products

Opt for products specifically designed for volumizing, such as volumizing shampoo and conditioner or mousse. Avoid heavy products like oils and serums, which can weigh down your hair.

Wash and Condition Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help remove excess oil and buildup, which can weigh down your hair. Be sure to use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner.

Avoid Excessive Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it more difficult to achieve volume. If you must use heat tools, be sure to use a heat protectant and limit use to once or twice a week.

How to Big Hair Natural

Now that you have a solid hair care routine down, it's time to learn how to big hair natural. Here are some styling techniques to help you achieve voluminous locks:

Blow Dry with a Round Brush

Blow drying your hair with a round brush can help add volume and bounce. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to lift and add volume as you go.

Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair at the roots can help add volume and create the appearance of thicker, fuller hair. Use a fine-tooth comb to backcomb small sections of hair at the roots, then smooth the top layer with a brush.

Use Velcro Rollers

Velcro rollers can help add volume and create soft, bouncy curls. Roll sections of hair starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Leave the rollers in for at least 20 minutes before removing.

Tips for Maintaining Big Hair

Now that you know how to big hair natural, it's important to maintain your voluminous locks. Here are some tips:

Avoid Touching Your Hair

Touching your hair throughout the day can cause it to flatten and lose volume. Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible, and use a hairspray or texturizing spray to help hold your style in place.

Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and maintain your hairstyle. Silk is gentler on your hair than cotton, which can cause friction and damage.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair look flat and lifeless. Aim for a trim every 8-12 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I big hair natural if I have fine hair?

Absolutely! Fine hair can benefit from volumizing products and styling techniques just as much as thicker hair. Just be sure to avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

How long will my big hair natural style last?

The longevity of your style will depend on your hair type and the products and techniques you use. Generally, you can expect your style to last 1-2 days with proper maintenance.

What's the best way to wash my hair for big hair natural?

When washing your hair for big hair natural, be sure to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it more difficult to achieve volume.

Can I big hair natural if I have short hair?

Absolutely! Volumizing products and techniques can help add volume and texture to short hair. Try using a volumizing mousse or teasing your hair at the roots to create the appearance of thicker hair.
