Long Hairstyles For Over 60 With Glasses

Written by Aprilia Aug 26, 2023 · 5 min read

As we age, our style and preferences change. One such change is in the way we style our hair. Long hairstyles for over 60 with glasses are trendy and stylish, and they can be customized to suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. In this article, we will be sharing some tips on how to style long hair if you are over 60 and wear glasses.

How To

The first step in styling long hair is to ensure that it is healthy and well-maintained. Regular trims and conditioning treatments can help keep your hair looking and feeling healthy. Once you have healthy hair, you can begin experimenting with different styles.

Step by Step

1. Start by parting your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your face shape.

2. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or soft curls for a feminine and romantic look.

3. You can also straighten your hair for a sleek and chic look.

4. If you have bangs, you can style them to the side or keep them straight.

5. Finish off your look with a spritz of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

1. Experiment with different parting styles to find one that flatters your face shape.

2. Use hair accessories such as clips and headbands to add a touch of glam to your hairstyle.

3. If you have thin hair, consider getting layers to add volume and texture.

4. If you have thick hair, consider getting a blunt cut to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Q. Can I still wear my hair long if I’m over 60?

A. Yes, you can. Long hair can be very flattering and stylish, regardless of age.

Q. What is the best way to care for long hair?

A. Regular trims, conditioning treatments, and using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair can help keep it healthy and looking great.

Hair Care

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

2. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

3. Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage and breakage.

4. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

5. Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet as it can cause breakage.

In conclusion, long hairstyles for over 60 with glasses can be elegant and chic. With the right care and styling, you can achieve a look that flatters your face shape and complements your glasses. Use these tips and tricks to find a style that suits you and your lifestyle.
