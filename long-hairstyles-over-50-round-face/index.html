Long Hairstyles Over 50 Round Face Tips And Ideas

Written by Robby Mar 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out, become less voluminous, and lose its natural shine. But that doesn't mean we can't still rock a fabulous hairstyle! If you have a round face and are over 50, long hairstyles can be a great option. In this post, we'll share some tips and ideas on how to choose the right long hairstyle for your round face and how to take care of your hair.

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. Long hairstyles can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. However, not all long hairstyles are suitable for round faces. The key is to choose a style that adds height and volume to the crown of your head while keeping the sides of your hair sleek and relatively flat.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for a Round Face

Here are some tips on how to choose the right long hairstyle for your round face:

1. Layers and Bangs

Layers can add texture and movement to your hair, while bangs can help break up the roundness of your face. Opt for long, side-swept bangs that start at the cheekbones, as they can create the illusion of a narrower face. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as they can make your face look even rounder.

2. Length and Volume

Long hair can be heavy, which can make your face look even fuller. To avoid this, consider adding layers or opting for a shorter style that still falls below the shoulders. Adding volume to the crown of your head can also help create the illusion of a longer face.

3. Waves and Curls

Soft waves or loose curls can add volume and texture to your hair, which can help elongate your face. Use a curling iron or a set of hot rollers to add some waves or curls to your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for a Round Face

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling long hair for a round face:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some body to your hair.

2. Add Texture

If you have straight hair, add some texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create some waves or curls.

3. Create Volume

Add some volume to the crown of your head by teasing your hair with a comb. Take small sections of hair and backcomb them at the roots, then smooth the top layer with a brush.

4. Style Your Bangs

If you have bangs, style them to the side with a round brush and a blow dryer. Use some hairspray to keep them in place.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Spray some hairspray all over your hair to set the style and add some shine.

Tips for Long Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your long hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Long hair can be more prone to breakage and split ends, so it's important to keep it moisturized. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add some extra moisture to your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like curling irons, straighteners, and blow dryers can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Avoid using these tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

FAQs

1. What long hairstyles are best for a round face?

Long hairstyles with layers, bangs, and volume at the crown of the head are best for a round face. Soft waves or loose curls can also help elongate the face.

2. How often should I wash my long hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, you should wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

3. What should I do if my long hair is thinning?

If your hair is thinning, consider adding some layers or opting for a shorter style that still falls below the shoulders. Avoid heavy, flat hairstyles that can make your hair look even thinner.

Long hairstyles can be a great option for women over 50 with round faces. By choosing the right style and taking good care of your hair, you can look and feel fabulous at any age.
