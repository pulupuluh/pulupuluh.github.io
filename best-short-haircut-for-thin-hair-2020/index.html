Best Short Haircut For Thin Hair 2020 A Guide To Finding The Perfect Cut

Written by Bardi Jul 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling with thin hair and searching for the perfect haircut to make it look fuller and more voluminous? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best short haircuts for thin hair in 2020, with tips on how to style and care for your new cut.

Description

Thin hair can be a challenge to work with, but with the right haircut, you can make it look thicker and more voluminous. Short haircuts are a great option for thin hair, as they create the illusion of fullness and can be styled in a variety of ways.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for thin hair, it's important to keep a few things in mind. First, opt for a cut that adds volume and texture to your hair. This can be achieved through layers, choppy ends, and asymmetrical cuts.

Second, consider your face shape when choosing a haircut. A short pixie cut may look great on someone with a heart-shaped face, but may not be the best option for those with a round or square face.

Finally, think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair. Some short haircuts require more maintenance than others, so choose a cut that fits your lifestyle.

Step by Step

1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which short haircut will work best for your hair type and face shape.

2. Before getting your hair cut, make sure it's clean and dry.

3. Ask your hairstylist to add layers or texture to your hair to create volume.

4. Style your new haircut using products that add volume and texture, such as mousse or texturizing spray.

Tips

- Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create dimension and make it look thicker.

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my thin hair look even thinner?

A: Not necessarily. A short haircut can actually make thin hair look fuller and more voluminous, as long as it's cut and styled correctly.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if I have thin hair?

A: Absolutely! There are many styling products and techniques you can use to create volume and texture in your hair.

Hair Care

After getting a short haircut for thin hair, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or straighteners too often.

Regular trims are also important to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

With the right short haircut and proper care, you can make your thin hair look fuller and more voluminous than ever before!
