How To Know What Hairstyle Fits Your Face

Written by Eveline Mar 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of constantly changing hairstyles because they don't seem to fit your face? Choosing the right hairstyle that complements your face shape can make a big difference in your overall appearance. In this article, we will guide you on how to determine what hairstyle suits your face shape and give you tips on hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide in Choosing Hairstyles for Your Face Shape
  • Tips in Choosing the Right Hairstyle

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the right hairstyle for your face shape, you need to know what your face shape is. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, diamond, heart, and oblong. Here is a simple way to determine your face shape:

  1. Take a selfie or stand in front of a mirror.
  2. Trace your face shape using a lipstick or an eyeliner.
  3. Compare your traced shape to the six common face shapes.

Here are some characteristics of each face shape:

Oval

An oval face shape has balanced proportions with a gently rounded hairline and jawline. The forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face.

Round

A round face shape has a circular shape with a wide hairline and full cheeks. The forehead and jawline are rounded, and the length and width of the face are almost equal.

Square

A square face shape has a strong and angular jawline with a square hairline. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline have nearly the same width.

Diamond

A diamond face shape has a narrow hairline and chin with wide cheekbones. The forehead is narrower than the cheekbones, and the jawline is pointed.

Heart

A heart face shape has a wider forehead and cheekbones with a narrow chin. The hairline may be widows peak or straight, and the jawline is slightly pointed.

Oblong

An oblong face shape has a longer shape with a straight hairline and jawline. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline have nearly the same width, and the face has a narrow appearance.

Step-by-Step Guide in Choosing Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Once you have determined your face shape, you can now choose the right hairstyle that complements your features. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Consider your hair type, length, and texture.
  2. Choose hairstyles that balance your face shape.
  3. Choose hairstyles that highlight your best features.
  4. Choose hairstyles that flatter your face shape.

Here are some examples of hairstyles that suit each face shape:

Oval

Almost any hairstyle suits an oval face shape. You can try a pixie cut, bob, long layers, or bangs.

Round

Choose hairstyles that create angles and add length to your face. You can try a side-swept bangs, layered bob, or long layers.

Square

Choose hairstyles that soften your angular features. You can try a side-parted long layers, textured pixie cut, or chin-length bob.

Diamond

Choose hairstyles that balance your narrow forehead and chin. You can try a side-swept bangs, textured bob, or long layers.

Heart

Choose hairstyles that balance your wider forehead and cheekbones. You can try a side-swept bangs, long layers, or shoulder-length bob.

Oblong

Choose hairstyles that add width to your face. You can try a layered bob, wavy lob, or side-swept bangs.

Tips in Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle and personality.
  • Consult with your hairstylist.
  • Experiment with temporary hairstyles like wigs or clip-ins.
  • Accessorize with hair accessories like headbands or barrettes.

FAQs

Can I wear any hairstyle I want?

While you can wear any hairstyle you want, choosing the right one that flatters your face shape can make a big difference. It can enhance your features and make you look more attractive.

Can I change my face shape with a hairstyle?

No, a hairstyle cannot change your face shape. It can only enhance or soften your features.

How often should I change my hairstyle?

It depends on your preference and hair growth. Some people change their hairstyle every few months, while others stick to the same style for years.

Hair Care Tips

Aside from choosing the right hairstyle, maintaining healthy hair is also important. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water.

Now that you know how to determine your face shape and choose the right hairstyle for it, you can confidently rock a new look that flatters your features. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
