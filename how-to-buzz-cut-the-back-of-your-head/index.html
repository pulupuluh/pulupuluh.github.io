How To Buzz Cut The Back Of Your Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to give yourself a buzz cut, you're not alone. Many people prefer the low-maintenance, clean look of a buzz cut. However, it can be tricky to get the back of your head just right. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to buzz cut the back of your head like a pro.

Description

A buzz cut is a style of haircut that involves clipping the hair to a very short length using clippers. It's a popular style for men, but many women also choose to buzz their hair. Buzz cuts are great for people who want a low-maintenance haircut that doesn't require a lot of styling or upkeep.

How to Buzz Cut the Back of Your Head

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers with a guard, a handheld mirror, and a regular mirror. You may also want to have a friend or family member help you.

Step 1: Choose the Right Guard Size

The guard size you choose will determine how short your hair will be. If you're not sure what guard size to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the length you like.

Step 2: Start at the Nape of Your Neck

Begin by placing the clippers at the nape of your neck and working your way up. Use slow, even strokes, and be careful not to press too hard or move too quickly. Make sure you're using the same guard size all over.

Step 3: Use the Handheld Mirror to Check Your Progress

Hold the handheld mirror up to the back of your head so you can see the areas you've missed. This will help you make sure you're cutting all the hair evenly.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Back

Once you've finished the back, blend the sides and back together by using a slightly shorter guard size on the sides. Use the same technique as before, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

Step 5: Fine-Tune Your Cut

After you've finished buzzing your hair, use a pair of scissors or clippers without a guard to clean up any stray hairs around your ears and the back of your neck. You can also use a comb to blend the hair around your hairline for a more natural look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting your hair too short.
  • Use slow, even strokes and be careful not to press too hard or move too quickly.
  • Hold the clippers at a slight angle to the scalp for a more even cut.
  • Check your progress frequently using the handheld mirror.
  • Ask a friend or family member for help if you're having trouble reaching the back of your head.

FAQ

Q: How short should I cut my hair?

A: The length of your hair is a personal preference, but most buzz cuts are between 1/16 and 1/4 inch.

Q: Can I buzz cut my hair without a guard?

A: Yes, but be very careful not to cut your scalp. It's best to use a guard to avoid any accidents.

Q: How often should I buzz cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Most people buzz their hair every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care

After you've buzzed your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Protect your scalp from the sun by wearing a hat or using a sunscreen spray.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a perfect buzz cut every time. Remember to take your time and be careful, and you'll have a great-looking haircut in no time.
