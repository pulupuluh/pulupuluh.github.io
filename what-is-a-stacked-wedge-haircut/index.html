What Is A Stacked Wedge Haircut

Written by Aprilia Apr 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for a change? A stacked wedge haircut might be just what you need! This trendy hairstyle has been around for decades and is still popular today. In this article, we will define what a stacked wedge haircut is, how to achieve it, and provide tips and haircare advice to keep your locks looking fabulous.

Table of Contents

  2. How To Achieve a Stacked Wedge Haircut
  3. Step-By-Step Guide
  4. Tips for a Perfect Stacked Wedge Haircut
  5. FAQs
  6. Haircare Tips for Stacked Wedge Haircut

Definition

A stacked wedge haircut is a hairstyle that features layers of hair that gradually increase in length from the nape of the neck to the crown of the head. The hair is cut at an angle, creating a "stacked" effect. The back of the hair is shorter than the front, with the hair gradually getting longer towards the top of the head. This creates a wedge shape that frames the face and accentuates the cheekbones.

Stacked wedge haircuts are popular among women of all ages and hair types. This versatile haircut can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style, making it a great choice for anyone looking for a new look.

How To Achieve a Stacked Wedge Haircut

If you're interested in getting a stacked wedge haircut, the first step is to find a hairstylist who is experienced in this technique. It's important to find someone who understands your hair type and can work with you to create the perfect haircut.

During your consultation, you can discuss the length and angle of the cut, as well as any other styling preferences. Your hairstylist will then create the cut using scissors and/or a razor to create the desired effect.

Step-By-Step Guide

If you're interested in trying a stacked wedge haircut at home, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight, using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  3. Using a comb, section off the hair at the nape of your neck.
  4. Cut the hair at an angle, starting with the shortest layer at the back and gradually increasing the length towards the crown of your head.
  5. Repeat this process with the remaining sections of hair, working your way up towards the top of your head.
  6. Once all the hair has been cut, use a razor to add texture and movement to the ends.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for a Perfect Stacked Wedge Haircut

To get the most out of your stacked wedge haircut, follow these tips:

  • Choose a hairstylist who specializes in this technique.
  • Bring pictures of the style you want to your consultation.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing the angle of the cut.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and texture.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add definition to your layers.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQs

How often do I need to get my stacked wedge haircut trimmed?

You should get your haircut trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

What face shapes work best with a stacked wedge haircut?

A stacked wedge haircut works best on oval, heart, and square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, consider a modified version of the cut that is longer in the front.

Can I style my stacked wedge haircut in different ways?

Yes! A stacked wedge haircut is versatile and can be styled in many ways, including straight, wavy, curly, and updos.

Haircare Tips for Stacked Wedge Haircut

To keep your stacked wedge haircut looking its best, follow these haircare tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

Now that you know what a stacked wedge haircut is and how to achieve it, it's time to book your appointment with a hairstylist! Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or a subtle update, a stacked wedge haircut is a chic and stylish choice for any occasion.
