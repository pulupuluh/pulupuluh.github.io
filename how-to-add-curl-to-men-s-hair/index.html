How To Add Curl To Men s Hair

Written by Daniel Apr 05, 2023 · 8 min read

Curly hair has been trending for quite some time now, and it’s not just women who are sporting those luscious curls. Men too can add some bounce to their hair and look stylish. If you’re wondering how to add curl to men’s hair, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we will be sharing some easy tips and tricks to add curls to your hair.

Description

Adding curls to men’s hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the desired results. There are many ways to add curl to your hair, and we will be discussing some of the most popular and effective methods in this article. Whether you have short hair or long hair, we’ve got you covered.

How to Add Curl to Men’s Hair

1. Using a Curling Iron

If you have straight hair and want to add some curls, a curling iron is your best bet. Here’s how you can use a curling iron:

Step 1: Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it.

Step 2: Heat up your curling iron and apply some heat protectant spray to your hair.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, starting from the root and moving towards the tip.

Step 4: Hold the hair in the curling iron for a few seconds and release it.

Step 5: Repeat the process for the rest of your hair.

Pro Tip: Use a hairspray to set the curls and make them last longer.

2. Using Rollers

If you want to add some curls to your hair without using heat, rollers are a great option. Here’s how you can use rollers:

Step 1: Wash your hair and apply some styling mousse to it.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a roller, starting from the tip and moving towards the root.

Step 3: Secure the roller with a clip or a bobby pin.

Step 4: Repeat the process for the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Leave the rollers in your hair for a few hours or overnight.

Pro Tip: Apply some hairspray to set the curls and make them last longer.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to add curls to your hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair and blow-dry it.

Step 2: Decide on the method you want to use to add curls to your hair.

Step 3: If you’re using a curling iron, heat it up and apply some heat protectant spray to your hair.

Step 4: If you’re using rollers, apply some styling mousse to your hair.

Step 5: Take a small section of hair and use the chosen method to add curls to it.

Step 6: Repeat the process for the rest of your hair.

Step 7: Apply some hairspray to set the curls and make them last longer.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while adding curls to your hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • Apply some styling mousse to your hair before using rollers to add curls.
  • Use a hairspray to set the curls and make them last longer.
  • If you’re using a curling iron, let the hair cool down before touching it to avoid damaging the curls.

FAQs

Q. Can I add curls to my short hair?

A. Yes, you can add curls to short hair using a curling iron or rollers.

Q. How long will the curls last?

A. The curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on the method you used and the products you applied.

Q. Can I add curls to my hair if it’s naturally curly?

A. Yes, you can enhance the curls using the methods mentioned in this article.

Hair Care

Adding curls to your hair can be fun, but it’s important to take care of your hair to avoid damage. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage the curls.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

That’s it! Now you know how to add curl to men’s hair. Try these methods and see which one works best for you. Don’t be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
