How To Trim Mens Long Hair With Clippers

Written by Luffy Jul 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Long hair has been in fashion for men for quite some time now. But maintaining it can be a bit of a hassle, especially when it comes to trimming. Trimming long hair can be tricky, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done easily. In this article, we’ll guide you on how to trim mens long hair with clippers.

Description

Trimming long hair with clippers is a cost-effective and practical way to maintain your hair. Clippers are easy to use, and you can do it on your own without any help. It is also ideal for those who are always on the go and don't have the time to visit a salon regularly.

How to Trim Mens Long Hair with Clippers

Before starting, make sure that you have the right tools, including a good quality clipper, scissors, comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps on how to trim mens long hair with clippers:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair and make sure it is dry before cutting. Wet hair is difficult to cut, and it can also make the clipper blades dull. Comb your hair to remove any tangles, and then decide on the length you want to trim.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Choose the right clipper guard depending on the length you want. The clipper guard numbers determine the length of your hair. The higher the number, the longer the hair will be. Start with a longer guard to avoid cutting too much hair.

Step 3: Start Trimming

Start trimming your hair using the clipper. Use long, smooth strokes while holding the clipper in a downward direction. Avoid going against the grain, as this can cause uneven cuts. Start from the back of the head and move towards the front. Use the comb to keep your hair in place while trimming.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

After trimming, use the scissors to blend your hair. Cut the hair that was missed by the clipper and blend it with the rest of the hair. This will give a more natural look to your hair.

Step 5: Clean Up

After trimming, clean up any stray hair using a brush or a towel. Take a shower to remove any hair that may have fallen on your body. Clean the clippers and store them properly for future use.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while trimming your long hair with clippers:

  • Choose a quiet clipper to avoid disturbing others while trimming.
  • Trim your hair in a well-lit area to avoid any accidents.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while trimming.
  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.

FAQ

Can I trim my long hair with scissors?

Yes, you can. But using clippers is more efficient and practical, especially if you have thick hair.

How often should I trim my long hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, you should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length.

Can I trim my long hair on my own?

Yes, you can. But if you are not confident, it is best to seek help from a professional.

Hair Care

Trimming your hair regularly is essential for maintaining healthy hair. It helps get rid of split ends and promotes hair growth. Along with trimming, make sure to take good care of your hair by using a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and protect it from the sun.

With these tips and steps, you can easily trim your long hair with clippers. It’s a cost-effective and practical way to maintain your hair without visiting the salon regularly. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
