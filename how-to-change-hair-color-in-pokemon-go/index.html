How To Change Hair Color In Pokemon Go A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Feb 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your character's same old hair color in Pokemon Go? Want to switch things up and stand out from the crowd? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to change hair color in Pokemon Go in just a few simple steps. So, let's get started!

How to Change Hair Color in Pokemon Go

Before we dive into the steps, it's important to note that changing your hair color in Pokemon Go requires a special item called a "Hair Dye." You can purchase this item in the in-game shop for PokeCoins. Once you have the Hair Dye, follow these steps:

Step 1: Open the Character Customization Menu

First, open the Pokemon Go app and tap on your character's icon in the bottom left corner of the screen. From there, tap on the "Style" button to access the Character Customization menu.

Step 2: Select "Hair Style"

In the Character Customization menu, select the "Hair Style" option.

Step 3: Choose Your New Hair Color

Once you're in the Hair Style menu, scroll down until you see the "Hair Dye" section. Tap on this section to access the different Hair Dye colors available. Choose the color you want and tap "Select."

Step 4: Apply Your New Hair Color

After selecting your new Hair Dye color, tap on the "Apply" button to make the change. Voila! Your character now has a brand new hair color.

Tips for Changing Hair Color in Pokemon Go

Now that you know how to change your hair color in Pokemon Go, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Make sure you have enough PokeCoins to purchase the Hair Dye item.
  • Experiment with different hair colors to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't forget that you can also change your character's outfit, accessories, and more in the Character Customization menu.

FAQs

Can I change my hair color without a Hair Dye item?

No, the only way to change your hair color in Pokemon Go is by using a Hair Dye item.

Do I have to buy a new Hair Dye item every time I want to change my hair color?

Yes, you'll need to purchase a new Hair Dye item each time you want to change your hair color.

Can I change my hair color to any color I want?

No, you can only choose from the Hair Dye colors available in the game.

Hair Care for Your Pokemon Go Character

Now that you've changed your character's hair color, it's important to take care of it! Here are a few hair care tips for your Pokemon Go character:

  • Make sure to wash your character's hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your character's hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your character's hair to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil to nourish your character's hair.

And there you have it! With these simple steps, you can change your hair color in Pokemon Go and give your character a fresh new look. Happy playing!
