What Haircut Is Best For Thick Long Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick long hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it is gorgeous and enviable, it can also be challenging to manage and style. The right haircut can make all the difference in making thick long hair more manageable and enhancing its natural beauty. In this article, we will explore what haircut is best for thick long hair, how to achieve it, and tips for maintaining it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The best haircut for thick long hair is one that adds movement, shape, and dimension while maintaining its length and volume. Layers are a popular choice for thick long hair as they add texture and depth without sacrificing length. A layered haircut can also make thick long hair more manageable by reducing its weight and creating more movement.

Another option for thick long hair is the blunt cut. This haircut creates a sleek and polished look by maintaining the same length throughout the hair. It is an excellent choice for those who want to showcase their thick hair's fullness and volume.

How to

The first step in achieving the best haircut for thick long hair is to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the best style for your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle. Once you have decided on a haircut, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Ask your stylist to use thinning shears or a razor to create texture and reduce weight.
  • Consider adding layers to add movement and dimension.
  • For a blunt cut, ask your stylist to use a razor or point-cutting technique to create softness and texture.
  • Ensure that the length of the haircut complements your face shape and features.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the best haircut for thick long hair:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best style for your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle.
  2. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  3. Discuss the length and style of the haircut with your stylist.
  4. If adding layers, ask your stylist to use thinning shears or a razor to create texture and reduce weight.
  5. If opting for a blunt cut, ask your stylist to use a razor or point-cutting technique to create softness and texture.
  6. Ensure that the length of the haircut complements your face shape and features.
  7. Style your hair according to your stylist's recommendations.

Tips

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to enhance your hair's natural volume.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • For added texture, use a sea salt spray or texturizing spray.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

FAQ

What face shapes are best suited for a layered haircut?

A layered haircut can complement most face shapes, but it is particularly flattering for those with oval or round faces. It can help elongate the face and create more balance.

Can I still wear my hair up with a blunt cut?

Absolutely! A blunt cut can be versatile and styled in various ways. You can wear it up in a ponytail, bun, or braid.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is crucial, especially for those with thick long hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top shape:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools daily and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Deep condition your hair weekly to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know what haircut is best for thick long hair, it's time to book that appointment and give your hair the love and attention it deserves. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can showcase your gorgeous locks and feel confident every day.
