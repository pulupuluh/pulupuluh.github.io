What Kind Of Hair Is Used For Extensions

Written by William Jul 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Extensions are the perfect way to add length and volume to your hair. If you're looking to get extensions, you may wonder what kind of hair is used for extensions. In this article, we'll discuss the different types of hair that are used for extensions, how to choose the right type of hair for extensions, and how to take care of your hair extensions.

Description

Hair extensions are made from either synthetic or natural human hair. Synthetic hair is made from synthetic fibers, while natural human hair is harvested from real human hair. Natural human hair is the preferred choice for hair extensions because it looks and feels more like your own hair. It's also easier to style and maintain than synthetic hair.

How to Choose the Right Type of Hair for Extensions

When selecting the right type of hair for extensions, you need to consider the texture, color, and length of your natural hair. You want to choose hair that is as close to your natural hair as possible. This will ensure that your extensions blend seamlessly with your natural hair.

Texture

When it comes to texture, there are several options to choose from. You can choose hair that is straight, wavy, curly, or kinky. It's important to choose the texture that matches your natural hair.

Color

The color of your hair extensions should match your natural hair color. If you have highlights or lowlights, you can choose hair extensions that have the same highlights or lowlights. If you're unsure of what color to choose, you can always consult with a professional stylist.

Length

The length of your hair extensions should also match the length of your natural hair. If you have short hair, you can choose shorter extensions. If you have long hair, you can choose longer extensions.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when getting hair extensions:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the right type of hair extensions for you.
  2. Select the type of hair extensions you want.
  3. Have the hair extensions installed by a professional stylist.
  4. Style and maintain your hair extensions.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair extensions:

  • Brush your hair extensions regularly to prevent tangling.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair extensions.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair extensions.
  • Wash your hair extensions with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair extensions.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair extensions:

Q: How long do hair extensions last?

A: Hair extensions can last up to six months with proper care and maintenance.

Q: Can I color my hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can color your hair extensions. However, it's best to consult with a professional stylist to ensure that the color is done correctly.

Q: Can I swim with hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can swim with hair extensions. However, it's important to rinse your hair after swimming to remove any chlorine or salt water.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair extensions. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair extensions every two to three days with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Condition your hair extensions to keep them soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair extensions.
  • Brush your hair extensions gently to prevent tangling.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair extensions.

Now that you know what kind of hair is used for extensions, you can make an informed decision when choosing the right type of hair for your extensions. Remember to consult with a professional stylist and follow proper hair care practices to keep your extensions looking their best.
