Women s Hair Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Aug 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a bold and edgy new hairstyle? Look no further than the women's hair short sides long top look. This trendy style has been taking the fashion world by storm, and it's not hard to see why. With its combination of shaved sides and long, voluminous top, this look is both stylish and practical. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look, provide tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Achieve Women's Hair Short Sides Long Top
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Women's Hair Short Sides Long Top
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Achieve Women's Hair Short Sides Long Top

The first step in achieving this look is finding a stylist who is experienced in creating it. You'll want to bring in photos of the look you're going for to make sure you're both on the same page.

Next, you'll need to decide on the length of the shaved sides. Many women opt for a short buzz cut, but you can also choose a longer length if you prefer. The length of the top will depend on your personal preference and face shape, but generally, it should be long enough to style and add volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by sectioning off the hair on the top of your head.

2. Use clippers to shave the sides of your head to your desired length.

3. Use scissors or a razor to blend the shaved sides with the longer hair on top.

4. Style the top of your hair using your preferred method. Some popular styles for women's hair short sides long top include slicked-back, tousled, and braided.

Tips for Maintaining Your Women's Hair Short Sides Long Top

1. Keep your hair trimmed regularly to maintain the shape of the cut.

2. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

3. Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and desired look.

4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will this style work for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style can work for all hair types. However, it's important to work with a stylist who can tailor the cut to your specific hair type and texture.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair trimmed?

A: You should plan on getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the cut.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this style. Experiment with different updo styles that work with the length of the top of your hair.

Hair Care Tips

1. Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat tools.

2. Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

4. Consider investing in a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage while you sleep.

Now that you know how to achieve women's hair short sides long top, it's time to book your appointment with a skilled stylist and rock this edgy and fashionable look. With proper maintenance and care, your hair will look stunning and head-turning for a long time to come.
