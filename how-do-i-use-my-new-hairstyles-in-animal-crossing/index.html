How Do I Use My New Hairstyles In Animal Crossing

Written by Oliver Sep 13, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to the ultimate guide on how to use your new hairstyles in Animal Crossing! Whether you've just unlocked a new hairstyle or you're looking for ways to switch up your look, this guide has got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description

Animal Crossing is a popular life simulation game that has taken the world by storm. One of the many things that make this game so much fun is the ability to customize your character's appearance, including their hairstyle. With so many different hairstyles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about using your new hairstyles in Animal Crossing.

How To

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a simple process. First, you'll need to unlock new hairstyles by purchasing them from the Nook Stop terminal in Resident Services. Once you have a new hairstyle, head to a mirror in your house or visit Harriet at Shampoodle to change your hairstyle.

To change your hairstyle at a mirror, simply stand in front of it and press A. Choose "Change Look" and then "Haircut." You'll be able to choose from any of the hairstyles you've unlocked.

If you'd rather visit Harriet at Shampoodle, she'll be able to offer you more customization options, including hair color and eye color. Keep in mind that Harriet will only offer these options once you've changed your hairstyle at least once.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

  1. Unlock a new hairstyle by purchasing it from the Nook Stop terminal in Resident Services.
  2. Head to a mirror in your house or visit Harriet at Shampoodle.
  3. Stand in front of the mirror and press A.
  4. Choose "Change Look" and then "Haircut."
  5. Choose the hairstyle you want to use.
  6. If you're at Shampoodle, choose any additional customization options you want, such as hair color or eye color.
  7. Confirm your new look and enjoy your new hairstyle!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using your new hairstyles in Animal Crossing:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that best suits your character.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match different hairstyles with different outfits.
  • Consider using accessories like hats or headbands to complement your hairstyle.
  • Remember that your hairstyle can be changed at any time, so don't stress too much about making the "perfect" choice.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using new hairstyles in Animal Crossing:

How many hairstyles are in Animal Crossing?

There are currently over 70 different hairstyles in Animal Crossing, so there's plenty to choose from!

Can I change my hair color in Animal Crossing?

Yes, you can change your hair color by visiting Harriet at Shampoodle.

Do I need to pay to change my hairstyle?

Yes, you'll need to purchase new hairstyles from the Nook Stop terminal in Resident Services. The price varies depending on the hairstyle.

Hair Care

While your character's hair may not need to be washed or styled, it's still important to take care of it! Here are some tips for keeping your character's hair looking its best:

  • Avoid wearing hats or headbands for extended periods of time, as this can cause "hat hair."
  • Consider using a hair accessory like a flower or bow to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.
  • Change your hairstyle regularly to prevent your character's hair from looking dull or lifeless.
  • Have fun with your character's appearance and don't be afraid to try new things!

Congratulations, you now know everything there is to know about using your new hairstyles in Animal Crossing! Happy styling!
