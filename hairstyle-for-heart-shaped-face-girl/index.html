Hairstyle For Heart Shaped Face Girl

Written by Oliver Aug 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are a heart shaped face girl, you might have struggled to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for heart shaped face girls that will make you look stunning.

Description

Heart shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the wide forehead and narrow chin. You want to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones while minimizing the width of your forehead.

How to

Here are some tips on how to choose the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face:

  1. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head. This will only make your forehead look wider. Instead, choose hairstyles that add volume to the bottom half of your hair.
  2. Side-swept bangs are perfect for heart shaped faces. They draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones while minimizing the width of your forehead.
  3. Long hairstyles work well for heart shaped faces. They help to balance out the narrow chin and elongate the face.
  4. Avoid short hairstyles that end at the chin. They will only accentuate the narrowness of your chin. Instead, go for longer hairstyles that end at the collarbone or below.

Step by Step

Here are some step by step instructions on how to style your hair for a heart shaped face:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush. This will add volume to the bottom half of your hair.
  2. If you have long hair, consider adding layers. This will add movement and dimension to your hair.
  3. If you have short hair, consider adding texture. This will help to balance out the narrowness of your chin.
  4. Part your hair to the side and add side-swept bangs. This will draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.
  5. Finish off your look with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your heart shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or severe. They will only make your face look harsh.
  • Consider adding highlights to your hair. They will help to brighten up your face and draw attention to your best features.
  • If you want to wear your hair up, consider a loose, messy bun or ponytail. This will add volume to the bottom half of your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for heart shaped face girls:

Q: Can heart shaped face girls wear short hair?

A: Yes, heart shaped face girls can wear short hair. However, it's important to choose a style that balances out the narrowness of your chin.

Q: Can heart shaped face girls wear bangs?

A: Yes, heart shaped face girls can wear bangs. Side-swept bangs are the best option as they draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Q: What hairstyle should heart shaped face girls avoid?

A: Heart shaped face girls should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of their head. This will only make their forehead look wider.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry or oily.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can make the most of your heart shaped face and look stunning every day!
