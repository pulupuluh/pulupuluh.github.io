Best Short Haircuts For Thick Coarse Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Jun 27, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thick, coarse, and wavy hair, you know how difficult it can be to find a haircut that suits your hair type. Fortunately, there are many short haircuts that can flatter your hair and make it more manageable. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for thick coarse wavy hair.

Description

Thick coarse wavy hair can be difficult to maintain, but with the right haircut, you can make it work. Short haircuts are a great option for people with thick hair because they can make it look more manageable and less frizzy. With the right cut, you can also make your waves look more defined and less messy.

How to

When selecting a short haircut for thick coarse wavy hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Your hairstylist can help you choose a style that flatters your features and suits your hair type. Here are some of the best short haircuts for thick coarse wavy hair:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for thick coarse wavy hair. This cut is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. It's a great option for people who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish. The pixie cut can be styled in many different ways, including slicked back, tousled, or textured.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great option for people with thick coarse wavy hair. This cut is short to medium-length and is typically styled straight or with loose waves. The bob cut can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, making it a versatile option for many people.

3. Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered haircut that works well for thick coarse wavy hair. This cut is ideal for people who want a messy, textured look. The shag cut can be styled with loose waves or left natural for a more effortless look.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when getting a short haircut for thick coarse wavy hair:

1. Research different short haircuts and find one that suits your face shape and hair texture.

2. Book an appointment with a hairstylist who has experience working with thick coarse wavy hair.

3. Discuss your hair goals with your hairstylist and ask for their professional opinion on which haircut would be best for you.

4. Sit back and relax while your hairstylist works their magic.

5. Follow your hairstylist's recommendations for styling and maintaining your new haircut.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut for thick coarse wavy hair:

- Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and less frizzy.

- Invest in a good quality hair dryer and diffuser to help define your waves.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet, and avoid brushing it when it's dry.

FAQ

Q: Can I still style my short haircut if I don't have time to blow dry it?

A: Yes, you can still style your short haircut even if you don't have time to blow dry it. You can use a texturizing spray or mousse to define your waves and give your hair some volume.

Q: How often do I need to get my short haircut trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for people with thick coarse wavy hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.

- Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can make your thick coarse wavy hair more manageable and stylish. Experiment with different short haircuts to find the one that suits your face shape and hair texture best.
