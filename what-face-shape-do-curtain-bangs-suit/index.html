What Face Shape Do Curtain Bangs Suit A Complete Guide

Are you thinking of getting curtain bangs but wondering if they will suit your face shape? Curtain bangs are a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can suit various face shapes. In this article, we will guide you through what face shape do curtain bangs suit and give you tips on how to style and maintain your bangs.

What are Curtain Bangs?

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that is parted down the middle, framing the face on either side. They are called "curtain" bangs because they resemble the drapes that hang on either side of a window. Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that first became popular in the 1960s and 70s, and have recently made a comeback.

What Face Shape Do Curtain Bangs Suit?

Curtain bangs can suit various face shapes, but they are particularly flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces. Oval faces have balanced proportions, which means that they can pull off most hairstyles, including curtain bangs. Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrower chin, which makes curtain bangs a great option to balance out the face shape.

If you have a round face, curtain bangs can also work for you, provided they are cut in a way that complements your face shape. For example, instead of having straight-across bangs, you can have longer, side-swept bangs that elongate your face.

If you have a square face, curtain bangs can soften your features and add some femininity to your look. The key is to have bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle, which will create an illusion of a narrower forehead.

For those with a diamond-shaped face, curtain bangs can also work, especially if you have a wider forehead and a more pointed chin. Again, the key is to have bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle, which will balance out your face shape.

How to Style Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are versatile and can be styled in different ways, depending on your preference. Here are some styling ideas:

  • Straight and sleek: Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs and keep them sleek.
  • Wavy: Use a curling wand to create loose waves in your bangs.
  • Pin-up: Pin your bangs to the side with bobby pins for a cute and vintage-inspired look.
  • Half-up: Pull back the top section of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip, leaving your bangs loose.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cut Curtain Bangs

If you want to cut your own curtain bangs, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle, using the arch of your eyebrows as a guide.
  3. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape, with the base of the triangle at your hairline.
  4. Comb your hair forward, and cut your bangs in a straight line, following the triangle shape.
  5. Start with longer bangs, and gradually cut more if you want them shorter.
  6. Repeat on the other side.
  7. Use scissors to cut any uneven pieces.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips for maintaining your curtain bangs:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the desired length.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting greasy between washes.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them oily.
  • Use a small amount of hair oil to keep your bangs smooth and frizz-free.

FAQ about Curtain Bangs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

How long do curtain bangs take to grow out?

Curtain bangs can take around six months to grow out, depending on how fast your hair grows. In the meantime, you can style your bangs in different ways or use hair accessories to keep them out of your face.

Can you style curtain bangs if you have curly hair?

Yes, you can style curtain bangs if you have curly hair. You can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls, or use a curling wand to create loose waves in your bangs.

Can you wear glasses with curtain bangs?

Yes, you can wear glasses with curtain bangs. Make sure your bangs are trimmed in a way that doesn't interfere with your glasses, and choose glasses that complement your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Here are some general hair care tips for curtain bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron or curling wand.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, curtain bangs can suit various face shapes, including oval, heart-shaped, round, square, and diamond-shaped faces. They are versatile and can be styled in different ways, depending on your preference. If you're considering getting curtain bangs, make sure to consult with your hairstylist and choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture. With proper styling and maintenance, curtain bangs can be a fun and trendy hairstyle that you'll love.
