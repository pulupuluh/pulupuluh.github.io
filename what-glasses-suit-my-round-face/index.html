What Glasses Suit My Round Face

Are you someone with a round face and always find it difficult to choose the perfect glasses? Don't worry; you're not alone. It's a common problem faced by many. But, with the right guidance and tips, you can choose the perfect glasses that suit your round face.

Description

A round face is characterized by its curved lines, and it's nearly as wide as it is long. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the jawline is soft and rounded. The goal of choosing glasses for a round face is to find a frame that adds angles and balances the face's proportions.

How to Choose Glasses for a Round Face

When choosing glasses for a round face, the main goal is to add angles and balance out the face's proportions. Angular frames with sharp edges can help add definition to your face and reduce the roundness. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Determine your face's widest point.
  3. Measure the length of your face.
  4. Choose a frame that is wider than your face's widest point.
  5. Choose frames with sharp angles and edges.
  6. Avoid round or oval frames as they will only accentuate the roundness of your face.
  7. Choose frames with high temples to add length to your face.
  8. Choose frames with a bridge that sits high on your nose to make your face look longer.
  9. Consider frames with bold colors or patterns to add interest to your face.
  10. Try on different frames to see which one suits your face best.

Tips to Keep in Mind

When choosing glasses for a round face, keep these tips in mind:

  • Choose frames that are wider than your face's widest point.
  • Avoid round or oval frames.
  • Choose frames with sharp angles and edges.
  • Choose frames with high temples to add length to your face.
  • Consider frames with bold colors or patterns to add interest to your face.
  • Try on different frames to see which one suits your face best.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about glasses for round faces:

What are the best frame materials for round faces?

The best frame materials for round faces are acetate, titanium, and metal. These materials are lightweight, durable, and offer a lot of flexibility in terms of shape and design.

Can I wear oversized frames with a round face?

Yes, you can wear oversized frames with a round face. However, make sure that the frames are not too large and that they have some angles and sharp edges to add definition to your face.

What are the worst frame shapes for round faces?

The worst frame shapes for round faces are round and oval frames. These frames will only accentuate the roundness of your face and make it look even more circular.

Hair Care Tips to Complement Your Glasses

Choosing the right glasses is only half the battle. You also need to make sure that your hair complements your frames. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height to your face to balance out the roundness.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider getting a side-swept fringe to add angles to your face.
  • Choose a hairstyle that doesn't cover your eyebrows, as they can help add length to your face.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add interest to your hair.

Choosing the perfect glasses for a round face is all about creating balance and adding definition. By following these tips, you can find the perfect frames that suit your face shape and complement your style.
