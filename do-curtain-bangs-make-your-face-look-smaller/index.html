Do Curtain Bangs Make Your Face Look Smaller

Written by William May 20, 2023 · 5 min read
Do Curtain Bangs Make Your Face Look Smaller? - Tips and Hair Care

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that has been around for years. They are a versatile style that can be worn by people of all ages and hair types. Curtain bangs are face-framing layers that are parted in the center, creating a curtain-like effect. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some dimension to their hair and create a more youthful look. But the question is, do curtain bangs make your face look smaller?

How to Get Curtain Bangs

Getting curtain bangs is easy and can be done at home or at a salon. If you want to do it yourself, you will need a pair of sharp scissors and a comb. It is important to remember that cutting your own hair can be tricky, so it is always best to seek professional help if you are unsure.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by parting your hair in the center.

2. Take a small section of hair from the middle of your forehead.

3. Hold the hair between your fingers and comb it straight down.

4. Use your scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. It is best to start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.

5. Repeat on the other side.

6. Style your curtain bangs by using a round brush and blow dryer to create a natural wave.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs

1. Use a volumizing shampoo to add thickness to your hair.

2. Blow dry your hair upside down to add more volume.

3. Use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your curtain bangs.

4. Use a round brush to create a natural wave in your bangs.

5. Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do curtain bangs make your face look smaller?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can make your face look smaller by framing your face and drawing attention to your eyes.

Q: Are curtain bangs suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can be worn by people with all hair types, but it is important to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your hair.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

It is important to take care of your hair to keep your curtain bangs looking great. Here are some tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading and damage.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

3. Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

4. Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
