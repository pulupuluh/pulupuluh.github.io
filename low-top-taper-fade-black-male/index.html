Low Top Taper Fade Black Male A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Robby May 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a black male looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, then the low top taper fade might just be the perfect hairstyle for you. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about the low top taper fade, including how to achieve the perfect look, tips for maintenance and care, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

The low top taper fade is a popular hairstyle among black men. It involves a gradual tapering of the hair on the sides and back of the head, which then blends into a short cut on top. The fade can be low, medium or high, depending on your preference. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

How to Achieve the Low Top Taper Fade Look

Here are the steps to achieve the perfect low top taper fade:

Step 1: Choose your desired length

Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. This can be anywhere between a buzz cut and a few inches long.

Step 2: Cut the top of your hair

Use clippers or scissors to cut the top of your hair to your desired length.

Step 3: Start the fade

Begin by using clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard as you move towards the bottom.

Step 4: Blend the fade

Use a comb and clippers to blend the fade so that it looks natural and seamless.

Step 5: Clean up the edges

Use a razor or trimmer to clean up the edges around your hairline and sideburns.

Step 6: Style your hair

Use a styling product to achieve your desired look on the top of your hair.

Tips for Low Top Taper Fade Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining and caring for your low top taper fade:

- Keep your hair clean and moisturized with a good shampoo and conditioner. - Use a styling product that works well with your hair type. - Schedule regular appointments with your barber to maintain the shape of your fade. - Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can lead to dryness and breakage. - Protect your hair from the sun and heat damage by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

FAQs About Low Top Taper Fade Black Male

Q: How often should I get a low top taper fade?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your fade.

Q: Is the low top taper fade suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the low top taper fade can work well with all face shapes, but it's important to work with your barber to find the right length and shape for your specific face shape.

Q: Can I style my hair differently with a low top taper fade?

A: Yes, there are many different styling options for the top of your hair with a low top taper fade, including slicked back, curly, or messy styles.

Conclusion

The low top taper fade is a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that can work well for black men. By following the steps for achieving the look, taking care of your hair, and working with your barber, you can achieve the perfect low top taper fade that suits your style and preferences.
