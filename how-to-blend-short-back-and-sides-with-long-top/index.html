How to Blend Short Back and Sides with Long Top

The short back and sides with a long top hairstyle is a classic look that can be customized to suit your personal style. This hairstyle is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. The key to achieving a seamless blend between the two lengths is to use the right cutting technique and tools.

What You Will Need

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hairbrush

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect short back and sides with a long top haircut:

Step 1: Decide on Your Desired Lengths

Before you start cutting your hair, decide on the length you want for the sides and the top. The sides are usually cut to a short length, while the top can be left longer. The exact lengths will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair, and comb it to remove any tangles. Apply some hair gel or pomade to the top of your hair to make it easier to work with.

Step 3: Start Cutting the Sides

Use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to blend the lengths. Make sure to blend the hairline at the nape of the neck and around the ears.

Step 4: Blend the Top with Scissors

Use scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head. Start by cutting the front section to the desired length, and then work your way back. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle to blend it with the shorter sides.

Step 5: Clean Up the Edges

Use clippers or scissors to clean up the edges around the hairline and ears. Make sure to blend the hair evenly for a seamless look.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Apply some hair gel or pomade to the top of your hair, and use a hairbrush to style it into the desired shape. You can choose to comb it to the side, slick it back, or leave it messy for a more casual look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your short back and sides with a long top hairstyle looking fresh, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup of product.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I style my short back and sides with a long top haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can style your hair in different ways depending on your personal preference and the occasion. You can comb it to the side for a classic look, slick it back for a more formal look, or leave it messy for a casual look.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

What kind of product should I use to style my hair?

You should use a high-quality hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place and add shine. Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve a flawless short back and sides with a long top haircut. This versatile hairstyle can be customized to suit your personal style and can give you a polished and trendy look. Experiment with different styles and products to find the one that works best for you.
