How To Cut Guys Hair Medium Length A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to give your male clients a stylish haircut that's easy to maintain? A medium length cut might be the perfect option. Not too short, not too long, this versatile cut can work for a variety of hair types and face shapes. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut guys hair medium length in simple steps.

Description

Medium length haircuts for men are a popular choice because they offer a balance between short and long hair. This length can be styled in many ways, from slicked back to messy, and can flatter different face shapes. If you're new to cutting hair, this guide will help you get started with the basics.

How to Cut Guys Hair Medium Length

Before you begin, you'll need to gather some tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel
  • Clip or hair tie

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry it. Use a blow dryer to dry the hair completely.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section the hair by parting it down the middle and then from ear to ear. Clip or tie the top section away from the bottom section.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Section

Using clippers, cut the hair on the bottom section to the desired length. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up, using a comb to guide the clippers.

Step 4: Blend the Top Section

Unclip or untie the top section of hair. Use scissors to blend the top section with the bottom section, creating a seamless transition. Start at the back and work your way forward, cutting small sections at a time.

Step 5: Create Texture

To create texture and movement, use scissors or a razor to add layers and texture to the hair. This will help the hair look more natural and less flat.

Step 6: Finish the Cut

Use scissors to trim any stray hairs and make sure the cut is even. Check the length from different angles and make any final adjustments.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting medium length hair:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and scissors for a more precise cut.
  • Don't cut too close to the scalp to avoid razor burn and irritation.
  • Take breaks and step back to assess the cut from different angles.

FAQ

1. Can I cut medium length hair with just scissors?

Yes, you can cut medium length hair with just scissors. However, using clippers can help you achieve a more precise and even cut.

2. How often should I cut medium length hair?

Medium length hair can be cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

After the cut, advise your client on how to care for their hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for their hair type.
  • Apply a styling product to create the desired look.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.

Cutting guys hair medium length doesn't have to be difficult. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a stylish and easy-to-maintain haircut for your clients. Use this guide to get started and experiment with different styles and textures.
