How To Pick Perfect Hair Color A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline May 23, 2023 · 10 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to pick the perfect hair color for you! Whether you're looking to switch up your look or just enhance your natural hair color, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll take you through the steps of finding the perfect hair color for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. We'll also answer some common questions about hair color and provide tips on how to care for colored hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Determining Your Skin Tone
  2. Matching Your Eye Color
  3. Considering Your Personal Style
  4. Taking Your Hair Type into Account
  5. Exploring Color Options
  6. Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair
  7. FAQs

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding your perfect hair color is determining your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of both warm and cool undertones.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If they appear blue or purple, you likely have cool undertones. If they appear both green and blue, you likely have neutral undertones.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can use it to guide your hair color choice. Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors, such as golden blondes, copper reds, and chocolate browns. Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors, such as ash blondes, burgundy reds, and jet blacks. Neutral skin tones can pull off both warm and cool hair colors.

Matching Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining your perfect hair color. The right hair color can enhance the natural beauty of your eyes and make them stand out even more.

Blue eyes look best with cool hair colors, such as platinum blondes, ashy browns, and jet blacks. Green eyes look best with warm hair colors, such as honey blondes, auburn reds, and warm browns. Brown eyes can pull off a variety of hair colors, from warm to cool.

Considering Your Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when choosing a hair color. Do you prefer a natural look, or do you like to make a statement with your hair? Are you willing to spend a lot of time and money on upkeep, or do you prefer a low-maintenance style?

If you prefer a natural look, consider a hair color that is close to your natural color, with subtle highlights or lowlights for dimension. If you like to make a statement, consider a bold color, such as bright red or platinum blonde. Keep in mind that these colors may require more upkeep, such as frequent touch-ups and deep conditioning treatments.

Taking Your Hair Type into Account

Your hair type can also affect how a hair color looks on you. For example, fine hair may look better with a lighter hair color, while thick hair may look better with a darker hair color.

If you have curly hair, keep in mind that the curls may make the hair color appear lighter or darker than it actually is. Additionally, curly hair is often more prone to dryness and damage, so be sure to choose a hair color that won't exacerbate these issues.

Exploring Color Options

When it comes to hair color, the options are endless. From natural shades to bold hues, there's a color out there for everyone.

If you're looking for a natural look, consider shades such as honey blonde, chestnut brown, or auburn red. These colors will enhance your natural beauty without looking too drastic.

If you're looking to make a statement, consider bold colors such as platinum blonde, bright red, or even pastel hues like lavender or pink. Keep in mind that these colors may require more upkeep, so be prepared to invest time and money in maintaining them.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your colored hair:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as these can cause damage and fading.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

FAQs

What hair color looks best on fair skin?

Fair skin looks best with cool hair colors, such as platinum blonde, ashy brown, and burgundy red.

What hair color looks best on dark skin?

Dark skin looks best with warm hair colors, such as golden blonde, copper red, and chocolate brown.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but keep in mind that it can be tricky to get the color just right. Consider consulting with a professional hair stylist before attempting to dye your hair at home.

How often should I touch up my hair color?

This depends on the type of hair color you have and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you should touch up your hair color every 4-6 weeks.

Can I color my hair if it's damaged?

It's best to avoid coloring your hair if it's severely damaged, as this can exacerbate the damage. However, if your hair is only slightly damaged, you may be able to color it with a gentle, nourishing hair color.

We hope this guide has helped you find the perfect hair color for you! Remember to take your skin tone, eye color, personal style, and hair type into account when choosing a color, and always take good care of your colored hair. Happy coloring!
