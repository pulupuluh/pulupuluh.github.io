Mens Haircuts Short Sides Long Top Curly A Guide To Styling

If you're a man with curly hair, you might be looking for a haircut that can accommodate your curls while still keeping a stylish look. One popular option is the mens haircut short sides long top curly style. This hairstyle features shorter sides with a longer top that can be styled in a variety of ways. In this guide, we will discuss how to achieve this hairstyle, tips for styling, and hair care for maintaining healthy curls.

How to Achieve the Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Curly Style

The mens haircut short sides long top curly style can be achieved by asking your barber or stylist for a fade on the sides and leaving the top longer. The fade can be a high fade, mid fade, or low fade depending on your preference. For curly hair, it's best to leave the top longer to allow for more styling options and to showcase your curls.

Another option is to ask for a undercut hairstyle, which features shaved sides and a longer top. This style can also work well for curly hair, as it allows for more volume and texture on top.

Tips for Styling Mens Haircuts Short Sides Long Top Curly

When styling mens haircuts short sides long top curly, it's important to use the right products and techniques to maintain healthy curls and prevent frizz. Here are some tips for styling:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to hydrate and define your curls.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as scrunching, twisting, or finger coiling, to find what works best for your curls.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mens Haircuts Short Sides Long Top Curly

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling mens haircuts short sides long top curly:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to hydrate and define your curls.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to style your curls into the desired shape.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to the tips of your curls to add definition and hold.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your curls in place.

FAQs about Mens Haircuts Short Sides Long Top Curly

What face shapes work well with mens haircuts short sides long top curly?

Mens haircuts short sides long top curly can work well with a variety of face shapes, but it's best suited for oval, square, or diamond-shaped faces.

Can men with straight hair achieve the mens haircut short sides long top curly style?

While this style is best suited for curly hair, men with straight hair can achieve a similar look by using a curling iron or hot rollers to add texture and volume to the top of their hair.

How often should men with curly hair get a trim?

Men with curly hair should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of their haircut.

Hair Care for Maintaining Healthy Curls with Mens Haircuts Short Sides Long Top Curly

To maintain healthy curls with mens haircuts short sides long top curly, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with products designed for curly hair to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to hydrate and define your curls.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish mens haircut short sides long top curly style that showcases your natural curls while maintaining healthy hair. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
