Mens Haircut Short On Sides Long On Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Apr 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a versatile haircut that's both stylish and easy to maintain, then the mens haircut short on sides long on top might be the perfect choice for you. This popular haircut features short hair on the sides and back, and longer hair on top, creating a trendy, modern look that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect mens haircut short on sides long on top, along with some tips and tricks for styling and maintaining your new 'do.

Description

The mens haircut short on sides long on top is a modern, versatile hairstyle that's perfect for guys who want a trendy, low-maintenance look. This haircut features short hair on the sides and back, while the hair on top is left longer, creating a sleek, stylish look that's perfect for any occasion.

How To

If you're looking to get a mens haircut short on sides long on top, then the first step is to find a skilled, experienced barber who can help you achieve the look you want. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your barber starts cutting your hair, it's important to have a consultation to discuss your desired look. Bring in some pictures of hairstyles that you like, and talk to your barber about your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. Your barber will be able to recommend a haircut that will work best for you.

Step 2: Cutting the Sides and Back

Next, your barber will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head. Depending on your desired look, your barber may use different clipper guards to achieve the right length.

Step 3: Cutting the Top

After the sides and back are cut, your barber will move on to the top of your head. This is where the longer hair comes in. Your barber will use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length, blending it with the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 4: Styling

Once your haircut is complete, it's time to style your hair. The mens haircut short on sides long on top is a versatile style that can be worn in many different ways. You can slick it back for a classic look, or add some texture for a more modern, edgy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your mens haircut short on sides long on top:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find a look that works for you
  • Keep your hair trimmed regularly to maintain your desired length and style
  • Consider adding some color or highlights to your hair to add dimension and texture

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut short on sides long on top:

What face shape does this haircut work best for?

The mens haircut short on sides long on top works well for most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for guys with square or oval faces.

How often should I get my hair cut with this style?

You should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired length and style.

Can I style this haircut in different ways?

Yes, the mens haircut short on sides long on top is a versatile style that can be worn in many different ways. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find a look that works for you.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut short on sides long on top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a styling product that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Get regular trims to maintain your desired length and style

By following these tips, you can keep your mens haircut short on sides long on top looking stylish and healthy.

In conclusion, the mens haircut short on sides long on top is a stylish, versatile haircut that's perfect for guys who want a trendy, low-maintenance look. Whether you're looking for a classic, slicked-back style or a more modern, textured look, this haircut is sure to turn heads. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can get the perfect mens haircut short on sides long on top and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
