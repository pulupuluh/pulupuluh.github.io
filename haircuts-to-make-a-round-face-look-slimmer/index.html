Haircuts To Make A Round Face Look Slimmer

Written by Luffy Apr 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you may want to consider a haircut that can make your face look slimmer. In this article, we will discuss different haircuts that can make your face appear more angular and provide you with a slimmer look. We will also provide tips on how to care for your hair after the haircut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by fullness and a lack of angular features. A haircut can help add angles and make your face appear slimmer. The right haircut can accentuate your best features and downplay the roundness of your face.

How to

When choosing a haircut to make your face look slimmer, you should consider your hair type and texture, as well as your personal style. Some haircuts that can help make your face look slimmer include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help add volume and movement to your hair. They can also help elongate your face and give it a more angular appearance. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to add long layers to your hair, starting at your chin or below.

2. Bob

A bob can help add structure to your hair and make your face look more angular. A short bob that ends at your jawline can be particularly flattering for a round face. You can also try a longer bob, known as a lob, which ends just above your shoulders.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can help draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. It can also make your face appear smaller and more angular. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, while leaving it longer on top.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help frame your face and draw attention away from your roundness. They can also help elongate your face and give it a more angular appearance. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle, starting at your temples and ending at your cheekbones.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve a slimmer face with a new haircut:

  1. Research different haircuts that can help make your face look slimmer.
  2. Choose a haircut that complements your hair type and texture, as well as your personal style.
  3. Book an appointment with a stylist who has experience cutting hair for round faces.
  4. Bring pictures of the haircut you want to your appointment.
  5. Communicate your preferences to your stylist, including the length and style of your hair.
  6. Trust your stylist to create a haircut that will make your face look slimmer.
  7. After your haircut, use styling products to enhance your new look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut to make your face look slimmer:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your natural features.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair.
  • Be open to trying new styles and haircuts.
  • Use styling products to enhance your haircut and give your hair volume and movement.
  • Consider getting a hair color that complements your new haircut.

FAQ

What is a round face?

A round face is characterized by fullness and a lack of angular features. People with round faces typically have a rounded jawline, full cheeks, and a wide forehead.

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face depends on your hair type and texture, as well as your personal style. Some haircuts that can help make your face look slimmer include long layers, a bob, a pixie cut, and side-swept bangs.

How can I make my face look slimmer with a haircut?

You can make your face look slimmer with a haircut by choosing a style that adds angles and structure to your hair. A haircut that elongates your face, such as long layers or a bob that ends at your jawline, can help make your face appear more angular.

Hair Care

After getting a new haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Use styling products to enhance your haircut and give your hair volume and movement.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid overusing styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

By taking care of your hair and choosing a haircut that complements your natural features, you can make your face look slimmer and feel more confident in your appearance.
