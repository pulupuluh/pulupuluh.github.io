What Colours Suit Different Skin Tones A Guide To Finding Your Best Colours

Written by Aprilia Jun 16, 2023 · 6 min read

Choosing the right colours that flatter your skin tone is essential to looking your best. Wearing the wrong shades can make you look tired, dull, or washed out. In this article, we'll guide you through what colours suit different skin tones, so you can feel confident and stylish in whatever you wear.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Best Colours
  • Tips for Choosing Colours That Flatter Your Skin Tone
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Skin Tone

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step to finding the right colours for your skin tone is to determine whether you have a warm, cool, or neutral undertone. Here are some tips to help you figure it out:

  • If your skin has a golden or peachy undertone and you tan easily, you likely have a warm undertone.
  • If your skin has a pink or rosy undertone and you burn easily in the sun, you likely have a cool undertone.
  • If your skin has a mix of both warm and cool undertones, you likely have a neutral undertone.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Best Colours

Once you've determined your skin tone, use this guide to find colours that flatter your complexion:

Warm Undertones:

  • Colours to wear: earthy tones, such as terracotta, olive, and mustard. Soft pastels, such as peach, coral, and dusty pink. Bold, warm colours, such as red, orange, and yellow.
  • Colours to avoid: cool colours, such as blue, purple, and silver.

Cool Undertones:

  • Colours to wear: jewel tones, such as emerald, sapphire, and amethyst. Cool pastels, such as baby blue, lavender, and mint. Bright, cool colours, such as fuschia, royal blue, and bright pink.
  • Colours to avoid: warm colours, such as orange, yellow, and brown.

Neutral Undertones:

  • Colours to wear: pretty much anything goes! Neutral undertones have the advantage of being able to wear both warm and cool colours.
  • Colours to avoid: none!

Tips for Choosing Colours That Flatter Your Skin Tone

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing colours to flatter your skin tone:

  • Consider your eye and hair colour. Your overall colour palette should complement your natural features.
  • Think about the occasion. Certain colours may be more appropriate for formal events, while others may be better suited for casual outings.
  • Experiment with different shades. Just because a colour is generally considered "good" for your skin tone doesn't mean that every shade will work equally well.

FAQs

What if I don't like the colours that flatter my skin tone?

Ultimately, you should wear what makes you feel confident and comfortable. If you don't like a certain colour, don't force yourself to wear it just because it's "right" for your skin tone.

Can I wear black if I have a warm undertone?

Yes! While black is generally considered a cool colour, it can still work for those with warm undertones. Just be sure to balance it out with warm-coloured accessories.

Hair Care Tips for Your Skin Tone

Your hair colour can also play a role in how well certain colours flatter your skin tone. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • If you have warm undertones, consider hair colours such as golden blonde, caramel, or copper.
  • If you have cool undertones, consider hair colours such as platinum blonde, ash brown, or jet black.
  • If you have neutral undertones, you can pretty much pull off any hair colour!

By following these tips and experimenting with different colours, you can find the perfect palette to flatter your skin tone and make you look and feel your best.
