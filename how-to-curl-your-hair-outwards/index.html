How To Curl Your Hair Outwards Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Apr 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old straight hair every day? Do you want to add some volume and bounce to your hair with some curls? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you on how to curl your hair outwards, giving you the perfect look for any occasion.

Description

Curling your hair outwards is a great way to add volume and definition to your hair. This technique involves curling the hair away from your face, creating a natural-looking wave that frames your face perfectly. This style is perfect for those who want to add some movement to their hair without going overboard.

How to Curl Your Hair Outwards

Before we get started, it is important to know that you will need a few items to achieve this look. These include a curling iron, heat protectant spray, and a comb. Once you have these items, you are ready to start.

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Once your hair is clean, apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. After applying the heat protectant, use a comb to detangle your hair, making it easier to curl.

Step 2: Heat Up Your Curling Iron

Plug in your curling iron and let it heat up. While waiting for it to heat up, divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness of your hair. For thicker hair, divide your hair into more sections, while for thinner hair, divide your hair into fewer sections.

Step 3: Start Curling

Take a section of your hair and wrap it around the curling iron, making sure that the hair is wrapped away from your face. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process with all the sections of your hair, making sure to curl away from your face.

Step 4: Set Your Curls

Once you have curled all of your hair, use your fingers to separate the curls and create a more natural-looking wave. Finally, set your curls in place with a hairspray.

Tips

To get the best results, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a curling iron with a barrel size that matches the size of the curls you want to achieve.
  • Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to prevent heat damage.
  • Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to curl.
  • Hold the curling iron for a few seconds to ensure that the curl is set.
  • Use your fingers to separate the curls for a more natural-looking wave.
  • Set your curls in place with a hairspray to make them last longer.

FAQ

1. Can I curl my hair outwards without a curling iron?

Yes, you can use other methods to achieve this look, such as using hot rollers or a straightening iron. However, a curling iron is the easiest and most effective way to curl your hair outwards.

2. How long will my curls last?

The length of time your curls will last depends on several factors, such as the thickness of your hair, the products you use, and the weather. However, if you set your curls with a hairspray, they should last for several hours.

3. How can I make my curls last longer?

To make your curls last longer, use a hairspray to set them in place. You can also use other styling products, such as mousse or gel, to add more hold and definition to your curls.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools that involve heat.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to curl your hair outwards, you can experiment with different styles and looks. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
