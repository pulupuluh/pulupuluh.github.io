How To Pick The Right Color Hair Extensions

Written by Petter May 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are looking to change up your hairstyle, hair extensions are a great option. They can add length, volume, and even a pop of color to your hair. However, picking the right color hair extensions can be a daunting task. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the perfect shade for your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Hair extensions come in a variety of colors, from natural shades to bold and bright hues. When picking the right color, it is important to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and the overall look you are trying to achieve.

How to

The first step in picking the right color hair extensions is to determine your skin tone. Skin tones are generally categorized as warm, cool, or neutral. If you have warm undertones, you will look best in shades of gold, honey, and caramel. If you have cool undertones, go for shades of ash, platinum, and champagne. If you have neutral undertones, you can pull off both warm and cool shades.

Next, consider your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, it is best to stick with shades that are no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural color. For lighter hair, shades that are no more than two shades lighter or darker will also work well.

Step by step

1. Determine your skin tone: warm, cool, or neutral.

2. Consider your natural hair color and choose shades that are no more than two shades lighter or darker.

3. Look at swatches or pictures of the hair extensions in natural light to get a better idea of the color.

4. If you are still unsure, consult with a professional stylist who can help you pick the perfect shade.

Tips

- When selecting hair extensions, choose a shade that will blend well with your natural hair color.

- If you plan on dyeing your hair, do so before getting hair extensions.

- Consider the length and thickness of the hair extensions when making your selection.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair extensions?

A: It is not recommended to dye hair extensions as they have already undergone a chemical process. If you must dye them, seek the help of a professional stylist.

Q: How long do hair extensions last?

A: This depends on the type of hair extensions and how well they are cared for. Generally, they can last anywhere from 2-6 months.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the longevity of your hair extensions. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Brush your hair gently and avoid tugging or pulling on the extensions. If you are unsure about how to care for your specific type of hair extensions, consult with a professional stylist.

With these tips and guidelines, you can confidently pick the right color hair extensions for your hair. Remember to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and desired look, and don't be afraid to seek the advice of a professional stylist.
