How Do I Get Rid Of Brassy Hair At Home

Written by William May 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of brassy hair color? Do you want to know how to get rid of it without spending a lot of money at a salon? In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to eliminate brassy hair at home. We will also give you some tips on how to maintain your hair color and prevent it from turning brassy in the future.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How to Get Rid of Brassy Hair at Home
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care

Description:

Brassy hair can be a result of several factors, including exposure to sunlight, using harsh shampoos, and swimming in chlorinated water. It can be frustrating to deal with, but the good news is that it's not permanent. You can easily fix it at home without having to spend a lot of money.

How to Get Rid of Brassy Hair at Home:

The easiest way to get rid of brassy hair at home is to use a purple shampoo or a toner. These products have a purple pigment that counteracts the yellow and orange tones in your hair, leaving you with a cooler, more neutral color.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Here's how to use a purple shampoo or toner:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and open up the hair cuticles. Rinse well with warm water and towel dry your hair.
  2. Apply the purple shampoo or toner to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Make sure to saturate your hair evenly.
  3. Leave the product on for the recommended time, usually between 5-10 minutes. Check the instructions on the product packaging for specific directions.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water. Make sure to remove all the product from your hair.
  5. Condition your hair with a moisturizing conditioner to restore any lost moisture.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color:

Here are some tips on how to maintain your hair color and prevent it from turning brassy:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners that contain sulfates. These can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun. Sunlight can cause your hair color to fade and turn brassy.
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water. If you do swim in a pool, wear a swim cap or apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair.
  • Get regular trims to remove any split ends and keep your hair healthy.

FAQs:

Q: Can I use a purple shampoo on colored hair?

A: Yes, you can use a purple shampoo on colored hair. It won't damage your hair or affect the color, but it will help to neutralize any brassy tones.

Q: How often should I use a purple shampoo?

A: It depends on how brassy your hair is and how often you wash your hair. If your hair is very brassy, you can use it once a week. If it's not too bad, you can use it every other week or as needed.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and prevent it from turning brassy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

Now that you know how to get rid of brassy hair at home, you can enjoy your beautiful, cool-toned locks all year round. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep it healthy and vibrant.
