Good Hairstyles For Big Foreheads Guys Tips How To And Faq

Are you a guy with a big forehead? Do you feel self-conscious about it? Well, worry no more! There are plenty of hairstyles that can help you feel confident and look great. In this article, we'll go over some of the best hairstyles for big foreheads guys, how to achieve them, and some tips for hair care. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, let's talk about how to choose the right one for you. When it comes to big foreheads, you want to avoid styles that accentuate the forehead even more. Instead, you want to choose hairstyles that draw attention away from it. Here are some tips for choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Avoid side parts that expose the forehead
  • Try bangs or fringes that cover the forehead
  • Avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or flat
  • Choose hairstyles with volume on top

By following these tips, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and draws attention away from your forehead.

10 Good Hairstyles for Big Foreheads Guys

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle, let's take a look at some specific styles that work well for big foreheads guys.

The Fringe

The fringe is a classic hairstyle that works well for big foreheads. It involves cutting the hair so that it falls just above the eyebrows. This style draws attention away from the forehead and gives the face a more balanced look.

The Quiff

The quiff is a popular hairstyle that works well for big foreheads. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving it longer on top. The hair on top is then styled into a voluminous, upward sweep.

The Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are another great option for big foreheads. This style involves cutting the hair so that it falls to one side, covering part of the forehead. It's a simple but effective way to draw attention away from the forehead.

The Messy Top

The messy top is a low-maintenance hairstyle that works well for big foreheads. It involves leaving the hair longer on top and tousling it for a messy, textured look. This style is great for guys who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a stylish and modern hairstyle that works well for big foreheads. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving it longer on top. The hair on top is then styled into a textured, messy look.

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a simple but stylish hairstyle that works well for big foreheads. It involves cutting the hair very short all over the head. This style draws attention away from the forehead and gives the face a more balanced look.

The Modern Pompadour

The modern pompadour is a trendy hairstyle that works well for big foreheads. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving it longer on top. The hair on top is then styled into a voluminous, upward sweep with a slight quiff at the front.

The Undercut

The undercut is a popular hairstyle that works well for big foreheads. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving it longer on top. The hair on top is then styled into a voluminous, upward sweep.

The Mohawk

The mohawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle that works well for big foreheads. It involves shaving the hair on the sides of the head and leaving a strip of longer hair on top. This style draws attention away from the forehead and gives the face a more balanced look.

The Short Caesar Cut

The short caesar cut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that works well for big foreheads. It involves cutting the hair short all over the head and styling it forward. This style is easy to maintain and looks great on guys with big foreheads.

Tips for Hair Care

Now that you have some hairstyle ideas, let's talk about hair care. Taking care of your hair is important for keeping it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a big forehead?

Avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or flat. These styles accentuate the forehead and make it appear even larger. Instead, choose hairstyles with volume on top or bangs that cover the forehead.

Can I still have short hair if I have a big forehead?

Absolutely! There are plenty of short hairstyles that work well for guys with big foreheads, such as the buzz cut or the short caesar cut.

Do I need to use hair products to style my hair?

Not necessarily. While hair products can help you achieve certain styles, there are plenty of hairstyles that don't require any products at all. It all depends on the look you're going for.

With these tips, hairstyle ideas, and hair care advice, you're well on your way to feeling confident and looking great, no matter how big your forehead may be!
