Haircuts For Long Faces 2022 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Aug 22, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have a long face, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your features and accentuates your best assets. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this guide, we'll break down the best haircuts for long faces in 2022, including how to choose the right style, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

Description

A long face shape is characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow cheekbones. The goal of a good haircut is to balance out the length of the face and create the illusion of width. The right style can make all the difference, giving you a more proportionate and balanced look. Whether you prefer long or short hair, there are plenty of options that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Long Face

When choosing a haircut for a long face, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Length: The length of your hair can make a big difference in how your face looks. Generally, hairstyles that are shorter or fall at the collarbone are most flattering for long faces.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and texture, which can help balance out a long face. However, be careful not to go too short or choppy, which can make your face look longer.
  • Bangs: Bangs can be a great way to shorten the appearance of your forehead and balance out the length of your face. Opt for side-swept or wispy bangs rather than blunt ones.
  • Texture: Adding texture to your hair can help create the illusion of width and volume. Consider adding waves, curls, or even straightening your hair to achieve the perfect look.

Step-by-Step Instructions for the Best Haircuts for Long Faces 2022

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, let's dive into some of the best styles for long faces:

1. Long Layers

This style features long layers that fall around the collarbone. The layers add volume and texture, while the length helps to balance out a long face. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair.

How to Achieve the Look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Part your hair down the middle.
  • Section your hair into layers, starting at the back and working your way forward.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to add waves or curls if desired.
  • Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help shorten the appearance of your forehead and balance out a long face. They are also versatile and can work with a variety of hair lengths and textures.

How to Achieve the Look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Part your hair to the side.
  • Section off the front portion of your hair for the bangs.
  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to blow dry the bangs to the side.
  • Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that works well with long faces. The length should fall just below the chin to balance out the length of the face. This style can be worn straight or with waves for added texture.

How to Achieve the Look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Part your hair down the middle or to the side.
  • Use a straightening iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron to add waves or curls.
  • Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use quality hair care products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your locks healthy and hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for a long face?

The best haircut for a long face is one that balances out the length and creates the illusion of width. Some great options include long layers, side-swept bangs, and bobs.

Can I wear my hair short if I have a long face?

Yes, you can wear your hair short if you have a long face. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your features and balances out the length of your face.

What hair care products should I use for my long face?

The hair care products you use will depend on your hair type and specific needs. However, it's important to choose high-quality products that are appropriate for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your locks.

Hair Care Advice for Long Faces

Keeping your hair looking its best is important, especially if you have a long face. Here are a few tips to help you care for your locks:

  • Wash your hair regularly with quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your locks healthy and hydrated.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect haircut for a long face can be a challenge, but with the right style, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your look. From long layers to side-swept bangs and bobs, there are plenty of options to choose from. Just remember to consider your hair length, layers, bangs, and texture when choosing a style, and be sure to maintain your locks with quality hair care products and regular trims. With these tips and tricks, you'll be sure to find the perfect haircut for your long face in 2022.
