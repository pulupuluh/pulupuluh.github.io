Would I Look Good With Medium Length Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering changing up your hairstyle and wondering if medium length hair would suit you? You're not alone! Many people ask themselves this question before taking the plunge. In this article, we'll explore how to determine if medium length hair would look good on you, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

Medium length hair typically falls between the chin and shoulders. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways, from straight and sleek to curly and voluminous. If you're looking for a change from your current hairstyle, medium length hair could be a great option.

How to Determine if Medium Length Hair Would Suit You

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's important to consider a few factors such as your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you determine if medium length hair would suit you:

Face Shape

Medium length hair looks great on many different face shapes, but some styles may be more flattering than others. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round face: go for a style with layers and volume on top to elongate the face
  • Square face: soft layers around the face can soften the angles
  • Heart-shaped face: a side-swept fringe can balance out a wider forehead
  • Oval face: lucky you! Almost any style will suit you

Hair Texture

Consider the natural texture of your hair when choosing a medium length style. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut with minimal layers can help reduce bulk. If you have fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and movement.

Personal Style

Think about your personal style and how a medium length hairstyle would fit into your lifestyle. Are you low maintenance or do you like to spend time styling your hair? Do you prefer classic or edgy looks? These are all important factors to consider when choosing a new hairstyle.

Step by Step: How to Style Medium Length Hair

There are many ways to style medium length hair, depending on your personal preference and the occasion. Here's a simple step-by-step guide to achieve a classic, sleek look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling
  3. Section your hair and use a flat iron to straighten it
  4. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to your ends to add shine and prevent frizz
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair

Like any hairstyle, medium length hair requires some maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Regular trims every 6-8 weeks will prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished
  • Protect your hair from heat styling by using a heat protectant spray and avoiding excessive use of hot tools

FAQ

Will medium length hair suit me if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Medium length hair can look great on curly hair, especially if you opt for layers to add movement and volume.

Can I still wear my hair up with a medium length style?

Yes! There are many ways to style medium length hair in updos, from sleek ponytails to messy buns.

Do I have to use heat styling tools to achieve a sleek look?

No, there are many products available that can help you achieve a sleek look without heat styling, such as smoothing creams and serums.

Conclusion

If you're considering a new hairstyle, medium length hair could be a great option. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can determine if this style would suit you. With the right styling tools and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, classic look or something more edgy and trendy. Just remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and conditioning treatments to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
