Best Hair Style For Square Face Female A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi May 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have a square face and are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features? Look no further! We have put together a comprehensive guide to help you find the best hair style for square face female. From long locks to short bobs, we have got you covered. Keep reading to find out more!

Description

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead, and angular features. If you have a square face, you want to choose a hairstyle that softens your features and creates balance. The right haircut can make your face appear longer and slimmer, while the wrong one can make it look wider and shorter.

How To

When choosing the best hair style for square face female, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Soften Your Jawline

Choose a hairstyle that softens your jawline. Soft layers or waves can help to create a more feminine look and balance out your features. Avoid blunt cuts or straight hairstyles that accentuate your strong jawline.

2. Add Height

Adding height to your hair can help to elongate your face and create the illusion of length. Try a voluminous updo or a high ponytail to add height to your look.

3. Avoid Center Parts

Avoid center parts that can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a side part that creates asymmetry and balance.

4. Consider Length

Consider the length of your hair. Long hair can be flattering on a square face, but it can also make your features appear more angular. If you want to go for a shorter cut, try a bob or a lob that hits just below your chin.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape.

2. Decide on the length of your hair.

3. Choose a style that softens your jawline.

4. Add height to your hair.

5. Avoid center parts.

6. Experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one.

Tips

1. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape.

2. Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and movement.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a square face?

The best haircut for a square face is one that softens your features and creates balance. Soft layers or waves can help to soften your jawline, while a side part can create asymmetry and balance. Consider the length of your hair, and avoid center parts that can make your face appear wider.

Can I wear my hair up if I have a square face?

Absolutely! Wearing your hair up can be a great way to add height and elongate your face. Try a voluminous updo or a high ponytail to create height and balance.

What styling products should I use to add volume to my hair?

There are many styling products that can help to add volume and texture to your hair. Try a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray to add body and movement to your locks.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and well-nourished is key to maintaining a great hairstyle. Make sure to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your locks hydrated and nourished. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair style for square face female that flatters your features and creates balance. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect cut for your face shape, and keep your locks healthy and nourished for a flawless look.
