How To Cut A Short Pixie Wig

Written by Bardi Jul 09, 2023 · 8 min read

If you've decided to rock a short pixie wig, you're in for a trendy and low-maintenance look. However, you may need to trim or cut your wig to keep it looking fresh and stylish. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps of cutting a short pixie wig, answer some FAQs, and share some hair care tips to keep your wig looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut a Short Pixie Wig
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting a Short Pixie Wig
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Short Pixie Wig

How to Cut a Short Pixie Wig

Cutting a wig can be intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done easily at home. Here are some basic steps to follow:

Step 1: Gather Tools

Before you begin cutting your wig, you'll need to gather a few tools. These include:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Clips to hold the hair in place

Step 2: Wash Your Wig

It's always best to start with a clean wig. Wash and dry your wig according to the manufacturer's instructions before you start cutting.

Step 3: Comb Your Wig

Using a wide-tooth comb, gently comb your wig to remove any tangles or knots. Be sure to comb in the direction of the hair growth to avoid damaging the wig.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Using clips, divide your wig into sections. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you cut the hair evenly.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Starting with the back of the wig, trim the hair using small, precise cuts. Work your way forward, trimming each section of hair as you go. Be sure to check your progress in a mirror to ensure that the hair is even.

Step 6: Final Touches

Once you've finished cutting your wig, comb it out and check for any uneven spots. Make any final adjustments as needed.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to help you cut your short pixie wig:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Before you start cutting your wig, decide on the style you want to achieve. Look at pictures of short pixie hairstyles to get inspiration or consult with a stylist for advice.

Step 2: Wash Your Wig

Wash your wig according to the manufacturer's instructions. Allow it to air dry or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting.

Step 3: Comb Your Wig

Using a wide-tooth comb, gently comb through your wig to remove any tangles or knots. Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Using clips, divide your wig into sections. Start with the back of the wig and work your way forward.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Using sharp scissors, trim the hair in small sections. Cut the hair at an angle to create layers and texture. Be sure to check your progress in a mirror to ensure that the hair is even.

Step 6: Final Touches

Once you've finished cutting your wig, comb it out and check for any uneven spots. Make any final adjustments as needed.

Tips for Cutting a Short Pixie Wig

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your short pixie wig:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to larger ones
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging the hair
  • Take breaks if your hands get tired
  • Check your progress in a mirror as you go
  • Consult with a stylist if you're unsure about cutting your wig

FAQs

Can I cut my wig while wearing it?

No, it's not recommended to cut a wig while wearing it. This can lead to uneven cuts and damage to the hair.

How often should I cut my wig?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you wear your wig. As a general rule, you should trim your wig every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking its best.

Can I color my wig after cutting it?

Yes, you can color your wig after cutting it. However, it's best to consult with a stylist to ensure that the coloring process is done correctly.

Hair Care Tips for Your Short Pixie Wig

Here are some tips to keep your short pixie wig looking its best:

  • Wash your wig every 7-10 wears
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your wig
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or in a hair net to avoid tangling
  • Protect your wig from sunlight and heat

By following these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your short pixie wig at home and keep it looking stylish and fresh.
