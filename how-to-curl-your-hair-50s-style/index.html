How To Curl Your Hair 50S Style A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a vintage-inspired hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion, look no further than the 50s style curls. Whether you're attending a wedding, prom, or just want to add a touch of glamour to your everyday look, this classic hairstyle is sure to turn heads. In this article, we'll show you how to curl your hair 50s style, from prepping your hair to finishing touches.

How to Curl Your Hair 50s Style

Before we get started, let's talk about the tools you'll need to achieve this look. You'll need a curling iron (preferably with a 1-inch barrel), a heat protectant spray, a comb, and some hairspray. Now that you have your tools ready, let's get started on how to curl your hair 50s style.

Prep Your Hair

The first step to achieving 50s style curls is to prep your hair. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair some body. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.

Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into small sections using a comb. Pin up the top section of your hair to keep it out of the way while you curl the bottom sections.

Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding it in place for 10-15 seconds. Release the curl and repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.

Brush Out Your Curls

Once you've curled all sections of your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to gently brush out your curls. This will create a softer, more natural look.

Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your 50s style curls with a light mist of hairspray to hold them in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're a visual learner, here's a step-by-step guide on how to curl your hair 50s style:

  1. Wash your hair with volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections using a comb.
  4. Pin up the top section of your hair to keep it out of the way.
  5. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding it in place for 10-15 seconds.
  6. Release the curl and repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.
  7. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently brush out your curls.
  8. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Perfect 50s Style Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 50s style curls:

  • Use a 1-inch curling iron for best results.
  • For tighter curls, use smaller sections of hair.
  • For looser curls, use larger sections of hair.
  • Don't hold the curling iron in your hair for too long, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hairspray with a light hold to avoid crunchy curls.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve 50s style curls with a flat iron?

A: Yes, you can achieve 50s style curls with a flat iron. Simply twist your hair around the flat iron and pull downwards to create curls.

Q: How long do 50s style curls last?

A: 50s style curls can last up to a few days with the right hair care.

Q: Can I achieve 50s style curls on short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve 50s style curls on short hair. Use a smaller curling iron and smaller sections of hair to create tight curls.

Hair Care for 50s Style Curls

To keep your 50s style curls looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your curls between washes.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know how to curl your hair 50s style, it's time to show off your new look. Whether you're attending a special event or just want to add some vintage-inspired glamour to your everyday look, this classic hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
