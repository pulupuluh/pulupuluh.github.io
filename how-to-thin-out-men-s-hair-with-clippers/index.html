How To Thin Out Men s Hair With Clippers

Written by Frank Apr 08, 2023 · 9 min read

Thinning out men's hair with clippers is a great way to achieve a more natural and textured look. Thinning out your hair can also help to reduce the amount of volume in your hair, making it easier to manage and style. In this article, we'll take you through the step-by-step process of how to thin out men's hair with clippers. We'll also provide some tips and tricks to help you achieve the best results.

Description

Thinning out men's hair with clippers is a simple process that involves using a pair of clippers to remove some of the hair from your head. This process is particularly useful for men with thick or curly hair, as it can help to reduce volume and make your hair easier to style. Thinning out your hair can also help to create a more natural and textured look, which is ideal for men who want a more laid-back style.

How To

Before you start thinning out your hair with clippers, you'll need to gather a few tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, a comb, and a pair of scissors. You may also want to invest in a thinning shears, which will help to create a more natural-looking texture.

Once you have your tools ready, you can start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get the best results. Next, use your comb to separate your hair into sections. You'll want to start at the back of your head and work your way forward.

Take your clippers and attach the appropriate guard. The guard you choose will depend on how much hair you want to remove. If you're not sure, it's better to start with a longer guard and work your way down. Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up, using the clippers to remove some of the hair from each section.

Once you've worked your way through all of the sections, you can use your scissors to trim any remaining sections that are too thick. You can also use your thinning shears to create a more natural-looking texture. Be sure to blend any sections that look too choppy, and don't be afraid to go back over any areas that need more thinning.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get the best results.

Step 2: Separate Your Hair into Sections

Use your comb to separate your hair into sections. You'll want to start at the back of your head and work your way forward.

Step 3: Attach the Appropriate Guard to Your Clippers

Take your clippers and attach the appropriate guard. The guard you choose will depend on how much hair you want to remove. If you're not sure, it's better to start with a longer guard and work your way down.

Step 4: Start at the Bottom of Your Hair and Work Your Way Up

Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up, using the clippers to remove some of the hair from each section.

Step 5: Trim Any Remaining Sections with Scissors

Once you've worked your way through all of the sections, you can use your scissors to trim any remaining sections that are too thick.

Step 6: Use Thinning Shears to Create a More Natural-Looking Texture

You can also use your thinning shears to create a more natural-looking texture. Be sure to blend any sections that look too choppy, and don't be afraid to go back over any areas that need more thinning.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down
  • Blend any sections that look too choppy
  • Don't be afraid to go back over any areas that need more thinning
  • Invest in a pair of thinning shears to create a more natural-looking texture

FAQ

How often should I thin out my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, you should aim to thin out your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Can I thin out my hair at home?

Yes! Thinning out your hair with clippers is a simple process that you can do at home with the right tools and a little bit of practice.

Will thinning out my hair damage it?

No, thinning out your hair with clippers should not damage your hair. However, it's important to be gentle and avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Hair Care

Thinning out your hair can help to reduce volume and make your hair easier to manage and style. However, it's important to take good care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools. You may also want to invest in a good quality hair oil or serum to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.
