How To Cut Your Hair Really Short At Home

Written by Petter Sep 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and ready for a drastic change? Cutting your hair really short can be a bold and refreshing choice. But before you grab your scissors, it's important to know how to do it properly. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your hair really short at home.

Description

Cutting your hair really short can give you a new and edgy look. It can also be a good way to start fresh and get rid of damaged ends. However, going to a salon can be expensive and time-consuming. That's why we're here to help you achieve a stylish short haircut from the comfort of your own home.

How to

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It's also a good idea to have a mirror and a spray bottle filled with water. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow-drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before cutting it.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Clip each section securely.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin cutting the sides of your hair first. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut straight across. Repeat this process for each section of hair on both sides.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Next, unclip the back section of your hair and comb it straight down. Use the same technique as before to cut straight across. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, unclip the top section of your hair and comb it straight up. Cut small sections of hair at a time to achieve the desired length. Be sure to check the length as you go along.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, release all the clips and check for evenness. If you notice any uneven spots, trim them accordingly.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to ensure a successful haircut:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter if you're not sure how short you want to go.
  • Use a comb to make sure your hair is straight before cutting.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so don't rush it.
  • Ask a friend or family member for help to reach difficult spots.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair really short with regular scissors?

It's best to use sharp haircutting scissors for a clean cut. Regular scissors may cause split ends and make the hair look uneven.

How short should I go?

The length of your haircut depends on your personal preference and face shape. It's best to start longer and gradually go shorter if you're not sure.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Hair grows back, and you can always get it fixed at a salon.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair really short, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat tools and harsh chemicals that can damage the hair.

Cutting your hair really short at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a stylish and edgy look without breaking the bank. Just remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll end up with a haircut you love.
