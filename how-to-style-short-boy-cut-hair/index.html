How To Style Short Boy Cut Hair

Written by Robby Aug 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short boy cut hair is a popular hairstyle that is easy to maintain and looks great on everyone. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways to suit your personality and mood. In this article, we will discuss how to style short boy cut hair and provide tips on how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Boy Cut Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

How to Style Short Boy Cut Hair

There are many ways to style short boy cut hair, depending on your hair type, face shape, and personal preference. Here are some popular ways to style short boy cut hair:

1. Textured and Messy

This style is perfect for those who want a natural and effortless look. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture to your hair, then use your fingers to create a messy and tousled look. This style works best on hair that is naturally wavy or curly.

2. Side Part

A side part is a classic and sophisticated way to style short boy cut hair. Use a comb to create a clean and sharp part, then use a styling product to keep your hair in place. This style works best on hair that is straight or slightly wavy.

3. Slicked Back

A slicked back style is a great way to add a touch of elegance and glamour to your short boy cut hair. Use a strong hold gel or pomade to slick your hair back, then use a comb to create a smooth and polished look. This style works best on hair that is straight or slightly wavy.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide on how to style short boy cut hair:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Use a towel to dry your hair, then use a hair dryer to blow dry your hair while brushing it with a comb.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product that is suitable for your hair type, such as a texturizing spray or pomade. Use your fingers to work the product into your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.

3. Style your hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair according to the desired look. You can create a messy and textured look, a side part, or a slicked back style.

4. Finish with hairspray

Finish by applying a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your short boy cut hair:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a comb or brush to create a polished and refined look.
  • Use your fingers to create a natural and effortless look.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and experiment with your hair.

FAQ

1. Can short boy cut hair be styled for formal events?

Yes, short boy cut hair can be styled for formal events. A slicked back style or a side part can add a touch of elegance and sophistication to your look.

2. How often should I wash my short boy cut hair?

It is recommended to wash your short boy cut hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry and brittle.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to care for your short boy cut hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dye.

By following these tips, you can keep your short boy cut hair looking healthy, stylish, and easy to maintain.
