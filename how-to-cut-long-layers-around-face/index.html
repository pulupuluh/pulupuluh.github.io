How To Cut Long Layers Around Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Long layers around the face can add dimension and movement to your hair, making it look more voluminous and stylish. But cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have never done it before. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting long layers around your face in a relaxed English language. We will cover everything from preparation to aftercare, so you can achieve the perfect haircut at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Long Layers Around Face

How to Cut Long Layers Around Face

Cutting long layers around your face requires some preparation and the right tools. Here's what you need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb or hairbrush
  • Hair clips or ties
  • Water spray bottle

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut long layers around your face:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. This will help you see the natural texture and movement of your hair. Avoid using any styling products or heat tools before cutting your hair.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips or ties. Start by sectioning the top part of your hair and securing it with a clip. Then, divide the sides and back of your hair into two sections each and secure them with clips as well.

Step 3: Cut the first layer

Start by cutting the first layer around your face. Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it forward. Hold the section between your index and middle fingers and cut it with sharp scissors. Repeat this process on both sides of your face until you have cut the first layer around your face.

Step 4: Cut the second layer

Take the next section of hair from the top of your head and comb it forward. Hold the section between your index and middle fingers and cut it slightly shorter than the first layer. Repeat this process on both sides of your head until you have cut the second layer.

Step 5: Cut the remaining layers

Continue cutting the remaining layers of your hair using the same technique. Take small sections of hair and cut them slightly shorter than the previous layer. Make sure to blend each layer with the previous one to achieve a natural look.

Step 6: Check for symmetry

Once you have cut all the layers, check for symmetry by comparing both sides of your hair. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure both sides are even.

Tips for Cutting Long Layers Around Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting long layers around your face:

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Start with small sections of hair and cut a little at a time to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will appear longer when it dries.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as it may result in an uneven haircut.

FAQs

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my hair when it's wet?

It's not recommended to cut your hair when it's wet, as it will appear longer when it dries. It's best to cut your hair when it's dry.

How can I fix a bad haircut?

If you've had a bad haircut, it's best to go to a professional stylist to fix it. They can help you achieve the desired look and fix any mistakes.

Hair Care After Cutting Long Layers Around Face

After cutting long layers around your face, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Cutting long layers around your face can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a stylish haircut at home. Follow our step by step guide and tips to get the perfect long layers around your face.
