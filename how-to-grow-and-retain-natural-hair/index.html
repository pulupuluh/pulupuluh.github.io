How To Grow And Retain Natural Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 26, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of dealing with dry and damaged hair? Do you want to learn how to grow and retain natural hair? Look no further! In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about natural hair growth and maintenance. From how to create a hair care routine to tips for preventing breakage, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow and Retain Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Grow and Retain Natural Hair

Growing and retaining natural hair can be a challenge, but it’s not impossible. With the right hair care routine and a few lifestyle changes, you can achieve healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to get you started:

1. Keep Your Hair Moisturized

Dry hair is more prone to breakage, so it’s important to keep your hair moisturized. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to lock in moisture and prevent dryness. You can also try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can cause damage to your hair. Try to limit your use of these tools or avoid them altogether. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Friction from your pillowcase can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Use a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce friction and protect your hair while you sleep. You can also try wearing a silk or satin bonnet or scarf to keep your hair protected.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Your hair needs nutrients to grow and stay healthy. Make sure to eat a diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals. Foods like eggs, spinach, and salmon are great for hair growth and maintenance.

5. Reduce Stress

Stress can have a negative impact on your hair. Try to reduce stress by practicing relaxation techniques like meditation or yoga. You can also try taking a break from social media or other sources of stress.

Step-by-Step Guide

Creating a hair care routine can help you grow and retain natural hair. Here’s a step-by-step guide to get you started:

1. Cleanse Your Hair

Start by shampooing your hair to remove any dirt or buildup. Make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition Your Hair

Apply a conditioner to your hair to help detangle and moisturize. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes before rinsing out.

3. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner or Hair Oil

Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to lock in moisture and prevent dryness. Focus on the ends of your hair, which are more prone to breakage.

4. Style Your Hair

Style your hair using techniques that don’t cause damage, like braiding or twisting. Avoid heat styling tools if possible.

5. Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Use a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce friction and protect your hair while you sleep. You can also try wearing a silk or satin bonnet or scarf to keep your hair protected.

Tips for Healthy Hair

Here are some additional tips for growing and retaining healthy natural hair:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. This will help minimize breakage and damage.

3. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair no more than once or twice a week.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun’s rays can damage your hair, so make sure to protect it by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

FAQs

1. Can I Grow Natural Hair if I’ve Relaxed it Before?

Yes, you can still grow natural hair even if you’ve relaxed it before. However, it may take longer for your natural hair to grow out.

2. How Long Does it Take to Grow Natural Hair?

Everyone’s hair grows at a different rate, but on average, hair grows about half an inch per month. With proper hair care, you can expect to see noticeable growth in 6-12 months.

3. Can I Color My Natural Hair?

Yes, you can color your natural hair. However, it’s important to use a gentle hair dye and to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damage.

Hair Care Routine

Here’s a sample hair care routine for growing and retaining natural hair:

1. Shampoo your hair once a week with a sulfate-free shampoo.

2. Condition your hair every time you wash it.

3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair after washing.

4. Style your hair using techniques that don’t cause damage, like braiding or twisting.

5. Protect your hair while sleeping by using a satin or silk pillowcase or wearing a silk or satin bonnet or scarf.

By following these tips and creating a hair care routine, you can grow and retain natural hair that’s healthy and beautiful. Remember to be patient and consistent, and your hair will thank you!
