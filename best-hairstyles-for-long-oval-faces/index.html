Best Hairstyles For Long Oval Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy May 31, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have a long oval face, you may find it challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. However, with the right cut and styling, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a look that suits your personal style. In this guide, we’ll explore the best hairstyles for long oval faces, including how to achieve them, tips for maintaining your new look, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Your Long Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for Long Oval Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Long Oval Face Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions about Hairstyles for Long Oval Faces
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Oval Faces

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Your Long Oval Face

The key to finding the best hairstyle for your long oval face is to create balance and proportion. You want to avoid styles that make your face appear too long or too narrow. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can play a significant role in determining the best hairstyle for you. If you have thick, voluminous hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut that adds movement and dimension. On the other hand, if you have fine, straight hair, you may prefer a sleek, blunt cut that creates the illusion of thickness.

2. Think About Your Face Shape

While you have an oval face shape, there are still variations to consider. For example, if you have a long and narrow oval face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add too much height or length. Instead, opt for styles that create width and volume.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in determining the best hairstyle for you. If you have an active lifestyle, you may prefer a low-maintenance style that doesn’t require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you have a more formal job, you may want to opt for a sleek, polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for Long Oval Faces

Once you’ve chosen the best hairstyle for your long oval face, it’s time to learn how to style it. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair using products that are appropriate for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have thick hair, use a smoothing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start styling your hair, apply a heat protectant to protect your strands from damage. This is especially important if you plan to use heat styling tools like a curling iron or flat iron.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair until it’s about 80% dry. While blow drying, use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.

4. Use Heat Styling Tools (Optional)

If you want to add curls or waves to your hair, use a curling iron or flat iron to create your desired style. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to hold your look in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Oval Face Hairstyle

Once you’ve achieved your desired hairstyle, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

1. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

2. Use High-Quality Hair Products

Invest in high-quality hair products that are appropriate for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and looking great.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use heat protectant products to protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling tools. Also, avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent excessive damage.

Frequently Asked Questions about Hairstyles for Long Oval Faces

1. Can I wear bangs with a long oval face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a long oval face. However, it’s important to choose a style that flatters your features. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are great options for long oval faces.

2. What hairstyles should I avoid with a long oval face?

Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear too long or too narrow. This includes hairstyles with too much height or length, as well as styles that are too flat or lack volume.

3. Should I add layers to my hair if I have a long oval face?

Yes, adding layers to your hair can create movement and dimension, which can be flattering for long oval faces. However, make sure the layers are not too short, as this can make your face appear longer.

Hair Care Tips for Long Oval Faces

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is appropriate for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

2. Condition Your Hair Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight conditioner. If you have thick hair, use a more intense conditioner.

3. Avoid Excessive Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as this can cause damage to your hair. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

In conclusion, choosing the best hairstyle for your long oval face can be challenging, but with the right cut and styling, you can create a look that flatters your features and suits your personal style. Use the tips and techniques outlined in this guide to achieve the perfect hairstyle for your long oval face.
