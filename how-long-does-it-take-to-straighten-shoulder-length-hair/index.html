How Long Does It Take To Straighten Shoulder Length Hair

Written by William Jul 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have shoulder length hair, you may be wondering how long it will take to straighten your hair. The answer depends on a few factors, including the thickness and texture of your hair, the tools you use, and your experience with styling. In this article, we'll explore how long it typically takes to straighten shoulder length hair, as well as some tips and tricks to help make the process easier.

How to Straighten Shoulder Length Hair

If you're new to straightening your hair, it can seem overwhelming at first. However, with a few simple steps, you can achieve a sleek, straight look in no time. Here's how:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start straightening your hair, it's important to wash and condition it thoroughly. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup, as well as condition your hair to make it more manageable.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Next, apply a heat protectant to your hair to help prevent damage from the heat of the straightener. You can find heat protectant sprays and serums at most beauty supply stores.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Once you've applied your heat protectant, it's time to blow dry your hair. Use a round brush to help smooth your hair as you dry it, and be sure to focus on the roots to add volume.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

To make the straightening process easier, it's important to section your hair. Use hair clips to separate your hair into sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Finally, it's time to straighten your hair. Use a straightening iron to glide over each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to move the straightener slowly and smoothly to avoid damaging your hair.

Tips for Straightening Shoulder Length Hair

Now that you know how to straighten shoulder length hair, here are some tips to help make the process easier:

  • Use a high-quality straightening iron
  • Don't straighten your hair every day
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using too much heat
  • Use a hair serum to add shine

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about straightening shoulder length hair:

How long does it take to straighten shoulder length hair?

The time it takes to straighten shoulder length hair can vary depending on the thickness and texture of your hair, as well as your experience with styling. On average, it can take anywhere from 20 minutes to an hour to straighten shoulder length hair.

How often should I straighten my hair?

It's best to avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can cause damage and make your hair more prone to breakage. Instead, try to limit straightening to once or twice a week.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, straight hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind

In conclusion, straightening shoulder length hair can take anywhere from 20 minutes to an hour, depending on your hair type and experience with styling. To make the process easier, be sure to use high-quality tools and products, section your hair, and take good care of your hair between styling sessions.
