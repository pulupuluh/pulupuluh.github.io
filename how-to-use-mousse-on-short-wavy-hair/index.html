How To Use Mousse On Short Wavy Hair

Written by Petter Aug 03, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizz and unruly waves on your short hair? Mousse can be a great styling product to help tame your waves and give your hair some hold and texture. In this article, we’ll go over how to use mousse on short wavy hair and share some tips for getting the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Use Mousse on Short Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Mousse on Short Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Wavy Hair

How to Use Mousse on Short Wavy Hair

Mousse is a styling product that can add volume, texture, and hold to your hair. It can be used on wet or dry hair, but for best results on short wavy hair, we recommend applying it to damp hair. Here’s how:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start with clean, damp hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel dry, leaving it slightly damp.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Shake the mousse can well and dispense a golf ball-sized amount into your palm. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly. Apply the mousse to your hair from roots to ends, focusing on the areas where you want more volume and texture.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Use your hands to scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp. This will help to define your waves and add texture. Continue scrunching until your hair is evenly coated with mousse.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can cause frizz and damage to your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally. If you’re in a rush, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Tips for Using Mousse on Short Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your mousse:

  • Start with a small amount of mousse and add more as needed. Using too much can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before applying mousse.
  • For extra hold, try using a mousse that is specifically formulated for curly or wavy hair.
  • If you’re short on time, you can speed up the drying process by using a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently scrunch your hair.

FAQs

Can I use mousse on dry hair?

Yes, you can use mousse on dry hair, but for best results on short wavy hair, we recommend applying it to damp hair.

How often should I use mousse on my hair?

This depends on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. If you have dry or damaged hair, it’s best to use mousse sparingly to avoid drying it out further. If you have oily hair, you may be able to use mousse more frequently.

Can I use mousse on colored hair?

Yes, you can use mousse on colored hair. Look for a mousse that is specifically formulated for color-treated hair to help protect your color from fading.

Hair Care for Short Wavy Hair

In addition to using mousse, there are a few other things you can do to keep your short wavy hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve beautiful, defined waves on your short hair using mousse. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you and your hair type.
