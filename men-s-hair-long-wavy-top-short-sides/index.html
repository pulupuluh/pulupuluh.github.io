Men s Hair Long Wavy Top Short Sides The Trending Hairstyle

Written by Luffy Jul 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair with wavy texture on top and short sides is a trending hairstyle among men. This style is versatile, making it suitable for different occasions and outfits. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, you can achieve this style with the right products and tools.

Description

Men's hair long wavy top short sides is a hairstyle that features longer hair on top, styled in waves or curls, and shorter hair on the sides and back. The length of the hair on top varies depending on personal preference, but it usually extends beyond the crown of the head. The sides and back are buzzed or faded, creating a contrast between the top and sides.

How to Achieve Men's Hair Long Wavy Top Short Sides

To achieve the men's hair long wavy top short sides style, you need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Grow your hair out

The first step is to grow your hair out to the desired length on top. Depending on your natural hair texture and growth rate, this may take a few months or longer.

Step 2: Decide on the length

Decide on the length of the hair on top. You can consult with your stylist or use a hair clipper to trim the sides and back to the desired length.

Step 3: Add texture

If your hair is naturally straight, you can add texture by using a curling iron or applying a texturizing product such as sea salt spray or mousse. Work the product into your hair, scrunch it, and let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer.

Step 4: Style the top

Once your hair is dry, use a comb or your fingers to style the top into waves or curls. You can add more product if needed to enhance the texture and hold.

Step 5: Maintain the style

To maintain the men's hair long wavy top short sides style, you need to trim the sides and back regularly to prevent them from growing out too much. You can also touch up the waves or curls on top with more product as needed.

Tips for Men's Hair Long Wavy Top Short Sides

Here are some tips to keep in mind when rocking the men's hair long wavy top short sides style:

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently. - Apply a heat protectant before using a curling iron or hairdryer to prevent damage. - Don't overuse styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it greasy. - Consider getting a professional trim every few weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

FAQ

Can I achieve the men's hair long wavy top short sides style if I have naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can. Depending on your curl pattern, you may need to use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer or apply a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.

Can I wear the men's hair long wavy top short sides style for formal occasions?

Yes, you can. You can style the hair on top into tighter waves or curls and slick back the sides and back for a more polished look.

Do I need to use a specific type of shampoo and conditioner for men's hair long wavy top short sides style?

No, you don't. However, using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Hair Care for Men's Hair Long Wavy Top Short Sides

Here are some hair care tips to keep your men's hair long wavy top short sides looking its best:

- Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage. - Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. - Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or applying a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve and maintain the men's hair long wavy top short sides style. Remember to consult with your stylist if you have any questions or concerns about your hair.
