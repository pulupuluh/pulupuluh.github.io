Best Haircut For Long Curly Hair And Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel May 27, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have long curly hair and a round face, finding the perfect haircut can be a daunting task. The wrong cut can make your face look even rounder, while the right cut can accentuate your best features and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we'll explore the best haircuts for long curly hair and round face, including tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Description

Before we dive into the best haircuts for long curly hair and round face, let's define what we mean by "round face." A round face is characterized by soft curves and a circular shape, with the widest point located at the cheeks. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a haircut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more oval shape. Long curly hair can be challenging to style, but with the right cut and care, it can be a stunning and effortless look.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Curly Hair and Round Face

When choosing the best haircut for long curly hair and round face, there are a few factors to consider:

Face Shape

As we mentioned above, if you have a round face, you'll want to choose a haircut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more oval shape. Look for cuts with layers, angles, and asymmetry.

Hair Texture

Long curly hair can be thick, heavy, and difficult to manage. Consider a cut that removes some of the weight and allows your curls to bounce and move freely. Layers and texture are your friends!

Lifestyle

Do you have time to style your hair every day, or do you need a low-maintenance cut? Consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. A long bob or shaggy layers can be easy to style and maintain, while a pixie cut or blunt bangs may require more upkeep.

Step-by-Step Guide to Best Haircut for Long Curly Hair and Round Face

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, let's explore some of the best styles for long curly hair and round face. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect cut:

Step 1: Consult with Your Stylist

Your stylist is your best resource for finding the right haircut for your face shape and hair texture. Bring in photos of styles you like and discuss your lifestyle needs.

Step 2: Consider Layers

Layers can add dimension and movement to long curly hair, while also removing some of the weight. Ask your stylist to add layers throughout your hair, focusing on the crown and ends.

Step 3: Add Angles

Angles can create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. Ask your stylist to add angled layers or a side-swept bang to your cut.

Step 4: Embrace Texture

Curly hair is all about texture! Ask your stylist to add texture throughout your hair, either with layers or by using a razor to create soft, feathered edges.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Curly Hair and Round Face Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's essential to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your long curly hair and round face looking its best:

Tip 1: Invest in Quality Products

Curly hair requires moisture and hydration to look its best. Invest in quality shampoos, conditioners, and styling products designed for curly hair.

Tip 2: Don't Over-Wash

Curly hair can be dry and fragile, so avoid over-washing it. Aim for washing your hair every 2-3 days and use a leave-in conditioner to keep it hydrated between washes.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Damage

Curls are sensitive to heat, so avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons as much as possible. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for a round face with long curly hair?

The best haircut for a round face with long curly hair is one that elongates the face and adds movement and texture. Consider a layered cut with angles and texture throughout.

How do I style long curly hair for a round face?

To style long curly hair for a round face, focus on volume and texture. Use a volumizing mousse or spray and scrunch your curls with your hands. Avoid flattening your hair to your head, as this can make your face look rounder.

Can I wear bangs with long curly hair and a round face?

Yes! Bangs can be a great way to add dimension and shape to a round face. Consider side-swept bangs or angled bangs that elongate the face.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair and Round Face

Long curly hair can be stunning and effortless, but it requires proper care to look its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Curly hair is prone to tangles, so use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, being gentle as you go.

Tip 2: Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are gentle on curly hair and can help reduce frizz and breakage. Plus, they feel luxurious!

Tip 3: Deep Condition Regularly

Curly hair needs moisture, so deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy. Look for a deep conditioner designed for curly hair.

With the right haircut and care, long curly hair and a round face can be a stunning and effortless look. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice. With a little effort and care, you can rock your curls and feel confident in your beautiful round face!
