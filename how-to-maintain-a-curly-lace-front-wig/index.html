Maintaining Your Curly Lace Front Wig

Written by Luffy Jul 21, 2023 · 8 min read

Curly lace front wigs are a popular choice for those who want to achieve a natural-looking hairstyle. However, it's essential to take proper care of your wig to ensure it stays looking great. Here are some tips on how to maintain your curly lace front wig.

How to Maintain a Curly Lace Front Wig

Maintaining your curly lace front wig is simple if you follow these steps:

Step 1: Detangle Your Wig

Before washing your wig, you should detangle it gently. Use a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush to remove knots and tangles. Start from the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots.

Step 2: Wash Your Wig

Fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Gently swirl the wig in the water and let it soak for a few minutes. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water until all the shampoo is removed.

Step 3: Condition Your Wig

Apply a small amount of wig conditioner to the hair and distribute it evenly with your fingers. Let it sit for a few minutes before rinsing it out with cool water.

Step 4: Dry Your Wig

Blot your wig gently with a towel to remove excess water. Do not rub or twist the hair. Hang the wig on a wig stand or a mannequin head to air dry. Do not use a hair dryer or any heat styling tools on the wig.

When you're not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand or a mannequin head to maintain its shape. Keep it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wig

Here are some tips to help you maintain your curly lace front wig:

  • Use a wig brush or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig gently.
  • Wash your wig every 8-10 wears or as needed.
  • Avoid using hot water or heat styling tools on your wig.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or a mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Use a wig cap to protect your natural hair and to keep your wig in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

A: No, you should use wig shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for synthetic hair. Regular shampoos and conditioners can damage the fibers of your wig.

Q: How often should I wash my wig?

A: You should wash your wig every 8-10 wears or as needed.

Q: Can I use heat styling tools on my wig?

A: No, you should avoid using heat styling tools on your wig. The high temperatures can damage the fibers of the wig.

Hair Care Products for Your Wig

  • Wig shampoo and conditioner
  • Detangling spray

  • Wig stand or mannequin head
  • Wig cap
