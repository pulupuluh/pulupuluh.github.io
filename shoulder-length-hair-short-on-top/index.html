Shoulder Length Hair Short On Top A Relaxed And Trendy Look

Description

Shoulder length hair short on top is a trendy and relaxed hairstyle that has gained popularity in recent years. This hairstyle combines the best of both worlds – the length of shoulder-length hair and the edginess of a shorter cut on top. This look is versatile and can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and personal style. It is one of the most popular hairstyles for both men and women, and it is perfect for those who want to switch up their hair game without going too drastic.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair Short on Top

If you are looking to achieve shoulder length hair short on top, you can go to a salon and ask your hairstylist to create this look for you. You can also achieve this look at home using a few simple steps.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve shoulder length hair short on top:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, and apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Make sure to dry your hair in sections to ensure that it dries evenly.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten it. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using the flat iron.
  5. Separate the top section of your hair from the bottom section using a hair tie or clip.
  6. Using a pair of scissors, cut the top section of your hair to your desired length. Make sure to cut your hair in small sections to ensure that it is even.
  7. Once you have cut the top section of your hair, blend it with the rest of your hair using a comb or your fingers.
  8. Style your hair as desired using a styling product.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair Short on Top

Here are some tips for maintaining shoulder length hair short on top:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair short on top:

1. Is shoulder length hair short on top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, shoulder length hair short on top is suitable for all face shapes. However, it is essential to customize the length and style to suit your face shape and personal style.

2. Can I achieve shoulder length hair short on top with curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve shoulder length hair short on top with curly hair. However, it is important to consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair to ensure that the cut suits your hair texture and curl pattern.

3. Can I style my shoulder length hair short on top in different ways?

Yes, shoulder length hair short on top is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways. You can style it sleek and straight, wavy, or curly, depending on your mood and personal style.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair Short on Top

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining shoulder length hair short on top:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent your scalp from drying out.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Shoulder length hair short on top is a relaxed and trendy hairstyle that is perfect for those who want to switch up their hair game without going too drastic. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain this versatile and edgy look.
