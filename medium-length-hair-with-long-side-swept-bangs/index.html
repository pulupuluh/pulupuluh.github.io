Medium Length Hair With Long Side Swept Bangs A Trendy And Versatile Hairstyle

Written by Aprilia Jun 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is both trendy and versatile? Look no further than medium length hair with long side swept bangs. This hairstyle has been popular for years and continues to be a favorite among women of all ages. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this hairstyle, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

How to Achieve Medium Length Hair with Long Side Swept Bangs

The first step in achieving medium length hair with long side swept bangs is to determine the length of your hair. This hairstyle typically works best with hair that falls between the shoulders and the collarbone. If your hair is too short or too long, it may not be possible to achieve the desired look.

Once you have determined the length of your hair, it's time to add the bangs. Long side swept bangs can be cut at an angle to frame the face and add dimension to the hairstyle. It's important to work with a stylist who has experience cutting bangs to ensure that they are cut evenly and at the right angle.

After the bangs have been cut, it's time to style the hair. To achieve the perfect side swept look, blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, pulling the brush in the direction that you want the hair to sweep. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or kinks, and finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair with Long Side Swept Bangs

Now that you have achieved the perfect medium length hair with long side swept bangs, it's important to maintain the hairstyle. Here are some tips:

Trim your bangs regularly

To keep your bangs looking fresh and stylish, it's important to trim them every 4-6 weeks. This will prevent them from becoming too long or uneven.

Use a heat protectant

When styling your hair with heat tools, it's important to use a heat protectant. This will prevent your hair from becoming damaged and help to maintain its overall health.

Invest in quality hair care products

To keep your hair looking its best, invest in quality hair care products. This includes shampoo, conditioner, and styling products that are designed for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can medium length hair with long side swept bangs work for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can work for all face shapes. However, the length and angle of the bangs may need to be adjusted to flatter your unique features.

Can I wear my hair in other styles besides the side swept look?

Yes, medium length hair with long side swept bangs is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. Try pulling your hair back into a ponytail or bun, or wearing it straight and sleek.

What hair colors work well with this hairstyle?

This hairstyle looks great with all hair colors, from blonde to brunette to red. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to the hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips

Lastly, here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length hair with long side swept bangs looking its best:

Wash your hair every other day

Washing your hair every other day will help to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Avoid using hot tools every day

Using hot tools every day can damage your hair over time. Try to limit your use to a few times a week.

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

A deep conditioning treatment can help to nourish and moisturize your hair, leaving it looking soft and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you are sure to achieve the perfect medium length hair with long side swept bangs. Whether you're headed to work, out with friends, or on a date, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
