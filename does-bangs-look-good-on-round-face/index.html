Does Bangs Look Good On Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Round faces have always been a topic of discussion when it comes to hairstyles. People with round faces often wonder if they should get bangs or not. If you are one of them, then this blog post is for you. In this article, we will discuss whether bangs look good on round faces or not. We will also share tips and tricks to help you choose the right bangs for your face shape. So, without further ado, let's get started.

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some personality to your hairstyle. However, not all bangs are created equal, and not all bangs look good on round faces. When it comes to round faces, it's important to choose the right type of bangs that will complement your face shape and enhance your features. In this article, we will discuss the different types of bangs that work best for round faces and how to style them.

How to

The first step to determining whether bangs look good on your round face is to identify your face shape. Round faces are typically characterized by their soft, curved lines and full cheeks. If you have a round face, you should avoid bangs that are too short or too straight, as they can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for bangs that are longer and angled, such as side-swept bangs or curtain bangs.

Side-swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces because they help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the cheeks. To style side-swept bangs, blow dry them with a round brush and then use a flat iron to smooth them out. You can also add some texture to your bangs with a texturizing spray or a small amount of pomade.

Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are another great option for round faces because they create a diagonal line across the face, which helps to elongate it. To style curtain bangs, blow dry them with a round brush and then use a flat iron to create a slight curve. You can also add some texture to your bangs with a texturizing spray or a small amount of pomade.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions to help you style your bangs:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your bangs with a round brush, pulling them to the side as you go.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out your bangs and create a slight curve.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray or a small amount of pomade to add some texture to your bangs.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you choose the right bangs for your round face:

  • Opt for longer bangs that are angled, such as side-swept bangs or curtain bangs.
  • Avoid bangs that are too short or too straight, as they can make your face appear even rounder.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing your bangs.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to help you choose the right bangs for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Q. What types of bangs should I avoid if I have a round face?

A. You should avoid bangs that are too short or too straight, as they can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for longer and angled bangs, such as side-swept bangs or curtain bangs.

Q. How do I know if I have a round face?

A. Round faces are typically characterized by their soft, curved lines and full cheeks. To determine if you have a round face, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all similar in width, then you have a round face.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair if you want your bangs to look good on your round face. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

So, there you have it â a complete guide on whether bangs look good on round faces or not. Remember, the key is to choose the right type of bangs that will complement your face shape and enhance your features. With the tips and tricks shared in this article, you are sure to find the perfect bangs for your round face.
