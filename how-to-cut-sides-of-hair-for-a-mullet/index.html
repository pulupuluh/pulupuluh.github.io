How To Cut Sides Of Hair For A Mullet

Written by Eveline Apr 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to rock a mullet hairstyle, one of the most important things to get right is the sides of your hair. The right cut can make all the difference between a great-looking mullet and a hairstyle that falls flat. In this article, we'll give you step-by-step instructions on how to cut the sides of your hair for a mullet, as well as some tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect look.

How To:

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the tools you need. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to keep your hair out of the way. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it dry completely. This will help you get a better idea of what your hair will look like once it's cut, and it will also make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into two sections, with the top section pinned up and out of the way. This will allow you to focus on the sides of your hair without getting in the way of the top section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair from the side, and comb it straight down. Using your scissors, cut the hair to the desired length. It's important to only cut a small amount at a time, so you don't accidentally cut off too much. Continue cutting sections of hair until you've finished one side.

Step 4: Repeat on the Other Side

Once you've finished cutting one side, repeat the process on the other side, making sure to cut the hair to the same length as the first side.

Step 5: Blend the Sides

After you've finished cutting both sides, it's important to blend them together. Use your scissors to carefully blend the hair at the back of your head, making sure there are no noticeable lines where the two sides meet.

Tips:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mullet:

  • Start with longer hair, so you can cut it to the desired length.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're not sure what you're doing.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • Remember to blend the sides together for a seamless look.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the sides of hair for a mullet:

Can I cut the sides of my hair myself?

While it's possible to cut the sides of your hair yourself, it's often better to ask for help from a friend or family member.

How long should the sides of my hair be for a mullet?

The length of the sides of your hair will depend on your personal preference, but they should be shorter than the top section.

What if I make a mistake while cutting the sides of my hair?

If you accidentally cut off too much hair or make a mistake, don't panic. It's better to have slightly uneven hair than to have no hair at all. You can always fix it later or ask a professional for help.

Hair Care:

After you've cut the sides of your hair for a mullet, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dye.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and be gentle when brushing it.

With these tips and instructions, you should be able to cut the sides of your hair for a mullet with confidence. Just remember to take your time, cut small sections at a time, and ask for help if you're not sure what you're doing. Good luck!
