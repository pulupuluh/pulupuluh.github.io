How To Keep Curly Hair From Getting Frizzy In Humidity

Written by Daniel Jun 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you struggle with frizzy curly hair in humid weather? It can be frustrating to spend time styling your hair, only to have it turn into a frizzy mess as soon as you step outside. But don't worry, there are ways to keep your curls looking great even in high humidity. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to keep curly hair from getting frizzy in humidity.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The key to keeping curly hair from getting frizzy in humidity is to minimize moisture absorption. When curly hair absorbs moisture from the air, the hair shaft swells and causes frizz. Here are some ways to prevent this from happening:

Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

When you wash your hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help your hair retain moisture. Look for products that contain natural oils like argan oil, coconut oil, or jojoba oil. These oils will help to nourish and hydrate your hair, making it less prone to frizz.

Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After you wash your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help lock in moisture. This will create a barrier between your hair and the humid air, preventing your hair from absorbing excess moisture.

Use a Microfiber Towel

When you dry your hair, use a microfiber towel instead of a regular towel. Microfiber towels are gentler on curly hair and help to minimize frizz.

Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Brushing curly hair can cause it to become frizzy. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to keep curly hair from getting frizzy in humidity:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.
  3. Use a microfiber towel to dry your hair.
  4. Avoid brushing your hair.
  5. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair.
  6. Apply a frizz-fighting serum or oil to your hair.
  7. Style your hair as desired.
  8. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for keeping curly hair from getting frizzy in humidity:

  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment or using a hair mask to help smooth and tame your curls.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from styling products.

FAQ

Why does curly hair get frizzy in humidity?

Curly hair is more prone to frizz because it has a more porous hair shaft. When the hair absorbs moisture from the air, it causes the hair shaft to swell and creates frizz.

How can I prevent my curly hair from getting frizzy in humidity?

To prevent curly hair from getting frizzy in humidity, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, apply a leave-in conditioner, use a microfiber towel to dry your hair, avoid brushing your hair, and use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair.

Hair Care

Taking care of your curly hair is essential to keeping it healthy and frizz-free. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat tools.

By following these tips and taking good care of your curly hair, you can keep it looking great even in humid weather.
