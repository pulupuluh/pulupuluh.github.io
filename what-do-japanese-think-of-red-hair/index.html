What Do Japanese Think Of Red Hair

Written by Petter Mar 27, 2023 · 6 min read

Red hair has always been a topic of conversation in Japan. Many Japanese people are fascinated by the unique color and texture of red hair. In this article, we’ll discuss what Japanese people think of red hair and how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Red hair is not a common sight in Japan. In fact, only about 2% of the Japanese population has naturally red hair. This rarity has made red hair a topic of conversation and fascination in Japan. Many Japanese people view red hair as exotic and unique.

However, there are also negative connotations associated with red hair in Japan. Some people believe that those with red hair are hot-headed and have a fiery temper. This stereotype has led to discrimination and bullying against those with red hair.

How To

If you’re interested in dyeing your hair red or simply want to embrace your natural red hair, there are a few things to keep in mind.

Choose the Right Shade

There are many shades of red to choose from, so it’s important to find the right one for your skin tone. Cooler skin tones look best with cooler shades of red, while warmer skin tones look best with warmer shades of red.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Red hair requires a bit more maintenance than other hair colors. It’s important to consider your lifestyle and how much time and effort you’re willing to put into your hair. If you’re someone who likes to keep things low-maintenance, red hair may not be the best option for you.

Step By Step

If you’ve decided to dye your hair red, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect shade:

  1. Choose the right shade of red hair dye for your skin tone
  2. Bleach your hair if necessary
  3. Apply the hair dye evenly to your hair
  4. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly
  6. Apply a conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy

Tips

Here are a few tips for maintaining your red hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause the color to fade
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often, as this can also cause the color to fade
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray

FAQ

What do Japanese people think of red hair?

Many Japanese people view red hair as unique and exotic, but there are also negative stereotypes associated with red hair.

Is red hair popular in Japan?

No, red hair is not a common sight in Japan. Only about 2% of the population has naturally red hair.

Hair Care

Red hair requires a bit more maintenance than other hair colors. Here are a few tips for keeping your red hair looking healthy:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

Overall, red hair is viewed as unique and exotic in Japan. However, it’s important to be aware of the negative stereotypes associated with red hair and to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
