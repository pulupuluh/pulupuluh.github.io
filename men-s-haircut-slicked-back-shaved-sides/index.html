Description

The slicked back shaved sides men's haircut has been a popular look for several years, and it's still going strong in 2023. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a modern, edgy look that's easy to maintain. The slicked back top creates a sleek, polished appearance, while the shaved sides add a touch of toughness.

This hairstyle is versatile and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. It's also a great option for men who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling.

How to Get the Look

To get the slicked back shaved sides look, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. The first step is to decide on the length and style of the top section. You can opt for a short, neat style or a longer, more tousled look, depending on your preference.

The sides will need to be shaved close to the head, leaving a small strip of hair at the top that blends into the longer hair on top. The length of this strip can vary depending on the individual's preference, but it's usually around 2-3 inches.

To create the slicked back effect, you'll need to apply a styling product such as pomade or wax to the top section of your hair. Use a comb to smooth the hair back and create a sleek, polished finish.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the slicked back shaved sides look:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it until it's damp.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hands and work it evenly through the top section of your hair.
  3. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting at the hairline and working towards the crown of your head.
  4. Use clippers or a razor to shave the sides of your head, leaving a small strip of hair at the top that blends into the longer hair on top.
  5. Style the top section of your hair into the desired shape, using a comb or your fingers to create texture and volume.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Here are some tips for keeping your slicked back shaved sides haircut looking sharp:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain the length of the top section and keep the shaved sides neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality styling product that is suited to your hair type and the look you want to achieve.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to style.
  • When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the slicked back shaved sides men's haircut:

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the slicked back shaved sides look can be adapted to suit different face shapes by adjusting the length and style of the top section.

Q: Can I style this haircut without using product?

A: While it's possible to style this haircut without using product, it will be harder to achieve the sleek, polished look that defines this style.

Q: How often should I wash my hair with this haircut?

A: It's best to avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to style. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and strong:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, which can damage the hair and scalp.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf when outdoors.

By following these tips and maintaining your slicked back shaved sides haircut, you can achieve a modern, edgy look that's both stylish and low-maintenance.
