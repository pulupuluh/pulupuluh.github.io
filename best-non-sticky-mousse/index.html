Best Non Sticky Mousse The Ultimate Guide For Perfect Hair

Written by Eveline May 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for the best non-sticky mousse to achieve the perfect hairstyle? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the top non-sticky mousses in the market. From defining curls to adding volume and shine, these mousses will help you achieve the perfect hairstyle without making your hair sticky or greasy.

What is Non-Sticky Mousse?

Non-sticky mousse is a hair styling product that is used to add volume, texture and hold to your hair. Unlike other mousses, non-sticky mousses are formulated to provide hold without making your hair sticky or greasy. They are perfect for people with fine or thin hair who want to add volume to their hair without weighing it down.

How to Use Non-Sticky Mousse?

Using non-sticky mousse is easy and straightforward. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply the Mousse

Shake the mousse can well and dispense a small amount of mousse into your palm. Rub your palms together and apply the mousse evenly to your damp hair, starting from the roots to the tips. Do not apply too much mousse as it can weigh your hair down.

Step 3: Style your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. If you have curly hair, scrunch your hair while blow drying to enhance your curls. Once your hair is dry, style it as desired using a hairbrush or your fingers.

Tips for Using Non-Sticky Mousse

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using non-sticky mousse:

  • Use a small amount of mousse to avoid weighing your hair down
  • Apply the mousse evenly to your hair to avoid any clumps
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture
  • Scrunch your hair while blow drying to enhance your curls
  • Style your hair as desired using a hairbrush or your fingers

FAQ

Q: Can non-sticky mousse be used on all hair types?

A: Yes, non-sticky mousse can be used on all hair types. It is especially useful for people with fine or thin hair as it adds volume without weighing the hair down.

Q: Can non-sticky mousse be used on dry hair?

A: No, non-sticky mousse should be applied to damp hair. This helps the mousse to distribute evenly and provides a better hold.

Q: Can non-sticky mousse be used with other styling products?

A: Yes, non-sticky mousse can be used with other styling products such as hair spray or serum. However, it is important to use them in moderation to avoid weighing your hair down.

Hair Care Tips

Using non-sticky mousse is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are some additional tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions

With the right non-sticky mousse and a healthy hair care routine, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle without damaging your hair. So, go ahead and try out these amazing mousses to take your hair game to the next level!
