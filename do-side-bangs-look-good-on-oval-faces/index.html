Do Side Bangs Look Good On Oval Faces

Written by Aprilia Jul 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Side bangs have always been a popular hairstyle among women. They add a touch of glamour and style to your overall look. But the question is, do side bangs look good on oval faces? In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about side bangs and whether they are suitable for oval faces or not.

Description

Oval faces are considered the ideal face shape as they have balanced facial features. They have a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and the cheekbones are prominent. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with different hairstyles as almost every style suits this face shape.

How to Style Side Bangs?

Styling side bangs is relatively easy. Here are the steps you need to follow:

  • Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow-dry it.
  • Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the opposite direction to create volume.
  • Apply some heat protectant to your hair and use a flat iron to straighten your bangs.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your bangs in place.

Step by Step Guide to Get the Perfect Side Bangs

Here is a step-by-step guide to get the perfect side bangs:

  • Start by dividing your hair into two parts, the front and the back.
  • Take a section of hair from the front and comb it straight down.
  • Determine the length of your bangs and cut them at an angle.
  • Continue cutting at an angle until you have reached the desired length.
  • Repeat the process on the other side.
  • Blow dry and style your bangs as desired.

Tips for Styling Side Bangs on Oval Faces

Here are some tips for styling side bangs on oval faces:

  • Go for longer side bangs that fall below your eyebrows.
  • Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look too long.
  • Experiment with different styles such as wavy, curly, or straight bangs.
  • Get regular trims to avoid split ends and maintain the shape of your bangs.

FAQ About Side Bangs and Oval Faces

1. Can oval faces pull off any hairstyle?

Yes, oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle. This face shape is considered versatile and balanced.

2. Are side bangs suitable for round faces?

Yes, side bangs can make round faces look slimmer and more defined.

3. How often should I trim my side bangs?

You should trim your side bangs every four to six weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Tips for Side Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for side bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant before using any styling tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray as it can make your hair look greasy.

In conclusion, side bangs can look good on oval faces if styled correctly. Follow the tips and tricks mentioned above to get the perfect side bangs that suit your face shape. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
