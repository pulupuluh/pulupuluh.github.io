Trimming The Back Of Your Own Short Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune to get your hair trimmed every few weeks? Do you want to learn how to trim the back of your own short hair? Well, you're in luck because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to trim the back of your short hair like a pro.

Description

Trimming the back of your own short hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're not used to doing it. However, with a little practice and the right tools, you can learn how to do it like a pro. Trimming the back of your own short hair not only saves you money, but it also gives you the freedom to do it whenever you want. Plus, it's a great skill to have in your back pocket.

How to Trim the Back of Your Own Short Hair

Before you start trimming your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a handheld mirror, and a comb. Once you have these tools, you can follow these steps to trim the back of your own short hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get a clean cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to section off the back of your hair. You can do this by creating a part down the middle of your head and then parting each section to the side.

Step 3: Use the Handheld Mirror

Hold the handheld mirror in front of you and use it to see the back of your head. This will help you get a better view of what you're doing.

Step 4: Trim Your Hair

Using the scissors, trim the ends of your hair. Start with small snips and work your way up until you achieve the desired length. Be sure to trim both sides of your hair evenly.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Use the handheld mirror to check your work. Make sure both sides are even and that you're happy with the length.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're still a little unsure about how to trim the back of your own short hair, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section off the back of your hair using a comb.
  3. Hold the handheld mirror in front of you and use it to see the back of your head.
  4. Using the scissors, trim the ends of your hair. Start with small snips and work your way up.
  5. Check your work using the handheld mirror.

Tips for Trimming the Back of Your Own Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trimming the back of your own short hair:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up.
  • Use a handheld mirror to get a better view of the back of your head.
  • Section off your hair to make it easier to work with.
  • Trim both sides of your hair evenly.
  • If you're not sure about the length, start with a longer length and work your way down.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I trim the back of my own short hair without a handheld mirror?

You can, but it will be more difficult. A handheld mirror allows you to see the back of your head, which makes it easier to trim your hair evenly.

What kind of scissors should I use to trim the back of my own short hair?

You should use a pair of sharp scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair.

How often should I trim the back of my own short hair?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it gets damaged. As a general rule, you should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it healthy and looking its best.

Hair Care After Trimming the Back of Your Own Short Hair

Once you've trimmed the back of your own short hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know how to trim the back of your own short hair, you can save money and do it yourself. With a little practice and the right tools, you can master this skill and have a great-looking haircut every time.
