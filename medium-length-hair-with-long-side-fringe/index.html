Medium Length Hair With Long Side Fringe A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than medium length hair with long side fringe. This trendy and versatile style works for all hair types and face shapes, and it's easy to maintain with the right products and techniques. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect medium length hair with long side fringe look, including step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve Medium Length Hair with Long Side Fringe
  • Step by Step Instructions

How to Achieve Medium Length Hair with Long Side Fringe

Medium length hair with long side fringe is a classic and timeless look that can be customized to suit your individual style. To achieve this look, you will need to have hair that falls between your shoulders and your collarbone. It's also important to have a long side fringe that falls just below your eyebrows.

There are a few different ways to achieve medium length hair with long side fringe, depending on your hair type and face shape. Some people prefer to cut their own hair at home, while others prefer to visit a professional stylist. No matter which method you choose, it's important to have the right tools and products on hand.

Step by Step Instructions

Here is a step by step guide to achieving medium length hair with long side fringe:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  3. Use a comb to create a part on the side of your head where you want your fringe to fall.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and comb it straight down.
  5. Using a pair of scissors, cut the hair to the desired length.
  6. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you have achieved the desired length and shape.
  7. Release the rest of your hair from the clips and style as desired.
  8. Apply a small amount of styling product to your fringe to keep it in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are a few tips for maintaining your medium length hair with long side fringe:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a detangling brush or comb to avoid damaging your hair when you brush it.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hair with long side fringe:

  • Will this hairstyle work for me if I have curly hair?

    • Yes, medium length hair with long side fringe can work for all hair types, including curly hair. You may need to use extra styling product to keep your curls in place.

  • How often should I wash my hair?

    • It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and how oily it gets. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

  • How long does it take to style medium length hair with long side fringe?

    • It only takes a few minutes to style your hair once you have achieved the desired length and shape. Simply apply a small amount of styling product to your fringe and brush your hair into place.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair with Long Side Fringe

Here are a few tips for keeping your medium length hair with long side fringe healthy and strong:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the sun and wind.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients to keep your hair strong and healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect medium length hair with long side fringe look that will turn heads wherever you go. So why wait? Start experimenting with your hair today!
