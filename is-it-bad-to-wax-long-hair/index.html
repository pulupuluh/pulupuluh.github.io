Is It Bad To Wax Long Hair

Written by Luffy Apr 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you someone who has been contemplating whether or not to wax your long hair? If yes, then this is the article for you. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about whether it is bad to wax long hair. We will cover the benefits, how to wax long hair, tips, and hair care. So, let's jump right in!

Description

Waxing is a popular hair removal method where a sticky wax is applied to the skin and then removed, taking the hair with it. Although it is commonly used on shorter hair, many people wonder if it is safe to use on long hair. The answer is yes, but there are some things to keep in mind.

How to Wax Long Hair

Waxing long hair is similar to waxing shorter hair, but you need to take a few extra precautions. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Cleanse the area

Before you start waxing, make sure the area is clean and dry. Use a gentle cleanser to remove any dirt or oil, and then pat the area dry with a towel.

Step 2: Apply the wax

Apply the wax in small sections, making sure to spread it evenly. Use a wooden spatula to apply the wax in the direction of hair growth.

Step 3: Apply the strip

After you've applied the wax, place a strip over it and press it down firmly. Make sure the strip is long enough to cover the entire area you're waxing.

Step 4: Remove the strip

Hold the skin taut with one hand and use the other hand to quickly pull the strip off in the opposite direction of hair growth. Repeat these steps until you've removed all the hair you want to remove.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when waxing long hair:

  • Trim your hair before waxing to make the process easier.
  • Use a hard wax instead of a soft wax for better results.
  • Apply a pre-wax oil to protect your skin.
  • Apply pressure to the waxed area after waxing to reduce any discomfort.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about waxing long hair:

Is it safe to wax long hair?

Yes, it is safe to wax long hair as long as you follow the proper precautions. Make sure to trim your hair before waxing and use a hard wax for best results.

Does waxing long hair hurt?

Waxing can be uncomfortable, but it shouldn't be painful. If you experience pain, try applying pressure to the waxed area or using a numbing cream.

How long does waxing long hair last?

Waxing long hair can last anywhere from three to six weeks, depending on your hair growth cycle.

Hair Care

After waxing, make sure to take care of your skin to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs. Here are some tips:

  • Apply a soothing lotion or aloe vera gel to the waxed area.
  • Avoid hot showers and baths for 24 hours after waxing.
  • Avoid touching the waxed area to prevent infection.
  • Exfoliate the waxed area regularly to prevent ingrown hairs.

In conclusion, waxing long hair is safe as long as you follow the proper precautions. Use a hard wax, trim your hair before waxing, and take care of your skin afterward. By doing so, you can enjoy smooth, hair-free skin for weeks to come.
