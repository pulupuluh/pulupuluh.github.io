How To Style Thick Straight Hair For Guys A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Mar 22, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re a guy with thick straight hair, you might find it difficult to style your hair in a way that looks good and suits your personality. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of hairstyles that will make you look and feel your best.

Table of Contents

  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Thick Straight Hair
  • FAQs about Styling Thick Straight Hair for Guys

How to Style Thick Straight Hair for Guys

Before you start styling your hair, it’s important to choose the right haircut for your face shape and hair type. Consult with your hairstylist to find a cut that will flatter your features and complement your hair texture.

Once you have the right cut, you can start styling your hair using the following techniques:

Blow-Drying

Use a hair dryer to blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair. Use a round brush to lift the roots and create more volume.

Straightening

If you want a sleek and straight look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Apply heat protectant spray before straightening to prevent damage to your hair.

Texturizing

If you want a more textured look, use a texturizing spray or wax. Apply the product to your hair and style it with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your thick straight hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go, using a round brush to lift the roots.
  4. Straighten your hair with a flat iron if you want a sleek and straight look.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray or wax if you want a more textured look.
  6. Style your hair with your fingers to create the desired look.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips for Styling Thick Straight Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your thick straight hair:

  • Use the right products for your hair type. Consult with your hairstylist to find products that will work for you.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on styling your hair.
  • Use a hair dryer with a concentrator nozzle to direct the heat and add volume to your hair.
  • Use a flat iron with ceramic plates to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs about Styling Thick Straight Hair for Guys

Q. How often should I wash my hair?

A. It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q. Can I use a texturizing spray on wet hair?

A. It’s best to use texturizing spray on dry hair. If you apply it to wet hair, it may not work as well and could make your hair look greasy.

Q. How can I prevent damage to my hair when using a flat iron?

A. Always use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron, and don’t use it on wet hair. Use a flat iron with ceramic plates, which will distribute heat evenly and prevent hot spots.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Straight Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your thick straight hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your thick straight hair in a way that looks great and suits your personality. Consult with your hairstylist to find the right haircut and products for your hair type, and experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.
