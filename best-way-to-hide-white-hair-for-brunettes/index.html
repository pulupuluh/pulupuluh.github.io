The Best Way To Hide White Hair For Brunettes

Written by Eveline Aug 05, 2023 · 6 min read

As we age, it’s common to start seeing a few strands of white hair. While some people embrace their silver strands, others prefer to hide them. If you’re a brunette looking for the best way to hide white hair, you’re in luck! There are several options available to you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hide White Hair for Brunettes
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Brunettes

How to Hide White Hair for Brunettes

The best way to hide white hair for brunettes depends on several factors, including how much white hair you have, your hair texture, and your personal preferences. Below are some popular options:

Hair Dye

The most popular way to hide white hair for brunettes is to dye your hair. You can either go to a salon or do it yourself at home using a boxed hair dye. If you have a lot of white hair, you may want to go for a permanent hair dye. However, if you just have a few strands, a semi-permanent hair dye may be enough.

Hair Extensions

If you don’t want to dye your hair or want to add volume to your hair, hair extensions are a great option. You can either get clip-in extensions or sew-in extensions. Make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color.

Hair Mascara

Hair mascaras are temporary hair color products that can help you cover up white hair for a day or two. They are easy to apply and come in a range of colors. However, they can be messy and may rub off on your clothes.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you decide to dye your hair, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose the right hair dye for your hair type and color.
  2. Read the instructions carefully before you start.
  3. Wear gloves to protect your hands.
  4. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to prevent the dye from staining your skin.
  5. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  6. Apply the dye to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  7. Leave the dye on for the recommended time.
  8. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  9. Apply the conditioner that comes with the dye to your hair.
  10. Rinse with cool water.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Once you’ve hidden your white hair, you’ll want to maintain your new look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair color from fading.
  • Limit your use of hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

FAQs

Can I dye my hair if I have already used hair mascara?

Yes, you can. However, make sure to wash out the hair mascara completely before you dye your hair.

Can I dye my hair if I have recently permed it?

No, you should wait at least two weeks before you dye your hair after perming it. Dyeing your hair too soon can damage it.

How often should I touch up my roots?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. You may need to touch up your roots every four to six weeks.

Hair Care Products for Brunettes

Here are some hair care products specifically designed for brunettes:

  • John Frieda Brilliant Brunette Multi-Tone Revealing Shampoo
  • Aveda Clove Shampoo
  • Bumble and Bumble Brownish Hair Powder
  • Oribe Signature Moisture Masque
  • Moroccanoil Treatment

Now that you know the best way to hide white hair for brunettes, you can choose the option that works best for you. Whether you decide to dye your hair, use hair extensions, or try hair mascara, make sure to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
