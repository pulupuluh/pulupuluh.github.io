How To Style Short Natural Pixie Hair

Written by Frank Mar 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance and stylish haircut, a short natural pixie cut might be just what you need. This haircut is perfect for those who have busy lifestyles and don't have a lot of time to spend on their hair. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to style short natural pixie hair so that you can look your best every day.

How to Style Short Natural Pixie Hair

Styling short natural pixie hair is easy and doesn't require a lot of effort. Here are some tips on how to style your hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Use a towel to dry your hair and then apply a small amount of styling gel or mousse to your hair.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to create the shape you want. This might involve lifting the hair at the crown or sweeping it to the side.
  4. Use a blow dryer to set the style, using your fingers or a brush to shape your hair as you go.
  5. Finish the style with a hairspray or styling wax to hold it in place.

That's it! Your short natural pixie hair is now styled and ready to go.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short natural pixie hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Use a towel to dry your hair and then apply a small amount of styling gel or mousse to your hair.
  3. Take a comb or your fingers and create the shape you want.
  4. Use a blow dryer to set the style, using your fingers or a brush to shape your hair as you go.
  5. Finish the style with a hairspray or styling wax to hold it in place.

Remember to take your time and be gentle with your hair. Short natural pixie hair is delicate and can easily be damaged if you're too rough with it.

Tips for Styling Short Natural Pixie Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your short natural pixie hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Don't use too much product or your hair might look greasy or weighed down.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you're using a blow dryer or other heat styling tool.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, or other hair accessories to add some variety to your look.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my short natural pixie hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you might need to wash your hair every day or every other day. If your hair is dry or you don't sweat much, you can probably get away with washing it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I color my short natural pixie hair?

A: Yes, you can color your hair if you want. Just make sure to use a gentle hair dye that won't damage your hair.

Hair Care for Short Natural Pixie Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your short natural pixie hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

With these tips and some practice, you'll be able to style your short natural pixie hair in no time. Remember to be patient and have fun experimenting with different looks!
