How To Create Curls In Black Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As a black man, you may be looking to switch up your hairstyle and add some curls to your hair. Whether you have short hair or long hair, creating curls can be a fun and unique way to change your look. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to create curls in black men's hair.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Create Curls in Black Men's Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Creating Curls in Black Men's Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

How to Create Curls in Black Men's Hair:

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first talk about the different ways you can create curls in black men's hair:

  • Using a curling iron
  • Using hot rollers
  • Using flexi rods
  • Using a perm rod set
  • Using a twist-out or braid-out method

Each method has its own unique benefits and drawbacks. Using a curling iron or hot rollers can create tighter curls, while using flexi rods or perm rod sets can create looser, more natural-looking curls. The twist-out or braid-out method is another option that can create curls without the use of heat.

Step-by-Step Guide:

For this guide, we will focus on using flexi rods to create curls in black men's hair. Here are the steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, use a blow dryer to dry it completely.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair to help hold the curls. This can be a curl cream, mousse, or gel.
  3. Section your hair into small sections. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl will be.
  4. Take one section of hair and wrap it around the flexi rod, starting at the base of the hair and rolling it all the way up to the scalp.
  5. Bend the ends of the flexi rod to secure it in place.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 for each section of hair.
  7. Once all sections are wrapped, let your hair dry completely. This can take several hours, so it's best to do this before bed and let your hair dry overnight.
  8. Once your hair is completely dry, remove the flexi rods from your hair.
  9. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create your desired look.
  10. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Creating Curls in Black Men's Hair:

Here are some additional tips to help you create the perfect curls:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  • Don't wrap the hair too tightly around the flexi rod or perm rod, as this can cause breakage or damage to the hair.
  • Sleep with a satin or silk pillowcase to help maintain your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.

FAQs:

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The longevity of your curls will depend on the method you use and how well you maintain them. Using a styling product and sleeping with a satin or silk pillowcase can help your curls last longer.

Q: Can I create curls in short hair?

A: Yes, you can create curls in short hair using any of the methods mentioned in this guide. For shorter hair, you may want to use smaller flexi rods or perm rods to create tighter curls.

Q: Will creating curls damage my hair?

A: Using heat or improper techniques can cause damage to your hair. It's important to use a heat protectant spray and to wrap the hair around the flexi rod or perm rod loosely to prevent breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Curls:

To keep your curls looking their best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Creating curls in black men's hair can be a fun and unique way to switch up your hairstyle. Whether you use a curling iron or flexi rods, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy curls. With these tips and techniques, you'll be on your way to achieving the perfect curls.
