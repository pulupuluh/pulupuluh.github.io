Best Short Hair Styles For Ladies Over 60

Written by Robby Apr 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a woman over 60 looking for a new short hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we'll go over the best short hair styles for ladies over 60. From how to style them to hair care tips, we'll cover everything you need to know to make the most of your hair. Let's get started!

Description

As we age, our hair changes. It may become thinner, drier, or more brittle. That's why it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your hair type and face shape. Short hair is a great option for women over 60 because it's easy to manage and can make you look more youthful. Plus, there are so many short styles to choose from!

How To

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking at different short styles. Some popular options for women over 60 include pixie cuts, bobs, and shags.

When styling your hair, use products that are designed for your hair type. If you have thin hair, use a volumizing mousse to add body. If you have curly hair, use a curl-enhancing cream to define your curls. And always use a heat protectant when using hot tools like a flat iron or curling wand.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling a short bob:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling cream to your hair.
  3. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try a new style â you might be surprised at how much you love it!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair for women over 60:

Q: Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

A: No! In fact, a short hairstyle can make you look more youthful and vibrant.

Q: Can I still wear my hair curly if it's short?

A: Absolutely! There are many short styles that look great with curls.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, there are a few things you can do to keep your short hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-blocking spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a fabulous short hairstyle no matter your age. Remember, the key is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
