Best Short Haircuts For Square Faces 2020

Written by Bardi Jun 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a square face, you may find it difficult to choose the right haircut that complements your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll share the best short haircuts for square faces in 2020. So, without further ado, let's dive in.

Description

Square faces have strong jawlines and wide foreheads, which can make it difficult to find a suitable haircut. But with the right cut, you can soften your features and highlight your best assets. Short haircuts are a great option for square faces as they create angles and structure that complement your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for your square face, you want to create balance and soften your features. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Soften the Jawline

Avoid haircuts that accentuate your jawline, such as blunt bobs or straight-across bangs. Instead, opt for styles that soften your jawline, such as layered bobs or side-swept bangs.

2. Add Height

Add height to your hair to balance out your wide forehead. Pixie cuts or short shaggy cuts with volume at the crown can achieve this.

3. Emphasize the Cheekbones

Highlighting your cheekbones can bring attention away from your jawline. Short haircuts with volume around the crown, such as a layered pixie cut, can achieve this.

Best Short Haircuts for Square Faces 2020

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut, here are the best short haircuts for square faces in 2020:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that suits square faces. It adds height to your hair and emphasizes your cheekbones. It's also low maintenance and easy to style.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

The bob with side-swept bangs is a great option for square faces as it softens your jawline and adds volume to the crown. The side-swept bangs also highlight your cheekbones.

3. Layered Bob

The layered bob is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape. For square faces, opt for a layered bob with volume at the crown and soft layers around the jawline.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts

Now that you've chosen your haircut, here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Towel dry your hair gently.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to your damp hair. This will help hold your hairstyle in place.

3. Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. For a pixie cut, blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction to create height at the crown.

4. Style your hair

Use a styling tool, such as a flat iron or curling wand, to add texture and shape to your hair. For a bob with side-swept bangs, curl the ends of your hair away from your face.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your haircut:

1. Schedule regular trims

Short haircuts need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and style.

2. Use the right products

Use products that suit your hair type and the style of your haircut. This will help keep your hair looking healthy and styled.

3. Protect your hair from heat damage

Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

What are the best short haircuts for square faces?

The best short haircuts for square faces are pixie cuts, bobs with side-swept bangs, and layered bobs with volume at the crown.

How do I style short haircuts?

Start by washing your hair, applying a styling product, and blow-drying your hair. Use a styling tool to add texture and shape to your hair.

How do I maintain short haircuts?

Schedule regular trims, use the right products, and protect your hair from heat damage.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require minimal hair care compared to longer hairstyles. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use a conditioner

Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair moisturized and soft.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

And there you have it, the best short haircuts for square faces in 2020. Choose the right haircut for your face shape, style it with our step-by-step guide, and maintain it with our tips and FAQs. With the right haircut and hair care, you'll look and feel your best.
