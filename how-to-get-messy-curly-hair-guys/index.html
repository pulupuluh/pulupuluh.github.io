How To Get Messy Curly Hair Guys

Are you tired of your curly hair looking too neat and polished? Do you want to rock the messy, effortless look? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss how to get messy curly hair for guys. We will cover everything from how to style your hair to hair care tips. Let's get started!

  • How to Get Messy Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Messy Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Get Messy Curly Hair

The first step in achieving messy curly hair is to start with the right haircut. A layered cut will allow your curls to fall naturally and create movement. Make sure to communicate with your hairstylist about the level of messiness you want to achieve.

Next, you will need to invest in the right hair products. Look for a lightweight, texturizing spray or cream that will enhance your curls without weighing them down. A sea salt spray is a great option as it will add volume and texture to your hair.

Products you will need:

  • Texturizing spray or cream
  • Sea salt spray
  • Hair dryer
  • Hair diffuser attachment

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a texturizing spray or cream to your hair and work it through with your fingers.

Step 2: Use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and add volume.

Step 3: Once your hair is dry, apply a sea salt spray to create texture and hold. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create a messy, tousled look.

Step 4: Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Curly Hair:

Now that you have achieved the perfect messy curly hair look, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your curls.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a daily basis as it can damage your hair.

FAQs:

Q: Can I achieve messy curly hair with short hair?

A: Absolutely! The key is to create texture and movement with your hair. A layered cut and the right hair products can help you achieve a messy, tousled look even with shorter hair.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's best to wash curly hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair:

Curly hair requires a little extra care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you care for your curly locks:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair as it can dry it out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can cause breakage and frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect messy curly hair look. Remember to be patient with your hair and experiment with different products and techniques until you find what works best for you. Good luck!
