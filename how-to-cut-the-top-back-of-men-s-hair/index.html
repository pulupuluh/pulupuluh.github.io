How To Cut The Top Back Of Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read

When it comes to men's haircuts, the top back can be one of the trickiest areas to get right. However, with the right tools, techniques, and a little bit of practice, anyone can master this crucial aspect of men's grooming. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to cut the top back of men's hair, as well as provide some tips and tricks to help you get the best results possible.

Description

The top back of a man's hair refers to the section of hair at the crown of the head, just above the occipital bone. This area is often the focus of men's haircuts, as it can greatly affect the overall look and style of the hair. Cutting the top back of men's hair requires precision and attention to detail, as any mistakes in this area can be very noticeable.

How to Cut the Top Back of Men's Hair

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting the top back of men's hair is to gather all of the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror (preferably one that can be angled or adjusted). If you're using electric clippers, you'll also need a guard attachment.

Step 2: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut accurately, and can also make it harder to see the natural fall and texture of the hair. Comb the hair straight back from the forehead, and use a clip or hair tie to keep it in place.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Next, you'll need to determine the length you want to cut the hair. This will depend on the style you're going for, as well as the natural texture and thickness of the hair. Use the comb to lift up a section of hair at the crown of the head, and hold it between your fingers at the desired length. Use the scissors to cut across the hair in a straight line.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the first section, you'll need to blend the hair to create a seamless transition between the top back and the rest of the hair. To do this, use the comb to lift up a section of hair from the sides or back of the head, and hold it at the same length as the first section. Cut the hair in a similar straight line, making sure to blend the two sections together.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you've cut the top back of the hair, take a step back and check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments or touch-ups, taking care not to remove too much hair or create any unevenness. Use the comb to style the hair as desired, and finish with a styling product if desired.

Tips for Cutting the Top Back of Men's Hair

  • Take your time and work carefully, especially if you're new to hair cutting.
  • Use the mirror to check your work from multiple angles, including the sides and back of the head.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter, as it's easier to remove more hair than to add it back.
  • Consider using thinning shears or a razor to create texture and movement in the hair.
  • Practice on a mannequin head or with a willing volunteer before attempting to cut someone's hair for the first time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I cut the top back of my hair?

This will depend on the individual's hair type and growth rate, as well as the desired length and style. As a general rule, most men should get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their desired look.

Can I cut the top back of my hair myself?

While it's certainly possible to cut your own hair, we recommend leaving it to a professional if you're unsure of your skills or techniques. A trained stylist can help you achieve the best results possible, and can also offer advice and guidance on maintaining your hair at home.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

Don't panic! Mistakes happen, especially when cutting hair. If you make a mistake, try to even out the hair as best you can, and consider consulting with a professional stylist for further assistance.

Hair Care for Men

In addition to regular haircuts, it's important for men to take care of their hair on a daily basis. This can include using high-quality shampoos and conditioners, avoiding harsh styling products, and protecting the hair from heat and sun damage. A healthy diet and lifestyle can also contribute to strong, healthy hair.

Cutting the top back of men's hair can be a challenging task, but with the right tools and techniques, anyone can achieve great results. Whether you're looking for a classic style or something more modern, taking the time to master this important aspect of men's grooming can help you look and feel your best.
