Mens Haircut Short Sides Back Long Top How To Get The Look In 2023

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, the mens haircut short sides back long top is a great option to consider. This style has been popular for several years now, and in 2023, it's still going strong. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to get this haircut, share some tips on styling it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this popular style.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get the Look

The mens haircut short sides back long top is a classic style that's both timeless and modern. This haircut is characterized by short hair on the sides and back, while the top is left longer and styled in various ways.

To get this look, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist. They will be able to recommend the best haircut for your face shape and hair type. You can also bring in a photo of the style you want to show your stylist.

When getting this haircut, be sure to ask for a tapered cut on the sides and back. This means that the hair gradually gets shorter as it goes down. The top should be left long enough to style however you like.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the mens haircut short sides back long top:

  1. Visit a professional hairstylist and show them a photo of the style you want.
  2. Ask for a tapered cut on the sides and back.
  3. Leave the top long enough to style.
  4. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling the mens haircut short sides back long top:

  • Use a quality hair styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting a fade on the sides for a more dramatic look.
  • Try using a hair dryer to add volume to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut short sides back long top:

Q: Is this style suitable for all hair types?
A: Yes, this style can be adapted for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. Q: How often do I need to get this haircut?
A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look. Q: Can I still style my hair if it's thinning?
A: Yes, you can still style your hair if it's thinning. Consider using a hair volumizing product to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your mens haircut short sides back long top looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Consider using a hair serum to add shine and control frizz.

Overall, the mens haircut short sides back long top is a classic style that's still going strong in 2023. With the right haircut and styling products, you can achieve this trendy look and make it your own. Be sure to follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your hair looking its best.
