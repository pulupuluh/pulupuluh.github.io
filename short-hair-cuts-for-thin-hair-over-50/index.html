Short Hair Cuts For Thin Hair Over 50

Written by Aprilia Feb 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out, and it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that suits us. Short haircuts for thin hair over 50 are an excellent option as they not only look stylish but also make hair appear fuller. In this post, we will discuss how to choose the right short haircut for thin hair over 50, tips on styling, and hair care.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Thin Hair over 50

When choosing a short haircut for thin hair over 50, it is essential to consider your face shape and hair texture. Here are some tips on how to choose the right short haircut:

  • Consider getting a layered haircut as it adds volume to thin hair.
  • A pixie cut is a great option for those with a round or heart-shaped face.
  • A bob haircut is perfect for those with a square or oval face.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, a short shag haircut is an excellent choice.

It is also important to discuss your hair type and desired hairstyle with your hairstylist. They can recommend the best short haircut for your hair and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cut Short Hair for Thin Hair over 50

If you are considering cutting your hair short, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: the crown, sides, and back.
  3. Starting from the back, use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to your desired length.
  4. Move on to the sides and cut them to match the back.
  5. Finally, cut the crown section, making sure it blends well with the rest of the hair.
  6. Use a razor to add texture and volume to your hair.
  7. Style your hair using a hairdryer and a round brush.

Tips for Styling Short Hair for Thin Hair over 50

Styling short hair for thin hair over 50 can be a bit of a challenge. Here are some tips to help you:

  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root lift spray, to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy or greasy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to the roots.
  • Use a round brush to add curl or wave to your hair.
  • Avoid using a straightener or curling iron on thin hair as it can damage the hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I cut my short hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short haircut.

2. Can I color my short hair if it is thin?

Yes, you can color your hair, but it is important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and does not damage your hair.

3. Can I wear a short haircut if I have a double chin?

Yes, you can wear a short haircut even if you have a double chin. A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can help to slim down the face.

Hair Care for Short Hair for Thin Hair over 50

Proper hair care is essential to keep your short hair healthy and luscious. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for thin hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to moisturize and nourish your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein and vitamins to promote hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Short haircuts for thin hair over 50 can be both stylish and practical. By following these tips, you can choose the right short haircut, style it, and take care of it to keep it looking its best. Remember to consult your hairstylist for their expert opinion on what will suit you best.
