How To Know The Hairstyle That Fits You

Written by William Aug 28, 2023 · 8 min read

When it comes to hairstyling, choosing the right hairstyle can make a big difference in your overall look. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personality. In this guide, we’ll provide you with tips and tricks on how to know the hairstyle that fits you and help you make a confident decision.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Know the Hairstyle that Fits You
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Know the Hairstyle that Fits You

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to identify your face shape. There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Your face shape will help determine which hairstyles will look best on you.

Once you’ve identified your face shape, consider your hair texture. Do you have straight, wavy, curly, or kinky hair? Different hairstyles will work better with different hair textures.

Next, think about your personality and lifestyle. Are you someone who likes to keep things low-maintenance or do you enjoy experimenting with different styles? Do you need a hairstyle that’s easy to maintain while working out or running errands?

Finally, consider any specific features you want to highlight or downplay. For example, if you have a prominent forehead, you may want to consider bangs to draw attention away from it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture.
  3. Think about your personality and lifestyle.
  4. Consider any specific features you want to highlight or downplay.
  5. Research different hairstyles that complement your face shape and hair texture.
  6. Bring pictures of your desired hairstyles to your hairstylist to ensure they are achievable with your hair texture and face shape.
  7. Ask your hairstylist for their opinion and recommendations. They are experts in their field and can provide valuable insight.
  8. Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to get a visual representation of how the hairstyle will look on you.
  9. Consider taking baby steps if you’re unsure about a drastic change. Start with a trim or subtle highlights to ease into a new look.
  10. Lastly, trust your instincts and choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair’s natural texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle. A style that looks great on thin hair may not work for thick hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles. Hair grows back, so it’s okay to take risks!
  • Be realistic about the amount of time and effort you’re willing to put into styling your hair.
  • If you’re unsure about a drastic change, consult with a hairstylist or try on wigs to get a feel for the hairstyle before committing.
  • Take care of your hair to ensure it looks healthy and vibrant. This includes regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and using quality hair products.

FAQs

What hairstyle suits a round face?

Hairstyles that add height and volume, such as long layers or a side-swept bang, complement a round face shape. Avoid blunt cuts or chin-length bobs, as they can make the face appear wider.

What is the best hairstyle for an oval face?

An oval face shape is the most versatile, and most hairstyles will suit this face shape. Consider a lob (long bob), a layered cut, or a side part.

What hairstyle suits a square face?

Hairstyles that soften the angles of a square face shape, such as a side-swept fringe or a long layered cut, are most flattering. Avoid blunt cuts or straight bangs, as they can accentuate the squareness of the face.

What is the best hairstyle for a heart-shaped face?

Hairstyles that balance the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin, such as a long bob or a side-swept fringe, complement a heart-shaped face. Avoid styles that add height to the crown, such as a high ponytail, as they can make the forehead appear larger.

Hair Care Tips

Choosing the right hairstyle is only half the battle. To ensure your hair looks and feels its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in quality hair products, such as a heat protectant spray, to protect your hair from damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to choose the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personality. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
