Best Hairstyle For Round Face Hair In 2023

Written by Aprilia Sep 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't seem to flatter your round face? Well, worry no more because we've got you covered with the best hairstyle for round face hair in this year 2023. Read on to find out the perfect hairstyle for you!

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with fuller cheeks and a rounded chin. The best hairstyle for round face hair is one that elongates the face shape and adds more definition to the cheekbones and jawline. It's essential to choose a style that complements your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.

How to

To achieve the best hairstyle for round face hair, you'll need to consider various factors such as the length, texture, and style of your hair. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Identify your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all similar in size, you have a round face shape.

Step 2: Choose the right length

Opt for a hairstyle that elongates your face shape, such as long layers or a shoulder-length cut. Avoid styles that end at the chin or ear level, as they can make your face look wider.

Step 3: Add volume

Adding volume to the crown of your head can make your face look slimmer. Try a layered cut with volume at the top or add waves or curls to your hair.

Step 4: Define your cheekbones and jawline

Choose a style that adds definition to your cheekbones and jawline, such as a side-swept fringe or a bob that ends just below the jawline.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the best hairstyle for round face hair:

  • Avoid blunt bangs or straight haircuts that end at the chin or jawline.
  • Consider a side-parted style to add more definition to your face shape.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head or crown.
  • Try a layered cut to add movement and texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands or clips to add more interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for round face hair:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that end at the chin or jawline, blunt bangs, and straight haircuts. These styles can make your face look wider and shorter.

What is the best length for round face hair?

Long layers or a shoulder-length cut are the best lengths for round face hair. They elongate your face shape and add more definition to your cheekbones and jawline.

What hairstyles can I try if I have short hair?

If you have short hair, try a pixie cut or a bob that ends just below the jawline. You can also add volume to the crown of your head to make your face look slimmer.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to ensure it looks healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to keep your hair in great condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for round face hair, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist! Remember to consider your face shape, hair length, and style when choosing the perfect hairstyle. With the right cut and style, you'll look and feel your best.
