Which Hairstyle Suits Big Face A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Feb 24, 2023 · 8 min read

Having a big face can sometimes be a challenge when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. You want to enhance your features and bring out your best self, but not every hairstyle suits big face shapes. In this article, we’ll guide you through the best hairstyles for big faces, including how to style them and some hair care tips to keep your locks looking healthy.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Big Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  4. Tips to Keep Your Hair Looking Healthy
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Big Face Shapes

Description

Before we dive into the hairstyles that suit big face shapes, let’s first define what we mean by a big face. A big face is typically characterized by a wider forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If you have a big face, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that balances out these features and creates an overall harmonious look.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Big Face

When choosing a hairstyle for big face shapes, you’ll want to consider a few key factors:

  1. Length: Long hair can elongate a big face, while short hair can make it look wider. Consider opting for a medium-length hairstyle, such as a lob or shoulder-length cut.
  2. Layers: Layers can add dimension and texture to your hair, which can help balance out a big face. Consider getting long layers or face-framing layers.
  3. Bangs: Bangs can help shorten a big forehead and balance out a wide face. Consider getting side-swept bangs or curtain bangs.
  4. Curls: Soft curls can add volume to your hair and help create a more balanced look for big face shapes. Consider using a curling iron or flexi rods to create loose curls.

Hairstyles to Avoid for Big Face Shapes

While there are plenty of hairstyles that suit big face shapes, there are also a few styles to avoid:

  • Slicked-Back Hair: This style can emphasize a wide forehead and make a big face look even wider.
  • Super Short Pixie Cut: This style can make a big face look wider and more angular.
  • Center Part: A center part can emphasize a wide forehead and make a big face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen a hairstyle that suits your big face shape, it’s time to style it. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Wash and Condition: Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type. If you have thick or curly hair, you may want to use a deep conditioner to help detangle your locks.
  2. Towel-Dry: Use a towel to gently dry your hair, removing as much moisture as possible.
  3. Apply Products: Apply any styling products you’re using, such as mousse or curling cream.
  4. Blow-Dry: Use a blow dryer and a round brush to dry your hair. If you’re going for a straight look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair once it’s dry.
  5. Curl: If you’re going for a curly look, use a curling iron or flexi rods to create loose curls.
  6. Finish: Finish your look with a hairspray or other styling products to hold your style in place.

Tips to Keep Your Hair Looking Healthy

Now that you’ve chosen a hairstyle and styled your hair, it’s important to keep your locks looking healthy. Here are some tips to help:

  • Trim Your Hair Regularly: Regular trims can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use Heat Protectant: If you’re using heat tools, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Deep Condition: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Avoid Over-Washing: Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

FAQs

What hairstyles suit round faces?

Hairstyles that suit round faces typically include long layers, side-swept bangs, and soft curls. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a super short pixie cut.

What hairstyles suit heart-shaped faces?

Hairstyles that suit heart-shaped faces typically include long layers, side-swept bangs, and bobs. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail.

Hair Care for Big Face Shapes

When it comes to hair care for big face shapes, it’s important to choose products that suit your hair type. If you have thick or curly hair, you may want to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help detangle your locks. If you have fine hair, look for volumizing products that can help add body and texture to your hair.

Remember to trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy, and avoid over-washing your hair to prevent dryness and damage. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you’ll be able to enhance your big face shape and look your best.
