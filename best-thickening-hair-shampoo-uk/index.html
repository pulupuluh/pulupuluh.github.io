Best Thickening Hair Shampoo Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Apr 08, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of your thin, lifeless hair? Do you want to add volume and thickness to your hair? If yes, then you must try the best thickening hair shampoo UK. This article will guide you through the best thickening hair shampoos available in the UK and how to use them.

What is Thickening Hair Shampoo?

Thickening hair shampoo is specially formulated to add volume and thickness to your hair. It contains ingredients that help to strengthen the hair shaft, create fuller hair, and reduce breakage. Thickening hair shampoos are perfect for people with thin or fine hair who want to add volume and bounce to their hair.

How to Choose the Best Thickening Hair Shampoo UK?

Choosing the best thickening hair shampoo can be a daunting task as there are so many options available in the market. Here are some factors that you should consider before buying a thickening hair shampoo:

1. Hair Type

You should choose a thickening hair shampoo based on your hair type. If you have oily hair, then you should look for a shampoo that is light and does not weigh down your hair. If you have dry hair, then you should choose a shampoo that moisturizes your hair and prevents breakage.

2. Ingredients

Look for a shampoo that contains ingredients such as biotin, keratin, collagen, and wheat protein. These ingredients help to strengthen the hair shaft, add volume, and reduce breakage.

3. Brand Reputation

Choose a shampoo from a reputable brand that has positive reviews from customers. This will ensure that you are buying a high-quality product that delivers results.

Top 5 Best Thickening Hair Shampoo UK

Here are the top 5 best thickening hair shampoos available in the UK:

1. Nioxin Cleanser Shampoo

Nioxin Cleanser Shampoo is a popular choice for people with thin or fine hair. It contains a blend of botanicals and amino acids that help to strengthen the hair shaft, create fuller hair, and reduce breakage. It is also free from sulfates and parabens, making it gentle on the hair.

2. L'Oreal Professionnel Serioxyl Shampoo

L'Oreal Professionnel Serioxyl Shampoo is another popular choice for people with thin or fine hair. It contains a blend of ingredients such as Glucoboost, Incell, and Stemoxydine that help to create thicker, fuller hair. It also has a fresh and invigorating scent.

3. TRESemme Collagen+ Fullness Shampoo

TRESemme Collagen+ Fullness Shampoo is a great option for people on a budget. It contains collagen and glycerin that help to add volume and thickness to the hair. It also has a pleasant scent and is suitable for all hair types.

4. OGX Biotin & Collagen Shampoo

OGX Biotin & Collagen Shampoo is a vegan and cruelty-free option that contains biotin, collagen, and wheat protein. These ingredients help to strengthen the hair shaft, add volume, and reduce breakage. It also has a pleasant scent and is free from sulfates and parabens.

5. Philip Kingsley Body Building Shampoo

Philip Kingsley Body Building Shampoo is a premium option that contains keratin, wheat protein, and hydrolyzed elastin. These ingredients help to create thicker, fuller hair and add shine to the hair. It is also free from sulfates and parabens and is suitable for all hair types.

How to Use Thickening Hair Shampoo?

Here are the steps to use thickening hair shampoo:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.

Step 2: Apply Shampoo

Apply a small amount of thickening hair shampoo to your scalp and massage gently with your fingertips. Work the shampoo into a lather and then rinse with warm water.

Step 3: Repeat

If necessary, repeat the shampooing process to ensure that your hair is thoroughly clean.

Step 4: Condition

Apply a conditioner to the ends of your hair to add moisture and prevent breakage. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes and then rinse with warm water.

Tips for Using Thickening Hair Shampoo

Here are some tips for using thickening hair shampoo:

1. Use a Small Amount

Use a small amount of shampoo as using too much can weigh down your hair.

2. Massage Gently

Massage the shampoo into your scalp gently with your fingertips to avoid damaging your hair.

3. Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove all traces of shampoo.

4. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently after washing to avoid breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Can Thickening Hair Shampoo Cause Hair Loss?

No, thickening hair shampoo does not cause hair loss. In fact, it can help to strengthen the hair shaft and reduce breakage.

2. Can Thickening Hair Shampoo be Used on Colored Hair?

Yes, thickening hair shampoo can be used on colored hair. However, you should choose a shampoo that is specifically formulated for color-treated hair.

3. How Often Should I Use Thickening Hair Shampoo?

You can use thickening hair shampoo every time you wash your hair. However, if you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Here are some hair care tips for people with thick hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently, especially when it is wet.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.

3. Trim Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

4. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.

Conclusion

Thickening hair shampoo is a great way to add volume and thickness to your hair. Choose a shampoo based on your hair type, ingredients, and brand reputation. Use the shampoo as directed and follow the tips for best results. Remember to take care of your hair by using a wide-tooth comb, avoiding heat styling, and trimming regularly.
