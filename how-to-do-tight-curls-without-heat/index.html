How To Do Tight Curls Without Heat A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 10, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools to achieve the perfect curls? Not only can they damage your hair, but they can also take up a lot of time. Luckily, there are ways to achieve tight curls without using any heat. In this article, we’ll show you a step-by-step guide on how to do tight curls without heat.

How to Do Tight Curls Without Heat

Before we dive into the steps, let’s talk about the products and tools you’ll need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Small hair elastics
  • Hair mousse or curl enhancing cream
  • Hair spray

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner. Gently comb through your hair with a wide-tooth comb to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Apply mousse or curl enhancing cream

Apply a small amount of hair mousse or curl enhancing cream to your hair. Be sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Divide your hair into small sections

Divide your hair into small sections, about 1-2 inches wide. Use small hair elastics to secure each section.

Step 4: Twist each section

Take one section of hair and twist it tightly. Keep twisting until the hair starts to coil up on itself.

Step 5: Secure the twist with a hair elastic

Once the hair is coiled up, secure it with a small hair elastic.

Step 6: Repeat on all sections

Repeat steps 4 and 5 on all sections of your hair.

Step 7: Let your hair air dry

Leave the hair elastics in place and let your hair air dry. This can take several hours, so it’s best to do this before bed and leave them in overnight.

Step 8: Remove the hair elastics

Once your hair is completely dry, remove the hair elastics.

Step 9: Separate the curls

Use your fingers to gently separate the curls. Be careful not to pull too hard or you may lose the curl.

Step 10: Apply hair spray

Apply a light mist of hair spray to set the curls in place.

Tips for Achieving Tight Curls Without Heat

  • Use a styling cream or mousse that is specifically designed for curly hair.
  • For a more natural look, try twisting your hair in different directions.
  • For tighter curls, twist smaller sections of hair.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before removing the hair elastics.

FAQs

Can I use this method on any hair type?

Yes, this method can be used on any hair type. However, results may vary depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

How long will the curls last?

The curls can last up to 3 days, depending on how well you take care of them.

Hair Care for Tight Curls Without Heat

To keep your curls looking their best, make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help prevent your hair from drying out. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.

Now that you know how to do tight curls without heat, you can achieve the perfect curls without damaging your hair. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
