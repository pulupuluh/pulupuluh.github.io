How To Soften Frizzy Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank May 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how frustrating it can be to deal with frizz. Sometimes, no matter how much you try, it seems impossible to keep your curls looking defined and smooth. Fortunately, there are ways to soften frizzy curly hair and achieve the perfect look. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to soften frizzy curly hair.

How to Soften Frizzy Curly Hair

Softening frizzy curly hair requires a combination of the right products and techniques. Here are some steps you can take to achieve smoother, more defined curls:

Step 1: Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

The right shampoo and conditioner can make all the difference when it comes to softening frizzy curly hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair and contain ingredients such as argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter. These ingredients can help to moisturize and nourish your hair, which can result in softer, more manageable curls.

Step 2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can also help to soften frizzy curly hair. After washing your hair, apply a lightweight leave-in conditioner to your damp hair, focusing on the ends. This can help to add moisture to your hair and prevent it from drying out, which can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply a Curl Cream

A curl cream can help to define your curls and keep them looking smooth and frizz-free. Look for a lightweight curl cream that won't weigh your hair down or make it feel greasy. Apply a small amount to your damp hair, scrunching your curls as you go, and let your hair air dry.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

If you prefer to use a blow dryer, using a diffuser attachment can help to soften frizzy curly hair. A diffuser can help to distribute heat evenly, which can prevent your hair from becoming too dry and frizzy.

Tips for Softening Frizzy Curly Hair

In addition to the steps outlined above, there are a few other tips you can follow to soften frizzy curly hair:

Tip 1: Avoid Heat

Heat can be damaging to curly hair, causing it to become dry and frizzy. Try to avoid using heated styling tools as much as possible, or use them sparingly.

Tip 2: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and frizzy. Try to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week, and use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Sleeping on a cotton pillowcase can cause friction and breakage, leading to frizz. Try using a silk or satin pillowcase, or wrap your hair in a silk scarf before bed.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about softening frizzy curly hair:

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often.

Can I use regular shampoo on my curly hair?

Regular shampoo can be too harsh for curly hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils. Look for a shampoo that is specifically designed for curly hair, or use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.

How can I prevent frizz in humid weather?

Humidity can cause curly hair to become frizzy. To prevent this, try using a humidity-resistant hairspray or styling cream, or wear your hair in a protective style such as a bun or braids.

Hair Care for Softening Frizzy Curly Hair

In addition to the tips and tricks outlined above, taking good care of your hair can help to soften frizzy curly hair. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

Tip 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends, which can make your hair look frizzy and damaged. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, causing it to become dry and frizzy. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun's rays.

Tip 3: Be Gentle with Your Hair

Curly hair can be fragile, so it's important to be gentle with it. When brushing or combing your hair, use a wide-toothed comb or a brush specifically designed for curly hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can soften frizzy curly hair and achieve the perfect look. Remember, it's all about finding the right products and techniques that work for your hair type and lifestyle.
