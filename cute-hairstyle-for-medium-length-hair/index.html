Cute Hairstyle For Medium Length Hair How To Style And Care For Your Hair

Written by Robby Mar 01, 2023 · 8 min read
Cute Hairstyle for Medium Length Hair | How to Style and Care for Your Hair

If you have medium length hair, you know how versatile it can be. From braids to buns, there are so many cute hairstyle options to choose from. In this article, we'll show you some of the best ways to style your medium length hair, as well as tips for keeping it healthy and looking great.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cute Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair
  • Tips for Keeping Your Hair Healthy and Looking Great
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

How to Style Your Medium Length Hair

When it comes to styling your medium length hair, the options are endless. Here are some of the most popular styles:

Braids

Braids are a great way to add texture and interest to your hair. You can do a French braid, a fishtail braid, or a simple three-strand braid. You can also try braiding your hair into a crown or a side braid.

Buns

Buns are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. You can do a top knot, a low bun, or a messy bun. You can also add braids or twists to your bun for a more interesting look.

Ponytails

Ponytails are a quick and easy way to style your hair. You can do a high ponytail, a low ponytail, or a side ponytail. You can also add braids or twists to your ponytail for a more interesting look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cute Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Here are some step-by-step guides to some of the most popular cute hairstyles for medium length hair:

The French Braid

1. Brush your hair to get rid of any tangles.

2. Divide your hair into three equal sections.

3. Cross the right section over the middle section.

4. Cross the left section over the middle section.

5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you reach the end of your hair.

6. Secure the braid with a hair tie.

The Messy Bun

1. Brush your hair to get rid of any tangles.

2. Pull your hair back into a ponytail.

3. Twist the ponytail into a bun.

4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

5. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

The Side Braid

1. Brush your hair to get rid of any tangles.

2. Part your hair on one side.

3. Take a small section of hair near your part and divide it into three sections.

4. Cross the right section over the middle section.

5. Cross the left section over the middle section.

6. Add more hair to the right section and cross it over the middle section.

7. Add more hair to the left section and cross it over the middle section.

8. Continue adding hair and crossing the sections until you reach the end of your hair.

9. Secure the braid with a hair tie.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Healthy and Looking Great

Here are some tips for keeping your medium length hair healthy and looking great:

Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant. This will help to prevent damage to your hair.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Use a Deep Conditioner

Using a deep conditioner once a week will help to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils. Try to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

FAQs

How often should I wash my medium length hair?

You should wash your hair every other day or every three days.

How can I prevent split ends?

You can prevent split ends by getting regular trims and using a deep conditioner.

What is a good heat protectant to use?

A good heat protectant to use is the TRESemmé Thermal Creations Heat Tamer Spray.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for medium length hair:

Avoid Using Hot Water

Using hot water can strip your hair of its natural oils. Try to use lukewarm or cool water when washing your hair.

Avoid Brushing Wet Hair

Wet hair is more prone to breakage, so avoid brushing or combing your hair when it is wet.

Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase will help to prevent tangles and breakage while you sleep.

We hope you found these cute hairstyle and hair care tips helpful. With these tips, you'll be able to keep your medium length hair healthy and looking great!
