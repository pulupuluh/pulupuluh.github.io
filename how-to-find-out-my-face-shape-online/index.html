How To Find Out My Face Shape Online Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Knowing your face shape is crucial when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle and makeup. If you're unsure of your face shape, you can easily find out online. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding out your face shape online, along with some tips and FAQs.

Table of Content

  • How to Find Out My Face Shape Online
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Determining Your Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

How to Find Out My Face Shape Online

You can easily find out your face shape online through various face shape analyzers available on the internet. All you need is a clear photo of your face with your hair pulled back.

Step 1: Take a Clear Photo

Find a well-lit area and take a clear photo of your face with your hair pulled back. Make sure your face is in full view and your hair is not covering any part of your face.

Step 2: Upload Your Photo

Go to any face shape analyzer website and upload your photo. Some popular face shape analyzer websites are:

  • Face++
  • FaceShape
  • SheKnows

Step 3: Analyze Your Results

The face shape analyzer will analyze your photo and provide you with your face shape. It will also give you suggestions on the best hairstyles and makeup for your face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to find out your face shape online:

  1. Take a clear photo of your face with your hair pulled back.
  2. Go to any face shape analyzer website.
  3. Upload your photo.
  4. Wait for the website to analyze your photo.
  5. Read the results and suggestions provided by the website.

Tips for Determining Your Face Shape

If you don't want to use a face shape analyzer, you can determine your face shape by following these tips:

  • Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  • Use a washable marker to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  • Step back and look at the shape you traced.
  • Determine which of the following face shapes best describes the outline you traced:
    • Oval
    • Round
    • Square
    • Heart
    • Diamond
    • Triangle

FAQs

What is the most common face shape?

The most common face shape is oval, followed by round and square.

How many face shapes are there?

There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangle.

Can my face shape change?

Yes, your face shape can change due to various factors such as weight gain or loss, aging, and hairstyle.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Once you know your face shape, you can choose the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Oval: Any hairstyle suits oval face shape, but avoid hairstyles that cover your face.
  • Round: Go for hairstyles that add height and volume to your crown area.
  • Square: Soften your jawline with wavy, layered hairstyles.
  • Heart: Go for hairstyles that add volume to your chin area.
  • Diamond: Try hairstyles that add width to your forehead and chin area.
  • Triangle: Go for hairstyles that add volume to your crown area.

Now that you know how to find out your face shape online, you can choose the right hairstyle and makeup that flatters your face shape. Happy styling!
