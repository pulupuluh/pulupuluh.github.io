Short Hairstyles For Fine Straight Hair 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 20, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have fine straight hair, you know the struggle of finding the perfect hairstyle that won’t make your locks look flat and lifeless. Fortunately, 2020 brought us a ton of trendy and chic short hairstyles that are perfect for fine straight hair. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about short hairstyles for fine straight hair in 2020, including how to style them, tips for maintaining them, and more.

  • How to Style Short Hair for Fine Straight Hair

Description

Short hair is a fantastic option for fine straight hair, as it adds volume and texture to your locks. In 2020, we’ve seen a ton of short hairstyles that are perfect for fine straight hair, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shags. These styles can be customized to suit any face shape and personal style, making them a versatile and trendy option for anyone with fine straight hair.

How to Style Short Hair for Fine Straight Hair

The key to styling short hair for fine straight hair is to add volume and texture. This can be achieved through a variety of techniques, including blow-drying, curling, and using texturizing products. When styling short hair, it’s important to use products that won’t weigh down your locks, as this can make your hair look flat and lifeless.

Blow-drying

Blow-drying your hair is one of the easiest ways to add volume and texture to fine straight hair. To do this, start by applying a volumizing mousse to your roots and blow-dry your hair upside down, using your fingers to lift your locks as you go. Once your hair is dry, flip it back over and use a round brush to add volume to the ends.

Curling

Curling your hair is another great way to add volume and texture to fine straight hair. To do this, start by applying a heat protectant to your locks, then use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair. Once your hair is curled, use your fingers to tousle the curls and add volume to your locks.

Texturizing Products

Using texturizing products, such as sea salt spray or dry shampoo, is another great way to add texture and volume to fine straight hair. These products can be applied to your roots and throughout your locks to add texture and lift to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Fine Straight Hair

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for fine straight hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots and blow-dry your hair upside down, using your fingers to lift your locks as you go.
  3. Once your hair is dry, flip it back over and use a round brush to add volume to the ends.
  4. Curl your hair using a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your locks.
  5. Use your fingers to tousle the curls and add volume to your hair.
  6. Apply a texturizing product, such as sea salt spray or dry shampoo, to your roots and throughout your locks to add texture and lift.
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Fine Straight Hair

Here are a few tips for maintaining short hair for fine straight hair:

  • Be sure to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your locks.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your short hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your locks healthy and hydrated.

FAQs about Short Hair for Fine Straight Hair

What is the best short hairstyle for fine straight hair?

The best short hairstyle for fine straight hair depends on your personal style and face shape. Pixie cuts, bobs, and shags are all popular options for anyone with fine straight hair.

How often should I wash my fine straight hair?

Fine straight hair should be washed every other day or every two days to avoid stripping your locks of natural oils.

What products should I use to style my short hair for fine straight hair?

When styling short hair for fine straight hair, it’s best to use lightweight products that won’t weigh down your locks, such as volumizing mousse, sea salt spray, and dry shampoo.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Straight Hair

Here are a few hair care tips for fine straight hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your locks.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your locks.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your locks healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock a trendy and chic short hairstyle for fine straight hair in 2020. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, bob, or shag, remember to add volume and texture to your locks for a voluminous and textured look.
