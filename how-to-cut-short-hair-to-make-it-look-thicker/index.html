How To Cut Short Hair To Make It Look Thicker A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair, you know that it can be challenging to make it look thick and full. Fortunately, there are a few tricks you can use to create the illusion of thicker hair. In this blog post, we will show you how to cut short hair to make it look thicker. We will discuss the best hairstyles, tips, and tricks to help you achieve that full-bodied look you desire.

Description

Short hair can be a challenge, especially if you want to make it look thicker. There are a few things you can do to create the illusion of fuller hair. The first step is to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair type. Once you have the right cut, you can use a few tricks to make your hair look thicker and fuller.

How to Cut Short Hair to Make it Look Thicker

Step 1: Choose the right haircut - The first step to making your short hair look thicker is to choose the right haircut. A layered cut is ideal for adding volume and texture to your hair. It is also important to choose a cut that complements your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Use the right products - Once you have the right cut, you can use the right products to make your hair look thicker. A volumizing mousse or spray can help add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a texturizing powder to add grip and volume to your hair.

Step 3: Use the right tools - The right tools can make a big difference when it comes to making your short hair look thicker. A round brush can help add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to create volume and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Shampoo and condition your hair - Before you start cutting your hair, you need to shampoo and condition it. This will help remove any dirt or product buildup and make your hair easier to work with.

Step 2: Section your hair - Once your hair is clean and dry, you need to section it. Use clips to separate your hair into sections, starting at the back of your head.

Step 3: Cut your hair - Begin cutting your hair, starting at the back of your head. Use a comb and scissors to cut your hair in small sections. Be sure to cut your hair in a layered style to add volume and texture.

Step 4: Style your hair - Once you have finished cutting your hair, you can style it. Use a round brush and a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a texturizing powder or spray to add grip and volume to your hair.

Tips

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume and texture to your hair.

- Use a round brush and a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

- Use a texturizing powder or spray to add grip and volume to your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my short hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you want to achieve. Generally, you should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired style.

Q: Can I make my short hair look thicker without cutting it?

A: Yes, you can use a volumizing mousse or spray, a round brush, and a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential if you want to make your short hair look thicker. Be sure to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed to add volume and texture to your hair. You should also avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. Finally, be sure to get regular trims to maintain your desired style and keep your hair healthy and strong.
