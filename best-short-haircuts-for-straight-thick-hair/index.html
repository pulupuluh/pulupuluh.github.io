Best Short Haircuts For Straight Thick Hair In 2023

Written by Robby Aug 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of long, thick hair that takes forever to style? Or maybe you're just looking for a fresh, new look? Either way, a short haircut might be just what you need. But with straight, thick hair, it can be hard to find a style that works. That's why we've put together this guide to the best short haircuts for straight thick hair in 2023. Read on for tips, tricks, and inspiration!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts are a great way to change up your look without sacrificing too much length. They can be low-maintenance, stylish, and easy to style. But when you have straight, thick hair, it can be tricky to find the right cut. You don't want something that will make your hair look even thicker, but you also don't want a cut that will make your hair look too thin. That's where these short haircuts come in. They're perfect for straight, thick hair and will leave you looking and feeling amazing.

How To

Before you head to the salon, it's important to know what kind of cut you want. Do you want something super short, like a pixie cut? Or maybe something a little longer, like a bob? Once you've decided on a general length, it's time to think about the details. Do you want bangs? Layers? A side part or a middle part?

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, for example, you might want to avoid super short cuts that will make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for a longer bob or a cut with layers. If you have a square face, on the other hand, a shorter cut with bangs can help soften your features.

Bring Inspiration Photos

When you're heading to the salon, it's always a good idea to bring inspiration photos. This will help your stylist understand exactly what you're looking for and make sure you're on the same page. Plus, it's always fun to look at pictures of cute haircuts!

Step By Step

Once you've got your new short haircut, it's time to start styling! Here's a basic step-by-step guide to styling short, straight, thick hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Use a straightening iron to straighten your hair, working in small sections.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray to add volume and texture.
  5. Use a hair wax or pomade to define your ends and add shine.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling short, straight, thick hair:

  • Invest in a high-quality straightening iron to make sure your hair stays straight and smooth.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different parts and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a hair wax or pomade to define your ends and add shine.

FAQ

What short haircuts work best for straight, thick hair?

The best short haircuts for straight, thick hair include pixie cuts, bobs, and lob cuts. These cuts work well because they help to thin out the hair and add movement.

How do I know if a short haircut will look good on me?

Before getting a short haircut, it's a good idea to consider your face shape and hair texture. You can also try using a virtual haircut simulator to see what different styles will look like on you.

Hair Care

When you have short, straight, thick hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to rocking the best short haircut for straight, thick hair in 2023. Happy styling!
