How To Use Fringe Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having the same old haircut? Well, why not try something new and fresh? Fringe scissors are an amazing tool to add some spice to your hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to use fringe scissors, tips to make your hair look better, and answer some common questions that may come to your mind. So, without further ado, let's get started!

What Are Fringe Scissors?

Fringe scissors, also known as hair cutting scissors, are a type of scissors used to cut hair in a specific way. They have a unique blade design that cuts hair in a zigzag pattern, creating a feathered or layered effect. This makes it ideal for cutting bangs, fringes, or any other part of the hair that needs a little texture and volume.

How To Use Fringe Scissors?

Using fringe scissors is simple and easy. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Wash And Dry Your Hair

Before using fringe scissors, it's essential to wash and dry your hair thoroughly. This will help to get rid of any dirt, oil, or product buildup that may interfere with the cutting process.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you need to section your hair according to how you want your fringe to look. Use a comb to separate your hair and clip the rest of your hair away from the section you want to cut.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Now, it's time to cut your hair. Hold the fringe scissors at an angle and start cutting from the bottom to the top. Make sure to cut small sections of hair at a time and avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Step 4: Check Your Progress

After cutting your hair, check your progress in a mirror. If you want to add more layers or texture, repeat the previous steps until you get the desired result.

Tips To Make Your Hair Look Better

Here are some tips that can help you achieve the best results when using fringe scissors:

  • Start with small cuts and gradually increase the length if necessary.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it can cause your hair to shrink when it dries.
  • Use a comb to help guide your cuts and prevent any unevenness.
  • Don't cut your hair too short as it may take a while to grow back.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and texture.

FAQ

1. How Often Should I Cut My Fringe?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to change your hairstyle. Generally, you should trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and texture.

2. Can I Use Fringe Scissors On Curly Hair?

Yes, you can use fringe scissors on curly hair. However, it's essential to be careful not to cut too much hair as it can cause your curls to lose their shape and bounce.

3. Can I Use Fringe Scissors On Long Hair?

Yes, you can use fringe scissors on long hair. However, it's essential to be patient and take your time when cutting to avoid any mistakes.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's essential to take good care of it. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Congratulations! You have now learned how to use fringe scissors, tips to make your hair look better, and how to take care of your hair after cutting it. With these simple steps, you can create a new and fresh look that will make you stand out from the crowd. So, what are you waiting for? Go ahead and try it out!
