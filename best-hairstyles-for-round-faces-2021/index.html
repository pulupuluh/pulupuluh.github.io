Best Hairstyles For Round Faces 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel May 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, then you know that finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for round faces in 2021. From short to long, curly to straight, we have something for everyone. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

A round face typically has a softer and fuller appearance. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the jawline is rounded. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and definition. This can be achieved through layers, angles, and texture. The right hairstyle can accentuate your best features and balance out your face shape.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Length: Longer hairstyles tend to elongate the face, while shorter hairstyles can make the face appear wider.
  • Layers: Layers can add movement and texture to the hair, which can create a more angular appearance.
  • Angles: Angled cuts can create the illusion of a longer face shape.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help balance out a round face by drawing attention to the eyes and forehead.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best hairstyles for round faces in 2021:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can create a more elongated appearance, especially when paired with loose waves or curls. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can make the face appear wider.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out a round face by drawing attention to the eyes and forehead. Avoid blunt bangs or straight-across cuts, as they can make the face appear wider.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can add angles and definition to a round face. Opt for a longer, choppy style with side-swept bangs for a more flattering look.

4. Bob with Angled Layers

A bob with angled layers can create the illusion of length and definition. Avoid a blunt cut or a bob that is too short, as it can make the face appear wider.

5. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can add movement and texture to the hair, which can create a more angular appearance. Avoid tight curls or ringlets, as they can make the face appear rounder.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid center parts, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Opt for hairstyles with volume at the crown, as this can create the illusion of a longer face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face depends on your personal style and hair texture. Some of the most flattering cuts for round faces include long layers, side-swept bangs, a pixie cut, a bob with angled layers, and beachy waves.

What hairstyles should round faces avoid?

Round faces should avoid hairstyles that are too short, too blunt, or too sleek. Avoid center parts, tight curls, and blunt bangs, as they can make the face appear wider.

Hair Care

Regardless of your face shape, proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a heat protectant when using styling tools. Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

In conclusion, finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. By keeping in mind the length, layers, angles, and texture, you can find a style that accentuates your best features and balances out your face shape. Whether you opt for long layers, side-swept bangs, a pixie cut, a bob with angled layers, or beachy waves, there's a style out there for everyone. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
