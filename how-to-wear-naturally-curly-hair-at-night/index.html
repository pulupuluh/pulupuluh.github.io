How To Wear Naturally Curly Hair At Night A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of waking up with frizzy curls in the morning? Worry no more! We’ve got you covered with this complete guide on how to wear naturally curly hair at night. From protective styles to hair care tips, we’ll teach you how to maintain your curls while you sleep.

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

How to Wear Naturally Curly Hair at Night

The first step to wearing naturally curly hair at night is to determine which style works best for you. Here are a few options:

  • Braids or twists
  • Pineapple
  • Satin bonnet or pillowcase

Let’s take a closer look at each of these styles and how to achieve them.

Braids or Twists

Braids or twists are a great option for those with longer hair. They help to keep your curls intact and reduce frizz. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, moisturized hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Braid or twist each section, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Secure the ends with a hair tie or clip.

Remember to keep the braids or twists loose to avoid damaging your hair.

Pineapple

The pineapple method is a popular option for those with shorter hair. It involves gathering your hair into a high ponytail on the top of your head, resembling the shape of a pineapple. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Flip your head forward and gather your hair at the top of your head.
  2. Tie your hair with a scrunchie or soft hair tie.
  3. Flip your head back up and adjust your curls as needed.

The pineapple method helps to keep your curls intact and prevent them from getting flattened while you sleep.

Satin Bonnet or Pillowcase

A satin bonnet or pillowcase is a great option for those who want to protect their curls while they sleep without having to change their hairstyle. Satin helps to reduce friction and prevent frizz. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Put on a satin bonnet or use a satin pillowcase.
  2. Make sure your curls are gathered in the bonnet or resting on the pillowcase.
  3. Go to sleep!

A satin bonnet or pillowcase is a simple yet effective way to protect your curls while you sleep.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know which style works best for you, let’s go over a step-by-step guide on how to wear naturally curly hair at night.

  1. Start with clean, moisturized hair.
  2. Choose your preferred style (braids or twists, pineapple, or satin bonnet/pillowcase).
  3. Follow the instructions outlined above for your chosen style.
  4. Go to sleep!
  5. In the morning, gently remove the braids or twists, take down the pineapple, or remove the bonnet/pillowcase.
  6. Fluff your curls with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb.
  7. Apply a curl-defining product if desired.

And voila! You’re ready to start your day with gorgeous, defined curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Here are a few additional tips to help you maintain your curls while you sleep:

  • Avoid using cotton pillowcases, as they can cause friction and frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream before bed to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid touching your curls while you sleep, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin scarf to protect your curls while you sleep if you don’t have a bonnet or pillowcase.

By following these tips, you’ll wake up with beautiful, defined curls every morning.

FAQs

How often should I wash my naturally curly hair?

It’s best to wash your curly hair once a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Can I use gel or mousse on my naturally curly hair before bed?

Yes! Applying a curl-defining product before bed can help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

What should I do if my curls are still frizzy in the morning?

Try using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to moisturize your curls and reduce frizz. You can also use a diffuser to dry your curls after washing them.

Hair Care Recommendations

Maintaining healthy, moisturized hair is key to achieving beautiful, defined curls. Here are a few hair care recommendations:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your curls, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these hair care recommendations, you’ll have healthy, happy curls that are easy to maintain.

Now that you know how to wear naturally curly hair at night, you can say goodbye to frizz and hello to gorgeous, defined curls every morning. Happy sleeping!
