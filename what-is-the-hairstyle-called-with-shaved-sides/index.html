What Is The Hairstyle Called With Shaved Sides

Written by Luffy Apr 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? If so, the hairstyle with shaved sides might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has gained popularity over the years, and many people are rocking it with confidence. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including what it's called, how to achieve it, tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Hairstyle with Shaved Sides
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Hairstyle
  • FAQ

Description

The hairstyle with shaved sides is also known as an undercut. It is a hairstyle that involves cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head very short or shaving it completely. The hair on the top of the head is left long, and it can be styled in different ways. The undercut can be worn by both men and women, and it can be customized to suit individual preferences.

How to Achieve the Hairstyle with Shaved Sides

The hairstyle with shaved sides is a bold look that requires a bit of courage to pull off. If you're ready to take the plunge, here's how to achieve the look:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Decide on the length of the hair on the top of your head.
  2. Choose the style you want to achieve with the long hair on top.
  3. Visit a professional hairstylist or barber who has experience with the undercut.
  4. Show the stylist a picture of the style you want to achieve.
  5. Have the sides and back of your head shaved or cut very short.
  6. Style the hair on top of your head according to your preference.

It's essential to choose a skilled hairstylist or barber who has experience with the undercut. A professional will be able to give you the best advice on how to achieve the look you want and ensure that the cut is done correctly.

Tips for Maintaining the Hairstyle

Maintaining the hairstyle with shaved sides requires a bit of effort. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

  • Visit your hairstylist or barber regularly for touch-ups.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair with products that suit your hair type.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Experiment with different styles to keep things fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the hairstyle with shaved sides:

Can I have an undercut if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! The undercut works well with all hair types, including curly hair. However, you may need to use different products and styling techniques to achieve the desired look.

How often do I need to touch up the sides?

The frequency of touch-ups will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the shaved sides grow out. It's best to visit your hairstylist or barber every four to six weeks for touch-ups.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your hair healthy and looking great. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective product.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.
  • Visit your hairstylist or barber regularly for touch-ups and advice on how to take care of your hair.

Now that you know everything about the hairstyle with shaved sides, are you ready to try it out? Remember to choose a skilled hairstylist, maintain your hair regularly, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
