How Long Is A Number 4 Haircut Uk

Written by Bardi Jul 24, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you wondering how long is a number 4 haircut in the UK? Well, you're not alone. Many people are confused about haircut numbers and what they actually mean. In this article, we'll explain what a number 4 haircut is and how long it is in the UK.

Description

A number 4 haircut is a popular style among men. It is a medium-length cut that is versatile and easy to maintain. The number 4 refers to the length of the guard used on the clippers. The guard determines how much hair is left on the head after the cut. In the case of a number 4, the guard leaves 1/2 inch (12.7mm) of hair on the head.

How to

If you want to get a number 4 haircut, you can either go to a barber or do it yourself at home. If you're going to a barber, simply tell them you want a number 4 cut. They will know what that means and will use the appropriate guard on their clippers. If you're doing it yourself, make sure you have the right tools, including clippers with a number 4 guard. Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Attach the number 4 guard to your clippers.
  3. Begin at the sides and work your way up to the top of the head, using smooth, even strokes.
  4. Trim the hair on top of the head to the desired length, using scissors if necessary.
  5. Blend the top and sides for a seamless look.
  6. Use a comb to check for any uneven areas and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Clean up the neckline and around the ears with a trimmer.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a number 4 haircut:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Get your clippers ready with a number 4 guard.
  3. Start at the sides and work your way up to the top of the head, using smooth, even strokes.
  4. Trim the hair on top of the head to the desired length, using scissors if necessary.
  5. Blend the top and sides for a seamless look.
  6. Use a comb to check for any uneven areas and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Clean up the neckline and around the ears with a trimmer.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a number 4 haircut:

  • Make sure you have the right tools, including clippers with a number 4 guard.
  • Start with clean, dry hair to ensure an even cut.
  • Use smooth, even strokes to avoid any choppy areas.
  • Check for any uneven areas with a comb and make adjustments as necessary.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber for advice on styling your new haircut.

FAQ

How often should I get a number 4 haircut?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your cut. Generally, a number 4 haircut will last for 4-6 weeks before it starts to look overgrown.

Can I style a number 4 haircut in different ways?

Yes, a number 4 haircut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or spiked.

Can I get a number 4 haircut if I have curly hair?

Yes, a number 4 haircut can work well with curly hair. Just make sure your stylist knows how to work with curly hair and takes your hair's natural texture into account when cutting.

Hair Care

To keep your number 4 haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a styling product, such as gel or wax, to achieve your desired look.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.

So, there you have it – everything you need to know about how long a number 4 haircut is in the UK. Whether you're going to a barber or doing it yourself at home, a number 4 is a versatile and easy-to-maintain haircut that looks great on men of all ages.
