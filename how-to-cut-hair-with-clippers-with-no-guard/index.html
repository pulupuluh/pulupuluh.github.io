How To Cut Hair With Clippers With No Guard A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of going to the barbershop and paying for haircuts every month? Or are you just looking to save some money and learn a new skill? Cutting hair with clippers without a guard is a great way to achieve a low maintenance look without having to visit a professional. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair with clippers with no guard.

Description

Cutting hair with clippers without a guard is a technique that involves using clippers to trim hair down to a short length. This technique is often used to create a buzz cut or a crew cut. It is important to note that cutting hair with clippers without a guard requires some skill and practice. However, with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.

How to

Before you begin, make sure that you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of clippers, a comb, a mirror, and scissors. It is also important to have a clean, dry head of hair.

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

When cutting hair with clippers without a guard, it is important to choose the right clippers. Look for clippers that are designed for professional use and have a powerful motor. This will ensure that the clippers can cut through thick hair easily.

Step 2: Set Up Your Work Area

Find a comfortable and well-lit area to work in. Place a towel or cape around the person whose hair you will be cutting to catch any loose hair. Make sure that you have all your tools within reach.

Step 3: Start at the Sides

Begin by cutting the hair on the sides of the head. Use the clippers to trim the hair down to a short length. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a comb to guide the clippers. Be sure to cut the hair evenly and avoid cutting too much off at once.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Once you have finished cutting the sides, move to the back of the head. Again, use the clippers to trim the hair down to a short length. Use the comb to guide the clippers and make sure that the hair is cut evenly.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Next, move to the top of the head. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it down to the same length as the sides and back. Be extra careful when cutting the hair on the crown of the head, as this area can be tricky to cut evenly.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the hair down to a short length, use the clippers to blend the hair. This will create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines. Use the clippers to feather the hair around the edges and blend the hair evenly.

Step 7: Clean Up

After you have finished cutting the hair, use the scissors to trim any stray hairs. Use a brush to remove any loose hair from the person's head and clothing. Finally, wash the person's hair to remove any stray hairs and leave the scalp feeling clean and refreshed.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting hair with clippers without a guard:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down to a shorter length
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut
  • Be careful not to cut too much hair off at once
  • Take your time and be patient

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair with clippers without a guard:

Q: Is it difficult to cut hair with clippers without a guard?

A: Cutting hair with clippers without a guard requires some skill and practice. However, with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.

Q: What should I do if I make a mistake?

A: If you make a mistake, don't panic. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs and try to blend the hair as best as you can. If necessary, you can always go to a professional barber to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care

After cutting hair with clippers without a guard, it is important to take care of your hair. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to your hair.

Cutting hair with clippers without a guard is a great way to achieve a low maintenance look at home. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without having to visit a salon. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.
