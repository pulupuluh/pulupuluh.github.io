Mens Short Spiky Hair Cut Creating A Bold And Edgy Look

Written by William Feb 26, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that is both bold and edgy, the mens short spiky hair cut may be just what you need. This hairstyle is popular among men who want to create a look that is both trendy and low-maintenance. In this article, we’ll show you how to achieve this hairstyle, provide tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve the Mens Short Spiky Hair Cut
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining the Mens Short Spiky Hair Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Mens Short Spiky Hair

How to Achieve the Mens Short Spiky Hair Cut

The mens short spiky hair cut is a versatile hairstyle that can be achieved with a variety of hair types and textures. This haircut is typically short on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that is styled into spikes. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on personal preference, but it is typically no longer than two inches.

To achieve this hairstyle, you will need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting mens hair. They will be able to assess your hair type and texture and recommend a cut that will work best for you. Be sure to bring in a photo of the hairstyle you want to achieve so that your stylist can get a clear idea of what you’re looking for.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are the step-by-step instructions for achieving the mens short spiky hair cut:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling gel or pomade to your hair.
  3. Use a comb to part your hair on the side or create a messy, textured look.
  4. Use your fingers to create spikes on the top of your head.
  5. Use a blow dryer to set the style in place.
  6. Use a strong hold hairspray to keep your spikes in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Short Spiky Hair Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens short spiky hair cut:

  • Visit your barber or stylist every four to six weeks to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens short spiky hair cut:

Q: Can I achieve this hairstyle with thinning hair?
A: Yes, the mens short spiky hair cut can be achieved with thinning hair. Your stylist can recommend a cut that will work best for your hair type and texture.

Q: How long does it take to style this hairstyle?
A: The mens short spiky hair cut can be styled in just a few minutes with the right products and tools.

Q: Can I wear this hairstyle to work?
A: Yes, the mens short spiky hair cut can be worn to work depending on your workplace’s dress code and personal style.

Hair Care for Mens Short Spiky Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair with the mens short spiky hair cut:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, as they can weaken your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain a bold and edgy mens short spiky hair cut that will turn heads wherever you go. Remember to consult with your barber or stylist to find the best cut and products for your hair type and texture. With a little bit of effort and care, you can have a hairstyle that is both trendy and low-maintenance.
