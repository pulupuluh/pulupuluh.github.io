Most Flattering Hairstyles For Over 50S

Written by Aprilia May 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll be discussing the most flattering hairstyles for over 50s. Whether you're looking for a short haircut or a longer style, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As we age, our hair can become thinner and lose its natural shine. However, there are many hairstyles that can help to enhance our natural beauty and make us feel confident and stylish. The most flattering hairstyles for over 50s are those that work with your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

How To

When choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. A hairstyle that looks great on someone else may not work for you. If you're not sure what your face shape is, try measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are similar, you likely have a round face. If your jawline is more angular, you may have a square or heart-shaped face. If your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have an oval face.

Step by Step

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to start looking for a hairstyle that will flatter your features. Here are some of the most flattering hairstyles for over 50s:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can be very flattering for over 50s. A pixie cut, for example, can help to add volume and texture to thinning hair. If you prefer a longer style, a bob or lob can be a great option. These cuts can be styled with layers and side-swept bangs to add volume and movement.

Medium Haircuts

For those who prefer a medium-length haircut, a shoulder-length cut with layers can be very flattering. This style can be worn straight or with curls to add volume and texture.

Long Haircuts

If you prefer longer hair, try a layered cut with face-framing layers. This style can help to add volume and texture to thinning hair. Long hair can also be styled into loose waves or curls for a more glamorous look.

Tips

When choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair texture. If you have thinning hair, a shorter style with layers can help to add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a longer style with layers can help to thin out your hair and make it more manageable.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for over 50s?

The best hairstyles for over 50s are those that flatter your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Short haircuts, medium haircuts, and long haircuts can all be very flattering for over 50s.

What haircut makes you look younger?

A layered haircut can help to add volume and texture to thinning hair, which can make you look younger. A bob or lob can also be very flattering for over 50s.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. If you have thinning hair, look for products that contain biotin and keratin, which can help to strengthen your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with hot tools, and avoid using hot tools too often. Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and ideas, you're sure to find a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
