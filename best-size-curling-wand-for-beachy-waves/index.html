Best Size Curling Wand For Beachy Waves A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to achieve effortless and chic beachy waves, a curling wand is your go-to tool. However, with so many sizes and shapes available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for your hair type and desired look. In this article, we'll guide you through the best size curling wand for beachy waves and share some tips and tricks for flawless results.

Description

Beachy waves are a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can be worn on any occasion. This look is achieved by creating loose and tousled waves that resemble the texture of saltwater-tousled hair. To create this style, you need the right tools, and a curling wand is a must-have.

How to

If you're new to using a curling wand, don't worry. It's easy to master with a little practice. Here are the steps to achieve beachy waves with a curling wand:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean, dry, and free of any tangles. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and hold the style. You can also add some texturizing spray to create more grip and volume.

Step 2: Choose the Right Size Curling Wand

The size of your curling wand depends on your hair length and the type of waves you want to achieve. For beachy waves, we recommend a 1-inch or 1.25-inch barrel size. This size creates loose and natural-looking waves that hold their shape. If you have shorter hair, you can go for a 0.75-inch barrel size to create tighter waves.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start from the bottom and work your way up. This will ensure that every strand is evenly curled.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling wand. Hold it for 10-15 seconds and release. Don't curl the ends to create a more natural look. Repeat this process for all sections of hair.

Step 5: Finish Your Look

Once you've curled all your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more relaxed look. You can also add some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect beachy waves:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to prevent frizz.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Don't curl the ends of your hair for a more natural look.
  • Use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more relaxed look.
  • Add some texturizing spray to create more grip and volume.
  • For a more defined look, curl smaller sections of hair.

FAQ

What size curling wand is best for beachy waves?

We recommend a 1-inch or 1.25-inch barrel size for beachy waves. This size creates loose and natural-looking waves that hold their shape.

What's the difference between a curling wand and a curling iron?

A curling wand doesn't have a clamp, which makes it easier to create natural-looking curls and waves. On the other hand, a curling iron has a clamp, which can create more defined curls.

How do I prevent my curls from falling out?

Use a hairspray to hold the style in place. You can also curl smaller sections of hair for a more defined look.

Hair Care

Using a curling wand can damage your hair if you're not careful. To prevent damage, make sure you're using a heat protectant spray before styling. You should also avoid using a high heat setting and limit your use of heating tools to two to three times a week. Additionally, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know the best size curling wand for beachy waves, it's time to get styling. With a little practice and the right tools, you can achieve this effortless and chic look in no time.
