Best Medium Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by William Jul 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best medium haircuts for round faces. Whether you have fine or thick hair, curly or straight, we have got you covered. Read on to find out the best hairstyles that will flatter your round face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Medium Haircut for Round Faces
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Hair

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved lines and a lack of strong angles. If you have a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of length and slimness. Medium haircuts are perfect for achieving this as they provide a balance between short and long hair. The right medium haircut can elongate your face and make it look more oval-shaped. There are many different medium haircuts to choose from, including layered cuts, shaggy cuts, and bob hairstyles.

How to Choose the Best Medium Haircut for Round Faces

Before choosing a medium haircut for your round face, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. The right haircut will enhance your best features and minimize any flaws. Here are some tips for choosing the best medium haircut for round faces:

  • Choose a haircut that falls below your chin to elongate your face.
  • Opt for layers to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your jawline as they will make your face look wider.
  • Consider a side part to create the illusion of length.

Best Medium Haircuts for Fine Hair and Round Faces

If you have fine hair and a round face, a layered lob is a great option. This cut falls just above your shoulders and features subtle layers throughout. The layers add volume and texture, making your hair appear thicker. You can also add subtle highlights to create dimension and depth.

Best Medium Haircuts for Thick Hair and Round Faces

If you have thick hair and a round face, a shaggy bob is a great option. This cut features choppy layers and a textured finish. The layers help to thin out your hair and create a more flattering shape. To style, apply a texturizing spray and scrunch your hair with your fingers.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to achieving the perfect medium haircut for round faces:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and movement.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray to finish off your look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair to prevent it from becoming dry and damaged.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear bangs with a medium haircut for round faces?

A: Yes, you can! Bangs can add a fun and flirty touch to your medium haircut. Opt for side-swept bangs or a wispy fringe to create a more flattering shape.

Q: Will a bob haircut make my face look wider?

A: It depends on the length and style of the bob. A chin-length bob can actually elongate your face and make it appear more oval-shaped. However, a blunt bob that ends at your jawline can make your face look wider.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for medium hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know the best medium haircuts for round faces, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist! Remember, the key is to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture.
