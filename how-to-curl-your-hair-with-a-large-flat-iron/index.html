How To Curl Your Hair With A Large Flat Iron

Written by Daniel Jul 28, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of using the same old curling iron to style your hair? Try using a large flat iron instead! Not only is it a versatile tool that can be used for straightening and curling, but it also creates bouncy, voluminous curls that last all day. In this article, we’ll show you how to curl your hair with a large flat iron, step by step. Let’s get started!

How to Curl Your Hair with a Large Flat Iron

Curling your hair with a flat iron may seem daunting at first, but with the right technique, it’s actually quite simple. Here’s how to do it:

1. Prep Your Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the flat iron. You may also want to apply a volumizing mousse to add body and hold to your curls.

2. Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. The number of sections will depend on the thickness of your hair, but usually, 3-4 sections will suffice.

3. Heat Your Flat Iron

Heat your flat iron to the desired temperature. For thicker hair, a higher temperature will be necessary, but be careful not to damage your hair with excessive heat.

4. Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron at the root. Twist the flat iron away from your face and pull it down to the end of the hair section. Release the flat iron and gently tug on the curl to loosen it. Repeat this process with the remaining sections of hair.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Once you’ve curled all of your hair, finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling with a Flat Iron

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when curling your hair with a flat iron:

  • Use a large flat iron for loose, voluminous curls.
  • Don’t clamp the flat iron too tightly or hold it in one spot for too long, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • For a more natural look, alternate the direction of the curls by twisting the flat iron towards your face and then away from your face.
  • For tighter curls, use smaller sections of hair and hold the flat iron in place for a few seconds longer.

FAQs

Can you curl your hair with a large flat iron?

Yes, a large flat iron can be used to create loose, voluminous curls.

What is the best temperature to curl hair with a flat iron?

The best temperature will vary depending on your hair type and thickness. For thicker hair, a higher temperature may be necessary, but be careful not to damage your hair with excessive heat.

How long do flat iron curls last?

Flat iron curls can last up to 24 hours or more with the right hair care products and techniques.

Hair Care Tips

Curling your hair with a flat iron can be damaging if proper precautions are not taken. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Always use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron.
  • Don’t use a flat iron on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using a flat iron too often, as this can cause damage and breakage over time.

With these tips and techniques, you can create beautiful, bouncy curls with a large flat iron. Give it a try and see for yourself!
