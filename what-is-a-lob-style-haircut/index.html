What Is A Lob Style Haircut

Written by Aprilia Feb 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that is both stylish and easy to maintain? Look no further than the lob style haircut! This popular haircut has been around for several years and is still a fan favorite among women of all ages. In this blog post, we will discuss what a lob style haircut is, how to get it, and how to maintain it for optimal results.

Description

A lob style haircut is a long bob haircut that falls somewhere between the chin and the collarbone. It is shorter than a traditional bob haircut but longer than a pixie cut. The lob style haircut can be worn straight, wavy, or curly, depending on your preference. This versatile haircut can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type, making it a great option for anyone looking to switch up their hairstyle.

How to Get a Lob Style Haircut

If you are interested in getting a lob style haircut, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who is familiar with this type of haircut. When you go in for your appointment, be sure to bring pictures of what you want your hair to look like. This will help your hairstylist understand your vision and create the perfect lob style haircut for you.

During the haircut, your hairstylist will likely use a combination of scissors and a razor to create layers and texture throughout your hair. They may also use a variety of cutting techniques to create movement and shape. The end result should be a flattering and low-maintenance haircut that you can easily style at home.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before getting a lob style haircut, it is important to have clean, dry hair. This will allow your hairstylist to see your hair's natural texture and create a customized haircut that suits your needs.

Step 2: Discuss Your Vision with Your Hairstylist

During your appointment, be sure to discuss your vision for your lob style haircut with your hairstylist. Bring in pictures of what you want your hair to look like, and be open to their suggestions and recommendations.

Step 3: Let Your Hairstylist Work Their Magic

Once you and your hairstylist have discussed your vision and goals, it's time to sit back and let them work their magic. Your hairstylist will use a combination of scissors and a razor to create layers and texture throughout your hair, creating a flattering and low-maintenance lob style haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Lob Style Haircut

Now that you've got your lob style haircut, it's important to maintain it for optimal results. Here are a few tips for keeping your lob style looking fresh and stylish:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep your lob style looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat tools to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening, to switch up your look.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

What face shapes suit a lob style haircut?

A lob style haircut can be customized to suit any face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a longer lob with layers to add dimension. If you have a square face, go for a shorter lob with soft, wispy layers around the face to soften your features.

Will a lob style haircut work for thin hair?

A lob style haircut can work well for thin hair, as it adds volume and texture to the hair. Be sure to ask your hairstylist to create layers throughout your hair to add movement and body.

What styling products should I use for my lob style haircut?

For a sleek and smooth lob style, use a lightweight serum or oil to tame any flyaways and add shine. If you prefer a more textured look, use a sea salt spray or texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and beautiful lob style haircut. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you can enjoy a beautiful and low-maintenance lob style haircut for years to come.
