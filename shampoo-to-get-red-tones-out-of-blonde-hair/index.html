Shampoo To Get Red Tones Out Of Blonde Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Blonde hair is beautiful, but sometimes red tones can sneak in and ruin the look. Luckily, there are shampoos available that can help get rid of those unwanted tones. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about shampoos to get red tones out of blonde hair. From how to use them to the best products on the market, we have got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

Shampoo to get red tones out of blonde hair is designed to neutralize the warm tones that can appear in blonde hair. These shampoos are typically purple, as purple is the opposite of yellow on the color wheel. By using a purple shampoo, you can counteract the red and yellow tones in your hair, leaving you with a cooler, ashier blonde.

How to Use Shampoo to Get Red Tones Out of Blonde Hair

The process for using shampoo to get red tones out of blonde hair is simple. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair thoroughly. Make sure to get all of your hair wet, from the roots to the ends.

Step 2: Apply Shampoo

Apply a generous amount of shampoo to your hair. Make sure to focus on the areas where you see the most red or yellow tones.

Step 3: Massage Your Scalp

Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair for a few minutes. This will help the shampoo penetrate your hair and do its job.

Step 4: Rinse

Rinse the shampoo out of your hair thoroughly. Make sure to get all of the shampoo out, as leaving any in your hair can cause it to look dull or greasy.

Step 5: Condition

Apply conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes, then rinse it out.

Tips for Using Shampoo to Get Red Tones Out of Blonde Hair

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your shampoo to get red tones out of blonde hair:

  • Use the shampoo once or twice a week, depending on how often you wash your hair.
  • Leave the shampoo in your hair for a few minutes before rinsing it out.
  • Follow up with a good conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much shampoo, as this can cause your hair to dry out.
  • If you have particularly stubborn red or yellow tones, you can leave the shampoo in your hair for up to 10 minutes for a deeper treatment.

FAQ

Q: Can I use shampoo to get red tones out of blonde hair on colored hair?

A: Yes, you can use shampoo to get red tones out of blonde hair on colored hair. However, be aware that it may strip some of the color out of your hair, so use it sparingly.

Q: How often should I use shampoo to get red tones out of blonde hair?

A: You should use shampoo to get red tones out of blonde hair once or twice a week, depending on how often you wash your hair.

Q: Will shampoo to get red tones out of blonde hair dry out my hair?

A: If you use too much shampoo or use it too often, it can dry out your hair. Make sure to follow the instructions on the bottle and use it sparingly.

Hair Care Tips for Blonde Hair

Here are a few more tips to keep your blonde hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips and using a good shampoo to get red tones out of blonde hair, you can keep your blonde locks looking beautiful and vibrant.

Conclusion

Shampoo to get red tones out of blonde hair is a great way to keep your blonde hair looking cool and ashy. By following the tips in this article and using a good shampoo, you can keep your hair looking its best. So, go ahead and try out some of the products we recommended and see the difference for yourself!
