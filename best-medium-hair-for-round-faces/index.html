Best Medium Hair For Round Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Aug 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you a round-faced individual who is looking for the perfect medium-length haircut? Look no further! In this guide, we will discuss the best medium haircuts for round faces, how to style them, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Medium Hair for Round Faces
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Medium Hair for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Hair for Round Faces

How to Choose the Best Medium Hair for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing the best medium hair for round faces, there are a few key factors to keep in mind. The first is length. Medium haircuts that fall just below the chin or shoulder work well for round faces because they help to elongate the face. Additionally, layers can also help to add dimension and create a more angular appearance.

Another factor to consider is the shape of the haircut. Round faces tend to look best with haircuts that have angles and sharp lines. A straight bob or a long layered cut with face-framing angles can make a round face appear slimmer.

Lastly, consider your hair texture. Fine hair will require a different cut than thick hair. A layered cut can add volume to fine hair, while thinning shears can help to remove bulk from thick hair.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Medium Hair for Round Faces

Once you've chosen the perfect medium haircut for your round face, it's time to style it. Here's a step by step guide to help you achieve a salon-worthy look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair.
  5. Finish your look with a lightweight hairspray or serum.

Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Hair for Round Faces

To keep your medium hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant product before styling.
  • Invest in high-quality hair styling tools, such as a good blow dryer, curling iron, and straightener.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo in between washes to extend the life of your hairstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions About Medium Hair for Round Faces

Q: Can I wear bangs with a medium haircut if I have a round face?

A: Yes! Bangs can be a great way to add dimension to a round face, just make sure they are cut at an angle to create a more angular appearance.

Q: Should I avoid certain hair colors if I have a round face?

A: No, you can wear any hair color that you like! However, highlights or lowlights can help to create dimension and add depth to your hair.

Hair Care for Medium Hair for Round Faces

To keep your medium hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair, as it is less likely to cause breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as it is more prone to breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect medium haircut for your round face and keep it looking healthy and fabulous. Happy styling!
