Mens Short Layered Haircut Styles A Guide To Choose The Right One

Written by Petter Mar 14, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to freshen up your look? Mens short layered haircut styles are a great option to consider. Whether you have thick or thin hair, straight or curly, there is a layered haircut that can work for you. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about mens short layered haircut styles, including how to choose the right one, tips for styling, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Mens Short Layered Haircut Style
  • How to Get a Mens Short Layered Haircut Style
  • Tips for Styling Mens Short Layered Haircut Styles
  • FAQs about Mens Short Layered Haircut Styles
  • Hair Care for Mens Short Layered Haircut Styles

How to Choose the Right Mens Short Layered Haircut Style

The first step in choosing the right mens short layered haircut style is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes look better with different styles. For example, if you have a round face, a layered cut with longer hair on top can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a layered cut with shorter hair on top and longer sides can soften your features.

Another factor to consider is your hair type. If you have thick hair, a layered cut can help reduce bulk and make your hair more manageable. If you have thin hair, a layered cut can add volume and texture.

Finally, consider your personal style and how much maintenance you're willing to do. A shorter layered cut will require more frequent trims to maintain its shape, while a longer layered cut can be more low-maintenance.

How to Get a Mens Short Layered Haircut Style

If you're ready to get a mens short layered haircut style, here's what to expect:

  1. Consult with your stylist to choose the right style for you.
  2. Wash your hair and dry it completely.
  3. Sit in the chair and let your stylist section your hair.
  4. Your stylist will then use scissors to cut your hair in layers, starting with the top and working down.
  5. Once your hair is cut, your stylist will style it and show you how to maintain the look at home.

Tips for Styling Mens Short Layered Haircut Styles

Here are some tips for styling your mens short layered haircut:

  • Use a lightweight styling product, like a wax or pomade, to add definition and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and lift.
  • Experiment with different partings and styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo or texture spray to refresh your style between washes.

FAQs about Mens Short Layered Haircut Styles

How often should I get a trim?

You should plan on getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your layered haircut style.

Can I still style my hair if it's thin?

Yes! A layered cut can actually help add volume and texture to thin hair, making it easier to style.

What if I don't like my new haircut?

If you're not happy with your new haircut, talk to your stylist about making adjustments or trying a different style.

Hair Care for Mens Short Layered Haircut Styles

Proper hair care is important for maintaining your mens short layered haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like flat irons and curling irons, too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and elements by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know everything you need to know about mens short layered haircut styles, it's time to book your appointment with a stylist and try out a new look!
