How To Hide Long Hair Under A Baseball Cap

Written by William Mar 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your long hair getting in the way while wearing a baseball cap? Whether you're playing a game, out for a run, or just trying to keep your hair out of your face, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to hide long hair under a baseball cap in a few simple steps.

How to

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair and pulling it back into a low ponytail. Make sure your hair is smooth and free of tangles.

Step 2: Take your baseball cap and put it on your head. Make sure it sits comfortably and securely, with the bill facing forwards.

Step 3: Now, take your ponytail and twist it into a tight bun. You may want to use a hair tie or bobby pins to keep it in place.

Step 4: Tuck the bun into the back of your baseball cap. Make sure it's snug and secure.

Step 5: Adjust the cap as needed. You may want to pull it down slightly to cover any loose hairs or adjust the bill to your preference.

Tips

- Use a baseball cap with a larger opening at the back if you have thick hair.

- If you have a lot of flyaway hairs, use a light hairspray or hair gel to keep them in place.

- Practice this technique a few times before you need to wear it out.

FAQ

Q: Will this work for shorter hair?

A: Yes, this technique can work for shorter hair as well. Simply pull your hair back into a low ponytail or braid and follow the same steps.

Q: Can I wear a hat with wet hair?

A: It's not recommended to wear a hat with wet hair as it can cause damage to both your hair and the hat.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, even when you're hiding it under a baseball cap. Here are a few tips:

- Avoid wearing hats for long periods of time, as it can cause your hair to become greasy and flat.

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.

- Try not to pull your hair too tightly when putting it up in a bun or ponytail, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Now that you know how to hide your long hair under a baseball cap, you can stay stylish and comfortable no matter the occasion. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
