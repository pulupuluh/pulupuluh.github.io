Do Guys Like Purple Hair The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Written by Eveline May 24, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you considering dyeing your hair purple but wondering if guys will like it? Look no further! In this guide, we will be discussing everything you need to know about guys and purple hair. From why guys like it to how to care for it, we’ve got you covered. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents:

  • Why Guys Like Purple Hair
  • How to Dye Your Hair Purple
  • Tips for Maintaining Purple Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Why Guys Like Purple Hair

Many guys find purple hair attractive because it’s unique and bold. Unlike natural hair colors, purple hair stands out and can make a statement. It also shows that someone is confident and not afraid to take risks with their appearance.

Additionally, purple is often associated with creativity, spirituality, and royalty. This can make someone with purple hair seem more interesting and intriguing to some guys.

How to Dye Your Hair Purple

If you’re ready to take the plunge and dye your hair purple, here’s how to do it:

  1. Choose your shade of purple. There are many different shades of purple to choose from, such as lavender, lilac, and deep violet.
  2. Decide on the dye. You can use semi-permanent or permanent dye, depending on how long you want the color to last.
  3. Prepare your hair. Bleach your hair if necessary to achieve the desired shade of purple. Make sure to follow the instructions on the bleach carefully.
  4. Apply the dye. Follow the instructions on the dye to apply it evenly to your hair.
  5. Wait. Let the dye sit for the recommended amount of time before rinsing it out.
  6. Maintain your hair. To keep your purple hair looking vibrant, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner and avoid washing it too frequently.

Tips for Maintaining Purple Hair

To keep your purple hair looking its best, here are some tips to follow:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Consider using a purple shampoo or toner to keep the color from fading.

FAQ

Will guys find me less attractive with purple hair?

No! Everyone has different preferences, but many guys find purple hair attractive.

What should I wear with purple hair?

Anything you feel confident in! Purple hair can go with any style, so wear what makes you feel good.

Is it hard to maintain purple hair?

It can be more challenging than maintaining natural hair colors, but with the right products and care, it’s doable.

Hair Care

Regardless of your hair color, it’s essential to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Don’t wash your hair too frequently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

And there you have it! Everything you need to know about guys and purple hair. Remember, the most important thing is to do what makes you happy and confident in your own skin. Rock that purple hair with pride!
