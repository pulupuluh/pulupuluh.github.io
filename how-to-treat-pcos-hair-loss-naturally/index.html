How To Treat Pcos Hair Loss Naturally A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have been diagnosed with Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS), you may have noticed hair loss as one of the symptoms. PCOS hair loss can be distressing, but the good news is that it is treatable. In this article, we will discuss how to treat PCOS hair loss naturally, without resorting to expensive and potentially harmful medications.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Treat PCOS Hair Loss Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing PCOS Hair Loss
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for PCOS Hair Loss

Description

PCOS hair loss is caused by hormonal imbalances in the body. Women with PCOS have higher levels of androgens, which are male hormones that can cause hair loss. This hair loss can occur on the scalp, face, and body. While there are medications available to treat PCOS hair loss, they can have side effects and be expensive. Natural treatments can be just as effective, and are often safer and more affordable.

How to Treat PCOS Hair Loss Naturally

There are several natural treatments that can help to manage PCOS hair loss:

1. Diet Changes

Your diet can have a significant impact on PCOS hair loss. A diet high in sugar and refined carbohydrates can cause insulin resistance, which can worsen PCOS symptoms. Eating a diet rich in whole foods, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins, can help to manage insulin resistance and reduce hair loss.

2. Supplements

Supplements can help to address nutrient deficiencies that can contribute to PCOS hair loss. Biotin, a B vitamin, can improve hair health and reduce shedding. Iron, zinc, and vitamin D are also important for healthy hair growth.

3. Exercise

Regular exercise can help to manage PCOS symptoms, including hair loss. Exercise can improve insulin sensitivity and reduce androgen levels in the body.

4. Stress Management

Stress can worsen PCOS symptoms, including hair loss. Practicing stress-management techniques, such as meditation, yoga, and deep breathing, can help to reduce stress levels and improve hair health.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for treating PCOS hair loss naturally:

Step 1: Assess Your Diet

Assess your diet and make changes to include more whole foods and fewer refined carbohydrates and sugar.

Step 2: Consider Supplements

Consider taking supplements to address nutrient deficiencies that can contribute to hair loss. Biotin, iron, zinc, and vitamin D are all important for healthy hair growth.

Step 3: Incorporate Exercise

Incorporate regular exercise into your routine to improve insulin sensitivity and reduce androgen levels in the body.

Step 4: Practice Stress Management

Practice stress-management techniques, such as meditation, yoga, and deep breathing, to reduce stress levels and improve hair health.

Step 5: Be Patient

Remember that natural treatments take time to work. Be patient and consistent with your treatment plan.

Tips for Managing PCOS Hair Loss

Here are some additional tips for managing PCOS hair loss:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles and hair accessories that can pull on your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, that can damage hair.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free from harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it is more prone to breakage.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.

FAQs

Can PCOS hair loss be reversed?

Yes, PCOS hair loss can be reversed with the right treatment plan. Natural treatments can be just as effective as medications.

How long does it take for PCOS hair loss to grow back?

It can take several months for PCOS hair loss to grow back. Be patient and consistent with your treatment plan.

Hair Care for PCOS Hair Loss

Here are some additional tips for caring for your hair if you have PCOS hair loss:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles and hair accessories that can pull on your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, that can damage hair.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free from harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it is more prone to breakage.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.

By following these natural treatments and hair care tips, you can manage PCOS hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.
