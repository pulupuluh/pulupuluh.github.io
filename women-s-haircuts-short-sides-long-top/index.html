Women s Haircuts Short Sides Long Top

Written by Petter Mar 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on women's haircuts short sides long top. This popular hairstyle has been around for a while and continues to be one of the most sought-after hairstyles for women. In this post, we will discuss everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle, from how to get it, to how to maintain it. Let's dive in!

Description

The women's haircut short sides long top is a versatile and edgy hairstyle that involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides and keeping the hair on the top long. This hairstyle can be customized to suit different hair types, face shapes, and personal styles. It can be worn in different ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured, making it a popular choice for women who want to switch up their look.

How to

If you're considering getting a women's haircut short sides long top, here's how to get it:

  1. Start by deciding on the length and style you want for the top of your hair. This will depend on your personal preference, face shape, and hair type.
  2. Next, decide on the length you want for the sides. You can go for a buzz cut, a fade, or a taper, depending on your desired look.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist to get their opinion on what will work best for you. They will be able to give you advice on what will suit your face shape and hair type.
  4. Once you've decided on the length and style, sit back and let your stylist work their magic!

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your women's haircut short sides long top:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Apply a styling product of your choice to the top of your hair. This can be a pomade, mousse, or gel, depending on your desired look.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair on the top of your head. You can slick it back, create a quiff, or leave it messy and textured.
  4. For the sides, use clippers or scissors to trim them to the desired length. You can choose to fade the hair from the sides to the top, or keep it all one length.
  5. Apply some product to the sides of your hair to keep it in place and add some shine.
  6. Finish off the look with some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting and maintaining a women's haircut short sides long top:

  • Choose a hairstylist who has experience with this hairstyle to ensure that you get the best results.
  • Invest in good quality styling products to keep your hair looking great.
  • Be prepared to maintain your hairstyle regularly, as the short sides will need to be trimmed regularly to avoid looking unkempt.
  • Experiment with different styles and looks to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about women's haircuts short sides long top:

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit different face shapes, making it a versatile option for women.

Q: Can I wear this hairstyle to work?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be styled in a professional and polished way, making it suitable for the workplace.

Q: How often do I need to trim the sides?

A: The sides will need to be trimmed every 2-4 weeks to maintain the look and avoid looking unkempt.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential when it comes to any hairstyle. Here are some tips to take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suit your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

That concludes our guide on women's haircuts short sides long top. We hope you found this post helpful and informative. Remember to choose a style that suits you and experiment with different looks to find the perfect one!
