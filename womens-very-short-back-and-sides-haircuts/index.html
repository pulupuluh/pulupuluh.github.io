Women s Very Short Back And Sides Haircuts A Guide To The Popular Hairstyle

Written by Bardi Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're thinking about getting a women's very short back and sides haircut, you're not alone. This trendy hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years. In this guide, we'll discuss how to achieve the look, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

A very short back and sides haircut involves cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head very short, while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a dramatic contrast between the short sides and the longer hair on top. This hairstyle can be worn in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're thinking about getting a very short back and sides haircut, the first step is to find a stylist who specializes in this type of cut. It's important to choose someone who has experience with this style, as it requires precision and skill to achieve the right look.

Once you've found a stylist, you'll need to decide on the length of the cut. Very short back and sides haircuts can be anything from a buzz cut to a cropped pixie cut. Your stylist will be able to help you choose a length that suits your face shape and personal style.

Once the length is decided, your stylist will begin by cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head very short. They will use clippers or scissors to create a clean, even cut. The hair on top of your head will be left longer and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step by Step

1. Find a stylist who specializes in very short back and sides haircuts.

2. Decide on the length of the cut.

3. Your stylist will cut the hair on the sides and back of your head very short.

4. The hair on top of your head will be left longer.

5. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining a Very Short Back and Sides Haircut

1. Regular trims are important to maintain the shape of the haircut.

2. Use a lightweight styling product to add texture and hold to the longer hair on top.

3. Keep the sides and back of your head clean-shaven or neatly trimmed to maintain the contrast between the short sides and longer top.

4. Consider investing in a high-quality pair of clippers to maintain the haircut at home between salon appointments.

FAQ

How often do I need to get a very short back and sides haircut?

This will depend on the speed of your hair growth and the length of the cut. Generally, you'll need to get a trim every four to six weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Can I still style my hair with a very short back and sides haircut?

Yes! The longer hair on top of your head can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

Is a very short back and sides haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes! Your stylist will be able to help you choose a length and shape that flatters your face shape.

Hair Care

Keeping your very short back and sides haircut looking its best requires a little bit of maintenance. Be sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy. Use a lightweight styling product to add texture and hold to the longer hair on top, and keep the sides and back of your head neatly trimmed or clean-shaven.

In summary, a very short back and sides haircut is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways. With the right stylist and a little bit of maintenance, you can rock this look with confidence!
