Chubby Face Hairstyles For Round Faces With Glasses

Written by Frank Jun 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face and wear glasses, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But fear not, there are plenty of cute and stylish options that will flatter your features and make you feel confident. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for finding the perfect chubby face hairstyles for round faces with glasses.

Description

Round faces tend to have soft, curved features, and can appear wider than they are long. Glasses can accentuate this shape, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that balances out your proportions. You'll want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider.

How To

The key to finding a flattering hairstyle for round faces with glasses is to create the illusion of length. This can be achieved with a variety of cuts and styles, including:

Long Layers

A long layered haircut can help elongate your face and draw attention away from the roundness. Layers can also add movement and texture to your hair, making it appear fuller and more voluminous.

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can help break up the symmetry of a round face and make it appear longer. They also draw attention to your eyes, which can be flattering when wearing glasses.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob with longer pieces on one side can create the illusion of length and add interest to your haircut. This style is also low-maintenance and easy to style, making it a great choice for a busy lifestyle.

Step by Step

To achieve the perfect chubby face hairstyle for round faces with glasses, follow these simple steps:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to find a cut and style that flatters your face shape and works with your glasses.
  2. Invest in quality hair products that will help you achieve the desired look, such as a volumizing spray or texturizing pomade.
  3. Experiment with different styling techniques, such as blow-drying with a round brush or using a curling iron to add volume and texture.
  4. Accessorize your hairstyle with cute hair clips or headbands that complement your glasses and add interest to your look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for round faces with glasses:

  • Avoid blunt bangs or a straight-across cut, as this can accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.
  • Go for a choppy, layered cut instead of a blunt, one-length cut to add movement and texture.
  • Experiment with different partings to see what works best for your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about chubby face hairstyles for round faces with glasses:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face and wear glasses?

You'll want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or a straight-across cut. Instead, go for a layered cut or side swept bangs that create the illusion of length.

What hair products should I use to achieve a voluminous look?

Invest in quality hair products, such as a volumizing spray or texturizing pomade, to help you achieve the desired look. You can also experiment with different styling techniques, such as blow-drying with a round brush or using a curling iron to add volume and texture.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

In conclusion, there are plenty of cute and stylish chubby face hairstyles for round faces with glasses. By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
