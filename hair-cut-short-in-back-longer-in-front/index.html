Hair Cut Short In Back Longer In Front A Guide For 2023

Written by Aprilia May 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut? Do you want to try something new and trendy? Look no further than the hair cut short in back longer in front. This edgy and stylish haircut has been popular for years and continues to be a go-to for those looking to switch up their look. In this blog post, we will provide a guide on how to achieve this haircut, tips for styling, and answer frequently asked questions.

Description

The hair cut short in back longer in front, also known as the "angled bob" or "graduated bob," is a haircut where the hair is cut shorter in the back and gradually gets longer towards the front. This creates a dramatic and angled look that is both edgy and stylish. It works on many hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. This haircut is perfect for those looking to add some dimension to their hair and highlight their facial features.

How to

If you're interested in getting a hair cut short in back longer in front, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consultation

Before getting any haircut, it's essential to have a consultation with your hairstylist. Bring in pictures of the haircut you want and discuss your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. Your hairstylist will be able to recommend the best version of the haircut for you and answer any questions you may have.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition

On the day of your haircut, make sure to shampoo and condition your hair. This will ensure your hair is clean and ready to be cut.

Step 3: Section and Cut

Your hairstylist will section your hair and start cutting the back of your hair short. They will gradually work their way towards the front, cutting longer pieces. This creates the dramatic angle that the haircut is known for.

Step 4: Style

After the haircut, your hairstylist will style your hair to perfection. They may use a flat iron to create sleekness or a curling iron to add some bounce.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your hair cut short in back longer in front:

- Use a volumizing mousse to add volume to the roots - Use a texturizing spray to add definition to the ends - Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create a sleek look - Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the hair cut short in back longer in front:

Q: Will this haircut work on curly hair?

A: Yes, this haircut works on many hair types, including curly hair. Your hairstylist will be able to customize the haircut to suit your hair type.

Q: Is this haircut easy to maintain?

A: Yes, this haircut is relatively easy to maintain. It does require regular trims to keep the shape, but it doesn't require much styling time.

Q: Will this haircut flatter my face shape?

A: This haircut works on many face shapes, but your hairstylist will be able to recommend the best version of the haircut for you.

Hair Care

To keep your hair cut short in back longer in front looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner - Avoid using hot tools too often - Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools - Get regular trims to maintain the shape

Conclusion

The hair cut short in back longer in front is a trendy and edgy haircut that works on many hair types and face shapes. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect angled bob and maintain it for months to come. So go ahead, book that appointment with your hairstylist and try out this stylish haircut for yourself.
