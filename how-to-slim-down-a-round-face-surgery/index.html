How To Slim Down A Round Face Surgery Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Apr 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

A round face is a common facial shape that many people have. While there is nothing wrong with having a round face, some people may want to change their facial structure to achieve a more defined and slim look. One of the most effective ways to do this is through surgery. In this article, we will discuss how to slim down a round face surgery and provide tips and tricks to help you achieve your desired look.

Description

Facial surgery is a cosmetic procedure that involves altering the structure of the face to achieve a more defined and slim look. The most common types of facial surgery for a round face include liposuction, buccal fat removal, and jawline surgery. These procedures can help remove excess fat from the face, reduce the size of the cheeks, and create a more defined jawline.

How to Slim Down a Round Face Surgery

Liposuction

Liposuction is a surgical procedure that removes excess fat from the body. During this procedure, a small incision is made in the face, and a cannula is inserted to suction out the fat. This procedure is often used to remove excess fat from the cheeks, chin, and neck area. Liposuction is an effective way to slim down a round face, but it is important to consult with a board-certified plastic surgeon to determine if it is the right procedure for you.

Buccal Fat Removal

Buccal fat removal is a surgical procedure that involves removing excess fat from the cheeks. During this procedure, a small incision is made inside the mouth, and the surgeon removes the buccal fat pad. This procedure is often used to create a more defined and contoured look to the cheeks. However, it is important to note that removing too much buccal fat can result in a sunken appearance, so it is important to consult with a board-certified plastic surgeon to determine the right amount of fat to remove.

Jawline Surgery

Jawline surgery is a surgical procedure that involves reshaping the jawline to create a more defined and slim look. During this procedure, the surgeon will make small incisions in the face and use specialized tools to reshape the jawline. This procedure is often used to create a more defined and contoured look to the face.

Step by Step

The following steps will help you prepare for your facial surgery:

  1. Consult with a board-certified plastic surgeon to determine the right procedure for you.
  2. Follow all pre-operative instructions provided by your surgeon.
  3. Make arrangements for transportation to and from the surgical facility.
  4. Arrange for someone to stay with you for the first 24 hours after surgery.
  5. Follow all post-operative instructions provided by your surgeon, including taking prescribed medication and attending follow-up appointments.

Tips

The following tips can help you achieve the best results from your facial surgery:

  • Maintain a healthy diet and exercise regularly to maintain your results.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these can interfere with the healing process.
  • Avoid sun exposure and use sunscreen to protect your skin from damage.
  • Be patient with the healing process, as it can take several weeks to see the full results of your surgery.

FAQ

Is facial surgery safe?

Facial surgery is generally safe when performed by a board-certified plastic surgeon. However, as with any surgical procedure, there are risks involved. It is important to discuss the risks and benefits of the procedure with your surgeon before undergoing surgery.

How long does it take to recover from facial surgery?

The recovery time for facial surgery varies depending on the type of procedure performed. In general, it can take several weeks to see the full results of the surgery, and patients may experience swelling and bruising during this time. It is important to follow all post-operative instructions provided by your surgeon to ensure a smooth and successful recovery.

Hair Care

After facial surgery, it is important to avoid washing your hair for the first few days following the procedure. When you do wash your hair, be gentle and avoid using hot water or harsh shampoos. It is also important to avoid applying pressure to the face, so be careful when brushing or styling your hair.

Conclusion

If you are interested in slimming down a round face, facial surgery can be an effective solution. By consulting with a board-certified plastic surgeon and following all pre- and post-operative instructions, you can achieve a more defined and slim look to your face. Remember to be patient with the healing process and take care of your skin and hair to ensure the best possible results.
