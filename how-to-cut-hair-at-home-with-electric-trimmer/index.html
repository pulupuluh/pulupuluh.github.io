How To Cut Hair At Home With Electric Trimmer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending a lot of money on haircuts at the salon? Cutting your hair at home can be a great way to save money and time. With the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can achieve salon-quality haircuts in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your hair at home with an electric trimmer.

How to Cut Hair at Home with Electric Trimmer

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

Before starting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need an electric trimmer, a comb, a mirror, and a towel. It is also helpful to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp throughout the process.

Step 2: Choose the Right Blade

Different blades are designed for different hair lengths and textures. For short hair, use a shorter blade and for longer hair, use a longer blade. It is important to choose the right blade for your hair type to achieve the desired result.

Step 3: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It is important to start with clean, dry hair. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and then towel-dry it.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Begin by using the longest blade guard and cut the hair against the grain. Start at the sides and work your way around the head. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it to the desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

After trimming the hair, it is important to blend the hair to avoid any harsh lines. Use a smaller blade guard to trim the hair around the ears, neck, and hairline.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.

Step 7: Clean Up

Once you are finished cutting your hair, clean up any loose hairs with a brush or vacuum.

Tips

- Practice on a smaller section of hair before starting on the entire head.

- Use a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp throughout the process.

- Use a smaller blade guard to trim around the ears, neck, and hairline.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with kitchen scissors?

While it is possible to cut your hair with kitchen scissors, it is not recommended. Kitchen scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can cause damage to your hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it. Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.

Cutting your hair at home with an electric trimmer can be a great way to save time and money. With the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can achieve salon-quality haircuts in the comfort of your own home.
