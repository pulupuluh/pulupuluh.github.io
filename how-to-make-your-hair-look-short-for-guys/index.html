How To Make Your Hair Look Short For Guys

Written by Eveline Jul 03, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re a guy with long hair, you might be looking for ways to make it look shorter without actually cutting it. Whether you’re going for a new style or just trying to switch things up, there are plenty of ways to make your hair appear shorter. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps to achieve a shorter look for your hair.

How to

1. Use a Hair Tie

One of the easiest ways to make your hair look shorter is to tie it up. Use a hair tie to pull your hair back and create the illusion of a shorter haircut. You can go for a classic ponytail or try a messy bun for a more relaxed look.

2. Style with Wax or Pomade

Styling products like wax or pomade can help you achieve a shorter look for your hair. Apply a small amount of product to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends to create a textured, choppy look. This will make your hair look shorter and more manageable.

3. Trim Your Bangs

If you have long bangs, trimming them can instantly make your hair look shorter. Use a pair of scissors to cut your bangs to the desired length, being careful not to cut too much at once. This will give your hair a more polished look and make it appear shorter overall.

4. Add Layers

Adding layers to your hair can create the illusion of a shorter cut. Ask your stylist to add layers around your face and at the back of your head. This will give your hair more movement and texture, making it appear shorter and more voluminous.

5. Try a Faux Hawk

A faux hawk is a great way to achieve a shorter look without actually cutting your hair. Use styling products to create a sleek, spiky look on the top of your head while leaving the sides longer. This will create the illusion of a shorter cut while still allowing you to keep your length.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that’s right for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and use a blow dryer to dry it completely.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends to create a choppy, textured look.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair Up

Use a hair tie to pull your hair back into a ponytail or messy bun. This will create the illusion of a shorter cut.

Step 4: Trim Your Bangs

If you have long bangs, use a pair of scissors to trim them to the desired length. Be careful not to cut too much at once.

Step 5: Add Layers

Ask your stylist to add layers to your hair to create more movement and texture. This will make your hair appear shorter and more voluminous.

Tips

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut to achieve the perfect look.
  • Use a comb to create a sleek look or your fingers for a more relaxed style.
  • Accessorize with hats or headbands to add more style to your look.

FAQ

How can I make my long hair look shorter without cutting it?

You can use a hair tie to create a ponytail or messy bun, apply styling products to create a choppy, textured look, trim your bangs, or add layers to your hair.

What styling products work best for creating a shorter look?

Wax or pomade can work well to create a choppy, textured look. Gel can also be used to create a sleeker, more polished style.

Do I need to visit a salon to achieve a shorter look?

While it’s not necessary, visiting a salon can help you achieve the perfect look and ensure that your hair is cut and styled properly.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by washing it regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that’s right for your hair type. Use styling products in moderation and avoid using heat tools too often. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
