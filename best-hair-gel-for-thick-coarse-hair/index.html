Best Hair Gel For Thick Coarse Hair

Description

Thick coarse hair can be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to styling. Hair gel can be a great solution to keep your hair in place and under control. However, not all hair gels are created equal. When it comes to thick coarse hair, it's important to choose a gel that can provide hold and control without leaving your hair feeling sticky or crunchy.

In this article, we'll explore the best hair gel for thick coarse hair and provide tips on how to apply and maintain your hair with gel.

How to choose the Best Hair Gel for Thick Coarse Hair

When it comes to choosing the best hair gel for thick coarse hair, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Hold: Look for a hair gel that provides a strong hold to keep your hair in place throughout the day.
  • Moisture: Thick coarse hair tends to be dry, so it's important to choose a hair gel that can provide some moisture to your hair.
  • Ingredients: Avoid hair gels that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair even more. Look for natural ingredients like aloe vera, jojoba oil, and shea butter.

Best Hair Gel for Thick Coarse Hair

Based on the above factors, here are some of the best hair gels for thick coarse hair:

  • SheaMoisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curling Gel Soufflé: This gel is perfect for defining curls in thick coarse hair. It contains natural ingredients like coconut oil and shea butter to provide moisture and hold.
  • DevaCurl Ultra Defining Gel: This gel provides a strong hold without leaving your hair feeling crunchy or sticky. It's also free from sulfates, parabens, and silicone.
  • Aveda Confixor Liquid Gel: This gel provides a medium hold and contains natural ingredients like flaxseed and aloe vera to provide moisture to your hair.

Step by Step: How to Apply Hair Gel on Thick Coarse Hair

Here's how to apply hair gel on thick coarse hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a quarter-sized amount of gel to your palm.
  3. Rub your hands together to evenly distribute the gel.
  4. Apply the gel to your hair, starting with the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to evenly distribute the gel throughout your hair.
  6. Style your hair as desired using a blow dryer or air drying.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Coarse Hair with Hair Gel

Here are some tips for maintaining your thick coarse hair with hair gel:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much gel, as this can leave your hair feeling sticky and crunchy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or oil to provide extra moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your style.

FAQs about Hair Gel for Thick Coarse Hair

What is the best hair gel for thick coarse hair?

The best hair gel for thick coarse hair is one that provides a strong hold and moisture without leaving your hair feeling sticky or crunchy. Some of the top options include SheaMoisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curling Gel Soufflé, DevaCurl Ultra Defining Gel, and Aveda Confixor Liquid Gel.

How do I apply hair gel on thick coarse hair?

To apply hair gel on thick coarse hair, start with clean, damp hair. Apply a quarter-sized amount of gel to your palm, rub your hands together to evenly distribute the gel, and then apply it to your hair, starting with the roots and working your way down to the tips. Use a comb or your fingers to evenly distribute the gel throughout your hair, and then style as desired using a blow dryer or air drying.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Coarse Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for thick coarse hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and avoid split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioner or hair mask once a week to provide extra moisture to your hair.

With the right hair gel and hair care routine, you can keep your thick coarse hair under control and looking great.
