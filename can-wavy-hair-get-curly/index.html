Can Wavy Hair Get Curly The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of your wavy hair and craving for some bouncy curls? Well, you’re not alone! Many people with wavy hair wonder if they can get curly hair naturally or through some treatment. In this ultimate guide, we’ll explore the answer to the question “Can wavy hair get curly?” and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you achieve your dream curls.

Description

Wavy hair is a type of hair that falls somewhere between straight and curly. It has a slight wave pattern that can range from loose to tight. On the other hand, curly hair has a distinct spiral shape that can be either loose or tight. While wavy hair is beautiful in its own right, some people want to add more texture and definition to their hair by making it curly.

How to Get Curly Hair from Wavy Hair

If you want to get curly hair from wavy hair, you have two options: natural methods and treatments. Here’s a breakdown of each option:

Natural Methods

If you don’t want to subject your hair to harsh chemicals or heat, there are natural methods you can use to enhance your hair’s curl pattern. Here are some tips:

  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair while it’s damp
  • Twist or braid your hair while it’s wet and let it air dry
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to create natural waves or curls
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance its curl pattern

Treatments

If you want a more permanent solution to getting curly hair from wavy hair, you can try the following treatments:

  • Perm: A perm is a chemical treatment that uses a solution to break the bonds in your hair and reform them in a curly pattern. This treatment can last for several months.
  • Keratin Treatment: A keratin treatment involves applying a protein solution to your hair to smooth out frizz and add shine. This treatment can also enhance your hair’s curl pattern.
  • Curling Irons: If you want to get curly hair for a special occasion, you can use a curling iron to create bouncy curls. However, this method is not recommended for frequent use as it can damage your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Getting Curly Hair from Wavy Hair

If you want to try the natural method of enhancing your hair’s curl pattern, here’s a step-by-step guide to follow:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair while it’s damp.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly.
  4. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying.
  5. Once your hair is dry, gently unravel the twists and use your fingers to separate the curls.
  6. Apply a curl-defining product to your curls to add definition and hold.
  7. Enjoy your new curly hair!

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you’ve achieved your dream curls, it’s important to take good care of them to maintain their shape and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons on a daily basis as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush to prevent frizz.
  • Sleep with your hair in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to keep it moisturized and prevent dryness.

FAQ

Can wavy hair turn curly on its own?

While it’s rare, some people’s hair can change texture over time due to hormonal changes, age, or other factors. However, most people’s hair texture remains the same throughout their life.

Can I get curly hair without heat or chemicals?

Yes, you can enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern by using natural methods like twisting or braiding your hair while it’s wet and letting it air-dry.

Can I get curly hair if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can get curly hair from straight hair by using treatments like a perm or a curling iron. However, these treatments can be damaging to your hair and should be used sparingly.

Hair Care is Key

Whether you have wavy hair or curly hair, taking good care of it is key to maintaining its health and beauty. By following the tips and tricks in this guide, you can achieve your dream curls and keep them looking their best!
