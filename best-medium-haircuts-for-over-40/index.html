Best Medium Haircuts For Over 40

Written by Daniel Jul 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're in your 40s and looking for a new hairstyle, medium-length haircuts can be a great choice. Not only are they versatile and easy to maintain, but they can also help you look youthful and stylish. In this article, we'll go over some of the best medium haircuts for over 40, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips

Description

Medium haircuts are typically defined as hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. They can be layered, blunt, or asymmetrical, and can be styled in a variety of ways. For women over 40, medium haircuts can help to soften facial features, add volume, and create a more youthful appearance. Some of the best medium haircuts for over 40 include:

1. Layered Lob

A layered lob (long bob) can be a great choice for women over 40. This haircut features longer layers in the front that gradually get shorter towards the back. The length can be adjusted to suit your face shape and hair texture, and the layers can add volume and movement to your hair.

2. Shaggy Bob

A shaggy bob is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish. This haircut features layers throughout the hair, with the shortest layers at the crown and longer layers towards the ends. This can create a messy, textured look that works well with a variety of hair types.

3. Blunt Cut

A blunt cut is a classic hairstyle that can work well for women over 40. This haircut features straight, blunt ends that can be styled in a variety of ways. This style can work well with thicker hair types, but may require some thinning for those with finer hair.

How To

When it comes to styling your medium haircut, there are a variety of options. Here are a few ideas:

1. Straight and Sleek

If you have naturally straight hair, you can create a sleek and polished look by blow-drying your hair straight and using a flat iron to smooth out any kinks. You can finish with a shine serum or spray for extra gloss.

2. Beachy Waves

For a more relaxed, beachy look, you can create waves by using a curling iron or wand. Start at the mid-length of your hair and wrap sections of hair around the wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Finish with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

3. Messy Bun

For a quick and easy updo, you can pull your medium-length hair back into a messy bun. This can be done by pulling your hair back into a ponytail, twisting it into a bun, and securing with bobby pins. Leave some strands loose for a more casual look.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a sleek, straight hairstyle:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair straight using a round brush.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks, starting at the roots and pulling downwards.
  5. Finish with a shine serum or spray.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your medium-length haircut:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Invest in quality styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling wand.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: What face shapes work well with medium haircuts?

A: Medium haircuts can work well with a variety of face shapes, but generally look best on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a medium haircut?

A: Yes! Medium haircuts can be styled in a variety of updos, including buns, braids, and ponytails.

Q: Will medium haircuts make me look younger?

A: While a haircut can't turn back the clock, medium haircuts can help to soften facial features and create a more youthful appearance.

Hair Care

To keep your medium-length haircut looking its best, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes:

  • Washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoiding hot tools and excessive heat styling.
  • Using a heat protectant spray when heat styling.
  • Getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can enjoy your medium haircut for years to come.
