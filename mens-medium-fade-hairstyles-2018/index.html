Mens Medium Fade Hairstyles 2018 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut in 2023, you might want to consider the mens medium fade hairstyles 2018. This style has been popular for a while now and it's easy to see why. It's versatile, stylish, and easy to maintain. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about the mens medium fade hairstyles 2018, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

  2. How to Get the Mens Medium Fade Hairstyles 2018

Description

The mens medium fade hairstyles 2018 is a type of haircut that features a gradual taper from the top of the head down to the sides and back. The fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on the length of the hair and the style you want to achieve. The top of the hair can be styled in many different ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. This style is perfect for guys who want a low-maintenance haircut that looks great on all occasions.

How to Get the Mens Medium Fade Hairstyles 2018

The first step to getting the mens medium fade hairstyles 2018 is to find a good barber or stylist. Look for someone who has experience cutting this type of haircut and who can give you the look you want. Once you've found your barber, you'll need to decide how high you want your fade to be. A low fade will start just above the ears, while a high fade will start near the top of the head. Your barber will be able to help you make this decision based on the shape of your face and the style you want to achieve.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to getting the mens medium fade hairstyles 2018:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide how high you want your fade to be.
  3. Using clippers, your barber will start with a longer guard and work their way down to a shorter guard as they move towards the bottom of your head.
  4. The hair on top of your head should be longer than the hair on the sides and back.
  5. Style the hair on top of your head as desired.

And that's it! Now you have the mens medium fade hairstyles 2018.

Tips for Styling Mens Medium Fade Hairstyles 2018

Here are some tips for styling your mens medium fade hairstyles 2018:

  • Use a good quality hair product to keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • If you have a beard, consider styling it to complement your haircut.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain your fade and keep it looking fresh.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens medium fade hairstyles 2018:

  • Is the mens medium fade hairstyles 2018 suitable for all hair types?
  • Yes, the mens medium fade hairstyles 2018 can be styled on all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.
  • How often do I need to visit my barber to maintain my fade?
  • You should visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your fade and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Medium Fade Hairstyles 2018

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your mens medium fade hairstyles 2018:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

And there you have it, everything you need to know about the mens medium fade hairstyles 2018. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut or a stylish new look, the mens medium fade hairstyles 2018 is a great option. So why not give it a try?
