How To Grow A Man Bun With Shaved Sides

Written by Frank May 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking to grow a trendy man bun with shaved sides? This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. It’s a great way to show off your style and personality, while also keeping your hair out of your face. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to grow a man bun with shaved sides. We’ll also give you some tips on hair care and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow a Man Bun with Shaved Sides
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing a Man Bun with Shaved Sides
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for a Man Bun with Shaved Sides

Step-by-Step Guide

The first step in growing a man bun with shaved sides is to let your hair grow out. It’s important to have at least 6 inches of hair on the top of your head before you start shaving the sides. Once you have the desired length, follow these steps:

Step 1: Decide on the Style

Before you start shaving, you need to decide on the style you want. Do you want a high or low fade? Do you want a disconnected undercut? Look at pictures of different styles and decide which one you like best.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clippers

You’ll need clippers to shave the sides of your head. Make sure you choose the right ones for your hair type. If you have thick hair, you’ll need clippers with a powerful motor. If you have fine hair, you can use clippers with a less powerful motor.

Step 3: Shave the Sides

Start by trimming the sides of your hair with a longer clipper guard. Then, work your way down to a shorter guard until you reach the desired length. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once. You can always go shorter, but you can’t go back.

Step 4: Style the Top

Once you’ve shaved the sides, it’s time to style the top. You can either tie it back into a bun or style it in a different way. Use a brush or comb to style your hair to your desired look.

Tips for Growing a Man Bun with Shaved Sides

Here are some tips to help you grow a man bun with shaved sides:

  • Be patient. It takes time to grow your hair out to the desired length.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Brush or comb your hair regularly to prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a heat protectant if you do.

FAQ

Can anyone grow a man bun with shaved sides?

Most men can grow a man bun with shaved sides as long as they have the right hair length and texture. If you have curly hair, it may be more difficult to achieve the desired look.

Do I need to use hair products to style my man bun?

It depends on your hair type and the style you want. If you have thick or coarse hair, you may need to use a pomade or wax to keep your hair in place. If you have fine hair, you may not need any products.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying and to keep your scalp healthy.

Hair Care for a Man Bun with Shaved Sides

Keeping your hair healthy is important when growing a man bun with shaved sides. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair. Use lukewarm water instead.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of protein and vitamins to promote hair growth.

Follow these tips and steps to grow a stylish man bun with shaved sides. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
