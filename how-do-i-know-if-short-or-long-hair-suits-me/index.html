How Do I Know If Short Or Long Hair Suits Me

Written by Petter Feb 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Deciding whether to go for short or long hair can be a daunting task. It requires a lot of thought and consideration as your hair is one of the most noticeable features of your appearance. So, how do you know which hair length suits you best? This article will guide you through the process of figuring that out.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine If Short or Long Hair Suits You
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Hair Length
  • Tips to Consider When Deciding on Short or Long Hair
  • FAQs About Choosing Hair Length
  • Hair Care Tips for Short and Long Hair

How to Determine If Short or Long Hair Suits You

The first step to figuring out whether short or long hair suits you is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you understand which hair length will complement your features the most. Here are the different face shapes and the hair lengths that suit them:

  • Oval face shape: This face shape can carry off both short and long hair.
  • Round face shape: Long hair is the best option for this face shape as it elongates your face.
  • Square face shape: Soft, layered haircuts look great on square faces. Avoid straight, blunt cuts.
  • Heart face shape: Chin-length hair with layers and side-swept bangs are perfect for this face shape.
  • Diamond face shape: Short hair with volume on top and long bangs work well for this face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape and the hair lengths that suit you, the next step is to consider your hair texture and thickness. Thick hair looks great in longer lengths while fine hair looks better in shorter lengths. Your lifestyle and personality also play a role in the decision-making process.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Hair Length

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you determine which hair length suits you:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Determine the hair lengths that suit your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair texture and thickness.
  4. Think about your lifestyle and personality. Do you have the time to style long hair every day?
  5. Look for inspiration. Browse through magazines or celebrity hairstyles to find a style that you like.
  6. Consult your hairstylist. They will be able to give you expert advice on which hair length will suit you best.
  7. Take the plunge. Once you've made your decision, go for it!

Tips to Consider When Deciding on Short or Long Hair

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when deciding on short or long hair:

  • Think about the maintenance. Short hair may require more frequent trips to the salon to maintain the shape while long hair may require more time and effort to style.
  • Consider your face shape and hair texture. These factors play a big role in determining which hair length will suit you.
  • Take into account your lifestyle. Do you have the time and patience to style long hair every day?
  • Experiment with wigs. Wigs are a great way to try out different hair lengths before committing to a cut.

FAQs About Choosing Hair Length

Here are some commonly asked questions about choosing hair length:

Q: Does hair length affect hair health?
A: No, hair length does not affect hair health. The health of your hair depends on how well you take care of it. Q: Can I change my hair length frequently?
A: Yes, you can change your hair length as frequently as you like. However, keep in mind that frequent coloring, styling, and cutting can damage your hair. Q: Can I pull off both short and long hair?
A: It depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some people can carry off both short and long hair while others look better with one or the other.

Hair Care Tips for Short and Long Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short and long hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

In conclusion, choosing between short and long hair can be a difficult decision. However, by taking into account your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style, you can determine which hair length suits you best. Remember to consult your hairstylist for expert advice and to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
