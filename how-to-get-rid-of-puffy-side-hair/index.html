How To Get Rid Of Puffy Side Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read
Are you tired of dealing with puffy side hair that ruins your hairstyle? Do you struggle to tame your hair, especially on humid days? If you answered yes, then this article is for you! In this guide, we will show you how to get rid of puffy side hair and achieve a sleek, polished look that you can be proud of.

Description

Puffy side hair is a common problem that many people face, especially those with curly or wavy hair. This type of hair tends to be frizzy, unruly, and difficult to manage, which can be frustrating when trying to achieve a particular hairstyle. However, with the right techniques and products, you can tame your puffy side hair and achieve the look you desire.

How to Get Rid of Puffy Side Hair

Getting rid of puffy side hair requires a combination of the right techniques and products. Here are the steps you should follow to achieve a sleek, polished look:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in getting rid of puffy side hair is to wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and frizzy. After washing, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends and the areas that tend to get the most frizzy. This will help to moisturize your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Step 3: Use a Hair Oil

After applying the leave-in conditioner, apply a hair oil to your hair, focusing on the ends and the areas that tend to get the most frizzy. This will help to seal in the moisture and prevent your hair from becoming dry and frizzy.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Once you have applied the leave-in conditioner and hair oil, blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help to smooth out your hair and prevent it from becoming puffy.

Step 5: Flat Iron Your Hair

After blow drying your hair, use a flat iron to straighten your hair, focusing on the areas that tend to get the most frizzy. This will help to achieve a sleek, polished look and prevent your hair from becoming puffy.

Step 6: Apply a Hair Serum

Finally, apply a hair serum to your hair to add shine and prevent frizz. This will help to keep your hair looking polished and sleek throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get rid of puffy side hair: - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing it. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage. - Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent your hair from becoming tangled and frizzy while you sleep. - Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth and prevent dryness.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a hair mask to get rid of puffy side hair? A: Yes, using a hair mask once a week can help to moisturize your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and frizzy. Q: Should I use a heat protectant before blow drying and flat ironing my hair? A: Yes, using a heat protectant can help to prevent damage and breakage caused by heat styling tools.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to prevent puffy side hair and achieve a healthy, polished look. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair: - Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools. - Use a leave-in conditioner and hair oil to moisturize your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. - Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth. By following these tips and techniques, you can get rid of puffy side hair and achieve a sleek, polished look that you can be proud of. Remember to take good care of your hair to prevent dryness and frizz, and use the right products to achieve the results you desire.
