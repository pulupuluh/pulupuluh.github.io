How To Do Cute Hairstyles For Afro Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 08, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have afro hair, you know that it requires special care and attention when it comes to styling. While there are plenty of options out there, it can be tough to find hairstyles that are both cute and easy to create. In this article, we’ll walk you through some of our favorite cute hairstyles for afro hair so you can look your best every day.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles themselves, there are a few things you should know about styling afro hair. First, it’s important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for products that are moisturizing and nourishing, as afro hair tends to be dry and brittle. Second, be gentle when handling your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and opt for protective styles like braids and twists whenever possible. Finally, don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

Step by step

The High Ponytail

The high ponytail is a classic hairstyle that works well with afro hair. Here’s how to create it:

  1. Brush your hair up and away from your face, gathering it into a high ponytail.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Use a small amount of gel or edge control to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.

The Twist Out

A twist out is a great way to add texture and volume to your afro hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Apply a twisting cream or butter to each section.
  4. Twist each section tightly, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Allow your hair to dry completely, then gently unravel each twist.
  6. Use your fingers to separate the twists and create volume.

The Braided Crown

The braided crown is a beautiful and elegant hairstyle that’s perfect for special occasions. Here’s how to create it:

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Starting at the front of your head, braid a small section of hair and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Wrap each braid around the back of your head, securing them with bobby pins.
  5. Repeat on the other side, crossing the braids over each other in the back.
  6. Secure any loose ends with bobby pins.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hair accessories to add some extra flair to your hairstyles.
  • Don’t be afraid to try new styles – you might be surprised at what looks great on you!
  • Be gentle when detangling your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping with a satin or silk pillowcase.

FAQ

What products should I use for styling afro hair?

Look for products that are moisturizing and nourishing, like leave-in conditioners, oils, and butters. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or alcohols, as these can dry out your hair.

How often should I wash my afro hair?

This will depend on your individual hair type and how much styling product you use. Generally, you should aim to wash your hair every 1-2 weeks.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful afro hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s specifically designed for afro hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Opt for protective styles like braids and twists whenever possible.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to create cute hairstyles for afro hair that look great and are easy to maintain. Whether you’re heading to work, school, or a special event, you’ll be able to rock your afro hair with confidence!
