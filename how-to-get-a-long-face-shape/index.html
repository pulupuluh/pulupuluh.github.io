How To Get A Long Face Shape Tips Steps And Hair Care

Are you looking for ways to get a longer face shape? A long face shape can make you look slimmer, more refined, and elegant. In this article, we will provide you with tips, steps, and hair care routines to get a long face shape. Read on to learn more.

Table of Contents

What is a Long Face Shape?

A long face shape is characterized by a narrow and elongated face. People with a long face shape have a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are all the same width. This face shape is also known as an oblong face shape.

If you want to achieve a long face shape, there are several ways to do so. Here are some tips and steps you can follow:

How to Get a Long Face Shape

1. Contour your face

Contouring your face can help you create the illusion of a longer face. Use a contour powder or stick that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply the contour powder or stick along your hairline, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline.

2. Use a highlighter

Using a highlighter on the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, and your chin can help elongate your face. This will draw attention to the center of your face and make it appear longer.

3. Wear sunglasses

Wearing sunglasses with a wide frame can help make your face appear longer. Look for sunglasses with frames that are wider than your face to create the illusion of a longer face.

4. Choose the right hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also have an impact on the shape of your face. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face and keeps the top of your head relatively flat. This will help balance out the proportions of your face and make it appear longer.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Begin by applying your foundation and concealer as usual.

2. Apply a contour powder or stick that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone along your hairline, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline.

3. Blend the contour powder or stick using a brush or sponge to create a natural-looking shadow.

4. Apply a highlighter on the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, and your chin.

5. Use a wide frame sunglasses to make your face appear longer.

6. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face and keeps the top of your head relatively flat.

Tips to Maintain a Long Face Shape

1. Maintain a healthy diet and exercise regularly to keep your face slim and toned.

2. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear shorter.

3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, as this can add volume to the sides of your face.

FAQs

Q: Can I get a long face shape naturally?

A: Yes, some people are born with a long face shape. However, there are ways to enhance or create the illusion of a long face shape using makeup and hairstyles.

Q: Will losing weight help me get a long face shape?

A: Losing weight can help you slim down your face and make it appear longer.

Hair Care for Long Face Shape

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the sides of your face.

2. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head. Instead, choose hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, as this can add volume to the sides of your face.

4. Avoid straightening your hair too often, as this can make your face appear longer.

In conclusion, achieving a long face shape is possible with the right makeup and hairstyle techniques. Follow the tips and steps in this article to create the illusion of a longer face, and maintain it with proper hair care routines.
