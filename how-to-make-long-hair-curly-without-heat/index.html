How to Make Long Hair Curly Without Heat

If you're tired of using heat to curl your hair, there are several ways to achieve beautiful curls without damaging your hair. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to make long hair curly without heat.

How to Make Long Hair Curly Without Heat

There are several methods you can use to make long hair curly without heat, including:

  • Twist Method
  • Braid Method
  • Plopping Method

Each method gives a different type of curl, so it's best to try them all out and see which one works best for your hair type.

Twist Method

The twist method involves twisting sections of your hair and leaving them overnight. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and squeeze out the excess water.
  2. Divide your hair into sections, depending on how big you want your curls to be.
  3. Take a section of hair and twist it tightly until it starts to coil on itself.
  4. Secure the end of the twist with a hair tie or hairpin.
  5. Repeat the process until all sections of your hair are twisted.
  6. Leave the twists in overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  7. Take out the twists and gently separate the curls with your fingers.
  8. Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to set the curls.

Braid Method

The braid method involves braiding sections of your hair and leaving them overnight. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and squeeze out the excess water.
  2. Divide your hair into sections, depending on how big you want your curls to be.
  3. Take a section of hair and braid it tightly until you reach the end.
  4. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  5. Repeat the process until all sections of your hair are braided.
  6. Leave the braids in overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  7. Take out the braids and gently separate the curls with your fingers.
  8. Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to set the curls.

Plopping Method

The plopping method involves using a t-shirt to create curls. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and squeeze out the excess water.
  2. Flip your hair over and place a t-shirt on a flat surface.
  3. Place your hair in the center of the t-shirt and use the sides of the t-shirt to wrap your hair around your head.
  4. Secure the t-shirt at the nape of your neck with a hair tie.
  5. Leave the t-shirt on for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Take off the t-shirt and gently separate the curls with your fingers.
  7. Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to set the curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a general step-by-step guide on how to make long hair curly without heat:

  1. Wash your hair and squeeze out the excess water.
  2. Choose a method to create curls (twist, braid, or plopping).
  3. Create sections of your hair and use your chosen method to create curls.
  4. Leave the curls in overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  5. Take out the curls and gently separate them with your fingers.
  6. Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to set the curls.

Tips for Making Long Hair Curly Without Heat

Here are some tips to help you make long hair curly without heat:

  • Use a light hold hairspray to set the curls.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your curls.
  • Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and maintain the curls.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about making long hair curly without heat:

  • Can I use a hairdryer to speed up the process? No, using heat will damage your hair and defeat the purpose of making curls without heat.
  • How long will the curls last? The curls can last anywhere from a few days to a week, depending on how well you maintain them.
  • Can I make curls without using any products? While it's possible to create curls without using products, using a light hold hairspray will help set the curls and make them last longer.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to keep your curls hydrated and defined.

Now that you know how to make long hair curly without heat, try out these methods and enjoy your beautiful curls!
