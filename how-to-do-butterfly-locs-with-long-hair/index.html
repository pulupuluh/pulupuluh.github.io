How To Do Butterfly Locs With Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William May 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new and trendy protective hairstyle, butterfly locs may be just what you need. These stylish locs are inspired by butterfly wings and are created by using a crochet method to add texture and dimension to your hair. In this article, we'll show you how to do butterfly locs with long hair, including tips on hair care, maintenance, and styling.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Do Butterfly Locs with Long Hair

Butterfly locs are a great protective hairstyle for anyone with long hair who wants to switch up their look. Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • Crochet needle
  • Butterfly locs hair
  • Rat tail comb
  • Clips
  • Leave-in conditioner

Before starting the process, make sure your hair is clean, detangled, and well-moisturized. This will help to prevent breakage and ensure your locs look smooth and frizz-free.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create beautiful butterfly locs:

Step 1: Part Your Hair

Using a rat tail comb, part your hair into small sections. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want your locs to be.

Step 2: Attach the Hair

Take a section of the butterfly locs hair and fold it in half. Insert the crochet needle through the base of the section of hair and grab the folded end with the hook. Pull the hair through the base of your own hair, leaving a small loop.

Step 3: Pull the Hair Through

Take the two ends of the butterfly locs hair and pull them through the loop. Pull tight to secure the hair in place.

Step 4: Repeat the Process

Continue this process until you have covered your entire head with butterfly locs. Be sure to check the tension of each loc to ensure they are not too tight or too loose.

Step 5: Style Your Locs

Once you have completed your butterfly locs, you can style them in a variety of ways. You can wear them down, in a ponytail, or in a bun. You can also add accessories, such as beads or wraps, to give your locs some extra flair.

Tips for Maintaining Butterfly Locs

Here are some tips to help you maintain your butterfly locs:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly to prevent dryness and itching.
  • Wash your locs every two weeks to keep them clean and fresh.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your locs.
  • Keep your locs away from water as much as possible to prevent frizzing.

FAQs about Butterfly Locs

Here are some frequently asked questions about butterfly locs:

How long do butterfly locs last?

Butterfly locs can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks with proper maintenance.

Can I swim with butterfly locs?

Avoid getting your butterfly locs wet as much as possible. If you do go swimming, wear a swim cap or wrap your hair in a scarf to protect it from the water.

Can I color my butterfly locs?

Yes, you can color your butterfly locs. However, it's important to use a gentle, ammonia-free hair dye and to consult with a professional stylist to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Butterfly Locs

To keep your butterfly locs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your locs.
  • Wrap your hair in a satin or silk scarf at night to prevent breakage and frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • When washing your hair, use a gentle shampoo and focus on your scalp to avoid tangling and matting.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to create beautiful butterfly locs with your long hair. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
