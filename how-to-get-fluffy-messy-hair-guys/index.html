How To Get Fluffy Messy Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both trendy and effortless, then fluffy messy hair might be the perfect option for you. This hairstyle is popular among guys because it gives off a laid-back vibe that's perfect for casual occasions. In this article, we'll show you how to get fluffy messy hair guys in a few easy steps.

  • How to Get Fluffy Messy Hair Guys

How to Get Fluffy Messy Hair Guys

Getting fluffy messy hair is simple and straightforward. Here are the steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair.

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and damp. This will make it easier to work with and style the way you want it to look.

Step 2: Apply a styling product.

Apply a styling product like a texturizing spray or a pomade to your hair. This will help give your hair some texture and hold, which is essential for fluffy messy hair.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair.

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while using your fingers or a comb to style it. Make sure to blow-dry your hair in different directions to create volume and texture.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray.

Once you've styled your hair the way you want it, spray some hairspray to hold it in place. This will help your hair maintain its shape and texture throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Fluffy Messy Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your fluffy messy hair:

  • Don't wash your hair every day. Fluffy messy hair looks better when it's a bit dirty, so try not to wash it every day.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to style your hair. This will help prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Can I get fluffy messy hair with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still get fluffy messy hair with short hair. Just use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

Q: Can I use a hair gel instead of a pomade?

A: Yes, you can use a hair gel instead of a pomade. However, make sure to use a lightweight gel that won't weigh down your hair.

Hair Care for Fluffy Messy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your fluffy messy hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

And there you have it! Follow these simple steps to get fluffy messy hair guys and maintain it with proper hair care.
