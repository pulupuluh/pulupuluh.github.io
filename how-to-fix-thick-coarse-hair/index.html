How To Fix Thick Coarse Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Frank Mar 13, 2023 · 7 min read

Thick, coarse hair can be a nightmare to manage. It can be difficult to style and prone to frizz and breakage. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for fixing thick coarse hair, as well as some hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fix Thick Coarse Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Thick Coarse Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Coarse Hair

How to Fix Thick Coarse Hair

There are a few things you can do to fix your thick coarse hair, including:

1. Get a Haircut

Getting a haircut can help to remove some of the bulk from your hair and make it more manageable. Talk to your stylist about the best cut for your hair type.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Look for products that are designed for thick, coarse hair, such as shampoos, conditioners, and styling products.

3. Try a Keratin Treatment

A keratin treatment can help to smooth out your hair and reduce frizz. It can also make your hair more manageable and easier to style.

4. Use Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, can help to tame your hair and make it more manageable. Just be sure to use them sparingly and protect your hair with a heat protectant spray.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the things you can do to fix your thick coarse hair, here’s a step-by-step guide:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for thick, coarse hair.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help keep it moisturized and reduce frizz.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to help smooth it out and reduce frizz.

4. Use Heat Styling Tools

Use heat styling tools, such as a straightener or curling iron, to style your hair the way you want it.

5. Finish with a Serum

Finish with a serum to add shine and help keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

Tips for Managing Thick Coarse Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing your thick coarse hair:

1. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it even more difficult to manage. Try washing your hair every other day or every few days instead.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb can help to detangle your hair without causing damage or breakage.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, such as ponytails and braids, can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Try to avoid them, or at least wear them loosely.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my thick coarse hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a dye that is specifically designed for your hair type and follow the instructions carefully.

Q: Will a keratin treatment make my hair straight?

A: A keratin treatment can help to smooth out your hair and reduce frizz, but it won’t necessarily make it straight.

Hair Care for Thick Coarse Hair

Finally, here are some tips for taking care of your thick coarse hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help to remove split ends and keep your hair healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Be sure to use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools, and try to limit your use of them as much as possible.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fix your thick coarse hair and keep it healthy and beautiful.
