Short Haircuts For Thick Coarse Curly Hair Over 50

Are you over 50 and tired of dealing with your thick, coarse, curly hair? Short haircuts might be the solution you're looking for! In this article, we'll discuss the best short haircuts for thick coarse curly hair over 50, including how to style and care for your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts are a great option for women with thick, coarse, curly hair over 50. Not only are they low maintenance, but they can also help to tame unruly curls and showcase your natural texture. Plus, short haircuts can help to make you look younger and more stylish!

How to

Before getting a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A professional hairstylist can help you choose the best haircut for your features and lifestyle.

When styling your hair, start with a good shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair, then use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Finish with a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and tame frizz.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a short haircut for thick coarse curly hair over 50:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a curl-friendly shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel.
  4. Blow dry your hair with a diffuser attachment, using your hands to scrunch your curls as you go.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any pieces that need it.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and tame frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting the best short haircut for thick coarse curly hair over 50:

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider getting layers to add movement and volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to embrace your natural texture!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for thick coarse curly hair over 50:

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: No! In fact, a well-chosen short haircut can actually make you look younger and more stylish.

Q: Will a short haircut be difficult to style?

A: Not necessarily. With the right products and styling techniques, a short haircut can be low maintenance and easy to style.

Hair Care

When caring for your short haircut, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz. Instead, embrace your natural texture and use lightweight styling products to enhance your curls.

Regular trims are also important to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy. Visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim and to discuss any changes you might want to make to your haircut or styling routine.

With the right short haircut and hair care routine, you can rock your thick, coarse, curly hair over 50 and look fabulous doing it!
