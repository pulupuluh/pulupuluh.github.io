Description

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, the mens haircut short sides long curly top is a great option. This haircut features short sides and a longer, curly top, creating a unique and eye-catching look.

The short sides of the haircut provide a clean and tidy appearance while the long curly top adds volume and texture to the hair. This style is perfect for men who want to showcase their curls and add some personality to their look.

How to

The mens haircut short sides long curly top can be achieved by following these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose your length

Decide on the length of the short sides and the long top. Make sure to consider your face shape and hair type when making this decision.

Step 2: Cut the sides

Use clippers to cut the sides of the hair to the desired length. Gradually blend the hair from the sides to the top to create a seamless transition.

Step 3: Trim the top

Trim the top of the hair to the desired length, leaving enough length for the curls to form. Use scissors to create layers and texture in the hair.

Step 4: Style the curls

Apply a curl-enhancing product to the top of the hair and use your fingers to scrunch the curls. Allow the hair to air dry or use a diffuser on a low heat setting to dry the curls.

Step by step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to achieving the mens haircut short sides long curly top:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Towel dry your hair gently, leaving it slightly damp.

Step 2: Section the hair

Section the hair into three parts: the top, left side, and right side. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 3: Cut the sides

Start with the sides of the hair. Use clippers to cut the hair to the desired length, starting with a longer guard and gradually decreasing the length. Blend the hair from the sides to the top to create a seamless transition.

Step 4: Trim the top

Move on to the top of the hair. Use scissors to trim the hair to the desired length, leaving enough length for the curls to form. Create layers and texture in the hair by cutting at different angles.

Step 5: Style the curls

Apply a curl-enhancing product to the top of the hair, using a generous amount. Use your fingers to scrunch the curls, encouraging them to form. Allow the hair to air dry or use a diffuser on a low heat setting to dry the curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results with the mens haircut short sides long curly top:

  • Choose a stylist who is experienced with curly hair to ensure a great cut.
  • Use a curl-enhancing product that works well with your hair type.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to tame frizz and add shine.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Invest in a good diffuser attachment for your hair dryer to enhance your curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut short sides long curly top:

Q: Will this haircut work for me if I have straight hair?

A: This haircut is specifically designed for curly hair, but you can still achieve a similar look with straight hair by using a curling iron or hot rollers on the top of the hair.

Q: How often should I get this haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the style loses its shape. Generally, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Q: Can I still tie my hair back with this haircut?

A: Yes, you can still tie your hair back, but you may need to use bobby pins or clips to secure the shorter sides.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut short sides long curly top looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

In conclusion, the mens haircut short sides long curly top is a stylish and trendy option for men who want to showcase their curls. With the right cut and styling products, you can achieve a unique and eye-catching look that is sure to turn heads.
