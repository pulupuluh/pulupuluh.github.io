How To Cut Layers On Side Of Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking to switch things up? Cutting layers on the side of your head can add texture and movement to your hair. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect layered look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Layers on Side of Head
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Layers on Side of Head
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Layers on Side of Head

How to Cut Layers on Side of Head

Cutting layers on the side of your head can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a beautiful layered look. Here are the steps:

1. Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a fine-tooth comb, and hair clips to section off your hair.

2. Section Off Your Hair

Using the fine-tooth comb, create a horizontal parting across the back of your head, just above the nape of your neck. Clip the top section of your hair up and out of the way.

Next, create a diagonal parting from the back of your ear towards the top of your head. Clip the top section of hair again, leaving the bottom section loose.

3. Start Cutting

Starting at the bottom section, comb out any tangles and hold the hair between your index and middle fingers. Point your scissors vertically towards the hair and make a small cut to create a guide.

Using the guide, cut the rest of the hair in small sections, cutting at a slight angle to create layers. Repeat this process for the top section.

Step-by-Step Guide

To help you visualize the process, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1:

Gather your tools: sharp scissors, fine-tooth comb, and hair clips.

Step 2:

Section off your hair with the fine-tooth comb.

Step 3:

Start cutting the bottom section of your hair, using a guide and cutting at a slight angle.

Step 4:

Cut the top section of your hair using the same technique.

Step 5:

Blend the layers by combing through your hair and making small snips where necessary.

Tips for Cutting Layers on Side of Head

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting layers on the side of your head:

  • Start with a small cut and work your way up. It's easier to take off more hair than to add it back on.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and it's important to take care of yourself.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Cutting hair can be difficult, especially if you're new to it.

FAQs

Can I cut my own layers on the side of my head?

Yes, you can cut your own layers on the side of your head. Just make sure you have the right tools and take your time.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always cut more hair to even it out or style your hair in a way that hides the mistake.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care After Cutting Layers on Side of Head

Once you've cut your layers, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting layers on the side of your head can be a fun and easy way to switch up your hairstyle. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a beautiful layered look. Just remember to take your time and take care of your hair afterwards.
