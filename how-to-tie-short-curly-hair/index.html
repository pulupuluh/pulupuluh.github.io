How To Tie Short Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 13, 2023 Â· 12 min read

Short curly hair can be a challenge to style, especially when it comes to tying it up. However, with the right technique and a few tips, you can create a variety of hairstyles that are both practical and stylish. In this article, we will walk you through the process of tying short curly hair, from preparation to finishing touches. We will also provide some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful curls.

Preparation

Before tying up your short curly hair, it is important to prepare your hair properly. This will help to ensure that your hairstyle stays in place and looks polished.

Start by washing your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and defined. After washing, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help tame frizz and add moisture to your hair.

Next, detangle your hair gently using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Be careful not to pull or tug on your curls, as this can cause damage and frizz.

You may also want to consider using a styling product, such as a gel or mousse, to help define your curls and hold your hairstyle in place. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that tend to frizz or lose their shape.

The Curly Ponytail

The curly ponytail is a classic hairstyle that is both practical and stylish. To create this look, follow these steps:

  1. Gather your hair into a high or low ponytail, depending on your preference.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the underside of the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to hide it.
  4. Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

The curly ponytail is perfect for a casual day out or a workout session. You can also dress it up by adding a headband or some hair accessories.

The Curly Bun

The curly bun is a chic and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for a night out or a special occasion. Here's how to create it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape.
  3. Pin the bun in place using bobby pins or hair clips.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a soft, romantic look.
  5. Spray your hair with a strong hold hairspray to keep the bun in place.

The curly bun works best with hair that is shoulder length or longer. You can also add some hair accessories, such as a decorative hairpin or a flower crown, to make the look more festive.

The Curly Braids

The curly braids are a fun and playful hairstyle that is perfect for a day at the beach or a music festival. Here's how to create them:

  1. Divide your hair into two sections, either by parting it down the middle or creating a deep side part.
  2. Take one section of hair and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Braid the hair by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section.
  4. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair, then secure the braid with a hair tie.
  5. Repeat the process on the other side.
  6. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a soft, romantic look.
  7. Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep the braids in place.

The curly braids work best with hair that is shoulder length or longer. You can also add some hair accessories, such as beads or feathers, to make the look more bohemian.

Accessorizing Your Curly Hair

One of the easiest ways to dress up your short curly hair is by adding some accessories. Here are some ideas:

  • Headbands: A simple headband can add a pop of color or pattern to your hair.
  • Hair clips: Decorative hair clips can be used to pin back your bangs or add some sparkle to your hair.
  • Scrunchies: A colorful scrunchie can add a fun, retro vibe to your curly ponytail.
  • Hairpins: Delicate hairpins can be used to create a half-up, half-down hairstyle or to pin back stray hairs.

When choosing hair accessories, look for ones that complement your hair color and skin tone. You can also experiment with different styles to find the ones that suit your personality and taste.

Tips for Tying Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for tying short curly hair:

  • Avoid using tight hair ties or rubber bands, as these can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to help reduce frizz and preserve your curls while you sleep.
  • Try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the ones that work best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Don't be afraid to seek advice from a hairstylist or fellow curly-haired friend.

FAQ

How often should I wash my short curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry or curly hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week. If you have oily or fine hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. You can also use dry shampoo in between washes to help absorb excess oil and refresh your hair.

How do I prevent my curly hair from getting tangled?

Regular detangling is key to preventing tangles and knots in your curly hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair when it is wet, starting at the ends and working your way up. You can also apply a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to help ease the process.

How do I define my curls?

There are a variety of products that can help define your curls, such as curl creams, gels, and mousses. Apply the product to your hair evenly, focusing on the ends and any areas that tend to frizz or lose their shape. You can also try diffusing your hair with a hair dryer on a low heat setting, using a diffuser attachment to help enhance your curls.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Here are some general tips for keeping your short curly hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often, as these can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create a variety of beautiful hairstyles for your short curly hair. Whether you prefer a casual ponytail or an elegant bun, there are plenty of options to choose from. Remember to take good care of your hair, and it will reward you with healthy, gorgeous curls.
