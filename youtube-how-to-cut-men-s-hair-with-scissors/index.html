How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide From Youtube

Written by Daniel Aug 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a cost-effective way to get a haircut or simply want to try your hand at hair styling, then cutting men's hair with scissors may just be the perfect option for you. But before you begin, you need to know the basics. Fortunately, there are plenty of YouTube tutorials that can help you master the skill of cutting men's hair with scissors. In this article, we'll walk you through the process and provide you with some tips, tricks, and FAQs.

Description

Cutting men's hair with scissors is a popular way to achieve a neat, clean-cut look. It's also a great way to save money on haircuts over time. However, it can be intimidating if you've never done it before. That's where YouTube comes in. There are plenty of tutorials available that can help you learn the basics of hair cutting and give you the confidence to try it at home.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors

Step One: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting men's hair with scissors is to gather your tools. You'll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also want to invest in a trimmer or razor for detailing.

Step Two: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you begin cutting, it's important to wash and dry the hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a clean cut.

Step Three: Section the Hair

Using your comb, section the hair into manageable portions. Clip each section out of the way until you're ready to work on it.

Step Four: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the hair by starting at the back of the head and working your way forward. Use your comb to guide the scissors and make sure you're cutting at the desired length.

Step Five: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the hair to the desired length, it's time to blend it. Use your scissors to gently feather the hair and create a natural look.

Step Six: Detailing

Finally, use your trimmer or razor to detail the hair around the ears and neck. This will give the cut a polished look.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you cut men's hair with scissors:

  • Always start with clean, dry hair
  • Use the comb to guide the scissors and ensure an even cut
  • Feather the hair to create a natural look
  • Take your time and be patient
  • Invest in quality hair cutting scissors

FAQs about Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

Is it safe to cut men's hair with scissors?

Yes, it's safe to cut men's hair with scissors as long as you follow the proper techniques and take your time.

What types of scissors should I use?

You should use hair cutting scissors, which are sharper and more precise than regular scissors.

How often should men get their hair cut?

It depends on the individual and their hair type. On average, men should get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care after Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

After cutting men's hair with scissors, it's important to care for the hair to maintain the cut. This includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as using styling products to keep the hair in place.

Conclusion

With the help of YouTube tutorials and some basic tools, cutting men's hair with scissors is a simple and cost-effective way to achieve a clean-cut look. By following the steps outlined in this article and keeping our tips and FAQs in mind, you'll be well on your way to becoming a hair cutting pro.
