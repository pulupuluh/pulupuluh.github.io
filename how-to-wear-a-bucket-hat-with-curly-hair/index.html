How To Wear A Bucket Hat With Curly Hair

Written by Petter Jun 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you might be hesitant to try out the bucket hat trend. But fear not, there are ways to wear a bucket hat with curly hair that look stylish and effortless. In this article, we will be sharing tips on how to wear a bucket hat with curly hair, step-by-step instructions, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Bucket Hat with Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Bucket Hat with Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Wear a Bucket Hat with Curly Hair

The first step to wearing a bucket hat with curly hair is to choose the right hat. Look for a hat that fits well and complements your face shape. You can opt for a classic denim bucket hat or go for a bold print or color.

Next, consider the style of your hair. If you have tight, voluminous curls, you may want to wear your hair down to balance out the hat's proportions. If you have looser curls, you can try tucking your hair behind your ears or pulling it back into a low ponytail or bun.

When wearing a bucket hat with curly hair, it's important to avoid flattening your curls. Instead, embrace the natural shape and volume of your curls. You can gently scrunch your hair with your hands while wearing the hat to enhance your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear a bucket hat with curly hair:

  1. Choose a bucket hat that fits well and complements your face shape.
  2. Style your hair as desired. For tight curls, wear your hair down. For looser curls, consider pulling your hair back.
  3. Place the bucket hat on your head, positioning it slightly back on your forehead.
  4. Adjust the hat so that it sits comfortably on your head and doesn't flatten your curls.
  5. Gently scrunch your curls with your hands to enhance their natural shape and volume.

Tips for Wearing a Bucket Hat with Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for wearing a bucket hat with curly hair:

  • Opt for a hat with a wider brim to provide more coverage and protect your curls from the sun.
  • Consider wearing a silk scarf or bonnet underneath your hat to protect your hair from friction and prevent frizz.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a half-up half-down style or a messy bun, to find what works best with your hat.
  • Accessorize your hat with statement earrings or a bold lip to elevate your look.

FAQs

Can I wear a bucket hat with short curly hair?

Absolutely! Bucket hats can look great on all hair lengths. For short curly hair, consider wearing your hair in a pixie cut or a tapered style to balance out the proportions of the hat.

Can I wear a bucket hat with straightened hair?

Yes, you can wear a bucket hat with straightened hair. However, keep in mind that the hat may flatten your straightened hair and affect its shape. Consider using a volumizing product to add some texture and volume to your hair before wearing the hat.

How do I clean my bucket hat?

Check the label on your hat for specific cleaning instructions. In general, you can spot clean the hat with a damp cloth and mild soap. Avoid submerging the hat in water, as this can affect its shape.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Wearing a bucket hat with curly hair is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, hydrated curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your curls to keep them hydrated and defined.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and protect your curls from tangling.

By following these hair care tips and wearing a bucket hat with curly hair, you can enjoy healthy, hydrated curls and a stylish, on-trend look.
