How To Wear Lace Front Wig Without Glue A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 05, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of using adhesive products to secure your lace front wig? Do you want to know how to wear it without glue? If so, you’re in the right place. In this guide, we’ll show you how to wear a lace front wig without the use of glue. We’ll cover everything from preparation to hair care, so you can confidently wear your wig without fear of it falling off.

Table of Contents

  • Preparation
  • Wearing a Wig Cap
  • Making Adjustments
  • Creating Baby Hairs
  • Wig Maintenance
  • Tips for Wearing a Wig without Glue
  • FAQs

Preparation

The first step in wearing a lace front wig without glue is to prepare your natural hair. You need to make sure that your hair is clean, dry, and flat. You can achieve this by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying it, and flat ironing it.

Next, you need to prepare your wig. Gently comb it out to remove any tangles or knots. If your wig has baby hairs, make sure to comb them forward as well.

Wearing a Wig Cap

The next step is to wear a wig cap. A wig cap is a thin cap that you wear over your natural hair to keep it flat and smooth. You can choose from different types of wig caps, such as nylon or mesh.

To wear a wig cap, place it over your head and tuck your hair underneath it. Make sure that it’s snug and secure.

Making Adjustments

Once you’ve worn the wig cap, it’s time to put on your lace front wig. Position the wig on your head and adjust it until it fits perfectly. You can use the adjustable straps located at the back of the wig to tighten or loosen it.

Make sure that the wig is aligned with your natural hairline. You can use a comb or your fingers to push the wig forward or backward until it looks natural.

Creating Baby Hairs

If your wig has baby hairs, you can create more by using a razor or small scissors. Gently cut the hairs along the hairline to create a natural-looking hairline.

Wig Maintenance

Wearing a wig without glue requires proper maintenance. You need to remove the wig at night and store it on a wig stand to keep its shape. You should also wash it regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.

When washing your wig, avoid rubbing or twisting it. Gently massage it with your fingers and rinse it thoroughly with cold water.

Tips for Wearing a Wig without Glue

  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair flat and smooth.
  • Adjust the wig until it fits perfectly.
  • Create baby hairs to make the hairline look natural.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape.
  • Wash the wig regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a lace front wig without a wig cap?

A: Technically, you can wear a lace front wig without a wig cap. However, wearing a wig cap can help keep your natural hair flat and smooth, making it easier to wear the wig.

Q: Can I swim or shower with a lace front wig?

A: It’s not recommended to swim or shower with a lace front wig, as it can damage the wig and the adhesive.

Q: How long can I wear a lace front wig without glue?

A: You can wear a lace front wig without glue for up to a day. However, it’s recommended to remove the wig at night to give your scalp a break.

Now that you know how to wear a lace front wig without glue, you can confidently wear your wig anytime, anywhere. With proper preparation and maintenance, you can enjoy a natural-looking hairstyle without the hassle of adhesive products.
