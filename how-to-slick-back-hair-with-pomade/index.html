How To Slick Back Hair With Pomade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi May 23, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for a classic, stylish hairstyle that is easy to achieve? Look no further than the slicked-back look. With the help of pomade, you can create a sleek, shiny style that is perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to achieve this look and provide some tips for maintaining it.

How to Slick Back Hair with Pomade

Before we get started, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Pomade

The key to achieving a great slicked-back style is choosing the right pomade. Look for a product with a strong hold and a high shine. Oil-based pomades are great for achieving a shiny finish, while water-based pomades are easier to wash out.

Step 2: Apply Pomade to Your Hair

Start with a small amount of pomade, about the size of a dime. Rub it between your palms to warm it up, then apply it to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Make sure to distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair back from your forehead. Start at the front and work your way to the back, making sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to lay. Use the comb to smooth out any bumps or tangles.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is slicked back, you can style it to your liking. Some people like to add a part or a quiff at the front, while others prefer a simple, straight-back style. Use your fingers or a comb to create the look you want.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your hair in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will also help to add extra shine to your style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Slicked-Back Style

Here are a few tips to keep your slicked-back style looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid buildup of pomade
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup
  • Apply pomade sparingly to avoid a greasy look
  • Use a comb to touch up your style throughout the day

FAQ

What kind of hair is best for a slicked-back style?

Any type of hair can be styled in a slicked-back look, but the style tends to work best for those with medium to thick hair. If you have thin hair, you may need to use more pomade to achieve the desired look.

How do I choose the right pomade?

When choosing a pomade, look for one with a strong hold and high shine. If you want a product that is easy to wash out, choose a water-based pomade. If you want a shiny finish that lasts all day, choose an oil-based pomade.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, be sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo, using a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized, and avoiding heat styling tools whenever possible.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, stylish slicked-back look that is perfect for any occasion. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
