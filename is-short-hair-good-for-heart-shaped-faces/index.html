Is Short Hair Good For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you may be wondering if short hair is a good option for you. The answer is yes! Short hair can be a great choice for heart-shaped faces, as long as it's done in the right way. In this article, we'll discuss why short hair is a good option, how to choose the right style, and some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Why Short Hair is Good for Heart Shaped Faces
  • How to Choose the Right Short Hair Style
  • Tips for Keeping Short Hair Healthy and Looking Great
  • FAQs About Short Hair and Heart Shaped Faces

Why Short Hair is Good for Heart Shaped Faces

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead and a narrower chin. Short hair can help balance out these proportions by drawing attention away from the forehead and towards the eyes and cheeks. Additionally, short hair can help elongate the neck, creating a more balanced and flattering look.

Another benefit of short hair for heart-shaped faces is that it can highlight your best facial features. If you have a heart-shaped face, you likely have beautiful cheekbones and eyes. Short hair can help draw attention to these features and make them stand out.

How to Choose the Right Short Hair Style

When choosing a short hair style for a heart-shaped face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid styles that add volume to the forehead, as this can make it appear wider. Instead, look for styles that add volume at the crown, which can help balance out the face.

A pixie cut can be a great option for heart-shaped faces, as it can highlight the eyes and cheekbones while minimizing the forehead. A bob or lob with layers can also be a good choice, as it can add volume at the crown while still being flattering to the face shape.

When in doubt, it's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist who can help you choose the right style for your face shape and hair type.

Tips for Keeping Short Hair Healthy and Looking Great

Short hair requires some maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for keeping your short hair healthy and looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to your hair.

FAQs About Short Hair and Heart Shaped Faces

Q: Will short hair make my forehead look bigger?

A: It depends on the style. Avoid styles that add volume to the forehead, and instead look for styles that add volume at the crown.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short style?

A: Yes! There are plenty of ways to style short hair, including braids, buns, and half-up styles.

Q: Will short hair make my face look too wide?

A: No. Short hair can actually help balance out the proportions of a heart-shaped face.

Hair Care for Short Hair and Heart Shaped Faces

When caring for short hair with a heart-shaped face, it's important to choose products and styles that are both flattering and healthy for your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking its best.

Overall, short hair can be a great option for heart-shaped faces. By choosing the right style and taking good care of your hair, you can create a flattering and stylish look that highlights your best features.
