How To Repair Bleached Damaged Hair With Coconut Oil

Written by Petter Apr 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you've ever bleached your hair, you know the damage it can cause. Bleaching strips the hair of its natural oils and moisture, leaving it dry, brittle, and prone to breakage. But don't worry, there's a natural remedy that can help repair your bleached hair - coconut oil. In this article, we'll show you how to repair bleached damaged hair with coconut oil.

Table of Contents

  • How to Repair Bleached Damaged Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Repair Bleached Damaged Hair

Coconut oil is a natural moisturizer that can help repair bleached damaged hair. It contains fatty acids that penetrate the hair shaft and nourish the hair from within. Here's how to use coconut oil to repair your bleached hair:

Step 1: Choose a High-Quality Coconut Oil

Not all coconut oils are created equal. Choose a high-quality, organic coconut oil that is cold-pressed and unrefined. This type of coconut oil retains more of its natural nutrients, making it more effective for repairing damaged hair.

Step 2: Apply Coconut Oil to Your Hair

Start by warming up the coconut oil in your hands. Apply the oil to your hair, focusing on the damaged areas. Make sure to saturate your hair with the oil from the roots to the tips.

Step 3: Leave the Coconut Oil in Your Hair

After applying the coconut oil, cover your hair with a shower cap or towel and leave it on for at least an hour. For best results, leave the coconut oil in your hair overnight.

Step 4: Wash Your Hair

After leaving the coconut oil in your hair, wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any excess oil.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to repair bleached damaged hair with coconut oil:

  1. Choose a high-quality coconut oil
  2. Apply coconut oil to your hair
  3. Leave the coconut oil in your hair for at least an hour or overnight
  4. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your coconut oil hair treatment:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the coconut oil evenly throughout your hair
  • Wrap your hair in a warm towel to help the coconut oil penetrate the hair shaft
  • Use coconut oil as a weekly treatment to keep your hair healthy and moisturized

FAQ

Is coconut oil good for bleached hair?

Yes, coconut oil is good for bleached hair. It is a natural moisturizer that can help repair the damage caused by bleaching.

How often should I use coconut oil on my bleached hair?

You can use coconut oil on your bleached hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.

What other natural remedies can I use to repair bleached damaged hair?

Other natural remedies that can help repair bleached damaged hair include olive oil, honey, and avocado.

Hair Care

Aside from using coconut oil, there are other things you can do to care for your bleached hair:

  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends

With these tips and the help of coconut oil, you can repair your bleached damaged hair and keep it healthy and moisturized.
