How To Get Korean Waves A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Mar 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Korean waves, also known as Hallyu, have taken the world by storm. From K-pop music to K-dramas, Korea is dominating the entertainment industry. But one of the biggest trends to come out of Korea is their gorgeous wavy hair. In this article, we will show you how to get Korean waves in relaxed English language. So if you want to achieve that effortless, tousled look, keep reading!

How to Get Korean Waves

Getting Korean waves might seem difficult, but it's actually quite simple. Follow these steps to get the perfect wave:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair some extra body. After towel-drying your hair, blow-dry it until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant.

Before styling your hair, it's important to apply a heat protectant to prevent damage. Spray a heat protectant all over your hair, focusing on the ends where the most damage occurs.

Step 3: Section your hair.

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the bottom section and work your way up. This will ensure that all of your hair gets curled.

Step 4: Use a curling iron.

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds, then release the hair. Repeat this process for all of the sections of hair.

Step 5: Apply texturizing spray.

Once you've curled all of your hair, apply a texturizing spray to give your waves some extra hold. Lightly scrunch your hair to distribute the product.

Tips for Getting Korean Waves

Here are some additional tips to help you get the perfect Korean waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel for looser waves.
  • For tighter waves, use a curling iron with a smaller barrel.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. Loose, beachy waves are the goal.
  • Use a comb to comb through your curls for a more natural look.

FAQ About Korean Waves

How long will Korean waves last?

Korean waves can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Can I get Korean waves on naturally straight hair?

Yes, you can! It might take a little more effort, but it's definitely possible to get Korean waves on naturally straight hair.

Do I need to use a specific type of curling iron?

No, you can use any curling iron to get Korean waves. Just make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Korean Waves

Once you've achieved your perfect Korean waves, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Let it air dry whenever possible.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.

Now that you know how to get Korean waves, it's time to give it a try! With a little practice and some patience, you'll have gorgeous, beachy waves in no time.
