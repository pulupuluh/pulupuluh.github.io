Best Hair Part For Diamond Shaped Face In 2023

Best Hair Part for Diamond Shaped Face in 2023 | Hair Care Tips

If you have a diamond shaped face, you may have noticed that certain hairstyles or hair parts are more flattering than others. The good news is that there are plenty of hair part options that will complement your unique face shape. In this article, we'll discuss the best hair part for diamond shaped face and provide tips for hair care and styling.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hair Part for Diamond Shaped Face
  • Tips for Styling Hair with Diamond Shaped Face
  • FAQs About Hair Part for Diamond Shaped Face
  • Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Face

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair part for diamond shaped face, it's important to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. Look for the following characteristics:

  • A narrow forehead
  • Widest point at the cheekbones
  • Narrow chin
  • Elongated face shape

If you have these characteristics, you likely have a diamond shaped face.

Best Hair Part for Diamond Shaped Face

The best hair part for diamond shaped face is a side part. This will help balance out the widest point of your face at the cheekbones and create the illusion of a more oval face shape. Additionally, a deep side part can add volume and texture to your hair, making it appear fuller and more voluminous.

However, if you're not a fan of a side part, you can also try a middle part. Just make sure to add volume at the crown of your head to balance out the width of your face.

Other Hair Parts to Avoid

Avoid a severe middle part or a straight back hair part, as these can accentuate the widest point of your face and make it appear more elongated.

Tips for Styling Hair with Diamond Shaped Face

Now that you know the best hair part for diamond shaped face, here are some tips for styling your hair to complement your unique face shape:

  • Add volume at the crown of your head
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or severe
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture

FAQs About Hair Part for Diamond Shaped Face

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair part for diamond shaped face:

Can I still wear bangs with a diamond shaped face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to avoid blunt bangs that hit at the widest point of your face. Instead, opt for side swept bangs or textured, wispy bangs.

Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail?

Yes, but make sure to add volume at the crown of your head to balance out the width of your face.

What hairstyles should I avoid?

Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or severe, such as slicked back ponytails or severe middle parts.

Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Face

In addition to styling your hair to complement your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's tailored to your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair

With these tips and the best hair part for diamond shaped face, you'll be able to rock any hairstyle with confidence.
