What Bangs Suit Oval Faces The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck because you can rock almost any type of bangs! However, with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which style suits you best. In this ultimate guide, we'll go over the different types of bangs that work well with oval faces and provide tips on how to style and maintain them.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best bangs for oval faces, let's first determine if you have an oval face shape. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take a tape measure and measure the width of your forehead at the widest point, which is typically just above your eyebrows.
  2. Measure the width of your cheekbones at the widest point.
  3. Measure the width of your jawline at the widest point.
  4. Measure the length of your face from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

If the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all relatively similar, and your face length is longer than the width, then you have an oval face shape!

The Best Bangs for Oval Faces

As mentioned earlier, oval faces can pull off almost any type of bangs. However, some styles work better than others. Here are some of the best bangs for oval faces:

1. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a classic and versatile style that looks great on oval faces. They add softness to the face and can be worn with any hair length. To style, simply blow-dry your bangs to the side using a round brush.

2. Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are making a comeback and are perfect for oval faces. They frame the face and add dimension without overpowering it. To style, part your bangs down the middle and blow-dry them to the side using a round brush.

3. Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs are a bold and edgy style that can work well on oval faces with strong features. They can be worn straight across or slightly angled. To style, use a flat iron to straighten your bangs and keep them in place with hairspray.

4. Wispy Bangs

Wispy bangs are a softer and more natural-looking style that works well on oval faces. They add texture and movement to the hair and can be worn with any hair length. To style, blow-dry your bangs using your fingers to create a tousled look.

Tips for Styling Bangs

Now that you know which bangs work well with oval faces, here are some tips for styling them:

  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs and keep them in place.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to your bangs to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape and prevent them from getting too long.

FAQ

Q: Can oval faces pull off blunt bangs?

A: Yes, oval faces can pull off blunt bangs! However, it's important to consider your facial features before opting for this bold style.

Q: Are bangs high maintenance?

A: Bangs can be high maintenance, especially if you have oily hair or live in a humid climate. However, with the right styling tools and hair products, you can keep them looking great with minimal effort.

Q: Can I wear bangs with glasses?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with glasses! Just make sure to choose a style that doesn't interfere with your frames and doesn't make your face look too crowded.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

To keep your bangs looking their best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect bangs for your oval face shape and keep them looking great all year round!
