Best Haircuts For Small Round Faces In 2023

Written by Frank Aug 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't suit your small, round face? It's time to change that. In this article, we will explore the best haircuts for small round faces in 2023. From short to long haircuts, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Small round faces have a unique charm, but finding the right haircut can be a challenge. The right haircut can enhance your features and make you look more confident. In this article, we will explore the best haircuts for small round faces that will flatter your face shape and make you look stunning.

How to

The first step in finding the right haircut for your small round face is to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are roughly the same width, you have a round face. Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose a haircut that suits you.

Step by step

Here are the best haircuts for small round faces in 2023:

1. Short Bob

The short bob is a classic haircut that suits small round faces. It creates the illusion of length and creates a more angular shape. This haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look. You can add layers to create texture and volume.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is another great haircut for small round faces. It creates a more defined jawline and adds volume to the crown. This haircut is perfect for those who want a bold and edgy look. You can add side-swept bangs to soften the look.

3. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for those who want to keep their long hair. This haircut adds volume and creates a more angular shape. You can add side-swept bangs to frame your face.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great addition to any haircut. They create a more defined jawline and add volume to the crown. This haircut is perfect for those who want to keep their hair long or short.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain your haircut.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions:

1. Will these haircuts suit all small round faces?

Yes, these haircuts are versatile and can suit most small round faces. However, it's important to consult with your stylist before getting a new haircut.

2. Can I add highlights to my hair?

Yes, highlights can add dimension and texture to your hair. However, it's important to choose a color that suits your skin tone.

3. Can I style my hair differently?

Yes, you can experiment with different hairstyles. However, it's important to use the right products and tools to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Now that you know the best haircuts for small round faces in 2023, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist. Remember to consult with your stylist before getting a new haircut and to maintain your hair with the right products and tools.
