1960 Hairstyle Images A Nostalgic Look Back

Written by Christine May 22, 2023 · 6 min read

Take a journey back in time with us to the 1960s, a decade known for its bold fashion and iconic hairstyles. In this article, we'll be exploring some of the most popular hairstyles of the era through stunning 1960 hairstyle images. Whether you're looking for inspiration for a themed party or simply appreciate the vintage style, we've got you covered. Read on to learn more!

Description

The 1960s was a time of great change and upheaval, and this was reflected in the fashion and beauty trends of the era. Women began to embrace shorter haircuts and bold, statement-making styles. The beehive, bouffant, and pixie cut were just a few of the most popular hairstyles of the time, and they continue to inspire hairdressers and fashion enthusiasts to this day.

How to Achieve a 1960s Hairstyle

If you're looking to recreate a 1960s hairstyle for a special event or just for fun, there are a few key steps you'll need to follow. First, start with clean, dry hair. Add volume and texture using a volumizing mousse or spray, then use a round brush and blow dryer to create the desired shape. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture.

3. Use a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and shape.

4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect 1960s Hairstyle

There are a few additional tips and tricks you can use to get the most authentic 1960s hairstyle possible. First, consider using hot rollers or a curling iron to create curls and waves in your hair. This will add texture and volume to your style, making it more reminiscent of the era. Additionally, be sure to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type. Not all 1960s hairstyles will work for everyone, so it's important to choose a style that flatters your unique features.

FAQs

Q: What was the most popular hairstyle of the 1960s?

A: The beehive was one of the most iconic hairstyles of the 1960s, popularized by celebrities like Audrey Hepburn and Brigitte Bardot.

Q: Can I achieve a 1960s hairstyle with short hair?

A: Absolutely! The pixie cut was a popular hairstyle of the era, and it can be easily adapted for modern short haircuts.

How to Care for Your 1960s Hairstyle

Once you've achieved your desired 1960s hairstyle, it's important to take care of it properly to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause the style to flatten or lose its shape. Use a lightweight hairspray to hold the style in place without weighing it down. And, of course, be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.

In Conclusion

Whether you're a fan of vintage style or simply appreciate the bold, iconic looks of the 1960s, there's no denying that this era had some of the most memorable hairstyles in history. By following our tips and using 1960 hairstyle images as inspiration, you can recreate these looks for yourself and enjoy a blast from the past.
