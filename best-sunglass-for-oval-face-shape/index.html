The Best Sunglasses For Oval Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Sep 08, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're someone with an oval face shape, you're in luck! Your face shape is considered to be one of the most versatile of all the face shapes, which means you can rock almost any style of sunglasses. However, with so many different options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right pair for you. In this article, we'll break down the best sunglasses for oval face shape and provide you with some tips on how to choose the perfect pair.

What is an Oval Face Shape?

Before we dive into the best sunglasses for oval face shape, it's important to understand what an oval face shape is. An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. The cheekbones are typically high and the face tapers towards the chin. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile because it can pull off almost any style of sunglasses.

How to Choose the Best Sunglasses for Oval Face Shape

When choosing the best sunglasses for oval face shape, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Consider the Frame Size

When it comes to frame size, oval face shapes can pull off both larger and smaller frames. However, it's important to make sure that the frames aren't too wide or too narrow for your face. Look for frames that are in proportion to your face shape.

2. Choose the Right Shape

While oval face shapes can pull off almost any shape of sunglasses, there are a few shapes that tend to work best. Look for sunglasses with square or rectangular shapes, as well as cat-eye or aviator styles.

3. Consider the Color

When it comes to color, oval face shapes can pull off almost any color of sunglasses. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair color.

The Best Sunglasses for Oval Face Shape

Now that you know what to look for when choosing the best sunglasses for oval face shape, let's take a look at some of the best options:

1. Ray-Ban Aviator Sunglasses

The Ray-Ban Aviator Sunglasses are a classic choice for oval face shapes. The timeless aviator style complements the balanced proportions of an oval face, and the large lenses provide ample coverage.

2. Oakley Holbrook Sunglasses

The Oakley Holbrook Sunglasses are another great option for oval face shapes. The rectangular shape of the frames complements the balanced proportions of an oval face, and the stylish design adds a touch of sophistication.

3. Maui Jim Peahi Sunglasses

The Maui Jim Peahi Sunglasses are a great choice for oval face shapes who want a sporty look. The rectangular shape of the frames complements the balanced proportions of an oval face, and the polarized lenses provide excellent sun protection.

Tips for Wearing Sunglasses with an Oval Face Shape

Here are some tips for wearing sunglasses with an oval face shape:

1. Don't Be Afraid to Try Bold Styles

With an oval face shape, you can pull off almost any style of sunglasses. Don't be afraid to try bold styles like oversized frames or bright colors.

2. Consider Your Hair Style

When choosing sunglasses, it's important to consider your hair style. If you have long hair, you may want to choose sunglasses with wider frames to balance out your hair. If you have short hair, you may want to choose smaller frames to avoid overwhelming your face.

3. Choose Sunglasses That Complement Your Outfit

When choosing sunglasses, it's important to consider your outfit. Choose sunglasses that complement your outfit and add to your overall look.

The best shapes of sunglasses for oval face shapes are square or rectangular frames, as well as cat-eye or aviator styles.

What color of sunglasses is best for oval face shapes?

Oval face shapes can pull off almost any color of sunglasses. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair color.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shapes

Here are some hair care tips for oval face shapes:

1. Experiment with Different Haircuts

With an oval face shape, you can pull off almost any haircut. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a shorter haircut to add volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a longer haircut to add weight.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Embrace your natural hair texture and work with it, rather than against it. If you have curly hair, embrace your curls and choose a haircut that complements them.

Overall, choosing the best sunglasses for oval face shape comes down to finding a pair that complements your face shape and personal style. With so many options out there, it's important to take the time to find the perfect pair for you. Whether you prefer classic aviators or trendy cat-eye frames, there's a pair of sunglasses out there that will make you look and feel your best.
