Haircuts With Layers And Side Bangs For Long Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 09, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have long hair and are looking for a change, a haircut with layers and side bangs can be a great option. This style adds dimension and movement to your hair, while also framing your face. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about haircuts with layers and side bangs for long hair, including how to get this style, tips for maintenance, and more.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get Haircuts with Layers and Side Bangs for Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Long, Layered Hair

How to Get Haircuts with Layers and Side Bangs for Long Hair

The first step in getting a haircut with layers and side bangs is to find a stylist who is experienced with this style. Look for reviews online or ask for recommendations from friends who have a similar haircut. Once you've found a stylist you trust, you can discuss your desired look with them.

When discussing your haircut with your stylist, it's important to be specific about the length and style of layers you want, as well as the length and angle of your side bangs. Bringing in pictures can also be helpful in communicating your desired look.

Overall, a haircut with layers and side bangs for long hair typically involves longer layers throughout the hair, with shorter layers added around the face to frame it. The side bangs can be cut at a slight angle to create a softer, more natural look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a haircut with layers and side bangs for long hair:

  1. Find a stylist who is experienced with this style.
  2. Discuss your desired look with your stylist, being specific about the length and style of layers and side bangs you want.
  3. Have your hair washed and combed out.
  4. Starting at the back of your head, your stylist will begin cutting long layers into your hair.
  5. Shorter layers will then be added around the face to frame it.
  6. The side bangs will be cut to your desired length and angle.
  7. Your stylist will then style your hair to finish the look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've gotten a haircut with layers and side bangs for long hair, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a nourishing hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using products with harsh chemicals, as these can damage your hair over time.

FAQ

Q: Will a haircut with layers and side bangs work for all face shapes?

A: While this style can work for many face shapes, it's important to discuss your desired look with your stylist to ensure that it will flatter your face shape.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with this style?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with this style. However, you may need to use pins or clips to keep the layers and bangs in place.

Q: Will this style work for thick hair?

A: Yes, this style can work well for thick hair. However, your stylist may need to add more layers to prevent your hair from looking too bulky.

Hair Care Tips for Long, Layered Hair

Long, layered hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the time between washes.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as these can damage your hair.

With these tips, you can keep your long, layered hair looking great for weeks to come.

Overall, a haircut with layers and side bangs for long hair can be a great way to update your look and add dimension to your hair. By finding a stylist you trust and following these tips for maintenance, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
