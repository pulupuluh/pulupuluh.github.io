What Makes A Face Androgynous A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 23, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’ve ever wondered what makes a face androgynous, you’re not alone. Many people are interested in this topic because androgynous beauty is becoming increasingly popular in the fashion industry. But what is an androgynous face, and how can you achieve it? In this article, we’ll explore the key features of an androgynous face, and we’ll provide you with some tips for achieving this look.

Table of Contents

  • Definition of Androgyny
  • Key Features of an Androgynous Face
  • How to Achieve an Androgynous Look
  • Hair Care Tips for Androgynous Looks
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Definition of Androgyny

Androgyny is a term used to describe a person who has both masculine and feminine qualities. An androgynous person may have a physical appearance that is neither clearly male nor female, or they may have a personality that is a mix of masculine and feminine traits. Androgyny is often associated with non-binary gender identities, but it can also be used to describe people who identify as male or female.

Key Features of an Androgynous Face

There are several key features that make a face androgynous:

Strong Jawline

Both men and women can have a strong jawline, and it’s a key feature of an androgynous face. A strong jawline gives the face a chiseled, angular look that is neither traditionally masculine nor feminine.

High Cheekbones

High cheekbones are another feature that is associated with androgynous beauty. Androgynous faces tend to have a more angular look, and high cheekbones contribute to this.

Small Nose

A small, delicate nose is another feature that is often associated with androgynous beauty. A small nose gives the face a more feminine look, but it can also contribute to a more androgynous appearance.

Full Lips

Full lips are a feature that is often associated with femininity, but they can also contribute to an androgynous look. Androgynous faces tend to have a more balanced look, with both masculine and feminine features.

Almond-Shaped Eyes

Almond-shaped eyes are another feature that is associated with androgynous beauty. Almond-shaped eyes are neither traditionally masculine nor feminine, and they contribute to a more balanced, androgynous look.

How to Achieve an Androgynous Look

If you’re interested in achieving an androgynous look, there are several things you can do:

Experiment with Makeup

Makeup can be a powerful tool for achieving an androgynous look. Experiment with bold, dark eye makeup, and use contouring to create a more angular, chiseled look.

Wear Gender-Neutral Clothing

Wearing gender-neutral clothing can help you achieve an androgynous look. Look for clothing that is tailored and fitted, with clean lines and minimal embellishments.

Get a Short Haircut

A short, choppy haircut can help you achieve an androgynous look. Consider a pixie cut, a bob, or a shaved head.

Hair Care Tips for Androgynous Looks

If you’re interested in achieving an androgynous look, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.

Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

Try a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray can help you achieve a messy, tousled look that is perfect for androgynous hairstyles. Spray it on damp hair, and scrunch your hair with your fingers to create texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can anyone achieve an androgynous look?

A: Yes, anyone can achieve an androgynous look with the right makeup, clothing, and hairstyle.

Q: Do I have to cut my hair short to achieve an androgynous look?

A: No, you don’t have to cut your hair short to achieve an androgynous look. You can also achieve this look with a longer, more layered haircut.

Q: Can I achieve an androgynous look without makeup?

A: Yes, you can achieve an androgynous look without makeup. Focus on wearing gender-neutral clothing and choosing a hairstyle that suits your face shape.

Now that you know what makes a face androgynous, you can experiment with different makeup, clothing, and hairstyles to achieve this look. Remember to take good care of your hair, and have fun with your new androgynous style!
