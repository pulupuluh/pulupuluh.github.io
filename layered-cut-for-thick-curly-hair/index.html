Layered Cut For Thick Curly Hair

Written by Christine Apr 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your thick curly hair looking dull and lifeless? If so, a layered cut may be the answer to your problems. In this article, we will go over everything you need to know about getting a layered cut for thick curly hair.

Description

A layered cut is a haircut where the hair is cut at different lengths to create layers. This type of haircut is perfect for those with thick curly hair because it helps to reduce the weight of the hair and adds movement and bounce.

How To

If you are looking to get a layered cut for your thick curly hair, it is recommended that you go to a professional stylist who specializes in curly hair. The stylist will be able to assess your hair and determine the best way to cut it. Here are the steps you can expect:

Step 1: Wash and Detangle Your Hair

Before your stylist starts cutting your hair, they will wash and detangle it. This will make it easier for them to work with your hair and ensure that they are cutting it evenly.

Step 2: Consultation

During the consultation, your stylist will discuss with you what type of layered cut you are looking for. They will take into consideration your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Step 3: Cutting the Hair

Your stylist will start cutting your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working their way up. They will use thinning shears to create layers and texture. The length of the layers will depend on the look you are going for.

Step 4: Styling

Once your hair is cut, your stylist will style it using a diffuser and curly hair products. They may also use a curling iron to add more definition to your curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind before and after getting a layered cut for your thick curly hair:

  • Be sure to communicate with your stylist about the type of cut you want.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before washing it.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Will a layered cut work for all types of curly hair?

A: While a layered cut can work for most types of curly hair, it is important to consult with a stylist who specializes in curly hair to determine what type of cut will work best for your hair texture and face shape.

Q: How often should I get a layered cut?

A: It is recommended that you get a layered cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and freshness of your layers.

Hair Care

After getting a layered cut, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.

With these tips and a layered cut, your thick curly hair will be looking its best in no time!
