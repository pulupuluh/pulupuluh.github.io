How To Give Yourself A Medium Length Haircut In 2023

Written by Oliver Jul 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of going to the hair salon and spending a lot of money on haircuts? If you have medium length hair, you can give yourself a haircut at home. Not only will you save money, but also you will have the satisfaction of doing it yourself. In this blog post, we will show you how to give yourself a medium length haircut in 2023.

How to

Before starting the haircut, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Follow these steps to give yourself a medium length haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it dry completely. It's easier to cut your hair when it's clean and dry.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting the back section of your hair first. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Continue doing this until you have cut all the sections in the back.

Once you have finished cutting the back section, move on to the front sections. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, so it frames your face. Repeat this for all the front sections.

Step 4: Check Your Hair

After you have finished cutting your hair, check it in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can blow-dry it or let it air dry.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when giving yourself a medium length haircut:

  • Start with small cuts. You can always cut more if needed, but you can't add hair back once it's cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about giving yourself a medium length haircut:

Can I use a razor to cut my hair?

We don't recommend using a razor to cut your hair. Razors can cause split ends and damage your hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Is it better to cut my hair when it's wet or dry?

It's better to cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can stretch, making it difficult to cut evenly.

Hair Care

After giving yourself a haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair. If you need to use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Giving yourself a medium length haircut can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
