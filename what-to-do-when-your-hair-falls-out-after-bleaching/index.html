What To Do When Your Hair Falls Out After Bleaching

Written by Frank Jul 15, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you experiencing hair fall after bleaching? Don't panic. This is a common problem faced by many people who bleach their hair. In this article, we will discuss what to do when your hair falls out after bleaching. We will also provide you with some tips on how to prevent hair fall after bleaching.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

If you are experiencing hair fall after bleaching, it is important to take immediate action to prevent further damage to your hair. Here are some steps you can take:

Step by Step

Step 1: Stop Bleaching Your Hair

The first step to take is to stop bleaching your hair. Bleaching can cause damage to your hair and scalp, which can lead to hair fall. Give your hair a break and let it recover.

Step 2: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo to clean your hair. Harsh shampoos can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make your hair brittle and prone to breakage. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

Step 3: Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair. Hair masks help to restore moisture to your hair and prevent further damage. Look for a hair mask that contains natural ingredients like coconut oil, avocado, and honey.

Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair, as this can cause further damage. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall after bleaching:

  • Get regular trims to remove split ends
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated

FAQ

Q: How long will it take for my hair to stop falling out after bleaching?

A: It can take several weeks for your hair to stop falling out after bleaching. It is important to be patient and give your hair time to recover.

Q: Can I still dye my hair after experiencing hair fall after bleaching?

A: It is best to avoid dyeing your hair until it has fully recovered. Dyeing your hair can cause further damage and make the hair fall worse.

Hair Care

It is important to take good care of your hair, especially if you have experienced hair fall after bleaching. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair while it is wet
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Avoid using too many hair products

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can prevent hair fall after bleaching and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
