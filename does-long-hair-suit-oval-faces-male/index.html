Does Long Hair Suit Oval Faces Male

Written by Robby Jun 15, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Long hair has been a trend for men for a while now, and it has become more and more popular for men with oval faces. If you are an oval-faced man wondering if long hair would suit you, then you are in the right place. This article will help you determine if long hair would look good on you, and provide tips on how to care for your long hair.

Table of Contents

  • How To Determine If Long Hair Suits Oval Faces Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Hair for Oval Faces Male
  • FAQs

Description

An oval face is known for its balanced proportions, with a slightly curved jawline and forehead. Men with oval faces are lucky because they can pull off a wide range of hairstyles, including long hair. However, not all oval-faced men can rock long hair as it depends on factors such as hair texture, hair type, and personal style preferences.

How To Determine If Long Hair Suits Oval Faces Male

Before growing out your hair, you need to determine if long hair would suit your oval face. Here are some steps to help you decide:

  1. Consider your hair texture. Long hair looks great on men with thick and straight hair. If you have curly hair, it may not be the best choice as it can be difficult to manage.
  2. Think about your personal style preferences. Long hair requires a lot of maintenance and styling, so you need to be willing to put in the effort. If you prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle, then long hair may not be for you.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you determine if long hair would suit your face shape and advise you on the best hairstyles for your hair type and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you have determined that long hair would suit your oval face, here is a step-by-step guide on how to grow out your hair:

  1. Start with a short haircut. If you have short hair, it will be easier to grow out your hair. Start with a short haircut and gradually grow out your hair over a few months.
  2. Visit your hairstylist regularly. As your hair grows, you need to trim it regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy. A regular trim every six to eight weeks will prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  3. Invest in quality hair products. Long hair requires a lot of maintenance, so you need to invest in quality hair products such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture.
  4. Experiment with different hairstyles. Long hair offers a lot of versatility, so you can experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best. Consider a messy bun, a slicked-back look, or a half-up, half-down hairstyle.

Tips for Styling Long Hair for Oval Faces Male

If you have long hair and an oval face, here are some tips to help you style your hair:

  • Use a hair tie to tie your hair back into a ponytail or bun. This will keep your hair out of your face and create a sleek and polished look.
  • Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair to create a slicked-back look. This style works well for formal occasions.
  • Experiment with different parting styles. Consider a middle part or a side part to change up your look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about long hair and oval faces:

Q: Can men with oval faces wear bangs?
A: Yes, men with oval faces can wear bangs. However, it is important to consider your hair texture and personal style preferences before opting for bangs. If you have thick hair, bangs can be a great way to add some dimension to your hair.

Q: Can men with oval faces wear long hair with a beard?
A: Yes, men with oval faces can wear long hair with a beard. However, it is important to keep your beard well-groomed and trimmed to avoid looking unkempt.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

If you have long hair, it is important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated. Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair and rinse thoroughly.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hairdryers and flat irons too often. These tools can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, long hair can suit oval-faced men, but it depends on factors such as hair texture, hair type, and personal style preferences. If you have an oval face and are considering growing out your hair, follow the steps in this article and consult with a hairstylist for advice. With proper care and maintenance, long hair can be a great way to add some style and personality to your look.
