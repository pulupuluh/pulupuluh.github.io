How To Give A Basic Men s Haircut With Scissors

Written by Daniel May 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to give a basic men's haircut with scissors, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know to give a great haircut using just scissors. We'll cover everything from how to prepare the hair, to the actual cutting process, and even some tips for taking care of the hair afterwards. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

Before we get started with the actual haircutting process, it's important to prepare the hair properly. Here's what you'll need to do:

  • Wash and dry the hair
  • Comb the hair in the direction it naturally falls
  • Section the hair into manageable parts

Once you've prepared the hair, it's time to start cutting!

Step by Step

Here's a basic step-by-step guide to cutting men's hair with scissors:

  1. Start at the sides and back of the head
  2. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers
  3. Snip the hair at an angle, using the length of your fingers as a guide
  4. Continue working around the head, taking small sections of hair at a time
  5. Blend the hair using scissors-over-comb technique
  6. Trim the top of the head, using a comb and scissors
  7. Blend the top of the head with the sides and back using scissors-over-comb technique
  8. Check for any missed spots and trim as needed

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting men's hair with scissors:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends
  • Take your time and work in small sections
  • Blend the hair as you go to avoid any harsh lines
  • Use a mirror to check your work from all angles
  • Don't be afraid to ask for feedback from the person you're cutting hair for

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with scissors:

Can I cut men's hair with regular household scissors?

You can, but it's not recommended. Household scissors aren't as sharp as hair cutting scissors, which can lead to split ends and a less precise cut.

How often should men get their hair cut?

It depends on the person's hair type and desired style, but a good rule of thumb is every 4-6 weeks.

How do I know if I've cut too much hair?

If you're unsure, it's better to err on the side of caution and cut less hair than you think you need to. You can always go back and trim more if needed.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting the hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash the hair with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep the hair soft and moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to give a great men's haircut with scissors. Remember to take your time, work in small sections, and always use sharp scissors. Good luck!
