Best Hair Mousse For Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 31, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of struggling to style your thick curly hair? Do you find yourself spending hours on end trying to tame your unruly locks? If so, you’re not alone. Many people with thick curly hair struggle to find the right products to help them achieve their desired look. One product that can be particularly helpful is hair mousse. In this article, we’ll discuss the best hair mousse for thick curly hair, how to use it, and some tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents:

  • What is Hair Mousse?
  • Best Hair Mousse for Thick Curly Hair
  • How to Use Hair Mousse
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQ

What is Hair Mousse?

Hair mousse is a styling product that is used to add volume, texture, and hold to hair. It is typically applied to damp hair and then blow-dried to create the desired style. Mousse is a popular product for people with thick curly hair because it can help define curls and reduce frizz.

Best Hair Mousse for Thick Curly Hair

When it comes to choosing the best hair mousse for thick curly hair, there are several factors to consider. One of the most important is the product’s hold level. If you have thick hair, you’ll likely need a mousse with a strong hold to keep your curls in place. Here are some of the best hair mousses for thick curly hair:

1. Moroccanoil Curl Control Mousse

The Moroccanoil Curl Control Mousse is a lightweight, yet strong-hold mousse that helps define curls and reduce frizz. It contains argan oil, which helps nourish and hydrate hair, and is free of parabens and sulfates.

2. OGX Locking + Coconut Curls Decadent Creamy Mousse

The OGX Locking + Coconut Curls Decadent Creamy Mousse is a budget-friendly option that still delivers great results. It has a medium hold and contains coconut oil and shea butter to help moisturize and condition hair.

3. DevaCurl Frizz-Free Volumizing Foam

The DevaCurl Frizz-Free Volumizing Foam is a great option for those with thick curly hair who want to add volume without sacrificing definition. It has a strong hold and contains wheat protein and jojoba oil to help strengthen and nourish hair.

How to Use Hair Mousse

Now that you’ve chosen the best hair mousse for your thick curly hair, it’s important to know how to use it properly. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Be sure to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Be careful not to rub your hair too vigorously, as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply Mousse

Dispense a small amount of mousse into your hand and distribute it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Use your hands to scrunch your hair, working the mousse into your curls. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting. Be sure to scrunch your hair as you dry it to enhance your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Using the right hair mousse is just one part of maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some additional tips:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling and damaging your curls.

2. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help reduce frizz and prevent breakage.

3. Deep Condition Your Hair

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to help nourish and strengthen your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I use hair mousse on dry hair?

A: While mousse is typically used on damp hair, you can use it on dry hair to revive your curls and add volume.

Q: Can I use hair mousse every day?

A: Yes, you can use hair mousse every day. However, be sure to use a clarifying shampoo once a week to prevent buildup.

Q: Do I need to use other styling products with hair mousse?

A: It depends on your hair type and the look you’re trying to achieve. Some people with thick curly hair may need to use additional products, such as a leave-in conditioner or hair oil, to help tame frizz and define curls.


With the right hair mousse and styling techniques, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls. Be sure to choose a mousse with a strong hold and follow our step-by-step guide for best results. And don’t forget to take care of your hair by using a wide-tooth comb, sleeping on a silk pillowcase, and deep conditioning regularly.
