How To Style Guys Long Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Apr 22, 2023 · 7 min read

Long curly hair is a trend that’s here to stay. However, it can be challenging to style without the proper knowledge. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style guys long curly hair in a way that looks great and is easy to maintain. Whether you want a sleek and polished look or a more relaxed vibe, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Guys Long Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair
  • FAQs About Styling Long Curly Hair
  • Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

How to Style Guys Long Curly Hair

Before we dive into the specifics of styling long curly hair, it’s important to understand the basics. Long curly hair requires special care and attention to look its best. Here are a few tips to get you started:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage hair.
  • Invest in a good hairbrush or comb that won’t damage your curls.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define and enhance your curls.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you have the basics down, let’s dive into how to style long curly hair. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This can be a leave-in conditioner, curl cream, or styling gel.
  3. Divide your hair into sections. This will make it easier to style and ensure that all of your curls are evenly coated with product.
  4. Twist each section of hair around your finger to define your curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.
  5. Once your curls are dry, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently separate them.
  6. For a polished look, use a pomade or hair wax to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.

Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair

Styling long curly hair can be a challenge, but with these tips, you’ll be able to achieve the look you want:

  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different products until you find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your curls once they’re dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • If you’re looking for a more polished look, consider using a hair wax or pomade to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.

FAQs About Styling Long Curly Hair

Here are a few frequently asked questions about styling long curly hair:

Q: Can I straighten my long curly hair?

A: While it’s not recommended to straighten long curly hair on a regular basis, you can use a flat iron or straightening brush to achieve a different look from time to time. Just be sure to use a heat protectant to minimize damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my long curly hair?

A: It’s best to wash your long curly hair no more than twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my long curly hair?

A: Using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoiding brushing your hair when it’s dry, and using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can all help prevent frizz in long curly hair.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Maintaining healthy long curly hair requires a few extra steps:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage hair.
  • Invest in a good hairbrush or comb that won’t damage your curls.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve a stylish and healthy look that’s perfect for long curly hair.
