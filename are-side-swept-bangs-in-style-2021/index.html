Are Side Swept Bangs In Style 2021

Written by Aprilia Sep 01, 2023 · 6 min read

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get Side Swept Bangs

Description

Side swept bangs have been in style for years, and they are still popular in 2021. These bangs are versatile and look great on almost anyone, regardless of their face shape or hair type. Side swept bangs can be cut to be short or long, and they can be styled in many different ways.

Side swept bangs are a great way to change up your hairstyle without making a drastic change. They can add a touch of elegance to any look and can be worn with both casual and formal outfits.

How to Get Side Swept Bangs

If you are considering getting side swept bangs, you should consult with a hairstylist to see if they are a good fit for your face shape and hair type. They can also give you advice on how to style your new bangs.

When getting side swept bangs, you should consider the length and thickness of the bangs. If you have thin hair, you should opt for shorter and thinner bangs, while thicker hair can handle longer and thicker bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Consult with a hairstylist to see if side swept bangs are right for you.

2. Decide on the length and thickness of your bangs.

3. Have your hairstylist cut your bangs to the desired length and thickness.

4. Style your bangs with a round brush and a blow dryer to achieve a sleek and smooth look.

Tips for Styling Side Swept Bangs

1. Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs.

2. Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs for a sleek look.

3. Use a curling iron to curl your bangs for a more voluminous look.

4. Use a hair serum to add shine to your bangs.

5. Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh in between washes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can side swept bangs work for any face shape?

A: Yes, side swept bangs can work for any face shape. It's all about finding the right length and style for your face.

Q: How often should I trim my side swept bangs?

A: You should trim your side swept bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

When it comes to caring for your side swept bangs, it's important to keep them clean and moisturized. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair, and avoid using hot tools on your bangs too often.

Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair and bangs moisturized, and avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

Overall, side swept bangs are still in style in 2021 and are a great way to add a touch of elegance to any look. With the right cut and styling, these bangs can work for anyone and can be a great way to change up your hairstyle without making a drastic change.
