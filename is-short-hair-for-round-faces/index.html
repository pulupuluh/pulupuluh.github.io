Is Short Hair For Round Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair has always been a popular trend, but is it suitable for those with round faces? The answer is yes! With the right cut and style, short hair can accentuate your facial features and make you look stunning. In this guide, we will go through everything you need to know about whether short hair is for round faces, including how to choose the perfect haircut, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Perfect Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair for Round Faces
  • FAQs About Short Hair for Round Faces
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Choose the Perfect Haircut

When it comes to choosing the perfect haircut for round faces, you want to look for styles that add volume and height to your hair. This will help elongate your face and make it appear less round. Some popular haircuts for round faces include:

  • Pixie Cut
  • Bob Cut
  • Layered Cut
  • Asymmetrical Cut

It's important to consult with your hairstylist to determine which haircut would flatter your face shape the most. They can also recommend which styles would complement your hair texture and lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Once you've chosen your ideal haircut, it's time to style it! Here are some simple step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the perfect short hair look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products suitable for your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to the roots.
  3. Use a round brush to shape your hair while blow-drying it.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair for added texture.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short hair for round faces:

  • Try to avoid styles that hug your cheeks too closely or add too much volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to draw attention to your eyes and forehead.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add interest to your look.
  • Remember to use heat protectant products before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs About Short Hair for Round Faces

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to add volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a round face?

A: Not necessarily! Just make sure to choose a style that adds height and volume to your hair.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have a round face?

A: Of course! Just make sure to add some volume to the crown of your head to balance out your face.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different hair care routine than long hair. Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent excessive dryness.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

With these tips and tricks in mind, you'll be able to rock short hair with confidence, regardless of your face shape. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to determine which haircut would flatter your unique features the most.
