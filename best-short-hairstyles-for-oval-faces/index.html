Description

Having an oval face shape is a blessing as it is considered the most versatile and balanced face shape. It means that almost any hairstyle can suit you. However, choosing the right short hairstyle can enhance your features and make you look even more stunning. In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for oval faces that you can try in 2023.

How to

Before we dive into the best short hairstyles for oval faces, it is essential to understand your face shape. To determine if you have an oval face shape, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three are equal, then you have an oval face shape.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Step 2: Research and choose the best short hairstyles for oval faces

Step 3: Consult with your hairstylist to determine which hairstyle suits you the most

Step 4: Take care of your hair regularly to maintain its health and shine

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle for oval faces, consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Also, do not be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one. Lastly, remember to maintain your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bangs with an oval face shape?

A: Yes, you can. Bangs can add dimension and frame your face beautifully. However, it is essential to choose the right type of bangs that suit your face shape and hair texture.

Q: Are pixie cuts suitable for oval faces?

A: Yes, pixie cuts can look stunning on oval faces. However, it is crucial to choose the right length and style that complements your features.

Q: Should I go for a sleek or textured short hairstyle?

A: It depends on your hair texture and personal style. A sleek short hairstyle can look chic and sophisticated, while a textured short hairstyle can add volume and movement to your hair.

Hair Care

Regular hair care is essential to maintain healthy and shiny hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Condition your hair after every wash
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends
  • Eat a balanced and healthy diet to nourish your hair from the inside out
