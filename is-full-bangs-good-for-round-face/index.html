Is Full Bangs Good For Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Christine May 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you planning to get a haircut but not sure what style would suit your round face? One of the most popular choices is full bangs, but is it a good option for you? In this guide, we will discuss everything you need to know about full bangs for round faces.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Full bangs can be a great option for round faces as they can help create an illusion of length and balance out the wider parts of the face. However, it's important to consider other factors such as hair texture, forehead size, and personal style before making a decision.

There are various types of full bangs that can suit different face shapes, including blunt, side-swept, choppy, and curtain bangs. Blunt bangs that hit right above the eyebrows can work well for round faces, while side-swept bangs that are longer on one side can help elongate the face.

How to

If you've decided to go for full bangs, here's how you can get them:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Choose the length and thickness of your bangs based on your forehead size and personal preference.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs dry to ensure they are the right length and shape.
  4. Style your bangs using a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and a smooth finish.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your full bangs:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your bangs.
  3. Blow dry your bangs using a round brush, starting from the roots and pulling the brush down to the ends.
  4. Continue blow drying until your bangs are smooth and straight.
  5. Use a flat iron to touch up any areas that need extra smoothing.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair serum to your bangs to add shine and prevent frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting and styling full bangs:

  • Consider getting thin or wispy bangs if you have a small forehead to avoid overwhelming your face.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting greasy between washes.
  • Avoid cutting your bangs too short as they may end up looking too heavy and blunt.
  • Get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.

FAQ

Q: Can full bangs make my face look rounder?

A: It's possible if the bangs are cut too short or too thick. To avoid this, opt for longer bangs that are tapered at the ends and not too blunt.

Q: What face shapes are best suited for full bangs?

A: Full bangs can work well for round, oval, and heart-shaped faces, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your full bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, including blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your bangs, as this can cause breakage and split ends.

Overall, full bangs can be a great option for round faces as long as they are chosen and styled carefully. Follow the tips and guidelines above to achieve the perfect full bangs for your face shape and personal style.
