How To Cut Guys Hair Long A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 14, 2023 · 7 min read

Cutting men's hair long can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a simple process. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting long hair for men, as well as provide tips and advice for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Guys Hair Long
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Long Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Cut Guys Hair Long

Before you begin cutting long hair for men, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off the hair. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair as you go.

When it comes to cutting long hair, it's important to remember that less is often more. Start with a smaller amount of hair and work your way up as you become more comfortable with the technique. Remember, you can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's been cut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut long hair for men:

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Section off the hair using hair clips. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward.
  3. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut the ends with scissors.
  4. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way around the head.
  5. When you reach the top of the head, take smaller sections of hair and cut them at an angle to create layers.
  6. Blend the layers together by taking small sections of hair and cutting them at different lengths.
  7. Once you've finished cutting the hair, style it as desired.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting long hair:

  • Always cut less hair than you think you need to. You can always go back and cut more if necessary.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors and ensure a straight cut.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. Small sections are easier to manage and will result in a more even cut.
  • When cutting layers, cut at an angle to create a softer, more natural look.
  • Blend the layers together by cutting small sections of hair at different lengths.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about cutting long hair for men:

Q: How often should I cut my long hair?

A: It's recommended that you get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: How do I know if I've cut too much hair?

A: If you're unsure, it's always better to err on the side of caution and cut less hair than you think you need to. You can always go back and cut more if necessary.

Q: Can I cut long hair myself, or should I go to a professional?

A: It's possible to cut long hair yourself, but it's recommended that you go to a professional if you're unsure of what you're doing. A professional stylist will be able to give you the best results.

Hair Care for Long Hair

After cutting long hair for men, it's important to take care of the hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for long hair to keep it healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim the ends regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or hair dye, as they can damage the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes foods rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut long hair for men with ease, and keep it looking healthy and stylish.
