How To Find The Right Hairstyle For My Face A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel May 11, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of constantly trying out different hairstyles that don't seem to suit your face? Finding the right hairstyle for your face can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can become a breeze. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape. We will also answer some frequently asked questions and provide hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

Table of Content

  • Step 2: Research Hairstyles That Suit Your Face Shape
  • Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type and Lifestyle
  • Step 4: Consult a Professional Hairstylist

  • FAQ

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. Look for the following features:

  • Oval: Forehead is wider than chin, and cheekbones are prominent.
  • Round: Cheeks are full, and the width and length of the face are almost equal.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost equal in width.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the jawline, and the chin is pointed.
  • Diamond: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the forehead and jawline are narrower.
  • Oblong: Face is longer than it is wide, with a prominent chin and forehead.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can move on to the next step.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles That Suit Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to research hairstyles that suit your face shape. Look for celebrities or models with similar face shapes and see what hairstyles they have. You can also search for hairstyle ideas on Pinterest or Instagram. Make a list of hairstyles that you like and think would suit your face shape.

It's important to remember that not all hairstyles will suit your face shape, so it's essential to choose ones that complement your features.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type and Lifestyle

Before you choose a hairstyle, consider your hair type and lifestyle. If you have curly hair, for example, a short haircut may not be the best option. If you have a busy lifestyle and don't have much time to style your hair, a low-maintenance hairstyle may be a better choice.

It's also essential to consider your personal style and what makes you feel comfortable and confident.

Step 4: Consult a Professional Hairstylist

Once you have a few hairstyles in mind, it's time to consult a professional hairstylist. They can provide expert advice on what hairstyles would suit your face shape and hair type. They can also recommend haircuts that will work with your lifestyle and provide tips on how to style your hair at home.

Be open to their suggestions and ask questions if you're unsure about anything. Remember that they are there to help you find the perfect hairstyle for your face.

Tips for Finding the Right Hairstyle

  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles.
  • Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle before choosing a hairstyle.
  • Look for inspiration from celebrities or models with similar face shapes.
  • Consult a professional hairstylist for expert advice.
  • Be open to their suggestions and ask questions.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

Hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as a voluminous bob or a layered haircut, can elongate a round face.

What is the best hairstyle for an oval face?

An oval face shape is versatile and can suit many different hairstyles, from short pixie cuts to long, layered hair.

Can I still wear a certain hairstyle even if it doesn't suit my face shape?

Yes, you can wear any hairstyle you like, but it's essential to choose ones that complement your features and make you feel confident.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and beautiful, it's essential to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or a swimming cap.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy, shiny, and beautiful.

Conclusion

Finding the right hairstyle for your face can be a challenging task, but by following these steps and tips, you can make it a breeze. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle before choosing a hairstyle, and consult a professional hairstylist for expert advice. With the right hairstyle, you can feel confident and beautiful every day.
