Classic Ivy League Cut

The classic Ivy League cut, also known as the Harvard Clip or Princeton Cut, is a timeless hairstyle that is perfect for guys with medium hair. This hairstyle is characterized by short hair on the sides and the back, and longer hair on the top. The hair on the top is usually combed to the side or slicked back.

To achieve the Ivy League cut, you will need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting this type of hairstyle. You will also need to use a good quality pomade or wax to keep your hair in place.

How to Style:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your hair.
  3. Use a comb to style your hair to the side or slick it back.
  4. Finish off by using a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Visit a barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.
  • Use a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Messy Textured Crop

The messy textured crop is a popular hairstyle that is perfect for guys with medium hair. This hairstyle is characterized by short hair on the sides and the back, and longer hair on the top that is styled in a messy, textured way.

To achieve the messy textured crop, you will need to use a good quality hair product such as a texturizing spray or wax. You will also need to use a blow dryer to create volume and texture in your hair.

How to Style:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or wax to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Use your fingers to style your hair in a messy, textured way.
  5. Finish off by using a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips:

  • Use a good quality texturizing spray or wax to create volume and texture in your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  • Use your fingers to style your hair in a messy, textured way.

Curly Hair
