How To Say What Haircut You Want In Relaxed English Language

Written by Daniel Jul 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, communication is key to ensure that you get the exact haircut you want. However, communicating your desired haircut can be difficult, especially if you're not familiar with the technical terms used by hairdressers. In this article, we'll guide you through how to say what haircut you want in relaxed English language.

Table of Contents

  • How to Say What Haircut You Want
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Effective Communication
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Say What Haircut You Want

The first step to communicating your desired haircut is to have a clear idea of what you want. Look for pictures of hairstyles that you like, and take note of the length, layers, texture, and any other specific details that you want to incorporate into your haircut.

Next, it's important to use language that is easy for your hairdresser to understand. Avoid using technical terms that you may not be familiar with, as this can lead to misunderstandings. Instead, use simple and descriptive language to explain your desired haircut.

For example, instead of saying "I want a layered bob with feathered ends", you can say "I want a short haircut that has layers and soft edges". This gives your hairdresser a clear idea of what you want, without confusing them with technical terms.

Use Descriptive Words

When describing your desired haircut, it's important to use descriptive words that help your hairdresser understand what you're looking for. Some descriptive words that you can use include:

  • Short
  • Long
  • Straight
  • Curly
  • Layers
  • Bangs
  • Choppy
  • Textured
  • Volume
  • Thin
  • Thick
  • Blunt
  • Soft

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to help you communicate your desired haircut to your hairdresser:

  1. Start by researching and finding pictures of hairstyles that you like.
  2. Note down the length, layers, texture, and any other specific details that you want to incorporate into your haircut.
  3. Use simple and descriptive language to explain your desired haircut to your hairdresser.
  4. Show your hairdresser the pictures that you've collected to give them a visual idea of what you're looking for.
  5. Be open to your hairdresser's suggestions and recommendations, as they may have insights that you haven't considered.
  6. Ask your hairdresser to repeat back to you what you've said to ensure that they have understood your desired haircut.
  7. If you're not happy with the outcome, communicate your concerns to your hairdresser and work together to find a solution.

Tips for Effective Communication

Here are some tips to help you communicate your desired haircut effectively:

  • Be clear and specific about what you want.
  • Use simple and descriptive language.
  • Show pictures to give your hairdresser a visual idea of what you're looking for.
  • Be open to your hairdresser's suggestions and recommendations.
  • Ask your hairdresser to repeat back to you what you've said to ensure that they have understood your desired haircut.

FAQs

What should I do if my hairdresser doesn't understand what I want?

If your hairdresser doesn't understand what you want, try to explain it in a different way or show them pictures to give them a visual idea. If you're still not happy, consider finding a different hairdresser who can better understand your needs.

Is it okay to bring pictures to the hair salon?

Yes, it's a good idea to bring pictures to the hair salon to give your hairdresser a visual idea of what you're looking for. This can help to avoid misunderstandings and ensure that you get the exact haircut you want.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've gotten your desired haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great and maintain your desired haircut for longer.
