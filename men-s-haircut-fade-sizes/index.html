Men s Haircut Fade Sizes A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jun 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a stylish haircut that's easy to maintain? Look no further than a fade haircut! Fades have become increasingly popular among men of all ages and hair types. In this article, we'll dive into the different fade sizes and how to choose the best one for you.

Description

A fade haircut is a type of men's haircut that involves gradually cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head shorter as it reaches the hairline. The hair at the top of the head can be styled in various ways, such as a crew cut or a pompadour. Fades come in different sizes, ranging from a low fade to a high fade.

How to Get a Fade Haircut

To get a fade haircut, you'll need to visit a barber or hair stylist who has experience with this type of haircut. It's best to bring a picture of the type of fade you want, so the stylist can get a better idea of what you're looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Fade Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a fade haircut:

  1. Choose the type of fade you want (low, medium, or high)
  2. Decide on the length you want for the hair on top of your head
  3. Sit in the barber's chair and let them know what you want
  4. The barber will start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head
  5. They will gradually blend the hair as they move up your head
  6. Once they reach the top of your head, they will cut and style the hair to your desired length
  7. The barber will then clean up any stray hairs and style your hair as desired

Tips for Maintaining Your Fade Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your fade haircut:

  • Visit your barber regularly (every 2-4 weeks) to keep your fade looking fresh
  • Use a quality hair product to style your hair
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat damage by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray

FAQ About Men's Haircut Fade Sizes

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircut fade sizes:

What is a low fade?

A low fade is a type of fade haircut where the hair is cut short around the ears and gradually gets longer as it reaches the top of the head.

What is a high fade?

A high fade is a type of fade haircut where the hair is cut very short on the sides and back of the head and gradually gets longer as it reaches the top.

What is a medium fade?

A medium fade is a type of fade haircut that falls in between a low fade and a high fade. The hair is cut short around the ears and gradually gets longer as it reaches the top.

Hair Care for Faded Haircuts

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a stylish and healthy fade haircut. Here are some tips for caring for faded haircuts:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat damage by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray
  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your fade looking fresh and well-maintained

In conclusion, a fade haircut is a versatile and stylish option for men of all ages and hair types. By choosing the right fade size and following proper hair care tips, you can maintain a fresh and trendy look.
