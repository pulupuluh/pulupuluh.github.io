How To Cut Your Own Hair Guys Long A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Mar 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of shelling out money for haircuts every few weeks? Cutting your own hair may seem intimidating, especially for guys with longer locks, but with the right tools and techniques, it's definitely doable. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps for cutting your own hair guys long, and provide some tips and tricks to help you get the perfect cut every time.

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You'll Need
  2. Prepping Your Hair
  3. Sectioning Your Hair
  4. Cutting Your Hair
  5. Clean Up
  6. Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair Guys Long
  7. FAQ
  8. Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair Guys Long

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting your own hair guys long, you'll need to gather a few essential tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Clipper with guards
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Handheld mirror or second mirror
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Make sure your tools are sharp and in good condition before you start cutting. Dull scissors or clippers can make cutting your hair more difficult and may result in an uneven cut.

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start cutting your own hair guys long, you'll want to make sure your hair is clean and dry. It's also a good idea to comb or brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Sectioning Your Hair

Once your hair is prepped, it's time to section it. This will help you work on one section of hair at a time and ensure that you get an even cut. Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair ties or clips to secure each section.

Cutting Your Hair

Now it's time to start cutting your own hair guys long. Start with the back sections and work your way forward. Use your clippers with guards to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you reach your desired length.

Next, switch to your scissors and cut the hair on the top of your head. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle to create layers. Be sure to cut evenly and check your progress in the mirror as you go.

Clean Up

Once you've finished cutting your own hair guys long, it's time to clean up. Use your clippers to trim any stray hairs around your ears and the back of your neck. Then, remove the hair ties or clips and comb through your hair to make sure it's even.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair Guys Long

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's been cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, especially for guys with longer locks.
  • Use a handheld mirror or second mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Watch tutorials or ask a friend for help if you're feeling unsure.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair guys long with just scissors?

While it's possible to cut your own hair with just scissors, using clippers with guards will make the process easier and ensure a more even cut.

How often should I cut my own hair guys long?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. As a general rule, plan to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair Guys Long

After cutting your own hair guys long, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or styling product to keep your hair looking its best. And don't forget to schedule regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
