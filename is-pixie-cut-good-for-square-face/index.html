Is Pixie Cut Good For Square Face

Written by Aprilia Apr 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a square face, you might wonder if a pixie cut is a good hairstyle for you. In this article, we will discuss whether a pixie cut is a good choice for a square face and provide tips on how to style and care for your pixie cut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style a Pixie Cut for Square Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide on Styling a Pixie Cut for Square Face
  • Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut for Square Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cut for Square Face

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, with the width and length of the face being roughly equal. When it comes to hairstyles, you want to choose a style that softens the angles of your face and creates balance. A pixie cut can be a good choice for a square face, but it depends on the specific features of your face and the style of the pixie cut.

How to Style a Pixie Cut for Square Face

The key to styling a pixie cut for a square face is to create volume on top and soften the angles of the face. This can be achieved through layers and texture. You can also consider adding bangs to draw attention away from the jawline and create a more balanced look.

Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut can add volume and movement to your hair. This style works well for a square face because it softens the jawline and creates balance. You can also add highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

Textured Pixie Cut

A textured pixie cut is another option for a square face. This style involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create a messy, tousled look. The texture can soften the angles of your face and add volume.

Pixie Cut with Bangs

If you want to draw attention away from your jawline, consider adding bangs to your pixie cut. Bangs can create a more balanced look and soften the angles of your face. You can choose from a variety of bang styles, such as side-swept, blunt, or choppy.

Step-by-Step Guide on Styling a Pixie Cut for Square Face

Here are some steps to follow when styling a pixie cut for a square face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the top layers to create volume.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture to your hair.
  5. Apply a small amount of styling wax or pomade to your hair, focusing on the ends to create definition.
  6. If you have bangs, use a flat iron to style them and create a side-swept look.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut for Square Face

Here are some tips to keep your pixie cut looking great:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to extend the time between washes.

FAQs

What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

A pixie cut can work for a variety of face shapes, but it is especially flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Can a square face pull off a short haircut?

Yes, a square face can pull off a short haircut, such as a pixie cut, as long as the style is chosen carefully to soften the angles of the face.

What are some other hairstyles that work well for a square face?

Other hairstyles that work well for a square face include long layers, side-swept bangs, and beachy waves.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cut for Square Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep your pixie cut looking great:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep it moisturized.

In conclusion, a pixie cut can be a good choice for a square face as long as the style is chosen carefully to soften the angles of the face. Follow these tips for styling and caring for your pixie cut to keep it looking great.
