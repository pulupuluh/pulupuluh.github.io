Best Haircuts For Curly Hair Over 60

Written by Luffy Jun 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair and are over 60, you may be wondering what the best haircuts are for your hair type and age. You're in luck! In this article, we'll be discussing the best haircuts for curly hair over 60, as well as how to care for and style your hair to keep it looking its best.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As we age, our hair texture and density can change, and our face shape may also become more defined. These factors can all influence which haircuts will look best on us. If you have curly hair, you'll want to choose a haircut that complements your natural curls and highlights your best features.

How to

The first step in choosing a haircut for curly hair over 60 is to consider the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a shorter cut that adds height to your crown. If you have a square face, a longer cut that frames your face can be flattering. For those with an oval face, almost any haircut will look great.

Next, consider the density and texture of your hair. If you have thick hair, a layered cut can help to remove some of the bulk and add movement. If your hair is thin, a shorter cut with layers can create the illusion of volume.

Step by Step

When you're ready to get your haircut, bring photos of hairstyles that you like, as well as photos of yourself from different angles. This will help your stylist understand what you're looking for and create a cut that flatters your face shape and hair type.

Once you have your new haircut, you'll want to style it in a way that enhances your natural curls. To do this, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and scrunch your curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without disturbing their shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for caring for and styling your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, comb through your hair with a wide-toothed comb when it's wet.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut shape.

FAQ

Q: What is the best haircut for curly hair over 60?

A: The best haircut for you will depend on your face shape, hair density, and personal style preferences. A layered cut can be flattering for those with thick hair, while a shorter cut with layers can add volume to thin hair.

Q: How can I style my curly hair without causing frizz?

A: To avoid frizz, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and scrunch your curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without disturbing their shape.

Hair Care

Caring for your curly hair involves using the right products and techniques. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, comb through your hair with a wide-toothed comb when it's wet.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut shape.

With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can keep your curly hair looking its best well into your 60s and beyond. Whether you opt for a short, layered cut or a longer, face-framing style, embrace your natural curls and wear your hair with confidence.
