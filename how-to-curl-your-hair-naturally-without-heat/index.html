How To Curl Your Hair Naturally Without Heat

Written by Robby Jul 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of damaging your hair with heat tools like curling irons, wands, and straighteners? You're not alone. Many people are looking for ways to curl their hair naturally without heat. Not only is it better for your hair, but it's also less time-consuming and cheaper than using heat tools. In this article, we'll show you how to curl your hair naturally without heat, step by step.

How to Curl Your Hair Naturally Without Heat

There are several methods to curl your hair naturally without heat:

  • Braiding
  • Twisting
  • Rolling
  • Plopping

Each method has its own benefits and works better for certain hair types. Experiment with each method to find the one that works best for you.

Braiding

To braid your hair, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Braid each section tightly.
  5. Leave the braids in overnight.
  6. Untie the braids in the morning and finger-comb your hair.

Braiding works well for people with wavy or curly hair. The tighter you braid your hair, the more defined your curls will be.

Twisting

To twist your hair, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Twist each section tightly.
  5. Leave the twists in overnight.
  6. Untwist the twists in the morning and finger-comb your hair.

Twisting works well for people with kinky or coily hair. The smaller you twist your hair, the tighter your curls will be.

Rolling

To roll your hair, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Wrap a section of hair around a roller.
  3. Secure the roller in place.
  4. Repeat with the rest of your hair.
  5. Leave the rollers in for a few hours or overnight.
  6. Remove the rollers and finger-comb your hair.

Rolling works well for people with straight or wavy hair. The smaller the rollers, the tighter your curls will be.

Plopping

To plop your hair, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Spread a t-shirt out on a flat surface.
  4. Flip your hair over and place it on the t-shirt.
  5. Wrap the t-shirt around your head and tie it in the back.
  6. Leave the t-shirt on for a few hours or overnight.
  7. Untie the t-shirt and finger-comb your hair.

Plopping works well for people with wavy or curly hair. The longer you leave the t-shirt on, the more defined your curls will be.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to curl your hair naturally without heat:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Choose a method to curl your hair (braiding, twisting, rolling, or plopping).
  4. Follow the steps for your chosen method.
  5. Leave your hair to dry overnight or for a few hours.
  6. Untie, untwist, remove the rollers, or untuck the t-shirt.
  7. Finger-comb your hair.
  8. Apply a curl-enhancing product if desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you curl your hair naturally without heat:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before curling it.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair before curling it to prevent frizz.
  • Experiment with different methods and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid touching your hair while it's drying to prevent frizz.

FAQ

Can I curl my hair naturally without heat if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can curl your hair naturally without heat if you have straight hair. Try using the rolling method with small rollers to create tight curls.

Can I curl my hair naturally without heat if I have short hair?

Yes, you can curl your hair naturally without heat if you have short hair. Try using the twisting method with small sections of hair to create tight curls.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls will last for a few days or until you wash your hair.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is important, especially when you're using natural methods to curl it. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Avoid using heat tools like curling irons, wands, and straighteners.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach and hair dye.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using natural methods to curl your hair, you can have healthy, beautiful curls without damaging your hair.
