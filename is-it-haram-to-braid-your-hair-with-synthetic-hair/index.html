Is It Haram To Braid Your Hair With Synthetic Hair

Written by Robby Jun 16, 2023 · 8 min read

As Muslims, we are taught to live our lives in accordance with the teachings of the Quran and the Sunnah. This means that we must be mindful of our actions and ensure that they align with Islamic principles. One question that has been asked by many Muslim women is, "Is it haram to braid your hair with synthetic hair?" In this blog post, we will explore this question in detail and provide you with the information you need to make an informed decision.

What is Synthetic Hair?

Synthetic hair is a type of hair extension that is made from synthetic fibers such as polyester, nylon, or acrylic. Synthetic hair is a popular alternative to human hair extensions because it is more affordable and easier to maintain. Synthetic hair comes in a variety of textures and colors and can be used to create a range of hairstyles, including braids.

Is it Haram to Braid Your Hair with Synthetic Hair?

There is no clear consensus among Islamic scholars on whether it is haram to braid your hair with synthetic hair. Some scholars believe that it is permissible as long as the hair extensions do not contain any haram materials such as animal hair or silk. Other scholars argue that it is haram to wear any type of hair extension, including synthetic hair, because it alters the natural appearance of the hair which is considered a gift from Allah.

How to Braid Your Hair with Synthetic Hair

If you have decided to braid your hair with synthetic hair, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start braiding your hair, you should wash and condition your hair to ensure that it is clean and moisturized. This will make it easier to braid your hair and will also help to prevent breakage.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using a comb. The size of the sections will depend on the size of the braids you want to create. Use hair clips to secure the sections so that they don't get mixed up.

Step 3: Attach the Synthetic Hair

Take a section of synthetic hair and attach it to your natural hair using a braiding technique. Repeat this process until all the sections of your hair have been braided with synthetic hair.

Step 4: Style Your Braids

Once you have finished braiding your hair with synthetic hair, you can style your braids in any way you like. You can wear your braids loose or tie them up in a ponytail or bun.

Tips for Braiding Your Hair with Synthetic Hair

Here are some tips to help you braid your hair with synthetic hair:

  • Choose high-quality synthetic hair to ensure that it looks natural and lasts longer
  • Be gentle when braiding your hair to prevent breakage
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness and breakage
  • Protect your hair while sleeping by wearing a silk or satin bonnet or using a silk or satin pillowcase

FAQs

Can I Wear Synthetic Hair During Ramadan?

Yes, you can wear synthetic hair during Ramadan. There is no prohibition against wearing hair extensions during Ramadan.

Can I Wear Synthetic Hair for Hajj or Umrah?

Yes, you can wear synthetic hair for Hajj or Umrah. However, you should ensure that the hair extensions do not contain any haram materials and that they are securely attached to your natural hair.

How Long Can I Wear Synthetic Hair?

Synthetic hair can be worn for up to 6-8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of it. It is important to moisturize your hair regularly and to avoid using heat styling tools which can damage the synthetic fibers.

Hair Care for Braided Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your braided hair:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly using a leave-in conditioner or oil
  • Wash your hair once a week with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Avoid using heat styling tools which can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

In conclusion, whether or not it is haram to braid your hair with synthetic hair is a matter of personal interpretation. However, if you do decide to wear synthetic hair, it is important to choose high-quality hair extensions and to take good care of your hair to prevent breakage and damage.
