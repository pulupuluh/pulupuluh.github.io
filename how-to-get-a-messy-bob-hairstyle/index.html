How To Get A Messy Bob Hairstyle

Written by Frank Sep 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle, a messy bob might be just what you need. This hairstyle is perfect for anyone who wants to look effortlessly chic while also keeping things simple. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get a messy bob hairstyle, including step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Messy Bob Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Messy Bob
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice for Messy Bob Hairstyles

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in getting a messy bob is to decide on the length that you want. A messy bob can be anywhere from chin-length to shoulder-length, so choose the length that works best for your face shape and hair texture. If you're unsure, consult with your hairstylist for guidance.

Step 2: Ask for Layers

Layers are key to achieving a messy bob, as they add texture and movement to your hair. Ask your hairstylist to add layers throughout your hair, starting at the crown and working downwards. This will create a tousled, messy look that's perfect for a bob.

Step 3: Add Texture

To create the messy look, your hairstylist may also use texturizing scissors or a razor to add texture to your hair. This will help create the messy, tousled look that you're going for.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have your messy bob, you'll need to style it to keep it looking great. Start by applying a texturizing spray or mousse to your damp hair, then blow dry your hair using your fingers to create volume and texture. You can also use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Messy Bob

Now that you have your messy bob, you'll want to make sure that it stays looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your messy bob:

  • Use a dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Regular trims will help keep your messy bob looking fresh and tousled.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I get a messy bob if I have curly hair?

A: Absolutely! A messy bob can work on any hair texture, including curly hair. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right length and layers to create a tousled, messy look that works with your curls.

Q: How often do I need to get my messy bob trimmed?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your style loses its shape. Generally, you should plan on getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your messy bob looking fresh and tousled.

Hair Care Advice for Messy Bob Hairstyles

When it comes to caring for your messy bob, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Second, avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness. Finally, embrace your natural texture and work with your hairstylist to create a look that works with your hair type and face shape.

With these tips and tricks, you're well on your way to achieving the perfect messy bob hairstyle. Remember to consult with your hairstylist for personalized advice and guidance, and experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.
