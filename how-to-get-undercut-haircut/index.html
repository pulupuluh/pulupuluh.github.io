How To Get Undercut Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Mar 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, the undercut might be the perfect choice for you. This versatile haircut has been popular for several years now and is still going strong. If you're wondering how to get undercut haircut, we've got you covered. In this post, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look.

  2. How to Get Undercut Haircut

Description

The undercut is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides and back of the head is cut short, while the hair on the top is left longer. This creates a contrast between the two lengths, which makes the top hair stand out. The undercut can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, side-parted, or messy. It's a popular haircut for men, but women can also rock this style.

How to Get Undercut Haircut

Getting an undercut haircut involves cutting the sides and back of your hair shorter than the top. The length of the hair on top depends on your preference, but it should be longer than the sides and back. You can ask your hairstylist for a disconnected undercut, where there's a clear contrast between the top and sides, or a blended undercut, where the top blends seamlessly into the sides.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get an undercut haircut:

  1. Decide on the length you want for the hair on top. This will depend on your preference and the shape of your face. Longer hair on top works well for round or square faces, while shorter hair on top suits oval or rectangular faces.
  2. Show your hairstylist a picture of the undercut style you want. This will help them understand the look you're going for and make suggestions on how to tailor it to your hair type and face shape.
  3. Choose between a disconnected or blended undercut. A disconnected undercut has a clear contrast between the top and sides, while a blended undercut has a gradual transition between the two lengths.
  4. Section off the hair on top and clip it away. This will allow your hairstylist to focus on the sides and back.
  5. Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down to a shorter guard. This will create a gradient effect on the sides and back. Your hairstylist might also use scissors to blend the hair between the top and sides.
  6. Once the sides and back are cut, unclip the hair on top and style it as desired. You can use wax, pomade, or gel to achieve the look you want.

Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut

Here are some tips for maintaining your undercut:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep the sides and back short.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent oil buildup and maintain the style.
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and the look you want to achieve.
  • If you have a long undercut, tie the hair on top in a bun or ponytail to change up the style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting an undercut haircut:

What face shapes suit an undercut?

An undercut can work for various face shapes, but longer hair on top suits round or square faces, while shorter hair on top suits oval or rectangular faces.

Can women get an undercut?

Yes, women can get an undercut haircut. It's a popular choice for those who want a bold and edgy look.

How do I style my undercut?

You can style your undercut in various ways, such as slicked back, side-parted, or messy. Use a hair product that suits your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

Hair Care Tips for Undercut Haircut

If you have an undercut haircut, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every other day with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons regularly, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.
  • If you have a long undercut, tie the hair on top in a bun or ponytail to change up the style.

Getting an undercut haircut is a bold and trendy choice that can suit various hair types and face shapes. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve the look you want and maintain it with ease.
