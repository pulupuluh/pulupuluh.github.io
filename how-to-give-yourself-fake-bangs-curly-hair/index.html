How To Give Yourself Fake Bangs Curly Hair

Written by Robby Aug 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a way to switch up your hairstyle without committing to a real bang cut? Fake bangs are a great way to experiment with a new look, and they're super easy to do at home. In this article, we'll show you how to give yourself fake bangs curly hair without any hassle. Keep reading to find out more!

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first thing you need to do is decide where you want your fake bangs to sit. You can opt for a blunt cut just above your eyebrows, or a longer, side-swept look. Once you've decided, gather the hair you want to use for your fake bangs.

Next, you'll need to create a small ponytail with the hair you've gathered. This will help you control the length and thickness of your fake bangs. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail, and make sure it's sitting exactly where you want your fake bangs to be.

Step by step

Follow these steps to create your fake bangs:

  1. Take the hair from your ponytail and bring it forward over your forehead.
  2. Use a pair of scissors to cut the hair to your desired length.
  3. If you want to add some texture to your fake bangs, use a curling wand or flat iron to create loose waves or curls.
  4. Use a small amount of hair gel or hairspray to hold your fake bangs in place.
  5. Blend the rest of your hair with your fake bangs to create a seamless look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Choose a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • If you have thick hair, you may need to create two or three small ponytails to get the right amount of hair for your fake bangs.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling wand or flat iron to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect fake bangs for you.

FAQ

Can I use clip-in bangs instead of creating my own?

Yes, you can definitely use clip-in bangs if you have them. Just make sure they match your hair color and style them to blend in with the rest of your hair.

How long will my fake bangs last?

Your fake bangs should last until your next wash day. Just make sure you use enough hairspray or gel to hold them in place.

Can I create fake bangs with curly hair?

Yes, you can create fake bangs with curly hair. Just make sure you use a curling wand or flat iron to create defined curls or waves.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure you follow a good hair care routine. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat on your hair too often. If you're unsure about how to take care of your curly hair, talk to a hairstylist for advice.

Now that you know how to give yourself fake bangs curly hair, you can experiment with a new look without any commitment. Have fun and enjoy your new style!
