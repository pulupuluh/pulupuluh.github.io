Does Long Hair Make You Look Shorter Or Taller

Written by Aprilia Sep 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long hair has been a fashion trend for many years, and it's not going away anytime soon. As more and more people are jumping on the long hair bandwagon, there's one question that keeps popping up: does long hair make you look shorter or taller?

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The length of your hair can have an impact on how tall or short you appear. While there's no definitive answer to whether long hair makes you look shorter or taller, it's safe to say that it can do both depending on how you style it.

How to

The way you style your hair can either make you look taller or shorter. Here are some tips to help you achieve the look you want:

Style it upward

If you want to look taller, style your hair upward. This doesn't necessarily mean that you have to have a towering hairdo. A simple updo or a high ponytail can do the trick. These styles create the illusion of height and make you appear taller.

Keep it straight

Straight hair is another way to make you look taller. This style elongates your body and gives you a sleek appearance. You can achieve this look by using a straightening iron or a blow dryer.

Layer it

If you want to add volume to your hair, layering it is a good option. This will give the illusion of more height and make you appear taller. However, be careful not to go overboard with the layers, as too many can make you look shorter.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair to look taller:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair straight using a round brush.
  3. If you have long hair, tie it up in a high ponytail. If you have short hair, create an updo.
  4. Use hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Wear high heels to add extra height.
  • Choose clothes that elongate your body, such as vertical stripes.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your hair appear flat, as this can make you look shorter.
  • Play around with different hairstyles to see which one works best for you.

FAQ

Does long hair make you look shorter?

Long hair can make you look shorter if it's styled in a way that doesn't elongate your body. For example, if you have long, flat hair, it can make you appear shorter.

Does long hair make you look taller?

Long hair can make you look taller if it's styled in a way that creates the illusion of height. For example, if you have an updo or a high ponytail, it can make you appear taller.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

So there you have it - whether long hair makes you look shorter or taller really depends on how you style it. With these tips, you can achieve the look you want and feel confident in your appearance.
