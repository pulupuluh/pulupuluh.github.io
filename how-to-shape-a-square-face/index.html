How To Shape A Square Face Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by William May 27, 2023 Â· 11 min read

If you have a square-shaped face, you might be wondering how to make your features appear softer and more feminine. Fortunately, there are plenty of makeup techniques and hair styles that can help you enhance your natural beauty and create a more balanced look. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways to shape a square face and enhance your features.

Table of Contents

  • How to Shape a Square Face
  • Step-by-Step Techniques
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Hair Care for Square Faces
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Shape a Square Face

The key to shaping a square face is to create balance and soften the angles of your jawline and forehead. Here are some basic techniques that can help:

  • Use contouring makeup to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Apply a darker shade of foundation or powder to the corners of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline to create shadows that will make your face appear more angled.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and soften the angles of your jawline. Consider a layered bob, a side-swept fringe, or a shoulder-length cut with soft waves.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, blunt, or angular, as they can emphasize the sharpness of your jawline and create an overly masculine look.

Contouring Makeup for Square Faces

Contouring makeup can be a powerful tool for shaping a square face. Here are some basic steps to follow:

  1. Apply your regular foundation or tinted moisturizer to your entire face, blending it in well.
  2. Choose a darker shade of foundation or powder that is two to three shades darker than your natural skin tone. Use a brush to apply this shade to the corners of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline.
  3. Blend the darker shade well, making sure to create a gradual transition between the light and dark areas.
  4. Apply a highlighter to the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, and the tops of your cheekbones to create a luminous, youthful glow.

Remember, less is more when it comes to contouring. You want to create a subtle, natural-looking effect that enhances your features without looking too heavy or obvious.

Step-by-Step Techniques

Here are some step-by-step techniques to help you shape your square face:

  1. Start by applying your regular foundation or tinted moisturizer to your entire face.
  2. Use a darker shade of foundation or powder to create shadows on the corners of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline.
  3. Blend the darker shade well, making sure to create a gradual transition between the light and dark areas. Use a blending brush or sponge to achieve a natural-looking effect.
  4. Apply a highlighter to the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, and the tops of your cheekbones to create a luminous, youthful glow.
  5. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and softens the angles of your jawline. Consider a layered bob, a side-swept fringe, or a shoulder-length cut with soft waves.
  6. Avoid hairstyles that are too short, blunt, or angular, as they can emphasize the sharpness of your jawline and create an overly masculine look.
  7. When applying makeup, focus on enhancing your eyes and lips to draw attention away from your jawline. Consider using bold lip colors, false lashes, or dramatic eyeliner to create a more feminine look.
  8. Experiment with different makeup and hairstyles to find the look that works best for you. Don't be afraid to try new things and step out of your comfort zone!

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you shape your square face:

  • Use a light-reflecting primer to create a smooth, even base for your makeup.
  • Choose makeup shades that complement your natural skin tone and eye color.
  • Use a volumizing hair product to add volume to the top of your head.
  • Consider wearing statement jewelry or scarves to draw attention to your face and create a more balanced look.
  • Practice good skincare habits, such as drinking plenty of water, using sunscreen, and moisturizing daily.

Hair Care for Square Faces

When it comes to hair care for square faces, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and softens the angles of your jawline.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, blunt, or angular, as they can emphasize the sharpness of your jawline and create an overly masculine look.
  • Consider adding layers or soft waves to your hair to create a softer, more feminine look.
  • Use a volumizing hair product to add bounce and body to your hair.
  • Consider visiting a professional stylist for personalized advice and recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I still wear bold makeup looks if I have a square face?

A: Absolutely! In fact, bold makeup can be a great way to draw attention to your eyes and lips and create a more feminine look. Just be sure to balance your bold makeup with soft, subtle contouring and a volumizing hairstyle.

Q: What kind of jewelry should I wear with a square face?

A: Statement jewelry, such as chandelier earrings or a bold necklace, can be a great way to draw attention to your face and create a more balanced look. Avoid jewelry that is too angular or geometric, as it can emphasize the sharpness of your jawline.

Q: Can I still wear short hairstyles if I have a square face?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and softens the angles of your jawline. Consider a layered bob or a pixie cut with soft, wispy bangs to create a more feminine look.

Q: How often should I trim my hair if I have a square face?

A: It's generally recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and well-maintained. However, the frequency of your trims may vary depending on your hair type and personal preferences.

Q: Can hair color help to shape a square face?

A: Yes, hair color can be a great way to add dimension and shape to your hair. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and texture, or try a bold hair color to make a statement.

Conclusion

Shaping a square face can be a fun and rewarding process, as it allows you to experiment with different makeup and hair styles and enhance your natural beauty. Whether you choose to focus on contouring makeup, volumizing hairstyles, or statement jewelry, remember to have fun and embrace your unique features!
