Best Way To Slick Back Natural Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have natural hair, you might be wondering how to achieve a sleek and elegant look for a special occasion or just for a change. Slicking back natural hair can be a challenge, especially if you have coarse or curly hair. In this article, we will guide you through the best way to slick back natural hair and provide some tips and tricks for maintaining the style.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Slick Back Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Back Natural Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Slicked Back Natural Hair

Description

When it comes to slicking back natural hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to make sure your hair is clean and free of any product buildup. Second, you want to use the right products to help your hair stay in place. And finally, you want to be gentle when styling your hair to avoid breakage and damage.

How to Slick Back Natural Hair

The best way to slick back natural hair is to use a combination of gel and oil. The gel will provide hold and the oil will add shine and moisture to your hair. You can also use a brush or comb to help smooth out your hair and create a sleek look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to slick back natural hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. You can wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup.
  2. Apply a small amount of oil to your hair to add moisture and shine. You can use coconut oil, olive oil, or any other oil that works well with your hair.
  3. Apply a small amount of gel to your hair, focusing on the roots and edges. Use a brush or comb to smooth out your hair and create a sleek look.
  4. You can use a scarf or headband to help hold your hair in place while it dries. Leave the scarf or headband on for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Remove the scarf or headband and use a brush or comb to touch up your hair. You can add more gel or oil as needed to keep your hair in place.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips for Slicking Back Natural Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply oil and gel in small amounts to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Use a boar bristle brush or wide-tooth comb to help distribute the products evenly through your hair.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid breakage and damage.
  • If you have coarse or curly hair, you may need to use a stronger hold gel or add more product to help your hair stay in place.

FAQs

Can I slick back natural hair without using gel?

Yes, you can use a styling cream or mousse instead of gel to slick back natural hair. Just make sure to use a product that provides enough hold to keep your hair in place.

How often should I wash my hair when slicking it back?

You should wash your hair as often as you normally would, depending on your hair type and lifestyle. However, if you are using a lot of product to slick back your hair, you may need to wash it more frequently to avoid buildup.

Can I slick back natural hair if I have a lot of flyaways?

Yes, you can use a toothbrush or small brush to help smooth down flyaways and create a neater look.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Natural Hair

To maintain your slicked back natural hair, you should follow a few basic hair care tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve a sleek and elegant look with your natural hair.
