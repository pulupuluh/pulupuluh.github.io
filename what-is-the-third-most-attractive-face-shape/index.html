What Is The Third Most Attractive Face Shape

Written by Frank May 21, 2023 · 6 min read

When it comes to facial features, the shape of one’s face plays a significant role in their overall appearance. Although beauty is subjective, certain face shapes tend to be more attractive than others. In this article, we will be discussing the third most attractive face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Accentuating Your Features
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Face Shape

Description

The third most attractive face shape is the heart-shaped face. This shape is characterized by a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The overall shape resembles a heart, which is where the name comes from.

Heart-shaped faces are considered attractive because they are symmetrical and balanced. The wider forehead and prominent cheekbones give the face a youthful and feminine appearance, while the narrow chin creates a delicate and refined look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape is essential in finding the most flattering hairstyles and makeup techniques. Here are the basic steps to determine your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a measuring tape or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Note the measurements and compare them to the following face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, and diamond.

Step by Step Guide

If you have determined that you have a heart-shaped face, here are some tips to accentuate your features:

  • Highlight your cheekbones with blush or bronzer.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume at the chin to balance out the narrowness of the face.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs to soften the forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles with too much volume at the crown, as they can make the forehead appear wider.

Tips for Accentuating Your Features

Here are some general tips for accentuating your facial features, regardless of your face shape:

  • Use highlighter to accentuate your cheekbones, brow bone, and cupid’s bow.
  • Define your eyebrows to frame your face.
  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use makeup to enhance your natural features, rather than completely changing them.

FAQs

What is the most attractive face shape?

The most attractive face shape is the oval shape. This shape is considered the most balanced and symmetrical, with features that are proportionate and evenly distributed.

What is the rarest face shape?

The rarest face shape is the diamond shape. This shape is characterized by a narrow forehead, wide cheekbones, and a pointed chin.

Can you change your face shape?

You cannot change your natural face shape, but you can use makeup and hairstyles to create the illusion of a different shape.

Hair Care for Your Face Shape

When it comes to hair care, your face shape should play a role in the hairstyles you choose. Here are some tips for hair care based on your face shape:

  • Oval: Any hairstyle looks great with an oval face shape, so you have the most flexibility.
  • Round: Choose hairstyles that add height at the crown and volume at the sides to elongate the face.
  • Square: Soften the angles of the face with layers and waves.
  • Heart: Add fullness at the chin to balance out the narrowness of the face.
  • Diamond: Choose hairstyles that add width at the forehead and jawline to balance out the cheekbones.

In conclusion, the heart-shaped face is the third most attractive face shape. If you have a heart-shaped face, use the tips mentioned in this article to accentuate your features and find the most flattering hairstyles and makeup techniques.
