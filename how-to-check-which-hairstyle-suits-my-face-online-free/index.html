How To Check Which Hairstyle Suits My Face Online Free Tips Steps And Hair Care

Written by Eveline Mar 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle but you're not sure which one will suit your face shape, you're not alone. There are many factors to consider, such as your facial features, hair texture, and personal style. Fortunately, there are several online tools and resources that can help you find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape. In this article, we'll show you how to check which hairstyle suits your face online for free, step by step. We'll also provide tips and hair care advice to help you maintain your new look. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Find Online Hairstyle Tools

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. There are several common face shapes, including:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

You can determine your face shape by looking in the mirror and measuring the length and width of your face. There are also several online face shape calculators that can help you determine your face shape if you're not sure.

Step 2: Find Online Hairstyle Tools

Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for online hairstyle tools. There are many websites and apps that offer virtual hairstyle try-on tools, such as:

  • Virtual Hairstyler
  • TAAZ Virtual Makeover
  • InStyle Virtual Hair Try-On
  • Hairfinder Virtual Hairstyler

These tools allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles to see how they look with your face shape.

Step 3: Upload Your Photo

Once you've found an online hairstyle tool that you like, the next step is to upload a photo of yourself. Make sure the photo is clear and shows your face from the front. You may also want to pull your hair back or use a headband to keep it out of your face.

Step 4: Try Different Hairstyles

Now it's time to start trying on different hairstyles! Most virtual hairstyle tools allow you to choose from a variety of hairstyles, lengths, and colors. You can also adjust the size and position of the hairstyle to make it look more natural. Try on several different hairstyles to see which ones look best with your face shape and features.

Tips for Finding the Right Hairstyle

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Look for hairstyles that flatter your best features, such as your eyes or cheekbones.
  • Consider your personal style and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. Will it be easy to maintain and style?
  • Don't be afraid to try something new, but also be realistic about what will suit you.
  • Ask your hairstylist for advice and recommendations.

FAQs

Can I use these online hairstyle tools on my phone?

Yes, most online hairstyle tools are mobile-friendly and can be accessed on your phone or tablet.

Do I need to create an account to use these tools?

It depends on the tool. Some tools require you to create an account, while others allow you to use them without signing up.

Can I save my virtual hairstyles to show my hairstylist?

Yes, most virtual hairstyle tools allow you to save your favorite hairstyles and share them with your hairstylist or friends.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using heat styling tools.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

By following these hair care tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape, you can achieve a beautiful and confident new look. Have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and finding the one that suits you best!
