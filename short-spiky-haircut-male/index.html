Short Spiky Haircut Male A Stylish And Low Maintenance Look

Written by Christine Jun 29, 2023 · 8 min read

When it comes to men’s hairstyles, short spiky haircuts have always been a popular choice. With their edgy, textured look and low-maintenance styling, they’re the perfect choice for guys who want to look stylish without spending hours in front of the mirror. In this post, we’ll cover everything you need to know about getting a short spiky haircut male – from how to style it to how to care for it.

Description

A short spiky haircut male is a style that’s characterized by short, textured hair that’s styled into spikes. This style is perfect for guys who want a low-maintenance look that’s still stylish and trendy. The length of the hair can vary, but it’s typically cut short on the sides and back with longer hair on top that’s styled into spikes.

How to Get a Short Spiky Haircut Male

Getting a short spiky haircut male is relatively easy. Here’s how to do it:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

Before you head to the salon, decide how short you want your hair to be. A short spiky haircut can range from a buzz cut to a longer, textured style, so make sure you have a clear idea of what you want before you start.

Step 2: Find a Good Barber

Once you know what you want, it’s time to find a good barber. Look for someone who specializes in men’s haircuts and has experience with short spiky styles. You can also ask for recommendations from friends or read reviews online to find a reputable barber in your area.

Step 3: Get the Cut

When you go to the salon, show your barber a picture of the style you want and explain how short you want your hair to be. They’ll then use clippers to cut your hair to the desired length and use scissors to add texture and shape to the top of your hair.

How to Style a Short Spiky Haircut Male

Styling a short spiky haircut male is easy and requires minimal effort. Here’s how to do it:

Step 1: Apply Product

Start by applying a small amount of hair product to your hair. A pomade or wax will work well for this style, as they provide a strong hold and add texture to your hair.

Step 2: Spike Your Hair

Using your fingers, spike your hair in the direction you want it to go. You can create a messy, textured look by spiking your hair in different directions, or a more polished look by spiking it all in one direction.

Step 3: Finish with Hairspray

Once you’ve spiked your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep it in place. This will help your style last all day without needing touch-ups.

Tips for Maintaining a Short Spiky Haircut Male

While a short spiky haircut male is low-maintenance, there are still a few things you can do to keep it looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Don’t over-style your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style in the future.

FAQ

What face shapes suit a short spiky haircut male?

A short spiky haircut male suits most face shapes, but it’s particularly flattering on guys with a square or oval face shape. If you have a round face, you might want to avoid this style as it can make your face appear even rounder.

What hair types suit a short spiky haircut male?

A short spiky haircut male works well on most hair types, but it’s best suited to guys with thick, straight hair. If you have curly or fine hair, you might find it more difficult to achieve the spiky look.

Can you wear a short spiky haircut male to work?

A short spiky haircut male is a versatile style that can be dressed up or down to suit any occasion. It’s a great choice for guys who work in a casual or creative environment, but it might not be suitable for more formal workplaces.

Hair Care for Short Spiky Haircut Male

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your short spiky haircut male looking its best. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • If you use product in your hair, make sure to wash it out thoroughly at the end of the day.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips, you can keep your short spiky haircut male looking stylish and low-maintenance all year round. So why not give it a try and see how it works for you?
