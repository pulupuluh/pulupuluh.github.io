Best Long Hair For Oval Face A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 04, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have an oval face, you are in luck as you have the most versatile face shape. You can pull off almost any hairstyle, including long hair. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect long hairstyle that flatters your face shape. In this guide, we will walk you through the best long hair for an oval face, how to style it, and tips to maintain your locks.

Description

An oval face is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, high cheekbones, and a face length that is longer than the width. The best long hair for an oval face is one that adds volume and creates an illusion of width. The key is to avoid hairstyles that elongate your face, making it appear longer than it is.

How to Choose the Best Long Hair for Oval Face

When choosing the best long hair for your oval face, consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Here are some of the best long hairstyles that flatter an oval face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are one of the best hairstyles for an oval face. They add volume and dimension to your hair, creating an illusion of width. Long layers work well with any hair texture, from fine to thick. They are also low maintenance and easy to style.

2. Side-swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to frame your face and add volume to your hair. They work well with any long hairstyle, from straight to curly. Side-swept bangs also soften your facial features, making them ideal for those with a sharp jawline.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are perfect for those who want a low maintenance yet stylish look. They add volume and texture to your hair, creating an illusion of width. Beachy waves also work well with any hair texture, from fine to thick.

Step by Step: How to Style Long Hair for Oval Face

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your long hair for an oval face:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair using products that are suitable for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and pat it dry with a towel.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Heat protectants also help to seal in moisture and add shine to your locks.

Step 3: Blow-dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish. Start from the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use a diffuser attachment if you have curly hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a curling iron or flat iron, depending on the look you want to achieve. If you want beachy waves, use a curling iron with a large barrel. If you want straight hair, use a flat iron.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place without making it stiff or crunchy.

Tips to Maintain Long Hair for Oval Face

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long hair:

1. Regular Trims

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Deep Conditioning Treatments

Use deep conditioning treatments once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools to prevent heat damage.

4. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush to prevent breakage.

FAQ

What is the best long hair for an oval face?

The best long hair for an oval face is one that adds volume and creates an illusion of width. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and beachy waves are some of the best hairstyles for an oval face.

Can I pull off a center part with an oval face?

Yes, you can pull off a center part with an oval face. However, a side part is more flattering as it adds volume and creates an illusion of width.

What should I avoid when styling long hair for an oval face?

Avoid hairstyles that elongate your face, making it appear longer than it is. Also, avoid hairstyles that add too much height to your crown, making your face look longer.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to help you take care of your long locks:

1. Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle.

2. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair and keep it moisturized.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent heat damage.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's damaging rays.

In conclusion, choosing the best long hair for an oval face is all about creating an illusion of width and volume. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and beachy waves are some of the best hairstyles for an oval face. With proper care and maintenance, you can keep your long locks healthy and shiny.
