If you're a wig-wearer, you know that human hair wigs can be a great investment. They're versatile, long-lasting, and look just like real hair. However, over time, your human hair wig may start to lose its shape or become damaged. Don't worry - with a few simple tips and tricks, you can bring your wig back to life. How to Bring Back a Human Hair Wig

If your wig is looking a little worse for wear, don't fret. With a little effort, you can bring it back to life. Here are a few tips to help you get started:

1. Detangle Your Wig

Before you do anything else, you'll want to detangle your wig. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be careful not to pull too hard, as this can damage the hair.

2. Wash Your Wig

Next, you'll want to wash your wig. Use a gentle shampoo and lukewarm water to clean the hair, being careful not to rub too hard. Rinse thoroughly and then apply conditioner, focusing on the ends of the hair. Rinse again, and then gently pat the wig dry with a towel.

3. Style Your Wig

Once your wig is clean and dry, you can start styling it. Use a low-heat setting on your curling iron or flat iron to style the hair, being careful not to burn it. If you prefer a more natural look, you can also air-dry your wig and then use a wide-tooth comb to style it.

Tips to Maintain Your Wig

To keep your human hair wig looking its best, here are a few tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Wash your wig every 7-10 days using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it maintain its shape.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about bringing back a human hair wig:

Q: Can I use regular shampoo on my wig?

A: It's best to use a gentle shampoo that's specifically formulated for wigs. Regular shampoo may contain harsh chemicals that can damage the hair.

Q: How often should I wash my wig?

A: You should wash your wig every 7-10 days, or as needed if it becomes dirty or starts to smell.

Q: Can I use heat styling tools on my wig?

A: Yes, but be careful not to use too much heat or to hold the tool in one place for too long. This can damage the hair.

Hair Care for Human Hair Wigs

Here are some tips to help you take care of your human hair wig:

  • Wash your wig every 7-10 days using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it maintain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to harsh chemicals or chlorine, as this can damage the hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can bring your human hair wig back to life and enjoy a beautiful, natural-looking hairstyle. With a little effort, your wig can look just as good as the day you bought it.
