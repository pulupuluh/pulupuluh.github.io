What To Wear 70 Degrees And Rainy A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for the perfect outfit for a 70-degree and rainy day? Look no further than this guide! We've compiled a list of the best clothing options and accessories to keep you comfortable and stylish no matter the weather.

How to Dress for 70 Degrees and Rainy Weather

When the temperature is in the low 70s and it's raining outside, it can be tough to know what to wear. You don't want to be too hot, but you also don't want to be cold and wet. The key is to find a balance between comfort and style. Follow these steps to create the perfect outfit for a rainy day.

Step 1: Start with a Base Layer

The first layer of your outfit should be something that will keep you dry. We recommend a lightweight, water-resistant jacket or raincoat. Look for something that is breathable so you don't get too hot.

Step 2: Add Some Layers

Next, add some layers to your outfit. You can't go wrong with a comfy sweater or cardigan. If it's really chilly outside, you can even add a scarf or a hat to keep your head and neck warm.

Step 3: Choose Your Bottoms

When it comes to choosing your bottoms, there are a few options. You could go with jeans or leggings, both of which are comfortable and easy to move around in. If you want something a little dressier, you could opt for a skirt or dress with tights. Just be sure to choose something that won't get ruined if it gets wet.

Step 4: Don't Forget Your Feet

Your feet are important, too! Make sure you wear shoes that are appropriate for the weather. Sneakers or boots are a great option for a rainy day. Avoid sandals or anything that is open-toed. If you're worried about your shoes getting wet, you can always wear a pair of rain boots.

Tips for Dressing for 70 Degrees and Rainy Weather

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when dressing for a rainy day:

  • Wear waterproof mascara to avoid smudging
  • Carry an umbrella or a rain poncho with you in case the rain gets heavier
  • Choose fabrics that are quick-drying, such as polyester or nylon
  • Opt for darker colors, which won't show stains as easily

FAQ

Q: Can I wear shorts on a rainy 70-degree day?

A: While it's technically possible to wear shorts on a rainy day, we don't recommend it. You'll be much more comfortable in pants or leggings.

Q: Can I wear a skirt or dress on a rainy 70-degree day?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure you wear tights or leggings underneath to keep your legs warm.

Hair Care Tips for Rainy Days

Rainy weather can wreak havoc on your hair. Follow these tips to keep your locks looking great, even in the rain.

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help prevent frizz
  • Tie your hair back in a low ponytail or bun to avoid getting it wet
  • Wear a hat or a hood to protect your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on rainy days

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to dress comfortably and stylishly for a 70-degree and rainy day. Don't let the weather get you down!
