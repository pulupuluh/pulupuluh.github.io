How To Grow Swept Back Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to achieve the trendy and effortless swept back hair look, you'll need to grow out your hair to the right length. But growing out your hair can be a frustrating and time-consuming process. In this guide, we'll show you how to grow swept back hair with ease. From understanding the right length to styling techniques, we've got you covered.

Description

Swept back hair is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. This hairstyle is characterized by long hair that is slicked back and away from the face. Swept back hair is a versatile style that can be worn in both casual and formal settings. However, growing out your hair to the right length can be challenging. In this guide, we'll help you grow out your hair and achieve the perfect swept back look.

How to Grow Swept Back Hair

The first step in growing out your hair for the swept back look is to stop cutting your hair altogether. This may seem counterintuitive, but it's essential to let your hair grow out to the right length. The ideal length for swept back hair is around 6-8 inches. Once your hair has reached this length, you can start styling it.

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The key to healthy hair growth is to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner at least twice a week. This will prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle, which can lead to breakage and split ends.

Step 2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Applying a leave-in conditioner to your hair can help keep it hydrated and healthy. This will also make it easier to style your hair. Apply the leave-in conditioner to damp hair and comb it through with a wide-toothed comb.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair can help give it volume and make it easier to style. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow-drying it. This will create volume and give your hair a natural-looking bounce.

Step 4: Apply Hair Gel or Pomade

To achieve the swept back look, you'll need to apply hair gel or pomade to your hair. These products will help hold your hair in place while giving it a shiny finish. Apply the product to your hair and comb it back with a wide-toothed comb. You can use your fingers to shape your hair and create the desired look.

Tips for Growing Swept Back Hair

  • Be patient. Growing out your hair takes time, so don't get discouraged if it doesn't happen overnight.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair. This can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.
  • Eat a healthy diet. Your hair needs essential nutrients to grow, so make sure you're eating a balanced diet.
  • Get regular trims. While you're growing out your hair, it's important to get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to grow out hair for the swept back look?
A: It can take anywhere from 6 months to a year to grow out your hair to the ideal length for the swept back look.

Q: Can I grow out my hair if I have thinning hair?
A: Yes, you can still grow out your hair if you have thinning hair. However, it may take longer to achieve the desired length.

Hair Care for Swept Back Hair

Once you've achieved the swept back look, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner at least twice a week.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dye.

By following these tips and techniques, you'll be able to grow out your hair and achieve the perfect swept back look. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair along the way.
