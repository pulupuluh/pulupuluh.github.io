What Hair Color Do Most Guys Prefer

Written by Oliver Jul 09, 2023 · 12 min read

As a woman, changing your hair color can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. But if you're wondering what hair color do most guys prefer, then you're not alone!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hair Color
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Colored Hair

Description

When it comes to what hair color do most guys prefer, the answer may surprise you. While some men may have a preference for blondes or brunettes, the truth is that most men are attracted to a woman who exudes confidence and self-assurance.

That being said, there are some hair colors that tend to be more popular among men. Generally, men tend to prefer women with natural-looking hair colors, such as brown or black. However, if you're looking to go for a bolder, more eye-catching look, there's no harm in trying out a new hair color!

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but there are a few things you can do to make the process easier. First, consider your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, then warmer hair colors like honey blonde or chestnut brown may be a good choice. If you have a cooler skin tone, then cooler hair colors like ash blonde or jet black may be more flattering.

Next, think about your lifestyle. If you have a low-maintenance lifestyle, then a natural-looking hair color that requires minimal upkeep may be the way to go. On the other hand, if you're willing to put in the time and effort to maintain a more dramatic hair color, then a bold shade like electric blue or fire engine red may be a good choice.

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are two main types of skin tones: warm and cool. Warm skin tones have yellow, peach, or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones.

You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, then you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue, then you have a cool skin tone.

Step 2: Choose a Hair Color

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can start thinking about what hair color will be most flattering. Generally, warm skin tones look best with warmer hair colors like honey blonde, chestnut brown, or copper red. Cool skin tones, on the other hand, look best with cooler hair colors like ash blonde, jet black, or platinum blonde.

Of course, there's no hard and fast rule when it comes to choosing a hair color. If you're feeling adventurous, you can always go for a bold, unconventional shade like pink, purple, or blue!

Step 3: Visit a Professional

Unless you're an experienced hair colorist, it's best to visit a professional stylist when coloring your hair. A professional can help you choose the right shade, apply the color evenly, and ensure that your hair stays healthy and strong.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

If you're planning on coloring your hair at home, then it's important to follow some basic steps to ensure that you get the best results.

Step 1: Choose a High-Quality Hair Dye

When coloring your hair at home, it's important to choose a high-quality hair dye. Look for a product that is specifically designed for your hair type and color, and choose a shade that will complement your skin tone.

Step 2: Do a Strand Test

Before coloring your entire head of hair, it's a good idea to do a strand test. This will help you determine how the color will look on your hair and whether you're allergic to any of the ingredients in the dye.

Step 3: Protect Your Skin and Clothing

When coloring your hair, it's important to protect your skin and clothing from the dye. Wear an old T-shirt and use petroleum jelly or a barrier cream around your hairline and ears to prevent the dye from staining your skin.

Step 4: Apply the Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye package to apply the color to your hair. Be sure to work in sections and apply the color evenly to avoid any patchy spots.

Step 5: Rinse and Condition

After the dye has set, rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water. Then, apply the conditioner that comes with the dye to help lock in the color and keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've colored your hair, it's important to take steps to maintain your new look. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair color looking fresh and vibrant:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the color from your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause the color to fade more quickly.
  • Consider getting regular touch-ups to keep your hair looking fresh and vibrant.

FAQ

What hair color do most guys prefer?

While some men may have a preference for blondes or brunettes, most men are attracted to a woman who exudes confidence and self-assurance, regardless of her hair color.

Is it better to color your hair at home or go to a salon?

While coloring your hair at home can be convenient and cost-effective, it's generally best to go to a salon if you want professional-looking results.

How often should you touch up your hair color?

The frequency of touch-ups will depend on the type of hair color you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your colored hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the color from your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause the color to fade more quickly.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

So there you have it – everything you need to know about what hair color do most guys prefer. Whether you choose to stick with a natural-looking shade or go for a bold, eye-catching color, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin!
