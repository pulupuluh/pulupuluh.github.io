Hairstyles For Face Shape Male Finding The Perfect Look

Written by Petter Apr 29, 2023 · 8 min read

If you’re a guy, choosing a hairstyle can be a daunting task. You may wonder which style will look best on you, or what will complement your face shape. Fortunately, there are plenty of hairstyles for face shape male that can help you achieve the perfect look. Here are some tips to help you find the right hairstyle for your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different hairstyles for face shape male, let’s first determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Square
  • Round
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Based on these measurements, you can determine your face shape.

Hairstyles for Oval Faces

If you have an oval face shape, you’re in luck. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile, which means you can pull off a variety of hairstyles. Some great options for oval faces include:

  • Short buzz cut
  • Crew cut
  • Side part
  • Pompadour

These hairstyles all work well with the proportions of an oval face shape. However, be sure to consider your hair texture and personal style when choosing a hairstyle.

Hairstyles for Square Faces

If you have a square face shape, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that softens your strong jawline. Some great options for square faces include:

  • Textured crop
  • Side-swept fringe
  • Curly top
  • Short sides and back with longer top

These hairstyles will help balance out your face shape and draw attention away from your jawline.

Hairstyles for Round Faces

If you have a round face shape, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face. Some great options for round faces include:

  • Short sides and back with longer top
  • Textured crop
  • Side-swept fringe
  • Undercut

These hairstyles will help elongate your face and make it appear more oval-shaped.

Hairstyles for Heart Faces

If you have a heart-shaped face, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and balances out your pointed chin. Some great options for heart faces include:

  • Side-swept fringe
  • Textured crop
  • Short sides and back with longer top
  • Curly top

These hairstyles will help balance out your face shape and draw attention away from your pointed chin.

Hairstyles for Diamond Faces

If you have a diamond face shape, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and chin. Some great options for diamond faces include:

  • Textured crop
  • Side-swept fringe
  • Short sides and back with longer top
  • Curly top

These hairstyles will help balance out your face shape and draw attention away from your cheekbones.

Hairstyles for Triangle Faces

If you have a triangle face shape, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and balances out your strong jawline. Some great options for triangle faces include:

  • Short sides and back with longer top
  • Textured crop
  • Side-swept fringe
  • Undercut

These hairstyles will help balance out your face shape and draw attention away from your strong jawline.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair texture
  • Think about how much time you’re willing to spend styling your hair
  • Consider your personal style and the image you want to project
  • Consult with a stylist to get their professional opinion

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for a man with a receding hairline?

A buzz cut or a shaved head can be a good option for men with receding hairlines. These styles are low-maintenance and can help draw attention away from your hairline.

Can I still wear a hairstyle that doesn’t match my face shape?

Of course! These guidelines are just suggestions. Ultimately, you should choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair growth rate and how fast your hair starts to look unkempt. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care Tips

Once you’ve chosen the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great and maintain your new hairstyle.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your face shape can be a challenge, but with these tips and guidelines, you can find a style that works for you. Remember to consider your hair texture, personal style, and maintenance preferences when choosing a style. And don’t forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
