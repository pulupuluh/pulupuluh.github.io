How To Make Your Face Look Less Round In Photos

Written by Luffy Jul 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to taking photos, many people want to accentuate their best features and minimize their flaws. One of the most common concerns people have is a round face. Fortunately, there are several ways to make your face look less round in photos. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve your desired look.

Description

A round face is defined by the width of your cheekbones and the fullness of your cheeks. While a round face can be beautiful, it can also make you feel self-conscious in photos. By using a few simple techniques, you can create the illusion of a slimmer, more contoured face in your pictures.

How to

Here are some tips on how to make your face look less round in photos:

1. Angle Your Face

One of the easiest ways to make your face look less round is to angle your face slightly. Instead of facing the camera head-on, turn your face slightly to one side. This will create a more angular look and make your face appear slimmer.

2. Use the Right Lighting

The right lighting can make all the difference in how your face looks in photos. Avoid harsh overhead lighting, which can create shadows and make your face look rounder. Instead, use soft, diffused lighting, such as natural light or a softbox, to create a more flattering look.

3. Contour Your Face

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using a darker shade of foundation or powder to create shadows on your face. By strategically placing these shadows, you can create the illusion of a more defined, contoured face. Apply the darker shade to the hollows of your cheeks, your temples, and your jawline.

4. Wear Your Hair Down

Wearing your hair down can help to frame your face and create a more slimming effect. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bouffant or a high ponytail. Instead, opt for a sleek, straight style or loose waves.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your face look less round in photos:

Step 1: Angle your face

Turn your face slightly to one side to create a more angular look.

Step 2: Use the right lighting

Avoid harsh overhead lighting and use soft, diffused lighting instead.

Step 3: Contour your face

Apply a darker shade of foundation or powder to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline.

Step 4: Wear your hair down

Frame your face with a sleek or wavy hairstyle, avoiding styles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making your face look less round in photos:

1. Use a longer lens

A longer lens can create a more flattering look by compressing facial features and minimizing distortion.

2. Avoid direct flash

Direct flash can create harsh shadows and emphasize roundness in your face. Use natural or diffused lighting instead.

3. Practice good posture

Good posture can make all the difference in how you look in photos. Stand up straight and elongate your neck to create a more elegant, elongated look.

4. Experiment with different poses

Try different poses to find what works best for you. For example, tilting your head slightly or placing your hand on your hip can create a more flattering look.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my face look less round without makeup?

A: Yes, there are several ways to make your face look less round without makeup. For example, using the right lighting and angling your face can create a more slimming effect.

Q: Can I make my face look less round in selfies?

A: Yes, the same techniques that work for regular photos can also be applied to selfies. Experiment with different angles and lighting to find your most flattering look.

Q: Should I avoid smiling in photos?

A: No, smiling can make you look more approachable and friendly in photos. However, be mindful of how wide you're smiling, as a big, open-mouthed grin can make your face appear rounder.

Hair Care

Your hairstyle can play a big role in how your face looks in photos. If you have a round face, consider a style that adds height to the crown of your head, such as a high bun or a top knot. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bouffant or a curly bob. Consult with your stylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your face look less round in photos and feel more confident in front of the camera. Remember to experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to have fun with your photos!
