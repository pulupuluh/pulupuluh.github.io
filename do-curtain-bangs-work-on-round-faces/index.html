Do Curtain Bangs Work On Round Faces

Written by Robby Jun 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you thinking of trying out curtain bangs but worried if they will suit your round face shape? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this post, we will discuss everything you need to know about curtain bangs for round faces.

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular hair trend that has been around for a while now. They are a type of bangs that are parted in the center, framing the face on both sides. These bangs are called curtain bangs because they resemble a curtain, with the hair parted in the middle like curtains on a window.

Round faces are characterized by a circular shape with soft and curved lines. The width and length of the face are almost equal, with a rounded jawline and cheekbones. If you have a round face, you need to be careful when choosing a hairstyle that can complement your face shape.

How to

If you have a round face and want to try curtain bangs, here's how you can make them work:

Step 1: Consult with your stylist

The first step is to consult with your stylist. They can help you determine if curtain bangs will suit your face shape and suggest the best length and style for you.

Step 2: Opt for longer bangs

When getting curtain bangs for a round face, it's best to go for longer bangs that fall below the cheekbones. This will help elongate the face and make it look slimmer.

Step 3: Part your bangs in the center

Parting your curtain bangs in the center is the key to making them work for a round face. This will help create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Step 4: Style your bangs

Styling your curtain bangs is essential to make them work for your round face. You can use a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and movement. You can also use a straightener to create a sleek and polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting curtain bangs for a round face:

  • Go for soft and wispy bangs instead of blunt bangs.
  • Avoid heavy and thick bangs that can make your face look even rounder.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what suits you best.
  • Consider getting layers around your face to add dimension and structure.

FAQ

Can curtain bangs make a round face look slimmer?

Yes, curtain bangs can help create the illusion of a longer face shape, making it look slimmer.

What length of curtain bangs is best for a round face?

It's best to go for longer curtain bangs that fall below the cheekbones.

Can I style my curtain bangs differently?

Yes, you can style your curtain bangs in different ways, such as adding volume or making them sleek and polished.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful curtain bangs. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly and use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to avoid split ends and maintain their shape.

In conclusion, curtain bangs can work for round faces if done correctly. Consult with your stylist and follow the steps and tips mentioned in this post to achieve the perfect curtain bangs for your round face shape. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful bangs.
