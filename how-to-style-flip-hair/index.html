How To Style Flip Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 07, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for something new? Why not try flipping your hair? It's a simple yet stylish way to change up your look. In this article, we'll show you how to style flip hair and give you some tips on how to maintain it.

Description

Flipping your hair is a great way to add volume and movement to your hairstyle. It's also an easy way to create a more youthful and playful look. Whether you have short or long hair, you can achieve the flip hair style with a little bit of effort and some styling products.

How to Style Flip Hair

Here are the steps to style flip hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra bounce to your hair. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of mousse or styling gel to your hair. This will help to add volume and hold your hair in place. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair evenly.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start from the roots and work your way to the ends. Use the brush to flip the ends of your hair outwards. This will create the signature flip hair look.

Step 4: Set with Hairspray

Once you've finished blow drying, use a light hold hairspray to set your hair in place. This will help to maintain the flip throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling flip hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair from blow drying
  • Experiment with different hair lengths to find the perfect flip for you
  • Don't overdo it with the styling products – too much can weigh down your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before blow drying

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about flip hair:

Can I style flip hair with a straightener?

Yes, you can use a straightener to create the flip hair look. Simply straighten your hair and then use the straightener to curl the ends outwards.

Can flip hair be worn with bangs?

Yes, flip hair can be worn with or without bangs. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your individual look.

How often should I wash my hair if I have flip hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day if you have flip hair. This will help to maintain the volume and bounce in your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your flip hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep split ends at bay
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style flip hair like a pro. It's a fun and easy way to switch up your look and add some extra volume and movement to your hair.
