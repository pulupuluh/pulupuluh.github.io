How To Do Pixie Cut Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that exudes confidence and style, a pixie cut wig might be the perfect option for you. But how do you achieve this look without committing to a haircut? In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps on how to do a pixie cut wig.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Choose Your Wig
  • Step 2: Prep Your Natural Hair
  • Step 3: Put On Your Wig
  • Step 4: Cut Your Wig
  • Step 5: Style Your Wig
  • Tips for Styling Your Pixie Cut Wig
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Pixie Cut Wig

Step 1: Choose Your Wig

The first step in achieving a pixie cut wig is to find the right wig. Look for a wig that is made with high-quality synthetic or human hair that can withstand heat styling. You also want to choose a wig that has a short cut and a natural-looking hairline.

When selecting a wig, consider the shape of your face, the texture of your natural hair, and your personal style. It's important to choose a wig that complements your features and makes you feel confident.

Step 2: Prep Your Natural Hair

Before you put on your wig, you'll need to prep your natural hair. If your hair is long, you'll want to braid it or put it into a low bun to keep it flat against your head. If your hair is short, you can use a wig cap to keep it in place.

It's also important to use a wig cap to protect your natural hair from the wig's material, which can be rough and cause breakage or damage.

Step 3: Put On Your Wig

Once your natural hair is prepped, it's time to put on your wig. Start by placing the wig's cap over your head and adjusting it so that it sits comfortably on your head. Use the wig's adjustable straps to secure it in place, making sure that it's snug but not too tight.

Next, use the wig's combs or clips to secure it to your natural hair. Make sure that the wig is centered on your head and that the hairline is natural-looking.

Step 4: Cut Your Wig

Now that your wig is in place, it's time to cut it into a pixie cut. Start by sectioning off the hair at the top of your head, leaving the sides and back longer. Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut the hair at an angle, creating layers that frame your face.

Work your way down the sides and back of your head, cutting the hair to your desired length. Remember to take your time and use small snips to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Step 5: Style Your Wig

Now that your wig is cut into a pixie cut, it's time to style it. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and volume to your hair. You can also use styling products, such as hairspray or mousse, to hold your style in place.

Experiment with different styles and techniques to find the look that works best for you. Pixie cut wigs are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to messy and tousled.

Tips for Styling Your Pixie Cut Wig

  • Use heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your wig.
  • Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to keep your wig looking shiny and healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle your wig gently.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to help it maintain its shape.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my pixie cut wig?

A: It depends on the material of your wig. Human hair wigs can be dyed, but synthetic wigs cannot. If you're unsure, check the manufacturer's instructions or consult with a professional stylist.

Q: How do I wash my pixie cut wig?

A: Fill a sink with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Gently swish the wig in the water and rinse it thoroughly. Use a towel to pat the wig dry, and then let it air dry on a wig stand.

Hair Care for Your Pixie Cut Wig

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the quality and longevity of your pixie cut wig. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed.
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh products on your wig.
  • Protect your wig from direct sunlight and heat sources, such as ovens or blow dryers.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.

With these tips and steps, you can achieve a stunning pixie cut wig that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle or want to switch up your look, a pixie cut wig is a great option.
