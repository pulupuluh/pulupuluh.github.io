How To Cut Curtain Bangs At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and want to switch things up with some curtain bangs? You're in luck! In this article, we will guide you through the steps of cutting curtain bangs at home. So, grab your scissors and let's get started!

How to Cut Curtain Bangs at Home

Before we start, let's gather the essentials:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Water spray bottle

Now that we have everything we need, let's start cutting those curtain bangs!

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's important to start with clean and dry hair. Use a water spray bottle to dampen your hair if necessary.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair in the middle, creating two equal sections on each side of your head.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape. The base of the triangle should start from the center of your forehead and the point should end at the tip of your nose. Use clips to secure the rest of your hair away from this section.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Hold the triangle section of hair between your index and middle finger, and cut it straight across. Start with a little bit of hair and continue to cut more if you want shorter bangs.

Step 5: Cut the Sides

Starting from the center of your forehead, cut diagonally towards the sides of your face. This will create a softer, more natural look for your curtain bangs.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Release the rest of your hair and check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to the length or shape of your bangs.

Step 7: Style Your Bangs

Use a hair straightener or curling iron to style your new curtain bangs.

Tips for Cutting Curtain Bangs at Home

  • Start with a little bit of hair and cut more if you want shorter bangs.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short. You can always cut more, but it's harder to fix if you cut too much.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting bangs can be a daunting task, but with practice, you'll get the hang of it.

FAQs

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

It's recommended to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Can I cut my curtain bangs when they're wet?

No, it's best to cut your curtain bangs when they're dry. Wet hair can stretch and appear longer, which can result in uneven bangs.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't panic! Mistakes happen, especially when cutting your own hair. If you make a mistake, don't try to fix it yourself. It's best to seek the help of a professional stylist to avoid making the mistake worse.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Now that you have your new curtain bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your bangs to avoid damage.
  • Wash your bangs regularly to avoid oil build-up.
  • Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall to avoid any kinks or cowlicks.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut and take care of your curtain bangs like a pro. Happy cutting!
