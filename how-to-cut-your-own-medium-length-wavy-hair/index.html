How To Cut Your Own Medium Length Wavy Hair

Written by Luffy Sep 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a rewarding experience. If you have medium length wavy hair, you may be hesitant to cut it yourself. However, with a few simple steps, you can achieve a beautiful and stylish haircut in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your own medium length wavy hair.

Description

Medium length wavy hair is a versatile and popular hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. It is typically shoulder length and has natural waves or curls. This hairstyle can be worn with a center or side part and can be styled with loose waves or tighter curls.

How to Cut Your Own Medium Length Wavy Hair

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure that you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Follow these steps to cut your own medium length wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before cutting it. This will help you to see the natural texture and length of your hair.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Part your hair in the desired direction. Use the comb to create a straight part or a side part.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into equal sections using hair clips. Start with the back of your head and work your way to the front.

Step 4: Begin Cutting

Start cutting your hair with small sections. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Be sure to cut only a little at a time to avoid making mistakes.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Check your progress by comparing each section to the previous one. This will help you to achieve an even cut.

Step 6: Trim the Ends

Once you have finished cutting your hair, trim the ends to remove any split ends or frizz.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using a curling iron or straightener. You can also add hair products to enhance your natural waves or curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut your own medium length wavy hair:

  • Start with a small section of hair and cut only a little at a time.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Trim the ends of your hair to remove split ends and frizz.
  • Take breaks to rest your arms and hands.

FAQ

Can I cut my own medium length wavy hair?

Yes, you can cut your own medium length wavy hair with the right tools and techniques.

What tools do I need to cut my own medium length wavy hair?

You will need sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

How do I style my medium length wavy hair?

You can style your medium length wavy hair with a curling iron, straightener, or hair products.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length wavy hair healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and techniques, you can cut your own medium length wavy hair and achieve a beautiful and stylish look. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you will be rewarded with a gorgeous new hairstyle!
