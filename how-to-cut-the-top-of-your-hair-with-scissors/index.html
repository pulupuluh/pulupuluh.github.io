How To Cut The Top Of Your Hair With Scissors

Written by Luffy Sep 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting your own hair can seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done successfully. In this article, we will discuss how to cut the top of your hair with scissors. Whether you are looking for a simple trim or a more drastic change, these tips and tricks will help you achieve the look you desire.

Description

The top of your hair refers to the hair on the crown of your head. This area can be tricky to cut because it is the most visible part of your hair. However, with some practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking cut at home.

How to Cut the Top of Your Hair with Scissors

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It is also helpful to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen your hair.

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Comb it out to remove any tangles or knots. If your hair is thick, you may want to dampen it with water to make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, separate the top section of your hair from the rest of your hair. Clip the bottom section away so it does not get in the way while you are cutting.

Step 3: Trim the Ends

Take a small section of hair and twist it. Hold the twisted section between your fingers and use your scissors to trim the ends. Start by cutting off a small amount and gradually cut more if needed.

Step 4: Work Your Way Around

Continue trimming small sections of hair, working your way around the top of your head. Be sure to check your work in the mirror to make sure the hair is even.

Step 5: Blend the Sides

Once you have trimmed the top of your hair, it is time to blend the sides. Using your comb, comb the hair from the sides towards the top. Use your scissors to trim any longer pieces so that they blend with the top of your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Start with less hair and gradually cut more as needed
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the top of your hair with scissors:

Is it easy to cut the top of your hair with scissors?

With practice and patience, cutting the top of your hair with scissors can be easy. It may take some time to get the hang of it, but once you do, you can achieve great results.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. It is just hair and it will grow back. You can always go to a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair can cause damage, so it is important to take care of it afterwards. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. You can also use a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair.

Now that you know how to cut the top of your hair with scissors, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a professional-looking cut at home.
