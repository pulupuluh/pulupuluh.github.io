Long Hairstyles After 50 Tips And Ideas

Written by Aprilia Jul 13, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a new long hairstyle to try? You've come to the right place! In this article, we'll explore some of the best long hairstyles for women over 50, as well as tips on how to care for your locks and keep them looking healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle

When choosing a long hairstyle after 50, there are a few things to consider. First, think about your face shape and hair texture. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to opt for a style with layers to add some dimension and elongate your face. If you have thin hair, a blunt cut may work better to give the illusion of thicker locks.

Another factor to consider is your lifestyle. If you're busy and don't have a lot of time to spend on styling your hair, you may want to go for a low-maintenance style like long layers or beachy waves. On the other hand, if you have more time to devote to your hair, you may want to try a more intricate style like braids or updos.

Long Layered Hair

One of the most popular long hairstyles for women over 50 is long layers. This style adds movement and dimension to your hair, and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, long layers can work for you.

To achieve this style, ask your stylist for long layers starting around your chin or collarbone. You can also add some face-framing layers for a more flattering look.

Braids

Braids are a great way to add some interest and texture to your long hair. Whether you opt for a simple braid or a more intricate style like a fishtail or waterfall braid, this look is both elegant and practical.

To create a braid, start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Then, separate your hair into three sections and begin braiding, adding in sections of hair as you go. Once you reach the end of your hair, secure the braid with a hair tie or bobby pins.

Beachy Waves

If you're looking for a low-maintenance style that still looks chic and stylish, beachy waves may be the way to go. This style is perfect for those with wavy or curly hair, but can also be achieved with a curling iron or wand.

To create beachy waves, start by applying a texturizing spray to your hair. Then, use a curling iron or wand to curl small sections of hair, alternating the direction of the curls. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to tousle and separate the curls for a relaxed, beachy look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair

Styling long hair can be a bit more time-consuming than styling shorter locks, but the results are worth it. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling long hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow dry it on a low heat setting until it's completely dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. If you want to add some texture or volume to your hair, apply a texturizing spray or mousse.
  4. Choose your styling tool - a curling iron, straightener, or wand - and heat it up according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  5. Section your hair into manageable sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  6. Using your styling tool, curl or straighten each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  7. Once you've styled all of your hair, use your fingers to tousle and separate the curls or straightened sections for a more natural look.
  8. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair

Long hair requires a bit more upkeep than shorter styles, but with the right care, your locks can stay healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining long hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

FAQs

What are some long hairstyles for women over 50?

Some popular long hairstyles for women over 50 include long layers, beachy waves, and braids.

How can I keep my long hair healthy?

To keep your long hair healthy, use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid pulling or breaking your hair, avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, get regular trims, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoid over-washing your hair.

Can I still wear my hair long after 50?

Absolutely! There's no age limit on hair length, and long hair can be a beautiful, elegant choice at any age.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Long hair requires some extra care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for your long locks:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as these can pull and break your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid tangles and breakage while you sleep.

With these tips and ideas, you'll be well on your way to rocking a beautiful, long hairstyle after 50!
