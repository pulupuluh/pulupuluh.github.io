How To Cut Side Layers In Long Hair At Home

Written by Frank Jul 04, 2023 · 8 min read
Are you tired of your long hair looking plain and boring? Adding some side layers can give it a fresh new look, but going to a salon can be expensive. Luckily, with a few simple steps, you can cut side layers in long hair at home. In this article, we will show you how to achieve this look in easy-to-follow steps.

Description

Cutting side layers in long hair can be a bit intimidating, especially if you have never done it before. However, with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a beautiful layered look that will give your hair more volume and movement. This article will guide you through the process of cutting side layers in long hair at home, providing you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

How to

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and then let it air dry or blow-dry it using a round brush to add some volume.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts, using a comb to create a part in the middle. Clip one side of your hair away and focus on the other side.

Step 3: Cut the First Layer

Take a small section of your hair from the front, and comb it straight down. Hold it between your fingers and cut it at a slight angle, starting from the middle of your hair and going towards the ends. Repeat this step with small sections of hair, moving towards the back of your head.

Step 4: Cut the Second Layer

Once you have cut the first layer, take another small section of hair from the front and place it on top of the first layer. Cut it at a slightly steeper angle, starting from the middle and going towards the ends. Repeat this step with small sections of hair, moving towards the back of your head.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

To blend the layers, take a small section of hair from the top and comb it straight down. Hold it between your fingers and cut it at a slight angle, starting from the middle and going towards the ends. Repeat this step with small sections of hair, moving towards the back of your head.

Step 6: Repeat on the Other Side

Unclip the other side of your hair and repeat the same steps to cut the layers. Make sure you are cutting the hair at the same angle as the first side.

Step 7: Check for Symmetry

Once you have cut both sides, check for symmetry by comparing the lengths of the layers. Make any necessary adjustments until they are even.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Now that you have cut your side layers, you can style your hair as desired. Use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or straighten your hair. Apply some hair serum or oil to add shine and tame any frizz.

Tips

- Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid split ends. - Cut your hair slowly and carefully, making small adjustments as needed. - Keep your hair damp while cutting to ensure an even cut. - Don't cut too much hair at once, as it can result in uneven layers.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut side layers in curly hair? A: Yes, you can. However, be careful not to cut too much hair, as it can result in a shorter and less curly look. Q: Do I need to cut my hair when it's wet or dry? A: It's better to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can shrink when it dries, resulting in uneven layers. Q: Can I cut my hair using regular scissors? A: No, it's better to use sharp hair cutting scissors, as regular scissors can cause split ends.

Hair Care

After you have cut your side layers, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Apply a hair mask or oil once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. In conclusion, cutting side layers in long hair at home is easy and affordable. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a beautiful layered look that will give your hair more volume and movement. Follow the steps in this article, and don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain its beauty.
