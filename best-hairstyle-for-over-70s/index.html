Best Hairstyles For Over 70S

Written by Luffy Jul 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair changes and so do our preferences for hairstyles. Women over 70 may find that their hair is thinner or more brittle, making it difficult to style. However, having the right hairstyle can make all the difference. Here are some of the best hairstyles for women over 70.

Description

These hairstyles are designed to flatter and enhance the natural beauty of mature women. They are easy to maintain and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. Whether you prefer short, medium or long hair, there is a hairstyle that will suit you.

How to

The first step in choosing a hairstyle is to consider your face shape. Oval faces can wear almost any style, while round faces look best with longer hair. Square faces look great with layered haircuts, while heart-shaped faces can benefit from bangs or shorter haircuts.

Next, consider your hair type. Thin or fine hair can benefit from shorter haircuts that add volume and texture. Thick or curly hair can be tamed with layers or a long bob.

Finally, think about your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend on your hair. Short hairstyles are low maintenance and easy to style, while longer hairstyles require more upkeep.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the best hairstyles for women over 70:

The Pixie Cut:

This short, choppy hairstyle is perfect for women with fine hair. It adds volume and texture and can be styled with a little wax or gel. To achieve this look:

  1. Ask your hairdresser for a short, choppy cut with lots of layers.
  2. Apply some wax or gel to your hair and style it to your liking.
  3. Use a hairdryer to add volume and texture.

The Bob:

The classic bob is a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. To achieve this look:

  1. Ask your hairdresser for a chin-length bob with layers.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume.
  3. Use a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.

The Shag:

This layered hairstyle is perfect for women with thick or curly hair. It adds movement and texture and can be styled with a little mousse or hairspray. To achieve this look:

  1. Ask your hairdresser for a shag haircut with lots of layers.
  2. Apply some mousse or hairspray to your hair and scrunch it with your fingers.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a diffuser to enhance your natural curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for mature hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for women over 70:

What is the best hairstyle for thinning hair?

Short haircuts with layers are a great choice for women with thinning hair, as they add volume and texture.

Can I still have long hair over 70?

Yes, you can still have long hair over 70, but it may require more upkeep. Long hair can be heavy and difficult to manage, so consider getting regular trims to keep it healthy.

What is the easiest hairstyle to maintain?

Short hairstyles are the easiest to maintain, as they require less styling and upkeep. A pixie cut, for example, can be styled with a little wax or gel and requires minimal fuss.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and looking its best is essential at any age. Here are some tips for taking care of mature hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for mature hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Choosing the right hairstyle can make all the difference in how you look and feel. These hairstyles are designed to flatter and enhance the natural beauty of mature women, and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. Whether you prefer short, medium or long hair, there is a hairstyle that will suit you.
