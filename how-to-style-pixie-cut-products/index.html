How To Style Pixie Cut Products A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 18, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have a pixie cut, you know how versatile and convenient it can be. But sometimes, you might want to switch up your look or add some extra flair to your hair. That’s where styling products come in! Whether you want to add volume, texture, or shine, there are plenty of pixie cut products that can help you achieve your desired look. In this article, we’ll show you how to style pixie cut products in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents:

  • Section 1: How to Prep Your Hair
  • Section 2: How to Choose the Right Products
  • Section 3: How to Style Your Pixie Cut
  • Section 4: Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut
  • Section 5: FAQ
  • Section 6: Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Section 1: How to Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling your pixie cut, it’s important to prep your hair properly. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or buildup. Towel-dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub it too vigorously. If you have fine or thin hair, you may want to use a volumizing spray or mousse to add some extra body.

Section 2: How to Choose the Right Products

When it comes to styling pixie cut products, there are a few different options to consider. Some popular choices include:

  • Hair wax or pomade: These products can help add texture and hold to your hair, making it easier to style and control.
  • Hair spray: A light mist of hairspray can help set your style in place and add some extra shine.
  • Dry shampoo: If you’re short on time or don’t want to wash your hair every day, dry shampoo can help absorb oil and refresh your hair.

When choosing pixie cut products, think about your hair type and the look you want to achieve. If you have thick or coarse hair, you may need a stronger hold product to keep your hair in place. If you have fine or thin hair, a lighter product may be better to avoid weighing down your hair.

Section 3: How to Style Your Pixie Cut

Once you’ve prepped your hair and chosen your products, it’s time to start styling! Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with dry hair: Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start styling. Use a blow dryer or let your hair air dry if you have time.
  2. Apply product: Take a small amount of your chosen product (such as hair wax or pomade) and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Then, apply it to your hair, focusing on the roots and any areas where you want to add volume or texture.
  3. Style your hair: Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair as desired. You can create a messy, textured look or a sleek, polished style depending on your preference.
  4. Finish with hairspray: Once you’ve styled your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to set your style in place and add some extra shine.

Remember, the key to styling pixie cut products is to use a small amount of product at a time and build up as needed. You can always add more product if you need more hold or texture, but it’s harder to remove product if you’ve used too much.

Section 4: Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Once you’ve styled your pixie cut products, you’ll want to make sure your hair stays looking great all day long. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Touch up your style throughout the day with a small amount of product or hairspray.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Get regular trims to keep your pixie cut looking fresh and healthy.

Section 5: FAQ

Q: Can I use the same products on my pixie cut as I did on longer hair?

A: It depends on the product and your hair type. Some products may work differently on shorter hair, so it’s important to choose products that are specifically designed for pixie cuts. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in different styles with a pixie cut?

A: Absolutely! While a pixie cut may be shorter than other haircuts, there are still plenty of ways to style it. You can add braids, twists, or accessories like headbands or barrettes to change up your look.

Section 6: Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Finally, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips for hair care for pixie cuts:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and using the right pixie cut products, you can achieve a stylish, low-maintenance look that’s perfect for any occasion.
