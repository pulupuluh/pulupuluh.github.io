Will Long Hair Suit Me Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair has been a popular trend among men in recent years. But, the question remains, will long hair suit me male? It's a valid concern, as not every hairstyle looks good on everyone. In this post, we will explore how to determine if long hair will suit your face shape and features, how to grow and maintain long hair, and some helpful tips and FAQs.

How to Determine if Long Hair Will Suit You

Before growing out your hair, it's essential to determine if the style will suit your face shape and features. Here are some guidelines to follow:

Face Shape

Long hair typically looks good on men with oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, long hair may not be the best option, as it can make your face appear even rounder.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also impact how long hair looks on you. Men with thick, wavy, or curly hair tend to look better with long hair, while men with thin or straight hair may want to avoid the style.

Features

If you have prominent or sharp features, long hair can soften your look. On the other hand, if you have softer features or a baby face, long hair can make you look younger.

How to Grow and Maintain Long Hair

If you've determined that long hair will suit you, the next step is to grow and maintain it. Here are some tips:

Be Patient

Growing out your hair takes time and patience. It can take up to a year to grow out your hair long enough for a style, so don't get discouraged if it takes a while.

Get Regular Trims

While growing out your hair, it's essential to get regular trims to keep split ends at bay. Trimming your hair every six to eight weeks can help keep it healthy and prevent breakage.

Use Quality Products

Using quality hair products can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Look for products specifically designed for long hair, such as shampoos and conditioners that promote growth and prevent breakage.

Tips for Long Hair Maintenance

Here are some additional tips for maintaining long hair:

Protect Your Hair

Avoid exposing your hair to harsh chemicals, such as chlorine in swimming pools, and always wear a hat or sunscreen when out in the sun to protect your hair from damage.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, can damage your hair and cause breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low.

Brush Your Hair

Brushing your hair regularly can help distribute natural oils and prevent tangles. Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to avoid pulling and breaking your hair.

FAQs about Long Hair for Men

Here are some frequently asked questions about long hair for men:

Can I still wear a hat with long hair?

Yes, wearing a hat with long hair is possible. Choose a hat with a larger brim or one that sits higher on your head to avoid flattening your hair.

How often should I wash my long hair?

Washing your hair every two to three days is usually sufficient. Over-washing can strip your hair of natural oils and cause dryness and breakage.

Can I still style my hair with long hair?

Yes, there are many styling options for men with long hair, such as top knots, braids, and ponytails.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Finally, here are some hair care tips specifically for long hair:

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your long hair healthy and prevent breakage.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, such as tight ponytails or braids, can pull on your hair and cause breakage. Avoid tight hairstyles, or if you must wear them, use a looser style.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth and keep your long hair healthy.

In conclusion, whether long hair will suit you male depends on your face shape, hair texture, and features. If you've determined that long hair is the right style for you, be patient and follow these tips for growing and maintaining healthy, beautiful long hair.
