How To Cut The Back Of Your Hair Straight Across

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune to get your hair cut at a salon? Do you want to learn how to cut the back of your hair straight across at home? If so, you’ve come to the right place! In this step-by-step guide, we’ll show you how to achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving the comfort of your own home.

Description

Cutting your own hair can seem daunting, especially when it comes to the back. However, with the right tools and techniques, it’s easier than you might think. Cutting your hair straight across the back is a simple way to achieve a classic and polished look. It’s perfect for those who prefer a blunt cut or want to maintain a straight and even length.

How to Cut the Back of Your Hair Straight Across

Before we get started, here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp haircutting scissors
  • A handheld mirror or a wall-mounted mirror
  • A comb
  • Hair clips or elastics

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to comb through and ensure an even cut. If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, consider blow-drying it straight before you start cutting.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into two equal sections by parting it down the middle. Secure each section with hair clips or elastics to keep them out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Starting with one section, remove the hair clip or elastic and comb through the hair to remove any tangles. Hold the hair straight down with your fingers and cut straight across with your scissors. Repeat this process on the other side.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layer

Once you’ve cut the bottom layer, release the top layer and comb it down over the cut layer. This will help you see where the hair needs to be trimmed. Again, hold the hair straight down with your fingers and cut straight across with your scissors.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you’ve cut both sections, release your hair and check your work. Use a handheld mirror or a wall-mounted mirror to check the back of your head. If you notice any uneven spots or pieces that are too long, simply trim them until you achieve your desired length.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Invest in a good pair of haircutting scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create uneven cuts.
  • Don’t rush the process. Take your time and cut small sections at a time to ensure an even cut.
  • Always cut your hair when it’s dry. Wet hair can stretch and appear longer, which can result in an uneven cut.
  • Use a handheld mirror or a wall-mounted mirror to check your work as you go.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair straight across with regular scissors?

While you can technically use regular scissors to cut your hair, we recommend investing in a pair of haircutting scissors. These scissors are designed specifically for cutting hair and will give you a cleaner and more precise cut.

How often should I cut my hair?

How often you cut your hair depends on your hair type and length. As a general rule, it’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain a healthy and even length.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair can be a great way to maintain a healthy and polished look. However, it’s important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished.
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to cut the back of your hair straight across with ease. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you’ll achieve a professional-looking haircut in no time!
