How To Take Care Of Hair After Permanent Hair Straightening

Written by William Aug 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Permanent hair straightening has become a popular hair treatment among women who want to have a sleek and straight hair look. However, taking care of hair after permanent hair straightening is important to maintain the health and strength of the hair. In this blog post, we will discuss how to take care of hair after permanent hair straightening.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Permanent hair straightening is a chemical process that breaks down the bonds of the hair and rearranges them in a straight pattern. This process can cause damage to the hair, making it important to take care of it properly after the treatment. Failure to take care of hair after permanent hair straightening can lead to dryness, breakage, and hair fall.

How to

There are several ways to take care of hair after permanent hair straightening:

1. Avoid washing hair for at least 2 days after the treatment

It is important to let the hair rest for at least 2 days after the treatment. This will allow the hair to settle and the chemicals to fully penetrate the hair shaft.

2. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can strip away the natural oils of the hair, making it dry and brittle. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Avoid using hot tools

Using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons can cause further damage to the hair. Avoid using these tools, or if necessary, use a heat protectant spray before styling.

4. Use a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help keep the hair moisturized and prevent dryness and frizz. Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner on damp hair and style as usual.

Step by Step

1. Wash hair properly

Wash hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Avoid rubbing the hair harshly while washing as it can cause tangles and breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair while it is still wet.

2. Apply a hair mask

Apply a hair mask once a week to keep the hair moisturized and healthy. Leave the mask on for 10-15 minutes and rinse with lukewarm water.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids as they can cause tension and breakage to the hair. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles like a messy bun or a low ponytail.

4. Trim hair regularly

Trim hair every 6-8 weeks to get rid of split ends and breakage. This will help keep the hair healthy and prevent further damage.

Tips

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage while sleeping.
  • Avoid exposing the hair to chlorine and saltwater.
  • Protect the hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQ

Q: Can I color my hair after permanent hair straightening?

A: It is best to wait at least 2 weeks before coloring your hair after permanent hair straightening. This will allow the hair to fully recover from the chemical treatment.

Q: How often should I wash my hair after permanent hair straightening?

A: It is recommended to wash hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage.

Hair Care

Taking care of hair after permanent hair straightening is important to maintain the health and strength of the hair. Follow the tips and steps mentioned above to keep your hair healthy, moisturized, and frizz-free.
