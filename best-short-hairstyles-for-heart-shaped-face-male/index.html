Best Short Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face Male

Written by Petter May 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to find a style that complements your facial features and enhances your overall look. Short hairstyles can be a great option for those with a heart-shaped face, as they can help balance out the face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for heart shaped face male.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, a narrower jawline, and a pointed chin. The best short hairstyles for heart-shaped face male are those that balance out the face shape and enhance the facial features. A good hairstyle can draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, while minimizing the width of your forehead and creating the illusion of a broader jawline.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a heart-shaped face, it's important to consider the length, texture, and shape of your hair. A shorter style that adds volume to the crown of your head can balance out your face shape and make your jawline appear wider. You may also want to consider a style with layers or texture, as this can add dimension and movement to your hair.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating some of the best short hairstyles for heart shaped face male:

1. The Side Part

Start by washing and towel-drying your hair. Use a comb to create a deep side part on one side of your head. Apply some pomade or styling gel to your hair and use your fingers to comb your hair back and to the side. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. The Buzz Cut

For a low-maintenance style, consider the classic buzz cut. Use a clipper to trim your hair to a short length all over. This style works well for those with a heart-shaped face, as it minimizes the width of the forehead and creates the illusion of a broader jawline.

3. The Textured Crop

This style works well for those with wavy or curly hair. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Apply some texturizing paste or cream to your hair and use your fingers to create a messy, textured look. This style adds volume to the crown of your head and balances out the shape of your face.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best short hairstyles for heart shaped face male:

  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a style.
  • Avoid styles that add volume to the forehead.
  • Choose a style that adds volume to the crown of your head.
  • Consider adding layers or texture to your hair.
  • Use products like pomade or texturizing cream to enhance your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best short hairstyles for heart shaped face male:

What is a heart-shaped face?

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, a narrower jawline, and a pointed chin.

What are the best short hairstyles for heart shaped face male?

The best short hairstyles for heart shaped face male are those that balance out the face shape and enhance the facial features. A good hairstyle can draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, while minimizing the width of your forehead and creating the illusion of a broader jawline.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your style looking fresh.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

Overall, the best short hairstyles for heart shaped face male are those that complement your facial features and enhance your overall look. Consider your hair texture, face shape, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.
