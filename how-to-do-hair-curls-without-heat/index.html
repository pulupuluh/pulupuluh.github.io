How to Do Hair Curls Without Heat

There are several ways to curl your hair without using any heat. Here are some of the most popular methods:

1. Braids

Braiding your hair is one of the easiest ways to get curls. You can either make one big braid or multiple small braids. Leave the braids overnight or for a few hours, and then take them out to reveal beautiful curls.

2. Twist and Pin

Section your hair and twist each section. Then, pin the twisted section to your head using bobby pins. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight to get bouncy curls.

3. Rollers

You can use foam or Velcro rollers to curl your hair without heat. Section your hair and wrap it around the roller. Leave them in for a few hours, and then remove the rollers to reveal curls.

4. Headband Curls

Put a headband around your head and tuck your hair into it. Then, twist the hair around the headband until you reach the end. Leave it overnight or for a few hours and then take it out to get bouncy curls.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the different ways to curl your hair without using heat, let's take a look at the step-by-step guide.

1. Braids

  1. Wash your hair and let it air-dry until it's damp.
  2. Section your hair and braid each section.
  3. Leave the braids overnight or for a few hours.
  4. Take out the braids and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.

2. Twist and Pin

  1. Wash your hair and let it air-dry until it's damp.
  2. Section your hair and twist each section.
  3. Pin the twisted section to your head using bobby pins.
  4. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight.
  5. Remove the pins and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.

3. Rollers

  1. Wash your hair and let it air-dry until it's damp.
  2. Section your hair and wrap it around the roller.
  3. Leave them in for a few hours.
  4. Remove the rollers and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.

4. Headband Curls

  1. Wash your hair and let it air-dry until it's damp.
  2. Put a headband around your head and tuck your hair into it.
  3. Twist the hair around the headband until you reach the end.
  4. Leave it overnight or for a few hours.
  5. Take it out and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.

Tips to Keep Your Curls Intact

Here are some tips to keep your curls intact:

  • Use a hairspray to hold your curls in place.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent your curls from getting tangled.
  • Apply a hair serum to add shine to your curls.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long do I need to leave the braids in to get curls?

You can leave the braids in overnight or for a few hours to get curls.

2. How many rollers do I need to curl my hair?

The number of rollers you need depends on the thickness and length of your hair. You may need around 10-12 rollers for medium-length hair.

3. Can I use a hairdryer to speed up the process?

No, using a hairdryer will defeat the purpose of curling your hair without heat. It may also cause damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Healthy Curls

Caring for your hair is important to maintain healthy curls. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools frequently as it can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

Now that you know how to do hair curls without heat, try out these methods and flaunt your beautiful curls!
