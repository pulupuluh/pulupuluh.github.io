Does The Dyson Airwrap Work On Short Fine Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have short fine hair, you might be wondering if the Dyson Airwrap is the right styling tool for you. With its innovative technology and high price tag, you don't want to invest in a product that won't work for your hair type. In this article, we will explore whether the Dyson Airwrap works on short fine hair, how to use it, and some tips for achieving the best results.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Best Results
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The Dyson Airwrap is a hair styling tool that uses air to style hair without extreme heat. It comes with different attachments for different styling needs including smoothing brushes, volumizing brushes, and curling barrels. The Airwrap uses the Coanda effect to attract hair to the barrel or brush and wrap it around for a smooth and shiny finish. The Airwrap is a popular choice for those who want to style their hair without damaging it with high heat.

How to Use

Before using the Dyson Airwrap, make sure your hair is clean and dry. The Airwrap works best on hair that is slightly damp, so you can either towel dry your hair or use the pre-styling dryer attachment to dry it. Choose the appropriate attachment for the style you want to achieve and attach it to the Airwrap. Turn on the Airwrap and adjust the speed and heat settings if necessary. Start styling your hair by placing the attachment at the root of your hair and slowly moving it towards the ends. Repeat until you achieve your desired style.

Step by Step Guide

1. Wash and dry your hair

2. Choose the appropriate attachment for your desired style

3. Attach the chosen attachment to the Airwrap

4. Turn on the Airwrap and adjust settings if necessary

5. Place the attachment at the root of your hair and slowly move it towards the ends

6. Repeat until you achieve your desired style

Tips for Best Results

1. Use the pre-styling dryer attachment to dry your hair before styling

2. Section your hair to make styling easier

3. Use the cool shot button to set your style

4. Experiment with different attachments to find the best one for your hair type

5. Use the Airwrap on low heat settings to avoid damaging your hair

FAQ

Q: Can the Dyson Airwrap be used on short fine hair?

A: Yes, the Dyson Airwrap can be used on short fine hair. However, it is important to choose the appropriate attachment for your hair type and use low heat settings to avoid damage.

Q: Can the Dyson Airwrap be used on wet hair?

A: No, the Dyson Airwrap is designed to be used on slightly damp hair. Use the pre-styling dryer attachment to dry your hair before styling.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a heat protectant before using the Dyson Airwrap to avoid damage. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and let it air dry whenever possible.

In conclusion, the Dyson Airwrap can work on short fine hair if used correctly. Choose the appropriate attachment, use low heat settings, and take care of your hair to achieve the best results. With its innovative technology and versatile attachments, the Dyson Airwrap is a great investment for those who want to style their hair without damaging it with high heat.
