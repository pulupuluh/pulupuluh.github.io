Are you an oblong face shape looking for the perfect haircut to flatter your features? Look no further! In this article, we'll explore the best haircut options for oblong face shapes, as well as tips for hair care and maintenance. Let's dive in!

Description

Oblong face shapes are longer than they are wide, with a prominent forehead and a narrow chin. The goal when choosing a haircut for an oblong face shape is to balance out the length and create the illusion of width. This can be achieved through certain styles and techniques, which we'll explore in the sections below.

How to Find Your Oblong Face Shape

If you're not sure whether you have an oblong face shape, here's how to find out:

  1. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Compare the measurements:
    • If all three are roughly the same, you have a square face shape.
    • If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline, you have a heart face shape.
    • If your jawline is wider than your forehead and cheekbones, you have a diamond face shape.
    • If your forehead and jawline are roughly the same width, but your cheekbones are narrower, you have an oblong face shape.
  3. Look at photos of yourself to confirm the shape.

Best Haircuts for Oblong Face Shapes

Now that you've confirmed you have an oblong face shape, let's take a look at some of the best haircut options for you.

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help break up the length of an oblong face shape and create movement and volume. Keep the layers soft and blended to avoid adding too much bulk.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out the forehead and create the illusion of width. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as these can make the face look longer.

3. Bob

A bob that hits just below the chin can help create the illusion of width and balance out the length of an oblong face shape. Add some soft layers and side-swept bangs for extra volume and movement.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can work well for oblong face shapes, as long as it's not too short on the sides. Keep some length on the top and front to create the illusion of width, and add some texture for movement.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut for your oblong face shape, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep the ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage hair.
  • Protect hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that add too much height on top, as this can make the face look longer. Also avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as these can make the face look narrower.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have an oblong face shape?

Yes, but it's important to add some volume and movement to avoid making the face look too long. Consider adding layers or side-swept bangs to create the illusion of width.

Hair Care for Oblong Face Shapes

In addition to regular trims and deep conditioning treatments, here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Choose products that are specifically designed for your hair type and concerns.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip away natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and haircut options, you're sure to find the perfect style to flatter your oblong face shape. Happy styling!
