How To Do An Old Lady Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a simple haircut that will make you look and feel younger, an old lady haircut could be the perfect choice. Not only is it easy to maintain, but it also adds a touch of elegance to your overall appearance. In this post, we'll go through how to do an old lady haircut step by step, along with some tips and FAQs to help you get the best results.

Description

An old lady haircut is a short, layered haircut that is typically styled with a side parting. It is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades, and it is still a great choice for women who want a low-maintenance haircut that is easy to style. The haircut is ideal for women with thin hair, as the layers can add volume and texture to the hair.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools, including a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have someone to help you, as it can be difficult to cut your own hair.

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to dry your hair thoroughly with a towel or blow dryer.

Step 2: Comb your hair

Use a comb to part your hair on one side. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Trim the ends

Use your scissors to trim the ends of your hair. Make small cuts at a time, and keep checking the length as you go.

Step 4: Add layers

To add layers, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, creating a soft, layered effect. Repeat this process all over your head, focusing on the back and sides.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use a hair dryer and a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

- Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut your hair.

- Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member.

- Use a comb to section your hair and ensure you cut it evenly.

FAQ

Q: Is an old lady haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: An old lady haircut is ideal for thin, fine hair, but it can also work for other hair types.

Q: How often should I trim my old lady haircut?

A: You should trim your old lady haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Q: Can I color my hair after an old lady haircut?

A: Yes, you can color your hair after an old lady haircut. However, it's best to wait at least two weeks before coloring your hair to allow it to settle.

Hair Care

To keep your old lady haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.

In conclusion, an old lady haircut is a classic and elegant hairstyle that is easy to maintain and style. By following the steps in this guide, you can achieve a beautiful and timeless look that will make you feel confident and stylish.
