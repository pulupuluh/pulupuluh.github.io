How To Style Short Emo Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Aug 27, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking for a unique and edgy hairstyle, emo hair might be the perfect choice for you. With its signature choppy layers, bold colors, and asymmetrical cuts, emo hair has been a popular trend for years. If you have short hair and want to try out this style, don't worry – you can still rock the emo look with ease. In this article, we'll show you how to style short emo hair step by step.

Description

Emo hair is all about expressing yourself and standing out from the crowd. This style is characterized by its dramatic texture and bold colors. Emo haircuts usually feature choppy layers, asymmetrical cuts, and side-swept bangs. The style is often associated with punk and rock music and has been a popular trend for years.

How to Style Short Emo Hair

Styling short emo hair can be easier than you think. Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect emo look:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Emo hair requires a lot of styling products, so it's important to start with clean hair.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and texture. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply a small amount of styling wax or pomade to your hair to create texture and hold. Emo hair is all about the choppy, messy look, so don't worry about making it perfect.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

If you have bangs, style them to the side for a classic emo look. Use a flat iron to create a sleek, straight look or a curling iron to add some texture.

Step 5: Add Some Color

Emo hair is all about bold colors, so don't be afraid to add some highlights or dye your hair a bright color. You can also add some temporary color with hair chalk or spray.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect emo hairstyle:

  • Use a texturizing spray to create volume and definition.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like hair clips and headbands.
  • If you have thin hair, use a volumizing mousse to create a fuller look.
  • Try different styles, like a messy bun or a half-up, half-down look.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

FAQ

What kind of haircut is best for emo hair?

Emo haircuts usually feature choppy layers, asymmetrical cuts, and side-swept bangs. Talk to your hairstylist about what will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Can I still have emo hair if I have short hair?

Absolutely! Emo hair can work with any length of hair. Just make sure to style it with lots of texture and volume.

What colors are popular for emo hair?

Bright colors like pink, blue, and green are popular for emo hair. You can also try a two-tone look with contrasting colors.

Hair Care

Emo hair requires a lot of styling products, which can be drying for your hair. Make sure to use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

It's also important to protect your hair from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools. Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to style short emo hair, it's time to rock the look with confidence. Whether you're going for a bold color or a choppy cut, emo hair is all about expressing yourself and standing out from the crowd.
