Best Curling Iron For Short Hair 2020 Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 30, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you looking for the best curling iron for short hair 2020 UK? Look no further because we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best curling irons suitable for short hair in 2020. From the description, how-to, step-by-step, tips, FAQ, and hair care, we’ve got it all covered. So, let’s dive in!

Description

Curling irons are essential styling tools for creating beautiful curls and waves with ease. However, finding the right curling iron for short hair can be a daunting task, especially with the abundance of options available on the market. The best curling iron for short hair 2020 UK should be compact, easy to handle, and capable of creating tight curls and waves on short hair without damaging it.

How To Choose the Best Curling Iron for Short Hair 2020 UK

When choosing the best curling iron for short hair 2020 UK, there are certain factors to consider:

Barrel Size

The barrel size of the curling iron determines the size of the curls or waves it creates. For short hair, it’s best to opt for a curling iron with a smaller barrel size, preferably between 0.5 to 1 inch. This size will create tight and defined curls suitable for short hair.

Barrel Material

The material of the barrel is also essential. Ceramic or tourmaline barrels are the best for short hair as they distribute heat evenly, reducing the risk of damage to the hair.

Heat Settings

The heat settings of the curling iron should also be adjustable to ensure that the temperature is suitable for short hair. It’s best to choose a curling iron with a temperature range of 250°F to 350°F.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Use the Best Curling Iron for Short Hair 2020 UK

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to use the best curling iron for short hair 2020 UK:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before using the curling iron, it’s essential to prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair to ensure that it’s clean and moisturized. Then, blow-dry your hair to ensure that it’s completely dry.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. This will also help to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don’t miss any sections.

Step 4: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the barrel of the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds and release. Repeat the process until all sections of your hair are curled.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off with a hairspray to hold the curls in place and give your hair a beautiful finish.

Tips for Using the Best Curling Iron for Short Hair 2020 UK

Here are some tips to help you get the best out of your curling iron:

Tip 1: Start with Clean and Dry Hair

Ensure that your hair is clean and completely dry before using the curling iron. This will help to prevent damage to your hair and give you the best results.

Tip 2: Use a Heat Protectant

Always use a heat protectant spray before using the curling iron to protect your hair from heat damage.

Tip 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips to ensure that you don’t miss any sections and your curls are uniform.

Tip 4: Choose the Right Barrel Size

Choose a curling iron with the right barrel size for your hair length. For short hair, a barrel size between 0.5 to 1 inch is ideal.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a curling iron on short hair?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron on short hair. However, it’s essential to choose a curling iron with a smaller barrel size to create tight and defined curls.

Q: How do I avoid damage to my hair when using a curling iron?

A: To avoid damage to your hair, always use a heat protectant spray before using the curling iron. Also, ensure that your hair is completely dry before using the curling iron.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful:

Tip 1: Regular Trims

Regular trims will help to keep your short hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Tip 2: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools too often and always use a heat protectant spray before using the curling iron.

In conclusion, choosing the best curling iron for short hair 2020 UK can be overwhelming, but with the right information, you can make an informed decision. Follow our guide, and you’ll be on your way to creating beautiful curls and waves on your short hair with ease!
