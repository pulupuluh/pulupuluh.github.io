Can A Round Face Pull Off Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Apr 20, 2023 · 9 min read

Short hair is a bold and trendy choice for any woman, but it’s not always easy to pull off. If you have a round face, you might be wondering if short hair is the right choice for you. The good news is that a round face can definitely pull off short hair! In this article, we’ll take a look at how to make short hair work for a round face, including some tips and tricks for styling.

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. It’s a cute and youthful shape, but it can also be a challenge when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. Short hair is a great way to show off your face shape and highlight your best features. However, it can also make your face look even rounder if you don’t choose the right cut and style.

How to

The key to pulling off short hair with a round face is to choose a cut and style that will balance out your features. Here are some tips:

1. Choose a layered cut

A layered cut will add volume and texture to your hair, which can help balance out your face shape. The layers should be longer around your face and shorter at the back to create a flattering shape.

2. Go for a side part

A side part can help create the illusion of length in your face, making it look less round. Avoid a center part, which can make your face look even rounder.

3. Add some volume

A little bit of volume at the crown can also help elongate your face. Use a volumizing mousse or spray and blow dry your hair with a round brush to create some lift.

4. Keep it short

Short hair is the way to go for a round face, but don’t go too short. A pixie cut or a bob that hits at the chin are both great options that will flatter your face shape.

Step by step

Here’s how to get the perfect short hair look for a round face:

Step 1: Choose the right cut

Consult with your hairstylist to find the best cut for your face shape. A layered bob or a pixie cut with longer layers around your face are both great options.

Step 2: Add some texture

Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair. Scrunch your hair with your fingers as you blow dry it to create a messy, tousled look.

Step 3: Create a side part

Use a comb to create a deep side part in your hair. This will help elongate your face and create a more flattering shape.

Step 4: Style as desired

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add some waves or curls, or leave it straight for a sleek, modern look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making short hair work for a round face:

1. Avoid blunt cuts

A blunt cut can make your face look even rounder. Stick with a layered cut to add some texture and movement to your hair.

2. Embrace asymmetry

An asymmetrical cut can help break up the roundness of your face. Consider a bob that’s longer on one side or a pixie cut with an angled fringe.

3. Try a deep side part

A deep side part can create the illusion of length in your face. Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear short hair if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be a great choice for a round face as long as you choose the right cut and style.

Q: What kind of products should I use for short hair?

A: Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a pomade or wax to create definition and hold.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your style starts to lose its shape. Generally, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Short hair can get oily quickly, so it’s important to wash it regularly. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your hair feeling clean.

2. Use a deep conditioner

Short hair can be prone to dryness, especially if you use heat styling tools. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

3. Protect your hair from heat

If you use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Avoid using high heat settings and try to air dry your hair as much as possible.

4. Get regular trims

Regular trims will help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

In conclusion, a round face can definitely pull off short hair with the right cut and style. Follow these tips and tricks to find the perfect look for you.
