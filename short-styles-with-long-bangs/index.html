Short Styles With Long Bangs A Trendy Look For 2023

Written by Eveline Jun 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a chic and trendy hairdo, then short styles with long bangs may be just what you need. This hairstyle has been popular for years, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about short styles with long bangs, including how to achieve the look, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Content

  • How to Achieve Short Styles with Long Bangs

How to Achieve Short Styles with Long Bangs

Short styles with long bangs are perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish hairdo. Here's how you can achieve the look in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Short Style

The first step in achieving short styles with long bangs is to choose the right short haircut for you. Some popular short styles include the bob, pixie cut, and blunt cut. Make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Add Long Bangs

Once you've chosen your short style, it's time to add long bangs. Long bangs can be side-swept or straight, depending on your preference. Make sure to ask your hairstylist to cut the bangs longer than usual, so they blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Styling short styles with long bangs is quick and easy. You can blow-dry your hair for a sleek look, or add some texture by using a curling iron or texturizing spray. Don't forget to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Styles with Long Bangs

Now that you've achieved the perfect short style with long bangs, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Keep your hair trimmed regularly to maintain the shape of your short style.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to keep your hair looking fresh in between washes.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs About Short Styles with Long Bangs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short styles with long bangs:

Q: Will short styles with long bangs suit my face shape?

A: Short styles with long bangs can suit any face shape, as long as you choose the right style and bang length.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your short style.

Q: Can I still style my hair if I have short styles with long bangs?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair with short styles with long bangs. Experiment with different textures and accessories to find a style that suits you.

Hair Care for Short Styles with Long Bangs

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips for caring for short styles with long bangs:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Protect your hair from UV rays by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, short styles with long bangs are a trendy and low-maintenance hairdo that anyone can rock. Use the tips and tricks in this article to achieve and maintain the perfect short style with long bangs.
