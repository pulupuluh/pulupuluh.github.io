Best Long Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Christine Mar 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, then you know that finding the right haircut can be a challenge. Your face shape is characterized by a wider forehead and a narrower chin, which means you need to find a hairstyle that balances out your features. Fortunately, there are many long haircuts that look great on heart-shaped faces. In this article, we'll take a look at the best long haircuts for heart-shaped faces, as well as some tips for maintaining your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Long Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right haircut for your face shape is to determine what your face shape actually is. To do this, start by pulling your hair back away from your face. Then, take a look in the mirror and determine which of the following categories best describes your face shape:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond

If you have a heart-shaped face, then you'll want to focus on haircuts that soften your features and create balance between your forehead and chin.

Best Long Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

When it comes to long haircuts for heart-shaped faces, there are several options that work well. Here are some of the best:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for heart-shaped faces because they help to create balance between your forehead and chin. By adding layers to your hair, you can soften your features and make your face look more proportional. Plus, long layers are versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for heart-shaped faces. They help to create balance by drawing attention away from your forehead and towards your eyes. Plus, they're easy to maintain and can be styled in many different ways.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great option for heart-shaped faces because they create softness and movement. This look works well with long hair and can be achieved with a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight. Plus, beachy waves are low-maintenance and look great with any outfit.

Straight and Sleek

If you prefer a more polished look, then straight and sleek hair is a great option for heart-shaped faces. This look works well with long hair and can be achieved with a flat iron or by blow-drying your hair straight. Plus, it's low-maintenance and looks great with any outfit.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a long haircut that works well for your heart-shaped face, it's important to know how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.
  4. If you're adding waves or curls, use a curling iron or hot rollers to create your desired look.
  5. If you're straightening your hair, use a flat iron to create a sleek and polished finish.
  6. Finish your look with a hairspray or other styling product to hold your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Once you've found the perfect long haircut for your heart-shaped face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a heart-shaped face. Just make sure to leave some hair down around your face to create balance.

Q: What should I do if I'm not sure which long haircut is right for my face shape?

A: If you're not sure which long haircut is right for your face shape, then it's a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose a haircut that flatters your features and works well with your lifestyle.

Hair Care

When it comes to finding the best long haircuts for heart shaped faces, it's important to choose a style that balances your features and creates a sense of proportion. From long layers to side-swept bangs, there are many options to choose from. Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect long haircut for your heart-shaped face.
