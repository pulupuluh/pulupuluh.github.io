How To Use A Diffuser For Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Apr 12, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have wavy hair, you know that it can be a challenge to style it without making it look frizzy or flat. One way to achieve defined, bouncy waves is by using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair. In this post, we’ll go over the steps on how to use a diffuser for wavy hair, as well as some tips and hair care advice to keep your waves looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Use a Diffuser for Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using a Diffuser
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

How to Use a Diffuser for Wavy Hair

A diffuser is an attachment that goes on the end of your blow dryer and distributes the heat evenly to help enhance your natural waves without causing frizz or damage. Here’s how to use it:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a mousse or styling cream to enhance your waves and add moisture to your hair. Comb through with a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

Step 2: Attach the Diffuser

Attach the diffuser to your blow dryer. Make sure it’s securely attached and the heat settings are on medium to low.

Step 3: Begin Drying Your Hair

Flip your head upside down and place your hair into the diffuser. Hold it in place and turn on the blow dryer. Use a low speed and medium heat to start. Move the diffuser around your head, cupping your hair with it and scrunching your hair up towards your scalp. This will help encourage your natural waves.

Step 4: Finish Drying

Once your hair is about 80% dry, turn off the blow dryer and let your hair air dry the rest of the way. Don’t touch your hair too much while it’s drying, as this can cause frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a more detailed guide on how to use a diffuser for wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start with clean hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for wavy hair to help enhance your natural waves.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a mousse or styling cream to your damp hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Attach the Diffuser

Attach the diffuser to your blow dryer. Most diffusers will fit most blow dryers, but make sure to check the size before purchasing.

Step 4: Begin Drying Your Hair

Flip your head upside down and place your hair into the diffuser. Turn on the blow dryer and use a low speed and medium heat. Move the diffuser around your head, cupping your hair with it and scrunching your hair up towards your scalp.

Step 5: Dry the Back of Your Head

Flip your head back over and work on the back of your head. Use the same technique as before, cupping your hair with the diffuser and scrunching your hair up towards your scalp.

Step 6: Finish Drying

Once your hair is about 80% dry, turn off the blow dryer and let your hair air dry the rest of the way. Don’t touch your hair too much while it’s drying, as this can cause frizz.

Tips for Using a Diffuser

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using a diffuser:

  • Use a low speed and medium heat to start. You can increase the heat and speed if needed, but start slow to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don’t touch your hair too much while it’s drying. This can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser with prongs or fingers to help enhance your natural waves.
  • Don’t use a brush when blow-drying your hair. This can cause your hair to become straight and lose its natural wave.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to help protect your hair from damage.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a diffuser on straight hair?

A: While a diffuser is designed to enhance natural waves, you can use it on straight hair to add volume and texture.

Q: How often should I use a diffuser?

A: It’s best to use a diffuser no more than once a week to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Can I use a diffuser on wet hair?

A: No, you should never use a diffuser on wet hair. Make sure your hair is towel-dried before using a diffuser.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for wavy hair to help keep your waves looking their best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to help add moisture to your hair.
  • Don’t brush your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

Using a diffuser is a great way to enhance your natural waves and add volume to your hair. With these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to achieve defined, bouncy waves every time.
