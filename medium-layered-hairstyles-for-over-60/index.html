Medium Layered Hairstyles For Over 60 A Guide To Flattering Haircuts

Written by Frank Aug 26, 2023 · 9 min read

As we age, our hair may lose some of its thickness, texture, and luster. But that doesn't mean we can't have stylish and flattering haircuts that suit our face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Medium layered hairstyles for over 60 are a great option for women who want to add movement, volume, and dimension to their locks without going too short or too long.

Description

Medium layered hairstyles for over 60 refer to haircuts that fall between the shoulders and the collarbone and feature layers that are cut at different lengths to create a textured and feathered effect. These hairstyles can be customized to suit your hair color, skin tone, and personal taste, and can be worn with or without bangs, depending on your face shape and hairline. Some of the benefits of medium layered hairstyles for over 60 include:

  • Adding volume and body to fine or thin hair
  • Softening the features and creating a youthful look
  • Giving versatility and styling options for different occasions
  • Complementing various hair colors, including gray, blonde, brunette, and red

How to

If you're considering a medium layered hairstyle for over 60, here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Consult with a professional hairstylist who has experience working with mature hair and can suggest the right haircut for your hair type and face shape.
  • Bring pictures of medium layered hairstyles that you like and that match your hair length and texture.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.
  • Invest in quality hair products that nourish and protect your hair, such as shampoo, conditioner, serum, and hairspray.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as blow-drying, curling, straightening, or braiding, to find the ones that suit your hair and personality.

Step by step

If you want to try a medium layered hairstyle for over 60 at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and shine.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently and detangle it with a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray or lotion to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and use a round brush and a hair dryer to create volume and movement.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or a straightener to add texture and definition to your layers.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray or a texturizing spray to hold your style in place and add shine.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you rock your medium layered hairstyle for over 60:

  • Opt for a side part or a center part, depending on your face shape and hairline.
  • Consider adding wispy or choppy bangs to frame your face and soften your features.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, or scarves, to add interest and color to your hair.
  • Ask your hairstylist for a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your layers and prevent split ends.
  • Be confident and embrace your natural beauty, no matter what your age or hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium layered hairstyles for over 60:

Q: Will medium layered hairstyles make me look younger?

A: While a haircut alone cannot reverse the aging process, a well-chosen and well-styled medium layered hairstyle can create a more youthful and vibrant appearance by adding volume, movement, and texture to your hair, and by framing your face in a flattering way.

Q: Can I wear a medium layered hairstyle if I have thin or fine hair?

A: Yes, medium layered hairstyles can actually help to enhance the appearance of thin or fine hair by creating the illusion of thickness and fullness. Just make sure to ask your hairstylist for a haircut that suits your hair type and texture, and that adds volume without weighing down your hair.

Q: Do I need to use special hair products for my medium layered hairstyle?

A: While you don't necessarily need to use expensive or fancy hair products, it's important to choose ones that are formulated for your hair type and that address your specific hair concerns, such as dryness, frizz, or breakage. Look for products that contain natural ingredients, such as argan oil, jojoba oil, or shea butter, and that are free from harsh chemicals, such as sulfates, parabens, or silicones.

Hair Care

To keep your medium layered hairstyle for over 60 looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair no more than 2-3 times a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils and to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair, especially if you color it or use heat styling tools.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat, such as from blow dryers or flat irons, and use a heat protectant spray or lotion when you do.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and pollution by wearing a hat or a scarf, especially if you spend a lot of time outdoors.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein, as this will help to promote healthy hair growth and reduce hair loss.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a medium layered hairstyle for over 60 with confidence and style. Whether you prefer a classic bob, a shaggy lob, or a layered pixie, there's a medium layered hairstyle that can suit your needs and preferences. So why not book a salon appointment today and give your hair a refreshing and flattering update?
