How To Know What Hair Color Looks Good On You

Written by Aprilia Apr 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering changing your hair color, it's important to choose a shade that will complement your skin tone, eye color, and overall appearance. With so many options available, it can be challenging to determine which color will look best on you. This guide will help you determine the best hair color for your skin tone, so you can confidently rock a new look.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining the best hair color for you is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. Here's how to determine which category you fall into:

Cool Skin Tone

If you have a cool skin tone, your skin will have pink, red, or bluish undertones. You might also have blue or green eyes. Your veins on your wrist will appear blue or purple.

Warm Skin Tone

If you have a warm skin tone, your skin will have yellow, peachy, or golden undertones. You might also have brown, amber, or hazel eyes. Your veins on your wrist will appear green or olive.

Neutral Skin Tone

If you have a neutral skin tone, your skin will have a mix of cool and warm undertones. You might have hazel eyes or brown eyes. Your veins on your wrist will appear blue-green.

Choosing the Right Hair Color

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can begin to choose a hair color that will look good on you. Here are some general guidelines:

Cool Skin Tone

If you have a cool skin tone, look for hair colors that have cool undertones. This includes shades of blonde, brown, and black that have blue or ash undertones. Avoid warm colors like golden blondes or coppery browns.

Warm Skin Tone

If you have a warm skin tone, look for hair colors that have warm undertones. This includes shades of blonde, brown, and red that have golden or coppery undertones. Avoid cool colors like ash blondes or black.

Neutral Skin Tone

If you have a neutral skin tone, you can pretty much pull off any hair color. However, you might want to stick with neutral shades like beige blondes or natural browns.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your eye color. If you have blue or green eyes, consider cool hair colors like ash blonde or cocoa brown. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warmer colors like caramel or honey might be more flattering.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you have a low-maintenance lifestyle, choose a hair color that will require minimal upkeep. If you're willing to put in the work, go for a more high-maintenance color.
  • Ask for a professional opinion. If you're still unsure about which hair color to choose, ask your hairstylist for their opinion.

FAQs

What hair color looks good on pale skin?

If you have pale skin, look for hair colors that have cool undertones, like platinum blonde or ash brown. Avoid warm colors like golden blonde or coppery red.

What hair color looks good on dark skin?

If you have dark skin, look for hair colors that have warm undertones, like caramel or chestnut brown. Avoid cool colors like ash blonde or platinum blonde.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it's important to choose a hair dye that is gentle and free of harsh chemicals. Look for natural or organic hair dyes, and do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

If you do decide to dye your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for colored hair:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, which can also cause color to fade.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can choose a hair color that will look amazing on you and keep it looking great for months to come.
