Mens Hair Short Back And Sides A Timeless Look For Men

Written by Frank Mar 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a classic and versatile hairstyle, the mens hair short back and sides is the perfect choice. This style has been popular for decades and continues to be a go-to look for men of all ages. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this timeless hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

How to Achieve the Mens Hair Short Back and Sides

The mens hair short back and sides is a simple but sophisticated hairstyle that involves keeping the hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top slightly longer. This look can be achieved with any hair type, making it a versatile option for men of all ages and hair types.

To achieve the mens hair short back and sides, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who specializes in this type of cut. They will use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair short, while leaving the top longer. The length of the top can be tailored to your individual preferences, but a classic mens hair short back and sides usually has around 2-3 inches of length on top.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Mens Hair Short Back and Sides

1. Choose a reputable barber or stylist who specializes in mens hair short back and sides.

2. Communicate your desired length and style to your barber or stylist.

3. Have your hair washed and towel dried.

4. Your barber or stylist will use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair.

5. Your barber or stylist will use scissors to cut the top of your hair to the desired length.

6. Your hair will be styled with product, if desired.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining Your Mens Hair Short Back and Sides

Once you've achieved the mens hair short back and sides, there are a few tips to keep in mind to maintain and style your hair:

  • Use a high-quality styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups and maintenance.
  • Consider adding a fade or taper to your mens hair short back and sides for a more modern look.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to switch up your look.

Frequently Asked Questions About Mens Hair Short Back and Sides

What face shapes does the mens hair short back and sides suit?

The mens hair short back and sides is a versatile style that suits most face shapes. However, it tends to work particularly well on men with square or oval-shaped faces.

Can I style my mens hair short back and sides without product?

You can certainly style your mens hair short back and sides without product, but it may not hold its shape as well or have as much texture.

What hair types work best with the mens hair short back and sides?

The mens hair short back and sides can be achieved with any hair type, but it tends to work particularly well on straight or wavy hair.

Hair Care Advice for Mens Hair Short Back and Sides

Keeping your mens hair short back and sides looking its best requires proper hair care. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, on your hair too often as this can damage it.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain a stylish and classic mens hair short back and sides. Whether you're looking for a professional or casual look, this style is a timeless choice that will never go out of fashion.
