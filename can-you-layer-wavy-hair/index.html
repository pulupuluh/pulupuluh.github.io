Can You Layer Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks For The Perfect Cut

Written by Petter Mar 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you someone blessed with wavy hair but struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your natural texture? Look no further than layered haircuts. Not only do they add dimension and movement to your locks, but they also work wonders in enhancing your waves. But can you layer wavy hair? The answer is a resounding yes! In this blog post, we’ll walk you through everything you need to know about layering wavy hair.

How to Layer Wavy Hair

Layering wavy hair involves cutting the hair at different lengths to add volume, texture, and dimension. It’s a versatile option that works for all hair types and lengths, whether you have long or short hair. Here’s how to layer wavy hair:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional Hairstylist

The first step to getting a perfect layered cut is finding a professional hairstylist who specializes in cutting wavy hair. They can guide you on the best haircut that complements your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Whether you prefer a subtle or dramatic look, a hairstylist can help you achieve the perfect layered cut.

Step 2: Choose the Right Layering Technique

There are different layering techniques that hairstylists use to create a layered cut. Some of the popular ones include:

  • Long Layers
  • Short Layers
  • Choppy Layers
  • Face-Framing Layers

Each technique works differently for different hair types, so consult with your hairstylist to find the best one for you.

Step 3: Maintain Your Layers

Once you get your layered cut, maintaining your layers is essential to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for wavy hair, and avoid over-styling your locks. Use a diffuser to dry your hair and let it air-dry as much as possible. This will help you maintain your waves and prevent damage.

Tips for Layering Wavy Hair

Layering wavy hair is an art that requires precision and skill. Here are some tips to help you get the perfect layered cut:

  • Choose a hairstylist who specializes in cutting wavy hair
  • Consult with your hairstylist before getting a cut
  • Use pictures to show your hairstylist the type of layered cut you want
  • Go for a layered cut that complements your face shape and hair texture
  • Maintain your layers with regular trims

FAQs About Layering Wavy Hair

Can you layer short wavy hair?

Yes, you can layer short wavy hair. Short layers can add volume and texture to your locks and make them look fuller.

Can you layer thick wavy hair?

Yes, you can layer thick wavy hair. Thick hair can be challenging to manage, but layering can help reduce the volume and make it more manageable.

Can you layer wavy hair if you have a round face?

Yes, you can layer wavy hair if you have a round face. Face-framing layers can work wonders in creating an illusion of a slimmer face. Consult with a professional hairstylist to find the best layering technique for you.

How to Take Care of Layered Wavy Hair

Layered wavy hair requires proper care and maintenance to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you take care of your locks:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for wavy hair
  • Avoid over-styling your hair
  • Use a diffuser to dry your hair
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Maintain your layers with regular trims

Layering wavy hair is a great way to add texture, volume, and movement to your locks. By following these tips and tricks, you can get the perfect layered cut that complements your natural texture and enhances your overall look.
