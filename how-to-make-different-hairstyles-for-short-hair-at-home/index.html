How To Make Different Hairstyles For Short Hair At Home

Written by Bardi Apr 24, 2023 · 7 min read

Short hair can be versatile and easy to style. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a variety of hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to make different hairstyles for short hair at home. Whether you have a pixie cut or a bob, these hairstyles will work for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we get started, here are some tools and products you will need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Small hair elastics
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray
  • Texturizing spray
  • Flat iron or curling iron (optional)

1. The Half-Up Top Knot

This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face but still look stylish. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair.
  2. Tie the top section into a high ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Use a hairspray to keep the bun in place.

2. The Messy Bob

If you have a bob, you can create a messy, textured look with just a few products. Here's how:

  1. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair and work it through with your fingers.
  2. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create loose waves.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. The Faux Hawk

This edgy hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some volume and texture to their short hair. Here's how:

  1. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair.
  2. Using a comb, create a deep side part.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the side with less hair and twist it towards the back of your head.
  4. Secure the twisted section with a bobby pin.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Use your fingers to create volume and texture on the top section of your hair.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Here are more detailed step-by-step instructions on how to make different hairstyles for short hair at home:

The Half-Up Top Knot

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Section off the top half of your hair using a comb.
  3. Tie the top section into a ponytail using a small hair elastic.
  4. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  5. Spray a hairspray to keep the bun in place.

The Messy Bob

  1. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair and work it through with your fingers.
  2. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create loose waves.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

The Faux Hawk

  1. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair.
  2. Using a comb, create a deep side part.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the side with less hair and twist it towards the back of your head.
  4. Secure the twisted section with a bobby pin.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Use your fingers to create volume and texture on the top section of your hair.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using a flat iron or curling iron.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles!

FAQ

Q: Can I make these hairstyles with any length of short hair?

A: These hairstyles are designed for short hair, but some may work better on longer or shorter hair. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Do I need any special tools to make these hairstyles?

A: You will need a few basic tools, such as a wide-tooth comb, small hair elastics, bobby pins, and hairspray. You may also want to use a flat iron or curling iron, but this is optional.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular trims to keep it looking healthy and styled. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and nourished. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray when using a flat iron or curling iron.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create a variety of hairstyles for short hair at home. Have fun experimenting and finding what works best for you!
