How To Keep Short Curly Hair From Frizzing Tips And Techniques

Written by Oliver May 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short curly hair, you know how frustrating it can be to deal with frizz. Frizz can make your hair look unruly and messy, and it can be difficult to style. Fortunately, there are several tips and techniques that you can use to keep your short curly hair from frizzing.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Short Curly Hair from Frizzing
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Products

How to Keep Short Curly Hair from Frizzing

There are several ways to keep your short curly hair from frizzing. The first step is to start with clean, well-conditioned hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it more prone to frizz.

Another key to keeping your short curly hair from frizzing is to avoid over-washing it. Washing your hair too often can dry it out, which can lead to frizz. Try washing your hair every other day or every three days, and use dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil.

When you do wash your hair, be sure to use a deep conditioning treatment. This will help to moisturize your hair and prevent frizz. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated throughout the day.

Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can also help to keep your short curly hair from frizzing. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl defining cream, mousse, or gel. These products can help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Another product that can help to prevent frizz is a hair oil or serum. Apply a small amount of oil or serum to your hair after styling to help keep it smooth and shiny.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to keeping your short curly hair from frizzing:

  1. Start with clean, well-conditioned hair
  2. Avoid over-washing your hair
  3. Use a deep conditioning treatment
  4. Use a leave-in conditioner
  5. Use products specifically designed for curly hair
  6. Apply a hair oil or serum after styling

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for keeping your short curly hair from frizzing:

  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping short curly hair from frizzing:

How often should I wash my hair?

It is best to wash your hair every other day or every three days to prevent drying it out.

What is the best way to dry my hair?

Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to gently squeeze excess water out of your hair, and then let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

How can I prevent frizz while sleeping?

Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz. You can also try braiding your hair or using a satin bonnet or cap.

Hair Care Products

Here are some recommended hair care products for keeping short curly hair from frizzing:

  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie
  • DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler
  • Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Heat & Humidity Gel
  • Moroccanoil Treatment
  • Living Proof No Frizz Nourishing Styling Cream

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your short curly hair looking smooth, shiny, and frizz-free. Experiment with different products and hairstyles to find what works best for your hair, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice.
