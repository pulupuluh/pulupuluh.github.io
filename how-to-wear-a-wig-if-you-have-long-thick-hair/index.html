How To Wear A Wig If You Have Long Thick Hair

Written by Bardi Apr 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have long thick hair, you might be wondering how to wear a wig without it looking bulky or unnatural. The good news is, it's easier than you think! In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for wearing a wig with long thick hair, as well as how to care for your wig and natural hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Wig with Long Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Wear a Wig with Long Thick Hair

The first step to wearing a wig with long thick hair is to prepare your natural hair. This means braiding or twisting your hair into a flat style that won't create lumps or bumps under the wig. You can also use a wig cap to keep your hair in place and provide a smooth surface for the wig.

Next, choose a wig that is designed for long hair. Look for wigs with adjustable straps or combs that can be used to secure the wig in place. You may also want to choose a wig with a lace front, which will give you a natural-looking hairline.

When putting on the wig, start by placing it at the front of your head and pulling it back over your natural hair. Use the adjustable straps or combs to secure the wig in place, making sure it feels comfortable and snug.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to wearing a wig with long thick hair:

  1. Prepare your natural hair by braiding or twisting it into a flat style.
  2. Put on a wig cap to keep your hair in place and provide a smooth surface for the wig.
  3. Place the wig at the front of your head, making sure it's centered.
  4. Slide the wig back over your natural hair, adjusting the straps or combs as needed.
  5. Tuck any stray hairs under the wig to create a natural-looking hairline.
  6. Style the wig as desired, using a wig brush or comb to create a natural-looking finish.

Tips for Wearing a Wig

Here are some tips to help you wear a wig with long thick hair:

  • Choose a wig that is designed for long hair, with adjustable straps or combs for a secure fit.
  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place and provide a smooth surface for the wig.
  • Style the wig as desired, using a wig brush or comb to create a natural-looking finish.
  • Consider using wig glue or tape for extra security.
  • Wash your wig regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep it looking fresh and clean.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a wig with long thick hair:

  • Can I wear a wig if I have long thick hair?
  • What kind of wig should I choose for long hair?
  • How do I prepare my natural hair for wearing a wig?
  • How do I secure the wig in place?
  • How do I care for my wig and natural hair?

Hair Care for Your Wig and Natural Hair

Caring for your wig and natural hair is important to keep both looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to keep it in shape.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig, as this can damage the fibers.
  • Protect your natural hair by wearing a wig cap and avoiding tight hairstyles.
  • Moisturize your natural hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or oil.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear a wig with long thick hair and achieve a natural-looking finish. Remember to take care of both your wig and natural hair for the best results!
