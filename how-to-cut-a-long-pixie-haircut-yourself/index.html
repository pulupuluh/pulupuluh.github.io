How To Cut A Long Pixie Haircut Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of constantly going to the salon to maintain your long pixie haircut? Why not try cutting it yourself? With a few tools and some patience, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut from the comfort of your own home. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to cut a long pixie haircut yourself.

How to Cut a Long Pixie Haircut Yourself

Tools You Will Need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Start by washing and blow-drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Section your hair into four parts. Use hair ties or clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Starting with the back section, use your clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Use a longer guard for the top of your head and a shorter guard for the sides and back.

Step 4: Move on to the side sections and use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Use a longer guard near the top of your head and a shorter guard near your ears.

Step 5: Use your scissors to cut the top section of your hair. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle, following the shape of your head.

Step 6: Blend the sides and top of your hair together by using your scissors to cut at a slight angle.

Step 7: Use your comb to create a parting and style your hair as desired.

Tips:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.
  • Use caution when using clippers near your ears and neck.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring.

FAQ:

Q: How often should I cut my long pixie haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Aim to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Q: Can I color my hair after cutting it myself?

A: Yes, you can color your hair after cutting it yourself. Just make sure you follow the instructions on the hair dye and wait at least 24 hours after cutting your hair before coloring it.

Hair Care:

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.

Now that you know how to cut a long pixie haircut yourself, you can save time and money by doing it at home. With a little practice, you can become an expert at cutting your own hair!
