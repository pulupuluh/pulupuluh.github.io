Ladies Short Hairstyles For Thick Hair 2019

If you have thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect hairstyle that is easy to manage and maintain. Short haircuts are a great option for thick hair as they can help to reduce the weight and volume of your hair while still looking stylish and trendy. In this article, we will explore some of the best ladies short hairstyles for thick hair 2019 and provide you with tips on how to care for your hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Thick Hair

When choosing a short hairstyle for thick hair, it is important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Some short hairstyles may not work for everyone, so it is important to consult with your hair stylist to determine which style will work best for you.

One of the most popular short hairstyles for thick hair in 2019 is the pixie cut. This style is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance haircut that is easy to style and maintain. Another popular short hairstyle for thick hair is the bob. This style can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, and can be worn with or without bangs.

Best Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair in 2019:

  • Pixie Cut
  • Bob
  • Layered Bob
  • Short Shag
  • Asymmetrical Bob

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Thick Hair

Styling short hair for thick hair can be quick and easy with the right tools and techniques. Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect short hairstyle for your thick hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as mousse or gel.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or add curls if desired.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Thick Hair

Maintaining short hair for thick hair can be easy with the right care and styling techniques. Follow these tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the perfect look for your hair.

FAQs about Ladies Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair 2019

Q: Will a short hairstyle work for thick hair?

A: Yes, short hairstyles can work for thick hair. The key is to choose a style that will reduce the weight and volume of your hair, such as a pixie cut or bob.

Q: What is the best way to style short hair for thick hair?

A: The best way to style short hair for thick hair is to use a blow dryer and round brush to add volume and texture. You can also use a flat iron to straighten your hair or add curls if desired.

Q: How often should I trim my hair if I have a short hairstyle?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Follow these hair care tips to maintain your thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from styling products.
  • Avoid using heavy or oily styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, short hairstyles are a great option for women with thick hair. With the right care and styling techniques, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look that is easy to manage and maintain. Consult with your hair stylist to determine which short hairstyle will work best for you and follow these tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
