What Hairstyle Looks Best On Round Faces

Written by Eveline Jul 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Round faces can be tricky when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. You want a style that will flatter your face shape and enhance your features. In this article, we will discuss what hairstyle looks best on round faces and provide tips on how to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Round Faces
  • Tips for Styling Round Faces
  • FAQ about Hairstyles for Round Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for round faces, it's important to know how to determine your face shape. To determine your face shape, follow these easy steps:

  1. Using a tape measure, measure the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Write down each measurement.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine which part of your face is the widest:
    • If your forehead is the widest part of your face, you have a heart-shaped face.
    • If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have an oval-shaped face.
    • If your jawline is the widest part of your face, you have a square-shaped face.
    • If all of your measurements are about the same, you have a round-shaped face.

Now that you know your face shape, let's discuss the best hairstyles for round faces.

Best Hairstyles for Round Faces

When it comes to hairstyles for round faces, the goal is to elongate the face and create angles. Here are some of the best hairstyles for round faces:

Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate the face and create angles. Try adding long layers to your hair to draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create angles and draw attention to your eyes. Avoid straight across bangs, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Bob

A bob can be a great hairstyle for round faces. Try a long bob that hits just below the chin to create angles and elongate the face.

High Ponytail

A high ponytail can help to elongate the face and create angles. Be sure to pull the ponytail tight to create a sleek look.

Tips for Styling Round Faces

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, there are some tips you can follow to help flatter your round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Try adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture.
  • Consider adding highlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are straight and flat, as they can make your face appear rounder.

FAQ about Hairstyles for Round Faces

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a bun if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a bun if you have a round face. Try pulling some pieces of hair out around your face to create angles.

Q: What is the best hair color for round faces?

A: There is no one "best" hair color for round faces. However, adding highlights can help to create depth and dimension, which can be flattering for round faces.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. However, be sure to add volume at the crown of your head to create angles.

Hair Care for Round Faces

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and flatter your round face.
