Mens Short Haircuts For Thinning Hair On Top Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jun 10, 2023 · 8 min read

If you’re a man with thinning hair on top, you might feel like your options for haircuts are limited. But fear not, there are plenty of stylish and easy-to-maintain haircuts that can help you make the most of your thinning hair. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best mens short haircuts for thinning hair on top, and share tips and tricks for making the most of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing a haircut for thinning hair, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want a cut that will help to minimize the appearance of thinning hair on top. This means avoiding styles that are too short on top, as they can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, opt for styles that have a bit more length on top, and that can be styled in a way that adds volume and thickness.

Another thing to consider is your face shape. Different haircuts will flatter different face shapes, so it’s important to choose a style that works well with your particular features. If you’re not sure what style will look best on you, ask your barber or stylist for their input.

Some of the best haircuts for men with thinning hair on top include:

  • The crew cut
  • The buzz cut
  • The textured crop
  • The side part
  • The messy crop

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Once you’ve chosen a style that you like, it’s time to head to the barber shop. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any product buildup and leave your hair feeling clean and fresh.
  2. When you arrive at the barber shop, show your stylist a picture of the style you want. This will give them a clear idea of what you’re looking for.
  3. Your stylist will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair to the desired length.
  4. Next, they’ll focus on the top of your hair. Depending on the style you’ve chosen, they may use scissors or clippers to create the desired shape and length.
  5. If you’re getting a textured crop or messy crop, your stylist may use a razor to add texture and movement to your hair.
  6. Once your hair is cut, your stylist will style it using a blow dryer and styling product. They may also give you tips on how to style your hair at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you’ve got a great haircut, it’s important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for thinning hair. These products can help to nourish and strengthen your hair, making it look thicker and healthier.
  • If you’re using styling products, choose ones that are lightweight and won’t weigh your hair down. Look for products that add volume and texture, like sea salt spray or volumizing mousse.
  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim. This will help to keep your hair looking neat and tidy, and prevent split ends from forming.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it look even thinner.

FAQs

Q: Can I still get a fade if I have thinning hair on top?

A: Yes, you can still get a fade with thinning hair on top. However, you’ll want to make sure that the fade is not too high or too short, as this can make your hair look even thinner. Talk to your barber or stylist about the best fade for your hair type and face shape.

Q: Will coloring my hair help to make it look thicker?

A: Coloring your hair can help to make it look thicker, as it adds dimension and texture. However, it’s important to choose a color that’s close to your natural shade, as this will look the most natural. Avoid going too light or too dark, as this can make your hair look even thinner.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips that can help to keep your hair looking healthy and thick:

  • Eat a healthy diet that’s rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals. These nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these can damage your hair and stunt growth.
  • Get plenty of rest and manage stress levels, as stress can contribute to hair loss.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can damage the follicles and lead to hair loss.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can make the most of your thinning hair and look your best.
