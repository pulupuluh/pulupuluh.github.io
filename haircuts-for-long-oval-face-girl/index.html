Haircuts For Long Oval Face Girl Flattering Styles To Try In 2023

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 · 8 min read

Long oval faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape, as they can carry off almost any hairstyle with ease. However, it’s important to choose a haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. In this blog post, we will share some of the best haircuts for long oval face girl that you can try in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Long Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut for a Long Oval Face
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Oval Face Girls

Description

A long oval face is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with the forehead and jawline being almost the same width. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile, as it can carry off various hairstyles. However, it’s important to choose a haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Long Oval Face

The key to choosing the right haircut for a long oval face is to create balance and add width to the face. The best haircuts for long oval face girl are those that add volume and fullness to the sides of the face, while softening the angles of the face.

Some of the best haircuts for long oval face girls are:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob cuts
  • Wavy or curly hair
  • Pixie cuts

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut for a Long Oval Face

If you’re unsure about which haircut to choose, it’s always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose a haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a haircut for a long oval face:

  1. Consult with a professional hairstylist
  2. Choose a haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty
  3. Ask for layers to add volume and fullness to the sides of the face
  4. Consider adding side-swept bangs to soften the angles of the face
  5. Opt for a bob cut that falls just below the chin
  6. If you have wavy or curly hair, embrace your natural texture
  7. Consider a pixie cut for a bold and edgy look

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve chosen a haircut, it’s important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut
  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools
  • Invest in good quality hair products that are suited for your hair type
  • Don’t wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for long oval face girls:

What is the best haircut for a long oval face?

The best haircuts for long oval face girl are those that add volume and fullness to the sides of the face, while softening the angles of the face. Some of the best haircuts for long oval face girls are long layers, side-swept bangs, bob cuts, wavy or curly hair, and pixie cuts.

Can I pull off a short haircut with a long oval face?

Yes, you can definitely pull off a short haircut with a long oval face. In fact, a pixie cut can look great on a long oval face, as it accentuates the cheekbones and adds definition to the face.

Do I need to have a certain hair type to get a haircut for a long oval face?

No, you don’t need to have a certain hair type to get a haircut for a long oval face. However, certain haircuts may work better for certain hair types. For example, if you have thick hair, a layered haircut may work better than a blunt cut.

Hair Care Tips for Long Oval Face Girls

Here are some hair care tips for long oval face girls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Brush your hair gently to prevent breakage

Conclusion:

Long oval face girls have a lot of options when it comes to haircuts. Whether you prefer long layers, side-swept bangs, or a bold pixie cut, there’s a haircut that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist and take proper care of your hair to maintain your haircut.
