How To Cut Your Own Bangs For Curly Hair

Are you tired of having to go to the salon every time you need a trim? Do you have curly hair and find it challenging to cut your bangs? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut your own bangs for curly hair.

Description

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, and cutting your bangs can be even more challenging. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can cut your bangs at home without any hassle. This article will provide you with all the information you need to know to cut your own bangs for curly hair.

How to

Before you start cutting your bangs, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. Gather all the necessary tools, which include a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Make sure that you are cutting your bangs in a well-lit room.

Step by Step

1. Start by sectioning your hair. Clip the rest of your hair away from your face, leaving only your bangs out.

2. Comb your bangs straight down and divide them into three sections. The middle section should be the thickest, and the side sections should be thinner.

3. Take the middle section of your bangs and comb them straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle finger and cut it straight across. Make sure that you are cutting the hair in a straight line.

4. Move on to the side sections of your bangs. Comb them straight down and hold them between your index and middle finger. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle section of your bangs and moving towards the outside. The angle should be slight, and you should be careful not to cut too much hair.

5. Comb your bangs again and make any necessary adjustments. If you feel that your bangs are too long, you can always trim them a little more.

Tips

1. It's always better to cut your bangs when they are dry. Wet hair tends to shrink, and you may end up cutting your bangs too short.

2. Always cut your bangs a little longer than you want them to be. You can always trim them if they are too long, but you can't add hair if they are too short.

3. Use sharp scissors to cut your bangs. Dull scissors can damage your hair and cause split ends.

FAQ

Q. How often should I cut my bangs?
A. It depends on how fast your hair grows. If your hair grows quickly, you may need to trim your bangs every two to three weeks. If your hair grows slowly, you can wait up to six weeks before trimming your bangs.

Q. What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my bangs?
A. Don't panic. You can always go to a salon and have a professional fix your bangs. Alternatively, you can try styling your hair differently to hide the mistake.

Hair Care

After cutting your bangs, make sure that you take good care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends. If you need to use heat styling tools, make sure that you use a heat protectant spray.

Cutting your own bangs can be a little intimidating, especially if you have curly hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut at home. Follow the steps outlined in this article, and you will have perfect bangs in no time.
