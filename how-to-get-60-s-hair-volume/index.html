How To Get 60 S Hair Volume

Written by Aprilia Mar 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a fan of the voluminous hairdos of the 60s, you know that achieving that level of volume can be tricky. But fear not, because we've got you covered with this step-by-step guide on how to get 60's hair volume. From the right products to the perfect styling techniques, we'll show you how to get that retro look without sacrificing your modern style.

Description

The 60s was an era of big hair, and that's exactly what we're going to help you achieve. Whether you're going for a classic bouffant or a modern take on the 60s look, this guide will give you the tools you need to add volume to your hair.

How To

Getting 60s hair volume starts with the right products. Here's what you'll need:

  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Hair mousse
  • Hair spray
  • Teasing comb
  • Round brush

Once you have your products ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume to your hair from the very beginning.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Apply a golf ball-sized amount of hair mousse to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will help give your hair the volume it needs to start styling.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair starting at the roots and pulling the brush through to the ends. This will help lift your hair and add volume.

Step 4: Tease

Using a teasing comb, backcomb your hair at the crown. This will help create a lifted look and add even more volume.

Step 5: Style

With your hair teased and volumized, it's time to style it into the 60s look of your choice. Whether you're going for a bouffant or a beehive, use your teasing comb and hairspray to perfect your look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your 60s hair volume:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair from the very beginning.
  • Apply hair mousse to your roots for extra lift and volume.
  • Use a round brush while blow drying your hair to add lift and volume.
  • Tease your hair at the crown for a lifted look.
  • Use hairspray to hold your style in place.

FAQ

What if I have fine hair?

If you have fine hair, you can still achieve 60s hair volume. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a small amount of hair mousse to your roots. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, and tease your hair at the crown for added lift. Use hairspray to hold your style in place.

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, you can still achieve 60s hair volume. Follow the same steps as above, but be sure to blow dry your hair with a diffuser attachment. This will help enhance your curls while still adding volume.

How can I make my 60s hair volume last all day?

To make your 60s hair volume last all day, be sure to use a strong hold hairspray. You can also touch up your style throughout the day with a little bit of hairspray and teasing.

Hair Care

While achieving 60s hair volume is a fun way to change up your look, it's important to take care of your hair. Be sure to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. And always use heat protectant spray when blow drying or styling your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect 60s hair volume in no time. Whether you're going for a classic look or a modern twist, you'll have the volume you need to make a statement.
