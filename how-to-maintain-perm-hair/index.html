How To Maintain Perm Hair Tips And Tricks

Perm hair is a great way to add some extra volume and texture to your hair. However, maintaining a perm can be a bit tricky. In this article, we will give you some tips and tricks on how to maintain perm hair.

  • Step by Step Guide

How to Maintain Perm Hair

Perm hair requires a bit of extra care and attention, but it's worth it for the added volume and texture. Here are some tips on how to maintain perm hair:

1. Use a Mild Shampoo

When washing your hair, use a mild shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are specifically designed for use on permed hair.

2. Condition Your Hair Regularly

Permed hair can be prone to dryness, so it's important to condition your hair regularly. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Don't Over-Brush Your Hair

Brushing your hair too much can cause damage and breakage, especially when it's permed. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair instead of a brush.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners can be damaging to permed hair. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

5. Don't Wash Your Hair Too Often

Washing your hair too often can cause your perm to lose its shape. Try to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to maintain perm hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Use a mild shampoo to wash your hair, being careful not to rub your hair too vigorously.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair, focusing on the ends. Let the conditioner sit in your hair for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 4: Air Dry Your Hair

Avoid using a hair dryer if possible, as it can be damaging to permed hair. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for maintaining perm hair:

1. Don't Use Hair Products that Contain Alcohol

Hair products that contain alcohol can be drying to permed hair. Look for products that are alcohol-free.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to permed hair, so be sure to wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF when you're spending time outdoors.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get a perm?

A: It's best to wait at least six months before getting another perm.

Q: Can I color my permed hair?

A: Yes, you can color your permed hair. However, it's important to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help keep your hair healthy and strong.

2. Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for overall health, including the health of your hair.

3. Reduce Stress

Stress can contribute to hair loss and other hair problems, so it's important to find ways to reduce stress in your life.

By following these tips and tricks, you can maintain your perm hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
