How To Cut Front Hair Bangs At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 30, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of paying for salon visits just to get your front hair bangs trimmed? Well, we have good news for you! With a few tools and the right technique, you can easily cut your front hair bangs at home. In this post, we’ll guide you through the process, answering some common questions and providing tips for a successful cut. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Front Hair Bangs at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Successful Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Bangs

How to Cut Front Hair Bangs at Home

Cutting front hair bangs at home is easier than you might think. Here are the tools you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle (optional)

Once you have these tools, follow these basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangular shape.
  3. Clip the remaining hair back and out of the way.
  4. Comb the section of hair you want to cut straight down.
  5. Hold the hair between your fingers where you want to make the cut. Be sure to hold it tightly.
  6. Cut the hair straight across using your sharp scissors. Be sure to cut small sections at a time.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 until you’ve cut all the hair in the section.
  8. Release the clipped hair and check for any uneven spots. If you notice any, repeat the cutting process.

Tips for a Successful Cut

Follow these tips for a successful cut:

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut slowly. You can always take more off, but you can’t put it back on!
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can cause split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Don’t cut your hair when it’s wet. Wet hair can stretch, which can result in a shorter cut than you intended.
  • Be patient. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, especially if you’re new to it. Take your time and don’t rush.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting front hair bangs:

  • How often should I cut my bangs? It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to bother you. Some people need to trim their bangs every two weeks, while others can go a month or more between cuts.
  • Can I cut my bangs if I have curly hair? Yes, you can! Just be sure to cut them when your hair is dry, so you can see how they’ll look.
  • What if I make a mistake? Don’t panic! If you make a mistake, you can always clip your hair back and start over. Or, you can visit a salon and have a professional fix the mistake.

Hair Care After Cutting Bangs

After you’ve cut your front hair bangs, be sure to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Style them while they’re wet for the best results.
  • Use a round brush to blow dry them if you want a more polished look.
  • Avoid touching them too much, as oils from your hands can make them look greasy.
  • Use dry shampoo to extend the time between washes.

Cutting front hair bangs at home is a great way to save money and achieve the look you want. Just be sure to follow our step-by-step guide, use sharp scissors, and be patient. Good luck!
