Short Spiky Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair A Trendy And Edgy Look

Written by Luffy Jun 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you may have faced difficulty in finding the perfect haircut that suits your hair texture. But, worry not! Short spiky haircuts for thick wavy hair are the way to go. This style not only adds a trendy and edgy look to your personality but also helps to manage your thick waves. In this article, we will guide you on how to get the perfect short spiky haircut for your thick wavy hair. So, let's get started!

Description

Short spiky haircuts for thick wavy hair are a popular trend that has been around for a while. This style is perfect for those who have thick and coarse hair, making it difficult to manage. It is a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling, making it ideal for busy individuals. The short spiky haircuts are versatile and can be customized according to your preferences. You can choose to have a messy or a neat look, depending on your style.

How to Get the Perfect Short Spiky Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair

Getting the perfect short spiky haircut for thick wavy hair requires some effort and attention. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short spiky haircut for your thick wavy hair. They will assess your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle to provide you with the best options.

Step 2: Choose the right haircut

Choose the right short spiky haircut for your thick wavy hair. Some popular options include the classic short spiky haircut, the textured spiky haircut, and the messy spiky haircut.

Step 3: Style your hair

Style your hair according to your preferences. You can use a hair gel or wax to create the spiky look. Use your fingers to create the spikes and avoid using a comb.

Tips for Maintaining Short Spiky Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short spiky haircut for thick wavy hair:

Tip 1: Use the right products

Use the right hair products that are specifically designed for thick and wavy hair. This will help to manage your hair better and prevent frizz.

Tip 2: Regular trimming

Regularly trim your hair to maintain the short spiky look. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Tip 3: Avoid washing your hair too often

Avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and frizzy. Instead, wash your hair every 2-3 days.

FAQs

Q. Will short spiky haircuts work for all face shapes?

A. Yes, short spiky haircuts can work for all face shapes. Your hairstylist will help you choose the right haircut based on your face shape.

Q. Can I style my short spiky haircut without using products?

A. Yes, you can style your short spiky haircut without using products, but this may not give you the desired look.

Q. Can I color my short spiky haircut?

A. Yes, you can color your short spiky haircut. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best color for your hair.

Hair Care for Short Spiky Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short spiky haircut for thick wavy hair:

Tip 1: Brush your hair regularly

Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots. Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush specifically designed for thick and wavy hair.

Tip 2: Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy. This will prevent dryness and breakage.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from heat

Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as this can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

In conclusion, short spiky haircuts for thick wavy hair are a trendy and edgy look that can be customized according to your preferences. Follow the steps mentioned above to get the perfect short spiky haircut for your thick wavy hair, and use the tips and FAQs to maintain and care for your hair. With the right care and attention, you can rock this look with confidence!
