Is Curly Hair Trendy 2021

Written by Oliver May 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair has always been a fashion statement, and in 2021, it has become more popular than ever. With the rise of social media and influencers, curly hair has become a trend that everyone wants to follow. But what makes curly hair so popular? And how can you achieve those perfect curls? In this article, we will answer all your questions about curly hair in 2021.

Description

Curly hair has been a trend for a long time, but in 2021 it has become even more popular. People are embracing their natural curls and trying new styles to enhance their look. With so many products and techniques available, it is easier than ever to achieve those perfect curls. From Hollywood celebrities to social media influencers, everyone is sporting curly hair this year.

How to Get Curly Hair

If you have naturally curly hair, then you are lucky. All you need is to find the right products that suit your hair type, and you are good to go. But if you have straight hair, then you can achieve curls by using various techniques such as curling irons, rollers, and braids. You can also get a perm to make your hair permanently curly. However, getting a perm can damage your hair, so it is advisable to consult a professional before making the decision.

Step by Step Guide to Get Curly Hair

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid damage to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product such as a mousse or gel to your damp hair. This will help to define your curls and hold them in place.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

If you want tighter curls, then use a curling iron to define your curls. Make sure to use a heat protectant before using the curling iron to avoid damage to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a hairspray to hold your curls in place for a longer time.

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep your curly hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can dry out your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as it can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQ

Q1. Can I get curly hair if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can get curly hair if you have straight hair. You can use various techniques such as curling irons, rollers, and braids to achieve curls. You can also get a perm to make your hair permanently curly.

Q2. How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair type. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle.

Q3. How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

You can prevent frizz in your curly hair by using a sulfate-free shampoo, applying a leave-in conditioner, and using a diffuser attachment while blow drying your hair. You can also use a serum or oil to tame frizz.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Use a diffuser attachment while blow drying your hair
  • Use a curl enhancing product to define your curls
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

Curly hair is trendy in 2021, and it's here to stay. Whether you have naturally curly hair or want to achieve those perfect curls, there are various techniques and products available to help you. Follow the above tips and tricks to keep your curly hair healthy and shiny.
