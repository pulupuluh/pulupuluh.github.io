What Does A 6 Guard Haircut Look Like A Guide For Men

Written by Petter Sep 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you curious about what a 6 guard haircut looks like? If so, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll explain everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle for men. From how to get the cut to how to style it, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

A 6 guard haircut is a type of buzz cut that leaves hair 3/4 inch (19mm) in length. It's a versatile style that suits many face shapes and hair types. The cut is achieved using a clipper guard labeled with the number 6. The guard is attached to the clippers and the hair is cut evenly all over the head.

How to

Getting a 6 guard haircut is relatively easy, but it's best to go to a professional barber for the best results. Here's what you can expect during the haircut:

Step 1:

The barber will begin by washing your hair and trimming any longer sections with scissors. They will then attach the number 6 guard to the clippers and start cutting your hair evenly all over your head.

Step 2:

Once the hair is cut, the barber may trim the edges and neckline with scissors or a razor for a cleaner look. They may also use a smaller guard to trim around the ears and hairline.

Step 3:

Finally, the barber will style your hair according to your preferences. You can ask for a textured, messy look or a more polished, clean-cut style.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're considering a 6 guard haircut:

  • Make sure you choose a reputable barber who has experience with this type of cut.
  • Bring a photo of the style you want to show the barber for reference.
  • Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing this style.
  • Use a good quality hair product to style your hair and keep it in place.

FAQ

Q: Is a 6 guard haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a 6 guard haircut can work for most hair types, but it may not be suitable for very curly or thick hair.

Q: How often do I need to get a 6 guard haircut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows, but typically every 4-6 weeks is recommended to maintain the length and style.

Q: Can I style my 6 guard haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your hair in different ways depending on your preference. You can use a hair product to create a messy, textured look, or comb it for a cleaner, more polished style.

Hair Care

To keep your 6 guard haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or flat irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and style to keep your hair in place.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Conclusion

Now that you know what a 6 guard haircut looks like, you can decide if it's the right style for you. Remember to choose a reputable barber, consider your hair type and face shape, and follow proper hair care to keep your hair looking and feeling its best. Whether you prefer a messy, textured look or a clean-cut style, a 6 guard haircut is a versatile and easy-to-maintain option for men of all ages.
