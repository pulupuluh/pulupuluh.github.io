Men s Fade Haircut Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi May 26, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you a man with curly hair and looking for a stylish haircut? Look no further than the men's fade haircut. This versatile cut works well on all hair types, including curly hair. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about getting the perfect men's fade haircut for your curly locks. From the description and how-to to step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQ, and hair care, we've got you covered.

Description

The men's fade haircut is a popular and stylish cut that involves gradually trimming the hair shorter towards the neck. It creates a clean and polished look, and works well on all hair types, including curly hair. The fade can be high, medium, or low, depending on your preference, and can be paired with various hair lengths on top. With curly hair, a fade can help to define your curls and create a more structured look.

How-To

The first step in getting a men's fade haircut for curly hair is to find a skilled barber who can work with your hair type. Once you've found the right barber, follow these steps:

  1. Decide on the length you want for the hair on top. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face.
  2. Choose the fade length you want. A high fade will have a greater contrast between the long hair on top and the short hair on the sides, while a low fade will have a more gradual transition.
  3. Ask your barber to use scissors or clippers to cut the hair on top to the desired length.
  4. Ask your barber to start the fade at the desired length on the sides and blend it into the longer hair on top.
  5. Ask your barber to use a comb and scissors to shape and blend the hair on top with the fade.
  6. Finish with styling products to define your curls and add volume.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step breakdown of how to get a men's fade haircut for curly hair:

Step 1: Find a Skilled Barber

The first step is to find a skilled barber who can work with your curly hair. Look for someone with experience cutting curly hair and who can offer advice on the best style for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for the hair on top. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Use pictures for inspiration and show them to your barber.

Step 3: Choose the Fade Length

Choose the fade length you want. A high fade will have a greater contrast between the long hair on top and the short hair on the sides, while a low fade will have a more gradual transition. Discuss your preferences with your barber so they can create the perfect fade.

Step 4: Cut the Hair on Top

Ask your barber to use scissors or clippers to cut the hair on top to the desired length. They may also use thinning shears to remove bulk and create a more textured look.

Step 5: Start the Fade

Ask your barber to start the fade at the desired length on the sides and blend it into the longer hair on top. They may use clippers or scissors to create the fade, depending on your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

Step 6: Shape and Blend

Ask your barber to use a comb and scissors to shape and blend the hair on top with the fade. They may use texturizing techniques to create a more natural look and enhance your curls.

Step 7: Style

Finish with styling products to define your curls and add volume. Use a curl-enhancing product and apply it evenly throughout your hair. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to create more defined curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a men's fade haircut for curly hair:

  • Choose a barber with experience cutting curly hair.
  • Bring pictures for inspiration and to show your barber what you want.
  • Discuss your preferences for the length and fade with your barber.
  • Use styling products to enhance your curls and add volume.
  • Try a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer for more defined curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's fade haircuts for curly hair:

What is a fade?

A fade is a haircut that involves gradually trimming the hair shorter towards the neck. It creates a clean and polished look.

What is the best length for curly hair on top?

The best length for curly hair on top depends on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Talk to your barber to find the right length for you.

What is the difference between a high fade and a low fade?

A high fade will have a greater contrast between the long hair on top and the short hair on the sides, while a low fade will have a more gradual transition.

Hair Care

To keep your men's fade haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls healthy and hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf, as UV rays can cause damage and dryness.

With this complete guide to men's fade haircuts for curly hair, you'll be able to get the perfect cut for your locks. Remember to find a skilled barber, discuss your preferences, and use styling products to enhance your curls. With proper hair care, your fade will look great for weeks to come.
