How To Start Freeform Dreads A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Have you ever considered trying out freeform dreads? Freeform dreads are a popular hairstyle that does not require much maintenance, making it perfect for those who have busy schedules. In this article, we will guide you through the process of starting freeform dreads from scratch.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Start Freeform Dreads
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Starting Freeform Dreads
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Freeform Dreads

Description

Freeform dreads are a hairstyle that involves letting your hair naturally clump together to form dreadlocks. Unlike traditional dreadlocks, freeform dreads do not require much maintenance, making them a popular choice for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle. This hairstyle is perfect for those who have busy schedules and do not have much time to spend on their hair.

How to Start Freeform Dreads

Starting freeform dreads is a simple process that involves letting your hair naturally clump together. Follow these steps to start your journey towards freeform dreads:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Stop using shampoo and conditioner in your hair. This will help your hair to naturally clump together and form dreadlocks.

Step 2: Stop brushing or combing your hair. This will help your hair to naturally tangle and form dreadlocks.

Step 3: Allow your hair to grow for at least three to six months without any maintenance. This will give your hair enough time to form dreadlocks.

Step 4: Once your hair has formed dreadlocks, you can start separating them to make them more defined. Use your fingers to separate the clumps of hair and create individual dreadlocks.

Step 5: Continue to let your hair grow and separate the dreadlocks as needed. Over time, your dreadlocks will become more defined and easier to manage.

Tips for Starting Freeform Dreads

Starting freeform dreads can be an exciting journey, but it is important to remember that this hairstyle requires patience and dedication. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Be patient. It takes time for your hair to naturally clump together and form dreadlocks.
  • Avoid using any products in your hair, including wax or gel.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Be gentle when separating your dreadlocks to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Consider using a natural oil, such as coconut oil, to moisturize your hair and scalp.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I start freeform dreads if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can start freeform dreads with short hair. However, it may take longer for your hair to clump together and form dreadlocks.

Q: Can I still wash my hair while starting freeform dreads?

A: Yes, you should still wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

Q: Can I use hair products, such as wax or gel, while starting freeform dreads?

A: No, it is best to avoid using any products in your hair while starting freeform dreads. This will allow your hair to naturally clump together and form dreadlocks.

Hair Care for Freeform Dreads

Once you have started your freeform dreads, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and keep it clean.
  • Use a natural oil, such as coconut oil, to moisturize your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid using any products in your hair, as this can cause buildup and damage your dreadlocks.
  • Be gentle when separating your dreadlocks to avoid damaging your hair.

Starting freeform dreads is a great way to change up your hairstyle and embrace a low-maintenance look. With patience and dedication, you can achieve beautiful and natural dreadlocks that are unique to you. Follow these steps and tips to start your journey towards freeform dreads today!
