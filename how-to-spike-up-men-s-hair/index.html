How To Spike Up Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you want to add some edge to your hairstyle? Spiking up your hair is a great way to achieve a bold and confident look. In this guide, we'll show you how to spike up men's hair in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Spike Up Men's Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Spiking Up Men's Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips for Spiked Hair

How to Spike Up Men's Hair

Spiking up your hair requires two main things: the right type of hair product and the right technique. Here are the steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose the right hair product for your hair type. Pomade, wax, or clay are good options for spiking up hair.
  3. Take a small amount of product and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  4. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  5. Use your fingers or a comb to create the desired spiky look. You can spike all of your hair or just certain sections.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Spiking Up Men's Hair

To achieve the perfect spiky hairstyle, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Less is more when it comes to hair product. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, use a lightweight product to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • For thick or curly hair, use a strong-hold product to keep the spikes in place.
  • Don't be afraid to try different styles and techniques to find the one that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does spiky hair last?

The duration of your spiky hairstyle largely depends on the type of hair product you use. Some products provide a stronger hold than others, which means your style will last longer. On average, spiky hair can last anywhere from a few hours to a full day.

Can you spike up short hair?

Yes, you can spike up short hair. However, it may be more difficult to achieve a dramatic spiky look with shorter hair.

How do you wash out spiky hair product?

To wash out spiky hair product, use a clarifying shampoo that is designed to remove buildup from hair. Make sure to thoroughly rinse your hair to avoid any leftover product.

Hair Care Tips for Spiked Hair

To keep your spiky hairstyle looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent product buildup.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your spiky style.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to rock a spiky hairstyle with confidence. Experiment with different products and styles to find the look that works best for you.
