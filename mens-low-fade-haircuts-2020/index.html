Mens Low Fade Haircuts 2020 A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Low fade haircuts for men have been trending for a while now and are still popular in 2020. This hairstyle is characterized by a gradual tapering of the hair towards the nape of the neck, resulting in a clean and modern look. In this article, we will take a closer look at mens low fade haircuts 2020, including how to get the style, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

The low fade haircut is a classic style that has been around for a long time. It is a versatile hairstyle that works well with different hair types and lengths. The key feature of this haircut is the gradual tapering of the hair towards the nape of the neck. This creates a clean, sharp look that is perfect for any occasion. The low fade can be combined with different haircuts, such as a buzz cut, crew cut, or pompadour, to create a unique look that suits your face shape and personal style.

How to Get the Style

If you want to get a low fade haircut, you should go to a professional stylist who specializes in men's haircuts. The stylist will start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head using clippers. They will gradually increase the guard size to create the fade effect. The hair on top of your head should be longer than the hair on the sides and back, and it can be styled in different ways depending on your preference. You can ask your stylist for advice on which style would suit you best.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Getting a low fade haircut can be done in a few simple steps:

  1. Choose the right length: Before you start, decide on the length of hair you want on the top of your head and the sides and back. This will help your stylist to create the perfect fade.
  2. Wash your hair: Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly before your appointment.
  3. Consult with your stylist: Talk to your stylist about the style you want and any concerns you may have.
  4. Clipper cut the hair: The stylist will start by clipper cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head, gradually increasing the guard size to create the fade effect.
  5. Blend the hair: The stylist will blend the hair on the top of your head with the faded sides and back, creating a seamless look.
  6. Style your hair: Style your hair on top in the way that you prefer.
  7. Finish with product: Finish off your look by applying some hair product to keep your hair in place.

Remember to ask your stylist for advice on how to maintain your new hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a low fade haircut:

  • Make sure you choose the right length for your hair type and face shape.
  • Find a professional stylist who specializes in men's haircuts.
  • Take care of your hair by washing and conditioning it regularly.
  • Use hair product to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about low fade haircuts:

  1. Is a low fade suitable for all hair types?

    2. Yes, a low fade works well with different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

  2. How often should I get a low fade haircut?

    3. You should get a low fade haircut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the look.

  3. Can I style my hair differently with a low fade?

    4. Yes, you can style your hair on top in different ways, such as a quiff, pompadour, or slick back.

  4. Do I need to use hair product with a low fade?

    5. Using hair product can help keep your hair in place and maintain the style.

  5. How can I maintain my low fade haircut?

    6. You can maintain your low fade haircut by washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using hair product, and getting regular trims.

Hair Care

To keep your low fade haircut looking fresh and stylish, it is essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use hair product to keep your hair in place and add texture.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your low fade haircut.

In conclusion, mens low fade haircuts 2020 are a versatile and stylish option for men who want a modern and clean look. By following these tips and recommendations, you can achieve the perfect low fade haircut for your face shape and hair type. Remember to consult with a professional stylist for the best results.
