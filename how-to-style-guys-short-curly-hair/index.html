How To Style Guys Short Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Aug 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a man with short curly hair who is struggling to find the right hairstyle that suits you? Look no further! This article will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style your short curly hair and give you some tips on how to maintain your curls.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Guys Short Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

How to Style Guys Short Curly Hair

Styling short curly hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques, it can be effortless. The first step is to determine your hair type and texture. This will help you choose the right products that will work best for your hair.

Short curly hair can be styled in various ways, including a messy look, slicked-back look, or a textured look. The key is to find a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your short curly hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Pat your hair dry with a towel, but do not rub it vigorously as it can damage your curls.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it hydrated and prevent frizz.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  5. Apply a styling product such as a curl cream, gel, or mousse to your hair.
  6. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and enhance your curls.
  7. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to prevent frizz and maintain your curls' shape.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray or serum to hold your curls and add shine.

Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your short curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using a hairbrush as it can cause frizz and damage your curls.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from tangling and breaking.
  • Invest in a good quality diffuser attachment for your hairdryer to help maintain your curls' shape.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup and keep your curls bouncy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short curly hair:

  • Q: Can I straighten my short curly hair?
  • A: Yes, but it is not recommended as it can damage your curls and cause breakage.
  • Q: Can I use a hairbrush on my short curly hair?
  • A: It is not recommended as it can cause frizz and damage your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers instead.
  • Q: How often should I wash my short curly hair?
  • A: It depends on your hair type and texture. Generally, it is recommended to wash your hair once or twice a week to prevent dryness and breakage.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your short curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair as it can cause dryness and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your curls' shape.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle for your short curly hair. Remember to take good care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
