How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers By Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Mar 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts every month? Do you want to learn how to cut men's hair with clippers by yourself? Look no further! In this guide, we'll provide you with everything you need to know to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Read on for tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions.

Description

Cutting men's hair with clippers can seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it's actually quite simple. Not only can you save money by cutting your own hair, but you also have the freedom to experiment with different styles and lengths.

How to

Before you get started, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a towel or cape to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

Step 1: Choose Your Clipper Guard Size

Clipper guards come in different sizes, which determine the length of the cut. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you achieve your desired length. It's always better to start longer and cut more if needed, rather than starting too short and having to wait for hair to grow back.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be harder to cut and may cause the clipper blades to rust. Use a towel to dry your hair thoroughly.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to remove any tangles and make sure your hair is evenly distributed. This will help you achieve a more even cut.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start at the back of your head and work your way up. Use the clippers to trim the hair in a straight line, moving from the bottom to the top. Be sure to use a mirror to check your progress and make any necessary adjustments.

Step 5: Use Scissors for Detailing

Once you've finished using the clippers, switch to scissors to trim any stray hairs or make any necessary adjustments. Use your fingers to hold the hair upright and trim it to your desired length.

Tips

- Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down until you achieve your desired length.

- Use a mirror to check your progress and make any necessary adjustments.

- Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or make any necessary adjustments.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can! Cutting your own hair with clippers is a great way to save money and experiment with different styles.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair with clippers?

You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a towel or cape to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

Can I use clippers to cut long hair?

Clippers are best used for shorter hairstyles. If you have long hair, it's best to use scissors to avoid uneven cuts.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, be sure to wash it to remove any loose hairs. You can also use a hairbrush or comb to remove any remaining hairs. If you notice any irritation or redness, apply a soothing cream or lotion to the affected area.

Cutting men's hair with clippers by yourself is a great way to save money and achieve a professional-looking haircut. With the right tools and techniques, you can master the art of hair cutting in no time. Happy cutting!
