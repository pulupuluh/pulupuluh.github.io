How To Cut Your Hair In Short Layers Yourself

Written by Daniel Jun 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are looking for a new hairstyle, you might want to consider cutting your hair in short layers. Short layers can add volume and texture to your hair, giving it a fresh and modern look. The best part? You can do it yourself! In this article, we'll go over how to cut your hair in short layers step by step. Let's get started!

How to Cut Your Hair in Short Layers: Step by Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step is to gather all the tools you'll need to cut your hair. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to see the different layers and textures in your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into sections. You can do this by parting your hair down the middle and then dividing it into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting with the front sections, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle, creating a layered effect. Repeat this process for each section, working your way towards the back of your head.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all the sections, it's time to blend the layers. Take a comb and run it through your hair, blending the shorter layers with the longer ones. This will give your hair a natural and effortless look.

Step 6: Trim the Ends

Finally, trim the ends of your hair to give it a polished look. Make sure to cut straight across and avoid cutting too much off.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair in Short Layers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair in short layers:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean and even cut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it can result in an uneven cut.
  • Take breaks and step back to check your progress.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair in short layers if it's curly?

Yes, you can! However, it's important to cut curly hair when it's dry to avoid cutting too much off and causing frizz. Use a diffuser to dry your hair and then follow the steps above.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't worry! Hair grows back and mistakes can be fixed. If you make a mistake, try to blend it in with the rest of your hair or visit a professional stylist to fix it.

Hair Care After Cutting in Short Layers

Now that you've cut your hair in short layers, it's important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type and apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently and always use a heat protectant spray before styling.

Now that you know how to cut your hair in short layers yourself, you can create a new and fresh look anytime you want. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
