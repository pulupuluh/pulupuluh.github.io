How To Cut Hair Short Sides Long Top A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile hairstyle, the short sides long top haircut is a popular choice. This style can be worn by both men and women and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair textures. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut hair short sides long top and provide tips on how to care for your new haircut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Hair Short Sides Long Top
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Sides Long Top Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The short sides long top haircut is characterized by short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This style can be achieved with a variety of haircuts, including the undercut, fade, and tapered cut. The cut is versatile and can be styled in many ways, including slicked back, messy, and textured.

How to Cut Hair Short Sides Long Top

To get a short sides long top haircut, you will need to visit a professional stylist. However, if you have some experience with cutting hair, you can attempt to do it yourself. Here's how:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Use a comb to section off the top of your hair. The top section should be about 2-3 inches long.
  3. Clip the top section out of the way.
  4. Use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back of your hair short. The length of the sides will depend on your preference, but a good starting point is a #2 or #3 guard.
  5. Blend the sides and back into the top section using scissors or clippers.
  6. Take down the top section and comb it straight up.
  7. Use scissors to trim the top section to your desired length. You can go shorter or longer depending on your preference.
  8. Blend the top section into the sides and back using scissors or clippers.
  9. Use scissors to add texture and layers to the top section for a more natural look.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Styling Short Sides Long Top Haircut

Now that you have your short sides long top haircut, here are some tips for styling it:

  • Use a matte finish styling product for a more natural look.
  • Slick back the top section for a classic look.
  • Messy the top section for a more casual and relaxed look.
  • Use a comb to create a side part for a more polished look.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs

What face shapes work well with short sides long top haircut?

The short sides long top haircut works well with most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on those with square, oval, or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I get my short sides long top haircut trimmed?

It's best to get your short sides long top haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Can I still style my hair if I have a receding hairline?

Yes, you can still style your hair with a short sides long top haircut if you have a receding hairline. Just make sure to keep the sides and back short to balance out the longer top section.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your short sides long top haircut looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Cutting your hair short on the sides and long on the top can be a stylish and versatile haircut. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this trendy look at home or at the salon. Follow our guide and tips to get the perfect short sides long top haircut for you.
