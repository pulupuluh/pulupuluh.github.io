Which Hair Style Suit My Face

Written by William Apr 24, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Choosing the right hair style can be challenging, especially if you are not sure which hair style suits your face shape. Your hair is an essential part of your appearance, and the right hair style can enhance your looks and boost your confidence. In this post, we will discuss how to determine which hair style suits your face shape and some popular hair styles that you can try.

How to determine your face shape?

Before choosing a hair style, you need to determine your face shape. To do this, you need to measure your face's length and width and compare them to the following face shapes:

  • Round face
  • Oval face
  • Square face
  • Heart-shaped face
  • Diamond-shaped face

You can measure your face using a tape measure or ruler. Measure the length of your face from the hairline to the chin and the width of your face at the widest point. Once you have your measurements, compare them to the face shapes above.

Round face

If you have a round face, your face length and width are almost equal, and your cheeks are the widest part of your face. The best hair styles for round faces are those that add height and volume to the top of the head.

Some popular hair styles for round faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Pixie cut
  • Asymmetrical bob

Oval face

If you have an oval face, your face length is greater than the width, and your forehead and jawline are almost the same width. The best hair styles for oval faces are those that frame the face and highlight your best features.

Some popular hair styles for oval faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Braids
  • Updos

Square face

If you have a square face, your face length and width are almost equal, and your jawline is the most prominent feature of your face. The best hair styles for square faces are those that soften the jawline and add volume to the top of the head.

Some popular hair styles for square faces include:

  • Soft waves
  • Layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob

Heart-shaped face

If you have a heart-shaped face, your forehead is the widest part of your face, and your chin is narrow. The best hair styles for heart-shaped faces are those that add volume to the chin and balance the forehead.

Some popular hair styles for heart-shaped faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Pixie cut
  • Bob

Diamond-shaped face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and chin are narrow. The best hair styles for diamond-shaped faces are those that add volume to the forehead and chin and balance the cheekbones.

Some popular hair styles for diamond-shaped faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Pixie cut
  • Bob

Tips for choosing the right hair style

Now that you know which hair styles suit your face shape, here are some tips to help you choose the right hair style:

  • Consider your lifestyle: Choose a hair style that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.
  • Take inspiration from celebrities: Look for celebrities with a similar face shape and hair type and see what hair styles they are wearing.
  • Consult a stylist: A professional stylist can help you choose a hair style that suits your face shape and hair type.

FAQs about hair styles

What hair style suits a round face?

The best hair styles for round faces are those that add height and volume to the top of the head, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, pixie cut, and asymmetrical bob.

What hair style suits an oval face?

The best hair styles for oval faces are those that frame the face and highlight your best features, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, braids, and updos.

What hair style suits a square face?

The best hair styles for square faces are those that soften the jawline and add volume to the top of the head, such as soft waves, layers, side-swept bangs, and bob.

What hair style suits a heart-shaped face?

The best hair styles for heart-shaped faces are those that add volume to the chin and balance the forehead, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, pixie cut, and bob.

What hair style suits a diamond-shaped face?

The best hair styles for diamond-shaped faces are those that add volume to the forehead and chin and balance the cheekbones, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, pixie cut, and bob.

Hair care tips

No matter which hair style you choose, it is essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your hair's health.

Choosing the right hair style can be a daunting task, but with these tips, you can find a hair style that suits your face shape and boosts your confidence. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny, no matter which hair style you choose.
