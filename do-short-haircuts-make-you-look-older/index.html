Do Short Haircuts Make You Look Older

Written by Petter Jun 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short haircuts are a popular choice for women of all ages, but there is a common misconception that they can make you look older. In this blog post, we will explore whether or not short haircuts actually make you look older and provide tips on how to choose the perfect short haircut for you.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Short Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut

When it comes to choosing a short haircut, there are a few factors to consider:

Face Shape:

The first factor to consider is your face shape. Certain short haircuts may be more flattering for certain face shapes. For example, a pixie cut may look best on someone with a heart-shaped face, while a bob may be better suited for someone with a round face.

Hair Texture:

Your hair texture can also play a role in which short haircut is best for you. If you have fine hair, a layered bob may be the best option to add volume. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut may be more flattering.

Lifestyle:

Your lifestyle can also affect which short haircut is best for you. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance pixie cut may be the best option. If you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles, a choppy bob may be a better choice.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut

If you've decided to take the plunge and get a short haircut, follow these steps:

1. Research:

Before your appointment, research different short haircuts and find inspiration photos to show your stylist.

2. Consultation:

During your appointment, have a consultation with your stylist to discuss your desired haircut and any concerns you may have.

3. Cut:

Your stylist will then cut your hair to your desired length and shape.

4. Styling:

After your haircut, your stylist will style your hair and provide tips on how to maintain your new short haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

1. Trim:

Regular trims are essential for maintaining the shape of your short haircut. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

2. Styling:

Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and desired look.

3. Color:

If you color your hair, consider opting for a low-maintenance color that won't require frequent touch-ups.

FAQs

Do short haircuts make you look older?

While short haircuts can sometimes be associated with an older demographic, they do not inherently make you look older. The key is to choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

What short haircut is best for a round face?

A bob or a lob with layers is a great option for someone with a round face.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short haircuts require a different hair care routine than long hair. Here are a few tips:

1. Shampoo:

Shampoo your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.

2. Conditioner:

Use a lightweight conditioner on the ends of your hair to keep it moisturized.

3. Styling:

Use a heat protectant before using hot tools and avoid using them every day to prevent damage.

4. Bedtime:

Consider sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

In conclusion, short haircuts do not inherently make you look older. The key is to choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture, and to maintain it with regular trims and a proper hair care routine.
