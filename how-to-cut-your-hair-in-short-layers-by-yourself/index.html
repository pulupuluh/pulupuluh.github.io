How To Cut Your Hair In Short Layers By Yourself

Written by Aprilia Apr 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the salon for a simple haircut? Do you want to learn how to cut your hair in short layers by yourself? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of achieving a trendy short layered haircut that will make you feel like a pro.

Description

A short layered haircut is a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the top layer of hair shorter than the bottom layer. This creates a textured and voluminous look that is easy to maintain and style. The best part is, you can achieve this look from the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut Your Hair in Short Layers by Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step to cutting your hair in short layers is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. It is also recommended to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Once you have all your tools, it's time to section your hair. Start by dividing your hair down the middle into two equal sections. Then, divide each section into two more sections, creating a total of four sections. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin by taking one of the bottom sections and combing it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut the ends at a slight angle. Repeat this process on the other bottom section.

Next, move on to the top sections. Comb the hair straight up and hold it between your fingers. Cut the ends at a slightly shorter length than the bottom sections. This will create the layered effect.

Step 4: Check Your Progress

Once you have finished cutting all four sections, take a look in the mirror to make sure everything is even. If you notice any uneven sections, go back and trim them as needed.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Congratulations! You have successfully cut your hair in short layers by yourself. Now it's time to style it. You can either blow dry your hair or leave it to air dry. Use a styling product to add texture and volume, and you're ready to go.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect short layered haircut:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up
  • Use a comb to make sure the sections are even
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Be patient and take your time

FAQ

Can I cut my hair in short layers if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to cut your hair when it's dry and use a pair of scissors made specifically for curly hair.

How often should I cut my hair in short layers?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your hairstyle. On average, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair in short layers, it's important to take care of it to maintain the style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep the ends healthy
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

By following these tips, you can keep your short layered haircut looking fresh and stylish.

Conclusion

Learning how to cut your hair in short layers by yourself can save you time and money, and give you a sense of accomplishment. By following this step-by-step guide, you can achieve a trendy short layered haircut that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
