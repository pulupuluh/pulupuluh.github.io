What Hairstyles Make You Look Older Male

Written by Bardi Jun 09, 2023 · 6 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, men have a lot of options. However, not all hairstyles are created equal. Some styles can actually make you look older than you are. So, what hairstyles make you look older male? In this article, we’ll take a look at some of the most common styles that can add years to your appearance and what you can do to avoid them.

Table of Contents

  1. Haircuts to Avoid
  2. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Right Hairstyle
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Men

Haircuts to Avoid

There are certain hairstyles that can make you look older than you are. These include:

1. The Combover

The combover is a classic example of a hairstyle that can add years to your appearance. This style involves combing your hair over to one side to hide any bald spots. However, it can look outdated and unflattering.

2. The Mullet

The mullet was popular in the 1980s but it’s not a good look for anyone in 2023. This style involves having short hair on the top and sides and long hair at the back. It can make you look dated and out of touch.

3. The Caesar Cut

The Caesar cut is a short, textured style that was popular in the 1990s. While it can look good on some men, it can also make you look older than you are. This style works best for men with square or oval-shaped faces.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be tricky, but there are a few things you can do to make the process easier:

  • Consider your face shape
  • Think about your lifestyle
  • Look at pictures for inspiration

By considering these factors, you can find a hairstyle that works for you and won’t make you look older than you are.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Right Hairstyle

Once you’ve chosen a hairstyle, it’s important to get it right. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Find a good hairstylist
  2. Bring pictures for inspiration
  3. Communicate your preferences clearly
  4. Ask for styling advice

By following these steps, you can ensure that you get the right hairstyle for your face shape and lifestyle.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you’ve got the right hairstyle, it’s important to maintain it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use the right products
  • Get regular trims
  • Avoid over-styling

By following these tips, you can keep your hairstyle looking fresh and stylish.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for older men?

The best hairstyle for older men depends on their face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. A short, textured style can work well for many men.

What hair color makes you look younger?

Highlights or lowlights can add depth to your hair and make it look more youthful. Avoid going too dark, as this can actually make you look older.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Finally, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Avoid using too much heat
  • Use a good conditioner
  • Eat a healthy diet

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and youthful.

In conclusion, there are certain hairstyles that can make you look older than you are. By avoiding these styles and choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape and lifestyle, you can keep your look fresh and stylish. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and following a good hair care routine.
