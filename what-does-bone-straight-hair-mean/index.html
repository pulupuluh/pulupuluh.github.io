What Does Bone Straight Hair Mean A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Bone straight hair has become increasingly popular in recent years, especially among women who have relaxed their hair. It's a term that's used to describe hair that is straightened to the point where it's completely flat and has no visible curls or waves. In this article, we'll take a closer look at what bone straight hair means, how to achieve it, and how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • Definition of Bone Straight Hair
  • How to Achieve Bone Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Bone Straight Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Bone Straight Hair
  • FAQs About Bone Straight Hair
  • Hair Care for Bone Straight Hair

Definition of Bone Straight Hair

As previously mentioned, bone straight hair is hair that has been straightened to the point where there are no visible curls or waves. It's called "bone straight" because the hair is so straight that it appears flat and lifeless, almost like bone. This type of hair is achieved through the use of heat styling tools such as a flat iron, and is often popular among women who have relaxed their hair.

How to Achieve Bone Straight Hair

There are a few different methods that can be used to achieve bone straight hair. The most popular method is to use a flat iron or straightening brush to straighten the hair. This method involves applying heat to the hair, which breaks down the hydrogen bonds in the hair shaft and makes it more pliable. The hair is then straightened using the flat iron or brush.

Another method that can be used to achieve bone straight hair is chemical straightening. This involves using a chemical relaxer to permanently straighten the hair. This method is more permanent than using a flat iron or brush, but can also be more damaging to the hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Bone Straight Hair

If you want to achieve bone straight hair using a flat iron or straightening brush, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant product.
  2. Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry.
  3. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  4. Start straightening your hair from the bottom sections and work your way up to the top.
  5. Repeat the process until all sections of your hair are straightened.
  6. Apply a finishing product to add shine and smoothness.

Tips for Maintaining Bone Straight Hair

Once you've achieved bone straight hair, it's important to take steps to maintain it. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid getting your hair wet, as this can cause it to revert back to its natural texture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools excessively, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair healthy.
  • Wrap your hair at night to prevent it from tangling or getting frizzy.

FAQs About Bone Straight Hair

Q: Can bone straight hair be achieved on natural hair?

A: Yes, bone straight hair can be achieved on natural hair using a flat iron or chemical straightening. However, it's important to note that these methods can be damaging to the hair.

Q: How long does bone straight hair last?

A: The length of time that bone straight hair lasts depends on a variety of factors, including the method used to achieve it, the condition of the hair, and how well it's maintained. Generally, bone straight hair achieved through a flat iron or brush will last until it's washed, while hair that's been chemically straightened will last until it grows out or is cut.

Hair Care for Bone Straight Hair

When caring for bone straight hair, it's important to use products that will help keep it healthy, shiny, and smooth. Here are a few products to consider:

  • A heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • A smoothing cream or serum to add shine and smoothness.
  • A deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • A finishing spray or serum to add extra shine and hold.

By following these tips and taking proper care of your bone straight hair, you can achieve a sleek, polished look that's sure to turn heads.
