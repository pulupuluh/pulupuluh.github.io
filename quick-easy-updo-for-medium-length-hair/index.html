Quick Easy Updo For Medium Length Hair

Written by Daniel Apr 11, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have medium length hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the perfect hairstyle that is both easy and elegant. A quick and easy updo is a great way to achieve a chic look without spending hours in front of the mirror. In this article, we will show you how to create a simple updo that will take you from work to a night out, using just a few products and tools.

Table of Contents

  • How to Create a Quick and Easy Updo
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Perfecting Your Updo
  • FAQs about Updos for Medium Length Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Updo

How to Create a Quick and Easy Updo

Creating a quick and easy updo for medium length hair is easier than you think. All you need are a few products and tools, and you can achieve a chic and elegant look in minutes. Here are some of the products and tools you will need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair elastics
  • Hairspray
  • Teasing comb
  • Hair straightener or curling iron (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these easy steps to create a quick and easy updo for medium length hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Using a teasing comb, tease the crown of your head to add volume and texture.
  3. Pull your hair back into a low ponytail, leaving a few strands loose in the front.
  4. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Take the loose strands at the front and twist them back, securing them with bobby pins behind the bun.
  6. Finish the look with a spritz of hairspray to hold everything in place.
  7. For an extra touch of glamour, use a hair straightener or curling iron to add waves or curls to the loose strands around your face.

Tips for Perfecting Your Updo

Here are some tips to help you perfect your quick and easy updo:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Don’t be afraid to add volume and texture to your hair using a teasing comb.
  • If you have thin hair, consider using a hair donut to create a fuller-looking bun.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for your face shape and hair type.

FAQs about Updos for Medium Length Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about updos for medium length hair:

Q: How long does it take to create a quick and easy updo?

A: It should take no more than 5-10 minutes to create a quick and easy updo.

Q: Can I create a quick and easy updo without using any products?

A: While products like hairspray and bobby pins can help hold your updo in place, it is possible to create a simple updo without them.

Q: What if my hair is too thick or too thin for an updo?

A: There are many different updo styles that can work for all hair types, so experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Updo

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your updo:

  • Avoid washing your hair the day you plan to create an updo, as freshly washed hair can be difficult to work with.
  • Use a dry shampoo to add texture and hold to your hair before creating your updo.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause your updo to fall apart.
  • When you take your updo down at the end of the day, gently brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a quick and easy updo for medium length hair that will take you from day to night with ease. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
