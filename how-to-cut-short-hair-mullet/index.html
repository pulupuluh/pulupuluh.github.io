How To Cut Short Hair Mullet A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both trendy and low-maintenance, the short hair mullet might just be the perfect option for you. This edgy and versatile haircut has been making a comeback in recent years, and it's a great way to add some personality to your look without sacrificing too much length. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting a short hair mullet, as well as some tips and tricks to keep it looking great.

How to Cut Short Hair Mullet

Before you get started, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair. It's also a good idea to have a mirror set up so you can see the back of your head while you're cutting.

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

The first step in cutting a short hair mullet is to start with clean, dry hair. This will help you get a more accurate cut and prevent your hair from getting tangled or frizzy.

Step 2: Section Off Your Hair

Use your comb to section off your hair into three parts: the top, middle, and bottom. Clip the top section out of the way for now, and focus on the bottom section first.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Section

Using your scissors, cut the bottom section of your hair straight across, just above your shoulders. This will create the classic mullet shape, with shorter hair in the back and longer hair in the front.

Step 4: Blend the Middle Section

Next, blend the middle section of your hair with the bottom section by cutting at a slight angle. This will help to create a seamless transition between the shorter and longer sections.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Finally, unclip the top section of your hair and cut it to your desired length. This can be anywhere from chin-length to shoulder-length, depending on your preference.

Step 6: Add Some Texture

To add some texture and volume to your short hair mullet, you can use your scissors to point-cut the ends of your hair. This will create a more choppy, layered look and prevent your hair from looking too blunt.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair Mullet

Now that you've got your short hair mullet cut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make your hair look limp
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type

FAQ

Can I cut a short hair mullet myself?

While it's possible to cut your own hair, we recommend going to a professional stylist for best results. They'll be able to give you a more precise cut and help you achieve the look you want.

What face shape is best for a short hair mullet?

The great thing about a short hair mullet is that it can work for a variety of face shapes. However, it tends to look best on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How do I style a short hair mullet?

There are a variety of ways to style a short hair mullet, depending on your personal preferences. Some popular options include adding volume with a texturizing spray, creating loose waves with a curling iron, or slicking it back with some gel.

Hair Care

One of the most important things you can do to maintain a healthy and vibrant short hair mullet is to take good care of your hair. This includes using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding too much heat styling, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends. With the right care, your short hair mullet is sure to turn heads wherever you go.
