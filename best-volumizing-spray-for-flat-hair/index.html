Best Volumizing Spray For Flat Hair

Written by Frank Jun 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Do you have flat, lifeless hair that just won't hold a style? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with flat hair, but the good news is that there are products out there that can help. One of the best solutions is a volumizing spray. In this post, we'll take a look at the best volumizing sprays for flat hair, and how to use them to get the best results.

Description

A volumizing spray is a product that is applied to the hair to add volume, texture, and hold. It works by coating the hair strands, making them thicker and more resilient. Volumizing sprays can be used on wet or dry hair, and they are perfect for creating styles that require volume and lift, such as updos, ponytails, and tousled waves.

How to Use Volumizing Spray

Using a volumizing spray is easy. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow-dry it first.
  2. Shake the volumizing spray well before use.
  3. Spray the product onto the roots of your hair, lifting sections as you go.
  4. Massage the product in with your fingertips.
  5. Style your hair as desired.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to using volumizing spray, here's a more detailed guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual. Towel-dry your hair and comb it out.
  2. Shake the volumizing spray well before use.
  3. Hold the bottle about 6 inches away from your head and spray the product onto the roots of your hair, lifting sections as you go. Be sure to concentrate the spray at the crown of your head, where you want the most volume.
  4. Massage the product in with your fingertips, working it through your hair from the roots to the ends.
  5. Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair on the cool setting. This will help to set the product and add more volume.
  6. Once your hair is dry, style it as desired.

Tips for Using Volumizing Spray

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results from your volumizing spray:

  • Use a light touch when spraying the product onto your hair. Too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Focus on the roots of your hair when applying the product. This is where you need the most volume.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing spray that is specifically designed for fine hair. These products are lighter and won't weigh your hair down.

FAQs

Q: Can volumizing spray damage your hair?

A: No, volumizing spray is designed to be gentle on your hair. However, if you use too much product or apply it too often, it can build up on your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.

Q: Can I use volumizing spray on curly hair?

A: Yes, volumizing spray can be used on all hair types, including curly hair. Just be sure to apply the product evenly and use a diffuser when blow-drying to avoid frizz.

Q: Can I use volumizing spray on wet hair?

A: Yes, volumizing spray can be used on wet or dry hair. However, if you're using it on wet hair, be sure to blow-dry your hair completely before styling.

Hair Care Tips for Flat Hair

If you have flat hair, there are a few things you can do to help boost volume and add texture:

  • Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are specially formulated to add body and lift to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products, such as oils and serums, which can weigh your hair down.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add more volume.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying to help create lift at the roots.
  • Try teasing your hair at the crown to add height and volume.

Best Volumizing Sprays for Flat Hair

Here are some of the best volumizing sprays for flat hair:

  1. Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray - This spray is formulated with keratin amino acids and other nourishing ingredients to add volume and texture to your hair.
  2. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Hairspray - This spray is lightweight and provides a flexible hold, making it perfect for creating tousled, beachy waves.
  3. Living Proof Full Dry Volume Blast - This spray provides instant volume and texture, and is perfect for refreshing your hair between washes.
  4. TRESemme TRES Two Extra Hold Hair Spray - This hairspray provides all-day hold and is perfect for creating updos and other styles that require extra hold.

With the right volumizing spray and a few styling tips, you can say goodbye to flat, lifeless hair and hello to voluminous, gorgeous locks.
