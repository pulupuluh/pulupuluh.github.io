Best Men s Haircuts For Curly Hair 2020 A Guide

Written by Frank May 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair is a blessing and a curse. While it can look amazing when styled correctly, it can also be difficult to manage. Finding the right haircut for curly hair can be a challenge, especially when you want to stay up-to-date with the latest trends. In this article, we will provide you with a guide to the best men's haircuts for curly hair in 2020.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The best men's haircuts for curly hair in 2020 vary in length and style. Whether you prefer short, medium or long hair, there's a style that will suit your curly locks. From classic cuts to modern twists, there's a haircut that will flatter your face shape and enhance your curls.

How To

When choosing a haircut for curly hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair type. Curly hair can be thick, thin, fine, or coarse, and each type requires a different approach. To achieve the best results, consider consulting with a professional stylist who specializes in curly hair. They can guide you in choosing the right haircut and give you tips on how to maintain your curls.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best men's haircuts for curly hair in 2020:

1. Short Curly Haircut

A short curly haircut is a classic look that never goes out of style. This cut is perfect for those with tight, spiral curls or loose waves. It's easy to maintain and can be styled in various ways. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a short cut on the sides and back of your head, leaving the top longer. Use a styling product to enhance your curls, and you're good to go!

2. Medium Curly Haircut

If you prefer a longer style, a medium curly haircut is a great option. This cut works well with all face shapes and hair types. Ask your stylist to add layers to your hair to give it more volume and texture. Use a curl-enhancing product to keep your curls defined and bouncy.

3. Long Curly Haircut

For those who want to embrace their curls, a long curly haircut is the way to go. This style works well with loose waves or big, bouncy curls. Ask your stylist to add layers to your hair to prevent it from looking too heavy. Use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your curls healthy and shiny.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your curly locks:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut.

FAQ

What face shapes suit curly hair?

Curly hair works well with most face shapes. However, those with round or square faces should opt for longer styles to elongate their face shape. Those with oval faces can pull off almost any haircut.

What products should I use for my curly hair?

Use a sulfate-free shampoo, a deep conditioning treatment, and a curl-enhancing product. Avoid products with alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

How often should I trim my curly hair?

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and bouncy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.

In conclusion, the best men's haircuts for curly hair in 2020 vary in length and style. When choosing a haircut, consider your face shape and hair type. Consult with a professional stylist to get the best results. Use the tips and products mentioned in this article to keep your curls healthy and bouncy. With the right haircut and care, your curly hair can look amazing!
