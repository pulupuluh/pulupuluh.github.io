Best Sunglass Style For Face Shape How To Choose The Perfect Pair

Written by Petter Jul 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Choosing the right pair of sunglasses can be a daunting task, especially when you have no idea which style suits your face shape. But worry not, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we will guide you through the process of selecting the best sunglass style for your face shape. Read on to find out more!

How to Choose the Best Sunglass Style for Your Face Shape

First things first, you need to determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you have identified your face shape, you can narrow down your choices and find the perfect pair of sunglasses that will complement your features and enhance your overall look.

Best Sunglass Styles for Oval Face Shapes

If you have an oval face shape, consider yourself lucky as you can pull off almost any sunglass style. However, we recommend going for oversized frames, aviators, and wayfarers to add definition to your face and balance out your features.

Best Sunglass Styles for Round Face Shapes

For round face shapes, the key is to choose frames that are wider than your face and have angular lines to create the illusion of length. Square, rectangular, and cat-eye frames are your best bet.

Best Sunglass Styles for Square Face Shapes

Those with square face shapes should go for round or oval frames to soften their features and create balance. Avoid sharp and angular frames that will only emphasize your strong jawline.

Best Sunglass Styles for Heart Face Shapes

Heart-shaped faces look great in cat-eye, aviator, and wayfarer frames that are wider at the top and have rounded edges to balance out your forehead and narrow chin.

Best Sunglass Styles for Diamond Face Shapes

Diamond face shapes have high cheekbones and narrow foreheads and chins. To complement this shape, try oval or cat-eye frames with soft curves that will highlight your cheekbones and soften your angles.

Best Sunglass Styles for Oblong Face Shapes

Oblong faces need frames that add width and break up the length of their face. Oversized frames, aviators, and wayfarers are great options as they create balance and proportion.

Tips for Choosing the Best Sunglass Style for Your Face Shape

  • Consider your skin tone and hair color when choosing the color of your frames.
  • Choose frames that fit snugly and comfortably on your face.
  • Try on different styles and shapes to see which ones suit you best.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different colors and patterns.
  • Invest in high-quality lenses that offer 100% UV protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear any sunglass style if I have an oval face shape?

Yes, oval face shapes can pull off almost any sunglass style, but oversized frames, aviators, and wayfarers are particularly flattering.

What frame shape should I choose if I have a round face?

Round faces look great in square, rectangular, and cat-eye frames that are wider than your face and have angular lines.

Can I wear sharp and angular frames if I have a square face shape?

Avoid sharp and angular frames if you have a square face shape as they will only emphasize your strong jawline. Instead, opt for round or oval frames that will soften your features.

How to Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Just like your skin, your hair can also be damaged by the sun’s harmful rays. To protect your hair from sun damage, you can take the following steps:

  • Wear a hat or scarf to cover your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner with UV protection.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling wands.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Choosing the best sunglass style for your face shape can enhance your features and elevate your style. Remember to consider your face shape, skin tone, and hair color when selecting frames and always opt for high-quality lenses with 100% UV protection. Happy shopping!
