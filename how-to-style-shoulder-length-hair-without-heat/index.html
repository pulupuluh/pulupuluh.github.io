How To Style Shoulder Length Hair Without Heat

Written by Daniel May 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools to style your shoulder length hair? Heat styling can damage your hair and leave it dry and brittle. Fortunately, there are many ways to style your shoulder length hair without heat. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks for achieving beautiful hairstyles without using heat tools.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Shoulder Length Hair Without Heat
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Hair Without Heat
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair Without Heat

There are many ways to style shoulder length hair without heat. Here are some popular options:

Braids

Braids are a versatile and trendy way to style shoulder length hair without heat. You can create many different braid styles, including French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids. Braids work well for both casual and formal occasions.

Ponytails

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. You can create a sleek and polished ponytail or a messy and relaxed style. To add volume to your ponytail, tease your hair at the crown before securing it with a hair tie.

Buns

Buns are a chic and easy way to style shoulder length hair without heat. You can create a high or low bun, a messy bun, or a sleek ballerina bun. To add volume to your bun, tease your hair at the crown before pulling it back.

Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can add a pop of color and style to your shoulder length hair. You can use headbands, hair clips, and hair ties to create different looks. Try a floral headband for a bohemian feel or a sparkly hair clip for a special occasion.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to create a braided hairstyle without using heat:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Choose the braid style you want to create.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and start braiding.
  4. Secure the end of your braid with a hair tie.
  5. If desired, pull on the edges of your braid to loosen it.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Hair Without Heat

Here are some tips to help you style your shoulder length hair without heat:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray before styling to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties that can damage your hair. Instead, use a hair tie that is gentle on your hair, such as a scrunchie or a ribbon.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and hair clips, to create different looks.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

FAQs

Can I curl my shoulder length hair without heat?

Yes, you can curl your shoulder length hair without heat using foam rollers or braids. Simply wrap your hair around the roller or braid and leave it in overnight. In the morning, remove the rollers or braids and enjoy your curls.

How often should I wash my shoulder length hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less frequently, such as every 2-3 days. Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and leave it dry and brittle.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your shoulder length hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as wet hair is more prone to breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair health.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your shoulder length hair without heat and keep it healthy and beautiful. Whether you prefer braids, ponytails, buns, or hair accessories, there are many options to choose from. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
