How To Deal With Messy Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 24, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to keep it looking neat and tidy. Curls are beautiful, but they can also be challenging to manage. If you're struggling with messy curly hair, don't worry – there are plenty of tips and tricks you can use to tame your locks. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways to deal with messy curly hair and keep your hair looking beautiful and healthy.

How to Deal with Messy Curly Hair: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with a good shampoo and conditioner. Curly hair tends to be dry and prone to frizz, so it's important to use products that will moisturize and nourish your hair. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for curly hair, and avoid products that contain sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Never use a brush on curly hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, gently comb through your hair with a wide-tooth comb or use your fingers to separate any tangles.

Step 3: Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 4: Style your hair using a diffuser. A diffuser is an attachment that you can add to your hair dryer to help distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Use the diffuser on a low heat setting to dry your hair, and scrunch your hair with your hands as you go to enhance your curls.

Tips for Dealing with Messy Curly Hair

Tip 1: Don't over-wash your hair

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important not to over-wash your hair. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. Try to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week, and use a gentle shampoo that won't dry out your hair.

Tip 2: Use a satin pillowcase

Sleeping on a cotton pillowcase can cause friction, which can lead to frizz and breakage. To keep your curls looking their best, use a satin pillowcase instead. Satin is smoother than cotton, so it won't cause as much friction, and it will help to keep your hair looking smooth and shiny.

Tip 3: Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can be damaging to curly hair, so it's best to avoid it whenever possible. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser on a low heat setting. If you do need to use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

FAQs about Dealing with Messy Curly Hair

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my curly hair?

A: To reduce frizz in curly hair, avoid brushing your hair and use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle it instead. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized, and use a diffuser on a low heat setting to dry your hair.

Q: What should I do if my curls are flat?

A: If your curls are flat, try using a volumizing mousse or spray to give them some lift. You can also try flipping your hair upside down and scrunching it with your hands to enhance your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you take care of your curly locks:

Tip 1: Use a deep conditioner

Deep conditioning treatments can help to nourish and moisturize curly hair, keeping it healthy and shiny. Look for a deep conditioning treatment that is specifically designed for curly hair, and use it once a week to keep your hair looking its best.

Tip 2: Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your curls looking neat and tidy, and can prevent split ends and breakage. Make sure to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and strong.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from the sun

The sun can be damaging to curly hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. To protect your hair from the sun, wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner that contains SPF.

Conclusion

Dealing with messy curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can keep your locks looking beautiful and healthy. Remember to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers, and use a diffuser on a low heat setting to dry your hair. Avoid over-washing your hair and heat styling whenever possible, and use a satin pillowcase to reduce friction while you sleep. With these tips and tricks, you can keep your curly hair looking its best.
