Long Hairstyles For Big Forehead And Round Face Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a male with a big forehead and a round face? Do you struggle with finding the perfect hairstyle that complements your features? Look no further! In this guide, we will provide you with various long hairstyles that will suit your face shape and make you look your best. Read on to find out more.

Description

Having a big forehead and a round face can be challenging when it comes to selecting a hairstyle. The wrong cut can make your forehead appear even bigger, and your face more round. However, with the right hairstyle, you can draw attention away from your forehead and create the illusion of a more elongated face shape.

How to

To achieve a long hairstyle that suits your big forehead and round face, there are a few things you need to consider:

  • The length of your hair should be at least shoulder-length or longer.
  • Layers can help create volume and add dimension to your hair.
  • Side-swept bangs can help cover your forehead and make it appear smaller.
  • Avoid short hairstyles or blunt cuts that accentuate your round face shape.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the best long hairstyles for big forehead and round face male:

The Classic Side Part

  1. Apply a small amount of styling gel to damp hair.
  2. Create a deep side part using a comb.
  3. Blow-dry your hair while brushing it to the side.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or waves.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

The Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.
  4. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when selecting a long hairstyle for big forehead and round face male:

  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style and lifestyle.
  • Invest in good hair care products to maintain the health and shine of your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long hairstyles for big forehead and round face male:

Q: Can I still have short hair?

A: While short hair may not be the best option for big forehead and round face male, you can still opt for a short haircut with textured layers and side-swept bangs.

Q: What type of bangs should I go for?

A: Side-swept bangs are the best option for big forehead and round face male. They help cover your forehead and create a more elongated face shape.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and shine of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollutants by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

With these long hairstyles for big forehead and round face male, you can feel confident and stylish. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your personal style and lifestyle, and invest in good hair care products to maintain the health and shine of your hair. Happy styling!
