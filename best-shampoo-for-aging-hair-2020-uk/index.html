Best Shampoo For Aging Hair 2020 Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 13, 2023 · 8 min read

As we age, our hair undergoes various changes, including thinning, dryness, dullness, and breakage. To counteract these issues, it’s essential to invest in the right hair care products, particularly shampoo. With so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best shampoo for aging hair. In this article, we’ll discuss the top shampoos for aging hair in the UK in 2020 and help you make an informed decision.

Description:

Our hair changes as we age, and it’s essential to adjust our hair care routine accordingly. Aging hair tends to become drier, thinner, and more prone to breakage. Using the right shampoo can help address these issues, leaving your hair looking fuller, shinier, and healthier.

How to choose a shampoo for aging hair:

Choosing the right shampoo for aging hair can be confusing, but keeping a few factors in mind can help you make an informed decision. Here are some things to consider:

Hair type:

It’s essential to choose a shampoo that caters to your specific hair type. If you have color-treated hair, look for a shampoo that’s safe for colored hair. If you have curly hair, choose a shampoo that helps define your curls.

Ingredients:

Look for shampoos that contain ingredients that are beneficial for aging hair. Some ingredients to look for include biotin, keratin, collagen, and argan oil. Avoid shampoos that contain harsh chemicals like sulfates and parabens, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause further damage.

Brand reputation:

Choose a shampoo from a reputable brand with positive reviews. Reading reviews from other customers who have similar hair concerns can be helpful in making an informed decision.

Top shampoos for aging hair in the UK in 2020:

1. L’Oreal Paris Elvive Full Restore 5 Repairing Shampoo:

This shampoo is designed to strengthen and nourish damaged hair, leaving it looking fuller and shinier. It contains ceramide, a protein that helps repair damaged hair, and arginine, an amino acid that promotes hair growth.

2. OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo:

This shampoo is infused with biotin and collagen, two ingredients that are beneficial for aging hair. Biotin helps strengthen hair, while collagen helps improve elasticity and prevent breakage.

3. Nioxin System 2 Cleanser Shampoo:

This shampoo is specifically designed for thinning hair and contains ingredients like biotin, niacin, and caffeine that help promote hair growth. It also contains botanicals like white tea extract and peppermint oil, which help soothe the scalp and improve hair texture.

Step-by-step guide:

Using the right shampoo can make a significant difference in the appearance and health of your hair. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to use shampoo for aging hair:

Step 1:

Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.

Step 2:

Apply a quarter-sized amount of shampoo to your palms and rub them together to create a lather.

Step 3:

Massage the shampoo into your scalp using your fingertips, working your way from the roots to the ends.

Step 4:

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until all the shampoo is washed out.

Step 5:

Follow up with a conditioner that’s suitable for aging hair to lock in moisture and nourish your hair.

Tips for caring for aging hair:

Using the right shampoo is just one part of caring for aging hair. Here are some additional tips:

1. Avoid using hot tools:

Hot tools like straighteners and curling irons can damage hair, causing it to become brittle and prone to breakage. Try to avoid using them as much as possible, and if you must use them, use a heat protectant.

2. Eat a balanced diet:

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth. Focus on eating foods that are high in protein, iron, and vitamin C, which are essential for healthy hair.

3. Protect your hair from the sun:

The sun’s UV rays can damage hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair when you’re out in the sun.

FAQ:

Q: Can shampoo make your hair fall out?

A: Shampooing your hair doesn’t cause hair loss, but using the wrong shampoo or washing your hair too frequently can cause damage, leading to breakage and thinning.

Q: How often should you wash aging hair?

A: It’s generally recommended to wash aging hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming too dry. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Conclusion:

Choosing the right shampoo for aging hair is crucial in maintaining healthy, vibrant hair. Consider your hair type, ingredients, and brand reputation when choosing a shampoo, and follow a proper hair care routine to keep your hair looking its best.
