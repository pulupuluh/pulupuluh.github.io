Easy Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Thick Curly Hair

Written by Luffy Sep 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have shoulder length, thick, curly hair, you've probably found that styling it can be a challenge. But fear not, we've compiled a list of easy hairstyles that you can try out to switch up your look and keep your hair looking fabulous.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length, thick, curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have plenty of volume and texture to work with. On the other hand, it can be difficult to find hairstyles that work with your hair type and length. But don't worry, we've got you covered with some easy and stylish options.

How to

Before we get into the hairstyles, it's important to know how to prep your hair for styling. Start by using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls. Apply a curl-defining product, like a mousse or gel, to help enhance your natural texture. Then, either air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your curls without disrupting them too much.

Step by Step

1. Half-Up Half-Down

This is a classic style that works great for shoulder length, thick, curly hair. Start by taking a section of hair from the crown of your head and tying it back with a hair tie or clip. Leave the rest of your hair down to showcase your curls.

2. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and easy option for days when you don't have much time to spend on your hair. Gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it around itself, and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out some pieces around your face for a relaxed, undone look.

3. Side Braid

A side braid is a cute and easy way to keep your hair out of your face. Start by parting your hair to one side and braiding it over your shoulder. Use a clear elastic to secure the end of the braid and pull out some pieces to create a more casual, messy look.

4. Top Knot

A top knot is another quick and easy option. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and wrap it around itself to create a bun. Secure it with bobby pins and pull out some pieces around your face to soften the look.

Tips

- Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your curls without disrupting them too much.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands or scarves, to add some extra flair to your hairstyles.

- Consider getting layers cut into your hair to add more movement and texture.

FAQ

Q. Can I straighten my curly hair?

A. Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and a flat iron with adjustable heat settings to avoid damaging your hair.

Q. How often should I wash my curly hair?

A. It's best to wash curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking its best. Be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy. And don't forget to trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your curls looking bouncy.

With these easy hairstyles and care tips, you can rock your shoulder length, thick, curly hair with confidence and style.
