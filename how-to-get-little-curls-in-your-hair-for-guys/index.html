How To Get Little Curls In Your Hair For Guys

Written by Eveline Mar 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a guy looking to add some texture and style to your hair, little curls can be a great way to do it. Not only do curls add volume and dimension to your hair, but they can also help create a unique and eye-catching look. In this article, we'll show you how to get little curls in your hair for guys, step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Little Curls in Your Hair for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Getting the Best Results
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Little Curls

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair, and make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Next, apply a curl enhancing product to your hair. This can be a curl cream, gel, or mousse, depending on your hair type and texture. Use a generous amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into small sections, using clips or hair ties to keep them separated. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that each section gets enough attention.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or wand, curl each section of your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, holding the curling iron in place for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair have been curled.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Cool

Once you've curled your hair, let it cool completely before touching it. This will help set the curls and prevent them from falling out too quickly.

Step 6: Separate Your Curls

Once your hair has cooled, gently separate your curls with your fingers. Be careful not to pull or tug too hard, as this can cause your curls to become frizzy or lose their shape.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your look with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your curls in place and keep them looking great all day.

Tips for Getting the Best Results

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results when curling your hair:

  • Choose a curling iron or wand that's the right size for your hair. For little curls, a smaller barrel size (around 1 inch) is best.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair from the curling iron.
  • Don't curl your hair when it's wet or damp, as this can cause damage and make your curls fall out faster.
  • If you have thick or coarse hair, you may need to use a higher heat setting on your curling iron to get your curls to hold.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques, such as wrapping your hair around the barrel or twisting it as you curl, to create different types of curls.

FAQ

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The length of time your curls will last depends on your hair type, the products you use, and how well you maintain your curls. On average, you can expect your curls to last for several hours to a day or two.

Q: Can I curl my hair without a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use other methods to curl your hair, such as braiding or twisting it while it's damp and letting it dry overnight. You can also try using foam rollers or flexi rods to create curls.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to curl my hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener to create curls, but it can be a bit trickier than using a curling iron. Look up tutorials to get a better idea of how to do it.

Hair Care for Little Curls

To keep your little curls looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent your curls from becoming dry or frizzy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your curls and make them harder to maintain.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your curls too much, as this can cause them to lose their shape and become frizzy.
  • Sleep on a satin or silk pillowcase to help prevent your curls from getting tangled while you sleep.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve little curls in your hair that will turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
