Is Olaplex Good For Curly Hair

Written by Petter Jun 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to maintain its health and manageability. From frizz to breakage, curly hair can be a challenge to keep in top condition. That's why many people with curly hair are turning to Olaplex for help.

What is Olaplex?

Olaplex is a hair care product that was created to prevent damage to hair during chemical treatments, such as coloring or perming. It works by repairing the bonds in hair that are broken during these treatments, leaving it stronger and healthier. Olaplex is now used as a standalone treatment to improve the health of all hair types.

How Does Olaplex Work on Curly Hair?

Olaplex works on curly hair in the same way it works on all other hair types. The product repairs the bonds that are broken during chemical treatments, as well as those that are damaged by heat styling, environmental factors, and other stressors. This means that Olaplex can help curly hair to become stronger, healthier, and more manageable.

How to Use Olaplex on Curly Hair

The process for using Olaplex on curly hair is the same as it is for other hair types. It involves a three-step system that includes:

Step 1: Bond Multiplier

The first step in the Olaplex system is to apply the Bond Multiplier to the hair. This product is designed to penetrate the hair shaft and repair the bonds that are broken or damaged. It is left on the hair for around 10 minutes before being washed out.

Step 2: Bond Perfector

The second step is to apply the Bond Perfector. This product helps to further repair and strengthen the bonds in the hair. It is left on the hair for around 20 minutes before being washed out.

Step 3: Hair Perfector

The final step is to use the Hair Perfector at home once a week. This product helps to maintain the strength and health of the hair, and is left on for around 10 minutes before being washed out.

Tips for Using Olaplex on Curly Hair

Here are some tips for using Olaplex on curly hair:

  • Use Olaplex as a standalone treatment or in conjunction with other hair care products.
  • Apply Olaplex evenly to all sections of your hair, including the roots and ends.
  • Use Olaplex once a week for best results.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute Olaplex through your hair.
  • Don't use Olaplex on damaged hair that is breaking or shedding excessively.

FAQ: Is Olaplex Safe for Curly Hair?

Yes, Olaplex is safe for curly hair. It is a gentle and non-toxic product that is designed to repair and strengthen hair without causing damage. However, if you have any concerns about using Olaplex on your hair, you should speak to your hairstylist or a hair care professional.

The Bottom Line

Olaplex is a great product for anyone who wants to improve the health and manageability of their curly hair. By repairing and strengthening the bonds in the hair, Olaplex can help to reduce breakage, frizz, and other common issues that people with curly hair face. If you want to try Olaplex on your curly hair, be sure to follow the instructions carefully and use it once a week for best results.
