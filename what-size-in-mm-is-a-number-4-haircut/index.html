What Size In Mm Is A Number 4 Haircut

Written by Christine Aug 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, there are so many different terms and phrases that can be confusing for anyone who isn't a professional hairdresser. One of the most common questions people have is, "what size in mm is a number 4 haircut?".

Description

A number 4 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a short, tidy cut without going too close to the scalp. It's also a commonly used clipper guard size for longer haircuts, creating a length of around 1/2 inch or 12mm. This length is perfect for showing off texture and layers in the hair, while still keeping it neat and manageable.

How to

If you're looking to get a number 4 haircut, the first step is to find a good hairdresser or barber who can help you achieve the look you want. Once you've found someone you trust, simply ask for a number 4 clipper guard and they'll know what to do.

The hairdresser will use the clippers to create an even length all over your head, working from the bottom up. They may also use scissors to tidy up any longer hairs or create some additional texture. Once the haircut is complete, you can style it as desired using your favourite hair products.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a number 4 haircut:

  1. Find a reputable hairdresser or barber.
  2. Ask for a number 4 clipper guard.
  3. Sit back and relax while they cut your hair with clippers, working from the bottom up.
  4. They may also use scissors to tidy up any longer hairs or create texture.
  5. Once the haircut is complete, style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your number 4 haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Invest in some styling products to help you achieve the look you want.
  • Visit your hairdresser or barber regularly to keep your haircut looking sharp.
  • Consider trying out different clipper guard sizes to find the perfect length for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about number 4 haircuts:

What other clipper guard sizes are commonly used?

Other popular clipper guard sizes include number 2 (1/4 inch or 6mm), number 3 (3/8 inch or 9.5mm), and number 5 (5/8 inch or 16mm).

Can I get a number 4 haircut with scissors instead of clippers?

Yes, you can. Simply ask your hairdresser to use scissors to create an even length all over your head.

Is a number 4 haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a number 4 haircut can work well for all hair types, from curly to straight to thick or fine.

Hair Care

Getting a number 4 haircut is a great way to keep your hair looking neat and tidy while still showing off some length and texture. To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to use good quality hair care products and visit your hairdresser regularly for maintenance.

With these tips and tricks in mind, you're sure to rock your number 4 haircut with confidence and style.
