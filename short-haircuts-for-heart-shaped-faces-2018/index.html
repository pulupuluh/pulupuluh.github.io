Short Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces 2018 A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide to short haircuts for heart-shaped faces 2018. If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll know how important it is to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. This guide will take you through everything you need to know about choosing a short haircut that suits your face shape, including how to identify a heart-shaped face, how to choose the right length and style, and the best hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

What is a Heart-Shaped Face?

Before we dive into the best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces, let's first talk about what a heart-shaped face actually is. A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and temples, a narrower jawline, and a pointed chin. If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to choose a haircut that balances out your features and draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

How to Choose the Right Length and Style

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for a heart-shaped face, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to choose a length that flatters your face shape. Generally, shorter haircuts work well for heart-shaped faces, as they draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Next, you'll want to choose a style that balances out your features. If you have a wider forehead, for example, you may want to choose a style that adds volume to the sides of your hair. If you have a pointed chin, on the other hand, you may want to choose a style that adds volume to the top of your hair.

Short Bob

The short bob is a classic haircut that works well for heart-shaped faces. This style is versatile and can be worn with or without bangs, depending on your preference. To add volume to the sides of your hair, try curling the ends of your hair under with a curling iron.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and daring haircut that can look great on heart-shaped faces. This style is short and choppy, and can be customized to suit your individual features. To add volume to the top of your hair, try using a volumizing mousse or root lifter.

Layered Crop

The layered crop is a fun and edgy haircut that can work well for heart-shaped faces. This style features layers of different lengths, which can help to balance out your features. To add volume to the sides of your hair, try using a texturizing spray or pomade.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Heart-Shaped Faces

Now that you've chosen the perfect short haircut for your heart-shaped face, it's important to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips for maintaining your new style:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.

FAQs

What other haircuts work well for heart-shaped faces?

In addition to the short haircuts listed above, heart-shaped faces can also look great with long hair, side-swept bangs, and layered cuts. The key is to choose a style that balances out your features and draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a heart-shaped face?

Yes! While shorter haircuts work well for heart-shaped faces, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail or updo. Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape and draws attention to your best features.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the right short haircut for a heart-shaped face can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it's easy to find a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Use the tips and tricks in this guide to choose the perfect short haircut for your heart-shaped face, and don't forget to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny with the right hair care routine.
