How To Style Long Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 19, 2023 · 9 min read

Long hair on guys is becoming increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. It can give you a unique and stylish look that sets you apart from the crowd. However, styling long hair can be challenging, and it requires a bit of effort to maintain the look you want. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style long hair guys, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Long Hair Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Hair Guys
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Hair Guys

How to Style Long Hair Guys

Styling long hair guys can seem intimidating at first, but it doesn't have to be. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve a variety of looks, from sleek and polished to grungy and textured. Here are some popular hairstyles for long hair guys:

Man Bun

The man bun is a classic and versatile hairstyle for guys with long hair. To achieve this look, simply gather your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head and wrap it in a bun shape. Secure it with a hair tie or bobby pins. You can also add some texture or volume by teasing the hair at the crown before gathering it into a bun.

Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is perfect if you want to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off your length. Simply gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail or bun, leaving the rest of your hair down. You can also add braids or twists to the top section for a more intricate look.

Long and Straight

If you have naturally straight hair, you can embrace your length by keeping it sleek and polished. Use a straightening iron or blow dryer to smooth out your hair and add shine. You can also add some layers or face-framing pieces for dimension.

Textured Waves

If you have wavy or curly hair, you can enhance your natural texture by adding some product and scrunching your hair. Use a salt spray or mousse to add volume and texture, and diffuse your hair with a blow dryer to create defined waves or curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have some ideas for hairstyles, let's go through a step-by-step guide on how to style long hair guys:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type. Long hair can get easily tangled and damaged, so it's important to keep it clean and moisturized.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture. Don't rub your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a styling product that is suited for your desired look. For sleek and polished styles, use a wax or pomade. For texture and volume, use a salt spray or mousse. Work the product through your hair evenly, starting from the roots and working your way down.

Step 4: Style

Use a blow dryer, straightening iron, or diffuser to style your hair according to your desired look. Use your fingers or a comb to shape your hair as you go, and don't be afraid to experiment with different techniques.

Step 5: Finish

Once you've achieved your desired look, finish with a hairspray or finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Long Hair Guys

Styling long hair guys can be challenging, but with these tips, you can make the process easier:

  • Invest in quality hair care products that are suited for your hair type.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip away natural oils and cause damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid damaging your hair while brushing it.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but generally, washing your hair every 2-3 days is sufficient. If you have very dry or very oily hair, you may need to adjust your washing frequency accordingly.

Q: Can I still style my long hair if it's thinning or receding?

A: Yes, there are many hairstyles and techniques that can help disguise thinning or receding hair. Consult with your hairstylist for personalized advice.

Hair Care for Long Hair Guys

Long hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care for long hair guys:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension and damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Styling long hair guys can be a fun and rewarding process. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of looks that showcase your unique style. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new things. Who knows, you might just discover your new signature look!
