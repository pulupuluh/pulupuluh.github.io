How To Restore Your Synthetic Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Apr 09, 2023 · 7 min read

If you own a synthetic wig, you know how important it is to take proper care of it. However, even with the best of care, your wig may start to lose its shine and become tangled over time. The good news is that restoring your synthetic wig is easier than you might think. In this post, we’ll give you some tips and tricks to help you bring your wig back to life.

How to Restore Your Synthetic Wig

Here are the steps you need to follow to restore your synthetic wig:

Step 1: Detangle Your Wig

The first thing you need to do is detangle your wig. To do this, gently comb through the hair using a wide-toothed comb. Start from the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle and patient as you work through any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Wash Your Wig

Next, you need to wash your wig. Fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Gently swirl the wig in the water for a few minutes, then rinse it thoroughly with cool water.

Step 3: Condition Your Wig

After washing your wig, you should condition it to help restore its softness and shine. Apply a small amount of wig conditioner to the hair, focusing on the ends. Let the conditioner sit for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.

Step 4: Dry Your Wig

Once you’ve washed and conditioned your wig, gently squeeze out any excess water. Do not twist or wring the hair, as this can cause damage. Instead, lay the wig flat on a towel and let it air dry naturally.

Tips for Restoring Your Synthetic Wig

Here are some additional tips to help you restore your synthetic wig:

Tip 1: Use a Wig Stand

A wig stand can be a helpful tool when restoring your wig. It will help the wig maintain its shape while it dries, and it will also make it easier to detangle and style.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat

Synthetic wigs cannot withstand heat, so it’s important to avoid using any heat styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners. Instead, use a wig brush or comb to style your wig.

Tip 3: Store Your Wig Properly

When you’re not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand or in a box to help maintain its shape. Avoid leaving it in direct sunlight or in a humid environment, as this can cause damage.

FAQs About Restoring Your Synthetic Wig

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my synthetic wig?

A: No. Regular shampoo and conditioner can be too harsh for synthetic wigs and can cause damage. It’s important to use a wig shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for synthetic wigs.

Q: How often should I wash my synthetic wig?

A: It depends on how often you wear your wig. If you wear it daily, you should wash it every 2-3 weeks. If you wear it less frequently, you can wash it less often.

Hair Care for Your Synthetic Wig

Proper hair care is important for maintaining the life and appearance of your synthetic wig. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Tip 1: Avoid Heat

As we mentioned earlier, synthetic wigs cannot withstand heat, so it’s important to avoid using any heat styling tools.

Tip 2: Don’t Sleep in Your Wig

It’s important to remove your wig before going to bed. This will help prevent tangles and damage to the hair.

Tip 3: Use a Wig Cap

Wearing a wig cap can help protect your wig from oils and sweat, which can cause damage over time.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily restore your synthetic wig and keep it looking great for years to come. Whether you wear your wig daily or only on special occasions, taking proper care of it will help it last longer and maintain its beautiful appearance.
