What Product To Use For Thin Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Having thin hair can be frustrating, especially when trying to achieve a voluminous hairstyle. Fortunately, there are many products on the market that can help add volume and thickness to your hair. In this article, we’ll discuss what product to use for thin hair and provide tips on how to improve the overall health of your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. What Product to Use for Thin Hair
  2. How to Use the Product
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Routine for Thin Hair

What Product to Use for Thin Hair

The best product to use for thin hair is a volumizing mousse or spray. These products work by coating the hair strands and adding volume to each individual strand. Some popular products to consider include:

  • Redken Guts 10 Volume Spray Foam
  • Bumble and Bumble Thickening Full Form Mousse
  • Living Proof Full Thickening Mousse
  • Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray

When choosing a product, look for one that is specifically formulated for thin hair. These products will typically contain ingredients that help to plump and thicken the hair, without weighing it down.

How to Use the Product

When using a volumizing mousse or spray, it’s important to apply it to damp hair. This will help the product distribute evenly throughout the hair and provide maximum volume. Here’s how to use the product:

For Volumizing Mousse:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair as normal.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Shake the can of mousse and dispense a golf-ball sized amount into your hand.
  4. Apply the mousse to the roots of your hair, working your way towards the ends.
  5. Comb through your hair to distribute the product evenly.
  6. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.

For Volumizing Spray:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair as normal.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Hold the bottle of spray 6-8 inches away from your hair and spray evenly throughout.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re still unsure how to use the product, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair as normal.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Apply a golf-ball sized amount of volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair, working your way towards the ends.
  4. Comb through your hair to distribute the product evenly.
  5. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  6. Once your hair is dry, apply a volumizing spray to the roots for extra lift.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to using volumizing products. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Using the right products is just one part of maintaining healthy, voluminous hair. Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish the hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to support healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Q: Can I use volumizing products on dry hair?

A: While it is possible to use volumizing products on dry hair, it is best to apply them to damp hair for best results.

Q: Will volumizing products make my hair feel heavy or greasy?

A: Not if you choose the right product for your hair type. Look for products that are specifically formulated for thin hair, as these will be lightweight and won’t weigh your hair down.

Hair Care Routine for Thin Hair

Here’s a simple hair care routine to follow if you have thin hair:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair every other day to avoid over-washing.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and blow dry using a round brush to create volume.
  • Once your hair is dry, apply a volumizing spray to the roots for extra lift.
  • Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.
  • Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to nourish the hair.

Remember, the key to healthy, voluminous hair is using the right products and following a consistent hair care routine. With these tips and product recommendations, you’ll be on your way to achieving the hair of your dreams!
