Best Bob Hair For Over 50 A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Petter Jun 30, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're over 50 and looking for a chic and easy-to-manage hairstyle, a bob may be just what you need. This classic cut has been a favorite for decades, and there are many variations that can be tailored to suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we'll explore some of the best bob haircuts for women over 50, and offer tips on how to care for and style your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Bob Haircut for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Bob Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Bob Haircut
  • FAQs About Bob Haircuts for Women Over 50
  • Hair Care Tips for Women Over 50

How to Choose the Best Bob Haircut for Your Face Shape

When selecting a bob haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. Different styles can flatter different features and balance out your overall look. Here are some tips for choosing the right bob for your face shape.

Round Face

If you have a round face, a bob with layers and volume at the crown can help elongate your face and create a more oval appearance. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as they can make your face look wider.

Square Face

A bob with soft, angled layers can soften a square jawline and create a more feminine look. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that end at your jawline, as they can emphasize your square shape.

Heart-Shaped Face

For a heart-shaped face, a chin-length bob with layers around the chin can balance out a wider forehead and create a more proportional look. Avoid bobs that are too short, as they can make your forehead appear even larger.

Oval Face

Lucky you! If you have an oval face, you can pull off just about any bob haircut. Consider your hair texture and personal style when choosing a cut that suits you best.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Bob Haircut

Now that you've chosen the perfect bob haircut for your face shape, it's time to style it. Here's a simple guide to achieving a polished, sophisticated look.

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start with clean, damp hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from your styling tools. Then, apply a styling product such as mousse or gel to add volume and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the back and working your way forward. Use the brush to lift and add volume to your roots, and create a slight curl under at the ends.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

If you have bangs, use a small round brush to blow dry them in the direction you want them to lay. If you don't have bangs, skip this step.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can be a low-maintenance style that's easy to care for. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best.

  • Get regular trims to keep your ends from becoming too blunt or damaged
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day to prevent heat damage
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun

FAQs About Bob Haircuts for Women Over 50

Q: Will a bob haircut suit my hair type?

A: Yes! There are bob haircuts that can be tailored to suit any hair type, from fine and straight to thick and curly.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a bob haircut?

A: Absolutely! A bob can be styled in many ways, including updos and half-up styles.

Q: Will a bob haircut make me look older?

A: Not at all! A bob can be a youthful and trendy style that flatters women of all ages.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 50

As we age, our hair may become thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best.

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day to prevent heat damage
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth
  • Consider using a hair growth supplement or topical treatment to promote hair health

Now that you know the best bob haircuts for women over 50, it's time to find the one that's right for you. Whether you prefer a sleek and simple bob or a layered and textured style, there's a bob out there that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. Follow our tips for caring for and styling your hair, and you'll be rocking your new 'do in no time!
