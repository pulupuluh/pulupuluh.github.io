Is Short Hair Still In Style 2020

Written by Petter Jun 05, 2023 · 8 min read

Short hair has always been a popular hairstyle among women. It is low maintenance and easy to style, making it a favorite of busy women. But is short hair still in style in 2020? The answer is yes! Short hair is still trending in 2020, and it’s not going away anytime soon. In this article, we’ll explore why short hair is still in style, how to style short hair, and tips for maintaining a short hairstyle.

Why Short Hair is Still in Style in 2020?

Short hair has been a trend for a long time, and it’s not going away any time soon. In 2020, short hair is still in style because it’s versatile, low maintenance, and easy to style. Short hair can be styled in various ways, from sleek bobs to textured pixie cuts, making it a favorite of women who want to experiment with their look. Moreover, short hair can be styled in a way that complements any face shape, making it accessible to everyone.

How to Style Short Hair?

Styling short hair is easy and quick. Here are some tips on how to style short hair:

1. Blow Dry

Blow-drying short hair can add volume and texture to your hair. Use a round brush to lift the roots and create volume. Blow-dry in sections, starting from the back and moving forward.

2. Use Hair Products

Styling products can help you achieve your desired look. Use hair wax or pomade for a sleek look, or use sea salt spray for a textured look. Apply the product to your hair and style as desired.

3. Accessorize

Accessorizing your short hair can add some fun to your look. Use hairpins, headbands, or scarves to add a pop of color to your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair

Cutting short hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done at home. Here’s a step-by-step guide to cutting short hair:

1. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and dry it completely before cutting. Wet hair can stretch, making it difficult to cut accurately.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable parts. Use clips to hold the sections in place.

3. Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair from the back and work your way forward. Use scissors to cut the hair at an angle, creating a layered look. Cut the sides and the front of your hair to match the back.

4. Style Your Hair

Once you’ve cut your hair, style it as desired. Use a blow dryer and styling products to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining a Short Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining a short hairstyle:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short hair needs to be trimmed regularly to maintain its shape. Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks for a trim.

2. Use the Right Products

Short hair requires less product than long hair. Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat and environmental damage by using heat protectant and wearing a hat when outdoors.

FAQ

Is short hair in style in 2020?

Yes, short hair is still in style in 2020. It’s versatile, low maintenance, and easy to style, making it a favorite of women.

Can I cut my short hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your short hair at home. Use the right tools and technique, and follow a step-by-step guide to cutting short hair.

How often should I trim my short hair?

You should trim your short hair every six to eight weeks to maintain its shape.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs to be cared for. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Protect your hair from heat damage by using heat protectant before using heat styling tools.

3. Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.

Short hair is still in style in 2020 and beyond. It’s versatile, low maintenance, and easy to style, making it a favorite of women. With the right tools and technique, you can cut your short hair at home and maintain its shape. Follow these tips for styling and maintaining your short hair, and you’ll have a trendy and chic look all year long.
