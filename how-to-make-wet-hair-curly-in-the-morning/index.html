How To Make Wet Hair Curly In The Morning

Written by Luffy May 27, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to make your wet hair curly in the morning, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you how to get beautiful, bouncy curls in just a few simple steps.

Description

Curly hair is beautiful, but it can be difficult to maintain. If you have straight hair, you might think that you can't achieve those gorgeous curls. But don't worry – with the right technique, you can make wet hair curly in the morning and get the look you want.

How to

Here's how to make wet hair curly in the morning:

Step 1: Wash your hair

The first step is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair to help define your curls and keep them looking great. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel-dry it so that it's damp but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Apply a curl-enhancing product

Next, apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This could be a mousse, gel or cream. Work the product through your hair, making sure to apply it evenly from roots to ends. This will help define your curls and keep them looking great all day long.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you get even curls all over your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, clipping each section out of the way as you go.

Step 4: Curl your hair

Using a curling iron or wand, curl each section of your hair. Hold the iron vertically and wrap the hair around it, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Make sure to curl each section in the same direction to get uniform curls.

Step 5: Let your hair cool

After you've curled all of your hair, let it cool for a few minutes. This will help set the curls and make them last longer.

Step 6: Remove the clips

Once your hair has cooled, remove the clips or hair ties and gently run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls. Be careful not to pull too hard, as this can cause frizz.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you make wet hair curly in the morning:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your curls.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much after you've curled it, as this can cause frizz.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques to find the one that works best for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making wet hair curly in the morning:

Q: Can I make my hair curly without using heat?

A: Yes, you can use a variety of techniques to make your hair curly without using heat. Try braiding your hair while it's wet and leaving it in overnight, or using a hair roller system.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: That depends on a variety of factors, including your hair type and the products you use. Generally, you can expect your curls to last for a few hours to a full day.

Q: How can I prevent my curls from falling flat?

A: Use a strong-hold hairspray to help keep your curls in place. You can also use a volumizing spray or powder to add texture and lift to your hair.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use a hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra boost of moisture.


With these tips and techniques, you can make wet hair curly in the morning and get the beautiful, bouncy curls you've always wanted. Try it out and see how it works for you!
