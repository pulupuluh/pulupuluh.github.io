Does Short Hair Suit Long Faces

Description

If you have a long face, you might be wondering if short hair would suit you. The good news is that short hair can look great on long faces, but it all depends on the cut you choose. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short haircuts for long faces and offer tips for maintaining your new look.

How to

If you're considering a short haircut, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, it's important to choose a cut that flatters your face shape. For long faces, this typically means choosing a style that adds width to the sides of your face.

Another thing to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you might want to choose a cut that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you might want to opt for a more streamlined look.

Step by step

Here are some steps to take when choosing a short haircut for a long face:

  1. Research different short haircuts that flatter long faces.
  2. Consider your hair texture and choose a cut that works well with it.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair type.
  4. Maintain your new haircut with regular trims and styling.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for a long face:

  • Choose a cut that adds width to the sides of your face.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a cut.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair type.
  • Choose a cut that is easy to style and maintain.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for long faces:

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look longer?

A: It depends on the cut you choose. Some short haircuts can make a long face look even longer, while others can add width to the sides of your face and create a more balanced look.

Q: What are some good short haircuts for long faces?

A: Some good short haircuts for long faces include a bob, a lob, a pixie cut, and a shaggy bob.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Depending on the length of your hair, you might need to use hairpins or other styling tools to keep it in place.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a short haircut for your long face, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

Remember that everyone's hair is different, and what works for one person might not work for another. If you're unsure about how to care for your new short haircut, consult with a hairstylist for personalized advice.
