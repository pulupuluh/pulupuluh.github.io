Does Short Hair Make You Look Skinnier

Written by Christine Sep 05, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Short hair is a trendy and classic style that can transform your look in a matter of minutes. Many people believe that short hair can make them look skinnier, but is this true? In this article, we will explore if short hair really makes you look slimmer, and provide some tips on how to style short hair to enhance your features.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair can make you look skinnier, but it depends on your face shape and the style of your haircut. If you have a round or square face, short hair can create angles and make your face appear slimmer. On the other hand, if you have a long or oval face, short hair can emphasize the length of your face and make you look thinner.

Moreover, short hair can draw attention to your neck and collarbone, which can create an illusion of a longer and leaner body. Short hair can also make your features stand out, such as your eyes, cheekbones, and jawline, which can create a more defined and sculpted look.

How To

If you want to try short hair to look skinnier, here are some tips on how to style it:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

Before choosing a short haircut, consider your face shape and the features you want to enhance or downplay. For example, if you have a round face, opt for a pixie or a bob with angles and layers that can create a slimming effect. If you have a square face, go for a short shag or a textured bob that can soften your jawline.

2. Add Volume

Adding volume to your short hair can create a fuller and more youthful look. You can use a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray to add texture and lift to your hair. You can also blow-dry your hair upside down to create more volume at the roots.

3. Emphasize Your Features

Short hair can emphasize your best features, such as your eyes, cheekbones, and lips. You can use makeup to enhance your features and create a more polished look. For example, you can use a highlighter to accentuate your cheekbones, or a bold lipstick to draw attention to your lips.

Step By Step

If you want to style your short hair to look skinnier, here are some step-by-step instructions:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and oils. Towel-dry your hair gently, and comb it with a wide-tooth comb to detangle it.

2. Apply a Volumizing Product

Apply a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray to your damp hair, focusing on the roots. Use your fingers to massage the product into your hair, lifting it up and scrunching it for more texture.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. Use your fingers to lift and tousle your hair for more volume and texture. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style.

4. Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron or a straightener to style your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve. For a fuller and bouncier look, use a curling iron to curl your hair away from your face. For a sleek and straight look, use a straightener to smooth your hair and create a sharp angle at the ends.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with a light hairspray to hold the style in place and add shine. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to make short hair look skinnier:

  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your features.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and scarves, to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too voluminous, as they can make your face appear wider or shorter.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair suit my face shape?

A: Short hair can suit any face shape, as long as you choose the right haircut and style for your features. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best short haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Q: Can short hair make me look older?

A: Short hair can make you look more mature and sophisticated, but it depends on the style and the way you wear it. To avoid looking too old, opt for a trendy and youthful haircut that flatters your features.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs proper care to look healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day, or as needed, to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as it can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, short hair can make you look skinnier, but it depends on your face shape, hair type, and the style of your haircut. Choose a short haircut that flatters your features and enhances your best assets. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to create a unique and personalized look. With proper care and styling, short hair can be a chic and versatile hairstyle that suits any occasion.
