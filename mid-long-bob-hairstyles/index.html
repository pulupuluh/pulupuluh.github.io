Mid Long Bob Hairstyles A Trendy And Chic Look

Written by Petter Mar 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish hairstyle, the mid long bob is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is a combination of the classic bob and longer locks, making it perfect for those who want to keep their hair short, but not too short. It's also a great way to add some volume and texture to your hair.

Description

The mid long bob, also known as the "lob," is a type of bob haircut that falls somewhere between the chin and the collarbone. It's a popular and versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to messy and textured. The length of the hair can vary, but it usually falls just below the shoulders. It's a great option for those who want to keep some length in their hair, but still want the benefits of a shorter hairstyle.

How To

Getting a mid long bob is relatively easy, but it's important to find a skilled hairstylist who can give you the look you want. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to the desired length, keeping in mind your face shape and hair texture.
  3. If you want some texture and volume, ask your hairstylist to add some layers to your hair.
  4. Style your hair using a flat iron or curling iron, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  5. Finish off with some hairspray or hair oil to add some shine and hold.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a mid long bob:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Section your hair and use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair the way you want.
  5. Finish off with some hairspray or hair oil to add some shine and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a mid long bob:

  • Choose a length that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Ask your hairstylist to add some layers to your hair to add some texture and volume.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling iron to achieve the look you want.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and textures to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mid long bob hairstyles:

What face shape is best for a mid long bob?

A mid long bob looks great on almost any face shape, but it's particularly flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Can I wear a mid long bob if I have thick hair?

Absolutely! A mid long bob is a great option for those with thick hair, as it can help to reduce some of the weight and create a more manageable style.

Do I need to use heat styling tools to achieve a mid long bob?

No, you don't need to use heat styling tools to achieve a mid long bob. You can achieve a more natural look by air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Hair Care

To keep your mid long bob looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

A mid long bob is a great way to add some style and versatility to your hair. Whether you prefer a sleek and straight look or a messy and textured style, this hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair short, but not too short. With the right care and styling, your mid long bob will look great all year round.
