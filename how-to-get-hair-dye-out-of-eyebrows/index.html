How To Get Hair Dye Out Of Eyebrows A Step By Step Guide

Description

While dyeing your hair can give you a fresh, new look, it can also lead to a common beauty mishap – dyeing your eyebrows. If you accidentally get hair dye on your eyebrows, it can leave them looking mismatched and unnatural. Fortunately, there are ways to remove hair dye from your eyebrows without causing any damage or irritation to the skin around your eyes. Here’s how to get hair dye out of eyebrows.

How To Get Hair Dye Out of Eyebrows

Before you start removing the hair dye, you need to determine the type of dye you used. If you used a temporary or semi-permanent hair dye, it will be easier to remove than a permanent one. Here are some effective methods for removing hair dye from eyebrows:

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Use a Makeup Remover

If you notice the hair dye on your eyebrows immediately after dyeing your hair, you can use a makeup remover to remove it. Apply the makeup remover to a cotton pad and gently rub it on your eyebrows. This should remove the hair dye without damaging the skin around your eyes.

2. Use Petroleum Jelly

If you notice the dye on your eyebrows a few hours after dyeing your hair, you can use petroleum jelly to remove it. Apply a small amount of petroleum jelly on your eyebrows and let it sit for a few minutes. Then, use a cotton ball to gently rub the petroleum jelly off your eyebrows. This should remove the hair dye without causing any irritation to your skin.

3. Use Baking Soda

If the hair dye has already stained your eyebrows, you can use baking soda to remove it. Mix equal parts of baking soda and water to create a paste. Apply the paste to your eyebrows and let it sit for a few minutes. Then, use a damp cloth to wipe the paste off your eyebrows. This should remove the hair dye without causing any irritation to your skin.

Tips for Removing Hair Dye from Eyebrows

Here are some additional tips for removing hair dye from your eyebrows:

  • Always test the method on a small patch of skin before applying it to your eyebrows.
  • Use a gentle, fragrance-free makeup remover or petroleum jelly to avoid irritating the skin around your eyes.
  • Do not use hot water or harsh soaps to remove the hair dye from your eyebrows.
  • If you have sensitive skin, avoid using baking soda as it can cause irritation.

FAQs

Can I use hair dye remover on my eyebrows?

No, you should not use hair dye remover on your eyebrows. Hair dye remover contains harsh chemicals that can damage the skin around your eyes and cause irritation.

Can I use bleach to remove hair dye from my eyebrows?

No, you should not use bleach to remove hair dye from your eyebrows. Bleach is a harsh chemical that can cause severe irritation and damage to the skin around your eyes.

How long does it take for hair dye to fade from eyebrows?

It can take up to a week for hair dye to fade from your eyebrows naturally. However, the methods mentioned above can speed up the process.

Hair Care After Dyeing Your Eyebrows

Here are some tips for caring for your eyebrows after dyeing them:

  • Avoid using any harsh products on your eyebrows, including exfoliants and toners.
  • Avoid rubbing or scratching your eyebrows, as this can cause the dye to fade faster.
  • Avoid swimming, sauna, or any activities that can cause excessive sweating for at least 48 hours after dyeing your eyebrows.

By following these tips, you can safely remove hair dye from your eyebrows and keep them looking natural and healthy.
