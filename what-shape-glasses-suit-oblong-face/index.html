What Shape Glasses Suit Oblong Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 19, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for the perfect glasses that can flatter your oblong face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on what shape glasses suit an oblong face. From how to determine your face shape to the best frames for your features, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s dive in!

What is an Oblong Face Shape?

Before we jump into the best glasses for oblong faces, let’s first understand what an oblong face shape is. An oblong face is characterized by a long and narrow shape with a straight jawline and forehead. This face shape is longer than it is wide, and it may have high cheekbones or a prominent chin.

How to Determine Your Face Shape?

The first step in finding the perfect glasses for your oblong face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by standing in front of a mirror and examining the proportions of your features. Here are a few steps to help you:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Use a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face. Write down the measurements.

Step 2: Identify Your Face Shape

Compare your measurements to the following face shapes:

  • Oval: Length is greater than width, and forehead and jawline are slightly curved.
  • Round: Length and width are almost equal, and the chin is rounded.
  • Square: Length, width, and jawline have similar measurements, and the forehead is straight.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the jawline, and the chin is pointy.
  • Diamond: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the forehead and jawline are narrow.

Tips for Choosing Glasses for an Oblong Face

Now that you’ve determined that you have an oblong face shape, it’s time to choose the perfect glasses. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose glasses that are wider than your face to add balance.
  • Look for frames with a deep bridge to shorten the nose.
  • Avoid small or narrow frames that accentuate the length of your face.
  • Choose frames with curves or round edges to soften your features.
  • Consider glasses with decorative temples or details to draw attention to the sides of your face.

Best Glasses for Oblong Face

Now that you know what to look for in glasses for an oblong face, here are some of the best styles to consider:

1. Round Glasses

Round glasses are a great option for oblong faces as they add balance and soften your features. Look for frames with a wide bridge and round lenses to create a flattering look.

2. Cat-Eye Glasses

Cat-eye glasses are another great option for oblong faces. The upswept corners of the frames create a flattering contrast to the straight lines of your face. Look for cat-eye glasses with wider frames to balance your features.

3. Square Glasses

While it may seem counterintuitive, square glasses can actually work well for oblong faces. Look for frames with rounded edges or a softer shape to balance your features.

4. Wayfarer Glasses

Wayfarer glasses are a classic style that can work well for oblong faces. Look for frames with a wide bridge and round lenses to create a flattering look. You can also consider glasses with a pop of color or pattern to add interest to your look.

FAQ

What glasses should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

Avoid small or narrow frames that accentuate the length of your face. Also, avoid glasses with a flat or straight top as they can make your face look even longer.

Can I wear oversized glasses if I have an oblong face?

Absolutely! Oversized glasses can work well for oblong faces if they are wide enough to balance your features. Look for frames with rounded edges or a softer shape to balance your features.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Face

Your hairstyle can also make a big difference in how your glasses look on your oblong face. Here are some hair care tips to consider:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Consider hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layers, waves, or curls.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can further elongate your face shape.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or bangs to add balance to your features.

Now that you know what shape glasses suit oblong face, you can confidently choose the perfect frames to flatter your features. Remember to keep in mind the tips we’ve shared and try on different styles to find the perfect match. With the right glasses and hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your look.
