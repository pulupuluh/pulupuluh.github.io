How To Get Shaggy Look Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that's both trendy and easy to achieve, the shaggy look might be just what you need. This hairstyle is characterized by messy, layered locks that give off a carefree vibe. In this post, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get shaggy look hair, along with some tips and FAQs to help you perfect this look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Shaggy Look Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Shaggy Look Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Shaggy Look Hair

How to Get Shaggy Look Hair

The shaggy look is all about messy, textured layers. To achieve this hairstyle, you'll need to cut your hair in a way that creates lots of layers throughout. You can also add some texture and volume by using styling products like mousse or texture spray.

Choosing the Right Cut

The first step in getting shaggy look hair is choosing the right cut. This style works best on medium to long hair, so if your hair is currently shorter, you may need to wait a bit before trying this look.

When you're ready to get your hair cut, ask your stylist for lots of layers throughout. You can also ask for some face-framing layers to add some dimension to your look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have the right cut, it's time to style your hair into the shaggy look. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of mousse or texture spray to your hair to add some volume and texture.
  3. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Focus on creating volume at the crown of your head and near your roots.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves throughout your hair. Don't worry about making the waves too perfect - the shaggy look is all about messiness!
  5. Use your fingers to separate the waves and create a messy, tousled look.
  6. Finish off with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Shaggy Look Hair

The shaggy look is a low-maintenance style, but there are a few things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to help absorb oil and add some texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a brush can create frizz and mess up your texture.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about the shaggy look:

Is the shaggy look good for all hair types?

Yes! The shaggy look can work for all hair types, although it will look a bit different depending on your hair's texture and thickness.

How often should I get my hair cut to maintain the shaggy look?

It's a good idea to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shaggy look and keep your layers looking fresh.

Hair Care for Shaggy Look Hair

To keep your shaggy look hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and a bit of practice, you'll be able to master the shaggy look and rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence!
