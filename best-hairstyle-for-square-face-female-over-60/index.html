Best Hairstyle For Square Face Female Over 60

Written by Luffy Aug 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose its luster. This can be especially challenging for women over 60 who have a square face shape. But don't worry, there are plenty of hairstyles that can accentuate your best features and make you look and feel fabulous. In this article, we'll discuss the best hairstyle for square face female over 60.

Description

The square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and a wide forehead. The goal is to soften these features and create balance. The best hairstyles for this face shape are those that add volume to the crown and sides, and have layers that frame the face. It's also important to avoid hairstyles that accentuate the squareness of the face, such as blunt bangs or straight hair that falls flat against the face.

How To

To achieve the best hairstyle for square face female over 60, you'll want to focus on creating volume and texture. This can be achieved with layers and curls. Here's how:

Step 1: Consult with a hairstylist

A hairstylist can help you determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type. They can also offer styling tips and product recommendations. Be sure to bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your consultation.

Step 2: Add layers

Layers can add volume and movement to your hair. A layered haircut that frames the face can soften a square jawline. Avoid blunt cuts that make the face look boxy.

Step 3: Create curls or waves

Curls or waves can add volume and texture to your hair. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls or waves. Avoid tight curls that can make your face look even more square.

Step 4: Use volumizing products

Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root lifter, to add volume to the crown and sides of your hair. Apply the product to damp hair and blow dry with a round brush for added volume.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving the best hairstyle for square face female over 60:

  • Avoid straight, flat hair that falls against the face.
  • Consider a side-swept bang or a long, face-framing fringe.
  • Experiment with different lengths to find what looks best on you.
  • Accessorize with headbands or hair clips to add interest and volume.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for square face female over 60:

What hairstyles should I avoid?

Avoid blunt cuts, straight hair that falls flat against the face, and tight curls that accentuate the squareness of the face.

What hairstyles are best for thinning hair?

Layers are great for adding volume to thinning hair. Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of teasing or styling, as this can damage the hair.

What hair color is best for a square face?

Warm, rich colors can soften a square jawline. Consider shades of brown, red, or blonde with warm undertones.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often as they can damage the hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for square face female over 60, it's time to book that hair appointment! Remember, the key is to add volume and texture to soften your features and create balance. With the right hairstyle and hair care regimen, you'll be turning heads in no time.
