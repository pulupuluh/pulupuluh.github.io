Which Bikini Bottom Is Most Flattering A Guide For 2023

Written by Petter Aug 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

When it comes to dressing for the beach, finding the perfect bikini can be a daunting task. Choosing the right bikini bottom is especially important, as it can make or break your beach look. In this guide, we'll explore which bikini bottom is the most flattering for your body type and provide tips on how to style it.

Determining Your Body Type

The first step in finding the most flattering bikini bottom is to determine your body type. Here are the four main body types and the bikini bottoms that are most flattering for each:

Hourglass

If you have an hourglass figure, you have a defined waist and curves in all the right places. High-waisted bikini bottoms are the most flattering for this body type, as they accentuate your waist and elongate your legs.

Pear

If you have a pear-shaped figure, you have a smaller upper body and wider hips and thighs. Cheeky bikini bottoms are the most flattering for this body type, as they draw attention to your bottom and balance out your proportions.

Apple

If you have an apple-shaped figure, you carry weight around your midsection. Brazilian bikini bottoms are the most flattering for this body type, as they provide coverage while still showing off your legs.

Straight

If you have a straight figure, your waist is not well-defined and your hips and bust are about the same size. String bikini bottoms are the most flattering for this body type, as they create the illusion of curves.

High Waisted Bikini Bottoms

High waisted bikini bottoms have been a popular trend for several years, and for good reason. They are incredibly flattering for a variety of body types and provide ample coverage while still being stylish. If you have an hourglass figure, high waisted bikini bottoms will accentuate your waist and elongate your legs. They can also be flattering for apple-shaped figures, as they provide coverage around the midsection.

When styling high waisted bikini bottoms, opt for a bikini top that has a similar vibe. For example, a crop top or bralette-style bikini top will complement the retro vibe of high waisted bottoms.

Cheeky Bikini Bottoms

If you have a pear-shaped figure, cheeky bikini bottoms are the way to go. They draw attention to your bottom and balance out your proportions. Cheeky bikini bottoms are also a great option if you want to show off a bit more skin without going for a full-on thong.

When styling cheeky bikini bottoms, opt for a bikini top that has a bit more coverage. A bandeau or triangle bikini top will balance out the cheekiness of the bottoms.

Brazilian Bikini Bottoms

Brazilian bikini bottoms are perfect for apple-shaped figures, as they provide coverage while still showing off your legs. They are also a great option if you want to show off your curves. Brazilian bikini bottoms have a low-rise cut and minimal coverage in the back, so they are not the best option if you're looking for more coverage.

When styling Brazilian bikini bottoms, opt for a bikini top that has a bit more coverage. A halter or bandeau bikini top will balance out the minimal coverage of the bottoms.

String Bikini Bottoms

If you have a straight figure, string bikini bottoms are the way to go. They create the illusion of curves and add a playful touch to your beach look. String bikini bottoms have minimal coverage, so they are not the best option if you're looking for more coverage.

When styling string bikini bottoms, opt for a bikini top that has a bit more coverage. A triangle or bandeau bikini top will balance out the minimal coverage of the bottoms.

Tips for Flattering Bikini Bottoms

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a flattering bikini bottom:

  • Try on bikinis in natural light to get a better idea of how they look on your skin tone.
  • Invest in high-quality swimwear that will last for several seasons.
  • Accessorize your beach look with a hat, sunglasses, and jewelry to add some flair.
  • Consider the activities you'll be doing at the beach and choose a bikini bottom that will stay in place.

FAQs

What if I have a different body type than the ones listed?

While the four body types listed are the most common, everyone's body is unique. Try on different bikini bottoms to see what works best for your body type and personal style.

Can I mix and match bikini tops and bottoms?

Absolutely! Mixing and matching bikini tops and bottoms is a great way to create a personalized beach look.

Hair Care Tips for the Beach

Finally, don't forget to take care of your hair at the beach. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Apply a leave-in conditioner before hitting the beach to protect your hair from the sun and saltwater.
  • Wear a hat to protect your scalp from the sun.
  • Rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming in saltwater to remove any salt buildup.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair at the beach, as the heat can damage your strands.

Now that you know which bikini bottom is most flattering for your body type, it's time to hit the beach and show off your style!
