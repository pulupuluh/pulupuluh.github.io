Men s Hair Short Sides Long Back A Trendy Hairstyle In 2023

Description

If you're looking for a trendy hairstyle in 2023, then men's hair short sides long back is for you. This hairstyle is also known as the "mullet" and has been around for decades. However, it has made a comeback in recent years and has become a popular choice among men who want to stand out with a unique and edgy look.

The hairstyle features short hair on the sides and long hair at the back. It is versatile and can be styled in various ways, making it perfect for different occasions. Whether you're going to a formal event or a casual gathering, this hairstyle will make you look stylish and confident.

How to Achieve Men's Hair Short Sides Long Back

To achieve the men's hair short sides long back hairstyle, you need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to choose the right length for your hair. The sides should be shorter than the back, and the length of the back should depend on your preference. You can opt for a longer back if you want a more dramatic look, or a shorter back if you want a subtle one.

Step 2: Find a Skilled Barber

It's important to find a skilled barber who can give you the right cut. You can bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to show your barber so that they can have a better idea of what you're looking for.

Step 3: Maintain the Style

Once you've achieved the men's hair short sides long back hairstyle, it's essential to maintain it regularly. You need to trim the sides every few weeks to keep them short, and you can trim the back to maintain the length.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the men's hair short sides long back hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will make your hair clean and easier to work with.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to dry your hair until it's slightly damp. Don't dry it completely, as it's easier to work with when it's damp.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair on the side. Then, comb the sides down and the back downwards.

Step 4: Trim the Sides

Use clippers with a guard to trim the sides to the desired length. Start with a higher guard and work your way down until you achieve the length you want.

Step 5: Trim the Back

Use scissors to trim the back to the desired length. You can ask your barber to help you with this step if you're not confident in doing it yourself.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Apply hair wax or pomade to your hair to style it. You can slick it back for a formal look or leave it messy for a casual look.

Tips for Maintaining Men's Hair Short Sides Long Back

Here are some tips for maintaining the men's hair short sides long back hairstyle:

  • Trim the sides regularly to keep them short.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair with hair wax or pomade to keep it in place.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer, as it can damage your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQs about Men's Hair Short Sides Long Back

Q: Is the men's hair short sides long back hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the hairstyle is versatile and can be adjusted to suit different face shapes.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, you can style your hair differently for different occasions. You can slick it back for a formal event or leave it messy for a casual gathering.

Q: Is it easy to maintain the hairstyle?

A: Yes, it's easy to maintain the hairstyle. You need to trim the sides regularly and use good quality hair products to style it.

Hair Care for Men's Hair Short Sides Long Back

Here are some hair care tips for men's hair short sides long back:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer, as it can damage your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply hair wax or pomade to your hair to style it.
  • Trim the sides regularly to keep them short.

Men's hair short sides long back is a trendy hairstyle that's perfect for those who want to stand out with a unique and edgy look. It's versatile and can be styled in various ways, making it suitable for different occasions. By following the steps and tips mentioned above, you can achieve and maintain the hairstyle with ease.
