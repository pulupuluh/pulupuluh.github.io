How To Tell If Short Hair Will Suit Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William May 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short hair is a bold and daring choice that can make a statement about your personality and style. However, it can be daunting to take the plunge and chop off your locks. How do you know if short hair will suit you? In this guide, we will provide you with tips and tricks to help you determine if short hair is the right choice for you.

  • Considering Your Hair Type

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in deciding if short hair will suit you is to determine your face shape. The shape of your face can greatly influence how a particular haircut will look on you. There are six main face shapes: round, square, heart, oval, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a look in the mirror.

If you have a round face, short hair can help to elongate your face. A square face can be softened with layers and texture. Heart-shaped faces look great with short bobs or pixie cuts. Oval faces can pull off almost any short haircut. Diamond-shaped faces can benefit from a short haircut that adds volume at the top. Oblong faces look great with a short haircut that adds width to the sides.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider when deciding if short hair will suit you is your hair type. If you have thick hair, a short haircut can be a great way to manage the weight and make styling easier. If you have thin hair, a shorter cut can make your hair appear fuller. If you have curly hair, a short haircut can help to define your curls.

Assessing Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in determining if short hair will suit you. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, a short haircut can be a great option. If you work in a professional environment, a short, polished bob can look chic and sophisticated. If you're an active person who enjoys sports or outdoor activities, a short haircut can be low maintenance and easy to care for.

Options for Short Haircuts

If you've determined that short hair will suit you, there are a variety of options to choose from. A pixie cut is a short, edgy haircut that can be styled in a variety of ways. A bob is a classic, timeless style that can be worn straight or with waves. A lob, or long bob, is a slightly longer version of the bob that is still considered a short haircut. A shag haircut is a layered, textured cut that can add volume and movement to your hair.

Styling Tips for Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy with the right tools and products. A texturizing spray can add volume and texture to your hair. A flat iron can be used to create sleek, straight styles. A curling iron can be used to add waves or curls to your hair. A dry shampoo can be used to refresh your hair between washes. Experiment with different styles to find what works for you.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks can prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will short hair make my face look wider?

It depends on your face shape and the style of the haircut. A skilled stylist can create a haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your features.

Can I still pull my hair back with a short haircut?

Depending on the length of your hair, you may be able to pull your hair back into a ponytail or bun. If your hair is too short, you may need to use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair.

What should I do if I don't like my short haircut?

Speak to your stylist about your concerns. They may be able to make adjustments to the haircut or provide tips for styling your hair in a way that you like.

Short hair can be a bold and exciting choice that can enhance your features and reflect your personality. Use these tips and tricks to determine if short hair will suit you and find the perfect haircut for your style and lifestyle.
