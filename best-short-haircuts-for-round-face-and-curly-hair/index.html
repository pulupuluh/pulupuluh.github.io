Best Short Haircuts For Round Face And Curly Hair In 2023

Written by Daniel Jun 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for the best short haircuts for round face and curly hair? Look no further because we’ve got you covered. This article will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best short haircuts for round face and curly hair, including tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts are perfect for round face and curly hair as they give your face shape and make your curls more defined. However, finding the right haircut can be a challenge, especially if you’re not familiar with the latest trends. The right haircut can make all the difference in how you look and feel, so it’s important to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for round face and curly hair, you need to consider the length, shape, and texture of your hair. Look for a style that will help elongate your face and balance out your features. Shorter haircuts with layers and texture work well for curly hair as they add volume and movement to your curls. Avoid blunt cuts as they can make your face appear rounder.

Step by step

1. Determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the widest part of your face and compare it to the length of your face. If the measurements are similar, you have a round face shape.

2. Look for inspiration. Browse through magazines or online galleries to find short haircuts that you like. Pay attention to the length, texture, and shape of the haircuts.

3. Consult with your hair stylist. Your hair stylist can help you choose the right short haircut for your face shape and hair type. They can also recommend hair products that will help you maintain your new haircut.

Tips

1. Avoid bangs. Bangs can make your face appear shorter and rounder. Instead, opt for a side part or a layered cut that frames your face.

2. Use hair products that enhance your curls. Curly hair can be prone to frizz and dryness, so it’s important to use hair products that will keep your curls defined and moisturized.

3. Embrace your natural texture. Short haircuts work well for curly hair as they showcase your natural texture. Avoid straightening your curls as this can damage your hair and make it look unnatural.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have a short haircut if I have a round face and straight hair?

A: Yes, you can still have a short haircut if you have a round face and straight hair. Look for a style that will elongate your face and add volume to your hair.

Q: How often should I get my haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your hair loses its shape. Generally, short haircuts require more frequent maintenance, so you may need to get your haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it’s important to use products that are specifically formulated for curly hair. Look for products that contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible as they can damage your hair and make your curls frizzy. Instead, air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

In conclusion, finding the best short haircut for round face and curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and advice, it’s possible. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a short haircut. Embrace your natural texture and use hair care products that will keep your curls defined and moisturized.
