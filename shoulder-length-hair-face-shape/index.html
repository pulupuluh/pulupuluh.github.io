Shoulder Length Hair Face Shape A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi May 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that is both versatile and flattering, shoulder length hair is a great option. Not only is it easy to maintain, but it can be styled in many different ways to suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about shoulder length hair face shape, including how to determine your face shape, which hairstyles are best suited for each face shape, and how to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for shoulder length hair, it's important to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, look in a mirror and take note of the widest part of your face, the length of your face, and the shape of your jawline.

If the widest part of your face is at your cheeks and you have a rounded chin, you have a round face shape. If your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and a slightly pointed chin, you have an oval face shape. If your face is roughly equal in width and length, with a strong jawline and square forehead, you have a square face shape. If your forehead is wider than your cheeks and your chin is pointed, you have a heart-shaped face. If your cheekbones are wider than your forehead and chin, with a pointed chin, you have a diamond-shaped face. If your face is longer than it is wide, with a pointy chin and a high forehead, you have a long face shape.

Best Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Each Face Shape

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length, such as a long bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

Oval Face Shape

Those with an oval face shape can pull off just about any hairstyle, including shoulder length hair. If you have an oval face, consider a shoulder-length cut with layers or a side-swept fringe to add dimension to your face.

Square Face Shape

For square face shapes, hairstyles that soften the sharp angles of the face work best. Try a shoulder-length cut with layers and soft waves, or a deep side part with side-swept bangs.

Heart Face Shape

Heart-shaped faces look great with shoulder-length hairstyles that frame the face, such as a lob with side-swept bangs or a layered cut with face-framing layers.

Diamond Face Shape

For diamond-shaped faces, hairstyles that add width to the forehead and chin work well. Consider a shoulder-length cut with layers and a side-swept fringe, or a bob with soft waves.

Long Face Shape

If you have a long face shape, avoid hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head. Instead, opt for shoulder-length hairstyles with layers and soft waves, or a long bob with side-swept bangs.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Shoulder Length Hair

Now that you've found the perfect shoulder-length hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray first.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it is more susceptible to damage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I still wear my hair up with shoulder length hair?

Absolutely! Shoulder-length hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, including updos and ponytails.

What is the difference between a lob and a bob?

A lob is a longer version of a bob, typically falling just above or below the shoulders. A bob is a shorter, chin-length cut.

Can I dye my hair if it's shoulder length?

Yes, you can dye your hair if it's shoulder length. However, it's important to take proper care of your hair afterwards to prevent damage.

Conclusion

Shoulder length hair is a great option for those looking for a versatile and flattering hairstyle. By determining your face shape and choosing a hairstyle that complements it, you can create a look that is both stylish and flattering. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles!
