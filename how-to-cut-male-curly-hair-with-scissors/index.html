How To Cut Male Curly Hair With Scissors A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank May 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a male with curly hair, then you know how hard it can be to find a haircut that works for you. Many men with curly hair opt for buzz cuts or short styles because they believe it's easier to manage, but that's not necessarily the case. In fact, with the right technique and tools, cutting curly hair can be a breeze. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut male curly hair with scissors in a relaxed English language.

How to Cut Male Curly Hair with Scissors

Cutting curly hair requires a different technique than cutting straight hair. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to wash and dry the hair. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup that could interfere with the cutting process. Once the hair is clean, gently towel dry or use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to dry the hair.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Next, section the hair into manageable pieces. Use hair clips or ties to keep the sections separate. This will make it easier to cut the hair evenly and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Using sharp scissors, start cutting the hair in the direction of the curl. It's important to cut the hair when it's dry and in its natural state, as wet hair can stretch and give you an uneven cut.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting a section, check for evenness by comparing it to the other sections. If there are any uneven areas, trim them until the hair is even.

Step 5: Finish with Detailing

Once you've finished cutting all the sections, use the scissors to detail any areas that need extra attention. This could include the sideburns, nape of the neck, or around the ears.

Tips for Cutting Male Curly Hair with Scissors

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors specifically designed for cutting curly hair.
  • Use a light hand when cutting to avoid creating choppy or uneven layers.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more hair as needed.
  • Take breaks as needed to avoid fatigue or cramping in your hands.

FAQ

Can I cut my own curly hair with scissors?

Yes, you can cut your own curly hair with scissors, but it's important to take your time and follow the steps outlined above. It may also be helpful to have a friend or family member assist you with the process.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

The frequency of haircuts depends on your personal preference and the length of your hair. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Hair Care for Male Curly Hair

In addition to regular haircuts, it's important to take good care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized and defined.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.

By following these tips and the steps outlined in this guide, you can achieve a great haircut for your curly hair.
