Best Way To Cut A Man s Hair With Scissors

Written by Aprilia Apr 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of going to the hair salon every month to get a haircut? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair with scissors? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss the best way to cut a man's hair with scissors. We will cover everything from the equipment you need to the step-by-step process of cutting your hair. So, let's get started.

Equipment Needed

The first thing you need to do is to gather all the necessary equipment. Here is a list of things you will need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clipper (optional)
  • Hair cutting cape or towel
  • Hair cutting shears (optional)

Make sure that your scissors are sharp and clean. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it look uneven.

How to Cut a Man's Hair with Scissors

Cutting a man's hair with scissors can be challenging, but it is not impossible. The key is to have patience and take your time. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash your hair and let it dry completely. Wet hair is difficult to cut, and it can make your hair look shorter than you intended.

Step 2: Put on your hair cutting cape or towel to avoid getting hair all over your clothes.

Step 3: Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Use hair clipper to trim the sides and back of your hair. You can use a number 3 or 4 guard size for a short haircut. If you want a longer haircut, you can use a number 5 or 6 guard size.

Step 5: Use scissors to cut the top of your hair. Start by cutting a small section of your hair and then trim the rest slowly. You can use your fingers to measure the length of your hair.

Step 6: Blend the sides and top of your hair using scissors. This will give your hair a natural look.

Step 7: Trim the hair around your ears and neck using scissors or hair clipper.

Step 8: Check your hair in the mirror to see if it is even. If it is not, you can trim the uneven parts again.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Start with a longer length and trim your hair slowly.
  • Use a comb to separate the hair and cut it evenly.
  • Trim the hair around your ears and neck carefully.
  • Check your hair in the mirror to see if it is even.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair with scissors?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with scissors. However, it can be challenging, and it requires patience and practice.

What size scissors should I use to cut men's hair?

You should use hair cutting scissors that are between 5 and 7 inches long.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, you should wash it with shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any loose hair and dirt. You should also apply a hair styling product to give your hair a polished look.

Cutting your own hair with scissors can be a daunting task, but with patience and practice, you can achieve a great haircut. Follow these tips, and you will be able to cut your hair like a pro. Good luck!
