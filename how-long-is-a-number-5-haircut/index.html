How Long Is A Number 5 Haircut Everything You Need To Know

Written by Aprilia Jul 14, 2023 · 7 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, there are often different numbers involved that can be confusing. One of the most commonly asked questions is, "How long is a number 5 haircut?" In this blog post, we'll break down everything you need to know about this popular haircut length.

Description

A number 5 haircut refers to the clipper guard that is used to trim the hair. The number 5 guard is 5/8 of an inch, or 16mm long. This means that a number 5 haircut will leave the hair 5/8 of an inch long all around the head.

How To

If you want to get a number 5 haircut, you can either do it yourself or go to a barber or hairdresser. Here's how to do it:

DIY

To give yourself a number 5 haircut, you'll need a set of hair clippers with a number 5 guard attachment. Follow these steps:

  1. Start with dry, clean hair.
  2. Attach the number 5 guard to your clippers.
  3. Starting at the bottom of your head, run the clippers up through your hair, using the guard to ensure an even length all around.
  4. Continue working your way up your head, section by section, until all your hair has been trimmed to the desired length.

Barber/Hairdresser

If you prefer to have a professional do it for you, simply ask for a number 5 haircut at the salon or barber shop. Your stylist will use clippers with a number 5 guard to trim your hair to the desired length.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a number 5 haircut:

  1. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly.
  2. Choose a clipper guard size. For a number 5 haircut, use a 5/8 inch guard.
  3. Attach the guard to your clippers.
  4. Starting at the nape of your neck, run the clippers up towards the crown of your head. Repeat until all the hair on the back and sides of your head is trimmed to the same length.
  5. Move to the top of your head and trim your hair to the desired length using the same clipper guard.
  6. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that were missed by the clippers.
  7. Clean up any stray hairs with a trimmer around the ears and neckline.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a number 5 haircut:

  • Always start with clean, dry hair to get the most even cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head for any missed spots.
  • If you're doing it yourself, go slow and steady to avoid making any mistakes.
  • If you're unsure about the length, start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter length.

FAQ

Is a number 5 haircut short?

Yes, a number 5 haircut is considered a short haircut. It will leave the hair 5/8 of an inch long all around the head.

What other clipper guard sizes are there?

Clipper guard sizes range from 0 (no guard) to 8 (1 inch guard). Each guard size will leave a different length of hair. The smaller the guard, the shorter the hair will be.

Can I get a number 5 haircut with scissors?

Technically, yes, but it would be difficult to achieve an even length all around the head without using clippers.

Hair Care

After getting a number 5 haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the length and style. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a styling product, such as gel or pomade, to keep your hair in place.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to maintain the length.

Now that you know how long a number 5 haircut is, you can decide if it's the right length for you. Whether you do it yourself or go to a professional, a number 5 haircut can be a stylish and low-maintenance option for men and women alike.
