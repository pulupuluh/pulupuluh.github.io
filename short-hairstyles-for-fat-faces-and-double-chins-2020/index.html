Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins 2020 A Guide

Written by Bardi May 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face or a double chin, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. The right haircut can help slim down your face and draw attention away from your chin. In this guide, we will explore some of the best short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2020.

Description

Short hairstyles are a great option for anyone with a round face or a double chin. They can help create the illusion of a slimmer face and draw attention to your best features. Whether you prefer a pixie cut, a bob, or something in between, there is a short hairstyle that will flatter your face shape and enhance your natural beauty.

How To

The key to finding the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape is to choose a cut that flatters your features and complements your personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the perfect short hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are four main face shapes: round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. If you have a round face, your cheeks are the widest part of your face, and your jawline is round. If you have a double chin, you may also have a round face shape.

Step 2: Choose Your Haircut

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose your haircut. Here are some of the best short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2020:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, layered haircut that is perfect for anyone with a round face or a double chin. This cut is low maintenance and can be styled in a variety of ways. To create a more dramatic look, add some texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or wax.

Bob

A bob is another great option for anyone with a round face or a double chin. This cut is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. To create a more modern look, try a choppy bob with layers.

Pixie Bob

A pixie bob is a short, layered haircut that combines the best of both worlds. This cut is perfect for anyone who wants a short, low maintenance hairstyle that still has some volume and texture. To create a more edgy look, try adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair.

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to keep in mind your personal style and the texture of your hair. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

- Consider your face shape and choose a cut that flatters your features.

- Choose a cut that works with your hair texture.

- Look for inspiration in magazines and online.

- Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect cut for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a round face or double chin?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a round face or double chin. However, it's important to choose a cut that flatters your face shape and draws attention away from your chin. Consider adding layers or face-framing highlights to create a more flattering look.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: The frequency of haircuts depends on your hair type and personal preference. In general, most people should get their hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Whether you have a short or long hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

- Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

- Protect your hair from the sun and heat with a hat or scarf.

With these tips and the right short hairstyle, you can feel confident and beautiful no matter your face shape or chin size. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a cut that makes you feel good about yourself.
