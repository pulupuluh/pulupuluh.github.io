How To Make Your Hair Curly Forever Without A Perm

Written by Eveline Jul 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of using heat styling tools and chemical treatments to achieve curly hair? Do you want to make your hair permanently curly without damaging it? If so, you are in the right place. In this article, we will show you how to make your hair curly forever without a perm.

Description

Many people assume that the only way to get permanent curls is by getting a perm. However, getting a perm can be damaging to your hair and can cause breakage and dryness. Fortunately, there are natural ways to make your hair curly without using chemicals or heat styling tools.

How To

1. Twist-Out Method

The twist-out method is a great way to create defined curls without using heat or chemicals. To do this, you will need to wash and condition your hair as usual. Once your hair is damp, divide it into small sections and twist each section tightly. Allow your hair to air dry overnight, or for several hours. Once your hair is completely dry, unravel the twists and separate the curls with your fingers. You can use a curl defining cream or gel to enhance the curls and reduce frizz.

2. Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are another natural way to create curls. To do this, you will need to wash and condition your hair as usual. Once your hair is damp, divide it into small sections and twist each section tightly. Wrap the twisted section around itself to create a small knot. Repeat this process until all of your hair is in knots. Allow your hair to air dry overnight, or for several hours. Once your hair is completely dry, unravel the knots and separate the curls with your fingers.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner. This will help to moisturize your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to apply products and style your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product, such as a curl defining cream or gel, to each section of your hair. This will help to create defined curls and reduce frizz.

Step 4: Twist or Knot Your Hair

Using either the twist-out or bantu knot method, twist or knot each section of your hair. Make sure to twist or knot tightly to create defined curls.

Step 5: Allow Your Hair to Air Dry

Allow your hair to air dry overnight or for several hours. This will give your curls time to set and create a natural curl pattern.

Step 6: Separate Your Curls

Once your hair is completely dry, unravel the twists or knots and separate the curls with your fingers. This will create a more natural and voluminous look.

Tips

- Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. This will reduce frizz and breakage.

- Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing heat damage.

- Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and keep your curls intact.

FAQ

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: Your curls should last several days to a week, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Q: Can I use this method on all hair types?

A: Yes, the twist-out and bantu knot methods can be used on all hair types to create natural curls.

Q: Will this method work on color-treated hair?

A: Yes, this method can be used on color-treated hair. However, be sure to use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to maintain a healthy hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

By following these tips and using natural methods to create curls, you can achieve beautiful, healthy, and permanent curls without the use of damaging chemicals or heat styling tools.
