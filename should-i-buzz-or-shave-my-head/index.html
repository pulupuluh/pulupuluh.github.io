Should I Buzz Or Shave My Head

Written by Aprilia Jun 19, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you contemplating whether to buzz or shave your head? It's a big decision that requires careful consideration. In this article, we'll explore the pros and cons of each option, offer tips on how to do it, and provide hair care advice to help you maintain your new look. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Pros and Cons of Buzzing and Shaving
  • How to Buzz or Shave Your Head
  • Tips for Buzzing or Shaving Your Head
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Buzzed or Shaved Heads

Pros and Cons of Buzzing and Shaving

The first thing to consider when deciding whether to buzz or shave your head is the pros and cons of each option. Here are a few to keep in mind:

Pros of Buzzing Your Head

  • Low maintenance
  • Quick and easy to do at home
  • Cool and edgy look

Cons of Buzzing Your Head

  • May not suit all face shapes
  • May highlight imperfections on your scalp
  • May require frequent touch-ups to maintain the look

Pros of Shaving Your Head

  • Bald heads are associated with confidence and strength
  • Low maintenance
  • No need for frequent touch-ups

Cons of Shaving Your Head

  • May not suit all face shapes
  • May highlight imperfections on your scalp
  • May take longer to get used to the new look

How to Buzz or Shave Your Head

If you've decided to buzz or shave your head, here's a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

How to Buzz Your Head

  1. Choose the right clipper guard for your desired length
  2. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward
  3. Go over your head in different directions to ensure an even cut
  4. Use a mirror to check for any missed spots

How to Shave Your Head

  1. Use clippers to trim your hair as short as possible
  2. Take a warm shower to soften your hair and open your pores
  3. Apply shaving cream to your head
  4. Use a razor to shave your head in the direction of hair growth
  5. Rinse your head with cool water to close your pores

Tips for Buzzing or Shaving Your Head

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect buzz or shave:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to your desired length
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head
  • Take your time to avoid any cuts or nicks
  • Use aftershave balm or lotion to soothe your scalp after shaving
  • Wear sunscreen or a hat to protect your scalp from the sun

Frequently Asked Questions

Is buzzing or shaving better for hair growth?

Neither option affects hair growth. Your hair will continue to grow at its normal rate after buzzing or shaving.

Will buzzing or shaving make my hair grow back thicker?

No, this is a common myth. Buzzing or shaving does not change the texture or thickness of your hair.

Can I buzz or shave my head if I have dandruff?

Yes, but be gentle when shaving and avoid using a razor if you have severe dandruff. Use a moisturizing shampoo to help prevent dryness and flakiness.

Hair Care for Buzzed or Shaved Heads

Now that you've buzzed or shaved your head, it's important to take care of your scalp to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your scalp with a gentle shampoo
  • Moisturize your scalp with a lightweight oil or lotion
  • Avoid using harsh or drying hair products
  • Wear a hat or use sunscreen to protect your scalp from the sun

So, should you buzz or shave your head? Ultimately, the decision is up to you and what you feel most comfortable with. Just remember to take your time, follow our tips, and take care of your scalp to keep it looking and feeling great.
