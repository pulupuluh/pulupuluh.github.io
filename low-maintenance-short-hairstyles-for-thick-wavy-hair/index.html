Low Maintenance Short Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Daniel Mar 31, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know how challenging it can be to maintain it. The good news is that there are plenty of short hairstyles that are low maintenance and perfect for thick wavy hair. In this article, we will explore some of the best low maintenance short hairstyles for thick wavy hair, how to achieve them, and tips on how to care for your hair.

How to Achieve Low Maintenance Short Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

The first step to achieving a low maintenance short hairstyle for thick wavy hair is to find the right haircut. You want to choose a haircut that works with your natural waves and is easy to style. Some popular options include a bob, pixie cut, or shaggy lob.

Once you have the right haircut, you can focus on styling your hair in a way that is low maintenance. The key is to embrace your natural waves and use products that enhance them. Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair and require more maintenance.

Step-by-Step Guide to Low Maintenance Short Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving a low maintenance short hairstyle for thick wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling cream or mousse to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your natural waves.
  4. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to create texture and volume.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Low Maintenance Short Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your low maintenance short hairstyle for thick wavy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and frizzy.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can damage your hair and require more maintenance.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and humidity by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

FAQs about Low Maintenance Short Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

Q: Can I still straighten my hair if I have a low maintenance short hairstyle for thick wavy hair?

A: While it's best to avoid using hot tools on your hair, you can still straighten your hair occasionally. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid applying too much heat to your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my hair if I have a low maintenance short hairstyle for thick wavy hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I color my hair if I have a low maintenance short hairstyle for thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can color your hair, but it's important to choose a color that works with your natural hair color and skin tone. Talk to your stylist about the best options for your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for thick wavy hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a low maintenance short hairstyle for thick wavy hair that looks great and requires minimal upkeep. Remember to choose a haircut that works with your natural waves and use products that enhance your hair's texture and volume. With a little bit of effort, you can have healthy, beautiful, and low maintenance hair that you love!
