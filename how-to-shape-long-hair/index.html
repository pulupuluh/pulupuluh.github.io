How To Shape Long Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jul 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have long hair, you know how versatile it can be. From braids to updos, the options are endless. However, sometimes you just want to wear your hair down and show off your natural length. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to shape long hair so that it looks its best.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Shape Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Shaping Long Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Shape Long Hair

The key to shaping long hair is to create layers. Layers add volume and movement to the hair, making it easier to style. You can either go to a professional stylist to get your hair cut or you can do it yourself at home. Here's how:

Option 1: Professional Stylist

If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, it's best to go to a professional stylist. They will be able to create the perfect layers for your hair type and face shape. Make sure to communicate your desired length and style with your stylist before they start cutting.

Option 2: DIY

If you're feeling brave, you can cut your own hair at home. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  3. Take a small section of hair at the back and cut off the desired length. Use this section as a guide for the rest of your hair.
  4. Use scissors to create long layers. Start at the bottom and work your way up.
  5. Blend the layers by cutting diagonally into the hair.
  6. Check for any uneven spots and adjust accordingly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you have your layers, it's time to shape your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add texture.
  5. Brush your hair to create a natural-looking wave.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair to add shine.

Tips for Shaping Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you shape your long hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Don't over-wash your hair. Washing it every other day or every third day will help keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in a high-quality hair dryer and styling tools.

FAQs

How often should I trim my long hair?

You should trim your long hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

What's the best way to add volume to long hair?

The best way to add volume to long hair is to create layers. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow drying.

How can I prevent my long hair from getting tangled?

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help prevent tangles.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day. Use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.
  • Don't over-brush your hair. Brush it once a day to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid tying your hair up in tight ponytails or buns. Use a scrunchie instead of a hair tie to prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can shape your long hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
