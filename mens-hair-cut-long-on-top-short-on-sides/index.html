The Ultimate Guide To Men s Haircut Long On Top Short On Sides

Written by Christine Mar 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, then a men's haircut long on top and short on sides might be the perfect choice for you. This style has been popular for many years and is still going strong in 2023. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQs

Description

The men's haircut long on top and short on sides is a stylish and versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. The hair on top is left longer and can be styled in various ways, while the sides and back are cut short, usually with clippers.

This haircut is popular among men of all ages and is suitable for different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. It can also be combined with different hair designs, such as fades, undercuts, and shaved lines, to create a unique look.

How to Get the Haircut

To get the men's haircut long on top and short on sides, you'll need to visit a professional hair salon or barbershop. It's important to choose a stylist who has experience with this style and can give you the best results.

When you're at the salon, you should bring a photo of the haircut you want or be able to describe it clearly to your stylist. They will then use clippers and scissors to cut the sides and back short, leaving the hair on top longer. Depending on your preference, they may also add a fade or taper to blend the hair lengths seamlessly.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you want to try cutting your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide you can follow:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair, starting with a longer guard and gradually going shorter.
  3. Blend the hair lengths using a fade or taper, depending on your preference.
  4. Use scissors to cut the hair on top, leaving it about 2-3 inches long.
  5. Style the hair on top using a comb or your fingers, depending on the look you want.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips to help you style your men's haircut long on top and short on sides:

  • Use a quality styling product, such as pomade or wax, to keep the hair in place.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as slicked back, messy, or textured.
  • Consider adding a hair design, such as a shaved line or geometric shape, to make your haircut stand out.
  • Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQs

Is the men's haircut long on top and short on sides suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit different face shapes. Your stylist can adjust the length and shape of the haircut to flatter your features.

How often should I get a haircut with this style?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it. Generally, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and neat.

Can I still wear a hat with this haircut?

Yes, you can wear a hat with this haircut. However, it's best to choose a hat that fits well and doesn't flatten your hair too much.

Hair Care

To keep your men's haircut long on top and short on sides looking its best, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a quality hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

You should also visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to maintain the shape and length of your haircut. With proper care and maintenance, your men's haircut long on top and short on sides can last for several months and keep you looking stylish and confident.
