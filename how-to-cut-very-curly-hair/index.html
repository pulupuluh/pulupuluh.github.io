How To Cut Very Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 03, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have very curly hair, you know that it can be difficult to maintain the perfect haircut. But fear not! With the right tools and some simple techniques, you can cut your curly hair at home with ease. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to achieve a great haircut and help you keep your curls looking their best.

Description

Cutting curly hair can be a challenge, but it can also be a rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a style that flatters your face and enhances your natural curls. This guide will show you how to cut your curly hair at home, while also providing tips for maintaining your style and keeping your hair healthy.

How to Cut Very Curly Hair

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Clippers or trimmers (optional)

Make sure your scissors are sharp and clean. Dull scissors can crush the hair and make it difficult to achieve a clean cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help detangle your curls. Blot your hair dry with a towel, but avoid rubbing it, as this can cause frizz.

Once your hair is damp, comb or brush it to remove any tangles or knots. You can also use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly and ensure that you don’t miss any sections.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Starting with the bottom section, use your scissors to trim your hair. Start with small snips and work your way up, cutting the hair to the desired length. If you want to add layers, cut the hair at an angle.

For a more textured look, you can use clippers or trimmers to create a fade or taper. Use the longest guard for the top of your head and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard for the sides and back.

Step 5: Check Your Cut

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, check to make sure it’s even. If you notice any uneven sections, trim them carefully with your scissors.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Apply a hair product that works well with your curls, such as a curl cream or gel. Scrunch your hair gently with your hands to enhance your curls and create texture.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

  • Always cut curly hair when it’s dry, as wet hair can shrink and make it difficult to achieve the desired length.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head or ask a friend to help you.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and lengths.

FAQs

Can I cut my curly hair myself?

Yes, you can cut your curly hair yourself, but it’s important to use the right techniques and tools to achieve the best results.

What’s the best way to cut curly hair?

The best way to cut curly hair is to cut it when it’s dry, use sharp scissors, and cut small sections at a time.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It’s recommended to cut your curly hair every 8-12 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain your style.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to cutting your curly hair, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help detangle your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a scarf.

By following these tips and techniques, you can cut your curly hair at home and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
