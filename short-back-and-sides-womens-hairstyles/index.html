Short Back And Sides Women s Hairstyles The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than short back and sides women's hairstyles! This trendy look has been popular for years and shows no signs of slowing down. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about short back and sides women's hairstyles, including how to style them, hair care tips, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Short Back and Sides Women's Hairstyles
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Short back and sides women's hairstyles typically involve a buzzed or closely cropped cut on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. This style can be worn in a variety of ways, from slicked back and polished to messy and textured. It's a great option for women who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

How to Style Short Back and Sides Women's Hairstyles

The key to styling short back and sides women's hairstyles is to focus on the top section of your hair. This is where you'll have the most length to work with and can create different looks depending on your preferences. Here are some popular ways to style short back and sides women's hairstyles:

Slicked Back

To achieve a sleek and polished look, use a comb or brush to slick back the top section of your hair. You can use a styling product like gel or pomade to keep your hair in place. This is a great option for formal occasions or when you want to look put together.

Messy and Textured

If you prefer a more relaxed and casual look, try adding texture to the top section of your hair. You can use a texturizing spray or salt spray to create beachy waves or tousled curls. This is a great option for everyday wear or when you want to look effortlessly chic.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're interested in getting a short back and sides women's haircut, here's what you can expect during the process:

  1. The stylist will start by sectioning off the top section of your hair.
  2. They will then use clippers or scissors to buzz or cut the sides and back of your head to your desired length.
  3. Once the sides and back are trimmed, the stylist will blend the top section of your hair with the shorter sections to create a seamless look.
  4. Finally, they will style the top section of your hair according to your preferences.

Tips for Maintaining Short Back and Sides Women's Hairstyles

Here are some tips for keeping your short back and sides women's hairstyle looking fresh:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to the top section of your hair for added dimension.

FAQs

What face shapes work best with short back and sides women's hairstyles?

Short back and sides women's hairstyles can work with a variety of face shapes, but they tend to look best on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Can short back and sides women's hairstyles be worn curly?

Absolutely! Short back and sides women's hairstyles can be worn curly, straight, or wavy. It's a versatile look that can be customized to suit your preferences.

Hair Care for Short Back and Sides Women's Hairstyles

When it comes to caring for short back and sides women's hairstyles, it's important to use products that will keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Here are some recommended products:

  • Shampoo and conditioner formulated for short hair
  • Styling products like gel, pomade, or texturizing spray
  • Hair oil or serum to add shine and prevent frizz

With the right styling techniques and hair care products, you can rock a short back and sides women's hairstyle with confidence. So why not give it a try?
