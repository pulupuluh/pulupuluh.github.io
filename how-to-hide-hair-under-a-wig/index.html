How To Hide Hair Under A Wig A Step By Step Guide

Wigs are a great option, but if you have long or thick hair, it can be challenging to hide it underneath. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to hide hair under a wig.

How to Hide Hair Under a Wig

Hiding hair under a wig can be challenging, especially if you have long or thick hair. Here are the steps you can follow to ensure that your hair is well-hidden:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step is to prepare your hair by braiding it, twisting it, or putting it in a low ponytail. This will make it easier to hide your hair under the wig. Make sure that the braids or twists are flat and not bulky, as this will affect the fit of the wig.

Step 2: Put on a Wig Cap

Once your hair is prepared, put on a wig cap. Wig caps are essential as they help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from sticking out under the wig. Make sure that the wig cap is tight-fitting and covers your entire head, including your hairline.

Step 3: Secure Your Natural Hair

After you have put on the wig cap, take the hair that is sticking out and tuck it under the cap. Use hairpins or bobby pins to secure your natural hair in place. Make sure that the pins are not visible, as this will affect the look of the wig.

Step 4: Put on Your Wig

Finally, it's time to put on your wig. Start by placing the front of the wig at your hairline and pull it over your head. Adjust the wig until it fits snugly on your head. Make sure that the wig covers all of your natural hair and that none of it is visible.

Tips for Hiding Hair Under a Wig

Here are some tips to help you hide your hair under a wig:

  • Use a wig cap that matches your skin tone to make it less visible under the wig.
  • Choose a wig that fits well and has adjustable straps to ensure that it stays in place.
  • Use a wig with a lace front to create a natural-looking hairline.
  • Apply some powder or dry shampoo to your natural hair before putting on the wig to reduce oiliness and make it easier to style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding hair under a wig:

Can I wear a wig if I have short hair?

Yes, you can wear a wig if you have short hair. You will still need to use a wig cap to keep your hair in place and ensure that it does not stick out from under the wig.

Can I wear a wig if I have thick hair?

Yes, you can wear a wig if you have thick hair. However, you may need to use more hairpins or bobby pins to secure your hair in place.

Can I wear a wig if I have a sensitive scalp?

Yes, you can wear a wig if you have a sensitive scalp. Look for wigs made from high-quality, breathable materials that will not irritate your skin.

Hair Care for Wearing Wigs

Wearing wigs can be a fun way to change up your look, but it's essential to take care of your natural hair as well. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair while wearing wigs:

  • Wash your natural hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your natural hair as this can cause damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your natural hair moisturized.
  • Avoid wearing wigs for extended periods to prevent hair breakage.

Now that you know how to hide hair under a wig, you can enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles and looks. With these tips, you'll be able to rock a wig with confidence and style.
