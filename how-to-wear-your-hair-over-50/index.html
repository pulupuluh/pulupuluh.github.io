How To Wear Your Hair Over 50 Tips Tricks And Styles

Written by Daniel Apr 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair changes, and so do our styles. But this doesn't mean that we can't have fabulous hair over 50! In fact, there are plenty of hairstyles and tips that can help us look and feel our best. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways to wear your hair over 50, whether you have short hair, long hair, curly hair, or straight hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Your Hair Over 50
  • Short Hair Styles
  • Long Hair Styles
  • Curly Hair Styles
  • Straight Hair Styles

  • FAQ

How to Wear Your Hair Over 50

First and foremost, it's important to find a style that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Whether you prefer short and sassy, long and flowing, curly and wild, or straight and sleek, there's a style out there for you. Here are some tips to help you choose the right style:

  • Consider your face shape: If you have a round face, a short bob or pixie cut can help elongate your face. If you have a long face, longer layers can help balance out your features.
  • Think about your hair texture: If you have fine hair, a shorter cut can help add volume. If you have thick hair, longer layers can help lighten the weight and make it more manageable.
  • Consider your lifestyle: If you're active and on-the-go, a shorter cut may be more practical. If you have more time to style your hair, longer styles may be more your speed.

Short Hair Styles

Short hair can be chic, sophisticated, and easy to maintain. Here are some styles to consider:

  • Pixie cut: A classic short cut that's low-maintenance and easy to style.
  • Bowl cut: A trendy and fashion-forward cut that can be edgy or playful.
  • Bob: A versatile cut that can be layered, angled, or blunt.
  • Shag: A textured cut that's great for adding volume to fine hair.

Short hair is also great for adding highlights or lowlights, which can give your hair more depth and dimension.

Long Hair Styles

Long hair can be romantic, feminine, and beautiful. Here are some styles to consider:

  • Layered: A layered cut can add movement and volume to long hair.
  • Straight: Sleek and straight hair can be chic and sophisticated.
  • Curls: Soft, loose curls can add a playful and youthful touch to long hair.
  • Braids: Braids can be a fun and stylish way to keep long hair out of your face.

Long hair is also great for experimenting with different hair accessories, such as headbands, hair clips, and hair ties.

Curly Hair Styles

Curly hair can be wild, romantic, and beautiful. Here are some styles to consider:

  • Short and curly: A short and curly cut can be playful and fun.
  • Long and curly: Long, loose curls can be romantic and feminine.
  • Layered: A layered cut can help control the volume of curly hair.
  • Braids: Braids can be a great way to tame curly hair and add a touch of elegance.

Curly hair is also great for experimenting with different hair products, such as mousse, gel, and curl-defining cream.

Straight Hair Styles

Straight hair can be sleek, chic, and sophisticated. Here are some styles to consider:

  • Blunt cut: A blunt cut can be a bold and modern look for straight hair.
  • Layers: Layers can add movement and volume to straight hair.
  • Bangs: Bangs can be a stylish and playful addition to straight hair.
  • Ponytail: A sleek and simple ponytail can be a great way to wear straight hair.

Straight hair is also great for experimenting with different hair treatments, such as keratin treatments or straightening irons.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what style you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to help keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ

Q: Can I still dye my hair over 50?

A: Yes, you can still dye your hair over 50. Just be sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone and doesn't look too harsh.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on your hair and the style you choose. Short hair may need to be cut every 4-6 weeks, while longer hair may only need to be cut every 8-12 weeks.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid over 50?

A: Not necessarily. It's more important to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair texture, rather than focusing on age-specific styles.

No matter what style you choose, remember that confidence is key. Wear your hair with pride and embrace your natural beauty!
