Does Hair Thickening Spray Damage Hair

Written by Daniel Aug 15, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you someone who is struggling with thinning hair and looking for ways to add volume to your locks? Hair thickening spray has become a popular solution for those who desire fuller and thicker hair. However, you may have heard rumors that thickening sprays can cause damage to your hair. So, does hair thickening spray damage hair? In this article, we will explore this question and provide you with some tips on how to use hair thickening spray without causing harm to your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Hair Thickening Spray
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Hair Thickening Spray
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thinning Hair

Description

Hair thickening spray is a product that is designed to add volume and thickness to your hair. It usually contains a combination of polymers and proteins that coat the hair shaft and make it appear fuller. Some hair thickening sprays also contain ingredients like keratin and biotin, which are known to promote hair growth.

While hair thickening spray can be a great solution for those with thinning hair, it is important to use it correctly to avoid damaging your hair. Some hair thickening sprays contain alcohol, which can dry out your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Others contain harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become brittle.

How to Use Hair Thickening Spray

The key to using hair thickening spray without causing damage to your hair is to choose the right product and use it correctly. Here are some tips on how to use hair thickening spray:

Choose the Right Product

Look for a hair thickening spray that is alcohol-free and contains natural ingredients like keratin and biotin. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals like sulfates and parabens, which can be damaging to your hair.

Use the Right Amount

Using too much hair thickening spray can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down. Start with a small amount and gradually add more if needed.

Apply to Damp Hair

Apply hair thickening spray to damp hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots. Avoid using high heat, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use hair thickening spray:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  4. Shake the hair thickening spray bottle well.
  5. Hold the bottle 6-8 inches away from your head.
  6. Spray the thickening spray onto your roots, working your way down to the ends.
  7. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  8. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Using Hair Thickening Spray

Here are some additional tips to help you use hair thickening spray without causing damage to your hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying the spray.
  • Avoid using hair thickening spray every day, as this can lead to product buildup and make your hair look greasy.
  • Choose a hair thickening spray that is specifically designed for your hair type (e.g. fine, medium, or thick).
  • Avoid using hair thickening spray on your scalp, as this can clog your hair follicles and cause irritation.

FAQs

Can hair thickening spray cause hair loss?

No, hair thickening spray does not cause hair loss. However, using too much of the product or not washing it out properly can lead to product buildup, which can make your hair look thin and limp.

Can hair thickening spray be used on colored hair?

Yes, hair thickening spray can be used on colored hair. However, it is important to choose a product that is color-safe and will not cause your hair color to fade.

Can hair thickening spray be used on wet hair?

Yes, hair thickening spray can be used on wet hair. In fact, it is recommended to apply the product to damp hair, as this will help it to distribute more evenly.

Hair Care for Thinning Hair

In addition to using hair thickening spray, there are other things you can do to care for your thinning hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can cause damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth, such as biotin, vitamin E, and iron.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails, which can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for thinning hair.
  • Consider using a hair growth supplement or topical treatment to promote hair growth.

Now that you know the answer to the question "does hair thickening spray damage hair," you can use this product with confidence. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can add volume and thickness to your hair without causing harm. Remember to choose the right product, use the right amount, and apply it correctly to achieve the best results.
