Men s Hairstyle Short Back And Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby May 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read
If you're a man looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, you might want to consider the short back and sides long top hairstyle. This trend has been around for a while now, and it's not going away anytime soon. In this article, we'll take a closer look at this popular hairstyle and provide you with all the information you need to rock this look with confidence.

Description

The short back and sides long top hairstyle is a classic men's haircut that features a close-cropped back and sides with longer hair on the top. This style can be adapted to suit different hair types, face shapes, and personal preferences, making it a popular choice for men of all ages and lifestyles.

How to

If you're interested in getting a short back and sides long top haircut, the first step is to find a reputable barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Here's how the process typically goes: 1. Consultation: Your barber or hairstylist will ask you about your hair type, face shape, lifestyle, and personal style to determine the best way to cut your hair. 2. Preparation: Your hair will be washed and combed to remove any tangles or product buildup. 3. Cutting: Your barber or hairstylist will use clippers to cut the back and sides of your hair to the desired length, usually a number 2 or 3 guard. Then, they'll use scissors to cut the top to the desired length and style. 4. Styling: Your barber or hairstylist will apply a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to the top of your hair and style it according to your preferences.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the steps involved in getting a short back and sides long top haircut: 1. Start with clean, dry hair. 2. Use clippers to cut the back and sides of your hair to the desired length, leaving the top untouched. 3. Use scissors to cut the top of your hair to the desired length and style. 4. Blend the sides and top together by using scissors to feather the hair at the point where they meet. 5. Apply a styling product to the top of your hair and style it according to your preferences.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your short back and sides long top hairstyle: - Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look. - Consider adding a fade or undercut to the sides for a more modern twist on the classic look. - Ask your barber or hairstylist for advice on how to maintain your haircut between appointments.

FAQ

Q: Is the short back and sides long top hairstyle suitable for all face shapes? A: Yes, this hairstyle can be adapted to suit different face shapes, but your barber or hairstylist will need to customize the cut to flatter your features. Q: How often do I need to get my short back and sides long top haircut trimmed? A: This will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows, but most men will need to get their hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style. Q: Can I style my short back and sides long top haircut in different ways? A: Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked-back to messy and textured.

Hair Care

To keep your short back and sides long top haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips: - Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. - Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners. - Avoid harsh chemicals and treatments that can damage your hair, such as bleaching or perming. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut. In conclusion, the short back and sides long top hairstyle is a timeless and versatile look that can be customized to suit any man's style and preferences. Whether you prefer a classic, clean-cut look or something more modern and edgy, this hairstyle is definitely worth considering. So, go ahead and book an appointment with your barber or hairstylist today and get ready to rock this stylish and practical haircut!
