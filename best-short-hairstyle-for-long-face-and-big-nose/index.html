Best Short Hairstyle For Long Face And Big Nose

Written by Aprilia Sep 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you someone with a long face and big nose? Do you often find yourself struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features? Look no further, as we bring to you the best short hairstyles that will accentuate your features and make you look stunning. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about the best short hairstyle for long face and big nose, from how to choose the right style to hair care tips.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle for a long face and big nose, it is essential to opt for a cut that will balance out your features. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a cut that adds width to your face, such as a bob or a pixie cut.
  • Opt for hairstyles with layers that will soften your features and add texture to your hair.
  • Side-swept bangs can be your best friend as they help to balance out your features and draw attention away from your nose.
  • Avoid styles that are too blunt or straight, as they can make your face appear longer.

Short Bob

A short bob is an excellent option for those with a long face and big nose. It is a classic style that has been around for years and is still popular today. A bob cut that ends just above the chin can help to balance out your features and give your face a more symmetrical look.

The great thing about a short bob is that it is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can add layers to create texture and volume or opt for straight hair for a sleek and elegant look.

Pixie Cut

If you are looking for a more daring and edgy hairstyle, a pixie cut is an excellent option. It is a short and low-maintenance cut that can help to draw attention away from your nose and accentuate your eyes and cheekbones.

A pixie cut is also perfect for those with a busy lifestyle as it requires minimal styling and upkeep. You can add layers and texture to create a more voluminous look or opt for a sleek and straight style.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add balance to your features and draw attention away from your nose. They help to create a more symmetrical look and can be styled in many different ways.

You can opt for short or long bangs and style them to the side or straight. They look great with both bob and pixie cuts and can help to soften your features and add texture to your hair.

Layered Bob

A layered bob is another great option for those with a long face and big nose. It is a cut that adds volume and texture to your hair and helps to balance out your features.

You can add layers to create a more voluminous look, or you can opt for a sleek and straight style. A layered bob is a versatile cut that can be styled in many different ways and is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style.

Tips to Keep in Mind

  • Choose a cut that flatters your face shape and features.
  • Consult with your hairstylist before getting a new cut.
  • Consider your lifestyle and choose a style that is low maintenance and easy to maintain.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q. Will a short hairstyle make my nose look bigger?

A. No, a short hairstyle will not make your nose look bigger. In fact, the right cut can help to balance out your features and draw attention away from your nose.

Q. How often should I get my hair cut?

A. It depends on your hair type and the style you choose. Short hairstyles require more frequent cuts to maintain their shape, while longer styles can go a bit longer between cuts.

Q. Can I style my short hair for a special occasion?

A. Yes, you can style your short hair for a special occasion. There are many different products and techniques you can use to create a more formal look.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when styling.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Use a styling product that adds texture and volume to your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair and use dry shampoo in between washes.

With these tips and suggestions, you can now confidently choose the best short hairstyle for long face and big nose. Remember to consult with your hairstylist before getting a new cut and experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. With the right cut and care, you can flaunt your features and look stunning every day!
