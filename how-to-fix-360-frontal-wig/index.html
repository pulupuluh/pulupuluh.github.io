How To Fix A 360 Frontal Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of struggling with your 360 frontal wig? Do you want to know how to fix it properly? Then, you've come to the right place!

Description

A 360 frontal wig is a type of wig that gives you a natural and full hairline. It is becoming increasingly popular among women because of its versatility and ease of use. However, fixing it can be a little challenging for beginners.

How to Fix a 360 Frontal Wig

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to fix a 360 frontal wig:

Step 1: Prepare Your Wig and Head

Before you begin, make sure that your wig and head are clean and dry. If your wig is dirty or wet, it will be difficult to fix it properly.

Step 2: Position the Wig

Place the wig on your head and position it properly. Adjust the straps and combs to ensure that the wig fits snugly on your head.

Step 3: Cut the Lace

Take a pair of scissors and cut the lace along the hairline of the wig. Be careful not to cut too much of the lace as it may damage the wig.

Step 4: Apply Glue or Tape

Apply glue or tape on the lace of the wig. Make sure that the glue or tape is evenly distributed to ensure a secure hold.

Step 5: Press the Wig onto Your Head

Press the wig onto your head and hold it firmly for a few seconds to ensure that the glue or tape sets properly.

Step 6: Style Your Wig

Style your wig as desired. You can use a curling iron or straightener to achieve the look that you want.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you fix your 360 frontal wig:

  • Make sure that your wig is clean and dry before fixing it.
  • Be careful not to cut too much of the lace when cutting it.
  • Apply the glue or tape evenly to ensure a secure hold.
  • Hold the wig firmly for a few seconds to ensure that the glue or tape sets properly.
  • Use a wig stand when not wearing your wig to help maintain its shape and style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing a 360 frontal wig:

1. Can I fix my wig without using glue or tape?

Yes, you can use wig clips or bobby pins to secure your wig instead of glue or tape.

2. How long does the glue or tape hold?

The glue or tape can hold your wig for up to a week, depending on the type of glue or tape used and your hair's natural oils.

3. Can I sleep with my wig on?

It is not recommended to sleep with your wig on as it can damage the wig and your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the quality and longevity of your wig. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Do not use hot water or a hairdryer on your wig.
  • Store your wig in a cool and dry place.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to direct sunlight or heat.

With these tips and steps, you can now confidently fix your 360 frontal wig and rock any hairstyle you desire!
