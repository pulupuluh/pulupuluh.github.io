Best Hairstyles For Face Shapes Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jun 01, 2023 · 9 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on the best hairstyles for face shapes male. Whether you have a round, square, oval, or heart-shaped face, we have got you covered. In this article, we will provide you with the latest hairstyles that will flatter your face shape, tips on how to style them, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Hairstyles for Round Face

Round faces have soft curves and no angles, and they tend to be widest at the cheeks. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that create angles and add height to the crown of the head, making the face appear longer and slimmer.

The following are some of the best hairstyles for men with round faces:

  • Short back and sides with longer hair on top
  • Textured crop
  • Side part
  • Pompadour
  • Spiky hair

Hairstyles for Square Face

Square faces have strong and angular jawlines, and their forehead, cheekbones, and jawline tend to be of the same width. The best hairstyles for square faces are those that soften the angles and add volume to the top of the head.

The following are some of the best hairstyles for men with square faces:

  • Textured crop
  • Side-swept fringe
  • Short back and sides with longer hair on top
  • Crew cut
  • Quiff

Hairstyles for Oval Face

Oval faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape as they have balanced features and a slightly rounded hairline. The best hairstyles for oval faces are those that maintain the natural balance of the face, and any hairstyle can work well with this face shape.

The following are some of the best hairstyles for men with oval faces:

  • Short back and sides with longer hair on top
  • Side part
  • Textured crop
  • Quiff
  • Pompadour

Hairstyles for Heart-Shaped Face

Heart-shaped faces have a wide forehead and a narrow chin, creating a heart-like shape. The best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces are those that add volume to the chin area and minimize the width of the forehead.

The following are some of the best hairstyles for men with heart-shaped faces:

  • Side-swept fringe
  • Textured crop
  • Quiff
  • Pompadour
  • Short back and sides with longer hair on top

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can be confusing, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Determine your face shape: Take a picture of your face from the front and compare it to the different face shapes to determine which one you have.
  • Consider your hair type: Certain hairstyles work better with certain hair types. For example, if you have thick hair, a textured crop or a pompadour will work well.
  • Consult with a hairstylist: A hairstylist can recommend the best hairstyles for your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Your Hair

Styling your hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a breeze. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a pomade or a gel.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go, using a round brush to add volume.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Health

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving a great hairstyle. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair health:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners and curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair health.
  • Avoid using too much styling product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
