How To Know Which Haircut Suits My Face

Written by Oliver Aug 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right haircut that suits your face can be a daunting task. There are so many different hairstyles to choose from, and it can be overwhelming to decide which one will look best on you. In this article, we will guide you through the steps to determine which haircut suits your face shape, and provide tips on how to take care of your hair once you have found the perfect style.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture
  • Step 3: Consult With a Hairstylist
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining which haircut will suit your face is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror.

If your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded jawline and forehead, you have an oval face shape. If your face is circular with full cheeks and a rounded chin, you have a round face shape. If your face is angular with a square jawline and forehead, you have a square face shape. If your face is wider at the forehead and temples, with a pointed chin, you have a heart face shape. If your face is narrow at the forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones, you have a diamond face shape. If your face is longer than it is wide, with a prominent chin and forehead, you have an oblong face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

The second step in determining which haircut will suit your face is to consider your hair texture. Different hair textures require different haircuts to look their best. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to consider a layered haircut to help control the volume. If you have fine, straight hair, you may want to consider a shorter haircut to add volume.

Step 3: Consult With a Hairstylist

The final step in determining which haircut will suit your face is to consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you determine which haircut will look best on you based on your face shape and hair texture. They can also provide recommendations for hair care products and styling techniques to keep your hair looking its best.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right haircut:

  • Consider your lifestyle: Choose a haircut that works with your lifestyle. If you don't have a lot of time to style your hair, choose a low-maintenance haircut.
  • Bring pictures: Bring pictures of haircuts you like to your hairstylist to help them understand the look you are going for.
  • Try before you buy: Consider trying on wigs or using virtual makeover tools to see how different haircuts will look on you before committing to a new style.

FAQs

How often should I get my haircut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

What should I do if I don't like my new haircut?

If you don't like your new haircut, speak with your hairstylist to see if they can make any adjustments. If you are still unhappy with the result, you may need to wait for your hair to grow out before trying a new style.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have found the perfect haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Wash your hair with a mild shampoo every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use conditioner: Use a conditioner after shampooing to help keep your hair hydrated and to prevent damage.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, can damage your hair over time. Try to limit your use of these tools or use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims: Get regular trims to prevent split ends and to keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you can find the perfect haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist for personalized recommendations and to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
