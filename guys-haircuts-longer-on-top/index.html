Guys Haircuts Longer On Top A Guide For 2023

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut for men, then you might want to consider going for a longer top cut. In this guide, we're going to take a look at everything you need to know about guys haircuts longer on top, including how to get one, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get a Guys Haircut Longer on Top

Description

Guys haircuts longer on top are a popular style that has been around for a while now. This style typically involves having longer hair on the top of your head, with shorter hair on the sides and back. There are many different variations of this style, including the slick back, the comb over, the pompadour, and the quiff.

This style can be worn by men of all ages and works well with a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. It's a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion.

How to Get a Guys Haircut Longer on Top

If you're interested in getting a guys haircut longer on top, the first step is to find a good barber or stylist who is experienced with this style. You'll want to bring in some pictures of the style you're interested in, so your barber or stylist can get a better idea of what you're looking for.

During your appointment, your barber or stylist will typically start by cutting the sides and back of your hair shorter, while leaving the hair on top longer. They may use scissors or clippers, depending on the style you're going for.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a guys haircut longer on top:

  1. Find a good barber or stylist.
  2. Bring in pictures of the style you're interested in.
  3. Discuss the style with your barber or stylist.
  4. Sit back and relax while your barber or stylist cuts your hair.
  5. Ask for any styling tips or product recommendations.

Tips for Maintaining Your Guys Haircut Longer on Top

To keep your guys haircut longer on top looking fresh and stylish, you'll want to follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep the sides and back short.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Q: How long should the hair on top be?

A: The length of the hair on top will depend on the style you're going for and your personal preference. Generally, the hair on top should be long enough to style, but not too long that it becomes unmanageable.

Q: Can guys with curly hair get a haircut longer on top?

A: Yes, guys with curly hair can definitely get a haircut longer on top. In fact, this style can look great with curly hair, as it allows you to showcase your natural texture.

Hair Care Advice for Guys with Haircuts Longer on Top

Here are some hair care tips for guys with haircuts longer on top:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your guys haircut longer on top looking stylish and fresh for weeks to come.
