Curtain Bangs Round Face Asian The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby May 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you an Asian with a round face and looking for a hairstyle that can flatter your features? Look no further than curtain bangs! This trendy hairstyle has been making waves in the fashion industry, and it's not hard to see why. Curtain bangs are versatile, stylish, and can suit any face shape, including round faces. In this ultimate guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about curtain bangs for round face Asian, from how to style them to hair care tips.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that is parted down the middle, creating a curtain-like effect. This style has been popularized by celebrities like Brigitte Bardot and Alexa Chung and has become a favorite among fashion-forward individuals. Curtain bangs are suitable for all hair types and face shapes, including round faces. They help to elongate the face, making it appear slimmer and more defined.

How to Cut Curtain Bangs for Round Face Asian

If you're planning to cut curtain bangs for the first time, it's best to go to a professional hairstylist. However, if you're confident in your skills, you can do it at home. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and dry it completely. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair while creating volume at the crown.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair from the crown to the top of your ears. Clip the rest of your hair away.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Start cutting your bangs at the center of your forehead. Cut your hair at an angle, following the curve of your eyebrows. Keep the length of your bangs longer at the sides to create the curtain effect.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your bangs. Create a middle parting, and let your bangs fall naturally on both sides of your face.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs for Round Face Asian

Here are some tips to help you style your curtain bangs:

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create volume at the crown.

- Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair while creating volume at the crown.

- Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your bangs. Create a middle parting, and let your bangs fall naturally on both sides of your face.

- Use a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

FAQ

Can curtain bangs suit round face Asian?

Yes, curtain bangs can suit round face Asian. They help to elongate the face and make it appear slimmer and more defined.

How do I style my curtain bangs?

Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your bangs. Create a middle parting, and let your bangs fall naturally on both sides of your face.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every four to six weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs for Round Face Asian

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curtain bangs looking their best:

- Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent oil buildup.

- Use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron or curling iron.

- Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

- Trim your bangs every four to six weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Conclusion

Curtain bangs are a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can suit any face shape, including round faces. With the right cut and styling, you can make this look work for you. Follow our guide, and you'll be rocking curtain bangs for round face Asian in no time.
