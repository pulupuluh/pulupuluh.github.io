Short Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair Over 50

Written by Robby Jun 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick wavy hair over 50, it can be challenging to find a style that is both manageable and stylish. Short hairstyles are the perfect solution for women who want to look great without spending hours on their hair every day. In this article, we will explore some of the best short haircuts for thick wavy hair over 50.

Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also be difficult to manage. That's why short hairstyles are a great option for women over 50. They are easy to style and require minimal maintenance. Plus, they can make you look younger and more modern.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect short hairstyle for your thick wavy hair is to determine your face shape. Oval, round, square, heart, and diamond-shaped faces all require different styles. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that flatter your features.

When it comes to short hairstyles for thick wavy hair over 50, the pixie cut is a classic choice. This style is short and edgy, with layers that add volume and texture to your hair. The pixie cut is also easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways. You can add bangs, spikes, or even highlights to make it your own.

If you prefer a more feminine look, the bob is a great option. This style can be worn straight or wavy, and it looks great on all face shapes. The bob is also versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. You can add layers, bangs, or highlights to make it your own.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Choose a short hairstyle that flatters your features
Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to ensure that the style is right for you
Step 4: Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture
Step 5: Use products that are specifically designed for thick wavy hair to keep your style looking great all day.

Tips

1. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair
2. Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves
3. Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling iron to style your hair
4. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
5. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQ

Q: Will short hairstyles make me look older?

A: No, short hairstyles can actually make you look younger and more modern.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short hairstyle?

A: Yes, there are many ways to style short hair, including updos and half-up styles.

Q: Do I need to use special products for my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, using products that are specifically designed for thick wavy hair can help enhance your natural texture and keep your style looking great all day.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your short hairstyle, there are a few things you can do to keep it looking great. First, use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage. Second, use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. Finally, get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Short hairstyles for thick wavy hair over 50 are a great option for women who want to look stylish and modern. With the right style and care, you can rock a short haircut at any age. Use the tips and advice in this article to find the perfect style for you!
