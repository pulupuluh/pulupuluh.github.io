How To Trim Men s Curly Hair With Scissors

Written by Luffy Sep 01, 2023 · 6 min read

Curly hair requires a special touch when it comes to trimming. While clippers can be a useful tool, trimming men's curly hair with scissors is often the best way to achieve a natural, effortless look. Here's everything you need to know about trimming men's curly hair with scissors.

Description

Trimming men's curly hair with scissors requires some knowledge of curly hair and some practice. With the right technique and tools, you can achieve a great-looking haircut that enhances your natural curls.

How to Trim Men's Curly Hair with Scissors

Before you start trimming, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Comb it out to remove any tangles or knots, and section it into manageable portions. Here's how to trim men's curly hair with scissors:

Step 1: Start with the Back

Begin by trimming the back of your hair. Use your fingers to separate a small section of hair, and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Use the scissors to trim the ends of the hair, making small cuts at a slight angle. Repeat this process for each section of hair until you have trimmed the entire back of your head.

Step 2: Move to the Sides

Next, move to the sides of your head. Use the same technique you used for the back of your head, trimming small sections of hair at a slight angle. Make sure to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on the back of your head for a seamless look.

Step 3: Trim the Top

Finally, trim the top of your head. Use the same technique as before, but be careful not to cut too much off the top. Leave some length to allow for natural curl definition.

Tips for Trimming Men's Curly Hair with Scissors

Trimming men's curly hair with scissors can be tricky, but these tips can help you achieve a great-looking haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts.
  • Take your time and trim small sections of hair at a time.
  • Blend the hair on the sides and back of your head for a seamless look.
  • Don't cut too much off the top of your head.
  • Trim your hair when it's dry to avoid cutting too much off.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming men's curly hair with scissors:

How often should I trim my curly hair?

It's recommended to trim curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I use regular scissors to trim my curly hair?

No, it's best to use scissors designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors can cause split ends and damage to your hair.

Should I trim my curly hair when it's wet or dry?

It's best to trim curly hair when it's dry to avoid cutting too much off.

Hair Care for Men's Curly Hair

Proper hair care is important for maintaining healthy, well-defined curls. Here are some tips for caring for men's curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and defined.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hair.

Trimming men's curly hair with scissors can be a great way to achieve a natural, effortless look. With the right technique and tools, you can maintain healthy, well-defined curls that look great. Follow these tips and techniques to achieve the perfect haircut for your curly hair.
