What Parting Suits A Round Face

Written by Luffy Jul 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your hair looking the same every day? Maybe it's time to switch up your parting! But, as a person with a round face, you might be wondering what parting suits you the best. In this article, we will be discussing the different types of parting that suit round faces, how to get them, and some tips on how to maintain and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • What Parting Suits a Round Face?

What is a Round Face?

A round face is characterized by its soft features and a width that is almost equal to its height. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the jaw is rounded. People with round faces often have a fuller look and lack prominent angles.

What Parting Suits a Round Face?

For people with round faces, the best parting is usually a deep side parting. A deep side parting helps to create the illusion of length in the face, creating a more oval shape. It will also add volume to the crown, giving the appearance of a higher forehead.

Another great option for round faces is a center parting. However, this only works if you have long hair. A center parting helps to elongate the face, creating the illusion of a more oval shape.

How to Part Your Hair for a Round Face

Parting your hair for a round face is not difficult, but it requires some precision. The key is to create the illusion of length and angles on your face. Here are some steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Decide where you want your parting to be. For a deep side parting, it should start above the arch of your eyebrow on the opposite side of your face.
  3. Using the end of a tail comb, create a straight line from your hairline to the back of your head.
  4. Section off the hair that you want to move to the opposite side of your parting.
  5. Brush the remaining hair over to the opposite side of the parting.
  6. Secure the hair with a hair clip or bobby pin if necessary.
  7. Smooth down any flyaways with a bit of hairspray or gel.

Tips for Maintaining Hair with Your New Parting

To keep your hair looking great with your new parting, here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you do need to use heat, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about parting for round faces:

Can I have a side parting if I have short hair?

Yes, you can have a side parting with short hair. Just make sure that the parting starts above the arch of your eyebrow on the opposite side of your face.

Can I have a center parting if I have short hair?

No, a center parting only works with long hair. If you have short hair, opt for a deep side parting instead.

Can I switch up my parting every day?

Yes, you can switch up your parting every day. Just make sure that you follow the steps outlined above to ensure that your hair looks great and suits your face shape.

Hair Care

Parting your hair can be a great way to switch up your look, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoid using too much heat, invest in a good quality hairbrush, and regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends. With the right care, you can have great-looking hair no matter what parting you choose.
