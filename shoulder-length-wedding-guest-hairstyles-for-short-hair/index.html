Shoulder Length Wedding Guest Hairstyles For Short Hair

Written by Oliver Apr 27, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Attending a wedding as a guest is always a fun and exciting experience. You get to dress up, socialize, and witness a beautiful union between two people. However, finding the perfect hairstyle for short hair can be a bit of a challenge, especially if you have shoulder-length hair. But don't worry, we've got you covered with some stunning shoulder length wedding guest hairstyles for short hair that are sure to make you feel like the belle of the ball.

Description

Short hair can be incredibly versatile, and there are plenty of ways to style it for a wedding. From elegant updos to chic braids, there is a hairstyle for every type of wedding and personal style. The key is to find a look that complements your outfit and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

How to

If you're struggling to come up with a hairstyle for a wedding, here are some tips to help you get started:

Consider the Dress Code

The first thing you should do is consider the dress code for the wedding. If it's a formal or black-tie event, you may want to opt for a more polished and sophisticated hairstyle. If it's a more casual or outdoor wedding, you can be a bit more relaxed and playful with your hair.

Think About Your Outfit

Your hairstyle should complement your outfit, so take some time to think about what you'll be wearing. If you have a high neckline or a lot of embellishments on your dress, a simple updo or sleek ponytail may be the best option. If your dress is more understated, you can add some drama with a bold braid or voluminous curls.

Consider Your Face Shape

The shape of your face can also play a role in the hairstyle you choose. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a style that adds some height to your hair, like a half-up, half-down style with some volume at the crown. If you have a square face, a soft and romantic updo with some face-framing tendrils can help soften your features.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of our favorite shoulder length wedding guest hairstyles for short hair:

1. Braided Crown

Start by parting your hair down the center and braiding two small sections on either side of your head. Pull the braids back and secure them with bobby pins, creating a crown-like effect. Finish the look by adding some loose curls with a curling iron.

2. Low Chignon

Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face for a softer look.

3. Side Swept Curls

Create a deep side part and use a curling iron to add loose curls to your hair. Sweep your hair to one side and secure it with bobby pins. Finish the look by adding a sparkly hair accessory.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect shoulder length wedding guest hairstyle for short hair:

1. Use the Right Products

The right hair products can make a huge difference in how your hairstyle turns out. Use a volumizing mousse if you want some extra lift, or a smoothing serum if you want a sleek and polished look.

2. Don't Overdo It

While you want your hair to look beautiful, you don't want it to overpower your outfit or steal the spotlight from the bride. Keep your hairstyle simple and elegant, and avoid anything too elaborate or over-the-top.

3. Practice Makes Perfect

Don't wait until the day of the wedding to try out your hairstyle. Give it a test run a few days before to make sure you're happy with how it looks and that it stays in place throughout the day.

FAQ

What if I have really short hair?

If you have a pixie cut or very short hair, you can still create a beautiful wedding guest hairstyle. Consider adding some texture with a texturizing spray or wax, or try a bold hair accessory like a headband or hair clip.

Can I wear my hair down?

Of course! Wearing your hair down can be a great option, especially if you have natural curls or waves. Just make sure to add some volume and texture to your hair to keep it from looking flat.

Do I need to get my hair done by a professional?

While it's always nice to get your hair done by a professional, it's not necessary. With a little bit of practice and some helpful tips, you can create a beautiful wedding guest hairstyle on your own.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair leading up to the wedding. Make sure to use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, and consider getting a trim a few weeks before the event to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

With these shoulder length wedding guest hairstyles for short hair and some simple tips and tricks, you're sure to turn heads at your next wedding event. Just remember to have fun and enjoy the day!
