How To Cut Your Own Hair U Cut

Written by Bardi Aug 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this article, we are going to discuss the step-by-step process of cutting your own hair in a U shape. This style is a popular and trendy way to add layers and volume to your hair. It is easy to do at home, and you do not need to be a professional stylist to achieve this look. So, let's get started!

How to Cut Your Own Hair U Cut

The U cut is a versatile style that can work for many hair types and lengths. This style is best suited for those who want to add volume and layers to their hair. Before starting, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to cut your own hair in a U shape:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into two equal parts by parting it down the middle.
  3. Clip one side of your hair up and out of the way.
  4. Start with the other side of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the back and comb it straight down.
  5. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, creating a U shape. Be sure to cut at a slight angle, not straight across.
  6. Continue cutting small sections of hair in this manner, working your way around the back of your head.
  7. Repeat the process on the other side of your head.
  8. Check your work in the mirror to make sure both sides are even.
  9. Trim any stray hairs to tidy up the U shape.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect U cut:

  • Start with small sections of hair, as it is easier to cut more if you need to.
  • Be sure to cut at an angle to create the U shape.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Check your work frequently to make sure both sides are even.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair in a U shape:

  • Can I cut my hair in a U shape if it is curly?

    • Yes, the U cut works well with curly hair, but be sure to cut it when it is dry to avoid any surprises.

  • What if I make a mistake?

    • If you make a mistake, do not panic. You can always trim a little more or go to a professional stylist for help.

  • How often should I cut my hair in a U shape?

    • It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, every 6-8 weeks is a good time to get a trim.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair in a U shape, it is important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to cut your own hair in a U shape, you can save money on salon visits and keep your hair looking great at home. Remember to follow the steps carefully and take your time, and you will have a beautiful U cut in no time!
