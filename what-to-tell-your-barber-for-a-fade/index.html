What To Tell Your Barber For A Fade A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Getting a fade haircut is a popular trend among men of all ages. However, it can be challenging to explain to your barber the exact style you want. In this guide, we will discuss what to tell your barber for a fade haircut, step-by-step instructions, tips, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get a Fade Haircut
  3. Step-by-Step Instructions
  4. Tips for Getting a Perfect Fade
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Fade

Description

A fade is a haircut that gradually fades from short to long from the neck to the top of the head. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit different hair types and lengths. Depending on your preference, you can choose a low fade, mid-fade, or high fade.

How to Get a Fade Haircut

To get a fade haircut, you need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in this style. The barber will use clippers and scissors to create a gradual transition from short to long hair. You need to explain to your barber the type of fade you want, including the length and the style on the top of your head. You can bring a picture of the style you want to make it easier for your barber to understand.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are the step-by-step instructions on what to tell your barber for a fade haircut:

  1. Choose the type of fade you want – low, mid, or high.
  2. Determine the length you want on the top of your head.
  3. Ask your barber to use clippers to fade the hair at the sides and the back of your head.
  4. Use scissors to blend the hair on the top of your head with the faded sides and back.
  5. Ask your barber to clean up the hairline and sideburns.
  6. Check the mirror to ensure you are satisfied with the result.

Tips for Getting a Perfect Fade

Here are some tips to help you get a perfect fade haircut:

  • Choose a barber who is experienced in fading hair.
  • Bring a picture of the style you want to make it easier for your barber to understand.
  • Communicate clearly with your barber about the type of fade you want.
  • Be open to the barber's suggestions and advice.
  • Regularly maintain your fade by visiting the barber every two to three weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does a fade haircut last?

A: A fade haircut can last anywhere from two to four weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Q: Can I style my hair after a fade haircut?

A: Yes, you can style your hair after a fade haircut. You can use hair gel, pomade, or wax to create different styles.

Q: Is a fade haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a fade haircut can be customized to suit different hair types and lengths.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Fade

To maintain your fade haircut, you need to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip the natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the fade.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.

Now that you know what to tell your barber for a fade haircut, you can confidently rock this trendy style. Remember to communicate clearly with your barber, maintain your hair regularly, and follow a proper hair care routine to keep your fade looking fresh.
