How To Make A Messy Bob Hairstyle

Written by Petter Apr 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both chic and effortless, a messy bob is the perfect choice. This versatile cut can be worn by women of all ages and hair types, and it's easy to achieve at home. In this article, we'll show you how to make a messy bob hairstyle step by step, and provide tips for styling and hair care.

How to Make a Messy Bob Hairstyle

To create a messy bob, you'll need to start with a bob haircut that's either chin length or slightly longer. This cut should be layered to add texture and movement to your hair. Here's how to make a messy bob hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a lightweight, volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add body and texture to your hair, which is key for creating a messy bob.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Next, blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, lifting your hair up as you go. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves or curls.

Step 3: Apply Texturizing Spray

Once your hair is dry, apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add texture and hold. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

Step 4: Add Waves or Curls

If you have straight hair, you may want to add some waves or curls to your messy bob. You can use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves, or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your messy bob with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a more natural, effortless look.

Step by Step Guide

To make it easier, here's a step by step guide on how to make a messy bob hairstyle:

  1. Start with a bob haircut that's either chin length or slightly longer.
  2. Wash and condition your hair with a lightweight, volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add texture and hold.
  5. Add waves or curls using a curling iron or flat iron, or enhance your natural waves or curls using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your messy bob in place.

Tips for Styling

To make the most of your messy bob, here are some tips for styling:

  • Use a lightweight, volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add texture and hold.
  • Use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves, or enhance your natural waves or curls using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your messy bob in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a messy bob hairstyle:

What face shape is best for a messy bob?

A messy bob can work for any face shape, but it's particularly flattering for oval and heart-shaped faces.

Can you make a messy bob with short hair?

Yes, a messy bob can work for short hair as long as it's chin length or slightly longer.

How often should you wash a messy bob?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep your messy bob looking fresh and voluminous. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look limp and lifeless.

Hair Care

To keep your messy bob looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a lightweight, volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, which can strip it of its natural oils and make it look limp and lifeless.
  • Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or flat iron.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now you know how to make a messy bob hairstyle! With these tips and tricks, you can create a chic and effortless look that's perfect for any occasion.
