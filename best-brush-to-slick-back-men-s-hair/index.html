Description

Slicked back hair has been a popular hairstyle for men for decades. It's a classic, timeless look that never goes out of style. Whether you're going for a formal or casual look, slicked back hair can work for any occasion. However, achieving the perfect slicked back hairstyle requires the right tools, including the best brush to slick back men's hair.

How to Slick Back Men's Hair

Before we discuss the best brush to use for slicking back men's hair, let's first talk about how to achieve the style. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, focusing on the top and sides.
  3. Using your fingers, comb your hair back from your forehead.
  4. Use a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  5. Continue combing and brushing until you achieve the desired look.

Tips for Slicking Back Men's Hair

Now that you know the basics of how to slick back men's hair let's go over some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent frizz.
  • Choose the right brush. We'll discuss this in more detail below.
  • Don't apply too much product. A little goes a long way, and too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a hairdryer on low heat to set the style in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back men's hair:

What type of hair is best for slicking back?

Any hair type can be slicked back, but it's easiest to achieve the style with thick, straight hair.

How often should I wash my hair if I'm slicking it back?

You should wash your hair every two to three days to prevent product buildup.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

Proper hair care is essential if you want to maintain a slicked back hairstyle. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product to prevent buildup.
  • Brush your hair regularly to distribute natural oils and prevent tangles.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to slick back men's hair, it's time to choose the right brush for the job. The best brush to use for slicking back men's hair is a boar bristle brush. Boar bristle brushes are gentle on the hair and scalp, and they help distribute natural oils throughout the hair, making it healthier and shinier. With the right brush and some practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle in no time.
