Easy To Maintain Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Christine Aug 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending hours styling your hair every morning? Do you want to switch things up with your medium length hair? Look no further! We have compiled a list of easy to maintain hairstyles for medium length hair that will save you time and effort while still looking fabulous.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's talk about some basic hair care tips to keep your medium length hair healthy and shiny. First, make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils. Second, avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently to prevent damage. Finally, get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Here are some easy to maintain hairstyles for medium length hair:

Step by Step

1. Messy Bun

To create a messy bun, pull your hair back into a ponytail and twist it around itself until it forms a bun. Secure it with a hair tie and use bobby pins to secure any loose strands. To make it look more effortless, pull some strands out around your face.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

For a half-up half-down style, simply take the top half of your hair and pull it back with a hair tie. You can leave it as is or add some texture by braiding the pulled-back section or teasing the crown for some volume.

3. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a classic and simple style. Pull your hair back into a low ponytail and wrap a strand of hair around the hair tie to conceal it. You can also add some waves or curls to the ponytail for some added texture.

4. Beachy Waves

To create beachy waves, use a curling wand or flat iron to curl your hair away from your face. Then, use your fingers to comb through the curls for a more relaxed look. Finish with some sea salt spray for a beachy texture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to make these styles even easier to maintain:

  • Use dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle and avoid washing your hair every day.
  • Invest in some hair accessories like headbands, clips, and scrunchies to add some variety to your hairstyles.
  • Experiment with different partings and braids for a unique look.

FAQ

1. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

2. What products should I use for these hairstyles?

You can use a variety of products depending on your hair type and desired style. Some common products include dry shampoo, sea salt spray, hairspray, and hair ties.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. This includes regular trims, using the right products for your hair type, and avoiding excessive heat styling.

Try out these easy to maintain hairstyles for medium length hair and see which ones work best for you. With a little effort and some creativity, you can have a stylish and effortless look every day.
