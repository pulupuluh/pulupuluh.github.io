How To Use Pomade For Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Mar 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Pomade is a popular hair styling product for men that helps to create a sleek, polished look. Whether you're going for a classic slicked-back style or a more relaxed, tousled look, pomade can give you the hold and shine you need. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to using pomade for men's hair, including tips and tricks for getting the perfect style.

Description

Pomade is a hair styling product that comes in a variety of textures and finishes. It's typically made from a blend of wax and oil, which helps to create a strong hold while also adding shine to your hair. Pomade can be used to create a variety of styles, from slicked-back looks to messy, textured styles. It's also easy to apply and can be used on a variety of hair types.

How to Use Pomade for Men's Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Pomade

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to choose the right pomade for your hair type and style. If you have thick, coarse hair, you'll want to choose a pomade with a stronger hold. If you have fine or thin hair, a lighter pomade will work better. Look for pomades that are specifically designed for your hair type and style, and read reviews to find one that works well.

Step 2: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Pomade works best on clean, dry hair. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then dry it thoroughly with a towel. Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can make your hair too dry and difficult to style. If your hair is too damp, the pomade won't hold as well.

Step 3: Apply the Pomade

Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Then, apply it evenly to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Be sure to distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair, using your fingers to work it in.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you've applied the pomade, it's time to style your hair. Use a comb or your fingers to create the look you want. For a slicked-back style, use a comb to smooth your hair back, starting at the front and working your way to the back. For a more textured, messy look, use your fingers to create a tousled style. Be sure to work the pomade in well to ensure a strong hold.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've styled your hair, finish it off with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to keep your style in place all day, and will also add extra hold and shine to your hair. Be sure to use a light hairspray, as heavy products can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Tips for Using Pomade for Men's Hair

Tip 1: Use a Small Amount

Pomade is a powerful styling product, so it's important to use it sparingly. Start with a small amount and add more as needed. Too much pomade can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

Tip 2: Experiment with Different Styles

Pomade can be used to create a variety of styles, so don't be afraid to experiment. Try a classic slicked-back style or a more relaxed, tousled look to see what works best for your hair type and style.

Tip 3: Use a Comb or Brush

For a more polished look, use a comb or brush to style your hair. This will help to distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair and create a sleek, polished look.

FAQ

What is the difference between pomade and wax?

Pomade is typically made from a blend of wax and oil, while wax is made primarily from wax. Pomade is generally easier to apply and has a shinier finish, while wax provides a stronger hold and a matte finish.

How often should I use pomade?

Pomade can be used daily, but it's important to wash your hair regularly to avoid buildup. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.

Can I use pomade on wet hair?

No, pomade is best used on dry hair. Wet hair can dilute the pomade and make it less effective.

Hair Care with Pomade

While pomade can help you achieve a great style, it's important to take care of your hair as well. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and avoid buildup. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it more difficult to style. And be sure to use a light hairspray to finish off your look and keep it in place all day.
