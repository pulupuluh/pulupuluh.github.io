How To Keep Hair Healthy While Using Heat

Written by William May 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Using heat styling tools like curling irons, straighteners, and blow dryers can help you achieve the perfect hairstyle, but it can also cause damage to your hair if not done properly. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to keep your hair healthy while using heat.

Table of Contents

  • How to Use Heat Styling Tools Safely
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Protecting Your Hair
  • Tips for Keeping Your Hair Healthy
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products to Keep Your Hair Healthy

How to Use Heat Styling Tools Safely

Before you start using heat styling tools, it's important to understand how they work and how to use them safely. Here are a few tips:

  • Always use a heat protectant spray or serum before using any heat styling tool. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy.
  • Don't use heat styling tools on wet hair. Wet hair is more prone to damage and breakage, so make sure your hair is completely dry before using any heat styling tool.
  • Use the lowest heat setting possible. High heat can damage your hair, so start with the lowest heat setting and gradually increase it if necessary.
  • Don't use heat styling tools every day. Give your hair a break and try to limit your use of heat styling tools to once or twice a week.

Step-by-Step Guide to Protecting Your Hair

If you want to keep your hair healthy while using heat, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray or serum.
  3. Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting until it's about 80% dry.
  4. Section your hair and use a round brush to blow-dry each section. Make sure to use the lowest heat setting possible and keep the blow dryer moving to avoid damaging your hair.
  5. Once your hair is completely dry, use a heat styling tool to create your desired style. Again, make sure to use the lowest heat setting possible and apply a heat protectant spray or serum before using the tool.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Healthy

Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dyes.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, which can help to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.
  • Avoid using tight hairstyles, which can pull on your hair and cause damage.

FAQs

Q: Can heat styling tools cause hair damage?

A: Yes, heat styling tools can cause hair damage if not used properly. That's why it's important to use a heat protectant spray or serum and to use the lowest heat setting possible.

Q: How often should I use heat styling tools?

A: It's best to limit your use of heat styling tools to once or twice a week to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Products to Keep Your Hair Healthy

Here are some hair care products that can help to keep your hair healthy:

  • Heat protectant spray or serum
  • Gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Hair oil
  • Hair mask

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can keep your hair healthy while using heat styling tools. Remember to always use a heat protectant spray or serum and to use the lowest heat setting possible to avoid damaging your hair.
