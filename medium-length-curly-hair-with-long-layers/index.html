Medium Length Curly Hair With Long Layers The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's easy to manage and looks great, medium length curly hair with long layers is a great option. This style works well for all hair types, whether your hair is naturally curly or you use a curling iron to create the look. In this guide, we'll share tips on how to achieve this style, how to care for your curls, and more.

Description

Medium length curly hair with long layers is a hairstyle that features layers that start at the chin and go down to the ends of your hair. The layers add volume and shape to your curls, making them look fuller and bouncier. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that looks great all the time.

How to Achieve This Style

To achieve this style, you'll need to start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for curly hair. Once your hair is clean and conditioned, use a leave-in conditioner to help define your curls and reduce frizz. Next, apply a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse, gel or cream, to your hair to enhance your curls and help them hold their shape.

After applying your styling products, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help prevent frizz and create defined curls. While drying, use your fingers to scrunch your hair to encourage your curls to form.

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to touch up any curls that need a little extra definition. To finish, apply a shine serum or hairspray to your hair for added shine and hold.

Step by Step

1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to help define your curls and reduce frizz.

3. Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse, gel or cream, to your hair to enhance your curls and help them hold their shape.

4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

5. Scrunch your hair with your fingers while drying to encourage your curls to form.

6. Use a curling iron to touch up any curls that need extra definition.

7. Apply a shine serum or hairspray to your hair for added shine and hold.

Tips

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush to prevent frizz.

- Sleep with your hair in a pineapple or loose bun to help preserve your curls overnight.

- Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze the moisture out of your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will this style work on all hair types?

A: Yes, this style works well on all hair types, whether your hair is naturally curly or you use a curling iron to create the look.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I achieve this style without using heat?

A: Yes, you can achieve this style without using heat by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer or air-drying your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to care for them properly. Here are some tips for caring for your curls:

- Use products that are designed for curly hair.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

- Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

- Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.

- Sleep with your hair in a pineapple or loose bun to help preserve your curls overnight.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve and maintain beautiful medium length curly hair with long layers. Try it out for yourself and enjoy your new low-maintenance, yet stylish look!
