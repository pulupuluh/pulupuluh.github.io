Haircuts For Oblong Face Female A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 28, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your oblong face? Don’t worry; you are not alone. Many women with an oblong face shape find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that complements their features. But with the right guidance and knowledge, you can easily enhance your looks with the perfect haircut.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with a high forehead and a prominent chin. The key to finding the perfect haircut for this face shape is to create the illusion of width and volume. The right haircut can enhance your features, balance out your face shape, and highlight your best features.

How To Choose The Right Haircut

The right haircut can make a world of difference to your overall appearance. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect haircut for your oblong face.

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

When choosing a haircut, it is essential to consider your hair texture. If you have thick hair, opt for layers to add volume and movement. If you have fine hair, go for a blunt cut to create the illusion of thickness.

2. Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a haircut, it is essential to consider your lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle, opt for a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, you can choose a haircut that requires more styling.

3. Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a haircut, it is essential to consider your face shape. For an oblong face shape, opt for haircuts that add width to the sides of your face. Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear longer.

Step By Step Guide To The Perfect Haircut

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect haircut for your oblong face shape.

1. Consult With A Hairstylist

The first step to achieving the perfect haircut is to consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you choose the right haircut that complements your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

2. Choose The Right Length

The right length for an oblong face shape is a shoulder-length haircut or shorter. Avoid haircuts that fall below your shoulders, as this can make your face appear longer.

3. Add Layers

Adding layers to your haircut can help add volume and movement to your hair. Layers can also help balance out your face shape and highlight your best features.

4. Opt For Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help add width to the sides of your face and balance out your face shape. Avoid blunt bangs or center-parted hairstyles, as this can make your face appear longer.

Tips For Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your perfect haircut.

1. Use The Right Haircare Products

Using the right haircare products can help keep your hair healthy and maintain your haircut. Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and hair concerns.

2. Visit Your Hairstylist Regularly

Regular visits to your hairstylist can help maintain the shape and style of your haircut. Visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim or touch-up.

FAQs

1. Can I wear a long hairstyle with an oblong face?

It is best to avoid long hairstyles that fall below your shoulders, as this can make your face appear longer. Opt for a shoulder-length haircut or shorter.

2. Can I wear bangs with an oblong face?

Side-swept bangs can help add width to the sides of your face and balance out your face shape. Avoid blunt bangs or center-parted hairstyles, as this can make your face appear longer.

Hair Care Tips For Obong Face Female

1. Use Haircare Products Designed For Your Hair Type

Using the right haircare products can help keep your hair healthy and maintain your haircut. Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and hair concerns.

2. Avoid Over-Styling Your Hair

Over-styling your hair can damage your hair and make it appear dry and brittle. Avoid over-styling your hair and use heat protectant products before using heat styling tools.

3. Protect Your Hair From The Sun

The sun can damage your hair and make it appear dry and brittle. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using haircare products that contain SPF.

4. Eat A Balanced Diet

A balanced diet can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Eat a diet that is rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily find the perfect haircut for your oblong face shape. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and choose a haircut that complements your hair texture, lifestyle, and face shape.
