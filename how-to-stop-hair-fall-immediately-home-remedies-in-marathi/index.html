How To Stop Hair Fall Immediately Home Remedies In Marathi

Written by Bardi May 23, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of seeing your hair falling out every time you comb or wash it? Hair fall can be caused by various factors such as stress, poor nutrition, genetics, and hormonal changes. However, there are several natural remedies that you can use to stop hair fall immediately at home. In this article, we will provide you with some effective home remedies in Marathi to prevent hair fall and promote hair growth.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some easy and effective home remedies to stop hair fall immediately at home:

1. Amla

Amla or Indian gooseberry is a rich source of vitamin C, which helps in promoting hair growth and preventing hair fall. To use amla for hair, you can either apply amla oil on your scalp or mix amla powder with water to make a paste and apply it on your hair. Leave it for 30 minutes and then wash it off with water.

2. Onion Juice

Onion juice contains sulfur, which helps in promoting collagen production and increasing blood circulation to the scalp. To use onion juice for hair, extract the juice from an onion and apply it on your hair. Leave it for 15-20 minutes and then wash it off with water.

3. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is rich in fatty acids that nourish the hair and prevent hair fall. To use coconut oil for hair, warm up the oil and apply it on your scalp. Massage for a few minutes and leave it overnight. Wash your hair in the morning with a mild shampoo.

4. Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek seeds are rich in protein and nicotinic acid, which help in promoting hair growth and preventing hair fall. To use fenugreek seeds for hair, soak them in water overnight and grind them into a paste. Apply the paste on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes. Rinse it off with water.

Tips for Preventing Hair Fall

Here are some tips that can help you prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools on your hair
  • Do not comb or brush your hair when it is wet
  • Reduce stress levels through meditation, yoga, or exercise
  • Get enough sleep and rest to promote hair growth

FAQs

1. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress can cause hair fall as it disrupts the normal hair growth cycle.

2. Can genetics cause hair fall?

Yes, genetics can play a role in hair fall as it can cause hormonal imbalances that lead to hair loss.

3. Can hair fall be reversed?

It depends on the cause of hair fall. If it is caused by poor nutrition or stress, it can be reversed by adopting a healthy lifestyle and using natural remedies.

Hair Care Routine

Along with using these home remedies, it is important to maintain a proper hair care routine to prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Do not tie your hair too tightly as it can cause hair breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By using these home remedies and following a proper hair care routine, you can prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. It is important to be patient and consistent with your hair care regimen to see results.
