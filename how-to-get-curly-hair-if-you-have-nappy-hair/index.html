How To Get Curly Hair If You Have Nappy Hair

Written by Frank Jul 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of having nappy hair and want to switch things up with curly hair? We’ve got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing everything you need to know about how to get curly hair if you have nappy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Nappy hair is natural, curly or coily hair that has not been chemically relaxed. It can be difficult to manage and style, but with the right techniques, it can be transformed into beautiful, curly hair. Curling nappy hair requires the use of heat and styling products, but with proper care, you can maintain healthy, curly hair.

How To

There are various methods to get curly hair if you have nappy hair. Here are a few:

  • Use a curling iron or wand.
  • Use flexi rods or perm rods.
  • Get a perm or texturizer.

Step by Step

Using a Curling Iron or Wand

Here are the steps to curling nappy hair using a curling iron or wand:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply heat protectant to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small parts.
  4. Wrap each section around the curling iron or wand.
  5. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  6. Repeat until all sections are curled.
  7. Apply hair oil or serum to add shine.

Using Flexi Rods or Perm Rods

Here are the steps to curling nappy hair using flexi rods or perm rods:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small parts.
  4. Wrap each section around the rod.
  5. Secure the rods in place.
  6. Leave the rods in overnight.
  7. Remove the rods in the morning.
  8. Apply hair oil or serum to add shine.

Getting a Perm or Texturizer

Here are the steps to getting a perm or texturizer:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist.
  2. Choose the type of perm or texturizer you want.
  3. Follow the stylist’s instructions for preparation and aftercare.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when curling nappy hair:

  • Use heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent friction and reduce breakage.
  • Do not use too much heat or leave styling products in your hair for too long.

FAQ

Can I curl nappy hair without heat?

Yes, you can curl nappy hair without heat by using flexi rods or perm rods.

Can I use a curling iron on nappy hair?

Yes, you can use a curling iron on nappy hair, but be sure to use heat protectant to prevent damage.

What is the difference between a perm and a texturizer?

A perm permanently changes the texture of your hair, while a texturizer loosens the curl pattern without permanently altering it.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to maintain healthy, curly hair:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat on your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, curly hair even if you have nappy hair. Remember to take care of your hair and be patient as you experiment with different styling methods.
