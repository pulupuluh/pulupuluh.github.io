How To Cut Men s Hair Short With Scissors Only A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby May 24, 2023 · 7 min read

In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's hair short using only scissors. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced hairdresser, this guide will help you achieve a sleek and stylish haircut for any occasion. Let's get started!

Description

Cutting men's hair short with scissors only is a great way to achieve a clean and polished look. This method is perfect for those who want to avoid clippers or razors, or simply prefer the control and precision that scissors provide. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a variety of short hairstyles that suit any face shape or hair type.

How to Cut Men's Hair Short with Scissors Only

Step 1: Prep the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair thoroughly. Brush or comb it to remove any tangles or knots. If the hair is particularly thick or curly, you may want to use a thinning shear to reduce the bulk before cutting.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start at the back and work your way forward, creating a horizontal parting at the nape of the neck. Use the comb to lift a section of hair and cut it to the desired length. Repeat this process for each section, working your way up the head.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

For the sides, comb the hair straight down and cut it to the desired length using the scissors. You can use a comb or your fingers to create a guide for the length. Be sure to keep the blade of the scissors parallel to the hair to achieve an even cut.

Step 4: Trim the Top

To trim the top, comb the hair straight up and cut it to the desired length. You can use a comb or your fingers to create a guide for the length. Be sure to cut the hair in small sections to avoid any mistakes.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

To blend the hair, use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle, moving from the shorter hair to the longer hair. This will create a seamless transition between the different lengths.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors to achieve a clean cut.
  • Start with longer hair and work your way shorter to avoid any mistakes.
  • Take your time and be patient.
  • Keep the hair wet to make it easier to cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the haircut from different angles.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what suits you best.

FAQ

Can you cut men's hair short with scissors only?

Yes, you can cut men's hair short with scissors only. This method is perfect for those who want to avoid clippers or razors, or simply prefer the control and precision that scissors provide.

What kind of scissors should I use to cut men's hair?

You should use sharp, high-quality scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Avoid using regular household scissors, as they can damage the hair and create uneven cuts.

How do I know what length to cut the hair?

The length you cut the hair depends on your personal preference and the style you're going for. You can use a comb or your fingers to create a guide for the length, or consult with your client if you're cutting someone else's hair.

Hair Care

After cutting the hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the style and prevent damage. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often. Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the haircut.

That's it! With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut men's hair short with scissors only. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
