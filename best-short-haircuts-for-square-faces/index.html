Best Short Haircuts For Square Faces

Written by Aprilia Jul 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for the perfect short haircut for your square face shape? Square faces often have a strong jawline and wide forehead, which can make it challenging to find a style that complements your features. Fortunately, we've compiled a list of the best short haircuts for square faces to help you find the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Short Haircuts for Square Faces
  • Tips for Styling Short Haircuts
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Short Haircuts

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best short haircuts for square faces, it's essential to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Take a photo of yourself facing the camera.
  2. Draw a line across your forehead and another line across your cheekbones.
  3. Measure the distance between your cheekbones.
  4. Measure the distance between your jawline and forehead.
  5. Compare your measurements to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

If your measurements indicate a square face shape, read on for the best short haircuts for your features.

Best Short Haircuts for Square Faces

When it comes to short haircuts for square faces, the goal is to soften your angular features. Here are some of the best cuts to consider:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that flatters square faces by drawing attention to the eyes and cheekbones. Opt for a slightly longer cut on top with layered sides to soften your features.

Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs is a great choice for square faces because it adds movement and softness to your features. The side-swept bangs draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, balancing out your jawline.

Layered Lob

A layered lob (long bob) is another excellent option for square faces. The layers add movement and texture to your hair, while the length helps to elongate your face shape. Opt for a side part and soft waves for a flattering look.

Tips for Styling Short Haircuts

Now that you've found the perfect short haircut for your square face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what flatters your face shape.
  • Add volume to your roots with a volumizing spray or mousse.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What other factors should I consider when choosing a short haircut?

When choosing a short haircut, consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. If you have fine hair, for example, you may want to opt for a style with more layers to add volume. If you lead an active lifestyle, a low-maintenance cut may be best.

Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

Yes! Short haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos and half-up styles. Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your hair length and texture.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair gently.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and the best short haircuts for square faces, you'll be sure to find a style that flatters your features and boosts your confidence. Don't be afraid to experiment with different looks until you find the perfect one for you!
