Mens Haircuts Short Sides Long On Top

Written by Luffy Aug 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Short sides long on top is a popular hairstyle for men that has been around for quite some time, and it shows no signs of going out of style anytime soon. It’s a versatile haircut that can be styled in a variety of ways, and it looks great on most face shapes and hair types. In this article, we’ll be discussing everything you need to know about mens haircuts short sides long on top, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care.

Description

The short sides long on top haircut is exactly what it sounds like – short hair on the sides and longer hair on top. The length on top can vary depending on personal preference, but it’s typically long enough to be styled in different ways. This haircut is also known as the undercut, as the sides are undercut to create a sharp contrast with the longer hair on top.

How to

If you’re thinking of getting a short sides long on top haircut, here’s how to get the look:

  1. Decide on the length you want on top, and communicate this to your barber or stylist.
  2. Ask for a fade or undercut on the sides, depending on how drastic you want the contrast to be.
  3. Style your hair on top using a pomade or wax, depending on the hold and shine you want.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style your short sides long on top haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your fingertips.
  3. Rub your hands together to evenly distribute the product.
  4. Begin styling your hair on top, starting at the front and working your way towards the back.
  5. Use your fingers to create texture and volume.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or finishing product, if desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short sides long on top haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the style you want to show your barber or stylist.
  • Be clear about the length you want on top and the type of fade or undercut you prefer.
  • Invest in a good quality pomade or wax to style your hair on top.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens haircuts short sides long on top:

Is the short sides long on top haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can work on most hair types, but it may require different styling techniques depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain this style?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows, but most men will need to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length on top and the sharpness of the fade or undercut.

Can I style my short sides long on top haircut differently for different occasions?

Absolutely! This haircut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, from a slicked-back look for formal events to a messy, textured look for more casual occasions.

Hair Care

To keep your short sides long on top haircut looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and cause split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length on top and the sharpness of the fade or undercut.

In conclusion, the short sides long on top haircut is a classic, versatile style that looks great on most men. Whether you prefer a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style, this haircut can be tailored to suit your personal style and preferences. Follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your short sides long on top looking its best!
