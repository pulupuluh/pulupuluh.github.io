Is Having Cornrows Haram Everything You Need To Know

Written by William Apr 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cornrows have been a popular hairstyle for centuries, and for many, they are a cultural symbol of pride and identity. However, for some, there is a question of whether having cornrows is haram, or forbidden, in Islam. In this article, we will explore this topic in detail and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

What Are Cornrows?

Cornrows are a hairstyle that involves braiding the hair close to the scalp in a continuous, raised row. They can be simple or complex, and can be styled in a variety of patterns and designs. Cornrows are typically worn by people of African descent, but can be worn by people of all ethnicities.

Is Having Cornrows Haram?

There is no clear answer to this question, as opinions on the matter vary widely among Islamic scholars and communities. Some believe that cornrows are haram because they involve altering the natural state of the hair, which is seen as a form of imitating the opposite gender. Others argue that cornrows are permissible as long as they are not done for the purpose of imitating the opposite gender or to attract attention.

What Do the Hadith and Quran Say?

There is no specific mention of cornrows in either the Hadith or Quran, so there is no clear guidance on whether they are permissible or not. However, there are general guidelines in Islam that may be applicable to this topic.

How to Style Cornrows?

If you decide that you would like to style your hair in cornrows, there are a few things that you should keep in mind. Here are some tips:

  1. Choose a skilled hairstylist who has experience with cornrows.
  2. Make sure your hair is clean and well-moisturized before getting your cornrows done.
  3. Avoid excessive tension on the scalp, as this can cause hair loss and damage.
  4. Take care of your cornrows by washing them regularly, moisturizing your scalp, and avoiding excessive pulling or tugging.

FAQs

Can Men Wear Cornrows?

Yes, men can wear cornrows. In fact, cornrows have a long history of being worn by men in many African cultures.

Can Women Wear Cornrows?

Yes, women can wear cornrows. In fact, cornrows are a popular hairstyle for women of African descent.

Can You Pray with Cornrows?

Yes, you can pray with cornrows. However, it is important to make sure that your hair is clean and free from any products that may interfere with your ablutions.

Hair Care Tips for Cornrows

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair while wearing cornrows:

  1. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  2. Avoid using heavy oils or products that can clog your pores.
  3. Moisturize your scalp with a light oil or leave-in conditioner.
  4. Avoid pulling or tugging on your cornrows, as this can cause hair loss and damage.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the question of whether having cornrows is haram is a complex one that does not have a clear answer. Ultimately, it is up to each individual to decide whether they believe wearing cornrows is permissible or not. However, if you do decide to wear cornrows, it is important to take good care of your hair and scalp to avoid any potential damage or hair loss.
