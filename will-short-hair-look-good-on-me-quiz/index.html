Will Short Hair Look Good On Me Quiz A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair is a bold and trendy hairstyle that can completely transform your look. But, before you chop off your locks, it's important to ask yourself the question: will short hair look good on me? To help you answer this question, we've created a quiz that will guide you towards the right decision. In this article, we'll give you step-by-step instructions on how to take the quiz, tips on how to style short hair, and hair care advice to keep your hair looking its best. Let's get started!

How to Take the Quiz

The quiz is designed to help you determine if short hair will suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. To take the quiz, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

To take the quiz, you'll need a mirror, a measuring tape, and a notebook or piece of paper to record your answers.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Shape

Your face shape is an important factor in determining whether short hair will look good on you. To determine your face shape, use the measuring tape to measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure from the peak of one eyebrow arch to the peak of the opposite arch.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jaw angles upward. Multiply that number by two to get your jawline measurement.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, use the chart below to determine your face shape:

Record your face shape in your notebook or piece of paper.

Step 3: Answer the Quiz Questions

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to answer the quiz questions. The quiz consists of 10 questions that will help you determine if short hair will look good on you. Here are some sample questions:

  • What is the texture of your hair?
  • Do you have a high or low hairline?
  • What is your personal style?

Answer each question honestly and record your answers in your notebook or piece of paper.

Step 4: Calculate Your Results

Once you've answered all the quiz questions, it's time to calculate your results. Use the chart below to assign points to each of your answers:

Based on your results, you'll have a better idea of whether short hair will look good on you.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

If you've decided to go for the chop, here are some tips for styling short hair:

  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Talk to your hairstylist about the best short hairstyle for your face shape and hair texture.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and clips, to add some flair to your short hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain your short hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on your face shape and the specific short hairstyle you choose. Talk to your hairstylist about the best short hairstyle for your face shape.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if I have thin hair?

A: Yes! There are many short hairstyles that work well with thin hair. Talk to your hairstylist about the best options for your hair texture.

Q: Will short hair be difficult to maintain?

A: It depends on the specific short hairstyle you choose. Some short hairstyles require more maintenance than others. Talk to your hairstylist about the best short hairstyle for your lifestyle.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh hair products, such as those containing sulfates.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your short hairstyle.

With these tips and the results from our quiz, you'll be able to confidently decide whether short hair will look good on you. Good luck!
