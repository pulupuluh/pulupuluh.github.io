How To Do An Undercut On A Boy

Written by Aprilia Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for your boy? An undercut is a popular hairstyle that has been in vogue for a while now. It involves keeping the hair on the sides and back of the head short while leaving the top long. If you're wondering how to do an undercut on a boy, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll take you through the step-by-step process of achieving the perfect undercut. So, let's get started!

Description

The undercut is a versatile hairstyle that suits almost every face shape and hair type. It involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of the head, leaving the hair on top long. This gives the hairstyle a sharp and edgy look that is perfect for boys who want to make a statement. The undercut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling and is perfect for those who lead busy lives.

How to

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here's how to do an undercut on a boy:

Step 1: Choose the Length

Decide on the length of the undercut you want. It's best to start with longer hair and gradually work your way down. Use the clippers to shave the sides and back of the head to the desired length. Keep in mind that the shorter you go, the more prominent the undercut will be.

Step 2: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the top of the hair to the desired length. You can leave it long or go for a shorter look. Make sure the top is even and blends well with the sides and back.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

Use the clippers to blend the hair between the top and the sides. This will create a seamless transition and give the undercut a polished look.

Step 4: Style the Hair

You can style the hair on top any way you like. You can use pomade or wax to give it texture and hold. For a more casual look, you can leave it messy and tousled.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing an undercut:

  • Start with longer hair and gradually work your way down.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head.
  • Blend the hair between the top and the sides for a polished look.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

1. Will an undercut suit my boy's face shape?

Yes, an undercut suits almost every face shape. However, it's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your boy's face shape.

2. How often should I get my boy's undercut trimmed?

It depends on how fast your boy's hair grows. On average, you should get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

3. Is an undercut high-maintenance?

No, an undercut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. However, you'll need to get it trimmed regularly to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care

To keep your boy's undercut looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition the hair to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of the undercut.

Now that you know how to do an undercut on a boy, it's time to give it a try. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to achieve the perfect undercut that your boy will love.
