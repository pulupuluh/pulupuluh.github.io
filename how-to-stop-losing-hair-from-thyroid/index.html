How To Stop Losing Hair From Thyroid Tips And Hair Care

Written by Bardi Mar 02, 2023 · 6 min read

Losing hair from thyroid can be a frustrating and stressful experience. If you are one of the millions of people who suffer from hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism, you may be wondering what you can do to stop the loss of your hair. Fortunately, there are steps you can take to minimize hair loss and promote healthy hair growth. In this article, we’ll explore how to stop losing hair from thyroid and provide tips to help you care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Stop Losing Hair from Thyroid
  2. Hair Care Tips
  3. FAQs

How to Stop Losing Hair from Thyroid

When thyroid hormone levels are imbalanced, it can cause hair loss. Here’s what you can do to stop losing hair from thyroid:

1. Get Treatment for Your Thyroid Condition

The first step in stopping hair loss from thyroid is to get treatment for your thyroid condition. Whether you have hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism, getting your thyroid hormone levels back to normal can help stop hair loss.

2. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth. Foods rich in iron, zinc, and biotin can be particularly beneficial for hair health.

3. Take Supplements

If you’re not getting enough nutrients from your diet, taking supplements can help fill in the gaps. Biotin, iron, and vitamin D are all important for healthy hair growth.

4. Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair relaxers, which can damage your hair and cause it to break.

5. Be Gentle with Your Hair

Be gentle with your hair when you wash and style it. Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, which can damage your hair. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging on it.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to the above tips, here are some hair care tips to help you care for your hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Apply it to your hair after you wash it and leave it in.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a scarf. The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.

4. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

5. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help promote healthy hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back after thyroid treatment?

A: It can take several months for hair to grow back after thyroid treatment. Be patient and continue taking care of your hair.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss from thyroid?

A: Yes, stress can contribute to hair loss in people with thyroid conditions.

Q: Does thyroid medication cause hair loss?

A: In rare cases, thyroid medication can cause hair loss. Talk to your doctor if you experience hair loss after starting thyroid medication.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can stop losing hair from thyroid and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient, as it can take time for your hair to grow back.
