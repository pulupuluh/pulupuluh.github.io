How Can I Change My Hair In Animal Crossing New Leaf

Written by Oliver May 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Animal Crossing New Leaf is a popular game where players can create their own avatar and live in a virtual world. One of the fun features of the game is the ability to change your character's appearance, including their hair. In this article, we will discuss how you can change your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf.

Description

Changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf is a great way to switch up your look and add some personality to your character. There are a variety of hairstyles to choose from, including short, long, curly, and straight. Additionally, you can choose different hair colors and even add accessories like hats or headbands.

How To

To change your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf, you will need to visit the hair salon in the game, which is located on Main Street. Once inside, you will be able to talk to Harriet, the owner of the salon, and choose from a variety of hairstyles.

Here are the steps to change your hair:

Step 1: Visit the Hair Salon

Go to Main Street and locate the hair salon. It is located on the left side of the street, next to the shoe store.

Step 2: Talk to Harriet

Once you enter the salon, talk to Harriet and choose the option "Make Me Over!" She will then ask you what you want to change about your appearance.

Step 3: Choose Your Hairstyle

Select the hairstyle that you want from the menu. You can choose from a variety of styles, including short, long, curly, and straight. You can also choose the hair color that you want.

Step 4: Confirm Your Choice

Once you have chosen your hairstyle, Harriet will show you a preview of what it will look like on your character. If you are happy with your choice, confirm it and your character's hair will be changed.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf:

  • Make sure to save your game before you change your hair, just in case you don't like the new style.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and colors to find the perfect look for your character.
  • Consider adding accessories like hats or headbands to complete your character's look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf:

Can I Change My Hair More Than Once?

Yes, you can change your hair as many times as you want. However, there is a fee of 3,000 bells each time you change your hair.

Can I Change My Hair Color?

Yes, you can choose from a variety of hair colors when changing your hair. You can even choose multiple colors for a unique look.

Hair Care

Just like in real life, it's important to take care of your character's hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit the hair salon regularly to keep your character's hair looking fresh.
  • Try not to wear hats or other accessories too often, as they can cause damage to your character's hair.
  • Consider using hair care products like shampoo and conditioner to keep your character's hair healthy and shiny.

Changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf is a fun way to switch up your character's look and add some personality to the game. Just remember to save your game before making any changes and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and colors!
