How To Make Your Hair Look Short Like A Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and want to try something new, why not try making your hair look short like a boy's? It's a bold and daring look that can be pulled off by anyone, regardless of gender. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of achieving this look, including tips and tricks for maintaining it.

How To:

1. Start by washing your hair thoroughly. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and make sure your hair is clean and free of dirt and oil.

2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp. Avoid using a hair dryer as it can cause damage and frizz.

3. Use a comb to part your hair in the middle. Make sure the part is straight and even.

4. Use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Start by cutting small sections at a time and work your way around your head.

5. Use a razor to texture the ends of your hair. This will give it a more natural, messy look.

6. Use hair wax or pomade to style your hair. Apply a small amount to your fingers and work it through your hair, starting from the back and working forward.

Step-by-Step:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry until damp.

Step 2: Part your hair

Use a comb to part your hair in the middle. Make sure the part is straight and even.

Step 3: Cut your hair

Using a pair of scissors, cut your hair to the desired length. Start by cutting small sections at a time and work your way around your head.

Step 4: Texture your hair

Use a razor to texture the ends of your hair. This will give it a more natural, messy look.

Step 5: Style your hair

Apply hair wax or pomade to your fingers and work it through your hair, starting from the back and working forward. Style your hair as desired.

Tips:

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting your hair

- Start with a longer length and gradually cut it shorter until you achieve the desired look

- Experiment with different hair products to find one that works best for your hair type

FAQ:

1. Can I achieve this look with long hair?

It's possible, but it may take more time and effort to achieve the desired look. You may need to cut your hair shorter and use more hair product to style it.

2. Do I need to visit a salon to achieve this look?

No, you can achieve this look at home with the right tools and products. However, if you're unsure or uncomfortable cutting your own hair, it's always best to visit a professional stylist.

3. How often should I cut my hair to maintain this look?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the length. Generally, a trim every 4-6 weeks should suffice.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair after achieving this look. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and dryness. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible and use a heat protectant spray if you must use them.

Overall, making your hair look short like a boy's is a fun and daring look to try. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect style and maintain it with proper hair care.
