How To Style Permed Hair After Shower Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jun 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have permed hair, you know how difficult it can be to style it after a shower. The curls can become frizzy, and the hair can lose its shape. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll share with you some tips and tricks on how to style permed hair after a shower.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Permed Hair After Shower
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Permed Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Permed Hair

How to Style Permed Hair After Shower

The first thing you need to do is to apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to detangle your hair without causing any damage.

Next, use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze out excess water from your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as this can cause frizz.

Once your hair is damp, apply a curl-defining product to your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and keep them in place.

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to prevent frizz and keep your curls intact.

Once your hair is dry, use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through your curls. Avoid using a brush as this can break up your curls and cause frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style permed hair after a shower:

  1. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair
  2. Squeeze out excess water with a microfiber towel
  3. Apply a curl-defining product to your hair
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through your curls

Tips for Styling Permed Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your permed hair after a shower:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to detangle your hair
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel to prevent frizz
  • Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to prevent frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through your curls
  • Avoid using a brush as it can break up your curls and cause frizz

FAQs

How often should I wash my permed hair?

You should wash your permed hair every 2-3 days. Washing it too often can cause the curls to lose their shape.

Can I use a regular towel to dry my permed hair?

It's best to avoid using a regular towel to dry your permed hair as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.

Can I use a brush to comb my permed hair?

It's best to avoid using a brush to comb your permed hair as it can break up your curls and cause frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb instead.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your permed hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your permed hair after a shower without causing any damage or frizz.
