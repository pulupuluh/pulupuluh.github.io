Description

Inverted triangle face shape is characterized by a wide forehead and narrow jawline. This type of face shape is also known as heart-shaped face. The aim of hairstyles for inverted triangle face shape is to balance the forehead and jawline, and create an illusion of a more oval face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape is essential in choosing the right hairstyles that will flatter your features. To determine if you have an inverted triangle face shape, measure your face from the widest point of your forehead to the tip of your chin. Then, measure your jawline from the widest point of your jaw to the tip of your chin. If your forehead measurement is wider than your jawline measurement, you have an inverted triangle face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Inverted Triangle Face Shape Hairstyles

When choosing hairstyles for inverted triangle face shape, go for styles that create volume at the jawline and adds width to the chin area. Here are some step by step guides to inverted triangle face shape hairstyles:

Short Hair

Short hairstyles work well with inverted triangle face shape as they draw attention away from the forehead. Go for pixie cuts or bob hairstyles that add volume at the back of the head and create width at the jawline.

Medium Hair

For medium-length hair, try layered hairstyles that add volume to the chin area. Bouncy curls also work well for inverted triangle face shape as they create an illusion of a fuller jawline.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, go for hairstyles that add width at the bottom such as layered waves or loose curls. Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the top as they will only emphasize the wide forehead.

Tips for Inverted Triangle Face Shape Hairstyles

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing hairstyles for inverted triangle face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the top.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume at the jawline.
  • Try side-swept bangs that cover some of the forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight at the sides as they will only emphasize the narrow jawline.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I still wear long hairstyles if I have an inverted triangle face shape?

A: Yes, you can still wear long hairstyles as long as they add width at the bottom and avoid adding volume at the top.

Q: What are some hairstyles to avoid for inverted triangle face shape?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the top such as high buns or ponytails. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short at the sides as they will emphasize the narrow jawline.

Hair Care for Inverted Triangle Face Shape

Maintaining healthy hair is essential in achieving a flattering hairstyle for inverted triangle face shape. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the hair.
  • Avoid over-styling with heat tools as this can damage the hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
