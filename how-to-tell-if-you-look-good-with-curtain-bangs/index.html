How To Tell If You Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Eveline May 26, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you thinking about getting curtain bangs, but not sure if they'll look good on you? Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add some volume and texture to your hair while framing your face. But not everyone can pull them off. In this article, we'll show you how to tell if you look good with curtain bangs.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tell If You Look Good with Curtain Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Curtain Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

How to Tell If You Look Good with Curtain Bangs

The first thing you should consider when thinking about getting curtain bangs is your face shape. Curtain bangs work best on oval or heart-shaped faces, as they can help balance out the proportions of your face. If you have a round or square face, you may want to avoid curtain bangs, as they can make your face look wider.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. Curtain bangs work best on hair that is straight or has a slight wave. If you have curly hair, you may find that your bangs are difficult to style and maintain.

Finally, think about your personal style. If you prefer a more classic or conservative look, curtain bangs may not be for you. But if you like to experiment with different hairstyles and are willing to put in the time to style your bangs, then you may be a good candidate for curtain bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you've considered the above factors and still think curtain bangs may be right for you, here's how to tell if you look good with them:

  1. Take a selfie or stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use your fingers or a comb to create a mock bang. This can help you visualize how you'll look with curtain bangs.
  3. Consider the length and thickness of the bangs. Curtain bangs should be longer on the sides and slightly shorter in the middle, creating a "V" shape. They should also be thin and wispy, rather than thick and blunt.
  4. Think about how the bangs will frame your face. Curtain bangs should start at the outer corners of your eyes and reach down to your cheekbones.
  5. Consider how the bangs will blend with the rest of your hair. Curtain bangs should blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair, rather than looking like a separate piece.

Tips for Curtain Bangs

Now that you've decided to go for it, here are some tips for rocking your new curtain bangs:

  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs. This will help create volume and texture.
  • Consider using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some grit to your bangs.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and neat.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add some dimension to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Do curtain bangs work on curly hair?

Curtain bangs can be more difficult to style and maintain on curly hair, but they can still look great if you're willing to put in the effort.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and neat.

Can I wear my curtain bangs up?

Yes, you can wear your curtain bangs up or down, depending on your preference.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Finally, here are some tips for taking care of your curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your bangs.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray before blow drying your bangs.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them look greasy.

By following these tips, you can rock your new curtain bangs with confidence!
