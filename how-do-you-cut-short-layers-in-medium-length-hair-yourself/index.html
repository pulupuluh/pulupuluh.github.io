How Do You Cut Short Layers In Medium Length Hair Yourself

Written by Christine Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of paying hefty salon bills for a simple haircut? Do you want to try something new with your medium length hair? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will guide you on how you can cut short layers in medium length hair yourself. We will give you step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut. So, let's get started!

Description

Short layers are a great way to add volume and texture to medium length hair. They are also easy to maintain and style. However, getting a haircut at a salon can be expensive, and not everyone has the time to visit a salon regularly. Cutting your hair at home is an excellent way to save money and time. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.

How to Cut Short Layers in Medium Length Hair Yourself

Cutting short layers in medium length hair may seem like a daunting task, but it is relatively simple. Here are the things you will need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these step-by-step instructions to cut short layers in medium length hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair as you usually would.
  2. Part your hair in the middle and comb it straight.
  3. Divide your hair into two sections and clip them up.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of one of the sections and comb it straight.
  5. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the chin and working your way up.
  6. Repeat this process on the other side of your head.
  7. Release the top section of your hair and comb it straight.
  8. Take a small section of hair from the back and cut it at an angle.
  9. Continue cutting small sections of hair at an angle until you have cut all the hair in the back.
  10. Release the front sections of your hair and cut them at an angle.
  11. Trim any uneven sections and adjust the length if necessary.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair at Home

Cutting your hair at home can be a little intimidating, but with the right tips, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors.
  • Start with small sections of hair and cut a little at a time.
  • Use a comb to keep your hair straight while cutting.
  • Make sure your hair is dry before cutting it.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will shrink when it dries.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or uncomfortable.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you cut the back of your hair.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

It is not recommended to cut your hair with regular scissors, as they may not be sharp enough and can damage your hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Is it possible to fix a bad haircut?

Yes, it is possible to fix a bad haircut. You can either trim it yourself or visit a salon and have a professional fix it for you.

Hair Care Tips

After you cut your hair, it is essential to take care of it to maintain its health and shape. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and health.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair.

With these tips and step-by-step instructions, you can cut short layers in medium length hair yourself. Remember to take your time, be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help. Happy cutting!
