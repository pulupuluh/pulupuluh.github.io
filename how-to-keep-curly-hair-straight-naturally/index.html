How To Keep Curly Hair Straight Naturally

Written by Eveline Jul 04, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to keep it straight. Straightening your hair can be damaging and time-consuming, but there are natural ways to achieve straight hair without harming your locks. In this article, we’ll discuss tips and tricks to keep curly hair straight naturally without the use of heat or chemicals.

Description

Many people with curly hair desire straight hair, but traditional methods such as using a flat iron or chemical treatments can be damaging to the hair. Natural methods, however, can be gentle and effective in achieving straight hair. By using natural techniques and products, you can keep your curls at bay and flaunt straight hair without any harm to your hair.

How to

1. Use a Hair Mask

One of the best ways to keep curly hair straight naturally is to use a hair mask. This will keep your hair moisturized and conditioned, which is important for maintaining straight hair. You can use a store-bought hair mask or make your own with ingredients like coconut oil, honey, and avocado.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair is a great way to achieve straight hair without using a flat iron. First, apply a heat protectant to your hair. Then, use a round brush to blow dry your hair in sections. Make sure to use the cool setting on your blow dryer to avoid damaging your hair.

3. Use a Hair Straightening Brush

A hair straightening brush is a great tool for keeping curly hair straight. It’s much gentler on your hair than a flat iron and can be used on damp hair. Simply brush your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

4. Tie Your Hair in a Bun

Another natural way to keep curly hair straight is to tie it in a bun. After washing your hair, comb it straight and tie it in a bun. Leave it in overnight and when you take it down in the morning, your hair will be straighter.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to keeping your curly hair straight naturally is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for straight hair.

Step 2: Apply a Hair Mask

After washing your hair, apply a hair mask to keep your hair moisturized and conditioned. Leave it in for 10-15 minutes and then rinse it out.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair in sections using a round brush. Use the cool setting on your blow dryer to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Use a Hair Straightening Brush

Use a hair straightening brush to brush your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Tie Your Hair in a Bun

Tie your hair in a bun after combing it straight. Leave it in overnight and take it down in the morning for straighter hair.

Tips

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using products with harsh chemicals such as sulfates and parabens.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I use a hair mask?

A: You can use a hair mask once a week or as needed to keep your hair moisturized and conditioned.

Q: Can I use a flat iron to straighten my curly hair?

A: While a flat iron can be used to straighten curly hair, it can be damaging to your hair. It’s best to use natural methods such as a hair straightening brush or tying your hair in a bun.

Hair Care

Keeping your curly hair straight naturally requires proper hair care. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for straight hair, and avoid using products with harsh chemicals. Use a hair mask once a week or as needed to keep your hair moisturized and conditioned. When blow drying your hair or using a hair straightening brush, use the cool setting to avoid damaging your hair. Tie your hair in a bun after combing it straight to achieve straighter hair naturally.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your curly hair straight naturally without the use of heat or chemicals. Not only will your hair look great, but it will also be healthier and stronger in the long run.
