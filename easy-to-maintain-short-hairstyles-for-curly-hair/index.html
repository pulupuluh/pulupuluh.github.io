Easy To Maintain Short Hairstyles For Curly Hair

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it looks amazing and can make you feel like a million bucks. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to styling. This is why many curly-haired women opt for short hairstyles that are easy to maintain. In this article, we will be discussing some of the best short hairstyles for curly hair that are not only easy to maintain but also look amazing.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Curly Hair

Choosing the right short hairstyle for curly hair can be a daunting task. You want a hairstyle that is not only easy to maintain but also looks great. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short hairstyle for your curly hair:

Consider your face shape

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, a short hairstyle with volume on top can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a short hairstyle with soft curls can help soften your features.

Think about your hair texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in choosing the right short hairstyle. If you have fine hair, a short layered haircut can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a short bob can help tame your curls.

Consider your lifestyle

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you're someone who is always on the go, a low-maintenance hairstyle like a pixie cut or a bob can be a great option. If you have more time on your hands, you can opt for a more intricate hairstyle like a curly bob or a layered haircut.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair

Now that you have chosen the right short hairstyle for your curly hair, it's time to learn how to style it. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short curly hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

The first step to styling short curly hair is to wash it with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your curls hydrated and defined.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your curls and add moisture.

Step 3: Apply a styling product

Next, apply a styling product like a curl cream or a gel to help define your curls and add texture.

Step 4: Use a diffuser

When blow-drying your hair, use a diffuser attachment to help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 5: Finish with a hairspray

Finish off your hairstyle with a light hold hairspray to help keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Now that you know how to style your short curly hair, it's important to know how to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your short curly hair:

Get regular trims

To keep your short curly hair looking its best, it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your curls and cause them to lose their shape. Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

Sleep on a silk pillowcase

Silk pillowcases can help reduce frizz and prevent your curls from getting tangled while you sleep.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy to maintain short hairstyles for curly hair:

What are the best short hairstyles for curly hair?

Some of the best short hairstyles for curly hair include pixie cuts, curly bobs, and layered haircuts.

How do you style short curly hair?

To style short curly hair, you should wash it with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, apply a leave-in conditioner, apply a styling product, use a diffuser when blow-drying, and finish with a light hold hairspray.

How do you maintain short curly hair?

To maintain short curly hair, you should get regular trims, avoid heat styling, and sleep on a silk pillowcase.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfate can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause your curls to become dry and brittle. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.

Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry

Brushing your hair when it's dry can cause your curls to become frizzy and lose their shape. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.

Deep condition your hair regularly

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

Now that you know how to choose the right short hairstyle for curly hair, style it, and maintain it, you're ready to rock your new look. Remember to take care of your hair by using sulfate-free products and avoiding heat styling as much as possible. With these tips, you'll have healthy, beautiful curls in no time.
