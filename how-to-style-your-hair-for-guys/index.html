How To Style Your Hair For Guys A Complete Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Jul 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a guy looking to improve your hair game, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll show you how to style your hair for guys, covering everything from how to choose the right haircut to the best products to use for your hair type. Whether you're looking for a classic style or something more modern, we've got you covered.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to styling your hair is choosing the right haircut. This will depend on a variety of factors, including your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Consider your face shape: Certain haircuts look better on certain face shapes. For example, a short, textured cut works well for round faces, while longer styles can help balance out a long face.
  • Think about your hair type: Different hair types require different cuts. If you have curly hair, you might want to go for a cut that enhances your curls, while if you have fine hair, you might want to opt for a shorter style that adds volume.
  • Consider your personal style: Your haircut should reflect your personal style. If you're more classic, a short, neat cut might be for you, while if you're more edgy, you might want to try a longer style with shaved sides.

How to Style Your Hair

Once you have the right haircut, it's time to style your hair. Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the look you want:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. If you have longer hair, you might want to blow dry it first to remove excess moisture.
  2. Apply a styling product. The type of product you use will depend on your hair type and the style you're going for. For a classic look, try a pomade or wax, while for a more modern style, a styling cream or clay might work better.
  3. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, using your fingers or a comb.
  4. Style your hair as desired. You might want to use a comb or brush to create a more polished look, or use your fingers to create a more messy, textured style.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your hair:

  • Don't use too much product. This can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and style.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles. You might be surprised by what looks good on you!
  • Consider getting regular haircuts to maintain your style and keep your hair looking healthy.

Hair Care for Guys

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. Here are some tips for hair care for guys:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to support healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to style your hair for guys:

What is the best product to use for styling hair?

The best product to use for styling your hair will depend on your hair type and the style you're going for. Pomades and waxes work well for classic styles, while creams and clays are better for modern, textured looks.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and how much product you use. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.

What is the best haircut for guys with thinning hair?

The best haircut for guys with thinning hair is a short, textured cut. This can help create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair.

Conclusion

Styling your hair as a guy doesn't have to be complicated. By choosing the right haircut, using the right products, and taking care of your hair, you can achieve a look that's both stylish and easy to maintain. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to try something new!
