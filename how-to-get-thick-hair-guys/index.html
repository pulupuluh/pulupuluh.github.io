How To Get Thick Hair Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 08, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're a guy struggling with thin or fine hair, you may be wondering how to get thicker and fuller locks. Fortunately, there are several things you can do to achieve a thicker head of hair. In this article, we'll cover everything from hair care to lifestyle changes to help you get the thick hair you've always wanted.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • Lifestyle Changes
  • Supplements
  • Styling Tips
  • FAQ

Hair Care

One of the easiest things you can do to get thicker hair is to take care of it properly. This means using the right products, washing it regularly, and avoiding damaging it with heat or harsh chemicals.

Use the Right Products

Choosing the right shampoo and conditioner can make a big difference in the thickness and health of your hair. Look for products that are designed specifically for men, as they tend to be more effective at thickening hair. Some key ingredients to look for include biotin, keratin, and caffeine.

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly helps to remove excess oil and dirt, which can weigh down your hair and make it look thinner. However, be careful not to over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Avoid Heat and Harsh Chemicals

Heat styling tools like blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons can damage your hair and make it look thinner. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible, or use them on a low heat setting. Similarly, harsh chemicals like bleach and hair dyes can weaken your hair and cause breakage, so use them sparingly.

Lifestyle Changes

In addition to taking care of your hair, there are several lifestyle changes you can make to help promote hair growth and thickness.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Your diet plays a big role in the health of your hair. Make sure you're eating a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, healthy fats, and vitamins and minerals like biotin, zinc, and vitamin D. Some good foods for hair health include salmon, eggs, avocados, and spinach.

Exercise Regularly

Regular exercise helps to improve blood flow and circulation, which can promote hair growth. Aim to exercise for at least 30 minutes a day most days of the week.

Reduce Stress

Stress can cause hair loss and thinning, so it's important to find ways to manage your stress levels. Some effective stress-relieving techniques include meditation, yoga, and deep breathing exercises.

Supplements

In addition to making lifestyle changes, you may also want to consider taking supplements to promote hair growth and thickness.

Biotin

Biotin is a B vitamin that is essential for healthy hair growth. It helps to strengthen the hair follicles and improve the thickness and texture of your hair. You can find biotin supplements at most health food stores.

Vitamin D

Vitamin D plays a role in the production of new hair follicles, so it's important to make sure you're getting enough of it. You can get vitamin D from sunlight, food sources like fatty fish and fortified dairy products, or supplements.

Styling Tips

Finally, there are several styling tips you can use to make your hair look thicker and fuller.

Get a Shorter Haircut

Shorter haircuts can make your hair look thicker and more voluminous. Talk to your stylist about getting a cut that works with your hair type and face shape.

Add Texture

Adding texture to your hair can make it look thicker and more full. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to create volume and definition.

FAQ

Can hair thickening shampoos really make a difference?

Yes, hair thickening shampoos can be effective at improving the thickness and fullness of your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and caffeine.

Are there any foods that can help thicken hair?

Yes, foods like salmon, eggs, avocados, and spinach are all good for hair health and can help improve thickness and texture.

Is it possible to regrow lost hair?

It depends on the cause of the hair loss. In some cases, hair can regrow on its own, while in others, medical treatments like hair transplants may be necessary.


By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve thicker, fuller hair in no time. Remember to take care of your hair, make healthy lifestyle choices, and consider using supplements or styling products to enhance your locks.
