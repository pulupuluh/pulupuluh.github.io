How To Know Which Haircut Suits Your Face Male

Written by William Jul 20, 2023 · 9 min read

Choosing the right haircut can be a challenging task, especially if you are not familiar with the different face shapes and hairstyles that complement them. Whether you have a round, square, oval, or diamond-shaped face, there is a perfect haircut out there for you. In this article, we will guide you on how to know which haircut suits your face male.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Right Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right haircut is to determine your face shape. This can be done by measuring the width and length of your face and identifying its unique features. Here are the different face shapes and their characteristics:

Round Face

A round face has soft curves and no sharp angles. It is characterized by equal width and length, with the widest point being the cheeks. The chin is rounded and the forehead is usually wide.

Square Face

A square face has a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The width and length of the face are almost equal, with the jawline being the most prominent feature. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.

Oval Face

An oval face has a balanced and proportional shape, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. The jawline is rounded and not too prominent, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face.

Diamond Face

A diamond face is characterized by a narrow forehead and chin, and wide cheekbones. The face is longer than it is wide, and the jawline is pointed.

Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Right Haircut

Once you have determined your face shape, the next step is to choose the right haircut. Here are some guidelines to help you:

Round Face

If you have a round face, you should opt for haircuts that add volume and height to the crown of your head. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer. A short, layered cut with a side part or a high fade with a longer top are both great options. Avoid haircuts that add width to the sides of your face.

Square Face

If you have a square face, you should opt for haircuts that soften your features and add volume to the top of your head. A side part with a short taper or a messy, textured crop are both great options. Avoid haircuts that accentuate your jawline.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you are lucky, as almost any haircut will suit you. However, you should avoid haircuts that cover your forehead or add too much volume to the top of your head.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond face, you should opt for haircuts that add width to your forehead and chin, and soften your cheekbones. A messy fringe or a side-swept undercut are both great options. Avoid haircuts that accentuate your cheekbones.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have chosen the right haircut, it is important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips to help you:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use the right products for your hair type and style.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat or styling products, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

If you have a combination of face shapes, you should choose a haircut that complements the dominant shape. For example, if you have a square jawline and a round forehead, you should opt for a haircut that softens your jawline and adds volume to the top of your head.

Can I still wear long hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have a round face. However, you should opt for layered haircuts that add volume and movement to your hair, and avoid haircuts that make your face appear wider.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage the hair follicles.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the elements by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut for your face shape, you can confidently visit your barber or hairstylist and ask for the perfect look. Remember to maintain your haircut properly and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
