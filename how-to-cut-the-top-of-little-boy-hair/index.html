How To Cut The Top Of Little Boy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 29, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re looking for a way to save money on haircuts or simply want to learn how to cut your little boy’s hair at home, you’re in the right place. In this guide, we’ll show you exactly how to cut the top of little boy hair in a relaxed and easy-to-follow way.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Cut the Top of Little Boy Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Success
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

Cutting a little boy’s hair can be a daunting task, especially if you’ve never done it before. However, with the right tools and techniques, it’s actually quite simple. In this guide, we’ll focus specifically on how to cut the top of little boy hair, which is often the most challenging part of the haircut.

How to Cut the Top of Little Boy Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it’s important to have the right tools on hand. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clippers (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools ready, here’s how to cut the top of little boy hair:

  1. Start by combing your little boy’s hair straight up.
  2. Use your scissors to trim the hair that sticks out above the comb. Be sure to cut in a straight line.
  3. Continue working your way around the head, always combing the hair straight up and cutting in a straight line.
  4. If you want a shorter cut, you can use hair clippers to trim the hair on top. Simply attach the appropriate guard and run the clippers over the hair. Be sure to start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually.
  5. Once you’ve finished cutting the top of the hair, you can move on to the sides and back.

Tips for Success

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut the top of little boy hair:

  • Start with small cuts – you can always cut more later.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your little boy what he thinks. If he doesn’t like the way it looks, you can always make adjustments.
  • Take breaks as needed. Cutting hair can be tiring, so don’t be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.

FAQ

Here are a few commonly asked questions about cutting the top of little boy hair:

What if I make a mistake?

Don’t panic! If you make a mistake, you can always trim a little more off or try to blend it in with the rest of the hair. Remember, hair grows back.

How often should I cut my little boy’s hair?

This depends on how quickly his hair grows and how short you want it to be. As a general rule, every 4-6 weeks is a good timeframe.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you know how to cut the top of little boy hair, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash his hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep his hair soft and manageable.
  • Trim the ends every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on his hair, as this can damage the hair and scalp.

With these tips, you’ll be able to create a great haircut for your little boy at home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
