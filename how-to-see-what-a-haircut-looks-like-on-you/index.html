How To See What A Haircut Looks Like On You

Written by Robby Apr 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Getting a new haircut can be both exciting and nerve-wracking. Sometimes, we have an idea in our heads of what we want, but it's hard to imagine how it will look on us. Luckily, there are several ways to see what a haircut will look like on you before you commit to the chop.

Description

In this article, we will explore different methods for visualizing how a haircut will look on you. Whether you're considering a drastic change or just a little trim, these tips will help you make an informed decision.

How to See What a Haircut Looks Like on You

1. Try on Wigs

One of the easiest ways to see what a new haircut will look like on you is to try on wigs. You can find wigs in a variety of styles and lengths, so you can experiment with different looks until you find one that suits you. Wigs are also a great option if you're considering a drastic change, such as cutting off all your hair.

2. Use Virtual Hairstyle Apps

Another way to see what a new haircut will look like on you is to use virtual hairstyle apps. These apps allow you to upload a photo of yourself and then try on different hairstyles. Some apps even let you change the color of your hair, so you can experiment with different shades as well.

3. Get a Consultation

If you're considering a new haircut, it's always a good idea to get a consultation with a stylist. During the consultation, you can discuss your ideas and the stylist can offer suggestions based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. The stylist may also be able to show you photos of similar cuts they have done on other clients.

4. Look at Photos

If you have a specific haircut in mind, try looking at photos of people who have a similar cut. You can find inspiration on social media platforms like Instagram and Pinterest. This can give you an idea of how the cut will look on someone with a similar face shape and hair type to yours.

Step by Step

Step 1: Gather Inspiration

Before you book your appointment, gather inspiration for your new haircut. Look at photos of people who have a similar face shape and hair type to yours. This will give you an idea of what styles will suit you best.

Step 2: Try on Wigs

If you're still not sure what style you want, try on wigs in different lengths and styles. This will help you visualize how different cuts will look on you.

Step 3: Use Virtual Hairstyle Apps

Another option is to use virtual hairstyle apps. Upload a photo of yourself and experiment with different cuts and colors until you find one you like.

Step 4: Get a Consultation

Once you have a few ideas in mind, schedule a consultation with a stylist. They can offer suggestions based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Step 5: Make the Cut

After you've gathered inspiration and consulted with a stylist, it's time to make the cut. Remember, hair grows back, so don't be afraid to try something new!

Tips

  • Bring photos of your desired cut to your stylist
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a cut
  • Ask your stylist for maintenance tips
  • Experiment with different styles and colors

FAQ

What if I don't like my new haircut?

Hair grows back, so don't worry if you don't like your new cut. Talk to your stylist about what you don't like and they can suggest ways to make it work for you.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and the style you have. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care

After you get a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a good shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type, and avoid over-styling with heat tools.

Now that you know how to see what a new haircut will look like on you, it's time to book that appointment and try something new. Remember, hair is a fun way to express yourself, so don't be afraid to experiment!
