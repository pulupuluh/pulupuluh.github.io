How To Wear Your Hair With A Square Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Feb 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your square-shaped face? Look no further! In this guide, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to wear your hair with a square face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square-shaped hairline. When choosing a hairstyle, you want to soften your features and create a balance between your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape and highlights your best features.

How to

There are various hairstyles that can flatter a square face shape. The most popular ones include:

  • Long layers
  • Soft waves
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob with layers and texture
  • Pixie cut

Long layers can help soften your jawline and add volume to your hair. Soft waves can create a more feminine look and add dimension to your hair. Side-swept bangs can draw attention away from your jawline and highlight your eyes. A bob with layers and texture can add movement to your hair and balance your features. Lastly, a pixie cut can showcase your features and highlight your cheekbones.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of these hairstyles:

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  4. Take a section of hair and comb through it.
  5. Hold the section of hair with one hand and use the other hand to cut the ends of the hair at an angle.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 for each section of hair.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Soft Waves

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  3. Take a section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron.
  4. Hold the hair around the curling iron for a few seconds.
  5. Release the hair and repeat steps 3 and 4 for each section of hair.
  6. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create soft waves.
  7. Spray with hairspray to hold the style.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing a hairstyle for a square face shape:

  • Avoid straight and blunt hairstyles that can accentuate your jawline.
  • Consider adding volume to your hair to balance your features.
  • Experiment with different parts to find the most flattering look.
  • Use hair accessories such as headbands or scarves to add interest to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for square face shapes:

What is the most flattering hairstyle for a square face?

The most flattering hairstyle for a square face is one that softens your features and creates balance between your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Long layers, soft waves, side-swept bangs, bobs with layers and texture, and pixie cuts are all great options.

Can I wear my hair straight with a square face?

Straight hair can accentuate your jawline, so it's best to avoid straight and blunt hairstyles. If you want to wear your hair straight, consider adding layers or soft waves to create movement and balance.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your square face shape and feel confident and beautiful every day!
