How To Make Big Hair Clip A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you want to add some volume and glamour to your hair? Creating a big hair clip can do wonders for your look. It's a simple yet effective way to elevate your hairstyle and make it stand out. In this guide, we'll show you how to make a big hair clip in just a few easy steps. From choosing the right materials to styling your hair, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Materials
  • Step 1: Prepare the Materials
  • Step 2: Create the Base
  • Step 3: Add the Hair
  • Step 4: Attach the Clip
  • Tips for Making a Big Hair Clip
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Materials

Before we start, let's gather the materials you'll need:

  • A large hair clip
  • A piece of felt (or any sturdy fabric)
  • A hot glue gun (or fabric glue)
  • A pair of scissors
  • A comb
  • Hairspray
  • Hairpins (optional)

Step 1: Prepare the Materials

First, cut a piece of felt into your desired shape and size. This will serve as the base of your hair clip. Make sure it's big enough to support your hair and the clip.

Next, heat up your hot glue gun. If you're using fabric glue, follow the instructions on the packaging.

Step 2: Create the Base

Using your hot glue gun, attach the hair clip to the felt. Make sure it's centered and secure. You can reinforce it by adding more glue around the edges.

Once the clip is attached, you can start adding volume to the base. Use your comb to tease your hair and create some texture. Then, apply hairspray to hold it in place. Carefully place your hair on the felt, making sure it covers the base completely.

Step 3: Add the Hair

Now it's time to add more hair to your clip. Start by sectioning your hair and teasing it for volume. Then, spray some hairspray on each section and pin it to the base using hairpins. Continue adding more hair until you're satisfied with the volume and shape.

Make sure your hair is evenly distributed and covers the base. You can also use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.

Step 4: Attach the Clip

Once you're happy with your big hair clip, it's time to attach it to your hair. Simply open the clip and position it where you want it. Then, push down on the clip to secure it in place. You can also use additional hairpins for extra security.

Tips for Making a Big Hair Clip

  • Choose a hair clip that's big enough to support your hair.
  • Use a sturdy fabric for the base, like felt or leather.
  • Tease your hair for volume and texture.
  • Apply hairspray to hold your hair in place.
  • Use hairpins to secure your hair to the base.
  • Position the clip where it looks best, whether it's on the side or on top of your head.
  • Experiment with different shapes and sizes to find the perfect big hair clip for you.

FAQ

How long does a big hair clip last?

A properly made big hair clip can last for several hours, depending on the quality of the materials and the amount of hair you used. However, it's always a good idea to bring extra hairpins and hairspray for touch-ups.

Can I make a big hair clip with curly hair?

Yes, you can! In fact, curly hair can add more volume and texture to your big hair clip. Just make sure to tease your hair gently and use hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Hair Care Tips

After wearing a big hair clip, make sure to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage. You can also apply a hair mask or oil to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

Remember to remove the clip gently and avoid pulling your hair. With these tips and tricks, you can create a stunning big hair clip that will turn heads wherever you go!
