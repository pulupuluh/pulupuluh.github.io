Guy Haircut Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut for men? Look no further than the guy haircut short sides long top. This versatile and popular style is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance but fashionable look. In this article, we'll give you the ultimate guide to this haircut, including how to get it, tips, and hair care.

Description

The guy haircut short sides long top is a style where the hair on the sides and back is cut short with clippers, while the hair on the top is left longer. The length of the top can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on your preference. This haircut is also known as the "undercut" or "disconnected" haircut because of the contrast between the short sides and long top.

How to Get the Guy Haircut Short Sides Long Top

The guy haircut short sides long top is a popular style that can be achieved in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the Length of the Top

The first step in getting this haircut is to decide how long you want the hair on the top to be. You can choose a length that suits your face shape and personal style. You can also show your hairstylist a picture of the style you want to achieve.

Step 2: Trim the Sides and Back

Once you've decided on the length of the top, your hairstylist will use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back. The length of the hair can be adjusted according to your preference. The hair is usually tapered at the bottom for a clean and neat look.

Step 3: Cut and Style the Top

After the sides and back are trimmed, the hairstylist will cut and style the hair on the top. This can include layering, texturizing, or adding volume. You can also choose to comb the hair to one side or create a messy, tousled look.

Tips for Maintaining the Guy Haircut Short Sides Long Top

Here are some tips for keeping your haircut looking sharp and stylish:

Tip 1: Trim the Sides Regularly

The hair on the sides and back grows faster than the hair on top, so it's important to trim it every few weeks to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.

Tip 2: Use Styling Products

Using styling products such as pomade, wax, or gel can help you achieve your desired look and keep your hair in place throughout the day. Be sure to choose a product that works well with your hair type and style.

Tip 3: Wash and Condition Your Hair Regularly

Washing and conditioning your hair regularly can help keep it healthy and shiny. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using hot water, which can damage your hair.

FAQ

Q: Is the guy haircut short sides long top suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit different face shapes. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right length and style for your face shape.

Q: Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style the hair on top in many different ways, such as combing it to one side, slicking it back, or creating a messy look.

Hair Care

Good hair care is essential for keeping your guy haircut short sides long top looking great. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and use them regularly to keep your hair clean and healthy.

Tip 2: Avoid Using Hot Water

Hot water can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle. Use lukewarm or cool water instead when washing your hair.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair and make it dry and frizzy. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

In conclusion, the guy haircut short sides long top is a stylish and trendy haircut for men that is easy to maintain. With the right length and style, it can suit any face shape and personal style. Follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your haircut looking sharp and stylish!
