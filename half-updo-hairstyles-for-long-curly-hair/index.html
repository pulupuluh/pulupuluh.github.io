Half Updo Hairstyles For Long Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel May 19, 2023 · 7 min read

When it comes to styling long curly hair, half updos are a popular option. Not only do they look beautiful, but they also allow you to show off your curls while keeping your hair out of your face. In this article, we’ll take you through everything you need to know about half updo hairstyles for long curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Create Half Updo Hairstyles for Long Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Half Updo Hairstyles
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Curly Hair

Description

A half updo is a hairstyle where the top half of your hair is pulled back or pinned up while the rest of your hair is left down. This style is perfect for long curly hair because it allows you to showcase your curls while keeping them out of your face. There are many different half updo hairstyles to choose from, including braids, twists, and buns.

How to Create Half Updo Hairstyles for Long Curly Hair

Creating a half updo hairstyle for long curly hair is relatively easy. The first step is to decide on the style you want to create. Do you want a braid, twist, or bun? Once you have decided on the style, you can begin the styling process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create a half updo hairstyle for long curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb to create a part in your hair.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and pull it back.
  4. Secure the section of hair with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  5. If you want to add a braid or twist, create it with the section of hair you pulled back.
  6. For a bun, twist the section of hair and wrap it around itself.
  7. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  8. Let the rest of your hair hang down.
  9. Spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Half Updo Hairstyles

Here are some tips to help you style half updo hairstyles for long curly hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product to help define your curls.
  • Experiment with different braids, twists, and buns to find the style that works best for you.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to keep the style looking natural.
  • Add a hair accessory, such as a flower or clip, to dress up the style.
  • Don’t be afraid to play around with different partings and placements to find the most flattering style for your face shape.

FAQ

Can I create a half updo hairstyle with short curly hair?

Yes, you can create a half updo hairstyle with short curly hair. However, the style may look slightly different than it would with long hair.

How long does a half updo hairstyle for long curly hair last?

A half updo hairstyle for long curly hair can last all day if it is secured properly with bobby pins and hairspray.

Hair Care Tips for Long Curly Hair

To keep your long curly hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as they can damage your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you can create beautiful half updo hairstyles for your long curly hair. Whether you’re heading to a formal event or just want a stylish way to keep your curls out of your face, a half updo is the perfect choice.
