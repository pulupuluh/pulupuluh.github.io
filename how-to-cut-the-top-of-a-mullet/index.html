How To Cut The Top Of A Mullet A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to rock a mullet hairstyle, it's important to know how to cut the top of a mullet. The top of your hair is where all the action is, and it's the part that sets the tone for the rest of the hairstyle. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect mullet top.

Description

A mullet is a hairstyle that is shorter on the sides and longer in the back. The top of the hair is usually styled in a way that creates volume and texture. Cutting the top of a mullet is a delicate process that requires precision and attention to detail.

How to Cut the Top of a Mullet

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you get started, make sure you have all the necessary tools: scissors, a comb, a clipper, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Separate the top of your hair from the sides and back. Use a comb to create a straight line from your temples to the back of your head. Clip the sides and back out of the way.

Step 3: Wet Your Hair

Spray the top of your hair with water to make it easier to cut.

Step 4: Cut the Top of Your Hair

Starting at the front of your hairline, use your scissors to cut the top of your hair at a slight angle. Cut small sections at a time and use your comb to lift the hair and create texture. Continue cutting until you reach the back of your head.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Back

Use your clippers to blend the sides and back of your hair into the top. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until the sides and back are the desired length.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect mullet top:

  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you cut.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Don't cut too much off the top. You can always go back and cut more if needed.
  • Use a good pair of scissors that are sharp and easy to handle.

FAQ

How often should I cut the top of my mullet?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your mullet to be. As a general rule, you should trim the top every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and volume.

Can I cut the top of my mullet myself?

Yes, you can cut the top of your mullet yourself if you have the right tools and are comfortable with scissors and clippers. However, if you're not confident in your abilities, it's best to go to a professional stylist.

Hair Care

Once you've cut the top of your mullet, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your mullet looking fresh and stylish.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut the top of your mullet like a pro. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun with your new hairstyle!
