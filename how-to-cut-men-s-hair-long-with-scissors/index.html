How To Cut Men s Hair Long With Scissors

Written by Oliver May 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long hair has become a popular trend for men in recent years, and many are opting for a DIY haircut using only a pair of scissors. While it may seem daunting, cutting long hair with scissors can be done with the right techniques and tools. In this post, we will guide you through the process of how to cut men's hair long with scissors, step by step.

Description

Cutting long hair with scissors requires patience, precision, and a steady hand. It is important to have the right tools, including a pair of sharp scissors, comb, and clips to section off the hair. Before starting, it is also important to have a clear idea of the desired hairstyle and length.

How to

Follow these steps to cut men's hair long with scissors:

Step 1: Wash and Dry

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will remove any dirt or product buildup and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section Off the Hair

Using clips, section off the hair into manageable parts. Start by dividing the hair in the middle and clipping each side. Then, clip the back section and the top section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the hair at the back, starting from the nape of the neck. Using the comb, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, following the desired length. Continue cutting small sections of hair in this manner, moving upwards towards the crown of the head.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Once the back is complete, move on to the sides. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers, cutting in a straight line towards the desired length. It is important to cut both sides evenly.

Step 5: Cut the Top

The top section of the hair should be cut last. Take small sections of hair and cut in a straight line towards the desired length.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Once all sections are cut, use the scissors to blend the hair by cutting into the ends. This will create a more natural and even look.

Step 7: Style the Hair

After cutting, style the hair as desired using products like hair gel, wax, or pomade.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take small sections of hair to ensure precision.
  • Trim a little at a time to avoid cutting too much.
  • Keep the hair wet while cutting to ensure an even cut.

FAQ

How often should I cut my long hair?

It is recommended to trim long hair every 8-12 weeks to maintain healthy ends and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, cutting your own hair is possible with the right techniques and tools. However, it is recommended to seek professional help for more complex haircuts.

How do I maintain long hair?

Maintain long hair by using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting, it is important to take care of the hair to maintain its health and appearance. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools, and protect the hair from sun damage. Regular trims are also important to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair long with scissors, give it a try and create your desired hairstyle. Remember to take your time and use the right techniques and tools for the best results.
