Is Short Curly Hair In Style Tips Faqs And Hair Care

Written by William Jul 11, 2023 · 8 min read

Short curly hair has been in style for decades and it continues to be one of the most popular looks for women today. But is short curly hair still in style in 2023? The answer is a resounding yes! In this article, we’ll discuss why short curly hair is still in style, how to style it, and provide tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls.

Table of Contents

  • Why is Short Curly Hair in Style?
  • How to Style Short Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair
  • FAQs about Short Curly Hair
  • Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Why is Short Curly Hair in Style?

Short curly hair has been a popular look for generations because it’s versatile, easy to style, and flattering on many face shapes. In recent years, we’ve seen a resurgence in the popularity of short curly hair thanks to celebrities like Zendaya, Yara Shahidi, and Viola Davis who have embraced their natural curls.

Short curly hair is also a great option for those who want to embrace a low-maintenance lifestyle. With the right haircut and styling products, you can achieve a chic and effortless look that requires minimal upkeep.

How to Style Short Curly Hair

The key to styling short curly hair is to find the right haircut and products for your hair type. If you have thick, coarse curls, a layered bob or pixie cut can help to add texture and movement to your hair. If you have fine curls, a short curly shag or tapered cut can help to create volume and definition.

When it comes to styling products, it’s important to find a lightweight formula that won’t weigh down your curls. Look for products that contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil to help keep your curls shiny and hydrated.

To style your short curly hair, start by washing and conditioning your hair with a curl-friendly shampoo and conditioner. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to gently dry your curls. Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling wand or styling gel to define your curls and add volume.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Maintaining healthy, beautiful curls requires a little extra effort. Here are some tips to help you keep your short curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while they’re wet to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or applying a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking fresh and bouncy.

FAQs about Short Curly Hair

Q: Can I still straighten my short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your short curly hair if you want to change up your look. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your curls.

Q: What’s the best way to sleep with short curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz and maintain your curls’ shape, consider sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase. You can also pineapple your curls by gathering them at the top of your head and securing them with a scrunchie or satin scarf.

Q: How often should I wash my short curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash your hair once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair more frequently. Experiment with different washing schedules to find what works best for your hair.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

In addition to using the right styling products and techniques, it’s important to take care of your hair from the inside out. Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to strengthen your hair and promote healthy growth. Drinking plenty of water can also help to keep your curls hydrated and shiny.

Regular exercise and stress management techniques like meditation or yoga can also help to promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss. And don’t forget to get regular trims to keep your curls looking their best!

Short curly hair is still in style in 2023, and for good reason. Whether you have natural curls or you’ve opted for a permed or curled look, short curly hair is a versatile and low-maintenance option that can help you look and feel your best. With the right styling products, techniques, and hair care tips, you can maintain healthy, beautiful curls that turn heads wherever you go.
