Short Hair Styles For Very Thin Hair

Written by Daniel Jun 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have very thin hair, you might feel like you're limited when it comes to hairstyle options. But don't worry! There are plenty of short hair styles that can work wonders for those with fine hair. In this post, we'll explore some of the best short hair styles for very thin hair, as well as some tips for caring for and styling your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Very Thin Hair

  • Hair Care Tips for Very Thin Hair

How to Choose a Short Hair Style for Very Thin Hair

Before choosing a short hair style for very thin hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Some short hair styles may look great on one person but not on another. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Consider a bob or lob (long bob) as these styles can add the illusion of volume to your hair.
  • Opt for layers to add texture and volume.
  • A pixie cut can work if you have the right face shape and hair texture.
  • A side-swept bang can add the illusion of volume to your hair.

Examples of Short Hair Styles for Very Thin Hair

Here are some short hair styles that work well for those with thin hair:

  • The bob
  • The lob (long bob)
  • The pixie cut
  • The shag
  • The asymmetrical bob
  • The layered bob

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Very Thin Hair

Styling short hair for very thin hair can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can create a look that adds volume and texture to your hair.

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and condition your hair with products designed for thin hair. Towel dry your hair and then blow dry it using a round brush to add volume.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing product.

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair, concentrating on the roots. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Use a curling iron to add texture.

Use a curling iron to add texture to your hair. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. This will create loose waves and add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray.

Once you've styled your hair, finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Very Thin Hair

Maintaining short hair for very thin hair requires a bit of effort, but with the right products and techniques, you can keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous.

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner designed for thin hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and full.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair between washes.

FAQs About Short Hair Styles for Very Thin Hair

Q: Can I still get a pixie cut if I have very thin hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of pixie cut for your hair texture and face shape. Talk to your stylist about which style would work best for you.

Q: What type of bangs work best for very thin hair?

A: A side-swept bang can add the illusion of volume to your hair. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs as these can weigh down your hair.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and full.

Hair Care Tips for Very Thin Hair

In addition to choosing the right short hair style and styling your hair properly, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining healthy, voluminous locks.

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as this can damage your hair and make it appear thinner.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for thin hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair health.

By following these tips and choosing the right short hair style for your very thin hair, you can achieve a look that's both stylish and voluminous.
