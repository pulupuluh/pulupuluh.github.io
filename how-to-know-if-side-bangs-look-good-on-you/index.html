How To Know If Side Bangs Look Good On You

Written by Petter Feb 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Side bangs can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some dimension to your look. But how do you know if they will look good on you? In this blog post, we will discuss how to determine if side bangs are right for you, and provide some tips and hair care advice for maintaining your new style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know if Side Bangs Look Good on You
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Side Bangs
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Know if Side Bangs Look Good on You

The first step in determining whether or not side bangs are right for you is to consider your face shape. Side bangs can work well for those with oval or heart-shaped faces, as they can help to balance out the proportions of the face. However, if you have a round or square-shaped face, side bangs may not be the best choice, as they can accentuate the roundness or angularity of your features.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. Side bangs can work well for those with straight or wavy hair, but if you have curly hair, you may find that your bangs are difficult to style or manage.

Finally, it's important to consider your lifestyle and personal style. If you're someone who likes to keep things low-maintenance and natural, side bangs may not be the best choice, as they require regular trimming and styling to look their best. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with your look and don't mind putting in the time and effort to maintain your hairstyle, side bangs could be a great option.

Step by Step Guide

If you've decided that side bangs are right for you, here's a step-by-step guide for getting the look:

  1. Start by sectioning off the front portion of your hair that you want to use for your bangs.
  2. Decide how thick you want your bangs to be, and make a horizontal cut across the section of hair.
  3. Using sharp hair cutting scissors, angle the scissors downwards and cut the hair at a slight angle, starting from the middle of the section and working your way outwards.
  4. Repeat this process on the other side of your head, making sure that your bangs are even.
  5. Style your bangs using a round brush and a blow dryer, or use a flat iron to straighten them.

Tips for Maintaining Side Bangs

Here are some tips for keeping your side bangs looking their best:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to prevent them from getting too long and heavy.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh and clean between washes.
  • If you have curly hair, consider getting a keratin treatment to help smooth out your bangs and make them easier to style.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about side bangs:

Q: Will side bangs make my face look slimmer?

A: Side bangs can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face, particularly if you have a round or square-shaped face.

Q: Can I wear my side bangs in different styles?

A: Yes, side bangs can be styled in a variety of ways, including swept to the side, pinned back, or worn straight across the forehead.

Hair Care Advice for Side Bangs

Here are some general hair care tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful side bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye, as this can weaken the hair and make it more prone to breakage.

By following these tips and considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can determine whether or not side bangs are right for you. With a little bit of maintenance and care, you can enjoy your new hairstyle and feel confident and beautiful every day.
