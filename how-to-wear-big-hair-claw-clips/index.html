How To Wear Big Hair Claw Clips A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Big hair claw clips are back in style! These versatile hair accessories can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, from simple, everyday looks to elegant updos. But if you've never worn a hair claw clip before, you may be wondering how to use them. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to wear big hair claw clips, along with some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of these stylish accessories.

  • How to Wear Big Hair Claw Clips
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQ

How to Wear Big Hair Claw Clips

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let's cover the basics of how to wear big hair claw clips. First, you'll need to choose the right size clip for your hair. If you have thick hair, you'll want to opt for a larger clip, while those with thinner hair can use a smaller clip.

Next, you'll need to decide on the placement of the clip. Claw clips can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, including half-up/half-down styles, messy buns, and French twists. Depending on the style you're going for, you may want to place the clip at the crown of your head, at the back of your head, or on the side.

Once you've decided on the size and placement of your clip, you're ready to start styling!

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear big hair claw clips:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather the section of hair you want to clip and twist it into a loose bun or twist. You can also leave some hair loose for a half-up/half-down style.
  3. Hold the twist or bun in place with one hand.
  4. Using your other hand, open the claw clip and position it over the twist or bun.
  5. Slide the clip down over the twist or bun, making sure to capture all of the hair in the clip.
  6. Secure the clip by snapping it shut.
  7. Adjust the clip as needed to create your desired style.

And that's it! With a little practice, you'll be able to create a variety of hairstyles using big hair claw clips.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your big hair claw clips:

  • Experiment with different sizes and styles of clips to find the ones that work best for your hair and your desired style.
  • For a more secure hold, tease the section of hair before twisting it into a bun or twist.
  • If you have short hair, you can still use claw clips! Try using a smaller clip to create a half-up/half-down style.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize your clips with ribbons, flowers, or other embellishments.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about big hair claw clips:

What are big hair claw clips?

Big hair claw clips are hair accessories that are used to hold hair in place. They typically have a claw-like design that opens and closes to grip the hair.

How do I choose the right size clip for my hair?

If you have thick hair, you'll want to opt for a larger clip. Those with thinner hair can use a smaller clip.

What hairstyles can I create with big hair claw clips?

You can use big hair claw clips to create a variety of hairstyles, including half-up/half-down styles, messy buns, and French twists.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair when using big hair claw clips. Avoid using clips that are too tight, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair. And be sure to remove your clips before going to bed to prevent tangling and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create beautiful hairstyles using big hair claw clips. So go ahead and give them a try!
