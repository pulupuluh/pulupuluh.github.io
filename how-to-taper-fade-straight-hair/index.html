How To Taper Fade Straight Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for a change? A taper fade may be just what you need to spice up your look. A taper fade is a popular haircut that involves gradually decreasing the length of hair from the top of the head down to the neck. It creates a clean and modern look that is suitable for any occasion. In this article, we will discuss how to taper fade straight hair and provide some tips for maintaining your new haircut.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Taper Fade Straight Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Taper Fade
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care for Taper Fades

Description

A taper fade is a haircut that gradually fades from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back. It creates a clean, modern look that is suitable for any occasion. Taper fades can be customized to suit your personal style, with options ranging from a low or high fade to a skin fade. While taper fades can be done on any hair type, they are especially popular for straight hair.

How to Taper Fade Straight Hair

Taper fades can be done at home or by a professional stylist. However, if you are new to taper fades or have never cut your own hair before, it is best to seek the help of a professional. Here are some basic steps to follow when getting a taper fade:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can make it difficult to see the natural texture and shape of your hair.
  2. Choose the length of hair you want to keep on top. Use clippers or scissors to trim the hair on top of your head to the desired length.
  3. Choose the length of hair you want to fade to. Use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length. Be sure to blend the hair so that there is a smooth transition from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back.
  4. Blend the hair between the top and sides. Use clippers or scissors to blend the hair between the top and sides of your head. Be sure to use a comb to keep the hair even and to avoid any choppy lines.
  5. Trim the edges. Use scissors or clippers to trim any stray hairs around the ears and neckline.
  6. Style your hair. Use a comb or brush to style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Taper Fade

Here are some tips for maintaining your taper fade:

  • Get regular touch-ups. Taper fades require regular touch-ups to maintain their shape and style.
  • Use the right products. Use products that are designed for your hair type and style to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray or serum when using heat styling tools to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Wash your hair regularly. Regular washing helps to remove buildup and keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about taper fades:

What is a taper fade?

A taper fade is a haircut that gradually fades from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back.

Can taper fades be done on any hair type?

Yes, taper fades can be done on any hair type.

Should I get a low or high taper fade?

It depends on your personal style and preference. Low taper fades are more subtle, while high taper fades are more dramatic.

Hair Care for Taper Fades

Here are some hair care tips for taper fades:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your taper fade.

Now that you know how to taper fade straight hair, it's time to try it out for yourself. Whether you choose a low or high taper fade, this hairstyle is sure to give you a fresh and modern look that you'll love.
