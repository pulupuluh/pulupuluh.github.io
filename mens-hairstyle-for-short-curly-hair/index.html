Mens Hairstyle For Short Curly Hair A Guide To Great Hair

Written by Petter Jun 18, 2023 · 9 min read

Short curly hair can be a challenge to style for men, but with the right cut and products, it can look fantastic. In this guide, we’ll take a look at some of the best mens hairstyles for short curly hair, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair to keep it looking great.

  9. Conclusion

Description

Short curly hair is a popular style for men, but it can be difficult to manage without the right cut and products. The best hairstyles for short curly hair are those that work with your natural texture and shape, rather than trying to force it into a style that doesn’t suit you.

There are a variety of mens hairstyles for short curly hair, from the classic short back and sides to longer, shaggy styles. The key is to find a style that works with your face shape and hair type, and to keep it well-maintained to avoid frizz and tangles.

How to Style Short Curly Hair

The first step in styling short curly hair is to find the right cut. A good barber or stylist will be able to recommend a style that works with your natural texture and face shape. From there, you can experiment with different products and techniques to create the look you want.

Some popular styling techniques for short curly hair include using a diffuser to dry your hair, using a curling cream or gel to define your curls, and using a hair oil or serum to tame frizz and add shine.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair

  1. Start with freshly washed hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too vigorously.
  3. Apply a curling cream or gel to your hair, using your fingers to distribute it evenly.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to shape your curls and add volume.
  6. Finish with a hair oil or serum to add shine and tame frizz.

Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your short curly hair:

  • Don’t over-wash your hair. Curly hair tends to be dryer than other hair types, so washing it too often can strip away natural oils and cause frizz.
  • Invest in a good diffuser attachment for your hair dryer. This will help to distribute heat evenly and prevent damage to your curls.
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair, rather than a regular towel. This will help to reduce frizz and tangles.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens hairstyles for short curly hair:

What is the best haircut for short curly hair?

The best haircut for short curly hair depends on your face shape and hair texture. A good barber or stylist will be able to recommend a style that works for you.

How do I keep my short curly hair from getting frizzy?

To keep your short curly hair from getting frizzy, avoid washing it too often, use a good quality conditioner, and apply a hair oil or serum to tame frizz.

Can I use a hair straightener on short curly hair?

Yes, you can use a hair straightener on short curly hair, but be careful not to damage your hair with heat. Use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting to minimize damage.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Taking care of your short curly hair is important to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping away natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Best Mens Hairstyles for Short Curly Hair

Here are some of the best mens hairstyles for short curly hair:

  • The classic short back and sides, with longer curls on top.
  • A messy, textured style with a side part.
  • A shaggy, layered cut with defined curls.
  • A buzz cut with a fade on the sides.
  • A curly fringe with a taper fade.

Products for Styling Short Curly Hair

Here are some of the best products for styling short curly hair:

  • Curling cream or gel
  • Diffuser attachment for your hair dryer
  • Microfiber towel or t-shirt
  • Hair oil or serum
  • Heat protectant spray

Conclusion

Short curly hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right cut and products, it can look fantastic. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
