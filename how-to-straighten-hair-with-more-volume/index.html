How To Straighten Hair With More Volume

Written by Bardi Jun 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

In this blog post, we will be discussing how to straighten hair with more volume. If you have naturally straight hair, you might want to add some volume to your hair to make it look fuller and healthier. There are several ways to achieve this look, and we will be discussing them in detail below.

Description

Straight hair can often look flat and lifeless, especially if you have thin or fine hair. Adding volume to your hair can make it look fuller and more vibrant. Many people opt for hair extensions or volumizing products to add volume to their hair, but there are also several ways to achieve this look naturally.

How to Straighten Hair with More Volume

There are several ways to straighten hair with more volume. Below are some of the most effective methods:

1. Blow Drying

Blow drying your hair can add volume to your hair while also straightening it. To achieve this look, start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, use a round brush to blow dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will create a sleek and voluminous look.

2. Straightening Iron

A straightening iron can also be used to straighten hair with more volume. To achieve this look, start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, use a straightening iron to straighten your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will create a sleek and voluminous look.

3. Hair Rollers

Hair rollers can also be used to add volume to your hair. To achieve this look, start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, use hair rollers to roll your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Leave the rollers in for a few hours or overnight, then remove them to reveal voluminous and straight hair.

Step by Step Guide to Straighten Hair with More Volume

Here is a step by step guide to straighten hair with more volume:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume to your hair while also cleaning it.

Step 2: Apply Volumizing Product

Apply a volumizing product to your hair, such as a mousse or volumizing spray. This will help to add even more volume to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will create a sleek and voluminous look.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Use a straightening iron to straighten your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will create a sleek and voluminous look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a spritz of hairspray to hold your hair in place and add even more volume.

Tips for Straightening Hair with More Volume

Here are some tips for straightening hair with more volume:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help to add volume to your hair and make it look fuller.

2. Use a Round Brush

Using a round brush while blow drying your hair can help to add volume and create a sleek look.

3. Don't Overdo It

Don't overdo it with the heat tools, as this can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat tools every day.

4. Use Hair Rollers

Using hair rollers can help to add volume to your hair while also straightening it.

FAQs

1. Can I Straighten My Hair Without Heat?

Yes, you can straighten your hair without heat using hair rollers or a straightening brush. However, these methods may not give you the same sleek look as using a straightening iron.

2. How Often Should I Straighten My Hair?

You should avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can damage your hair. Try to limit your use of heat tools to once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look dull.

Conclusion

Straightening hair with more volume is a great way to add some life to your hair. Whether you use a blow dryer, straightening iron, or hair rollers, there are several ways to achieve this look. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid overusing heat tools to keep your hair healthy and looking great.
