How To Cut Long Boy Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 11, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to give your little boy a fresh new haircut, a long boy haircut may be just what you need. Long boy haircuts are trendy and stylish, and they're perfect for boys who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that looks great. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut long boy haircut step by step, and we'll also give you some tips on how to care for your child's hair after the cut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Long Boy Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Long Boy Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Long Boy Haircut

How to Cut Long Boy Haircut

Cutting a long boy haircut at home is easy and straightforward. Here's what you'll need:

  • A pair of sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Once you have everything you need, follow these simple steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by wetting your child's hair with the spray bottle. Make sure the hair is damp but not too wet.
  2. Comb the hair straight down and make a center part from the forehead to the nape of the neck.
  3. Starting at the back of the head, take small sections of hair and cut them straight across. Use the previous section as a guide for the next section. Repeat until you reach the top of the head.
  4. For the top of the head, comb the hair forward and cut it in a straight line. Cut the hair shorter towards the front of the head and longer towards the back.
  5. Blend the top and sides by combing the hair down and cutting it at a slight angle.
  6. Trim the hair around the ears and neck with scissors or clippers.
  7. Check for any uneven spots and trim as necessary.

And that's it! You've just cut a long boy haircut at home.

Tips for Cutting Long Boy Haircut

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting a long boy haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid snagging or pulling the hair.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Don't cut the hair too short, as it will look shorter when it dries.
  • Use a comb to help guide your scissors and keep the hair straight.
  • Have your child sit still and distract them with a toy or TV show if necessary.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long boy haircuts:

How often should I cut my child's hair?

It depends on how quickly their hair grows and how long you want it to be. Generally, a long boy haircut can last for 4-6 weeks before needing a trim.

Should I shampoo my child's hair before cutting it?

It's not necessary to shampoo the hair before cutting it, but you can if you prefer. Just make sure the hair is damp but not too wet when you start cutting.

Can I use clippers to cut a long boy haircut?

You can use clippers to trim the hair around the ears and neck, but scissors are best for cutting the rest of the hair.

Hair Care After Cutting Long Boy Haircut

After cutting a long boy haircut, it's important to take care of your child's hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and moisturized.
  • Brush or comb the hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.
  • Avoid using heat tools like blow dryers or straighteners, as they can damage the hair.

Cutting a long boy haircut at home is easy and affordable, and it's a great way to give your child a fresh new look. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to master this hairstyle in no time.
