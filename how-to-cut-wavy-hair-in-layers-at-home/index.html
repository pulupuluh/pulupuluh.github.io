How To Cut Wavy Hair In Layers At Home A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of paying a fortune to get your hair cut at the salon? Do you have wavy hair and want to try cutting it in layers at home? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting wavy hair in layers at home. From the tools you need to the step-by-step instructions, we have got you covered. Let's get started!

Tools You Will Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you will need a few tools. Here is what you will need:

  • Sharp scissors (preferably hair cutting scissors)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Spray bottle
  • Mirror

Make sure you have all these tools before you start cutting your hair. Using the right tools will make the process much easier and give you better results.

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to make sure it is clean and dry. Wash your hair with your favorite shampoo and conditioner and let it air dry or blow-dry it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Sectioning Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you need to section it. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then divide each side into two sections. Clip or tie up the top sections and leave the bottom sections down. This will make it easier to cut your hair in layers.

Cutting Your Hair

Take a small section of hair from the bottom and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. This will be your guide for the rest of your layers. Take another section of hair from the bottom and place it on top of the previous section. Cut it to the same length as the first section. Keep doing this until you have cut all the hair in the bottom section.

Once you have finished the bottom section, let down the next section and repeat the same process. Remember to use the previous layer as your guide and cut each section to the same length. Continue doing this until you have cut all your hair in layers.

Blending Your Layers

Once you have cut all your hair in layers, you need to blend them. Take a comb and run it through your hair from roots to ends. This will blend the layers and create a natural look.

Trimming Your Ends

After you have blended your layers, you need to trim your ends. Take small sections of hair and trim the ends to get rid of any split ends or damaged hair. Be careful not to cut too much, as this can ruin the shape of your layers.

Styling Your Layers

Now that you have cut your hair in layers, it's time to style it. You can use a curling iron or a straightener to create different looks. Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

Tips and Tricks

  • Start by cutting a little bit of hair at a time. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting hair in layers can be time-consuming, but the results are worth it.
  • Use hair cutting scissors, as they are sharper and will give you cleaner cuts.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it can look shorter when it dries.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair in layers if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can. Follow the same process as cutting wavy hair in layers, but be careful not to cut too much, as curly hair tends to bounce up.

Q: Will my layers look different on each side?

A: They might, but that's okay. Hair is not always symmetrical, and having slightly different layers on each side can create a more natural look.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Now that you have cut your hair in layers, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb instead.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Let your hair air dry whenever possible.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.

Cutting wavy hair in layers at home can be challenging, but with the right tools and instructions, anyone can do it. Follow these steps and enjoy your new, layered look!
