What To Wear Hiking In 75 Degree Weather

Written by Robby Jul 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Hiking is a great way to explore the great outdoors while also getting exercise. However, dressing appropriately for the weather is crucial to ensure a comfortable and safe hike. If you're planning to hit the trails in 75 degree weather, here's what you should wear to make the most out of your hike.

Description

In 75 degree weather, you want to dress in lightweight and breathable clothing that will keep you cool and comfortable throughout your hike. You also want to protect yourself from the sun's harmful rays, so it's important to wear clothing that covers your skin and a hat to shade your face.

How to

Here are the steps to follow when deciding what to wear for a hike in 75 degree weather:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clothing

When hiking in 75 degree weather, it's important to wear lightweight and breathable clothing that will keep you cool and comfortable. Choose synthetic or moisture-wicking fabrics that will wick sweat away from your body and dry quickly. Avoid cotton clothing, as it will retain moisture and make you feel hot and sticky.

Step 2: Protect Yourself from the Sun

When hiking in 75 degree weather, it's important to protect yourself from the sun's harmful rays. Wear a hat to shade your face and neck and sunglasses to protect your eyes. Use sunscreen with at least SPF 30 and reapply every two hours or after sweating or swimming.

Step 3: Wear the Right Footwear

Choose hiking shoes or boots that are comfortable and provide good traction on the trails. Make sure your footwear fits properly and is broken in before your hike to avoid blisters and discomfort.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you dress appropriately for a hike in 75 degree weather:

  • Avoid wearing dark colors, as they absorb heat and can make you feel hotter
  • Wear layers that can be easily removed or added as needed
  • Bring a light rain jacket or windbreaker in case of unexpected weather changes
  • Wear comfortable and moisture-wicking socks to avoid blisters

FAQ

Q: Can I wear shorts on a hike in 75 degree weather?

A: Yes, you can wear shorts on a hike in 75 degree weather, as long as they are made from lightweight and breathable materials. Make sure your shorts are long enough to protect your thighs from rubbing against each other or getting scratched by the trails.

Q: Should I wear a backpack on a hike in 75 degree weather?

A: Yes, you should wear a backpack on a hike in 75 degree weather to carry essentials such as water, snacks, sunscreen, and a first aid kit. Choose a backpack that fits properly and has adjustable straps for a comfortable fit.

Hair Care

When hiking in 75 degree weather, it's important to take care of your hair to avoid damage from the sun, wind, and sweat. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair on your hike:

  • Wear a hat to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays
  • Tie your hair in a bun or braid to keep it away from your face and neck
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to hydrate and protect your hair from the elements
  • Bring a comb or brush to detangle your hair after your hike

In conclusion, dressing appropriately for a hike in 75 degree weather is crucial to ensure a comfortable and safe experience. Choose lightweight and breathable clothing, protect yourself from the sun, wear the right footwear, and take care of your hair to make the most out of your hike.
