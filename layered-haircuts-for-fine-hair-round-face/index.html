Layered Haircuts For Fine Hair Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you someone with fine hair and a round face? Do you struggle to find a haircut that flatters your features while also giving your hair some volume and texture? Look no further than layered haircuts!

Description

Layered haircuts are a popular choice for those with fine hair, as they add texture and volume to otherwise flat locks. When combined with a round face shape, layered haircuts can create the illusion of a slimmer face and a more defined jawline.

How to

The first step in getting a layered haircut is to find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Once you've found your stylist, bring in some inspiration photos to give them an idea of what you're looking for.

During the haircut itself, your stylist will likely start by cutting your hair into a blunt shape. From there, they will create layers by cutting into the hair at different lengths. The goal is to create a graduated effect, with shorter layers on the top and longer layers towards the bottom.

Step by Step

Step 1: Find a skilled hairstylist

Look for a hairstylist who has experience working with fine hair and round face shapes. Check out reviews and ask for recommendations from friends to ensure you find someone who can help you achieve the look you want.

Step 2: Bring in inspiration photos

Bring in photos of layered haircuts that you like to give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for.

Step 3: Start with a blunt cut

Your stylist will likely begin by cutting your hair into a blunt shape. This sets the foundation for the layers.

Step 4: Create layers

Your stylist will then create layers by cutting into the hair at different lengths. They will likely start with shorter layers on the top and gradually add longer layers towards the bottom.

Tips

When it comes to layered haircuts for fine hair round face, there are a few things to keep in mind.

Tip 1: Avoid heavy layers

While layers are great for adding volume and texture, too many heavy layers can actually make fine hair look thinner. Stick to lighter, more subtle layers for the best results.

Tip 2: Consider bangs

If you have a round face, consider adding some bangs to your layered haircut. Bangs can help elongate the face and create a more flattering shape.

Tip 3: Use the right products

To keep your layered haircut looking its best, use the right products for your hair type. Opt for lightweight, volumizing products that won't weigh down your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a layered haircut work for all face shapes?

While layered haircuts can work for many face shapes, they may not be the best choice for those with very angular or square-shaped faces.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a layered haircut?

Absolutely! In fact, layered haircuts can make updos look even more voluminous and textured.

Q: How often should I get my layered haircut trimmed?

Most hairstylists recommend getting a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your layered haircut looking its best.

Hair Care

To keep your layered haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair.

Tip 1: Use a volumizing shampoo

Look for a shampoo that is specifically designed to add volume to fine hair.

Tip 2: Don't over-wash

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and leave it looking limp and lifeless. Stick to washing every other day or every two days.

Tip 3: Use a lightweight conditioner

Conditioner is important for keeping your hair hydrated and healthy, but avoid heavy, thick formulas that can weigh down your hair. Look for a lightweight conditioner instead.

In conclusion, a layered haircut is an excellent choice for those with fine hair and a round face. With the right stylist and a little bit of care, you can have a voluminous, textured haircut that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
