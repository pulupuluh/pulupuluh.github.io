How To Bring Your Human Hair Wig Back To Life

Written by William Aug 24, 2023 · 6 min read

Human hair wigs are a great investment, but they require proper care and maintenance to keep them looking their best. Over time, your wig may start to lose its luster and become tangled or frizzy. Fortunately, there are several ways to bring your human hair wig back to life. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for restoring your wig to its former glory.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Bring Your Human Hair Wig Back to Life
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Wig
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care Tips

Step by Step Guide

If you want to bring your human hair wig back to life, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Detangle

The first step is to detangle your wig. Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to gently work through any knots or tangles. Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots. Be careful not to pull or tug on the hair, as this can cause damage.

Step 2: Wash

Next, wash your wig using a gentle shampoo. Fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of shampoo. Gently swish the wig around in the water, being careful not to rub or scrub the hair. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water.

Step 3: Condition

After washing, apply a quality conditioner to the wig. Use your fingers to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout the hair. Let the conditioner sit for a few minutes, then rinse thoroughly with cool water.

Step 4: Dry

Blot the wig gently with a towel to remove excess water. Do not rub or wring the hair. Allow the wig to air dry on a wig stand or mannequin head. Do not use a hair dryer or other heat source to dry the wig, as this can damage the hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wig

Now that you’ve brought your human hair wig back to life, it’s important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to heat, such as from a hair dryer, straightening iron, or curling iron.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your wig, and be gentle when working through knots or tangles.
  • Wash your wig regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Protect your wig from the sun and other sources of UV radiation, which can cause damage.

FAQ

How often should I wash my wig?

You should wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed if it becomes dirty or oily.

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

No, you should use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for human hair wigs. Regular shampoo and conditioner can contain harsh chemicals that can damage the hair.

Can I style my wig with heat?

Avoid exposing your wig to heat, as this can damage the hair. If you must style your wig with heat, use a low-heat setting and a heat protectant spray.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep your natural hair and wig looking their best:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your hair, and be gentle when working through knots or tangles.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other sources of UV radiation, which can cause damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

By following these tips and tricks, you can bring your human hair wig back to life and keep it looking its best for years to come.
