How To Cut Your Hair Short Like A Boy A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a fresh, low-maintenance haircut, cutting your hair short like a boy may be the perfect solution. Not only is it trendy and stylish, but it can save you time and money on styling products and salon visits. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your hair short like a boy, as well as some tips on hair care and maintenance.

How to Cut Your Hair Short Like a Boy

Cutting your hair short like a boy is a great way to achieve a low-maintenance, edgy look. Here are the steps to follow:

1. Gather Your Supplies

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary supplies. You'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror
  • Water spray bottle

2. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and dry it completely before you start cutting. This will make it easier to see the shape of your head and the length of your hair.

3. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable pieces using hair clips. Start by dividing your hair into two sections at the back of your head, then divide each section into two more sections on either side of your head.

4. Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair with the clippers, using a guard to achieve your desired length. Work from the sides of your head to the back, using the comb to hold your hair in place and create a straight line. Then, use the scissors to trim any uneven ends or longer pieces.

5. Blend the Sides and Back

To blend the sides and back of your hair, use the clippers without a guard to create a fade. Start at the bottom and work your way up, gradually increasing the guard size until you reach the desired length.

6. Trim the Top

Trim the top of your hair with scissors, cutting it to the desired length. Use the comb to hold your hair in place and create a straight line.

7. Check for Evenness

Check your hair for evenness by running your fingers through it and looking in the mirror from different angles. Make any necessary adjustments with the clippers or scissors.

8. Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers to clean up the hairline at the back of your neck. Start at the center and work your way outwards, creating a straight line.

9. Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using a hair product such as gel, wax, or pomade.

10. Clean Up

Clean up any loose hair with a brush or vacuum. Dispose of hair clippings properly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a detailed step-by-step guide on how to cut your hair short like a boy:

  1. Gather your supplies.
  2. Wash and dry your hair.
  3. Section your hair.
  4. Start cutting with the clippers.
  5. Blend the sides and back.
  6. Trim the top with scissors.
  7. Check for evenness.
  8. Clean up the neckline with clippers.
  9. Style your hair.
  10. Clean up any loose hair.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Short Like a Boy

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short boy haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down to achieve the desired length.
  • Use a spray bottle to keep your hair damp and easier to cut.
  • Take your time and be patient. It's better to cut too little than too much.
  • Have someone else help you if possible.
  • Invest in high-quality hair cutting scissors and clippers for the best results.

FAQ

1. Can I cut my hair short like a boy if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Short haircuts can look great on all hair types. Just make sure to find a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair.

2. How often should I get my hair cut?

Short haircuts require more frequent maintenance than longer styles. You should plan on getting your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking sharp.

3. Can I dye my hair after cutting it short?

Yes, you can dye your hair after cutting it short. Just make sure to follow the instructions on the hair dye package and consider seeking the help of a professional stylist.

Hair Care and Maintenance

Here are some tips for caring for and maintaining your short boy haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a hair product such as gel, wax, or pomade to style your hair as desired.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.

Cutting your hair short like a boy can be a great way to change up your look and save time and money on styling products and salon visits. With the right tools and a little patience, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that looks great on you.
