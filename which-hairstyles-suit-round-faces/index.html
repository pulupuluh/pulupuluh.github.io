Which Hairstyles Suit Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Are you struggling to find a hairstyle that suits your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on which hairstyles suit round faces. We will also give you tips on how to style your hair and take care of it. Read on to find out more.

  • Step-by-Step Guide on Which Hairstyles Suit Round Faces
  • Tips on Styling Your Hair

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. The aim of choosing the right hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of a longer face shape. This can be achieved by adding volume to the top of the head and keeping the sides of the hair sleek.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for a round face is to determine your face shape. To do this, follow these simple steps:

  1. Take a photo of your face with your hair pulled back.
  2. Print out the photo or use an editing software to draw the outline of your face.
  3. Compare the outline of your face to the following shapes: oval, square, round, heart, and diamond.
  4. Determine which shape is the closest match to your face.

If you have determined that you have a round face, continue reading to find out which hairstyles suit your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide on Which Hairstyles Suit Round Faces

Now that you have determined your face shape, it's time to find the perfect hairstyle for you. Here are some hairstyles that suit round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate a round face by creating volume and movement. Ask your stylist to add layers that start at your chin and work their way down to the ends of your hair. This will create a flattering and feminine look.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a longer face shape by drawing attention away from the width of the face. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle so that they are longer on one side than the other. Then, sweep them to the side of your face.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and daring choice for a round face. However, if done correctly, it can be very flattering. Ask your stylist to add layers and texture to the top of your hair to create volume. Keep the sides and back of your hair short and sleek.

4. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that can suit a round face. Ask your stylist to cut your hair so that it is slightly longer in the front than in the back. This will create an angled effect that will elongate your face. You can also add layers to create volume and movement.

Tips on Styling Your Hair

Now that you have chosen a hairstyle that suits your round face, here are some tips on how to style your hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the top of your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create even more volume.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as a blunt fringe or a hairstyle that sits at chin length.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can! However, avoid pulling your hair back too tightly as this can accentuate the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for a loose and messy ponytail.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a center part if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can! However, a center part can accentuate the roundness of your face. If you want to wear a center part, consider adding volume to the top of your hair to create balance.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have chosen a hairstyle that suits your round face, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as a straightener or curling iron.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get your hair trimmed regularly to get rid of split ends.

Now that you have read our comprehensive guide on which hairstyles suit round faces, we hope that you feel confident in choosing the right hairstyle for you. Remember to take into account your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Happy styling!
