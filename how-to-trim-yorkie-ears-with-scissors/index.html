How To Trim Yorkie Ears With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline May 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Yorkshire Terriers, or Yorkies, are adorable little dogs with long, silky hair. One of the most important aspects of Yorkie grooming is trimming their ears. Trimming their ears can be a bit challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. In this article, we will guide you through a step-by-step process on how to trim Yorkie ears with scissors.

How to Trim Yorkie Ears with Scissors

Before you start trimming your Yorkie's ears, you need to prepare the following:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Cotton balls
  • Cleaner for ear wax

Step 1: Clean the Ears

The first step in trimming Yorkie ears is to clean them. Use a cotton ball and ear wax cleaner to remove any dirt, wax, or debris in the ears. Make sure to clean the ears thoroughly before trimming.

Step 2: Comb the Hair

Once the ears are clean, use a comb or brush to comb the hair on the ears. This will help remove any tangles or mats in the hair, making it easier to trim.

Step 3: Trim the Hair Around the Ears

Start by trimming the hair around the ears. Use the scissors to cut the hair around the edges of the ear, being careful not to cut too close to the skin. Trim the hair evenly around both ears.

Step 4: Trim the Hair Inside the Ears

Next, trim the hair inside the ears. Use the scissors to carefully trim the hair inside the ear, making sure not to cut the skin. Trim the hair evenly inside both ears.

Step 5: Check for Unevenness

Once you have trimmed the hair inside and around the ears, check for unevenness. Use the scissors to make any necessary adjustments to ensure that the hair is even on both ears.

Step 6: Clean the Ears Again

After trimming the ears, clean them again to remove any loose hair or debris. Use a cotton ball and ear wax cleaner to gently clean the ears.

Tips for Trimming Yorkie Ears with Scissors

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trimming your Yorkie's ears:

  • Make sure to use sharp scissors to avoid pulling hair or causing discomfort to your dog.
  • Be careful not to cut too close to the skin, as this can cause irritation or bleeding.
  • Take breaks if your dog becomes restless or uncomfortable during the trimming process.
  • Consider using treats or toys to distract your dog during the trimming process.

FAQs About Trimming Yorkie Ears with Scissors

Q: How often should I trim my Yorkie's ears?

A: It's recommended to trim your Yorkie's ears every 4-6 weeks to maintain their appearance and hygiene.

Q: Can I use electric clippers to trim my Yorkie's ears?

A: Electric clippers can be used to trim the hair around the ears, but they should not be used to trim the hair inside the ears. Scissors are better suited for trimming the hair inside the ears.

Hair Care for Yorkies

Aside from trimming their ears, Yorkies require regular grooming to keep their hair looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips for Yorkies:

  • Brush their hair daily to prevent tangles and mats.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for dogs.
  • Trim their hair regularly to maintain their appearance and hygiene.
  • Consider taking them to a professional groomer for a more thorough grooming.

Trimming Yorkie ears with scissors can be a bit challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Follow the steps and tips we've outlined in this article to ensure that your Yorkie's ears look beautiful and healthy.
