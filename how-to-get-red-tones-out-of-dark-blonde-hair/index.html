How To Get Red Tones Out Of Dark Blonde Hair

Written by Christine Aug 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have dark blonde hair, you may have noticed that it sometimes appears reddish. This can be frustrating, especially if you prefer cool or neutral tones. Fortunately, there are several ways to get rid of red tones in your hair. In this article, we'll explore some effective methods for achieving the perfect shade of dark blonde.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Red Tones Out of Dark Blonde Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Products to Try

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some steps you can take to remove red tones from your dark blonde hair:

1. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Start by using a clarifying shampoo to remove any build-up or residue from your hair. This will help to create a clean slate for your new color.

2. Apply a Toner

Apply a toner to your hair to neutralize any remaining red tones. Look for a toner that is specifically designed for dark blonde hair. Leave the toner on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse it out thoroughly.

3. Use a Purple Shampoo

Follow up with a purple shampoo to maintain your cool or neutral tone. Purple shampoo helps to counteract any brassy or yellow tones in your hair. Use it once a week or as needed to keep your color looking fresh.

4. Add Highlights

If you still have some stubborn red tones, consider adding some highlights to your hair. Highlights can help to break up the color and make it appear more dimensional. Be sure to work with a professional colorist to achieve the best results.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you maintain your color and keep unwanted red tones at bay:

1. Use Color-Safe Products

Use hair care products that are specifically designed for color-treated hair. These products are formulated to protect your color and keep it looking fresh.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. Heat can cause your color to fade more quickly.

3. Wear a Hat

Wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the sun. UV rays can cause your color to fade and turn brassy.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I remove red tones from my hair without using bleach?

Yes, you can remove red tones from your hair without using bleach. Follow the steps outlined in this article to create a cool or neutral tone in your dark blonde hair.

2. How often should I use purple shampoo?

You should use purple shampoo once a week or as needed to maintain your color. Be careful not to overuse it, as it can dry out your hair.

Hair Care Products to Try

If you're looking for hair care products to help you achieve the perfect shade of dark blonde, here are some options to consider:

1. Redken Color Extend Blondage Shampoo

This purple shampoo is specifically designed for blonde hair and helps to neutralize any brassy or yellow tones.

2. Joico Color Balance Purple Conditioner

This conditioner helps to maintain your cool or neutral tone and adds moisture to your hair.

3. L'Oreal Paris EverPure Blonde Shampoo

This sulfate-free shampoo is gentle on color-treated hair and helps to protect your color.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve the perfect shade of dark blonde without any unwanted red tones. Remember to work with a professional colorist if you're unsure about the process or need help achieving your desired results.
