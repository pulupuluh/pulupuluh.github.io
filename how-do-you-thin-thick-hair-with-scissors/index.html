How Do You Thin Thick Hair With Scissors

Written by Daniel May 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks great when styled properly, it can also be difficult to manage and can feel heavy. One solution to this problem is to thin out your hair using scissors. In this blog post, we’ll show you how to thin thick hair with scissors in a relaxed English language.

Description

Thinning your hair with scissors is a technique that involves cutting into the hair shaft to remove some of the hair’s bulk. This technique is commonly used in hair salons, but you can also do it at home with the right tools and techniques. Thinning your hair can help reduce its weight, making it more manageable and easier to style.

How to Thin Thick Hair with Scissors

Thinning thick hair with scissors requires a bit of skill and practice. Here are the steps you can follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you start thinning your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small, manageable sections using hair clips. This will help you work on one section at a time without getting overwhelmed.

Step 3: Use the Right Scissors

It’s important to use the right scissors when thinning your hair. Look for hair thinning scissors that have teeth or blades with gaps in them. These scissors are designed to remove some of the hair’s bulk without creating blunt lines.

Step 4: Start Thinning Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the thinning scissors to cut into the hair shaft, removing some of the hair’s bulk. Work your way down the section of hair, cutting into the hair at different angles to create a natural-looking effect.

Step 5: Repeat on All Sections

Repeat this process on all sections of your hair, making sure to work evenly and gradually. Take breaks if you need to, and check your progress regularly in a mirror.

Tips for Thinning Thick Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you thin your thick hair with scissors:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections as you gain confidence.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or overwhelmed.
  • Always use sharp, high-quality scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don’t over-thin your hair – a little goes a long way.
  • Take your time and work carefully to avoid cutting too much hair.

FAQ

Can I Thin My Hair with Regular Scissors?

While you can technically use regular scissors to thin your hair, it’s not recommended. Regular scissors are not designed for this purpose and can create blunt lines or uneven cuts.

How Often Should I Thin My Hair?

How often you should thin your hair depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it’s recommended to thin your hair every 3-6 months to keep it manageable and healthy.

Will Thinning My Hair Make It Look Thinner?

Thinning your hair will not make it look thinner overall. Instead, it will reduce the bulk and weight of your hair, making it easier to style and manage.

Hair Care

Thinning your hair with scissors can be a great way to manage thick hair and create a more manageable style. However, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.
