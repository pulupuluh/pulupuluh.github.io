Description

The long slicked back look is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It is a popular choice for men who want a polished and refined look. Achieving this look requires the right products and techniques. The right product will help you achieve a sleek and polished finish, while the wrong one can leave you with a greasy or sticky mess. In this article, we'll be discussing the best men's hair product for long slicked back look that will give you the desired look you want.

How to Achieve Long Slicked Back Look

The first step to achieving the long slicked back look is to have long hair. The hair at the top should be long enough to be slicked back, while the sides and back should be shorter. Once you have the right length, you'll need the right product to achieve the desired look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to achieve the long slicked back look using the best men's hair product:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup or dirt.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Take a small amount of the best men's hair product and rub it between your palms.
  4. Starting from the top of your head, comb your hair back using your fingers to apply the product evenly.
  5. Use a comb to create a clean and sleek look by combing your hair backwards.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Slicked Back Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long slicked back hair:

  • Regularly trim the sides and back to keep them neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb when brushing your hair to prevent breakage and damage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best men's hair product for long slicked back look:

  • What is the best men's hair product for long slicked back look? The best men's hair product for long slicked back look is a pomade. Pomades provide a strong hold and a slick finish that lasts all day.
  • How do I apply pomade to achieve the long slicked back look? Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms. Apply it to your hair starting from the top of your head and working your way down to the back. Use a comb to create a clean and sleek look.
  • How often should I wash my hair if I have long slicked back hair? It's recommended to wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily. However, if your hair gets dirty and greasy faster, you can wash it every day.

Hair Care Products for Long Slicked Back Hair

Here are some hair care products that are perfect for maintaining long slicked back hair:

  • Shampoo and Conditioner: Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Try the Tea Tree Special Shampoo and Conditioner by Paul Mitchell.
  • Pomade: The best men's hair product for long slicked back look is a pomade. Try the American Crew Pomade for a strong hold and a slick finish.
  • Hairspray: Use a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place. Try the TRESemme Tres Two Extra Hold Hairspray.
  • Deep Conditioning Treatment: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Try the Moroccanoil Intense Hydrating Mask.

Now that you know the best men's hair product for long slicked back look and how to achieve it, go ahead and try it out for yourself. With the right product and technique, you'll have a sleek and polished look that's sure to turn heads!
