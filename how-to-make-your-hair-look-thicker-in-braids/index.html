How To Make Your Hair Look Thicker In Braids

Written by William Feb 26, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have thin or fine hair, you may find it challenging to create braids that look thick and full. However, with a few simple techniques, you can achieve a voluminous braid that will make your hair look thicker and more beautiful. In this post, we’ll share some tips on how to make your hair look thicker in braids.

Description

Braids are a great way to style your hair, but if you have thin hair, they can be a bit of a challenge. Creating a braid that looks thick and full can seem impossible, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a beautiful, voluminous braid that will make your hair look thicker and more beautiful.

How to

If you want to make your hair look thicker in braids, here are some tips to follow:

1. Prep Your Hair

Before you start braiding your hair, you need to prepare it. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume to your hair and make it easier to braid. Once your hair is clean, dry it with a towel, and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.

2. Use Hair Extensions

If you want to create a braid that looks thicker and fuller, consider using hair extensions. There are many different types of extensions available, including clip-ins, tape-ins, and sew-ins. Clip-in extensions are the easiest to use and can be added to your hair in just a few minutes.

3. Tease Your Hair

When you’re creating a braid, teasing your hair can help to add volume and make it look thicker. To tease your hair, take a small section of hair and backcomb it with a fine-tooth comb. Repeat this process on all the sections of your hair that you’ll be braiding.

4. Create a Dutch Braid

A Dutch braid is a great way to make your hair look thicker. Instead of braiding your hair over the top, you’ll be braiding it underneath. This creates a raised braid that looks thicker and more voluminous.

5. Use a Hair Donut

If you’re creating a bun or updo with your braids, consider using a hair donut. A hair donut is a circular piece of foam that you can use to create a fuller, more voluminous bun. Simply wrap your hair around the donut and secure it with bobby pins.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair look thicker in braids:

Step 1:

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

Step 2:

Dry your hair with a towel and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.

Step 3:

Tease your hair to add volume and make it look thicker.

Step 4:

Create a Dutch braid to make your hair look thicker and more voluminous.

Step 5:

Use hair extensions to add thickness and volume to your braids.

Step 6:

Finish off your braids with a hair donut to create a fuller, more voluminous bun.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair look thicker in braids:

- Use a thickening spray or powder to add volume to your hair

- Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair

- Experiment with different braid styles to find the one that works best for your hair type

- Take care of your hair by using a deep conditioning treatment once a week

FAQ

Q: Can I use hair extensions with a Dutch braid?

A: Yes, you can use hair extensions with a Dutch braid to add thickness and volume to your hair.

Q: How do I create a hair donut?

A: You can purchase a hair donut at most beauty supply stores or online. They come in a variety of sizes and colors to match your hair.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair if you want it to look thick and healthy. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

- Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools

- Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair

- Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth

- Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage

By following these tips, you can create beautiful, voluminous braids that will make your hair look thicker and more beautiful.
