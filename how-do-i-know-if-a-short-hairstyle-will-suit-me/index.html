How Do I Know If A Short Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Written by Luffy Apr 19, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're considering cutting your hair short, you might be wondering whether the style will suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Short haircuts can be both chic and practical, but they're not for everyone. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks for figuring out whether a short hairstyle is right for you.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out whether a short hairstyle will suit you is to determine your face shape. Face shapes can be categorized as round, oval, heart-shaped, square, or diamond. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a haircut that will complement your features.

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Use a tape measure or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Based on these measurements, you can determine your face shape.

For example, if your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width and your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have an oval face shape. Oval faces can pull off a variety of short hairstyles, from pixie cuts to bobs.

Considering Your Hair Texture

In addition to your face shape, your hair texture is also an important factor to consider when choosing a short hairstyle. If you have fine, straight hair, a short bob or pixie cut can add volume and texture. If you have thick, curly hair, a short haircut can help to control frizz and make styling easier.

However, if you have very curly hair, you might want to avoid a super-short haircut, as it can be difficult to manage. Instead, opt for a shoulder-length bob or a curly shag.

Assessing Your Personal Style

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to consider your personal style. Do you prefer classic, elegant looks, or are you more of a trendsetter? Do you like to experiment with bold colors and edgy cuts, or do you prefer a more natural look?

If you're not sure what your personal style is, take a look at your wardrobe and makeup collection. Do you tend to go for classic pieces and neutral colors, or do you like to mix and match patterns and textures?

Your personal style should be reflected in your haircut, so choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Trying On Short Hairstyles

Before you commit to a short haircut, it's a good idea to try on different styles to see what looks best on you. This can be done by using a virtual hairstyle app or by trying on wigs or hairpieces in a salon.

When trying on different styles, pay attention to how they frame your face and how they make you feel. Do you feel confident and stylish, or do you feel self-conscious and uncomfortable?

Remember, a short haircut can be a big change, so it's important to take your time and choose a style that you'll be happy with.

Tips for Pulling Off a Short Haircut

If you've decided to go for a short haircut, here are some tips for pulling it off:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture
  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and styled
  • Consider adding layers or bangs to create volume and texture
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening
  • Accessorize with earrings or scarves to add interest to your look

FAQs About Short Hairstyles

What short hairstyle is best for a round face?

A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can help to elongate a round face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones.

Can I wear a short hairstyle if I have thick hair?

A short haircut can actually be a great option for thick hair, as it can help to control volume and make styling easier. Consider a layered bob or a shag haircut to add texture and movement.

Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

Not necessarily! A well-styled short haircut can actually make you look younger and more vibrant. Just be sure to choose a style that complements your features and personal style.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Once you've chosen a short hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Invest in a quality shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with hot tools
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut and prevent split ends
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, or scarves to add interest to your look

By following these tips and choosing a short hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can rock a chic and practical haircut that makes you feel confident and stylish.
