Men s Haircut 4 On Top A Guide For A Stylish Look

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then a men's haircut 4 on top might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is versatile and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice among men of all ages. In this guide, we'll provide you with all the information you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

How to get a Men's Haircut 4 on Top

A men's haircut 4 on top is a style that features a number 4 clipper guard on top of the head. This haircut is perfect for those who want a stylish and low-maintenance look. To get this haircut, you can either visit a professional barber or do it yourself at home.

If you choose to visit a barber, be sure to bring a picture of the style you want to achieve. This will help your barber understand exactly what you're looking for and ensure that you leave the salon with the perfect haircut.

On the other hand, if you prefer to do it yourself, you will need a set of clippers, a comb, and a mirror. It's important to start with a longer guard and work your way down to the number 4 guard. This will give you more control and prevent you from cutting too much hair at once.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a men's haircut 4 on top:

Step 1: Start with a longer guard

Start by using a longer guard to trim the hair on top of your head. This will help you achieve an even cut and prevent you from cutting too much hair at once.

Step 2: Trim the sides and back

Once you've trimmed the hair on top of your head, use a shorter guard to trim the sides and back. Be sure to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top to create a smooth transition.

Step 3: Use a number 4 guard on top

Finally, use a number 4 guard to trim the hair on top of your head. Start at the front and work your way back, trimming the hair evenly as you go.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your men's haircut 4 on top:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax for a sleek and stylish look.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I style my men's haircut 4 on top in different ways?

Yes, you can style your men's haircut 4 on top in a variety of ways. You can slick it back for a sleek and sophisticated look, or you can tousle it for a more relaxed and casual look. Experiment with different styling products to find the perfect look for you.

Will a men's haircut 4 on top suit my face shape?

A men's haircut 4 on top can suit a variety of face shapes, including round, square, and oval. However, it's important to consult with your barber or hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your men's haircut 4 on top looking its best:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

A men's haircut 4 on top is a stylish and versatile choice for men who want a low-maintenance look. Whether you visit a barber or do it yourself at home, this haircut is easy to achieve and maintain. Follow our tips and advice to keep your hair looking its best, and experiment with different styling products to find the perfect look for you.
