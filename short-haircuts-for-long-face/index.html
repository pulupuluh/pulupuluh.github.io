Short Haircuts For Long Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank May 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of having long hair that seems to drag your face down? Do you want to try something new and fresh? Short haircuts for long face might just be the answer you're looking for! In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about short haircuts for long face, including how to choose the right style, how to maintain it, and more. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Choose the Right Style

Choosing the right short haircut for your long face can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the right style:

Consider Your Face Shape

The first thing you should consider when choosing a short haircut is your face shape. If you have a long face, you'll want to choose a style that adds width and volume to your face. Avoid styles that are too straight or flat, as they can make your face appear even longer.

Think About your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in choosing the right style. If you have fine hair, you'll want to choose a style that adds volume and texture. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you'll want to choose a style that thins out your hair and makes it more manageable.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in choosing the right short haircut. If you're someone who's always on the go and doesn't have time to style your hair, you'll want to choose a low-maintenance style. On the other hand, if you have more time to style your hair, you can choose a more intricate style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you've chosen the right short haircut for your long face, it's time to get it done. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you get your haircut, make sure to wash your hair. This will ensure that your stylist has a clean canvas to work with.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using clips or rubber bands, section your hair into manageable sections so that your stylist can work on one section at a time.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Stylist

Communicate with your stylist about the style you want and make sure they understand what you're looking for. You can also show them pictures for reference.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Your stylist will now cut your hair according to the style you've chosen. Sit back, relax, and let them work their magic.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, your stylist will style it according to your preference. You can choose to add some product or leave it natural.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Now that you have your perfect short haircut for your long face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for short haircuts. These products will help you maintain your style and keep your hair looking healthy.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims will keep your haircut looking fresh and prevent split ends.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and make it harder to maintain your style.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are some good short haircuts for long face?

Some good short haircuts for long face include pixie cuts, bobs, and shags.

Will a short haircut make my face look even longer?

No, the right short haircut can actually make your face look shorter by adding width and volume to your face.

Do I need to use specific hair care products for my short haircut?

Yes, it's important to use hair care products that are specifically designed for short haircuts to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Here are some additional hair care tips for short haircuts:

Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it harder to maintain your style.

Invest in a Good Hairbrush

Invest in a good quality hairbrush that won't damage your hair and will help you style it properly.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using products that contain UV protection.

In conclusion, short haircuts for long face can be a great way to switch up your look and add some volume to your hair. By choosing the right style and maintaining it properly, you can achieve a fresh and stylish look that's perfect for you. Follow the tips and advice in this article to get started!
