How To Style Super Short Pixie Cut A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Apr 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, a super short pixie cut may be just the thing for you. This daring haircut can be both liberating and challenging, especially when it comes to styling it. In this article, we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to style super short pixie cut, as well as some tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Instructions

How to Style Super Short Pixie Cut

Styling a super short pixie cut is all about creating texture and volume. Here are some steps to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or use a blow dryer. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start styling.

2. Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your fingertips and rub it together. Then, work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots and the crown of your head. This will help create volume and texture.

3. Use a comb or brush

Use a comb or brush to create the shape you want. For a messy and textured look, use your fingers to style your hair instead.

4. Finish with hairspray

Once you have achieved the desired look, finish off with some hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here is a more detailed guide on how to style super short pixie cut:

1. Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer on low heat until it is completely dry.

2. Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your fingertips and rub it together. We recommend using a matte paste or a texturizing spray to create volume and texture.

3. Work the product through your hair

Starting at the roots, work the product through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly. Use your fingers to create texture and volume, pulling your hair in different directions.

4. Use a comb or brush

Once you have applied the product, use a comb or brush to create the shape you want. You can create a side part, or sweep your hair back for a more edgy look.

5. Finish with hairspray

Once you have achieved the desired look, finish off with some hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Super Short Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to help you style your super short pixie cut:

1. Use the right products

When it comes to styling a super short pixie cut, less is more. Use a lightweight styling product that won't weigh down your hair, such as a texturizing spray or a matte paste.

2. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to try different styles and play around with your hair. You can create a messy and textured look, or slick your hair back for a more polished look.

3. Use your fingers

Your fingers can be your best styling tool when it comes to a super short pixie cut. Use your fingers to create texture and volume, pulling your hair in different directions.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my super short pixie cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less frequently. As a general rule, we recommend washing your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener on my super short pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener on your super short pixie cut to create a sleek and polished look. Just make sure to use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.

Hair Care for Super Short Pixie Cut

Here are some hair care tips to keep your super short pixie cut looking its best:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is gentle and won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

3. Get regular trims

Super short pixie cuts require regular trims to maintain their shape and style. We recommend getting a trim every 4-6 weeks.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your super short pixie cut with ease and confidence. Whether you prefer a messy and textured look or a sleek and polished style, there are endless ways to make this daring haircut your own.
