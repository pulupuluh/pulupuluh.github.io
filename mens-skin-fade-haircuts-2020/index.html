Mens Skin Fade Haircuts 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to the ultimate guide on mens skin fade haircuts 2020. This article will cover everything you need to know about the trendiest haircut of the year. From the basics of what a skin fade is, to how to maintain it, we've got you covered. So, let's dive in!

Description

A skin fade is a type of haircut where the hair gradually fades from longer to shorter, down to the skin. It's also known as a bald fade, zero fade, or buzz cut fade. The hair is usually kept longer on top and gradually fades to shorter hair on the sides and back of the head. It's a popular haircut for men because of its versatility and low maintenance.

How to Get the Perfect Skin Fade

If you're looking to get a skin fade, the first step is to find a barber who specializes in this type of cut. It's important to communicate with your barber about your preferences and the style you're looking to achieve. Here are the steps to getting the perfect skin fade:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you go to the barber, make sure to wash your hair thoroughly. This will make it easier for the barber to cut your hair evenly.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. This will determine how short the fade will be.

Step 3: Start with a High Guard

The barber will start with a high guard and gradually work their way down to a lower guard. This will create a gradual fade.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

Once the barber has created the fade, they will blend the sides and top of your hair. This will make the haircut look seamless.

Step 5: Finish with Detailing

The barber will finish the haircut with detailing, such as trimming the edges and cleaning up any stray hairs.

Tips for Maintaining Your Skin Fade

Now that you have your skin fade, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh. Here are some tips:

  • Visit the barber every 2-3 weeks for touch-ups
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair
  • Use a trimmer to maintain the length of your fade between visits to the barber

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a skin fade and a taper fade?

A skin fade is a type of fade that gradually fades down to the skin, while a taper fade gradually fades down to a shorter length, but doesn't necessarily go all the way down to the skin.

Can I get a skin fade with curly hair?

Yes, you can get a skin fade with curly hair. However, it's important to find a barber who knows how to work with curly hair.

What face shapes work best with a skin fade?

A skin fade can work with any face shape. However, it's important to communicate with your barber about the length and style that will work best for your features.

Hair Care for Skin Fade Haircuts

Now that you have your skin fade, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair
  • Use a trimmer to maintain the length of your fade between visits to the barber
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair shiny and healthy

And there you have it, the ultimate guide on mens skin fade haircuts 2020. Follow these tips and you'll be sure to have a stylish and low maintenance haircut that looks great on you.
