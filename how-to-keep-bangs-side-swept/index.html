How To Keep Your Bangs Side Swept

Written by Luffy Apr 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have bangs, you know how frustrating it can be when they won't stay in place. Keeping your bangs side swept can be a challenge, especially if you have hair that is prone to frizz or flyaways. In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to keep your bangs looking great all day long.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Keep Your Bangs Side Swept
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Side Swept Bangs
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Bangs

How to Keep Your Bangs Side Swept

The first step in keeping your bangs side swept is to make sure they are cut correctly. Your stylist should cut your bangs so they are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help them naturally fall to the side.

Next, you'll need to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go. Use a round brush to smooth out any kinks or bumps. If you have curly hair, you may want to straighten your bangs with a flat iron before blow drying.

Once your bangs are dry, use a small amount of hairspray to hold them in place. Be sure to spray the hairspray onto your brush or comb, not directly onto your hair. This will help prevent buildup and keep your bangs looking natural.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to keeping your bangs side swept:

  1. Make sure your bangs are cut correctly.
  2. Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go.
  3. Straighten your bangs with a flat iron if necessary.
  4. Use a small amount of hairspray to hold your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Side Swept Bangs

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your side swept bangs:

  • Use a high-quality hairspray that won't leave your hair feeling sticky or crunchy.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, as this can cause them to become oily and lose their shape.
  • Wear a headband or hair clip on days when your bangs just won't cooperate.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to absorb oil between washes.
  • Try using a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your bangs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about keeping your bangs side swept:

How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Can I use gel instead of hairspray?

Gel can be too heavy for fine hair and can weigh down your bangs. Stick with a lightweight hairspray instead.

What should I do if my bangs won't stay in place?

If your bangs won't stay in place, try using a stronger hold hairspray or wearing a headband to keep them back.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind when maintaining your bangs:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent your bangs from becoming oily.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your bangs.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or straightening your bangs.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to keep your bangs side swept all day long. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type.
