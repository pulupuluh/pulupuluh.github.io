Curly Hair Makes You Look Younger A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Curly hair has always been admired for its unique texture and versatility. But did you know that curly hair can also make you look younger? In this article, we will explore how curly hair can give you a youthful appearance and share some tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful curls.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curls
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Products for Curly Hair

Description

Curly hair has a bouncy and voluminous texture that can make you look more youthful. The natural curls in your hair add volume and dimension to your face, making it appear fuller and more vibrant. This is especially true for women who are over 40, as their hair tends to thin out and lose its natural luster. Curly hair can also help soften harsh features, like a prominent jawline or cheekbones.

How to Get Curly Hair

If you do not have naturally curly hair, there are several ways to achieve the look. One of the easiest ways is to use a curling iron or wand to create loose, bouncy curls. You can also use hot rollers, flexi rods, or perm rods to achieve more defined curls. Another option is to get a perm, a chemical treatment that creates permanent curls. However, it is important to note that perms can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly.

Using a Curling Iron or Wand

To use a curling iron or wand, you will need to section your hair and wrap each section around the barrel of the iron. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are curled.

Using Hot Rollers, Flexi Rods, or Perm Rods

To use hot rollers, flexi rods, or perm rods, you will need to section your hair and wrap each section around the roller or rod. Secure with a clip or pin and leave in for several hours or overnight.

Getting a Perm

If you decide to get a perm, it is important to choose a reputable salon and stylist. The process involves applying a chemical solution to your hair, which breaks down the bonds that give your hair its shape. The hair is then wrapped around rods and a neutralizer is applied to reform the bonds into a new shape. The result is permanent curls, which can last for several months.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, wet hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl defining cream to your hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section into a coil.
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.
  6. Once your hair is dry, gently shake your curls to release them.
  7. Apply a styling gel or mousse to hold your curls in place.
  8. Scrunch your hair to enhance the curls and create volume.
  9. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curls

Maintaining healthy curls requires a bit of effort, but it is worth it for the beautiful, youthful look it can give you. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your curls.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like bleach or hair dyes.

FAQ

Does curly hair really make you look younger?

Yes, curly hair can make you look younger by adding volume and dimension to your face. It can also soften harsh features and give your hair a more youthful, vibrant look.

Can I get curly hair if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can achieve curly hair through the use of styling tools, like curling irons or hot rollers, or through a chemical treatment like a perm.

How do I maintain curly hair?

To maintain curly hair, it is important to use gentle, sulfate-free products, avoid heat styling tools, and protect your hair from the sun and wind. It is also important to deep condition your hair regularly and avoid touching it too much.

Hair Care Products for Curly Hair

There are many hair care products on the market that are specifically designed for curly hair. Here are some of our top picks:

  • DevaCurl No-Poo Original Cleanser

Curly hair can be a beautiful and youthful look for women of all ages. By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve healthy, bouncy curls that will make you look and feel younger. So embrace your natural texture or try out some new techniques to get the curly hair of your dreams!
