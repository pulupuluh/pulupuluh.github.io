Best Short Hairstyles For 60 Year Old Woman A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jul 10, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best short hairstyles for 60 year old woman. As we age, our hairstyles can often become outdated and may not suit our changing features. Short hairstyles can be a great option for women over 60 as they can be low maintenance, flattering and modern. In this guide, we will provide you with a variety of short hairstyles that can suit your face shape, hair texture and personal style.

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for You

Before choosing a short hairstyle, it is important to consider your face shape, hair texture and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Face Shape

The shape of your face can greatly influence the type of hairstyle that will suit you. Generally, women over 60 have a round, square, heart or oval face shape. Here are some hairstyles that can suit each face shape:

  • Round face shape: Short layered hairstyles, pixie cut or a bob with side-swept bangs
  • Square face shape: Short hairstyles with soft layers or a bob with side bangs
  • Heart face shape: Pixie cut, short layered hairstyles or a bob with side-swept bangs
  • Oval face shape: Almost any short hairstyle can suit an oval face shape

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also impact the type of hairstyle that will suit you. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a hairstyle that thins out your hair and adds movement. Here are some hairstyles that can suit different hair textures:

  • Fine hair: Pixie cut, short bob, or a layered haircut with side-swept bangs
  • Thick hair: Short layered hairstyles, textured bob or a pixie cut
  • Straight hair: Bob with side-swept bangs or a pixie cut
  • Curly hair: Short layered hairstyles or a pixie cut

Personal Style

Lastly, it is important to consider your personal style when choosing a short hairstyle. Do you prefer a classic or modern look? Do you like bold or subtle hairstyles? Here are some hairstyles that can suit different personal styles:

  • Classic style: Bob with side-swept bangs or a layered haircut with soft waves
  • Modern style: Pixie cut or a short bob with choppy layers
  • Bold style: Textured bob or a short layered haircut with a bold hair color
  • Subtle style: Pixie cut or a short bob with subtle highlights

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Now that you have chosen a short hairstyle, it is important to know how to style it. Here is a step by step guide to styling short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry your hair, but do not rub it vigorously as this can damage your hair
  3. Apply a styling product such as a mousse or a styling cream to your hair
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture
  5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hairstyles can be low maintenance, but they still require some upkeep to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as this can damage it
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking clean and fresh
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray
  • Try different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type

FAQs

What are the best short hairstyles for fine hair?

Pixie cut, short bob and a layered haircut with side-swept bangs can be great options for fine hair as they can add volume and texture.

What are the best short hairstyles for thick hair?

Short layered hairstyles, textured bob and a pixie cut can be great options for thick hair as they can thin out your hair and add movement.

Can I still wear bangs with a short hairstyle?

Yes, you can still wear bangs with a short hairstyle. Side-swept bangs or short blunt bangs can be a great addition to a short haircut.

How often should I get my short hair trimmed?

It is recommended to get your short hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires some care to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for taking care of short hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as this can damage it
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using too many styling products as this can weigh down your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to choose the best short hairstyle for you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. With the right hairstyle and proper maintenance, you can look and feel great at any age!
