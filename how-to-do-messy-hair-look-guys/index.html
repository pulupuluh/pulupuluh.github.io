How To Do Messy Hair Look Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 09, 2023 · 7 min read

In recent years, the messy hair look has become a popular trend among men. This hairstyle is effortless, casual, and versatile, making it perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will show you how to do messy hair look guys in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. We will also provide you with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect messy hair look. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Messy Hair Look Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Messy Hair Look Guys
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

How to Do Messy Hair Look Guys

The messy hair look is all about creating an effortless and natural-looking hairstyle. The key to achieving this look is to avoid using too much product, as it can make your hair look greasy and weighed down. Here are some steps to follow to get the perfect messy hair look:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. You can use a matte clay, wax or pomade. Rub the product between your palms and apply it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.

Step 4: Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured look. Don't worry about making it too neat or perfect – the key is to keep it relaxed and effortless.

Step 5: Finish off the look with a hairspray or sea salt spray. This will help to hold the style in place and give your hair a natural-looking texture.

Tips for Messy Hair Look Guys

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hair look:

  • Avoid using too much product, as it can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Try different styling products to find one that works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling it.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and looks until you find one that suits you.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve the messy hair look with short hair?

A: Yes, the messy hair look can be achieved with short hair. However, you may need to use more product to create texture and volume.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain the messy hair look?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every other day to keep it clean and healthy. However, if you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these simple steps and tips, you can easily achieve the perfect messy hair look guys. Remember, the key is to keep it relaxed, effortless, and natural-looking. So, go ahead and experiment with different hairstyles and looks until you find one that suits you the best.
