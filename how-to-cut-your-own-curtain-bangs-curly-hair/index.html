How To Cut Your Own Curtain Bangs For Curly Hair

Written by Luffy May 10, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re someone who loves to experiment with their hair, then you’ve probably heard of curtain bangs. They are the latest trend in hairstyling and are a great way to add some dimension to your curly hair. Curtain bangs are face-framing layers that fall on either side of your face in a curtain-like fashion. The best part? You can easily cut them at home! In this blog post, we’ll take you through a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own curtain bangs for curly hair.

Description

Curtain bangs are a great way to add some volume and texture to your curly hair. They are face-framing layers that fall on either side of your face, creating a curtain-like effect. The best part? You can easily cut them at home with just a few simple steps.

How to Cut Your Own Curtain Bangs for Curly Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of good quality hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to cut your own curtain bangs for curly hair:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and clip it away.
  2. Take the remaining hair and divide it into two sections. Make sure they are equal in size.
  3. Take the section on your left side and twist it away from your face. This will help you see the length of your hair and make it easier to cut.
  4. Using the scissors, cut the hair at an angle. Start from the middle of the section and work your way down, creating a diagonal line.
  5. Repeat the same process on the right side of your hair.
  6. Once you’ve cut both sides of your hair, take the top section and bring it forward. This will be the section for your curtain bangs.
  7. Using your comb, comb the hair straight down. This will help you see how long your bangs are.
  8. Using the scissors, cut your bangs at an angle. Start from the middle and work your way down, creating a diagonal line.
  9. Make sure to cut your bangs a little longer than you want them to be, as curly hair tends to shrink when it dries.
  10. Once you’ve cut your bangs, style them as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own curtain bangs:

  • Make sure you have good lighting when cutting your hair.
  • Start by cutting a little bit of hair and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can’t put the hair back once it’s cut.
  • Don’t cut your hair when it’s wet, as it tends to shrink when it dries.
  • Use hair cutting scissors instead of regular scissors, as they are sharper and will give you a cleaner cut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Can I cut my own curtain bangs?

Yes, you can easily cut your own curtain bangs at home. Just make sure you have the necessary tools and follow the steps carefully.

What face shape suits curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs look great on all face shapes, but they are especially flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep your curly hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized and defined.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz.

Now that you know how to cut your own curtain bangs for curly hair, go ahead and give it a try! With a little practice and patience, you’ll have the perfect curtain bangs in no time.
