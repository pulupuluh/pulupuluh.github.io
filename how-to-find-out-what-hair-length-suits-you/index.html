How To Find Out What Hair Length Suits You

Written by Daniel May 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of always having the same hairstyle? Do you want to try something new but don't know what hair length suits you? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll give you tips on how to find out what hair length suits you.

Table of Contents

  1. Measuring Your Face Shape
  2. Consider Your Hair Type
  3. Try on Wigs
  4. Ask Your Stylist
  5. Hair Care Tips
  6. FAQ

Measuring Your Face Shape

One of the first things you should do when trying to find out what hair length suits you is to measure your face shape. There are different face shapes such as oval, round, heart-shaped, square, and diamond. Each face shape has a hair length that suits it best.

To measure your face shape, you can:

  • Take a picture of your face and trace the outline with a pen or marker.
  • Measure your face from your hairline to your chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone.
  • Use an online face shape calculator.

Once you determine your face shape, you can look for hairstyles that suit it best.

Consider Your Hair Type

Another thing you should consider when finding the right hair length for you is your hair type. Hair types can be curly, straight, wavy, thin, thick, or coarse. Different hair types have different hair lengths that suit them best.

For example, if you have thick hair, a shorter hairstyle may be more manageable for you. On the other hand, if you have thin hair, a longer hairstyle may give the illusion of more volume.

Try on Wigs

If you're not sure what hair length suits you, try on wigs! Wigs are a great way to experiment with different hairstyles without committing to one. You can buy wigs or borrow them from a friend.

When trying on wigs, make sure they are of good quality and fit well. You can also ask a stylist to help you choose wigs that suit your face shape and hair type.

Ask Your Stylist

Your stylist is an expert when it comes to hair. They can help you find the right hair length that suits you based on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. They can also give you tips on how to maintain your new hairstyle.

When asking your stylist for advice, make sure to bring pictures of hairstyles that you like. This will give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for.

Hair Care Tips

Once you find the right hair length that suits you, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much hair products as they can weigh your hair down.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth.

FAQ

What hair length suits a round face?

A shoulder-length or longer hairstyle suits a round face.

What hair length suits an oval face?

An oval face can suit any hair length but a medium-length hairstyle is most flattering.

What hair length suits a heart-shaped face?

A chin-length or longer hairstyle with layers suits a heart-shaped face.

What hair length suits a square face?

A shoulder-length or longer hairstyle with layers suits a square face.

Now that you know how to find out what hair length suits you, it's time to experiment with different hairstyles! Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
