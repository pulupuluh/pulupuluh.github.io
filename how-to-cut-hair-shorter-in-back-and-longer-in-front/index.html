How To Cut Hair Shorter In Back And Longer In Front

Written by Frank Aug 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting hair shorter in the back and longer in the front is a popular hairstyle that has been around for a while, and it's not going away anytime soon. It is perfect for those who want to add some layers to their hair without going too short. This style is also known as the angled bob or the graduated bob. In this article, we will discuss how to cut hair shorter in the back and longer in the front, step by step.

Description

The hairstyle is characterized by a shorter length in the back and longer length in the front. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit any face shape and hair texture. It's also easy to maintain, making it perfect for those who have busy schedules.

How to Cut Hair Shorter in Back and Longer in Front

Step 1: Preparation

The first step is to prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Make sure to remove any tangles and knots from your hair. Dry your hair with a towel until it is slightly damp.

Step 2: Sectioning

Section your hair into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use hair clips to secure each section of your hair. Start with the back section.

Step 3: Cutting the Back

Start by cutting the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair in a straight line, making sure to keep it as short as you want it. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you finish the back section.

Step 4: Cutting the Sides

Move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair in a straight line, making sure to keep it longer than the back section. Repeat this process until you finish both side sections.

Step 5: Cutting the Top

Finally, move on to the top section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair in a straight line, making sure to keep it longer than the side sections. Repeat this process until you finish the top section.

Tips

Before cutting your hair, make sure to have the right tools. Use sharp scissors and a comb to ensure that your cuts are clean and precise. It's also important to cut your hair when it's dry, so you can see the exact length and shape of your hair.

FAQ

Q: What face shape is best for this hairstyle?

A: This hairstyle is versatile and can suit any face shape. It's important to customize the length and shape of the hair to suit your face shape.

Q: Can I cut my hair shorter in the front and longer in the back?

A: Yes, you can customize this hairstyle to suit your preference. You can cut your hair shorter in the front and longer in the back, or vice versa.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length of your hairstyle.

In conclusion, cutting hair shorter in the back and longer in the front is a popular hairstyle that is easy to maintain and customize. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve this look at home. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
