Best Layered Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Luffy Sep 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read
If you have thick wavy hair, you know that it can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, your hair has volume and texture that many people envy. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. That's where layered haircuts come in. Layering your hair can help to reduce bulk, add movement, and enhance your natural waves. In this article, we'll explore the best layered haircuts for thick wavy hair, and provide tips for caring for your locks.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Best Layered Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Layered Haircut
  • FAQs

Description

Layered haircuts are a great option for people with thick wavy hair because they help to add movement and texture to your hair. There are many different types of layered haircuts, including long layers, choppy layers, and feathered layers. The key is to find a style that works with your natural waves and enhances them.

How to Get the Best Layered Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair

Getting the best layered haircut for thick wavy hair starts with finding a stylist who understands your hair type and can recommend a style that will work for you. Before you go to the salon, do some research and bring pictures of styles that you like. This will help your stylist to get a sense of what you're looking for.

Types of Layered Haircuts

  • Long Layers: Long layers are a great option for people with thick wavy hair because they help to remove bulk and add movement.
  • Choppy Layers: Choppy layers are a more edgy option that can help to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Feathered Layers: Feathered layers are a classic style that work well with thick wavy hair. They help to add movement and texture to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. 2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray. 3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to add volume. 4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to create loose waves. 5. Divide your hair into sections and use a razor to create your desired layers. 6. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Layered Haircut

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet. 2. Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair. 3. Schedule regular trims to maintain your layers and prevent split ends. 4. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. 5. Embrace your natural texture and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

FAQs

Q: Will a layered haircut work for my thick wavy hair?
A: Yes, a layered haircut can work for thick wavy hair. The key is to find a style that works with your natural waves and enhances them. Q: How often should I get a trim?
A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your layers and prevent split ends. Q: Can I still straighten my hair with a layered haircut?
A: Yes, you can still straighten your hair with a layered haircut. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

In addition to getting a layered haircut, there are a few other things you can do to care for your thick wavy hair: 1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. 2. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. 3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves. 4. Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. 5. Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap. In conclusion, getting a layered haircut can be a great way to enhance your natural waves and add movement to your thick wavy hair. By finding a style that works for you and following these tips for hair care, you can keep your locks looking healthy and beautiful.
