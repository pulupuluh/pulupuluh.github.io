Do Curtain Bangs Look Good On Oblong Faces

Written by Daniel May 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you an individual with an oblong face shape wondering if curtain bangs are a good hairstyle choice for you? Look no further because we have all the information you need to make an informed decision. In this article, we will discuss whether or not curtain bangs look good on oblong faces, how to style them, and tips for maintaining this hairstyle. So, let's dive in!

Description

Oblong faces are characterized by a longer face shape with a narrow width. This face shape usually has a high forehead and a prominent chin. Haircuts and hairstyles for oblong faces should aim to make the face appear wider and shorter. Curtain bangs are a type of bangs that are parted down the middle and frame the face on either side. They are a popular hairstyle trend that can add softness to any face shape.

How To

If you have an oblong face and want to try curtain bangs, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure your bangs are cut to the right length. The bangs should be long enough to reach the cheekbones and blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair. Second, consider the thickness of your hair. If you have thin hair, curtain bangs may not be the best option as they can appear limp and lifeless. Third, make sure to style your curtain bangs correctly. Use a round brush to blow-dry them while aiming the dryer down to achieve a curved shape.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair

2. Section off the hair you want to turn into bangs

3. Comb the hair forward and decide the length you want

4. Cut the hair using scissors

5. Style the bangs using a round brush and blow dryer

Tips

1. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools

2. Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their length and shape

3. Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes

4. Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

FAQ

Q: Are curtain bangs suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can be tailored to suit any face shape.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: It is recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it is essential to take care of your hair. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Additionally, avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dyes, as they can damage your hair and make it harder to style.

Conclusion

In conclusion, curtain bangs can look great on oblong faces. By following our tips and steps, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering hairstyle that suits your face shape. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. So, go ahead and give curtain bangs a try!
