Cutting your own hair can seem intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. However, with the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of cutting your hair by yourself using the Wikihow method.

How to Cut Your Hair By Yourself Wikihow

The Wikihow method is a popular way of cutting your own hair that involves using a comb and a pair of scissors to create even layers. This method works best for people with straight or slightly wavy hair, and is not recommended for those with very curly hair or hair that requires a more complex cut.

What You'll Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather some essential tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • Clips to section off your hair
  • A mirror
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your hair using the Wikihow method:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural texture of your hair and make it easier to cut.
  2. Section off your hair. Use clips to divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Leave the bottom section loose.
  3. Start with the front sections. Take a section of hair from the front and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut the ends. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Move to the back sections. Take a section of hair from the back and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut the ends. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Blend the layers. Take the loose section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut the ends. This will blend the layers together.
  6. Check your work. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for a Successful DIY Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with small cuts. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair can stretch, making it harder to cut evenly.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create uneven cuts.
  • Take breaks. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so take breaks as needed.

FAQ about Cutting Your Own Hair

Can I use this method on curly hair?

This method works best on straight or slightly wavy hair. If you have very curly hair, you may want to consider a different method or consult a professional.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, you should aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

What do I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always try to fix it or wait for it to grow out. If you're really unhappy with the results, you can always visit a professional stylist to get it fixed.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Treat your hair to a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep it soft and shiny.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience, and with the Wikihow method, it's easier than ever to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and follow the steps carefully for the best results.
