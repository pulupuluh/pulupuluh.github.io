How To Tell What Hairstyle Suits You

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of choosing a hairstyle, it's important to understand that there are a few factors that can affect how a hairstyle looks on you. These include your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Understanding these factors will make it easier for you to choose a hairstyle that suits you.

There are a few ways to tell what hairstyle suits you:

  • Look at your face shape
  • Consider your hair type
  • Think about your lifestyle
  • Consult with a hairstylist

Let's take a closer look at these methods.

1. Look at your face shape

Your face shape plays a big role in determining what hairstyle will suit you. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has its own set of characteristics, and certain hairstyles will flatter them better than others.

Oval face: An oval face is considered the ideal face shape. You can pretty much pull off any hairstyle with an oval face shape.

Round face: Round faces have soft, curved features. To add definition, opt for a hairstyle that adds height and length, such as a side-parted pixie cut or a long bob.

Square face: Square faces have a strong jawline and forehead. Soften these features with a hairstyle that adds volume around the crown, such as a layered bob or a wavy lob.

Heart face: Heart-shaped faces have a wide forehead and narrow jawline. A side-swept fringe or a chin-length bob can balance out your features.

Diamond face: Diamond-shaped faces have wide cheekbones and a narrow forehead and jawline. Soften your features with a hairstyle that adds volume around the chin, such as a layered bob or a side-swept pixie cut.

Oblong face: Oblong faces are longer than they are wide. To add width, opt for a hairstyle that adds volume around the sides, such as a curly bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers.

2. Consider your hair type

Your hair type can also affect what hairstyle will suit you. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can make your hair look limp. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut to add movement and texture.

3. Think about your lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in what hairstyle will suit you. If you're constantly on the go, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you have a more relaxed schedule, you may be able to opt for a more high-maintenance hairstyle.

4. Consult with a hairstylist

If you're still unsure what hairstyle will suit you, it's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist. They can assess your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle and recommend a hairstyle that will flatter you.

Step-By-Step Guide

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, let's go through a step-by-step guide.

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Take a look in the mirror and determine your face shape. Use the guidelines we listed above to determine which face shape you have.

Step 2: Consider your hair type

Determine your hair type - is it fine, thick, curly, straight, etc.? This will help you choose a hairstyle that will flatter your hair type.

Step 3: Think about your lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle - are you always on the go or do you have a more relaxed schedule? This will help you choose a low-maintenance or high-maintenance hairstyle.

Step 4: Gather inspiration

Look for inspiration for hairstyles that flatter your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. You can use Pinterest, Instagram, or even celebrity hairstyles as inspiration.

Step 5: Consult with a hairstylist

If you're still unsure what hairstyle will suit you, book an appointment with a hairstylist. They can assess your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle and recommend a hairstyle that will flatter you.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle
  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your best features
  • Don't be afraid to try something new
  • Take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner that's suited for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
