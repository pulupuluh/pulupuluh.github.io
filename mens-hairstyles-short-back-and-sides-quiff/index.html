Mens Hairstyles Short Back And Sides Quiff A Relaxed Guide For 2023

Description

Mens hairstyles short back and sides quiff have been popular for decades, and continue to be a stylish option for men in 2023. The short back and sides quiff is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways, from sleek and sophisticated to messy and casual. This classic hairstyle is characterized by short, tapered sides and back, with longer hair on top that is styled into a voluminous quiff or pompadour.

How to Achieve the Short Back and Sides Quiff

The short back and sides quiff is a relatively simple hairstyle to achieve, but it does require some skill and patience. To achieve the look, you will need to have at least 2-3 inches of hair on top, with shorter hair on the sides and back. You can achieve this by visiting a barber or stylist, or by using clippers at home.

Tools and Products:

  • Clippers or scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Pomade or wax
  • Hairspray

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the short back and sides quiff:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Use clippers or scissors to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to your desired length. The hair should gradually get shorter as it approaches the neck.
  3. Use a comb or brush to lift the hair on top of your head, and trim it to your desired length. This hair should be longer than the hair on the sides and back.
  4. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your hair, and use your fingers to work it through your hair. This will help to create volume and texture.
  5. Use a comb or brush to style your hair into a quiff or pompadour. Lift the hair at the front of your head and push it back towards the crown of your head.
  6. Finish off the look with a spritz of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Quiff

To keep your short back and sides quiff looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Use a high-quality pomade or wax to style your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and variations of the quiff to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a comb or brush to maintain the shape of your quiff throughout the day.
  • Consider using a hair dryer to add more volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve the short back and sides quiff with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve the short back and sides quiff with curly hair. However, you may need to use more product and spend more time styling your hair to get the desired look.

Q: How do I know if the short back and sides quiff is right for me?

A: The short back and sides quiff is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by most men. However, it is important to consider your face shape and hair texture before deciding on this hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for the Short Back and Sides Quiff

To keep your hair looking healthy and stylish, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve and maintain a stylish short back and sides quiff hairstyle that will turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
