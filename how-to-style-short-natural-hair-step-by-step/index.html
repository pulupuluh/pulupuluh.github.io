How To Style Short Natural Hair Step By Step

Written by Petter Sep 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have short natural hair, you may be wondering how to style it. Short hair can be versatile and easy to manage, but it can also be challenging to style. In this article, we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to style short natural hair. We will also provide tips, advice, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

How to Style Short Natural Hair

When it comes to styling short natural hair, there are many options to choose from. You can wear your hair in a variety of styles, from curly to straight, and from sleek to voluminous. Here are some popular hairstyles for short natural hair:

  • Afro
  • Tapered cut
  • Finger coils
  • Bantu knots
  • Twist out
  • Braid out

No matter what style you choose, there are some basic steps you should follow to ensure your hair looks its best. These steps include washing, conditioning, and moisturizing your hair regularly, as well as using the right styling products.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions for styling short natural hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo.
  2. Condition your hair using a deep conditioner or hair mask.
  3. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  4. Towel-dry your hair gently, or use a microfiber towel to prevent breakage.
  5. Apply a leave-in conditioner or moisturizer to your hair, using a wide-tooth comb to distribute it evenly.
  6. Section your hair into small sections, using hair clips.
  7. Apply styling product to each section, such as gel or mousse.
  8. Style your hair using your fingers, a comb, or a brush.
  9. Use a diffuser to dry your hair, or let it air-dry.
  10. Finish with a hair oil or serum to add shine and prevent frizz.

Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips for styling short natural hair:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent breakage and preserve your hairstyle.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin or silk bonnet or scarf.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find what works best for your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short natural hair:

Q: How often should I wash my short natural hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Some people wash their hair every week, while others wash it every other week. You should wash your hair when it feels dirty or oily.

Q: What products should I use to style my short natural hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Some popular products for styling short natural hair include gel, mousse, leave-in conditioner, and hair oil.

Q: How do I prevent my short natural hair from frizzing?

A: You can prevent frizz by using a hair oil or serum, avoiding heat styling tools, and protecting your hair at night with a satin or silk bonnet or scarf.

Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your short natural hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo.
  • Condition your hair using a deep conditioner or hair mask.
  • Moisturize your hair daily with a leave-in conditioner or moisturizer.
  • Protect your hair at night with a satin or silk bonnet or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve beautiful and healthy short natural hair. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you!
