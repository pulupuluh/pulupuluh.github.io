How To Soften A Pixie Cut Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Mar 08, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’ve recently gotten a pixie cut, you might be finding it difficult to style and soften your new ‘do. Don’t worry, you’re not alone! Many people with pixie cuts struggle to find ways to soften their hair without compromising the shape of the cut. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for how to soften a pixie cut without sacrificing its unique style.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Soften a Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Softening a Pixie Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

How to Soften a Pixie Cut

When trying to soften a pixie cut, it’s important to keep in mind that the style is meant to be sharp and edgy. However, there are a few ways to soften the overall look without losing its unique shape. Here are some things to try:

1. Add Layers

Adding layers to your pixie cut can help to soften the overall look while maintaining the cut’s shape. Talk to your stylist about adding layers to your hair.

2. Use Texturizing Products

Texturizing products can help to add volume and movement to your hair, making it look softer and more natural. Try using a texturizing spray or pomade to achieve this effect.

3. Get a Side-Swept Fringe

A side-swept fringe can help to soften the look of a pixie cut while also framing your face. Talk to your stylist about adding a side-swept fringe to your cut.

4. Try a New Hair Color

Changing up your hair color can also help to soften your pixie cut. Consider trying a lighter or warmer color to achieve a softer look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to softening your pixie cut:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it’s damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Apply a texturizing product, such as a spray or pomade, to your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair while using your fingers to tousle it and create volume.
  5. If desired, use a flat iron or curling wand to add waves or curls to your hair.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Softening a Pixie Cut

Here are a few additional tips for softening your pixie cut:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and soft.
  • Try using a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut while keeping it soft and healthy.
  • Play around with different texturizing products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Q: Can I soften a pixie cut without adding layers?

A: Yes! You can soften a pixie cut by using texturizing products, getting a side-swept fringe, or trying a new hair color.

Q: Will adding layers to my pixie cut make it too thin?

A: Not necessarily. Talk to your stylist about adding layers that will enhance the shape of your cut without making your hair look too thin.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Here are some tips for keeping your pixie cut healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can soften your pixie cut while still maintaining its unique style. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and personal style. Happy styling!
