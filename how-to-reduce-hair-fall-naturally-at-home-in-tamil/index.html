How To Reduce Hair Fall Naturally At Home In Tamil

Written by Oliver May 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Hair fall is a common problem faced by many people, and it can be caused by various factors such as stress, pollution, improper diet, and genetics. However, there are natural remedies that can help reduce hair fall and promote hair growth. In this article, we will discuss how to reduce hair fall naturally at home in Tamil.

How to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally at Home in Tamil

Here are some natural remedies you can try to reduce hair fall:

1. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a plant that has been used for centuries to promote hair growth and reduce hair fall. It contains enzymes that help nourish and strengthen hair follicles. To use aloe vera for hair growth, extract the gel from the plant and apply it directly to your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then wash it off with a mild shampoo.

2. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is rich in nutrients that help nourish hair follicles and reduce hair fall. Massage warm coconut oil into your scalp for 10-15 minutes and leave it on for a few hours or overnight before washing it off with shampoo.

3. Onion Juice

Onion juice contains sulfur, which helps improve blood circulation to the scalp and promotes hair growth. Extract juice from an onion and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then wash it off with a mild shampoo.

4. Fenugreek

Fenugreek seeds contain hormones that help promote hair growth and reduce hair fall. Soak fenugreek seeds in water overnight and grind them into a paste. Apply the paste to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step by Step Guide to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally at Home in Tamil

Follow these step by step instructions to reduce hair fall naturally at home in Tamil.

Step 1: Choose a Natural Remedy

Choose a natural remedy that works best for you. Aloe vera, coconut oil, onion juice, and fenugreek are some of the effective remedies.

Step 2: Prepare the Remedy

Prepare the remedy according to the instructions. For example, extract aloe vera gel or soak fenugreek seeds in water overnight.

Step 3: Apply the Remedy

Apply the remedy to your scalp and hair. Massage it into your scalp for 10-15 minutes.

Step 4: Leave it on

Leave the remedy on your scalp and hair for the recommended time.

Step 5: Wash it off

Wash off the remedy with a mild shampoo.

Tips to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally at Home in Tamil

Here are some additional tips to help reduce hair fall naturally:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth and reduce hair fall.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners can damage hair and cause hair fall. Avoid using them as much as possible.

3. Avoid Chemical Treatments

Chemical treatments such as coloring and perming can weaken hair and cause hair fall. Avoid them as much as possible.

FAQ

Q. How long does it take to see results?

A. It may take a few weeks or months to see results, depending on the severity of your hair fall.

Q. Are natural remedies safe?

A. Natural remedies are generally safe, but it's always a good idea to do a patch test before trying them on your scalp.

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall. It's important to manage stress through exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important to promote hair growth and reduce hair fall. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids and ponytails that can pull on your hair and cause hair fall.

By following these natural remedies and tips, you can reduce hair fall and promote hair growth. Remember to be patient and consistent in your efforts.
