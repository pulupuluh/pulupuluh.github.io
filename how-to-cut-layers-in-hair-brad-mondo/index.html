How To Cut Layers In Hair Brad Mondo

Written by Eveline Jul 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting layers in hair is a great way to add texture, volume, and movement to your hair. If you're looking for a professional and stylish cut, Brad Mondo is a great inspiration. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to cut layers in hair Brad Mondo style, along with some tips, FAQ, and hair care advice.

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure that you have all the necessary tools such as scissors, comb, and hair clips. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.
  2. Part your hair in the middle or on the side using a comb. This will help you create an even cut on both sides.
  3. Starting from the back of your head, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair with your fingers where you want to start the layer.
  4. Using the scissors, cut the hair in a straight line. This will be the guide for the rest of the layers.
  5. Take another section of hair, slightly above the first one, and comb it straight down. Use the previous layer as a guide and cut the hair in a straight line.
  6. Continue cutting small sections of hair, moving towards the front of your head. Make sure that the length of each layer becomes shorter as you move towards the front of your head.
  7. Repeat the same process on the other side of your head.
  8. Once you have finished cutting all the layers, check for any unevenness and trim any stray hair.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

It is important to note that cutting layers in hair can be tricky, especially if you are doing it for the first time. If you are unsure about how to do it, it is best to seek the help of a professional hairdresser.

Tips to Remember

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting layers in hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Start with small sections of hair and gradually move towards bigger ones.
  • Make sure that the length of each layer becomes shorter as you move towards the front of your head.
  • Always comb the hair downwards before cutting to avoid any tangles.
  • Check for any unevenness and trim any stray hair after cutting.

FAQs

1. How long does it take to cut layers in hair?

The time it takes to cut layers in hair depends on the thickness and length of your hair. On average, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour.

2. Can I cut layers in my hair myself?

While it is possible to cut layers in your hair yourself, it is not recommended, especially if you are doing it for the first time. It is best to seek the help of a professional hairdresser.

3. How often should I cut my hair to maintain the layers?

To maintain the layers in your hair, it is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care Advice

After cutting layers in your hair, it is important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and shine. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when necessary.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly, as it can cause breakage and damage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the layers and avoid split ends.

Cutting layers in hair Brad Mondo style can give you a trendy and stylish look. However, it is important to follow the right steps and take care of your hair properly to maintain its health and shine. With the help of this guide, you can achieve the perfect layered haircut in no time!
