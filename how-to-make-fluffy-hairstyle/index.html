How To Make Fluffy Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a fun and easy way to add volume to your hair, then a fluffy hairstyle might be just what you need. This look is perfect for those who want to add some texture and bounce to their hair without spending too much time styling it. In this article, we will show you how to make a fluffy hairstyle step-by-step, along with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

How to Make Fluffy Hairstyle

A fluffy hairstyle is all about creating volume and texture. Here are the steps you need to follow to achieve this look:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to creating a fluffy hairstyle is to wash and condition your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra bounce to your locks. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Mousse or Texturizing Spray

Apply a small amount of mousse or texturizing spray to your hair. This will help to add some texture and hold to your locks. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, using your fingers or a comb.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start from the bottom and work your way up, focusing on the roots. Use the round brush to lift your hair at the roots, creating volume and bounce.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a teasing comb to backcomb your hair at the crown. This will help to add some extra volume and texture to your locks. Be gentle when teasing your hair, and avoid pulling too hard.

Step 5: Add Hairspray

Finish off your fluffy hairstyle by adding some hairspray. This will help to hold your locks in place and prevent them from falling flat. Hold the hairspray about 10 inches away from your hair and spray lightly.

Tips for Making Fluffy Hairstyle

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect fluffy hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra bounce to your locks.
  • Apply a small amount of mousse or texturizing spray to your hair to add texture and hold.
  • Blow dry your hair in sections, focusing on the roots to create volume and bounce.
  • Tease your hair gently to avoid damaging your locks.
  • Finish off your look with some hairspray to hold your locks in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a fluffy hairstyle:

What hair types are best for a fluffy hairstyle?

A fluffy hairstyle works best on medium to long hair. If you have fine or thin hair, this look can help to add some extra volume and bounce to your locks.

How long does a fluffy hairstyle last?

A fluffy hairstyle can last up to a day or two, depending on your hair type and the products you use. To make your look last longer, use a strong hold hairspray.

Can I make a fluffy hairstyle without teasing my hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a fluffy hairstyle without teasing your hair. Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add some extra bounce and hold to your locks.

Hair Care Tips for Fluffy Hairstyle

Here are some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your locks moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your locks looking healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you can make a fluffy hairstyle that looks great and lasts for days. Whether you have fine or thick hair, this look is perfect for adding some extra volume and bounce to your locks.
