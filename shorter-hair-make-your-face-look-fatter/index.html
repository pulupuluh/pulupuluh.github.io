Does Shorter Hair Make Your Face Look Fatter

Written by Bardi Jun 29, 2023 · 8 min read

If you've been considering getting a new haircut, you may have heard the rumor that shorter hair can make your face look fatter. But is there any truth to this statement? In this article, we'll explore the answer to this question and provide tips for choosing the right haircut for your face shape.

Description

Many people believe that shorter hair can make your face look wider or rounder. This belief may stem from the fact that longer hair can provide a slimming effect by elongating the face. However, the truth is that the length of your hair is just one factor to consider when choosing a haircut that flatters your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. Different face shapes can benefit from different haircut styles. Here are some tips to help you find the right haircut for your face shape:

Round Face

If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a haircut that adds height and length to your face. A layered haircut with volume at the crown can be a great choice. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as they can make your face look wider.

Square Face

If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your features with a haircut that has layers and texture. A side-swept fringe can also be flattering. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your jawline, as they can make your face look boxy.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! Almost any haircut will flatter your face shape. However, if you want to add some dimension, consider a layered haircut or a side-swept fringe.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to draw attention away from your forehead and create balance. A chin-length bob or a layered haircut with volume at the chin can be a great choice. Avoid short, blunt cuts that end at your cheekbones, as they can make your forehead look wider.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond face, you'll want to balance your features with a haircut that has volume at the crown and soft layers around your face. A side-swept fringe can also be flattering. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your cheekbones, as they can make your face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut for your face shape, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get the perfect haircut:

  1. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape.
  2. Find inspiration photos to show your stylist.
  3. Consult with your stylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and lifestyle.
  4. Discuss the length and style of your haircut with your stylist.
  5. Ask your stylist to show you how to style your new haircut at home.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a haircut.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new, but also be realistic about what will work for your face shape.
  • Bring photos of hairstyles you like to your stylist.
  • Ask your stylist for their professional opinion on what will work best for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Does shorter hair make your face look fatter?

Shorter hair can make your face look wider or rounder if the cut is not flattering for your face shape. However, the length of your hair is just one factor to consider when choosing a haircut.

What is the most flattering haircut for a round face?

A layered haircut with volume at the crown can be a great choice for a round face. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin.

Can I pull off a short haircut?

Short haircuts can be flattering on many different face shapes and hair types. Consult with your stylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and lifestyle.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

In conclusion, shorter hair can make your face look wider or rounder if the cut is not flattering for your face shape. However, by choosing a haircut that flatters your face shape and taking care of your hair, you can look and feel your best with any length of hair.
