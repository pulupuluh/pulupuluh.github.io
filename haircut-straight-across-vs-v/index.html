Haircut Straight Across Vs V Which One Is Right For You

Written by Robby Aug 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you confused about whether to get a haircut straight across or a V-shaped haircut? Don't worry! We've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss the differences between the two haircuts, their pros and cons, and which one is suitable for your hair type and face shape. So, let's get started!

Description

A straight across haircut is a blunt cut that goes straight across the ends of your hair. It gives a neat and polished look to your hair. On the other hand, a V-shaped haircut has an angled cut that creates a V-shape at the back of your head. It adds layers and texture to your hair, giving it a more voluminous and bouncy look.

How to

Getting a haircut straight across or a V-shaped haircut is not difficult. However, it's crucial to choose the right haircut that suits your hair type and face shape. Here are a few tips to help you decide which one is best for you:

1. Consider your face shape

Face shape plays a significant role in determining the right haircut for you. A straight across haircut is ideal for people with a round face shape as it creates the illusion of length. On the other hand, a V-shaped haircut is perfect for people with a square or heart-shaped face as it softens the angles and adds volume to the hair.

2. Analyze your hair type

Your hair type is another essential factor to consider while choosing a haircut. A straight across haircut works well for people with thick and straight hair. It gives a sleek and polished look to the hair. However, if you have thin or fine hair, a V-shaped haircut is preferable as it adds layers and texture to the hair, making it look fuller and bouncy.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a straight across or V-shaped haircut:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Before getting a haircut, make sure to wash your hair and dry it thoroughly. Clean hair makes it easier for the stylist to cut your hair accurately.

Step 2: Consult with your stylist

Consult with your stylist about the haircut you want. Show them pictures or describe the hairstyle you desire, and they will advise you on the best haircut for your hair type and face shape.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into small parts using clips. This will help your stylist to cut your hair accurately and evenly.

Step 4: Cut your hair

Your stylist will cut your hair according to the selected haircut. A straight across haircut involves cutting your hair straight across the ends, while a V-shaped haircut involves cutting your hair at an angle to create a V-shape at the back of your head.

Step 5: Style your hair

After the haircut, your stylist will style your hair according to your preference. You can choose to blow-dry, straighten or curl your hair.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut:

  • Choose a haircut that suits your hair type and face shape.
  • Consult with your stylist before getting a haircut.
  • Bring pictures or describe the hairstyle you desire to your stylist.
  • Wash your hair before getting a haircut.
  • Use good quality hair care products to maintain your haircut.

FAQ

1. Is a straight across or V-shaped haircut better for thick hair?

A straight across haircut is better for thick hair as it creates a sleek and polished look. However, a V-shaped haircut also works well for thick hair as it adds layers and texture to the hair.

2. How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair growth and the hairstyle you have. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain the length and shape of your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain your haircut, it's crucial to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair as it can cause breakage.

In conclusion, a haircut straight across or a V-shaped haircut can add a unique touch to your hair. Consider your hair type and face shape before choosing a haircut, and consult with your stylist to get the best results. Follow the tips and hair care routine to maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy and shiny.
