Hairstyle For Oval Face Indian Girl

Written by Petter Apr 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As an Indian girl with an oval face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task. You want something that accentuates your features, but also complements your face shape. In this article, we'll cover how to choose the best hairstyle for oval face Indian girls, as well as some tips and tricks for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Oval Face Indian Girls

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the best hairstyle for an oval face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to highlight your face shape by choosing a style that complements your features. Second, you want to create balance and proportion by avoiding hairstyles that are too long or too short.

Some of the best hairstyles for oval face Indian girls include:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great choice for oval face Indian girls because they help to create volume and movement. This style works well with both straight and curly hair, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Side-swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for oval face Indian girls. They help to create a soft, feminine look while also drawing attention to your beautiful features.

Bob Cut

If you're looking for a shorter style, a bob cut is a great choice. This style is versatile and can be worn straight or curly, and is easy to maintain.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Now that you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your oval face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Long Layers

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  3. Curl your hair using a curling iron, focusing on the ends to create movement.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Side-swept Bangs

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  3. Using a flat iron, straighten your bangs and sweep them to one side.
  4. Finish with a smoothing serum.

Bob Cut

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling cream.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is key to keeping your hairstyle looking great. Here are a few tips and tricks:

Avoid Heat Damage

Heat styling tools can cause damage to your hair over time. To avoid this, try to limit your use of heat tools and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water is important for keeping your hair hydrated and healthy. Aim for at least 8 glasses of water per day.

Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits and vegetables is important for keeping your hair healthy. Be sure to include foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals like biotin, iron, and zinc.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oval face?

You should avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can create an unbalanced look. You should also avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as they can make your face appear longer.

Can I wear my hair straight and curly?

Yes! Oval face Indian girls can wear their hair straight or curly. It's all about finding the style that works best for you.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care for Oval Face Indian Girls

When it comes to hair care for oval face Indian girls, it's important to choose products that work well with your hair type. Here are a few tips:

Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

If you have oily hair, look for a clarifying shampoo that will help to remove excess oil. If you have dry hair, look for a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that will help to hydrate your hair.

Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help to add moisture to your hair and prevent breakage. Look for a product that is lightweight and won't weigh your hair down.

Avoid Over-Brushing

Over-brushing your hair can cause damage and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and be gentle when brushing.

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your oval face can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a style that complements your features and highlights your natural beauty. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding heat damage, and you'll be sure to have healthy, beautiful hair for years to come.
