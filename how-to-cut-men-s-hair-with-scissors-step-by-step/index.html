Whatever the reason may be, cutting men's hair with scissors is not as difficult as it may seem. In this step by step guide, we will show you how to cut men's hair with scissors, from start to finish. So, grab your scissors and let's get started!

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors

Before we get started with the step by step guide, it's important to understand the basic techniques involved in cutting men's hair with scissors.

The first thing you need to do is wash and dry the hair. This will make it easier to cut and style. Next, you will need to section the hair using clips or combs. This will help you to work on one section at a time, making it easier to control the length and shape of the hair.

When cutting men's hair with scissors, it's important to use the right technique. The most common technique is to use the 'scissor over comb' method. This involves using the comb to lift the hair and the scissors to cut it. Another technique is the 'point cutting' method, which involves using the tips of the scissors to create texture and movement in the hair.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Section the Hair

Using clips or combs, section the hair into manageable sections. The smaller the sections, the easier it will be to control the length and shape of the hair.

Step 2: Start with the Sides

Using the 'scissor over comb' method, start cutting the hair on the sides, working your way up towards the top of the head. Remember to cut in small sections, taking your time to ensure that the length and shape are even.

Step 3: Move to the Top

Once you have finished cutting the sides, move on to the top of the head. Again, using the 'scissor over comb' method, cut the hair in small sections, working your way towards the front of the head.

Step 4: Create Texture and Movement

If you want to create texture and movement in the hair, use the 'point cutting' method. This involves using the tips of the scissors to cut into the hair, creating a choppy, textured look.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have finished cutting the hair, use the scissors to blend the hair together. This will create a more natural, seamless look.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

1. Use sharp scissors: Sharp scissors will make it easier to cut the hair and will give you a more precise cut.

2. Take your time: Cutting men's hair with scissors requires patience and attention to detail. Take your time and don't rush the process.

3. Use the right technique: The 'scissor over comb' method is the most common technique for cutting men's hair with scissors. However, if you want to create texture and movement, use the 'point cutting' method.

4. Use the right tools: Along with sharp scissors, you will also need combs and clips to section the hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut my own hair with scissors?

A: Yes, you can cut your own hair with scissors. However, it's important to have the right tools and to follow the proper techniques.

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut men's hair?

A: While you can use regular scissors to cut men's hair, it's best to use hair cutting scissors. Hair cutting scissors are designed to give you a more precise cut and are less likely to damage the hair.

Hair Care after Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

After cutting men's hair with scissors, it's important to take care of the hair to ensure that it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some tips:

1. Wash and condition the hair regularly.

2. Use a styling product to help keep the hair in place.

3. Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of the hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great after cutting it with scissors.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with scissors step by step, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Remember to have the right tools, take your time, and follow the proper techniques for the best results.
