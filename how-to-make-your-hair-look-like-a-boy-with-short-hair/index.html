How To Make Your Hair Look Like A Boy With Short Hair

How to Achieve a Boyish Look with Short Hair

Before we dive into the steps, let's first understand what a boyish look with short hair entails. Typically, this involves having a short haircut that is styled to look messy and unkempt. The goal is to have a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and trendy.

What You'll Need:

  • Hair clippers or scissors
  • Styling gel or wax
  • Comb or brush

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in achieving a boyish look with short hair is to choose the right haircut. This will depend on the shape of your face and personal preference. Some popular short haircuts for a boyish look include:

  • The buzz cut
  • The crew cut
  • The undercut
  • The textured crop

It's best to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine which haircut will work best for you.

Step 2: Cut Your Hair

Once you have chosen your desired haircut, it's time to cut your hair. You can either use hair clippers or scissors. If you're unsure how to cut your hair, it's best to seek the help of a professional hairstylist.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

After cutting your hair, it's time to style it. To achieve a messy and unkempt look, use styling gel or wax to create texture and volume. Apply the product to your hair and use a comb or brush to style it in the desired direction. You can also use your fingers to create a more natural look.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Now that you have achieved your desired boyish look with short hair, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you keep your new look:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired length.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles to keep your look fresh and trendy.

FAQs

Can I achieve a boyish look with long hair?

While it's possible to achieve a boyish look with long hair, it may require more maintenance and styling. It's best to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine if this look is achievable for you.

What styling products should I use?

For a messy and unkempt look, it's best to use styling gel or wax. These products will provide texture and volume to your hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different hair care routine compared to long hair. Here are some tips to keep your short hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired length.

There you have it, a comprehensive guide on how to achieve a boyish look with short hair. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine which haircut will work best for you. With the right haircut and styling products, you can achieve a low-maintenance, stylish, and trendy look.
