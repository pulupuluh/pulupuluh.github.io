How To Apply Mousse To Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 14, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have wavy hair, you know that it can be a challenge to style. You want to enhance your natural waves, but you don't want them to look frizzy or flat. That's where mousse comes in. Mousse is a great styling product for wavy hair because it adds volume, definition, and hold. But how do you apply it correctly? In this article, we'll show you how to apply mousse to wavy hair in a way that will give you beautiful, bouncy curls.

How to Apply Mousse to Wavy Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about why mousse is a great styling product for wavy hair. Mousse is a lightweight foam that adds volume and texture to your hair without weighing it down. It's easy to apply and can be used on damp or dry hair. Here's how to apply mousse to wavy hair:

1. Start with clean, damp hair

Before you apply mousse, make sure your hair is clean and damp. You can either shampoo and condition your hair in the shower or wet your hair with a spray bottle. Make sure your hair is evenly damp, but not soaking wet.

2. Choose the right mousse

Not all mousses are created equal. Look for a mousse that is specifically designed for wavy hair. These mousses will enhance your natural waves without making your hair look crunchy or stiff. You can also choose a mousse based on your hair type. If you have fine hair, look for a lightweight mousse. If you have thick hair, look for a mousse with more hold.

3. Apply the mousse

Shake the mousse can well before using it. Dispense a golf ball-sized amount of mousse onto your palm. Rub your hands together to distribute the mousse evenly. Then, apply the mousse to your hair from roots to ends. Make sure you distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

4. Scrunch your hair

Scrunching your hair is an important step in applying mousse to wavy hair. Scrunching helps to enhance your natural waves and create volume. To scrunch your hair, grab small sections of hair and squeeze them upwards towards your scalp. Repeat this process all over your head.

5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser

Once you've applied the mousse and scrunched your hair, you can either let your hair air dry or use a diffuser. If you let your hair air dry, don't touch your hair until it's completely dry. This will help to prevent frizz. If you use a diffuser, use it on low heat and low speed. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and scrunch your hair with the diffuser.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of how to apply mousse to wavy hair, let's break it down into a step-by-step guide:

1. Shampoo and condition your hair

Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair. Make sure you rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all product buildup.

2. Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Don't rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz.

3. Apply the mousse

Dispense a golf ball-sized amount of mousse onto your palm. Rub your hands together to distribute the mousse evenly. Then, apply the mousse to your hair from roots to ends. Make sure you distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

4. Scrunch your hair

Scrunch your hair to enhance your natural waves and create volume. Grab small sections of hair and squeeze them upwards towards your scalp. Repeat this process all over your head.

5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair. If you use a diffuser, use it on low heat and low speed. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and scrunch your hair with the diffuser.

Tips for Applying Mousse to Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when applying mousse to wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying mousse.
  • Don't use too much mousse, as this can make your hair look crunchy or greasy.
  • If you have thick hair, apply mousse in layers to make sure you distribute it evenly.
  • If you have fine hair, apply mousse to the roots of your hair to create volume.
  • Don't touch your hair while it's drying, as this can cause frizz.

FAQs

Can I use mousse on dry hair?

Yes, you can use mousse on dry hair. However, it's best to use mousse on damp hair, as this will help the mousse distribute more evenly.

Can I use mousse every day?

Yes, you can use mousse every day. However, it's important to use a mousse that is formulated for daily use and won't cause buildup.

How do I prevent frizz when using mousse?

To prevent frizz, make sure you don't touch your hair while it's drying. You can also use a diffuser on low heat and low speed to dry your hair without causing frizz.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy waves that last all day. Remember to choose the right mousse for your hair type, apply it correctly, and let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to prevent frizz. With a little practice, you'll become a pro at applying mousse to wavy hair.
