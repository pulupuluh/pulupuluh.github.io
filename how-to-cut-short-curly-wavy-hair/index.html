How To Cut Short Curly Wavy Hair Tips Steps And Hair Care

Written by Aprilia Sep 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have short curly wavy hair, you might think that cutting it is a complicated process. However, it doesn't have to be. With the right tips and steps, you can easily cut your hair at home. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the process of cutting short curly wavy hair, including how to prepare, cut, and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Short Curly Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Short Curly Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut Short Curly Wavy Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure that you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It's also a good idea to have a mirror or two, so you can see all angles of your hair.

When deciding on the length of your hair, start with a longer length than you want, as curly hair tends to shrink when it dries.

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to cutting your curly hair is to wash and condition it. This will help to detangle your hair and make it easier to cut. After washing, gently pat your hair dry with a towel.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through it. This will help to remove any tangles and make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start by dividing your hair in half, from ear to ear. Then, divide each section into smaller sections, using clips to hold them in place.

Step 4: Start Cutting Your Hair

Start at the back of your hair, and use your fingers to separate a small section of hair. Hold the hair between your fingers, and cut the hair at the desired length. Continue with the rest of your hair, making sure to cut each section the same length.

Step 5: Check for Balance

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check for balance by looking at both sides of your head. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair is even.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a summary of the steps to cut your short curly wavy hair:

  1. Gather the right tools
  2. Wash and condition your hair
  3. Comb your hair
  4. Section your hair
  5. Start cutting your hair
  6. Check for balance

Tips for Cutting Short Curly Wavy Hair

Here are a few tips to help you cut your short curly wavy hair:

  • Start with a longer length than you want, as your hair will shrink when it dries.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry, as curly hair can look different when it's wet.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Cut your hair in small sections to ensure that it's even.
  • Check for balance by looking at both sides of your head.

FAQs

How often should I cut my short curly wavy hair?

It's recommended that you cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my short curly wavy hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your hair at home if you have the right tools and follow the proper steps.

Should I cut my hair wet or dry?

It's best to cut your curly hair when it's dry, as it can look different when it's wet.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your curly hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair and prevent frizz.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid damaging your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

Cutting your short curly wavy hair can be a simple process with the right tools and steps. Remember to start with a longer length than you want, cut your hair in small sections, and check for balance. After cutting, take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
