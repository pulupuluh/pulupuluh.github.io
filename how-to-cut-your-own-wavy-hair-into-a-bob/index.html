How To Cut Your Own Wavy Hair Into A Bob

Written by Eveline Jul 06, 2023 · 8 min read

Welcome to our hair care blog! Today, we will be teaching you how to cut your own wavy hair into a bob. This is a great hairstyle for those who want a fresh and trendy look without having to go to the salon. We will be providing you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, as well as some tips and frequently asked questions to ensure that you achieve the perfect bob.

Description

A bob is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It is a short haircut that usually falls between the chin and the shoulders. A bob is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn straight, curly, or wavy. It can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. In this tutorial, we will be focusing on how to cut your own wavy hair into a bob.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, you will need the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror

Once you have all the tools, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. This will make it easier to cut.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and divide it into two sections.
  3. Take one section of hair and tie or clip it out of the way.
  4. Start with the other section of hair and comb it straight down.
  5. Take your scissors and cut the hair straight across at the desired length. It is best to start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 on the other section of hair.
  7. Once both sections are cut, check to make sure they are even. If not, trim any uneven pieces.
  8. Style your hair by adding some waves or curls using a curling iron or wand.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect bob:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Don't cut too much at once.
  • Use hair ties or clips to keep sections of hair out of the way.
  • Be patient and take your time.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own wavy hair into a bob:

Can I cut my hair when it's wet?

It is best to cut your hair when it's dry because wet hair tends to look longer than it actually is. If you must cut it when it's wet, make sure to cut less than you think you need to.

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, it is best to use sharp scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors can damage your hair and cause split ends.

How often should I trim my hair?

It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Cutting your own wavy hair into a bob can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and step-by-step guide, you will be able to achieve the perfect bob in no time. Remember to take care of your hair and enjoy your new look!
