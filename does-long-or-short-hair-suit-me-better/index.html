Does Long Or Short Hair Suit Me Better

Written by William Jun 12, 2023 · 12 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be exciting, but it can also be overwhelming. One of the biggest decisions you'll need to make is whether to go for long hair or short hair. It can be tough to figure out which option will suit you best, but don't worry – we're here to help! In this article, we'll explore the factors you should consider when deciding between long and short hair, and we'll help you determine which one will be the better choice for you.

Description

Long hair and short hair both have their advantages and disadvantages. Long hair can be glamorous, romantic, and versatile, but it can also be high-maintenance, heavy, and prone to tangling. Short hair, on the other hand, can be chic, modern, and low-maintenance, but it can also be limiting, and it may not suit all face shapes or hair types.

In this article, we'll look at the different factors that can influence whether long or short hair will be the better choice for you. We'll consider your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal preferences, and we'll give you some tips on how to style and care for your new hairdo.

How to Determine if Long or Short Hair Suits You Better

There are several factors you should consider when deciding whether long or short hair will suit you better. Here are some key things to keep in mind:

Face Shape

Your face shape can play a big role in determining which hairstyle will look best on you. Generally, long hair tends to suit people with longer faces, while short hair can be more flattering on those with rounder faces. However, there are exceptions to this rule, and it's important to consider other factors as well.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also influence which hairstyle will suit you best. Long hair can be beautiful if you have thick, luscious locks, but if your hair is thin or fine, it may look limp and lifeless. Short hair can be a great choice if you have curly or wavy hair, as it can help to define your curls and make them look more voluminous.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in deciding between long and short hair. If you're someone who likes to spend a lot of time styling your hair, then long hair might be a good choice for you, as it offers more versatility when it comes to updos and braids. However, if you're someone who prefers a low-maintenance look, then short hair might be a better option, as it requires less styling and upkeep.

Step by Step: How to Choose Between Long and Short Hair

If you're still not sure whether long or short hair is the better choice for you, here are some steps you can take to help you make your decision:

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

Take a good look at your face shape in the mirror. Do you have a round face, a square face, an oval face, or a heart-shaped face? This will help you determine which length of hair will be most flattering on you.

Step 2: Think About Your Hair Texture

Consider the texture of your hair. Is it thick, thin, curly, wavy, or straight? This will help you decide which type of haircut will work best for you.

Step 3: Evaluate Your Lifestyle

Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair. If you're someone who likes to experiment with different styles and spend time doing your hair, then long hair might be a good choice for you. However, if you're someone who prefers a low-maintenance look, then short hair might be a better option.

Step 4: Try on Wigs or Use Virtual Try-On Tools

If you're still not sure which length of hair will suit you best, try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different hairstyles will look on you. This can help you get a better idea of what you'll look like with long or short hair, and it can also help you narrow down your options.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining Long or Short Hair

Whether you decide to go for long or short hair, there are some tips you should keep in mind to help you style and maintain your new 'do:

Long Hair

  • Invest in high-quality haircare products to keep your locks healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with braids, updos, and other styles to keep your hair looking fresh and versatile.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your locks looking neat.

Short Hair

  • Use styling products like mousse or gel to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does long hair make you look younger or older?

A: Long hair can make you look younger or older, depending on how you style it. If you wear your hair in loose, romantic waves, it can give you a youthful, bohemian look. However, if you wear your hair in a severe, tight bun or a sleek, straight style, it can make you look more mature and sophisticated.

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: Short hair can make your face look rounder if you choose the wrong haircut. However, if you opt for a style that flatters your face shape, it can actually make your features look more defined and angular.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up if you have short hair. There are plenty of cute and stylish updos that work well with short hair, including braids, buns, and half-up styles.

Hair Care for Long and Short Hair

Whether you decide to go for long or short hair, it's important to take good care of your locks. Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a high-quality conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Overall, choosing between long and short hair is a personal decision that depends on a variety of factors. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can determine which length of hair will be most flattering and practical for you. Whether you opt for long, luxurious locks or a modern, chic bob, make sure to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
