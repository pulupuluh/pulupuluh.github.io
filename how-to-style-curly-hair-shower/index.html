Description

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look beautiful and voluminous, it can also be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to styling. In this blog post, we will discuss how to style curly hair in the shower, so you can have more control over your curls and achieve the look you desire.

How To Style Curly Hair Shower

Styling curly hair in the shower can be a great way to achieve the perfect curls without damaging your hair. Follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Hair

Start by cleansing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, and rinse thoroughly with warm water.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a generous amount of conditioner to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while the conditioner is still in. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, and then rinse with cool water. This will help to seal the cuticle and lock in moisture.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

While your hair is still wet, apply your styling products of choice. This can include a curl-enhancing cream, gel, or mousse. Work the product through your hair, scrunching it upwards towards your scalp to encourage curl formation.

Step 4: Plop Your Hair

Plopping is a technique that involves wrapping your hair in a towel to enhance your curls. Take a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt and lay it flat on a surface. Flip your head over and place your hair on the towel, with the ends at the top of your head. Fold the towel over your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave your hair wrapped for 20-30 minutes.

Step 5: Diffuse Your Hair

Once you've plopped your hair, it's time to diffuse it. Attach a diffuser to your hairdryer and set it to a low or medium heat. Flip your head over and use the diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching it upwards towards your scalp as you go. Be gentle and avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style curly hair in the shower:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply styling products to wet hair to help distribute them evenly.
  • Use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to plop your hair, as these materials are gentle on curls.
  • Diffuse your hair on a low or medium heat to avoid damaging your curls.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair every day, while others only wash it once or twice a week. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: What should I do if my curls are flat?

A: Try using a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your curls. You can also try diffusing your hair upside down to encourage lift at the roots.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful curls. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as these can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that will turn heads wherever you go.
