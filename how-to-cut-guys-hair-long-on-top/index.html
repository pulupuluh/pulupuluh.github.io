How to Cut Guys Hair Long on Top

Cutting guys hair long on top is a popular style that can be achieved with a few simple steps. Here is a general overview of how to cut guys hair long on top:

  1. Wash and dry the hair.
  2. Section the hair by parting it from the forehead to the nape of the neck.
  3. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward, cutting the hair to the desired length.
  4. Blend the sides and the back of the head with the top of the hair.
  5. Use scissors or clippers to trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck.
  6. Style the hair as desired.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you have a general idea of how to cut guys hair long on top, let's go over the step-by-step instructions:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. It's easier to cut hair when it is clean and dry, so make sure to wash and dry the hair before you begin.
  2. Section the hair. Use a comb to part the hair from the forehead to the nape of the neck. This will help you to work on one section of hair at a time.
  3. Start at the back. Begin cutting the hair at the back of the head, using scissors or clippers. Work your way forward, taking small sections of hair at a time.
  4. Cut the hair to the desired length. Use your fingers as a guide and cut the hair to the desired length. If you're not sure how much to cut, start with a longer length and gradually cut more until you reach the desired length.
  5. Blend the sides and the back. Use scissors or clippers to blend the sides and the back of the head with the top of the hair. This will create a natural, seamless look.
  6. Trim around the ears and the back of the neck. Use scissors or clippers to trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck. This will create a clean, polished look.
  7. Style the hair. Once you have finished cutting the hair, style it as desired. You can use a hair dryer, a brush, or styling products to create the look you want.

Tips for Cutting Guys Hair Long on Top

Cutting guys hair long on top can be challenging, especially if you're new to it. Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Invest in high-quality scissors or clippers. This will make the cutting process easier and help you to achieve a more precise cut.
  • Take your time. Cutting hair can be time-consuming, but it's important to take your time and work slowly and methodically.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to cut a little bit at a time and gradually work your way to the desired length.
  • Use a comb to guide your cuts. This will help you to create even, precise cuts.
  • Watch tutorial videos. There are many tutorial videos available online that can help you to learn the basics of cutting hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting guys hair long on top:

  • What length should I cut my hair? The length of your hair will depend on your personal preference and the style you're going for. Generally, guys hair long on top is about 2-3 inches in length.
  • How often should I cut my hair? It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and style.
  • Can I cut my hair myself? Yes, you can cut your hair yourself, but it's important to take your time and be patient. You can also enlist the help of a friend or family member if you're not comfortable cutting your own hair.

Hair Care for Guys with Long Hair

Once you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips for hair care for guys with long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, or use them sparingly.

Cutting guys hair long on top can seem daunting, but with these step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice, you'll be able to achieve the look you want with ease. Happy cutting!
