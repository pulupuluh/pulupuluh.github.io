Best Haircuts For Oblong Face Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank May 05, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're a man with an oblong face, you might find it difficult to choose a haircut that complements your features. But don't worry – with the right haircut, you can accentuate your best features and look your best. In this article, we'll take a look at the best haircuts for oblong face male, how to choose the right one, and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Best Haircuts for Oblong Face Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

  • Hair Care Tips for Oblong Face Male

How to Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your face shape. Men with oblong faces have a longer face shape with a slightly narrower chin and forehead. The goal of your haircut should be to balance out the length of your face and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider the length and style. Haircuts that are too short or too long can accentuate the length of your face. Instead, opt for a medium-length haircut that adds volume and width to your face.

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. Men with thicker hair can go for a more layered look, while those with thin hair should opt for a shorter, more structured cut.

Best Haircuts for Oblong Face Male

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut, let's take a look at some of the best haircuts for oblong face male:

1. The Classic Side Part

The classic side part is a timeless haircut that looks great on men with oblong faces. This style features a deep side part and short sides with longer hair on top. The longer hair on top adds volume and width to the face, while the short sides create a more balanced overall look.

2. The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern haircut that works well for men with oblong faces. This style features short, textured hair on top with slightly longer hair on the sides. The textured hair on top adds volume and width to the face, while the shorter sides create a more balanced overall look.

3. The Undercut

The undercut is a versatile haircut that looks great on men with oblong faces. This style features short hair on the sides and back with longer hair on top. The longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back or messy.

4. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a low-maintenance haircut that works well for men with oblong faces. This style features short hair all over the head, which can help to balance out the length of your face. It's also a great option if you have thinning hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you've chosen the right haircut, it's important to know how to get it. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut:

  1. Find a reputable barber or stylist.
  2. Show them a picture of the haircut you want.
  3. Explain your face shape and hair type.
  4. Ask for their input on the best length and style for your face shape.
  5. Sit back and relax while they work their magic.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've got the perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a hairdryer on a low heat setting to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut with an oblong face?

A: It's not recommended to go for a very short haircut if you have an oblong face, as it can accentuate the length of your face. Instead, opt for a medium-length haircut that adds volume and width to your face.

Q: Can I style my hair in a side part if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair in a side part if you have thinning hair. Just make sure to use a volumizing product to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Face Male

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for oblong face male:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid wearing hats for long periods of time, as they can flatten your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and the right haircut, you can look your best and feel confident with an oblong face.
