What To Tell Your Barber When Cutting Waves A Guide For Men

Written by Bardi Jun 06, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're a man with naturally curly or wavy hair, you might be interested in getting waves. Waves are a popular hairstyle that involve creating a pattern of ridges or waves in the hair. To get waves, you'll need to tell your barber what kind of wave pattern you want. In this article, we'll go over what to tell your barber when cutting waves, as well as some tips and tricks for maintaining your waves.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Waves
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Waves
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Waves

How to Get Waves

Before you go to the barber, it's important to understand what kind of waves you want. There are several different wave patterns to choose from, including:

  • 360 waves: These are small, tight waves that cover the entire head.
  • 720 waves: These waves are larger and more spaced out than 360 waves.
  • Swirl waves: These waves are circular and start at the crown of the head.
  • Side waves: These waves start on one side of the head and curve around to the other side.

Once you've decided on a wave pattern, it's time to talk to your barber. Be sure to bring a picture of the wave pattern you want or be able to describe it clearly. Your barber will need to know:

  • The size of the waves you want
  • The direction of the waves (front to back, side to side, or a combination)
  • The length of your hair (longer hair will require more time to train into waves)

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what to tell your barber, let's go over the step-by-step process for getting waves:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a wave cream or pomade to your damp hair.
  3. Brush your hair in the direction of the waves for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Put on a wave cap or durag to help set the waves in place.
  5. Sleep with the wave cap or durag on.
  6. In the morning, remove the wave cap or durag and brush your hair again.
  7. Repeat this process every day until your waves are fully formed.
  8. Visit your barber regularly to maintain your wave pattern.

Tips for Maintaining Your Waves

Once you've achieved your desired wave pattern, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair no more than once a week to avoid stripping the natural oils.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Apply a wave cream or pomade daily to keep your waves looking defined.
  • Brush your hair regularly to keep the wave pattern intact.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and disrupt the wave pattern.

FAQs

How long does it take to get waves?

It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to get waves, depending on the length and texture of your hair.

Can I get waves if I have straight hair?

It's possible to get waves with straight hair, but it will require more work and product than if you have naturally curly or wavy hair.

How often should I visit the barber to maintain my waves?

You should visit your barber every 2-4 weeks to maintain your wave pattern and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care for Waves

To keep your waves looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair no more than once a week to avoid stripping the natural oils.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Apply a wave cream or pomade daily to keep your waves looking defined.
  • Brush your hair regularly to keep the wave pattern intact.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and disrupt the wave pattern.

By following these tips and communicating clearly with your barber, you can achieve and maintain the perfect wave pattern for your hair. With a little patience and dedication, you'll be sporting stylish waves in no time.
