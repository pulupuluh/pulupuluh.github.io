Best Blonde Hair Color For 60 Year Old Woman A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a 60 year old woman who wants to switch up your hair color and go blonde? Choosing the right shade of blonde can be a daunting task, especially if you’re new to the world of hair coloring. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll discuss the best blonde hair color for 60 year old woman, how to achieve it, and how to take care of your newly colored locks.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Blonde hair is a popular choice for women of all ages, but it’s important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and features. As a 60 year old woman, you may want to opt for a more natural-looking blonde shade, like honey blonde or light golden blonde. These shades will add warmth to your skin and enhance your natural beauty.

How To

The first step in achieving the best blonde hair color for 60 year old woman is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to recommend the best shade of blonde for your skin tone and features. If you want to try coloring your hair at home, make sure to do a patch test first to avoid any allergic reactions.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving the best blonde hair color for 60 year old woman:

  1. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best shade of blonde for your skin tone and features.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it.
  3. Apply the hair color according to the instructions on the package.
  4. Leave the color on for the recommended amount of time.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water.
  6. Apply a toner if necessary to achieve the desired shade.
  7. Condition your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best blonde hair color for 60 year old woman:

  • Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and features.
  • Opt for a more natural-looking shade of blonde for a subtle change.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your new hair color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

1. Can I dye my hair blonde at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair blonde at home, but it’s important to do a patch test first and follow the instructions on the package carefully.

2. What shade of blonde is best for a 60 year old woman?

As a 60 year old woman, you may want to opt for a more natural-looking blonde shade, like honey blonde or light golden blonde.

3. How often do I need to touch up my blonde hair color?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the color fades. On average, you may need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair blonde, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

By following these tips, you can maintain your new blonde hair color and keep it looking beautiful for years to come!
