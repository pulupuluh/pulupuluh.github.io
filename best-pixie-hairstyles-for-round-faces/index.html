Best Pixie Hairstyles For Round Faces In 2023

If you have a round face, you might feel like your options for short haircuts are limited. However, with the right hairstyle, you can accentuate your features and create a look that is both stylish and flattering. In this article, we'll explore some of the best pixie hairstyles for round faces that you can try in 2023.

Description

A pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that usually features short layers and a wispy fringe. This style can be worn straight or textured, and it's a great option for those who want a low-maintenance haircut.

When choosing a pixie hairstyle for a round face, it's important to consider the shape of your face and the features you want to accentuate. For example, an angled pixie can help to elongate your face, while a side-swept pixie can draw attention to your cheekbones.

How to Choose the Best Pixie Hairstyle for Your Round Face

When choosing a pixie hairstyle for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider the shape of your face. A round face can be softened with an angled or asymmetrical pixie.
  • Think about your hair texture. A textured pixie can work well with curly or wavy hair, while a sleek pixie might be better for straight hair.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you're low-maintenance, a simple pixie with minimal styling might be the best option for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Pixie Haircut

Styling a pixie haircut is relatively simple, but there are a few steps you can follow to create a polished look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or pomade, to your hair.
  3. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style your hair, focusing on the roots and the crown of your head.
  4. Finish with a small amount of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Hairstyle

Here are a few tips for maintaining your pixie hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Invest in a good styling product to help hold your style in place.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to help extend the life of your pixie haircut between washes.

FAQs

Can you wear a pixie haircut with glasses?

A pixie haircut can look great with glasses. Consider choosing a style that doesn't cover your ears, as this can help to balance out the look.

Can you wear a pixie haircut with bangs?

Yes, you can wear a pixie haircut with bangs. Consider choosing a wispy or side-swept fringe to help soften the look.

What face shape is best for a pixie haircut?

A pixie haircut can work well for a variety of face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care for Pixie Haircuts

When caring for your pixie haircut, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to help nourish and strengthen your hair.

Best Pixie Hairstyles for Round Faces

Now that you know how to choose the best pixie hairstyle for your round face, let's explore some of the most popular options:

Angled Pixie

An angled pixie is a great option for those with a round face, as it can help to elongate your face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones. To style an angled pixie, blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the crown of your head. Use a small amount of texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

Side-Swept Pixie

A side-swept pixie can help to draw attention to your cheekbones and soften the look of a round face. To style a side-swept pixie, blow dry your hair to one side, using a round brush to add volume. Finish with a small amount of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Textured Pixie

A textured pixie can work well with curly or wavy hair, as it can help to define your natural texture. To style a textured pixie, apply a small amount of curl cream or mousse to your hair, scrunching your hair as you go. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, focusing on the roots.

Asymmetrical Pixie

An asymmetrical pixie can help to create the illusion of a longer face and add some edge to your look. To style an asymmetrical pixie, blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the longer side of your hair. Use a small amount of pomade to add texture and definition to your hair.

Pompadour Pixie

A pompadour pixie can help to add volume and height to your hair, creating a bold and stylish look. To style a pompadour pixie, blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the crown of your head. Use a small amount of pomade to create height and texture, finishing with a small amount of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Curly Pixie

A curly pixie can help to define your natural texture and soften the look of a round face. To style a curly pixie, apply a small amount of curl cream or mousse to your hair, scrunching your hair as you go. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, focusing on the roots.

With these options for the best pixie hairstyles for round faces, you can choose a style that suits your face shape and individual style. Remember to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and the features you want to accentuate when choosing a pixie haircut, and keep your hair healthy and hydrated with these hair care tips. Happy styling!
