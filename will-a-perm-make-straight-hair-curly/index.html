Will A Perm Make Straight Hair Curly

Written by Petter Mar 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and looking for a change? Do you want to know if a perm can make your straight hair curly? Well, you have come to the right place! In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about getting a perm and whether it can make your straight hair curly. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that is used to make hair curly or wavy. The process involves applying a solution to the hair that breaks down the bonds that make your hair straight. Once the bonds are broken down, the hair is wrapped around perm rods and the solution is applied again to set the curl/wave.

Many people wonder if a perm can make straight hair curly. The answer is yes, but there are some things you should know before getting a perm.

How to

If you want to get a perm to make your straight hair curly, you should visit a professional hairstylist. They will be able to assess your hair's texture and recommend the right type of perm for you. There are different types of perms, such as spiral, body, and beachy, and each one will give you a different type of curl/wave.

Step by Step

The process of getting a perm involves several steps:

  1. The hairstylist will shampoo your hair to remove any buildup.
  2. The hairstylist will wrap your hair around perm rods.
  3. The hairstylist will apply the perm solution to your hair.
  4. The hairstylist will rinse your hair with water to remove the perm solution.
  5. The hairstylist will apply a neutralizer to your hair to set the curl/wave.
  6. The hairstylist will rinse your hair again and remove the perm rods.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you want to get a perm:

  • Make sure to visit a professional hairstylist who has experience with perms.
  • Do a patch test before getting a perm to make sure you are not allergic to the chemicals.
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair for at least a week after getting a perm.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

1. Will a perm damage my hair?

A perm can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly. That's why it's important to visit a professional hairstylist who has experience with perms. They will be able to assess your hair's condition and recommend the right type of perm for you.

2. How long will a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 2 to 6 months depending on your hair's texture and the type of perm you get.

3. Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

It's best to wait at least a week before coloring your hair after getting a perm. This will give your hair time to recover from the chemical treatment.

Hair Care

To keep your perm looking its best, you should follow a proper hair care routine. This includes:

  • Using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoiding heat styling tools
  • Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trimming your hair regularly to prevent split ends

So, there you have it! Getting a perm can make your straight hair curly, but it's important to visit a professional hairstylist and follow a proper hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. If you have any more questions about getting a perm, feel free to ask your hairstylist.
