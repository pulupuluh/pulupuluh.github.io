How To Get My Hair Slicked Back A Relaxed Guide

Written by Robby Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a sleek and polished hairstyle that exudes confidence and sophistication? Look no further than the classic slicked back hairstyle. This timeless look works for any hair type and can be achieved with a few simple steps. Whether you're heading to a formal event or simply want to elevate your everyday look, follow these tips for getting your hair slicked back with ease.

How to Get My Hair Slicked Back

To achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle, you'll need a few key products and tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb
  • Hair Gel or Pomade
  • Hairspray
  • Blow Dryer

Once you have your supplies, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The key to a successful slicked back hairstyle is starting with clean, freshly washed hair. This will ensure that your hair is free of any excess oils or product buildup that can weigh it down.

Step 2: Apply Hair Gel or Pomade

Next, apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, making sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a comb, slowly comb your hair back starting from the front of your head. Use a gentle but firm hand to ensure that your hair is perfectly slicked back.

Step 4: Use a Blow Dryer

Once your hair is slicked back, use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to set the style in place. This will also help to dry any excess product and give your hair a sleek and polished finish.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure that your hair stays in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will also add shine and hold to your finished look.

Tips for Getting the Perfect Slicked Back Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade to ensure that your hair stays in place all day.
  • Experiment with different combing techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • If you have long hair, consider tying it back into a low ponytail before slicking it back for a more polished look.
  • Don't overdo it with product. A little goes a long way when it comes to achieving a slicked back hairstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a slicked back hairstyle:

How often should I wash my hair if I want to achieve a slicked back look?

It's recommended that you wash your hair every other day to avoid excess oil buildup. This will help your hair to stay in place and look polished throughout the day.

Can I achieve a slicked back hairstyle with curly hair?

Absolutely! While it may take a little extra effort to get your curls to slick back, it's definitely doable. Use a strong hold hair gel or pomade and work in small sections to ensure that your curls are slicked back evenly.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining a Slicked Back Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair while sporting a slicked back hairstyle:

  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and cause damage over time.
  • Be sure to wash your hair thoroughly to remove any excess product buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With these simple tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a perfect slicked back hairstyle with ease. Whether you're heading to a formal event or simply want to elevate your everyday look, this timeless style is sure to turn heads.
