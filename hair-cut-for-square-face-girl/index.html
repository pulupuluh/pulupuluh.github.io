Hair Cut For Square Face Girl Tips Styles And Care

Are you a girl with a square face shape? Do you want to find the perfect hair cut that flatters your features and enhances your beauty? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best hair cut options for square face girls, provide you with useful tips and tricks, and teach you how to take care of your hair.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the hair cut options, let's first determine whether you have a square face shape. Here are the characteristics of a square face:

  • Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost the same width
  • Straight sides from the temples to the jawline
  • Strong and angular jawline

If you have these features, then you have a square face shape. Now, let's move on to the hair cut options.

Hair Cut Options for Square Face Girl

The key to choosing the right hair cut for square face girls is to soften the angles and create a more oval or round shape. Here are some hair cut options that will help you achieve this:

Long Layers

Long layers add movement and softness to the hair, which can balance out the strong jawline of a square face. The layers should start at the cheekbones and blend into the ends of the hair. This hair cut can be worn straight or wavy.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to shorten the length of the face and create a more oval shape. The bangs should be long and swept to the side, starting from the temples. This hair cut can be paired with long layers for a more voluminous look.

Bob

A bob hair cut can work well for square face girls if it is angled and layered. The bob should be shorter at the back and longer at the front, with layers that start at the chin and blend into the ends. This hair cut can be styled straight or wavy.

Soft Waves

Soft waves can add volume and movement to the hair, which can help to balance out the strong jawline of a square face. The waves should start at the cheekbones and blend into the ends of the hair. This hair cut can be paired with long layers or side-swept bangs for a more flattering look.

Tips and Tricks for Square Face Girl

Here are some tips and tricks that can help you choose the best hair cut and style for your square face:

  • Avoid straight and blunt cuts that emphasize the angles of your face.
  • Opt for soft and rounded styles that create an oval or round shape.
  • Experiment with different hair textures, such as waves or curls, to add volume and movement.
  • Try different parting styles, such as a side part or a deep side part, to create asymmetry and balance out the face.
  • Accessorize with hair clips, headbands, or scarves to draw attention away from the jawline.

FAQ About Hair Cut for Square Face Girl

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair cut for square face girls:

Q: Can square face girls wear short hair?

A: Yes, but the hair cut should be layered and angled to soften the angles of the face. Side-swept bangs can also help to create a more oval shape.

Q: Can square face girls wear bangs?

A: Yes, but the bangs should be side-swept and long, starting from the temples. Blunt bangs can emphasize the angles of the face.

Q: Can square face girls have curly hair?

A: Yes, but the curls should be loose and soft, with layers that start at the cheekbones and blend into the ends. Tight curls can make the face appear wider.

Hair Care for Square Face Girl

Now that you have chosen the perfect hair cut and style, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips for square face girls:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hair cut.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair cut and style for square face girls can be a daunting task, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve a flattering and beautiful look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Happy hair styling!
