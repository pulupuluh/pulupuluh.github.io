Do Goatees Make You Look Thinner

Written by Aprilia Aug 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to slim down your face, you might be wondering if a goatee is the way to go. While facial hair can certainly change the appearance of your face, there's no one-size-fits-all answer to whether or not goatees make you look thinner. Let's take a closer look at the factors that might influence whether or not a goatee can help you achieve a slimmer look.

Description

A goatee is a facial hair style that involves hair on the chin and sometimes the upper lip, but not on the cheeks or sideburns. The size and shape of a goatee can vary depending on personal preference, but it typically involves a small patch of hair on the chin.

How to

If you're interested in trying out a goatee to see if it makes you look thinner, here's how to do it:

  1. Grow out your beard so you have enough hair to work with.
  2. Shave off all the hair on your cheeks and sideburns, leaving only the hair on your chin and upper lip.
  3. Trim your goatee to the desired shape and length.

Step by Step

Step 1: Grow Out Your Beard

In order to create a goatee, you need to have enough hair on your chin to work with. If you already have a beard, you're probably good to go. If not, you'll need to let your beard grow out for a few weeks until you have enough hair to work with.

Step 2: Shave Off Cheeks and Sideburns

Once you have enough hair on your chin, it's time to shave off the hair on your cheeks and sideburns. Use a razor or electric trimmer to remove all the hair on your cheeks and sideburns, leaving only the hair on your chin and upper lip.

Step 3: Trim Your Goatee

Now that you have the basic shape of your goatee, you can trim it to the desired length and shape. Use scissors or a trimmer to carefully shape your goatee, being sure to keep it symmetrical.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying out a goatee to see if it makes you look thinner:

  • Experiment with different shapes and sizes of goatees to find the one that works best for you.
  • Make sure to keep your goatee neatly trimmed and groomed to avoid a scruffy or unprofessional look.
  • Consider pairing your goatee with a short hairstyle to create a streamlined look.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about goatees and whether they can make you look thinner:

Do goatees make your face look longer?

Not necessarily. The shape and size of your goatee can influence the appearance of your face, but whether or not it makes your face look longer depends on the individual.

Can a goatee hide a double chin?

A goatee can help to draw attention away from a double chin, but it won't necessarily hide it entirely.

Do goatees look good on everyone?

Not necessarily. The best way to determine whether or not a goatee looks good on you is to try it out and see how it looks.

Hair Care

If you decide to try out a goatee, it's important to take good care of your facial hair to keep it looking neat and tidy. Here are a few tips for goatee care:

  • Wash your face and goatee regularly to keep it clean and prevent acne.
  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your goatee soft and moisturized.
  • Trim your goatee regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Overall, whether or not a goatee makes you look thinner depends on a variety of factors, including your facial structure, the shape and size of your goatee, and personal style preferences. If you're interested in trying out a goatee, follow the steps outlined above and experiment with different shapes and sizes to find the one that works best for you.
