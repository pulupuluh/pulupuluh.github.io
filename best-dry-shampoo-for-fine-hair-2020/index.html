Best Dry Shampoo For Fine Hair 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank May 18, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have fine hair, you know how challenging it can be to keep it looking fresh and voluminous. Oily roots and flat hair are common problems faced by many. However, with the right dry shampoo, you can easily refresh your hair and add volume in no time. In this blog post, we'll be taking a look at the best dry shampoo for fine hair in 2020.

Description

Dry shampoo is a hair product that allows you to skip washing your hair with water. It helps to absorb oil and sweat from the roots of your hair, leaving it looking and feeling fresh. Dry shampoo comes in various forms, including powders, sprays, and foams. Using the right dry shampoo for your hair type can make a significant difference in how your hair looks and feels.

How to Choose the Best Dry Shampoo for Fine Hair

When choosing the best dry shampoo for fine hair, there are a few things you need to keep in mind:

1. Look for a Lightweight Formula

You don't want a dry shampoo that weighs down your fine hair, so it's essential to look for a lightweight formula. Choose a dry shampoo that is specifically formulated for fine hair.

2. Consider the Ingredients

Some dry shampoos contain ingredients that can be harsh on fine hair, such as alcohol and sulfates. Look for a dry shampoo that is free from these ingredients and contains nourishing ingredients such as rice starch and oatmeal.

3. Choose a Product that Adds Volume

Adding volume to fine hair is crucial, so choose a dry shampoo that is specifically designed to add volume to your hair.

Our Top Picks for the Best Dry Shampoo for Fine Hair 2020

1. Batiste Dry Shampoo

Batiste is a popular dry shampoo brand that is perfect for fine hair. It is lightweight and absorbs oil and sweat from the roots of your hair, leaving it looking and feeling fresh. Batiste dry shampoo also comes in various scents, so you can choose the one that suits your preference.

2. Living Proof Perfect Hair Day Dry Shampoo

Living Proof Perfect Hair Day Dry Shampoo is a lightweight formula that is perfect for fine hair. It absorbs oil and sweat and adds volume to your hair. This dry shampoo is also free from sulfates and parabens, making it gentle on your hair.

3. Klorane Dry Shampoo with Oat Milk

Klorane Dry Shampoo with Oat Milk is a gentle formula that is perfect for fine hair. It contains nourishing ingredients such as oat milk and corn and rice starch, which absorb oil and sweat and add volume to your hair. This dry shampoo is also free from sulfates and parabens, making it ideal for those with sensitive skin.

Step by Step Guide: How to Use Dry Shampoo on Fine Hair

Using dry shampoo is easy, and here's how:

1. Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair, so you can apply dry shampoo to the roots of your hair.

2. Apply the Dry Shampoo

Spray or sprinkle the dry shampoo onto the roots of your hair, focusing on the areas that are oily. Be sure to hold the bottle around six inches away from your hair for an even distribution.

3. Massage the Dry Shampoo into Your Scalp

Using your fingertips, massage the dry shampoo into your scalp. This will help to distribute the product evenly and absorb excess oil and sweat.

4. Brush Your Hair

Using a brush, brush your hair to remove any excess product and to add volume to your hair.

Tips for Using Dry Shampoo on Fine Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your dry shampoo:

1. Use Dry Shampoo Sparingly

A little goes a long way when it comes to dry shampoo. Use it sparingly to avoid weighing down your fine hair.

2. Don't Use Dry Shampoo Too Often

While dry shampoo is a great alternative to washing your hair with water, it's essential not to use it too often. Overuse of dry shampoo can cause product buildup, making your hair look dull and lifeless.

3. Brush Your Hair

Brushing your hair after using dry shampoo helps to distribute the product evenly and adds volume to your hair.

FAQ: Dry Shampoo for Fine Hair

1. Can you use dry shampoo on fine hair?

Yes, you can use dry shampoo on fine hair. However, it's essential to choose a dry shampoo that is specifically formulated for fine hair.

2. How often should you use dry shampoo on fine hair?

You should only use dry shampoo on fine hair when necessary. Overuse of dry shampoo can cause product buildup, making your hair look dull and lifeless.

3. Can dry shampoo damage fine hair?

No, dry shampoo does not damage fine hair. However, it's essential to choose a dry shampoo that is gentle on your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your fine hair looking great:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is specifically formulated for fine hair. Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates and parabens, as these ingredients can be harsh on your hair.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to look dull and lifeless. Try to wash your hair every other day, or every two days if possible.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it's gentler on your hair than a brush.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

5. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims helps to keep your hair healthy and prevents split ends.

Conclusion

Dry shampoo is a great way to refresh your hair and add volume to fine hair. When choosing the best dry shampoo for fine hair, it's essential to look for a lightweight formula that adds volume and contains nourishing ingredients. Use dry shampoo sparingly and avoid overusing it to prevent product buildup. Follow these tips, and you'll be on your way to having healthy, voluminous hair in no time.
