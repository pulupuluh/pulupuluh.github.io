How To Style Curly Hair In The Shower Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jun 28, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. From frizz to tangles, it can be a struggle to achieve the perfect look. However, did you know that you can style your curly hair in the shower? Yes, you read that right! Styling your hair in the shower can make the process easier and more manageable. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to style curly hair in the shower.

How to Style Curly Hair in the Shower

Styling curly hair in the shower is easy, and it can save you time and effort. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair with warm water. Make sure your hair is fully saturated before applying any products.

Step 2: Apply Conditioner

Apply a generous amount of conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to detangle your hair. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots. This will prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

Step 4: Apply Styling Products

Apply your preferred styling products to your hair while it's still wet. Use a light touch to avoid weighing your hair down. Scrunch your hair to encourage your natural curls.

Step 5: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair to encourage your natural curls. This will give your hair volume and texture.

Step 6: Rinse Your Hair

Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water. This will help seal your hair cuticles and reduce frizz.

Step by Step Guide

If you need a more detailed guide on how to style curly hair in the shower, follow these steps:

Step 1: Shampoo Your Hair

Start by shampooing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. Curly hair tends to be dry, so avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair's natural oils.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a generous amount of conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to detangle your hair. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots. This will prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

Step 4: Apply Styling Products

Apply your preferred styling products to your hair while it's still wet. Use a light touch to avoid weighing your hair down. Scrunch your hair to encourage your natural curls.

Step 5: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair to encourage your natural curls. This will give your hair volume and texture.

Step 6: Rinse Your Hair

Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water. This will help seal your hair cuticles and reduce frizz.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your curly hair in the shower:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils.
  • Apply conditioner to your hair before detangling to reduce breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to avoid damage.
  • Scrunch your hair to encourage your natural curls.
  • Rinse your hair with cool water to reduce frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair to reduce frizz.
  • Try different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Experiment with different techniques and methods to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

Can I style my curly hair in the shower?

Yes, you can style your curly hair in the shower. Wetting and styling your hair in the shower can help reduce frizz and make the styling process easier.

What products should I use to style my curly hair in the shower?

You can use a variety of products to style your curly hair in the shower, such as leave-in conditioners, curl creams, and gels. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils. However, if you exercise frequently or have a very active lifestyle, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you take care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, to prevent damage.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair to reduce frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Try different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Experiment with different techniques and methods to find what works best for your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your curly hair in the shower and keep it healthy and beautiful. Remember to experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for your hair. Happy styling!
