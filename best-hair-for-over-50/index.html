Best Hair For Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Feb 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair can become thinner, drier, and more brittle. It can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that flatters our face, works with our lifestyle, and makes us feel confident. In this article, we will discuss the best hair for over 50, including how to choose the right style, step-by-step instructions, and tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Hair for Over 50
  • Step-by-Step Instructions for Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Over 50 Hair

How to Choose the Best Hair for Over 50

The best hair for over 50 is one that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips:

Consider Your Face Shape

Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, whether it is round, square, oval, or heart-shaped. For example, if you have a round face, choose a style that adds height and length to your face, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut. If you have a square face, choose a style that softens your jawline, such as long layers or a side-swept bang.

Work with Your Hair Texture

Choose a hairstyle that works with your hair texture, whether it is straight, wavy, or curly. For example, if you have straight hair, choose a style that adds volume and movement, such as layers or a shaggy cut. If you have curly hair, choose a style that enhances your curls, such as a curly bob or a layered cut.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a hairstyle that works with your lifestyle, whether you are a busy professional, a stay-at-home mom, or a retiree. For example, if you are a busy professional, choose a low-maintenance style that can be styled quickly, such as a sleek bob or a messy bun. If you are a retiree, choose a style that is easy to maintain, such as a short pixie cut or a layered bob.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Styling

Now that you have chosen the best hair for over 50, here are step-by-step instructions for styling:

Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel-dry your hair.

Apply a Heat Protectant

If you plan on using heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or a flat iron, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.

Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.

Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron or a flat iron to style your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve. Finish with a hairspray or a hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and to prevent split ends. Every six to eight weeks is recommended.

Use Gentle Hair Products

Use hair products that are gentle and designed for your hair type. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals, such as sulfates and parabens.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin. These nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen. The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair for over 50:

What is the best haircut for over 50?

The best haircut for over 50 is one that flatters your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Consult with your hairstylist to find the right haircut for you.

What is the best hair color for over 50?

The best hair color for over 50 is one that complements your skin tone and personal style. Consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.

How can I make my hair look thicker?

You can make your hair look thicker by adding layers, using volumizing products, and blow-drying your hair upside down to add volume.

Hair Care Products for Over 50 Hair

Here are some recommended hair care products for over 50 hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner for fine or thinning hair
  • Hair oil or serum for dry hair
  • Hairspray or mousse for volume
  • Heat protectant spray for heat styling tools

Choose products that are gentle and designed for your hair type. Consult with your hairstylist for recommendations.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair for over 50 can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to work with your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, and to maintain healthy hair with regular haircuts, gentle hair products, and a healthy diet.
