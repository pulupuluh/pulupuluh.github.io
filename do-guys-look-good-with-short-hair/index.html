Do Guys Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Christine Mar 19, 2023 · 8 min read

Short hair has always been a popular choice for men, but some guys wonder if it’s the right style for them. In this article, we’ll answer the question, “Do guys look good with short hair?” and provide tips for guys who want to try out this style.

Description

Short hair is a versatile style that can be tailored to suit any face shape or hair type. It’s easy to manage, requires less maintenance, and can be styled in a variety of ways. Short hair can also help to enhance your facial features, giving you a more polished and refined look.

How to

If you’re considering a short haircut, it’s important to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type. Talk to your stylist or barber about the different options available, and ask for their advice on which style would suit you best. Some popular short haircuts for men include the buzz cut, crew cut, and undercut.

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a popular short hairstyle that involves cutting the hair to a uniform length all over the head using clippers. It’s a low-maintenance style that can be easily maintained at home with clippers or scissors. The buzz cut is a good choice for guys who want a minimalist, no-fuss look.

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic short haircut that involves cutting the hair short on top and tapered on the sides and back. It’s a versatile style that can be styled in different ways, and it’s ideal for guys with round or square face shapes. The crew cut can also be styled with a side part or textured for a more modern look.

The Undercut

The undercut is a popular short hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and leaving the hair longer on top. It’s a stylish and versatile look that can be styled in many ways, and it’s ideal for guys with thick, wavy, or curly hair. The undercut can also be styled with a slicked-back or side-swept look for a more polished appearance.

Step by Step

If you’re ready to try out a short haircut, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.
  2. Talk to your stylist or barber about the style you want.
  3. Maintain your haircut regularly to keep it looking sharp.
  4. Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips for guys who want to rock a short haircut:

  • Invest in a good quality pair of clippers or scissors to maintain your haircut at home.
  • Try out different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Consider getting a fade or taper on the sides and back for a more polished look.
  • Experiment with different textures and styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair for guys:

Q: Will short hair suit my face shape?

A: Short hair can suit any face shape, but it’s important to choose a style that complements your features. Talk to your stylist or barber about the best style for your face shape.

Q: How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

A: It’s recommended to get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Q: Can I style my short hair in different ways?

A: Yes, short hair can be styled in many ways, depending on your hair type and the style you choose. Experiment with different textures and products to find the look that suits you best.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than longer hair, but it’s still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a styling product that works for your hair type and the style you want.
  • Get your hair trimmed regularly to maintain its shape and style.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great choice for guys who want a low-maintenance, versatile style. With the right cut and styling products, guys can look great with short hair, no matter what their face shape or hair type. So why not give it a try and see how you look?
