How Do You Know What You Would Look Like With Bangs

Written by Frank Sep 04, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're considering getting bangs, you're probably wondering how you'll look with them. Bangs can be a fun, playful addition to any hairstyle, but they can also be a big commitment. It's important to know what you're getting into before you take the plunge. Fortunately, there are a few ways to get a good idea of what you'll look like with bangs before you make the cut.

Description

Bangs are a hairstyle where the hair is cut short and worn over the forehead. They can be straight, choppy, or layered, and they can be styled in many different ways. Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some interest to your hairstyle. However, they can also be high maintenance, requiring frequent trims and styling.

How to Know What You Would Look Like With Bangs

1. Use a Virtual Makeover Tool

One of the easiest ways to see what you'll look like with bangs is to use a virtual makeover tool. These tools allow you to upload a photo of yourself and then try on different hairstyles, including bangs. You can adjust the length and style of the bangs to see what looks best on you. Some popular virtual makeover tools include Taaz and InStyle Makeover.

2. Try Clip-In Bangs

If you're not ready to commit to a full set of bangs, you can try clip-in bangs. These are extensions that clip onto your hairline and give the illusion of bangs. They come in a variety of styles and colors, so you can find a set that matches your hair perfectly. Clip-in bangs are a great way to test out the look without committing to a permanent cut.

3. Look at Photos

Another way to get an idea of what you'll look like with bangs is to look at photos of people with similar face shapes and hair types. You can search for photos on social media or look at celebrity photos for inspiration. This can give you a good idea of how bangs will look on you and what styles you might like.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you determine if bangs are right for you:

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

Bangs can look great on any face shape, but some styles may be more flattering than others. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to go for longer, side-swept bangs to help elongate your face. If you have a square face, you may want to try choppy, textured bangs to soften your features.

Step 2: Determine Your Hair Type

Bangs can work with any hair type, but some styles may be easier to manage than others. If you have thin hair, for example, you may want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can make your hair look even thinner. If you have curly hair, you may want to opt for longer, side-swept bangs that can blend in with your curls.

Step 3: Try On Virtual Bangs

Once you have an idea of what styles might work for you, try on virtual bangs using a virtual makeover tool. This can help you see what different styles will look like on your face and give you an idea of what you like.

Step 4: Test Out Clip-In Bangs

If you're still unsure, try clip-in bangs to see if you like the look. This can help you see how bangs will look on you without committing to a permanent cut.

Step 5: Get a Professional Opinion

Finally, if you're still not sure, talk to your stylist. They can give you their professional opinion on what styles will work best for your face shape and hair type. They can also help you determine if bangs are a good fit for your lifestyle and maintenance routine.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs

If you do decide to get bangs, there are a few tips to help you keep them looking great:

  • Trim them regularly to keep them from getting too long or falling in your eyes.
  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to style them.
  • Avoid touching them too much, as oils from your hands can make them look greasy.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep them looking fresh between washes.

FAQ

Can bangs make my face look slimmer?

Yes, bangs can help elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Side-swept bangs that fall diagonally across your forehead are particularly flattering.

What bangs are best for curly hair?

Longer, side-swept bangs that blend in with your curls are best for curly hair. Avoid blunt bangs that can make your hair look too heavy.

How often do I need to trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.

Hair Care

Bangs require a little extra care to keep them looking great. Here are a few tips for keeping your bangs healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days to keep them clean and fresh.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner on your bangs to keep them soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your bangs too often, as this can damage them.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot styling tools.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo on your bangs between washes to keep them looking fresh.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently decide if bangs are right for you and keep them looking great once you get them.
