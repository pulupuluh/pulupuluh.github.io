Prom Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Simple A Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Mar 15, 2023 · 7 min read

Prom season is just around the corner and it’s time to start thinking about your hairstyle. If you have medium length hair, you might be wondering what styles will work best for you. Don't worry! In this post, we will show you some simple prom hairstyles for medium length hair that you can do yourself or with the help of a friend. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Prom Hairstyle for Medium Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Simple Prom Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Prom Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Prom Season

How to Choose the Right Prom Hairstyle for Medium Length Hair

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's important to consider your personal style and the look you want to achieve. Are you going for a classic, elegant look or something more modern and edgy? Do you want your hair up or down? These are all things to consider when choosing your prom hairstyle.

Another factor to consider is your dress. If you have a high neckline or intricate back detailing, an updo may be the best option to showcase your dress. If your dress is more simple, a half-up, half-down style may be the perfect complement.

Related Keywords: prom hairstyles, medium length hair, simple, elegant, modern, updo, half-up, half-down, dress

Step-by-Step Guide to Simple Prom Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Now that you have an idea of what you want, let’s explore some simple prom hairstyles for medium length hair. These styles can be done at home with minimal products and tools.

The Low Bun:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle and brushing it out.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a low bun.
  3. Secure with bobby pins and spritz with hairspray to hold in place.

The Half-Up, Half-Down:

  1. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron or wand.
  2. Take the top half of your hair and pull it back, securing with a clear elastic band.
  3. Optional: tease the crown of your head for added volume.

The Messy Bun:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure with bobby pins and pull out a few strands for a messy, effortless look.

Related Keywords: low bun, half-up, half-down, messy bun, curling iron, wand, clear elastic band, volume, ponytail, effortless

Tips for Maintaining Your Prom Hairstyle

Once you have your perfect prom hairstyle, you want it to last all night long. Here are some tips for maintaining your style:

  • Use a strong hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Bring extra bobby pins and hair ties in case of emergency.
  • Try not to touch or play with your hair too much throughout the night.

Related Keywords: strong hold, hairspray, bobby pins, hair ties, emergency, touch, play

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about prom hairstyles for medium length hair:

Q: Can I do these hairstyles on my own?

A: Yes! These hairstyles are simple and can be done with minimal products and tools.

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle will work with my dress?

A: Consider the neckline and back detailing of your dress. If it’s high or intricate, an updo may be the best option. If it’s more simple, a half-up, half-down style may be the perfect complement.

Related Keywords: frequently asked questions, prom hairstyles, medium length hair, simple, products, tools, neckline, back detailing, updo, half-up, half-down, complement

Hair Care Tips for Prom Season

Prom season can be tough on your hair, so it’s important to take care of it leading up to the big night. Here are some tips:

  • Get a trim a few weeks before prom to get rid of split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair leading up to prom.

Related Keywords: hair care, prom season, tough, leading up, big night, trim, split ends, deep conditioning treatment, healthy, heat

With these simple prom hairstyles for medium length hair and hair care tips, you’re sure to have a great hair day on prom night. Just remember to have fun and enjoy the moment!
