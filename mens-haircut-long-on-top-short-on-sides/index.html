Description

The mens haircut long on top short on sides, also known as the undercut, has become one of the most popular styles for men in recent years. This style features longer hair on the top of the head, which can be styled in a variety of ways, and shorter hair on the sides and back.

The mens haircut long on top short on sides is a versatile style that can work for many different hair types, face shapes, and personal styles. It can be worn with a slicked-back look, a messy or textured style, or even with a pompadour or quiff.

If you're considering getting a mens haircut long on top short on sides, this guide will provide you with all the information you need to know to get the style you want and keep it looking great.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides

If you're interested in getting a mens haircut long on top short on sides, the first step is to find a barber or stylist who is experienced in this style. Look for someone who has experience cutting men's hair and who can show you examples of their work.

Before your appointment, it's a good idea to have an idea of the style you want. You can bring in pictures or examples to show your barber or stylist, or you can ask for their recommendations based on your hair type and face shape.

Once you're in the chair, your barber or stylist will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair short, usually with clippers. They will then blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back, creating a seamless transition.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a mens haircut long on top short on sides:

  1. Find a barber or stylist who is experienced in this style
  2. Bring in pictures or examples of the style you want
  3. Sit in the chair and let your barber or stylist cut the sides and back of your hair short with clippers
  4. Have them blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back
  5. Style your hair as desired

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides

Once you have your mens haircut long on top short on sides, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax for a polished look
  • Experiment with different styles to find what looks best on you

FAQs About Mens Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides

Q: Will a mens haircut long on top short on sides work for all hair types?

A: While this style can work for many different hair types, it may not be the best option for those with very curly or thick hair. Talk to your barber or stylist to see if this style is right for you.

Q: How do I style my hair with a mens haircut long on top short on sides?

A: There are many different ways to style this cut, depending on your personal style and the look you're going for. Try using a pomade or wax to create a sleek, polished look, or use a texturizing product for a messy or tousled style.

Hair Care for Mens Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides

To keep your mens haircut long on top short on sides looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and visit your barber or stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you and your hair type.
