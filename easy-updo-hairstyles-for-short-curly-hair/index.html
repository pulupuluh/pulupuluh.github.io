Easy Updo Hairstyles For Short Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Mar 23, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have short curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find hairstyles that work for you. But fear not! In this article, we’ll show you some easy updo hairstyles that you can do at home, without the need for a hairstylist. Whether you’re going to a wedding or a night out with friends, these hairstyles will make you look and feel fabulous.

Description

Short curly hair is beautiful, but it can be difficult to manage. Updos are a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still looking elegant. These hairstyles are perfect for any occasion and can be done quickly and easily at home.

How To

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz. Now, let’s get started!

1. Twisted Updo

Start by parting your hair down the middle. Take a small section of hair from one side and twist it. Pin it to the back of your head with a bobby pin. Repeat on the other side. Continue twisting and pinning until all your hair is up. You can leave some curls loose at the front for a softer look.

2. Braided Updo

Start by parting your hair on one side. French braid the larger section of hair, starting at the crown of your head and working your way down. Secure the braid with a clear elastic band. Take the smaller section of hair and twist it, then wrap it around the base of the braid. Pin it in place with bobby pins.

3. Messy Bun

Gather your hair into a high ponytail. Twist your hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail, forming a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins. Pull out some strands of hair around your face for a messy, effortless look.

Step by Step

1. Twisted Updo

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side and twist it.
  3. Pin it to the back of your head with a bobby pin.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Continue twisting and pinning until all your hair is up.
  6. Leave some curls loose at the front for a softer look.

2. Braided Updo

  1. Part your hair on one side.
  2. French braid the larger section of hair, starting at the crown of your head and working your way down.
  3. Secure the braid with a clear elastic band.
  4. Take the smaller section of hair and twist it.
  5. Wrap it around the base of the braid.
  6. Pin it in place with bobby pins.

3. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail, forming a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some strands of hair around your face for a messy, effortless look.

Tips

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Don’t worry about making your updo look perfect. Messy and imperfect is in style.
  • Accessorize your updo with a hair clip or headband for a more glamorous look.
  • Don’t forget to use a hairspray to keep your updo in place.

FAQ

What if my hair is too short for these updos?

Even if your hair is short, you can still try these updos. Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place and add some hair extensions if you want more length.

Can I do these updos on wet hair?

No, it’s best to do these updos on dry hair to make sure they hold their shape.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hair healthy and shiny, make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and let your hair air dry whenever possible. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to do these easy updo hairstyles for short curly hair, it’s time to try them out for yourself. Have fun experimenting with different styles and don’t be afraid to add your own personal touch!
