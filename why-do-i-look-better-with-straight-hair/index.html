Why Do I Look Better With Straight Hair

Written by Eveline May 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're someone who has curly or wavy hair, you might have noticed that when you straighten it, you look better. It's a common phenomenon that has puzzled many people over the years. But there's a scientific reason behind it. In this article, we'll explore why straight hair might make you look better and provide tips on how to achieve straight hair.

Description

Straight hair has always been considered the most desirable hair type. Many people believe that straight hair looks more professional, sleek, and polished. It's easier to manage and style, and it's less likely to get tangled or frizzy. On the other hand, curly or wavy hair can sometimes look messy and uncontrolled. It can be challenging to style and can require a lot of maintenance.

As a result, many people with curly or wavy hair turn to straightening methods to achieve a more polished look. Straightening can be done using a flat iron, chemical relaxers, or keratin treatments. But why does straight hair look better?

How to Achieve Straight Hair

If you want to achieve straight hair, there are several methods you can try:

Flat Iron

A flat iron is a tool that uses heat to straighten hair. To use a flat iron, you should start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. Then, section your hair and run the flat iron over each section, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Chemical Relaxers

Chemical relaxers are products that break down the natural bonds in your hair, making it easier to straighten. You should only use chemical relaxers on healthy hair, and it's essential to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damage.

Keratin Treatments

Keratin treatments are a type of hair smoothing treatment that uses keratin protein to fill in gaps in the hair shaft. The treatment can last up to six months and can make hair more manageable and shiny.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve straight hair:

Step 1: Wash Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Then, apply a deep conditioner to help soften and detangle your hair.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 3: Dry Hair

Dry your hair using a blow dryer until it's about 80% dry. You can use a round brush to help straighten your hair as you dry it.

Step 4: Section Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties.

Step 5: Straighten Hair

Starting at the roots, use a flat iron to straighten each section of hair. Be sure to work in small sections to ensure that each strand is straightened.

Step 6: Apply Finishing Products

Apply a finishing serum or spray to add shine and control flyaways.

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Once you've achieved straight hair, you'll want to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

Use a Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a satin pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage, as it's gentler on your hair than cotton.

Avoid Moisture

Avoid exposing your hair to moisture, as it can cause it to revert back to its natural texture. If you must go out in the rain, wear a hat or use an umbrella.

Avoid Heat

Avoid exposing your hair to heat, as it can cause damage and breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and don't use them every day.

FAQs

Q: Will straightening my hair damage it?

A: Straightening your hair can cause damage if it's not done correctly. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and don't use heat styling tools every day.

Q: What's the best way to straighten curly hair?

A: The best way to straighten curly hair is to use a flat iron or chemical relaxer. Keratin treatments can also be effective.

Hair Care

No matter what type of hair you have, it's essential to take care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Be sure to wash your hair regularly, use a deep conditioner, and avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools. And if you want to achieve straight hair, be sure to follow our tips and methods to achieve a sleek and polished look.
