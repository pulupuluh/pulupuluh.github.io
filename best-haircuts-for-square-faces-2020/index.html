Best Haircuts For Square Faces 2020

Written by William Jun 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a square face, you may be wondering what the best haircuts are for your face shape. In 2020, there were a few standout haircuts that were perfect for square faces. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for square faces, how to get them, and tips for maintaining your new cut.

Description

A square face has a strong jawline and forehead, with a width and length that are almost equal. The best haircuts for a square face will soften the angular features and create balance. Ideally, you want a haircut that will elongate the face and draw attention away from the jawline.

How to

The first step in finding the best haircut for your square face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your measurements are similar, you most likely have a square face.

Next, you should consider your hair type, texture, and lifestyle. Do you have thick or thin hair? Do you prefer a low-maintenance style or are you willing to spend time styling your hair each day?

Once you have a good idea of what you are looking for, you can talk to your hairstylist about the best haircuts for your face shape. They may recommend a bob, long layers, or an asymmetrical cut to balance your features and elongate your face.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

2. Consider your hair type, texture, and lifestyle.

3. Talk to your hairstylist about the best haircuts for your face shape.

4. Choose a haircut that will soften your angular features and create balance.

5. Ask your hairstylist for tips on maintaining your new cut.

Tips

- A side part can help to elongate your face.

- Soft layers can add movement and dimension to your hair.

- Bangs can be flattering for square faces, but they should be long and wispy rather than blunt and straight.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

- Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and movement.

FAQ

Q: What kind of bob is best for square faces?

A: A long bob (or "lob") is a great option for square faces. It should hit right around your collarbone and be slightly longer in the front than in the back.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a square face?

A: It's not recommended to wear your hair in a middle part if you have a square face, as it can make your face appear even more angular. Instead, try a side part to create balance.

Hair Care

Regardless of the haircut you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and protect it from the sun and chlorine if you are swimming. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair looking its best.

In conclusion, the best haircuts for square faces in 2020 were those that softened angular features and created balance. By following these tips and working with your hairstylist, you can find the perfect haircut for your face shape and rock it with confidence!
