How To Self Hair Cut With Scissors

Written by Eveline Jul 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Are you ready to take matters into your own hands and learn how to self hair cut with scissors? In this article, we will give you the information you need to give yourself a great haircut. We will cover everything from how to prepare for the haircut, to what tools you need, to how to cut your hair step by step. We will also provide you with tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

  • How to Self Hair Cut with Scissors
  • Preparation
  • Tools

  • FAQ

Preparation

Before you begin cutting your hair, you need to prepare yourself and your workspace. Make sure that you have plenty of light and a clean area to work in. You should also wear clothing that you don't mind getting hair on or use a hair cutting cape to protect your clothes. Additionally, it's a good idea to have a mirror in front of you and behind you so that you can see all angles of your head.

Tools

When it comes to tools, you don't need anything fancy. All you need is a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips. It's important to use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut. Dull scissors can damage your hair and lead to split ends. You can purchase a pair of hair cutting scissors at your local beauty supply store or online.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you're prepared and have the right tools, it's time to start cutting your hair. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will remove any product buildup and make it easier to cut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section your hair into manageable parts. Clip the sections you're not working on out of the way.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting with the bottom layer, use your comb to pull the hair straight down and trim the ends with your scissors. Be sure to cut straight across and not at an angle. Continue to work your way up the layers until you have cut all of your hair.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting your hair, comb through it and check for evenness. If you notice any uneven sections, trim them until they are even.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Use a comb to pull the hair straight down before cutting. This will ensure that you cut the hair evenly.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so it's okay to take breaks and come back to it later.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

While it's possible to cut your hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Regular scissors can be dull and damage your hair, leading to split ends.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. It's just hair and it will grow back. You can either leave it and wait for it to grow out or try to fix it by trimming the section again.

Hair Care

After you have cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant before styling with heat tools, and getting regular trims to maintain healthy hair.

By following these tips and steps, you can successfully learn how to self hair cut with scissors. With practice, you can save money and maintain a great hairstyle without ever leaving your home.
