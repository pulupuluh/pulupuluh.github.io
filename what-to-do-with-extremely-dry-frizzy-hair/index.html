What To Do With Extremely Dry Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jul 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Dry, frizzy hair can be a nightmare to manage, but it doesn't have to be. With the right hair care routine and products, you can tame even the most unruly locks. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about what to do with extremely dry frizzy hair, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Manage Dry Frizzy Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Treating Dry Frizzy Hair
  3. Tips for Managing Dry Frizzy Hair
  4. FAQs about Dry Frizzy Hair
  5. Hair Care Products for Dry Frizzy Hair

How to Manage Dry Frizzy Hair

First, it's important to understand what causes dry, frizzy hair. Some common causes include:

  • Over-washing your hair
  • Using hot tools too often
  • Exposure to harsh chemicals and weather
  • Using the wrong hair care products

To manage your dry, frizzy hair, you'll need to make some changes to your hair care routine. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Less Often

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Try washing your hair every other day or every three days instead of every day.

2. Use a Hydrating Shampoo and Conditioner

Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically formulated for dry, frizzy hair. These products will contain hydrating ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to give your hair a boost of hydration. Apply the treatment to your hair and leave it on for at least 10 minutes before rinsing it out.

4. Avoid Hot Tools

Hot tools like straighteners and curling irons can damage your hair and make it even more prone to dryness and frizz. Try to avoid using them as much as possible, or use a heat protectant spray before using them.

5. Protect Your Hair from the Elements

Exposure to harsh weather like wind, sun, and cold temperatures can make your hair even drier and more frizzy. Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair when you're outside, and use a leave-in conditioner with UV protection.

Step-by-Step Guide to Treating Dry Frizzy Hair

Follow these steps to give your dry, frizzy hair the TLC it needs:

Step 1: Shampoo Your Hair

Wet your hair and apply a hydrating shampoo. Massage it into your scalp and hair, then rinse it out thoroughly.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a hydrating conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave it on for a few minutes, then rinse it out.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to provide extra hydration and protection. Comb it through your hair to distribute it evenly.

Step 4: Use a Hair Oil

Apply a hair oil to your hair to smooth down any frizz and add shine. Use a small amount and focus on the ends of your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

If you need to style your hair, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle it. Avoid using hot tools if possible.

Tips for Managing Dry Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing dry, frizzy hair:

  • Don't brush your hair when it's wet, as it's more prone to breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair even more.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

FAQs about Dry Frizzy Hair

Q: Can I use a hair mask instead of a deep conditioning treatment?

A: Yes, a hair mask can be used instead of a deep conditioning treatment. Just make sure to choose one that is formulated for dry, frizzy hair.

Q: How often should I use a deep conditioning treatment?

A: It's recommended to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Q: Can I use a regular shampoo and conditioner on my dry, frizzy hair?

A: It's best to use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for dry, frizzy hair. Regular shampoos and conditioners may strip your hair of its natural oils, making it even drier.

Hair Care Products for Dry Frizzy Hair

Here are some hair care products that are great for managing dry, frizzy hair:

  • Hydrating shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Hair oil
  • Heat protectant spray

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can manage even the driest, frizziest hair. Don't let unruly locks get in the way of your confidence and style!
