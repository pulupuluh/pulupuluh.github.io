What Size Curling Wand For Beach Waves A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to achieve that effortless, beachy wave look, a curling wand is a must-have tool in your hair styling kit. However, choosing the right size curling wand can be a bit overwhelming as there are so many options available in the market. In this blog post, we'll help you determine what size curling wand for beach waves is perfect for your hair type and length.

How To Choose The Right Size Curling Wand For Beach Waves

When choosing a curling wand for beach waves, the size of the barrel is the most important factor. The diameter of the barrel will determine the size of the curls or waves. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

Short Hair

If you have short hair, you should opt for a curling wand with a smaller barrel size of around 1 inch. This will give you tighter and more defined curls that will help add volume to your hair.

Medium Hair

For medium hair, a curling wand with a barrel size between 1 and 1.5 inches is ideal. This size will give you loose, beachy waves that look natural and effortless.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, you can go for a larger barrel size of 1.5 to 2 inches. This will give you soft, flowing waves that will look stunning and stylish.

Step By Step Guide to Achieve Beach Waves

Now that you know what size curling wand for beach waves you should choose, let's move on to the steps on how to achieve this look:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat. You can also use a texturizing spray to add some grip to your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling wand. Hold it for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until all your hair is curled.

Step 4: Finish The Look

Once you've curled all your hair, run your fingers through it to loosen the curls and create a more natural look. Apply a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips For Achieving Perfect Beach Waves

Here are some tips to make sure you achieve perfect beach waves every time:

  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. Looser curls will look more natural and beachy.
  • Alternate the direction of the curls to create a more natural look.
  • Use a lower heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a texturizing spray to create more grip and hold.
  • Use a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

FAQs

Q: Can I Achieve Beach Waves Without a Curling Wand?

A: Yes, you can achieve beach waves without a curling wand. You can use a straightening iron or a hair waver to create waves in your hair.

Q: How Long Will My Beach Waves Last?

A: Your beach waves can last up to 2-3 days with the right technique and products. Make sure to use a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Hair Care Tips

While styling your hair is fun, it's also important to take care of it. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling.
  • Don't wash your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as this can weigh down your hair.

Now that you know what size curling wand for beach waves to choose, it's time to get styling! Remember to follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect beachy waves every time.
