How To Find Out What Hairstyle Suits You For Guys

Written by William Jun 02, 2023 Â· 10 min read

One of the most important things for a guy's appearance is his hairstyle. A good hairstyle can make you look stylish, confident, and attractive. However, not every hairstyle suits every guy. To find out what hairstyle suits you, you need to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we will give you some tips on how to find out what hairstyle suits you for guys.

Table of Contents

  • Face Shape
  • Hair Type
  • Personal Style
  • Haircuts
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Face Shape

The first thing that you need to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your face shape. Your face shape will determine which hairstyles will suit you best. There are four main face shapes: round, square, oval, and heart-shaped.

If you have a round face, you should go for hairstyles that add height and length to your face. This will help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as fringes or bangs.

If you have a square face, you should go for hairstyles that soften your features. Soft, layered haircuts that add volume to the top of your head will help to balance out your angular jawline. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they will accentuate your square jawline.

If you have an oval face, you are lucky, as most hairstyles will suit you. However, you should avoid hairstyles that cover your face, as they will hide your best features.

If you have a heart-shaped face, you should go for hairstyles that balance out your forehead and chin. Hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and taper at the sides will help to achieve this. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they will accentuate your chin.

Hair Type

The second thing that you need to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Your hair type will determine which hairstyles will be easy to maintain and which ones will require more effort. There are three main hair types: straight, wavy, and curly.

If you have straight hair, you should go for hairstyles that add volume and texture. Textured, layered haircuts will help to give your hair more body and movement. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too slicked back, as they will make your hair look lifeless.

If you have wavy hair, you should go for hairstyles that enhance your natural waves. Medium-length haircuts that are layered and textured will help to bring out your waves. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they will make your waves look messy.

If you have curly hair, you should go for hairstyles that keep your curls under control. Short, layered haircuts that are tapered at the sides will help to define your curls. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too flat, as they will make your curls look frizzy.

Personal Style

The third thing that you need to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your personal style. Your personal style will determine which hairstyles will suit your lifestyle and personality. There are three main personal styles: casual, formal, and edgy.

If you have a casual style, you should go for hairstyles that are easy to maintain and versatile. Medium-length haircuts that are layered and textured will help to achieve this. Avoid hairstyles that are too formal or too edgy, as they will not suit your laid-back style.

If you have a formal style, you should go for hairstyles that are polished and sophisticated. Short, classic haircuts that are well-groomed will help to achieve this. Avoid hairstyles that are too messy or too edgy, as they will not suit your professional image.

If you have an edgy style, you should go for hairstyles that are bold and daring. Short, textured haircuts that are asymmetrical will help to achieve this. Avoid hairstyles that are too plain or too formal, as they will not suit your rebellious spirit.

Haircuts

Now that you know which hairstyles will suit you best, it's time to choose a haircut. There are many different haircuts to choose from, but some of the most popular ones for guys are:

  • Buzz Cut
  • Crew Cut
  • Short Pompadour
  • Side Part
  • Taper Fade
  • Textured Crop
  • Undercut

Each of these haircuts has its own unique style and can be customized to suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style. To find the right haircut for you, consult with your hairstylist or barber and ask for their advice.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you find the right hairstyle:

  • Research hairstyles online and in magazines to get inspiration.
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles that you like to your hairstylist or barber.
  • Ask for their advice on which hairstyles will suit you best.
  • Try different hairstyles before committing to one.
  • Invest in quality hair products to maintain your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the right hairstyle:

What if I don't know my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring the width and length of your face and comparing them. Alternatively, you can ask your hairstylist or barber to help you determine your face shape.

What if I have thinning hair?

You can still find a hairstyle that suits you if you have thinning hair. Short, textured haircuts that are layered and styled with volume will help to create the illusion of thicker hair.

What if I have receding hairline?

You can still find a hairstyle that suits you if you have a receding hairline. Short, slicked-back haircuts that are tapered at the sides will help to frame your face and draw attention away from your hairline.

Hair Care

Once you have found the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after washing to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as it can damage your hair.
  • Invest in quality hair products, such as styling gel, hairspray, and pomade.
  • Visit your hairstylist or barber regularly to maintain your haircut.

By following these tips, you can find the right hairstyle that suits you and maintain it for a stylish and confident look.
