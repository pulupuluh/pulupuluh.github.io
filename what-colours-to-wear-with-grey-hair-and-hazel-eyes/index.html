What Colours To Wear With Grey Hair And Hazel Eyes

Written by Christine Jul 04, 2023 Â· 10 min read

As we age, our hair naturally turns grey or white. While this may be a sign of wisdom and maturity, it can also be a challenge when it comes to choosing the right colours to wear. If you have grey hair and hazel eyes, you may be wondering which colours will flatter your complexion and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we'll explore some tips and tricks for choosing the best colours to wear with grey hair and hazel eyes.

Why Do Colours Matter?

Colours play a crucial role in enhancing our natural beauty and making us look our best. When we wear the right colours, they can make our skin look brighter, our eyes more vibrant, and our hair more lustrous. On the other hand, wearing the wrong colours can make us look tired, washed out, or even sickly. That's why it's essential to choose colours that complement our skin tone, hair colour, and eye colour.

How to Choose the Right Colours

Choosing the right colours can be a bit of a challenge, especially if you're not familiar with colour theory. However, there are some general guidelines that can help you make the right choices. Firstly, you should consider your skin tone. Are you warm-toned or cool-toned? If you have warm undertones, you'll look best in colours that have a yellow or orange base. If you have cool undertones, you'll look best in colours that have a blue or purple base. Secondly, you should consider your hair colour and eye colour. These factors will also influence which colours will look best on you.

Best Colours to Wear with Grey Hair and Hazel Eyes

If you have grey hair and hazel eyes, you're in luck! There are many colours that will flatter your complexion and enhance your natural beauty. Here are some of the best colours to wear:

Neutral Colours

Neutral colours like black, white, grey, and beige are always a safe bet. They complement any skin tone and hair colour, and they're easy to mix and match. If you're not sure which colours to wear, you can't go wrong with neutrals.

Jewel Tones

Jewel tones like emerald green, sapphire blue, and ruby red are perfect for hazel eyes. They bring out the green and gold flecks in your eyes and make them look more vibrant. These colours also look great with grey hair, as they add a pop of colour without being too overwhelming.

Earth Tones

Earth tones like olive green, rust, and burnt orange are perfect for warm-toned skin and hazel eyes. They complement the natural colours in your complexion and bring out the gold flecks in your eyes. These colours also look great with grey hair, as they add warmth and depth to your overall look.

Pastel Colours

Pastel colours like lavender, baby blue, and pink are perfect for cool-toned skin and hazel eyes. They add a soft, feminine touch to your look and bring out the blue and green flecks in your eyes. These colours also look great with grey hair, as they add a subtle pop of colour without being too bright.

Tips for Wearing Colours with Grey Hair and Hazel Eyes

Now that you know which colours to wear, here are some tips for wearing them:

- Don't be afraid to mix and match colours. You can create a beautiful, cohesive look by combining different colours and textures. Just make sure that the colours complement each other and don't clash.

- Use accessories to add pops of colour. If you're not comfortable wearing bright colours, you can still add them to your outfit through accessories like scarves, jewellery, and shoes.

- Experiment with different shades of the same colour. For example, if you love blue, try wearing different shades of blue like navy, sky blue, and baby blue. This will add depth and interest to your outfit.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear black with grey hair?

A: Yes, black is a classic colour that looks great with grey hair. Just make sure to wear black in a way that complements your skin tone and eye colour.

Q: Can I wear bright colours with grey hair?

A: Yes, you can wear bright colours with grey hair, but make sure to choose colours that complement your skin tone and eye colour. You don't want the colours to clash or overwhelm your overall look.

Q: Can I wear white with grey hair?

A: Yes, white is a classic colour that looks great with grey hair. Just make sure to wear white in a way that complements your skin tone and eye colour.

Hair Care Tips for Grey Hair

If you have grey hair, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your grey hair looking healthy and vibrant:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

- Use a purple shampoo to prevent your hair from turning yellow or brassy.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and soft.

- Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

- Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and choosing the right colours, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and stylish with grey hair and hazel eyes.
