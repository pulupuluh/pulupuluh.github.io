Why Does My Hair Not Look Curly After A Perm

Written by Bardi Apr 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you frustrated with your hair not looking as curly as you hoped after getting a perm? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people experience this issue and it can be due to a variety of factors. In this article, we'll explore why your hair may not look curly after a perm and offer tips on how to achieve the desired look.

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that alters the structure of your hair to create curls or waves. While perms can be a great way to add texture and volume to your hair, they don't always turn out as planned. If your hair doesn't look as curly as you hoped after a perm, there are a few possible reasons why.

How to

The first step to achieving curly hair after a perm is to make sure you're taking care of your hair properly. This means using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a deep conditioning treatment regularly. Additionally, you may want to consider using a curl-enhancing product to help define your curls.

Step by Step

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
2. Avoid heat styling tools
3. Use a deep conditioning treatment
4. Consider using a curl-enhancing product

Tips

When getting a perm, it's important to communicate with your stylist about the type of curls you want. This can help ensure that the perm solution is applied correctly and that the curls turn out the way you hoped. Additionally, it's important to wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair after a perm to allow the curls to set.

FAQ

Q: Can I fix a bad perm?
A: It's possible to fix a bad perm, but it may require cutting off the damaged hair. It's best to consult with a professional stylist for advice.

Q: How often should I get a perm?
A: It's recommended to wait at least 6-8 weeks between perms to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for achieving and maintaining curly hair after a perm. Be sure to use products specifically designed for curly hair, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Additionally, it's important to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, if your hair isn't looking as curly as you hoped after a perm, there are steps you can take to achieve the desired look. By taking care of your hair properly and using the right products, you can enhance your curls and enjoy your new hairstyle.
