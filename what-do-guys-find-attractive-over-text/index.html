Texting has become a common way of communication in today's world. It's not uncommon for people to start a relationship through text messages. However, it's important to know what guys find attractive over text to keep them interested. Description

Texting can be a great way to get to know someone, especially when you're unable to meet them in person. However, it's important to know what guys find attractive over text to keep them interested. Guys are attracted to women who are confident, interesting, and have a sense of humor. They also like women who are easy to talk to and can keep a conversation going. In this article, we will discuss how to attract guys over text, step-by-step guide to attract guys over text, tips to keep guys interested over text, and some FAQs.

How to Attract Guys Over Text

To attract guys over text, you need to be confident and interesting. You should also have a sense of humor and be easy to talk to. Here are some tips to attract guys over text:

  • Be confident: Guys are attracted to confident women. Show them that you're confident in yourself and your abilities.
  • Be interesting: Ask them questions about themselves and show a genuine interest in their lives.
  • Have a sense of humor: Make them laugh and show them that you have a sense of humor.
  • Be easy to talk to: Respond to their messages in a timely manner and keep the conversation going.

Step by Step Guide to Attract Guys Over Text

Here's a step-by-step guide to attract guys over text:

  1. Start the conversation with a simple greeting like "Hey" or "Hi."
  2. Ask them how their day is going and show a genuine interest in their response.
  3. Ask them questions about themselves and their interests.
  4. Share some interesting facts or stories about yourself.
  5. Use emojis to add some personality to your messages.
  6. Flirt with them by complimenting them or making playful jokes.
  7. Suggest meeting up in person if the conversation is going well.

Tips to Keep Guys Interested Over Text

Here are some tips to keep guys interested over text:

  • Be yourself: Don't try to be someone you're not. Guys appreciate authenticity.
  • Don't play games: Be honest and straightforward in your messages.
  • Keep the conversation interesting: Ask open-ended questions and share interesting stories about yourself.
  • Don't be too clingy: Give them space and don't bombard them with messages.
  • Be flirty: Add some flirtatious comments or emojis to keep things interesting.

FAQs

What do guys find attractive in girls over text?

Guys find girls attractive when they are confident, interesting, and have a sense of humor. They also like girls who are easy to talk to and can keep a conversation going.

To keep a guy interested over text, be yourself, keep the conversation interesting, and don't be too clingy. Add some flirtatious comments or emojis to keep things interesting.

How do I flirt with a guy over text?

Flirt with a guy over text by complimenting him, making playful jokes, and using flirtatious emojis.

Hair Care and Attraction

Did you know that your hair can affect your attraction level? Guys are attracted to women with healthy, shiny hair. It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you want to add some extra shine, try using a hair serum or oil. Taking care of your hair not only makes you look more attractive, but it also boosts your confidence.

In conclusion, knowing what guys find attractive over text can help you keep them interested and start a successful relationship. Be confident, interesting, and have a sense of humor. Use emojis to add some personality to your messages and keep things flirty. Remember to be yourself, keep the conversation interesting, and take care of your hair to boost your confidence and attraction level.
