Best Short Haircuts For Curly Hair 2020 A Complete Guide

As a curly-haired person, it can be challenging to find the perfect short haircut that suits your hair type. But worry not, because we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you through the best short haircuts for curly hair in 2020. So, whether you have tight coils or loose waves, we have something for everyone.

Description

Curly hair is beautiful, but it can be challenging to manage. Short haircuts are an excellent way to minimize the amount of time you spend styling your hair. However, not every short haircut works best for curly hair. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for curly hair in 2020.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Curly Hair

When choosing the best short haircut for your curly hair, you need to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect short haircut for your curly hair:

  • Consider your face shape: Some short haircuts look better on certain face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid a chin-length bob.
  • Think about your hair texture: Curly hair has different textures, and some haircuts work better for certain textures. For example, a layered bob may work better for someone with loose waves, while a pixie cut may work better for someone with tight coils.
  • Personal style: Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and personal style. If you are more of a low-maintenance person, you may want to choose a haircut that requires minimal styling.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get the Best Short Haircut for Curly Hair

Getting a short haircut for curly hair can be intimidating, but with the right guidance, it can be a breeze. Here is a step-by-step guide to getting the best short haircut for curly hair:

  • Research: Look for inspiration pictures of short haircuts for curly hair that you like. This will help you communicate your vision to your stylist.
  • Consultation: Schedule a consultation with your hairstylist. This is the perfect time to discuss your hair type, face shape, and personal style.
  • Cut: Once you and your stylist have agreed on a haircut, it's time to cut your hair. Your stylist may cut your hair while it's dry to better see the natural curl pattern.
  • Style: After the cut, your stylist will style your hair. They may use a diffuser to enhance your natural curls or apply a styling product to define your curls.

Tips to Maintain Your Short Curly Hair

Now that you have the perfect short haircut for your curly hair, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Avoid using a brush, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much to prevent frizz.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant when using hot tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for curly hair:

  • What is the best short haircut for curly hair? The best short haircut for curly hair depends on your hair type, face shape, and personal style. Some popular options include the pixie cut, bob, and lob.
  • Is it better to cut curly hair when it's wet or dry? It's better to cut curly hair when it's dry to better see the natural curl pattern.
  • How often should I trim my short curly hair? It's recommended to trim your short curly hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for short curly hair:

  • Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh down your curls.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant.

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to find the perfect short haircut for your curly hair. Remember to consider your hair texture, face shape, and personal style when choosing a haircut. And don't forget to maintain your hair with proper hair care and styling techniques.
