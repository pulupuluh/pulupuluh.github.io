How To Style Spiky Asian Hair

Written by Daniel Mar 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both edgy and fashionable, then spiky hair may be the perfect fit. This trendy hairstyle is a popular choice among Asian men, and it can be styled in many ways to suit your personal taste. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps on how to style spiky Asian hair with ease.

Description

Spiky hair is a hairstyle that involves creating spikes or points on the top of the head. This style is often achieved by using a strong-hold styling product, such as hair gel, wax, or pomade. Spikes can be created in many different sizes and shapes, ranging from short and subtle to long and dramatic.

How to Style Spiky Asian Hair

Styling spiky Asian hair requires a few tools and products to achieve the perfect look. Here's what you'll need:

  • Hair gel, wax, or pomade
  • Hair dryer
  • Comb
  • Hairspray (optional)

Once you have all the necessary tools, follow these steps to style your spiky Asian hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or oil from your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Use a towel to dry your hair until it's damp. Avoid using a blow dryer at this stage, as it can cause damage to your hair and make it harder to style.

Step 3: Apply styling product

Take a small amount of hair gel, wax, or pomade and rub it between your palms. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the top of your head where you want to create the spikes.

Step 4: Blow dry your hair

Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to blow dry your hair. Use your fingers to shape the spikes as you dry your hair, making sure to direct the heat towards the roots of your hair.

Step 5: Create the spikes

Use a comb to create the spikes on the top of your head. Start at the front and work your way towards the back, using the comb to lift and shape the hair into spikes. You can create spikes of different sizes and shapes to achieve the look you want.

Step 6: Optional finishing touch

If you want your spikes to stay in place for longer, you can use hairspray to set the style. Hold the bottle of hairspray about 10 inches away from your hair and spray evenly over the spikes.

Tips for Styling Spiky Asian Hair

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best possible results when styling spiky Asian hair:

  • Use a strong-hold styling product for the best results.
  • Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting to avoid damage.
  • Experiment with different spike sizes and shapes to find the perfect look.
  • Use hairspray to set your spikes in place for longer lasting hold.

FAQ

Can spiky Asian hair be styled with other products besides gel?

Yes, you can use wax or pomade instead of gel to style your spiky Asian hair. These products provide a similar hold and are perfect for creating different textures and finishes.

How often should I wash my spiky Asian hair?

You should wash your spiky Asian hair every two or three days, depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to style.

How long will my spiky Asian hair last?

Your spiky Asian hair style can last for several hours or even a full day, depending on the strength of the styling product you use and the weather conditions. Using hairspray can help to prolong the hold and keep your spikes in place for longer.

Hair Care for Spiky Asian Hair

To keep your spiky Asian hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips to help you maintain healthy, stylish hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can lead to buildup and make it harder to style.

Now that you know how to style spiky Asian hair, it's time to experiment with different looks and find the perfect style that suits you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best, and don't be afraid to try new styles and products to achieve the perfect look.
