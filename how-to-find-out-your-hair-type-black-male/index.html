How To Find Out Your Hair Type Black Male

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 · 5 min read

Knowing your hair type is essential to determine the right hair care routine that suits your hair. Black males have a different hair texture than other races, and it is essential to identify the hair type to maintain healthy hair. In this article, we will discuss how to find out your hair type as a black male.

Table of Contents

  • How to Find Out Your Hair Type
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Find Out Your Hair Type

There are four types of hair textures for black males:

  • Straight hair
  • Wavy hair
  • Curly hair
  • Kinky hair

The type of hair texture you have depends on the shape of your hair follicles. Straight hair has round hair follicles, wavy hair has oval-shaped hair follicles, curly hair has elliptical hair follicles, and kinky hair has flat hair follicles.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to find out your hair type:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  2. Let your hair dry naturally without using any hair products.
  3. Examine your hair in a mirror and determine the shape of your hair strands.
  4. If your hair strands are straight and smooth, you have straight hair.
  5. If your hair strands are slightly curved and have a slight wave, you have wavy hair.
  6. If your hair strands are tightly curved and spiral, you have curly hair.
  7. If your hair strands are tightly coiled and have a zig-zag pattern, you have kinky hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you find out your hair type:

  • Don't use any hair products before examining your hair.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before examining it.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Don't use a hair dryer or any heat styling tools to dry your hair.

FAQ

Q: What if my hair has different textures?

A: It is common for black males to have different hair textures on different parts of their head. In this case, you can identify the dominant hair texture and choose hair care products that suit that texture.

Q: Can my hair type change over time?

A: Yes, your hair type can change due to hormonal changes, aging, or damage from heat styling or chemical treatments.

Hair Care

Once you have identified your hair type, you can choose hair care products that suit your hair. Here are some general tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools or chemical treatments that can damage your hair.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy hair and prevent hair damage. Remember to identify your hair type and choose hair care products that suit your hair texture.
