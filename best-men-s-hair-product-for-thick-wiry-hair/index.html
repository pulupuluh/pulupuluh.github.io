The Best Men s Hair Product For Thick Wiry Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Thick wiry hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right hair product, you can have the sleek, stylish look you desire. In this article, we will discuss the best men's hair product for thick wiry hair, and provide tips on how to use it effectively.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Thick Wiry Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Routine

Description

The best men's hair product for thick wiry hair is a pomade. Pomades are great for thick hair because they provide a strong hold that lasts all day without making your hair stiff or crunchy. They also add shine to your hair, making it look healthy and vibrant.

There are many types of pomades available, but the best one for thick wiry hair is a water-based pomade. This type of pomade is easy to apply and wash out, and it won't leave any residue or build-up in your hair.

How to Use

Using a water-based pomade is easy. First, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Then, take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.

For a stronger hold, you can apply more pomade, but be careful not to use too much, as this can make your hair look greasy.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Take a small amount of water-based pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  3. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  5. For a stronger hold, apply more pomade.

Tips for Thick Wiry Hair

When using a pomade on thick wiry hair, it's important to choose the right product and use it correctly. Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Choose a water-based pomade for easy application and removal.
  • Start with a small amount of pomade and add more as needed.
  • Don't apply too much pomade, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent build-up and keep it healthy.

How do I use pomade on thick wiry hair?

Hair Care Routine

In addition to using the best men's hair product for thick wiry hair, it's important to have a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to support hair growth and health.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With the right men's hair product and a good hair care routine, you can have the sleek, stylish look you desire. Choose a water-based pomade for easy application and removal, and use it correctly to avoid greasy or crunchy hair. Follow these tips for thick wiry hair to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
