How To Straighten Small Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Feb 23, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have small curly hair, you may have found that it can be difficult to straighten. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, straight look without damaging your hair. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to straighten small curly hair, along with some tips and FAQs to help you along the way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Straighten Small Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Straightening Small Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Small Curly Hair

How to Straighten Small Curly Hair

Straightening small curly hair requires some preparation and the use of the right tools. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to use products that are designed for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start straightening your hair, apply a heat protectant product to protect it from heat damage. This will also help to keep your hair looking smooth and shiny.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a hair dryer, blow-dry your hair until it is completely dry. Use a round brush to help smooth out your hair as you blow-dry it. If you have very curly hair, you may need to use a diffuser attachment to help dry your hair without frizzing it.

Step 4: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to straighten your hair and ensure that you don’t miss any sections.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Starting at the roots, use a flat iron to straighten each section of hair. Use a comb to help guide the flat iron through your hair, and make sure to move the flat iron slowly and evenly through each section. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 6: Finish with Hair Serum

Once your hair is completely straight, apply a small amount of hair serum to the ends of your hair. This will help to keep your hair looking smooth and shiny.

Tips for Straightening Small Curly Hair

Straightening small curly hair can be challenging, but with these tips, you can achieve a sleek, straight look:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates. This will help to distribute heat evenly and prevent hot spots.
  • Use a comb to help guide the flat iron through your hair. This will help to prevent tangles and ensure that the heat is evenly distributed.
  • Don’t use too much heat. Start with a low temperature and gradually increase it until you find the right setting for your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant product to your hair before straightening it. This will help to prevent heat damage and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my hair if it’s wet?

A: No, you should never straighten wet hair. This can cause damage to your hair and even burn your scalp. Make sure to blow-dry your hair completely before straightening it.

Q: How often can I straighten my hair?

A: It’s best to limit the amount of heat you use on your hair. Try to straighten your hair no more than once a week to prevent damage.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from frizzing after straightening it?

A: To prevent frizz, make sure to use a heat protectant product before straightening your hair. You can also apply a small amount of hair serum to the ends of your hair after straightening it to keep it looking smooth and shiny.

Hair Care Tips for Small Curly Hair

To keep your small curly hair looking healthy and beautiful, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you do use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant product.
  • Don’t brush your hair when it’s wet. Use a wide-tooth comb instead.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.

By following these tips and using the right techniques, you can achieve a sleek, straight look with your small curly hair. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
