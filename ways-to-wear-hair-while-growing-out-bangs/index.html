Ways To Wear Hair While Growing Out Bangs

Written by Aprilia Mar 16, 2023 · 8 min read

Growing out bangs can be a challenging process, but it doesn't have to be a frustrating one. With the right techniques and hairstyles, you can make the growing-out phase look chic and stylish. In this blog post, we will discuss some of the best ways to wear your hair while growing out bangs.

How to Grow Out Bangs

Growing out bangs can be a time-consuming process, but it is important to be patient and allow your hair to grow naturally. Avoid cutting your bangs too short or trimming them yourself, as this can hinder the growth process. Instead, work with your stylist to create a gradual transition from bangs to longer layers.

During the growing-out phase, it is important to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to keep your locks soft and shiny.

Step-by-Step Guide to Growing Out Bangs

If you are unsure about how to grow out your bangs, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the center or to one side.
  2. Gradually trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to give them a chance to grow out.
  3. As your bangs grow longer, try sweeping them to the side or pulling them back with bobby pins.
  4. Consider getting a layered haircut to help blend your bangs with the rest of your hair.
  5. Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to find what works best for you.

Tips for Growing Out Bangs

Here are some additional tips to help you grow out your bangs:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.
  • Try using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will help prevent breakage and damage.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your locks smooth and nourished.
  • Consider wearing a hair accessory, such as a headband or scarf, to help conceal your bangs as they grow out.

FAQs on Growing Out Bangs

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out bangs:

How long does it take to grow out bangs?

The length of time it takes to grow out bangs varies from person to person, but it typically takes about 3-6 months for bangs to grow out completely.

Can I speed up the process of growing out my bangs?

While you can't speed up the growth process, you can help your hair grow faster by eating a healthy diet, getting enough sleep, and staying hydrated.

Should I trim my bangs while growing them out?

Yes, it is important to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage. However, make sure to ask your stylist to only trim the ends and not cut them too short.

Hair Care Tips for Growing Out Bangs

To keep your hair healthy and strong while growing out bangs, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals on your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

Hairstyles for Growing Out Bangs

Here are some hairstyles that are perfect for growing out bangs:

  • Beachy waves
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Half-up, half-down hairstyles
  • Low ponytails
  • Braided hairstyles

Accessories to Wear While Growing Out Bangs

To help conceal your bangs while they grow out, try wearing these accessories:

  • Headbands
  • Hats
  • Scarves
  • Barrettes
  • Bobby pins

Updos for Growing Out Bangs

If you want to keep your hair off your face while growing out your bangs, try these updos:

  • Messy bun
  • Low chignon
  • Braided updo
  • Twisted updo
  • Top knot

Braids for Growing Out Bangs

Braids are a great way to keep your hair under control while growing out your bangs. Try these braided hairstyles:

  • Fishtail braid
  • Dutch braid
  • French braid
  • Waterfall braid
  • Crown braid

Ponytails for Growing Out Bangs

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that can help keep your hair out of your face while growing out your bangs. Try these ponytail styles:

  • High ponytail
  • Low ponytail
  • Sleek ponytail
  • Messy ponytail
  • Ponytail with a side part

With these tips and hairstyles, you can make growing out your bangs a fun and stylish process. Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair, and soon enough, you'll have beautiful, flowing locks.
