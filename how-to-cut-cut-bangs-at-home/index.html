How To Cut Cut Bangs At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts every time you need to trim your bangs? With a little bit of practice and patience, you can easily cut your own bangs at home. In this step by step guide, we will show you how to cut cut bangs at home like a pro. Let's get started!

Description

Bangs are a great way to switch up your hairstyle without making a drastic change. However, getting your bangs trimmed at the salon can be expensive and time-consuming. Learning how to cut cut bangs at home can save you money and give you the freedom to change up your hairstyle whenever you want.

How to Cut Cut Bangs at Home

Here is a step by step guide on how to cut cut bangs at home:

Step 1: Choose the Right Scissors

It's important to use the right scissors when cutting your bangs. Don't use regular scissors or kitchen scissors as they can damage your hair. Instead, invest in a pair of hair cutting scissors that are sharp and have a straight blade.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before cutting your bangs. Make sure your hair is dry and free from tangles. Comb your hair straight down in front of your face, so your bangs are in the center.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Take a section of hair from the center of your forehead, where you want your bangs to start. Comb this section of hair straight down and hold it between your index and middle fingers.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Using your hair cutting scissors, gently cut into the hair in a straight line. Don't cut too much at once, as you can always go back and trim more if needed. Make sure both sides are even by comparing them.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After cutting your bangs, brush them to the side and check for any uneven areas. If you notice any, trim them with your scissors until they are even.

Step 6: Style Your Bangs

Once you have cut your bangs, you can style them however you like. You can blow-dry them straight, curl them, or add some texture with a styling product.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your bangs at home:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short, as they will take longer to grow back.
  • Use a comb to keep your hair in place while cutting.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting bangs at home:

Can I cut my bangs when they are wet?

No, it's best to cut your bangs when they are dry. Wet hair can stretch, making it difficult to cut evenly.

How often should I cut my bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your bangs to be. On average, bangs need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

What do I do if I make a mistake while cutting my bangs?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a salon and have them fix it. If the mistake is minor, you can try to fix it yourself by trimming the uneven areas.

Hair Care

Cutting your own bangs at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. However, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Make sure to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently cut your own bangs at home like a pro. Have fun experimenting with different styles and lengths, and don't be afraid to try something new!
