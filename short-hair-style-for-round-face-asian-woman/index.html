Short Hair Styles For Round Face Asian Women

Written by Robby May 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a round-faced Asian woman looking for a trendy and chic hairstyle? Look no further than short hair styles! Short hair can be incredibly flattering for round faces, and there are plenty of options to choose from. Read on for tips on finding the perfect short haircut for your face shape, as well as tips on styling and caring for your new 'do.

Description

Round faces are characterized by a wider forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The key to finding a flattering short haircut for a round face is to choose a style that balances out the proportions of the face. This could mean opting for a cut with volume on top, or one that elongates the face.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect short haircut for your round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, and comparing them to one another. If they are all roughly the same width, you likely have a round face.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to start looking for inspiration! Browse online for photos of short haircuts on women with round faces, or consult with your hairstylist for recommendations. Consider factors such as your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a haircut.

Step by Step

  1. Choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to ensure the cut will work with your hair texture and lifestyle.
  3. Wash and condition your hair using products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  5. Style your hair according to your personal preferences, using products such as hairspray, pomade, or wax to hold the style in place.

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, consider the following tips:

  • Opt for a cut that adds height to the top of the head, such as a pixie cut or a bob with layers.
  • Avoid cuts that add volume to the sides of the head, such as blunt bobs or heavy bangs.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and desired style.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look wider?
A: Not necessarily! A well-chosen short haircut can balance out the proportions of a round face and make it appear slimmer.

Q: Can I still style my short hair in different ways?
A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and sophisticated to messy and textured.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days using a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as straighteners or curling irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a fabulous new short haircut in no time! Remember to choose a cut that flatters your face shape, and experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for you.
