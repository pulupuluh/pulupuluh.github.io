How To Restore My Synthetic Wig

Written by Petter Apr 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering how to restore your synthetic wig? Synthetic wigs can become dull and tangled over time, but restoring them is a simple process that can make them look like new again. In this article, we will guide you through the steps to restore your synthetic wig and provide you with tips for hair care to keep your wig looking great for a longer period of time.

Description

Synthetic wigs are a popular choice for those who want to change their hairstyle without damaging their natural hair. These wigs are made of synthetic fibers that mimic the look and feel of real hair. However, they can become dull and tangled over time, especially if they are not properly cared for. Restoring your synthetic wig can make it look like new again and help you get the most out of your investment.

How to Restore My Synthetic Wig

Step 1: Detangle

The first step in restoring your synthetic wig is to detangle it. Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to gently comb through the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Avoid pulling or tugging on the hair, as this can cause damage.

Step 2: Wash

Next, wash your synthetic wig using a wig shampoo. Fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of shampoo. Submerge the wig in the water and gently swirl it around for a few minutes. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water to remove all of the shampoo.

Step 3: Condition

After washing your wig, apply a wig conditioner to the hair. This will help to soften the fibers and make the hair easier to manage. Leave the conditioner in the hair for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.

Step 4: Dry

Once you have washed and conditioned your wig, gently squeeze out any excess water. Avoid wringing or twisting the hair, as this can cause damage. Place the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head and allow it to air dry. Do not use a hair dryer or other heat source to dry your wig, as this can damage the fibers.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your synthetic wig looking great:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your synthetic wig, as this can melt the fibers.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to direct sunlight for prolonged periods of time, as this can cause fading.
  • Avoid sleeping in your wig, as this can cause tangles.

FAQ

Q: Can I use regular shampoo on my synthetic wig?

A: No, you should use a wig shampoo specifically designed for synthetic wigs. Regular shampoo can damage the fibers and cause tangling.

Q: Can I dye my synthetic wig?

A: No, you should not attempt to dye your synthetic wig. The fibers are not designed to be dyed and attempting to do so can cause damage.

Q: How often should I wash my synthetic wig?

A: It is recommended that you wash your synthetic wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed if it becomes dirty or tangled.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your synthetic wig looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your wig.
  • Wash your wig using a wig shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for synthetic wigs.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig, as this can melt the fibers.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it maintain its shape.

By following these steps, you can restore your synthetic wig and keep it looking great for a longer period of time. Remember to always use the proper hair care products and avoid using heat styling tools to prevent damage to the fibers. With proper care, your synthetic wig can last for months or even years.
