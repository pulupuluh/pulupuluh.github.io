Mens Short Hairstyles For Thick Grey Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel May 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a man with thick grey hair, you might be wondering what hairstyle would suit you best. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be going over some of the best mens short hairstyles for thick grey hair. Whether you're looking for a classic look or something more modern, we've got something for everyone.

Description

Grey hair can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you wear it. But one thing's for sure, it can be a bit challenging to style, especially if you have thick hair. This is why finding the right hairstyle is crucial. The good news is that there are plenty of stylish and trendy short hairstyles that are perfect for men with thick grey hair. These hairstyles range from classic to modern, so you're sure to find something that suits your style.

How To

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for thick grey hair is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes suit different hairstyles, so it's important to find a style that complements your features. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to go for a style that adds height to your hair, like a pompadour or a quiff. If you have a square face, you might want to consider a style that softens your features, like a side-swept fringe or a messy crop.

Once you've considered your face shape, it's time to think about the length of your hair. Short hairstyles are perfect for men with thick grey hair because they're easy to maintain and style. Some popular short hairstyles for men with thick grey hair include the buzz cut, the crew cut, and the textured crop. These hairstyles are low-maintenance and can be styled in a variety of ways, from messy to sleek.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style some of the most popular mens short hairstyles for thick grey hair:

The Buzz Cut

  1. Start by using clippers to trim your hair to a uniform length all over.
  2. Use a comb to brush your hair in different directions to ensure that it's evenly cut.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair product to add texture and hold.

The Crew Cut

  1. Start by trimming the sides and back of your hair with clippers.
  2. Leave the hair on top slightly longer, around 1-2 inches.
  3. Use a comb to style the hair on top to one side or back for a sleek look.
  4. Alternatively, use your fingers to create a messy, textured look.

The Textured Crop

  1. Start by trimming the sides and back of your hair with clippers.
  2. Leave the hair on top slightly longer, around 2-3 inches.
  3. Use your fingers to add texture and movement to your hair.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair product to add hold and definition.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling mens short hairstyles for thick grey hair:

  • Use a high-quality hair product to add texture and hold.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider your skin tone when choosing a hair color. Grey hair can look great with a variety of shades, so don't be afraid to try something new.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about mens short hairstyles for thick grey hair:

What is the best short hairstyle for thick grey hair?

The best short hairstyle for thick grey hair depends on your face shape and personal style. Some popular options include the buzz cut, the crew cut, and the textured crop.

How do I style my thick grey hair?

To style your thick grey hair, start by considering your face shape and choosing a hairstyle that complements your features. Use a high-quality hair product to add texture and hold, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Can I dye my grey hair?

Yes, you can dye your grey hair. There are plenty of hair coloring products on the market that are specifically designed for men's hair. Consider consulting with a stylist to find the best color for your skin tone.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick grey hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to nourish and strengthen your hair.

In conclusion, mens short hairstyles for thick grey hair can be stylish and trendy. Whether you're looking for a classic or modern look, there's a hairstyle out there that will suit your style and personality. Remember to consider your face shape, experiment with different styles, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
