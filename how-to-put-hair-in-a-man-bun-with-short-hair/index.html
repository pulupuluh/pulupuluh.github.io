How To Put Hair In A Man Bun With Short Hair

Written by Robby Jun 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have short hair and want to rock a man bun, you might think it's impossible. But fear not, because with a few simple steps and some styling products, you can achieve the look you want! In this article, we'll go through the process of how to put hair in a man bun with short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step to putting your short hair in a man bun is to let it grow out a bit. You'll need at least 4-5 inches of hair to create the bun. Once your hair has reached the desired length, you'll need to gather it all together at the crown of your head.

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to make sure it's clean and dry. You don't want any product buildup or dampness to interfere with your styling efforts.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow to create a man bun with short hair:

  1. Brush your hair towards the crown of your head.
  2. Use an elastic hair tie to gather your hair into a ponytail.
  3. Loop the hair tie around the ponytail a second time, but only pull the hair halfway through to create a loop or bun shape.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  5. If desired, use hairspray or other styling products to hold your man bun in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when putting your short hair in a man bun:

  • If you're having trouble getting your hair to stay in place, try using a hair donut or sock bun to create a more voluminous bun shape.
  • Experiment with different placement options for your man bun. You can try placing it higher or lower on your head, or to one side for a more asymmetrical look.
  • If you're struggling with keeping your bun in place, try using a stronger hair tie or bobby pins for added hold.
  • Don't be afraid to add some texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to give your man bun some extra volume and hold.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about putting short hair in a man bun:

Can I put my hair in a man bun if it's too short?

If your hair is too short, it may be difficult to create a man bun. You'll need at least 4-5 inches of hair to work with.

What kind of hair tie should I use for a man bun?

You should use a thicker, elastic hair tie to hold your man bun in place. Avoid using thin or flimsy hair ties, as they may break or slip out of place.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong while growing it out for a man bun:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp and hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh styling products or heat tools that can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to support healthy hair growth.
  • Consider taking a hair growth supplement or using a topical hair growth treatment to speed up the process.

With these tips and techniques, you can put your short hair in a man bun and rock the look with confidence. Just remember to be patient, take good care of your hair, and experiment with different styling options until you find the perfect man bun for you!
