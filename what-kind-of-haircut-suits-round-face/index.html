What Kind Of Haircut Suits Round Face

Written by Robby Jul 23, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a round face, it's important to choose a haircut that complements your face shape. A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin, so you want a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of more defined facial features. In this article, we will discuss what kind of haircut suits round face and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How To Choose The Right Haircut
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips To Style Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How To Choose The Right Haircut

The first step in choosing the right haircut for a round face is to consider the length of your hair. Long hair can make a round face look even rounder, so it's best to opt for a shorter haircut. A chin-length bob or a pixie cut can look great on a round face.

The second consideration is the shape of your haircut. A layered haircut with soft, angled layers can help to elongate your face, while a blunt cut can make your face look wider. It's also important to avoid hairstyles with volume at the sides, as this can add width to your face.

Another option to consider is adding bangs to your hairstyle. Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a narrower face, while blunt bangs can make your face look wider. If you're unsure about bangs, it's best to consult with a hairstylist to find the best option for your face shape.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right haircut for a round face:

  1. Consider the length of your hair. Shorter haircuts are better for round faces.
  2. Choose a layered haircut with soft, angled layers to elongate your face.
  3. Avoid blunt cuts and hairstyles with volume at the sides.
  4. Consider adding side-swept bangs to create the illusion of a narrower face.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best option for your face shape.

Tips To Style Your Hair

Once you've chosen the right haircut for your round face, it's important to style your hair in a way that complements your face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots to add height to your hairstyle.
  • Avoid adding volume at the sides of your hair.
  • Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, directing the hair away from your face.
  • Consider adding texture to your hair with a curling iron or texturizing spray.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for round faces:

What kind of hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles with volume at the sides or blunt cuts that make your face look wider.

What length of hair looks best on a round face?

Shorter haircuts, such as a chin-length bob or a pixie cut, look best on a round face.

What kind of bangs should I get for a round face?

Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a narrower face.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look dull.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for a round face is all about creating the illusion of more defined facial features. Shorter haircuts with soft, angled layers can help to elongate your face, while side-swept bangs can create the illusion of a narrower face. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can look and feel your best with a flattering haircut that complements your round face.
