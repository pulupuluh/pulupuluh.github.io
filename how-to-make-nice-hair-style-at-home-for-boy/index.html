How To Make Nice Hair Style At Home For Boy

Written by Eveline Aug 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon and spending a lot of money on a simple haircut for your boy? Why not try making a nice hair style at home for your boy? With a little bit of practice and patience, you can easily create a stylish and trendy hair style for your boy at home.

Description:

In this article, we will guide you on how to make nice hair style at home for boy. We will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve a perfect hair style for your boy. This article is designed for parents who want to save time and money by doing their boy's hair at home.

How to:

Here are the steps you can follow to make a nice hair style at home for boy:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you start styling your boy's hair, you need to prepare the hair first. Start by washing the hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry the hair and comb it to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Choose the Hair Style

Decide on the hair style that you want to create for your boy. You can search the internet for inspiration or look at pictures of hairstyles in magazines. Choose a style that complements your boy's face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Gather the Tools

Gather all the tools that you will need to create the hair style. You will need a comb, hair clips, a hair dryer, and styling products such as hair gel or wax.

Step 4: Start Styling

Start styling your boy's hair by applying a small amount of styling product to the hair. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout the hair. Use hair clips to section off the hair and work on one section at a time.

Step 5: Use a Hair Dryer

Use a hair dryer to dry the hair and set the style in place. Use a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots. Finish off with a blast of cold air to set the style in place.

Step 6: Finish with Styling Product

Finish off the hair style with a small amount of styling product. Use your fingers to shape and define the hair. Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.

Tips:

  • Start with a simple hair style and gradually work your way up to more complex styles.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair as it can damage the hair and make it brittle.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your boy's hair type.

FAQ:

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my boy's hair?
A: Yes, you can use regular shampoo and conditioner on your boy's hair. Just make sure to choose a mild and gentle formula that is suitable for his hair type.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener on my boy's hair?
A: It is not recommended to use a hair straightener on a boy's hair as it can damage the hair and make it brittle. Instead, use a hair dryer and round brush to create volume and lift.

Hair Care:

It is important to take care of your boy's hair to keep it healthy and nourished. Here are some tips to help you maintain your boy's hair:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a mild and gentle shampoo.
  • Condition the hair after each wash to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair as it can damage the hair and make it brittle.
  • Trim the hair every 6 to 8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, making a nice hair style at home for boy is easy and fun. With the right tools, products, and techniques, you can create a stylish and trendy hair style for your boy at home. Just remember to take care of your boy's hair to keep it healthy and nourished.
