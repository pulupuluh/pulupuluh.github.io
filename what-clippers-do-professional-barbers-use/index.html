What Clippers Do Professional Barbers Use

Written by Robby Jul 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As a barber, one of the most essential tools in your arsenal is a good pair of clippers. Clippers are used to cut hair quickly and efficiently, and they come in a variety of shapes and sizes. In this article, we will discuss what clippers professional barbers use and why they are essential to the trade.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Professional barbers typically use clippers that are designed specifically for cutting hair. These clippers are designed to be powerful, precise, and durable, making them ideal for use in a busy barbershop. They also come with a variety of attachments that allow barbers to create a range of styles and lengths.

There are two main types of clippers that barbers use: corded and cordless. Corded clippers are plugged into an electrical outlet and provide a steady stream of power, while cordless clippers use batteries and offer greater flexibility in terms of movement.

How To

When choosing clippers for professional use, there are several factors to consider. These include the weight of the clippers, the length of the cord (if using corded clippers), the power of the motor, and the quality of the blades. It is also important to choose a reputable brand that is known for producing high-quality clippers that are designed to last.

Weight

The weight of the clippers is an important consideration for professional barbers, as they will be using them for extended periods of time. Clippers that are too heavy can cause fatigue and strain, which can lead to poor performance and even injury over time.

Length of Cord

If using corded clippers, it is important to choose a length of cord that is appropriate for the size of your workspace. A longer cord can provide greater flexibility, but it can also be cumbersome and get in the way. A shorter cord may be more convenient, but it can limit your movement and make it more difficult to reach certain areas.

Power of Motor

The power of the motor is another important consideration when choosing clippers for professional use. A more powerful motor will provide greater cutting ability and make it easier to cut through thicker or coarser hair. However, a more powerful motor may also generate more heat and noise, which can be uncomfortable for both the barber and the client.

Quality of Blades

The quality of the blades is perhaps the most important factor to consider when choosing clippers for professional use. High-quality blades will stay sharp for longer and provide a cleaner, smoother cut. They will also be less likely to cause irritation or injury to the scalp.

Step By Step

Using clippers in a barbershop requires a certain level of skill and precision. Here are some basic steps to follow:

  1. Ensure that the clippers are clean and oiled before use.
  2. Select the appropriate attachment for the desired length of cut.
  3. Begin cutting at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the crown.
  4. Use a comb to guide the clippers and create a clean, even cut.
  5. Blend the hair using a clipper-over-comb technique.
  6. Repeat the process on the sides and top of the head.
  7. Trim any stray hairs around the hairline and ears using a trimmer.
  8. Finish the cut with a neck shave and styling as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips for using clippers in a barbershop:

  • Keep the clippers clean and oiled to ensure optimal performance.
  • Use a light touch when cutting to avoid scalp irritation.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers for a more precise cut.
  • Use a trimmer to clean up any stray hairs around the hairline and ears.
  • Invest in high-quality clippers to ensure long-lasting performance.

FAQ

What are the best clippers for professional use?

There are many high-quality clippers on the market that are designed for professional use. Some of the most popular brands include Wahl, Andis, and Oster.

Can I use home clippers for professional use?

Home clippers are not designed for the rigors of a professional barbershop and are unlikely to provide the same level of performance and durability as professional-grade clippers.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and preventing damage. Here are some tips for keeping hair healthy:

  • Wash hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often.
  • Protect hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to support hair health.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

In conclusion, professional barbers use high-quality clippers that are designed for the rigors of a busy barbershop. When choosing clippers, it is important to consider factors such as weight, cord length, motor power, and blade quality. By following basic steps and tips, barbers can achieve a clean, precise cut that is both efficient and effective.
