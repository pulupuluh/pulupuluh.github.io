Why Is My Hair Straight Underneath And Curly On Top

Written by Frank May 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're someone who has straight hair at the bottom and curly hair on top, you're not alone. This hair type is quite common, and it can be quite tricky to manage. In this blog post, we will be discussing why your hair might be straight underneath and curly on top and how you can take care of it.

Description

Having straight hair underneath and curly hair on top is a common hair type. This phenomenon is called "mixed texture hair" or "multi-textured hair." Multi-textured hair can be quite tricky to manage as it requires different products and styling techniques for each texture.

How to

If you have multi-textured hair, you need to identify the texture of each section of your hair. Once you've done that, you can follow these steps to take care of your hair:

Step 1: Shampoo

Use a shampoo that is designed for your hair type. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo. Make sure you massage your scalp thoroughly to remove any dirt or buildup.

Step 2: Condition

Use a conditioner that is designed for your hair type. If you have dry hair, use a deep conditioner. If you have oily hair, use a lightweight conditioner. Make sure you apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair and avoid the scalp.

Step 3: Detangle

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

Step 4: Style

Use styling products that are designed for your hair type. If you have curly hair, use a curl cream or gel. If you have straight hair, use a straightening serum. Apply the product to the appropriate sections of your hair and style as desired.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools.
  • Avoid brushing your curly hair as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh your hair down.

FAQ

Q: Can I use the same products for both textures of my hair?

A: No, you need to use different products for each texture of your hair. Using the wrong products can cause damage and make your hair look worse.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care

Taking care of multi-textured hair can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, you can manage it. Remember to identify the texture of each section of your hair and use the appropriate products. Avoid using too much product and be gentle when detangling your hair.

With these tips, you can manage your multi-textured hair and have it looking healthy and beautiful.
