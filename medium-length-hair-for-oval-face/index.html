Medium Length Hair For Oval Face A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having the same old hairstyle and want to switch it up? Do you have an oval face and not sure what hairstyle would suit you best? Look no further! In this guide, we will discuss medium length hair for oval face and provide you with tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Medium length hair falls between the chin and shoulders, making it a versatile length for various hairstyles. An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile face shape, as it can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, medium length hair can bring out the best features of an oval face, highlighting the cheekbones and jawline.

How to

When it comes to medium length hair for oval face, there are various styles to choose from. One popular hairstyle is the lob, which is a long bob that falls just above the shoulders. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair length while still having a stylish cut. Another popular hairstyle is the layered cut, which adds texture and volume to the hair while framing the face.

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best medium length hairstyle for your face shape.

Step 2: Choose a hairstyle that compliments your personal style and maintenance preferences.

Step 3: Wash and condition your hair with products that cater to your hair type.

Step 4: Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and texture.

Step 5: Style your hair using a curling iron or straightener, depending on your desired look.

Tips

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for oval face, consider the following tips:

  • Opt for a side or center part to frame the face.
  • Add layers to add texture and volume.
  • Keep the ends blunt for a modern look.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as waves or curls.
  • Invest in quality hair products to maintain the health and shine of your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with medium length hair for oval face?

A: Yes! Medium length hair can be styled in various up-dos, such as a high ponytail or a messy bun.

Q: What face shape is best suited for medium length hair?

A: Medium length hair can be styled to suit any face shape, but it particularly compliments oval and heart-shaped faces.

Q: How often should I cut my medium length hair?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the health and shape of your hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining the health of your hair is crucial when it comes to achieving the perfect medium length hairstyle for oval face. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with a hot tool.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, too frequently.
  • Regularly apply a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Limit the use of hair products, such as hairspray or gel, to prevent build-up.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

In conclusion, medium length hair for oval face is a versatile and stylish option for those looking to switch up their hairstyle. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect look while maintaining the health of your hair.
