How To Cut Men s Hair Short On Sides Long Top In 2023

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut this year? The short on sides long top haircut is a popular choice among men. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in various ways. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut men's hair short on sides long top in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. Let's get started!

Description

The short on sides long top haircut is also known as the undercut. This hairstyle features short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This haircut is suitable for all hair types and face shapes. It can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. The short on sides long top haircut is a low-maintenance and stylish option for men.

How to Cut Men's Hair Short on Sides Long Top

To cut men's hair short on sides long top, you will need a good pair of clippers, scissors, comb, and a mirror. It is recommended to cut hair when it is dry as wet hair tends to appear longer. Start by determining the length of the sides and the top. The sides should be shorter than the top.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut men's hair short on sides long top:

  1. Divide the hair into sections, starting from the back of the head. Use a clipper to trim the hair on the sides to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and gradually decrease the length. Repeat on both sides.
  2. Use scissors to trim the hair on the top. Cut the hair in small sections and keep checking the length. The hair on top should be longer than the sides.
  3. Blend the sides and the top using scissors or clippers. Use a comb to blend the hair and create a seamless transition between the sides and the top.
  4. Use scissors or clippers to trim the hair around the ears and the nape of the neck.
  5. Style the hair as desired. You can use hair products such as pomade or wax to create a slicked-back or messy hairstyle.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair

Here are some tips for cutting men's hair:

  • Use sharp scissors and clippers to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Start with a longer guard when using clippers and gradually decrease the length.
  • Trim the hair in small sections to avoid cutting too much.
  • Keep checking the length to ensure that the sides and the top are even.
  • Use a comb to blend the hair and create a seamless transition between the sides and the top.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair short on sides long top:

1. Is the short on sides long top haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut is suitable for all face shapes. However, the length and the style of the hair on top can be adjusted to suit the face shape.

2. How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair growth and the desired length. Generally, it is recommended to cut hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and the length.

3. Can I style my hair in different ways with this haircut?

Yes, this haircut is versatile and can be styled in various ways. You can slick it back, create a messy look, or add texture to the hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining the short on sides long top haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and the length.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as hairdryers and straighteners too often as they can damage the hair.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair short on sides long top, you can try it out and create a stylish and trendy look. Remember to follow the tips and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and looking great.
