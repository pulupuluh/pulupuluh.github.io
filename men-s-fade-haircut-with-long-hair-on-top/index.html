Men s Fade Haircut With Long Hair On Top A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Daniel Mar 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, the men's fade haircut with long hair on top might be the perfect option for you. This haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years and is a great way to showcase your personal style. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to achieve it, tips for styling, and hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

Description of Men's Fade Haircut with Long Hair on Top

The men's fade haircut with long hair on top is a haircut that features short hair on the sides and back that gradually fades into longer hair on the top. The hair on the top of the head is typically left longer than the rest and can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. This hairstyle is popular among men of all ages and is a great option for those who want a modern and stylish look.

How to Achieve the Men's Fade Haircut with Long Hair on Top

If you're interested in getting a men's fade haircut with long hair on top, the first step is to find a skilled barber who can help you achieve the look you want. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving this haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in achieving the men's fade haircut with long hair on top is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal style and preferences, but a good rule of thumb is to leave at least 3-4 inches of hair on top.

Step 2: Decide on the Fade

The next step is to decide on the type of fade you want. The fade refers to the gradual transition from short hair on the sides and back to longer hair on the top. There are several types of fades to choose from, including the low fade, mid fade, and high fade.

Step 3: Get the Haircut

Once you've decided on the length and fade, it's time to get the haircut. Your barber will use clippers to create the fade, starting with a longer guard and gradually switching to a shorter guard as they move up the head. They will then cut and style the hair on top to your desired length and style.

Tips for Styling the Men's Fade Haircut with Long Hair on Top

Once you've achieved the men's fade haircut with long hair on top, it's important to know how to style it to achieve the perfect look. Here are some tips for styling this haircut:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

The right hair products can make all the difference when it comes to styling the men's fade haircut with long hair on top. Depending on your desired style, you may want to use a pomade, wax, or hair gel. Be sure to choose a product that works well with your hair type and texture.

Tip 2: Experiment with Different Styles

The great thing about the men's fade haircut with long hair on top is that it's versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Tip 3: Keep Your Hair Healthy

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut.

FAQ about Men's Fade Haircut with Long Hair on Top

Q: How often should I get a men's fade haircut with long hair on top?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Q: Is the men's fade haircut with long hair on top suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can work well with a variety of hair types and textures.

Q: Can I style the men's fade haircut with long hair on top in a professional setting?

A: Yes, this haircut can be styled in a professional manner by slicking the hair back or keeping it neatly styled.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Fade Haircut with Long Hair on Top

Tip 1: Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a quality shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

Tip 2: Avoid Using Heat Tools Too Often

Using heat tools too often can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Try to limit your use of heat tools and always use a heat protectant before styling.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help maintain the shape of the men's fade haircut with long hair on top and prevent split ends.

Overall, the men's fade haircut with long hair on top is a stylish and versatile haircut that can work well with a variety of personal styles. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve the perfect look and keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.
