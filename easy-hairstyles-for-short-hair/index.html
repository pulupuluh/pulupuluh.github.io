Easy Hairstyles For Short Hair In 2023

Written by Eveline Mar 19, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Short hair is the perfect way to show off your face and neck, but sometimes it can be a challenge to find new hairstyles that work with your length. In this article, we'll explore some easy hairstyles for short hair that you can try out in 2023. Whether you're looking for a new way to wear your hair to work or a fun style for a night out with friends, we've got you covered. Read on for some tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Hair: Tips and Tricks
  • Step-by-Step Instructions for Easy Short Hair Hairstyles
  • FAQ: Common Questions About Short Hair Styles
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Style Short Hair: Tips and Tricks

Before diving into specific hairstyles, let's talk about some general tips and tricks for styling short hair. First of all, it's important to have the right tools on hand. Invest in a good quality hair dryer, flat iron, and curling iron if you don't already have them. Additionally, consider using a volumizing mousse or spray to add body and texture to your hair.

When it comes to styling short hair, less is often more. Try not to overload your hair with product or accessories. Instead, focus on creating clean, simple lines that highlight your natural beauty. Don't be afraid to experiment with different looks, but always remember to be gentle with your hair to avoid damage.

Tip #1: Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have curly or wavy hair, consider embracing your natural texture instead of fighting against it. Short hair can look stunning with loose, natural waves or defined ringlets. Use a curling iron or diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls, then apply a small amount of styling cream to set the look.

Tip #2: Accessorize Wisely

Short hair can look great with a variety of accessories, from headbands and barrettes to statement earrings and necklaces. However, it's important not to overdo it. Choose one or two accessories that complement your outfit and hairstyle, and keep the rest of your look simple.

Tip #3: Experiment with Parting

Changing the part in your hair can completely transform your look. Try a deep side part for a dramatic effect, or a center part for a classic, symmetrical look. You can also experiment with zigzag or diagonal parts for a more playful vibe.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Easy Short Hair Hairstyles

Now that you have some general tips for styling short hair, let's dive into some specific hairstyles that you can try out. These styles are all easy to achieve and require minimal tools and products.

Hairstyle #1: The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can be customized to fit your face shape and personal style. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb to divide your hair into sections.
  3. Using scissors or clippers, trim your hair to the desired length.
  4. Create texture by using a small amount of styling cream or wax to separate the strands.

This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, edgy look.

Hairstyle #2: The Bob

The bob is another classic short hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb to divide your hair into sections.
  3. Using scissors or clippers, trim your hair to the desired length, keeping the ends blunt.
  4. Create texture by using a small amount of styling cream or wax to separate the strands.

This style is perfect for those who want a polished, sophisticated look.

Hairstyle #3: The Messy Updo

The messy updo is a fun and playful hairstyle that's perfect for a night out with friends. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the top half of your hair and pin it up with a clip.
  3. Twist the bottom half of your hair into a messy bun or chignon, securing it with bobby pins.
  4. Release the top section of your hair and tease it slightly with a comb to add volume.
  5. Pin the top section of your hair loosely to the back of your head, leaving a few strands loose for a messy, undone look.

This style is perfect for those who want a relaxed, bohemian vibe.

FAQ: Common Questions About Short Hair Styles

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with short hair?

A: Yes! Even if your hair is too short to pull back into a traditional ponytail, you can still create a mini ponytail or half-up style. Use bobby pins or a small hair elastic to secure the hair at the crown of your head, leaving the rest of your hair loose.

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. The key to finding a flattering short hairstyle is to choose a cut that complements your face shape. Consult with your stylist to find a style that will accentuate your best features and balance out any areas you're not as fond of.

Q: How often should I get my short hair trimmed?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your style. In general, it's a good idea to get a trim every 4-8 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Now that you've mastered some easy short hair hairstyles, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for short hair care:

Tip #1: Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type. Short hair can be prone to dryness and breakage, so choose a nourishing formula that will keep your hair soft and shiny.

Tip #2: Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you use heat styling tools regularly, be sure to use a heat protectant spray or cream to minimize damage to your hair. Additionally, try to limit your use of heat tools as much as possible to give your hair a break.

Tip #3: Get Regular Trims

As mentioned earlier, getting regular trims is key to maintaining a short hairstyle. Not only will it keep your hair looking neat and tidy, but it will also help prevent split ends and breakage.

With these easy hairstyles for short hair and hair care tips, you'll be able to rock your short hair with confidence and style. Whether you prefer a classic bob or a messy updo, there's a short hair style out there that's perfect for you. Happy styling!
