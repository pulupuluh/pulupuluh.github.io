Best Medium Haircuts For Over 50 A Guide To Flattering And Age Appropriate Styles

Written by Aprilia Apr 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose its luster, which is why finding the right haircut is essential for women over 50. Medium haircuts are a perfect choice for those who want to maintain a youthful and lively appearance while still being age-appropriate. In this guide, we'll highlight the best medium haircuts for over 50, including how to style them, tips for maintaining healthy hair, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Why Choose a Medium Haircut?

Medium haircuts are versatile and low-maintenance, making them a great choice for women over 50 who want to keep things simple while still looking stylish. They also work well with different hair types, textures, and face shapes, making them a universally flattering option.

How to Choose the Right Medium Haircut

When choosing a medium haircut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. A skilled stylist can help you determine which cut will flatter your features and suit your needs. Here are some general guidelines:

Face Shape

Women with round faces should opt for a layered cut that adds height and volume to the crown. Those with square faces should choose a soft, layered cut that frames the face. Oval faces can wear almost any style, while those with heart-shaped faces should choose a cut that adds fullness to the bottom half of the face.

Hair Texture

Women with fine hair should opt for a cut with layers and texture to add volume and movement. Those with thick hair should go for a blunt cut or a layered bob to thin out the hair. Curly hair looks great with a layered cut that enhances the curl pattern, while straight hair can benefit from a cut with layers and texture.

Lifestyle

If you have an active lifestyle, you may prefer a low-maintenance cut that requires minimal styling. If you have more time to devote to your hair, you may want a cut that allows for more styling options.

Best Medium Haircuts for Over 50

Now that you know how to choose the right medium haircut, let's take a look at some of the best styles for women over 50:

The Layered Bob

The layered bob is a classic cut that works well for women of all ages. It's especially flattering for those with round faces and fine hair, as the layers add volume and movement. To style, add a bit of texturizing spray to damp hair and blow-dry with a round brush for a sleek, polished look.

The Shag

The shag is a fun and lively cut that works well with all hair types. It's especially flattering for those with square faces and thick hair, as the layers thin out the hair and soften the angles of the face. To style, apply some mousse to damp hair and blow-dry with a diffuser for a tousled, beachy look.

The Lob

The lob is a longer version of the bob that falls just above the shoulders. It's a versatile cut that works well with all face shapes and hair types. To style, add some volumizing spray to damp hair and blow-dry with a paddle brush for a smooth, sleek look.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Regardless of which medium haircut you choose, maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving a flattering and youthful look. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep hair hydrated.
  • Limit the use of heat styling tools, or use a heat protectant spray.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and hair dyes that can damage hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can women over 50 wear long hair?

While there's no age limit on long hair, it's important to consider whether it's age-appropriate and flattering for your face shape and hair type. Long hair can be more challenging to maintain as we age, and it can sometimes make us look older if it's not styled properly.

What's the best way to style medium hair?

The best way to style medium hair depends on your personal preferences and the occasion. Some popular styling options include blowouts, curls, braids, and updos. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

What's the best haircut for thinning hair?

A layered cut is often the best choice for thinning hair, as it adds volume and texture. A pixie cut or a short bob can also work well for those with thinning hair, as they draw attention to the face and away from the hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the right medium haircut can make all the difference in maintaining a youthful and vibrant appearance as we age. With so many styles to choose from, there's sure to be a cut that flatters your features and suits your needs. Remember to take care of your hair by getting regular trims, using hydrating products, and avoiding harsh chemicals. With these tips in mind, you'll be sure to rock your new medium haircut with confidence.
