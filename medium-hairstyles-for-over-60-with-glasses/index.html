Medium Hairstyles For Over 60 With Glasses

Written by Luffy May 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a fashion-conscious senior who wears glasses and wants to try a new hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we will share with you some medium hairstyles that are perfect for women over 60 with glasses. We'll also provide some tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

As we age, our hair may become thinner and lose its luster. However, that doesn't mean we can't still have fun with our hair and try out new styles. Medium hairstyles are a great option for seniors as they are versatile and flattering. Glasses can also add to your overall look, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements them.

How to

Before we get into the hairstyles, here are some general tips on styling your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls or waves to your hair.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Step by Step

Now, onto the hairstyles!

1. Shoulder-Length Bob

The shoulder-length bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. It's a low-maintenance style that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a collarbone-length bob with layers. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair and add volume. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Layered Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a shorter hairstyle, the layered pixie cut is a great option. It's a modern and edgy style that works well with glasses.

To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a short pixie cut with layers. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture. Finish with a matte pomade to define the layers and add hold.

3. Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, the long layers style is a great option. It's a simple yet stylish look that can be easily dressed up or down.

To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long layers that frame your face. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to your hair. Finish with a shine spray to add luster and reduce frizz.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and scarves to add some variety to your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium hairstyles for women over 60 with glasses:

1. What is the best haircut for a woman over 60 with glasses?

The best haircut for a woman over 60 with glasses is one that complements her face shape and glasses. Medium hairstyles such as shoulder-length bobs, layered pixie cuts, and long layers are all great options.

2. How can I make my hair look thicker?

You can make your hair look thicker by using volumizing products such as shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, and using a texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

3. What are some hairstyles to avoid if I wear glasses?

Hairstyles that cover your face, such as bangs or heavy layers, may not be the best option if you wear glasses. Instead, opt for hairstyles that frame your face and showcase your glasses.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and looks its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent breakage and reduce frizz.

Now that you have some ideas for medium hairstyles for women over 60 with glasses, it's time to go to your stylist and try out a new look!
