How To Cut Short Back And Sides At Home With Clippers

Written by Christine Jun 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read
If you're looking for a fresh haircut but don't want to spend money at the salon, cutting your hair at home is a great option. In this article, we'll guide you through how to cut short back and sides at home with clippers. With some patience and practice, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your house.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a fun and rewarding experience. This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut short back and sides at home with clippers. We'll also give you some tips on how to maintain your new haircut and answer some common questions about hair care.

How to

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. It's also helpful to have a friend or family member to assist you. Here are the steps to cut short back and sides at home with clippers: 1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wash and dry your hair before starting the haircut. Wet hair can be harder to cut evenly. 2. Use the clippers to trim the sides and back. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it. Be sure to keep the clippers level to avoid uneven cutting. 3. Blend the sides and back with scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair and the scissors to trim any longer hairs. Blend the hair from the shorter back and sides to the longer top. 4. Cut the top to the desired length. Use the scissors to cut the hair on top of your head. Start with a longer length and work your way down until you achieve your desired style. 5. Trim the bangs. If you have bangs, use the scissors to trim them to the desired length.

Step by Step

1. Wash and dry your hair. 2. Use the clippers to trim the sides and back. 3. Blend the sides and back with scissors. 4. Cut the top to the desired length. 5. Trim the bangs.

Tips

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head. - Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair. - Use the comb to lift the hair before cutting it. - Keep the clippers level to avoid uneven cutting. - Take your time and be patient. It's better to cut small sections of hair at a time than to cut too much at once.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my hair? A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your haircut. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb. Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair? A: It's best to use hair-cutting scissors, as they are sharper and designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors can damage your hair and make it look uneven.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain your new style. Here are some tips for hair care: - Use a shampoo and conditioner that is right for your hair type. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons. - Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. - Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized. In conclusion, cutting short back and sides at home with clippers is a great way to save money and achieve a professional-looking haircut. With some patience and practice, you can master this skill and maintain your new style with proper hair care.
