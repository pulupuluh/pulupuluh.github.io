Spiky Hair Style For Ladies A Trendy Look In 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are looking for a daring and edgy hairstyle, then spiky hair might be your answer. This hairstyle is not just for men, but also for ladies who want to rock a bold and confident look. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about the spiky hair style for ladies in 2023.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The spiky hair style for ladies is a short haircut that features spikes on the crown of the head. This style is achieved by cutting the hair short on the sides and back and leaving the hair on top longer. The hair on top is then styled into spikes using styling products such as gel, wax, or pomade. This style is perfect for ladies who want to make a statement and show off their confident and bold personalities.

How to

To achieve the spiky hair style for ladies, you will need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Cut Your Hair

The first step is to cut your hair short on the sides and back. You can use clippers or scissors to achieve the desired length. The hair on top should be left longer, at least 2-3 inches.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product such as gel, wax, or pomade to the hair on top. Make sure to use a product that is strong enough to hold the spikes in place. Use a small amount of product and work it through the hair evenly.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Now, it's time to style your hair. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes on the crown of your head. Make sure to style the hair in the direction you want the spikes to go. You can also use a blow dryer to help set the spikes in place.

Step by step

To help you achieve the spiky hair style for ladies, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Cut your hair short on the sides and back.
  2. Leave the hair on top longer, at least 2-3 inches.
  3. Apply a styling product such as gel, wax, or pomade to the hair on top.
  4. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes on the crown of your head.
  5. Style the hair in the direction you want the spikes to go.
  6. Use a blow dryer to help set the spikes in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect spiky hair style for ladies:

  • Use a strong-hold styling product to keep the spikes in place.
  • Experiment with different lengths to find the perfect balance between short sides and long top.
  • Try different styling techniques such as using a comb or your fingers to create the spikes.
  • Use a blow dryer to help set the spikes in place and add volume.
  • Accessorize your look with bold earrings or a statement necklace to complete the edgy look.

FAQ

Q: Is the spiky hair style for ladies suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the spiky hair style for ladies can be adapted to suit all face shapes. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect spiky hair style for your face shape.

Q: How often do I need to style my spiky hair?

A: You will need to style your spiky hair every day to maintain the look. Use a strong-hold styling product to keep the spikes in place.

Q: Can I color my spiky hair?

A: Yes, you can color your spiky hair to add more dimension and depth to your look. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect color for your skin tone.

Hair Care

To keep your spiky hair style for ladies looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair and make it harder to style.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length of your spiky hair.
  • Use a strong-hold styling product to keep the spikes in place.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know everything about the spiky hair style for ladies, it's time to rock this bold and confident look. Remember to experiment with different lengths, styles, and colors to find the perfect spiky hair style for you. With the right hair care and styling products, you can achieve an edgy and trendy look that will turn heads wherever you go.
