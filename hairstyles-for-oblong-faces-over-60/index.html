Hairstyles For Oblong Faces Over 60

Are you over 60 and have an oblong face shape? Do you struggle with finding the perfect hairstyle that complements your features? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best hairstyles for oblong faces over 60.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As we age, our hair naturally becomes thinner and loses its volume. This can make choosing a hairstyle that flatters our face shape even more challenging. An oblong face shape means that your face is longer than it is wide, with a high forehead and a narrow chin. The goal is to choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face and creates balance.

How to

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for an oblong face shape is to add volume to the sides of your face. This can be achieved through layering, curls, or waves. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they will only elongate your face even more. Instead, opt for hairstyles that hit at or above your shoulders.

Step by step

Here are some hairstyles that are perfect for oblong faces over 60:

1. Bob

A chin-length bob with layers is a great option for oblong faces. The layers will add volume to the sides of your face, creating a more balanced look. You can also add some curls or waves to add even more volume.

2. Shoulder-length cut with layers

A shoulder-length cut with layers is another great option. The layers will add volume and movement to your hair, creating a more youthful look. You can also add some waves or curls to add even more volume and texture.

3. Pixie cut

A pixie cut is a great option for those who want a shorter hairstyle. The short layers will add volume to the sides of your face, creating a more balanced look. You can also add some texture with a pomade or wax.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight
  • Add volume to the sides of your face with layers, curls, or waves
  • Choose a hairstyle that hits at or above your shoulders
  • Add texture with a pomade or wax

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oblong faces over 60:

What is the best hairstyle for an oblong face?

The best hairstyle for an oblong face is one that adds volume to the sides of your face. This can be achieved through layers, curls, or waves. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they will only elongate your face even more.

What length should my hair be?

Your hair should hit at or above your shoulders. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they will only elongate your face even more.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

Now that you have some ideas for hairstyles for oblong faces over 60, go out and experiment! Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
