Best Hot Air Brush For Thick Hair Uk

Written by Eveline Jul 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how hard it can be to style it. It takes a lot of time and effort to get it to look good. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will be discussing the best hot air brush for thick hair UK. We will cover everything from how to choose the right hot air brush to some tips on how to use it.

Description

A hot air brush is a hair tool that uses hot air to style your hair. It's like a combination of a hair dryer and a brush. It's perfect for thick hair because it can help you dry and style your hair at the same time. The hot air brush can give you a salon-worthy blowout look in the comfort of your own home.

How to Choose the Right Hot Air Brush

Choosing the right hot air brush for your thick hair can be a daunting task. There are so many options out there, and it's hard to know which one is the best. Here are some things to consider when choosing a hot air brush:

Barrel Size

The barrel size of the hot air brush is important. For thick hair, you'll want a larger barrel size. This will help you cover more surface area and save you time.

Bristles

The bristles of the hot air brush are also important. For thick hair, you'll want bristles that are strong and can handle the thickness of your hair. Look for a hot air brush with nylon or boar bristles.

Heat Settings

The heat settings of the hot air brush are important too. For thick hair, you'll want a hot air brush with higher heat settings. This will help you style your hair faster.

Step by Step Guide on How to Use a Hot Air Brush on Thick Hair

Using a hot air brush on thick hair can be tricky. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use a hot air brush on your thick hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from the heat of the hot air brush.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to style your hair.

Step 3: Start Styling Your Hair

Start styling your hair with the hot air brush. Use the larger barrel size to cover more surface area. Use the higher heat settings to style your hair faster.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat the process until you've styled all of your hair. Once you're done, you can use a hair spray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Using a Hot Air Brush on Thick Hair

Here are some tips on how to use a hot air brush on your thick hair:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant Spray

Always use a heat protectant spray on your hair before using a hot air brush. This will protect your hair from the heat of the hot air brush.

Tip 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to style your hair with the hot air brush.

Tip 3: Use the Right Barrel Size

Use the right barrel size for your hair. For thick hair, you'll want a larger barrel size to cover more surface area.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a hot air brush on wet hair?

A: No, you should never use a hot air brush on wet hair. Always towel-dry your hair before using a hot air brush.

Q: Can I use a hot air brush every day?

A: Yes, you can use a hot air brush every day. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray on your hair before using the hot air brush.

Q: Can I curl my hair with a hot air brush?

A: Yes, you can curl your hair with a hot air brush. Just use the smaller barrel size and wrap your hair around the brush.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Here are some hair care tips for your thick hair:

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Tip 2: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Don't overwash your hair. This can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

In conclusion, choosing the best hot air brush for your thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy blowout look in the comfort of your own home. Remember to use a heat protectant spray, section your hair, and use the right barrel size for your hair type. Follow these tips, and your hair will look amazing!
