Best Styling Products For Super Fine Hair

Written by William Jul 05, 2023 · 8 min read

Super fine hair can be a challenge to style. It can easily fall flat, lack volume, and become greasy quickly. However, with the right styling products, you can achieve a voluminous and stylish look. In this article, we’ll share the best styling products for super fine hair and how to use them.

How to Style Super Fine Hair

Before we dive into the best styling products for super fine hair, let’s first talk about how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping the natural oils.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils and creams.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume.
  • Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow drying.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day to prevent it from becoming greasy.

Best Styling Products for Super Fine Hair

When it comes to styling super fine hair, you want to use lightweight products that add volume and texture without weighing down your hair. Here are some of the best products:

Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a must-have for anyone with super fine hair. It helps to absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair. Simply spray it onto your roots and massage it in with your fingers. We recommend Batiste Dry Shampoo.

Volumizing Mousse

Volumizing mousse is a great product for adding body to your hair. Apply it to damp hair and blow dry your hair upside down to create volume. We recommend Moroccanoil Volumizing Mousse.

Texturizing Spray

Texturizing spray is perfect for adding texture and definition to your hair. Spray it onto dry hair and scrunch your hair with your fingers to create beachy waves. We recommend Oribe Dry Texturizing Spray.

Hairspray

Hairspray is essential for keeping your style in place. Look for a lightweight hairspray that adds hold without making your hair stiff. We recommend L'Oreal Paris Elnett Satin Hairspray.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Super Fine Hair

Now that you know the best styling products for super fine hair, here’s a step-by-step guide to styling it:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to your roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair upside down using a round brush to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, spray a texturizing spray onto your hair and scrunch it with your fingers to create texture.
  5. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Super Fine Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling super fine hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it’s gentler on your hair than a brush.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs about Styling Super Fine Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling super fine hair:

Can I use hair oil on super fine hair?

Hair oil can weigh down super fine hair, so it’s best to avoid it. If you really want to use hair oil, apply it only to the ends of your hair.

Should I blow dry my hair upside down?

Yes, blowing dry your hair upside down is a great way to add volume to your hair.

Can I use a curling iron on super fine hair?

Yes, you can use a curling iron on super fine hair, but remember to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings.

Hair Care Tips for Super Fine Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for super fine hair:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip the natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it’s gentler on your hair than a brush.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils and creams.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, styling super fine hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a voluminous and stylish look. Remember to use lightweight products that add volume and texture without weighing down your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. With these tips, you’ll be able to rock your super fine hair with confidence.
