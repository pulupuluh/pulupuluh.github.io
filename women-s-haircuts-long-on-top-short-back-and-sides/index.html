Description

Women's haircuts long on top short back and sides are becoming increasingly popular. This type of haircut is perfect for those who want to keep their hair long on top while having a shorter cut on the back and sides. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways, from sleek and polished to messy and textured. This haircut is also great for those who want to add volume and dimension to their hair.

How to

Before getting the haircut, it's important to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting women's hair. Once you've found a stylist, bring in a photo of the haircut you want. This will help the stylist understand the look you're going for.

Choosing the Right Length

The length of the hair on top will depend on personal preference and face shape. For those with round faces, longer hair on top can help elongate the face. For those with square faces, shorter hair on top can help soften the angles of the face.

Creating the Cut

The stylist will start by cutting the back and sides of the hair. This can be done with clippers or scissors. The length of the back and sides will depend on personal preference and the desired look. Once the back and sides are cut, the stylist will move on to the hair on top. The hair on top can be layered to add volume and texture. The stylist may also use a razor to create a more textured look.

Step by step

  1. Find a stylist who is experienced in cutting women's hair
  2. Bring in a photo of the haircut you want
  3. Choose the right length for the hair on top
  4. Cut the back and sides of the hair
  5. Layer the hair on top to add volume and texture
  6. Use a razor to create a more textured look

Tips

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the hair
  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to create texture and hold
  • Blow dry the hair on top to create volume
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

FAQ

Can women with curly hair get this haircut?

Yes, women with curly hair can get this haircut. However, the stylist may need to adjust the length of the hair on top to accommodate for the curls.

How often should I get this haircut?

This haircut should be maintained every 4-6 weeks to keep the length on top and the back and sides looking balanced.

Hair Care

To keep this haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of the hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner and avoid using too much heat on the hair. Use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools and avoid brushing the hair too aggressively. Regular trims will also help keep the haircut looking fresh.
