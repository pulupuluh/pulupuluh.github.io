Shaggy Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Aprilia Aug 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you in your 50s and struggling to find a hairstyle that suits your thick curly hair? Well, shaggy hairstyles are the perfect option for you. They add texture, volume, and movement to your hair, making it look younger and more stylish. In this article, we will guide you on how to achieve shaggy hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50, along with some tips and hair care routines to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

Description

Shaggy hairstyles are popular among women of all ages, but they are especially flattering for women over 50. They add a youthful touch to your hair while also being low maintenance. Thick curly hair can be challenging to manage, but shaggy hairstyles make it easier by adding layers and texture. You can choose from short, medium, or long shaggy hairstyles depending on your preference and face shape.

How to Achieve Shaggy Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair Over 50

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve shaggy hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. These products are gentle on your hair and won't strip away its natural oils. Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so conditioning is essential to keep it moisturized.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Using a microfiber towel, gently squeeze out the excess water from your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as it can cause frizz and damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-in Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner helps to detangle your hair and keep it moisturized. Apply a dime-sized amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 4: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Curl enhancing products help to define your curls and add texture to your hair. Apply a quarter-sized amount of curl enhancing product to your hair, scrunching it from the ends up to the roots.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, dry your hair on low heat. Flip your head upside down and scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it. This technique helps to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 6: Add Layers

Adding layers to your hair helps to create a shaggy look. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that frame your face and blend with the rest of your hair.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand or iron to create loose curls. You can also use a straightener to create waves. Finish off with a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Shaggy Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair Over 50

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling shaggy hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality curl enhancing product to define your curls.
  • Regularly trim your hair to get rid of split ends and keep it looking healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQs

1. What face shapes suit shaggy hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50?

Shaggy hairstyles are versatile and can suit most face shapes. However, if you have a round face, avoid shaggy hairstyles with too much volume on the sides as they can make your face appear wider.

2. Can I achieve shaggy hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50 at home?

Yes, you can achieve shaggy hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50 at home by following our step-by-step guide. However, if you are unsure, it is best to visit a hairstylist who can give you the perfect shaggy hairstyle according to your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care Routine for Shaggy Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair Over 50

Here is a hair care routine you can follow to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner twice a week.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner and curl enhancing product to your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Regularly trim your hair to get rid of split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Shaggy hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50 are a great way to add texture and volume to your hair. With the right hair care routine and styling techniques, you can achieve a youthful and stylish look. Follow our guide and tips to achieve the perfect shaggy hairstyle for your hair type and face shape.
