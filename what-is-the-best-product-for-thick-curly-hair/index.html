What Is The Best Product For Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 18, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the right products that can effectively manage and enhance your curls. But fret not, for we’ve compiled a comprehensive guide on what is the best product for thick curly hair. In this article, we’ll discuss everything from the most effective hair care routines to the best hair products that you can use to make your curls look their best.

Why Do You Need Special Products for Thick Curly Hair?

Thick curly hair is unique in its texture and requires specialized products that can enhance its natural beauty. Regular hair products may not be able to provide the necessary moisture and definition that your curls need, which is why it’s crucial to choose the right products that are specifically designed for thick curly hair.

How to Choose the Best Product for Thick Curly Hair

Choosing the right product for your thick curly hair can be overwhelming, given the vast array of options available in the market. Here are a few things to keep in mind when choosing the best product for your hair:

1. Look for Products with Moisturizing Ingredients

Thick curly hair tends to be dry, and therefore, it’s essential to choose products that are rich in moisturizing ingredients such as shea butter, jojoba oil, and coconut oil. These ingredients can help hydrate your hair, prevent breakage, and promote hair growth.

2. Consider the Hold Factor

The hold factor of a product determines how well it can define your curls and keep them in place. If you prefer a more natural look, opt for a product with a lighter hold factor. However, if you want a more defined and structured look, choose a product with a stronger hold factor.

3. Avoid Products with Harsh Chemicals

Thick curly hair is more prone to damage from harsh chemicals, such as sulfates, parabens, and alcohol. Therefore, it’s important to choose products that are free from these harmful ingredients to prevent further damage to your hair.

Top Products for Thick Curly Hair

Now that you know what to look for in a product for your thick curly hair, let’s take a look at some of the best products available in the market:

1. Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie

The Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie is a leave-in conditioner that can define your curls while keeping them hydrated. It’s made with natural ingredients such as coconut oil, hibiscus flower extract, and silk protein, which can promote healthy hair growth and prevent breakage.

2. DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler

The DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler is a versatile product that can define, moisturize, and enhance your curls. It’s made with coconut oil, jojoba protein, and tapioca starch, which can provide long-lasting hold and prevent frizz.

3. Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Heat and Humidity Gel

The Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Heat and Humidity Gel is a lightweight gel that can protect your curls from heat and humidity. It’s made with wheat protein and hydrolyzed collagen, which can strengthen your hair and prevent breakage.

Hair Care Routine for Thick Curly Hair

Aside from using the right products, it’s also crucial to follow a proper hair care routine to keep your curls healthy and well-defined. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to care for your thick curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair with a Moisturizing Shampoo

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo that’s free from sulfates and parabens. These ingredients can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes. This can help hydrate your hair and prevent breakage.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to provide additional moisture and help define your curls.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Apply a styling product to your hair, depending on your preferred hold factor. You can use a gel, cream, or mousse to define your curls and prevent frizz.

Step 5: Air Dry Your Hair

Avoid using a blow dryer and let your hair air dry to prevent damage and maintain your curls’ natural shape.

Tips for Managing Thick Curly Hair

Here are a few additional tips that can help you manage your thick curly hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. This can prevent breakage and preserve the shape of your curls.

2. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can prevent frizz and breakage by reducing friction between your hair and the pillow.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can prevent split ends and breakage, which can help your hair grow healthier and stronger.

FAQ

Q: Can I use regular hair products on my thick curly hair?

A: Regular hair products may not be effective in managing and enhancing thick curly hair. It’s best to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type.

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

A: It’s recommended to wash your hair once or twice a week to prevent dryness and breakage.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my thick curly hair?

A: Using a leave-in conditioner, avoiding harsh chemicals, and sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz in your thick curly hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the best product for your thick curly hair can be challenging, but with the right knowledge and products, you can achieve healthy, well-defined curls. Remember to look for products with moisturizing ingredients, avoid harsh chemicals, and follow a proper hair care routine to keep your curls looking their best.
