Is It Good To Have Short Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to hair, one of the most frequently asked questions is whether it's good to have short hair or not. Short hair has become a trend in recent years, and many people have opted to chop off their locks for a fresh new look. In this article, we will discuss the pros and cons of having short hair and provide tips on how to maintain it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair can be a great choice for many reasons. Firstly, it's low maintenance, which is perfect for people with busy schedules. You don't have to spend hours brushing, detangling, and styling your hair. Secondly, it's versatile, and you can style it in many different ways, including messy buns, sleek ponytails, and pixie cuts.

However, short hair isn't for everyone. It can highlight certain facial features, such as a large forehead or ears, which some people may not feel comfortable with. Additionally, it may not suit all hair types, and some people may find that their hair looks too thin or lacks volume with a shorter cut.

How to

If you're considering getting a short haircut, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, you should choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type. You can look for inspiration online or consult with your hairstylist for advice.

Secondly, you should be prepared for the upkeep. Although short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, it still needs to be trimmed regularly to maintain its shape. You should also invest in good hair products, such as a quality shampoo and conditioner, to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Step-by-Step

If you've already decided to take the plunge and get a short haircut, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  2. Bring pictures to your hairstylist to ensure you're on the same page.
  3. Trust your hairstylist's advice and expertise.
  4. Be prepared for the upkeep and invest in good hair products.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the most of your short haircut:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you.
  • Try different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and scarves, to add some variety to your looks.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to embrace your natural texture, whether it's curly, wavy, or straight.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the style you choose. Some short haircuts, such as pixie cuts, can make your face look more angular, while others, such as bobs, can soften your features.

Q: Can I still put my hair up in a ponytail with short hair?

A: Yes! You can still put your hair up in a ponytail or messy bun, although it may require some bobby pins and hair ties to keep everything in place.

Q: Will my short hair grow back quickly?

A: Hair growth varies from person to person, but on average, hair grows about half an inch per month. With regular trims and good hair care, your short hair can grow back to a longer length over time.

Hair Care

Short hair still requires some maintenance to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

Overall, having short hair can be a great choice for many people. It's low maintenance, versatile, and can be a fresh new look. However, it's important to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type and to be prepared for the upkeep. With the right care and styling, your short hair can look fabulous and be a great confidence boost.
