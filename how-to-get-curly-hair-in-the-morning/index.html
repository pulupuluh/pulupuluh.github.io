How To Get Curly Hair In The Morning A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Do you want to achieve curly hair in the morning without spending hours in front of the mirror? If so, you've come to the right place! In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you get curly hair in the morning effortlessly. We'll cover everything from how to prep your hair the night before to which products to use in the morning. Let's get started!

Prepping Your Hair the Night Before

The key to achieving curly hair in the morning is to prep your hair the night before. Here's how:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help hydrate your hair and make it more manageable.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Next, apply a curl enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to your damp hair. This will help define your curls and keep them in place overnight.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into 4-6 sections and twist each section into a loose bun. Secure each bun with a hair tie or clip.

Step 4: Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Finally, sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and keep your curls intact.

Tip: If you don't have a silk pillowcase, you can wrap your hair in a silk scarf instead.

Using the Right Products

In the morning, start by removing the hair ties or clips and gently shaking out your hair. Here are some products you can use to enhance your curls:

Curl Enhancing Cream

A curl enhancing cream can help define your curls and add moisture to your hair. Apply a dime-sized amount to your hair and scrunch your hair to enhance your curls.

Hair Oil

A hair oil can help add shine and reduce frizz. Apply a small amount to your hair and scrunch your hair to distribute the oil evenly.

Scrunching Your Hair

Scrunching your hair is a great way to enhance your curls and add volume. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Apply Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel.

Step 2: Scrunch Your Hair

Using your hands, scrunch your hair from the ends up to the roots. This will help define your curls and add volume.

Blow Drying Your Hair

If you have time, you can blow dry your hair to enhance your curls even more. Here's how:

Step 1: Apply Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel.

Step 2: Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use it to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Touching Up Your Curls

If your curls start to lose their shape throughout the day, here are some tips to touch them up:

Use a Curl Refreshing Spray

A curl refreshing spray can help revive your curls and add moisture. Simply spritz the product onto your hair and scrunch your hair to distribute the product.

Use a Curling Iron

If you have a few stubborn curls that won't cooperate, you can use a curling iron to touch them up. Wrap the strand of hair around the curling iron and hold it for a few seconds. Release the curl and let it cool before touching it.

FAQs

Can I get curly hair in the morning without using heat?

Yes, you can achieve curly hair in the morning without using heat. Prepping your hair the night before and using the right products can help enhance your curls without having to use heat.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash your curly hair once or twice a week to prevent it from drying out.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your curly hair:

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help prevent breakage and keep your curls intact.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as much as possible. Heat can damage your hair and cause it to lose its shape.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture to your hair and keep it healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve curly hair in the morning effortlessly. Remember to prep your hair the night before, use the right products, and scrunch your hair to enhance your curls. Happy styling!
