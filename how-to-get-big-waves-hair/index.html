How To Get Big Waves Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both fun and glamorous, look no further than big waves hair! This style is perfect for a night out, a special occasion, or just for everyday wear. In this guide, we'll show you how to get big waves hair step-by-step, along with some tips and tricks for making the most of your style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Big Waves Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting the Perfect Big Waves Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Big Waves Hair

How to Get Big Waves Hair

Big waves hair is a style that can be achieved with either a curling iron or a flat iron. It's up to you which tool you choose, but keep in mind that a curling iron will give you looser waves, while a flat iron will give you tighter waves.

The first step in getting big waves hair is to make sure your hair is clean, dry, and tangle-free. You can either air dry your hair or blow dry it with a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat styling tool you'll be using. This is an important step that you should never skip!

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you've prepped your hair, it's time to start styling! Follow these steps to get big waves hair:

  1. Divide your hair into sections. Depending on how thick your hair is, you may need to divide it into more or fewer sections. Clip the sections you're not working on out of the way.
  2. Take a section of hair and clamp your curling iron or flat iron around it. If you're using a curling iron, wrap the hair around the barrel, leaving the ends out. If you're using a flat iron, twist the hair away from your face before clamping down.
  3. Hold the iron in place for a few seconds before releasing the hair. Repeat this process with the rest of your hair, alternating the direction you curl or twist each section to create a more natural look.
  4. Once all your hair is curled or twisted, run your fingers through it to break up the waves and create a more natural look.
  5. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Getting the Perfect Big Waves Hair

Getting big waves hair can take some practice, so here are some tips to help you get the perfect look:

  • Use a smaller barrel curling iron for tighter waves or a larger barrel for looser waves.
  • Twist your hair away from your face for a more flattering look.
  • Don't forget to curl the hair around your face away from your face to create a more flattering look.
  • Alternate the direction you curl or twist each section for a more natural look.
  • Use a texturizing spray on your hair before curling or twisting to give it more grip and hold.

FAQs

How long will big waves hair last?

Big waves hair can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use to hold the style in place.

Can I get big waves hair if my hair is naturally straight?

Yes! You can achieve big waves hair with a flat iron by twisting the hair away from your face before clamping down.

Do I need to use a heat protectant spray?

Yes! Using a heat protectant spray is important to prevent damage to your hair from the heat styling tool you're using.

Hair Care for Big Waves Hair

To keep your big waves hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose braid or bun to prevent tangles.

Now that you know how to get big waves hair, it's time to give it a try! With a little practice and some patience, you'll be rocking this glamorous style in no time.
