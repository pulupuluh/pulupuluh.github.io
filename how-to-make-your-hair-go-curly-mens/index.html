How To Make Your Hair Go Curly Mens A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to switch things up with some curls? Look no further because we have a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair go curly mens. Whether you have short hair or long hair, these tips and tricks can help you achieve the curly look you desire.

Description

Curly hair has always been a popular hairstyle for men, and it's not hard to see why. It's a versatile look that can be styled in many ways. However, not all men are blessed with naturally curly hair. If you're one of those men, don't worry because we've got you covered. This article will teach you how to make your hair go curly mens without damaging your hair or spending a fortune on expensive products.

How to Make Your Hair Go Curly Mens: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in making your hair go curly mens is to wash it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair. This will help to moisturize your hair and make it easier to curl. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply Mousse or Gel

Apply a small amount of mousse or gel to your hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place. Make sure to spread the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections. The smaller the sections, the tighter your curls will be. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

Use a curling iron to curl each section of your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release it. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 5: Scrunch Your Hair

Once you've curled all of your hair, scrunch it with your hands. This will help to loosen up the curls and give them a more natural look.

Step 6: Apply Hairspray

Apply a small amount of hairspray to your hair to keep the curls in place. Don't use too much hairspray, or your hair will look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Making Your Hair Go Curly Mens

- Use a diffuser on your blow dryer to help define your curls.

- Don't brush your hair after you've curled it, or you'll lose the curls.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

- Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.

FAQ

Can I make my hair go curly without using a curling iron?

Yes, you can use a hair straightener to create curls. Wrap small sections of your hair around the straightener and hold it in place for a few seconds. Then, release the hair and let it cool down before scrunching it with your hands.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls will last for several hours, depending on how much hairspray you use and how well you maintain them. Sleeping on a silk pillowcase and avoiding brushing your hair can help to preserve your curls for longer.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.

- Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage.

- Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair go curly mens and rock a new look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
