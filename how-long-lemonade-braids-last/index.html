How Long Do Lemonade Braids Last Tips And Hair Care For Long Lasting Braids

Written by Daniel Feb 20, 2023 · 7 min read

Lemonade braids are a popular protective hairstyle that can last for weeks. If you’re curious about how long lemonade braids last, this guide will give you all the information you need. In this article, we’ll cover how to properly care for your braids, how long they typically last, and tips for keeping them looking fresh.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Lemonade Braids
  • How Long Do Lemonade Braids Last?
  • Tips for Long-Lasting Braids
  • FAQs About Lemonade Braids
  • Hair Care for Lemonade Braids

How to Get Lemonade Braids

Lemonade braids are a type of cornrow braids that are styled to look like lemonade. They are also known as side braids, Beyoncé braids, or feed-in braids. The braids start off small at the top and gradually get bigger as they go down.

To get lemonade braids, you’ll need to follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair to ensure that it’s clean and moisturized.
  2. Part your hair into sections using a comb. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want your braids to be.
  3. Begin braiding your hair using the feed-in method. This involves adding small amounts of hair as you braid to create a seamless, natural look.
  4. Continue braiding until you reach the desired length. You can add beads or other hair accessories for extra flair.
  5. Repeat the process for each section of hair until your entire head is braided.

How Long Do Lemonade Braids Last?

Lemonade braids can last anywhere from 2 to 8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them. The average lifespan of lemonade braids is around 4-6 weeks.

The longevity of your braids will depend on several factors, including:

  • Your hair type: If you have fine or thin hair, your braids may not last as long as someone with thicker hair.
  • Your hair care routine: Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy braids. If you’re not taking care of your hair, your braids may become frizzy or start to unravel.
  • Your lifestyle: If you’re constantly swimming, sweating, or exposing your hair to harsh weather conditions, your braids may not last as long.

Tips for Long-Lasting Braids

If you want to extend the life of your lemonade braids, here are some tips to follow:

  • Protect your braids at night by wearing a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids and cause buildup.
  • Don’t scratch or pull on your braids, as this can cause them to unravel or become frizzy.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every 1-2 weeks to keep your scalp clean and moisturized.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your braids hydrated and prevent breakage.

FAQs About Lemonade Braids

Here are some frequently asked questions about lemonade braids:

Q: Can I swim or exercise with lemonade braids?

A: Yes, but be sure to wear a swim cap and rinse your hair with clean water afterwards. Sweat and chlorine can cause your braids to become frizzy or start to unravel.

Q: Can I wash my hair with lemonade braids?

A: Yes, but be gentle and use a sulfate-free shampoo. Avoid getting water directly on your braids, as this can cause them to loosen.

Q: Can I wear my lemonade braids in different styles?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different updos, half-up half-down styles, or even wear them in a ponytail. Just be sure not to pull too tight or use heavy hair accessories that can damage your braids.

Hair Care for Lemonade Braids

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy lemonade braids. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every 1-2 weeks to keep your scalp clean and moisturized.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your braids hydrated and prevent breakage.
  • Protect your braids at night by wearing a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids and cause buildup.
  • Don’t scratch or pull on your braids, as this can cause them to unravel or become frizzy.

With proper care, your lemonade braids can last for weeks and keep your hair looking stylish and protected. Follow these tips and enjoy your beautiful braids!
