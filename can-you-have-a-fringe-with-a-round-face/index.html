Can You Have A Fringe With A Round Face

Written by Frank Apr 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone with a round face, you might be wondering if you can pull off a fringe. A fringe, also known as bangs, is a hairstyle where the hair is cut and styled to fall over the forehead. It's a popular hairstyle choice for many people, but can it work for those with a round face? In this article, we'll explore whether or not you can have a fringe with a round face.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into whether or not you can have a fringe with a round face, let's first define what a round face is. A round face is characterized by soft, curved lines and is roughly equal in length and width. It's also known for having full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you may want to create the illusion of length and angles to balance out your features.

How To

The good news is that you can absolutely have a fringe with a round face! There are certain types of fringes that work best for those with a round face. The key is to choose a fringe that creates the illusion of length and angles, thus balancing out your features.

Types of Fringes for Round Faces

Here are some types of fringes that work well for those with a round face:

  • Side-swept fringe: This type of fringe is longer on one side and shorter on the other. It creates the illusion of length and angles, which is perfect for those with a round face.
  • Long fringe: A long fringe that falls just below the eyebrows is another great option for those with a round face. It helps to create the illusion of length and angles.
  • Asymmetrical fringe: An asymmetrical fringe is longer on one side and shorter on the other. It helps to create the illusion of angles and can balance out a round face.

Step by Step

Here's how to get a fringe with a round face:

  1. Decide on the type of fringe you want. Side-swept, long, and asymmetrical fringes work well for those with a round face.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.
  3. Get your fringe cut and styled by a professional.
  4. Maintain your fringe by trimming it regularly to keep it at the desired length.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a fringe with a round face:

  • Choose a fringe that creates the illusion of length and angles to balance out your features.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.
  • Consider getting layers to add volume and movement to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about having a fringe with a round face:

Can I have a blunt fringe with a round face?

A blunt fringe can be tricky for those with a round face. It can make the face appear more round and accentuate full cheeks. It's best to opt for a fringe that creates the illusion of length and angles.

Can I have a short fringe with a round face?

A short fringe can work for those with a round face, but it's important to choose a style that creates the illusion of length and angles. A side-swept fringe or an asymmetrical fringe can be a good choice.

Hair Care

To keep your fringe looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your fringe with heat tools.
  • Trim your fringe regularly to keep it at the desired length.

So, can you have a fringe with a round face? Absolutely! Just be sure to choose a style that creates the illusion of length and angles to balance out your features. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape, and maintain your fringe with regular trims and proper hair care.
