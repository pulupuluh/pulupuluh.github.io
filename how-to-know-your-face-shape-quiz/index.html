How To Know Your Face Shape Quiz A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Knowing your face shape is essential when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle and makeup. However, many people find it challenging to determine their face shape. But fear not, we've got you covered! In this article, we will guide you through a step-by-step process of taking the "how to know your face shape quiz."

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step-by-Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the quiz, let's first understand the importance of knowing your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle and makeup will suit you the most. Once you know your face shape, you can choose the right hairstyle to enhance your features and complement your face shape.

How To

The "how to know your face shape quiz" is a simple and easy-to-follow process that will help you determine your face shape. There are two ways to take the quiz:

  • Online quizzes
  • Manual measurement

You can choose any method that suits you the most. However, we recommend going for the manual measurement method, as it is more accurate.

Step-by-Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to taking the "how to know your face shape quiz" using the manual measurement method:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror.
  2. Pull your hair back and away from your face.
  3. Take a measuring tape and measure the following:
    • Forehead: Measure the distance across the widest part of your forehead.
    • Cheekbones: Measure the width of your cheekbones from one end to the other.
    • Jawline: Measure from the bottom of your ear to the tip of your chin.
    • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.
  4. Once you have all the measurements, compare them to the following face shapes:
    • Oval
    • Round
    • Square
    • Heart
    • Diamond
    • Rectangle
  5. Based on your measurements, determine which face shape you have.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while taking the "how to know your face shape quiz":

  • Make sure you are standing in front of a well-lit mirror.
  • Take accurate measurements to get the correct results.
  • If you are unsure about your measurements, ask someone to help you.
  • Don't worry if your face shape falls between two categories. It's common, and you can choose hairstyles that suit both shapes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about "how to know your face shape quiz":

1. What is the most common face shape?

The oval face shape is considered the most common face shape.

2. Can my face shape change over time?

No, your face shape remains the same throughout your life.

3. Can I have a combination of face shapes?

Yes, it's possible to have a combination of face shapes. For example, you can have a square jawline and a heart-shaped forehead.

Hair Care

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right hairstyle that complements your features. Here are some tips:

  • Oval: You can rock any hairstyle, but try to avoid hairstyles that cover your face.
  • Round: Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to your crown area.
  • Square: Soften your angular features with layers and wispy bangs.
  • Heart: Balance your face shape with chin-length hairstyles and side-swept bangs.
  • Diamond: Emphasize your cheekbones with hairstyles that add volume to the sides.
  • Rectangle: Create the illusion of width with layered hairstyles and side-swept bangs.

Now that you know how to determine your face shape, go ahead and try out different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the most. Remember, confidence is key, and if you feel good, you'll look good!
