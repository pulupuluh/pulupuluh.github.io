Best Haircuts For Small Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Apr 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a small face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want something that will accentuate your features without overwhelming them. Fortunately, there are plenty of options out there. In this guide, we'll explore the best haircuts for small faces, from short to long and everything in between.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Small Face
  • Short Haircuts for Small Faces
  • Medium Haircuts for Small Faces
  • Long Haircuts for Small Faces

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Small Face

When it comes to finding the right haircut for your small face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that will add volume to your hair and make your face appear fuller.
  • Avoid styles that are too long or too short.
  • Consider bangs to help balance out your features.
  • Experiment with layers to add dimension to your hair.
  • Consult with a professional stylist to find the best haircut for your face shape.

Short Haircuts for Small Faces

Short haircuts can be a great option for small faces, as they can help to elongate the neck and make the face appear longer. Here are a few of the best short haircuts for small faces:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bowl cut
  • Bob cut
  • Asymmetrical cut

Short haircuts can be easily maintained with regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

Medium Haircuts for Small Faces

Medium haircuts can be a good choice for small faces, as they can add volume and help balance out the features. Here are some of the best medium haircuts for small faces:

  • Shoulder-length cut
  • Layered cut
  • Blunt cut
  • Messy waves

Medium haircuts require regular trims every 6-8 weeks to maintain their shape.

Long Haircuts for Small Faces

Long haircuts can be tricky for small faces, as they can easily overwhelm the features. However, with the right cut and styling, long hair can look beautiful on small faces. Here are some of the best long haircuts for small faces:

  • Layered cut with face-framing layers
  • Straight cut with side-swept bangs
  • Beachy waves
  • Half-up, half-down style

Long haircuts require regular trims every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your small face, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent oil buildup.
  • Use a heat protectant before styling with heat tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have a small face?

A: Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have a small face. Just make sure to choose a cut that is balanced and not too overwhelming.

Q: Are bangs a good choice for small faces?

A: Yes, bangs can be a good choice for small faces, as they can help to balance out the features. Just make sure to choose a style that works with your hair texture and face shape.

Hair Care for Small Faces

Regardless of the haircut you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for small faces:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your small face, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering look that will make you feel confident and stylish.
