How To Use Thinning Shears On Short Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Feb 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this article, we will go over the steps to properly use thinning shears on short curly hair. Thinning shears are a great tool for reducing bulk in hair, creating layers, and adding texture. However, using them on curly hair can be tricky. Follow these steps for a successful and fabulous haircut!

Description

Thinning shears are scissors with teeth that cut some hair and leave some uncut. They are used to thin out hair and reduce bulk. They are a great tool for creating layers and adding texture to short curly hair. However, it is important to use them properly to avoid damaging the hair.

How to Use Thinning Shears on Short Curly Hair

Step 1: Clean and Dry Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Dry your hair completely. It is important to work with clean and dry hair to ensure an even cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate. This will make it easier to work with your hair and ensure an even cut.

Step 3: Use the Thinning Shears

Hold the thinning shears at a 45-degree angle and cut into the hair in a downward motion. Make sure to cut only a small amount of hair at a time. You can always go back and cut more if needed.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

After thinning out each section, blend the hair by combing it with a regular comb. This will help to ensure the hair is even and smooth.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Once you have thinned out all of the sections, check for evenness. If there are any areas that are too thick, go back and thin them out more.

Tips

  • Start by cutting a small amount of hair. You can always cut more if needed.
  • Use a light hand when cutting with thinning shears. It is easy to cut too much hair at once.
  • Work slowly and carefully to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Always use sharp thinning shears to ensure a clean cut.

FAQ

Q: Can thinning shears be used on all types of hair?

A: Yes, thinning shears can be used on all types of hair. However, they are particularly useful for thick and curly hair.

Q: Will thinning shears damage my hair?

A: Thinning shears, when used properly, should not damage your hair. However, it is important to use them carefully and not cut too much hair at once.

Hair Care

After using thinning shears on your short curly hair, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Avoid using heat tools, as they can damage your hair. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser on low heat. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to use thinning shears on short curly hair, you can create a fabulous and textured haircut. Remember to work slowly and carefully to avoid damaging your hair. With practice, you will become a pro at using thinning shears!
