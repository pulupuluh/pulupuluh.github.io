Does Short Curly Hair Suit Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jul 14, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering if short curly hair will suit you. The answer is: absolutely! Short curly hair can look fantastic on round faces, as long as you choose the right style and cut. In this guide, we’ll take you through everything you need to know about rocking short curly hair with a round face.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Short Curly Hair Style for Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair on Round Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Curly Hair on Round Faces

How to Choose the Right Short Curly Hair Style for Round Faces

The key to choosing the right short curly hair style for a round face is to create balance. You want to avoid styles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for styles that draw attention to the top of your head and add height.

Some great short curly hair styles for round faces include:

  • Curly pixie cut
  • Bob with side-swept bangs
  • Short curly shag
  • Tousled bob

When choosing a style, also consider the length. A shorter cut can make your face appear longer and slimmer, while a longer cut may add too much volume to the sides of your face.

Color Considerations

When it comes to color, adding highlights or lowlights can help create dimension and add interest to your style. However, be sure to avoid chunky highlights that add width to your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair for Round Faces

Now that you’ve chosen your short curly hair style, it’s time to learn how to style it. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a curl-defining product, such as mousse or curl cream, to your hair
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching it as you go
  4. If needed, use a curling wand to define any loose curls or add more curl to your hair
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair on Round Faces

Short curly hair can be high-maintenance, but with the right care, you can keep your style looking fabulous. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness
  • Avoid brushing your hair, as this can cause frizz
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to reduce frizz
  • Avoid using too much heat styling, which can damage your hair

FAQs

Can I still wear my hair straight with a round face?

Absolutely! While short curly hair can be a great choice for round faces, it’s not the only option. If you prefer straight hair, go for it. Just be sure to choose a style that creates balance and doesn’t add too much width to your face.

What if I have thick hair?

If you have thick hair, a short curly hair style can be a great choice, as it can help reduce volume. However, be sure to choose a style that works with your hair thickness and doesn’t add too much width to your face.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair on Round Faces

To keep your short curly hair looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your style

With these tips and tricks, you can rock short curly hair with a round face and look fabulous!
