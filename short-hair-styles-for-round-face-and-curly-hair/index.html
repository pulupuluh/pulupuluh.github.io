Short Hair Styles For Round Face And Curly Hair

Written by William Apr 28, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face and curly hair, finding the right short hair style can be challenging. Luckily, there are plenty of options that can flatter your features and embrace your natural curls. In this article, we will explore some of the best short hair styles for round face and curly hair, as well as provide tips and advice for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Short Hair Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair
  • FAQs About Short Hair Styles for Round Face and Curly Hair
  • Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

How to Choose the Right Short Hair Style

When selecting a short hair style for round face and curly hair, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a cut that flatters your face shape. If you have a round face, consider a short style with layers or angles to create the illusion of length.
  • Embrace your curls. Short hair styles can work well with curly hair, so don't be afraid to let your natural texture shine.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of styling.
  • Consult with a stylist. A professional stylist can help you choose a short hair style that works best for you and provide advice on maintenance and styling.

Short Hair Styles for Round Face and Curly Hair

Here are some of the best short hair styles for round face and curly hair:

  • Pixie cut: A classic short hair style that can work well with curly hair. Consider adding layers or texture to create volume and movement.
  • Bob cut: A versatile style that can be customized to flatter your face shape. Choose a length that falls just below the chin and add layers or angles to create dimension.
  • Shag cut: A layered style that can add texture and volume to curly hair. Consider adding bangs or a side part to create a more dramatic look.
  • Cropped cut: A very short style that can be edgy and modern. Consider adding a side part or texture to create interest.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair

Styling short curly hair can be easy with the right techniques. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product that works well with your hair type, such as a curl-defining cream or mousse.
  3. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Scrunch your hair gently to encourage your natural curls.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.
  6. Use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Keeping your short curly hair looking its best requires some maintenance. Here are some tips to help:

  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and frizz-free.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and disrupt your natural texture.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.

FAQs About Short Hair Styles for Round Face and Curly Hair

What is the best short hair style for round face and curly hair?

There are several short hair styles that can work well with round face and curly hair, such as the pixie cut, bob cut, shag cut, and cropped cut. Consult with a stylist to find the best style for you.

Can I wear bangs with short curly hair?

Yes, bangs can look great with short curly hair. Consider a side-swept or wispy style to frame your face.

How often should I wash my short curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go several days between washes. Experiment to find a schedule that works best for you.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

To keep your short curly hair looking its best, it's important to practice good hair care habits. Here are some tips to help:

  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair to keep your curls hydrated and defined.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, rather than a traditional towel, to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and disrupt your natural texture.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and reduce frizz.

With the right short hair style and hair care routine, you can embrace your round face and curly hair with confidence. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
