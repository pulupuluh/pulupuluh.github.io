Hair Thinning On Top Of Head Male Causes Prevention And Treatment

Written by Bardi Mar 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a male experiencing hair thinning on the top of your head? You're not alone. Hair thinning is a common issue for men, especially as they age. In fact, by the age of 50, about 85% of men have significant hair thinning. But don't worry, there are ways to prevent and treat hair thinning on top of the head. In this article, we'll discuss the causes of hair thinning, prevention tips, and treatment options.

What causes hair thinning on top of the head?

Hair thinning on top of the head is often caused by male pattern baldness, which is a genetic condition. Male pattern baldness is caused by the hormone DHT, which shrinks hair follicles and makes them unable to produce thick hair. Other causes of hair thinning on top of the head include stress, poor nutrition, certain medications, and underlying medical conditions.

How can you prevent hair thinning on top of the head?

While you can't prevent male pattern baldness, there are things you can do to slow down hair thinning on top of the head. Here are some prevention tips:

Eat a balanced diet

A healthy and balanced diet can help prevent hair thinning. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals. Foods that are good for hair health include eggs, nuts, fish, and leafy greens.

Reduce stress

Stress can contribute to hair thinning. To reduce stress, try practicing relaxation techniques like yoga or meditation. You can also try exercising regularly or getting enough sleep.

Avoid harsh hair treatments

Hair treatments like perms, bleach, and relaxers can damage hair and contribute to hair thinning. Try to avoid these kinds of treatments, or at least limit their use.

What are the treatment options for hair thinning on top of the head?

If you're already experiencing hair thinning on top of the head, there are treatment options available. Here are some of the most effective options:

Medications

There are two FDA-approved medications for hair thinning on top of the head: finasteride and minoxidil. Finasteride is a pill that blocks the hormone DHT, while minoxidil is a topical solution that helps hair grow thicker and fuller.

Hair transplant

A hair transplant is a surgical procedure that involves taking hair from one part of the body and transplanting it onto the scalp. This can be an effective treatment for hair thinning on top of the head, but it can be expensive and requires downtime for recovery.

Hair fibers and concealers

Hair fibers and concealers are products that can be sprinkled onto the scalp to make hair appear thicker. These products are temporary and need to be reapplied regularly, but they can be a good option for those who don't want to undergo surgery or take medication.

Hair care tips for men with hair thinning on top of the head

Finally, here are some hair care tips for men with hair thinning on top of the head:

Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain ingredients like biotin and keratin, which can strengthen hair.

Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like blow dryers and straighteners can damage hair and make it more prone to breakage. Try to avoid using these tools, or at least use them on a low heat setting.

Get regular haircuts

Regular haircuts can help make hair appear thicker and healthier. Ask your stylist to cut your hair in a way that makes it appear fuller, such as with layers or a textured cut.

FAQ

Is hair thinning on top of the head reversible?

While male pattern baldness is not reversible, there are treatments available that can slow down or even stop hair thinning. Medications like finasteride and minoxidil can be effective for many men.

Can stress cause hair thinning on top of the head?

Yes, stress can contribute to hair thinning. Stress can cause hair to enter the resting phase of the hair growth cycle, which can lead to hair shedding and thinning.

What should I do if I'm experiencing hair thinning on top of the head?

If you're experiencing hair thinning on top of the head, the first step is to see a dermatologist or hair specialist. They can help determine the cause of your hair thinning and recommend treatment options.

In conclusion, hair thinning on top of the head is a common issue for many men. While it can be frustrating, there are ways to prevent and treat hair thinning. By following the prevention tips and treatment options listed above, you can help keep your hair healthy and full.
