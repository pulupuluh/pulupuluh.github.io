Medium Length Hairstyles For Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Luffy Feb 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair tends to lose its natural luster and volume, which can make it difficult to style. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can achieve a youthful and vibrant look that complements your curly hair. In this article, we will discuss some popular medium length hairstyles for curly hair over 50, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Medium length hair is a popular choice for women over 50, as it offers versatility and low-maintenance styling options. Curly hair, in particular, can be difficult to manage, but with the right haircut and styling products, you can achieve a stunning look that accentuates your natural curls.

Whether you prefer a short bob, a shoulder-length cut, or a layered look, there are many options to choose from when it comes to medium length hairstyles for curly hair. It's important to work with a stylist who is experienced in cutting and styling curly hair, as they will be able to recommend the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

How to Style Medium Length Curly Hair

When it comes to styling medium length curly hair, there are a few key tips to keep in mind. First, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that is designed specifically for curly hair. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and prevent frizz.

Next, consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl defining cream to help enhance your natural curls. Apply the product to damp hair and scrunch your hair gently to encourage your curls to form. If you want to add more volume to your hair, consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Finally, consider using a hairspray or other styling product to help hold your curls in place throughout the day. Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to achieving a medium length curly hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl defining cream to your hair.
  3. Scrunch your hair gently to encourage your curls to form.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to add volume to your curls.
  5. Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray or other styling product to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb when your hair is wet.
  • Consider sleeping on a silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles for curly hair over 50:

Q: What is the best haircut for curly hair over 50?

A: The best haircut for curly hair over 50 depends on your face shape and hair type. A layered cut or a shoulder-length bob can be a good option for many women with curly hair.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz in curly hair, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoid using hot tools too often, and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

Q: Can I wear my curly hair up in a bun or ponytail?

A: Yes, you can wear your curly hair up in a bun or ponytail, but make sure to use gentle hair ties and avoid pulling your hair too tightly.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than once or twice a week to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Consider using a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet, as this can help prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your curls and make them look limp.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and youthful look with your medium length curly hair over 50. Experiment with different hairstyles and styling products to find the look that works best for you.
