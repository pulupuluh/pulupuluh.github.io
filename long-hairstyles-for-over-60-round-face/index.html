Long Hairstyles For Over 60 Round Face Tips And Ideas

Written by Oliver Aug 26, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're over 60 and have a round face, you may find it challenging to find long hairstyles that suit you. However, with the right cut and style, you can enhance your features and feel confident and beautiful. In this post, we'll share some tips, ideas, and step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the perfect long hairstyle for your round face.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for Over 60 Round Face

When choosing a long hairstyle for your round face, you want to create the illusion of length and height to balance out your features. Here are some tips:

1. Consider Layers

Layering your hair can create volume and movement, which is ideal for a round face. You can opt for long layers, choppy layers, or face-framing layers to add dimension to your hair.

2. Avoid Blunt Cuts

Blunt cuts can make your face appear wider, so it's best to avoid them if you have a round face. Instead, go for a layered cut that will create a soft and flattering look.

3. Opt for Side Bangs

Side bangs can help to elongate your face and draw attention away from the roundness. You can choose long, wispy bangs or side-swept bangs that graze your cheekbones.

4. Try an Off-Center Part

An off-center part can add asymmetry to your face, creating the illusion of length. You can experiment with different parting styles to see what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Long Hairstyles for Over 60 Round Face

Here's how to achieve some of the most popular long hairstyles for over 60 round face:

1. Long Layered Hair

Long layered hair is a classic and versatile style that suits all face shapes.

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections

Step 3: Cut the first layer at chin level

Step 4: Cut the remaining layers

Step 5: Use a curling iron to add loose waves

2. Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers can soften your features and create a youthful look.

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections

Step 3: Cut the layers around your face

Step 4: Blend the layers into the rest of your hair

Step 5: Use a straightening iron to create a sleek finish

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can add dimension and movement to your hair.

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Step 2: Section your hair and pull the rest back

Step 3: Cut your bangs at an angle

Step 4: Blend the bangs into the rest of your hair

Step 5: Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs

Tips for Maintaining Long Hairstyles for Over 60 Round Face

Here are some tips to keep your long hairstyle looking great:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. For example, if you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Also, avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant. How often you need a trim depends on your hair growth rate and style.

FAQs

1. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt cuts, full bangs, and short curly hair. Instead, opt for styles that add length and height.

2. Can I still have long hair if I'm over 60?

Absolutely! Long hair can look beautiful and youthful at any age, as long as it's maintained properly and suits your face shape and features.

3. How do I know if a long hairstyle suits me?

A good way to know if a long hairstyle suits you is to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. You can also consult with a hairstylist for personalized advice.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hairstyles for Over 60 Round Face

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your long hairstyle looking and feeling healthy:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. This will prevent breakage and minimize hair loss.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it soft, shiny, and nourished. You can use a store-bought conditioner or make your own using natural ingredients like avocado or coconut oil.

3. Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Protect your hair while sleeping by using a silk or satin pillowcase, or wrapping your hair in a silk scarf. This will prevent friction and minimize hair breakage.

With these tips and ideas, you can rock a long hairstyle that flatters your round face and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to consult with a hairstylist for personalized advice and to maintain your hair properly to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
