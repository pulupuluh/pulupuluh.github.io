How Do I Know If Hair Colour Will Suit Me

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Choosing a new hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you are unsure if it will suit you. With so many shades and tones to choose from, it can be difficult to know where to start. However, there are a few things you can do to help determine if a particular hair colour will look good on you or not.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To Know If Hair Colour Will Suit You
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips For Choosing The Right Hair Colour
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips After Colouring Your Hair

Description

Choosing a hair colour that suits you can enhance your natural features, brighten your complexion, and give you a new look. On the other hand, choosing the wrong colour can make you look washed out or even older than you are. Therefore, it's important to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone, eye colour, and overall appearance.

How To Know If Hair Colour Will Suit You

Here are a few things you can do to help determine if a particular hair colour will suit you:

1. Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair colour. It's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural features. Generally, people with warm skin tones look best in warm hair colours like golden browns, auburns, and honey blondes. People with cool skin tones look best in cool hair colours like ash blondes, cool browns, and burgundy reds.

2. Look at Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in determining which hair colour will suit you. If you have blue or green eyes, cool hair colours like ash blondes and cool browns will look great on you. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm hair colours like golden browns and auburns will complement your features.

3. Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

Your natural hair colour can also play a role in determining which hair colour will suit you. If you have dark hair, it's best to stick with darker shades. If you have lighter hair, you can experiment with lighter shades. It's important to choose a shade that is no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair colour for the most natural look.

Step By Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to help you choose the right hair colour:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Look at Your Eye Colour

Determine if you have warm or cool eye colour. If you have blue or green eyes, you have cool eye colour. If you have brown or hazel eyes, you have warm eye colour.

Step 3: Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

Determine your natural hair colour and choose a shade that is no more than two shades lighter or darker for the most natural look.

Tips For Choosing The Right Hair Colour

Here are a few tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

1. Consult a Professional

If you're unsure which hair colour will suit you best, consult a professional hair stylist. They can help you choose a shade that complements your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour.

2. Consider Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair colour. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low-maintenance hair colour that doesn't require frequent touch-ups.

3. Experiment with Temporary Hair Colour

If you're unsure which hair colour will suit you best, experiment with temporary hair colour. This will allow you to try different shades without committing to a permanent colour.

FAQs

Q: How do I know if a hair colour is too light or too dark for me?

A: A good rule of thumb is to choose a shade that is no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair colour.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair colour?

A: This depends on the type of hair colour you choose. Permanent hair colour typically needs to be touched up every four to six weeks, while semi-permanent hair colour can last up to six to eight weeks.

Hair Care Tips After Colouring Your Hair

Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind after colouring your hair:

1. Use Colour-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair colour.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as they can cause your hair colour to fade.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

In conclusion, choosing a hair colour that suits you can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, you can choose a shade that complements your features and enhances your overall appearance. Remember to consult a professional if you're unsure which hair colour will suit you best, and take care of your hair by using colour-safe products and avoiding heat styling.
