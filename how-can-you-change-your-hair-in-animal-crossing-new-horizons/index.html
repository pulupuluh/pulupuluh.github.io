How to Change Your Hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons

Changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons is easy, and can be done from the comfort of your own home. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

Step 1: Unlock the Beauty Salon

The first thing you need to do is unlock the Beauty Salon. To do this, you need to progress through the game until you have unlocked the Able Sisters Tailor Shop. Once you have done this, you will need to talk to Mabel and buy at least one item of clothing from her. After this, the Beauty Salon will be unlocked, and you can start changing your hair.

Step 2: Visit Harriet at the Beauty Salon

Once you have unlocked the Beauty Salon, you need to visit Harriet. She is the owner of the salon and will be able to change your hair for you. You can find her in the back room of the Able Sisters Tailor Shop.

Step 3: Choose Your New Hairstyle

When you talk to Harriet, she will give you the option to change your hair. You can then choose from a range of different hairstyles, including short, medium, and long styles. You can also choose from a variety of colors, allowing you to create a truly unique look.

Step 4: Confirm Your New Look

Once you have chosen your new hairstyle, Harriet will ask you to confirm your new look. If you are happy with your new hair, then confirm it, and your character's hair will be changed immediately.

Tips for Changing Your Hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

  • Try out different hairstyles before choosing the one you want. You can always change it again later if you don't like it.
  • Experiment with different hair colors to create a unique look.
  • Don't forget to match your hair with your outfit to create a cohesive look.

FAQ

How often can you change your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons?

You can change your hair as often as you like in Animal Crossing New Horizons. There are no restrictions or limits to how often you can do it.

Can you change your hair without visiting the Beauty Salon?

No, you need to visit the Beauty Salon to change your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons. This is the only way to do it.

Do you need to pay to change your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons?

Yes, there is a fee for changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons. The fee is 3,000 Bells, which is a small price to pay for a new look.

Hair Care

While changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons is a fun way to create a new look, it's important to take care of your hair too. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
