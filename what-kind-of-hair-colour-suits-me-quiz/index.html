What Kind Of Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Bardi Jun 13, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and looking for a change? Choosing a new hair colour can be daunting, especially with so many options available. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss how to take a "what kind of hair colour suits me quiz" to help you find your perfect shade. So, let's get started!

Description

A "what kind of hair colour suits me quiz" is a fun and interactive way to determine which hair colour will suit your skin tone, eye colour, and personality. With a few simple questions, you can get an idea of the hair colours that will look best on you.

How to Take the Quiz

Taking a "what kind of hair colour suits me quiz" is easy and can be done in the comfort of your own home. Simply search for a quiz online or use a quiz from a hair colour brand's website. Most quizzes will ask you questions about your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. Answer honestly and choose the option that best describes you.

Once you have answered all the questions, the quiz will generate a list of hair colours that will suit you. You can use this list as a guide when choosing your new hair colour.

Step by Step

1. Find a "what kind of hair colour suits me quiz" online.

2. Answer the questions honestly and choose the option that best describes you.

3. Once you have answered all the questions, the quiz will generate a list of hair colours that will suit you.

4. Use the list as a guide when choosing your new hair colour.

Tips

- When taking the quiz, make sure you are in natural lighting to accurately determine your skin tone.

- Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance your new hair colour will require.

- If you are unsure about a hair colour, try a temporary dye first to see how it looks on you.

FAQ

Q: Can I take a "what kind of hair colour suits me quiz" if I have dyed hair?

A: Yes, you can still take the quiz even if you have dyed hair. Just make sure to answer the questions based on your natural hair colour.

Q: How accurate are these quizzes?

A: These quizzes are a good starting point but may not be 100% accurate. It's always best to consult with a professional hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair.

Hair Care

After you have chosen your new hair colour, it's important to take care of your locks. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for coloured hair to prevent fading. Avoid using hot tools and harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Taking a "what kind of hair colour suits me quiz" can be a fun and useful tool when deciding on a new hair colour. Remember to take into consideration your skin tone, eye colour, and personality when choosing your new shade. And always take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
