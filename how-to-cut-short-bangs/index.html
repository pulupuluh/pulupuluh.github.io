How To Cut Short Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Feb 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a way to update your hairstyle, cutting your bangs might just be the answer. Short bangs, in particular, are a popular choice for those who want to add a touch of edginess to their look. But before you grab a pair of scissors and start snipping away, there are a few things you need to know. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting short bangs, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Short Bangs
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Short Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Short Bangs

How to Cut Short Bangs

Before you start cutting your bangs, it's important to decide what length you want them to be. Short bangs typically fall above the eyebrows, but you can adjust the length based on your personal preference. Once you've decided on the length, follow these steps:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb your hair straight down over your forehead.
  2. Using hair cutting scissors, make a small vertical cut in the center of your hairline. This will be the starting point for your bangs.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the center of your hairline and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length.
  4. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way outwards towards your temples. Make sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides.
  5. Once you've finished cutting your bangs, use a comb to tidy up any stray hairs.

Tips for Cutting Short Bangs

Cutting your bangs can be tricky, especially if you're doing it for the first time. Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean, even cut.
  • Start with a small section of hair and cut gradually. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.
  • Take breaks and step back from the mirror to check your progress. It's easy to get lost in the details and over-cut your bangs.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your bangs yourself, consider going to a professional hairdresser.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short bangs:

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and tidy.

Can I cut my bangs when they're wet?

It's best to cut your bangs when they're dry. Wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is, which can result in uneven cutting.

What if I make a mistake?

If you accidentally cut your bangs too short or unevenly, don't panic. Wait a few days for your hair to grow out a bit, and then visit a professional hairdresser to fix the problem.

Hair Care After Cutting Short Bangs

Once you've cut your short bangs, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly and use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your bangs, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • If you need to style your bangs, use a small amount of styling product, such as hair wax or pomade.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to cut short bangs, it's time to grab your scissors and start experimenting with your look. Remember to take it slow, follow our step-by-step guide, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.
