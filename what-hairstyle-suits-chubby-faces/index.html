What Hairstyle Suits Chubby Faces - Tips and Tricks 2023

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle that suits your chubby face? If you have a round face shape, you might find it challenging to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on what hairstyle suits chubby faces in 2023.

How to Determine Your Face Shape:

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's essential to determine your face shape. Here's how you can do it:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or marker.
  3. Compare your face shape to the following:
    • Oval
    • Square
    • Heart
    • Diamond
    • Round

If your face shape is round, continue reading to find out what hairstyle suits you best.

Best Hairstyles for Chubby Faces:

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of length and slimness. Here are some hairstyles that will flatter your round face shape:

1. Long Layers:

Long layers will elongate your face and draw attention away from the roundness. You can wear your hair straight or add some curls for a soft, feminine look. Avoid blunt cuts as they will make your face appear wider.

2. Side-Swept Bangs:

Side-swept bangs will break up the roundness of your face and add some angles. Make sure the bangs are long enough to cover a portion of your forehead but not too short that they create a horizontal line across your face.

3. Pixie Cut:

A pixie cut is a daring but flattering hairstyle for a round face. The short length will give the illusion of height, and the layers will add texture and movement. Avoid a straight-across fringe as it will make your face appear wider.

4. Bob Cut:

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that flatters all face shapes, including round faces. Opt for a layered bob that falls below your chin and frames your face. Avoid a blunt bob as it will make your face appear wider.

Hairstyle Tips for Chubby Faces:

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Add volume to the crown of your head to create the illusion of length.
  • Avoid center parts as they will make your face appear wider.
  • Use hair accessories such as headbands and hair clips to add some dimension to your hairstyle.
  • When in doubt, consult with your hairstylist.

FAQ:

1. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail, but make sure to add some volume to the crown of your head. You can also opt for a high ponytail to elongate your face.

2. Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a round face?

Avoid a middle part as it will make your face appear wider. Opt for a side part instead.

Hair Care:

When styling your hair, it's essential to take care of it to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair as it can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.

Now that you know what hairstyle suits chubby faces, it's time to try out some new looks! Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the one that flatters your features the most.
