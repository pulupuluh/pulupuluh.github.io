How To Spike Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read
If you're looking for a hairstyle that's edgy and easy to maintain, spiking your hair may be the way to go. Whether you're going for a casual or a more formal look, spiking your hair can add texture and volume to your locks. In this post, we'll show you how to spike men's hair in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner.

Table of Contents

  • How to Spike Men's Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Spiking Men's Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Spiked Hair

How to Spike Men's Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step process of spiking men's hair, let's first discuss the different types of hair products that you can use to achieve this look. Here are some of the most common hair products for spiking men's hair:
  • Hair gel
  • Hair wax
  • Hair pomade
  • Hair spray
Each of these products has its own unique properties, so it's important to choose the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different types of hair products for spiking men's hair, let's get started on the step-by-step process: 1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow dry it before applying any products.
2. Take a small amount of your chosen hair product and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
3. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
4. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into the desired shape. If you're going for a spiky look, use your fingertips to create spikes by pulling your hair upwards.
5. To make your spikes last longer, use a hair spray to set them in place. Hold the spray can about 6 inches away from your head and spray evenly all over your hair.
6. If you want a more natural look, you can use a hair wax or pomade instead of a hair gel. These products provide a softer hold and add shine to your hair.

Tips for Spiking Men's Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when spiking men's hair:
  • Start with a small amount of hair product and add more as needed. Using too much product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • For a more natural look, use a hair wax or pomade instead of a hair gel.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different styles and shapes. Spiking your hair is all about self-expression, so have fun with it!

FAQs

Q: Can I spike my hair if I have curly hair?
A: Yes, you can still spike your hair if you have curly hair. However, it may take a bit more effort to get your spikes to stay in place. You may need to use a stronger hold hair product or blow dry your hair straight before spiking it. Q: Can I spike my hair if it's short?
A: Yes, you can still spike your hair if it's short. In fact, short hair can be easier to spike than longer hair. Just be sure to use a small amount of hair product so that your spikes don't look too stiff.

Hair Care for Spiked Hair

To keep your spiked hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup of hair product.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
With these tips and tricks, you should be able to spike men's hair with ease. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find the look that works best for you. Happy spiking!
