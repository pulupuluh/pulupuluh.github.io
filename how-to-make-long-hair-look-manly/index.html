How To Make Long Hair Look Manly Tips And Tricks

Long hair has been associated with femininity for decades, but more and more men are embracing their long locks. However, some men may struggle with making their long hair look masculine. In this article, we will share tips and tricks on how to make long hair look manly.

Table of Contents

How to Make Long Hair Look Manly

There are many ways to make long hair look manly. Here are some of our top tips:

1. Get a Haircut

Getting a haircut is the first step in making your long hair look manly. A good haircut can add structure and shape to your hair, making it look more masculine. Consider getting a fade or undercut to add some edge to your hairstyle.

2. Style Your Hair

Styling your hair is crucial in making it look manly. Experiment with different hairstyles and find one that suits you. A slicked-back hairstyle is a classic choice for men with long hair. You can also try a messy, textured look for a more relaxed vibe.

3. Grow a Beard

A beard can help balance out your long hair and add to your overall masculine look. If you can't grow a full beard, consider a goatee or stubble.

4. Wear Masculine Clothing

Your hair is just one part of your overall appearance. Wearing masculine clothing, such as leather jackets or denim, can help enhance your manly look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make long hair look manly:

Step 1: Get a Haircut

Visit a barber or hairstylist and get a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Ask for a fade or undercut to add some structure to your hairstyle.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair regularly to keep it looking clean and healthy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Experiment with different hairstyles and find one that suits you. Consider using hair products like pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 4: Grow a Beard

If you can, grow a beard to balance out your long hair. Take care of your beard by using beard oil and trimming it regularly.

Step 5: Wear Masculine Clothing

Complete your manly look by wearing masculine clothing. Choose items like leather jackets, denim, and boots to enhance your overall appearance.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to make long hair look manly:

1. Embrace Your Natural Hair Texture

Don't fight your hair's natural texture. Embrace it and work with it to create a natural, effortless look.

2. Don't Overdo It with Hair Products

Using too much hair product can make your hair look greasy and unnatural. Use a small amount of product and build up as needed.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Visit your barber or hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim.

FAQs

Q: Can men with long hair still look professional?

A: Yes! With the right haircut and styling, men with long hair can look professional and polished.

Q: How do I prevent my long hair from looking greasy?

A: Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Use dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil.

Q: Can I still wear a hat with long hair?

A: Yes! Hats like beanies and fedoras can add to your manly look. Just make sure to style your hair accordingly.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips for men with long hair:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools like a blow dryer or flat iron, use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Be Gentle When Brushing

Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently brush your hair, especially when wet. Avoid harsh pulling or tugging on your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your long hair look manly and embrace your natural look. Remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and proper hair care.
