How Long Do Box Braids Last On White Hair

Written by Aprilia Mar 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Box braids are a popular protective hairstyle among women of all races. However, for those with white hair, there may be some questions about how long box braids can last and how to properly care for them. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about box braids on white hair, including how long they last, how to care for them, and some helpful tips.

Description

Box braids are a type of braiding style that involves sectioning the hair into small, square-shaped parts and braiding each section from the root to the end. This style is popular because it is low-maintenance and can last for several weeks. Box braids can be done with various types of hair, including natural hair, synthetic hair, and even white hair.

How to

If you are interested in getting box braids on your white hair, there are a few steps you should follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair

When it comes to box braids, the type of hair you use is important. If you have white hair, you may want to consider using synthetic hair or human hair extensions that closely match your natural hair color and texture.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before getting box braids, make sure your hair is clean and free of any product buildup or tangles. You may also want to trim any split ends or damaged hair to ensure a healthy foundation for your braids.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into small, square-shaped parts. The size of the sections will depend on how thick or thin you want your braids to be. You can either do this yourself or have a professional stylist do it for you.

Step 4: Braid Your Hair

Starting at the root of each section, braid your hair all the way down to the end. You can use a hair tie or rubber band to secure the end of each braid.

Tips

Here are some helpful tips for caring for your box braids on white hair:

  • Keep your scalp moisturized by using a lightweight oil or leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products or oils that can weigh down your braids or cause buildup.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or use a silk pillowcase at night to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids, as this can cause damage to your natural hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about box braids on white hair:

How long do box braids last on white hair?

Box braids can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks on white hair, depending on how well you care for them and how quickly your hair grows.

Can you wash box braids on white hair?

Yes, you can wash your box braids on white hair, but you should be gentle and avoid rubbing or twisting your braids. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner and let your hair air dry.

Can you wear box braids on white hair to work?

Box braids are a professional and acceptable hairstyle in most workplaces, regardless of your race.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy box braids on white hair. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp and hair with a lightweight oil or leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products or oils that can weigh down your braids or cause buildup.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids, as this can cause damage to your natural hair.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or use a silk pillowcase at night to prevent frizz and breakage.

With these tips, you can enjoy beautiful box braids on your white hair for several weeks without damaging your natural hair. Remember to be gentle and take proper care of your braids to ensure a healthy and long-lasting style.
