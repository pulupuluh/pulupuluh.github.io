The Men s Hairstyle Long Top Short Back How To Get It In 2023

Written by Daniel Sep 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a stylish yet low-maintenance hairstyle, the men's hairstyle long top short back might be just what you need. This haircut has been popular for a few years now, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this blog post, we'll show you how to get the look, step by step, and provide you with some tips and tricks to make it work for you.

What is the Men's Hairstyle Long Top Short Back?

The men's hairstyle long top short back is a modern take on the classic undercut. It's a versatile haircut that can be worn in many different ways, but the basic idea is to have longer hair on top and shorter hair at the back and sides. The length of the top hair can vary depending on your preference, but it's usually at least a few inches long. The back and sides are usually buzzed or faded, creating a clean and sharp contrast with the longer top.

How to Get the Men's Hairstyle Long Top Short Back

Getting the men's hairstyle long top short back is relatively easy, but it's best to leave it to a professional barber or stylist. Here are the steps involved:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. You can go for a longer, more dramatic look, or keep it shorter for a more subtle effect.

Step 2: Buzz the Back and Sides

The back and sides of your hair should be buzzed or faded. This creates a clean contrast with the longer hair on top. The length of the buzz can vary depending on your preference, but it's usually a few millimeters to an inch in length.

Step 3: Blend the Top and Sides

The top of your hair should be blended with the sides to create a smooth transition. A good stylist will know how to blend the hair seamlessly, so don't be afraid to ask for help.

Step 4: Style the Top

The final step is to style the top of your hair. You can use pomade, wax, or gel to create the look you want. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Tips for Making the Men's Hairstyle Long Top Short Back Work for You

Here are some tips for making the men's hairstyle long top short back work for you:

  • Choose a length that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on how to maintain your haircut.
  • Consider adding some texture to the top of your hair for a more modern look.
  • Keep the back and sides trimmed regularly to maintain the sharp contrast with the longer top.

FAQs

Is the men's hairstyle long top short back easy to maintain?

Yes, it's relatively easy to maintain. You'll need to get the back and sides trimmed regularly, but other than that, it's a low-maintenance hairstyle.

Can I wear the men's hairstyle long top short back to work?

Yes, the men's hairstyle long top short back is a versatile haircut that can be worn to work or to more casual events. Just make sure you style it appropriately for the occasion.

Can I wear a hat with the men's hairstyle long top short back?

Yes, you can wear a hat with the men's hairstyle long top short back. Just be mindful of how it affects the shape of your hair, and make sure it doesn't mess up your styling efforts.

Hair Care Tips for the Men's Hairstyle Long Top Short Back

Here are some hair care tips for the men's hairstyle long top short back:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking sharp.

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to rock the men's hairstyle long top short back with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a dramatic look or a more subtle effect, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
