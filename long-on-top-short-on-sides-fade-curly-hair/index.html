Long On Top Short On Sides Fade Curly Hair A Guide For Men

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for your curly hair? If yes, then long on top short on sides fade curly hair is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is becoming increasingly popular among men with curly hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to get this hairstyle, its maintenance, and some tips for taking care of your curly hair.

Description

The long on top short on sides fade curly hair is a combination of different hair lengths. The hair on the top is kept long, while the hair on the sides is cropped short. To achieve the fade look, the hair is tapered from long to short. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want to keep their curls intact while still maintaining a neat and clean look.

How to Get the Long on Top Short on Sides Fade Curly Hair

Getting the long on top short on sides fade curly hair is not a difficult task. Follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Barber

Choose a barber who is experienced in cutting curly hair. A good barber will know how to cut your hair in the right way to suit your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide how long you want the hair on the top to be. This will depend on your personal preference and face shape. The hair on the sides should be shorter, and you can choose the length based on your preference.

Step 3: Choose the Fade

Decide on the type of fade you want. You can choose from a low fade, mid fade, or high fade. A low fade will have a gradual taper, while a high fade will have a more abrupt taper.

Step 4: Maintain the Style

To maintain the long on top short on sides fade curly hair, you need to keep the hair on the top moisturized and conditioned. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep the curls defined and frizz-free.

Tips for Taking Care of Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.

3. Trim Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

4. Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

5. Avoid Overwashing

Avoid overwashing your hair as it can strip the natural oils from your hair, leaving it dry and brittle.

FAQs

1. How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and prevent split ends.

2. Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on curly hair?

No, you should use shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair as it is formulated to keep the curls defined and frizz-free.

3. Can I style my curly hair in different ways?

Yes, you can style your curly hair in different ways such as a messy bun, half-up half-down, or a slick-back look.

Hair Care

The long on top short on sides fade curly hair is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that requires maintenance and care. Follow the tips mentioned above for taking care of your curly hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair, avoid heat styling, and trim your hair regularly. With proper care, you can keep your curly hair healthy and shiny.
