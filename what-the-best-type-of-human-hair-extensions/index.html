What Are The Best Type Of Human Hair Extensions

Written by Luffy Sep 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Human hair extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your hair. They come in many different types and styles, so choosing the right one for you can be overwhelming. In this article, we will discuss the best types of human hair extensions and provide tips on how to care for them.

Clip-In Hair Extensions

Clip-in hair extensions are the best option for those who want to add length and volume to their hair without committing to a long-term solution. They are easy to install and remove, making them perfect for special occasions. Clip-in hair extensions come in different lengths and colors, so you can find the perfect match for your hair.

Tape-In Hair Extensions

Tape-in hair extensions are a semi-permanent solution for those who want to add length and volume to their hair. They are applied using a special tape that is heat-sealed to your natural hair. Tape-in hair extensions can last up to eight weeks with proper care and maintenance.

Sew-In Hair Extensions

Sew-in hair extensions are a long-term solution for those who want to add length and volume to their hair. They are applied by braiding your natural hair and then sewing the extensions onto the braids. Sew-in hair extensions can last up to three months with proper care and maintenance.

Fusion Hair Extensions

Fusion hair extensions are a long-term solution for those who want to add length and volume to their hair. They are applied by bonding the extensions to your natural hair using a special glue. Fusion hair extensions can last up to six months with proper care and maintenance.

Micro-Bead Hair Extensions

Micro-bead hair extensions are a long-term solution for those who want to add length and volume to their hair. They are applied by looping the extensions around a small section of your natural hair and then clamping them in place using a special tool. Micro-bead hair extensions can last up to four months with proper care and maintenance.

How to Choose the Best Type of Human Hair Extensions

When choosing the best type of human hair extensions for you, consider your lifestyle, budget, and hair type. If you are looking for a temporary solution, clip-in hair extensions are the best option. If you want a semi-permanent solution, tape-in hair extensions are a great choice. If you want a long-term solution, sew-in, fusion, or micro-bead hair extensions are the way to go.

Tips for Caring for Your Human Hair Extensions

  • Brush your hair extensions gently to avoid tangling.
  • Wash your hair extensions with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair extensions.
  • Store your hair extensions in a cool, dry place.
  • Avoid swimming or showering with your hair extensions.

Frequently Asked Questions About Human Hair Extensions

Q: Are human hair extensions better than synthetic hair extensions?

A: Yes, human hair extensions are better than synthetic hair extensions because they look and feel more natural.

Q: Can I color my human hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can color your human hair extensions, but it is best to have a professional colorist do it for you.

Q: How long do human hair extensions last?

A: The lifespan of human hair extensions depends on the type of extensions and how well you care for them. Clip-in extensions can last up to a year, while sew-in, fusion, and micro-bead extensions can last up to six months.

Hair Care Tips for Human Hair Extensions

When caring for your human hair extensions, it is important to use products specifically designed for extensions. Avoid using products that contain sulfates, which can strip the hair of its natural oils. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions. Always brush your hair extensions gently to avoid tangling, and store them in a cool, dry place when not in use.

By following these tips and choosing the best type of human hair extensions for you, you can achieve the long, voluminous hair of your dreams.
