Is There A Way To Make Your Hair Naturally Curly

Written by Petter Sep 01, 2023 · 11 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools and chemical treatments to achieve curly hair? Do you want to know if there is a way to make your hair naturally curly? The answer is yes – there are several methods you can try to enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern. In this article, we’ll explore how to make your hair naturally curly using simple techniques and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Naturally Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Your Hair Naturally Curly

There are several ways to make your hair naturally curly without using heat or chemicals. Some of the most popular methods include:

  • Plopping
  • The Curly Girl Method
  • Finger Coiling
  • Twist Outs
  • Bantu Knots

These techniques work by enhancing your hair’s natural curl pattern and encouraging your hair to form curls without causing damage.

Plopping

Plopping is a technique that involves using a t-shirt or microfiber towel to wrap your hair in a turban-style wrap. This helps to absorb excess water and enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply your favorite styling product.
  3. Flip your head upside down and place the t-shirt or towel on a flat surface.
  4. Lower your head onto the t-shirt or towel, making sure your hair is in the center of the fabric.
  5. Wrap the sides of the t-shirt or towel around your head and tie in a knot at the back.
  6. Leave the plop on for 20-30 minutes, or until your hair is dry.
  7. Remove the t-shirt or towel and fluff your curls with your fingers.

Plopping is a great way to enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern without using heat or chemicals.

The Curly Girl Method

The Curly Girl Method is a hair care routine that focuses on enhancing your hair’s natural curl pattern. The method involves avoiding sulfates, silicones, and heat tools, and using gentle hair care products that nourish your curls. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Avoid sulfates and silicones.
  2. Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo or co-wash.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner and curl-enhancing gel.
  4. Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation.
  5. Avoid heat tools and air-dry your hair instead.

The Curly Girl Method can take some time to get used to, but it can be a great way to enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern and improve the overall health of your hair.

Finger Coiling

Finger Coiling is a technique that involves using your fingers to twist sections of your hair into coils. This helps to enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern and encourage curl formation. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner and curl-enhancing gel.
  3. Take a small section of hair and twist it around your finger, starting at the root.
  4. Continue twisting until you reach the end of your hair.
  5. Repeat on all sections of your hair, twisting in the same direction.
  6. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Finger Coiling is a great way to enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern and define your curls.

Twist Outs

Twist Outs are a technique that involves twisting sections of your hair and allowing them to dry before unraveling them to reveal defined curls. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner and curl-enhancing gel.
  3. Take a small section of hair and twist it around itself, starting at the root.
  4. Continue twisting until you reach the end of your hair.
  5. Repeat on all sections of your hair.
  6. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  7. Unravel the twists to reveal defined curls.

Twist Outs are a great way to enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern and create defined curls.

Bantu Knots

Bantu Knots are a technique that involves twisting sections of your hair and wrapping them into small knots. This helps to enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern and encourage curl formation. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner and curl-enhancing gel.
  3. Take a small section of hair and twist it around itself, starting at the root.
  4. Wrap the twisted section of hair into a small knot and secure with a bobby pin.
  5. Repeat on all sections of your hair.
  6. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  7. Remove the bobby pins and unravel the knots to reveal defined curls.

Bantu Knots are a great way to enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern and create defined curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide for making your hair naturally curly:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner and curl-enhancing gel.
  3. Choose a technique from the list above (plopping, the Curly Girl Method, finger coiling, twist outs, or Bantu knots).
  4. Follow the steps for your chosen technique.
  5. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  6. Fluff your curls with your fingers and enjoy your naturally curly hair!

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your naturally curly hair:

  • Avoid heat tools and chemical treatments that can damage your hair.
  • Use gentle hair care products that nourish your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry – use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle.
  • Protect your curls while you sleep by wearing a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.
  • Refresh your curls between washes with a curl refreshing spray or mist.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my hair naturally curly if it’s naturally straight?

A: While you can enhance your hair’s natural curl pattern, you can’t change your hair type. If your hair is naturally straight, you may not be able to achieve the same level of curl as someone with naturally curly hair.

Q: How long does it take to make your hair naturally curly?

A: The amount of time it takes to make your hair naturally curly depends on your hair type and the technique you’re using. Some techniques, like plopping and finger coiling, can be done in as little as 20-30 minutes, while others, like the Curly Girl Method, may take several weeks or months to see results.

Q: Is it possible to damage your hair by trying to make it naturally curly?

A: While most of the techniques for making your hair naturally curly are gentle and non-damaging, it’s still possible to damage your hair if you’re not careful. Always use gentle hair care products and avoid using heat tools or chemical treatments that can damage your hair.
