How To Choose Warm Or Cool Hair Color

Written by Christine Sep 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to choosing a hair color, it's important to consider whether you want a warm or cool tone. Warm tones have a yellow or gold undertone, while cool tones have a blue or purple undertone. Not sure which one to choose? Here's a guide to help you decide!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose Warm or Cool Hair Color
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing Warm or Cool Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

How to Choose Warm or Cool Hair Color

First, you need to determine your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow or golden hue. If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink or blue hue. You can also determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they look greenish, you have warm undertones. If they look bluish, you have cool undertones.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can choose a hair color that complements it. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to choose a hair color with warm tones, such as golden blondes, copper reds, or honey browns. If you have cool undertones, you'll want to choose a hair color with cool tones, such as ash blondes, cool browns, or burgundy reds.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose a warm or cool hair color:

  1. Determine your skin tone. Look for yellow or golden undertones for warm skin tones, and pink or blue undertones for cool skin tones.
  2. Choose a hair color with warm tones if you have warm skin tones, and a hair color with cool tones if you have cool skin tones.
  3. Consider your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, you may need to lighten it first before adding warm or cool tones.
  4. Think about your lifestyle. Are you willing to maintain your hair color with regular touch-ups? Warm tones tend to fade faster than cool tones, so you may need to touch up your hair more often.
  5. Consult with a professional. A hairstylist can help you choose the right hair color for your skin tone and lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing Warm or Cool Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a warm or cool hair color:

  • Consider the season. Warmer tones tend to be more popular in the summer, while cooler tones are more popular in the winter.
  • Think about your wardrobe. If you wear a lot of cool colors, such as blues and greens, a cool hair color may complement your style better.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual try-on apps to see how different hair colors will look on you before committing.
  • Consider the maintenance. If you don't have time for regular touch-ups, a low-maintenance hair color may be the best option for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing warm or cool hair color:

  • Can I have warm and cool tones in my hair? Yes, you can have a mix of warm and cool tones in your hair. This can create a unique and multi-dimensional look.
  • Can I go from warm to cool or vice versa? Yes, you can switch from warm to cool or vice versa. However, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure a smooth transition.
  • What's the best way to maintain my hair color? Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling, and schedule regular touch-ups with your hairstylist.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires special care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you care for your colored hair:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid heat styling tools and opt for air-drying or using heat-free styling methods.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Schedule regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Invest in a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Choosing a warm or cool hair color can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect shade for you! Remember to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and lifestyle when choosing a hair color, and consult with a professional hairstylist for the best results. With the right care, your colored hair will look beautiful and vibrant for weeks to come.
