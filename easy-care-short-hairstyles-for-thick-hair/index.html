Easy Care Short Hairstyles For Thick Hair

Written by Oliver Aug 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending hours styling your thick hair every day? Do you want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to care for? Look no further than these easy care short hairstyles for thick hair. Not only are these hairstyles quick and easy to style, but they are also trendy and chic. Keep reading to learn more about how to achieve these effortless looks.

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's great to have voluminous locks, it can also be a hassle to style every day. Short hairstyles are a great option for those with thick hair who want to simplify their styling routine. These easy care hairstyles are low maintenance and require minimal styling. They are perfect for busy women who want to look great without spending hours in front of the mirror.

How To

The key to achieving an easy care short hairstyle for thick hair is finding the right cut. A layered bob or pixie cut is a great option as it adds texture and movement to thick hair. Once you have the right cut, styling is a breeze. Simply apply a small amount of styling product to damp hair, blow dry, and go. For added texture, use a texturizing spray or wax to create a tousled look.

Step by Step

1. Start with a layered bob or pixie cut that is tailored to your face shape and hair texture.
2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to damp hair.
3. Blow dry hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
4. Use a texturizing spray or wax to create a tousled look.
5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair before styling.
- Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying to protect hair from heat damage.
- Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle between washes.
- Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Q: Are short hairstyles suitable for all face shapes?

A: Short hairstyles can be tailored to suit all face shapes, but it's important to work with a stylist who can recommend the best cut for your face shape and hair texture.

Q: How often should I trim my short hair?

A: It's recommended to trim short hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if I have thick hair?

A: Absolutely! Short hairstyles are a great option for those with thick hair as they add texture and movement. With the right cut and styling products, you can achieve a chic and effortless look.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type and texture. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Finally, get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these easy care short hairstyles for thick hair, you can achieve a chic and effortless look with minimal effort. Whether you opt for a layered bob or a pixie cut, these low-maintenance hairstyles are perfect for busy women who want to look great without spending hours styling their hair.
