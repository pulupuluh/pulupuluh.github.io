Male Haircuts Long On Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 31, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, male haircuts long on top short sides might just be the perfect choice for you. This style has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason – it's stylish, easy to maintain, and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about this popular haircut, from how to style it to hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining the Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The male haircuts long on top short sides style features longer hair on the top of the head, which gradually tapers down to shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a contrast between the longer and shorter lengths, and can be customized with different fades or undercut styles. This haircut works well with different hair types, from straight to wavy or curly hair.

How to Get the Haircut

When getting a male haircut long on top short sides, it's important to communicate your desired style with your barber or stylist. They will likely ask you about the length you prefer on the top and sides, and whether you want any fades or undercuts. You can also bring in pictures of haircuts you like to show as a reference.

The haircut typically requires more length on the top, so be prepared to grow out your hair if it's currently shorter. The sides and back will be trimmed shorter, but the length can vary depending on the style you want. Your barber or stylist may use clippers or scissors to achieve the desired length and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Styling male haircuts long on top short sides is fairly easy, and can be done with just a few products and tools. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Shampoo and condition your hair before styling.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as pomade or styling cream, to the top of your hair. Work it in with your fingers or a comb.
  4. Use a hair dryer to dry your hair while combing it in the desired direction.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product to hold the style in place.

Experiment with different products and techniques to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style. You can also ask your barber or stylist for recommendations.

Tips for Maintaining the Haircut

To keep your male haircut long on top short sides looking great, here are some tips to follow:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural texture.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

FAQ

What face shapes work well with this haircut?

This haircut works well with oval, square, and diamond-shaped faces. If you have a round or heart-shaped face, you may want to opt for a slightly different style to balance out your features.

Can this haircut be styled in different ways?

Yes, this haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways depending on your preferences. You can slick it back, comb it over, or leave it messy and textured for a more casual look.

Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can work with different hair types, from straight to curly hair. Your barber or stylist can customize the haircut to suit your hair type and texture.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as it can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated for healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as they can damage the hair follicles.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your male haircut long on top short sides with confidence and style. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a more casual and textured style, this haircut is sure to turn heads.
