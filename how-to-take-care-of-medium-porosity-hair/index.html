How To Take Care Of Medium Porosity Hair

Written by William Sep 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Medium porosity hair is hair that is in the middle of the porosity spectrum. It is neither too porous nor too resistant to moisture, making it relatively easy to manage. However, it still requires proper care and attention to maintain its health and beauty. In this article, we will discuss how to take care of medium porosity hair, including how to identify it, its characteristics, and the best products and practices for keeping it healthy and vibrant.

How to Identify Medium Porosity Hair

Identifying the porosity of your hair is the first step in determining the best way to care for it. To determine if you have medium porosity hair, take a strand of your hair and drop it into a glass of water.

If your hair sinks to the bottom of the glass within a few seconds, you have high porosity hair. If your hair floats on the surface, you have low porosity hair. If your hair sinks slowly to the middle of the glass and stays there, you have medium porosity hair.

Characteristics of Medium Porosity Hair

Medium porosity hair has a balanced moisture level and is relatively easy to manage. It is less prone to breakage and damage than high porosity hair and requires less maintenance than low porosity hair.

Some common characteristics of medium porosity hair include:

  • Holds styles well
  • Easily absorbs and retains moisture
  • Resistant to damage
  • Requires minimal maintenance

How to Take Care of Medium Porosity Hair

To keep your medium porosity hair healthy and vibrant, it is important to follow a regular hair care routine. This routine should include washing, conditioning, and styling your hair using the right products and techniques.

Here are some tips for taking care of medium porosity hair:

Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair at least once a week to keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo that does not contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

Condition your hair after washing

After washing your hair, apply a deep conditioner to help restore moisture and nourishment. Leave the conditioner on your hair for at least 10 minutes before rinsing it out.

Use a leave-in conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. Apply the conditioner to your hair after washing and conditioning it.

Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle. Try to avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers.

Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Best Products for Medium Porosity Hair

Choosing the right products for your medium porosity hair is essential for keeping it healthy and vibrant. Here are some of the best products for medium porosity hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner: Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients such as aloe vera, coconut oil, and shea butter.
  • Deep conditioner: Look for products that are rich in protein and moisture, such as avocado oil, argan oil, and keratin.
  • Leave-in conditioner: Look for products that are lightweight and contain natural ingredients such as jojoba oil, almond oil, and glycerin.
  • Styling products: Look for products that are alcohol-free and contain natural ingredients such as flaxseed oil, castor oil, and honey.

Tips for Taking Care of Medium Porosity Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your medium porosity hair:

  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to prevent breakage when detangling your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my medium porosity hair?

A: You should wash your medium porosity hair at least once a week to keep it clean and healthy.

Q: Can I use heat styling tools on my medium porosity hair?

A: While it is best to avoid heat styling tools, you can use them occasionally. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and set your tools to a low or medium heat setting.

Q: How do I prevent my medium porosity hair from becoming dry and brittle?

A: To prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle, use a deep conditioner regularly, avoid using heat styling tools, and protect your hair from the sun.

Conclusion

Taking care of medium porosity hair is relatively easy and straightforward. By following a regular hair care routine, using the right products, and following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair healthy, vibrant, and beautiful.
