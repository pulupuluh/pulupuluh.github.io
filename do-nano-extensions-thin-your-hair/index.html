Do Nano Extensions Thin Your Hair What You Need To Know

Written by Frank Apr 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering getting nano extensions but worried about whether they will thin your hair? This is a common concern for many people, especially those who already have thin or fine hair. In this article, we will explore this topic and provide you with all the information you need to know before getting nano extensions.

Description

Nano extensions are a type of hair extension that uses tiny beads to attach the hair extensions to your natural hair. They are called nano extensions because the beads used are smaller than traditional micro-beads. Nano extensions are a popular choice among people who want to add length, volume, and thickness to their hair. They are also known for their natural look and feel.

How to Get Nano Extensions

Getting nano extensions requires a professional hair stylist. The stylist will first assess your hair to ensure it is healthy enough for the extensions. They will also help you choose the right length, color, and thickness of the extensions to match your natural hair. The stylist will then apply the extensions using the tiny beads and a special tool. The process can take anywhere from 2 to 5 hours depending on the amount of hair being added.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Consultation

The first step in getting nano extensions is to schedule a consultation with a professional hair stylist. During the consultation, the stylist will assess your hair and help you choose the right type of extensions for your hair type, color, and texture.

Step 2: Preparation

Before the application process begins, the stylist will wash and dry your hair thoroughly. They will also section your hair to make it easier to apply the extensions.

Step 3: Application

The stylist will use a special tool to thread the tiny beads onto your natural hair, then attach the extensions to the beads using pliers. This process is repeated until all the extensions have been applied.

Step 4: Styling

Once the extensions are in place, the stylist will trim and style your hair to blend the extensions with your natural hair. They may also use heat styling tools to create the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Nano Extensions

To keep your nano extensions looking their best, follow these tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Brush your hair gently and avoid tugging on the extensions
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair
  • Visit your stylist regularly for maintenance

FAQ

Do nano extensions thin your hair?

No, nano extensions do not thin your hair. In fact, they can make your hair appear thicker and fuller. However, it is important to have a professional stylist apply the extensions to ensure they are properly attached and do not cause damage to your natural hair.

How long do nano extensions last?

Nano extensions can last anywhere from 2 to 6 months, depending on how well they are maintained and how fast your hair grows. It is important to visit your stylist regularly for maintenance to ensure the extensions stay in place and look their best.

Can I color my hair with nano extensions?

Yes, you can color your hair with nano extensions, but it is important to have a professional colorist do it for you. They will need to work around the extensions and use a gentle, ammonia-free dye to avoid damaging the extensions.

Hair Care for Nano Extensions

It is important to take care of your hair and extensions to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for hair care with nano extensions:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the extensions
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Brush your hair gently and avoid tugging on the extensions
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair
  • Visit your stylist regularly for maintenance

Conclusion

Nano extensions are a great option for adding length, volume, and thickness to your hair. They do not thin your hair, but it is important to have a professional stylist apply them to avoid damage to your natural hair. Remember to follow the tips for maintaining your nano extensions and take care of your hair to ensure they last as long as possible.
