Best Pomade For Thick Wavy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have thick and wavy hair, you know that it can be difficult to style and manage. That's why finding the right pomade is crucial. In this article, we'll explore the best pomade for thick wavy hair, how to use it, and tips for maintaining healthy hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Pomade for Thick Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Using Pomade

  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Description

Pomade is a hair styling product that helps to add texture and hold to your hair. It's particularly useful for people with thick and wavy hair, as it can help to keep your hair in place and reduce frizz. Pomade is available in a variety of formulas, including water-based, oil-based, and clay-based. Each formula has its own unique benefits and drawbacks, so it's important to choose the one that's right for your hair type and styling needs.

How to Choose the Best Pomade for Thick Wavy Hair

When choosing a pomade for thick wavy hair, there are a few factors to consider:

Hold Strength

The hold strength of a pomade determines how well it will keep your hair in place. If you have thick and wavy hair, you'll likely need a pomade with a strong hold to keep your hair from falling flat. Look for pomades labeled as "strong hold" or "firm hold."

Formula

Pomades are available in a variety of formulas, including water-based, oil-based, and clay-based. Each formula has its own unique benefits and drawbacks. Water-based pomades are easy to wash out but may not provide as strong of a hold as oil-based pomades. Oil-based pomades tend to provide a stronger hold but can be difficult to wash out. Clay-based pomades are ideal for adding texture to your hair but may not provide as strong of a hold as water or oil-based pomades.

Texture

The texture of a pomade can also affect how well it works with thick wavy hair. If you have coarse or curly hair, you may want to look for a pomade with a creamier texture. If you have thinner hair, a lighter pomade may work better.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Pomade

Now that you've chosen the best pomade for your hair type and styling needs, here's how to use it:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any buildup and prepare your hair for styling.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Pat your hair dry with a towel or use a blow dryer on low heat. You want your hair to be slightly damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 3: Apply Pomade

Scoop a small amount of pomade onto your fingertips and rub your hands together to warm it up. Then, apply the pomade evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the areas where you want the most hold.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair into the desired shape. If you're going for a messy, textured look, use your fingers to create separation and definition.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are a few tips for keeping your thick wavy hair healthy:

Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that's gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. If you do need to use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQs

Q: Can I use pomade on dry hair?

A: Yes, you can apply pomade to dry hair. However, it may not distribute as evenly as it would on damp hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have thick and wavy hair, you may not need to wash it every day. Aim to wash your hair 2-3 times per week.

Q: Can pomade cause hair loss?

A: No, pomade itself does not cause hair loss. However, excessive use of hair styling products can cause buildup and clog hair follicles, which can contribute to hair loss over time.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are a few additional tips for taking care of your thick wavy hair:

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Avoid Brushing Your Hair When It's Wet

Brushing your hair when it's wet can cause breakage and damage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Excessive sun exposure can dry out your hair and cause damage. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's rays.

In conclusion, finding the best pomade for thick wavy hair can make all the difference in achieving the perfect hairstyle. Consider hold strength, formula, and texture when choosing a pomade, and follow these steps and tips for healthy hair. With the right pomade and proper hair care, you can rock any hairstyle with confidence!
