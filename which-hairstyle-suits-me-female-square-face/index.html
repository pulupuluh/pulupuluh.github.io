Which Hairstyle Suits Me Female Square Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Aug 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a woman with a square-shaped face, you may be wondering which hairstyle suits you best. Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, and the width of the face is almost equal to its length. The good news is that there are many hairstyles that can flatter your square face shape. In this article, we'll explore different haircuts and styles that will suit you perfectly.

Description

A square face shape is defined by a strong, angular jawline, a broad forehead, and a wide face. This face shape is often considered the most masculine, so the goal is to soften the angles and create a more feminine look. The ideal hairstyle for a square face should add softness to the face and create balance.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your square face is to determine your face shape. Take a good look in the mirror and note the shape of your face. Once you've determined that your face is square, it's time to start exploring different hairstyles.

There are many haircuts and styles that work well with a square face. The key is to add volume and softness to the face. Layers are a great way to achieve this, as they create movement and texture. Long layers that fall around the face can help to soften the jawline, while shorter layers around the crown can add volume to the top of the head.

Another great option for square faces is a side-swept fringe. This style helps to create a diagonal line across the face, which can soften the angles and create a more balanced look. You can also try a deep side part, which can add volume and texture to the hair.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating a hairstyle that suits a square face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  4. If you have long hair, add long layers around the face to soften the jawline.
  5. If you have short hair, ask your stylist to add layers around the crown to add volume.
  6. Create a deep side part to add texture and volume to the hair.
  7. If you want to add a fringe, create a side-swept fringe that falls diagonally across the face.
  8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for finding the perfect hairstyle for your square face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or angular, as they can make the face look more square.
  • Try hairstyles that create volume at the top of the head, as this can help to balance out the width of the face.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to the hair to create dimension and texture.
  • If you have curly hair, embrace the curls and add layers to create movement.
  • Experiment with different styles and don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about which hairstyle suits a female square face:

What is the best haircut for a square face?

The best haircut for a square face is one that adds softness and balance to the face. Long layers, side-swept fringes, and deep side parts are all great options.

Can I have a bob with a square face?

Yes, you can have a bob with a square face. However, it's important to choose a bob that is slightly longer in the front and has layers around the face to soften the angles.

Should I avoid short haircuts with a square face?

No, you don't have to avoid short haircuts with a square face. However, it's important to choose a style that adds volume and softness to the face. Layers around the crown can help to create this effect.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your square face, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and make it look dry and frizzy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools on your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Now that you know which hairstyle suits you female square face, it's time to experiment with different styles and find the perfect look for you. Remember to have fun and try new things, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice!
