How To Cut Hair Back And Sides A Guide For Beginners In 2023

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every month? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair at home? If so, you're in luck! In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting hair back and sides. This is a great starting point for beginners who want to learn the basics of cutting hair. Let's get started!

  • How to Cut Hair Back and Sides

Description

Cutting hair back and sides is a basic haircut that is easy to do at home. It involves trimming the hair on the sides and back of the head. This haircut is popular among men and women and can be done with clippers or scissors. By learning how to cut your own hair, you can save money and have more control over your hairstyle. With a little practice, you can become an expert at cutting hair back and sides.

How to Cut Hair Back and Sides

Before you begin cutting hair back and sides, gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of scissors or clippers, a comb, and a mirror. If using clippers, use a guard that is between 1/4 and 1 inch depending on your desired length. If using scissors, choose a pair that is sharp and comfortable to hold.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and can lead to uneven results.
  2. Section off the hair at the crown of the head using a comb. This will allow you to focus on the sides and back of the head.
  3. Begin at the nape of the neck and work your way up. Use the clippers or scissors to trim the hair to your desired length. Be sure to use the same length guard or guide when cutting to ensure even results.
  4. Move to the sides of the head and trim the hair in the same manner. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the same length guard or guide as before.
  5. Blend the sides and back of the head using scissors or clippers without a guard. Be sure to blend the hair from the shorter length to the longer length to create a natural look.
  6. Check for any uneven areas and trim as needed.
  7. Use the comb to blend the hair and create a smooth transition between the sides and top of the head.
  8. Finish by trimming any stray hairs and cleaning up the neckline.

Tips for Cutting Hair Back and Sides

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair takes practice, so don't rush the process.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.
  • Start with a longer guard or guide and work your way down to a shorter length if needed.
  • Be sure to clean your tools after each use to ensure they are in good condition for your next haircut.

FAQs

1. Can I use scissors instead of clippers?

Yes, you can use scissors instead of clippers. However, using clippers can make the process quicker and easier, especially for beginners.

2. How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on personal preference and hair type. Generally, hair should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

3. What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! Hair grows back, and you can always visit a professional stylist to fix any errors.

Hair Care After Cutting Hair Back and Sides

After cutting hair back and sides, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair soft and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

By following these tips and practicing your cutting skills, you can achieve a great haircut at home. Remember to be patient and enjoy the process!
