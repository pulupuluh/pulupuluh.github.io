How To Cut Hair Bald With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read
If you're considering going bald or just want a fresh, clean look, cutting your hair with clippers is a great option. Not only is it easy and affordable, but it also allows you to maintain your bald look from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we will walk you through how to cut your hair bald with clippers, including tips and tricks for achieving a perfect cut every time.

Description

Cutting your hair bald with clippers is a process that involves using clippers to shave off all the hair on your head. While it may seem intimidating at first, with the right tools and techniques, it can be a simple and easy process that can be done in just a few minutes.

How to Cut Hair Bald with Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair, gather all the necessary tools. You will need: - A pair of clippers - A mirror or two - A towel or cape to catch the hair - A comb or brush to help guide the clippers - Clippers guards of different lengths Once you have all the tools, follow these steps: 1. Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly. This will make it easier to cut and prevent any irritation or discomfort. 2. Choose the guard size that you want to start with. For a closer shave, start with a smaller guard size and work your way up. 3. Turn on your clippers and start cutting your hair in the opposite direction of the hair growth. This will help ensure an even cut. 4. Use the comb or brush to help guide the clippers and make sure you're cutting evenly. 5. Work your way around your head, cutting all the hair to the same length. 6. Once you're finished with the first guard size, switch to a larger guard size and repeat the process until you've achieved your desired length. 7. Finally, use the clippers without a guard to clean up any stray hairs or achieve a closer shave.

Tips for Cutting Hair Bald with Clippers

- Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles. - Take breaks if you need them to avoid fatigue or discomfort. - Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once. - Use a towel or cape to catch the hair and make cleanup easier. - Experiment with different guard sizes to find the perfect length for you.

FAQs

Q: Is it necessary to wash my hair before cutting it? A: Yes, it is recommended to wash your hair before cutting it to make it easier to cut and prevent any irritation or discomfort. Q: How often do I need to cut my hair bald with clippers? A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but typically every 1-2 weeks is recommended to maintain a bald look. Q: Can I cut my hair bald with regular scissors? A: It's not recommended to cut your hair bald with regular scissors as they are not designed for this type of cut and can result in an uneven or jagged look.

Hair Care after Cutting Hair Bald with Clippers

After cutting your hair bald with clippers, it's important to take care of your scalp to prevent any irritation or discomfort. Use a moisturizing scalp lotion or oil to keep your scalp hydrated and healthy. If you plan on spending time in the sun, be sure to wear a hat or apply sunscreen to protect your scalp from UV rays. Additionally, avoid using any styling products or harsh chemicals on your scalp for at least a few days after cutting your hair to allow your scalp to heal. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a perfect bald cut every time. So grab your clippers and get ready to rock your new look!
