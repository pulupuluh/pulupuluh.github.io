Description

Face framing haircuts with bangs are a trendy hairstyle that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. This haircut is characterized by long layers that start from the chin and work their way down to the ends of the hair. The bangs are usually cut to frame the face, and can be side-swept or blunt.

How to

To achieve a face framing haircut with bangs, it is recommended that you visit a professional hair stylist. They will be able to assess your hair type, face shape, and recommend a style that will suit you best. However, if you prefer to cut your hair at home, follow these steps:

Step by Step

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section off the hair at the nape of your neck and pin it up.
  3. Take a section of hair at the front of your head and comb it forward.
  4. Pin the rest of your hair back so that it doesn't get in the way.
  5. Cut the bangs to your desired length, making sure to cut them slightly longer than you want them to be.
  6. Release the section of hair you pinned back and cut it to match the length of the bangs.
  7. Continue cutting one section at a time, making sure to cut the hair in a straight line.
  8. Blend the layers together by cutting into them at a slight angle.
  9. Finish by trimming any stray hairs and styling your hair as desired.

Tips

  • Always use sharp scissors when cutting your hair.
  • Start by cutting a little bit of hair at a time and gradually work your way up to your desired length.
  • Make sure to cut your hair when it is dry, as hair tends to look longer when wet.
  • Use a comb to make sure your hair is straight and even before you start cutting.

FAQ

Q: What face shape is best for a face framing haircut with bangs?

A: A face framing haircut with bangs is suitable for most face shapes. However, it is particularly flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It is recommended that you get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your face framing haircut with bangs looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it is wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
