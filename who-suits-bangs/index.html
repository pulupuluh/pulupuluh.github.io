Who Suits Bangs Expert Tips And Advice

Written by Bardi Feb 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but not sure if they will suit you? Bangs are a popular hairstyle that can make a big impact on your overall look. However, not everyone can pull off this style. In this article, we will discuss who suits bangs and provide expert tips and advice to help you decide if bangs are right for you.

Face Shape

The first thing to consider when deciding if bangs are right for you is your face shape. Bangs tend to work best on oval, heart, and square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid bangs as they can make your face appear even rounder. However, if you have a long face, bangs can help shorten the appearance of your face.

Hair Type

Your hair type is also an important factor to consider when deciding if bangs are right for you. Bangs tend to work best on straight or slightly wavy hair. If you have curly hair, bangs may be more difficult to manage and style. Additionally, if you have very fine hair, bangs may make your hair appear even thinner.

Forehead Size

The size of your forehead is another important factor to consider. If you have a small forehead, bangs can help create the illusion of a larger forehead. On the other hand, if you have a large forehead, bangs can help create the illusion of a smaller forehead. However, if you have a very high hairline, bangs may not be the best option as they can make your forehead appear even larger.

Eye Shape

Your eye shape is also an important factor to consider when deciding if bangs are right for you. Bangs tend to work best on those with almond-shaped eyes. If you have round eyes or hooded eyes, bangs may not be the best option as they can make your eyes appear smaller.

Face Features

Finally, it's important to consider your other facial features when deciding if bangs are right for you. If you have prominent cheekbones, bangs can help accentuate them. If you have a strong jawline, bangs can help soften the appearance of your face.

How to Get Bangs

If you've decided that bangs are right for you, the next step is to get them. It's important to go to a professional hairstylist who has experience cutting bangs. You'll also want to bring in pictures of the type of bangs you want so that your hairstylist can get a better idea of what you're looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting bangs:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs
  3. Use a comb to separate the hair into the desired length of bangs
  4. Cut the hair in a straight line, starting at the center and working your way outwards
  5. Trim any uneven pieces
  6. Style your bangs using a blow dryer and round brush

Expert Tips

Here are some expert tips to keep in mind when getting bangs:

  • Start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your bangs
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling iron to help style your bangs
  • Use a dry shampoo to help keep your bangs looking fresh between washes

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs:

  • Q: Will bangs make my face look shorter or longer?
  • A: Bangs can make your face appear shorter or longer depending on your face shape and the length of your bangs.
  • Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?
  • A: You'll want to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Q: Can I still wear my hair up with bangs?
  • A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with bangs. You may just need to use bobby pins or a headband to keep them in place.

Hair Care for Bangs

Here are some tips for taking care of your bangs:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days
  • Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to help style them
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day as this can make them greasy
  • Use a dry shampoo to help keep your bangs looking fresh between washes

In conclusion, bangs can be a great hairstyle for those who suit them. By considering factors such as face shape, hair type, forehead size, eye shape, and face features, you can determine if bangs are right for you. If you do decide to get bangs, be sure to go to a professional hairstylist and follow our expert tips and advice to keep them looking their best.
