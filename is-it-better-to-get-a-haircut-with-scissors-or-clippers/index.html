Is It Better To Get A Haircut With Scissors Or Clippers

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, one of the most common questions people ask is whether it's better to get a haircut with scissors or clippers. While both options have their pros and cons, the answer ultimately depends on your personal preference, hair type, and the style you're looking to achieve.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

Scissors and clippers are both tools that hair stylists use to cut hair, but they work in different ways. Scissors are typically used to create more precise cuts, while clippers are used for more uniform cuts. Scissors are great for cutting hair in layers or creating texture, while clippers work well for buzz cuts or fades.

How to:

The process of getting a haircut with scissors or clippers is similar in many ways, but there are some key differences to keep in mind. Here's what you can expect from each:

Getting a Haircut with Scissors:

When you get a haircut with scissors, the stylist will typically comb your hair straight and then cut it in sections. They may use a variety of cutting techniques, such as point cutting or slide cutting, to create the desired shape and texture. Scissors are also great for thinning out thick hair or removing split ends.

Getting a Haircut with Clippers:

When you get a haircut with clippers, the stylist will typically use guards to create a uniform length. The guard size will determine how short or long your hair will be. Clippers are also great for creating fades or blending hair from one length to another.

Step by Step:

Regardless of whether you're getting a haircut with scissors or clippers, the basic steps are the same:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry naturally or with a blow dryer.
  2. Discuss your desired haircut with your stylist.
  3. Sit still and relax while your stylist cuts your hair.
  4. Pay for your haircut and tip your stylist if you're happy with the result.

Tips:

To ensure you get the best possible haircut, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Bring a photo of the haircut you want to show your stylist.
  • Communicate clearly with your stylist about your desired length and style.
  • Be honest about your hair type and any previous color or chemical treatments.
  • Trust your stylist's recommendations for products and styling tips.

FAQ:

Q: Is it better to get a haircut with scissors or clippers?

A: The answer depends on your personal preference, hair type, and the style you're looking to achieve. Scissors are great for creating texture and layers, while clippers are great for uniform cuts and fades.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your hairstyle. On average, most people get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I cut my own hair with scissors or clippers?

A: While it's possible to cut your own hair with scissors or clippers, it's not recommended unless you have experience or training. It's best to leave it to the professionals to avoid any mistakes or accidents.

Hair Care:

After getting a haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay.
  • Apply a hair mask or treatment at least once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you have a better understanding of the differences between getting a haircut with scissors or clippers, you can make an informed decision based on your hair type and desired style. Remember to communicate clearly with your stylist and take care of your hair after your haircut to maintain its health and style.
