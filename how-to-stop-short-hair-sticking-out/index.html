How To Stop Short Hair Sticking Out Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Apr 29, 2023 · 8 min read
How to Stop Short Hair Sticking Out: Tips and Tricks | Hair Care Guide 2023

Short hair can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you style it. One of the biggest challenges with short hair is keeping it in place and preventing it from sticking out in all directions. Thankfully, there are several tips and tricks you can use to tame those unruly strands and achieve a sleek, polished look. In this article, we’ll share some expert advice on how to stop short hair sticking out.

How to Stop Short Hair Sticking Out: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

The first step to preventing short hair from sticking out is to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to style and keep in place. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh your hair down or make it greasy. After washing, gently towel-dry your hair and let it air-dry until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Use a styling product

A styling product can help keep short hair in place and prevent it from sticking out. Choose a product that's appropriate for your hair type and desired style. For example, if you want a sleek, smooth look, use a smoothing serum. If you want more volume, use a volumizing mousse. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Blow-drying your hair can help set the styling product and keep your short hair in place. Use a round brush to smooth your hair as you blow-dry it. Make sure to direct the airflow down the hair shaft to prevent frizz and flyaways. Once your hair is completely dry, use a cool shot of air to set the style.

Step 4: Use a flat iron or curling iron

If you want to further style your short hair, use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. Use the iron to smooth out any remaining frizz or flyaways and set your style in place.

Tips for Preventing Short Hair from Sticking Out

Here are some additional tips for keeping short hair in place and preventing it from sticking out:

Tip 1: Use bobby pins or hair clips

If you have a few stubborn strands that just won't stay in place, use bobby pins or hair clips to secure them. This is a quick and easy way to tame unruly hair.

Tip 2: Don't overuse styling products

While styling products can help keep short hair in place, it's important not to overuse them. Too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy or limp.

Tip 3: Choose the right haircut

The right haircut can make all the difference when it comes to preventing short hair from sticking out. Talk to your stylist about a cut that will work with your hair type and desired style.

FAQs

Q: Can I use hairspray to keep my short hair in place?

A: Yes, you can use hairspray to keep short hair in place. However, make sure to choose a hairspray that won't make your hair stiff or sticky.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise regularly, you may need to wash your hair every day or every other day. If you have dry hair or a more sedentary lifestyle, you may be able to go longer between washes.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Keeping short hair in place is just one aspect of hair care. Here are some additional tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

Tip 1: Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

A gentle shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair clean and healthy without stripping it of its natural oils. Look for products that are free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals.

Tip 2: Protect your hair from heat

Heat can damage your hair and cause it to become brittle and break. Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools, and avoid using them too often.

Tip 3: Get regular trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Talk to your stylist about how often you should get a trim based on your hair type and desired style.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your short hair looking sleek and polished, without any pesky strands sticking out. Remember to be gentle with your hair, and avoid over-styling or using too many products. With a little care and attention, you can achieve the perfect short hair look.
