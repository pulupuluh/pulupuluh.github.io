Would Long Hair Suit Me Male Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Daniel Jul 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Long hair is a style that has been around for centuries, and it has been worn by both men and women. However, not every man can pull off this look. In this article, we will explore the question of "would long hair suit me male" and provide tips, tricks, and hair care advice for men who want to try this style.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

Long hair can be a great style for men, but it is not for everyone. Your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle are all factors that can determine whether or not long hair will suit you. If you have a round face, for example, long hair may not be the best option for you, as it can make your face look even rounder. On the other hand, if you have a more angular face, long hair can help soften your features and create a more balanced look.

How to

The first step in determining whether long hair will suit you is to consider your face shape. Look in the mirror and study your features. If you have a square or angular face, long hair may be a good option for you. If your face is more rounded, you may want to consider a shorter style.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have thick, curly hair, long hair may not be the best option for you, as it can be difficult to manage. If you have straight or wavy hair, however, long hair can be a great option.

Step by Step

If you have decided that long hair is the right style for you, here are some steps you can take to achieve the look:

  1. Start by growing out your hair. Depending on your current style, this may take several months to a year.
  2. Visit a stylist who specializes in long hair. They can help you determine the best length for your face shape and hair texture, as well as provide advice on how to care for your hair.
  3. Invest in high-quality hair care products, such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products. Long hair requires more maintenance than shorter styles, so it is important to use products that will nourish and protect your hair.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques, such as braids, ponytails, and buns. These can help keep your hair out of your face and add interest to your style.

Tips

  • Consider your lifestyle when deciding on a long hair style. If you are active or work in a profession that requires you to wear a helmet, for example, long hair may not be practical.
  • Be patient when growing out your hair. It can take time to achieve the length and style you want.
  • Visit a stylist regularly to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to prevent tangles and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Will long hair suit me if I have a receding hairline?

A: Long hair can actually be a good option for men with a receding hairline, as it can help cover up bald spots and create the illusion of thicker hair. However, it is important to work with a stylist who can help you determine the best style for your hair type and face shape.

Q: Can I still wear a hat with long hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear a hat with long hair. However, it is important to choose a hat that fits well and does not cause your hair to become flattened or tangled.

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: How often you wash your hair will depend on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work in a profession that causes you to sweat, you may need to wash your hair more frequently. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, so it is important to find a balance that works for you.

Hair Care

Long hair requires more maintenance than shorter styles, so it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your long hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hair ties that are too tight, as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With the right care and maintenance, long hair can be a great style for men. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, as well as following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a long hair style that suits you and makes you feel confident and stylish.
