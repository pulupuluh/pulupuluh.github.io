Face Frame Haircut For Curly Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 25, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the right haircut that flatters your face shape. A face frame haircut for curly hair is an excellent option to add dimension and definition to your curls while accentuating your best features. In this guide, we’ll show you how to get a face frame haircut for curly hair, provide some tips and tricks, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Get a Face Frame Haircut for Curly Hair

Getting a face frame haircut for curly hair is not as complicated as it seems. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step in getting a face frame haircut for curly hair is to consult with your stylist. Communication is key. Discuss your hair type, face shape, and the look you want to achieve. Your stylist will recommend the best face frame haircut for you, taking into consideration your hair texture, length, and curl pattern.

Step 2: Wash and Condition

Before your haircut, make sure your hair is clean and conditioned. It’s easier for your stylist to work with clean hair, and it will also give you a better idea of how your hair falls naturally.

Step 3: Sectioning

Your stylist will section your hair to ensure an even haircut. They will start by sectioning the top layer of your hair and work their way down.

Step 4: Cutting

Your stylist will cut your hair according to the face frame style you’ve chosen. They will use a combination of layering and texturizing techniques to create a natural-looking, face-framing effect that enhances your curls.

Step 5: Styling

Once your haircut is complete, your stylist will style your hair using the right products and tools. They will show you how to maintain your new haircut at home, including how to use a diffuser to enhance your curls.

Tips for Maintaining a Face Frame Haircut for Curly Hair

A face frame haircut for curly hair is low maintenance, but it still requires some care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your haircut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and frizz-free.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and disrupt your curl pattern.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about face frame haircuts for curly hair:

What face shapes suit a face frame haircut for curly hair?

A face frame haircut for curly hair can suit most face shapes, but it’s particularly flattering for those with oval, heart-shaped, or square faces.

Can I get a face frame haircut if I have short curly hair?

Yes, you can. Your stylist will adjust the length and layers to suit your hair length and texture.

How often should I get a face frame haircut for curly hair?

You should get a face frame haircut for curly hair every six to eight weeks to maintain your haircut’s shape and prevent split ends.

Final Thoughts

A face frame haircut for curly hair is an excellent way to enhance your natural curls and add dimension to your hair. With the right cut and care, you can rock this style with confidence. Use the tips and tricks in this guide to help you achieve your desired look.
