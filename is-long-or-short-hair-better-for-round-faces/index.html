Is Long Or Short Hair Better For Round Faces

Written by Petter May 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you trying to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape? Deciding between long and short hair can be a tough decision, but it ultimately depends on your personal preference and hair type. In this article, we will explore whether long or short hair is better for round faces and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded jawline. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face shape is to create the illusion of length and angles. Long hair can elongate the face, while short hair can add volume and shape to the hair.

When deciding between long and short hair, it's important to consider your hair type and whether you have the time and patience to style and maintain your hair. Long hair may require more time and effort to style, while short hair can be easier to manage and style.

How to

The best way to determine whether long or short hair is better for your round face shape is to try out different hairstyles and see what looks best on you. Here are some tips on how to style your hair for a round face:

Long Hair

If you have long hair, try adding layers or side-swept bangs to create the illusion of length and angles. Avoid blunt cuts or heavy bangs that can make the face appear rounder. Experiment with different hairstyles, such as loose waves or a side ponytail, to add texture and movement to your hair.

Short Hair

If you have short hair, consider adding volume and texture to your hair with layers or choppy cuts. Avoid blunt cuts or hairstyles that are too short, as they can make the face appear rounder. Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a pixie cut or a bob, to find the perfect look for your face shape.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style your hair for a round face:

Long Hair

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited for your hair type.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  3. Add layers or side-swept bangs to create the illusion of length and angles.
  4. Style your hair with loose waves or a side ponytail to add texture and movement.
  5. Finish with a hair spray or serum to keep your hair in place.

Short Hair

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited for your hair type.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  3. Add layers or choppy cuts to create volume and texture.
  4. Style your hair with a pixie cut or a bob to find the perfect look for your face shape.
  5. Finish with a hair spray or serum to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to style and care for your hair:

  • Use products that are suited for your hair type, such as shampoo and conditioner for color-treated hair or products for curly hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find the perfect look for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round faces:

Is long hair or short hair better for round faces?

It ultimately depends on your personal preference and hair type. Long hair can elongate the face, while short hair can add volume and shape to the hair.

What are some hairstyles that are good for round faces?

Hairstyles that are good for round faces include long layers, side-swept bangs, pixie cuts, and bobs.

How do I style my hair to make my face appear less round?

To make your face appear less round, try adding layers or side-swept bangs to create the illusion of length and angles. Avoid blunt cuts or heavy bangs that can make the face appear rounder.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is important to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Use products that are suited for your hair type and concerns, such as shampoo and conditioner for dry or damaged hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Ultimately, the decision between long or short hair for round faces is up to you. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the perfect look for your face shape and hair type. Remember to take care of your hair and try out new products and styles to keep your hair healthy and looking great!
