How To Deal With Excessive Hair Fall Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Apr 30, 2023 · 9 min read

If you’re experiencing excessive hair fall, you’re not alone. Many people suffer from hair loss due to a variety of factors, including genetics, stress, and poor nutrition. However, there are steps you can take to help prevent hair loss and promote hair growth. In this article, we’ll provide you with some tips and tricks to help you deal with excessive hair fall.

How to Deal with Excessive Hair Fall

Dealing with excessive hair fall can be a frustrating experience, but there are many things you can do to help prevent further hair loss and promote hair growth. Here are some tips to help you deal with excessive hair fall:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure your diet includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals. Foods that are rich in protein, such as eggs, fish, and lean meats, are particularly beneficial for hair growth.

2. Manage Stress

Stress is a common cause of hair loss. To help prevent hair loss, try to manage your stress levels. You can do this by practicing relaxation techniques, such as yoga or meditation, or by engaging in activities you enjoy, such as reading or hiking.

3. Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Using harsh hair care products can damage your hair and contribute to hair loss. To help prevent hair loss, use gentle hair care products that are free from harsh chemicals.

4. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, can damage your hair and contribute to hair loss. To help prevent hair loss, avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

5. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help promote hair growth by increasing blood flow to the hair follicles. To massage your scalp, use your fingertips to gently rub your scalp in circular motions.

6. Take Supplements

There are many supplements available that can help promote hair growth. Some of the most popular supplements for hair growth include biotin, vitamin D, and iron.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Deal with Excessive Hair Fall

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you deal with excessive hair fall:

Step 1: Evaluate Your Diet

The first step in dealing with excessive hair fall is to evaluate your diet. Make sure your diet includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals, and consider taking supplements if necessary.

Step 2: Manage Your Stress Levels

The next step is to manage your stress levels. Practice relaxation techniques, such as yoga or meditation, and engage in activities you enjoy.

Step 3: Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Use gentle hair care products that are free from harsh chemicals to help prevent hair loss.

Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to help prevent hair loss.

Step 5: Massage Your Scalp

Massage your scalp regularly to help promote hair growth.

Step 6: Take Supplements

Consider taking supplements, such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron, to help promote hair growth.

Tips for Dealing with Excessive Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips for dealing with excessive hair fall:

1. Don’t Brush Your Hair When It’s Wet

Wet hair is more fragile than dry hair, so avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet.

2. Be Gentle When Brushing Your Hair

Be gentle when brushing your hair to avoid damaging it.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair tight, as they can contribute to hair loss.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair care product that contains SPF.

FAQ: Excessive Hair Fall

Q: What Causes Excessive Hair Fall?

A: Excessive hair fall can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, stress, and poor nutrition.

Q: Can Excessive Hair Fall Be Reversed?

A: In many cases, excessive hair fall can be reversed with proper hair care and lifestyle changes.

Q: What Are the Best Supplements for Hair Growth?

A: Some of the best supplements for hair growth include biotin, vitamin D, and iron.

Hair Care: Tips for Healthy Hair

Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use a Good Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.

3. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and contribute to hair loss.

4. Use Hair Masks

Use hair masks regularly to nourish and hydrate your hair.

5. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Dealing with excessive hair fall can be a challenge, but by following these tips and tricks, you can help prevent further hair loss and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to eat a balanced diet, manage your stress levels, use gentle hair care products, avoid heat styling tools, massage your scalp, and take supplements if necessary. With a little care and attention, you can keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
