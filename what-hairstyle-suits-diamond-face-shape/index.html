What Hairstyle Suits Diamond Face Shape In 2023

What is Diamond Face Shape?

Diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and jawline with wider cheekbones. This face shape is often considered the most versatile as it can pull off a range of hairstyles. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that balances your features and complements your face shape.

What Hairstyle Suits Diamond Face Shape?

A diamond face shape can pull off a variety of hairstyles, but the best ones are those that soften the cheekbones while adding volume to the forehead and chin. Here are some hairstyles that suit diamond face shape:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bobs with volume
  • Soft curls
  • Pixie cut
  • High ponytail

These hairstyles help balance the face shape and draw attention away from the cheekbones. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and suits your personality and lifestyle.

How to Style Hair for Diamond Face Shape?

The key to styling hair for diamond face shape is to soften the cheekbones and add volume to the forehead and chin. Here are some tips on how to style your hair for diamond face shape:

  • Go for long layers to add volume to your hair
  • Side-swept bangs soften the cheekbones and draw attention to the eyes
  • A bob with volume helps balance the face shape
  • Soft curls add volume to the forehead and chin
  • A pixie cut can be edgy and modern while balancing the face shape
  • A high ponytail adds volume to the forehead and elongates the face

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair for diamond face shape:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume
  4. If you have long hair, add layers to add volume
  5. If you have bangs, sweep them to the side to soften the cheekbones
  6. If you want to add curls, use a curling iron or rollers
  7. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips for Diamond Face Shape Hairstyles

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for diamond face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the cheekbones
  • Avoid center-parted hairstyles that elongate the face
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the forehead and chin
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works for you
  • Consider your lifestyle and hair texture when choosing a hairstyle

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about diamond face shape hairstyles:

What is the best hairstyle for diamond face shape?

The best hairstyle for diamond face shape is one that softens the cheekbones and adds volume to the forehead and chin. Hairstyles like long layers, side-swept bangs, and bobs with volume work well for diamond face shape.

Can diamond face shape pull off a pixie cut?

Yes, diamond face shape can pull off a pixie cut. A pixie cut can be edgy and modern while balancing the face shape. However, it's important to choose a pixie cut that flatters your face shape and suits your personality and lifestyle.

How often should I trim my hair if I have diamond face shape?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and volume of your hairstyle. Regular trims also prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for diamond face shape:

  • Use a heat protectant before blow drying or using heat styling tools
  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions

Now that you know what hairstyle suits diamond face shape, it's time to experiment with different hairstyles and find what works for you. Remember to keep these tips in mind and take care of your hair to maintain healthy and voluminous locks. Happy styling!
