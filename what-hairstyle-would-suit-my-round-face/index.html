What Hairstyle Would Suit My Round Face

Written by Eveline Apr 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your features. However, with the right cut and style, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a look that flatters your face shape. In this article, we'll explore what hairstyles would suit a round face, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face typically has a soft, curved jawline, full cheeks, and a wide forehead. To balance the proportions of a round face, you want to create height and length in the hair. The best hairstyles for a round face are those that add volume and elongate the face shape. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face or emphasize the roundness of the cheeks.

How to

The key to finding a hairstyle that suits a round face is to create angles and lines that break up the roundness. Here are some tips on how to achieve this:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height and length to the face, such as a long bob or a layered cut.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheek level, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Side-swept bangs can help create a diagonal line that adds length to the face.
  • Curls or waves can add volume and texture to the hair, which can help balance the proportions of a round face.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair, as they can add movement and create angles that break up the roundness.

Step by step

Here's how to create a hairstyle that suits a round face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to create volume at the roots and stretch the hair outwards to add length.
  4. If you want to add curls or waves, use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose, natural-looking waves.
  5. Finish the look with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or close to the head, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Consider getting highlights or balayage to add dimension and texture to the hair.
  • If you have a round face and want to wear a short hairstyle, opt for a pixie cut with lots of layers and texture.
  • If you have a round face and want to wear a ponytail, create height at the crown of the head to elongate the face.

FAQ

1. Can I wear a center part with a round face?

A center part can emphasize the roundness of a face, so it's best to avoid this style if you have a round face. Instead, opt for a side part to create asymmetry and add length to the face.

2. Can I wear a bob with a round face?

A bob can work well with a round face as long as it's cut to add length and volume to the hair. Consider a long bob with layers or a graduated bob that's slightly longer in the front.

3. Can I wear bangs with a round face?

Yes, but it's important to choose the right style of bangs. Side-swept bangs that are longer at the sides than the center can help create a diagonal line that adds length to the face. Avoid blunt bangs that end at the cheek level, as they can make the face appear wider.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, finding a hairstyle that suits a round face is all about creating angles and lines that break up the roundness. By choosing the right cut and style, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a look that flatters your face shape. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
