How To Do A High Fade Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Feb 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Looking for a fresh new hairstyle? Why not try a high fade undercut? This edgy and versatile style has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's not hard to see why. With a high fade undercut, you get the best of both worlds: a sleek and polished look on top, and a bold and daring undercut on the sides and back. But how do you achieve this look? In this guide, we'll show you step-by-step how to do a high fade undercut, so you can rock this style with confidence.

Description

A high fade undercut is a hairstyle that features a short cut on the sides and back of the head, gradually blending into longer hair on top. The fade is typically high, meaning it starts near the temples and gradually tapers down to the nape of the neck. The undercut refers to the shaved or closely cropped hair underneath the longer hair on top. This creates a bold and striking contrast between the two lengths.

How to

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and some styling products. Here's how to do a high fade undercut:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length you want on top. You can go for a short, cropped look, or keep it longer for a more relaxed vibe. Once you've decided, use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.

Step 2: Create a Guide

Next, create a guide for your fade by using your clippers to buzz the hair around the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard as you move lower on the head.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

Using your clippers, start blending the hair around the sides and back of your head, working your way up towards the longer hair on top. Use a comb to lift the hair and create a smooth transition between the different lengths.

Step 4: Define the Undercut

Once you've blended the hair, it's time to define the undercut. Use your clippers to shave or closely crop the hair beneath the longer hair on top. Be sure to create a clean and even line around the entire head.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Finally, style your hair using your preferred products. You can slick it back for a more polished look, or leave it messy for a more relaxed vibe. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when doing a high fade undercut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to create a smooth and gradual transition.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and create a seamless blend between the different lengths.
  • Be careful when defining the undercut to create an even and symmetrical line around the head.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about high fade undercuts:

How long does a high fade undercut last?

A high fade undercut can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Can I do a high fade undercut at home?

While it's possible to do a high fade undercut at home, we recommend going to a professional stylist to ensure a clean and even cut.

What face shapes suit a high fade undercut?

A high fade undercut can suit a variety of face shapes, but it works particularly well for those with square or diamond-shaped faces.

Hair Care

To keep your high fade undercut looking fresh and polished, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a styling product that works well with your hair type and texture.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

With this step-by-step guide, you'll be able to achieve a high fade undercut in no time. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styling products to find your perfect look.
