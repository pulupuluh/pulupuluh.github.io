Are Bangs Good On Round Faces

Written by Bardi Aug 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

In this article, we will discuss whether bangs are a good option for those with round faces. We will cover everything from how to choose the right type of bangs to the best hair care practices. If you have a round face and are considering bangs, keep reading!

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you may be wondering if bangs are a good option for you. The answer is yes, but it depends on the type of bangs you choose. Bangs can help to balance out the proportions of a round face and draw attention to your features.

How to Choose the Right Type of Bangs

When it comes to choosing the right type of bangs for a round face, there are a few things to consider:

Face Shape

Consider the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you will want to choose bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help to elongate your face and create a more oval shape.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair will also play a role in which type of bangs you should choose. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for heavier bangs that will help to add weight and balance out your face. If you have fine hair, lighter, wispy bangs may be a better option.

Personal Style

Your personal style will also come into play when choosing the right type of bangs for your face. If you prefer a more edgy look, you may want to opt for choppy, asymmetrical bangs. If you prefer a softer look, side-swept bangs may be a better option.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right type of bangs for your round face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Look in the mirror and determine the shape of your face. If you have a round face, move on to step 2.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Think about the texture of your hair. If you have thick hair, heavier bangs may be a better option. If you have fine hair, lighter, wispy bangs may be a better option.

Step 3: Think About Your Personal Style

Consider your personal style. Do you prefer a more edgy look or a softer look? This will help you to determine which type of bangs will work best for you.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist. They can help you to determine the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair texture.

Tips for Styling Bangs on a Round Face

Here are a few tips for styling bangs on a round face:

Blow Dry Your Bangs

Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and lift. This will help to elongate your face.

Use a Flat Iron

Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz. This will help to create a clean, polished look.

Add Texture

Add texture to your bangs by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. This will help to add volume and movement.

FAQ

Q: Can bangs make a round face look even rounder?

A: Yes, if you choose the wrong type of bangs, they can make a round face look even rounder. Itâs important to choose bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle to help elongate the face.

Q: Are side-swept bangs a good option for a round face?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can be a good option for a round face. They help to create an elongated look and draw attention to your features.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: Itâs recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and polished.

Hair Care for Bangs

Here are a few tips for caring for your bangs:

Wash Them Separately

Wash your bangs separately from the rest of your hair. This will help to keep them looking clean and prevent them from becoming greasy.

Blow Dry Them Immediately

Blow dry your bangs immediately after washing them to prevent them from becoming frizzy or misshapen.

Use Dry Shampoo

Use dry shampoo to extend the life of your bangs between washes.

In conclusion, bangs can be a great option for those with round faces as long as you choose the right type of bangs. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair texture. With the right care and styling, bangs can help to balance out the proportions of a round face and draw attention to your features.
