Best Hairstyles For 60 Year Old Woman A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Sep 02, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're a 60-year-old woman, you're at a stage where you want to look and feel your best. One way to achieve this is by choosing the right hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. In this guide, we'll walk you through the best hairstyles for 60-year-old women, along with tips, how-to's, and hair care advice.

Description

As you age, your hair texture and face shape change. Therefore, choosing the right hairstyle is essential to enhance your beauty and confidence. The best hairstyles for 60-year-old women are those that flatter your features and suit your personality. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there's a perfect style for you.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle

Before you choose a hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle:

  • Round faces: Go for hairstyles with volume on top and length on the sides
  • Square faces: Opt for soft, layered hairstyles that frame your face
  • Oval faces: Almost any hairstyle works for this face shape
  • Heart-shaped faces: Look for hairstyles with layers that start from the chin
  • Thick hair: Choose hairstyles with layers to add texture and movement
  • Thin hair: Go for short hairstyles with volume on top
  • Busy lifestyle: Choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that's easy to style
  • Active lifestyle: Opt for a hairstyle that keeps your hair away from your face and neck

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, follow these steps to style it:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and apply conditioner to the ends
  2. Towel-dry your hair and comb it to remove tangles
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and lift
  4. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add curls or waves, if desired
  5. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or hairspray, to hold your hairstyle in place

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your hairstyle looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to hydrate and nourish your hair
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray
  • Avoid harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes and perms, which can damage your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and flat irons, too frequently

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for 60-year-old women:

Q: What are the best short hairstyles for 60-year-old women?

A: The best short hairstyles for 60-year-old women are pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts. These styles are easy to maintain and flatter most face shapes.

Q: What are the best medium hairstyles for 60-year-old women?

A: The best medium hairstyles for 60-year-old women are shoulder-length cuts with layers, soft waves, or curls. These styles add volume and movement to your hair.

Q: What are the best long hairstyles for 60-year-old women?

A: The best long hairstyles for 60-year-old women are layered cuts with side-swept bangs, beachy waves, or loose curls. These styles add texture and dimension to your hair.

Hair Care for Older Women

As you age, your hair becomes thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. Therefore, it's essential to take care of your hair by following these tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that hydrate and nourish your hair
  • Avoid using hot water, which can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to add moisture to your hair
  • Massage your scalp to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, which can cause breakage

Short Hairstyles for 60-Year-Old Women

Short hairstyles are perfect for 60-year-old women who want a low-maintenance, chic look. Here are some of the best short hairstyles:

  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Bob cut with layers
  • Cropped cut with volume on top
  • Sleek bob with blunt bangs
  • Short shag with choppy layers

Medium Hairstyles for 60-Year-Old Women

Medium hairstyles are versatile and flatter most face shapes. Here are some of the best medium hairstyles:

  • Shoulder-length cut with layers
  • Soft waves with side-swept bangs
  • Curls with volume on top
  • Sleek and straight with blunt bangs
  • Layered bob with highlights

Long Hairstyles for 60-Year-Old Women

Long hairstyles add texture and movement to your hair. Here are some of the best long hairstyles:

  • Layered cut with side-swept bangs
  • Beachy waves with highlights
  • Loose curls with volume on top
  • Straight and sleek with layers
  • Half up, half down with braids

Conclusion

Choosing the best hairstyle for 60-year-old women can be challenging, but it doesn't have to be. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and complements your personality. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there's a perfect style for you. Follow our tips, how-to's, and hair care advice to keep your hair looking its best at any age.
