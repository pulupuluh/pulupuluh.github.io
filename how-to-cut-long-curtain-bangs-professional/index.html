How To Cut Long Curtain Bangs Professional

Written by Eveline Apr 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile hairdo that can give you a fresh new look, long curtain bangs might be just what you need. This trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle can be easily achieved with a little bit of patience and some basic hair cutting skills. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting long curtain bangs professionally, step by step. Here's what you'll learn:

How to Cut Long Curtain Bangs Professional

Before getting started, it's important to have the right tools and materials at hand. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Once you have all these items ready, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. You can either do this by parting your hair in the middle or by creating a side part. Use your comb to create a straight line from the top of your head to the back of your ear on each side. Clip the rest of your hair away from your face.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide how long you want your bangs to be. Keep in mind that curtain bangs usually start from the middle of your forehead and end around your cheekbones or chin. Use your comb to comb your hair forward and decide on the length you want. You can use your fingers to hold your hair in place while you measure.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Use your scissors to cut your bangs. Start by cutting the middle section first, then work your way outwards. Cut your bangs in small sections to make sure they're even. Keep in mind that it's better to cut less hair than too much at once. You can always go back and trim more later.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting your bangs, check for evenness. Use your comb to comb your bangs straight down and check if they're at the same length on both sides. If not, use your scissors to trim any uneven parts.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Style your bangs by using your fingers to create a center part or a side part. You can also use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your bangs. Finish off with some hairspray to hold your bangs in place.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you achieve the perfect long curtain bangs:

  • Start by cutting a little bit of hair at a time. You can always go back and trim more if needed.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Keep your hair wet while cutting to make it easier to work with.
  • Make sure your bangs are at the same length on both sides.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short. You can always trim more later.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long curtain bangs:

Q: Can I cut my bangs myself?

A: Yes, you can cut your bangs yourself if you have the right tools and some basic hair cutting skills.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your bangs. Generally, you should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I style my bangs differently?

A: Yes, you can style your bangs in different ways. You can create a center part, a side part, or a messy look.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your long curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut long curtain bangs professionally and achieve a stylish and versatile hairdo. Just remember to start small, take your time, and be patient. Happy cutting!
