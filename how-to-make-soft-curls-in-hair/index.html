How To Make Soft Curls In Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to add some volume and bounce to your hair, soft curls are the perfect way to achieve that. Not only do they look great, but they're also trendy and easy to maintain. In this article, we'll go through the steps to achieve soft curls in your hair, as well as some tips and tricks to make the process smoother.

How to Make Soft Curls in Hair

Here are the steps to follow to make soft curls in your hair:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin curling your hair, you need to prepare it properly. This means washing and conditioning it as usual, and applying a heat protectant spray to protect it from heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take one section at a time and wrap it around a curling iron. Hold it for around 10 seconds, then release it. Repeat this process for each section until your entire head is curled.

Step 4: Brush Out Your Curls

Once you're done curling your hair, brush it out gently using a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush. This will help to loosen the curls and create a soft, bouncy effect.

Step 5: Apply Hairspray

To help your curls stay in place, apply a light mist of hairspray. Avoid using too much, as this can make your hair look stiff and crunchy.

Tips for Making Soft Curls in Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making soft curls in your hair:

Tip 1: Use a Curling Iron with a Large Barrel

A curling iron with a larger barrel will help to create bigger, softer curls.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

To achieve soft curls, you need to use the right products. This includes a heat protectant spray, a curling cream, and a hairspray.

Tip 3: Don't Over-Curl Your Hair

Over-curling your hair can result in tight, ringlet-like curls that don't look soft or natural.

FAQ

What is the Best Curling Iron for Soft Curls?

A curling iron with a large barrel, such as a 1.5-inch or 2-inch barrel, is best for creating soft curls.

How Long Will Soft Curls Last?

Soft curls can last for several hours or even a full day, depending on how well you maintain them.

Can You Make Soft Curls Without Heat?

Yes, you can make soft curls without heat by using foam rollers or flexi rods. Simply wrap your hair around the rollers or rods and leave them in for several hours or overnight.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take good care of it. This includes washing it regularly, using a deep conditioner once a week, and avoiding heat styling as much as possible.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve soft, bouncy curls that are perfect for any occasion. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to try new things. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at creating soft curls in no time!
