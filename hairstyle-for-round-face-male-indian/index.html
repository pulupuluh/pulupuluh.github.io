Hairstyles For Round Face Male Indian

Written by Oliver Apr 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But fear not, there are plenty of hairstyles that can suit your face shape and enhance your features. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for round face male Indian.

Description

Round face shape is characterized by soft, circular features such as full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. The key to finding the right hairstyle for this face shape is to create the illusion of length and height. This can be achieved by adding volume on top or by creating angles that balance out the roundness of the face.

How to

Here are some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle for round face male Indian:

1. Emphasize the top

Adding volume on top can elongate the face and create the illusion of height. This can be achieved with hairstyles such as the quiff, pompadour, or messy spikes. These styles work well with any hair type and can be easily maintained with the right hair products.

2. Short sides, long top

A hairstyle with short sides and a longer top can create the illusion of length and balance out the roundness of the face. This can be achieved with styles such as the undercut or the side part. These styles work well with straight or wavy hair and can be easily styled with a hair dryer and some pomade.

3. Angular Fringe

An angular fringe is a great hairstyle for round face male Indian. This style adds angles to the face, making it appear longer and leaner. This style works well with straight or wavy hair and can be easily styled with a hair dryer and some hair wax.

4. Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a low maintenance hairstyle that can work well for a round face male Indian. This style creates a clean, sharp look that can balance out the softness of the face. This style works well with any hair type and can be easily maintained with regular haircuts.

Step by Step

Here are some step by step instructions on how to achieve some of the best hairstyles for round face male Indian:

Quiff

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
2. Apply a small amount of pomade to your hands and work it through your hair.
3. Using a comb, create a side part.
4. Use a hair dryer and a round brush to create volume on top.
5. Finish off with a light hold hairspray.

Undercut

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
2. Use a clipper to trim the sides and back to a short length.
3. Use a hair dryer and a round brush to create volume on top.
4. Finish off with some hair wax to add texture and hold.

Angular Fringe

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
2. Create an angular fringe by cutting the hair on top shorter on one side and longer on the other.
3. Apply some hair wax to your hands and work it through your hair.
4. Use a hair dryer and a round brush to style the fringe.
5. Finish off with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for round face male Indian:

1. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face.

Hairstyles such as the bowl cut or the mushroom cut can make the face appear wider and should be avoided.

2. Choose hairstyles that create angles.

Hairstyles with angular lines can create the illusion of length and balance out the roundness of the face.

3. Experiment with hair products.

The right hair product can make all the difference in achieving the perfect hairstyle. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face male Indian?

The best hairstyle for a round face male Indian is one that creates the illusion of length and height. Hairstyles such as the quiff, pompadour, or angular fringe work well for this face shape.

Can a buzz cut work for a round face male Indian?

Yes, a buzz cut can work for a round face male Indian. This style creates a clean, sharp look that can balance out the softness of the face.

How do I style my hair for a round face?

Styling your hair for a round face involves creating the illusion of length and height. This can be achieved by adding volume on top or by creating angles that balance out the roundness of the face.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is essential for achieving any hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

1. Wash your hair regularly.

Washing your hair regularly can help remove dirt and oil buildup that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

2. Use conditioner.

Conditioner can help keep your hair soft and healthy. Use a conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type and apply it to the ends of your hair.

3. Protect your hair from heat damage.

Using heat styling tools such as a hair dryer or flat iron can damage your hair over time. Use a heat protectant spray before using these tools to help minimize damage.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and look your best as a round face male Indian.
