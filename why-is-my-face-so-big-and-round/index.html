Why Is My Face So Big And Round

Written by Aprilia Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you've been wondering why your face seems to be bigger and rounder than usual, you're not alone. This is a common concern for many people, and it can be caused by a variety of factors, such as genetics, weight gain, and hormonal changes. In this article, we'll explore the reasons behind a big and round face, as well as some tips on how to manage it.

Description

A big and round face can be characterized by a wide jawline, full cheeks, and a soft chin. It can make you look heavier and older than you actually are, and it can also affect your self-esteem. While some people may be born with a naturally round face, others may develop it over time due to factors such as weight gain or aging.

How to Determine if You Have a Big and Round Face

To determine if you have a big and round face, stand in front of a mirror and look at the shape of your face. If your face is wider than it is long, and you have a round jawline and full cheeks, you likely have a big and round face. You can also try measuring your face with a tape measure. Measure the distance from your hairline to your chin, and then measure the width of your face at its widest point. If your face is more than 1.5 times wider than it is long, you have a big and round face.

Step by Step Guide on How to Manage a Big and Round Face

Step 1: Lose Weight

If your big and round face is due to weight gain, losing weight can help reduce the size of your face. This can be achieved through a combination of a healthy diet and regular exercise. Aim to lose weight slowly and steadily, as rapid weight loss can cause your face to sag and make it look even bigger.

Step 2: Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone the muscles in your face and reduce the appearance of a big and round face. Some exercises you can try include puffing out your cheeks and holding for a few seconds, then blowing the air out slowly. You can also try smiling while keeping your lips closed, and then puckering your lips while keeping your teeth together.

Step 3: Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can help balance out a big and round face. Consider getting a haircut that adds height to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with layers. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face look wider.

Tips for Managing a Big and Round Face

Tip 1: Drink Plenty of Water
Drinking plenty of water can help flush out toxins and reduce water retention, which can contribute to a big and round face.

Tip 2: Avoid Salty Foods
Salty foods can cause water retention, which can make your face look puffy and bloated. Avoid processed foods and snacks that are high in sodium.

Tip 3: Get Enough Sleep
Getting enough sleep can help reduce stress and prevent your face from looking tired and puffy.

FAQ

Q: Can genetics cause a big and round face?
A: Yes, genetics can play a role in the shape of your face. If your parents have a big and round face, you may be more likely to develop one as well.

Q: Can hormonal changes cause a big and round face?
A: Yes, hormonal changes such as pregnancy or menopause can cause water retention and make your face look puffy and round.

Hair Care for a Big and Round Face

The right hair care routine can help manage a big and round face. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add height to the top of your head, and avoid hair products that add volume to the sides of your head. Consider getting bangs or layers to frame your face and draw attention away from a wide jawline.

Overall, a big and round face can be managed through a combination of lifestyle changes, facial exercises, and the right hairstyle. By following these tips, you can feel more confident and comfortable in your own skin.
