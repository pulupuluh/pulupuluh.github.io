How To Do A Messy Bun Updo A Relaxed Guide

Written by Daniel May 28, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old sleek and polished bun? Want to switch things up with a more relaxed and effortless look? Look no further than the messy bun updo. This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a lazy day at home or a night out with friends. In this article, we'll show you how to do a messy bun updo step by step and provide tips for maintaining it. Let's get started!

How to Do a Messy Bun Updo

To achieve the perfect messy bun updo, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture. Brush through your hair to distribute the product evenly.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Flip your hair upside down and gather it into a high ponytail. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 3: Create Your Bun

Twist your ponytail around the hair tie to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure any loose pieces in place. Don't worry about making it perfect – the messier, the better!

Step 4: Pull Out Some Strands

Pull out a few strands of hair around your face and at the nape of your neck to create a more relaxed look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep your messy bun updo in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Messy Bun Updo

Now that you know how to do a messy bun updo, here are some tips for maintaining it:

Tip 1: Embrace the Messiness

The key to a perfect messy bun updo is embracing the messiness. Don't worry about making it look perfect – the more imperfect it is, the better!

Tip 2: Use Bobby Pins

Bobby pins are your best friend when it comes to creating a messy bun updo. Use them to secure any loose pieces in place.

Tip 3: Add Accessories

Add a headband, scarf, or hair clip to your messy bun updo to add a pop of color or texture.

FAQ

Can I do a messy bun updo on short hair?

Yes! You may need to use more bobby pins to secure your hair in place, but a messy bun updo can be achieved on short hair.

Can I do a messy bun updo on curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, curly hair can add even more texture and volume to your messy bun updo.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools and deep conditioning regularly. Avoid using too much hairspray or other styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it harder to achieve a messy bun updo.

And there you have it – a step-by-step guide on how to do a messy bun updo. Have fun with this hairstyle and make it your own!
