How To Wear A Short Wig With Long Thick Hair

Written by Aprilia Jun 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long and thick hair but don't want to chop it off? A short wig can be a great solution for a new look without sacrificing your natural hair. However, wearing a short wig with long and thick hair can be a bit tricky. In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to wear a short wig with long thick hair.

Table of Content

  • How to Wear a Short Wig with Long Thick Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Short Wig
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Wearing a Short Wig

How to Wear a Short Wig with Long Thick Hair

The first thing you need to do is prepare your natural hair. You want to make sure your hair is as flat as possible to prevent any lumps or bumps under the wig. Here are some steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly and dry it completely. Use a blow dryer to get rid of any excess moisture.

Step 2: Put Your Hair Up in a Bun

Put your hair up in a tight bun, using bobby pins or a hair tie. Make sure there are no bumps or lumps.

Step 3: Wear a Wig Cap

Wear a wig cap over your natural hair. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent any stray hairs from poking out.

Step 4: Put on the Wig

Put the wig on over the wig cap, making sure it's snug and secure. Adjust the wig so it sits comfortably on your head.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to wear a short wig with long thick hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Wig

Choose a wig that is the right size for your head. You don't want a wig that's too tight or too loose. Measure your head if necessary.

Step 2: Prepare Your Natural Hair

Follow the steps mentioned above to prepare your natural hair.

Step 3: Put on the Wig Cap

Put on the wig cap over your natural hair. Make sure it's tight and secure.

Step 4: Put on the Wig

Put the wig on over the wig cap. Make sure it's centered on your head and the ear tabs are in the right position.

Step 5: Secure the Wig

Secure the wig using the combs or clips that come with it. Make sure the wig is snug and secure.

Step 6: Style the Wig

Style the wig to your liking. You can use a wig brush or comb to style it. If necessary, you can also use heat styling tools.

Tips for Wearing a Short Wig

Here are some tips to make wearing a short wig with long thick hair easier:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place.
  • Choose a wig that is the right size for your head.
  • Secure the wig using the combs or clips that come with it.
  • Style the wig to your liking using a wig brush or comb.
  • Use heat styling tools if necessary.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear a short wig with long thick hair?

Yes, you can wear a short wig with long thick hair. You just need to prepare your natural hair properly and choose the right wig size.

How do I keep my wig from slipping?

Use the combs or clips that come with the wig to secure it to your head. You can also use wig glue or tape for extra security.

Can I style my wig with heat?

Yes, you can style your wig with heat. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray and low heat setting.

Hair Care for Wearing a Short Wig

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair when wearing a short wig:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Take breaks from wearing the wig to give your hair a rest.

With these tips, you'll be able to wear a short wig with long thick hair confidently and comfortably. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your new look!
