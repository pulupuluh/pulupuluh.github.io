What Bright Colour Hair Suits Me A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Shade

Written by Christine Apr 21, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your natural hair colour and looking to switch things up with a bold and bright new shade? Choosing a bright colour can be a fun way to express yourself and make a statement, but with so many options out there, it can be hard to know where to start. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to pick the perfect bright colour hair for your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding your perfect bright hair colour is to determine your skin tone. This will help you choose a shade that complements your natural complexion and brings out your best features. There are three main categories of skin tone: warm, cool, and neutral.

Warm Skin Tone

If you have warm undertones in your skin, you'll want to choose a bright hair colour that has a warm base, such as golden yellow, copper, or peachy pink. Warm skin tones typically have yellow or golden undertones, and you may notice that your veins appear greenish in natural light.

Cool Skin Tone

If you have cool undertones in your skin, you'll want to choose a bright hair colour that has a cool base, such as blue, purple, or silver. Cool skin tones typically have pink or blue undertones, and you may notice that your veins appear bluish or purple in natural light.

Neutral Skin Tone

If you have a neutral skin tone, you're lucky because you can pull off a wide range of bright hair colours! Neutral skin tones have a balance of warm and cool undertones, so you can choose a shade that leans in either direction or even go for something bold and vibrant like neon green or electric blue.

Step-by-Step Guide for Choosing Your Bright Hair Colour

Now that you know your skin tone, it's time to choose your perfect bright hair colour. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the shade that's right for you:

Step 1: Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

When choosing a bright hair colour, it's important to consider your natural hair colour. If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach your hair before applying a bright colour to achieve the desired shade.

Step 2: Decide on the Level of Brightness

Do you want a subtle pop of colour, or are you ready to go all-out with a bold and vibrant shade? Decide on the level of brightness you're comfortable with before choosing your colour.

Step 3: Look at Colour Charts

Look at colour charts to see what shades are available and which ones catch your eye. You can find colour charts online or at your local hair salon.

Step 4: Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in determining which bright hair colour will suit you best. For example, if you have blue eyes, a bright pink or purple shade can make your eyes pop.

Step 5: Seek Professional Advice

If you're unsure which shade will suit you best, don't be afraid to seek professional advice. A hairstylist can help you choose the perfect shade based on your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bright Hair Colour

Once you've chosen your perfect bright hair colour, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking vibrant and fresh. Here are some tips for maintaining your bright hair colour:

Tip 1: Use Colour-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to help prevent your bright hair colour from fading.

Tip 2: Minimize Washing

Try to wash your hair as infrequently as possible to help preserve the colour. When you do wash your hair, use cool water to help prevent the colour from fading.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can cause your bright hair colour to fade, so be sure to protect your hair with a hat or scarf when you're outside.

FAQs

Q: Will I need to bleach my hair to achieve a bright colour?

A: If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach it before applying a bright colour to achieve the desired shade.

Q: How often will I need to touch up my bright hair colour?

A: This will depend on the colour you choose and how fast your hair grows. Some colours may require more frequent touch-ups than others.

Q: Can I dye my hair bright colours at home?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair bright colours at home, we recommend seeking professional advice to achieve the best results.

Conclusion

Choosing a bright hair colour can be a fun and exciting way to express yourself and make a statement. By considering your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style, you can find the perfect shade to suit you. And by taking care of your bright hair colour with colour-safe products and sun protection, you can keep your hair looking vibrant and fresh for as long as possible.
