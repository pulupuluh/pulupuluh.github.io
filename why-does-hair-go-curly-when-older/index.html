Why Does Hair Go Curly When Older

Written by Aprilia Jul 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, many changes occur in our bodies, including changes to our hair. One of the most common changes that people experience is the development of curly hair. While some people are born with curly hair, others may notice that their hair becomes curly as they get older. In this blog post, we will explore the reasons behind this phenomenon and provide tips for caring for curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curly hair is caused by a combination of genetics and environmental factors. Our hair follicles contain cells called keratinocytes that produce keratin, a protein that provides structure to our hair. The shape of our hair follicles determines whether our hair is straight, wavy, or curly. People with curly hair have hair follicles that are oval or asymmetrical, which causes the hair to curl as it grows.

As we age, the shape of our hair follicles may change, which can cause our hair to become curly. This change is often due to hormonal changes that occur as we age. As our hormone levels fluctuate, the shape of our hair follicles may also change, leading to the development of curly hair.

How to

Step by Step

If you have noticed that your hair has become curly as you have gotten older, there are several steps you can take to care for your new hair type:

  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  2. Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  4. Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage curly hair.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting the curl pattern.
  6. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define and enhance your curls.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal parts, as these can cause breakage and damage to curly hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

1. Is it normal for hair to become curly as you age?

Yes, it is normal for hair to become curly as you age. This is often due to hormonal changes that can affect the shape of your hair follicles.

2. Can you prevent your hair from becoming curly as you age?

No, you cannot prevent your hair from becoming curly as you age. However, you can take steps to care for your curly hair and keep it healthy and defined.

3. Do curly hair products work for hair that has become curly with age?

Yes, curly hair products can work for hair that has become curly with age. Look for products that are specifically formulated for curly hair and contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil.

Hair Care

Caring for curly hair requires a different approach than caring for straight hair. It is important to use products that are specifically formulated for curly hair and to avoid practices that can cause breakage and damage. With the right care, you can keep your curly hair healthy, defined, and beautiful.

Remember, if you have noticed that your hair has become curly as you have gotten older, embrace your new hair type and enjoy the unique beauty that curly hair can bring!
