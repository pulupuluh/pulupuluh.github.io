What Parting Is Best For Square Faces

Written by Robby Sep 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't seem to fit your square-shaped face? You're not alone! Many people with square faces struggle to find the perfect parting for their hair. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss the best parting for square faces, along with some tips and tricks to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The best parting for square faces is a side part. This is because a side part creates an illusion of length and softens the angles of the face. It also draws attention away from the square jawline and towards the eyes, which is always a plus. The idea is to create a diagonal line across the face, which helps to break up the symmetry of a square face.

How to

To achieve a side part, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry your hair and comb it straight down. Then, use a tail comb to create a deep part on one side of your head. The part should be in line with the highest point of your eyebrow arch. Use the comb to smooth down the hair on the larger side of the part, and tuck the smaller side behind your ear. You can also use hair clips to hold the hair in place while it dries.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect side part for square faces:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Use a tail comb to create a deep part on one side of your head.
  4. Smooth down the hair on the larger side of the part.
  5. Tuck the smaller side behind your ear.
  6. Use hair clips to hold the hair in place while it dries.
  7. Once your hair is dry, remove the clips and use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair as desired.
  8. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect side part:

  • Start with a small part and gradually make it deeper if you want a more dramatic look.
  • Use a tail comb to create a straight part.
  • Use hair clips to hold the hair in place while it dries.
  • Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best parting for square faces:

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a center part if I have a square face?

A: While a side part is the best option for square faces, you can still wear your hair in a center part if you prefer. Just be sure to add some volume at the crown of your head to help balance out your face shape.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a bob that ends at your chin. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add length and soften your features, such as long layers or a side-swept fringe.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and achieving the perfect side part. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know the best parting for square faces and how to achieve it, you can confidently rock your new hairstyle with ease. Remember to take care of your hair, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
