How To Style Permed Hair Black Girl Tips And Tricks

Permed hair is a popular hairstyle for black women. It adds volume and texture to the hair, making it easy to style in various ways. However, styling permed hair can be challenging, especially if you're new to it. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to style permed hair black girl.

How to Style Permed Hair Black Girl

Styling permed hair black girl is all about finding the right hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style. Here are some popular hairstyles for permed hair:

Ponytail

Ponytail is a simple and chic hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. To style permed hair in a ponytail, brush your hair back and gather it at the crown of your head. Secure it with a hair tie and you're done!

Bun

A bun is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. To style permed hair in a bun, gather your hair at the back of your head and twist it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins and you're good to go!

Half Up, Half Down

If you want to show off your permed curls, try the half up, half down hairstyle. To style permed hair in this way, gather the top half of your hair and tie it back with a hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down to showcase your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style permed hair black girl:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for permed hair to keep your curls looking fresh.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Permed hair can be prone to tangling, so be gentle when combing it.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair. This can be a curl cream, mousse, or gel, depending on your hair type and desired look.
  4. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and enhance your curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a hair pick or wide-tooth comb to fluff up your curls and add volume.
  6. Style your hair in your desired hairstyle, such as a ponytail, bun, or half up, half down.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Permed Hair

Here are some tips to help you style permed hair black girl:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your permed hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from getting tangled and frizzy while you sleep.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and desired look.
  • If you're unsure about how to style your permed hair, consult a professional stylist for advice.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my permed hair?

A: It's best to wash permed hair once or twice a week to avoid drying it out. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for permed hair to keep your curls looking their best.

Q: Can I color my permed hair?

A: It's possible to color permed hair, but it's important to be cautious. Coloring can weaken the hair and cause damage, so consult a professional stylist before attempting to color your permed hair.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of permed hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for permed hair to keep your curls looking fresh.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your permed hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Styling permed hair black girl is easy once you know the right techniques and products to use. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve any hairstyle you want and keep your permed hair looking healthy and beautiful.
