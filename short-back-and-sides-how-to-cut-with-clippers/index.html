Short Back And Sides How To Cut With Clippers

Are you looking for a stylish haircut that's easy to maintain? Look no further than the short back and sides. This classic haircut is perfect for any occasion and can be easily achieved with clippers. In this blog post, we'll go over how to cut a short back and sides with clippers, step-by-step. How to Cut a Short Back and Sides with Clippers

Cutting a short back and sides with clippers is a relatively simple process. All you need is a good pair of clippers, a comb, and a steady hand. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect short back and sides:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by selecting the appropriate guard size for your clippers. A size 2 or 3 is a good starting point for most hair types.
  2. Begin by cutting the sides of the head, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Use the comb to lift the hair and clipper it to the desired length.
  3. Continue cutting the sides, working your way around the head until you reach the other side.
  4. Next, move on to the back of the head. Use the same technique, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  5. Once you've cut the sides and back, it's time to blend the hair on top. Use a longer guard size and go over the hair on top of the head, blending it with the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  6. Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure it's all the same length.
  7. Finally, trim any stray hairs or uneven areas with scissors.
  8. Voila! You've successfully cut a short back and sides with clippers.

Tips for a Great Short Back and Sides Haircut

To ensure your short back and sides looks great, follow these tips:

  • Take your time. Cutting hair with clippers requires a steady hand, so don't rush.
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go.
  • Experiment with different guard sizes to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Ask for help if you're not confident in your abilities. A professional barber can help guide you through the process.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a short back and sides with clippers:

  • What is a short back and sides?
    A short back and sides is a classic men's haircut that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with slightly longer hair on top.
  • What type of clippers should I use?
    A good pair of hair clippers with different guard sizes is ideal.
  • What guard size should I use?
    It depends on your hair type, but a size 2 or 3 is a good starting point for most hair types.
  • Can I cut my own hair?
    Yes, but it's important to take your time and be patient.

Hair Care for Short Back and Sides

To keep your short back and sides looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Visit a barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut.
  • Use a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.

Cutting a short back and sides with clippers is a great way to achieve a stylish, low-maintenance haircut. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a great look that lasts for weeks. Follow our step-by-step guide and use our tips and hair care advice to keep your hair looking its best.
