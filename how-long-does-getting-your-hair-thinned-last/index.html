How Long Does Getting Your Hair Thinned Last

Written by Frank Aug 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering getting your hair thinned but wondering how long the results will last? Thinning hair is a popular technique used to reduce the thickness of hair and give it a more manageable look. In this article, we'll discuss how long you can expect the results to last and provide some tips and care instructions to help you maintain your newly thinned hair.

Table of Contents

  • How To Thin Your Hair
  • Step-By-Step Guide
  • Tips For Maintaining Thinned Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care For Thinned Hair

How To Thin Your Hair

Thinning hair can be done in a few different ways. One popular method is using thinning shears, which are scissors with teeth that cut hair at different lengths. Another option is razor cutting, where a razor blade is used to cut the hair at an angle, creating a more textured and layered look. A hairstylist can help you determine the best method for your hair type and desired style.

Step-By-Step Guide

If you're interested in thinning your hair, here are the basic steps you can expect during a typical appointment:

  1. The hairstylist will assess your hair and determine the best method for thinning based on your hair type and desired look.
  2. The hairstylist will wash and dry your hair.
  3. The hairstylist will use thinning shears or a razor blade to cut the hair to the desired length and texture.
  4. The hairstylist will style your hair to show off the new look.

Tips For Maintaining Thinned Hair

After getting your hair thinned, it's important to take care of it properly to ensure the results last as long as possible. Here are some tips to help you maintain your newly thinned hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the desired length and texture.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting your hair thinned:

  • How often should I get my hair thinned? This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get your hair thinned every 6-8 weeks.
  • Will thinning my hair make it look thinner overall? No, thinning your hair is meant to reduce the thickness of your hair while maintaining the overall volume and shape.
  • Will thinning my hair damage it? If done correctly by a professional hairstylist, thinning your hair should not cause any damage.

Hair Care For Thinned Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, thinned hair. Here are some tips for caring for your thinned hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair ties that can cause breakage and opt for scrunchies or hair clips instead.

Overall, getting your hair thinned can be a great way to achieve a more manageable, textured look. With proper care and maintenance, you can expect the results to last for several weeks before needing another appointment. Talk to a professional hairstylist to determine the best method for your hair type and desired style.
