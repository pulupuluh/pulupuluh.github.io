How to Do Medium Length Layered Hairstyles: Step by Step Guide

Medium length layered hairstyles are perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair. Layered haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many ways, from beachy waves to sleek straight styles. If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, keep reading for a step-by-step guide on how to do medium length layered hairstyles.

Table of Contents

  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Medium length layered hairstyles are a popular choice for women of all ages. They are versatile, easy to maintain, and can be styled in many different ways. Layered haircuts involve cutting the hair at different lengths to create layers that add depth, volume, and texture to the hair. This technique can also help to frame the face and flatter different face shapes.

How to

To achieve a medium length layered hairstyle, you will need to visit a hair salon or stylist. They will be able to cut your hair to the desired length and create layers that flatter your face shape and hair texture. However, if you want to maintain your layered haircut at home, here are some tips:

1. Invest in the right tools

To maintain your medium length layered hairstyle, you will need a few essential tools, including a good quality hairbrush, hair dryer, and styling products such as hairspray, mousse, and serum.

2. Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel dry it to remove excess water.

3. Apply styling products

Apply your favorite styling products to your hair, focusing on the roots and ends. Use a volumizing mousse to add volume and body to your hair, or a smoothing serum to tame frizz and add shine.

4. Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture. Use the brush to lift the roots and create a smooth, sleek finish. If you want to add more volume, flip your hair upside down and blow dry it that way.

5. Style your hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it in any way you like. You can use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or straight styles, or use a hairbrush to create a messy, tousled look.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do a medium length layered hairstyle:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start styling it.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. Start with the lower layers and work your way up.

Step 3: Cut your hair

Cut your hair at an angle to create layers. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up. Cut the hair at different lengths to create textured layers.

Step 4: Style your hair

Use a hairbrush and styling products to create your desired style. You can create waves, curls, or straight styles.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length layered hairstyle:

1. Use the right products

Use products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. This will help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

2. Visit a stylist regularly

Visit a stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your layered haircut. This will help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Protect your hair

Use heat protectant products when using heat styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners. This will help to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I do a medium length layered hairstyle at home?

A: It is recommended to visit a stylist to get a medium length layered hairstyle as they have the expertise to create a flattering haircut for your face shape and hair type. However, you can maintain your layered haircut at home with the right tools and products.

Q: What face shapes suit medium length layered hairstyles?

A: Medium length layered hairstyles can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, and heart-shaped faces. It is important to consult with a stylist to find a style that flatters your face shape.

Q: Can I style my medium length layered hair in different ways?

A: Yes, medium length layered hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in many different ways, from beachy waves to sleek straight styles. Experiment with different styles to find one that suits you.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for achieving a beautiful medium length layered hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth.

2. Use the right products

Use products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.


With these tips, you'll be able to achieve a beautiful medium length layered hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type. Remember to consult with a stylist to get the perfect cut, and to maintain your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
