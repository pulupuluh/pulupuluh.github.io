Short Hair For Oval Faces Over 50 A Guide

Welcome to our guide on short hair for oval faces over 50. If you have an oval face shape and are over 50, you might be wondering what kind of short haircuts would suit you best. We understand that getting a new haircut can be daunting, especially if you're used to longer hair. However, a short haircut can be both stylish and practical, especially as we age. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about short hair for oval faces over 50, including how to choose the right style, tips for styling and caring for your hair, and more. Let's get started!

Choosing the Right Short Hair Style for Oval Faces

When it comes to choosing a short hair style for oval faces over 50, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want a style that complements your face shape. Oval faces are typically longer than they are wide, with a slightly curved jawline and a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any short hair style. However, some styles may be more flattering than others.

A classic bob is a great option for oval faces over 50. It's a timeless style that never goes out of fashion, and it can be adapted to suit your individual preferences. Another option is a pixie cut, which is a shorter style that can be very flattering on oval faces. If you're looking for something a little edgier, a shaggy bob or a layered cut can also be a great choice.

How to Cut Short Hair for Oval Faces Over 50

When it comes to cutting short hair for oval faces over 50, it's important to work with a stylist who understands your face shape and hair type. A good stylist will be able to recommend a style that suits you and can help you maintain it over time.

Before you get your hair cut, it's a good idea to bring in a photo of the style you're interested in. This will give your stylist a visual reference and help them understand what you're looking for. You should also be prepared to discuss your hair type and any concerns you have about your hair, such as thinning or graying.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Oval Faces Over 50

Styling short hair for oval faces over 50 is relatively simple. Depending on your hair type and the style you choose, you may need to use some styling products to achieve the look you want. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or wax, to your hair.
  3. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. If you're using a flat iron, use it to smooth out any kinks or waves in your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Once you've got your new short haircut, you'll want to make sure you maintain it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • If you have gray hair, use a purple shampoo or toner to keep it from looking yellow.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs About Short Hair for Oval Faces Over 50

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. A well-chosen short haircut can be very stylish and flattering, no matter your age.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if it's short?

A: Yes! You can still wear your hair up if it's short. Try experimenting with different styles, such as a messy bun or a half-up, half-down look.

Q: What if I don't like my new haircut?

A: If you're not happy with your new haircut, talk to your stylist about what you don't like and see if they can make any adjustments. It may take a few days to get used to your new look, so give it some time before making any drastic changes.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair for Oval Faces Over 50

When it comes to caring for short hair for oval faces over 50, there are a few key things to keep in mind. Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle between washes.

We hope this guide has been helpful in your search for short hair for oval faces over 50. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable. With the right cut and styling, you can rock a short haircut at any age!
