Medium Length Haircut With Long Bangs A Trending Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is trendy yet easy to maintain? Look no further than the medium length haircut with long bangs. This stylish look is perfect for those who want a change without sacrificing too much length. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, tips for styling it, and how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Medium Length Haircut with Long Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs about the Medium Length Haircut with Long Bangs
  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

How to Get the Medium Length Haircut with Long Bangs

The first step to achieving this hairstyle is to find a hairstylist who specializes in medium length haircuts. Once you have found a stylist, you can show them a picture of the hairstyle you want or describe it to them in detail. The key to this hairstyle is the long bangs, which should reach down to your eyebrows.

When getting your haircut, make sure to ask your stylist to cut your hair to the desired length. The length of your hair should be between your chin and your collarbone. Your stylist should also cut your bangs to the desired length, which should be long enough to cover your forehead but not so long that they obstruct your vision.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Styling your medium length haircut with long bangs is easy and can be done in just a few simple steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the ends to create a slight curl.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a straightener to straighten your hair, focusing on the ends to create a slight curve.
  5. Use a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any flyaways.
  6. Finish off the look by using hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your medium length haircut with long bangs looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the length of your hair and keep the ends healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

FAQs about the Medium Length Haircut with Long Bangs

1. Is the medium length haircut with long bangs suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can be customized to suit any face shape.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day, while those with dry hair can go longer between washes. If you exercise regularly or live in a humid climate, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

3. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail or updo with this hairstyle?

Yes, the medium length haircut with long bangs can be styled in a variety of ways, including ponytails and updos.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

To maintain healthy hair, follow these tips:

  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein to nourish your hair from the inside.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as it is more prone to breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent damage to your hair while you sleep.

In conclusion, the medium length haircut with long bangs is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that is perfect for those who want a change without sacrificing too much length. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can achieve a stylish and healthy hairstyle that will turn heads wherever you go.
