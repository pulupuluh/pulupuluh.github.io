How To Make A Square Face Look Slimmer Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of having a square-shaped face that makes you look heavier than you actually are? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people with square faces struggle to find ways to make their faces look slimmer and more defined. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to make a square face look slimmer so that you can feel more confident and beautiful.

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and sharp angles. While this may look good on some people, it can make others feel self-conscious about their appearance. The good news is that there are ways to make a square face look slimmer and more defined. By using makeup techniques, hairstyles, and facial exercises, you can create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

How To

Here are some tips and tricks on how to make a square face look slimmer:

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves shading and highlighting certain areas of your face to create the illusion of depth and dimension. To make your face look slimmer, use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to shade the areas around your temples, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, use a highlighter on the areas of your face that naturally catch the light, such as the tops of your cheekbones and the bridge of your nose.

2. Hairstyles

Certain hairstyles can also make your face look slimmer. For example, a side-swept bang can help to soften your forehead and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. A lob (long bob) haircut that ends just below your chin can also help to elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bob that ends at your jawline.

3. Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help to tone the muscles in your face and create a more defined and slimming effect. One exercise that can help to slim down your face is to suck in your cheeks and hold for a few seconds, then release. Do this several times a day to help tone your cheek muscles. Another exercise is to smile as wide as you can, then pucker your lips as if you're about to kiss someone. Hold this for a few seconds, then release. Repeat several times a day to help tone the muscles around your mouth.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make a square face look slimmer:

Step 1: Start With a Clean Face

Before you start applying makeup or doing facial exercises, make sure your face is clean and moisturized. This will help to ensure that your makeup goes on smoothly and that your skin is healthy and hydrated.

Step 2: Contour Your Face

Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to shade the areas around your temples, cheekbones, and jawline. Blend well to create a natural-looking shadow.

Step 3: Highlight Your Face

Use a highlighter on the areas of your face that naturally catch the light, such as the tops of your cheekbones and the bridge of your nose. This will help to create the illusion of depth and dimension.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, such as a lob or side-swept bang. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Step 5: Do Facial Exercises

Incorporate facial exercises into your daily routine to help tone the muscles in your face and create a more defined and slimming effect.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to make a square face look slimmer:

  • Use a matte foundation to help create a more natural-looking finish
  • Use a lip gloss or lipstick that has a shiny finish to help create the illusion of fuller lips
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Use a brow pencil or powder to define your eyebrows and create a more slimming effect

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bold makeup if I have a square face?

A: Absolutely! While subtle makeup can help to create a more natural-looking slimming effect, bold makeup can also look great on square faces. Just make sure to use contouring and highlighting techniques to create a more defined and slimming effect.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that I should avoid if I have a square face?

A: Yes, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bob that ends at your jawline. Instead, opt for hairstyles that elongate your face, such as a lob or side-swept bang.

Hair Care

In addition to using makeup techniques and facial exercises to make your face look slimmer, it's also important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to help prevent damage to your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can create the illusion of a more defined and slimming effect on your square face. Remember, everyone's face is unique, so don't be afraid to experiment with different makeup techniques and hairstyles to find what works best for you.
