Description

Bald edges can be a common problem for those with relaxed hair. It can be caused by a variety of factors such as tight hairstyles, overuse of hair products, and genetics. Fortunately, there are products available that can help to promote hair growth and prevent further damage to your hairline.

In this article, we will discuss what to use for bald edges, how to use them, and provide tips on how to care for your hair to prevent further hair loss.

How to

When it comes to what to use for bald edges, there are several options available. Here are some of the most common:

Edge Control

Edge control is a popular choice for those with relaxed hair. It helps to keep your edges in place while promoting hair growth. To use, simply apply a small amount of edge control to your edges and smooth it down with a brush or your fingers.

Castor Oil

Castor oil is known for its ability to promote hair growth. To use, apply a small amount of castor oil to your edges and massage it in. You can also mix it with other oils such as coconut oil or olive oil for added benefits.

Scalp Stimulating Shampoo

A scalp stimulating shampoo can help to promote hair growth by increasing blood flow to the scalp. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients such as tea tree oil, peppermint oil, or eucalyptus oil. To use, simply apply the shampoo to your scalp and massage it in.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to use these products:

Edge Control

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of edge control to your edges.
  3. Smooth it down with a brush or your fingers.

Castor Oil

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of castor oil to your edges.
  3. Massage it in.

Scalp Stimulating Shampoo

  1. Wet your hair.
  2. Apply the shampoo to your scalp.
  3. Massage it in.
  4. Rinse thoroughly.

Tips

Here are some tips to help prevent further damage to your hairline:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids, weaves, and ponytails.
  • Avoid using too many hair products.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get plenty of rest and exercise.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about what to use for bald edges:

Q: Can I use castor oil on my entire scalp?

A: Yes, castor oil can be used on your entire scalp to promote hair growth.

Q: How often should I use scalp stimulating shampoo?

A: It is recommended to use scalp stimulating shampoo once or twice a week.

Q: Will edge control make my hair greasy?

A: It depends on the type of edge control you use. Look for a lightweight formula that won't leave your hair greasy.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is important when it comes to preventing further hair loss. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair.
  • Avoid using too many hair products.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, bald edges can be a frustrating problem for those with relaxed hair. However, with the right products and hair care routine, you can promote hair growth and prevent further damage to your hairline.
