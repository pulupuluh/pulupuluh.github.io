How To Do Wavy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Eveline Feb 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Wavy hairstyles are a popular choice for medium length hair. They add texture and volume to your hair, making it look more stylish and elegant. Whether you have a special occasion or just want to change up your look, creating wavy hairstyles for medium length hair is easy and can be done at home. In this article, we will show you how to do wavy hairstyles for medium length hair step by step, share tips, answer frequently asked questions, and provide hair care advice.

How to Create Wavy Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Before we get started, it is important to choose the right tools for creating wavy hairstyles for medium length hair. You will need a curling iron, a heat protectant spray, a hairbrush, and hairspray. Once you have gathered everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron. Blow-dry your hair until it is completely dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using a hairbrush. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up. Secure each section with a hair tie or clip.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold it for a few seconds and then release. Repeat this process for each section of hair, alternating the direction of the curls to create a more natural look.

Step 4: Brush Your Hair

Once you have curled all of your hair, use a hairbrush to gently brush out the curls. This will create a more relaxed and natural look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Spray a light layer of hairspray to hold the waves in place. Avoid using too much hairspray as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Step by Step Guide

Creating wavy hairstyles for medium length hair is easy with these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair until it is completely dry.
  4. Divide your hair into sections.
  5. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron.
  6. Hold for a few seconds and then release.
  7. Repeat for each section of hair, alternating the direction of the curls.
  8. Use a hairbrush to gently brush out the curls.
  9. Spray a light layer of hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips for Creating Wavy Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Follow these tips for creating the perfect wavy hairstyles for medium length hair:

  • Choose the right curling iron size for your hair length.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural look.
  • Brush out the curls for a more relaxed look.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will wavy hairstyles for medium length hair last?

Wavy hairstyles can last for several hours to a day depending on the hair type, humidity, and styling products used. To make your waves last longer, use a hairspray with strong hold.

Can I create wavy hairstyles for medium length hair without a curling iron?

Yes, you can create wavy hairstyles for medium length hair without a curling iron. You can use braids, buns, or twist techniques to create natural-looking waves. You can also use a flat iron to create waves.

How often should I wash my hair when creating wavy hairstyles for medium length hair?

It is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days when creating wavy hairstyles for medium length hair. Overwashing can strip your hair of natural oils, making it dry and brittle.

Hair Care Advice for Wavy Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Follow these hair care tips to keep your wavy hairstyles for medium length hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet as it can cause breakage.

Creating wavy hairstyles for medium length hair is easy and can be done at home. Follow our step-by-step guide, use the right tools and products, and follow our hair care advice for beautiful, healthy-looking hair.
