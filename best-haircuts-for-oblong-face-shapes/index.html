Best Haircuts For Oblong Face Shapes

Are you looking for the perfect haircut for your oblong face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best haircuts for oblong face shapes, provide tips on how to choose the perfect hairstyle, and give you advice on how to care for your hair.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face shape, there are a few things to keep in mind. The first thing to consider is your hair texture. Do you have thick, curly hair or fine, straight hair? The second thing to consider is your personal style. Do you prefer short, edgy haircuts or long, flowing locks?

Next, you'll want to take a look at your facial features. If you have a longer face shape, you'll want to choose a haircut that will help balance out the length. This can be achieved by adding layers to your hair or choosing a style with volume on the sides. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as these can make your face look even longer.

Best Haircuts for Oblong Face Shapes

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, let's take a look at some of the best haircuts for oblong face shapes:

1. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a great option for oblong face shapes. The length of the bob should be just below the chin to help balance out the length of your face. You can add some layers to the bob to create volume and texture.

2. Bangs

Bangs are a great way to add some dimension to your hair and balance out your facial features. If you have an oblong face shape, consider getting some side-swept bangs to help shorten the appearance of your face.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a short haircut, a pixie cut is a great option for oblong face shapes. This style can help balance out the length of your face and create a more youthful appearance.

4. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. This can help balance out the length of your face and create a more flattering look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair

If you're planning on cutting your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back.
  3. Start by trimming the ends of your hair with sharp scissors.
  4. Continue trimming your hair, working from the back to the front.
  5. Use a comb to help guide your scissors and create even layers.
  6. Once you've finished trimming your hair, use a pair of thinning shears to create texture and volume.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Oblong Face Shapes

Here are some tips to help you care for your oblong face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight.
  • Choose a hairstyle with volume on the sides to help balance out your facial features.
  • Use a round brush to add volume to your hair while blow-drying.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help detangle your hair without causing damage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for oblong face shapes:

What is an oblong face shape?

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a narrow forehead and jawline.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as these can make your face look even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too flat or lack volume on the sides.

What hairstyles are best for oblong face shapes?

Hairstyles with volume on the sides, such as a bob or layered haircut, are great options for oblong face shapes. Bangs can also help shorten the appearance of your face.

Hair Care for Oblong Face Shapes

Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help protect your hair from heat and environmental damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

In conclusion, choosing the perfect haircut for your oblong face shape is all about creating balance and dimension. Whether you opt for a bob, layered haircut, or pixie cut, make sure to choose a style that complements your personal style and facial features. With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to achieving your best look yet!
