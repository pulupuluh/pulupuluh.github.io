How To Cut A Pixie Cut Black Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you've been considering a drastic change to your hair, a pixie cut might be just what you need. Pixie cuts are short, stylish haircuts that are perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look. This guide will walk you through the steps of cutting a pixie cut on black hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prepare
  • Equipment Needed
  • Sectioning the Hair
  • Cutting the Hair
  • Styling the Hair
  • Tips for a Perfect Pixie Cut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

How to Prepare

Before you begin cutting your hair, there are a few things you need to do to prepare. First, make sure your hair is clean and free of any tangles. It's also important to have a clear idea of the style you want to achieve. Look at pictures of pixie cuts online and decide on the length and shape you want.

It's also a good idea to have someone assist you in cutting your hair. Cutting the back of your hair can be tricky, so having a friend or family member help you will make the process easier.

Equipment Needed

Here are the tools you'll need to cut a pixie cut on black hair:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A fine-toothed comb
  • Hair clips
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A mirror

Sectioning the Hair

Start by parting your hair down the middle and then from ear to ear. Use hair clips to secure the top section of your hair out of the way.

Take the bottom section of your hair and divide it into small sections. Use a fine-toothed comb to comb each section straight down.

Cutting the Hair

Now it's time to start cutting. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut straight across, making sure to follow the guide you created earlier.

Work your way around your head, cutting small sections of hair at a time. Remember to cut the back of your hair carefully, using a mirror to help you see what you're doing.

Styling the Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to style it. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and use your fingers to create the shape you want. You can use a blow dryer to add volume or texture to your hair.

Tips for a Perfect Pixie Cut

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will look longer than it actually is.
  • Use styling products to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Keep up with regular trims to maintain your pixie cut.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own pixie cut at home?

Yes, you can cut your own pixie cut at home, but it's important to have a clear idea of what you want before you start cutting. It's also a good idea to have someone help you with the back of your hair.

What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

Pixie cuts can work for any face shape, but they look especially good on those with oval, heart, or square faces.

How often do I need to trim my pixie cut?

It's important to trim your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut a pixie cut on your black hair with confidence. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun with your new look!
