Why Are My Beard Hairs So Thick

Written by Robby Jun 12, 2023 · 7 min read

In this article, we will explore the reasons behind why your beard hairs are so thick. From genetics to hormonal changes, there are many factors that contribute to the thickness of your beard hair. We will also provide tips on how to care for your thick beard hair to keep it healthy and manageable. Read on to learn more!

What Causes Thick Beard Hairs?

There are several factors that contribute to thick beard hair:

  • Genetics: Your genetic makeup plays a significant role in the texture and thickness of your beard hair. If your father or grandfather had a thick beard, chances are you will too.
  • Hormonal Changes: Testosterone is responsible for the growth of facial hair. As you age and your testosterone levels increase, your beard hair may become thicker.
  • Diet: Eating a diet rich in protein and vitamins can promote healthy hair growth, including beard hair.
  • Stress: Stress can cause hormonal imbalances and affect hair growth, including beard hair.

How to Care for Thick Beard Hair

Thick beard hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips:

  • Wash Regularly: Wash your beard hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Condition: Use a beard conditioner to keep your beard hair soft and hydrated.
  • Trim: Regularly trim your beard hair to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use Beard Oil: Apply beard oil to keep your beard hair soft, hydrated, and easy to manage.
  • Comb: Use a beard comb to detangle and style your beard hair.

Step-by-Step Guide for Beard Hair Care

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to care for your thick beard hair:

  1. Wash: Wet your beard hair with warm water and apply a gentle shampoo. Massage the shampoo into your beard hair and rinse thoroughly.
  2. Condition: Apply beard conditioner and leave it on for a few minutes. Rinse thoroughly with warm water.
  3. Dry: Gently pat your beard hair dry with a towel. Avoid rubbing as it can cause damage to your beard hair.
  4. Trim: Use a beard trimmer to trim your beard hair to your desired length.
  5. Apply Beard Oil: Apply a few drops of beard oil to your fingertips and massage it into your beard hair and skin.
  6. Comb: Use a beard comb to detangle your beard hair and style it as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Beard Hair

Here are some additional tips to maintain your thick beard hair:

  • Be Patient: Growing a thick beard takes time, so be patient and don’t give up.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to keep your beard hair hydrated from the inside out.
  • Eat a Balanced Diet: Eating a diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals can promote healthy beard hair growth.
  • Avoid Harsh Chemicals: Avoid using harsh chemicals on your beard hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

FAQs About Thick Beard Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about thick beard hair:

1. Is it normal to have a thick beard?

Yes, it’s normal to have a thick beard, especially if it runs in your family.

2. Can I thin out my thick beard hair?

Yes, you can thin out your thick beard hair with a beard trimmer or scissors. However, it’s best to leave this to a professional barber to avoid damaging your beard hair.

3. How often should I wash my thick beard hair?

You should wash your thick beard hair 2-3 times a week with a gentle shampoo to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

4. How do I prevent beard dandruff with thick beard hair?

You can prevent beard dandruff by regularly washing and conditioning your beard hair and using a beard oil to keep it hydrated.

Hair Care for Thick Beard Hairs

Thick beard hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and manageable. By following these tips and incorporating them into your daily routine, you can keep your beard hair looking and feeling its best.
