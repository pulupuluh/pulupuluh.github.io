How To Get Volume In Your Hair When Straightening

Written by Luffy Sep 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless hair after straightening? Don't worry, there are ways to add volume to your hair while still achieving a sleek and straight look. In this article, we will discuss how to get volume in your hair when straightening.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Volume in Your Hair When Straightening

  • Tips for Adding Volume to Your Hair

How to Get Volume in Your Hair When Straightening

The key to adding volume to your hair when straightening is to use the right techniques and products. Here are some tips:

1. Blow Dry Your Hair First

Before straightening your hair, make sure it is completely dry. Blow drying your hair with your head flipped over can help add volume to your roots. Use a round brush to lift your roots while blow drying.

2. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help add volume to your hair. These products can help remove buildup and add texture to your hair, making it easier to style.

3. Use a Root Lifter

A root lifter can be applied directly to your roots before blow drying. This product can help lift your roots and add volume to your hair.

4. Section Your Hair

Sectioning your hair can help you straighten your hair more efficiently and add volume to your roots. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.

5. Use a Round Brush While Straightening

Using a round brush while straightening can help add volume to your roots. Use the round brush to lift your roots while straightening.

6. Finish with a Volumizing Hairspray

After straightening your hair, finish with a volumizing hairspray. This can help hold your style and add volume to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to get volume in your hair when straightening:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair with your head flipped over using a round brush to lift your roots.
  3. Apply a root lifter to your roots.
  4. Section your hair using clips or hair ties.
  5. Straighten each section of hair using a round brush to lift your roots.
  6. Finish with a volumizing hairspray.

Tips for Adding Volume to Your Hair

Here are some additional tips for adding volume to your hair:

  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo to add texture to your hair and absorb excess oil.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can also weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Can I add volume to my hair without using heat?

Yes, you can add volume to your hair without using heat. Try using a volumizing mousse or spray and scrunching your hair while it dries.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may be able to wash it less frequently. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dye.

By following these tips, you can add volume to your hair while still achieving a sleek and straight look. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for your hair type.
