How To Twist Sides Of Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Mar 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Twisting hair is a popular hairstyle for both men and women. It is a great way to add texture and volume to your hair while also keeping it out of your face. Twisting the sides of your hair can create a unique and stylish look that is easy to achieve. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to twist the sides of your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Twist Sides of Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Twisting Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions

How to Twist Sides of Hair

Twisting the sides of your hair is a simple process that can be done in just a few minutes. Here are the basic steps to follow:

Step by Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to twist the sides of your hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it on a low heat setting.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and separate it into two sections, one on each side of your head.
  3. Take a small section of hair from one side and divide it into two equal parts.
  4. Cross the right section over the left section, then take a small section of hair from the left side and add it to the left section.
  5. Cross the left section over the right section and take a small section of hair from the right side and add it to the right section.
  6. Continue this pattern until you reach the end of the section of hair.
  7. Repeat this process on the other side of your head.
  8. When you have finished twisting both sides, use bobby pins or hair ties to secure the ends of the twists.
  9. You can also use a small amount of hair gel or pomade to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.
  10. Finally, use a hairspray to set the style and keep it in place.

Tips for Twisting Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when twisting the sides of your hair:

  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start twisting it.
  • Use a comb to make sure your hair is evenly divided into two sections.
  • Take small sections of hair to make the twists more defined.
  • Use hair ties or bobby pins to secure the ends of the twists.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most commonly asked questions about twisting the sides of your hair:

Q: How long will the twists last?

A: Depending on your hair type and the products you use, the twists can last anywhere from a few days to a week or more.

Q: Can I twist my hair if it is wet?

A: It is best to twist your hair when it is dry to prevent breakage and damage.

Q: Can I use a different twisting pattern?

A: Yes, there are many different twisting patterns you can try depending on the look you want to achieve.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Twisting the sides of your hair is a fun and easy way to switch up your hairstyle. Follow these simple steps and tips to achieve a stylish and unique look.
