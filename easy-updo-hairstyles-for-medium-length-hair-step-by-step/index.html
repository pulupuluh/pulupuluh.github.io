Easy Updo Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Step By Step

Are you looking for a quick and easy way to style your medium length hair? Updo hairstyles are a great option for any occasion, whether it's a formal event or a casual day out. In this article, we'll provide step-by-step instructions for easy updo hairstyles for medium length hair, as well as tips and hair care advice to keep your locks looking healthy and beautiful.

How to Create Easy Updo Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Before we get started, it's important to mention that you'll need a few basic styling tools to create these updos, including bobby pins, hair ties, and a comb. Once you have these items on hand, you're ready to start styling!

Step 1: Choose Your Updo Style

The first step in creating an updo hairstyle is deciding on the style you want. There are many options to choose from, including braids, buns, and twists. Consider the occasion and your personal style when selecting your updo.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling, it's important to prep your hair. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited for your hair type. Once your hair is clean and dry, use a comb to detangle any knots or tangles.

Step 3: Gather Your Hair

Next, gather your hair into a ponytail or bun. Depending on the style you've chosen, you may need to section your hair into smaller sections before gathering it. Use a hair tie to secure your hair in place.

Step 4: Twist or Braid Your Hair

Once you've gathered your hair, it's time to start twisting or braiding it. Follow the instructions for your chosen style and use bobby pins to secure any loose ends or sections.

Step 5: Secure with Bobby Pins

Finally, use bobby pins to secure your updo in place. Be sure to hide any pins by tucking them under your hair. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your style in place.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know the basics, let's dive into the step-by-step instructions for three easy updo hairstyles for medium length hair.

1. Messy Bun

Step 1: Brush your hair and gather it into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Secure with a hair tie.

Step 2: Twist your hair and wrap it around the hair tie, creating a messy bun. Use bobby pins to secure any loose ends.

Step 3: Pull out a few strands of hair around your face for a soft, relaxed look.

2. Half-Up, Half-Down Twist

Step 1: Part your hair down the middle and take a small section of hair from each side at the front.

Step 2: Twist each section of hair away from your face, securing each with a bobby pin at the back of your head.

Step 3: Gather the remaining hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail and wrap it into a bun, securing with bobby pins.

3. Braided Crown

Step 1: Part your hair down the middle and braid each side, securing each with a hair tie at the end.

Step 2: Wrap each braid over the top of your head, securing with bobby pins at the back of your head.

Step 3: Gather the remaining hair into a low ponytail and twist it into a bun, securing with bobby pins.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Updo

Creating an updo hairstyle may take some practice, but with a few tips and tricks, you'll be a pro in no time!

  • Start with clean, dry hair for the best results.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your updo, and tuck them under your hair to hide them.
  • Add volume to your updo by teasing the hair at the crown of your head.
  • Use a light hairspray to keep your style in place without weighing it down.

FAQs

What if I have thin or fine hair?

If you have thin or fine hair, consider adding a volumizing product to your hair before styling. You can also tease your hair at the crown of your head to add volume.

What if my hair is too short for an updo?

If your hair is too short for an updo, consider adding hair extensions or trying a different hairstyle that works better for your hair length.

How do I keep my updo in place all day?

Use bobby pins and a light hairspray to keep your updo in place all day. Be sure to avoid touching your hair too much, which can cause it to come undone.

Hair Care Advice for Healthy Locks

In addition to styling, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these easy updo hairstyles for medium length hair and our hair care advice, you'll be able to create beautiful, healthy locks in no time!
