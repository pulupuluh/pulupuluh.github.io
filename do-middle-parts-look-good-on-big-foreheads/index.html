Do Middle Parts Look Good On Big Foreheads A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Apr 02, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of always having to hide your big forehead with bangs or hair accessories? Well, you’re not alone. Many people with big foreheads struggle with finding the right hairstyle to flatter their facial features. One of the most popular options is the middle part, but does it really look good on big foreheads? In this article, we’ll explore the answer to that question and provide you with tips on how to rock a middle part with confidence.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The middle part is a hairstyle where the hair is parted down the middle, creating two equal sections on either side of the head. It has been a popular trend for years and is often seen on celebrities and influencers. However, some people with big foreheads may wonder if this style is suitable for them since it exposes more of their forehead. The answer to that question is yes, a middle part can look good on big foreheads, but it depends on a few factors.

How To

To achieve a middle part, you can follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. Section your hair down the middle using a tail comb.
  4. Straighten your hair using a flat iron or straightening brush.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine.

Step By Step

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair: Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. For big foreheads, it’s recommended to use a volumizing shampoo to add more body to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair: Use a blow dryer and a round brush to blow-dry your hair. This will add volume and make it easier to style. Point the nozzle of the blow dryer downwards to prevent frizz.
  3. Section your hair: Use a tail comb to part your hair down the middle. Make sure the part is straight and even.
  4. Straighten your hair: Use a flat iron or straightening brush to straighten your hair. Start from the roots and work your way down to the ends. Make sure to keep the iron or brush moving to prevent heat damage.
  5. Add shine: Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any flyaways. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your middle part look good on a big forehead:

  • Choose the right hair length: The length of your hair can affect how your forehead looks. If you have a big forehead, it’s recommended to go for longer hair, as it can help balance out your face.
  • Add layers: Layers can also help soften your forehead and add dimension to your hair.
  • Accessorize: Hair accessories like headbands, scarves, and hats can help draw attention away from your forehead.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles: Don’t be afraid to try out different hairstyles to find what works best for you. You can also consult with a hairstylist for personalized recommendations.

FAQ

What face shapes suit a middle part?

A middle part is suitable for most face shapes, but it’s especially flattering for oval faces. If you have a round or square face, you may want to try out a different hairstyle to complement your facial features.

Can I wear a middle part with curly hair?

Absolutely! A middle part can look great on curly hair, but you may need to use a diffuser to dry your hair instead of a round brush.

What should I do if I don’t like my middle part?

If you’re not happy with your middle part, you can always switch it up and try a different style. You can also consult with a hairstylist for personalized recommendations.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it’s important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and overall health.

Now that you know how to rock a middle part with a big forehead, it’s time to give it a try! Remember, the key to any hairstyle is confidence, so wear it with pride.
