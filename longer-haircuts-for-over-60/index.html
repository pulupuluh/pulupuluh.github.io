Longer Haircuts For Over 60 A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by William Mar 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are over 60 and looking for a new haircut, you may think that shorter styles are your only option. However, longer haircuts can be just as flattering and youthful, if you choose the right style. In this article, we will explore some of the best longer haircuts for over 60 and provide tips on how to care for your hair as you age.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Longer Haircut for Over 60
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Longer Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair as You Age
  • FAQs About Longer Haircuts for Over 60

How to Choose a Longer Haircut for Over 60

When choosing a longer haircut, it's important to consider your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • If you have thinning hair, consider adding layers or volume to create the illusion of fuller hair.
  • If you have a round face, avoid blunt cuts and opt for face-framing layers instead.
  • If you lead an active lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance haircut that doesn't require frequent styling.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.

Flattering Longer Haircuts for Over 60

Here are some longer haircuts that are universally flattering and perfect for women over 60:

  • Shoulder-length layers: This classic style is perfect for adding volume and movement to thinning hair.
  • Long bob: Also known as the "lob," this style hits just above the shoulders and is easy to maintain.
  • Wispy bangs: If you want to add some drama to your longer haircut, consider adding wispy bangs that graze your eyebrows.
  • Face-framing layers: These layers are cut around the face to draw attention to your best features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Longer Hair

Styling longer hair can be a bit more time-consuming than shorter styles, but it's worth the effort. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling longer hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from hot styling tools.
  3. Use a curling iron or flat iron to style your hair as desired.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Remember to use heat styling tools sparingly to prevent damage to your hair. You can also try air-drying your hair and using a volumizing mousse to add texture and volume.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair as You Age

As we age, our hair can become thinner, drier, and more brittle. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair as you age:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair health.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and dyes that can damage your hair.

FAQs About Longer Haircuts for Over 60

Q: Will longer hair make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. In fact, the right longer haircut can be just as youthful and flattering as a shorter style. It's all about choosing a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Q: Do I need to use special hair care products as I age?

A: Yes, as we age, our hair can become thinner and drier, so it's important to use products that are formulated for your hair type. Look for products that are designed for mature hair and contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or bun as I age?

A: Absolutely! Just be careful not to pull your hair too tight, as this can cause damage and breakage. Use soft, stretchy hair ties that won't tug on your hair.

Hair Care Products for Over 60 Hair

Here are some hair care products that are perfect for mature hair:

  • Volumizing mousse: Adds volume and fullness to thinning hair.
  • Deep conditioning treatment: Moisturizes and nourishes dry, brittle hair.
  • Heat protectant spray: Prevents damage from heat styling tools.
  • Hair growth supplement: Contains vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.

Remember, the key to maintaining healthy, beautiful hair as you age is to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture, and to use products that are formulated for your hair type. With the right care, you can have gorgeous, youthful-looking hair at any age!
