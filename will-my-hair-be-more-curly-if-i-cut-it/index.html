Will My Hair Be More Curly If I Cut It

Written by Petter Aug 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are considering getting a hair cut and are wondering if it will make your hair more curly, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will explore the question, "will my hair be more curly if I cut it?" and provide you with tips and advice on how to achieve the curly hair look you desire.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips

Description

Curly hair is a popular and desirable hairstyle for many people. However, not everyone is blessed with naturally curly hair. If you have straight or wavy hair and are looking to achieve a curly look, you may be wondering if cutting your hair will help.

The short answer is, it depends. Cutting your hair alone will not make it more curly. However, there are certain cutting techniques and hair care practices that can enhance your natural curl pattern and give you the curly hair look you desire.

How to

If you want to achieve more curly hair, there are several steps you can take.

Step 1: Consult with a Professional Stylist

The first step in achieving more curly hair is to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine your hair type and recommend the best cutting techniques and hair care practices for your hair.

Step 2: Consider a Deva Cut

A Deva Cut is a specialized cutting technique designed to enhance natural curl patterns. It involves cutting the hair while it is dry and in its natural, curly state. This helps to create a more defined and voluminous curl pattern.

Step 3: Use Curl-Enhancing Products

To enhance your natural curl pattern, it is important to use curl-enhancing products. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and aloe vera, which can help to define and moisturize your curls.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide to achieving more curly hair:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine your hair type and recommend cutting techniques and hair care practices.
  2. Consider a Deva Cut to enhance your natural curl pattern.
  3. Use curl-enhancing products to define and moisturize your curls.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques, such as diffusing or plopping, to enhance your curls even further.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving more curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and disrupt your natural curl pattern.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while they are wet.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your hair to achieve more curly hair:

Will cutting my hair make it more curly?

No, cutting your hair alone will not make it more curly. However, certain cutting techniques and hair care practices can enhance your natural curl pattern.

What is a Deva Cut?

A Deva Cut is a specialized cutting technique designed to enhance natural curl patterns. It involves cutting the hair while it is dry and in its natural, curly state.

Hair Care

To maintain your curly hair look, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine. This should include regular washing and conditioning, using curl-enhancing products, and avoiding heat styling tools.

In conclusion, cutting your hair alone will not make it more curly. However, by using certain cutting techniques and hair care practices, you can enhance your natural curl pattern and achieve the curly hair look you desire.
