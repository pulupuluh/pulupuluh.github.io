How To Style Soft Hair For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Mar 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're one of the guys who has soft hair, you might think that it's impossible to style it. However, there are several ways to style your soft hair that can make you look great. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you style your soft hair like a pro.

How to Style Soft Hair for Guys

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, it's essential to know what kind of hair you have. Soft hair is usually thin, fine, and lacks texture, which makes it challenging to style. However, it's not impossible to style soft hair if you know the right techniques.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Soft Hair for Guys

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your soft hair:

  1. Shampoo your hair with a volumizing shampoo to add volume to your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  2. Apply a lightweight conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave it on for a few minutes, then rinse it off with lukewarm water.
  3. Dry your hair with a towel, but don't rub it vigorously as it can damage your hair. Instead, pat it dry gently.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair mousse to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends. This will add volume and texture to your hair.
  5. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots, then blow-dry it in an upward direction.
  6. Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will add texture and hold to your hair.
  7. Style your hair using your fingers or a comb to achieve the look that you want. You can go for a messy, textured look or a sleek, polished look.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Following these steps will help you style your soft hair easily and efficiently.

Tips for Styling Soft Hair for Guys

Here are some tips to help you style your soft hair:

  • Use a lightweight hair product to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer on high heat as it can damage your hair. Use a low heat setting instead.
  • If you have long hair, consider tying it up in a ponytail or a bun to add some texture to your hair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to avoid split ends, which can make your hair look dull and lifeless.

FAQs

Q. Can I style my soft hair without using hair products?

A. Yes, you can style your soft hair without hair products, but it might not hold for long. Using hair products like wax or pomade can add texture and hold to your hair, making it easier to style.

Q. Can I use a hair straightener to style my soft hair?

A. Yes, you can use a hair straightener to style your soft hair, but it's essential to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Q. Can I use a hairbrush to style my soft hair?

A. Yes, you can use a hairbrush to style your soft hair, but it's recommended to use a round brush as it can add volume and shape to your hair.

Hair Care for Soft Hair

To maintain healthy and soft hair, here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly but avoid over-washing it as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like hair dye or bleach, as it can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy and soft hair.

Conclusion

Styling soft hair can be challenging, but it's not impossible. By following the tips and tricks mentioned in this article, you can style your soft hair like a pro. Remember to also take care of your hair to maintain healthy and soft hair.
