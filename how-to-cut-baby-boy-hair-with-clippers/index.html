How To Cut Baby Boy Hair With Clippers A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Mar 02, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of taking your baby boy to the salon every time he needs a haircut? Do you want to save money and time by cutting his hair at home? If so, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut baby boy hair with clippers.

How to Cut Baby Boy Hair with Clippers

Before we start, you need to ensure that you have the following tools:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guard
  • Comb
  • Scissors
  • Spray bottle
  • Towel

Once you have these tools, follow the steps below to cut your baby boy's hair:

Step 1: Prepare Your Baby Boy

Before you start cutting your baby boy's hair, make sure he is comfortable and relaxed. You can give him a toy or a pacifier to keep him calm.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Baby Boy's Hair

Wash your baby boy's hair with a mild shampoo and rinse thoroughly. Pat his hair dry with a towel or let it air dry.

Step 3: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Choose the clipper guard according to the length of hair you want to cut. Start with a longer guard and gradually move to a shorter one.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting from the back of your baby boy's head, moving to the sides and then to the top. Use the clipper guard to create an even length.

Step 5: Trim the Edges

Use scissors to trim the edges around the ears and the neckline.

Step 6: Clean Up

Use a comb to brush off any loose hair and a spray bottle to wet any unruly hair.

Step 7: Repeat the Process

If you need to cut more hair, repeat the process until you achieve the desired length.

Tips for Cutting Baby Boy Hair with Clippers

  • Use a spray bottle to wet your baby boy's hair if it becomes dry or unruly during the cutting process.
  • Use a comb to brush off any loose hair after cutting.
  • Start with a longer clipper guard and gradually move to a shorter one to achieve the desired length.
  • Trim the edges around the ears and neckline with scissors.
  • Keep your baby boy calm and relaxed during the cutting process.

FAQ

Can I cut my baby boy's hair with regular scissors?

While it is possible to cut your baby boy's hair with regular scissors, it is recommended to use clippers as they are designed for cutting hair.

How often should I cut my baby boy's hair?

It depends on the rate at which your baby boy's hair grows. You can cut his hair every 4-6 weeks or as needed.

Hair Care

After cutting your baby boy's hair, it is important to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner designed for babies and avoid using any harsh chemicals or styling products.

Cutting your baby boy's hair with clippers can be a fun and easy task if you follow the right steps. With this guide, you can save time and money by cutting his hair at home. Happy cutting!
