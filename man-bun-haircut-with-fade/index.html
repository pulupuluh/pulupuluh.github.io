Man Bun Haircut With Fade The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi May 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a trendy hairstyle that combines the classic look of a man bun with the modern edge of a fade, then the man bun haircut with fade is perfect for you. This style has been gaining popularity in recent years and is a great way to show off your fashion-forward style. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the man bun haircut with fade, including how to get it, tips for styling it, and how to take care of your hair.

Description

The man bun haircut with fade is a hairstyle that combines the classic man bun with a fade on the sides and back of the head. The hair is typically longer on the top and pulled back into a bun, while the sides and back are faded short. This style is perfect for those who want a trendy, modern look while still keeping some length on top.

How to Get the Man Bun Haircut with Fade

If you're interested in getting the man bun haircut with fade, it's important to find a skilled barber who can help you achieve the look you want. Here are the steps to getting this style:

Step 1: Grow Out Your Hair

The first step in getting the man bun haircut with fade is to grow out your hair. You'll need at least six inches of hair on the top of your head to create the bun. It's also important to keep the sides and back of your hair trimmed while you're growing out the top.

Step 2: Choose Your Fade

There are several different types of fades to choose from, including high fades, low fades, and skin fades. Talk to your barber about which type of fade will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 3: Cut and Style Your Hair

Your barber will cut your hair to the desired length and style the top into a bun. They'll also fade the sides and back of your hair to create a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Tips for Styling the Man Bun Haircut with Fade

Once you've got the man bun haircut with fade, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here are a few tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use a high-quality styling product like a pomade or hair wax to keep your hair in place. Apply the product to your hair while it's still damp, and then style it into a bun using your fingers or a comb.

Tip 2: Experiment with Different Styles

There are several different ways to style a man bun, so don't be afraid to experiment with different looks. You can try a messy bun, a sleek bun, or a half-up, half-down style.

Tip 3: Maintain Your Haircut

Make sure to get regular trims to maintain your haircut and keep the fade looking fresh. You should also wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and strong.

FAQ

What kind of hair works best for the man bun haircut with fade?

The man bun haircut with fade works best for those with thick, wavy hair. However, it can be adapted for other hair types as well.

How long does my hair need to be for the man bun haircut with fade?

You'll need at least six inches of hair on the top of your head to create the bun, but the length can vary depending on the style you want.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every four to six weeks to maintain your haircut and keep the fade looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

Tip 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every two to three days with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair clean and moisturized.

Tip 2: Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools like a blow dryer or straightener, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first. This will help protect your hair from damage.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every four to six weeks.

In conclusion, the man bun haircut with fade is a stylish and modern hairstyle that's perfect for those who want to show off their fashion-forward style. With the right barber, products, and care, you can rock this look with confidence.
