Mens Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides And Back

Written by Frank Mar 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy haircut, then a mens haircut long on top short on sides and back might be the perfect option for you. This style has become increasingly popular in recent years and can work for a variety of hair types and face shapes. In this blog post, we'll provide a comprehensive guide on how to get this haircut, some tips on how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

A mens haircut long on top short on sides and back features longer hair on the top of the head, with shorter hair on the sides and back. The length of the top hair can vary, but generally, it should be at least 2-3 inches long. The sides and back are typically clipped with a shorter guard, ranging from a 1 to a 4. This haircut can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

How to Get the Haircut

If you're interested in getting a mens haircut long on top short on sides and back, we recommend going to a professional barber or stylist. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape to determine the best length and style for you. To ensure that your stylist understands your desired look, bring in a photo or two of the style you want.

The haircut itself is relatively straightforward. Your stylist will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair with a shorter guard. They will then cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length, blending it into the shorter sides and back. The stylist may also add some texture to the top of your hair with scissors or a razor.

Step by Step

  1. Go to a professional barber or stylist
  2. Show them a photo of the style you want
  3. Have the sides and back of your hair cut with a shorter guard
  4. Cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length
  5. Blend the top hair into the shorter sides and back
  6. Add texture to the top of your hair with scissors or a razor (optional)

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style your mens haircut long on top short on sides and back:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to the top of your hair to add texture and hold
  • Experiment with different styles, such as slicked back or messy, to find the look that best suits you
  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh

FAQ

1. Is a mens haircut long on top short on sides and back suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style can work for a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly. Your stylist will be able to modify the cut to suit your hair type and texture.

2. How do I know if this style will suit my face shape?

A mens haircut long on top short on sides and back can work for a variety of face shapes, but you should consult with your stylist to determine the best style for you. Generally, this style works well for those with oval, round, or square-shaped faces.

3. Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, this style is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that best suits your needs.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut long on top short on sides and back looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Apply a small amount of styling product to the top of your hair to add texture and hold. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a mens haircut long on top short on sides and back with confidence and style.
