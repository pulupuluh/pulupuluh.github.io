Haircuts For Short Thin Hair And Round Faces

Written by Bardi May 09, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have short, thin hair and a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape, adds volume to your hair, and makes you feel confident. In this article, we’ll share some tips and ideas for haircuts for short thin hair and round faces. Whether you prefer a pixie cut, a bob, or something in between, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Short, thin hair can be a challenge to style, especially if you have a round face. You want a haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair while complementing your face shape. The right haircut can make you look younger, slimmer, and more stylish. In this article, we’ll share some of the best haircuts for short thin hair and round faces, as well as some tips for styling and maintenance.

How To

When choosing a haircut for short thin hair and a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want a style that adds height and volume to your hair, rather than flattening it. Second, you want a cut that frames your face and accentuates your best features. Here are some tips for choosing the right haircut:

Consider Your Face Shape

If you have a round face, you want a haircut that elongates your face and makes it appear slimmer. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face look wider. Instead, opt for a style that adds height and volume to the top of your head.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your haircut can have a big impact on how it flatters your face shape. If you have short hair, consider a pixie cut or a bob that falls just below your chin. If you have longer hair, a layered bob or a shaggy cut can add volume and texture to your hair.

Add Layers

Adding layers to your haircut can create the illusion of volume and thickness. A layered bob, for example, can add texture and movement to your hair. If you have fine hair, ask your stylist to add long layers to create the illusion of thickness.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for getting a haircut for short thin hair and a round face:

  1. Consult with your stylist to choose the right haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  3. Sit in the chair and let your stylist work their magic.
  4. After your haircut, style your hair using a volumizing mousse or spray to create volume and texture.
  5. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the roots to create lift.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Use a curling iron to add waves and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage it and make it appear thinner.
  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for short thin hair and a round face?

The best haircut for short thin hair and a round face is one that adds height and volume to your hair, rather than flattening it. A pixie cut, a bob, or a layered shaggy cut are all good options.

How do I style my short thin hair?

To style short thin hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume. Blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots, and use a curling iron to add waves and texture.

How often should I get my haircut?

You should visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your short thin hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often, as this can damage it and make it appear thinner.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and thickness.
  • Get plenty of rest and exercise to reduce stress, which can contribute to hair loss.

With the right haircut and styling tips, you can make your short thin hair look voluminous and stylish. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
