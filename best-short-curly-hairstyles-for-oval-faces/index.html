Description

If you have an oval face shape and short curly hair, you're in luck! Short curly hairstyles are perfect for those with oval faces as they highlight your natural facial features and add texture and volume to your hair. Finding the right short curly hairstyle can be a challenge, but we've got you covered with some of the best options out there.

How to Style Short Curly Hair for Oval Faces

When styling short curly hair for oval faces, it's important to keep in mind the natural shape of your face. Oval faces have a balanced shape with a slightly narrow forehead and a slightly wider jawline. To highlight your natural features, consider a short curly hairstyle that adds volume around your forehead and temples while keeping the sides and back relatively close to your head.

Some popular short curly hairstyles for oval faces include:

  • Curly Bob
  • Pixie Cut
  • Curly Shag
  • Tapered Cut
  • Short Curly Bangs

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Short Curly Hairstyles for Oval Faces

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect short curly hairstyle for your oval face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair
  3. Use a diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching the curls as you go
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define any loose curls
  5. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hairstyles for Oval Faces

Short curly hairstyles require some extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short curly hairstyle:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQs

What face shape is best for short curly hairstyles?

Short curly hairstyles look great on a variety of face shapes, but they're particularly flattering on those with oval faces. The balanced shape of an oval face makes it the perfect canvas for a short curly hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair.

What products should I use to style my short curly hair?

When styling short curly hair, it's important to use products that enhance your curls and add moisture to your hair. Look for curl-enhancing shampoos and conditioners, as well as styling products like mousses, gels, and creams that define your curls and prevent frizz.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Short curly hair requires some extra care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you care for your short curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often as they can cause damage to your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using products with alcohol as they can dry out your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF

With the right short curly hairstyle and some TLC, you can rock your oval face shape and show off your natural curls in style!
