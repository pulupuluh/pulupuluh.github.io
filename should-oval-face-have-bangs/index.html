Should Oval Face Have Bangs

Written by Robby Jul 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you an oval-faced beauty contemplating whether or not you should get bangs? It's a common question that many women ask themselves when they want to switch up their hairstyle. In this blog post, we'll explore the pros and cons of getting bangs with an oval face shape. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is considered the ideal face shape because it is well-proportioned and balanced. If you have an oval face, you have a forehead that is slightly wider than your chin, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face. Many hairstyles look great on oval faces, including bangs. However, it's essential to consider the shape of your face and the type of bangs that will suit you best.

How To

When deciding whether or not to get bangs with an oval face, there are a few things to consider. First, think about the texture of your hair. If you have thick, coarse hair, blunt bangs may work well for you. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, wispy bangs may be a better option.

Next, consider the length of your forehead. If you have a short forehead, it's best to avoid bangs altogether. If your forehead is long, you can experiment with different types of bangs, such as side-swept or curtain bangs.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine the texture of your hair

Step 2: Consider the length of your forehead

Step 3: Experiment with different types of bangs

Step 4: Consult with a hairstylist

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when deciding whether or not to get bangs:

  • Consider your face shape and hair texture
  • Experiment with different types of bangs
  • Consult with a hairstylist
  • Take into account your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your bangs

FAQ

Q: Will bangs make my face look shorter?

A: It depends on the type of bangs you get. Blunt bangs can make your face look shorter, while side-swept bangs can elongate your face.

Q: How do I style my bangs?

A: The best way to style your bangs will depend on the type of bangs you have. Generally, you can blow-dry your bangs with a round brush and then use a flat iron to smooth them out.

Hair Care

If you decide to get bangs, it's essential to take care of them properly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much product on your bangs. When styling, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

In conclusion, getting bangs with an oval face can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle. However, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and the type of bangs that will work best for you. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect bangs for your oval face shape, and remember to take care of them properly to keep them looking great!
