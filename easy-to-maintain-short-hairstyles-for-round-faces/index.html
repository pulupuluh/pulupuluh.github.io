Easy To Maintain Short Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by William Jul 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of long hair and looking for a change? Short hairstyles are the perfect solution to make a statement and look chic. However, if you have a round face, choosing the right short hairstyle can be challenging. The good news is that there are plenty of easy to maintain short hairstyles for round faces that you can choose from. In this article, we will explore different hairstyles, how to style them, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Description

Short hairstyles are low maintenance, easy to style, and perfect for women with round faces. Round faces are characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. The right short hairstyle can help balance out the proportions of your face, highlighting your best features and creating an overall flattering look.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Round Faces

When it comes to short hairstyles for round faces, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, you want to choose a style that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Secondly, you want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look wider. Here are some popular short hairstyles for round faces:

  • Pixie Cut
  • Bob Cut
  • Asymmetrical Cut
  • Layered Cut
  • Buzz Cut

Step-by-Step Guide to Style Short Hair for Round Faces

Styling short hair is easy and takes less time than long hair. Here are some simple steps to follow to style your short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and style to your hair.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray or wax to add definition and hold to your hair.

Tips to Maintain Short Hairstyles for Round Faces

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but there are a few things you can do to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain sulfates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a round face. However, you want to make sure that the bangs are side-swept and not too thick, as this can add volume to the sides of your face.

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

A: No, a pixie cut can actually elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it is essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain sulfates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Now that you know the different easy to maintain short hairstyles for round faces and how to take care of your short hair, it's time to book a hair appointment and try out a new style. Short hair is a great way to make a bold statement and look chic, without sacrificing your precious time and energy.
