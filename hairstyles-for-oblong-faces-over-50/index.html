Hairstyles For Oblong Faces Over 50

Hairstyles for Oblong Faces Over 50 - Tips, How To, FAQ and Hair Care

Are you over 50 and have an oblong face shape? Do you struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips, how-to's, and hair care advice for finding the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face shape.

Table of Contents

  • What is an oblong face shape?
  • Hairstyles for oblong faces over 50
  • How to find the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face shape
  • Hair care tips for oblong face shapes over 50
  • FAQ

What is an oblong face shape?

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a high forehead, long chin, and sharp jawline. It is often referred to as a rectangular face shape. This face shape is ideal for hairstyles that add width and volume to the sides of the face to balance out the length.

Hairstyles for oblong faces over 50

As we age, our hair tends to thin out, and our face shape may become more prominent. Here are some hairstyles that are perfect for oblong faces over 50:

1. Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut can add volume to the sides of your face and draw attention away from your forehead and chin. This hairstyle is perfect for those with fine hair as it adds texture and volume.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob with layers can add volume and width to the sides of your face. This hairstyle is perfect for those with thick hair as it can help to thin out and add movement.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to soften your face shape and draw attention away from your forehead. This hairstyle is perfect for those with medium to long hair.

4. Layered Bob with Bangs

A layered bob with bangs can add volume and texture to your hair while framing your face. This hairstyle is perfect for those with fine to medium hair.

How to find the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face shape

When looking for the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face shape, consider the following:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds width and volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight as they can elongate your face shape.
  • Consider adding layers and texture to your hair to add movement and volume.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find a hairstyle that complements your features and hair type.

Hair care tips for oblong face shapes over 50

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and more fragile. Here are some hair care tips for oblong face shapes over 50:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage and dry out your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

1. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have an oblong face shape?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have an oblong face shape. However, consider adding some volume and texture to the sides of your hair to balance out the length of your face.

2. Can I wear my hair straight if I have an oblong face shape?

Avoid wearing your hair straight if you have an oblong face shape as it can elongate your face. Instead, consider adding some waves or curls to your hair to add volume and texture.

3. Can I dye my hair if I have an oblong face shape?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have an oblong face shape. However, avoid going too dark or too light as it can wash out your complexion. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect hair color for your skin tone.
