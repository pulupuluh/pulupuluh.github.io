Do Brunettes Look Good With Blonde Hair Exploring The Possibilities

Written by Petter Feb 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a brunette who has been thinking about going blonde? Perhaps you've been wondering if blonde hair would suit your skin tone and facial features. You're not alone! Many women have considered making the switch from brunette to blonde, but they're not sure if it's the right move for them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Go Blonde as a Brunette
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Going Blonde
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Blonde Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Blonde Hair

Description

So, do brunettes look good with blonde hair? The answer is a resounding yes! Blonde hair can be a great look for brunettes, but it's important to choose the right shade of blonde for your skin tone and facial features. Going blonde can be a big change, but it can also be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look.

How to Go Blonde as a Brunette

If you're considering going blonde, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, it's important to choose the right shade of blonde for your skin tone. If you have cool undertones, you'll want to go for a cooler blonde, like a platinum or ash blonde. If you have warm undertones, a honey or golden blonde may be a better fit.

It's also important to consider the upkeep involved in maintaining blonde hair. Blonde hair requires more maintenance than darker shades, so you'll need to be prepared to touch up your roots regularly and use products that are specifically designed for blonde hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Going Blonde

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you go blonde:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best shade of blonde for your skin tone.
  2. Prepare your hair by deep conditioning it and avoiding any heat styling for a few days before your appointment.
  3. Have your hair professionally lightened to the desired shade of blonde.
  4. Use toner to get rid of any brassiness and create a more natural-looking blonde.
  5. Maintain your blonde hair by using products specifically designed for blonde hair and touching up your roots regularly.

Tips for Maintaining Your Blonde Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your blonde hair:

  • Use a purple shampoo to keep your blonde hair from turning brassy.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to keep your blonde looking fresh.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, which can damage your hair and turn it green.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about going blonde as a brunette:

1. Will going blonde damage my hair?

Lightening your hair can cause some damage, but a professional stylist can help minimize the damage and keep your hair healthy.

2. How often do I need to touch up my roots?

You'll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to keep your blonde looking fresh.

3. Can I go back to being a brunette after going blonde?

Yes, you can always go back to being a brunette if you decide that blonde isn't for you.

Hair Care for Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires some special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your blonde hair:

  • Use a purple shampoo to keep your blonde hair from turning brassy.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

So, do brunettes look good with blonde hair? Absolutely! With the right shade of blonde and proper maintenance, blonde hair can be a great look for brunettes. Talk to a professional stylist to find out if going blonde is right for you.
