How To Cut Guys Top Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Mar 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to give yourself or someone else a fresh haircut, one of the most important things to know is how to cut guys top hair. This is the part of the hair that sits on top of the head and can be styled in a variety of ways. Whether you're going for a classic crew cut, a trendy quiff, or something in between, cutting the top hair is an essential part of the process. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting guys top hair, from start to finish.

How to Cut Guys Top Hair

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you get started, make sure you have all the tools you'll need. This includes a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to consider investing in a set of clippers if you plan on cutting your hair regularly.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Starting at the front of the head, use the comb to section off the top hair. The width of the section will depend on how much hair you want to cut, but a good rule of thumb is to start with a section that's about as wide as the comb itself.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut it to the desired length. If you're going for a shorter style, you may want to use the clippers to trim the hair first. Make sure to cut the hair in small sections, and take your time to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Repeat

Continue sectioning off the top hair and cutting it to the desired length, working your way towards the back of the head. Make sure to check your work as you go, and make any necessary adjustments to ensure an even cut.

Tips for Cutting Guys Top Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting guys top hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see the lines and ensure an even cut.
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go. This will help you catch any uneven sections and make adjustments as needed.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you're having trouble cutting the back of your head, ask a friend or family member to assist you.
  • Take your time. Cutting hair is a process that requires patience and attention to detail.

FAQ

Q: Do I need to use clippers to cut guys top hair?

A: No, clippers are not necessary, but they can be helpful if you're going for a shorter style.

Q: How often should I cut my top hair?

A: This will depend on your personal preference and the style you're going for. Some people prefer to cut their hair every few weeks, while others may only need a trim every few months.

Hair Care After Cutting Guys Top Hair

After cutting guys top hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a quality hair product, such as a pomade or gel, to style your hair and keep it in place.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to confidently cut guys top hair and achieve the style you're going for. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it!
