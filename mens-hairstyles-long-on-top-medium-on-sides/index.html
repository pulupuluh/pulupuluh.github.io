Mens Hairstyles Long On Top Medium On Sides A Timeless Look For Men

Written by Aprilia Mar 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is both trendy and timeless? Look no further than the mens hairstyles long on top medium on sides. It's a look that has been popular for years and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve this hairstyle, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Achieve the Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Medium on Sides

The mens hairstyles long on top medium on sides is a versatile look that can be achieved with a variety of techniques. Here's a step-by-step guide to get you started:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

Before you start styling your hair, you need to have the right cut. The long on top medium on sides look is achieved by cutting the top of your hair long and leaving the sides and back shorter. You can ask your barber for a fade or taper cut to achieve this look.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Once you have the right cut, it's important to keep your hair clean and healthy. Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it looking its best.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture to the top. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to create the desired look. Apply the product to the top of your hair and use your fingers to style it in place. Use a comb to smooth down the sides and back.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Medium on Sides

To keep your mens hairstyles long on top medium on sides looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when blow drying.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place.

FAQ

Can this hairstyle work for any hair type?

Yes, the mens hairstyles long on top medium on sides can work for any hair type. However, it may require different techniques and products depending on your hair texture.

How long does it take to style this hairstyle?

It typically takes 5-10 minutes to style the mens hairstyles long on top medium on sides.

Can I wear this style to work?

Yes, the mens hairstyles long on top medium on sides is a versatile look that can be worn to work or any other occasion.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to washing and conditioning your hair regularly, there are a few other hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

The mens hairstyles long on top medium on sides is a classic look that never goes out of style. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve this look and keep it looking great with minimal effort. Follow our tips for maintaining healthy hair and you'll be sure to turn heads with your stylish new look.
