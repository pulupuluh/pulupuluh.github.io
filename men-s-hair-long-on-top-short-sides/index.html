Men s Hair Long On Top Short Sides A Modern Classic Style

Written by Frank Apr 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to achieve and maintain the popular hairstyle of men's hair long on top short sides. This style has been in trend for a while now and shows no signs of slowing down. It's a versatile haircut that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the style, from how to get it to how to maintain it. So, let's dive in!

Description

Men's hair long on top short sides is a classic style that features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides. It's a popular choice because it allows for a lot of versatility in styling. The longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy, while the shorter sides give the haircut a clean, modern look. The style is perfect for men who want to look stylish and put-together without being too high-maintenance.

How to Get the Style

If you're interested in getting the men's hair long on top short sides style, the first step is to find a good barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. It's important to make sure that your hair is long enough on top to achieve the desired look. Generally, you'll want at least 2-3 inches of hair on top and 1-2 inches on the sides.

Once you've found a stylist you trust, it's important to communicate exactly what you want. Bring in pictures of the style you're going for and be specific about the length you want on top and on the sides. Your stylist may also recommend adding some texture or layers to the longer hair on top, depending on your hair type and face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the men's hair long on top short sides style:

  1. Find a good barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of cut.
  2. Communicate exactly what you want, including the length you want on top and on the sides.
  3. Ask your stylist to add texture or layers to the longer hair on top, if needed.
  4. Once the cut is complete, style your hair using your preferred products, such as pomade or wax.
  5. Experiment with different styles, such as slicked back or messy, to find what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Now that you have the men's hair long on top short sides style, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep the style looking fresh and clean.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Try different styles, such as slicked back or messy, to keep the look fresh and interesting.

FAQ

Q: Is the men's hair long on top short sides style suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the style can be adapted to suit any hair type. Your stylist may recommend adding texture or layers to the longer hair on top, depending on your hair type and face shape.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh and clean.

Q: What styling products work best for this style?

A: It depends on your hair type and the look you're going for. Pomade and wax are popular choices for this style, but you may need to experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining any hairstyle, including men's hair long on top short sides. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

We hope this guide has been helpful in achieving and maintaining the men's hair long on top short sides style. Remember to communicate clearly with your stylist, experiment with different styles and products, and take good care of your hair for the best results.
