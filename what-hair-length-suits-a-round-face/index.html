What Hair Length Suits A Round Face

Written by Frank Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering which hair length suits you best. The good news is that there are many options to choose from, whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair. In this article, we will explore the best hair lengths for a round face and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face has soft, curved lines and a width that is almost equal to its length. The goal when choosing a hair length is to create an illusion of length and angles to balance out the roundness of the face. This can be achieved by adding layers, volume, and texture to the hair. The right hair length can make a round face look more oval and give it a more defined shape.

How to

The first step in choosing the right hair length for a round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing the two. If they are almost equal, you have a round face.

Next, consider your hair texture and personal style. If you have fine hair, a shorter length can add volume and make your hair look fuller. If you have thick hair, a longer length can help to weigh it down and prevent it from looking too bulky. If you prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle, a shorter length can be easier to style and maintain.

Step by Step

  1. Measure the length and width of your face to determine your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture and personal style.
  3. Choose a hair length that creates an illusion of length and angles.
  4. Add layers, volume, and texture to your hair to balance out the roundness of your face.
  5. Style your hair to accentuate the length and angles.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right hair length for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they can make a round face look wider.
  • Consider side-swept bangs or layers that frame the face to create angles.
  • Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as wide curls or bouffants.
  • Choose a length that falls below the chin to elongate the face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair length and round faces:

Q: Can I wear my hair in a pixie cut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can look great on a round face if it is styled to add volume and texture to the crown of the head.

Q: Will a bob make my face look wider?

A: It depends on the length of the bob. A longer bob that falls below the chin can elongate the face, while a shorter bob that ends at the jawline can make the face look wider.

Q: Should I avoid long hair if I have a round face?

A: Not necessarily. Long hair can look great on a round face if it is styled with layers and texture to create angles.

Hair Care

No matter which hair length you choose, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right hair length for your round face, you can create a flattering hairstyle that complements your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
