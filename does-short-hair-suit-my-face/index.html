Does Short Hair Suit My Face

Written by Eveline Mar 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short hair has become increasingly popular in recent years, with many women opting for a shorter, more manageable hairstyle. However, the question remains: does short hair suit my face? In this article, we will explore this question in detail and give you tips on how to choose the right short hairstyle for your face shape.

Description

Short hair can be a great option for women who are looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and maintain. However, not all short hairstyles will suit every face shape. The key to finding the right short hairstyle for you is to consider your face shape and choose a style that will enhance your best features.

How To Choose The Right Short Hairstyle For Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right short hairstyle for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are four main face shapes: round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. Once you have determined your face shape, you can then choose a hairstyle that will complement your features and enhance your natural beauty.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you should choose a short hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of length. A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs are great options for a round face shape.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky because almost any short hairstyle will suit you. However, a blunt bob or a short shaggy hairstyle are both great options for an oval face shape.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you should choose a short hairstyle that will soften your features and create the illusion of roundness. A textured pixie cut or a layered bob with soft waves are both great options for a square face shape.

Heart-Shaped Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face shape, you should choose a short hairstyle that will balance out your features and create the illusion of width at the jawline. A layered bob with side-swept bangs or a pixie cut with volume at the crown are both great options for a heart-shaped face shape.

Step By Step Guide To Choosing The Right Short Hairstyle For Your Face Shape

Step 1: Determine your face shape.

Step 2: Research short hairstyles that are suitable for your face shape.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to determine which short hairstyle will suit you best.

Step 4: Choose a short hairstyle that will complement your features and enhance your natural beauty.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair can be low maintenance, but it still requires some care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair nourished and moisturized.
  • Experiment with styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Try different styling techniques to create different looks, such as using a curling iron or flat iron.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair suit me if I have a large forehead?

A: Yes, short hair can suit you if you have a large forehead. Consider a pixie cut with side-swept bangs to balance out your features.

Q: Can I still style my short hair?

A: Yes, you can still style your short hair. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for you.

Q: Will short hair make my face look wider?

A: It depends on your face shape and the style of the short haircut. If you have a round face shape, a short haircut with volume on top can make your face look wider. However, if you have a square or heart-shaped face shape, a short haircut can create the illusion of width at the jawline, which can be flattering.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

With the right short hairstyle and proper care, short hair can be a great option for women of all ages and face shapes. Use this guide to determine if short hair suits your face and choose a style that will enhance your natural beauty. Happy hairstyling!
