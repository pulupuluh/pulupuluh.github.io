How To Fix Side Bangs Cut Too Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Side bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle and add some dimension to your face. However, if your side bangs have been cut too short, it can be frustrating and difficult to style. Don’t worry – we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to fix side bangs cut too short.

Description

There’s nothing worse than leaving the salon with bangs that are way too short. Not only can it be a blow to your self-confidence, but it can also be difficult to style and make you feel like you’re limited in your hairstyle options. This guide will help you fix your side bangs so that they’re the perfect length for your face shape and complement your overall hairstyle.

How to

Before we dive into the steps to fix your side bangs, it’s important to note that it’s best to wait a few days after your initial haircut before attempting to fix your bangs. This will give your hair some time to settle and make it easier to work with.

Step 1: Section Your Bangs

The first step is to section off your bangs from the rest of your hair. Use a comb to create a straight line across your forehead, separating your bangs from the rest of your hair.

Step 2: Determine the Desired Length

Next, determine the desired length of your bangs. It’s important to take into account your face shape and personal style when deciding on the length.

Step 3: Trim Your Bangs

Using a pair of sharp scissors, carefully trim your bangs to the desired length. It’s important to work slowly and in small sections to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Step 4: Blend Your Bangs

After trimming your bangs, use a razor comb or thinning shears to blend your bangs with the rest of your hair. This will create a seamless transition and make your bangs look natural.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Finally, style your newly trimmed bangs. You can use a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to create the desired look.

Tips

  • Always use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Work slowly and in small sections to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a razor comb or thinning shears to blend your bangs with the rest of your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the best one for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I fix my side bangs at home, or do I need to go to a salon?

A: You can definitely fix your side bangs at home, but if you’re unsure or uncomfortable, it’s always best to go to a salon.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: It’s best to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

Q: What styling products should I use for my bangs?

A: It depends on your hair type and desired style. Experiment with different products to find the best one for you.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to keeping your bangs looking their best. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

With these tips and steps, you’ll be able to fix your side bangs and have a hairstyle that complements your face shape and personal style. Remember to take your time and work slowly to avoid any mishaps, and don’t be afraid to seek professional help if needed.
