Is Crimped Hair in Fashion?

The short answer is yes! Crimped hair is making a comeback in 2023, and it's a great way to add texture and volume to your hair. This style was popular in the 80s and 90s, and it has been seen on runways and red carpets in recent years. Celebrities like BeyoncÃ©, Zendaya, and Gigi Hadid have all rocked crimped hair, proving that it's a versatile and trendy look.

How to Crimp Hair

Before we get into the step-by-step instructions, let's talk about the tools you'll need to crimp your hair. You'll need a crimping iron, which is a special heat styling tool that creates crimped waves in your hair. You'll also need a heat protectant spray, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the crimping iron.

2. Section off your hair into small sections using hair clips. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

3. Take a small section of hair and clamp the crimping iron down on it, holding it for a few seconds.

4. Release the crimping iron and move it down the section of hair, repeating the process until you reach the end.

5. Repeat this process on all of the sections of your hair.

6. Once you've crimped your entire head, use a comb to gently brush out your hair and create a softer, more natural look.

Tips for Crimped Hair

Crimped hair can be a little tricky to style, so here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Section off your hair into small sections to make sure you get an even crimp.
  • Don't over-crimp your hair. You want to create waves, not a crimped zig-zag pattern.
  • Brush out your hair gently to create a softer, more natural look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, like half-up half-down or a messy bun, to show off your crimped waves.

Crimped Hair FAQs

Is crimped hair damaging to your hair?

Using a crimping iron can cause damage to your hair if you don't use a heat protectant spray or if you overuse the tool. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray and limit your use of the crimping iron to once a week to prevent damage.

Can you crimp short hair?

Yes! You can crimp short hair, but you may need to use a smaller crimping iron and work in smaller sections.

How long does crimped hair last?

Crimped hair can last for several days if you take care of it properly. Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes, and avoid using too much heat styling tools to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Crimped Hair

Caring for crimped hair is similar to caring for any other type of hair. Here are some tips to keep your crimped hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using too many heat styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, which can weigh down your crimped waves.

Now that you know the answer to the question, "Is crimped hair in fashion?" and have all the tips and tricks to achieve this trendy look, it's time to try it out for yourself. Whether you're going for a bold, 80s-inspired look or a more subtle, natural wave, crimped hair is a fun and fashionable hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.
