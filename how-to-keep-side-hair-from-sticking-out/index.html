How To Keep Side Hair From Sticking Out

Written by Bardi May 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your side hair sticking out and ruining your perfectly styled hairdo? Don't worry, you're not alone. This is a common problem that many people face. In this article, we will discuss the different ways to keep your side hair from sticking out.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Side Hair from Sticking Out
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Keep Side Hair from Sticking Out

There are several ways to keep your side hair from sticking out:

1. Use hairspray

Before styling your hair, use a lightweight hairspray to keep it in place. This will prevent your side hair from sticking out throughout the day. Just be sure not to use too much, as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

2. Blow dry your hair

Blow drying your hair in the opposite direction of your side hair can help to keep it in place. Use a round brush to smooth out your hair and create a sleek look. Finish off with a blast of cool air to set your style in place.

3. Use bobby pins

If you have shorter hair, using bobby pins can be an effective way to keep your side hair from sticking out. Take a small section of hair and pin it back behind your ear. This will not only keep your hair in place but also create a stylish look.

4. Try a different hairstyle

If your side hair is constantly sticking out, it may be time to try a different hairstyle. Consider a style that incorporates your side hair, such as a side braid or a side ponytail. This can help to keep your hair in place while still looking fashionable.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to keep your side hair from sticking out:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any excess oil and dirt that can weigh down your hair.

2. Apply styling product

Apply a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair. This will help to create volume and hold your style in place.

3. Blow dry your hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair in the opposite direction of your side hair. Use a round brush to smooth out your hair and create a sleek look.

4. Use hairspray

Finish off with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day. Be sure not to use too much, as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep your side hair from sticking out:

  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent your hair from getting tangled while you sleep.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and cause it to stick out.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ

Q. How do I keep my side hair from sticking out when I wear a hat?

A. One way to keep your side hair from sticking out when you wear a hat is to use bobby pins to secure your hair in place. Take a small section of hair and pin it back behind your ear.

Q. Can I use a leave-in conditioner to keep my side hair from sticking out?

A. Yes, a leave-in conditioner can help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent it from sticking out. Just be sure to use a lightweight conditioner that won't weigh down your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keeping your side hair from sticking out. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and cause it to stick out.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can keep your side hair from sticking out and achieve a flawless, stylish look every time.
