How To Thin Long Hair With Scissors A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline May 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair can look beautiful and glamorous, but it can also be a hassle to maintain. If you have long hair, you may have experienced the frustration of having thick, heavy locks that are difficult to style. One solution to this problem is to thin your hair with scissors. Thinning your hair can help reduce bulk, add texture, and make your hair more manageable. In this article, we'll show you how to thin long hair with scissors step by step.

How to Thin Long Hair with Scissors

Thinning your hair with scissors is a relatively simple process that can be done at home. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you begin, wash and dry your hair. This will help you see the natural fall of your hair and ensure that you're not cutting too much off.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. You can divide your hair into as many sections as you like, but it's best to start with four sections (two on top and two on the bottom).

Step 3: Start Thinning

Take one section of hair and hold it straight up. Use your scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, about halfway down the shaft. Cut small sections of hair at a time, and be careful not to cut too much off.

Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way around your head.

Step 4: Blend

After you've thinned all of your hair, it's time to blend. Take a comb and comb through your hair to blend the sections together.

Step 5: Finish

Style your hair as usual, and enjoy your newly thinned locks!

Tips for Thinning Long Hair with Scissors

Thinning your hair with scissors can be a great way to add texture and reduce bulk, but it's important to do it correctly. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it more difficult to cut.
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time. This will help you avoid cutting too much off.
  • Be patient. Thinning your hair can take some time, especially if you have thick hair. Take breaks if you need to, and don't rush the process.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair is heavier than dry hair, so if you cut your hair when it's wet, you may end up cutting too much off.

FAQ

How often should I thin my hair?

How often you should thin your hair depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, you should aim to thin your hair every four to six months.

Can I thin my hair with regular scissors?

While you can technically thin your hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Regular scissors are not as sharp as hair-cutting scissors, and they can damage your hair. If you're going to thin your hair at home, invest in a pair of hair-cutting scissors.

Hair Care Tips

Thinning your hair can be a great way to make it more manageable, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. Heat can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help you avoid breakage and damage.
  • Get regular trims. Even if you're thinning your hair at home, it's still important to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

Thinning long hair with scissors can be a great way to add texture and make your hair more manageable. Whether you're doing it at home or at the salon, be sure to follow these tips to get the best results.
