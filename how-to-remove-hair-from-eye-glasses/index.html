How To Remove Hair From Eye Glasses Tips Tricks And Faqs

Written by Oliver Mar 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of constantly wiping your glasses because of hair getting in the way? Do you find yourself struggling to remove those pesky strands without leaving a smudge or scratch on your lenses? Fear not, for we have compiled some tips and tricks to help you remove hair from your eye glasses without any hassle.

How to Remove Hair from Eye Glasses

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, here are some basic steps you can follow to remove hair from your eye glasses:

  1. Remove your glasses from your face.
  2. Hold your glasses with one hand.
  3. Use the other hand to gently remove the hair strands from your lenses.
  4. Wipe your lenses with a microfiber cloth to remove any leftover hair or smudges.
  5. Put your glasses back on and enjoy the clear view!

Tips for Removing Hair from Eye Glasses

Use a Soft-Bristled Brush

If you don't have a microfiber cloth or if the hair strands are too stubborn to remove, try using a soft-bristled brush. This will gently remove the hair without scratching your lenses. You can use a toothbrush or a makeup brush for this purpose.

Use a Blow Dryer

If you're in a hurry and don't have time to clean your glasses, you can use a blow dryer to remove the hair strands. Set the dryer on low heat and aim it at your lenses. The heat will loosen the hair, making it easier to remove.

Use a Piece of Tape

If you don't have a brush or a blow dryer, you can use a piece of tape to remove the hair. Simply stick the tape on the lenses and gently pull it off. The tape will pick up the hair strands along with any other dirt or debris on your lenses.

FAQs About Removing Hair from Eye Glasses

Can I use vinegar to clean my glasses?

Yes, you can use vinegar to clean your glasses. Mix equal parts of vinegar and water and use a microfiber cloth to clean your lenses. Vinegar is an effective cleaner and will remove any dirt or smudges on your glasses.

Can I use rubbing alcohol to clean my glasses?

Yes, you can use rubbing alcohol to clean your glasses. Mix equal parts of rubbing alcohol and water and use a microfiber cloth to clean your lenses. Rubbing alcohol is a great disinfectant and will remove any bacteria on your glasses.

How often should I clean my glasses?

You should clean your glasses at least once a day to remove any dirt or smudges. If you wear your glasses for extended periods or if you have oily skin, you may need to clean them more frequently.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair from Getting on Your Glasses

Prevention is always better than cure. Here are some hair care tips to prevent hair from getting on your glasses:

  • Tie your hair back in a ponytail or bun.
  • Use a headband to keep your hair away from your face.
  • Avoid using hair products that make your hair sticky or greasy.
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any dirt or oil buildup.

Now that you've learned how to remove hair from your eye glasses, you can enjoy a clear view without any distractions. Remember to clean your glasses regularly and follow our hair care tips to prevent hair from getting in the way.
