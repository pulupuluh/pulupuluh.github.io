Mens Back Hairstyles A Guide To Looking Great From Behind

Written by Robby Apr 10, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking for ideas on how to style your hair from the back, you're in the right place. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about mens back hairstyles. From how to style your hair to tips on hair care, we've got you covered. Here's a breakdown of what you can expect to find in this article:

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Your Hair from the Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair from the Back
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Back Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Mens Back Hairstyles

Description

Your hair is an extension of your style and personality. It's what people notice first about you, and it's what can make or break your overall look. While most men focus on the front of their hair, the back is just as important. A well-groomed back hairstyle can add a touch of sophistication and style to any outfit. Whether you're looking for a new style or simply want to maintain your current one, this guide will help you achieve your desired look.

How to Style Your Hair from the Back

When it comes to mens back hairstyles, there are several options to choose from. The most popular styles include the undercut, the slicked-back look, and the man bun. Each style has its own unique look and requires different techniques to achieve. Here's a breakdown of each style:

Undercut

The undercut is a popular mens back hairstyle that involves shaving the sides of your hair while leaving the top long. This look is often paired with a slicked-back top for a sophisticated and stylish look.

Slicked-Back Look

The slicked-back look is a classic mens back hairstyle that involves combing your hair back and using a styling product to keep it in place. This style is perfect for those who want a clean and polished look.

Man Bun

The man bun is a trendy mens back hairstyle that involves tying your hair up in a bun at the back of your head. This style is perfect for those who want a more casual and laid-back look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair from the Back

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving each of the mens back hairstyles mentioned above:

Undercut

  1. Start by shaving the sides of your hair using clippers or a razor.
  2. Leave the top long and comb it back.
  3. Apply a styling product such as pomade or wax to the top of your hair.
  4. Use a comb to style your hair back and to the side.

Slicked-Back Look

  1. Apply a styling product such as gel or pomade to your hair.
  2. Comb your hair back and to the side.
  3. Use a hair dryer to set the style in place.

Man Bun

  1. Gather your hair at the back of your head and tie it in a bun.
  2. Use bobby pins or a hair tie to secure the bun in place.
  3. Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Back Hairstyle

Once you've achieved your desired mens back hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Apply a styling product such as pomade or wax to keep your hair in place.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

FAQs

What is the best mens back hairstyle?

The best mens back hairstyle depends on your personal style and preferences. Some popular options include the undercut, the slicked-back look, and the man bun.

How do I style my hair from the back?

To style your hair from the back, start by choosing a mens back hairstyle that you like. Then, follow the step-by-step guide provided in this article to achieve your desired look.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Back Hairstyles

Mens back hairstyles require proper hair care to maintain their shape and style. Here are a few tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your hair.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Now that you know everything there is to know about mens back hairstyles, it's time to choose the style that's right for you. Whether you prefer a sophisticated undercut or a casual man bun, you can achieve your desired look with the right technique and hair care routine. So go ahead and experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best. Good luck!
