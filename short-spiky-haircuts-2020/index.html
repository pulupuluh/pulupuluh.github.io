Short Spiky Haircuts 2020 A Trend That Stays

Short spiky haircuts have been in trend for a long time now. It is a popular hairstyle among both men and women. In 2020, we saw a lot of variations in this hairstyle. In this blog post, we will discuss everything related to short spiky haircuts 2020, including how to get the style, tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Short Spiky Haircuts 2020
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Get Short Spiky Haircuts 2020

Getting short spiky haircuts is not rocket science. You can visit your nearest salon or barber shop and ask for the style. However, if you want to save some money and do it yourself, then you can do that too. All you need is a good quality trimmer or clipper, a comb, and some hair gel or wax.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting short spiky haircuts:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Once your hair is dry, use a trimmer or clipper to trim the sides and back of your head. Leave the hair on top longer.
  3. Use a comb to lift the hair on top and trim it to your desired length.
  4. Apply some hair gel or wax to your hair and use your fingers to style it into spikes.
  5. Use a hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Spiky Haircuts

Short spiky haircuts require regular maintenance to look good. Here are some tips to help you maintain the style:

  • Trim your hair every 2-3 weeks to keep it looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much hair gel or wax as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • If you have thin hair, use a volumizing spray to add some volume to your hair.
  • Use a hairspray to set the style in place and prevent it from falling apart.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is short spiky haircuts suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, short spiky haircuts are versatile and can be customized to suit any face shape. However, it is best to consult with your hairstylist before getting the style.

Can I get short spiky haircuts if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can get short spiky haircuts if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use more hair gel or wax to hold the spikes in place.

How long does it take to style short spiky haircuts?

It takes around 10-15 minutes to style short spiky haircuts.

Hair Care for Short Spiky Haircuts

Short spiky haircuts require regular hair care to keep them looking good. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Do not use hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid using too much hair gel or wax as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent damage.

Short spiky haircuts are a timeless trend that never goes out of style. With the right styling and hair care, you can rock this hairstyle with confidence. Follow the tips and steps mentioned in this blog post to get short spiky haircuts 2020 and maintain them like a pro.
