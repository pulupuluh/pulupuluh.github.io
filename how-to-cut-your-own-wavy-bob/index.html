How To Cut Your Own Wavy Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every few weeks to maintain your wavy bob? Do you want to save money and time by cutting your own hair? We've got you covered! In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting your own wavy bob.

Description

A wavy bob is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that suits all face shapes and hair textures. It's a shoulder-length haircut that's slightly shorter in the back and longer in the front. The waves add volume and texture to the hair, making it look effortless and chic. To cut your own wavy bob, you need a few tools and some basic knowledge of hair cutting techniques.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Water spray bottle

Here are the steps to cut your own wavy bob:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and let it air dry until it's slightly damp. Don't blow dry your hair as it may affect the natural wave pattern.

Step 2: Part your hair

Part your hair in the middle or on one side, depending on your preference. Use a comb to create a straight parting.

Step 3: Section your hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Use clips to secure each section.

Step 4: Cut the back section

Start with the back section. Unclip one section and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, starting from the nape of your neck and ending at your desired length. Repeat this step for the other back section.

Step 5: Cut the front section

Unclip one front section and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, starting from the chin and ending at your desired length. Repeat this step for the other front section.

Step 6: Blend the layers

Blend the layers by combing your hair and cutting the ends at an angle. This will give your hair a natural and seamless look.

Step 7: Refine the shape

Check the length and shape of your hair in the mirror. Use your scissors to refine the shape and trim any uneven ends.

Step 8: Add texture

To add texture to your wavy bob, use your scissors to cut into the ends of your hair. This will create a choppy and textured look.

Step 9: Style your hair

Style your hair by scrunching it with your hands or using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will enhance the natural wave pattern and give your hair more volume.

Step 10: Clean up

Clean up any stray hairs and put away your tools.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and trim as needed.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it may appear longer than it actually is.
  • Use the right scissors for cutting hair.
  • Take breaks in between cutting to avoid fatigue.

FAQ

Can I cut my own wavy bob?

Yes, you can! Follow our step-by-step guide and use the right tools and techniques.

Do I need to have experience cutting hair?

No, you don't need to have professional experience. However, it's important to have some basic knowledge of hair cutting techniques.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! You can always visit a salon and have your hair fixed. Alternatively, you can try to fix it yourself by trimming the ends or blending the layers.

Hair Care

To maintain your wavy bob, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape.

Now that you know how to cut your own wavy bob, go ahead and give it a try! With some practice and patience, you'll be able to master this trendy hairstyle.
