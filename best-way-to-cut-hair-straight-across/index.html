Best Way To Cut Hair Straight Across A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 02, 2023 · 7 min read

Cutting your hair straight across can be a daunting task, especially if you’re doing it yourself. But with the right tools and techniques, it’s possible to achieve a salon-worthy haircut at home. In this post, we’ll walk you through the best way to cut hair straight across, complete with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Hair Straight Across
  • Tips for Cutting Hair Straight Across
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care After Cutting Hair Straight Across

How to Cut Hair Straight Across

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb or brush, and hair clips to section off your hair. Here’s how to cut hair straight across:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb or brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Section off your hair. Use hair clips to divide your hair into three or four sections: one at the back, two at the sides, and one at the top.
  3. Begin cutting. Start at the back section and work your way forward. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to ensure an even cut.
  4. Repeat on the side sections. Take small sections of hair and cut straight across. Keep your fingers parallel to the floor to ensure a straight cut.
  5. Finish with the top section. Comb or brush the top section forward and cut straight across. Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots.
  6. Trim any uneven spots. If you notice any areas that are longer than others, trim them carefully with your scissors.

Remember, it’s always better to cut less than more. You can always go back and trim more if needed.

Tips for Cutting Hair Straight Across

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect straight across haircut:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve a clean cut.
  • Take your time. Cutting hair straight across requires patience and precision. Don’t rush the process.
  • Use a comb or brush to guide your scissors. This will help you achieve a straight cut.
  • Work in a well-lit area. Good lighting will help you see any uneven spots.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my hair straight across if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still cut your hair straight across if you have curly hair. However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so you may need to cut it a bit longer than you would if you had straight hair.

How often should I cut my hair straight across?

This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how long you want your hair to be. As a general rule, most people need to trim their hair every six to eight weeks to maintain their style.

Can I cut my own hair straight across?

Yes, you can cut your own hair straight across. However, if you’re nervous about doing it yourself, it’s always a good idea to seek the help of a professional stylist.

Hair Care After Cutting Hair Straight Across

After cutting your hair straight across, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips, you can achieve a beautiful straight across haircut and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
