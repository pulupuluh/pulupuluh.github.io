How To Do A High Ponytail With Short Hair Black Girl Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 21, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a black girl with short hair who is struggling to achieve a high ponytail? Well, worry no more! In this article, we will show you how to create a stylish high ponytail with short hair in just a few easy steps.

Description

A high ponytail is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is a classic, sleek and versatile look that can be worn for any occasion, from casual to formal events. However, creating a high ponytail with short hair can be challenging, especially for black girls with relaxed hair.

But don't let your short hair discourage you from achieving this stylish look. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily create a high ponytail that will turn heads wherever you go.

How to

Here's how to do a high ponytail with short hair black girl:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Comb
  • Hair tie
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair gel or edge control

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Apply hair gel or edge control to your edges to keep them smooth and sleek.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, create a triangular section at the crown of your head. Secure the rest of your hair with a hair tie to keep it out of the way.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Tease the hair in the triangular section to create volume and texture.

Step 5: Create the Ponytail

Gather the teased hair and the rest of your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail.

Step 6: Secure the Ponytail

Use bobby pins to secure any loose hair around the base of the ponytail.

Step 7: Finish the Look

Apply hair gel or edge control to your edges to keep them smooth and sleek. Use a comb to smooth any flyaways.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail with short hair:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color for a seamless look.
  • Tease your hair gently to avoid damaging it.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  • Apply hair gel or edge control to your edges to keep them smooth and sleek.

FAQ

Can I still achieve a high ponytail with short hair if I have natural hair?

Absolutely! The steps to achieve a high ponytail with short hair are the same, regardless of whether you have natural or relaxed hair.

How can I make my high ponytail look fuller?

You can make your high ponytail look fuller by teasing your hair or using hair extensions.

How long does it take to create a high ponytail with short hair?

It should take no more than 10-15 minutes to create a high ponytail with short hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and strength. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove buildup and dirt.
  • Use a deep conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can achieve a beautiful, healthy high ponytail with short hair black girl.

So, there you have it – a step-by-step guide on how to do a high ponytail with short hair black girl. With a little practice, you'll be able to create this timeless look in no time.
