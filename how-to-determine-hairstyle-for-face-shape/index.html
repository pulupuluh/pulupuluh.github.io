How To Determine Hairstyle For Face Shape

Written by Christine Aug 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle, the first step is to determine your face shape. The right hairstyle can accentuate your best features and minimize any flaws, so it's important to choose one that suits your face shape. In this post, we'll show you how to determine your face shape and suggest hairstyles that will flatter you.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining your face shape is to look in a mirror and pull your hair back so that your face is fully visible. Then, use a tape measure to measure the distance across your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down the measurements for each area.

Next, determine which of the following face shapes you have:

Round Face Shape

If your face is as wide as it is long and you have soft curves with no angles, you have a round face shape. To balance out your face, go for hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown of your head, such as a high ponytail or top knot.

Square Face Shape

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width and you have a strong, angular jaw, you have a square face shape. Soften your features with hairstyles that have layers and long, side-swept bangs. Avoid blunt cuts and center parts.

Heart Face Shape

If your forehead is wider than your jawline and you have a pointed chin, you have a heart face shape. Hairstyles that add volume at the jawline, such as a bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers, will balance out your proportions.

Oval Face Shape

If your face is longer than it is wide and you have a gently rounded hairline, you have an oval face shape. Lucky you - nearly any hairstyle will suit you! Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

Diamond Face Shape

If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and you have a narrow forehead and jawline, you have a diamond face shape. Create balance with hairstyles that add width at the forehead and jawline, such as a side-swept fringe or a chin-length bob.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, follow these steps to choose the right hairstyle:

  1. Consider your hair type. Is it thick or thin, curly or straight? This will affect the way your hair falls and how much volume you can create.
  2. Think about your lifestyle. Do you have time to style your hair every day, or do you need something low-maintenance?
  3. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines or Pinterest for hairstyles that suit your face shape and hair type.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist. They can offer personalized advice and help you choose a hairstyle that suits you best.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen your new hairstyle, follow these tips to keep it looking great:

  • Invest in quality hair products that are tailored to your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-styling with heat tools, which can damage your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape:

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

It's possible to have a combination of face shapes, such as a heart-shaped face with a square jawline. In this case, focus on the dominant features of your face and choose a hairstyle that flatters them.

Can I still wear my favorite hairstyle if it doesn't suit my face shape?

Of course! These guidelines are just suggestions - ultimately, you should wear whatever hairstyle makes you feel confident and beautiful.

How often should I change my hairstyle?

There's no set rule for how often you should change your hairstyle. Some people prefer to stick with a tried-and-true style, while others like to switch things up frequently. It's up to you!

Hair Care

Remember, choosing the right hairstyle is only half the battle - taking care of your hair is just as important. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength. And don't forget to have fun with your new hairstyle!
