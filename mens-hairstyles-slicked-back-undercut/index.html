Mens Hairstyles Slicked Back Undercut

Written by Frank Jul 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle, look no further than the slicked back undercut. This popular hairstyle has been around for a few years now and shows no signs of slowing down. It's a versatile look that can be tailored to fit your personal style, whether you prefer a more classic or modern look.

Description

The slicked back undercut is a hairstyle that features shaved or very short sides and back with longer hair on top that is slicked back with a styling product. The contrast between the short sides and long top creates a dramatic look that is both edgy and sophisticated. This style is popular among men of all ages, from young professionals to older gentlemen.

How to

If you're interested in trying out the slicked back undercut, here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start by growing out the hair on top of your head. The length you need will depend on how long you want your hair to be slicked back.
  2. Visit a barber or stylist to get the undercut. You can choose how short you want the sides and back to be shaved or buzzed.
  3. Once the undercut is done, wash your hair and dry it with a towel.
  4. Apply a styling product, such as pomade, gel, or wax, to your hair. Use a comb to slick the hair back and create the desired style.
  5. Finish with hairspray if desired.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to achieving the slicked back undercut:

Step 1: Grow out your hair

Before you can achieve the slicked back look, you need to have enough hair on top to style. Let your hair grow out to the desired length. This can take a few weeks to a few months depending on how fast your hair grows.

Step 2: Visit a barber or stylist

Visit a professional barber or stylist to get the undercut. They can help you determine the best length for the sides and back based on your face shape and hair type. They can also blend the hair on top with the shorter sides and back for a seamless look.

Step 3: Wash and dry your hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and towel dry it. You want your hair to be damp, not wet, when you apply the styling product.

Step 4: Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Start with a dime-sized amount and add more as needed. Work the product through your hair with your fingers or a comb.

Step 5: Slick back your hair

Use a comb to slick back your hair. Start at the front and work your way back, smoothing the hair as you go. You can create a part if desired.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

If you want your hair to stay in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold the style in place and prevent flyaways.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back undercut:

  • Choose a styling product that works best for your hair type. Pomade works well for thick, coarse hair, while gel is better for fine hair.
  • Experiment with different parting options. You can create a side part or a center part depending on your personal style.
  • If you have a receding hairline, avoid slicking your hair straight back. Instead, try a side part or a more tousled look.
  • Consider getting a trim every few weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the slicked back undercut:

What face shape does this hairstyle work best on?

The slicked back undercut works well on most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on those with square or oval faces. It helps to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

What if I don't have enough hair on top to slick back?

If your hair is too short to slick back, consider a different hairstyle. You can try a classic crew cut, a textured crop, or a buzz cut instead.

Can I wear this hairstyle to work?

Yes, the slicked back undercut is a professional and polished look that is appropriate for most work environments. Just make sure to keep it neat and tidy.

Hair Care

To keep your slicked back undercut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect slicked back undercut and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
