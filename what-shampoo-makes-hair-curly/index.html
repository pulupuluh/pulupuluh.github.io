What Shampoo Makes Hair Curly A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jun 08, 2023 · 9 min read

Curly hair is a blessing, and if you have naturally straight hair, you might be wondering how to get those bouncy curls. Fortunately, there are shampoos that can help you achieve curly locks. In this blog post, we will discuss what shampoo makes hair curly and provide you with tips on how to take care of your curls.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Hair Curly with Shampoo
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine for Curly Hair

Description

Shampoos that make hair curly work by adding moisture to your hair and defining your natural curls. They contain ingredients such as glycerin, shea butter, and coconut oil, which are known for their ability to hydrate and nourish hair. These shampoos also have a low pH level, which helps to keep the hair cuticles closed, preventing frizz and dryness.

How to Make Hair Curly with Shampoo

The first step in making your hair curly with shampoo is to choose the right shampoo. Look for shampoos that are specifically designed for curly hair, as these contain ingredients that help enhance and define curls. Some of the best shampoos for curly hair are:

  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Shampoo
  • DevaCurl No-Poo Original Zero Lather Conditioning Cleanser
  • Kinky-Curly Come Clean Natural Moisturizing Shampoo

Once you have found the right shampoo, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  2. Apply a quarter-sized amount of shampoo to your hair and scalp.
  3. Massage the shampoo into your scalp using your fingertips. Be gentle and avoid using your nails, as this can damage your scalp.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  5. Apply a conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes before rinsing it out.
  6. Gently squeeze excess water out of your hair with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.
  7. Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  8. Scrunch your hair gently to encourage curl formation.
  9. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Avoid using a brush or comb, as this can cause frizz.
  10. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to separate your curls and add volume.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Curly hair requires extra care and attention to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your curls:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, wash your hair every other day or every few days.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shampoos that make hair curly:

Can any shampoo make hair curly?

No, not all shampoos can make hair curly. Look for shampoos that are specifically designed for curly hair, as these contain ingredients that help enhance and define curls.

Can using a shampoo for curly hair make my hair frizzy?

No, using a shampoo for curly hair should not make your hair frizzy. Curly hair shampoos contain ingredients that help keep the hair cuticles closed, preventing frizz and dryness.

Can using a shampoo for curly hair change my hair texture?

No, using a shampoo for curly hair should not change your hair texture. It can, however, enhance and define your natural curls.

Hair Care Routine for Curly Hair

Here is a sample hair care routine for curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo for curly hair every other day or every few days.
  • Apply a conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes before rinsing it out.
  • Gently squeeze excess water out of your hair with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  • Scrunch your hair gently to encourage curl formation.
  • Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Avoid using a brush or comb, as this can cause frizz.
  • Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to separate your curls and add volume.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.

Now that you know what shampoo makes hair curly, you can achieve those bouncy curls you have always wanted. Remember to choose the right shampoo, follow the steps, and take care of your curls to keep them looking their best.
