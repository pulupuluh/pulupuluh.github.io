Length Hairstyles For Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Apr 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape? Look no further! In this article, we’ll be discussing length hairstyles that are perfect for round faces. We’ll be covering everything from how to determine your face shape to the best hairstyles for your face shape. So, let’s get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your round face is to determine your face shape. Here’s how:

  • Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  • Look at the shape of your face.
  • If your face is widest at the cheeks and you have a rounded chin, you have a round face shape.

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, let’s move on to the best length hairstyles for round faces.

Best Length Hairstyles for Round Faces

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for round faces because they add dimension and movement to your hair. By adding layers, you can create the illusion of a longer face shape. Long layers also help to soften your features and create a more feminine look.

2. Shoulder-Length Hair

Shoulder-length hair is another great length for round faces. This length helps to elongate your face shape and create a more balanced look. You can add layers or bangs to create even more dimension and movement.

3. Bob Haircuts

Bob haircuts are perfect for round faces because they help to create a more angular look. A classic bob that hits just below the chin is a great option, but you can also try a longer bob or a layered bob.

Tips for Styling Length Hairstyles for Round Faces

Here are a few tips to help you style your length hairstyles for round faces:

  • Avoid blunt bangs. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or no bangs at all.
  • Add volume to the crown of your head to create a more elongated look.
  • Avoid straight, flat hair. Instead, add waves or curls to create more dimension.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a high ponytail if you have a round face. Just make sure to add volume to the crown of your head to create a more elongated look.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part if you have a round face. Just make sure to add volume to the crown of your head to create a more elongated look.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a round face?

Short haircuts can be tricky for round faces, but they’re not completely off-limits. A pixie cut or a short bob can work if it’s styled correctly. Just make sure to add volume to the crown of your head to create a more elongated look.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s right for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyles, you’re sure to find the perfect length hairstyle for your round face. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful!
