Best Haircut To Elongate Round Face

Written by Christine May 07, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about the roundness of your face? One simple solution to elongate your face is by getting the right haircut. In this article, we’ll discuss the best haircut to elongate a round face and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

People with round faces often want to elongate their face shape. The best way to achieve this is by choosing the right haircut. A good haircut can create an illusion of a longer face and make the face look less round. The right haircut can add angles and volume to the hair, which can create an elongated face shape.

How to

The best way to elongate a round face is by choosing the right haircut. Here are some tips to help you decide:

  • Choose a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head.
  • Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Choose a haircut that has layers to add movement and texture to your hair.
  • Opt for a side-swept bang to create an asymmetrical look.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as they can make your face look wider.

Step by Step

Here’s how to get the perfect haircut to elongate your round face:

  1. Start by deciding on the length of your hair. Hair that falls below your shoulders can elongate your face.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head such as a layered bob, choppy pixie or beachy waves.
  3. Ask your stylist for a side-swept bang to create an asymmetrical look.
  4. Choose a hair color that adds depth and dimension to your hair such as highlights or lowlights.
  5. Finish your look with a styling product to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help elongate your round face:

  • Avoid center parts as they can make your face look wider.
  • Try a messy bun or high ponytail to add height to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or sleek as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the top of your head.
  • Consider getting a haircut with layers to add movement and texture to your hair.

FAQ

What haircut is best for a round face?

A layered haircut with volume at the top of the head is best for a round face.

What hairstyles should I avoid with a round face?

Avoid hairstyles with volume at the sides of your face, blunt cuts that end at your chin, and center parts.

Can hair color elongate a round face?

Yes, hair color can add depth and dimension to your hair, which can create an elongated face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain your new haircut, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out. Use a heat protectant before using hot tools to prevent heat damage. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. And finally, use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

In conclusion, the right haircut can elongate a round face and create a more angular look. By choosing a haircut with volume at the top of your head, layers, and an asymmetrical look, you can create the illusion of a longer face shape. Follow these tips and take care of your hair to maintain your new look.
