How Much Is It To Change Your Face Shape

Written by Eveline Aug 25, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you unhappy with the shape of your face? Do you want to change it but don’t know how much it will cost you? In this blog post, we’ll discuss how much it is to change your face shape, including related keywords that will help you find the information you need on search engines. This article will provide you with a description, how-to guide, step-by-step process, tips, FAQ, and hair care advice.

Description

Changing your face shape is a cosmetic procedure that involves altering the bone structure of your face. It can be done through various methods, such as surgery or non-surgical treatments like fillers and Botox. The cost of changing your face shape depends on the method you choose, the extent of the procedure, and the expertise of the professional performing the treatment.

How to Change Your Face Shape

There are several ways to change your face shape. Below are some of the most popular methods:

Surgical procedures

Surgical procedures involve altering the bone structure of your face. This is done by cutting and repositioning the bones. The most common surgical procedures include:

  • Rhinoplasty (nose job)
  • Chin augmentation
  • Cheek augmentation
  • Jawline surgery

Non-surgical treatments

Non-surgical treatments involve using injectables like fillers and Botox to enhance or contour the face. These treatments are less invasive than surgery and have a shorter recovery time. The most common non-surgical treatments include:

  • Dermal fillers
  • Botox
  • Thread lifts

Step-by-Step Process

The step-by-step process of changing your face shape depends on the method you choose. Below are the general steps for surgical and non-surgical treatments:

Surgical procedures

  1. Consult with a plastic surgeon to determine the best procedure for your needs.
  2. Undergo a pre-operative evaluation, which includes medical history review, physical examination, and blood tests.
  3. Receive anesthesia during the surgery.
  4. Undergo the surgical procedure, which involves cutting and repositioning the bones.
  5. Recover from the surgery, which usually takes several weeks.

Non-surgical treatments

  1. Consult with a dermatologist or plastic surgeon to determine the best treatment for your needs.
  2. Undergo a pre-treatment evaluation, which includes medical history review and physical examination.
  3. Receive the treatment, which involves injecting fillers or Botox into specific areas of the face.
  4. Recover from the treatment, which usually takes a few days.

Tips

Before deciding to change your face shape, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Research the procedure or treatment thoroughly.
  • Choose a reputable professional with experience in the specific method you choose.
  • Discuss your expectations and concerns with the professional before the procedure or treatment.
  • Follow the post-operative or post-treatment instructions carefully to ensure proper healing.

FAQ

How much is it to change your face shape?

The cost of changing your face shape depends on the method you choose, the extent of the procedure, and the expertise of the professional performing the treatment. Surgical procedures can cost anywhere from $3,000 to $15,000, while non-surgical treatments can cost anywhere from $500 to $5,000.

Is changing your face shape safe?

Changing your face shape can be safe if done by a qualified professional. However, all cosmetic procedures come with some risks, so it’s important to discuss the risks and benefits with your professional before undergoing any treatment.

How long does it take to recover from changing your face shape?

The recovery time depends on the method you choose. Surgical procedures can take several weeks to recover from, while non-surgical treatments usually only take a few days.

Hair Care

After changing your face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair to complement your new look. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your new face shape.
  • Use products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

Conclusion

Changing your face shape can be a life-changing decision, but it’s important to research the procedure or treatment thoroughly before making a decision. The cost of changing your face shape depends on the method you choose, the extent of the procedure, and the expertise of the professional performing the treatment. Follow the tips and advice in this article to ensure a successful outcome.
