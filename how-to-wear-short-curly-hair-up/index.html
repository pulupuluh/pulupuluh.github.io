How To Wear Short Curly Hair Up A Step By Step Guide

If you have short curly hair, you might think that wearing it up is impossible. But fear not, there are plenty of ways to style your hair into a chic updo. In this article, we'll take you through a step-by-step guide on how to wear short curly hair up, along with some tips and frequently asked questions.

How to Wear Short Curly Hair Up

When it comes to wearing short curly hair up, there are a few different styles to choose from. Some popular options include:

  • Buns
  • Ponytails
  • Braids
  • Half-up styles

Ultimately, the style you choose will depend on your personal preference and the occasion you're dressing for. Below, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to create a few different updo styles for short curly hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Bun

To create a bun with short curly hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start by brushing your hair back and gathering it into a ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins, tucking the ends of your hair under the bun.
  4. Use hairspray to secure the style in place.

You can also leave some curls out around your face for a more relaxed look.

Ponytail

To create a ponytail with short curly hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start by brushing your hair back and gathering it into a ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Use bobby pins to pin any loose curls in place.
  4. Use hairspray to secure the style in place.

You can also create a messy ponytail by leaving some curls out around your face and pulling a few strands loose from the ponytail.

Braids

To create a braid with short curly hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start by brushing your hair back and gathering it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Divide the ponytail into three sections.
  3. Create a traditional braid by crossing the sections over each other.
  4. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  5. Use bobby pins to pin any loose curls in place.
  6. Use hairspray to secure the style in place.

You can also create a fishtail braid for a more intricate look.

Half-Up Styles

To create a half-up style with short curly hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start by brushing the top half of your hair back and gathering it into a ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Use bobby pins to pin any loose curls in place.
  4. Use hairspray to secure the style in place.

You can also create a half-up style by twisting the top half of your hair back and securing it with bobby pins.

Tips for Wearing Short Curly Hair Up

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing short curly hair up:

  • Use bobby pins to pin any loose curls in place.
  • Use hairspray to secure the style in place.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to leave some curls out for a more natural look.
  • Consider using hair accessories like headbands, clips, or scarves to add some extra flair to your updo.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear my short curly hair up for a formal occasion?

Absolutely! With the right styling techniques and accessories, you can create a chic updo that's perfect for any formal event.

How do I keep my short curly hair from getting frizzy when I wear it up?

Use a frizz-fighting product before styling your hair, and make sure to use bobby pins and hairspray to keep your style in place.

What if my short curly hair is too short to wear up?

Consider adding extensions or trying out a half-up style that works with your hair length.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

When it comes to caring for short curly hair, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are formulated to hydrate and define curls.

You may also want to consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to help keep your curls looking their best. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free from split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create a variety of stylish updos for short curly hair. Whether you're dressing up for a formal occasion or just want to switch up your everyday look, these styles are sure to impress.
