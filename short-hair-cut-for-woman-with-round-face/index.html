Short Hair Cut For Women With Round Face The Ultimate Guide

If you have a round face and are considering a short hair cut, then you're in the right place. In this article, we'll discuss the best short hair cut for women with a round face, how to achieve it, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. So, let's get started!

Best Short Hair Cut for Women with Round Face

The best short hair cut for women with a round face is a layered bob. A layered bob helps to elongate the face and adds volume to the crown. The layers also help to create movement and texture, which is perfect for women with round faces. You can opt for a side-swept fringe to add more dimension to your face.

Another option is a pixie cut. However, it is essential to add volume to the crown to balance out the roundness of the face. You can also add texture to the top of the hair to create more movement.

How to Achieve the Hair Cut

The first step to achieving the perfect short hair cut is to find a stylist who has experience working with women who have round faces. They will be able to recommend the best hair cut and style for your face shape and hair type.

Once you have found a stylist, you should bring in pictures of haircuts that you like. This will help the stylist get a better understanding of your style and preferences.

Step by Step Instructions

Here's a step by step guide to achieving the perfect short hair cut for women with round faces:

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into layers.
  3. Cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Add texture to the top of your hair to create movement.
  5. Add volume to the crown to balance out the roundness of your face.
  6. Style your hair using a texturizing product.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hair cut:

  • Use a texturizing product to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to maintain the shape of your hair cut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will prevent breakage.

FAQs

1. Will a short hair cut make my face look rounder?

No, a short hair cut can actually help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

2. Can I still have bangs with a short hair cut?

Yes, you can still have bangs with a short hair cut. However, it's essential to make sure that the bangs are not too thick, as this can make your face look rounder.

3. How often do I need to get a trim?

You should get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and to maintain the shape of your hair cut.

Hair Care Advice

Here's some hair care advice for women with short hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

In conclusion, a short hair cut can be a great option for women with round faces. A layered bob or a pixie cut with added volume and texture can help elongate the face and create movement. With the right stylist, maintenance, and hair care advice, you'll be able to rock a fabulous short hair cut.
