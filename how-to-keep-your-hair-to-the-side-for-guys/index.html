How To Keep Your Hair To The Side For Guys A Complete Guide

In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about keeping your hair to the side for guys. From the basics of hair care to the step-by-step process of styling your hair, we've got you covered. So, let's dive in!

Description

The side-swept hairstyle has been a popular choice for guys for many years. It's a versatile look that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion. However, keeping your hair to the side requires some effort and the right techniques. In this article, we'll be sharing the best tips and tricks to help you achieve this classic style.

How to Keep Your Hair to the Side for Guys

The key to keeping your hair to the side is to create volume and texture. You can achieve this by using the right products, blow-drying your hair in the right direction, and using the right styling techniques. Here's what you need to do:

1. Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to achieving a side-swept style is to choose the right haircut. Your hair should be longer on the top and shorter on the sides. This will create the volume and texture needed to keep your hair to the side. Speak to your barber or hairstylist and ask for a cut that will suit your face shape and hair type.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products is essential for keeping your hair to the side. Start by applying a volumizing mousse or foam to your damp hair. This will add body and texture to your hair. Then, use a blow dryer to dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.

Once your hair is dry, apply a matte pomade or wax to your hair. This will help you achieve a natural look and hold your hair in place. Avoid using products that are too heavy or greasy, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair in the Right Direction

When blow-drying your hair, make sure you're drying it in the right direction. Use a round brush to lift your hair up and away from your face, and blow-dry it in the direction you want it to go. This will create volume and keep your hair in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect side-swept hairstyle:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or foam to your damp hair and blow-dry it in the direction you want it to go.
  3. Apply a matte pomade or wax to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to sweep your hair to the side.
  5. Blow-dry your hair again to set the style.
  6. Apply a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you keep your hair to the side:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Blow-dry your hair on a cool setting to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a hairspray with a flexible hold to avoid making your hair look stiff or crunchy.

FAQs

Q. How often should I wash my hair?

A. It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day or a few times a week.

Q. Can I keep my hair to the side if I have short hair?

A. Yes, you can still achieve a side-swept style even if you have short hair. Speak to your barber or hairstylist and ask for a cut that will allow you to sweep your hair to the side.

Q. How long does it take to style my hair?

A. It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Generally, it can take anywhere from 5-15 minutes to style your hair.

Hair Care for Side-Swept Style

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily keep your hair to the side and achieve a classic, stylish look. Remember to choose the right products, blow-dry your hair in the right direction, and use the right techniques to keep your hair looking its best.
