Best Shape Frame For Oval Face A Complete Guide

Written by Robby May 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

In this article, we will discuss the best shape frame for oval face. If you have an oval face, you're in luck! Oval faces are versatile and can pull off almost any frame shape. However, some frames are more flattering than others. Read on to find out which frame shape is best for your oval face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Find the Best Shape Frame for Oval Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Best Frame for Oval Face
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Oval Faces

Description

An oval face has balanced proportions, with a slightly curved jawline and a forehead that's broader than the chin. This face shape is considered ideal because it can pull off many different styles. However, some frame shapes will enhance your features better than others.

How to Find the Best Shape Frame for Oval Face

The best way to find the right frame shape for your oval face is to try on different styles and see which ones suit you best. However, there are a few general guidelines that can help you narrow down your options.

The ideal frame should be wider than the broadest part of your face, but not too wide that it overwhelms your features. The frame should also be in proportion to the size of your face. If you have a small face, avoid oversized frames that can make your face look smaller. If you have a larger face, avoid small frames that can make your face look larger.

Consider the shape of your features when choosing a frame. If you have high cheekbones, choose a frame that sits high on the bridge of your nose. If you have a narrow forehead, choose a frame with a wider top to balance out your features.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to finding the best frame shape for your oval face:

  1. Measure the width of your face at the widest point.
  2. Choose a frame that is slightly wider than the widest point of your face.
  3. Consider the shape of your features and choose a frame that enhances them.
  4. Try on different styles of frames to see which ones suit you best.
  5. Take into account the color of the frame and how it complements your skin tone and hair color.

Tips for Choosing the Best Frame for Oval Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best frame for your oval face:

  • Avoid frames that are too narrow or too wide for your face.
  • Choose a frame that enhances your best features.
  • Consider the color of the frame and how it complements your skin tone and hair color.
  • Try on different styles of frames to see which ones suit you best.
  • Take into account the occasion and the type of outfit you will be wearing with the frame.

Frequently Asked Questions

What frame shape is best for an oval face?

An oval face can pull off almost any frame shape, but rectangular, square, and cat-eye frames are particularly flattering.

Can oval faces wear oversized frames?

Yes, but make sure the frame is in proportion to the size of your face. If you have a small face, avoid oversized frames that can make your face look smaller. If you have a larger face, avoid small frames that can make your face look larger.

What color frame is best for an oval face?

The best color for a frame depends on your skin tone and hair color. Generally, neutral colors like black, brown, and tortoise shell are safe choices.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Faces

Here are some hair care tips for oval faces:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face look longer.
  • Consider a side-swept bang to balance out your features.
  • Avoid center parts, as they can elongate your face.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add volume and dimension.

Now that you know how to find the best shape frame for oval face, it's time to go out and try on some frames! Remember to take into account the shape of your features, the proportion of the frame to your face, and the color of the frame. With these tips in mind, you're sure to find the perfect frame for your oval face.
