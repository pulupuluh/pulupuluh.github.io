How To Make Your Hair More Thick For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having thin, lifeless hair? Do you want to add more volume and thickness to your mane? Look no further, because we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be discussing the best ways to make your hair more thick for guys. From how to properly care for your hair to natural remedies to boost thickness, we’ve got all the tips and tricks you need to know. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How To Make Your Hair More Thick
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

Hair Care

The first step to making your hair more thick is to properly care for it. This means washing your hair regularly to remove excess oil and dirt, but not too often that it strips your hair of its natural oils. It’s also important to use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Additionally, avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons too frequently as they can damage your hair and make it thinner.

Another important factor in hair care is nutrition. Eating a balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals can help promote hair growth and thickness. Foods like eggs, spinach, nuts, and avocados are all great options for healthy hair.

How To Make Your Hair More Thick

Now that you know the basics of hair care, let’s dive into some ways to make your hair more thick:

1. Use volumizing products

There are many shampoos, conditioners, and styling products specifically designed to add volume and thickness to hair. Look for products with ingredients like biotin, keratin, and wheat protein for maximum effectiveness.

2. Change your haircut

A shorter haircut can make your hair look thicker by removing weight and allowing your hair to stand up more. Talk to your barber or stylist about the best haircut for your hair type and face shape.

3. Try a hair thickening treatment

There are many professional hair thickening treatments available at salons, such as keratin treatments and hair extensions. These treatments can add thickness and volume to your hair in a more permanent way.

4. Use natural remedies

There are many natural remedies that can help promote hair growth and thickness. Some popular options include coconut oil, castor oil, and aloe vera. These can be applied directly to your hair and scalp for maximum benefits.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to making your hair more thick:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.

2. Use volumizing products

Apply a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and use a volumizing mousse or spray before styling.

3. Change your haircut

Talk to your barber or stylist about a shorter haircut that can add volume and thickness.

4. Try a hair thickening treatment

Consider a professional hair thickening treatment at a salon for a more permanent solution.

5. Use natural remedies

Apply natural remedies like coconut oil or aloe vera to your hair and scalp for added thickness and growth.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to make your hair more thick:

  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too frequently
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and dyes on your hair
  • Brush your hair gently to prevent breakage
  • Avoid wearing tight hats or headbands that can damage your hair

FAQs

Q: Can genetics affect hair thickness?

A: Yes, genetics can play a role in hair thickness. Some people are naturally predisposed to having thin hair.

Q: Are there any supplements that can help promote hair growth?

A: Yes, there are many supplements on the market that claim to promote hair growth and thickness. However, it’s important to talk to your doctor before taking any new supplements.

Q: How long does it take to see results from hair thickening treatments?

A: Results can vary depending on the type of treatment, but it can take several weeks to see a noticeable difference in hair thickness.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your hair more thick and voluminous in no time. Remember to take care of your hair and be patient – healthy, thick hair takes time and effort to achieve.
