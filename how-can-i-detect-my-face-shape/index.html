How Can I Detect My Face Shape Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Sep 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Knowing your face shape can help you choose the most flattering hairstyle, glasses, and makeup for your features. However, not everyone knows how to identify their face shape. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks to help you determine your face shape and make the most of your unique beauty.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Detect Your Face Shape
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Identifying Your Face Shape
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

How to Detect Your Face Shape

There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangle. To determine your face shape, you need to measure the length and width of your face and compare the proportions. You can also look at the angles of your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead to help you identify your shape.

Measurements

Measure your face with a flexible tape measure or ruler. Write down the measurements of your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and face length. Then, compare the numbers to see which category your face falls into.

Angles

Look at your face in the mirror and examine the shape of your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead. Oval faces have soft angles and a slightly rounded chin. Round faces have a circular shape and full cheeks. Square faces have a strong jawline and forehead with similar width. Heart-shaped faces have a narrow chin and wide forehead. Diamond faces have high cheekbones and a pointed chin. Triangle faces have a wide jawline and narrow forehead.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to identify your face shape:

  1. Measure your forehead from the widest point to the hairline.
  2. Measure your cheekbones from the widest point across the face.
  3. Measure your jawline from the tip of the chin to the earlobe.
  4. Measure your face length from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.
  5. Compare the measurements to see which category your face falls into.
  6. Examine the angles of your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead to confirm your shape.

Tips for Identifying Your Face Shape

Here are some additional tips to help you determine your face shape:

  • Use a piece of paper or a photo editing app to draw the outline of your face and compare it to the six shapes.
  • Ask a friend or a hairstylist for their opinion.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, glasses, and makeup to see which ones flatter your features.
  • Remember that face shapes are not set in stone and can change over time due to weight loss or gain, aging, and other factors.

FAQ

What if my face doesn't fit into any of the six shapes?

Some people have a combination of two or more face shapes, or a unique shape that doesn't fit into any category. In this case, focus on the features you want to enhance or downplay, such as your cheekbones or chin, and choose hairstyles and makeup accordingly.

Can face shape affect my skin care routine?

Yes, certain face shapes may be more prone to breakouts, wrinkles, or dryness due to the distribution of oil and moisture on the skin. Consult a dermatologist or a skin care expert for personalized advice.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Once you have identified your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. Here are some suggestions:

  • Oval: any length or style, but avoid covering your forehead with bangs.
  • Round: long layers, side-swept bangs, and volume on top.
  • Square: soft waves, off-center part, and layered bob.
  • Heart: chin-length bob, side part, and layers around the face.
  • Diamond: short pixie cut, textured layers, and side-swept fringe.
  • Triangle: curly updo, half-up half-down style, and layers around the crown.

Remember that these are just guidelines, and you should choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Embrace your unique face shape and experiment with different looks to find your signature style.
