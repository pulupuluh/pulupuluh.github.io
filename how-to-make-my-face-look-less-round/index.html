How To Make Your Face Look Less Round Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Mar 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have a round face and want to make it look slimmer? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with this issue, but there are simple ways to make your face look less round. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to achieve this look.

What Causes a Round Face?

Before we jump into the tips, let's first understand what causes a round face. A round face is usually caused by genetics, but it can also be due to weight gain, water retention, or hormonal changes.

How to Make Your Face Look Less Round: Tips and Tricks

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. By using a darker shade of foundation or contour powder, you can create shadows that make your face appear more angular.

2. Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also make a big difference in how your face looks. A layered haircut with long, side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Avoid blunt cuts or bangs that sit straight across your forehead, as these can make your face look rounder.

3. Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone your facial muscles and give your face a more defined look. Some exercises to try include cheek lifts, jawline exercises, and neck stretches.

4. Drink Water

Water retention can cause your face to look puffy and round. By staying hydrated and drinking plenty of water, you can help flush out excess water and reduce facial swelling.

5. Eat a Balanced Diet

A balanced diet can help you maintain a healthy weight and reduce facial fat. Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks, and instead focus on eating whole foods like fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

6. Avoid Salt

Salt can cause water retention, which can make your face look puffy and round. Try to limit your salt intake and avoid processed foods that are high in sodium.

7. Wear the Right Accessories

Accessories can also help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Wear earrings that dangle and draw attention away from your face, and choose necklaces that are long and slimming.

8. Get Enough Sleep

Lack of sleep can cause your face to look tired and puffy. Make sure to get at least 7-8 hours of sleep each night to help reduce facial swelling and promote overall health.

9. Reduce Stress

Stress can cause your body to produce cortisol, a hormone that can lead to weight gain and water retention. Practice stress-reducing activities like yoga, meditation, or deep breathing to help reduce cortisol levels and promote overall health.

10. Use Skincare Products

Skincare products can help reduce facial puffiness and promote a more defined look. Look for products that contain caffeine, which can help tighten and firm the skin, or hyaluronic acid, which can help hydrate and plump the skin.

FAQ

Q: Can losing weight help reduce a round face?

A: Yes, losing weight can help reduce facial fat and create a more defined look. However, it's important to maintain a healthy weight and avoid crash diets, which can be harmful to your overall health.

Q: Can facial massage help reduce a round face?

A: Yes, facial massage can help tone your facial muscles and reduce puffiness. Use gentle, circular motions when massaging your face, and be sure to use a moisturizer or facial oil to prevent friction.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to promote a healthy, defined look. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type, and avoid using heat tools like flat irons or curling irons too often. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools, and try air-drying your hair whenever possible.

By following these tips and tricks, you can help make your face look less round and achieve a more defined look. Remember, it's important to maintain a healthy lifestyle and avoid crash diets or harmful beauty practices.
