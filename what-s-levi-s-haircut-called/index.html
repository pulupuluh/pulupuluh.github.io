What s Levi s Haircut Called

Written by Petter Apr 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Levi's haircut has become a popular style among men and women alike. This iconic haircut has been sported by many celebrities, including Levi Ackerman from the anime series Attack on Titan. In this blog post, we will discuss what Levi's haircut is called and how you can achieve this look.

Description

Levi's haircut is a short, layered cut with a tapered back and sides. The top of the hair is left longer, with choppy layers that add texture and movement to the style. The hair is styled with a messy, tousled look, giving it a nonchalant, effortless vibe.

How to Achieve Levi's Haircut

To achieve Levi's haircut, you will need to visit a skilled hairstylist. The stylist will first cut the back and sides of your hair short and tapered, leaving the top of the hair longer. The top of the hair is then layered, with choppy, textured cuts that add volume and movement to the style.

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair with a texturizing product, such as a pomade or wax. Apply the product to your hair and tousle it with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve Levi's haircut:

Step 1:

Visit a skilled hairstylist and ask for a short, layered cut with a tapered back and sides.

Step 2:

Ask the stylist to leave the top of your hair longer and add choppy, textured layers to create volume and movement.

Step 3:

Once the haircut is complete, wash your hair and towel-dry it.

Step 4:

Apply a texturizing product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair.

Step 5:

Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, tousled look.

Tips for Maintaining Levi's Haircut

To maintain Levi's haircut, it is important to keep your hair clean and well-groomed. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a texturizing product to style your hair and keep it looking messy and tousled.

Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of the cut and prevent it from looking overgrown. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

FAQ

Q: Is Levi's haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Levi's haircut is suitable for most face shapes, but it may not be the best option for those with round faces. If you have a round face, ask your hairstylist for a variation of Levi's haircut that is more flattering for your face shape.

Q: Can women sport Levi's haircut?

A: Yes, women can sport Levi's haircut. This haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit any gender and hair type.

Q: How do I know if Levi's haircut is right for me?

A: If you are looking for a short, low-maintenance haircut with a messy, tousled look, then Levi's haircut may be right for you. Consult with your hairstylist to determine if this style is suitable for your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for achieving and maintaining Levi's haircut. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, on your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you must use hot tools.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

By following these tips, you can achieve and maintain Levi's haircut while keeping your hair healthy and strong.
