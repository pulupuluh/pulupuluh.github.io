Best Hair Part for Long Oval Face: How to Choose and Style

Description

Long oval faces are characterized by a narrow chin and forehead, with a lengthened face shape. Choosing the right hair part can significantly enhance your facial features and balance your face shape. The best hair part for long oval face is the center part or the deep side part. These parts create an illusion of width on the forehead and cheekbones, making your face look more proportional.

How to Choose the Best Hair Part for Long Oval Face

Choosing the best hair part for long oval face can be tricky, but there are a few guidelines to follow:

  • Identify your face shape by measuring the length of your face from forehead to chin and the width of your forehead and cheekbones.
  • Aim to create balance by creating an illusion of width on your forehead and cheekbones.
  • Consider your hair texture and volume. If you have thick hair, a center part will work best. If you have fine hair, a deep side part will add volume.
  • Experiment with different parts to see which one suits you best.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair with the Best Part for Long Oval Face

Here are the steps to style your hair with the best part for long oval face:

  • Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a volumizing conditioner if you have fine hair.
  • Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. For a deep side part, blow-dry your hair to the opposite side of the part to create volume.
  • Use a flat iron or a curling wand to add curls or waves to your hair. For a center part, curl your hair away from your face. For a deep side part, curl your hair towards your face.
  • Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair with the Best Part for Long Oval Face

To keep your hair looking great with the best part for long oval face, follow these tips:

  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

FAQs about the Best Hair Part for Long Oval Face

1. Can I part my hair in the middle if I have a long oval face?

Yes, the center part is one of the best hair parts for long oval face. It creates an illusion of width on the forehead and cheekbones, making your face look more balanced.

2. What if I have thick hair?

If you have thick hair, a center part will work best. It will create a symmetrical look and balance out the width of your hair.

3. What if I have fine hair?

If you have fine hair, a deep side part will add volume and create an illusion of thickness. It will also create a flattering asymmetrical look.

Hair Care for Long Oval Face with the Best Part

Here are some hair care tips for long oval face with the best part:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet to prevent breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Choosing the best hair part for long oval face is crucial to enhance your facial features and balance your face shape. Follow the guidelines and experiment with different parts to find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair with regular trimming and conditioning to keep it healthy and shiny.
