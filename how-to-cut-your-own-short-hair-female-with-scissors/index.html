How To Cut Your Own Short Hair Female With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to save some money and give yourself a fresh new look, cutting your own short hair with scissors can be a great option. It may seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish haircut right at home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting your own short hair with scissors, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice to help you get the best results.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be a liberating experience, but it can also be nerve-wracking. With the right preparation and technique, however, you can achieve a stylish and professional-looking haircut right at home. In this guide, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut your own short hair with scissors, as well as tips on choosing the right scissors and maintaining your hair's health.

How to Cut Your Own Short Hair Female with Scissors

Step 1: Gather your tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. It can also be helpful to have a spray bottle filled with water to wet your hair as you go.

Step 2: Start with clean, dry hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair so you can see your natural texture and avoid any surprises. Wash your hair and let it air dry completely before beginning to cut.

Step 3: Section your hair

Using clips, section your hair into manageable sections. Start by creating a part down the middle of your head, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Clip the left side of your hair out of the way, then clip the right side into sections.

Step 4: Start cutting

Begin by cutting the hair at the back of your head. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut the desired length. Work your way from the back of your head to the front, making sure to cut each section to the same length.

Step 5: Trim the sides

To trim the sides, comb your hair straight down and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair to the desired length, making sure to keep the scissors vertical for a clean cut. Work your way from the front to the back, making sure to cut each section to the same length.

Step 6: Check for balance

After you've finished cutting, check for balance. Part your hair down the middle and make sure both sides are even. If you notice any unevenness, simply trim the longer side to match the shorter side.

Step 7: Clean up any strays

Use your scissors to clean up any stray hairs around your ears and neckline. Be careful not to cut too much, as this can create an uneven look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your own short hair with scissors:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Don't cut too much at once, as this can result in an uneven look.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles.
  • Take breaks if you need to, as cutting your own hair can be tiring.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own short hair with scissors:

Can I cut my own hair with regular scissors?

While it's possible to cut your own hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Hair scissors are specifically designed to cut hair cleanly and evenly, while regular scissors can cause split ends and unevenness.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. It's always better to err on the side of caution and cut less hair than you think you need. If you do make a mistake, you can always visit a professional stylist to fix it.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy after cutting it with scissors, be sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.

Now that you know how to cut your own short hair with scissors, you can save money and achieve a stylish new look right at home. With the right tools and techniques, cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. Just remember to take your time, start small, and be patient with yourself as you perfect your technique.
