Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair Up A Perfect Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 17, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Welcome to our guide on easy hairstyles for long hair up! If you have long hair, then you are already blessed with a lot of styling options. However, sometimes it can be overwhelming to choose the right hairstyle for a particular occasion or mood. That's why we have put together this guide to help you rock some of the easiest and most stylish updos for long hair. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Easy hairstyles for long hair up are those that don't require too much time, effort or accessories. They are simple, yet chic, and can be worn for different occasions, be it formal, casual or festive. Some of the popular updos for long hair include messy buns, braids, ponytails, chignons, and twists. The key is to find a style that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

How to

The first step to achieving easy hairstyles for long hair up is to prepare your hair. This means washing, conditioning, and detangling it with a wide-tooth comb or brush. You can also apply some heat protectant, serum or oil to protect your hair from damage and add shine. Once your hair is ready, you can follow these general steps:

Step 1: Decide on the style

Choose a hairstyle that you like and that suits the occasion or mood. You can browse online or check out some magazines or blogs for inspiration. You can also experiment with different styles and variations until you find the one that works for you.

Step 2: Gather the tools and accessories

Depending on the hairstyle, you may need some tools and accessories such as bobby pins, hair ties, elastics, clips, combs, and hairspray. Make sure you have everything within reach and that they match your hair color and texture.

Step 3: Section and tease your hair

Divide your hair into sections according to the hairstyle you have chosen. For example, if you want to do a messy bun, you can gather your hair into a high ponytail and tease it with a comb to create volume and texture. You can also use a sock bun or donut bun to achieve a fuller look.

Step 4: Twist, braid or wrap your hair

Once you have teased your hair, you can twist, braid or wrap it into the desired shape. For example, you can create a fishtail braid or a French braid for a more intricate look. You can also twist your hair into a rope braid or a Dutch braid for a chic and effortless style. If you want a more formal look, you can wrap your hair into a chignon or a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Step 5: Secure and accessorize your hair

Once you have created the shape you want, you can secure it with bobby pins, elastics or hair ties. You can also add some accessories such as flowers, ribbons, or jewels to enhance the style and make it more festive. Finally, you can spray some hairspray or texturizing spray to fix the style and add shine.

Step by Step

Here are some of the easiest and most popular updos for long hair with step-by-step instructions:

Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and tease it with a comb to create volume.
  2. Wrap the hair around the base of the ponytail to form a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out some strands to create a messy look.
  4. Accessorize with a ribbon or a flower.

Braid Crown

  1. Part your hair in the middle and create two braids on each side.
  2. Wrap each braid over the top of your head to create a crown-like shape.
  3. Secure the braids with bobby pins and pull out some strands to create a relaxed look.
  4. Accessorize with some jewels or pearls.

Twisted Ponytail

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail and secure it with an elastic.
  2. Split the ponytail in half and create a gap above the elastic.
  3. Flip the ponytail over the gap and pull it through to create a twist.
  4. Repeat the process until you reach the end of the ponytail.
  5. Accessorize with a scarf or a headband.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve easy hairstyles for long hair up:

  • Start with clean and dry hair to avoid frizz and tangles.
  • Use a good quality brush, comb or hair tool for better results.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as scrunchies, headbands, or clips to add variety to your styles.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles or variations, even if they seem complicated at first.
  • Practice makes perfect, so keep practicing until you master the style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for long hair up:

Q: What is the easiest updo for long hair?

A: The easiest updo for long hair is probably a messy bun. It only takes a few minutes to create and can be done with minimal tools and accessories.

Q: How do I make my updo last longer?

A: To make your updo last longer, you can use some hairspray or texturizing spray to fix the style and prevent flyaways. You can also secure the style with bobby pins or elastics and avoid touching or pulling your hair too much.

Q: Can I wear updos for long hair to formal events?

A: Yes, you can wear updos for long hair to formal events such as weddings, proms, or galas. Just make sure that the style matches the dress code and the occasion, and that it complements your overall look.

Hair Care

Finally, to keep your long hair healthy and beautiful, you need to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling and harsh chemicals, and trimming your ends every few months. You can also use some natural remedies such as coconut oil, aloe vera or apple cider vinegar to nourish and strengthen your hair.

We hope you enjoyed our guide on easy hairstyles for long hair up and that you found some inspiration for your next look. Remember, the key is to have fun, be creative and embrace your natural beauty. Happy styling!
