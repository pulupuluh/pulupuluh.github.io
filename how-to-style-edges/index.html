How To Style Edges Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 13, 2023 ¬∑ 6 min read

Styling edges is an essential part of any hair care routine. Whether you have natural hair or relaxed hair, edges are the finishing touch to any hairstyle. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style edges in relaxed English language. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Edges
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Edges
  4. FAQ About Styling Edges
  5. Hair Care for Edges

How to Style Edges

Styling edges refers to smoothing down the hairline and creating a sleek and polished look. It's important to note that edges are delicate, and excessive pulling or tugging can cause breakage. Here are some tips on how to style edges:

  • Use a small amount of edge control or gel
  • Apply with a small brush or your fingertips
  • Smooth down the hairline in the desired shape
  • Let the product dry and set

Now that you know the basics of how to style edges, let's dive into a step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve a sleek and polished edge:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair
  2. Apply a small amount of edge control or gel to your fingertips or a small brush
  3. Section off the hairline into small sections
  4. Smooth the product onto each section, starting from the middle and working your way outwards
  5. Use a brush or your fingertips to smooth down the hairline in the desired shape
  6. Repeat on all sections until the desired shape and hold is achieved
  7. Let the product dry and set for a few minutes
  8. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to lock in the style

And there you have it, a simple and easy guide on how to style edges. But wait, there's more! Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your edges.

Tips for Styling Edges

Follow these tips to ensure healthy and flourishing edges:

  • Don't use excessive amounts of product, as it can cause build-up and breakage
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as it can dry out the hair and cause breakage
  • Use a soft-bristled brush or your fingertips to smooth down the hairline
  • Avoid using tight headbands or hairstyles that pull on the edges

Now that you have the basics of styling edges down, here are some frequently asked questions about styling edges.

FAQ About Styling Edges

Here are some of the most commonly asked questions about styling edges:

How often should I style my edges?

Edges are delicate, and excessive styling can cause breakage. It's recommended to only style your edges when necessary, such as for special occasions or to refresh your hairstyle.

What should I do if my edges are thinning?

If you notice thinning edges, it's important to take action immediately. Avoid tight hairstyles and use products that promote hair growth and thickness. Consult with a hair care professional for additional advice.

Can I use edge control on natural hair?

Yes, edge control can be used on natural hair. However, it's important to choose a product that's specifically formulated for natural hair to avoid excessive dryness or breakage.

Hair Care for Edges

Proper hair care is essential for healthy and flourishing edges. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can cause breakage and thinning
  • Use a satin scarf or bonnet at night to protect your edges while you sleep
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the edges
  • Use products that promote hair growth and thickness
  • Consult with a hair care professional for additional advice and treatment

And there you have it, a comprehensive guide on how to style edges and maintain healthy hair. Remember to take it easy on your edges and give them the love and care they deserve!
