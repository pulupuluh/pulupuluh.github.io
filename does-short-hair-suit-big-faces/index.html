Does Short Hair Suit Big Faces

Description

Many people believe that short hair is not suitable for big faces. However, this is a myth that has been debunked by fashion experts and hairstylists. Short hair can actually complement a big face and accentuate its features. The key is to choose the right haircut that flatters your facial shape and features.

How to

The first step in determining whether short hair suits your big face is to identify your facial shape. There are several facial shapes, including round, square, oval, heart, and diamond. Once you have identified your facial shape, you can choose a haircut that complements your features and enhances your overall look.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, a short, layered haircut with side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer jawline. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as they can make your face appear wider.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, a short, textured pixie cut with choppy layers can soften your features and add dimension to your hair. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that accentuate your jawline.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off almost any short haircut. Consider a sleek, asymmetrical bob or a layered pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, a short, layered bob with side-swept bangs can balance your features and soften your forehead. Avoid blunt, straight cuts that accentuate your chin.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, a short, layered cut with volume on top can balance your features and create the illusion of a wider forehead. Avoid slicked-back styles that emphasize your narrow forehead.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to determine whether short hair suits your big face:

  1. Identify your facial shape.
  2. Research short haircuts that complement your features.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you.
  4. Experiment with different styles and hair products to find what works best for you.
  5. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using quality hair care products.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when considering a short haircut for your big face:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your features and enhances your facial shape.
  • Experiment with different styles and hair products to find what works best for you.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using quality hair care products.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, and other hair accessories to add dimension and style.

FAQ

Q: Can short hair make my big face look even bigger?

A: No, this is a myth. Short hair can actually complement a big face and accentuate its features.

Q: What is the best short haircut for a big face?

A: The best short haircut depends on your facial shape and features. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you.

Q: How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different hair products, such as mousse, gel, and hairspray, to find what works best for you.
