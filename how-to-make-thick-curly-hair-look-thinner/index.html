How To Make Thick Curly Hair Look Thinner

Written by Daniel Jun 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know that it can be a blessing and a curse. While you may love the volume and texture that your curls provide, it can also be difficult to manage and style. One common issue that many people with thick curly hair face is that it can look too voluminous and overwhelming. But don't worry, there are several ways to make your hair look thinner without sacrificing your curls. Here's how:

Description

In this article, we will discuss how to make thick curly hair look thinner. We will cover various techniques and tips to help you achieve a more manageable and flattering hairstyle. Whether you have long or short hair, these tips will work for you.

How to

1. Get a Haircut

The easiest way to make thick curly hair look thinner is to get a haircut. A layered haircut can help to remove some of the bulk from your hair and give it a more manageable shape. Be sure to go to a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair to ensure that your curls are not compromised.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Look for products that are designed for curly hair, such as a curl defining cream or mousse. These products can help to enhance your curls while also keeping them under control.

3. Blow Dry with a Diffuser

If you normally air dry your hair, try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer instead. This can help to tame your curls and reduce the volume of your hair. Be sure to use a low heat setting and avoid pulling your hair too much while drying.

4. Try a Half-Up Hairstyle

If you want to keep your curls but still reduce the volume of your hair, try a half-up hairstyle. This can help to keep your curls under control while also giving you a more polished and put-together look.

5. Flat Iron Your Hair

If you're looking for a more dramatic change, try flat ironing your hair. This can help to smooth out your curls and make your hair look thinner. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using a flat iron too often, as it can damage your hair over time.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. Be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Defining Cream or Mousse

Apply a curl defining cream or mousse to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly.

Step 3: Blow Dry with a Diffuser

Using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, dry your hair on a low heat setting. Be sure to avoid pulling your hair too much while drying.

Step 4: Style as Desired

Once your hair is dry, style it as desired. You can try a half-up hairstyle or leave it down and add some hair accessories for a more polished look.

Tips

- Avoid using heavy or greasy hair products, as these can weigh down your curls and make your hair look even thicker.

- Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

- Be gentle with your hair and avoid using harsh styling tools or techniques.

FAQ

Q: Will cutting my hair make it look thinner?

A: Yes, a layered haircut can help to remove some of the bulk from your hair and make it look thinner.

Q: Can I still have curls if I make my hair look thinner?

A: Yes, you can still have curls even if you make your hair look thinner. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair, especially if you have thick curly hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and manageable:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.

- Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

- Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your thick curly hair look thinner and more manageable. Whether you want to keep your curls or try a new hairstyle, these tips will help you achieve your desired look.
