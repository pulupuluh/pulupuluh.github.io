Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your fat face and double chin? Look no further than shoulder length hairstyles! With the right cut and style, shoulder length hair can be incredibly flattering for those with round faces and double chins. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about shoulder length hairstyles for fat faces and double chins, including how to choose the right cut, styling tips, and hair care advice.

How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the right shoulder length hairstyle for a round face and double chin, it's all about creating balance. You want to choose a cut that will elongate your face and draw attention away from your chin.

Best Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Fat Faces and Double Chins

Here are some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for those with round faces and double chins:

  • Long Layers: Long layers can help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the chin. Ask your stylist for long layers that start at your chin and gradually get longer.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help to create balance and draw attention away from the chin. Ask your stylist for a long, side-swept bang that starts at the crown of your head.
  • Textured Lob: A textured lob (long bob) can be incredibly flattering for those with round faces and double chins. Ask your stylist for a choppy, textured cut that hits just above your shoulders.
  • Asymmetrical Bob: An asymmetrical bob can help to create balance and draw attention away from the chin. Ask your stylist for a bob that is shorter on one side and longer on the other.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair

Once you've chosen the perfect shoulder length hairstyle, it's time to learn how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Use a straightening iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  • Add waves or curls to your hair with a curling iron or wand.
  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add even more volume and texture.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

Maintaining shoulder length hair is key to keeping it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims (every 6-8 weeks) to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a shoulder length cut?

Yes! You can still wear your hair up with a shoulder length cut. Try a half-up, half-down style or a messy bun.

Q: Is it okay to use heat on my hair every day?

No. Using too much heat on your hair can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools to a few times a week.

Q: Can I dye my shoulder length hair?

Yes, you can dye your shoulder length hair. Just be sure to use a dye that is safe for your hair type and follow the instructions carefully.

Final Thoughts

Shoulder length hairstyles can be incredibly flattering for those with fat faces and double chins. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can create a look that is both stylish and flattering. Follow the tips and advice in this guide to get started!
