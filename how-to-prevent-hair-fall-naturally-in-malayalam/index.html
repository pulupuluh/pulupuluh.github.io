How To Prevent Hair Fall Naturally In Malayalam

Written by Eveline Mar 27, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you struggling with hair fall and looking for natural remedies to prevent it? Hair fall is a common problem faced by many people, and it can be caused by various factors such as stress, nutritional deficiency, genetics, and environmental factors. In this article, we will discuss how to prevent hair fall naturally in Malayalam. Let's dive in!

How to Prevent Hair Fall Naturally in Malayalam

There are various natural remedies that can help prevent hair fall. Here are some of the most effective ones:

1. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a natural hair conditioner that can help nourish your hair and prevent hair fall. It contains lauric acid that can penetrate the hair shaft and prevent protein loss. Massage your scalp with warm coconut oil and leave it overnight. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo in the morning.

2. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera has enzymes that can promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. It can also help soothe an itchy scalp and reduce dandruff. Apply fresh aloe vera gel on your scalp and hair and leave it for 30 minutes. Rinse it off with water.

3. Onion Juice

Onion juice is a natural remedy that can help prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. It contains sulfur that can boost collagen production and strengthen hair follicles. Extract onion juice and apply it on your scalp for 30 minutes. Rinse it off with water and shampoo your hair.

4. Curry Leaves

Curry leaves are rich in antioxidants that can help prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. Boil some curry leaves in coconut oil and apply the mixture on your scalp. Leave it for an hour and wash your hair with a mild shampoo.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to prevent hair fall naturally:

  1. Massage your scalp with warm coconut oil and leave it overnight.
  2. Apply fresh aloe vera gel on your scalp and hair and leave it for 30 minutes. Rinse it off with water.
  3. Extract onion juice and apply it on your scalp for 30 minutes. Rinse it off with water and shampoo your hair.
  4. Boil some curry leaves in coconut oil and apply the mixture on your scalp. Leave it for an hour and wash your hair with a mild shampoo.
  5. Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that are essential for hair growth.
  6. Avoid using harsh chemicals and styling products that can damage your hair.
  7. Manage your stress levels by practicing relaxation techniques such as yoga and meditation.
  8. Get enough sleep and exercise regularly to improve blood circulation to your scalp.
  9. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water and fluids.
  10. Consult a dermatologist if your hair fall persists despite trying natural remedies.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull your hair tightly.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair and avoid brushing wet hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip off natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from UV rays by wearing a hat or using a scarf.
  • Avoid smoking and alcohol as they can damage your hair and lead to hair fall.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair fall:

1. How much hair fall is normal?

It is normal to lose about 50-100 hair strands per day as part of the hair growth cycle.

2. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress can cause hair fall as it can disrupt the hair growth cycle and lead to hair loss.

3. Can nutritional deficiency cause hair fall?

Yes, nutritional deficiency can cause hair fall as certain vitamins and minerals such as iron, biotin, and vitamin D are essential for hair growth.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray if necessary.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products and chemicals on your hair.

By following these natural remedies and tips, you can prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient and consistent in your hair care routine. If you have any concerns about your hair fall, consult a dermatologist for personalized advice.
