I Can t Curl The Left Side Of My Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to curl the left side of your hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people find it difficult to curl one side of their hair, whether it's the left or the right. In this blog post, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you curl the left side of your hair with ease.

Description

Trying to curl the left side of your hair can be frustrating, especially if you're right-handed. It can be tough to maneuver the curling iron or wand in the right direction while holding it with your non-dominant hand. The result? Uneven curls that don't look quite right.

But don't give up just yet. With a little practice and some helpful tips, you can curl the left side of your hair just as easily as the right.

How to Curl the Left Side of Your Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to curling the left side of your hair:

Step 1: Divide Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into sections. Use clips or hair ties to keep the sections separate while you work on one at a time.

Step 2: Start with the Bottom

Begin curling at the bottom of your hair, closest to your neck. Take a small section of hair and clamp it with the curling iron or wand. Make sure the barrel is facing downward and away from your face.

Step 3: Curl Away from Your Face

As you curl your hair, make sure to curl away from your face. This will create a more natural-looking curl and prevent your hair from falling into your face.

Step 4: Repeat on the Left Side

Once you've finished curling the right side of your hair, it's time to move on to the left. This is where things can get tricky, but don't worry. The key is to use your non-dominant hand to hold the curling iron or wand while using your dominant hand to guide your hair.

Step 5: Practice, Practice, Practice

The more you practice, the easier it will become to curl the left side of your hair. Don't get discouraged if your first few attempts don't turn out perfectly. Keep practicing and you'll get the hang of it.

Tips for Curling the Left Side of Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you curl the left side of your hair:

  • Use a smaller barrel curling iron or wand for more control.
  • Try using a mirror to help you see what you're doing on the left side of your head.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't hold the curling iron or wand on your hair for too long or you risk damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling the left side of your hair:

Why is it harder to curl the left side of my hair?

It's often more difficult to curl the left side of your hair if you're right-handed because you're using your non-dominant hand. This can make it harder to maneuver the curling iron or wand in the right direction.

What size curling iron or wand should I use?

Try using a smaller barrel curling iron or wand for more control when curling the left side of your hair.

How long should I hold the curling iron or wand on my hair?

Don't hold the curling iron or wand on your hair for too long, or you risk damaging your hair. 5-10 seconds is usually sufficient.

Hair Care

Remember to take good care of your hair before and after curling. Use a heat protectant spray, and avoid over-curling or using hot tools too often. Keep your hair healthy and moisturized with regular conditioning treatments.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to curl the left side of your hair with ease. Remember to practice and experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you. Happy curling!
