Long Hair Cut For Round Face Girl How To Choose The Perfect Hairstyle

Written by Luffy Aug 07, 2023 · 8 min read

If you’re a girl with a round face, you may have wondered what long hair cut would best complement your features. With so many styles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the perfect one that flatters your face shape. In this guide, we’ll go over some tips to help you choose the right long hair cut for a round face girl, as well as some step-by-step instructions on how to style it. Let’s get started!

Description: What is a Round Face?

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The width and length of the face are almost equal, and the jawline is not very defined. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and slims down your cheeks.

How to Choose the Perfect Long Hair Cut for a Round Face Girl

When choosing a long hair cut for a round face girl, the goal is to create the illusion of a longer face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Layers and Waves

Adding layers and waves to your hair can help elongate your face and create movement. Long layers that start at the chin and blend into the rest of your hair can help slim down your cheeks. Loose waves can also create the illusion of length.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out a round face and draw attention to your eyes. Avoid blunt bangs that can make your face look rounder.

3. Parting Your Hair to the Side

Parting your hair to the side can create the illusion of length and slim down your face. Try a deep side part for maximum effect.

4. Avoiding Too Much Volume

Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, such as a big bouffant or full curls. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, like a messy bun or a half-up half-down style.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Styling Your Long Hair Cut

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, straighten it with a flat iron before starting.

2. Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

3. Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron, curl your hair in sections. Start at the bottom and work your way up, holding each curl for a few seconds before releasing it.

4. Brush Out Your Curls

Once you’ve curled your entire head, use a brush to gently brush out your curls. This will create loose waves.

5. Tease Your Crown

Tease the crown of your hair to add volume. Use a fine-tooth comb to backcomb your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.

6. Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Hair Cut

Here are some tips to keep your long hair cut looking its best:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly will prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

2. Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and shiny.

3. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it look dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools and avoid using them too often.

Frequently Asked Questions

What long hair cut is best for a round face girl?

Hairstyles with layers, waves, and side-swept bangs are best for a round face girl. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face.

How often should I trim my hair?

It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Can I curl my hair without a curling iron?

Yes, you can curl your hair without a curling iron. You can use a straightener or hot rollers to create curls.

Choosing the right long hair cut for a round face girl can be tricky, but with these tips and step-by-step instructions, you’ll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. Remember to maintain and care for your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Happy styling!
