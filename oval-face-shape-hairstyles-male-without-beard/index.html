Oval Face Shape Hairstyles For Men Without Beard

Written by Daniel Aug 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an oval face shape and prefer to keep a clean-shaven look, there are plenty of hairstyles that will suit your features. In this article, we will discuss some of the best oval face shape hairstyles for men without a beard. Whether you have short, medium, or long hair, there are plenty of options to choose from.

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile of all face shapes. It is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is longer than its width. Men with oval face shapes have balanced facial features, which means they can pull off a wide range of hairstyles.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. The right hairstyle can enhance your best features and minimize any flaws. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle for your oval face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are similar, you have an oval face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type will also play a role in determining the best hairstyle for you. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to consider a shorter hairstyle that will be easier to manage. If you have fine, straight hair, you may want to consider a longer hairstyle that will add volume and texture.

Step 3: Look for Hairstyles that Complement Your Features

When choosing a hairstyle, look for styles that complement your facial features. For example, if you have sharp cheekbones, you may want to consider a hairstyle that highlights them. If you have a strong jawline, you may want to consider a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head.

Top Oval Face Shape Hairstyles for Men Without Beard

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape, let's take a look at some of the best hairstyles for men with oval face shapes:

Short Haircuts

For men with oval face shapes, short haircuts are a great option. A short, textured haircut with a side part can help to balance out your features. You can also consider a buzz cut or a crew cut if you prefer a more low-maintenance hairstyle.

Medium Length Haircuts

For men with medium length hair, a messy, textured hairstyle can look great with an oval face shape. You can also consider a slicked-back hairstyle, which will add volume to the top of your head and balance out your features.

Long Hairstyles

For men with longer hair, a layered haircut can help to add texture and movement to your hair. You can also consider a man bun or a ponytail, which can help to balance out your features.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your oval face shape, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your hairstyle looking great:

  • Visit your stylist regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use quality hair products that are suited to your hair type.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to style your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQ

What is an oval face shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is longer than its width.

What are the best hairstyles for men with oval face shapes?

Short haircuts, medium length haircuts, and long hairstyles can all look great with an oval face shape. It's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your facial features and hair type.

How do I maintain my hairstyle?

Regular visits to your stylist, using quality hair products, and avoiding excessive heat can all help to maintain your hairstyle.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to use products that are suited to your hair type. If you have dry hair, look for moisturizing shampoos and conditioners. If you have oily hair, look for products that will help to control oil production. It's also important to avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.

In conclusion, there are plenty of oval face shape hairstyles for men without a beard. By choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair type, and maintaining it properly, you can look and feel your best.
