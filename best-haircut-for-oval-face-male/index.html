Best Haircut For Oval Face Male

Written by Luffy Jul 24, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you an oval faced man looking for the perfect haircut? Look no further than this guide on the best haircut for oval face male. We'll show you how to choose a haircut that perfectly complements your face shape and leaves you looking your best.

Description

The oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile of all facial shapes. This means that oval faced men can pull off a wide variety of hairstyles, from short and simple to long and intricate. However, not all hairstyles will suit every oval faced man, so it's important to choose the right one for your individual features.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Oval Face Male

When choosing a haircut, there are a few key factors to consider. These include your hair type, personal style, and of course, your face shape. Here are some tips for choosing the best haircut for oval face male:

1. Consider Your Hair Type

The texture and thickness of your hair will determine which hairstyles will work best for you. For example, if you have thick hair, you might want to opt for a shorter cut to keep it manageable. If you have thin hair, longer styles with layers can help add volume and texture.

2. Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also an important factor to consider. Do you prefer a clean-cut, professional look? Or are you more of a laid-back, casual guy? Your haircut should reflect your personal style and help you feel confident and comfortable.

3. Look at Your Face Shape

Of course, the most important factor to consider when choosing a haircut is your face shape. Oval faced men have a lot of options, but there are a few styles that work particularly well. We'll cover those in the next section.

Best Haircuts for Oval Face Male

Now that you know what to look for when choosing a haircut, let's take a look at some of the best styles for oval faced men.

1. Short and Simple

A short, simple haircut can be a great choice for oval faced men. This style keeps the hair close to the head, which helps to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. A classic crew cut or buzz cut can work well for this face shape.

2. Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that works well for oval faced men. This style features a deep part on one side of the head, which helps to create the illusion of a more angular face. This style can be paired with a variety of lengths, from short to medium to long.

3. Textured Crop

A textured crop is a great way to add some interest and dimension to your hair while still keeping it short and manageable. This style features a short, choppy cut with lots of texture and movement. It works particularly well for oval faced men with thick hair.

4. Long and Layered

If you prefer longer hair, a layered cut can help to add some shape and definition to your face. This style typically features longer layers on top and shorter layers around the sides and back. It works particularly well for oval faced men with wavy or curly hair.

FAQ

What if I have a receding hairline?

If you have a receding hairline, a short, simple cut can be a good option. Avoid styles that require longer hair, as they can emphasize the hairline and make it more noticeable.

What if I have straight hair?

Straight hair can be a bit trickier to style, but there are plenty of options for oval faced men with straight hair. A side part or textured crop can work well, as can a longer, layered cut.

What if I want to grow my hair out?

If you want to grow your hair out, be sure to get regular trims to keep it looking neat and tidy. A layered cut can help to add some shape and definition as your hair grows.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Faced Men

Finally, here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

1. Use the Right Products

Choose hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair looking healthy and well-maintained.

2. Wash and Condition Regularly

Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help to keep your hair looking neat and tidy, and can also promote healthy hair growth.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and ideas for the best haircut for oval face male, you'll be able to find the perfect style for your unique features. Remember to take into account your hair type, personal style, and face shape to choose a haircut that leaves you feeling confident and looking your best.
