Long On Top Short On Sides Curly Hairstyle A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Apr 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is trendy, stylish, and easy to manage? Then look no further than the long on top short on sides curly hairstyle. This hairstyle is perfect for those with curly hair who want a modern twist on a classic look. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to achieve this hairstyle, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Long on Top Short on Sides Curly Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Long on Top Short on Sides Curly Hairstyle

Description

The long on top short on sides curly hairstyle is a modern take on the classic men's haircut. This style features longer hair on the top of the head, which can be styled in a variety of ways, while the sides and back are trimmed short. The curly texture of the hair adds to the style's unique appeal and makes it stand out from other men's hairstyles.

How to Achieve the Long on Top Short on Sides Curly Hairstyle

To achieve the long on top short on sides curly hairstyle, you will need to have curly hair that is at least a few inches long on the top of your head. If your hair is not naturally curly, you can use hair products such as curling cream or mousse to add texture and create curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the long on top short on sides curly hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo that is designed for curly hair. This will help to keep your curls defined and frizz-free.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub it too harshly. You want to keep your curls intact as much as possible.
  3. Apply a curling cream or mousse to your hair, focusing on the top section. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and give them more definition.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a comb or your fingers to style the top section of your hair. You can choose to leave it messy and tousled or slick it back for a more polished look.
  6. Trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. You can choose to have a fade or a more gradual taper.
  7. Use a styling product such as hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold to the sides and back of your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long on Top Short on Sides Curly Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your long on top short on sides curly hairstyle:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using a hair dryer or styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve the long on top short on sides curly hairstyle if my hair is not naturally curly?

A: Yes, you can use hair products such as curling cream or mousse to add texture and create curls.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It is recommended to wash curly hair once or twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: What styling products should I use for the sides and back of my hair?

A: Hair wax or pomade are good options for adding texture and hold to the sides and back of your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out and cause frizz.
  • Use a t-shirt or microfiber towel to dry your hair, as these are gentler on curls than regular towels.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent tangles.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can achieve the long on top short on sides curly hairstyle and keep your curly hair looking healthy and stylish. Whether you choose to leave your curls messy and tousled or slick them back for a more polished look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
