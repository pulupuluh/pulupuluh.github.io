Can You Get Curtain Bangs With Wavy Hair

Introduction

If you have wavy hair and are thinking about getting curtain bangs, you may be wondering if it's possible. The good news is that you can get curtain bangs with wavy hair, but there are a few things you need to know before you take the plunge. In this article, we'll explain what curtain bangs are, how to get them with wavy hair, and provide some tips and hair care advice to keep your new look looking great.

What are Curtain Bangs?

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted in the middle, framing the face on either side. They get their name from the way they resemble a pair of parted curtains. Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that has been seen on many celebrities and influencers, and are a great way to change up your look without having to make a drastic cut.

How to Get Curtain Bangs with Wavy Hair

If you have wavy hair, you can still get curtain bangs. The key is to make sure that your hair is cut to the right length and that the bangs are styled properly. Here's how to get curtain bangs with wavy hair:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Choose a stylist who has experience cutting bangs for wavy hair.
  2. Decide on the length of your bangs. Curtain bangs typically fall between the cheekbones and the chin.
  3. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs while your hair is dry. This is important because wavy hair can shrink when it dries, and you want to make sure your bangs are the right length.
  4. Part your bangs down the middle and style them using a round brush and a blow dryer. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your bangs in place.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs with Wavy Hair

Once you have your curtain bangs, here are some tips for styling them with wavy hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Apply a sea salt spray to your hair to give it texture and hold.
  • Avoid using a flat iron on your bangs, as this can make them look too straight and blunt.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

FAQ about Curtain Bangs with Wavy Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curtain bangs with wavy hair:

Q: Will curtain bangs work with all types of wavy hair?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can work with all types of wavy hair, from loose waves to tight curls. The key is to make sure your hair is cut to the right length and that the bangs are styled properly.

Q: Will curtain bangs make my face look wider?

A: No, curtain bangs can actually make your face look slimmer because they frame your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs. Simply pull your bangs back and secure them with bobby pins or a hair tie.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs with Wavy Hair

To keep your curtain bangs looking great, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your bangs too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

Conclusion

If you have wavy hair, you can definitely get curtain bangs. Just make sure to find a stylist who has experience cutting bangs for wavy hair, and follow our tips for styling and hair care. With the right cut and styling techniques, you'll be able to rock this trendy hairstyle in no time.
