Top Guys Long Haircuts 2021

When it comes to long haircuts for men, there are several options to choose from. The most popular styles this year include:

The Man Bun

The man bun has been a staple in men's long haircuts for years now. This style is achieved by tying your hair into a bun at the back of your head. You can wear it high or low, depending on your preference.

The Long Wavy Layers

If you have naturally wavy or curly hair, the long wavy layers style is perfect for you. This look involves cutting layers into your hair to add texture and movement. It's a low-maintenance style that looks great on its own or with a beard.

The Shaggy Surfer

The shaggy surfer style is popular among guys who want a messy, carefree look. This style involves cutting your hair into layers and adding texture with a razor. It's a great style for summer and looks best on guys with naturally wavy hair.

The Long Side Part

The long side part is a classic men's haircut that's been updated for 2021. This style involves growing your hair out to shoulder-length and parting it on the side. It's a sleek and sophisticated look that's perfect for the office or a night out.

How to Achieve Long Hair

If you're starting from short hair, growing out your locks can be a daunting task. However, with a little patience and some TLC, you can achieve long hair in no time. Here are some tips to get you started:

Be Patient

Growing out your hair takes time, so don't expect to have long locks overnight. It can take anywhere from 6 months to a year to achieve the length you want.

Get Regular Trims

While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow faster. Regular trims will prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down your hair growth.

Eat a Healthy Diet

What you eat can also affect your hair growth. Make sure you're getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Step-by-Step Guide to Growing Long Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you grow out your hair:

Step 1: Get a Trim

Before you start growing out your hair, get a trim to remove any split ends or damage. This will help your hair grow stronger and healthier.

Step 2: Be Patient

As mentioned earlier, growing out your hair takes time. Be patient and don't expect to see results overnight.

Step 3: Experiment with Styles

As your hair grows, experiment with different styles to see what looks best on you. This will help you stay motivated and excited about the growing process.

Step 4: Get Regular Trims

As your hair grows, make sure to get regular trims to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair

Once you've achieved your desired hair length, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that's designed for long hair. This will help keep your locks soft, shiny, and healthy.

Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause split ends. Try to air-dry your hair as much as possible, and if you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.

Get Regular Trims

Even though you're trying to maintain length, regular trims are still important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping your hair of natural oils.

Q: How do I prevent my long hair from getting tangled?

A: Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently comb out tangles. Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

Q: How do I keep my long hair from looking greasy?

A: Use dry shampoo between washes to absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for your long locks:

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, such as braids or ponytails, too often as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun.

Invest in a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking your long locks like a pro in no time. Remember to be patient, experiment with styles, and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
