No 2 Short Back And Sides The Ultimate Guide For A Classic Look

Written by Bardi Jul 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a classic and timeless haircut, the no 2 short back and sides might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut features a short length on the back and sides of the head, with a slightly longer length on top. It's a versatile look that can be styled in many ways, making it a popular choice for men of all ages and styles.

Description

The no 2 short back and sides is a classic haircut that has been around for decades. It's a simple and clean look that's easy to maintain and style. The "no 2" refers to the clipper size used to achieve the short length on the back and sides. This size is commonly used in barbershops and creates a neat and uniform length.

How to

If you're interested in getting a no 2 short back and sides haircut, the first step is to find a skilled barber who can give you the look you want. Once you've found a barber, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your barber starts cutting your hair, they'll likely ask you a few questions about the look you're going for. Be prepared to discuss the length you want on the top of your head, as well as the length you want on the back and sides.

Step 2: Clipper Cut

Your barber will start by using clippers to cut the hair on the back and sides of your head. They'll likely use a no 2 clipper size to achieve the short length. This part of the haircut is relatively quick and should only take a few minutes.

Step 3: Scissor Cut

Next, your barber will use scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head. They'll likely leave it slightly longer than the hair on the back and sides, creating a graduated look. This part of the haircut may take a bit longer, as your barber will need to carefully blend the lengths together.

Step 4: Styling

Once your haircut is complete, your barber will likely style it for you. They may use a variety of products, such as pomade or gel, to create the look you want. If you're not sure how to style your new haircut, ask your barber for tips and advice.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your no 2 short back and sides haircut:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you
  • Consider adding a beard or mustache to complete the classic look

FAQ

Here are a few common questions about the no 2 short back and sides haircut:

Q: How long does a no 2 short back and sides haircut last?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows, but most men will need to visit their barber every 3-4 weeks to maintain the look.

Q: Can I style my no 2 short back and sides haircut in different ways?

A: Yes! This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Try slicking it back for a classic look, or spiking it up for a more modern feel.

Q: Is the no 2 short back and sides haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes! This haircut is versatile and can work well with many different face shapes. Your barber can help you determine the best length and style for your face.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is important, whether you have a no 2 short back and sides or any other haircut. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking and feeling great:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and nutrients to support hair growth and health

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your no 2 short back and sides with confidence and style!
