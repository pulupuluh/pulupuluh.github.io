Mens Hairstyles Long On Top Wavy A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Petter Mar 19, 2023 · 9 min read

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that is both trendy and low maintenance, then mens hairstyles long on top wavy may be just what you need. This style has become increasingly popular over the years, thanks to its versatile nature and ability to suit a wide range of face shapes and hair types. In this article, we’ll be taking a closer look at how to achieve the perfect mens hairstyles long on top wavy look, as well as some tips for keeping it looking great.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy
  • Tips for Maintaining Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy
  • FAQs about Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy
  • Hair Care Tips for Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy

How to Achieve Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy

The mens hairstyles long on top wavy look can be achieved in a number of ways, depending on your hair type and personal preferences. Some of the most popular methods include:

  • Using a curling iron or wand to create loose waves
  • Applying a styling product, such as mousse or salt spray, and scrunching hair to create natural waves
  • Using a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to enhance natural waves or curls
  • Getting a perm or other chemical treatment to add texture and volume

Regardless of how you choose to achieve the look, the key to mens hairstyles long on top wavy is to keep the hair on top longer than the sides and back. This creates a contrast that draws attention to the wavy texture and adds dimension to the overall style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy

Here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect mens hairstyles long on top wavy look:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or salt spray, evenly throughout hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to dry hair on a low heat setting, scrunching hair with your hands as you go.
  4. If using a curling iron or wand, section hair and wrap small sections around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Once hair is dry and styled, use a small amount of pomade or wax to define waves and add texture on top.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Remember, mens hairstyles long on top wavy is all about embracing natural texture and keeping things relaxed. Don’t worry too much about getting every wave in place – a little bit of messiness is part of the charm of this style.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy

To keep your mens hairstyles long on top wavy looking its best, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid washing hair too frequently, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause it to dry out.
  • When styling, use a light touch and avoid pulling or tugging on hair too much.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep the sides and back neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

FAQs about Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy

Q: Can mens hairstyles long on top wavy work for any hair type?

A: Yes, mens hairstyles long on top wavy can work for a wide range of hair types, from straight to curly. The key is to embrace your natural texture and work with it, rather than trying to fight it.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have mens hairstyles long on top wavy?

A: It’s generally recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days if you have mens hairstyles long on top wavy. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from becoming too dry or brittle.

Q: Do I need to use a diffuser attachment on my hair dryer to achieve mens hairstyles long on top wavy?

A: No, you don’t necessarily need to use a diffuser attachment to achieve mens hairstyles long on top wavy. However, a diffuser can help to enhance natural waves and curls, and make the styling process easier overall.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy

To keep your hair looking and feeling its best, here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type and needs.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too frequently, as this can damage hair over time.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish hair and restore moisture.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that support hair health, such as B vitamins, iron, and zinc.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect mens hairstyles long on top wavy look and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
