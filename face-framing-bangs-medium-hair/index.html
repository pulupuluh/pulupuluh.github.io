Face Framing Bangs For Medium Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Mar 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, adding face framing bangs to your medium length hair can be a great way to freshen up your look. Not only do they add dimension and movement to your hair, but they also accentuate your facial features.

Table of Contents

  1. Description

Description

Face framing bangs are a type of bang that are cut to frame the face. They can be short, long, or somewhere in between, depending on your preference. Face framing bangs are a great option for those with medium length hair because they can add shape and dimension to your hair without requiring a major haircut.

How to Get Face Framing Bangs for Medium Hair

Getting face framing bangs for medium length hair is relatively easy. All you need to do is find a reputable hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Before you go in for your haircut, it's important to do some research and find pictures of face framing bangs that you like. This will help your hairstylist understand exactly what you're looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting face framing bangs for medium hair:

  1. Find pictures of face framing bangs that you like.
  2. Book an appointment with a reputable hairstylist.
  3. Discuss your desired look with your hairstylist.
  4. Sit back and relax while your hairstylist cuts your bangs.
  5. Style your bangs as desired.

Tips for Styling Face Framing Bangs

Now that you have your face framing bangs, it's important to know how to style them. Here are some tips:

  • Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume.
  • Use a flat iron to smooth out your bangs.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to your bangs to add shine.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and bobby pins, to change up your look.

FAQ

1. How often do I need to trim my face framing bangs?

You should trim your face framing bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent split ends.

2. Can I wear my face framing bangs with a ponytail?

Absolutely! Face framing bangs look great with a ponytail or any other hairstyle.

3. Will face framing bangs work with my face shape?

Face framing bangs can work with any face shape. It's all about finding the right length and style for you.

Hair Care Tips for Face Framing Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to keep your face framing bangs looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your bangs clean.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your bangs with heat tools.
  • Avoid using too much product on your bangs, as this can weigh them down.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can cause them to become greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your face framing bangs with confidence!
