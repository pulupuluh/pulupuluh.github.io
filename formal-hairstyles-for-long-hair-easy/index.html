Formal Hairstyles For Long Hair Easy A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 11, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for formal hairstyles for long hair easy to do at home? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll go over some of the best formal hairstyles for long hair that are both elegant and easy to create. Whether you’re attending a wedding, prom, or any other formal event, these hairstyles will ensure that you look your best.

Description

Formal hairstyles for long hair can be intimidating, but they don’t have to be. With a little bit of practice, you can create elegant and sophisticated hairstyles that are perfect for any formal occasion. From classic updos to trendy braids, there are countless options to choose from.

How to

If you’re new to creating formal hairstyles for long hair, don’t worry – it’s easier than you think. Here are some tips to get you started:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be harder to style and may not hold the shape as well.

2. Choose the right products

Using the right products can make a big difference in the outcome of your hairstyle. For example, if you’re creating an updo, you’ll want to use a strong hold hairspray to keep everything in place.

3. Practice, practice, practice

The more you practice, the easier it will be to create formal hairstyles for long hair. Don’t be afraid to try new styles and experiment with different techniques.

Step by Step

Now, let’s take a closer look at some of the best formal hairstyles for long hair easy to create:

1. Classic Updo

The classic updo is a timeless and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for any formal occasion. Here’s how to create it:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to hide the ends of your hair.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold everything in place.

2. Braided Crown

The braided crown is a trendy and romantic hairstyle that is perfect for weddings or other formal events. Here’s how to create it:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and braid it.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Bring the braids to the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when creating formal hairstyles for long hair easy:

1. Don’t be afraid to accessorize

Adding a hair accessory, such as a sparkly barrette or a floral crown, can take your hairstyle to the next level.

2. Consider your dress

The style of your dress should influence the style of your hair. For example, if you’re wearing a strapless dress, an updo will help to show off your shoulders.

3. Keep it simple

Sometimes, the simplest hairstyles are the most elegant. Don’t feel like you have to create a complicated style to look formal.

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to create a formal hairstyle for long hair?

A: The time it takes to create a formal hairstyle for long hair will depend on the style you choose and your level of experience. However, most styles can be created in under an hour.

Q: Can I create a formal hairstyle for long hair on my own?

A: Yes, with practice and the right tools, you can definitely create a formal hairstyle for long hair on your own.

Hair Care

Lastly, it’s important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks its best. Here are some tips:

1. Use a heat protectant

If you’re using heat tools, such as a curling iron or straightener, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Don’t overwash your hair

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Use a deep conditioner

Using a deep conditioner once a week can help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and techniques, you can create formal hairstyles for long hair easy to do at home. Whether you’re attending a wedding or prom, these hairstyles will ensure that you look your best.
