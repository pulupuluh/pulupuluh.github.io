How Do I Slick My Hair Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your hair falling in your face all day? Do you want to try a new hairstyle that's sleek and modern? Slicking your hair back is a classic look that can work for any occasion. Here's our step-by-step guide on how to slick your hair back.

How to Slick Your Hair Back

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to slicking your hair back is to wash it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to clean and soften your hair. This will make it easier to style and keep in place.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is completely dry. Use a hair dryer to speed up the process. If you have thick hair, use a diffuser attachment to help distribute the heat evenly.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your palms and rub them together. Make sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair, starting from the back and working your way forward. Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or tangles.

Step 4: Slick Your Hair Back

Use a comb to slick your hair back. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back. Make sure to evenly distribute the product as you go. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is slicked back, spray it with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place. This will help hold your hair in place all day without making it too stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

  • Use a high-quality hair product, such as pomade or gel, to keep your hair in place.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  • Don't slick your hair back too tightly or it could cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect look for you.

FAQs

Can I slick my hair back if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can slick your hair back even if you have curly hair. Use a high-quality hair product and a comb to help smooth out your curls. You may need to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type.

How long will my slicked back hair last?

Your slicked back hair should last all day if you use a high-quality hair product and finish with hairspray. However, if you have particularly fine or oily hair, you may need to touch up throughout the day to keep your hair in place.

Can I slick my hair back if I have a receding hairline?

Yes, you can still slick your hair back if you have a receding hairline. However, you may want to use a hair product with a lighter hold to avoid pulling on your hairline too much.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

It's important to take care of your hair when slicking it back. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can cause buildup and weigh down your hair.
  • Don't slick your hair back too tightly, as it can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect slicked back look. Experiment with different products and hairstyles to find what works best for you.
