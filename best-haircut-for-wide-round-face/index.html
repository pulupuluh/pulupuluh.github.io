Best Haircut For Wide Round Face Flatter Your Features

Do you have a wide round face and struggle to find the perfect haircut? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best haircut for your face shape and how to flatter your features. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there is a style that will suit you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A wide round face is characterized by having a wider forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The aim of a good haircut for this face shape is to create the illusion of length and slimness. Hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head and elongate the face will be most flattering.

How to

When choosing a haircut, consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. Consult with your stylist to find a style that will suit your face shape and personality. Bring pictures to help illustrate the look you want to achieve.

Step by step

Here are some of the best haircuts for a wide round face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers will elongate the face and draw attention away from the width. Ask your stylist for long layers that start at the collarbone and gradually get longer towards the ends.

2. Bob

A chin-length bob that is slightly longer at the front will create the illusion of length. Avoid blunt cuts as they will emphasize the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for a graduated bob that is shorter at the back and longer at the front.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs will soften the forehead and create a diagonal line that will elongate the face. Avoid straight across bangs as they will make your face appear wider.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut with volume at the crown of the head will elongate the face. Avoid flat styles that will emphasize the roundness of your face.

5. Wavy Lob

A wavy lob that hits just below the collarbone will create the illusion of length. Avoid straight styles as they will make your face appear wider.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that will emphasize the roundness of your face.
  • Add volume to the crown of the head to elongate the face.
  • Consider side-swept bangs to soften the forehead.
  • Avoid straight styles and opt for wavy or curly styles to create the illusion of length.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid with a wide round face?

Avoid blunt cuts, straight across bangs, and styles that are too flat. These will emphasize the roundness of your face.

What hairstyles are best for a wide round face?

Hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head and create the illusion of length are best for a wide round face. Long layers, a graduated bob, side-swept bangs, a pixie cut with volume, and a wavy lob are great options.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. If you have curly hair, use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls looking their best.

Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Finally, visit your stylist regularly to keep your haircut looking its best. A good haircut can make all the difference in how you look and feel.
