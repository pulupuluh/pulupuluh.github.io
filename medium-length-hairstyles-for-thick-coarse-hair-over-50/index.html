Medium Length Hairstyles For Thick Coarse Hair Over 50

Are you over 50 and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your thick and coarse hair? Look no further than medium length hairstyles! Medium length hair is versatile, easy to manage, and can be styled in various ways to flatter your face shape and hair texture. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right medium length hairstyle for your thick and coarse hair, step-by-step styling tips, and hair care recommendations to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.

How to Choose the Right Medium Length Hairstyle

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for thick and coarse hair, consider your face shape and hair texture. A layered cut can add movement and volume to your hair, while a blunt cut can create a sleek and sophisticated look. Bangs can also be a great option to frame your face and soften your features.

Another factor to consider is your lifestyle and personal style. Do you prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle or are you willing to spend time styling your hair every day? Are you comfortable with a bold and edgy look or do you prefer a classic and elegant style?

Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best medium length hairstyle for your hair type and personal preferences. They can also recommend products and styling techniques to enhance the look and health of your hair.

Step-by-Step Styling Tips

Styling medium length hair can be simple and effortless with the right tools and techniques. Here are some step-by-step tips to help you achieve your desired look:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling wand to create waves or curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Length Hairstyle

To keep your medium length hairstyle looking its best, follow these tips:

  1. Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
  2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  3. Avoid using hot styling tools every day to prevent damage and breakage.
  4. Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  5. Consider using hair extensions or accessories to change up your look and add volume and length to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles for thick and coarse hair over 50:

Q: Will a medium length hairstyle make me look older?

A: No, a medium length hairstyle can actually make you look more youthful and vibrant. The key is to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and hair texture, and to maintain it with regular trims and styling.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a medium length hairstyle?

A: Yes, bangs can be a great way to add dimension and style to your medium length hairstyle. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best bangs for your face shape and hair texture.
