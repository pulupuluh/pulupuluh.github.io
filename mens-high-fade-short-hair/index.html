Description

Mens high fade short hair is a popular hairstyle trend that has been around for several years. It is a style that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving the hair on top longer. The hair on top is styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.

The high fade refers to the way the hair is cut on the sides and back. It starts high up on the head and gradually tapers down to the neckline. This creates a sharp and clean look that is perfect for men who want a modern and stylish haircut.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the mens high fade short hair look, you will need to visit a professional hairstylist. The stylist will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head, gradually tapering it down to the neckline. The hair on top will be left longer and can be styled in a variety of ways.

When getting a high fade, it is important to communicate with your stylist about the length of the fade and the style you want on top. This will ensure that you get the exact look you are going for.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to achieve the mens high fade short hair look:

  1. Visit a professional hairstylist.
  2. Discuss the length of the fade and the style you want on top.
  3. Sit back and relax while the stylist cuts your hair.
  4. Style the hair on top using your preferred product.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are some tips for maintaining the mens high fade short hair look:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens high fade short hair:

Q: Will a high fade work for all hair types?

A: Yes, a high fade can work for all hair types, but the style on top may need to be adjusted depending on your hair texture.

Q: How often do I need to get a touch-up?

A: This will depend on how fast your hair grows, but most men will need a touch-up every 2-4 weeks.

Q: Can I style my hair differently on top?

A: Yes, the mens high fade short hair look is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways on top.

Hair Care for Mens High Fade Short Hair

To keep your mens high fade short hair looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

With the right care and styling, you can achieve the perfect mens high fade short hair look in 2023.
