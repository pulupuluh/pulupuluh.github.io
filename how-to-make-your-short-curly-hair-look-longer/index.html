How To Make Your Short Curly Hair Look Longer Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Apr 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having short curly hair that seems to always look the same? Do you want to make your hair look longer without having to wait for it to grow? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll show you how to make your short curly hair look longer with some easy tips and tricks.

Description

Short curly hair can be beautiful, but it can also be challenging to style. One of the biggest challenges is making it look longer. Fortunately, there are several things you can do to create the illusion of longer hair.

How to

1. Use Hair Extensions

Hair extensions are an easy way to add length and volume to your hair. They come in different lengths, colors, and textures, so you can choose the one that matches your hair.

2. Straighten Your Hair

By straightening your hair, you remove the curls, which can make it look longer. You can use a flat iron or a blow dryer to straighten your hair.

3. Use a Hair Weave

A hair weave is a technique where hair extensions are sewn onto your natural hair. This creates a fuller and longer look. However, it’s important to have a professional do this for you.

4. Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories, such as headbands, scrunchies, and clips, can add volume and length to your hair. You can use them to create different hairstyles, such as a half-up, half-down style or a ponytail.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for curly hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-in Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle and protect it.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural curls.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Straighten your hair using a flat iron or a blow dryer.

Step 5: Use Hair Extensions

Add hair extensions to your hair, using clips or by having them sewn in.

Step 6: Use Hair Accessories

Add hair accessories, such as headbands, scrunchies, or clips, to create different hairstyles.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make your short curly hair look longer:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow drying your hair to enhance your natural curls.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when straightening your hair to prevent damage.
  • Choose hair extensions that match your hair color and texture.
  • Have a professional stylist sew in hair extensions or a hair weave.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my short curly hair look longer without using hair extensions?

Yes, you can. You can straighten your hair, use hair accessories, or create different hairstyles to make your hair look longer.

Q: How long do hair extensions last?

Hair extensions can last up to six months, depending on how well you take care of them.

Q: Can I wash my hair with hair extensions?

Yes, you can. However, it’s important to use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for both your natural hair and the extensions.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hair looking longer, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for curly hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and protect your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow drying your hair to enhance your natural curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can make your short curly hair look longer and keep it healthy and beautiful.
