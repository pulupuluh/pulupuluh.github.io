Description

The men's hairstyle fade side part is a classic and timeless look that has been popular for decades. This hairstyle features a fade on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that is styled to one side. It's a versatile look that can be dressed up or down and is suitable for any occasion.

The fade side part is also known as the "businessman's haircut" because of its clean and polished appearance. It's a popular choice for professionals and men who want to look sharp and put together.

How to Achieve the Look

The men's hairstyle fade side part can be achieved with any hair type, but works best with straight or wavy hair. To achieve the look, you will need to go to a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in creating fades. They will use clippers to create a gradual fade on the sides and back, leaving longer hair on top.

Once the fade is complete, you can style the longer hair on top to one side. This can be done with a comb and some pomade or wax. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume and hold to the hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the men's hairstyle fade side part:

  1. Visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in creating fades.
  2. Decide on the length of the fade you want - a low fade, mid fade, or high fade.
  3. The barber will use clippers to create the fade, starting with a longer guard and gradually switching to a shorter guard as they move up the sides and back of your head.
  4. The hair on top should be left longer, with enough length to style it to one side.
  5. Apply pomade or wax to the hair on top.
  6. Use a comb to style the hair to one side.
  7. Use a blow dryer to add volume and hold to the hair.
  8. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect men's hairstyle fade side part:

  • Choose a fade length that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Use a comb with fine teeth to create a precise part.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to maintain the fade and keep the style looking sharp.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's hairstyle fade side part:

What face shapes suit the fade side part?

The fade side part suits most face shapes, but works particularly well for those with oval or square faces.

Can I create the fade side part at home?

While it's possible to create the fade side part at home, it's best to visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in creating fades. They will be able to create a precise and polished look that is difficult to achieve at home.

What styling products should I use for the fade side part?

You can use pomade, wax, or gel to style the hair on top. Choose a product that suits your hair type and desired level of hold.

Hair Care for the Fade Side Part

To keep your hair looking its best with the fade side part, there are a few hair care tips you should follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of the fade and keep the style looking sharp.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools on your hair.

The men's hairstyle fade side part is a classic and timeless look that is suitable for any occasion. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking sharp and polished for years to come.
