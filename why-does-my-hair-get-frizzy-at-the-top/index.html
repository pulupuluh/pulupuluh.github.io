Why Does My Hair Get Frizzy At The Top

Written by Oliver Apr 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're struggling with frizzy hair at the top, you're not alone. Many people experience frizz, especially in humid weather or when using certain hair products. In this blog post, we'll explore the reasons why your hair gets frizzy at the top and provide some tips to help you manage it.

Description

Frizzy hair at the top is a common problem that can be caused by a variety of factors. It can make your hair look unkempt, and it can be frustrating to deal with. Frizzy hair is often caused by humidity, but it can also be caused by damage to the hair, such as split ends or over-processing.

How to

If you're dealing with frizzy hair at the top, there are a few things you can do to manage it. Here are some tips:

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner

Dry hair is more prone to frizz, so it's important to keep your hair moisturized. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed to hydrate your hair, and avoid ones that contain drying ingredients like sulfates.

2. Use a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. Apply it to damp hair before styling, focusing on the ends and the top of your head.

3. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than three times a week, and use a dry shampoo in between washes if needed.

4. Use a heat protectant

If you use heat styling tools like a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. This can help to reduce frizz and keep your hair looking healthy.

5. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry

Brushing your hair when it's dry can cause it to become frizzy. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet, and use a boar bristle brush to smooth your hair when it's dry.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for managing frizzy hair at the top:

1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Make sure to massage the shampoo into your scalp and work it through your hair, and leave the conditioner on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair.

Focusing on the ends and the top of your head, apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner and comb through your hair with a wide-tooth comb.

3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser.

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use it to dry your hair on a low heat setting. This will help to reduce frizz and keep your hair looking smooth.

4. Use a heat protectant before styling.

If you're using a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for managing frizzy hair at the top:

1. Use a silk pillowcase.

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and prevent breakage.

2. Don't towel-dry your hair.

Towel-drying your hair can cause it to become frizzy. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze the water out of your hair.

3. Get regular trims.

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ

Q: What causes frizzy hair at the top?

A: Frizzy hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including humidity, damage to the hair, and certain hair products.

Q: How can I prevent frizzy hair at the top?

A: You can prevent frizzy hair by using moisturizing hair products, avoiding over-washing your hair, and using a heat protectant when styling your hair.

Q: Can I use hair oil to prevent frizzy hair?

A: Yes, using hair oil can help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz. Just be sure to apply it sparingly to avoid weighing your hair down.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is key to preventing frizz and keeping your hair looking healthy. Make sure to use hair products that are appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. With the right hair care routine, you can manage frizzy hair at the top and enjoy smooth, healthy-looking hair.
