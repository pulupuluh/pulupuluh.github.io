How To Cut Bangs Round Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that flatters your round face? One of the easiest and most versatile ways to update your look is by cutting bangs. However, it can be challenging to find the right style that complements your face shape. In this guide, we will show you how to cut bangs round face in a relaxed English language, so you can achieve the perfect look for your features.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Bangs Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Bangs are a great way to update your hairstyle without committing to a drastic change. However, not all bangs are created equal. For round faces, it's essential to choose a style that elongates your face and creates the illusion of more defined features. The right bangs can soften your face shape, highlight your eyes, and balance your features.

How to Cut Bangs Round Face

Before you start cutting, it's crucial to understand that not all bangs will look good on a round face. The goal is to create angles and length that complement your features. The best bangs for a round face are layered, side-swept, or asymmetrical. These styles will create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and a longer face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to cut bangs round face:

  1. Start with dry, clean hair. Wet hair can shrink once it dries, leading to uneven cuts.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and comb it straight down.
  3. Decide on the length and style of your bangs. For round faces, side-swept or asymmetrical bangs work best.
  4. Using hair-cutting scissors, cut your bangs in a straight line, starting from the center and working outward. Cut them longer than you think you need, you can always go back and trim more.
  5. Next, angle your scissors and cut the sides of your bangs diagonally, creating a V-shape. This will give your bangs more movement and texture.
  6. Use a razor comb to soften the edges of your bangs and blend them with the rest of your hair.
  7. Style your bangs by blow-drying them with a round brush, creating volume and movement.

Tips for Cutting Bangs

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting bangs:

  • Always start with longer bangs than you think you need. You can always go back and trim more if needed.
  • Use sharp hair-cutting scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Angle your scissors diagonally when cutting your bangs to create a V-shape and add texture.
  • Use a razor comb to soften the edges of your bangs and blend them with the rest of your hair.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and movement.

FAQs

Here are a few frequently asked questions about cutting bangs:

1. Can I cut my bangs at home?

Yes, you can cut your bangs at home. However, we recommend seeking the help of a professional stylist if you're unsure about the process or the style that will best flatter your face shape.

2. Which bangs are best for a round face?

Side-swept, layered, and asymmetrical bangs work best for round faces. These styles create angles and length that complement your features and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and a longer face shape.

3. How often should I trim my bangs?

How often you trim your bangs depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your style. We recommend trimming your bangs every four to six weeks to keep them looking fresh and neat.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind after cutting your bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to your ends to keep them moisturized and prevent split ends.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your bangs looking fresh and healthy.

Now that you know how to cut bangs round face, you can update your hairstyle and flatter your features with confidence. Remember to choose a style that complements your face shape, and follow our step-by-step guide and tips for the best results. Happy styling!
