How To Cut Side Bangs On A Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Apr 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same wig every day? Do you want to change your look without spending a fortune? One of the easiest ways to update your hairstyle is by cutting side bangs on your wig. In this blog post, we will show you how to cut side bangs on a wig, step by step.

How to Cut Side Bangs on a Wig: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right Wig

Before you start cutting your wig, make sure you choose the right one. Look for a wig with a natural-looking hairline and good quality synthetic hair. It's easier to cut side bangs on a wig that has a lace front or a monofilament top. These types of wigs allow you to part the wig and create a natural-looking fringe.

Step 2: Prepare Your Wig

Take your wig out of the box and place it on a wig stand. Comb the wig gently to remove any tangles or knots. Use a hair clip to separate the section of hair you want to cut into bangs. Make sure the section is even and centered on your forehead.

Step 3: Measure and Mark the Bangs

Using a ruler or a measuring tape, measure the length of the bangs you want to cut. Hold the hair section with your fingers and use a hair clip to mark the length of the bangs. Make sure the marks are even on both sides of your forehead.

Step 4: Cut the Bangs

Using a sharp pair of scissors, start cutting the bangs from the center of your forehead. Cut small sections of hair at a time and check the length after each cut. Be patient and take your time. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Once you've cut your bangs, style them to your liking. You can use a flat iron or a curling iron to create waves or curls. Use a small amount of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Cutting Side Bangs on a Wig

Here are some tips to help you cut side bangs on your wig:

  • Always use sharp scissors to cut your wig. Dull scissors can damage the hair and create split ends.
  • Start with small sections of hair and cut slowly. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back.
  • Measure and mark the length of the bangs before you start cutting. This will help you create an even and symmetrical fringe.
  • Use a wig stand to keep your wig in place while you're cutting it. This will make the process easier and more precise.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short. It's better to start with longer bangs and trim them gradually.

FAQs

Can I cut bangs on a human hair wig?

Yes, you can cut bangs on a human hair wig. The process is similar to cutting bangs on a synthetic wig. However, human hair wigs require more maintenance and styling than synthetic wigs.

How do I maintain my wig after cutting bangs?

To maintain your wig after cutting bangs, use a wig brush or a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair. Avoid using hot tools on synthetic wigs. Store your wig on a wig stand or in a wig box to prevent tangling and damage.

Hair Care for Wigs with Bangs

Wigs with bangs require more maintenance than wigs without bangs. Here are some tips to help you take care of your wig:

  • Use a wig brush or a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair.
  • Wash your wig every 6-8 wears with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on synthetic wigs.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or in a wig box to prevent tangling and damage.

Now that you know how to cut side bangs on a wig, you can update your hairstyle in no time. Follow our tips and tricks for a natural-looking fringe that will enhance your features and complement your style.
