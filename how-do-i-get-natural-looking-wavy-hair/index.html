How Do I Get Natural Looking Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 16, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to try something new? Wavy hair is a great option that can give you a natural and effortless look. In this article, we will show you how to get natural looking wavy hair in a few easy steps.

Table of Content

  • How to Get Natural Looking Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Get Natural Looking Wavy Hair

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let's take a look at some of the things you need to consider to get natural looking wavy hair:

  • Your hair length: Wavy hair looks great on all lengths, but the longer your hair, the more defined the waves will be.
  • Your hair type: Wavy hair works best on straight or slightly wavy hair. If you have curly hair, you may need to straighten it first.
  • Your hair texture: Fine hair will need different techniques than thick hair to get the best results.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to get natural looking wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add body to your hair and make it easier to create waves.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 4: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up.

Step 5: Use a curling iron

Using a curling iron, wrap small sections of hair around the barrel, starting from the root and working your way down. Hold for a few seconds and then release.

Step 6: Scrunch your hair

Once you have curled all sections of your hair, use your hands to scrunch your hair. This will help to create a natural looking wave.

Step 7: Use a texturizing spray

Finish off your look by using a texturizing spray to add volume and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your wavy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will help to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wavy hair:

Q: Can I get wavy hair without heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can get wavy hair without heat styling tools by braiding your hair while it's damp and leaving it overnight.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It's best to wash your wavy hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.

Now that you know how to get natural looking wavy hair, it's time to try it out for yourself. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to master the art of wavy hair and rock this look every day.
