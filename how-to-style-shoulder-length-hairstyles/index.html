How To Style Shoulder Length Hairstyles A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline May 14, 2023 · 9 min read

If you’re looking for a versatile and easy-to-maintain hairstyle, shoulder length hair is the way to go. With the right styling techniques, you can rock a variety of looks, from sleek and sophisticated to tousled and carefree. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style shoulder length hairstyles and share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Shoulder Length Hairstyles
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

How to Style Shoulder Length Hairstyles

One of the best things about shoulder length hair is that there are so many ways to style it. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or something more relaxed and tousled, there’s a hairstyle that’s perfect for you. Here are some popular shoulder length hairstyles:

Bob

The classic bob is always in style, and it’s a great choice for shoulder length hair. This hairstyle is a timeless look that is perfect for any occasion. You can wear it straight and sleek for a polished look or add some waves or curls for a more relaxed vibe.

Wavy Lob

The wavy lob is a popular hairstyle that looks great on shoulder length hair. This style is perfect for those who want a more relaxed and carefree look. To achieve this look, simply use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves throughout your hair.

Straight and Sleek

If you want a more polished and sophisticated look, go for straight and sleek hair. This style is perfect for formal occasions, but it can also be worn for everyday events. To achieve this look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair and add some shine serum for a glossy finish.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some popular shoulder length hairstyles, let’s get into the nitty-gritty of how to style them. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

Bob

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will give your hair some body and lift.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create some volume at the roots.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or add some waves or curls with a curling iron.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Wavy Lob

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, then towel dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from your curling iron.
  3. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves throughout your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up.
  4. Finish with a texturizing spray to give your hair some volume and texture.

Straight and Sleek

  1. Wash your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner to tame any frizz.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create some volume at the roots.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down.
  4. Finish with a shine serum to give your hair a glossy finish.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your shoulder length hair:

  • Invest in a good quality flat iron, curling iron, and hair dryer. These tools can make a huge difference in the way your hair looks and feels.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different products, such as texturizing sprays, volumizing mousses, and shine serums to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to try new hairstyles and techniques. You never know what might become your new signature look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling shoulder length hair:

Is shoulder length hair easy to maintain?

Yes, shoulder length hair is relatively easy to maintain. It’s short enough to be manageable, but long enough to style in a variety of ways.

Can I wear shoulder length hair to a formal event?

Yes, shoulder length hair can be styled in a variety of ways that are appropriate for formal events. Try a sleek and polished look or a chic updo.

What products should I use to style my shoulder length hair?

It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Some popular products for shoulder length hair include texturizing sprays, volumizing mousses, and shine serums.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

To keep your shoulder length hair looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every other day or every two days to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a conditioner every time you wash your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to minimize breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to style your shoulder length hair with ease. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or something more relaxed and tousled, there’s a hairstyle that’s perfect for you.
