How To Cut Your Own Curly Hair Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that getting a good haircut can be a challenge. This is especially true when it comes to bangs. But fear not! With a little patience and the right technique, you can cut your own curly hair bangs at home. In this post, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to do just that.

Description

Curly hair can be tricky to cut, but bangs can be especially challenging. They need to be cut in a way that complements your face shape and works with your curls. It's important to take your time and use the right technique to ensure a good result.

How to Cut Your Own Curly Hair Bangs

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, there are a few things you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A fine-toothed comb
  • A spray bottle of water
  • A mirror

Now that you have your tools, let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb your hair in the direction you want your bangs to fall.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs. Use the comb to create a triangle-shaped section of hair that starts at the top of your head and ends at the middle of your forehead.
  3. Spray the section of hair with water to make it easier to work with.
  4. Hold the hair with your non-dominant hand and use your dominant hand to cut the hair in a straight line. Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length.
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 with the other side of your hair.
  6. Check the length of your bangs in the mirror. If they're uneven, make small adjustments until they're even.
  7. Use the scissors to texturize the ends of your bangs. This will help them blend in with the rest of your hair.
  8. Style your bangs as desired.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair Bangs

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.
  • Wet your hair before cutting it. This will make it easier to work with and will help you avoid cutting too much off.
  • Take your time. Cutting your own hair can be nerve-wracking, but rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.

FAQ

  • Can I cut my own curly hair bangs if I have never done it before?

    • Yes, you can. Just take your time and follow the steps carefully.

  • How often should I cut my curly hair bangs?

    • It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to look uneven. As a general rule, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame.

  • Should I cut my curly hair bangs when my hair is wet or dry?

    • It's best to cut your hair when it's wet. This will make it easier to work with and will help you avoid cutting too much off.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Bangs

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for caring for your curly hair bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray.

Now that you know how to cut your own curly hair bangs, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Just remember to take your time and use the right technique, and your bangs will look great!
