Long Hair On Top Short On Sides And Back The Trend That Never Goes Out Of Style

Written by Robby Apr 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Long hair on top short on sides and back is a hairstyle that has been around for decades, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. This hairstyle is popular among men, and it's easy to see why. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and can be styled in many different ways. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about the long hair on top short on sides and back hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Description

The long hair on top short on sides and back hairstyle is a style where the hair on the top of the head is left long, while the hair on the sides and back is cut short. This hairstyle can be worn by both men and women, and it's often styled in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that looks great and can be styled in many different ways.

How to Achieve Long Hair on Top Short on Sides and Back

To achieve the long hair on top short on sides and back hairstyle, you will need to visit a hairstylist who is experienced in this style. The hairstylist will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair short, using clippers or scissors. They will then leave the hair on the top of your head longer, and they may use scissors or a razor to create texture and movement in the hair. The length of the hair on the top of your head will depend on your personal preference, but it's usually between 4-6 inches.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve the long hair on top short on sides and back hairstyle:

  1. Visit a hairstylist who is experienced in this style.
  2. Discuss the length and texture of the hair on the top of your head with the hairstylist.
  3. The hairstylist will use clippers or scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head short.
  4. They will then leave the hair on the top of your head longer, and they may use scissors or a razor to create texture and movement in the hair.
  5. The hairstylist will style the hair on the top of your head to your preference, such as slicked back, messy, or textured.
  6. Use hair products, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair on a daily basis.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair on Top Short on Sides and Back

Maintaining long hair on top short on sides and back is easy, and it requires minimal effort. Here are some tips for maintaining this hairstyle:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the length and shape of your hair.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair on a daily basis.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as this can damage your hair.

FAQ

Can women wear the long hair on top short on sides and back hairstyle?

Yes, women can wear the long hair on top short on sides and back hairstyle. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it perfect for women who want a low-maintenance style.

Can I style my long hair on top short on sides and back hairstyle in different ways?

Yes, you can style your long hair on top short on sides and back hairstyle in many different ways. You can slick it back, create a messy look, or add texture to it. The possibilities are endless.

How often should I visit my hairstylist to maintain my long hair on top short on sides and back hairstyle?

You should visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your hair. This will ensure that your hair looks great and is easy to style.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential when it comes to the long hair on top short on sides and back hairstyle. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein, as this can help your hair grow strong and healthy.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Long hair on top short on sides and back is a timeless hairstyle that looks great on men and women. With minimal effort, you can achieve a stylish and versatile look that can be styled in many different ways. By following the tips in this article, you can maintain healthy hair and keep your hairstyle looking great for years to come.
