Mid Long Hairstyles Men The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the old short haircuts and want to try a new look? Mid long hairstyles for men are the perfect solution! Not too long, not too short, just the right length to give you a stylish and sophisticated look. In this guide, we will take you through everything you need to know about mid long hairstyles for men, including how to style them, tips, and hair care.

What is a Mid Long Hairstyle for Men?

A mid long hairstyle for men is a haircut that falls between short and long hair. It is usually cut to a length that is around the jawline or just below the ears. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair length but still maintain a neat and tidy appearance.

How to Style a Mid Long Hairstyle for Men

Styling a mid long hairstyle for men can be done in a variety of ways. Here are some popular styles:

  • The Side Part: This classic style involves parting your hair on one side and combing it over to the other side.
  • The Slick Back: This style involves combing your hair back and using a hair product to keep it in place.
  • The Messy Look: This style involves using a hair product to create a tousled, messy look.

Make sure to use a good quality hair product that will hold your hair in place and give it the desired look.

Step by Step Guide to Styling a Mid Long Hairstyle for Men

Here is a step by step guide to styling a mid long hairstyle for men:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product to your hands.
  3. Work the product into your hair, starting from the back and working your way to the front.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired style.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Remember to experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Mid Long Hairstyles for Men

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mid long hairstyle for men:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q: Is a mid long hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a mid long hairstyle can be adapted to suit all face shapes.

Q: Can I style my mid long hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, you can style your mid long hair in a ponytail, but make sure to use a hair tie that won't damage your hair.

Hair Care for Mid Long Hairstyles for Men

Here are some hair care tips to keep your mid long hairstyle looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

With these tips, you can keep your mid long hairstyle looking great and maintain healthy hair.

Conclusion

Mid long hairstyles for men are a great way to change up your look without going too extreme. With the right styling and hair care, you can achieve a stylish and sophisticated look that suits you. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
