Which Shade Is Best For Medium Skin Tone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Mar 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you have a medium skin tone. With so many options available, it's easy to get overwhelmed. In this article, we'll help you find the perfect shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. Read on to learn more.

Description

Medium skin tone is a versatile skin tone that can carry a range of hair colors. It's neither too light nor too dark and ranges from olive to caramel. Women with medium skin tones can experiment with a variety of shades, from warm to cool tones, depending on their undertones.

How to Choose the Right Shade

Choosing the right shade for your medium skin tone can be tricky, but there are some general rules to follow. Firstly, it's important to determine your undertones. If you have warm undertones, you should opt for warm tones like golden, copper, or honey. If you have cool undertones, you should go for cool tones like ash, platinum, or caramel.

Another factor to consider is your eye color. If you have warm-toned eyes, like brown or hazel, warm colors will complement your eyes. If you have cool-toned eyes, like blue or green, cool tones will make your eyes pop.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right shade for your medium skin tone:

Step 1: Determine Your Undertones

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green or olive, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If you can't determine whether they are green or blue, you have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

If you have warm-toned eyes, like brown or hazel, warm colors will complement your eyes. If you have cool-toned eyes, like blue or green, cool tones will make your eyes pop.

Step 3: Choose Your Shade

If you have warm undertones, opt for warm tones like golden, copper, or honey. If you have cool undertones, go for cool tones like ash, platinum, or caramel. Neutral undertones can carry both warm and cool tones.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a shade for your medium skin tone:

  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different shades to find what works best for you.
  • Consider your lifestyle and maintenance requirements before choosing a shade.
  • Always use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.
  • If you're unsure, consult with a professional stylist who can guide you in choosing the right shade for your skin tone.

FAQ

Q: Can medium skin tones carry blonde hair?

A: Yes, medium skin tones can carry blonde hair, but it's important to choose the right shade. Warm-toned medium skin tones should opt for golden or honey blonde, while cool-toned medium skin tones should go for ash or platinum blonde.

Q: Can medium skin tones carry red hair?

A: Yes, medium skin tones can carry red hair, but it's important to choose the right shade. Warm-toned medium skin tones should opt for copper or auburn red, while cool-toned medium skin tones should go for burgundy or cherry red.

Q: Do I need to bleach my hair before coloring it?

A: It depends on the shade you want and your natural hair color. If you want a lighter shade than your natural hair color, you may need to bleach your hair first. However, bleaching can damage your hair, so it's important to consult with a professional stylist before proceeding.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen the perfect shade for your medium skin tone, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and fade the color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as it can strip away the natural oils and cause the color to fade.

Conclusion

Choosing the right shade for your medium skin tone can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your undertones, eye color, and lifestyle, you can find the perfect shade that complements your natural beauty. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Happy coloring!
