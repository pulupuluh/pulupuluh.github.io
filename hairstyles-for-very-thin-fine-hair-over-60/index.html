Hairstyles For Very Thin Fine Hair Over 60 A Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 23, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you over 60 and have very thin fine hair? It can be a challenge to find the right hairstyle that flatters your face and works with your hair type. In this guide, we’ll show you some of the best hairstyles for very thin fine hair over 60. From bobs to pixies, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Description

As we age, our hair gets thinner and finer. This can make it difficult to style and manage. However, there are many hairstyles that work well for very thin fine hair over 60. These hairstyles can add volume and dimension to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

How To

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let’s talk about how to style thin fine hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use volumizing products such as mousse or spray
  • Avoid heavy conditioners that can weigh down your hair
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume
  • Use a vent brush to lift your roots while blow drying

Step by Step

Now, let’s take a look at some of the best hairstyles for very thin fine hair over 60:

1. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for all ages. For very thin fine hair, a shorter bob is best. This will add volume and shape to your hair. Ask your stylist for a layered bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front. This will create movement and texture in your hair.

2. Pixie

A pixie is a short and sassy hairstyle that can work well for very thin fine hair. This hairstyle is low maintenance and easy to style. Ask your stylist for a choppy pixie with layers to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Shag

A shag is a layered hairstyle that can work well for very thin fine hair. This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn straight or curly. Ask your stylist for a shag with choppy layers that are shorter in the back and longer in the front. This will add volume and movement to your hair.

4. Layered Lob

A layered lob is a longer version of a bob. This hairstyle is great for very thin fine hair as it adds volume and shape. Ask your stylist for a lob with layers that start at the chin. This will create movement and texture in your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling very thin fine hair:

  • Avoid overusing hair products as they can weigh down your hair
  • Use a root lifter to add volume to your roots
  • Avoid overstyling your hair as this can cause damage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to avoid breakage

FAQ

Q: Can I still color my hair if I have very thin fine hair?

A: Yes, you can still color your hair. Just be sure to use gentle hair color products that won’t damage your hair.

Q: What length of hair is best for very thin fine hair over 60?

A: Shorter hairstyles such as bobs and pixies are best for very thin fine hair over 60. However, if you prefer longer hair, a layered lob can work well.

Hair Care

To keep your very thin fine hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to:

  • Use gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals

With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock your very thin fine hair over 60 with confidence. Remember to embrace your natural hair type and work with it, not against it. Happy styling!
