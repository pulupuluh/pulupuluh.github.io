How To Cut Short Curly Hair In Layers Yourself

Written by William Apr 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the salon to cut your short curly hair in layers? Do you want to learn how to do it yourself? Well, you've come to the right place! In this blog post, we'll be sharing a step-by-step guide on how to cut short curly hair in layers yourself.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Layered haircuts are perfect for those with curly hair as they add volume and definition to your curls. Cutting your hair in layers also helps to remove any damaged or split ends, leaving your hair looking healthier and more vibrant. With the right technique and tools, you can easily cut your short curly hair in layers yourself at home.

How to

To cut short curly hair in layers yourself, you'll need a few tools, including a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips. It's also important to have a good understanding of your hair's texture and type, as this will determine the best technique for your specific hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut short curly hair in layers yourself:

Step 1: Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Comb your hair to remove any tangles and divide it into small sections using hair clips.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and comb it straight up. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, then cut the hair at an angle using your scissors. This will create a layered effect.

Step 4: Move on to the next section of hair, taking small sections at a time. Repeat the process of combing the hair straight up, holding it between your fingers, and cutting it at an angle.

Step 5: Once you've finished cutting all the sections, comb your hair gently to check for any uneven layers. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair with your scissors until you're happy with the result.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your short curly hair in layers:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut and avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take small sections of hair at a time to ensure accuracy.
  • Don't cut your hair too short as it may shrink up when it dries, leaving it shorter than you intended.
  • Be patient and take your time to ensure you achieve the desired result.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short curly hair in layers:

Q: Can I cut my hair in layers if it's very curly?

A: Yes, you can cut your hair in layers regardless of how curly it is. However, you may need to adjust your technique to suit your hair's texture and type.

Q: What's the best length for layered curly hair?

A: The length of your hair will depend on your personal preference and style. However, it's best to avoid cutting your hair too short, as it may shrink up when it dries.

Hair Care

Once you've cut your short curly hair in layers, it's important to take proper care of it to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to remove any split ends and keep your hair looking fresh.

Now that you know how to cut short curly hair in layers yourself, you can save money and achieve the perfect haircut from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow these tips to achieve the best results.
