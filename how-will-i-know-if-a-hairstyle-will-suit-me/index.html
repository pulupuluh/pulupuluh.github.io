How Will I Know If A Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Written by Daniel Mar 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be challenging, especially if you're not sure what will suit you. However, there are a few things you can do to determine whether a particular hairstyle will work for you. In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you figure out how to know if a hairstyle will suit you.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Before you start looking for a new hairstyle, it's important to have a clear understanding of your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Your face shape will play a significant role in determining what styles will look best on you.

How to

The first step to determining whether a hairstyle will suit you is to consider your face shape. The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for styles that will complement your features.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, look for styles that add height to your crown and lengthen your face. Styles that are longer than chin length and have choppy layers can also be flattering.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This is the most versatile face shape and can pull off almost any style. Play around with different lengths and textures to find what suits you best.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, look for styles that soften your jawline and add width to your forehead. Soft, layered styles that fall around your face can be flattering.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, look for styles that add width to your chin and balance out your forehead. Styles that are shorter or have layers around your chin can be flattering.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, look for styles that add fullness to your forehead and jawline. Styles with layers around your cheekbones can be flattering.

Step by Step

Once you've determined your face shape, follow these steps to find a hairstyle that will suit you:

  1. Look for styles that complement your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair type and texture.
  3. Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion.
  5. Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different styles will look on you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hairstyle:

  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find what works for you.
  • Consider the maintenance required for each style before making a decision.
  • Take into account any hair concerns you may have, such as thinning hair or hair loss.
  • Choose a style that complements your personal style and fashion sense.

FAQ

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's not uncommon for people to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, look for styles that complement both shapes or focus on the shape that is most prominent.

What if I have curly hair?

Curly hair can be more challenging to style, but there are plenty of options available. Look for styles that work with the natural curl pattern of your hair, such as layers or a curly bob.

Should I follow current hair trends?

While it's always fun to try out new trends, it's important to choose a style that works for you and your face shape. Don't feel like you have to follow every trend just because it's popular.

Hair Care

Once you've found a hairstyle that suits you, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Follow these tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Use hair products that are designed for your hair type and concerns.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your style looking fresh.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and strength.

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair type, and personal style, you can find a style that suits you and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
