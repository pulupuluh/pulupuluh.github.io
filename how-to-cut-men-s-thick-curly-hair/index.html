How To Cut Men s Thick Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 13, 2023 · 6 min read

As a stylist, cutting men's thick curly hair can be a daunting task. However, with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a great cut that will leave your client feeling satisfied. In this guide, we'll go over the best practices for cutting men's thick curly hair.

Description

Men's thick curly hair can be challenging to cut due to its texture and volume. It's essential to use the right tools and techniques to ensure a great cut. A good cut will enhance the texture and volume of the hair while still maintaining its shape.

How to Cut Men's Thick Curly Hair

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin cutting, make sure your client's hair is clean and dry. Shampoo and condition the hair to remove any dirt or debris. This will make it easier to cut and style the hair.

Step 2: Assess the Hair

Take a close look at your client's hair and assess its length, texture, and volume. This will help you determine the best approach to cutting the hair. It's important to consider your client's face shape and personal style when deciding on a cut.

Step 3: Use the Right Tools

When cutting men's thick curly hair, it's essential to use the right tools. A good pair of scissors or clippers, a comb, and hair clips are all necessary. You may also want to consider using thinning shears to help remove bulk from the hair.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair and cutting one section at a time. Use your fingers or a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. It's best to cut the hair while it's dry to ensure an accurate cut. You may also want to consider cutting the hair in layers to help enhance its texture and volume.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the hair, use your scissors or clippers to blend the hair. This will help create a seamless transition between the different sections of hair.

Step 6: Style the Hair

After you've finished cutting and blending the hair, style it to your client's liking. Use products like mousse or gel to enhance the hair's texture and volume. You may also want to consider using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance the curls.

Tips for Cutting Men's Thick Curly Hair

  • Use a spray bottle to dampen the hair if it's too dry to cut
  • Work with the hair's natural texture instead of trying to fight it
  • Cut the hair in small sections to ensure an accurate cut
  • Use thinning shears to remove bulk from the hair
  • Don't be afraid to ask your client for feedback during the cutting process

FAQ

What is the best way to cut men's thick curly hair?

The best way to cut men's thick curly hair is to use the right tools and techniques. Start with clean, dry hair and assess the hair's length, texture, and volume. Use a good pair of scissors or clippers and cut the hair one section at a time. Blend the hair to create a seamless transition between the different sections.

How often should men with thick curly hair get a haircut?

Men with thick curly hair should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain their hairstyle and keep their hair healthy.

Hair Care for Men's Thick Curly Hair

Men with thick curly hair should use products that are designed for their hair type. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair hydrated and healthy. Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out the hair. It's also essential to protect the hair from the sun and heat styling tools.
