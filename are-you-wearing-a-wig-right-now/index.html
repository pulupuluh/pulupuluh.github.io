Are You Wearing A Wig Right Now Here s What You Need To Know In 2023

Written by Eveline Mar 16, 2023 · 8 min read

If you’re reading this blog post, chances are you’re wearing a wig right now or considering buying one. Wigs have become increasingly popular in recent years as a way to change up your hairstyle without committing to a cut or color. But are you wearing a wig right now that looks natural and feels comfortable? In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about wearing wigs in 2023, including how to choose the right wig and how to care for it.

What Is a Wig?

A wig is a hairpiece that covers your head and is typically made of synthetic or human hair. Wigs can be worn for a variety of reasons, including medical conditions, fashion, or personal preference. They come in many different styles, lengths, and colors, and can be customized to fit your head and style.

How to Choose the Right Wig

Choosing the right wig can be overwhelming, but it doesn’t have to be. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

The right wig can help balance out your face shape. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to look for a wig with layers to add height to your face. If you have a square face, a wig with soft layers can help soften your features.

2. Think About Color

When choosing a wig, you’ll want to consider your skin tone and eye color. If you have warm-toned skin, warm colors like reds and oranges may look best on you. If you have cool-toned skin, cooler colors like blues and greens may be more flattering.

3. Choose the Right Length

The length of your wig can also affect how it looks on you. If you’re used to short hair, a shorter wig may be a better choice. If you’re used to longer hair, you may want to opt for a longer wig.

How to Put on a Wig

Putting on a wig may seem daunting, but it’s actually quite simple. Here’s how to do it:

1. Prepare Your Hair

If you have long hair, you’ll want to braid it or put it in a low bun before putting on your wig. If you have short hair, you can simply brush it back.

2. Put on a Wig Cap

A wig cap can help keep your hair in place and make your wig more comfortable. Simply place the wig cap over your head and tuck in any loose hair.

3. Put on Your Wig

Hold your wig at the front of your head and pull it over your head, adjusting it as needed. Secure the wig in place with the adjustable straps or combs.

Wig Care Tips

To keep your wig looking its best, here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Wig

Wash your wig every 6-8 wears or when it starts to look dirty. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry on a wig stand.

2. Brush Your Wig

Brush your wig regularly to prevent tangles and keep it looking smooth. Use a wig brush or a wide-tooth comb, starting at the ends and working your way up.

3. Store Your Wig Properly

When you’re not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand to help maintain its shape. Keep it away from direct sunlight and heat sources.

FAQ

1. Can I Wear a Wig to Bed?

No, you should not wear a wig to bed. Sleeping in a wig can damage the fibers and cause tangling.

2. How Long Will My Wig Last?

The lifespan of your wig depends on how often you wear it and how well you care for it. A high-quality wig can last up to a year with proper care.

3. Can I Swim in My Wig?

No, you should not swim in your wig. Chlorine and saltwater can damage the fibers.

4. Can I Style My Wig with Heat Tools?

Yes, you can style your wig with heat tools, but be sure to use a low heat setting and a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Conclusion

Wearing a wig can be a fun and easy way to switch up your look, but it’s important to choose the right wig and care for it properly. By following the tips in this article, you can ensure that you’re wearing a wig right now that looks natural and feels comfortable.
