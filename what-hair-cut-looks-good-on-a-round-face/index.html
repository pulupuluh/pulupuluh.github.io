What Hair Cut Looks Good On A Round Face

Written by Luffy Sep 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face and are struggling with finding the perfect haircut, you're not alone. Many people with round faces find it challenging to choose a hairstyle that flatters their facial features. However, with the right haircut, you can accentuate your best features and look your best. In this article, we will discuss what hair cut looks good on a round face, and some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle.

Description

A round face is characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded jawline. The width and length of the face are almost equal, giving it a softer appearance. When choosing a haircut for a round face, the goal is to create an illusion of length and balance, giving the face a more oval shape.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Round Face

Choosing the right haircut for a round face can be a daunting task, but it's not impossible. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect haircut:

1. Keep it Long

Long hair can help elongate a round face, making it appear slimmer. However, avoid blunt cuts, as they can make the face appear wider. Instead, go for long layers that add movement and texture.

2. Avoid Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs can make a round face appear shorter, making it look wider. Instead, go for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides, as they can create the illusion of length.

3. Add Volume

Adding volume to the crown can help balance a round face. Go for hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, such as a voluminous updo or a high ponytail.

4. Go for Layers

Layers can help create the illusion of length and add movement to the hair. However, avoid short layers that add volume to the sides of the face, as they can make the face appear wider.

5. Embrace Waves and Curls

Waves and curls can help soften a round face and add dimension. Go for loose waves or curls that start from the mid-length of the hair, as they can help elongate the face.

Step by Step Guide on Getting the Perfect Haircut for a Round Face

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut for a round face, here is a step-by-step guide to help you get the perfect cut:

Step 1: Consult with Your Stylist

Before getting a haircut, consult with your stylist. They can help you choose the right haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Choose a Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that accentuates your best features and complements your face shape. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to show your stylist.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type

Consider your hair type when choosing a hairstyle. If you have thick hair, go for a layered haircut that adds movement and texture. If you have thin hair, go for a haircut that adds volume and thickness.

Step 4: Maintain Your Haircut

After getting a haircut, maintain it by getting regular trims and using the right hair care products. Use products that are suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your haircut:

1. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use the Right Hair Care Products

Use hair care products that are suitable for your hair type. If you have dry hair, use moisturizing products. If you have oily hair, use products that control oil.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dull. Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools.

FAQ

What haircut should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid blunt cuts and blunt bangs, as they can make a round face appear wider.

Can I wear short hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear short hair if you have a round face. However, go for a layered cut that adds volume and movement to the hair.

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that creates the illusion of length and balance. Go for long layers, side-swept bangs, and hairstyles that add volume to the crown.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it is gentler on the hair and prevents breakage.

2. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, making it look dull and dry. Aim to wash your hair every other day.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair care product that contains SPF.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for a round face can be challenging, but it's not impossible. By following the tips in this article, you can find the perfect haircut that accentuates your best features and complements your face shape.
