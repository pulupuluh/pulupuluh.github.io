Best Haircuts For Wavy Hair Guys

Written by Luffy May 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are a guy with wavy hair, you may find it difficult to find the right haircut that suits your hair type. Wavy hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right haircut, you can flaunt your waves with style. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for wavy hair guys, how to style them, and tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Wavy Hair Guys

Before choosing a haircut, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Wavy hair tends to have a natural volume and texture that can be emphasized with the right haircut. Here are some of the best haircuts for wavy hair guys:

1. Wavy Comb Over

The wavy comb-over is a classic haircut that works well for wavy hair. This hairstyle involves combing the hair to one side, creating a parting, and leaving the hair on top longer than the sides. This haircut is perfect for guys with oval or square-shaped faces.

2. Messy Waves

Messy waves are a popular hairstyle for guys with wavy hair. This haircut involves cutting the hair to a medium length and creating layers to add texture and volume. This hairstyle works well for guys with round or heart-shaped faces.

3. Short Wavy Haircut

If you prefer a shorter haircut, the short wavy haircut is an excellent option. This haircut works well for guys with thick wavy hair and involves cutting the hair to a short length on the sides and back, leaving the hair on top longer. This hairstyle works well for guys with oval or square-shaped faces.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Wavy Hair

Styling wavy hair can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a stylish look. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling wavy hair:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using hot water as it can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as it can cause damage and frizz.

3. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product such as a mousse or a gel to your damp hair. This will help define your waves and keep them in place.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help maintain your natural waves and prevent frizz. Avoid using a high heat setting as it can damage your hair.

5. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it using your fingers or a comb. You can create a side parting, slick back your hair or leave it messy for a more casual look.

Tips to Keep Your Wavy Hair Healthy and Shiny

Here are some tips to keep your wavy hair healthy and shiny:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Avoid Hot Water

Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

5. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair first.

FAQs

Q. Can I use a hairbrush on my wavy hair?

A. It's best to use a wide-tooth comb on your wavy hair to prevent damage and breakage.

Q. How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A. It's best to wash your wavy hair every other day or every three days to keep it healthy and hydrated.

Wavy Hair Care

Wavy hair requires a little extra care to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoid hot water, and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, and protect your hair from heat styling tools. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can flaunt your wavy hair with style.
