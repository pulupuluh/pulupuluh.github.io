How To Control Your Frizzy Curly Hair

Written by Robby Jun 22, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Frizzy curly hair can be a nightmare to manage, but don't worry, you're not alone! Many people with curly hair struggle with frizz, especially in humid weather. However, there are ways to control your frizz and embrace your beautiful curls. In this article, we'll discuss how to control your frizzy curly hair and provide you with some tips and tricks for managing your locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Control Your Frizzy Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Your Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Frizzy Curly Hair

How to Control Your Frizzy Curly Hair

The first step in controlling your frizzy curly hair is to understand your hair type. Curly hair is naturally drier and more prone to breakage than straight hair, so it requires extra moisture and care. Here's how to get started:

1. Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by choosing a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil. These ingredients will help moisturize your hair and reduce frizz.

2. Use a Microfiber Towel or T-Shirt to Dry Your Hair

Avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair after washing it, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or a soft t-shirt to gently blot your hair. This will help absorb excess water without roughing up your hair cuticle.

3. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing and drying your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize your curls. Apply the conditioner evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help smooth down any frizz and keep your curls looking defined.

4. Use a Diffuser When Blow-Drying Your Hair

If you need to blow-dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Use a low heat setting and hold the dryer several inches away from your hair to avoid damage.

5. Use a Curl-Defining Cream or Gel

Finish off your styling routine by applying a curl-defining cream or gel. This will help hold your curls in place and reduce frizz. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for controlling your frizzy curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  2. Gently blot your hair dry with a microfiber towel or t-shirt.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your curls.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  5. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Managing Your Curls

Here are some additional tips for managing your frizzy curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause even more frizz.
  • Try using a hair oil or serum to add extra moisture to your curls.
  • Consider getting a haircut that's specifically designed for curly hair, as this can help reduce frizz and make your curls more manageable.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about controlling frizzy curly hair:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair no more than 2-3 times per week, as washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even more prone to frizz.

Q: What's the best way to refresh my curls in between washes?

A: You can refresh your curls by spritzing them with a mixture of water and leave-in conditioner, or by using a curl refreshing spray.

Q: Can I use a regular towel to dry my hair?

A: It's best to avoid using a regular towel, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or a soft t-shirt to gently blot your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for managing your frizzy curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help moisturize your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and make it even more prone to frizz.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a month to give your curls an extra boost of moisture.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.

By following these tips and tricks, you can control your frizzy curly hair and embrace your beautiful curls. Remember, it's all about finding the right products and techniques that work for your hair type. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new things until you find what works best for you.
