Square Face Short Curly Hair How To Style And Care For Your Hair

Written by Luffy Apr 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a square face and short curly hair, you may be wondering how to style and care for your hair. Fortunately, there are many options available to you. In this blog post, we will provide you with tips on how to style your hair, how to care for it, and answer some frequently asked questions that you may have.

Description

A square face is defined by a wide forehead, strong jawline, and a square chin. Short curly hair can be a great choice for this face shape because it adds volume and texture to the hair, creating a more balanced look. However, it's important to choose the right style to complement your features.

How to Style

When it comes to styling your square face short curly hair, there are many options available. Here are some tips:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a great option for square faces because it softens the angles of the face. To style your pixie cut, apply some curl defining cream to your damp hair and scrunch it with your hands. Let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great option for square faces. To style your bob, use a curling iron to create loose waves. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

If you want to switch up your look, try a half-up half-down hairstyle. To create this style, simply take the top half of your hair and pull it back into a ponytail or bun. Leave the bottom half of your hair curly and loose.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your square face short curly hair:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and brittle.

2. Apply Curl Defining Cream

Apply a curl defining cream to your damp hair. This will help to enhance your natural curls and keep them defined.

3. Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to help distribute the product evenly. This will also help to enhance your curls.

4. Air Dry or Use a Diffuser

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your curls.

5. Style as Desired

Style your hair as desired. You can leave it loose and curly, or try one of the styles mentioned above.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for your square face short curly hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and damage.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as much as possible. Heat can cause your hair to become dry and brittle.

3. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQ

1. Can I dye my curly hair?

Yes, you can dye your curly hair. However, it's important to choose a dye that is safe for your hair type and to follow the instructions carefully.

2. How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days. Washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and brittle.

3. How can I prevent frizz?

To prevent frizz, try using a leave-in conditioner and a hair oil to help keep your hair moisturized. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your curls without causing frizz.

Hair Care

When it comes to taking care of your square face short curly hair, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, as well as curl defining creams and hair masks. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, and try to let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. With the right care and styling, you can rock your short curly hair with confidence!
