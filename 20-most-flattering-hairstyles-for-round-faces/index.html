20 Most Flattering Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Aprilia May 22, 2023 · 11 min read

If you have a round face, you know the struggle of finding the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. Fortunately, there are plenty of hairstyles that can enhance your beauty and make your face appear slimmer. In this article, we’ll be discussing the 20 most flattering hairstyles for round faces, along with tips and tricks on how to achieve them and take care of your hair.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that can be flattering for round faces. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance and edgy look. The length of the pixie cut can vary, but it’s usually cut short around the sides and back, with longer layers on top. To style a pixie cut, use a small amount of pomade or wax and run it through your hair with your fingers. You can also use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that can be customized to flatter any face shape, including round faces. The length of the bob can vary, but it’s typically cut just above the shoulders. To style a bob cut, blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement. You can also add some loose waves with a curling iron or straighten your hair for a sleek look.

Long Bob

A long bob, or “lob,” is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn straight or wavy. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a longer haircut that still flatters their round face. The length of the lob can vary, but it’s typically cut just below the shoulders. To style a long bob, use a curling iron to create loose waves or straighten your hair for a sleek look.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair while also flattering your round face. Layers can be added to any haircut, including the pixie cut, bob cut, and long bob. To style a layered cut, blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement. You can also use a curling iron to add some loose waves.

Angled Cut

An angled cut, also known as an A-line cut, is a great way to elongate your face and flatter your round features. This hairstyle is shorter in the back and longer in the front, creating an angled shape that frames your face. To style an angled cut, blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement. You can also add some loose waves with a curling iron or straighten your hair for a sleek look.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a unique and edgy hairstyle that can flatter round faces. This hairstyle is typically shorter on one side and longer on the other, creating an asymmetrical shape that adds dimension to your hair. To style an asymmetrical cut, use a small amount of pomade or wax and run it through your hair with your fingers. You can also use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

Curly Hairstyles

Curly hairstyles can be flattering for round faces, as long as the curls are loose and voluminous. Avoid tight curls or ringlets, as they can make your face appear rounder. To style curly hair, use a curl-enhancing product and diffuse your hair with a blow dryer. You can also use a curling iron to add some loose waves.

Wavy Hairstyles

Wavy hairstyles can be a great way to add texture and movement to your hair, while also flattering your round face. To style wavy hair, use a texturizing spray and scrunch your hair with your fingers. You can also use a curling iron to add some loose waves.

Braided Hairstyles

Braided hairstyles can be a fun and unique way to flatter your round face. Braids can be incorporated into any hairstyle, including the pixie cut, bob cut, and long bob. To style braids, use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to keep your hair in place, and braid your hair into your desired style.

Ponytail Hairstyles

Ponytail hairstyles can be a simple and easy way to flatter your round face. To style a ponytail, gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair tie. You can also add some volume to your ponytail by teasing the crown of your head with a comb.

Updo Hairstyles

Updo hairstyles can be a sophisticated and elegant way to flatter your round face. To style an updo, gather your hair at the back of your head and twist it into a bun or chignon. You can also add some volume to your updo by teasing the crown of your head with a comb.

Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to flatter your round face, as long as they are long and side-swept. Avoid blunt bangs or baby bangs, as they can make your face appear rounder. To style bangs, blow dry them with a round brush and sweep them to the side.

Highlights

Highlights can be a great way to add dimension and depth to your hair, while also flattering your round face. Highlights can be added to any hairstyle, including the pixie cut, bob cut, and long bob. To style highlighted hair, use a curling iron to add some loose waves or straighten your hair for a sleek look.

Lowlights

Lowlights can be a great way to add depth and richness to your hair, while also flattering your round face. Lowlights can be added to any hairstyle, including the pixie cut, bob cut, and long bob. To style lowlighted hair, use a curling iron to add some loose waves or straighten your hair for a sleek look.

Balayage

Balayage is a French hair coloring technique that can be a great way to flatter your round face. Balayage involves painting highlights onto your hair in a natural and subtle way. To style balayaged hair, use a curling iron to add some loose waves or straighten your hair for a sleek look.

Ombre

Ombre is a hair coloring technique that can be a great way to flatter your round face. Ombre involves fading your hair color from dark to light, creating a gradient effect. To style ombre hair, use a curling iron to add some loose waves or straighten your hair for a sleek look.

Fishtail Braid

A fishtail braid is a fun and unique way to flatter your round face. To style a fishtail braid, divide your hair into two sections and take a small piece of hair from the outside of one section.
