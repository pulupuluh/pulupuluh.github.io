Why Guys Like Curly Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Feb 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair has always been one of the most attractive features in women. It's no wonder why guys like curly hair. It adds a touch of personality, femininity, and playfulness to any woman's look. But why are guys so drawn to this type of hair? Here's everything you need to know about why guys love curly hair.

What Makes Curly Hair So Attractive?

Guys like curly hair because it's unique and different. It adds texture and volume to a woman's hair, making it more interesting than straight hair. Curly hair also has a certain bounce and movement that's alluring to guys.

Another reason why guys like curly hair is that it's playful and fun. Women with curly hair are often seen as carefree and adventurous, which can be attractive to guys.

How to Get Curly Hair

If you're interested in trying out curly hair, there are a few ways to achieve the look. One of the easiest ways is to use a curling iron or hot rollers. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. Then, section your hair and wrap it around the curling iron or hot roller. Hold the hair for a few seconds before releasing it. Repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.

You can also try using a perm. A perm is a chemical treatment that makes your hair curly. It's best to get a perm done by a professional stylist, as it can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curl formation.
  4. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on low heat.
  5. Once your hair is dry, scrunch it again to soften any crunchy curls.
  6. Optional: use a curling iron or wand to touch up any loose curls.
  7. Finish with a lightweight hairspray or oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Comb your hair with a wide-tooth comb while it's wet to avoid damaging your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can be damaging to your hair.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do all guys like curly hair?

No, not all guys like curly hair. Attraction is subjective and varies from person to person.

What if I have naturally straight hair?

If you have naturally straight hair and want to try out curly hair, you can use a curling iron or perm. Just make sure to use a heat protectant and deep condition your hair regularly to avoid damage.

Can I wear my curly hair in a professional setting?

Yes, you can wear your curly hair in a professional setting. Just make sure to style it neatly and use a lightweight hairspray or oil to keep it in place.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out. You can use a dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair.

Conclusion

In conclusion, guys like curly hair because it's unique, playful, and fun. If you're interested in trying out curly hair, there are a few ways to achieve the look. Just make sure to take care of your hair and maintain it properly to keep it healthy and vibrant.
