How To Look Good With Platinum Blonde Hair

Written by Luffy Jun 27, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you thinking of going platinum blonde? Platinum blonde hair is a striking and bold look. It can give you an edgy and unique style, but it can also be a tricky color to pull off. In this article, we’ll give you tips on how to look good with platinum blonde hair, including hair care, styling, and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Platinum Blonde Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Platinum Blonde Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Platinum Blonde Hair
  • FAQ about Platinum Blonde Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Platinum Blonde Hair

How to Get Platinum Blonde Hair

Before you can look good with platinum blonde hair, you need to know how to get it. If you’re a natural blonde, it may be easier for you to achieve the platinum blonde look. However, if you have dark hair, you will need to go through a bleaching process, which can be damaging to your hair. It’s important to find a professional hair stylist who specializes in platinum blonde hair to avoid damaging your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Platinum Blonde Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving platinum blonde hair:

  1. Find a professional hair stylist who specializes in platinum blonde hair.
  2. Have a consultation with your stylist to determine the best approach for your hair.
  3. If you have dark hair, your stylist will need to bleach your hair to remove the color.
  4. After the bleaching process, your stylist will apply a toner to achieve the platinum blonde color.
  5. Maintain your platinum blonde hair with regular touch-ups and hair care.

Tips for Maintaining Platinum Blonde Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining platinum blonde hair:

  • Use a purple shampoo to prevent your hair from turning yellow.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine or salt water, as they can cause your hair to become dry and brittle.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a deep conditioning treatment.

FAQ about Platinum Blonde Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about platinum blonde hair:

How often should I touch up my roots?

You should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to maintain your platinum blonde hair color.

Can I dye my hair back to its natural color after going platinum blonde?

Yes, but you will need to go through a color correction process with a professional hair stylist to avoid damaging your hair.

Is platinum blonde hair high maintenance?

Yes, platinum blonde hair is high maintenance. You will need to touch up your roots regularly and use hair care products specifically for platinum blonde hair.

Hair Care Tips for Platinum Blonde Hair

Here are some hair care tips for platinum blonde hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can cause your hair to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hair care products that contain alcohol, as they can be drying to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips and maintaining your platinum blonde hair, you can look good and feel confident with your bold and edgy style. Remember to find a professional hair stylist who specializes in platinum blonde hair to avoid damaging your hair during the bleaching process.
