How To Describe Men s Haircut To Stylist A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby May 28, 2023 · 8 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, communication is key. However, sometimes it can be difficult to describe exactly what you want to your stylist. This can be especially true for men who may not be as familiar with different haircut terms. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to describe men’s haircut to stylist in a relaxed English language that anyone can understand.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Describe Men’s Haircut to Stylist
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Describing Your Haircut to Stylist
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Before we get into how to describe men’s haircut to stylist, it’s important to understand the different types of haircuts. There are many different styles and lengths to choose from, and each requires a different approach to cutting and styling.

Some popular styles include:

  • Short back and sides
  • Crew cut
  • Buzz cut
  • Taper
  • Undercut
  • Pompadour
  • Quiff
  • Side part

Each style has its own unique characteristics, and it’s important to know what you want before you sit down in the stylist’s chair. This will help you explain your desired haircut to your stylist more effectively.

How to Describe Men’s Haircut to Stylist

Now that you have an idea of the different types of haircuts, it’s time to learn how to describe men’s haircut to stylist. Here are some tips:

Know the Terminology

One of the most important things you can do when describing your haircut to your stylist is to know the terminology. This includes understanding terms like fade, taper, and undercut. Knowing the terminology will help you communicate your desired haircut more clearly.

Show Pictures

If you’re having trouble describing your desired haircut, consider bringing in pictures. This can help your stylist understand exactly what you want and give them a visual reference to work from.

Be Specific

When describing your haircut, it’s important to be as specific as possible. This includes the length you want, the style you’re going for, and any other details that are important to you. The more specific you are, the better chance you have of getting the haircut you want.

Ask for Advice

Don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice. They are professionals and can offer suggestions that you may not have considered. They can also tell you if the style you want will work with your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to describe men’s haircut to stylist:

  1. Know the terminology
  2. Bring in pictures if necessary
  3. Describe the length you want
  4. Describe the style you’re going for
  5. Be specific about any details that are important to you
  6. Ask for advice if you’re unsure
  7. Confirm the details with your stylist before they start cutting
  8. Communicate any changes you want during the haircut
  9. Ask for styling tips
  10. Tip your stylist if you’re happy with the result

Tips for Describing Your Haircut to Stylist

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when describing your haircut to your stylist:

  • Don’t be afraid to speak up if you’re not happy with the result
  • Be open to suggestions from your stylist
  • Consider your hair type and face shape when choosing a style
  • Bring in pictures from different angles to give your stylist a clear idea of what you want
  • Ask for styling tips so you can maintain your new haircut at home

FAQ

What should I do if I’m not happy with my haircut?

If you’re not happy with your haircut, speak up. Let your stylist know what you don’t like and what you want changed. They should be willing to work with you to make sure you’re happy with the result.

What if I don’t know what style I want?

If you’re unsure about what style you want, consider asking your stylist for suggestions. They can recommend styles that will work with your hair type and face shape.

How often should I get a haircut?

The frequency of your haircuts will depend on your hair type and desired style. Generally, it’s recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care Tips

Once you’ve gotten your new haircut, it’s important to take care of it to ensure it looks great for as long as possible. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a comb or brush that’s appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style

By following these tips and communicating effectively with your stylist, you can ensure that you get the perfect haircut every time.
