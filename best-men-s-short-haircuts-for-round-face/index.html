Best Men s Short Haircuts For Round Face

Written by Christine Jul 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that don't suit your round face shape? Don't worry; we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best men's short haircuts for a round face shape. You can choose from these haircuts according to your preference and face shape to look your best. So, let's get started.

Description

A round face shape has a softer jawline and a wider forehead, which can make it challenging to find the right haircut. However, short haircuts can work wonders for a round face shape as they create an illusion of a longer face. Short haircuts also add volume to the top of the head and make the face appear slimmer.

When choosing a short haircut for a round face shape, it's essential to consider your hair texture, face shape, and personal style. We have compiled a list of the best men's short haircuts for round faces to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for yourself.

How To

When getting a short haircut for a round face shape, it's essential to keep a few things in mind. Firstly, avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of the face. Secondly, go for haircuts that add height to the crown of your head. Lastly, avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face appear wider.

When going to the salon, be specific about the type of haircut you want. Take a picture of the hairstyle you like and show it to your stylist. They will be able to advise you on whether the haircut will suit your face shape or not.

Step by Step

1. Choose a haircut that suits your hair texture, face shape, and personal style.

2. Show a picture of the hairstyle you want to your stylist.

3. Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face.

4. Go for haircuts that add height to the crown of your head.

5. Avoid haircuts that are too short.

6. Maintain your haircut by visiting the salon regularly.

Tips

1. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

2. Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair and make your face appear wider.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best.

4. Consult your stylist before getting a haircut.

5. Keep your hair trimmed to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

1. What are the best short haircuts for a round face shape?

The best short haircuts for a round face shape are buzz cuts, crew cuts, and textured crops.

2. Can I get a short haircut if I have a round face shape?

Yes, you can get a short haircut if you have a round face shape. However, it's essential to choose the right hairstyle to flatter your face shape.

3. How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every four to six weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain the shape of your haircut. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair and make your face appear wider. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best. Consult your stylist before getting a haircut and keep your hair trimmed to maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, these were the best men's short haircuts for round face shapes. Choose the one that suits your face shape and personal style to look your best. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the shape of your haircut. Happy hairstyling!
