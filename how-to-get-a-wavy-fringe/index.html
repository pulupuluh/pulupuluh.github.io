How To Get A Wavy Fringe Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Feb 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your straight, boring fringe? Want to add some texture and movement to your hair? Look no further than a wavy fringe! This trendy style is easy to achieve and can give your hair a fun, playful look. In this guide, we'll show you how to get a wavy fringe step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Wavy Fringe
  • Tips for Perfecting Your Wavy Fringe
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Fringes

How to Get a Wavy Fringe

The first step in achieving a wavy fringe is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can use a texturizing spray or mousse to add some grip to your hair before styling.

Start by sectioning off your fringe from the rest of your hair. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling wand, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Hold the hair on the wand for a few seconds before releasing it. Repeat this process until all of your fringe is curled.

Once your fringe is curled, use your fingers to gently comb through the curls. This will create soft, loose waves. You can also use a wide-toothed comb to create more defined waves.

Finish off your wavy fringe with a light mist of hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Alternative Method

If you don't have a curling wand, you can achieve a wavy fringe using a flat iron. Start by sectioning off your fringe and twisting it away from your face. Hold the flat iron at the base of the twisted section and slowly slide it down towards the ends. This will create a soft, wavy texture.

Tips for Perfecting Your Wavy Fringe

Here are some additional tips to help you get the perfect wavy fringe:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling to avoid damaging your hair.
  • For more defined waves, use a smaller curling wand or flat iron.
  • Experiment with different directions and angles when curling your fringe to create a more natural look.
  • Don't overdo it with hairspray - a light misting is all you need to hold the waves in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I maintain my wavy fringe?

To keep your wavy fringe looking its best, avoid brushing it too much as this can cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers to gently tousle your hair throughout the day. You can also use a texturizing spray to add some extra hold and definition.

Can I achieve a wavy fringe with naturally straight hair?

Yes, you can definitely achieve a wavy fringe with naturally straight hair. Just be sure to use a texturizing product before styling to add some grip to your hair.

Can I wear my wavy fringe with a middle part?

Absolutely! Wavy fringes look great with both side and middle parts. Experiment with different parting styles to see which one you like best.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Fringes

Wavy fringes require some extra care to keep them looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a perfect wavy fringe in no time. Happy styling!
