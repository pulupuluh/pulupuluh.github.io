How To Install A Frontal Wig Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 06, 2023 · 6 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on how to install a frontal wig. Frontal wigs are a popular option for those who want a natural-looking hairline. They come in different types, including lace frontals and silk frontals. Installing a frontal wig can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a flawless look. In this guide, we’ll take you through the steps to install a frontal wig.

Description

A frontal wig is a type of wig that has a lace or silk base that covers the entire front hairline. It is designed to give the illusion of a natural-looking hairline. Frontal wigs come in different lengths and styles, and you can customize them to suit your preferences.

How to Install a Frontal Wig

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair and Skin

Before installing your frontal wig, you need to prepare your hair and skin. First, braid your hair into cornrows to create a flat surface for the wig to sit on. Next, cleanse your skin with alcohol to remove any oils or dirt that could affect the adhesive.

Step 2: Cut the Lace

The next step is to cut the lace on the frontal wig. Use scissors to cut the lace along the hairline, leaving about a quarter-inch of lace. Be careful not to cut too close to the hairline as this could cause the wig to fray.

Step 3: Apply Adhesive

Apply a thin layer of adhesive along the hairline using a brush or applicator. Be careful not to apply too much as this could cause the wig to shift. Let the adhesive dry for a few seconds until it becomes tacky.

Step 4: Place the Wig

Position the wig on your head, starting from the front and working your way to the back. Use your fingers to press the wig onto your skin along the hairline. Be careful not to pull the wig too tight as this could cause discomfort.

Step 5: Style the Wig

Once the wig is in place, style it to your liking. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to create curls or waves. Be careful not to apply too much heat as this could damage the wig.

Tips

  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place.
  • Trim the lace to fit your hairline.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your wig.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape.

FAQ

How long does a frontal wig last?

A frontal wig can last up to a year with proper care and maintenance.

Can I swim with a frontal wig?

It is not recommended to swim with a frontal wig as the chlorine and saltwater can damage the hair.

How do I remove a frontal wig?

To remove a frontal wig, use a wig adhesive remover and gently lift the wig off your skin.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the look and feel of your frontal wig. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig every two weeks with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as this can damage the hair.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape.

Congratulations, you have successfully installed your frontal wig! With the right care and maintenance, your wig will look great for a long time.
