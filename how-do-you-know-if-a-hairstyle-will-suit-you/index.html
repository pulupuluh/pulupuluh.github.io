How Do You Know If A Hairstyle Will Suit You

Written by Bardi May 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure if it will suit you or not. It's important to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, accentuates your best features, and complements your personal style. Here are some tips on how to know if a hairstyle will suit you.

Table of Contents

  • Determine Your Face Shape
  • Consider Your Hair Type
  • Take Your Personal Style into Account

  • Consult a Hairstylist

Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a hairstyle that suits you is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has certain hairstyles that flatter it best.

If you're not sure what your face shape is, you can do a quick Google search or consult with a hairstylist. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will enhance your features and balance your proportions.

Consider Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Your hair type can range from straight and fine to curly and thick. Each hair type has its own set of challenges and styling options.

You should choose a hairstyle that works with your hair type, not against it. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, a short bob may not be the best choice. On the other hand, if you have fine, straight hair, a layered cut can add volume and texture.

Take Your Personal Style into Account

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and make you feel confident.

If you're someone who likes to keep things low-maintenance, a short pixie cut may be a great option. On the other hand, if you love to experiment with different styles and trends, a long layered cut may be more your style.

Use Haircut Try-On Apps

If you're still unsure about which hairstyle will suit you, you can use haircut try-on apps to see how different styles will look on you. These apps allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles virtually.

While these apps can be helpful, keep in mind that they may not always be accurate. Lighting and angles can affect how a hairstyle looks on camera, so it's always best to consult with a hairstylist before making a drastic change.

Consult a Hairstylist

The best way to know if a hairstyle will suit you is to consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can assess your face shape, hair type, and personal style to recommend a hairstyle that will flatter you best.

During your consultation, be sure to bring photos of hairstyles you like and be open to the stylist's suggestions. They may recommend a style you hadn't considered before that will look great on you.

FAQs

What hairstyles suit round faces?

Hairstyles that elongate the face, such as long layers and side-swept bangs, tend to flatter round faces. Avoid blunt cuts and full bangs, as they can make the face appear wider.

What hairstyles suit square faces?

Hairstyles that soften the angles of the face, such as layered cuts and side-swept bangs, tend to flatter square faces. Avoid straight, blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can accentuate the jawline.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found a hairstyle that suits you, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can maintain a hairstyle that suits you and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
