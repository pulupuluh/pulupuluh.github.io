Does Short Hair Make You Look Shorter Or Taller

Written by William Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair has always been a trend among women and men alike. It's easy to manage, requires less time, and can give you a refreshing look. However, there's an ongoing debate whether short hair makes you look shorter or taller. In this blog post, we'll answer this question and provide you with tips on how to choose the right short hairstyle that suits your body type.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Choose The Right Short Hairstyle
  3. Step By Step Guide
  4. Tips To Keep In Mind
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

Short hair can make you look both shorter and taller, depending on the length and style of your hair. If you have a short neck, a short bob can make your neck appear longer, making you look taller. On the other hand, if you have a long neck, a short hairstyle can make you look shorter.

Another factor that affects how short hair makes you look is your face shape. If you have a round face, a short hairstyle with layers and bangs can make your face appear slimmer and longer, making you look taller. However, if you have a long face, a short hairstyle can make your face appear even longer, making you look shorter.

How To Choose The Right Short Hairstyle

The key to choosing the right short hairstyle is to consider your body type and face shape. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short hairstyle:

  • If you have a short neck, choose a short bob that falls just below your chin.
  • If you have a long neck, choose a shorter bob that falls above your chin.
  • If you have a round face, choose a short hairstyle with layers and bangs that frame your face.
  • If you have a long face, choose a short hairstyle with volume on top to balance out the length of your face.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right short hairstyle:

  1. Determine your face shape and body type.
  2. Research different short hairstyles that suit your face shape and body type.
  3. Visit a hairstylist and ask for their opinion on which short hairstyle suits you best.
  4. Try on different short hairstyles using virtual makeover apps or by trying on wigs.
  5. Select the short hairstyle that you feel most comfortable and confident with.

Tips To Keep In Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

  • Consider the maintenance required for the short hairstyle.
  • Choose a short hairstyle that complements your personality and lifestyle.
  • Experiment with different hair colors to enhance your short hairstyle.
  • Accessorize your short hairstyle with headbands, clips, and scarves.

FAQs

Q: Does short hair make you look older?

A: Short hair can make you look older or younger, depending on the style and color of your hair. A simple, classic short hairstyle can make you look sophisticated and mature, while a trendy, edgy short hairstyle can make you look younger.

Q: Can short hair make your face look wider?

A: Yes, a short hairstyle that's too wide or bulky can make your face look wider. It's important to choose a short hairstyle that's proportional to your face shape and body type.

Q: Can short hair make you look taller?

A: Yes, a short hairstyle that's tailored to your body type and face shape can make you look taller.

Hair Care Tips

Short hair requires less maintenance compared to long hair, but it still needs proper care to look its best. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair moisturized and frizz-free.

Now that you know whether short hair makes you look shorter or taller, it's time to choose the right short hairstyle that suits your body type and face shape. Remember to consider your personality and lifestyle, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and colors. With the right care and maintenance, your short hair will look fabulous and make you feel confident.
