Description

Choosing the right hijab style for your face shape can be challenging, especially if you have a round face. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing the best hijab styles for round faces that will enhance your features and make you feel confident.

How to

Before we get started, it's important to understand the basic principles of hijab styling for round faces. The goal is to create the illusion of length and angles to balance out the roundness of your face. This can be achieved by:

  • Choosing hijabs with volume on the top and slimming down towards the bottom.
  • Wearing hijabs with angular edges to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Avoiding hijabs that are too tight and flat on the top.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step guides on how to achieve the best hijab styles for round faces:

1. The Turban Hijab

The turban hijab is a great option for round faces as it adds volume on top and elongates the face. Here's how to style it:

  1. Start by wearing an underscarf or bonnet to create volume on top.
  2. Take a rectangular scarf and fold it in half diagonally to create a triangle.
  3. Place the scarf on top of your head with the long side on one side of your shoulder and the short side on the other.
  4. Take the long side and wrap it around your head, tucking in the end at the back.
  5. Take the short side and wrap it around your head, tucking in the end at the front.
  6. Adjust the scarf to cover your neck and chest.

2. The Side-Drapped Hijab

The side-draped hijab is another great option for round faces as it creates angles and length. Here's how to style it:

  1. Start by wearing an underscarf or bonnet to create volume on top.
  2. Take a rectangular scarf and place it on top of your head with one side longer than the other.
  3. Wrap the longer side around your head and bring it to the other side of your neck.
  4. Take the shorter side and drape it over your shoulder, letting it fall diagonally across your chest.
  5. Adjust the scarf to cover your neck and chest.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing hijab styles for round faces:

  • Choose hijabs with bold prints or textures to add dimension and structure to your face.
  • Avoid hijabs that are too tight or flat on the top as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Experiment with different styles and see what works best for you!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hijab styles for round faces:

  1. Can I wear a hijab with a round face? Absolutely! The key is to choose styles that create angles and length to balance out the roundness of your face.
  2. What hijab styles should I avoid? Avoid hijabs that are too tight or flat on the top, as well as hijabs that add volume to the sides of your face.
  3. Can I wear a hijab with glasses? Yes, you can! Just make sure to choose a hijab style that doesn't interfere with your glasses.

Hair Care

When wearing hijabs, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
