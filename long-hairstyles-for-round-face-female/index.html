Long Hairstyles For Round Face Female Finding The Perfect Look

Written by Robby May 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want something that will flatter your features and make you feel confident, but it can be hard to know where to start. That's why we've put together this guide to long hairstyles for round face female. Whether you prefer curly or straight, voluminous or sleek, we've got you covered. Read on to find your perfect look.

Description:

A round face is typically characterized by a soft, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Long hairstyles are great for this, as they can help to draw the eye downward and create a more balanced appearance.

How to:

When choosing a long hairstyle for a round face, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for styles that add height and length to your face, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, and updos.

Another important consideration is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can make your hair look flat and lifeless. Instead, opt for layered styles that add volume and movement. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to avoid styles that add too much bulk, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for styles that are more streamlined and sleek.

Step by Step:

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating some of our favorite long hairstyles for round face female:

The Side-Swept Bang:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair to the side.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and comb it forward.
  4. Cut the hair at an angle, so that it's longer on one side than the other.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Use a flat iron to smooth your hair and create a sleek, polished look.
  7. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

The Layered Look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tease it to create volume.
  4. Cut layers into your hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  5. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to your hair.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips:

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a long hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as these can make your face appear wider.
  • Opt for styles that add height and length to your face.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a style.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about long hairstyles for round face female:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt cuts and heavy bangs. Instead, opt for styles that add height and length to your face, such as long layers and side-swept bangs.

What hairstyles are best for a round face?

Long hairstyles are great for a round face, as they can help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Consider styles that add height and length to your face, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, and updos.

Hair Care:

When it comes to maintaining your long hairstyle, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, make sure to use a quality shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. You may also want to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

When styling your hair, be sure to use heat protectant products to prevent damage from heat styling tools. You should also avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and leave it looking dull and lifeless.

Finally, consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. With these tips in mind, you'll be able to maintain your long hairstyle and keep your round face looking its best.
