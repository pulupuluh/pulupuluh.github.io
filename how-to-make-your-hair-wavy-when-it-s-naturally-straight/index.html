How To Make Your Hair Wavy When It s Naturally Straight

Written by Robby Mar 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to change up your look? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll be discussing how to make your hair wavy when it's naturally straight. Whether you're going for a beachy vibe or just want to add some texture to your hair, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description

Having naturally straight hair can sometimes feel like a curse. It can be hard to style and doesn't always hold curls or waves well. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful, wavy hair that will last all day. In this article, we'll be sharing our top tips for making your hair wavy when it's naturally straight.

How To

There are several ways to make your hair wavy when it's naturally straight. Here are our top methods:

Method 1: Braids

Braids are a classic way to create waves in your hair. Simply braid your hair while it's damp and leave it in overnight. In the morning, undo the braids and you'll have beautiful, natural-looking waves. You can also braid your hair while it's dry and use a curling iron to set the waves.

Method 2: Curling Iron

A curling iron is a great tool for creating waves in straight hair. To achieve a natural look, wrap small sections of hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds. Then release and move onto the next section. Once you've curled your entire head, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create waves.

Method 3: Salt Spray

A salt spray is a great product for creating beachy waves. Simply spray the product onto damp hair and scrunch with your hands. You can also blow dry your hair using a diffuser to enhance the waves.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair wavy when it's naturally straight:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add texture to your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a texturizing product, such as a salt spray, to your damp hair. This will help create waves and add texture to your hair.

Step 3: Braid or Curl

Choose your preferred method for creating waves, whether it's braids or a curling iron. If you're using a curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray beforehand.

Step 4: Set Waves

Once you've created your waves, use a hairspray to set them in place. This will help them last all day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair wavy when it's naturally straight:

  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of natural oils that help hold waves.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will help prevent breakage.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a bun or braids to create natural-looking waves.
  • Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to enhance natural waves.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about making your hair wavy when it's naturally straight:

Can I make my hair wavy without heat?

Yes! Braids are a great way to create waves without heat. Simply braid your hair while it's damp and leave it in overnight for natural-looking waves.

How long will my waves last?

This depends on your hair type and the method you used to create the waves. Generally, waves should last at least a day or two.

What type of products should I use?

Texturizing products, such as salt sprays or mousses, are great for creating waves in straight hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're using heat tools or styling products. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Avoid using heat tools every day, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your hair wavy when it's naturally straight. Experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
