What To Do When Hair Is Falling Out A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're experiencing hair loss, you're not alone. Hair loss affects millions of people around the world, and it can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, stress, hormonal imbalances, and more. Fortunately, there are steps you can take to slow down or even reverse hair loss. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about what to do when hair is falling out.

Table of Contents

  • Common Causes of Hair Loss
  • Diagnosing Hair Loss
  • Hair Loss Treatments
  • Lifestyle Changes to Prevent Hair Loss
  • Hair Care Tips for Preventing Hair Loss
  • Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Loss

Common Causes of Hair Loss

Before we dive into what to do when hair is falling out, it's important to understand some of the common causes of hair loss. Some of the most common causes include:

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal imbalances
  • Stress
  • Poor nutrition
  • Certain medications
  • Medical conditions such as alopecia areata or thyroid disorders

If you're experiencing hair loss, it's important to speak with your doctor to determine the underlying cause.

Diagnosing Hair Loss

When you visit your doctor for hair loss, they may perform a physical exam and ask you about your medical history. They may also perform blood tests or a scalp biopsy to determine the underlying cause. Once the underlying cause is determined, your doctor can recommend the best course of treatment.

Hair Loss Treatments

There are several treatments available for hair loss, including:

  • Medications such as minoxidil or finasteride
  • Hair transplant surgery
  • Laser therapy
  • Scalp micropigmentation

It's important to speak with your doctor to determine the best course of treatment for your specific case of hair loss.

Lifestyle Changes to Prevent Hair Loss

While there are treatments available for hair loss, there are also several lifestyle changes you can make to prevent hair loss from occurring in the first place. Some of these changes include:

  • Eating a healthy, balanced diet
  • Reducing stress
  • Avoiding tight hairstyles that pull on the hair
  • Avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools
  • Getting regular exercise

By making these changes, you can help prevent hair loss and promote hair growth.

Hair Care Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

In addition to lifestyle changes, there are also several hair care tips you can follow to prevent hair loss, including:

  • Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoiding excessive brushing or combing
  • Avoiding tight hairstyles that pull on the hair
  • Avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools
  • Using a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle hair

By following these hair care tips, you can help prevent hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.

Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Loss

Q: Is hair loss more common in men or women?

A: Hair loss affects both men and women, but it is more common in men.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. When you're under stress, your body produces hormones that can interfere with hair growth.

Q: Can hair loss be reversed?

A: In some cases, hair loss can be reversed. It's important to speak with your doctor to determine the best course of treatment for your specific case of hair loss.

Q: What is the most common cause of hair loss?

A: The most common cause of hair loss is genetics.

Q: Are there any natural remedies for hair loss?

A: There are several natural remedies that may help promote hair growth, including applying essential oils to the scalp, taking supplements such as biotin or vitamin D, and using onion juice on the scalp.

Conclusion

If you're experiencing hair loss, there are several steps you can take to slow down or even reverse the process. By speaking with your doctor, making lifestyle changes, and following hair care tips, you can promote healthy hair growth and prevent further hair loss. Remember to be patient and consistent in your efforts, as it may take time to see results.
