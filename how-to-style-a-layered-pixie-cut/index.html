How To Style A Layered Pixie Cut

Written by Oliver Jun 08, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking to revamp your hairstyle with a trendy and chic cut? Look no further than the layered pixie cut! This short and sassy style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look without sacrificing style. In this article, we’ll show you how to style a layered pixie cut with ease.

Description

A layered pixie cut is a short haircut that features layers on top and a shorter length on the sides and back. This style adds volume and texture to your hair, making it perfect for those with fine or thin hair. It’s also a great way to show off your facial features and add a touch of edginess to your look.

How to

Styling a layered pixie cut is easy and can be done in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited to your hair type. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner if you have thin hair to add volume and texture.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product of your choice, such as mousse, gel, or pomade, to your hair. Use a dime-sized amount for short hair and work it through your hair evenly using your fingers.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair in the direction you want it to lay. Use a round brush to add volume and smooth out any kinks or curls.

Step 5: Style

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair. You can also use your fingers to style your hair into a messy, textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your layered pixie cut:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to maintain your pixie cut.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands or hair clips for extra flair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling a layered pixie cut:

Q: Can I style a layered pixie cut without using heat tools?

A: Yes, you can use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair without using heat tools.

Q: Will a layered pixie cut work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a layered pixie cut can be tailored to suit different face shapes. Consult with your stylist to find the best cut for your face shape.

Q: Can I color my pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can add color to your pixie cut to enhance your look. Consult with your stylist to find the best color for your skin tone and hair type.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to style your layered pixie cut with ease and confidence. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you. Happy styling!
