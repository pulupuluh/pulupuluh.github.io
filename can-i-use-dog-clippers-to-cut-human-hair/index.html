Can I Use Dog Clippers To Cut Human Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are looking for a haircut but do not want to spend money on a hairdresser or barber, you may be wondering if you can use dog clippers to cut human hair. The answer is yes, you can use dog clippers to cut human hair, but there are some things you should know before you start. In this article, we will guide you on how to use dog clippers for human hair cutting.

Description

Dog clippers are designed to cut through thick and coarse hair, which is similar to human hair. However, dog clippers are not as powerful as human hair clippers, so they may take longer to cut through thicker parts of the hair.

How to Use Dog Clippers for Human Hair Cutting

Before you start cutting your hair with dog clippers, make sure you have the right tools. You will need:

  • Dog clippers
  • Clipper blade guard
  • Comb
  • Haircutting scissors
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Once you have all the tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you start cutting it. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the clippers do not get clogged with hair.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Blade Guard

Select the clipper blade guard that matches the length of hair you want to leave. If you are not sure which blade guard to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Comb your hair in the direction it naturally falls. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Hold the clippers at a 45-degree angle and start cutting your hair from the bottom to the top. Make sure you use slow and steady strokes to prevent any unevenness.

Step 5: Trim the Edges

Once you have cut the majority of your hair, use haircutting scissors to trim the edges and create a more polished look.

Step 6: Clean Up

After you have finished cutting your hair, clean up any loose hair and wash your clippers with warm soapy water.

Tips

Here are some tips to ensure that you get the best results when using dog clippers to cut human hair:

  • Use a clipper blade guard that matches the length of hair you want to leave.
  • Start with a longer blade guard and work your way down if you are not sure which guard to use.
  • Use slow and steady strokes to prevent unevenness.
  • Trim the edges with haircutting scissors to create a more polished look.
  • Clean your clippers after every use to prevent them from clogging and to ensure they last longer.

FAQs

Can I Use Any Dog Clippers for Human Hair Cutting?

No, not all dog clippers are suitable for human hair cutting. Look for clippers that have a motor that can handle human hair and come with different blade guards for different lengths of hair.

Can I Use Human Clippers on My Dog?

No, it is not recommended to use human clippers on dogs. Human clippers are not designed to cut through a dog's thick and coarse fur, and they can cause pain and discomfort to your furry friend.

Is It Cheaper to Cut My Hair with Dog Clippers?

Yes, it is cheaper to cut your hair with dog clippers than to go to a hairdresser or barber. However, it may take longer to cut your hair with dog clippers, and the results may not be as polished as a professional haircut.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair with dog clippers can be a cost-effective way to maintain your hair, but it is important to take care of your hair properly to ensure it stays healthy. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner regularly.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

Now that you know how to use dog clippers for human hair cutting, you can give yourself a haircut at home. Remember to follow the steps carefully and take care of your hair properly to ensure it stays healthy and looks great.
