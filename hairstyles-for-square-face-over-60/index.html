Hairstyles For Square Face Over 60 How To Find The Perfect Look

Written by Aprilia May 18, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a square face and are over 60, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and complements your features, but also one that suits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best hairstyles for square faces over 60, and provide you with tips on how to achieve them.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 60
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Square Faces Over 60

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into hairstyles, it’s important to determine your face shape. A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and a forehead that’s roughly the same width as your cheekbones. To determine if you have a square face, look in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner pencil. If you have a square face, the outline will be roughly the same width at your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 60

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to explore some hairstyles that flatter square faces over 60. Here are some options:

1. Short Bob

A short bob is a classic hairstyle that works well with a square face. The length should hit just below the chin and be styled with layers to soften the jawline. This style is easy to maintain and can be dressed up or down.

2. Shoulder-Length Layers

Shoulder-length layers are a great option for square faces over 60. The layers help to soften the jawline and add volume and movement to the hair. This style is versatile and can be worn straight or curled.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy option for square faces over 60. This style is short on the sides and back and longer on top. It can be styled with texture and volume to add interest and movement to the hair.

4. Soft Waves

Soft waves are a great way to add volume and movement to square faces over 60. This style is achieved by using a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves throughout the hair. The waves help to soften the jawline and add dimension to the hair.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for square faces over 60, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Look for styles that soften the jawline and add volume to the hair.
  • Avoid styles that are too harsh or angular, as they can make the face appear more square.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time and effort you’re willing to put into styling your hair.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find a style that works best for you.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for a square face over 60?

The best hairstyle for a square face over 60 is one that softens the jawline and adds volume to the hair. Some options include a short bob, shoulder-length layers, a pixie cut, and soft waves.

Can I still wear long hair if I have a square face?

Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have a square face. However, it’s important to add layers and volume to the hair to soften the jawline.

Do I need to change my hair color as I get older?

There’s no need to change your hair color as you get older, but some women choose to do so to complement their changing skin tone or to add interest to their hair.

Hair Care for Square Faces Over 60

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and hairstyle options, you can find the perfect look for your square face over 60. Whether you prefer a short bob or soft waves, there’s a style that will flatter your features and complement your lifestyle.
