How To Thin Out Short Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 02, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have short hair and are looking for ways to make it look more voluminous or to remove some of the bulk, thinning out your hair can be a great option. While you can always visit a hairstylist to have your hair professionally thinned, doing it yourself at home can save you time and money. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to thin out short hair at home.

How to Thin Out Short Hair at Home

Before you start thinning out your hair, it is important to gather the right tools. You will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Thinning scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to see which areas need thinning and will also ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to section your hair. You can start by parting your hair down the middle and then dividing it into four equal sections – two in the front and two in the back. Use the hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start Thinning

Take one section of hair and start thinning it out. Use the thinning scissors to cut into the hair, making sure to only cut a small amount at a time. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, taking care not to thin out too much hair in one go.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

After you have thinned out one section of hair, use the regular scissors to blend the hair. This will ensure that the hair looks natural and not choppy. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Once you have thinned out all sections of hair, check your progress in the mirror. If you feel like you need to thin out more hair, repeat the process until you are happy with the results.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You will notice that your hair looks more voluminous and that the bulk has been removed.

Tips for Thinning Out Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when thinning out short hair:

  • Start with small amounts of hair and work your way up. It is better to thin out too little hair than too much.
  • Use the thinning scissors sparingly. They are designed to remove hair, so be careful not to thin out too much in one go.
  • Make sure to blend the hair after thinning it out. This will ensure that the hair looks natural and not choppy.

FAQ

Can I Thin Out Short Hair with Regular Scissors?

While you can use regular scissors to thin out short hair, it is best to use thinning scissors. Thinning scissors are designed to remove hair without leaving any harsh lines or choppy ends.

How Often Should I Thin Out My Short Hair?

How often you should thin out your short hair depends on how quickly your hair grows and how much bulk you want to remove. Some people may need to thin out their hair every few months, while others may only need to do it once or twice a year.

Hair Care for Thinned Out Hair

After thinning out your hair, it is important to take proper care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

Thinning out short hair at home is a great way to achieve a more voluminous look and to remove some of the bulk. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve salon-worthy results from the comfort of your own home.
