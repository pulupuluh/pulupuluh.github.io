Best Haircuts For Round Faces With Curly Hair

Having a round face with curly hair can be a challenge when it comes to finding the perfect haircut that flatters your features. However, with the right cut and style, you can enhance your natural beauty and highlight your best features. In this article, we will share with you the best haircuts for round faces with curly hair, along with tips and tricks to help you maintain your gorgeous locks.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start looking for the best haircut for your round face, it is important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Here are some simple steps to determine your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a measuring tape to measure the distance between your temples, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

If your face is round, your measurements will be similar in width for your temples, cheekbones, and jawline, and your facial features will be soft and rounded.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces with Curly Hair

When it comes to haircuts for round faces with curly hair, the key is to add volume and dimension to your locks, while elongating your face shape. Here are some of the best haircuts for round faces with curly hair:

Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for adding volume and movement to your curly hair, while elongating your face shape. The layers should start at your cheekbones and continue down to the ends of your hair. This will create a flattering frame around your face and draw attention away from your round features.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob is a great choice for round faces with curly hair, as it adds edginess and dimension to your locks. This style features a shorter cut on one side and a longer cut on the other, creating an asymmetrical effect that elongates your face shape.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great addition to any haircut for round faces with curly hair. They add dimension to your locks and draw attention away from your round features. The bangs should start at the crown of your head and continue down to the side of your face, creating a flattering frame around your features.

Messy Updo

A messy updo is a great choice for special occasions, as it adds volume and dimension to your curls, while elongating your face shape. To create this style, simply pull your hair back into a loose bun or braid, leaving some curls loose around your face for a soft, romantic look.

Tips and Tricks for Styling Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve a gorgeous, bouncy look that highlights your features. Here are some tips and tricks for styling curly hair:

Avoid Brushing Your Curls

Brushing your curls can cause them to frizz and lose their shape. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your locks and create a bouncy, voluminous look.

Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a great tool for styling curly hair, as it helps to distribute heat evenly and prevent frizz. Simply attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and use it to dry your curls, scrunching them gently as you go.

Apply Hair Oil

Hair oil is a great way to add shine and moisture to your curls, while reducing frizz and flyaways. Simply apply a small amount of hair oil to your locks, working it in from the roots to the ends, for a soft, silky finish.

FAQ About Haircuts for Round Faces with Curly Hair

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut with a round face and curly hair?

A: Yes, you can! A short haircut can be a great choice for round faces with curly hair, as long as you choose the right style. Look for a cut that adds volume and dimension to your locks, while elongating your face shape, such as an asymmetrical bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

Q: Should I straighten my curly hair before getting a haircut?

A: No, you don't have to straighten your curly hair before getting a haircut. In fact, it's better to leave your locks in their natural state, so that your hairstylist can see the texture and movement of your curls and create a style that flatters your features.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut if I have curly hair?

A: It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks if you have curly hair, to keep your locks healthy and prevent split ends. However, you can stretch out the time between haircuts if you're trying to grow out your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy, shiny, and bouncy. Here are some tips for maintaining your curly locks:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle and sulfate-free, to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Look for a formula that is specifically designed for curly hair, to enhance your locks and tame frizz.

Condition Regularly

Conditioning is essential for curly hair, as it helps to detangle your locks and add moisture and shine. Choose a conditioner that is rich and nourishing, and apply it from the mid-lengths to the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your curly hair and cause it to lose its shape and bounce. Instead, try air-drying your locks or using a diffuser to dry them gently. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature on low.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a gorgeous, bouncy look that flatters your round face and enhances your natural beauty. Try out some of these haircuts and styles, and don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your curly locks!
