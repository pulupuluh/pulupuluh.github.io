How To Keep My Hair From Falling Out After Pregnancy Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Apr 03, 2023 · 6 min read

After giving birth, many women experience hair loss due to hormonal changes. However, there are steps you can take to prevent or reduce postpartum hair loss. In this article, we’ll explore how to keep your hair from falling out after pregnancy.

Table of Contents:

  • Hair Care
  • Diet
  • Supplements
  • Stress Management
  • FAQ

Hair Care

Proper hair care is crucial for preventing postpartum hair loss. Here are some tips:

Be Gentle

Avoid pulling your hair too tight, as this can cause damage and breakage. Use a wide-toothed comb instead of a brush, and don’t use heat-styling tools too often.

Wash Carefully

Use a gentle shampoo and don’t wash your hair too often. Over-washing can strip your scalp of natural oils, which can lead to hair loss. Also, avoid using hot water, as this can damage your hair.

Nourish Your Scalp

Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth. You can also use natural oils like coconut oil, castor oil, or olive oil to nourish your scalp.

Diet

Your diet can also affect the health of your hair. Here are some foods that can help:

Protein

Eating protein-rich foods like eggs, fish, and meat can help strengthen your hair and prevent hair loss.

Iron

Iron is important for healthy hair growth. You can get iron from foods like spinach, lentils, and red meat.

Vitamins

Vitamins like Biotin and Vitamin D can also help keep your hair healthy. You can get these vitamins from foods like eggs, nuts, and fatty fish.

Supplements

If you’re not getting enough nutrients from your diet, you can take supplements. Here are some supplements that can help:

Biotin

Biotin is a B-vitamin that can help strengthen your hair and prevent hair loss. You can take biotin supplements or get it from foods like eggs and nuts.

Iron

If you’re not getting enough iron from your diet, you can take iron supplements. However, it’s important to talk to your doctor before taking any supplements.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Omega-3 fatty acids can help promote healthy hair growth. You can get these from supplements or foods like fatty fish and nuts.

Stress Management

Stress can also contribute to hair loss. Here are some tips for managing stress:

Exercise

Regular exercise can help reduce stress and promote healthy hair growth.

Meditation

Meditation and deep breathing exercises can help reduce stress and promote relaxation.

Sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for reducing stress and promoting healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about postpartum hair loss:

When does postpartum hair loss occur?

Postpartum hair loss usually occurs 2-3 months after giving birth.

Is postpartum hair loss permanent?

No, postpartum hair loss is usually temporary and your hair will grow back.

Can breastfeeding cause hair loss?

Yes, hormone changes during breastfeeding can contribute to postpartum hair loss.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair healthy and prevent postpartum hair loss. Remember to be gentle with your hair, nourish your scalp, eat a healthy diet, take supplements if necessary, manage stress, and get enough sleep. If you’re concerned about hair loss, talk to your doctor.
