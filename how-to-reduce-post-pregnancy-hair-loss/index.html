How To Reduce Post Pregnancy Hair Loss Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jul 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you experiencing post pregnancy hair loss? Don’t worry, it’s completely normal. In fact, it’s estimated that up to 90% of women experience hair loss after giving birth. The good news is that there are several ways to reduce post pregnancy hair loss. In this article, we’ll discuss some effective tips and tricks to help you regain your luscious locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Reduce Post Pregnancy Hair Loss
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Hair Loss
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine

How to Reduce Post Pregnancy Hair Loss

The first step to reducing post pregnancy hair loss is to understand the cause. During pregnancy, hormonal changes can cause your hair to grow thicker and more luscious. However, after giving birth, these hormones drop rapidly, causing hair loss. The good news is that this hair loss is usually temporary and will stop within a few months.

To reduce hair loss after pregnancy, try the following:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals is crucial for healthy hair growth. Make sure to include plenty of protein, iron, and omega-3 fatty acids in your diet.

2. Take Supplements

If you’re not getting enough nutrients from your diet, consider taking supplements. Biotin, iron, and vitamin D are all essential for healthy hair growth.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make hair loss worse. Try to avoid using hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as much as possible.

4. Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Harsh chemicals in hair care products can also damage your hair and make hair loss worse. Choose gentle products that are free from sulfates and parabens.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re looking for a step-by-step guide to reducing post pregnancy hair loss, follow these tips:

Step 1: Start with a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet is crucial for healthy hair growth. Make sure to include plenty of protein, iron, and omega-3 fatty acids in your diet. Foods like salmon, sweet potatoes, and spinach are all great for promoting healthy hair growth.

Step 2: Take Supplements

If you’re not getting enough nutrients from your diet, consider taking supplements. Biotin, iron, and vitamin D are all essential for healthy hair growth.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make hair loss worse. Try to avoid using hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use these tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Step 4: Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Harsh chemicals in hair care products can also damage your hair and make hair loss worse. Choose gentle products that are free from sulfates and parabens. Look for products that are specifically designed to promote healthy hair growth.

Tips to Prevent Hair Loss

In addition to the steps outlined above, here are some additional tips to prevent hair loss:

1. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help promote healthy hair growth. Use your fingers to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

2. Don’t Tie Your Hair Too Tight

Tying your hair too tight can cause damage and breakage. Try to avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

FAQs

Q: How long does post pregnancy hair loss last?

A: Post pregnancy hair loss is usually temporary and will stop within a few months.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. If you’re experiencing stress, try to find ways to manage it, such as practicing yoga or meditation.

Q: Will cutting my hair help prevent hair loss?

A: Cutting your hair won’t prevent hair loss, but it can help prevent split ends and breakage.

Hair Care Routine

Here’s a sample hair care routine to help promote healthy hair growth:

Morning:

1. Use a gentle sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair.

3. Style as desired.

Evening:

1. Brush your hair gently to remove any tangles.

2. Apply a hair mask or treatment to your hair once a week.

3. Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent breakage.

Reducing post pregnancy hair loss takes time and patience, but with these tips and tricks, you can help promote healthy hair growth and regain your luscious locks.
