How To Make Straightened Hair Look Thicker

Written by Daniel Jul 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have naturally thin or fine hair, you may struggle to achieve the voluminous look you desire, even when you straighten your hair. However, there are several ways you can make your straightened hair look thicker without resorting to extensions or other drastic measures. In this article, we'll explore some tips and tricks to help you achieve fuller-looking hair.

Description

Straightened hair can often appear flat and lifeless, especially if you have thin or fine hair. However, with a few simple techniques and tools, you can create volume and thickness to your straightened hair. Thick hair not only looks great but also gives you confidence, so let's dive into how to make your straightened hair look thicker.

How to Make Straightened Hair Look Thicker

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

The foundation of any great hairstyle starts with the right shampoo and conditioner. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner that contain ingredients such as keratin or biotin, which help to strengthen hair and add volume. These products will help to nourish your hair and create a fuller look.

2. Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

When blow-drying your hair, flip your head upside down and dry your hair from the roots to the ends. This technique will help to lift your roots and create volume. Use a round brush to add extra volume and hold while drying.

3. Use a Root Lifting Spray

A root lifting spray is a great way to add volume to your straightened hair. Apply the spray at the roots and use your fingers to massage it in. Blow-dry your hair as usual, and you'll notice an immediate difference in the fullness of your hair.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is an old-school trick that still works wonders for adding volume. Take small sections of hair and backcomb them at the roots. This technique will create a cushion of hair that adds volume and fullness. Be gentle and don't overdo it, as it can damage your hair.

5. Use a Thickening Spray

A thickening spray is a great option for those who want to add volume without teasing their hair. Apply the spray at the roots and massage it in. Blow-dry your hair as usual, and you'll notice an instant boost in volume.

6. Curl Your Hair

Adding curls to your straightened hair is an excellent way to create volume and fullness. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls or waves. This technique will give your hair a lift and make it appear thicker.

7. Use Hair Extensions

If you're looking for a more dramatic way to add volume to your hair, hair extensions are a great option. Choose extensions that match your hair color and texture, and have them professionally installed for the best results.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to create a solid foundation for your hairstyle.

Step 2: Apply Root Lifting Spray

Apply a root lifting spray to your hair, focusing on the roots. Massage it in with your fingers to create volume.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair upside down, using a round brush to add volume and hold. Make sure to dry your hair completely.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Take small sections of hair and backcomb them at the roots to create volume. Be gentle and don't overdo it.

Step 5: Use a Thickening Spray

Apply a thickening spray to your hair, focusing on the roots. Massage it in with your fingers to create volume.

Step 6: Curl Your Hair

Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls or waves. This technique will give your hair a lift and make it appear thicker.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Visit a professional stylist for a haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair.
  • Invest in high-quality hair tools, such as a blow-dryer and curling iron.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my straightened hair look thicker without using products?

A: Yes, you can create volume in your hair by blow-drying it upside down or using a round brush to add lift. Additionally, you can tease your hair gently to create volume.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less often. Generally, washing your hair every other day is a good rule of thumb.

Q: Can I use hair extensions if I have thin or fine hair?

A: Yes, hair extensions are a great option for adding volume and fullness to thin or fine hair. However, make sure to choose extensions that are lightweight and won't damage your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy, thick hair, it's essential to take good care of it. Use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, and avoid using hot tools every day. Additionally, get regular haircuts to remove split ends and keep your hair looking its best. Lastly, protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or scarf.
