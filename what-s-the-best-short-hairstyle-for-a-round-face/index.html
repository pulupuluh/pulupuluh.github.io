What s The Best Short Hairstyle For A Round Face

Written by Petter Jun 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect short hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. In this post, we'll explore what's the best short hairstyle for a round face, and provide you with tips, tricks, and advice to help you achieve your dream look.

Description

A round face has soft curves, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of length and height, and minimize the width of your cheeks. The best short hairstyle for a round face will be one that elongates your face and draws attention to your eyes, cheekbones, and jawline.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for styles that add volume at the crown of your head or the back of your head, as this will make your face look longer.

Second, you want to choose a style that works with your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to consider a pixie cut or a bob with layers to add volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a short shag or a textured crop to thin out your hair and create a more manageable style.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to finding the best short hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Start by researching different short hairstyles that flatter round faces. Look online for inspiration, or consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion.
  2. Determine your hair texture and choose a style that works with your hair type.
  3. Consider the length of the style. Shorter styles that hit above the chin will elongate your face, while longer styles that hit at the jawline may make your face look wider.
  4. Choose a style that adds volume to the crown or back of your head, rather than the sides of your face.
  5. Think about your face shape and choose a style that highlights your best features.
  6. Consult with a hairstylist to ensure that the style you choose will work with your face shape and hair type.
  7. Be open to trying new things and experimenting with different styles until you find the perfect one.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face:

  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth in your hair. This can help elongate your face and draw attention to your best features.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to see what works best for your face shape.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, or barrettes to add interest and texture to your style.
  • Take care of your hair by using high-quality products and getting regular trims to maintain your style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best short hairstyle for a round face:

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

The best short hairstyle for a round face will be one that elongates your face and draws attention to your best features. This may include styles like a layered bob, a textured crop, or a pixie cut.

What should I avoid when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face?

You should avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face look rounder. Instead, choose styles that add volume at the crown or back of your head to create the illusion of length.

Do I need to have a certain hair texture to pull off a short hairstyle for a round face?

No, there are short hairstyles that work for all hair types, whether you have fine, thick, curly, or straight hair. Consult with a hairstylist to find a style that works best for your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect short hairstyle for your round face, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your style. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

By following these tips and choosing the best short hairstyle for your round face, you can achieve a look that flatters your features and highlights your unique beauty.
