How Long Is An 8 Guard On Clippers

Written by Christine Aug 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're someone who likes to cut their hair at home, you probably know how important it is to have the right tools. Clippers are one of the most essential tools for hair cutting, and guards are an essential accessory for clippers. One of the most common guards is the 8 guard, but how long is an 8 guard on clippers? In this blog post, we'll answer that question and provide some tips and tricks for using the 8 guard.

Description of 8 Guard on Clippers

The 8 guard is one of the most popular guards for clippers. It is used for cutting hair to a length of 1 inch or 25 mm. The guard is made of plastic and is attached to the clipper blade. It helps to control the length of the hair being cut and ensures that the hair is cut evenly.

How to Use an 8 Guard on Clippers

Using an 8 guard on clippers is quite easy. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Attach the Guard to the Clipper

First, make sure that the clipper is turned off. Then, attach the 8 guard to the clipper blade. Make sure that it is securely attached and that there are no gaps between the guard and the blade.

Step 2: Start Cutting

Turn on the clipper and start cutting your hair. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Use long, even strokes and move the clipper in the direction of the hair growth. Make sure that you don't cut too much hair at once.

Step 3: Check the Length

After you've finished cutting your hair, check the length with a mirror. If you're happy with the length, you're all done! If you want it shorter, you can repeat the process with a lower guard. If you want it longer, you can repeat the process with a higher guard.

Tips for Using an 8 Guard on Clippers

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when using an 8 guard on clippers:

  • Make sure that your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting
  • Use a comb to help guide the hair into the clipper
  • Go slowly and take your time
  • Be careful around the ears and neck
  • Check the length frequently
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head

FAQ

What is the length of an 8 guard on clippers?

The length of an 8 guard on clippers is 1 inch or 25 mm.

Can I use an 8 guard on all types of hair?

An 8 guard can be used on most types of hair, but it may not work well on very thick or curly hair. In that case, you may need to use a lower guard or go to a professional stylist.

How often should I replace my guards?

It's a good idea to replace your guards every 6-12 months, depending on how often you use them. Over time, the plastic can become brittle and may crack, which can cause uneven cutting.

Hair Care

Using an 8 guard on clippers is a great way to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. However, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health

By following these tips and using an 8 guard on clippers, you can keep your hair looking great and save money on trips to the salon.
