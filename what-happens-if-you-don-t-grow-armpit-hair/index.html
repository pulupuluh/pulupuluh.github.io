What Happens If You Don t Grow Armpit Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Armpit hair is a natural part of our body, and it serves a purpose. It helps regulate body temperature, prevents friction between skin, and acts as a barrier against bacteria. However, some people may wonder, what happens if you don't grow armpit hair? In this blog post, we will explore the answer to this question and provide some hair care tips.

Table of Content

  1. Why do we have armpit hair?
  2. What happens if you don't grow armpit hair?
  3. FAQs
  4. Hair Care Tips

Why do we have armpit hair?

Armpit hair is a secondary sexual characteristic that develops during puberty. It is more prevalent in males than females due to hormonal differences. The hair follicles in the armpit produce hair that is coarser and thicker than the hair on other parts of the body. It serves a few essential functions, including:

  • Regulating body temperature
  • Preventing friction between skin
  • Acting as a barrier against bacteria

What happens if you don't grow armpit hair?

If you don't grow armpit hair, it may not have any significant impact on your health. However, it can lead to some discomfort and irritation. Without the hair, your skin may rub together, causing friction and irritation. It can also lead to sweat accumulation, which can result in body odor.

Furthermore, without armpit hair, your body may have a harder time regulating temperature. The hair acts as an insulator, keeping your body warm in cold weather and cooling it down in hot weather. Without this insulation, you may feel colder or hotter than usual.

Is it normal not to have armpit hair?

It is relatively normal not to have armpit hair, especially in prepubescent children and some women. However, if you have never grown armpit hair and are an adult, it may be an indication of a medical condition. Some of the conditions that can cause hair loss include:

  • Alopecia areata
  • Hormonal imbalances
  • Thyroid disorders
  • Nutrient deficiencies

FAQs

Can I shave my armpit hair?

Yes, you can shave your armpit hair. It is a personal preference, and there is no harm in removing the hair. However, be mindful that shaving can cause irritation and ingrown hairs. If you choose to shave, make sure to use a clean razor and shave in the direction of the hair growth.

Does armpit hair grow back thicker after shaving?

No, armpit hair does not grow back thicker after shaving. This is a common myth. When you shave, you cut the hair at the surface level, giving it a blunt edge. As it grows back, it may feel coarser or thicker, but it is not.

How often should I shave my armpit hair?

It depends on your preference. Some people shave every day, while others shave once a week. It is entirely up to you. However, if you notice irritation or ingrown hairs, take a break from shaving for a few days.

Hair Care Tips

Moisturize

Applying a moisturizer to your armpits can help prevent irritation and ingrown hairs. Use a fragrance-free lotion or cream to avoid any reactions.

Exfoliate

Exfoliating your armpits can help prevent ingrown hairs and remove dead skin cells. Use a gentle exfoliator once a week to keep your skin smooth and healthy.

Avoid tight clothing

Tight clothing can cause friction and irritation, leading to discomfort and ingrown hairs. Wear loose-fitting clothing to allow your skin to breathe.

Use a clean razor

Using a clean razor can help prevent infection and irritation. Replace your razor blade every few weeks or after every use if it becomes dull.

Don't overdo it

Shaving too often can cause irritation and ingrown hairs. Give your skin a break and shave once or twice a week.

Conclusion

In conclusion, not growing armpit hair may not have a significant impact on your health, but it can lead to discomfort and irritation. If you choose to remove the hair, make sure to take care of your skin and follow the tips mentioned above. And if you have never grown armpit hair and are concerned, speak with your doctor.
