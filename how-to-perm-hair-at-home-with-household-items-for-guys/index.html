How To Perm Hair At Home With Household Items For Guys

Written by Luffy Aug 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Perming hair has been a popular hairstyle among guys for a long time. However, going to a hair salon to get a perm can be expensive. That's why we're here to show you how to perm hair at home with household items for guys. This step-by-step guide will help you achieve that curly hair you've always wanted without breaking the bank.

Description

Perming is a chemical process that changes the structure of your hair. It involves wrapping your hair around rods and applying a perming solution to create curls. The process can be done at a salon or at home with the right equipment and products. In this article, we will show you how to perm hair at home with household items for guys.

How to

Before you start, you will need to gather the following household items:

  • Perm solution
  • Neutralizer
  • Plastic gloves
  • Shower cap
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair clips
  • Towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to perm hair at home with household items for guys:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp but not dripping wet.
  3. Section your hair using hair clips.
  4. Put on plastic gloves to protect your hands from the perming solution.
  5. Apply the perming solution to each section of hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the solution evenly.
  6. Wrap each section of hair around a perm rod, securing it in place with a hair clip.
  7. Cover your head with a shower cap and let the perming solution sit for the recommended amount of time, usually around 20-30 minutes.
  8. Remove the hair clips and perm rods, and rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  9. Apply the neutralizer to each section of hair, following the same steps as the perming solution.
  10. Let the neutralizer sit for the recommended amount of time, usually around 10-15 minutes.
  11. Rinse your hair with warm water and style as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when perming hair at home with household items for guys:

  • Do not wash your hair for 24-48 hours after perming to allow the curls to set.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair moisturized and healthy after perming.
  • Choose the right size perm rods for the length and thickness of your hair.
  • Do a strand test before perming your entire head to check for any allergic reactions or adverse effects.

FAQ

Can you perm hair at home with household items for guys?

Yes, you can perm hair at home with household items for guys as long as you have the right products and equipment.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on the individual's hair type and how well they take care of their hair.

Is perming hair damaging?

Perming hair can be damaging if not done correctly or if the hair is already damaged. It is important to use the right products and follow the instructions carefully to minimize damage.

Hair Care

After perming hair at home with household items for guys, it is important to take care of your hair properly to maintain the curls and avoid damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair as much as possible.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips, you can enjoy your new curly hairstyle without causing damage to your hair.
