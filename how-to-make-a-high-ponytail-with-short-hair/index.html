How To Make A High Ponytail With Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 30, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have short hair, you may think that creating a high ponytail is impossible. But fear not! With the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a stylish high ponytail that will make heads turn. In this article, we will guide you through the process of making a high ponytail with short hair, from preparation to styling. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Preparation
  • Sectioning and Teasing
  • Creating the Ponytail
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for Making a High Ponytail with Short Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Preparation

Before you start styling your hair, it’s important to prepare it properly. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will add volume to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will add more volume to your hair and make it easier to style. Make sure you blow dry your hair in an upward direction to create more lift at the roots.

Step 3: Apply Hair Spray

Apply a volumizing hair spray to your hair. This will help your hair hold its shape and make it easier to style.

Sectioning and Teasing

Now that your hair is prepped and ready to go, it’s time to start creating your high ponytail.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts: the top and bottom. Use a hair clip to hold the top section in place.

Step 2: Tease the Bottom Section

Using a teasing comb, tease the bottom section of your hair. This will add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.

Creating the Ponytail

Now it’s time to create the ponytail!

Step 1: Gather Your Hair

Gather the bottom section of your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 2: Add the Top Section

Release the top section of your hair and comb it back. Smooth it over the ponytail and secure it with bobby pins.

Finishing Touches

Now that your ponytail is complete, it’s time to add some finishing touches.

Step 1: Smooth Your Hair

Using a comb, smooth out any bumps or lumps in your ponytail.

Step 2: Add Hairspray

Spray a light layer of hairspray over your hair to hold the style in place.

Tips for Making a High Ponytail with Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you create the perfect high ponytail with short hair:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to help it blend in.
  • Tease your hair in small sections to add more volume.
  • Don’t be afraid to use bobby pins to secure your hair in place.
  • Use a light hand when applying hairspray to avoid a stiff or crunchy look.

FAQ

Can I make a high ponytail with really short hair?

Yes, you can! Just follow the steps outlined in this article and use a teasing comb to add volume to your hair.

What if my hair is too thin to make a high ponytail?

If your hair is thin, try using a hair donut to add more volume to your ponytail. You can also try using hair extensions to add length and volume.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Now that you know how to make a high ponytail with short hair, it’s time to give it a try! With a little practice and patience, you’ll be able to create a stylish and sophisticated look that’s perfect for any occasion.
