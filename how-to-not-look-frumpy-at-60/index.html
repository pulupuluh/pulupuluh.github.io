How To Not Look Frumpy At 60 Tips For Ageless Style

Written by Aprilia Apr 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, many of us prioritize comfort over style. However, this does not mean we have to sacrifice looking fashionable and put together. In this article, we will share tips on how to not look frumpy at 60 without compromising comfort. Whether you are running errands or attending a social gathering, these tips will help you look ageless and stylish.

How to:

Step 1: Embrace Your Body Shape

One of the keys to looking fashionable at any age is to embrace your body shape. As we age, our bodies change, and it's essential to dress in a way that flatters our current shape. Don't hide behind loose-fitting clothes; instead, opt for well-fitting pieces that accentuate your best features.

Step 2: Invest in Quality Basics

Investing in quality basics is crucial for any wardrobe, regardless of age. These pieces should be versatile, comfortable, and timeless. Some examples of quality basics include a well-fitted pair of jeans, a classic white t-shirt, and a tailored blazer.

Step 3: Accessorize

Accessories are a great way to add interest and style to any outfit. Consider adding a statement necklace, scarf, or belt to elevate a simple outfit. However, be mindful not to overdo it; less is often more when it comes to accessorizing.

Step 4: Experiment with Color and Patterns

Don't be afraid to experiment with color and patterns. While black is a classic color that many of us rely on, adding pops of color can add interest to our outfits. Similarly, patterns such as stripes, florals, and polka dots can be fun and playful.

Step 5: Dress for the Occasion

It's essential to dress appropriately for the occasion. For example, if you're attending a formal event, a little black dress and heels are appropriate. However, if you're running errands, a pair of comfortable sneakers and leggings are more appropriate.

Tips:

  • Don't be afraid to try new styles.
  • Invest in comfortable shoes.
  • Consider tailoring your clothes for a better fit.
  • Choose fabrics that are comfortable and easy to care for.
  • Pay attention to your posture; standing up straight can make a big difference in how you look and feel.

FAQ:

Q: Can I still wear jeans at 60?

A: Absolutely! Jeans are a versatile and timeless staple that can be worn at any age. Opt for a well-fitted pair that flatters your body shape.

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a dress?

A: Yes, you can! Sneakers can add a fun and playful touch to a dress. However, be mindful of the occasion and choose a dress that is appropriate for the event.

Q: How can I look more put together without sacrificing comfort?

A: Investing in quality basics, accessorizing, and choosing well-fitted pieces are all great ways to look put together while remaining comfortable.

Hair Care:

Your hair can also play a significant role in how you look and feel. As we age, our hair can become thinner and drier. Consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. Additionally, a shorter haircut can make your hair look fuller and more youthful.

By following these tips, you can look ageless and stylish at 60 while remaining comfortable and confident. Remember, fashion should be fun, so don't be afraid to experiment and try new things!
