How To Spike Your Hair Without Gel A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for an edgy and stylish hairstyle, spiking your hair can be a great option. While many people use gel to achieve this look, you can actually create spiked hair without using any hair products at all. In this article, we'll show you how to spike your hair without gel in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Wash Your Hair
  • Step 2: Dry Your Hair
  • Step 3: Tease Your Hair
  • Step 4: Use Hairspray
  • Tips for Spiking Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Spiked Hair

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in spiking your hair without gel is to start with clean hair. Use a gentle shampoo to wash your hair and rinse thoroughly. Avoid using a conditioner, as this can make your hair too soft and difficult to style.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Once you've washed your hair, use a towel to gently dry it. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz. Instead, pat your hair dry and allow it to air dry for a few minutes.

Step 3: Tease Your Hair

To create volume in your hair, use a teasing comb to backcomb your hair at the roots. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down. This will give your hair the lift it needs to create spikes.

Step 4: Use Hairspray

Once you've teased your hair, use a strong hold hairspray to set the style. Spray the hairspray directly onto your hair, focusing on the areas where you want the spikes to be. Use your fingers to shape the spikes and hold them in place until the hairspray dries.

Tips for Spiking Your Hair

  • Use a teasing comb with fine teeth to create more texture in your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find the perfect look for you.
  • Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to help set the style.
  • Consider using a hair wax or clay to add more texture and hold to your hair.

FAQs

Can I spike my hair without using any products?

While it's possible to create some volume and texture in your hair without using any products, it can be difficult to create defined spikes without some kind of hold or styling product.

What kind of hairspray should I use?

For the best results, use a strong hold hairspray that is specifically designed for creating volume and texture in your hair.

How long will my spiked hair last?

The length of time your spiked hair will last depends on a variety of factors, including the texture and length of your hair, the products you use, and the weather conditions. In general, you can expect your spiked hair to last for a few hours to a full day.

Hair Care for Spiked Hair

To keep your spiked hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.

By following these simple steps, you can create spiked hair without using any gel or other hair products. With a little practice and experimentation, you can find the perfect style to suit your personality and lifestyle.
