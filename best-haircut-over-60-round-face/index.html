Best Haircut Over 60 Round Face How To Choose The Perfect One

Written by Luffy May 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for the best haircut over 60 round face? As we age, our hair and face shape change, and it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters our features. A round face is a common shape among women over 60, but with the right cut, you can look stunning and youthful.

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Description

A round face has soft, curved features with the widest point at the cheeks. It can be challenging to find a haircut that flatters this face shape, but there are plenty of options that can make you look fabulous. A good haircut can help to elongate your face, draw attention to your best features, and create balance.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing the best haircut over 60 round face, the first thing you need to consider is your hair type. Whether you have thin or thick hair, straight or curly, there are plenty of options that can work for you. You also need to consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend on styling your hair.

A short haircut is an excellent option for a round face as it can help to create the illusion of length. A bob or a pixie cut can be very flattering, but make sure to avoid a blunt cut as it can make your face look rounder. Layers are also a great option as they can add volume and movement to your hair.

If you prefer longer hair, go for a layered cut that falls below your chin. A side-swept fringe can also help to create the illusion of length and draw attention to your eyes.

Best Haircuts for Round Face

  • Long Layered Cut
  • Bob with Side-Swept Bangs
  • Pixie Cut with Textured Layers
  • Shaggy Bob
  • Asymmetric Bob

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the best haircut over 60 round face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Show your hairstylist pictures of hairstyles you like to give them an idea of what you want.
  3. Wash your hair and blow-dry it before your appointment.
  4. During the haircut, make sure to communicate with your hairstylist and let them know if you are happy with the length and shape.
  5. After the haircut, style your hair as desired using products that work for your hair type.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your best haircut over 60 round face:

  • Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks for a trim to keep your haircut in shape.
  • Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is a layered cut that falls below your chin, with a side-swept fringe or face-framing layers.

Can a bob haircut work for a round face?

Yes, a bob haircut can work for a round face as long as it is not a blunt cut. A bob with face-framing layers or a side-swept fringe can be very flattering.

Hair Care Tips for Women over 60

Here are some hair care tips for women over 60:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair in shape and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the best haircut over 60 round face, you can look stunning and confident at any age.
