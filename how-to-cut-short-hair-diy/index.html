How To Cut Short Hair Diy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 25, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to save some money or want to try something new, cutting your own short hair is easier than you might think. In this article, we'll go over the steps to cutting short hair DIY style, as well as some tips and frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

How to Cut Short Hair DIY

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style. Once your hair is dry, comb it straight and part it where you usually do.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into four parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Clip each section separately so you can work on one at a time.

Step 3: Start with the Back

Begin with the back section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the ends, holding them at a slight angle to create a layered effect. Continue cutting small sections until you've finished the back.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Next, move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair from the front and hold it out at a 45-degree angle. Cut the ends at an angle to create a layered effect. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Finish with the Top

Finally, move on to the top section. Take small sections of hair and hold them between your fingers. Cut the ends at an angle to create layers. Continue until you've finished the top section.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments by cutting small sections of hair. Remember to take your time and don't rush the process.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair DIY

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting short hair DIY style:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up.
  • Don't cut too much at once or you may end up with uneven layers.
  • Use your comb to guide your scissors and keep your lines straight.
  • Take breaks if you need them – cutting hair can be tiring!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short hair DIY style:

Can I cut my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can. Just make sure to cut your hair when it's dry so you can see the shape better.

How often should I cut my short hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, short hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always take a break and come back to it later. If you need to, you can also visit a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care After Cutting Short Hair DIY

After cutting your hair, make sure to take care of it properly. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. You can also use hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

Cutting short hair DIY style can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and some patience, you can achieve a great new look without leaving your home. Give it a try!
