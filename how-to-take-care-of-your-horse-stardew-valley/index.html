How To Take Care Of Your Horse In Stardew Valley

Written by Bardi Aug 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Stardew Valley is a popular farming simulation game where players can raise animals, grow crops, and interact with the villagers. One of the most beloved animals in the game is the horse. Having a horse not only makes traveling around the map faster but also helps with daily farm chores. However, owning a horse also comes with responsibilities. Here's a guide on how to take care of your horse in Stardew Valley.

Description

Horses are a type of animal that can be purchased from Marnie's Ranch for 10000 gold. They come in different colors and can be named by the player. Once a horse is bought, it can be ridden and used to pull carts. However, it needs to be taken care of in order to maintain its health and happiness.

How to

Step 1: Feed Your Horse

Feeding your horse is essential to its health. You can buy hay from Marnie's Ranch or cut grass using a scythe. The hay can be placed in the horse's stable or left outside for it to eat. The horse will eat the hay automatically when it's hungry.

Step 2: Brush Your Horse

Brushing your horse is important to keep its coat clean and shiny. You can buy a brush from Marnie's Ranch or craft one using 1x Wood and 1x Hardwood. To brush your horse, stand next to it and right-click the brush.

Step 3: Pet Your Horse

Petting your horse is a great way to increase its happiness. You can do this by standing next to it and right-clicking on it. You can also give it a treat, such as an apple or a carrot, to make it even happier.

Step 4: Ride Your Horse

Riding your horse is a fun way to explore the map and get around quickly. To ride your horse, equip a saddle and right-click on it. You can also use your horse to pull a cart by equipping a harness and right-clicking on it.

Tips

- Make sure to keep your horse's stable clean by removing any poop or debris.

- If your horse's health is low, you can buy a horse treat from Marnie's Ranch to increase its health.

- Don't overwork your horse. Make sure to give it breaks and let it rest in the stable.

FAQ

Q: Can I breed my horse in Stardew Valley?

A: No, breeding horses is not possible in Stardew Valley.

Q: Can my horse die in Stardew Valley?

A: No, horses cannot die in Stardew Valley. However, their health can decrease if not taken care of properly.

Hair Care

In addition to the basic care mentioned above, you can also customize your horse's appearance by changing its mane and tail. To do this, you need to buy a pair of scissors from Robin's Carpenter Shop and use them on your horse. You can then choose from a variety of hairstyles and colors.

Taking care of your horse in Stardew Valley is a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips, you can ensure that your horse stays healthy and happy.
