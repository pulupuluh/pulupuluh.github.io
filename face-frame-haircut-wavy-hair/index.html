Face Frame Haircut For Wavy Hair How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Christine Jul 22, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your wavy hair looking dull and flat? It’s time to try a face frame haircut! This style is perfect for those with wavy hair, as it adds dimension and movement to your locks. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about getting a face frame haircut for wavy hair, from how to get the perfect cut to tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Perfect Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Look
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Description

A face frame haircut is a versatile style that suits all face shapes and hair types. It involves cutting layers around the face to frame the features and create a flattering look. For those with wavy hair, this cut can add volume and movement to your locks, making them appear bouncier and more vibrant.

How to Get the Perfect Cut

To get the perfect face frame haircut for your wavy hair, you should consult with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to recommend the best cut based on your face shape and hair type. You can also bring in pictures of styles you like to give them an idea of what you’re looking for.

Things to Consider

When getting a face frame haircut for wavy hair, there are a few things to consider:

  • Your face shape: The cut should flatter your features and complement your face shape.
  • Your hair texture: Wavy hair needs a different cut than straight or curly hair to achieve the best results.
  • The length: The length of your hair will determine how much of a face frame you can achieve.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a face frame haircut for wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair type.
  3. Section your hair into small sections and cut the layers around your face. The layers should start at your chin and gradually get longer towards the back.
  4. Blend the layers into the rest of your hair for a seamless look.
  5. Style your hair with a curling iron or wand to enhance the waves and create a bouncy look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your face frame haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to enhance your waves.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about face frame haircuts for wavy hair:

What face shape is best for a face frame haircut?

A face frame haircut suits all face shapes, but the cut will vary depending on your features. Your hairstylist can recommend the best cut for your face shape.

How often should I get a face frame haircut?

You should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your cut.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl enhancer to define your waves and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can dry out your hair and scalp.

Now that you know everything about getting a face frame haircut for wavy hair, it’s time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. With the right cut and care, you can achieve bouncy, voluminous waves that are sure to turn heads.
