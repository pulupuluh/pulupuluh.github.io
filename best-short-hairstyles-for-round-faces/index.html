Best Short Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Eveline Mar 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best short hairstyles for round faces. Whether you're looking for something edgy or classic, we've got options for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hairstyles are perfect for round faces because they can help to create the illusion of length and slim down the face. However, not all short hairstyles are created equal. When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, you should aim for styles that add height to the crown of the head and have some volume on top. This will help elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

Some popular short hairstyles for round faces include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob haircut
  • Layered hairstyles
  • Asymmetrical cut
  • Side-swept bangs

Step by step

Here's how to achieve some of these popular short hairstyles:

Pixie Cut:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a comb to create a side part.

3. Use a pair of scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head short, leaving the hair on top longer.

4. Use a texturizing product to add volume and texture to the hair on top.

Bob Haircut:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a pair of scissors to cut the hair to chin-length.

3. Add some layers to the hair to add volume and movement.

4. Use a texturizing product to add texture and definition to the hair.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling short hair for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make the face appear rounder.
  • Add volume to the crown of the head to elongate the face.
  • Consider adding layers to the hair to create movement and texture.
  • Use a texturizing product to add volume and definition to the hair.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for your face shape.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

Some of the best short hairstyles for round faces include the pixie cut, bob haircut, layered hairstyles, asymmetrical cut, and side-swept bangs.

What should I avoid when styling my short hair?

Avoid blunt cuts and center parts, as they can make the face appear rounder.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as using a texturizing product to add volume and definition. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you're sure to find the perfect short hairstyle for your round face. Remember to consider your hair texture and personal style when choosing a hairstyle, and don't be afraid to experiment!
