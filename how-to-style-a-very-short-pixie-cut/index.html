How To Style A Very Short Pixie Cut

Written by Frank May 30, 2023 · 8 min read

A very short pixie cut can be a bold and edgy hairstyle choice for women. It’s a low-maintenance haircut that is perfect for those who don’t have a lot of time to style their hair. However, styling a very short pixie cut can be tricky, especially if you’re new to this hairstyle. In this article, we’ll show you how to style a very short pixie cut in different ways.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style a Very Short Pixie Cut
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Style a Very Short Pixie Cut

Before we get into the different ways to style a very short pixie cut, it’s important to have the right tools. You’ll need a hairbrush, a hairdryer, and some styling products like a hair gel, wax, or pomade.

The first step is to wash your hair and apply some styling product while your hair is still damp. Next, use a hairdryer to dry your hair while using your fingers to create some texture. Once your hair is dry, you can start styling it in different ways.

Option 1: Messy Pixie

The messy pixie is a popular style for those who want a relaxed and edgy look. To achieve this style, apply some hair wax or pomade to your hair and use your fingers to create a messy texture. You can also use a hairbrush to create some volume on the crown of your head.

Option 2: Sleek Pixie

The sleek pixie is a more polished and sophisticated look. To achieve this style, apply some hair gel to your hair and use a hairbrush to smooth down your hair. You can also use a hairdryer to set the style in place.

Option 3: Pixie with Bangs

Adding bangs to your pixie cut can give it a different look. To achieve this style, use a hairdryer and a round brush to create some volume on your bangs. You can also use a hair gel or wax to create some texture on your bangs.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style a very short pixie cut:

  1. Wash your hair and apply some styling product while your hair is still damp.
  2. Use a hairdryer to dry your hair while using your fingers to create some texture.
  3. Choose your preferred style: messy, sleek, or with bangs.
  4. Apply the appropriate styling product for your chosen style.
  5. Use your fingers or a hairbrush to style your hair.
  6. Use a hairdryer to set the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your very short pixie cut:

  • Use a good quality styling product that suits your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you.
  • Don’t be afraid to use a hairdryer to set your style in place.
  • Use a hairbrush or your fingers to create some volume or texture.
  • Add some accessories like a headband or hairpin to enhance your style.

FAQ

Can I style my very short pixie cut without any styling product?

Yes, you can style your very short pixie cut without any styling product. However, using some styling product can help you create more texture and hold.

How often should I wash my very short pixie cut?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. However, if you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day.

Can I color my very short pixie cut?

Yes, you can color your very short pixie cut. However, it’s important to choose a hair color that suits your skin tone and hair type.

Hair Care

Taking care of your very short pixie cut is important to maintain its shape and texture. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape.

Styling a very short pixie cut can be fun and easy once you know how to do it. Experiment with different styles and products until you find the one that suits you. Don’t be afraid to try something new and bold with your hair.
