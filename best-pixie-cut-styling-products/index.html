Best Pixie Cut Styling Products The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 21, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have recently chopped off your locks into a chic pixie cut, you might be wondering what products to use to style your new 'do. Pixie cuts are one of the most versatile hairstyles, but they do require a bit of maintenance to keep them looking sharp. In this article, we will go over the best pixie cut styling products to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style a Pixie Cut

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut close to the scalp on the sides and back while leaving a bit more length on top. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet chic look. Pixie cuts can be styled in various ways, from sleek and smooth to tousled and messy. However, to achieve your desired look, you will need to use the right styling products.

How to Style a Pixie Cut

Before we dive into the best pixie cut styling products, let's go over some general styling tips for this haircut. To style a pixie cut, you will need to have the right tools and techniques. Here are some basic steps to follow:

  • Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  • Apply a styling product to your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  • Use a flat iron or curling wand to create waves or curls.
  • Finish off your look with a hairspray or styling cream to hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut

Now that you know the general steps to styling a pixie cut, let's break it down further with a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a styling mousse or gel to your hair, focusing on the roots for added volume.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create lift and texture.
  5. Use a flat iron to straighten any flyaways or frizz.
  6. Take a small amount of styling wax or pomade and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends to create piecey texture.
  7. Use your fingers to tousle and shape your hair into your desired style.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray or styling cream to hold your look in place.

Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your pixie cut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking sharp.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and desired look.

FAQs About Pixie Cuts

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts:

  • Q: Will a pixie cut suit my face shape?
    A: Pixie cuts can be flattering on almost any face shape, but it's important to consult with your hairstylist to find the right cut for you.
  • Q: How often should I get a trim?
    A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Q: Can I still style my pixie cut if I have curly hair?
    A: Absolutely! Pixie cuts can look great on curly hair, and you can use styling products to enhance your natural curls.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

Here are some additional hair care tips for maintaining a pixie cut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Invest in a good quality brush or comb to detangle your hair without causing breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Product Recommendations

Now that you know how to style and maintain your pixie cut, let's take a look at some of the best pixie cut styling products:

  • Texturizing Spray: A texturizing spray can add volume and texture to your pixie cut. Try the Oribe Dry Texturizing Spray or the Bumble and Bumble Surf Spray.
  • Styling Wax: A styling wax can help you create piecey texture and definition in your hair. Try the Baxter of California Clay Pomade or the Redken Rough Paste 12.
  • Hairspray: A strong-hold hairspray can keep your style in place all day. Try the L'Oreal Paris Elnett Satin Hairspray or the Kenra Volume Spray.
  • Heat Protectant: If you use heat styling tools on your hair, it's important to use a heat protectant to prevent damage. Try the Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser's Invisible Oil Heat/UV Protective Primer or the Living Proof Restore Instant Protection.

With these products and tips, you can rock your pixie cut with confidence and style. Remember, the key to maintaining a pixie cut is to keep it healthy and well-styled, so be sure to invest in the right products and tools for your hair type and desired look.
