How To Style Men s Long Hair For A Wedding

Written by Bardi May 25, 2023 Â· 2 min read

If you're a groom with long hair, you may be wondering how to style it for your wedding day. Long hair can be challenging to work with, but with the right technique, you can achieve a look that is both elegant and stylish. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to style men's long hair for a wedding, including tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

How to Style Men's Long Hair for a Wedding

When it comes to styling men's long hair for a wedding, you have several options. You can wear your hair down, in a ponytail, or in a bun. The key is to choose a style that complements your face shape, hair texture, and wedding attire. Here are some popular options:

  • Down and Wavy: If you have naturally wavy hair, consider wearing it down and tousled. This look is perfect for a beach or bohemian wedding.
  • Ponytail: A ponytail is a classic look for men with long hair. Keep it neat and low for a formal wedding, or high and messy for a more casual affair.
  • Bun: A bun is a sophisticated and stylish option for men with long hair. It works well with a suit or tuxedo and is perfect for a black-tie wedding.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style men's long hair for a wedding:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is curly or wavy, you may want to use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.
  2. Decide on your hairstyle. Choose a style that complements your wedding attire and reflects your personal style.
  3. If you're wearing your hair down, use a styling product to add volume and texture. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create waves.
  4. If you're wearing a ponytail, brush your hair back and gather it at the nape of your neck. Secure it with a hair tie or elastic band.
  5. If you're wearing a bun, brush your hair back and gather it into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Tips for Styling Men's Long Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling men's long hair for a wedding:

  • Experiment with different styles before the wedding day to find the one that suits you best.
  • If you're wearing your hair down, consider adding a hair accessory, such as a flower or hairpin, to add interest.
  • If you're wearing a bun, consider adding a hair net or veil for a more formal look.
  • Don't forget to groom your facial hair. Trim your beard or mustache and style it to complement your hairstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's long hair for a wedding:

  • Q: Can I wear my hair up if it's not long enough for a bun?
  • A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a half-up, half-down style or a simple topknot.
  • Q: Should I use a gel or wax to style my hair?
  • A: It depends on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. Gel is best for creating a slick, polished look, while wax is better for a more natural, textured look.
  • Q: How can I keep my hair looking good throughout the wedding?
  • A: Use a strong-hold hairspray or styling product to keep your hair in place. You may also want to bring a small comb or brush for touch-ups.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Long Hair

Here are some hair care tips for men with long hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • If you're going to be in the sun for an extended period, wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from UV damage.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your long hair for your wedding day with confidence and ease. Whether you choose to wear your hair down, in a ponytail, or in a bun, you'll look stylish and sophisticated as you walk down the aisle.
