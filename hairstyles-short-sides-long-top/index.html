Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're searching for a hairstyle that is both trendy and stylish, you might want to try out the short sides long top haircut. This hairstyle has been trending for quite some time now, and it's not hard to see why. It's versatile, low-maintenance, and can be styled in different ways to suit your personality and style. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the hairstyles short sides long top, how to achieve them, and some tips on how to maintain them.

Description

Hairstyles short sides long top are characterized by shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. There are many variations of this style, from the classic undercut to the modern pompadour. The haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways, depending on your preference. It's ideal for men who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and trendy.

How to Achieve Short Sides Long Top Haircuts

If you're interested in getting a short sides long top haircut, here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Hairstyle

There are many variations of the short sides long top haircut, so you need to choose the style that suits you best. Some popular styles include the classic undercut, the disconnected undercut, and the modern pompadour.

Step 2: Find a Good Barber

Once you've chosen your hairstyle, the next step is to find a good barber who can give you the haircut you want. Look for a barber who has experience with short sides long top haircuts and can give you advice on the best style for your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Get the Haircut

When you're ready to get the haircut, show your barber a picture of the style you want. They'll then use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair, leaving the top longer. The length of the top hair will depend on the style you've chosen.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you've got the haircut, you can style your hair in different ways. You can use a hair gel or pomade to create a slicked-back look, or you can use a blow dryer and a brush to create a messy, textured look.

Tips for Maintaining Short Sides Long Top Haircuts

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short sides long top haircut:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. If you have curly hair, use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls. If you want a slicked-back look, use a pomade or gel.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short sides long top haircuts require regular trims to keep them looking sharp. Visit your barber every four to six weeks for a trim, especially if you have fast-growing hair.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Damage

Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray if you must use these tools.

FAQ

Q: Is the short sides long top haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the short sides long top haircut can be adapted to suit different hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair.

Q: Can women wear short sides long top haircuts?

A: Yes, women can also wear short sides long top haircuts. The style is versatile and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types.

Q: Can I still wear a hat with a short sides long top haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear a hat with a short sides long top haircut. Choose a hat that fits well and doesn't flatten your hair too much.

Hair Care

To keep your short sides long top haircut looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools too often. Protect your hair from damage by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray. Visit your barber regularly for trims and advice on how to style your hair.

In conclusion, hairstyles short sides long top are a trendy and stylish choice for men and women. With the right style and maintenance, this haircut can give you a look that is both low-maintenance and fashionable. Follow the steps in this article to achieve the perfect short sides long top haircut, and use the tips and FAQ to help you maintain your hair.
