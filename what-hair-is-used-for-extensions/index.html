What Hair Is Used For Extensions A Comprehensive Guide In 2023

Written by Christine Mar 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your short hair and looking for a quick fix to add length and volume? Hair extensions are the perfect solution! But before you choose the hair extensions that suit you, you need to know what hair is used for extensions. In this blog post, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on what hair is used for extensions, how to apply them, and how to take care of them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose Hair Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide on Application
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Extensions
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Extensions

Description

Extensions can be made from synthetic or human hair. Synthetic hair extensions are cheaper than human hair extensions but do not look as natural. Human hair extensions are more expensive, but they look more natural and last longer. They can be styled, washed, and treated just like your natural hair.

When it comes to human hair extensions, there are two types of hair that are commonly used: virgin hair and Remy hair. Virgin hair is hair that has never been chemically treated or colored, whereas Remy hair is hair that has been collected from a single donor and the cuticles have been kept intact.

How to Choose Hair Extensions

When choosing hair extensions, you need to consider the texture, color, and length of the hair. You want to make sure that the extensions blend in seamlessly with your natural hair. If your hair is straight, choose straight extensions. If your hair is curly, choose curly extensions. If you have highlighted hair, choose extensions that match the color of your highlights.

You also need to consider the length of the extensions. If you want to add length to your hair, you can choose extensions that are longer than your natural hair. If you want to add volume, choose extensions that are the same length as your natural hair.

Types of Hair Extensions

There are four types of hair extensions:

  1. Tape-in extensions
  2. Clip-in extensions
  3. Sew-in extensions
  4. Microlink extensions

Tape-in and clip-in extensions are temporary and can be applied and removed easily. Sew-in and microlink extensions are more permanent and require professional application and removal.

Step-by-Step Guide on Application

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to apply tape-in extensions:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Section your hair
  3. Peel off the backing from the tape
  4. Place the extension on the roots of your hair
  5. Press the extension firmly onto your hair
  6. Repeat on all sections of your hair
  7. Cut and style your extensions

Tips for Maintaining Your Extensions

Here are some tips for maintaining your extensions:

  • Brush your extensions regularly with a soft-bristle brush
  • Avoid heat styling your extensions
  • Wash your extensions with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heavy oils and products on your extensions
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place

FAQs

How long do hair extensions last?

Hair extensions can last from 3 to 6 months, depending on how well you take care of them.

Can you wash your hair with extensions?

Yes, you can wash your hair with extensions, but you need to be gentle and use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

Can you swim with hair extensions?

Yes, you can swim with hair extensions, but you need to wear a swim cap and rinse your hair with clean water after swimming in chlorinated water.

Hair Care Tips for Extensions

Here are some additional hair care tips for extensions:

  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair
  • Avoid using hot tools on your extensions
  • Trim your extensions every 6 to 8 weeks
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol

Now that you know what hair is used for extensions, how to apply them, and how to take care of them, you can confidently choose the hair extensions that are right for you. With the right extensions and care, you can achieve the long, voluminous hair you've always wanted!
