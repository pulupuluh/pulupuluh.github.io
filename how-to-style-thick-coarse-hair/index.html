How To Style Thick Coarse Hair Tips And Tricks

Thick, coarse hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of looks. In this article, we'll share our best tips and tricks for styling thick, coarse hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Thick Coarse Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Thick Coarse Hair

How to Style Thick Coarse Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, it's essential to prep it properly. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to add hydration to your strands. Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage and add extra moisture. Once your hair is prepped, you can start styling it.

1. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair is an essential step in styling it. Use a round brush to smooth your hair and add volume as you blow dry. Be sure to use a heat protectant before applying heat to your hair to prevent damage. Blow dry your hair in sections to ensure that each part is thoroughly dried and styled.

2. Use a Straightener or Curling Iron

Once your hair is dry, you can use a straightener or curling iron to create your desired style. Use a heat protectant before applying heat to your hair to prevent damage. For straight hair, run a straightener through your hair in sections. For curls, wrap small sections of hair around a curling iron and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

3. Finish with a Styling Product

To keep your style in place, finish with a styling product. Use a hair spray, pomade, or wax to add texture and hold to your hair. Be sure not to use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling thick, coarse hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair in sections, using a round brush to add volume.
  4. Use a straightener or curling iron to create your desired style.
  5. Finish with a styling product to add texture and hold.

Tips for Styling Thick Coarse Hair

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your thick, coarse hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Apply a hair serum to help smooth your hair and add shine.
  • Use a heat protectant before applying heat to your hair to prevent damage.
  • Blow dry your hair in sections to ensure that each part is thoroughly dried and styled.
  • Use a straightener or curling iron to create your desired style.
  • Use a hair spray, pomade, or wax to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick, coarse hair:

What products should I use to style my thick, coarse hair?

You should use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, a leave-in conditioner, a heat protectant, a hair serum, and a styling product like hair spray, pomade, or wax.

How often should I wash my thick, coarse hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry.

What hairstyles work best for thick, coarse hair?

Hairstyles that work best for thick, coarse hair include long layers, blunt cuts, and textured styles.

Hair Care for Thick Coarse Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your thick, coarse hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to add extra moisture and protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your thick, coarse hair with ease. Remember to use the right products and techniques to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
