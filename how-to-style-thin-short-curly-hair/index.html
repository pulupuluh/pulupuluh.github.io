How To Style Thin Short Curly Hair

Written by Luffy Jun 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thin short curly hair, you may feel like your styling options are limited. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create beautiful and voluminous curls that add definition to your hair. In this article, we will show you how to style thin short curly hair step by step, provide tips and answer frequently asked questions.

How to Style Thin Short Curly Hair

The key to styling thin short curly hair is to add volume and definition. To achieve this, you need to use products that enhance your curls while also providing hold and moisture. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it is clean and free of any product buildup. Use a gentle sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl enhancing cream to damp hair. This will help define your curls and prevent frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and add volume. Gently scrunch your hair with your hands while drying to encourage the curls to form.

Step 4: Add Volume

To add volume to your hair, use a root-lifting spray or mousse. Apply the product to your roots and use your fingers to massage it in. Blow dry your hair upside down to further enhance the volume.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Spray

To set your hairstyle in place, use a hairspray with a flexible hold. Spray it evenly over your hair and scrunch your curls with your hands to add definition.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to style thin short curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl enhancing cream to damp hair. This will help define your curls and prevent frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser Attachment

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and set it to low heat. Gently scrunch your hair with your hands while drying to encourage the curls to form.

Step 4: Add Volume

Apply a root-lifting spray or mousse to your roots to add volume. Use your fingers to massage the product in and blow dry your hair upside down to further enhance the volume.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To set your hairstyle in place, use a flexible hold hairspray. Spray it evenly over your hair and scrunch your curls with your hands to add definition.

Tips for Styling Thin Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your thin short curly hair:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your curls
  • Experiment with different haircuts and styles to find what works best for you

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I straighten my curly hair if it's thin and short?

Yes, you can straighten your hair if you want to change up your style. However, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat to prevent damage.

How often should I wash my thin short curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle or use a lot of hair products, you may need to wash your hair more frequently. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your thin short curly hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often to prevent damage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions with a hat or scarf

With these tips and techniques, you can style your thin short curly hair with confidence and create beautiful, voluminous curls that enhance your natural beauty.
