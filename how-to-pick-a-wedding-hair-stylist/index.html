How To Pick A Wedding Hair Stylist Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Planning a wedding involves a lot of decisions, from the venue to the flowers, the dress to the hair. Choosing the right wedding hair stylist can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. With a little research and some key considerations, you can find the perfect hair stylist to help you look your best on your big day.

Research Potential Stylists

The first step in choosing a wedding hair stylist is to do some research. Start by asking friends and family members for recommendations. You can also search online for hair stylists in your area. Look for stylists who specialize in wedding hair, as they will have the experience and expertise necessary to create the perfect look for your big day.

Read Reviews

Once you have a list of potential stylists, read reviews from past clients. This will give you an idea of their level of professionalism and the quality of their work. Look for reviews that mention the stylist's ability to create the specific style you are looking for.

Look at Their Portfolio

Most wedding hair stylists have a portfolio of their work. Take a look at their website or social media pages to see examples of their previous work. This will give you an idea of their style and whether it matches the look you are going for.

Consider Communication Skills

Communication is key when it comes to working with a wedding hair stylist. Make sure the stylist is easy to communicate with and understands your vision for your hair. They should be able to offer suggestions and work with you to create the perfect look.

Set a Budget

Before you start working with a wedding hair stylist, it's important to set a budget. This will help you avoid overspending and ensure that you can afford the stylist's services. Be upfront about your budget and ask for a detailed quote outlining the cost of their services.

Schedule a Trial Run

Before your wedding day, schedule a trial run with your chosen hair stylist. This will give you the opportunity to try out different styles and make sure you are happy with the final look. Bring along any hair accessories you plan to wear on your wedding day to ensure they work well with your chosen hairstyle.

Get Everything in Writing

Once you have chosen a wedding hair stylist, make sure to get everything in writing. This includes the cost of their services, the date and time of your appointment, and any other important details. Having a written contract will help ensure that there are no misunderstandings on the day of your wedding.

FAQ

What should I bring to my trial run?

Bring any hair accessories you plan to wear on your wedding day, as well as photos of hairstyles you like.

How far in advance should I book my wedding hair stylist?

It's best to book your wedding hair stylist at least six months in advance to ensure availability.

What if I don't like the final look?

If you are unhappy with your hairstyle, speak up and let your stylist know. They may be able to make adjustments to create a look you love.

Hair Care Tips

Leading up to your wedding day, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes regular trims, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week. On the day of your wedding, wash your hair the night before and avoid using any heavy styling products that could weigh down your hair.

Choosing the right wedding hair stylist is an important part of looking and feeling your best on your big day. With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect stylist to create the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day.
