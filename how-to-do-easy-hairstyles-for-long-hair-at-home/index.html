How To Do Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair At Home

Written by Frank Aug 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle every day? Do you want to switch it up without spending hours in front of the mirror? Look no further, because we've got you covered with these easy hairstyles for long hair that you can do at home.

Table of Contents:

  • Section 1: Description
  • Section 2: How to
  • Section 3: Step by Step
  • Section 4: Tips
  • Section 5: FAQ
  • Section 6: Hair Care

Section 1: Description

Long hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it provides plenty of styling options, it can also be overwhelming to figure out what to do with it all. These easy hairstyles for long hair are perfect for anyone who wants to switch up their look without spending hours in front of the mirror.

Section 2: How to

The first step to achieving these easy hairstyles for long hair is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can either air-dry your hair or use a blow dryer to speed up the process.

Once your hair is dry, gather the necessary tools such as a comb, hair ties, bobby pins, and hairspray. You may also want to use a curling iron or straightener depending on the hairstyle you choose.

Section 3: Step by Step

Here are three easy hairstyles for long hair:

1. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style.

2. Half-Up, Half-Down

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Curl the loose hair with a curling iron or straightener.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style.

3. Side Braid

  1. Part your hair to the side.
  2. Gather a section of hair at the front and divide it into three sections.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section.
  4. Add more hair to the right section and cross it over the middle section.
  5. Add more hair to the left section and cross it over the middle section.
  6. Continue adding hair and crossing the sections until you reach the end of your hair.
  7. Secure the braid with a hair tie.

Section 4: Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying out these easy hairstyles for long hair:

  • Use hairspray to hold the style in place.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles.
  • Accessorize with hair clips or headbands for added style.
  • Practice makes perfect, so keep trying until you get it right.

Section 5: FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for long hair:

Q: Can I do these hairstyles on curly hair?

A: Yes, these hairstyles can be done on curly hair. Just make sure to use a curl-defining product before styling.

Q: How long do these hairstyles take to do?

A: These hairstyles can be done in 10-15 minutes, depending on your hair length and skill level.

Q: Do I need any special tools for these hairstyles?

A: No, all you need are basic styling tools such as a comb, hair ties, and bobby pins.

Section 6: Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining long, healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

Now that you know how to do easy hairstyles for long hair at home, it's time to start experimenting. Whether you're going for a casual or fancy look, these hairstyles are sure to impress.
