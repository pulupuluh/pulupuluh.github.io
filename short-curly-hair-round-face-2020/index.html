Short Curly Hair Round Face 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jul 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. Short curly hair can be a great option, but it can also be tricky to work with. That's why we've put together this ultimate guide to short curly hair for round faces in 2020.

Description

Short curly hair is a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle that can look great on round faces. The curls add volume and texture to the hair, helping to balance out the roundness of the face. In 2020, short curly hair is all about embracing natural texture and adding layers for movement and dimension.

How to

If you're considering short curly hair for your round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the length of your hair. Shorter haircuts tend to work best for round faces, as they help to elongate the face shape. Second, think about the type of curls you have. Tight, coiled curls may require more maintenance and styling, while looser waves can be more low-maintenance.

Step by step

If you're ready to take the plunge and try short curly hair, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Consult with a stylist who has experience working with curly hair.
  2. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Invest in high-quality products specifically designed for curly hair.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short curly hair for a round face:

  • Embrace your natural texture.
  • Add layers for movement and dimension.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can make the face look rounder.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help define curls.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help tame frizz and enhance curls.

FAQ

What are some good short curly haircuts for round faces?

Some good options include a bob with layers, a pixie cut with longer layers on top, or a curly shag.

How can I style my short curly hair for a special occasion?

Consider using hot rollers to add volume and definition to your curls. You can also experiment with braids or updos that showcase your natural texture.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner formulated for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Short curly hair can be a great option for round faces, but it's important to choose a haircut and styling technique that works for your unique hair texture and face shape. With the right care and maintenance, you can rock your curls with confidence.
