How To Trim The Back Of Your Head Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Mar 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Trimming the back of your head can be a daunting task, especially if you’re doing it for the first time. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a neat and tidy look that you’ll be proud to show off. In this guide, we’ll take you through the steps to trim the back of your head, including tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Trim the Back of Your Head

Before you start trimming, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly. Wet hair is harder to cut and can result in an uneven trim.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Comb your hair straight back, so it’s smooth and tangle-free.

Step 3: Use Clippers to Trim the Sides

Using your clippers, start trimming the sides of your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 4: Trim the Back of Your Head

Using your handheld mirror, look at the back of your head and use your clippers to trim the hair at the nape of your neck. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to achieve a gradual fade.

Step 5: Blend the Top and Back

Using your scissors, blend the top and back of your hair together. Comb the hair from the back of your head forward and cut it to the desired length.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Use your handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make any final adjustments.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using your favorite products.

Tips for Trimming the Back of Your Head

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use your handheld mirror to check your work frequently.
  • Take your time – rushing can result in uneven cuts.

FAQ

Can I Trim the Back of My Head by Myself?

Yes, you can trim the back of your head by yourself, but it can be challenging. Using a handheld mirror can help you see what you’re doing.

What Tools Do I Need to Trim the Back of My Head?

You’ll need clippers, scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror.

How Often Should I Trim the Back of My Head?

How often you trim the back of your head depends on how quickly your hair grows. As a general rule, trim your hair every four to six weeks.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Trimming the back of your head can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a neat and tidy look. Remember to take your time, use a handheld mirror, and follow our step-by-step guide.
