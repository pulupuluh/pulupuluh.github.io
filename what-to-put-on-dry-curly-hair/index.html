What To Put On Dry Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by William Feb 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As someone with naturally curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to keep your locks moisturized and healthy. Dryness and frizz are common problems that many curly-haired individuals face, but there are ways to combat these issues. In this article, we'll explore what to put on dry curly hair to help you achieve the luscious, healthy curls you've always wanted.

How to Moisturize Dry Curly Hair

The first step in caring for dry curly hair is to moisturize it. Without proper moisture, your curls can become brittle and prone to breakage. Here are some tips for moisturizing dry curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help restore moisture to your hair.
  • Choose a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair after washing to help lock in moisture.

Step-by-Step Guide: What to Put on Dry Curly Hair

Now that you know how to moisturize dry curly hair, let's take a look at what products you should be using. Here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Shampoo

Begin by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. This will help to gently cleanse your hair without stripping it of its natural oils.

Step 2: Conditioner

Next, apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair. Leave it on for the recommended amount of time (usually 10-15 minutes) before rinsing it out with lukewarm water.

Step 3: Leave-In Conditioner or Hair Oil

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help lock in moisture. This will also help to prevent frizz and keep your curls looking healthy and shiny.

Step 4: Styling Products

If you're planning on styling your hair, be sure to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. This can include curl-defining creams, gels, or mousses.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curly Hair

Now that you know what to put on dry curly hair, here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my curly hair?

A: While you can use regular shampoo and conditioner on your curly hair, it's best to choose products that are specifically designed for curly hair. These products will help to keep your curls moisturized and defined.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: This can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go several days between washes. It's best to experiment and see what works best for your hair.

Hair Care: What to Put on Dry Curly Hair

To summarize, the key to caring for dry curly hair is to moisturize it regularly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help lock in moisture. Avoid using heat styling tools, and be sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage. With these tips, you can achieve healthy, beautiful curls that you'll love to show off.
