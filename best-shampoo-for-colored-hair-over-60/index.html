Best Shampoo For Colored Hair Over 60 A Guide To Keeping Your Hair Looking Vibrant

Written by Daniel Mar 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you over 60 and have colored hair? If so, you may be wondering what the best shampoo for colored hair over 60 is. As we age, our hair can become more fragile and prone to breakage, especially if it's been colored. That's why it's important to use a shampoo that's gentle enough for aging hair but still effective in preserving color. In this post, we'll give you tips on how to choose the best shampoo for colored hair over 60, as well as some top recommendations.

How to Choose the Best Shampoo for Colored Hair Over 60

When looking for the best shampoo for colored hair over 60, there are a few things you should consider:

1. Look for sulfate-free formulas

Sulfates are harsh detergents that can strip your hair of its natural oils and color. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free to avoid this.

2. Consider your hair type

Do you have fine or thinning hair? Or is your hair thick and coarse? Make sure to choose a shampoo that's appropriate for your hair type to get the best results.

3. Read the label

Look for shampoos that contain ingredients that are beneficial for colored hair, such as antioxidants like vitamin E and keratin, which can help strengthen and protect your hair.

Top Recommendations for the Best Shampoo for Colored Hair Over 60

Here are some of our top recommendations for the best shampoo for colored hair over 60:

1. L'Oreal Paris EverPure Moisture Shampoo

This sulfate-free shampoo is gentle enough for aging hair and contains rosemary and juniper extracts to help nourish and protect colored hair. It's also vegan and cruelty-free, making it a great choice for those who are environmentally conscious.

2. Nexxus Color Assure Shampoo

This shampoo is formulated with elastin protein and quinoa to help preserve color and strengthen hair. It's also sulfate-free and safe for all hair types.

3. Pureology Hydrate Shampoo

This sulfate-free shampoo is specifically designed for color-treated hair and contains natural ingredients like jojoba, green tea, and sage to help hydrate and protect hair. It's also vegan and cruelty-free.

Tips for Maintaining Colored Hair Over 60

In addition to using the best shampoo for colored hair over 60, there are some other tips you can follow to help maintain your colored hair:

1. Use a deep conditioner

Colored hair can be more prone to dryness, so it's important to use a deep conditioner regularly to help keep it hydrated and healthy.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons can be damaging to colored hair. Try to avoid using them as much as possible or use a heat protectant spray before styling.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can fade hair color, so make sure to wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF when you're out in the sun.

FAQs

What is the best shampoo for colored hair over 60?

The best shampoo for colored hair over 60 is one that's sulfate-free and contains ingredients that are beneficial for colored hair, such as antioxidants like vitamin E and keratin.

How often should I wash my colored hair?

It's generally recommended to wash colored hair every 2-3 days to help preserve color and prevent dryness.

Can I use regular shampoo on colored hair?

Regular shampoos can contain sulfates that can strip color from your hair. It's best to use a shampoo that's specifically designed for colored hair.

Hair Care for Colored Hair Over 60

Colored hair over 60 requires special care to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. By using the best shampoo for colored hair over 60, as well as following some simple tips for hair care, you can keep your hair looking its best.
