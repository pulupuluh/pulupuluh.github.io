How To Curl Short Hair With Bendy Foam Rollers

Written by Luffy Apr 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair can be a bit of a challenge when it comes to styling, but with bendy foam rollers, you can achieve beautiful curls in no time. In this article, we will guide you through the process of using bendy foam rollers to curl your short hair.

Description

Bendy foam rollers are a great option for curling short hair because they are easy to use and gentle on your hair. They come in various sizes, so you can choose the size that fits your hair length and desired curl size. Bendy foam rollers are also affordable and reusable, making them a great investment for your hair styling needs.

How to

Here are the steps to follow to curl your short hair with bendy foam rollers:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it is completely dry before starting the curling process.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections you create will depend on the thickness of your hair. For short hair, you can create 4-6 sections. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 3: Roll your hair

Take one section of hair and wrap it around a bendy foam roller. Start at the ends of your hair and roll the roller up towards your scalp. Once you reach your scalp, bend the ends of the roller to secure it in place. Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Step 4: Set your hair

Once all your hair is rolled up in the bendy foam rollers, use a hair dryer to set the curls. Set the hair dryer on cool or low heat, and blow dry your hair for 5-10 minutes. This will help set the curls in place.

Step 5: Remove the rollers

After your hair is completely dry, remove the bendy foam rollers. Start from the bottom and work your way up, gently pulling out each roller. Once all the rollers are removed, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create your desired hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when curling your short hair with bendy foam rollers:

  • Use a styling mousse or curl enhancing product before rolling your hair to help hold the curls in place.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before rolling it up in the bendy foam rollers to prevent frizz and uneven curls.
  • If you want tighter curls, use smaller bendy foam rollers. For looser curls, use larger rollers.
  • Don't roll your hair too tightly around the rollers to prevent discomfort and hair breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling short hair with bendy foam rollers:

Can I sleep with bendy foam rollers in my hair?

Yes, you can. Bendy foam rollers are comfortable to sleep in and won't damage your hair. Just make sure your hair is completely dry before going to bed.

How long should I leave bendy foam rollers in my hair?

You should leave the rollers in your hair for at least 3-4 hours or overnight for best results.

How often can I use bendy foam rollers on my hair?

You can use bendy foam rollers on your hair as often as you like. They are gentle on your hair and won't damage it.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair before and after using bendy foam rollers. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to curl short hair with bendy foam rollers, you can experiment with different curl sizes and styles. Have fun and enjoy your new curly look!
