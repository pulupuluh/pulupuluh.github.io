How To Style Hair With Soft Curls A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a way to add some volume and texture to your hair, soft curls are a great option. Not only do they look gorgeous, but they're also easy to achieve with the right tools and techniques. In this blog post, we'll show you how to style your hair with soft curls in a relaxed, natural way.

How to Style Hair with Soft Curls

Soft curls are a great way to add some volume and texture to your hair without going overboard. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any buildup or residue that may be weighing down your curls.

2. Apply a heat protectant

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage from heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners.

3. Blow dry your hair

Next, use a blow dryer to dry your hair until it's about 80% dry. Make sure to use a diffuser attachment to help distribute the heat evenly and prevent frizz.

4. Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that each section is evenly curled.

5. Curl your hair

Take a curling iron and wrap a small section of hair around the barrel. Hold it for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

6. Shake out your curls

Once you've curled all sections of your hair, shake out your curls to create a more natural, relaxed look.

7. Add some finishing touches

To finish off your look, you can add some hair serum or natural oils to your hair to add some shine and smoothness.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair with soft curls:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant to your hair.

Step 3: Use a blow dryer to dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.

Step 4: Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.

Step 5: Take a curling iron and wrap a small section of hair around the barrel. Hold it for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

Step 6: Shake out your curls to create a more natural, relaxed look.

Step 7: Add some finishing touches by applying hair serum or natural oils to your hair.

Tips for Soft Curls

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair with soft curls:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Apply a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to prevent frizz.
  • Section your hair into small sections to ensure that each section is evenly curled.
  • Shake out your curls to create a more natural, relaxed look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair with soft curls:

Q: What size curling iron should I use?

A: It depends on the length and thickness of your hair. For shorter hair, a smaller curling iron (around 1 inch) will work best. For longer hair, you can use a larger curling iron (around 1.5 inches).

Q: How long should I hold the curling iron on my hair?

A: It depends on the thickness and texture of your hair. For fine hair, hold the curling iron for around 5-7 seconds. For thicker hair, hold it for around 10-12 seconds.

Hair Care for Soft Curls

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair when you have soft curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your curls from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from becoming tangled or frizzy while you sleep.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, soft curls that will make your hair look voluminous and full of life. Whether you're going for a natural, relaxed look or a more polished style, soft curls are a great choice for any occasion.
