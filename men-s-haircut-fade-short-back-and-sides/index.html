Men s Haircut Fade Short Back And Sides A Complete Guide

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that's easy to maintain, then the men's haircut fade short back and sides is the perfect choice for you. This haircut is a classic style that has been around for decades, but it has recently gained popularity due to its versatility and modern look. In this article, we will go through the description, how-to, step-by-step, tips, FAQ, and hair care of the men's haircut fade short back and sides.

Description

The men's haircut fade short back and sides is a versatile and modern style that features a short cut on the back and sides of the head, with a gradual fade that blends into longer hair on the top. This haircut is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance style that can be easily styled and maintained.

The fade on the sides and back of the head can be customized to your preference, with different lengths and fades available. The top of the hair can be styled in various ways, including a slicked-back look, a messy fringe, or a textured crop.

How to

If you want to get the men's haircut fade short back and sides, it's best to go to a professional hairstylist. However, if you want to try it at home, here are the basic steps:

  1. Start by using clippers with a guard to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head to your preferred length.
  2. Use a shorter guard to create the fade, gradually blending the hair from short to longer as you move up towards the top of the head.
  3. Trim the hair on top of the head to your preferred length and style it as desired.

Step by step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Section off the hair on top of your head with a clip or hair tie.
  3. Choose the guard size you want to use for the sides and back of your head. Start with a bigger guard and work your way down to a shorter guard.
  4. Using the clippers, buzz the hair on the sides and back of your head to your preferred length.
  5. Use the next shortest guard to create the fade on the sides and back of your head. Start with the guard at the bottom of your hairline and gradually move it up as you blend the hair from short to long.
  6. Blend the hair on the sides and back of your head into the hair on top by using a comb to pull the hair up and blend it together.
  7. Trim the hair on top of your head to your preferred length and style it as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect men's haircut fade short back and sides:

  • Make sure you have the right tools, including clippers, guards, scissors, and a comb.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to create a gradual fade.
  • Use a comb to blend the hair on the sides and back of your head into the hair on top.
  • Experiment with different styles on top, including a slicked-back look, a messy fringe, or a textured crop.

FAQ

How often do I need to get a men's haircut fade short back and sides?

The frequency of your haircuts will depend on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain your style. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the fade.

Can I style my hair differently on top?

Yes, the top of your hair can be styled in various ways to suit your preference. Some popular styles include a slicked-back look, a messy fringe, or a textured crop.

Is the men's haircut fade short back and sides suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the men's haircut fade short back and sides is a versatile style that can be customized to suit any face shape. Your hairstylist can help you choose the best length and fade for your face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain your men's haircut fade short back and sides, it's important to keep your hair clean and well-conditioned. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Regular trims are also essential to maintain the shape and style of the fade. Use a comb to keep your hair looking neat and tidy, and experiment with different styling products to find the best one for your hair type and style.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can enjoy a stylish and low-maintenance men's haircut fade short back and sides that will make you look and feel your best.
