How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Bald On Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Mar 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to cut your own hair at home, or you're a barber looking to improve your skills, cutting men's hair with clippers bald on top is an essential technique to master. It's a popular style that's easy to achieve, but there are a few things you need to know before you get started. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to cut men's hair with clippers bald on top, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers Bald on Top: Step-by-Step

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather some equipment. You'll need a pair of clippers, a comb, a pair of scissors, and a towel. Once you have your equipment, you can start the cutting process:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

Before you start cutting, you need to decide on the length of the hair you want to cut. If you're going for a full bald look, you'll want to use the shortest guard on your clippers. If you want a little bit of hair left on top, you can use a longer guard.

Step 2: Cut the Top of the Hair

Start by cutting the top of the hair using the longest guard on your clippers. Work from the front to the back of the head, taking care to keep the clippers level and parallel to the scalp. Use the comb to lift the hair as you go, and take your time to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.

Step 3: Blend the Sides and Back

Once you've cut the top of the hair, you can move on to the sides and back. Use a shorter guard on your clippers to blend the sides and back into the top. Start by using the longest guard and work your way down to the shortest guard, taking care to blend the hair evenly.

Step 4: Clean Up the Edges

After you've cut the hair, use your scissors to clean up any stray hairs and to shape the hairline. Use your clippers to trim the hair behind the ears and at the nape of the neck, and use your comb to ensure that the hair is even all around.

Step 5: Wash and Style

After you've finished cutting the hair, wash it with shampoo and conditioner to remove any loose hairs. Style the hair as desired, using a hairbrush or comb to create the desired look.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers Bald on Top

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting men's hair with clippers bald on top:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to ensure a gradual fade.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair as you go, and take your time to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.
  • Use your scissors to clean up any stray hairs and to shape the hairline.
  • Wash the hair after cutting to remove any loose hairs and to ensure that the hair is clean and healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with clippers bald on top:

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers, but it's important to follow the proper technique to avoid any mistakes.

What equipment do I need to cut men's hair with clippers bald on top?

You'll need a pair of clippers, a comb, a pair of scissors, and a towel.

How do I clean my clippers?

Clean your clippers after each use by removing any hair and debris, and wiping them down with a disinfectant spray or alcohol wipe.

Hair Care Advice

Cutting men's hair with clippers bald on top can be a great way to achieve a simple, stylish look. However, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt and oil.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to ensure that your hair gets the nutrients it needs to stay strong and healthy.
  • Visit a barber regularly to keep your hair looking its best.

Cutting men's hair with clippers bald on top is a simple technique that anyone can master with a little practice. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to achieve the perfect look every time.
