Does Facial Hair Thicken With Age

Written by Aprilia Aug 23, 2023 · 6 min read

Facial hair has been a symbol of masculinity for centuries. From the beard of the Greek gods to the mustache of the 80s, facial hair has always been a statement of manliness. As men age, many wonder if their facial hair will thicken or if they will lose it altogether. In this article, we will answer the question, 'does facial hair thicken with age?' and provide tips on how to care for your facial hair.

Table of Content

  • What Causes Facial Hair to Grow?
  • Does Facial Hair Thicken with Age?
  • How to Care for Your Facial Hair
  • FAQ

What Causes Facial Hair to Grow?

Facial hair growth is caused by testosterone, a hormone found in both men and women. However, men have higher levels of testosterone, which is why they typically have more facial hair than women. Testosterone stimulates the hair follicles to grow, resulting in thicker and longer hair.

Facial hair growth usually starts during puberty and continues to grow throughout a man’s life. However, the rate of growth and thickness of the hair may vary depending on age, genetics, and lifestyle factors.

Does Facial Hair Thicken with Age?

Yes, facial hair can thicken with age. As men age, their testosterone levels decrease, which can lead to a decrease in facial hair growth. However, some men may experience an increase in facial hair growth as they age due to changes in hormone levels or genetics.

To promote facial hair growth, men can try using beard oils, which contain essential oils that may stimulate hair growth. Additionally, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, such as eating a balanced diet and exercising regularly, may promote healthy hair growth.

How to Care for Your Facial Hair

Proper facial hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, thick hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a beard shampoo to remove dirt and oil buildup
  • Use a beard oil to moisturize and nourish the hair and skin
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends
  • Brush your beard with a beard brush to distribute oils and detangle the hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your beard, as they can damage the hair and skin

FAQ

1. Can I use regular shampoo on my beard?

No, regular shampoo can be too harsh for your beard and can strip the hair of its natural oils. It is best to use a beard shampoo, which is formulated specifically for facial hair.

2. How often should I trim my beard?

It depends on the length and style of your beard. Generally, it is recommended to trim your beard every 2-4 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

3. Will shaving make my beard grow back thicker?

No, shaving does not make your beard grow back thicker. This is a myth. Shaving only removes the hair that has already grown, and it does not affect the rate or thickness of future hair growth.

In conclusion, facial hair can thicken with age, but it depends on various factors such as genetics, hormone levels, and lifestyle choices. To promote healthy hair growth, men should maintain a healthy lifestyle and take care of their facial hair by using proper grooming techniques and products.
