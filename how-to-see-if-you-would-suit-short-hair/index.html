How To See If You Would Suit Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 15, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you considering chopping off your locks and going for a shorter hairstyle? Short hair can be a bold and stylish choice, but it’s important to make sure it suits your face shape and personal style before taking the plunge. In this article, we’ll walk you through how to see if you would suit short hair, including steps to take, tips to consider, and hair care advice to keep your new ‘do looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture
  • Step 3: Look for Inspiration
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Short Hairstyle
  • FAQs about Short Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to seeing if you would suit short hair is to determine your face shape. There are a few different face shapes, including:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond

Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features. For example, a pixie cut might look great on someone with an oval face, while a bob with side-swept bangs could be a better choice for someone with a square face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider when choosing a short hairstyle is your hair texture. If you have thick, curly hair, you might want to opt for a shorter cut that will be easier to manage. On the other hand, if you have fine, straight hair, you might want to choose a style that adds volume and texture.

It’s also important to consider how much time you’re willing to spend styling your hair each day. If you’re someone who likes to roll out of bed and go, a low-maintenance cut like a pixie or a bob might be a good choice.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Now that you know your face shape and hair texture, it’s time to start looking for inspiration. Browse magazines, Pinterest, or Instagram for photos of short hairstyles that catch your eye. Pay attention to the shape of the cut, the length, and any additional styling elements like bangs or layers.

Once you’ve found a few styles you like, consider booking a consultation with a hairstylist to get their opinion on whether the style would suit you. They can also give you advice on how to maintain your new haircut and what styling products to use.

Tips for Choosing the Right Short Hairstyle

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

  • Think about your personal style. Are you someone who likes to stand out with a bold haircut, or do you prefer a more classic look?
  • Consider your lifestyle. Are you someone who’s always on the go, or do you have more time to spend on your hair each day?
  • Be open to trying something new. Even if you’ve always had long hair, a short haircut could be a fun and refreshing change.

FAQs about Short Hair

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. It’s all about choosing the right cut for your face shape. If you have a round face, a style with layers and volume at the crown can help elongate your features.

Q: Can I still have bangs with short hair?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of short hairstyles that incorporate bangs, from blunt fringes to side-swept styles.

Q: Will I need to get my hair cut more frequently with a short style?

A: Short hair does require more frequent trims to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. You may need to visit the salon every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Once you’ve chosen a short hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that’s suited to your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools.
  • Consider using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don’t forget to protect your hair from the sun and chlorine if you’re swimming outdoors.

By following these steps and tips, you’ll be well on your way to determining whether short hair is the right choice for you. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
