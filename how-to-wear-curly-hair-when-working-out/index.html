How To Wear Curly Hair When Working Out

Written by Petter Apr 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that maintaining your curls can be a challenge, especially when you're working out. Sweating, frizz, and tangling can all wreak havoc on your curls. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to wear curly hair when working out, so you can stay comfortable and confident during your workouts.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Curly Hair When Working Out
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Your Curls Intact
  • FAQs About Wearing Curly Hair When Working Out
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Wear Curly Hair When Working Out

When it comes to wearing curly hair when working out, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension on your scalp and lead to breakage.
  • Choose hairstyles that keep your hair out of your face and off your neck.
  • Use products that will help keep your curls intact and prevent frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to wear your curly hair when working out:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be more prone to tangling and frizzing during your workout.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair. This will help keep your curls defined and prevent frizz.
  3. Choose a hairstyle that works for you. Some good options include a high ponytail, braids, or a bun.
  4. Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as they can cause breakage. Instead, opt for hair ties made of fabric or elastic.
  5. If your hair tends to frizz, apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair.

Tips for Keeping Your Curls Intact

Here are some additional tips for keeping your curls intact while working out:

  • Invest in a good quality satin or silk pillowcase. This will help prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as they can cause damage and make your curls more prone to frizzing.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

FAQs About Wearing Curly Hair When Working Out

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing curly hair when working out:

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Some people with curly hair can get away with washing their hair once a week, while others may need to wash more often. Experiment to find out what works best for your hair.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair before working out?

A: We don't recommend it. Straightening your hair can cause damage and make your curls more prone to frizzing. Instead, opt for a hairstyle that works with your curls.

Q: What should I do if my curls get tangled during my workout?

A: Use your fingers to gently detangle your hair. Avoid using a brush, as it can cause breakage and make your curls more prone to frizzing.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as they can dry out your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips, you should be able to wear your curly hair when working out without any problems. Remember to take care of your hair, and it will take care of you!
