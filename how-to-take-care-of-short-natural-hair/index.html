How To Take Care Of Short Natural Hair

Written by Oliver Aug 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short natural hair is a beautiful and versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. However, taking care of short natural hair can be challenging, especially if you are new to the natural hair community. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to take care of short natural hair. We will cover everything from washing and conditioning to styling and maintenance. So, let's get started!

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step to taking care of short natural hair is to determine your hair type. Knowing your hair type will help you choose the right products and styling techniques. There are four main hair types: type 1 (straight), type 2 (wavy), type 3 (curly), and type 4 (coily/kinky). Short natural hair can fall under any of these hair types.

Once you have determined your hair type, you can start to build a hair care routine that works for you. Here are some general tips for taking care of short natural hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Condition your hair after every wash
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Protect your hair at night with a satin bonnet or pillowcase

Step by Step

Now let's break down the steps for taking care of short natural hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Begin by wetting your hair thoroughly. Apply a sulfate-free shampoo to your scalp and massage it in using your fingertips. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.

Step 2: Condition your hair

Apply a conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while the conditioner is still in. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.

Step 3: Deep condition your hair

Once a week, apply a deep conditioner to your hair. Cover your hair with a plastic cap and let the conditioner sit for at least 30 minutes. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.

Step 4: Style your hair

There are many ways to style short natural hair. You can wear it in a wash and go, twist out, braid out, or protective style. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Step 5: Maintain your hair

Maintaining short natural hair requires regular trimming to prevent split ends. You should also moisturize your hair daily and protect it at night with a satin bonnet or pillowcase.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for taking care of short natural hair:

  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them sparingly
  • Avoid using hair elastics, as they can cause breakage
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf

FAQ

Here are some common questions about taking care of short natural hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair as often as necessary to keep it clean. For most people, this means washing their hair once or twice a week.

What can I do to prevent breakage?

To prevent breakage, you should avoid using hair elastics, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and protect your hair at night with a satin bonnet or pillowcase.

How can I keep my hair moisturized?

You can keep your hair moisturized by using a leave-in conditioner, oil, or cream daily. You should also deep condition your hair once a week.

Hair Care

Taking care of short natural hair requires patience and consistency. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Remember to listen to your hair and adjust your routine as necessary. With time and practice, you will become a pro at taking care of your short natural hair!
