What Are Guys With Beards Called

Written by William Aug 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are a guy with a beard, you may have wondered what people call you. There are many terms that people use to describe men with beards, and in this article, we will explore some of the most popular ones.

Table of Contents

  2. How To Grow a Beard

Description

Men with beards have been around for centuries, and they have been called many different things throughout history. In the past, men with beards were often associated with wisdom, power, and masculinity. Today, beards are seen as a fashion statement and a symbol of masculinity.

How To Grow a Beard

For some men, growing a beard comes naturally, while for others, it can be a challenge. To grow a beard, you need to have patience, perseverance, and a few tips to help you along the way.

Here are some tips for growing a beard:

  • Start by letting your facial hair grow out for a few weeks.
  • Trim your beard regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and moisturized.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Exercise regularly to boost testosterone levels, which can promote beard growth.

Step By Step Guide

If you are new to growing a beard, it can be helpful to have a step-by-step guide to follow. Here is a basic guide to growing a beard:

  1. Stop shaving and let your facial hair grow out for at least two weeks.
  2. Trim your beard using scissors or clippers to keep it neat and tidy.
  3. Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and moisturized.
  4. Brush your beard regularly to remove any tangles or knots.
  5. Try different beard styles to find one that suits your face shape and personal style.

Tips For Maintaining Your Beard

Once you have grown a beard, it is important to keep it looking neat and tidy. Here are some tips for maintaining your beard:

  • Trim your beard regularly to keep it at the desired length.
  • Wash your beard regularly with a beard shampoo or soap.
  • Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and moisturized.
  • Brush your beard regularly to remove any tangles or knots.
  • Avoid using regular shampoo or soap on your beard as it can be too harsh and dry out the skin beneath.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are guys with beards called?

Men with beards are often called "bearded men" or "beardos." Other terms include "lumbersexuals," "hipsters," and "dudes with facial hair."

Why do men grow beards?

Men grow beards for various reasons, including cultural and religious traditions, personal style, and to enhance their masculinity.

How long does it take to grow a beard?

It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to grow a full beard, depending on genetics and other factors.

Beard Hair Care

Just like the hair on your head, your beard hair needs care and attention. Here are some tips for keeping your beard hair healthy:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a beard shampoo or soap.
  • Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and moisturized.
  • Brush your beard regularly to remove any tangles or knots.
  • Avoid using regular shampoo or soap on your beard as it can be too harsh and dry out the skin beneath.
  • Trim your beard regularly to keep it at the desired length.

Conclusion

So, what are guys with beards called? There are many terms that people use to describe men with beards, but ultimately, it comes down to personal preference. Whether you call yourself a "beardo," a "lumbersexual," or simply a man with a beard, wearing a beard is a personal choice and a statement of individuality.
