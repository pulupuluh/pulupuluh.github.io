What Products Can I Use To Make My Hair Thicker

Written by Eveline May 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone who struggles with thin or fine hair, you may be wondering what products you can use to make your hair appear thicker. The good news is, there are several products on the market that can help boost volume and thickness. In this blog post, we'll explore some of the best options available to you.

Description

Before we dive into specific products, it's important to understand what causes thin or fine hair. Genetics, age, and hormonal changes can all play a role in hair thickness. Additionally, certain styling techniques and products can damage hair and make it appear thinner. By using the right products, you can help improve the overall health and appearance of your hair.

How To

When it comes to making your hair appear thicker, there are a few key products to keep in mind. These include:

1. Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

One of the easiest ways to boost hair thickness is by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products work by coating each hair strand, giving it a fuller appearance. Look for formulas that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and panthenol, which can help strengthen hair as well.

2. Thickening Spray

A thickening spray is another great option for adding volume and fullness to your hair. Simply spray onto damp hair before blow-drying to help plump up each strand. Look for sprays that contain hydrolyzed wheat protein, which can help strengthen hair and prevent breakage.

3. Hair Fibers

If you're looking for a more dramatic effect, consider using hair fibers. These are tiny fibers that attach to your hair, creating the appearance of thicker locks. They come in a variety of colors and can be used on any hair type or texture.

Step by Step

So, how do you use these products to make your hair appear thicker? Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add fullness and thickness. Be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid any buildup.

2. Apply Thickening Spray

Once your hair is towel-dried, apply a thickening spray from roots to ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down, using the brush to lift each section and create volume.

4. Add Hair Fibers (Optional)

If you want to add even more thickness, you can use hair fibers at this point. Simply sprinkle onto your hair and pat gently to distribute.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when trying to make your hair appear thicker:

  • Avoid using heavy products like oils or serums, which can weigh down your hair and make it appear thinner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to avoid pulling or breaking hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, which can damage hair and make it appear thinner.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends, which can make hair appear thinner.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about making hair appear thicker:

1. Can I use these products on color-treated hair?

Yes, most volumizing and thickening products are safe to use on color-treated hair. However, be sure to check the label to make sure.

2. How often should I use these products?

You can use these products as often as needed to achieve your desired level of thickness and volume. However, be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid any buildup.

3. Will these products make my hair feel greasy?

Not necessarily. Most volumizing and thickening products are lightweight and won't leave your hair feeling greasy or weighed down. However, if you have particularly oily hair, you may want to avoid using too much product.

Hair Care

Remember, the key to making your hair appear thicker is to take care of it properly. This means using gentle products, avoiding damaging styling techniques, and getting regular trims. By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve the thick, full hair you've always wanted.
