How To Cut Bangs For Wavy Curly Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting bangs for wavy curly hair may sound like a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily at home. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of cutting bangs for wavy curly hair. We will also provide tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect bangs.

Table of Contents

  • Tools you will need
  • How to cut bangs for wavy curly hair
  • Step-by-step guide
  • Tips for cutting bangs for wavy curly hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair care tips for wavy curly hair

Tools you will need

Before you start cutting your bangs, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to separate hair
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Small mirror

How to cut bangs for wavy curly hair

The key to cutting bangs for wavy curly hair is to cut them dry. This will allow you to see how the hair will naturally fall and prevent any surprises after the cut. Follow these steps:

Step-by-step guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Separate the bangs from the rest of your hair with clips
  3. Make sure the bangs are even in length and thickness
  4. Take a small section of hair at the center of the forehead
  5. Hold the hair between your fingers and comb through it
  6. Start cutting at the tip of the hair with small, sharp scissors
  7. Work your way up, cutting small sections at a time
  8. Stop cutting when you reach your desired length
  9. Check the length by holding the hair down with your fingers and looking in the mirror
  10. Repeat on both sides, making sure the bangs are even

Tips for cutting bangs for wavy curly hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect bangs for wavy curly hair:

  • Take it slow and cut small sections at a time
  • Don't cut too much at once
  • Use the tip of the scissors to create texture
  • Don't pull the hair too tight while cutting
  • Consider the shape of your face and adjust the length accordingly

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting bangs for wavy curly hair:

Can I cut my bangs when they are wet?

No, it's best to cut bangs for wavy curly hair when they are dry. Wet hair can shrink up to 30%, so cutting them when wet can result in shorter bangs than you intended.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake while cutting your bangs, don't panic. You can always adjust the length later. It's better to cut less than to cut too much.

How often do I need to trim my bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the length. On average, you should trim your bangs every 2-3 weeks.

Hair care tips for wavy curly hair

Caring for your wavy curly hair is important to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle wet hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized

Now that you know how to cut bangs for wavy curly hair, you can try it at home. Follow our step-by-step guide and use our tips to achieve the perfect bangs. Remember to take it slow and adjust the length as needed. Happy cutting!
