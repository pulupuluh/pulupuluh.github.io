Which Hairstyle Looks Good For Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by William Mar 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a bit challenging. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll discuss which hairstyles look good for round faces, and how to achieve them.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Round Face
  3. Step by Step Guide for Round Face Hairstyles
  4. Tips for Round Face Hair Care
  5. Frequently Asked Questions

Description

A round face is characterized by a rounded chin and full cheeks. The width and length of the face are almost equal, with a soft and curvy jawline. When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, the goal is to create an illusion of length and definition to balance the roundness of the face.

Hairstyles that suit round faces are those that add height and volume on top, elongate the face, and create angles and definition around the jawline. As a general rule, avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, as they can make the face appear even rounder.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Round Face

Choosing the right hairstyle for a round face can be a bit tricky. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume on top, such as layered cuts, bangs, and side-swept styles.
  • Go for hairstyles that create angles and definition around the jawline, such as bob cuts, asymmetrical styles, and long, face-framing layers.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt cuts, center parts, and straight, long hair.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension to your hair.

Step by Step Guide for Round Face Hairstyles

Here are some hairstyles that look great on round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers add length and movement to the hair, creating an elongated look. To achieve this style:

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Create long layers that start below the chin and blend into the ends of your hair.
  3. Finish with a volumizing spray to add height and body to your hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs create angles and definition around the face, making it appear less round. To achieve this style:

  1. Part your hair to one side.
  2. Create a long, side-swept fringe that starts at the crown and blends into the rest of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry the fringe to create volume and shape.

3. Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that looks great on round faces. The length of the hair should be just below the chin, creating a slimming effect. To achieve this style:

  1. Create a center part.
  2. Cut your hair in a straight line just below the chin.
  3. Add some layers around the face to create definition.

Tips for Round Face Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.
  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add height and body to your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt cuts, center parts, and straight, long hair.

Should I go for a short or long hairstyle if I have a round face?

Both short and long hairstyles can look great on round faces, as long as they add height and volume on top and create angles and definition around the jawline.

What hair color looks best on round faces?

Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension to your hair. Ashy tones and cool colors can also help to slim down the face.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for a round face can be a bit challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Experiment with different styles and don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice. With the right hairstyle, you can accentuate your best features and create the illusion of length and definition.
