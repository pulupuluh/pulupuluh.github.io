How To Make Straight Hair Curly Without Heat

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have straight hair and want to add some curls without using heat, you're in luck! There are several methods you can try that don't require any heat tools. In this article, we'll cover the best ways to make straight hair curly without heat. From using hair rollers to braids, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Straight Hair Curly Without Heat
  • Using Hair Rollers
  • Using Braids
  • Using Hairbands
  • Hair Care Tips

Using Hair Rollers

Hair rollers are a great way to create curls without heat. Here's how to use them:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Roll each section of hair onto a roller, starting at the ends and rolling towards your scalp.
  4. Secure the roller in place with a clip or hair tie.
  5. Leave the rollers in your hair for several hours or overnight.
  6. Remove the rollers and gently brush out your curls with your fingers.

This method is great for creating tight curls or loose waves, depending on the size of the rollers you use. You can also use different types of rollers, such as foam or velcro, to achieve different curl types.

Using Braids

Braiding your hair is another way to create curls without heat. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Braid each section of hair tightly, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Secure the ends of the braids with a hair tie.
  5. Leave the braids in your hair for several hours or overnight.
  6. Remove the hair ties and gently unravel the braids.

This method is great for creating loose waves or more defined curls, depending on how tight you braid your hair.

Using Hairbands

Another way to create curls without heat is to use hairbands. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Take a hairband and place it at the roots of one section of hair.
  4. Wrap the hair around the hairband, twisting it as you go.
  5. Continue wrapping the hair around the hairband until you reach the ends.
  6. Secure the ends of the hair with another hairband.
  7. Repeat this process with the rest of your hair.
  8. Leave the hairbands in your hair for several hours or overnight.
  9. Remove the hairbands and gently shake out your curls.

This method is great for creating loose, bouncy curls.

Hair Care Tips

When trying to make straight hair curly without heat, it's important to take care of your hair to avoid damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as much as possible.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do need to use heat tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you make straight hair curly without heat permanently?

No, you can't make straight hair curly permanently without using a chemical treatment, such as a perm.

What is the best way to make straight hair curly without heat?

The best way to make straight hair curly without heat depends on your hair type and the type of curls you want. Hair rollers, braids, and hairbands are all great options to try.

How long do the curls last?

The length of time the curls last depends on your hair type and the method you used. Generally, curls created without heat will last for a day or two, but you can make them last longer by using hairspray or other styling products.

Can you use these methods on wet hair?

No, it's best to use these methods on dry hair to avoid damage and frizz.

There you have it, three ways to make straight hair curly without heat. Give them a try and see which method works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy, bouncy curls.
