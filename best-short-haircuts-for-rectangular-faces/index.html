Best Short Haircuts For Rectangular Faces In 2023

Written by Petter Aug 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a rectangular face shape, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your features. But worry not, as we have rounded up the best short haircuts for rectangular faces that will make you look stunning. Read on to find out more!

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Rectangular Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Short Haircut for Rectangular Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Rectangular Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Rectangular Faces

Description

A rectangular face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a strong jawline, and a forehead that's about the same width as the cheekbones. The key to choosing the best short haircut for this face shape is to add width and volume to the sides of the face while softening the sharp angles of the jawline.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Rectangular Faces

The best short haircuts for rectangular faces are those that add volume and width to the sides of the face, while softening the jawline. Here are some tips to help you choose the best short haircut for your rectangular face:

  • Look for haircuts that add volume to the sides of the face, such as pixie cuts or layered bobs.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or flat, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Consider adding bangs to your haircut, as they can help shorten the appearance of your face.
  • Consult with your stylist to determine the best haircut that flatters your face shape and features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Short Haircut for Rectangular Faces

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect short haircut for rectangular faces:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and features.
  2. Show your stylist pictures of the haircut you want to achieve.
  3. Ask your stylist to add volume to the sides of your face while softening the sharp angles of your jawline.
  4. Consider adding bangs to your haircut to help shorten the appearance of your face.
  5. Ask your stylist to style your hair in a way that flatters your face shape and features.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Rectangular Faces

Here are some tips for maintaining short haircuts for rectangular faces:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a pixie cut if I have a rectangular face?

A: Yes, you can still wear a pixie cut if you have a rectangular face. Just make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out the length of your face.

Q: Will adding bangs to my haircut help shorten the appearance of my face?

A: Yes, adding bangs to your haircut can help shorten the appearance of your face by drawing attention to your eyes and forehead.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Rectangular Faces

Here are some hair care tips for short haircuts for rectangular faces:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as it can weigh it down and make it look flat.

Now that you know the best short haircuts for rectangular faces, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out a new look. Remember to choose a haircut that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful!
