How Do You Cut A Pixie Style Haircut

Written by William Aug 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Pixie style haircuts are a popular choice among women who want to make a bold fashion statement. This short and sassy haircut is easy to style and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair textures. If you're thinking about getting a pixie cut, you might be wondering how to cut it yourself at home or how to communicate your desired style to your hairdresser. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the process of cutting a pixie style haircut step by step.

Description

A pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that usually features short layers at the back and sides and longer layers on top. It's a low-maintenance haircut that's perfect for women who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair. Pixie cuts can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, tousled, or with a side-swept fringe. This haircut suits most face shapes and hair textures, but it's important to choose the right style for your features.

How to Cut a Pixie Style Haircut

If you're confident with your hairdressing skills, you can try cutting a pixie style haircut at home. Follow these steps to achieve a stylish and professional-looking result:

Step 1: Prepare your hair and tools

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it with a hair dryer. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step 2: Section your hair

Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: the crown, the sides, and the nape. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the back and sides

Start cutting the back and sides of your hair using the shortest length on your scissors. Cut the hair in small sections, working your way up from the nape to the crown. Use your comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle. Make sure the hair is even on both sides.

Step 4: Cut the crown

Cut the hair on the crown of your head slightly longer than the sides and back. Use your comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle. Blend the hair with the sides and back to create a seamless look.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once you've cut your hair, you can style it in different ways. You can use a hair product to add texture and volume, or you can slick it back for a more polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect pixie style haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut your hair too short on the first try. You can always trim it later.
  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consult a hairdresser if you're not confident cutting your hair at home.

FAQ

Is a pixie style haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a pixie style haircut can be adapted to suit different face shapes. A hairdresser can help you choose a style that flatters your features.

How often should I trim my pixie style haircut?

You should trim your pixie style haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Can I style my pixie haircut in different ways?

Yes, a pixie haircut can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back or with a side-swept fringe. Experiment with different hair products to achieve the look you want.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie style haircut looking its best, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a hair mask once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Conclusion

A pixie style haircut is a chic and versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your individual style. Whether you cut it yourself at home or visit a hairdresser, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve a stylish and professional-looking pixie cut.
