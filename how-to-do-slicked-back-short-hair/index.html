How To Do Slicked Back Short Hair

Are you looking for a sleek and stylish hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion? Look no further than slicked back short hair! This classic look is easy to achieve and can be customized to fit your personal style. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to do slicked back short hair, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you maintain this look.

How to Slick Back Short Hair

Slicked back short hair is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. It is a versatile look that can be dressed up or down, and it is easy to achieve with the right tools and techniques.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect slicked back short hair look:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your fingertips and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.
  3. Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair back away from your face.
  4. Continue to comb your hair back until it is smooth and sleek.
  5. If necessary, use a blow dryer on low heat to help set the style.
  6. Finish with a spritz of hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

That's it! With these simple steps, you can achieve a sleek and stylish slicked back short hair look that is perfect for any occasion.

Tips for Slicked Back Short Hair

To achieve the best results with slicked back short hair, keep these tips in mind:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade to help hold your style in place.
  • Comb your hair back in small sections for a smooth and sleek look.
  • Use a blow dryer on low heat to help set your style and add volume.
  • Finish with a spritz of hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the look that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about slicked back short hair:

Q: Can slicked back short hair be worn by both men and women?

Yes, slicked back short hair is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by both men and women. It is a classic look that never goes out of style.

Q: What type of hair gel or pomade is best for slicked back short hair?

It is best to use a high-quality hair gel or pomade that is specifically designed for slicked back hairstyles. Look for products that provide a strong hold and a shiny finish.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have slicked back short hair?

It is best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Short Hair

To keep your slicked back short hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade to help hold your style in place.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to keep your look fresh and interesting.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your slicked back short hair looking its best and maintain your stylish and sophisticated look.

Now that you know how to do slicked back short hair, it's time to try out this classic look for yourself. With the right tools, techniques, and hair care tips, you can achieve a sleek and stylish look that is perfect for any occasion.
