Does Short Hair Make Your Head Look Smaller

Written by William Apr 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short hair has been a popular hairstyle for many years. It is low maintenance, easy to style, and can give a fresh, youthful look. However, some people wonder if short hair makes their head look smaller. In this article, we will explore this question and provide tips on how to rock short hair without worrying about the size of your head.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Short hair can make your head appear smaller, but it depends on the cut and style. Some short hairstyles can create an illusion of a smaller head, while others can make it look bigger. It also depends on the shape of your face and head. If you have a round or square face, a short haircut may make your head look smaller. On the other hand, if you have an oval or heart-shaped face, short hair can complement your features and make your head look proportional.

How To

If you are considering getting a short haircut and want to avoid making your head look smaller, here are some tips:

Choose the right cut

The right cut can do wonders for your head shape. Opt for a short haircut that adds volume to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut or a bob with layers. This will balance out your head and make it appear more proportional.

Consider your face shape

Your face shape plays a big role in how your head looks with short hair. If you have a round face, avoid blunt cuts that end at your jawline. Instead, go for a layered cut that adds texture and framing to your face. If you have a square face, consider a side-swept bob or a pixie cut with longer layers on top.

Style it right

The way you style your short hair can also affect the size of your head. Avoid sleek, flat hairstyles that can make your head look smaller. Instead, add volume and texture to your hair with mousse or texturizing spray. Experiment with different styles, such as a messy bun or a tousled look, to find what works best for you.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can follow to style your short hair and avoid making your head look smaller:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of mousse or texturizing spray to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, lifting it at the roots to add volume.
  4. Create loose waves with a curling iron or a flat iron.
  5. Flip your hair to one side and create a deep side part.
  6. Add a small amount of hair wax or pomade to define your waves and add texture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling short hair:

  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different cuts and styles until you find the one that suits you.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut to ensure that it flatters your head shape.
  • Invest in quality hair products that will help you achieve the style you want.
  • Maintain your short hair by getting regular trims and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Accessorize your short hair with headbands, clips, or scarves to add interest and volume.

FAQ

Does short hair make your head look bigger?

No, short hair can actually make your head look smaller if it is styled correctly. Adding volume to the top of your head and choosing a cut that flatters your face shape can create a balanced, proportional look.

Can I still wear hats with short hair?

Yes, you can still wear hats with short hair. Choose hats that fit snugly on your head and avoid styles that are too bulky or oversized.

Will my short hair grow back if I don't like it?

Yes, short hair will grow back if you don't like it. However, it may take some time to grow out, so make sure you are comfortable with the cut before making the change.

Hair Care

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it still needs proper care to look its best. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

Overall, short hair can be a great choice for those who want a low-maintenance, stylish look. By choosing the right cut, styling it correctly, and taking care of your hair, you can rock short hair without worrying about the size of your head.
