How Do I Know If A Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Written by Petter Jun 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be challenging to determine which one will work for you. The good news is that there are some things you can do to help narrow down your choices and find a hairstyle that will suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

What to Consider before Choosing a Hairstyle

Before you start browsing hairstyles, there are a few things you should consider to help narrow down your choices:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair type
  • Your personal style
  • Your lifestyle

These factors will help you determine which hairstyles will work best for you.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyles will suit you best. There are six basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Long

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a photo of your face head-on. Then, use a ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Compare the measurements to the descriptions of the six face shapes to determine which one best matches your face.

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another essential factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Different hairstyles work better for different hair types, so it's important to choose a style that will work with your hair's natural texture, thickness, and length. Some common hair types include:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Thick
  • Thin

Consider your hair type when choosing a hairstyle to ensure that it will look good and be easy to maintain.

Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Your hairstyle should complement your personal style and help you feel confident and comfortable. Think about whether you prefer a classic, trendy, or edgy look, and choose a hairstyle that fits your style.

Try Before You Buy

Before committing to a new hairstyle, it's a good idea to try it out first. You can do this by using a virtual hairstyle app or trying on wigs or hairpieces. This will give you an idea of how the hairstyle will look on you and whether it will suit your face shape and hair type.

Consult a Stylist

If you're still unsure which hairstyle will suit you best, consider consulting a stylist. A professional stylist can assess your face shape, hair type, and personal style and recommend hairstyles that will work for you. They can also give you tips on how to maintain your new hairstyle and style it to suit your needs.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  • Consider your hair type when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Think about your personal style and choose a hairstyle that fits your style.
  • Try before you buy by using a virtual hairstyle app or trying on wigs or hairpieces.
  • Consult a stylist if you're unsure which hairstyle will suit you best.

FAQs

How do I know if a short hairstyle will suit me?

Short hairstyles can work for anyone, but it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type. If you have a round face, a short hairstyle with volume on top can help elongate your face. If you have thick hair, a short, layered cut can help thin it out.

Will a long hairstyle suit me?

Long hairstyles can work for anyone, but they require more maintenance than shorter styles. Consider your hair type and lifestyle when choosing a long hairstyle. If you have thick hair, a long, layered cut can help add texture and movement. If you have fine hair, a one-length cut may be a better option.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that suits you, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your new hairstyle looking great for weeks to come.
