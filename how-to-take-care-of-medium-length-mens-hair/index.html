Description

Medium length mens hair can be a challenge to take care of, especially if you're not used to having longer hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to take care of medium length mens hair.

How to

Before we dive into the specifics of how to take care of medium length mens hair, let's go over some general tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too frequently. Aim for 2-3 times a week, depending on your hair type and lifestyle.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know the basics, let's move on to the specifics of how to take care of medium length mens hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo that is designed for your hair type. Massage the shampoo into your scalp, then rinse thoroughly. Follow up with a conditioner, focusing on the ends of your hair. Rinse thoroughly.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Gently squeeze the excess water out of your hair with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair, as this can cause damage.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

If you want to style your hair, now is the time to apply your styling product of choice. This could be a pomade, wax, gel, or mousse. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Blow Dry

If you're using a blow dryer, set it to a medium heat and use a round brush to style your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Once your hair is dry, use a cool shot to set the style.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

If you want your style to last all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place without making it look stiff or crunchy.

Tips

  • Invest in a high-quality brush or comb to detangle your hair without causing damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

How often should I wash my medium length mens hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes.

What kind of shampoo and conditioner should I use?

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for your hair type. If you have oily hair, look for a clarifying shampoo. If you have dry hair, look for a moisturizing conditioner.

Can I use hot tools on my medium length mens hair?

Yes, but use them sparingly to avoid damaging your hair. Always use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

Hair Care

Taking care of medium length mens hair doesn't have to be complicated. By following these simple steps and tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish. Remember to wash and condition regularly, avoid using hot tools too often, and eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth. With a little bit of effort, you can have the perfect medium length mens hair.
