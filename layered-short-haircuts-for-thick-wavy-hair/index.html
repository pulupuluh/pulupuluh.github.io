Layered Short Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair A Guide

Written by Christine Mar 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know that it can be a blessing and a curse. While your hair has volume and texture, it can also be challenging to manage and style. One way to make your life easier is by getting a layered short haircut. This style can help to control your hair's volume while enhancing its natural waves. In this article, we'll guide you through everything you need to know about layered short haircuts for thick wavy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A layered short haircut is a style where the hair is cut into layers of different lengths. This technique can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous. For thick wavy hair, a layered short haircut can help to control the hair's volume and enhance its natural waves. This style can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style preferences.

How to

If you're considering getting a layered short haircut for your thick wavy hair, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Research different layered short haircut styles and find one that you like.
  2. Find a hairstylist who has experience cutting thick wavy hair.
  3. Bring pictures of the layered short haircut you want to your hairstylist.
  4. Discuss your hair type and face shape with your hairstylist to determine the best layered short haircut for you.
  5. Maintain your new haircut with regular trims and styling.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a layered short haircut for your thick wavy hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  3. Cut the hair in the back of your head to the desired length.
  4. Move to the sides and cut the hair to the desired length, making sure to create layers.
  5. Finish by cutting the hair on top to the desired length and creating layers.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a layered short haircut for your thick wavy hair:

  • Choose a layered short haircut that complements your face shape.
  • Consider getting bangs to frame your face.
  • Use hair products that enhance your hair's natural waves.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and upkeep.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about layered short haircuts for thick wavy hair:

What face shapes suit layered short haircuts?

Layered short haircuts can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, and heart-shaped faces.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your layered short haircut.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for your thick wavy hair after getting a layered short haircut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them sparingly and with heat protectant products.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.

Overall, a layered short haircut can be a great option for those with thick wavy hair. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can achieve a stylish and manageable haircut that enhances your natural waves.
