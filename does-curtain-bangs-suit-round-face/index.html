Does Curtain Bangs Suit Round Face Find Out Now

Written by Petter Aug 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to switch up your look? Curtain bangs have been a popular trend in the hair industry for a while now. However, you may be wondering if they would suit your round face shape. In this article, we will delve into whether curtain bangs are a good fit for a round face shape and provide you with tips on how to style them.

What are Curtain Bangs?

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that is parted in the middle and frames the face. They are called curtain bangs because they resemble the opening of a curtain. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit various face shapes and hair types.

Face Shape Guide

Before we dive into whether curtain bangs suit round faces, let's first understand the different face shapes:

  • Oval - forehead is slightly wider than the chin and the face length is longer than the width.
  • Round - forehead and jawline have a similar width and the face length is shorter than the width.
  • Square - forehead, cheekbones, and jawline have similar widths.
  • Heart - forehead is wider than the cheekbones and jawline.
  • Diamond - cheekbones are wider than the forehead and jawline.
  • Triangle - jawline is wider than the forehead and cheekbones.

Does Curtain Bangs Suit Round Face?

The answer is yes! Curtain bangs can complement a round face shape. The key is to opt for longer bangs that will elongate the face and make it appear slimmer. The bangs should also be parted in the middle to create the illusion of length.

However, it's important to keep in mind that everyone's face shape is unique, and what may work for one person may not work for another. It's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine if curtain bangs are the right fit for you.

How to Style Curtain Bangs for Round Face

Here are some tips on how to style your curtain bangs:

  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs for a sleek look.
  • Curl your bangs away from your face to add texture.
  • Apply a small amount of hair serum to tame any frizz.

Tips for Curtain Bangs

Here are some general tips for maintaining your curtain bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Avoid cutting your bangs too short as it may make your face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can curtain bangs make my face appear shorter?

A: Yes, if the bangs are too short, they can make your face appear shorter. It's best to opt for longer bangs that will elongate the face instead.

Q: Will curtain bangs suit other face shapes?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can suit various face shapes. However, it's important to consult with a hairstylist to determine which style will work best for you.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy looking hair, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know that curtain bangs can suit round face shapes, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out this trendy hairstyle. Remember to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
