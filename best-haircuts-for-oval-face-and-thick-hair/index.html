Best Haircuts For Oval Face And Thick Hair

Written by Petter May 12, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you blessed with an oval face and thick hair? Lucky you! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any haircut. However, with thick hair, you need to find the perfect cut that flatters your face shape and manages your locks. In this article, we’ll be discussing the best haircuts for oval face and thick hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Oval faces are characterized by balanced features and a gently rounded hairline. With thick hair, you have a lot of styling options, from long layers to short bobs. The key is to find a cut that accentuates your best features and complements your face shape. In this article, we’ll be sharing some of the best haircuts for oval face and thick hair, along with tips on how to style and maintain them.

How to

When choosing a haircut for your oval face and thick hair, consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Thick hair can be heavy and hard to manage, so you want a cut that adds movement and dimension without sacrificing volume. Some of the best haircuts for oval face and thick hair include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for thick hair as they add texture and movement while maintaining volume. They also suit oval faces as they draw attention to the cheekbones and jawline. You can wear your long layers straight or add some loose waves for a beachy look.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is a classic haircut that suits almost all face shapes. For oval faces, a bob with subtle layers and a side parting can accentuate your best features while adding volume and texture to your hair.

3. Pixie Cut

If you’re feeling adventurous, a pixie cut is a great option for oval faces with thick hair. This cut adds volume and texture to your hair while showing off your beautiful face shape. You can style your pixie cut with a side-swept fringe or add some texture with a matte styling product.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style some of the best haircuts for oval face and thick hair:

1. Long Layers

  1. Wash your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to your roots.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  3. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to the ends of your hair.
  4. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  3. Use a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  4. Finish with a shine serum to add gloss and hold.

3. Pixie Cut

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry.
  2. Apply a styling cream to your hair and comb through.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment, scrunching your hair to add texture.
  4. Finish with a matte styling paste to add definition and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips on how to maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking healthy:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut’s shape.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for oval face and thick hair?

The best haircuts for oval face and thick hair include long layers, shoulder-length bobs, and pixie cuts.

How do I style my thick hair?

You can style your thick hair by blow-drying it with a round brush, using a straightening iron, or adding loose waves with a curling iron. Use a texturizing spray or styling cream to add definition and hold.

How do I maintain my haircut?

Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools too often. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools and experiment with different hair products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.

Hair Care

Aside from getting the right haircut, taking care of your hair is essential to maintaining its health and beauty. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

In conclusion, the best haircuts for oval face and thick hair are long layers, shoulder-length bobs, and pixie cuts. However, the key to finding the perfect cut is to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Use the tips and step-by-step instructions in this article to style and maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
