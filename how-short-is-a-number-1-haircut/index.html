How Short Is A Number 1 Haircut

Written by Frank Sep 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering getting a number 1 haircut, you may be wondering just how short it is. A number 1 haircut is one of the shortest clipper guards available, but just how short is it? In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about the number 1 haircut, including how to get it, how to care for it, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Number 1 Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Number 1 Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining a Number 1 Haircut
  • FAQs About the Number 1 Haircut
  • Hair Care Tips for a Number 1 Haircut

How to Get a Number 1 Haircut

A number 1 haircut is achieved by using a clipper guard with a length of 1/8 inch or 3mm. This guard is one of the shortest available, making it a popular choice for men who want a very short, low-maintenance haircut.

To get a number 1 haircut, you'll need to visit a barber or hair stylist who is experienced in using clippers. They will use the clipper guard to trim your hair to the desired length, typically leaving a little bit of stubble on the scalp.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Number 1 Haircut

Getting a number 1 haircut is relatively simple, but it's important to follow these steps to ensure that your haircut looks great:

  1. Choose a barber or hair stylist who is experienced in using clippers.
  2. Discuss the length and style of haircut you want with your stylist.
  3. Sit in the chair and let your stylist get to work.
  4. They will use the clipper guard with a length of 1/8 inch to trim your hair.
  5. They may also use scissors or thinning shears to create texture and remove bulk.
  6. Once the haircut is complete, your stylist will show you the back of your head with a mirror to ensure that you are happy with the length and style.

Tips for Maintaining a Number 1 Haircut

While a number 1 haircut is very low-maintenance, there are a few things you can do to keep it looking great:

  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the length.
  • Use a trimmer to touch up any areas that have grown out between haircuts.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

FAQs About the Number 1 Haircut

Is a number 1 haircut the same as a buzz cut?

A buzz cut is a very short haircut that is typically achieved by using a clipper with no guard attached. A number 1 haircut is slightly longer than a buzz cut, as it uses a clipper guard with a length of 1/8 inch.

Is a number 1 haircut suitable for all hair types?

A number 1 haircut can be suitable for most hair types, but it may not be the best choice for very curly or thick hair. In these cases, a longer guard may be needed to achieve the desired length.

How often should I get a number 1 haircut?

How often you get a number 1 haircut will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and style.

Hair Care Tips for a Number 1 Haircut

While a number 1 haircut is very low-maintenance, it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a trimmer to touch up any areas that have grown out between haircuts.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the length and style of your haircut.

Overall, a number 1 haircut is a great choice for men who want a very short, low-maintenance haircut. With the right care and maintenance, your number 1 haircut can look great for weeks at a time.
