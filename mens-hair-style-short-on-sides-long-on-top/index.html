Men s Hair Style Short On Sides Long On Top

Description

The men's hair style short on sides long on top has been popular for several years now and it looks like it's here to stay. This hairstyle features short hair on the sides and long hair on top, giving men a versatile, trendy and stylish look. The style can be worn in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy, and it can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. It's a great option for men who want to look sharp and put-together, without having to spend a lot of time on their hair.

How to

If you're interested in trying out the men's hair style short on sides long on top, there are a few things you'll need to know. First, you'll need to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the look. They will be able to assess your face shape and hair type, and recommend the best way to style your hair.

Once you've found a stylist you trust, you can work with them to decide on the length and style of your hair. Short hair on the sides can be anywhere from a buzz cut to a fade, while the top can be left longer and styled in a variety of ways.

Step by step

1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup, and leave your hair feeling soft and clean.

2. Next, towel dry your hair until it is damp but not soaking wet. This will make it easier to style.

3. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, depending on the look you're going for. For a slicked back style, use a pomade or wax. For a more textured look, use a clay or paste.

4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair, focusing on the longer hair on top. You can create a side part, slick it back, or leave it messy and tousled.

5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product, if desired. This will help to hold the style in place throughout the day.

Tips

- If you have a round face, consider leaving more length on the top to create the illusion of height.

- If you have a square face, a shorter style on top with more volume can help to balance out your features.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

FAQ

Q: Will this style suit my hair type?
A: The men's hair style short on sides long on top can be adapted to suit different hair types, from straight to curly. Your stylist will be able to recommend the best way to style your hair based on its texture and thickness.

Q: How often will I need to get a haircut?
A: This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how high-maintenance you want your style to be. Generally, you can expect to need a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

Hair care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. This means washing it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair. You should also protect your hair from the sun and wind, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to keep it hydrated and nourished. With the right care and styling, your men's hair style short on sides long on top can look great for weeks or even months at a time.
