What Hair Length Is Best For Long Faces

Written by Oliver Apr 21, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Long faces can be tricky to style when it comes to hair. The wrong haircut can make your face look even longer, while the right one can balance out your features and make your face look more proportional. In this article, we'll explore what hair length is best for long faces, as well as some tips and tricks for caring for and styling your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Choosing the Right Hair Length
  • Tips for Styling Long Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Faces

Description

Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow cheekbones. The best hair length for long faces is one that adds width to the face and balances out its length. The right hairstyle can create the illusion of a shorter face and make you look more proportionate. In general, hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, as well as those that create a horizontal line across the forehead, are the most flattering for long faces.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hair length for your face is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you've traced.

If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width, you have a square face. If your forehead and jawline are both narrow and your cheekbones are wide, you have a heart-shaped face. If your forehead and jawline are both wide and your cheekbones are narrow, you have a diamond-shaped face. If your face is roughly as wide as it is long, you have an oval face. If your face is as wide as it is long, with rounded edges, you have a round face.

Step-by-Step Guide for Choosing the Right Hair Length

Once you've determined that you have a long face, it's time to choose the right hair length. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you out:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as layered cuts or side-swept bangs.
  2. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as they can make your face look even longer.
  3. If you want to wear your hair long, consider adding layers to create volume and movement.
  4. If you prefer a shorter hairstyle, choose one that hits at or above your chin to create the illusion of width.
  5. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your face look even longer.
  6. If you want to wear your hair up, consider a half-up, half-down style that adds volume to the sides of your face.
  7. Avoid slicked-back styles, as they can make your face look even longer.
  8. If you want to add color to your hair, consider highlights or balayage to create dimension and movement.
  9. Avoid one-dimensional color, as it can make your face look even longer.
  10. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

Tips for Styling Long Faces

Here are some additional tips for styling long faces:

  • Use volumizing products to add body and movement to your hair.
  • Choose hairstyles that frame your face, such as side-swept bangs or face-framing layers.
  • Avoid center parts, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering one for your face shape.
  • Use accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add interest and texture to your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a long face, but avoid blunt cuts and styles that are too long, as they can make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for layered cuts or side-swept bangs to add volume and movement to your hair.

Q: What is the best haircut for a long face?

A: The best haircut for a long face is one that adds volume to the sides of the face and creates a horizontal line across the forehead. Layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and chin-length bobs are all great options.

Q: Can I wear my hair up if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair up if you have a long face, but avoid slicked-back styles, as they can make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for half-up, half-down styles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Long Faces

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to help minimize damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

In conclusion, the best hair length for long faces is one that adds volume to the sides of the face and creates a horizontal line across the forehead. Layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and chin-length bobs are all great options. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding heat damage, and consult with your hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
