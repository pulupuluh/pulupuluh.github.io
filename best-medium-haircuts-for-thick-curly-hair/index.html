Best Medium Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Aug 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your thick curly hair constantly getting in your way? Do you want a new look that will give you the perfect balance between style and manageability? Look no further than these best medium haircuts for thick curly hair!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium haircuts are perfect for those with thick curly hair because they provide enough length to showcase your curls while still being easy to maintain. Whether you prefer a layered cut or a blunt bob, there are plenty of options to choose from.

One of the best things about medium haircuts is that they can be customized to fit your personal style. From side-swept bangs to textured layers, there are endless possibilities for creating a unique look that’s all your own.

How to

Before getting a medium haircut for your thick curly hair, it’s important to find a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair. Look for someone who specializes in textured hair and has a portfolio of clients with similar hair types.

When discussing your desired haircut with your stylist, be sure to mention any specific concerns you have. Do you want to minimize frizz? Do you prefer a style that’s easy to maintain? By communicating your needs, your stylist can help you find the perfect medium haircut for your hair type and lifestyle.

Step by step

Here are some of the best medium haircuts for thick curly hair:

Layered Lob

A layered lob is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks polished. The layers add texture and movement to your hair, while the length is long enough to showcase your curls.

Curly Shag

A curly shag is a great option for those who want a lot of volume and texture. This cut is characterized by lots of layers and choppy ends, which create a messy, undone look that’s perfect for curly hair.

Blunt Bob

A blunt bob is a classic haircut that works well for those with thick, curly hair. This cut is characterized by a straight, even length, which creates a sleek and polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium haircut for thick curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair while it’s still damp to help define your curls.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for thick curly hair?

The best haircut for thick curly hair is one that provides enough length to showcase your curls while still being easy to maintain. Medium haircuts are a great option because they offer the perfect balance between style and manageability.

How do I find a stylist who specializes in cutting curly hair?

You can find a stylist who specializes in cutting curly hair by doing a quick online search or asking for recommendations from friends who have similar hair types. Look for someone who has experience working with textured hair and has a portfolio of clients with similar hair types.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining your medium haircut for thick curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz.

By following these tips and getting a medium haircut that’s tailored to your hair type and lifestyle, you can achieve the perfect balance between style and manageability for your thick curly hair.
