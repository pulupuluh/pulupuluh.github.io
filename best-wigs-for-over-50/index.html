Best Wigs For Over 50 A Guide To Choosing The Perfect Wig

Written by Robby Sep 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best wigs for over 50. As we age, our hair can lose its volume, shine, and thickness. But, that doesn't mean we have to put up with dull or thinning hair. A wig can help us look and feel our best, no matter our age. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about choosing the perfect wig, from the different types of wigs to the best hair care tips for maintaining your wig. Let's get started!

Why Choose a Wig?

Wigs are a great option for anyone who wants to change their hairstyle without committing to a permanent cut or color. Wigs are also a great option for women over 50 who may be experiencing hair loss or thinning hair. Wigs can be styled in a variety of ways, and they can help you achieve the volume, length, and color you desire.

Types of Wigs

There are several types of wigs to choose from, including synthetic wigs, human hair wigs, and blended wigs. Synthetic wigs are typically more affordable and require less maintenance than human hair wigs. Human hair wigs, on the other hand, are more expensive but offer a more natural look and feel. Blended wigs are a combination of synthetic and human hair, offering the benefits of both.

How to Choose the Right Wig

When choosing a wig, there are several factors to consider, including the style, color, and length. It's important to choose a wig that complements your face shape, skin tone, and personal style. You may want to consider trying on several wigs before making a final decision.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Wig:

  1. Measure your head to ensure a proper fit.
  2. Choose a wig style that complements your face shape.
  3. Choose a color that complements your skin tone.
  4. Decide on the length of the wig.
  5. Try on several wigs to find the perfect fit.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wig

Once you've chosen the perfect wig, it's important to take good care of it to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your wig:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Wash your wig regularly using a wig-specific shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your wig, as this can damage the fibers.
  • Use a wig cap to protect your wig from oils and sweat.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wigs:

How long do wigs last?

The lifespan of a wig depends on several factors, including the quality of the wig, how often it is worn, and how well it is maintained. On average, a wig can last anywhere from six months to two years.

Can you wear a wig in the shower?

No, it's not recommended to wear a wig in the shower or while swimming. Water can damage the fibers of the wig and cause it to lose its shape.

Can you style a wig?

Yes, you can style a wig using heat tools, such as a curling iron or straightener. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to the wig fibers.

Hair Care for Over 50s

Regardless of whether you choose to wear a wig or not, it's important to take good care of your hair as you age. Here are some hair care tips for women over 50:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and thickness.
  • Consider taking a hair supplement, such as biotin or collagen, to strengthen your hair.

Thank you for reading our guide on the best wigs for over 50. We hope this guide has helped you choose the perfect wig and provided you with some helpful hair care tips.
