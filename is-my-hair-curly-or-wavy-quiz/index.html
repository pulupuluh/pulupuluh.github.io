Is My Hair Curly Or Wavy Quiz Find Out Your Hair Type

Written by Aprilia May 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to determine whether your hair is curly or wavy? Do you want to learn more about your hair type and how to care for it properly? If so, take our "Is My Hair Curly or Wavy Quiz" and find out!

How to Take the Quiz

To take the quiz, answer the following questions:

  1. When your hair is wet, does it straighten out or stay curly/wavy?
  2. Does your hair form ringlets or loose waves?
  3. Do you have to use a lot of product to define your curls/waves?
  4. Does your hair frizz easily?
  5. Do you have a lot of volume or is your hair relatively flat?

After you answer these questions, tally up your score and use the key below to determine your hair type.

Scoring Key

If you answered mostly "yes" to these questions, your hair is likely curly:

  • Your hair stays curly when wet
  • Your hair forms tight ringlets
  • You need a lot of product to define your curls
  • Your hair frizzes easily
  • Your hair has a lot of volume

If you answered mostly "no" to these questions, your hair is likely wavy:

  • Your hair straightens out when wet
  • Your hair forms loose waves
  • You don't need a lot of product to define your waves
  • Your hair doesn't frizz easily
  • Your hair is relatively flat

FAQ

What if my hair doesn't fit neatly into either category?

That's okay! Hair types can be fluid and change over time. If you're not sure whether your hair is curly or wavy, try taking the quiz again in a few weeks or months and see if your answers change.

Do I need to use different products for curly and wavy hair?

Yes, curly and wavy hair have different needs when it comes to styling products. Curly hair typically requires heavier, more emollient products to define curls, while wavy hair can be weighed down by heavy products and needs lighter, more volumizing products.

How can I care for my curly or wavy hair?

Curly and wavy hair require different care routines than straight hair. Some tips for caring for curly and wavy hair include:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Condition regularly to keep hair moisturized
  • Avoid brushing hair when dry, as it can cause frizz
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying to enhance curls/waves
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent breakage

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair, regardless of whether it's curly or wavy. Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using heat tools too frequently
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions

By following these tips and taking our "Is My Hair Curly or Wavy Quiz," you can learn more about your hair type and how to care for it properly. Remember, every head of hair is unique, so don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you!
