How To Change Face Shape From Oval To Square Tips Faqs And Hair Care

How to Change Face Shape from Oval to Square | Tips, FAQs, and Hair Care

If you're wondering how to change face shape from oval to square, you're not alone. Many people desire a more defined jawline and angular features. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve a square face shape through various methods including makeup, hairstyles, and even facial exercises.

Table of Contents

  • How to Change Face Shape from Oval to Square
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving a Square Face Shape
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for a Square Face Shape

How to Change Face Shape from Oval to Square

Changing your face shape from oval to square can be achieved through various methods such as makeup, hairstyles, and facial exercises. Here are some tips to help you achieve a more defined jawline and angular features:

Makeup

One of the easiest and quickest ways to achieve a square face shape is through makeup. Here are some makeup tips to help you achieve this look:

  • Contour your cheekbones and temples to create a more angular look
  • Use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to create a shadow effect on the jawline
  • Highlight the center of your forehead and chin to create a more balanced look

Hairstyles

Your hairstyle can also play a big role in achieving a square face shape. Here are some hairstyles to consider:

  • A short bob with blunt ends can create a more angular look
  • Long, straight hair with layers around the jawline can help frame the face and create a more defined jawline
  • A pixie cut with textured layers can also create a more angular look

Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and define your facial muscles, leading to a more angular look. Here are some exercises to try:

  • Jaw Clenches: Clench your jaw and hold for 10 seconds, then release
  • Chin Lifts: Tilt your head back and lift your chin towards the ceiling, then lower
  • Cheek Puffs: Puff your cheeks out and hold for 10 seconds, then release

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to change face shape from oval to square:

  1. Start by contouring your cheekbones and temples with a darker shade of foundation or bronzer
  2. Next, highlight the center of your forehead and chin to create a more balanced look
  3. Use a short bob with blunt ends, long straight hair with layers around the jawline, or a pixie cut with textured layers to create a more angular look
  4. Try facial exercises such as jaw clenches, chin lifts, and cheek puffs to tone and define your facial muscles

Tips for Achieving a Square Face Shape

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a square face shape:

  • Wear statement earrings to draw attention to your angular features
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too soft and round, as they can emphasize a round face shape
  • Consider getting a chin implant or jawline filler for a more permanent solution

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my face shape permanently?

Yes, you can change your face shape permanently through procedures such as facial implants or fillers.

Are facial exercises effective?

Facial exercises can be effective in toning and defining your facial muscles, but results may vary.

Can I achieve a square face shape without makeup or surgery?

Yes, you can achieve a square face shape through hairstyles and facial exercises.

Hair Care for a Square Face Shape

When it comes to hair care for a square face shape, it's important to consider the length and style of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can emphasize a square face shape
  • Consider a side-swept bang to soften angular features
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and movement to your hair

Changing your face shape from oval to square can be achieved through various methods such as makeup, hairstyles, and facial exercises. Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to seek the help of a professional for more permanent solutions.

Remember, confidence is key - embrace your unique features and own your look!
