How To Alter A Dress That Is Too Long

Written by Daniel Jul 13, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of tripping over your dress that’s too long? Don’t worry, you’re not alone. In fact, altering a dress that is too long is a common problem faced by many women. In this article, we'll show you how to alter a dress that is too long in a few simple steps.

Description

Long dresses can be beautiful and elegant, but if they’re too long, they can be a nuisance to walk in. Altering a dress that is too long is a simple solution that can save you from tripping over your dress or ruining it. With a few easy steps, you can alter your dress to the perfect length and make it more comfortable to wear.

How to Alter a Dress That is Too Long

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to alter a dress that is too long:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

The first step is to gather all the supplies you will need. These include a measuring tape, a pair of scissors, a sewing kit, and an iron.

Step 2: Try on the Dress

Put on the dress and stand in front of a full-length mirror. Determine how much length you want to remove from the dress. Mark the dress with a safety pin or a piece of chalk where you want to cut.

Step 3: Measure and Cut

Take off the dress and measure the distance between the hemline and the marked line. Use this measurement to mark the same distance all around the dress. Cut the dress along the marked line using a pair of scissors.

Step 4: Hem the Dress

Once you have cut the dress, iron the hemline to make it easier to sew. Fold the hemline twice to create a neat finish and sew it using a needle and thread.

Step 5: Try on the Dress Again

Try on the dress again to make sure it’s the right length. If it’s still too long, repeat the process until you’re happy with the length.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when altering a dress that is too long:

  • Always measure twice and cut once to avoid mistakes.
  • Iron the dress before cutting and hemming to make it easier to work with.
  • Use a sewing machine if you're not comfortable with hand sewing.
  • Practice on an old dress before you alter your favorite dress.

FAQ

Can I alter a dress that is too short?

Yes, you can alter a dress that is too short by adding fabric to the hemline or wearing it with leggings or tights.

How much should I cut off a dress that is too long?

The amount you cut off depends on your personal preference. It’s best to try on the dress and mark the length before cutting.

Can I alter a dress that has a pattern or design?

Yes, you can alter a dress that has a pattern or design by matching the pattern or design when sewing the hemline.

Hair Care

When altering a dress, it's important to keep your hair out of the way to avoid it getting caught in the scissors or sewing machine. Tie your hair back or wear a hairband to keep it out of your face.

Now that you know how to alter a dress that is too long, you can save yourself from tripping over your dress and make it more comfortable to wear. With a little practice, you can become an expert at altering dresses and make your clothes fit perfectly.
