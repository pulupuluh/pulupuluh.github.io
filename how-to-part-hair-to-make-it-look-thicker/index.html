How To Part Your Hair To Make It Look Thicker

Description

Thinning hair is a common problem for many people, but luckily, there are ways to make your hair look thicker. One of the easiest ways to achieve this is by simply changing the way you part your hair. By parting your hair in a certain way, you can create the illusion of thicker hair without any expensive products or treatments.

How to Part Your Hair to Make it Look Thicker

The key to making your hair look thicker is to create volume at the roots. This can be achieved by parting your hair in a way that adds height and depth to your hair. Here are a few tips:

1. Side Part

A side part can instantly add volume to your hair. Start by parting your hair on one side, then tease the crown of your hair to create height. Brush the top layer of your hair back over the teased section to create a smooth finish.

2. Zigzag Part

A zigzag part creates an illusion of depth and fullness. Use a comb to create a zigzag part, then tease the roots of your hair to create volume. Brush the top layer of your hair back over the teased section to create a smooth finish.

3. Middle Part

A middle part can create a symmetrical look that makes your hair appear thicker. Use a comb to create a straight middle part, then tease the roots of your hair to create height. Brush the top layer of your hair back over the teased section to create a smooth finish.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create a side part:

Step 1:

Start by wetting your hair and combing it straight back.

Step 2:

Use a comb to create a side part on one side of your head.

Step 3:

Tease the crown of your hair to create volume.

Step 4:

Brush the top layer of your hair back over the teased section to create a smooth finish.

Tips to Make Your Hair Look Thicker

Here are a few additional tips to make your hair look thicker:

1. Use volumizing products:

Use volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and fullness to your hair.

2. Blow-dry your hair upside down:

Blow-drying your hair upside down can add volume at the roots.

3. Avoid heavy products:

Heavy products can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Use lightweight products instead.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it daily. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can hair dye make my hair look thicker?

A: Yes, hair dye can add depth and dimension to your hair, making it appear thicker.

Hair Care for Thicker Hair

Proper hair care can help keep your hair looking thick and healthy. Here are a few tips:

1. Use a wide-toothed comb:

A wide-toothed comb can help prevent breakage and damage.

2. Trim your hair regularly:

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage.

3. Protect your hair from heat:

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, and avoid using them too often.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily make your hair look thicker and more voluminous. Remember, the key is to create volume at the roots, which can be achieved by simply changing the way you part your hair.
