Kid Boy Haircut Short Sides Long Top The Trending Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Looking for a stylish and trendy haircut for your little boy? The kid boy haircut short sides long top is the perfect hairstyle for your little guy. This haircut has been trending for a while now and has become a popular choice for parents who want a stylish and low-maintenance haircut for their sons. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about the kid boy haircut short sides long top, including how to get the haircut, tips, and hair care.

Description

The kid boy haircut short sides long top is a versatile and stylish haircut that features short sides and a longer top. The hair on the sides and back of the head is cut short, while the hair on the top is left longer. The longer hair on the top can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. This haircut is perfect for active boys who want to look stylish without spending too much time on their hair.

How to Get the Haircut

If you want to get your little boy the kid boy haircut short sides long top, you can go to a professional hair salon or barber shop. It's essential to find a hairdresser who has experience cutting boys' hair and can help you achieve the perfect haircut. Before getting the haircut, you should have an idea of how you want your little boy's hair to look. You can bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to show the hairdresser.

Step by Step

The following are the steps to get the kid boy haircut short sides long top:

  1. Start by washing your little boy's hair to remove any dirt or product buildup.
  2. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. You can choose the length of the clippers depending on how short you want the hair to be.
  3. Blend the hair on the sides and top of the head using scissors or clippers.
  4. Leave the hair on the top of the head longer and style it according to your preference.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain the kid boy haircut short sides long top:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy.
  • Use a styling product, such as a pomade or gel, to style the hair on the top of the head.
  • Trim the hair every four to six weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.
  • Use a comb or brush to style the hair and remove any tangles.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the kid boy haircut short sides long top:

Q: Is the kid boy haircut short sides long top suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the haircut is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How long does the haircut last?

A: The haircut can last up to six weeks before needing a trim.

Q: How much does the haircut cost?

A: The cost of the haircut varies depending on the salon or barber shop. It's best to check with your local salon for pricing.

Hair Care

To keep your little boy's hair healthy and shiny, you should follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hairdryers and straighteners, on your little boy's hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized.

The kid boy haircut short sides long top is a stylish and low-maintenance haircut for your little boy. With the right styling products and hair care routine, your little boy can rock this hairstyle all year round. So, what are you waiting for? Book your little boy's haircut appointment today and give him a trendy and stylish haircut.
