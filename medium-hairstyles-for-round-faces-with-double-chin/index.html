Medium Hairstyles For Round Faces With Double Chin

Written by Bardi May 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face with a double chin? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best medium hairstyles for round faces with double chin. We'll also be sharing some tips on how to style your hair and take care of it. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces with double chin can be a bit tricky to style. You want to create a hairstyle that will draw attention away from your double chin, while also enhancing your facial features. Medium hairstyles are a great option because they are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. They also provide enough length to create volume and movement, which can help balance out your face shape.

How to

When choosing a medium hairstyle for your round face with double chin, it's important to consider the length, texture, and style. A layered bob or lob is a great option because it adds volume and movement to your hair. You can also add some highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

If you prefer to wear your hair up, a messy bun or ponytail can be a great option. You can also try a half-up, half-down hairstyle, which will draw attention to your upper face while also adding volume to your hair.

Step by Step

To create a layered bob or lob:

  1. Wash and towel dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement.
  4. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add some waves or curls to your hair.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair to add some definition and hold.

To create a messy bun:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your medium hair for round faces with double chin:

  • Choose a side part to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can accentuate your double chin.
  • Consider adding some layers around your face to create a slimming effect.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add some lift to your roots.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or sleek, as they can accentuate your double chin.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for round faces with double chin?

The best hairstyle for round faces with double chin is a medium length layered bob or lob. This hairstyle adds volume and movement to your hair, while also creating a slimming effect around your face.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face with double chin?

You should avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can accentuate your double chin. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too tight or sleek, as they can also accentuate your double chin.

How do I style my hair to hide my double chin?

You can style your hair to hide your double chin by choosing a hairstyle that adds volume and movement to your hair. You can also consider adding some layers around your face to create a slimming effect. Choosing a side part can also create the illusion of a longer face.

Hair Care

To keep your medium hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as it can weigh it down.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock your round face with double chin like a boss. Try out different styles until you find the perfect one that suits your personality and features. Have fun and be confident!
