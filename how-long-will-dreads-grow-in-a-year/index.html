How Long Will Dreads Grow In A Year A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Sep 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Dreadlocks have been around for centuries and are known for their unique and stylish look. They are a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal upkeep and can be worn by people of all ages and hair types. One of the most frequently asked questions about dreadlocks is, "how long will dreads grow in a year?" In this article, we will explore this question and provide you with all the information you need to know about dreadlock growth.

Description

Dreadlocks are formed by matting and twisting hair strands together to create a rope-like appearance. This hairstyle can be achieved naturally or artificially, depending on your hair type and preference. Natural dreads are formed by leaving your hair untouched and allowing it to mat and lock over time. Artificial dreads, on the other hand, are created by braiding or twisting your hair and then using a locking product to hold the strands together.

How to

If you are interested in getting dreadlocks, there are several methods you can use. The most popular methods include:

Backcombing Method:

This method involves sectioning your hair and combing it backward using a fine-toothed comb. This process creates knots and tangles, which are then worked into dreadlocks using a locking product.

Twist and Rip Method:

This method involves sectioning your hair and twisting each section into a tight spiral. The spirals are then ripped apart, creating knots and tangles that are worked into dreadlocks using a locking product.

Crochet Method:

This method involves using a crochet hook to weave your hair into dreadlocks. The hook is used to pull strands of hair through existing dreadlocks, creating a tighter and more uniform look.

Step by Step

If you are interested in creating dreadlocks, here are the basic steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small, manageable sections using a rat-tail comb. The sections should be about the size of a pencil, depending on your hair type and preference.

Step 2: Backcomb or Twist and Rip Your Hair

Use the backcombing or twist and rip method to create knots and tangles in each section of hair. Be sure to work from the ends of your hair up to the roots, as this will create stronger and more durable dreadlocks.

Step 3: Apply a Locking Product

Apply a locking product, such as beeswax or dread wax, to each section of hair. This will help hold the knots and tangles together and promote dreadlock formation.

Step 4: Maintain Your Dreadlocks

Once your dreadlocks are formed, it is important to maintain them regularly to prevent them from unraveling or becoming too loose. Maintenance may include washing your hair with a residue-free shampoo, retwisting loose strands, and trimming any split ends.

Tips

If you are considering getting dreadlocks, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Be patient. Dreadlocks take time to form, and it may take several months or even a year for them to reach their desired length.
  • Use a locking product sparingly. Overuse of locking products can lead to buildup and damage to your hair.
  • Be gentle with your hair. Avoid using harsh chemicals, pulling or tugging on your hair, or using heat styling tools.
  • Protect your hair while sleeping. Wear a satin or silk cap to prevent your hair from rubbing against your pillowcase, which can cause frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about dreadlocks:

How long will dreads grow in a year?

On average, dreadlocks can grow anywhere from 4 to 6 inches per year, depending on your hair type and how well you maintain them.

Do dreadlocks require special care?

Yes, dreadlocks require special care to maintain their shape and prevent them from unraveling or becoming too loose. This may include washing them regularly with a residue-free shampoo, retwisting loose strands, and trimming any split ends.

Can anyone get dreadlocks?

Yes, anyone can get dreadlocks regardless of their hair type or ethnicity. However, it is important to consult with a professional stylist or do your research before attempting to create dreadlocks on your own.

Hair Care

To keep your dreadlocks healthy and looking their best, it is important to follow a hair care routine that works best for you. This may include using a residue-free shampoo, conditioning your hair regularly, and avoiding harsh chemicals or styling tools. It is also important to maintain a healthy diet and stay hydrated, as this can have a positive impact on your hair and scalp.

In conclusion, dreadlocks are a unique and stylish hairstyle that can be achieved naturally or artificially. If you are considering getting dreadlocks, it is important to be patient and maintain them regularly to prevent them from unraveling or becoming too loose. With the right care and maintenance, your dreadlocks can grow up to 4 to 6 inches per year, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of them.
