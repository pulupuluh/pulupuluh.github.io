How To Cut Your Own Hair Shorter In The Back

Written by Christine Apr 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to switch things up by cutting your own hair shorter in the back? If so, you're in the right place! In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to help you achieve the perfect short haircut.

How to

Before we jump into the steps, there are a few things you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Hair ties
  • Mirror

Once you have all of your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair is clean and healthy.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to separate your hair into three sections: the top, the sides, and the back. Tie the top and side sections out of the way, leaving the back section loose.

Step 3: Trim the Back

Starting at the nape of your neck, use your scissors to trim the hair in small sections. Be sure to cut in a straight line and use your comb to guide you.

Remember to start with small sections and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.

Step 4: Check Your Progress

After you've trimmed the back section of your hair, use a mirror to check your progress. Make sure the length is even and adjust as needed.

Step 5: Blend the Sides

Untie the side sections of your hair and use your scissors to blend the back and sides together. Make sure to cut in a diagonal line to create a seamless transition from the back to the sides.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Finally, untie the top section of your hair and use your scissors to trim the length. Start with small sections and cut in a straight line, using your comb to guide you.

Tips

Here are a few tips to make your haircutting experience easier:

  • Use clippers to trim the back of your hair if you're uncomfortable using scissors.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Invest in a good pair of scissors. This will make a huge difference in the quality of your haircut.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair shorter in the back without a mirror?

While it's not recommended, you can cut your hair shorter in the back without a mirror. However, it's important to take your time and be careful not to cut too much at once.

What if I mess up?

It's okay if you make a mistake. Hair grows back, and you can always get a professional haircut to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these steps and tips, you'll be able to achieve the perfect short haircut in no time. Happy cutting!
