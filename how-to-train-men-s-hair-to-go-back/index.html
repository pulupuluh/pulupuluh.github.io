How To Train Men s Hair To Go Back A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of hair falling on your face throughout the day? Do you want to achieve a sleek and stylish look? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to train men's hair to go back, and maintain it with proper hair care. This step-by-step guide will help you achieve the desired look effortlessly.

How to Train Men's Hair to Go Back

Training your hair to go back requires patience and consistency. It is important to choose the right hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture. The right haircut will make it easier to train your hair in the desired direction. Here are some steps that you can follow:

1. Allow your Hair to Grow

Before you start training your hair, make sure it has enough length. You need at least 2-3 inches of hair on the top to achieve the desired look. Allow your hair to grow for a few weeks before you start training it.

2. Wash your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly will keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using hot water as it can damage your hair. Rinse your hair with cold water to keep it shiny and smooth.

3. Use a Styling Product

Using a styling product will make it easier to train your hair. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade on your hair and comb it in the desired direction.

4. Comb your Hair Regularly

Regular combing will help train your hair in the desired direction. Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid damaging your hair. Start from the back of your head and comb towards the front.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to train your hair to go back:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse it with cold water to keep it shiny and smooth.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade on your hair. Make sure to spread it evenly.

Step 3: Comb your Hair

Use a wide-toothed comb to comb your hair in the desired direction. Start from the back of your head and comb towards the front.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat the above steps every day to train your hair to go back. It may take several weeks to achieve the desired look.

Tips to Maintain the Look

Here are some tips that you can follow to maintain your hair:

Tip 1: Trim your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair every few weeks will keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Tip 2: Use a Hair Dryer

Using a hair dryer will help set your hair in the desired direction. Use a cool setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Tip 3: Avoid Touching your Hair

Avoid touching your hair throughout the day. This will prevent it from falling out of place.

FAQs

Q1: How long does it take to train hair to go back?

It may take several weeks to train your hair to go back. It depends on the length and texture of your hair.

Q2: How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days. Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Men

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that suits your hair type. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo.

Tip 2: Use a Conditioner

Using a conditioner will keep your hair soft and smooth. Apply it after shampooing and rinse it off with cold water.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons. They can damage your hair and make it prone to breakage.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy and stylish hair.

Training your hair to go back requires patience and consistency. Follow the above steps and tips to achieve the desired look. Remember to maintain your hair with proper care and enjoy your new, sleek hairstyle.
