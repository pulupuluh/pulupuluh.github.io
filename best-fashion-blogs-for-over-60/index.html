Description

As we age, our fashion choices change as well. But that doesn't mean we have to sacrifice style for comfort. In fact, there are plenty of fashion bloggers out there who are over 60 and still slaying their looks. They prove that age is just a number and that fashion has no age limit. In this article, we will be discussing the best fashion blogs for over 60 to help you find inspiration for your own fashion choices.

How to

If you're new to the world of fashion blogging, you might be wondering how to get started. Here are some steps you can take:

Step 1: Find Your Niche

Before you start blogging, you need to figure out what your niche is going to be. Are you going to focus on fashion for women over 60? Or are you going to cover a broader range of topics? Once you have a clear idea of what you want to focus on, it will be easier to create content that resonates with your audience.

Step 2: Create a Blog

Once you know what your niche is going to be, it's time to create a blog. You can use platforms like WordPress or Blogger to create your blog. Make sure to choose a design that reflects your personal style.

Step 3: Start Creating Content

Now it's time to start creating content for your blog. You can write about anything related to fashion, from outfit ideas to shopping tips. Make sure to include high-quality photos to make your blog visually appealing.

Step 4: Promote Your Blog

Once you have some content on your blog, it's time to start promoting it. Share your blog on social media and other online platforms to get more traffic to your site.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best fashion blogs for over 60:

1. Advanced Style

Advanced Style is a blog that celebrates the fashion choices of women over 60. The blog features stylish older women from all over the world and showcases their unique fashion sense.

2. Accidental Icon

Accidental Icon is a blog by Lyn Slater, a professor who became a fashion icon in her 60s. Her blog features her own unique style and also covers fashion events and trends.

3. Not Dead Yet Style

Not Dead Yet Style is a blog by Patti Gibbons, who is in her 60s. Her blog features her own personal style and also covers topics like aging and body positivity.

4. The Middle Page

The Middle Page is a blog by Cathy Williamson, who is in her 60s. Her blog features fashion and lifestyle content and is aimed at women who are in the "middle" of life.

5. That's Not My Age

That's Not My Age is a blog by Alyson Walsh, who is in her 60s. Her blog features fashion, beauty, and lifestyle content and is aimed at women who want to age with style.

Tips

Here are some tips for dressing stylishly over 60:

1. Invest in Quality Pieces

Invest in quality pieces that will last you a long time. Look for classic styles that will never go out of fashion.

2. Dress for Your Body Type

Dress for your body type and choose clothes that flatter your figure. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

3. Accessorize

Accessorize your outfits with jewelry, scarves, and hats to add some personality to your look.

FAQ

Q: Can I still be stylish in my 60s?

A: Absolutely! Style has no age limit.

Q: What should I do if I don't know what my personal style is?

A: Experiment with different styles until you find what works for you. Look to fashion bloggers and influencers for inspiration.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style. Here are some tips:

1. Choose a Low-Maintenance Style

Choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to maintain and doesn't require a lot of styling.

2. Embrace Your Natural Hair

Embrace your natural hair texture and color. Don't be afraid to let your gray hair shine.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

In conclusion, fashion has no age limit, and there are plenty of fashion blogs out there for women over 60. Whether you're looking for outfit ideas or inspiration for your personal style, these blogs are sure to provide plenty of inspiration.
