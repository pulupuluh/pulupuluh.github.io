What Hair Color Looks Best With Olive Skin And Green Eyes

Written by Bardi Aug 24, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking to change up your hair color and wondering what shade would look best with your olive skin and green eyes? Look no further, as we have gathered some helpful tips and recommendations to help you make the best choice for your hair color.

Description

Olive skin tones are characterized by a warm, golden undertone that can range from light to dark. If you have green eyes, you likely have a cool undertone that contrasts with your warm skin tone. Choosing the right hair color can help enhance your features and create a harmonious balance between your skin and eye color.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

The key to choosing the right hair color is to find a shade that complements your skin tone and eye color. Here are some tips to help you make the best choice:

Consider Your Skin Tone

When choosing a hair color for olive skin, it's important to consider your undertone. If you have a warm undertone, opt for shades like caramel, honey, or golden blonde. If you have a cool undertone, shades like ash brown, platinum blonde, or black can work well.

Think About Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining the best hair color for you. If you have green eyes, shades like copper, auburn, or warm browns can complement your eye color and make them pop.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hair color, it's important to consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you're willing to do. If you're looking for a low-maintenance option, opt for a shade close to your natural color. If you're willing to put in the effort, bold and vibrant colors like red or purple can be a fun choice.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hair color:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Undertone

The first step is to determine your skin undertone. Look at the veins on your wrist – if they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If they appear blue, you have a cool undertone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Take a look at your eye color and consider shades that will complement it. If you have green eyes, warm shades like copper or auburn can work well.

Step 3: Determine Your Maintenance Level

Consider how much maintenance you're willing to do. If you're looking for a low-maintenance option, stick to a shade close to your natural color. If you're willing to put in the effort, bold and vibrant colors can be a fun choice.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

Consult with a professional stylist who can help you choose the right shade and ensure a proper application.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've chosen the right hair color, it's important to take care of it to ensure it stays vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips:

Use Color-Safe Products

Use shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for color-treated hair to help prevent fading.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage and fade your hair color. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray when you do.

Avoid Chlorine

Chlorine can cause your hair color to fade. If you're planning to swim, wear a swim cap or rinse your hair with fresh water before and after swimming.

FAQ

What hair colors should I avoid with olive skin and green eyes?

It's best to avoid cool shades like ash blonde or platinum, as they can wash out your warm skin tone and clash with your eye color.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's best to consult with a professional stylist to ensure proper application and avoid damaging your hair.

How often should I touch up my hair color?

It depends on the color and how fast your hair grows. Typically, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color for olive skin and green eyes can enhance your features and create a harmonious balance between your skin and eye color. Consider your skin undertone, eye color, and maintenance level when choosing a hair color, and don't forget to take good care of your hair to ensure it stays vibrant and healthy.
