How To Cut Curtain Bangs For Round Face

Written by Daniel Aug 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face and want to try something new with your hair, curtain bangs might be the perfect option for you. Not only do they add a touch of glamour to your look, but they can also help to elongate your face and create a more angular appearance. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting curtain bangs for a round face, step by step.

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that has been trending for a while now. They are called "curtain" bangs because they are parted in the middle, just like a pair of curtains. This style is great for those who want to add some dimension to their hair without committing to a full fringe. Curtain bangs work well on all hair types and lengths, but they are particularly flattering on round faces.

How to Cut Curtain Bangs for Round Face

Before you start cutting your curtain bangs, make sure that your hair is clean, dry, and tangle-free. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, create a center part in your hair. Then, section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. You can choose to make your bangs as thick or as thin as you like, but make sure that they are even on both sides.

Step 2: Create a Triangle

Divide your sectioned hair into two parts, then create a triangle shape by combing the hair towards the center of your forehead. The point of the triangle should be where you want your bangs to start.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Hold the triangle-shaped section of hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Be careful not to cut too much at once, as you can always go back and trim more if needed. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once you have cut your bangs, you can style them to your liking. You can use a straightener to make them sleek and straight, or you can use a curling iron to add some volume and texture. Play around with different styles until you find one that you love.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your curtain bangs:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always go back and trim more if necessary.
  • Make sure that your scissors are sharp. Dull scissors can lead to uneven and jagged cuts.
  • Use a comb to make sure that your bangs are even on both sides.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're not confident in cutting your own hair.
  • Try to avoid cutting your bangs when you're in a rush or feeling stressed. Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

Q: Can anyone pull off curtain bangs?

A: Yes, curtain bangs work well on all face shapes and hair types. However, they are particularly flattering on those with round faces.

Q: How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

A: You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and neat.

Q: What should I do if I mess up my bangs?

A: If you accidentally cut too much hair, don't panic. You can try to trim the other side to match, or you can visit a professional stylist to fix the issue.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, make sure to take good care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use a hair serum or oil to add shine and nourishment to your hair.

Cutting curtain bangs for a round face can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a fun and rewarding experience. Follow our step-by-step guide, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find one that you love. With a little patience and practice, you'll be rocking your new curtain bangs in no time!
