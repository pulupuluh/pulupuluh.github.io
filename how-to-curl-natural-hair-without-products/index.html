How To Curl Natural Hair Without Products A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to style, especially if you don't want to rely on products to achieve your desired look. Luckily, there are a few easy tricks you can use to curl your natural hair without using any products at all. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve beautiful, natural curls without any added chemicals or styling products. Here's what you need to know:

How to Curl Natural Hair Without Products

Before we get started, it's important to note that the best way to achieve natural curls without products is to work with your hair's natural texture. This means that if you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you'll have an easier time achieving curls without using any products. However, even if your hair is straight, you can still achieve curls using the techniques we'll outline below.

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

The first step to achieving natural curls without products is to start with clean, dry hair. This will help your curls hold their shape better and prevent them from falling flat throughout the day. You can either wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner or simply wet your hair in the shower and let it air dry before styling.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Next, divide your hair into small sections. The size of the sections will depend on how tight you want your curls to be. If you want looser, more natural-looking curls, make larger sections. If you want tighter, more defined curls, make smaller sections.

Step 3: Twist Each Section of Hair

Take a section of hair and twist it tightly from the roots to the ends. You can twist the hair in either direction, depending on the direction you want the curls to go. Repeat this process with each section of hair, twisting in the same direction for a more uniform look or alternating the direction for a more natural look.

Step 4: Pin the Twisted Hair in Place

Once you've twisted each section of hair, use bobby pins or hair clips to hold the twists in place. Make sure the pins are secure so that the twists don't unravel overnight.

Step 5: Sleep on It

The best way to achieve natural curls without products is to let your hair dry overnight. This will give the twists plenty of time to set and hold their shape. Simply go to bed with your hair pinned up and wake up with beautiful, natural curls.

Tips for Curling Natural Hair Without Products

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect natural curls without using any products:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and preserve your curls overnight.
  • If you have straight hair, you can still achieve curls using the twisting method described above. Just keep in mind that the curls may not be as tight or long-lasting as they would be on naturally curly hair.
  • You can also achieve natural curls without products by braiding your hair instead of twisting it. Simply braid your hair in small sections and pin the braids in place overnight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I curl natural hair without using heat?

Yes, you can curl natural hair without using heat. The twisting and braiding methods described above both work well to achieve natural-looking curls without using any heat styling tools.

How long will my curls last?

The length of time your curls will last depends on a variety of factors, including your hair's natural texture, the size of the sections you twisted, and how well you secured the twists with pins or clips. Generally, you can expect your curls to last for at least a day or two.

How can I maintain my curls throughout the day?

To maintain your curls throughout the day, avoid touching or brushing your hair too much. You can also use a light hold hairspray to help keep your curls in place without weighing them down.

Hair Care for Natural Curls

Finally, it's important to take good care of your natural curls to keep them looking their best. Here are a few hair care tips for natural curls:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, natural curls without using any products. Whether you have naturally curly hair or straight hair, these methods can help you achieve the look you want without relying on chemicals or styling products. Give them a try and see how easy it can be to embrace your natural curls!
