How To Cut Short Curly Hair In Layers At Home The Ultimate Guide

In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve salon-worthy layers in your short curly hair from the comfort of your home. So, grab your scissors and let's get started!

How to Cut Short Curly Hair in Layers at Home

Cutting curly hair can be a daunting task, especially if you want to add layers. However, with the right technique and tools, it is possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Here are the steps you should follow:

1. Gather the Necessary Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

2. Wash and Detangle Your Hair

Wash your hair and detangle it with a wide-tooth comb. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

3. Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into four sections – two at the front and two at the back. Secure each section with hair clips.

4. Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair from the back. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair in a diagonal direction to create a layer. Repeat this process for each section until you have cut all your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather the Necessary Tools

Gather all the tools you need to cut your short curly hair in layers. Make sure your scissors are sharp, and your comb is wide-toothed.

Step 2: Wash and Detangle Your Hair

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair from the back. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair in a diagonal direction to create a layer. Repeat this process for each section until you have cut all your hair.

Step 5: Check for Unevenness

After cutting all your hair, check for unevenness. If you find any, trim the hair until all the layers are even.

Tips to Remember

  • Start with small sections of hair and gradually increase the size if you feel confident.
  • Always cut the hair in a diagonal direction to create layers.
  • Check for unevenness and trim the hair until all the layers are even.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting hair requires precision and practice.

FAQs

1. Can I cut my short curly hair in layers if I have never done it before?

Yes, you can. Just make sure you follow the steps carefully and start with small sections of hair.

2. Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, you should use hair scissors which are specifically designed for cutting hair.

3. How often should I cut my short curly hair in layers?

You should cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the layers.

Hair Care after Cutting Short Curly Hair in Layers

After cutting your hair, it's essential to take good care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the layers.

Now that you know how to cut short curly hair in layers at home, it's time to give it a try. Follow the steps carefully, and you'll be amazed at how easy it is to achieve a salon-worthy haircut at home.
