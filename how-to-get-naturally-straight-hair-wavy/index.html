How To Get Naturally Straight Hair Wavy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Do you have naturally straight hair and want to switch things up with some waves? You don't need to spend hours with a curling iron or rely on chemical treatments to achieve wavy locks. In this guide, we'll cover how to get naturally straight hair wavy using simple techniques and minimal heat.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prep Your Hair
  • How to Create Waves with Braids
  • How to Create Waves with Twists
  • How to Create Waves with a Headband
  • Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Prep Your Hair

Before you start creating waves, you need to prepare your hair. Follow these steps for optimal results:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would. Don't use too much conditioner, as this may weigh down your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently, without rubbing it too hard. Avoid using a blow dryer, as this will make your hair straight again.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair. This will help prevent damage from any heat styling tools you may use.

How to Create Waves with Braids

Braids are a simple and effective way to create waves in your hair. Follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair down the middle or to one side, depending on your preference.
  2. Create two braids on each side of your head. The braids should be tight, but not too tight that they hurt your scalp.
  3. Secure the ends of the braids with hair ties.
  4. Leave the braids in for at least 4-6 hours or overnight.
  5. Take out the hair ties and gently undo the braids with your fingers.
  6. Apply a light hold hairspray to keep the waves in place.

How to Create Waves with Twists

Twists are another easy way to create waves in your hair. Here's how:

  1. Part your hair down the middle or to one side.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and twist it away from your face.
  3. Continue twisting the section until it reaches the nape of your neck.
  4. Secure the twist with a bobby pin or hair tie.
  5. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  6. Leave the twists in for at least 4-6 hours or overnight.
  7. Take out the bobby pins or hair ties and gently undo the twists with your fingers.
  8. Apply a light hold hairspray to keep the waves in place.

How to Create Waves with a Headband

A headband can help create natural-looking waves in your hair. Follow these steps:

  1. Put a headband on your head. The headband should sit about an inch back from your hairline.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and tuck it into the headband.
  3. Take another section of hair and tuck it into the headband, but this time twist it before tucking it in.
  4. Continue twisting and tucking sections of hair into the headband until you reach the nape of your neck.
  5. Leave the headband in for at least 4-6 hours or overnight.
  6. Take out the headband and gently undo the twists with your fingers.
  7. Apply a light hold hairspray to keep the waves in place.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your waves looking great:

  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can make your waves disappear.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it straight again.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting naturally straight hair wavy:

Can I use a curling iron to create waves?

Yes, you can. However, using too much heat on your hair can damage it, so it's best to use a curling iron sparingly.

How long do the waves last?

The waves can last for a few days, depending on how well you maintain them.

Can I create waves without using any heat?

Yes, you can create waves without using any heat. The braids, twists, and headband methods mentioned above are all heat-free.


Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily get naturally straight hair wavy without damaging your locks. Try out different methods to see which one works best for you, and don't forget to take good care of your wavy hair once you achieve it!
