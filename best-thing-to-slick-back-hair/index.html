Best Thing To Slick Back Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a sleek and stylish look? Slicked back hair is a classic hairstyle that can give you a polished and sophisticated appearance. However, achieving the perfect slicked back look can be challenging, especially if you don't have the right products. In this guide, we will explore the best thing to slick back hair and provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Slicked back hair is a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. It involves combing your hair back, away from your forehead, using a styling product to hold it in place. Slicked back hair can be achieved with different hair lengths, textures, and styles, making it a versatile and adaptable hairstyle.

How to Slick Back Hair

The first step to achieving a perfect slicked back look is to gather the necessary tools and products. You will need a fine-toothed comb, a hairbrush, and a styling product that can provide a firm hold. The best products to use for slicked back hair are pomades, gels, and waxes. Here's how to slick back your hair:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup. Towel dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Apply the styling product

Take a small amount of the styling product and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Apply it to your hair, starting from the back and working your way forward. Be sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Use a fine-toothed comb to comb your hair back, away from your forehead. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down to the back of your neck. Use a hairbrush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Step 4: Set the style

Use your fingers to shape your hair into the desired style. You can create a slicked back look with a side part or a center part, depending on your preference. Once you're happy with the style, use a hairdryer on low heat to set it in place.

Tips for Slick Back Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed to avoid a greasy or heavy look.
  • Use a comb with fine teeth to create a sleek and polished look.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a hairdryer on low heat to set the style and avoid frizz or flyaways.
  • For a more natural look, use a matte finish product instead of a shiny one.

FAQs

What is the best product to use for slicked back hair?

The best products to use for slicked back hair are pomades, gels, and waxes. These products provide a firm hold and a shiny or matte finish, depending on your preference.

Can I slick back my hair if I have short hair?

Yes, slicked back hair can be achieved with different hair lengths and textures. You can use a gel or wax to create a sleek and polished look for short hair.

How do I maintain my slicked back hairstyle?

To maintain your slicked back hairstyle, you should wash your hair regularly and apply a small amount of product to keep it in place. Avoid touching your hair throughout the day to prevent it from becoming greasy or frizzy.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

Slicked back hair requires regular maintenance and care to keep it looking sleek and polished. Here are some hair care tips for slicked back hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup or dirt.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a hairbrush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day to prevent it from becoming frizzy or greasy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Conclusion

Slicked back hair is a classic and stylish hairstyle that can give you a polished and sophisticated look. The best thing to slick back hair is a styling product that provides a firm hold and a shiny or matte finish. With the right tools, products, and techniques, you can achieve the perfect slicked back look. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain your style with regular washing and conditioning.
