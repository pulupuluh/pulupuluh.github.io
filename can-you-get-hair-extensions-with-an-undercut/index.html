Can You Get Hair Extensions With An Undercut

Written by Petter Feb 20, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you someone who loves to experiment with your hair and try out new styles? If you have an undercut, you might be wondering if you can still get hair extensions. The good news is that it is possible to get hair extensions with an undercut, and in this blog post, we’ll tell you everything you need to know about it.

What is an Undercut?

An undercut is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides and back of your head is shaved or cut short, while the hair on top is left long. This style has become increasingly popular in recent years, and many people have opted for it as a way to stand out and make a statement.

How to Get Hair Extensions with an Undercut

If you have an undercut and want to get hair extensions, you’ll need to find a hairstylist who has experience working with this type of hairstyle. They will be able to determine the best method for attaching the extensions based on your hair type, the length of your undercut, and the type of extensions you want.

Clip-in Extensions

Clip-in extensions are a great option for those with an undercut, as they can be easily attached to the longer hair on top. All you need to do is section off your hair and clip the extensions in place. This method is quick and easy, and you can remove the extensions whenever you want.

Tape-in Extensions

Tape-in extensions are another option for those with an undercut. With this method, the extensions are attached using a special tape that is applied to your natural hair. The tape is then heated to bond the extensions to your hair. This method is more permanent than clip-in extensions, but it can be more time-consuming to apply.

Sew-in Extensions

Sew-in extensions are a popular option for those with all hair types, including undercuts. With this method, your natural hair is braided into cornrows, and the extensions are sewn onto the braids. This method can take several hours to complete, but the results can last for several months.

Tips for Maintaining Hair Extensions with an Undercut

Once you have your hair extensions in place, it’s important to take proper care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair extensions with an undercut:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the extensions
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the extensions
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid pulling on the extensions
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as this can cause tangling

FAQ

Can I get hair extensions with a short undercut?

Yes, you can get hair extensions with a short undercut. Your hairstylist will be able to determine the best method for attaching the extensions based on the length of your undercut.

How long do hair extensions with an undercut last?

The length of time that your hair extensions will last depends on the method of attachment and how well you take care of them. Clip-in extensions can be removed daily, while sew-in extensions can last for several months.

Can I dye my hair extensions?

It is possible to dye your hair extensions, but it’s best to have a professional do it to avoid damaging the extensions.

Hair Care for Undercuts

Regardless of whether you have hair extensions or not, it’s important to take care of your undercut. Here are some tips for maintaining a healthy and stylish undercut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your undercut looking fresh

Conclusion

Getting hair extensions with an undercut is possible, and it can be a great way to switch up your style and add some length and volume to your hair. With the right hairstylist and proper care, your extensions can look great for months. So go ahead and try out this fun and edgy hairstyle!
