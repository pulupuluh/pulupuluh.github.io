Best Short Haircuts For Round Faces Over 60

Written by Bardi Aug 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that suits your round face over 60? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this article, we will be discussing the best short haircuts for round faces over 60 that will make you look youthful and stylish.

Description

As you age, your hair tends to lose its texture, volume, and thickness. Therefore, it's important to choose a haircut that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. If you have a round face, you need a haircut that elongates your face and adds volume to the crown. A short haircut can do wonders for your round face, making you look younger and more vibrant.

How to

Before choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your hair type, texture, and thickness. If you have thin hair, go for a layered haircut that adds volume to your hair. If you have thick hair, go for a choppy haircut that removes excess weight from your hair. Here are some best short haircuts for round faces over 60:

1. Pixie Haircut

The pixie haircut is one of the best short haircuts for round faces over 60. It's a low-maintenance haircut that adds volume to the crown and elongates your face. You can style your pixie haircut in different ways, such as messy, sleek, or curly.

2. Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is another great short haircut for round faces over 60. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in different lengths and textures. The bob haircut adds volume to the crown and elongates your face. You can go for a classic bob or a layered bob depending on your hair type and texture.

3. Short Shag Haircut

The short shag haircut is a trendy and edgy haircut that adds texture and volume to your hair. It's a perfect haircut for thick hair and adds depth to your face. You can style your short shag haircut in different ways, such as messy, sleek, or curly.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a short haircut:

Step 1: Choose a haircut that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Step 2: Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you.

Step 3: Choose a haircut that adds volume to the crown and elongates your face.

Step 4: Style your haircut according to your preference and hair type.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your short haircut:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

2. Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.

3. Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat tools.

4. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

1. Can I get a short haircut if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can get a short haircut if you have thin hair. However, make sure to choose a layered haircut that adds volume to your hair.

2. Will a short haircut make me look older?

No, a short haircut can make you look younger and more vibrant if chosen correctly.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to maintain the health of your hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.

2. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

3. Avoid using heat tools frequently as they can damage your hair.

4. Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair growth.

In conclusion, a short haircut can do wonders for your round face over 60, making you look youthful and stylish. Choose a haircut that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and maintain it regularly to keep it looking its best.
