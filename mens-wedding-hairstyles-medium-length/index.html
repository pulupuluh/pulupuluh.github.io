Mens Wedding Hairstyles Medium Length A Guide For Grooms

Written by Christine Mar 26, 2023 Â· 9 min read

When it comes to planning a wedding, there are so many things to consider, from the venue to the guest list to the wedding party's outfits. And for the groom, one of the most important decisions is the hairstyle. For those with medium length hair, there are plenty of options that are both stylish and appropriate for the big day. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best mens wedding hairstyles medium length, including how to achieve them, tips for styling, and more.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hair for a Wedding

How to Choose the Right Mens Wedding Hairstyles Medium Length

Before you decide on a hairstyle for your wedding day, it's important to consider a few factors, such as your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Here are some tips for choosing the right mens wedding hairstyles medium length:

  • Consider your face shape: If you have a square face, a textured hairstyle with some volume on top can help balance out your features. For those with round faces, a more structured hairstyle with a side part can create the illusion of length.
  • Think about your hair type: If you have thick, wavy hair, a layered cut can help manage the volume and add some texture. If your hair is fine, a shorter, more structured cut can help create the appearance of thickness.
  • Consider your personal style: If you're someone who likes to keep things simple, a classic side part or slicked-back style can be a great option. For those who want to stand out, a more dramatic style with a lot of texture or volume can be a great choice.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hair for a Wedding

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your wedding day, it's important to know how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type to ensure it's healthy and easy to style.
  2. Apply a styling product. Depending on the style you're going for, you may want to use a mousse, gel, or styling cream. Apply it to your hair evenly, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.
  3. Blow-dry your hair. Use a hair dryer and a brush to achieve the desired shape and volume. If you're going for a more structured style, use a comb to create a side part.
  4. Style your hair. Use your fingers or a comb to create the final shape and texture. Apply a finishing product, such as a hairspray or pomade, to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wedding Hairstyle

Once you've achieved the perfect wedding hairstyle, you'll want to make sure it looks great all day long. Here are some tips for maintaining your mens wedding hairstyles medium length:

  • Use a strong-hold hairspray or pomade to keep your hair in place throughout the day.
  • Consider bringing a travel-sized bottle of your styling product with you in case you need to touch up your hair throughout the day.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause it to lose its shape and texture.
  • If you're getting married outdoors, be prepared for wind and humidity, which can affect your hairstyle.

FAQs About Mens Wedding Hairstyles Medium Length

What are some popular mens wedding hairstyles medium length?

Some popular options include a textured crop, slicked-back style, side part, and messy fringe.

How far in advance should I plan my wedding hairstyle?

It's a good idea to start thinking about your wedding hairstyle a few months in advance. This will give you enough time to experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you.

Should I get a haircut right before my wedding?

It's generally a good idea to get a haircut a week or two before your wedding day. This will give your hair enough time to settle and ensure it looks its best on the big day.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy and Shiny Hair

To ensure your hair looks its best on your wedding day, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for healthy and shiny hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these mens wedding hairstyles medium length tips, you'll be able to find the perfect style for your big day and ensure your hair looks its best. Whether you're going for a classic look or something more modern and edgy, the right hairstyle can help you feel confident and stylish as you walk down the aisle.
