How to Cut Very Short Pixie

A very short pixie cut is a super-short haircut that is typically cut with clippers. It's a bold and edgy hairstyle that is perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hair. Here are the steps to cutting a very short pixie cut:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, you'll need to gather your tools. You will need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four sections, using clips to hold them in place. Start at the nape of your neck and move upwards, clipping each section as you go.

Step 3: Start with the Clippers

Begin with the clippers and a guard that's the same length you want your hair to be. Starting at the nape of your neck, use the clippers to cut your hair in an upward motion. Repeat this process for each section of hair, making sure to blend the hair as you go.

Step 4: Use Scissors to Add Texture

Once you've finished with the clippers, use scissors to add texture to your hair. This will help to create a more natural and edgy look. Take small sections of hair and use the scissors to cut at an angle, creating a choppy texture.

Step 5: Finish with Styling

Once you've finished cutting your hair, you'll want to style it. Use a hair product, such as a wax or pomade, to add texture and hold to your hair. Style your hair as desired, using your fingers to create a messy and textured look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to cut a very short pixie haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help to remove any product buildup and make it easier to cut. Once your hair is dry, brush it out to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts, using clips to hold them in place. Section your hair from the nape of your neck to the crown of your head.

Step 3: Start with the Clippers

Begin with the clippers and a guard that's the same length you want your hair to be. Starting at the nape of your neck, use the clippers to cut your hair in an upward motion. Make sure to blend the hair as you go, so that there are no harsh lines.

Step 4: Move Upward and Repeat

Move upward, repeating the same process with the clippers. Make sure to blend the hair as you go, so that there are no harsh lines. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 5: Use Scissors to Add Texture

Once you've finished with the clippers, use scissors to add texture to your hair. Take small sections of hair and use the scissors to cut at an angle, creating a choppy texture. This will help to create a more natural and edgy look.

Step 6: Check for Balance

Check for balance by looking at your hair in the mirror. Make sure that both sides are even and that there are no harsh lines. If you need to make any adjustments, do so with the scissors.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, you'll want to style it. Use a hair product, such as a wax or pomade, to add texture and hold to your hair. Style your hair as desired, using your fingers to create a messy and textured look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you cut a very short pixie haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard, so that you don't cut your hair too short.
  • Use a spray bottle filled with water to wet your hair, so that it's easier to cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process. Cutting hair takes practice, so don't be too hard on yourself if it doesn't turn out perfectly the first time.
  • Use a mirror to check for balance and make sure that both sides are even.

FAQs

Q: How short should I cut my hair for a very short pixie?

A: A very short pixie is typically cut with clippers and is about 1/4 inch to 1/2 inch in length.

Q: Can I cut a very short pixie haircut at home?

A: Yes, you can cut a very short pixie haircut at home. However, it's important to have the right tools and to take your time.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to care for your new very short pixie haircut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Cutting a very short pixie haircut can be a bold and edgy choice. With the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can achieve this look at home. Remember to take your time and to be patient with yourself. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to reach out to a professional hairstylist for guidance.
