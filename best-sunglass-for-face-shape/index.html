Best Sunglasses For Face Shape

Written by Christine Jun 04, 2023 · 6 min read
Best Sunglasses for Face Shape

The right pair of sunglasses can be the perfect accessory to complete your look. However, not all sunglasses are created equal when it comes to fitting your face shape. In this article, we’ll guide you through the best sunglasses for face shape, so you can find a pair that flatters your features and suits your style.

Table of Contents

  • Oval Face Shape
  • Round Face Shape
  • Square Face Shape
  • Heart Face Shape
  • Diamond Face Shape
  • Tips for Choosing the Best Sunglasses
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Wearing Sunglasses

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, consider yourself lucky! This versatile face shape can pull off just about any style of sunglasses. Aviators, round frames, and square frames are all great options for oval faces.

Round Face Shape

For those with round faces, it’s best to choose sunglasses with angular frames to balance out the softness of your features. Rectangular, square, and cat-eye frames are all great choices for round faces.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, choose sunglasses with round or oval frames to soften your features. Aviators, round frames, and cat-eye frames can all work well for square faces.

Heart Face Shape

Heart-shaped faces have wider foreheads and narrower chins, so it’s best to choose sunglasses that are wider at the top and narrower at the bottom. Cat-eye frames and round frames are both great options for heart-shaped faces.

Diamond Face Shape

Diamond-shaped faces have angular features and are wider at the temples than at the jawline. To balance out your features, choose sunglasses with oval or round frames. Cat-eye frames can also work well for diamond faces.

Tips for Choosing the Best Sunglasses

  • Consider your face shape when choosing sunglasses
  • Choose frames that fit your face properly – they shouldn’t slide down your nose or dig into your temples
  • Look for sunglasses with UV protection to protect your eyes from the sun’s harmful rays
  • Consider your personal style when choosing sunglasses – there are many different frame styles to choose from!

FAQs

  • Q: Can I wear any style of sunglasses with any face shape?
  • A: While you can certainly wear any style of sunglasses you like, certain styles will flatter different face shapes more than others.
  • Q: Are expensive sunglasses worth the investment?
  • A: While there are certainly high-quality sunglasses available at all price points, more expensive sunglasses may offer better UV protection and durability.
  • Q: Can sunglasses damage my hair?
  • A: Sunglasses can sometimes cause damage to your hair if they are too tight or if you wear them on top of your head. To prevent damage, choose sunglasses that fit properly and avoid wearing them on top of your head for extended periods of time.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Sunglasses

Wearing sunglasses can sometimes cause damage to your hair if they are too tight or if you wear them on top of your head. To prevent damage, choose sunglasses that fit properly and avoid wearing them on top of your head for extended periods of time. You can also protect your hair by using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil before putting on your sunglasses, and by gently removing them from your face instead of pulling them off.
