What Your Moustache Says About You A Guide To Facial Hair In 2023

Written by Luffy Aug 21, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you curious about what your moustache says about you? If so, you're not alone! Facial hair has been a popular topic for centuries, and in 2023 it's more popular than ever. Whether you're sporting a handlebar, a pencil, or a full-on lumberjack beard, your facial hair says a lot about you as a person. In this article, we'll explore what your moustache says about you, and offer tips and advice on how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • Introduction
  • How to Grow a Moustache
  • Choosing a Moustache Style
  • Moustache Care Tips
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Introduction

First things first: why do we even have facial hair in the first place? The answer is simple - it's a natural part of being human! Men and women alike have hair follicles all over their bodies, and in men the hormones testosterone and dihydrotestosterone (DHT) cause these follicles to produce thicker, coarser hair on the face and body.

But what does your moustache say about you as a person? Here are a few things to consider:

Masculinity

Let's face it - a moustache is a quintessential symbol of masculinity. Whether you're going for a rugged, outdoorsy look or a refined, gentlemanly style, a well-groomed moustache can help you look and feel more manly.

Confidence

Wearing a moustache takes a certain amount of confidence - after all, you're making a bold statement with your facial hair. Whether you're going for a full-on handlebar or a subtle pencil-thin 'stache, owning your look and feeling confident in your appearance can go a long way in your personal and professional life.

Individuality

No two moustaches are exactly alike, which means that your facial hair can be a reflection of your individuality and personal style. Whether you're rocking a classic look or a trendy, modern style, your moustache can help you stand out from the crowd and express your unique personality.

How to Grow a Moustache

So, you're ready to grow a moustache - now what? Here are a few tips to help you get started:

Start with a Clean Slate

If you're currently clean-shaven, you'll need to stop shaving for a few weeks in order to grow out your moustache. Make sure to keep your face clean and moisturized during this time to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs.

Be Patient

It can take several weeks (or even months, in some cases) for a moustache to grow in fully. Be patient and resist the urge to trim or shape your facial hair too soon.

Experiment with Styles

There are dozens of different moustache styles to choose from, so don't be afraid to experiment with different looks until you find one that suits you.

Choosing a Moustache Style

Now that you've grown out your facial hair, it's time to choose a moustache style that suits your personality and appearance. Here are a few popular styles to consider:

The Handlebar

The handlebar is a classic moustache style that features long, curled ends that resemble the handlebars of a bicycle. This style is a great choice for men who want to make a bold statement with their facial hair.

The Pencil

The pencil moustache is a thin, delicate style that requires a steady hand and a lot of patience. This style is a good choice for men who want to add a touch of sophistication to their appearance.

The Chevron

The chevron is a thick, bushy style that covers the entire upper lip. This style is a great choice for men who want to add volume and texture to their facial hair.

Moustache Care Tips

Now that you've chosen a moustache style, it's important to take good care of your facial hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to help you care for your moustache:

Keep it Clean

Regular washing and conditioning can help keep your moustache looking and feeling healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for facial hair, and avoid using harsh soaps or chemicals that can dry out your skin.

Trim it Regularly

Regular trimming can help keep your moustache looking neat and tidy. Use a pair of sharp scissors or a trimmer to remove any straggly hairs or to shape your moustache to your desired style.

Use Moustache Wax

Moustache wax can help you tame unruly hairs and keep your moustache looking sharp. Choose a wax that's specifically designed for facial hair, and use a small amount to shape your moustache as desired.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will shaving make my facial hair grow back thicker?

A: No - this is a common myth. Shaving does not affect the thickness or texture of your facial hair.

Q: How often should I wash my moustache?

A: It depends on your personal preference and lifestyle. If you have a particularly oily or dirty job, you may need to wash your moustache more frequently. Otherwise, once or twice a week is usually sufficient.

Q: Can I dye my moustache?

A: Yes - there are several products on the market specifically designed for dyeing facial hair. However, be sure to follow the instructions carefully and choose a dye that's specifically designed for facial hair.

Q: How do I prevent ingrown hairs in my moustache?

A: Regular exfoliation can help prevent ingrown hairs. Use a gentle scrub or exfoliating brush to remove dead skin cells and keep your hair follicles clear.

In conclusion, your moustache says a lot about you as a person - whether you're going for a rugged, masculine look or a refined, sophisticated style, your facial hair can be a reflection of your personality and individuality. By following these tips and taking good care of your moustache, you can look and feel your best every day.
