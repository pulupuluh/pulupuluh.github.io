How To Make Thin Straight Hair Curly For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Robby May 08, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have thin, straight hair, you might be wondering if it's possible to get those coveted curls. The good news is that it is! With the right tools, products and techniques, you can transform your hair from limp and lifeless to full of body and bounce. In this article, we'll walk you through how to make thin straight hair curly for guys, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Thin Straight Hair Curly for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Thin Straight Hair Curly
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Thin Straight Hair Curly for Guys

The first step in making thin straight hair curly is to choose the right products. Look for a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner that will help to define your curls and add volume to your hair. You'll also need a curling iron or wand, hair clips, and a heat protectant spray.

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the curling iron.

Using a Curling Iron

If you're using a curling iron, start by clipping up the top section of your hair. Take a small section of hair (about 1 inch wide) and wrap it around the curling iron, starting at the base of the hair and working your way to the ends. Hold the curl for 10-15 seconds and then release. Repeat this process for the rest of your hair.

Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and give them a more natural look. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Using a Curling Wand

A curling wand is another great tool for making thin straight hair curly. To use a curling wand, take a small section of hair (about 1 inch wide) and wrap it around the wand, starting at the base of the hair and working your way to the ends. Hold the curl for 10-15 seconds and then release. Repeat this process for the rest of your hair.

Like with a curling iron, use your fingers to loosen the curls and finish with a light hold hairspray.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for making thin straight hair curly:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Clip up the top section of your hair.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, starting at the base of the hair and working your way to the ends.
  5. Hold the curl for 10-15 seconds and then release.
  6. Repeat this process for the rest of your hair.
  7. Use your fingers to loosen the curls and give them a more natural look.
  8. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Making Thin Straight Hair Curly

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to help define your curls and add volume to your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat of the curling iron or wand.
  • Start with a lower heat setting on your curling iron or wand and work your way up if needed.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as this can make your curls look unnatural and stiff.

FAQs

Can I make my hair curly without using heat?

Yes, you can make your hair curly without using heat. One way to do this is to braid your hair while it's damp and leave it overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you'll have natural-looking curls.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls should last for several hours, or even all day, depending on how you style them and the products you use. If you want your curls to last longer, try using a stronger hold hairspray or a curl-defining mousse.

Can I use a straightening iron to make my hair curly?

No, a straightening iron is designed to straighten hair, not curl it. If you try to use a straightening iron to make your hair curly, you'll likely end up with a mess of tangled hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make your curls lose their shape.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to help keep your curls looking their best.

By following these tips and using the right products and techniques, you can make thin straight hair curly for guys and achieve the perfect head of curls.
