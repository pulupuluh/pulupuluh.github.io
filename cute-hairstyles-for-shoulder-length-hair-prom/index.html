Cute Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair Prom A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Prom season is here and you're looking for the perfect hairstyle to match your shoulder length hair. You want something that's cute, trendy, and easy to do. Look no further! In this article, we'll give you a complete guide on cute hairstyles for shoulder length hair prom. From how to do them, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length hair is the perfect length for prom hairstyles. It's not too short that you can't do anything with it, but it's not too long that it's difficult to manage. There are so many cute hairstyles that you can do with shoulder length hair for prom. From updos to braids to curls, the options are endless. The key is to find a hairstyle that complements your dress and personality.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for shoulder length hair prom is to determine what type of prom dress you'll be wearing. This will help you decide whether you want an updo or a hairstyle that's down. Once you've decided on the dress, you can start looking for hairstyle ideas online or in magazines.

Updos:

Updos are a popular choice for prom hairstyles because they're elegant and sophisticated. Some cute updo options for shoulder length hair include:

  • Low chignon
  • Messy bun
  • Side braid updo
  • Twisted updo

Hairstyles Down:

If you prefer to wear your hair down for prom, there are plenty of cute options for shoulder length hair. Some ideas include:

  • Beachy waves
  • Straight and sleek
  • Half-up half-down
  • Braids

Step by step

Once you've decided on a hairstyle, it's time to start practicing. Follow these step-by-step instructions to achieve a cute hairstyle for shoulder length hair prom:

Low Chignon:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Brush your hair back into a low ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold in place.

Beachy Waves:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts.
  3. Curl each section with a curling iron, holding for 10 seconds each.
  4. Run your fingers through the curls to loosen them up.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cute hairstyle for shoulder length hair prom:

  • Start practicing your hairstyle a few weeks before prom to make sure you have enough time to perfect it.
  • Use hair products that work well with your hair type.
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with a cute clip, headband, or flower crown.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or professional stylist.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for shoulder length hair prom?

The best hairstyle for shoulder length hair prom depends on your dress and personal style. Some popular options include updos, beachy waves, and braids.

How can I make my shoulder length hair look longer for prom?

You can make your shoulder length hair look longer for prom by adding hair extensions or using a clip-in ponytail.

How do I keep my hair in place all night at prom?

To keep your hair in place all night at prom, use a strong hold hairspray and hairpins. Avoid touching your hair too much and keep it away from your face.

Hair Care

Before prom, make sure you're taking good care of your hair. This will help you achieve a healthy, shiny, and beautiful hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

With these tips and ideas, you're sure to find a cute hairstyle for shoulder length hair prom that will make you feel confident and beautiful on your special night.
