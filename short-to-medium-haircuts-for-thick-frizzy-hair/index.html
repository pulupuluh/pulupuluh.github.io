Short To Medium Haircuts For Thick Frizzy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of battling with your thick frizzy hair every morning? Do you want to try a new haircut that will make your hair more manageable? Look no further, because in this article, we will be discussing the best short to medium haircuts for thick frizzy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick frizzy hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right haircut, you can achieve a low-maintenance style that looks great. Short to medium haircuts are perfect for thick frizzy hair because they help to reduce the weight of the hair, making it easier to style and manage.

How To

Before you decide on a haircut, it's essential to consider your face shape and hair type. Short haircuts are best for round or square faces, while medium haircuts are ideal for oval or heart-shaped faces. If you have thick frizzy hair, a layered or textured cut will help to reduce the bulk of your hair and make it more manageable.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a short to medium haircut for thick frizzy hair:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Wash and condition your hair the day before your appointment.
  3. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to show your hairstylist.
  4. During the haircut, your hairstylist will likely use thinning shears or a razor to remove weight and add texture to your hair.
  5. After the haircut, your hairstylist may blow-dry and style your hair to give you an idea of how to style it at home.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short to medium haircut for thick frizzy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a smoothing serum or oil to your hair before blow-drying to help tame frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and preventing split ends.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for thick frizzy hair?

The best short haircut for thick frizzy hair is a layered bob or pixie cut. This will help to reduce the weight of your hair and make it more manageable.

What is the best medium haircut for thick frizzy hair?

The best medium haircut for thick frizzy hair is a shoulder-length cut with layers. This will help to reduce the bulk of your hair and make it more manageable.

How often should I get my haircut if I have thick frizzy hair?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

Here are some additional hair care tips for managing thick frizzy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to comb through your hair when wet to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel to dry it, as this can cause frizz. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water and let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on low heat.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and getting the right short to medium haircut for your thick frizzy hair, you can achieve a low-maintenance style that looks great and is easy to manage.
