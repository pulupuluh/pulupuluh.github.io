How To Do An Undercut At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your boring hairstyle and want to switch things up? An undercut might be the perfect solution for you! Not only is it trendy, but it's also easy to maintain. In this article, we'll show you how to do an undercut at home in just a few simple steps. Read on to find out more!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An undercut is a hairstyle that involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of your head while leaving the top section longer. This creates a dramatic contrast between the two lengths and adds a unique element to your overall look. Undercuts are popular among both men and women and can be styled in various ways.

How To

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, a comb, and a mirror. It's also important to have a clear idea of the type of undercut you want. You can browse online for inspiration or consult with a hairstylist.

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Wash your hair

It's easier to work with clean hair, so make sure to wash and dry your hair before starting.

Step 2: Section off the top of your hair

Using a comb, section off the top of your hair and clip it up. This will keep it out of the way while you work on the sides and back.

Step 3: Choose your clipper guard size

Depending on the length you want for the sides and back, choose the appropriate clipper guard size. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter length.

Step 4: Start clipping the sides and back

Using the clippers, start at the nape of your neck and work your way up to the sides. Make sure to go against the direction of hair growth for a clean cut. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Blend the top and sides

Once you've finished clipping the sides and back, blend it with the top section using scissors or clippers. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 6: Style your hair

Now that you've finished cutting your hair, you can style it however you like. Use a hair product like wax or gel to keep it in place.

Tips

  • Go slow and be careful when using the clippers to avoid any accidents.
  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist if you're unsure about the process.

FAQ

Can I do an undercut on my own?

Yes, you can do an undercut on your own if you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

How often should I maintain my undercut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep the sides and back. It's recommended to maintain it every 4-6 weeks.

Can I still tie my hair up with an undercut?

Yes, you can still tie your hair up with an undercut. The top section will provide enough length for a ponytail or bun.

Hair Care

After doing an undercut, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling. It's also recommended to get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh.

Now that you know how to do an undercut at home, you can experiment with different lengths and styles. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to avoid any mistakes. Good luck!
