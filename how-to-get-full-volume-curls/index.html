How To Get Full Volume Curls

Are you tired of having limp, lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and bounce to your curls? Look no further! In this guide, we will show you how to get full volume curls that will last all day long.

How to Get Full Volume Curls

There are several ways to get full volume curls, but the most effective method is by using a combination of heat and styling products. Here are some ways to achieve full volume curls:

1. Blow-Dry with a Diffuser

One of the best ways to get full volume curls is by blow-drying your hair with a diffuser. A diffuser is a tool that attaches to your blow dryer and distributes heat evenly to your curls. This method adds volume and definition to your curls, making them look fuller and bouncier.

2. Use a Curling Wand

Another way to achieve full volume curls is by using a curling wand. A curling wand is a styling tool that heats up and helps you create curls. By wrapping your hair around the barrel of the curling wand, you can create full, bouncy curls that last all day long.

3. Apply Mousse or Styling Foam

Before blow-drying or using a curling wand, it's important to apply a styling product that will add volume and hold to your curls. Mousse or styling foam is a great option for achieving full volume curls. Apply the product to your wet hair and distribute it evenly throughout your curls. This will help your curls hold their shape and add volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get full volume curls:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume and body to your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or styling foam, to your wet hair. Distribute the product evenly throughout your curls.

Step 3: Blow-Dry with a Diffuser

Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use your fingers to scrunch your curls as you blow-dry to add volume and definition.

Step 4: Use a Curling Wand

Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand to create full, bouncy curls. Wrap small sections of hair around the barrel of the curling wand and hold for a few seconds. Release the curl and repeat until you've curled all of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look by applying hairspray to hold your curls in place. Use a light hold hairspray to avoid weighing down your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Full Volume Curls

Here are some tips for maintaining full volume curls:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and maintain your curls overnight. Cotton pillowcases can cause friction, which can lead to frizz and flat curls.

2. Refresh Your Curls with Dry Shampoo

If your curls start to fall flat throughout the day, refresh them with dry shampoo. Spray the dry shampoo at the roots of your hair and massage it in to add volume and texture.

3. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to flat, lifeless hair. Try to wash your hair every other day to maintain its natural oils and volume.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve full volume curls on short hair?

A: Yes! You can achieve full volume curls on short hair using a curling wand and styling products.

Q: How can I prevent my curls from falling flat?

A: To prevent your curls from falling flat, use a light hold hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Hair Care Tips for Full Volume Curls

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining full volume curls:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, be sure to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

2. Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep conditioning can help keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Try to deep condition your hair once a week to maintain its health and volume.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and full.

By following these tips and using the right products and styling tools, you can achieve full volume curls that will last all day long. Say goodbye to limp, lifeless hair and hello to bouncy, voluminous curls!
