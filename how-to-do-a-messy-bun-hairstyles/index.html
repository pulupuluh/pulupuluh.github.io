How To Do A Messy Bun Hairstyle Step By Step Tutorial

How to Do a Messy Bun Hairstyle | Step by Step Tutorial

If you're looking for a quick and easy hairstyle that looks effortlessly chic, the messy bun is the way to go. Whether you're running errands, going to work, or heading out for a night on the town, a messy bun can be dressed up or down to suit any occasion.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a Messy Bun
  • Tips for a Perfect Messy Bun

How to Do a Messy Bun

Follow these steps to create a perfect messy bun:

  1. Start by gathering your hair into a high ponytail. Use a brush or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or tangles.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself until it forms a bun shape.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Don't worry about making it too neat or perfect, as a messy bun is meant to look relaxed and effortless.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a more natural, messy look.
  5. Spray the bun with hairspray to keep it in place.

And there you have it â a perfect messy bun!

Alternative Method

If you prefer a looser, more undone look, try this alternative method:

  1. Start by gathering your hair into a low ponytail, leaving a few strands loose around your face.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself until it forms a bun shape, but don't pull it too tight.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place, leaving some hair sticking out for a messy look.
  4. Loosen the bun slightly by gently pulling on the edges with your fingers.
  5. Spray the bun with hairspray to keep it in place.

And there you have it â a perfect messy bun with a more relaxed, undone feel.

Tips for a Perfect Messy Bun

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy bun:

  • Start with second-day hair, as it will have more texture and hold the style better.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair before styling.
  • Don't worry about making the bun too neat or perfect â the beauty of a messy bun is in its imperfection.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color for a seamless look.
  • Experiment with different bun placements â a low bun or side bun can create a different look.

FAQs about Messy Buns

Here are some frequently asked questions about messy buns:

How do I keep my messy bun from falling out?

Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place, and spray it with hairspray to keep it from unraveling. You can also try using a hair elastic with hooks to keep the bun in place.

Can I wear a messy bun to a formal event?

A messy bun can be dressed up or down to suit any occasion, so it's definitely an option for a formal event. Just make sure to keep it neat and polished, and accessorize with a statement hair clip or earrings.

Can I do a messy bun with short hair?

Yes, you can! Just gather your hair into a high ponytail and create a messy bun as usual. You can also try a half-up, half-down style with a messy bun on top.

Hair Care Tips for Messy Buns

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great while wearing messy buns:

  • Avoid wearing your hair in tight hairstyles for extended periods of time, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hydrating hair mask once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished.
  • Invest in a good quality brush or comb to detangle your hair without causing damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create the perfect messy bun every time. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this versatile hairstyle is sure to become a go-to in your hair repertoire.
