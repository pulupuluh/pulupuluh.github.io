How To Cut Thick Straight Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Feb 23, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thick straight hair, you know that not all haircuts are created equal. Finding the right haircut that suits your hair type and face shape can be a challenge. In this post, we’ll show you how to cut thick straight hair in a simple and easy way. From the tools you need to the actual cutting process, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You Need
  • How to Cut Thick Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Thick Straight Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Straight Hair

Tools You Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to make sure you have the right tools. Here are the tools you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Make sure your scissors are sharp to ensure a clean cut. A dull pair of scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut evenly.

How to Cut Thick Straight Hair

Cutting thick straight hair can be tricky, but with the right technique, you can achieve a great haircut. Here’s how:

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you’re cutting it at its natural length.

2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and divide it into sections. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

3. Determine the length you want to cut your hair. Use your comb to measure the length and mark it with your fingers.

4. Take a small section of hair from the back and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair straight across at the length you’ve marked.

5. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way up from the back to the top of your head. Make sure you’re cutting each section at the same length.

6. Once you’ve cut all the sections of your hair, use your comb to check for any unevenness. If you notice any, trim the hair using your scissors.

Tips for Cutting Thick Straight Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing can lead to uneven cuts.
  • Use your comb to measure the length of your hair and mark it with your fingers.
  • Don’t cut too much off at once. It’s best to cut a little at a time and check for evenness before continuing.
  • Trim the ends of your hair after cutting to ensure a clean and polished look.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair if it’s thick and straight?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if you have thick straight hair. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps outlined in this post.

How often should I cut my hair if it’s thick and straight?

It’s recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its health and shape.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Straight Hair

Here are some tips to keep your thick straight hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Cutting thick straight hair can be a challenge, but with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a great haircut. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don’t be afraid to trim a little at a time if you’re unsure. Follow these tips for cutting and caring for your thick straight hair, and you’ll have healthy, beautiful hair in no time.
