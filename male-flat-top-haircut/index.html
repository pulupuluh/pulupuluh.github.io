Male Flat Top Haircut A Classic Style That Never Gets Old

Written by Christine Jun 24, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a classic and timeless hairstyle? Look no further than the male flat top haircut. This style has been around for decades and has remained popular for its versatility and ease of maintenance. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about the male flat top haircut, including how to achieve it, tips for styling and caring for your hair, and frequently asked questions.

Description

The male flat top haircut is a short, military-inspired style that features a flat, horizontal top and short sides. It is typically cut to a length of about 1-2 inches on the sides and back, with the top cut to a uniform length and styled to stand straight up. The style can be customized to suit your face shape and personal preferences, with variations including a tapered or faded back and sides, a longer or shorter top, and a more textured or sleek finish.

How to Achieve a Male Flat Top Haircut

If you're interested in getting a male flat top haircut, the first step is to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who has experience with this style. Communicate your desired length, shape, and texture to the stylist, and be open to their recommendations and suggestions. The stylist will likely start by trimming the sides and back to the desired length, then use clippers, scissors, or a combination of both to create a flat top. They may use a comb and hair dryer to shape and style the top, finishing with a product such as pomade or hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving a male flat top haircut:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup.
  2. Sit in the barber's chair and communicate your desired length, shape, and texture.
  3. The stylist will likely start by trimming the sides and back to the desired length.
  4. Using clippers or scissors, they will create a flat top by cutting the hair to a uniform length and shaping it into a flat, horizontal plane.
  5. They may use a comb and hair dryer to shape and style the top, finishing with a product such as pomade or hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling and Caring for Your Hair

Once you have achieved your male flat top haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style and keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length of the style.
  • Use a styling product such as pomade or hairspray to hold the style in place and add shine.
  • Avoid over-styling or using too much product, which can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy or limp.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant product.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the male flat top haircut:

How long does a male flat top haircut last?

A male flat top haircut can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the style.

What face shape is best suited to a male flat top haircut?

A male flat top haircut can suit a variety of face shapes, but it is particularly flattering on those with square or oval faces.

Can I achieve a male flat top haircut at home?

While it is possible to achieve a male flat top haircut at home, it is recommended that you go to a professional stylist or barber to ensure the best results.

Hair Care

In conclusion, the male flat top haircut is a classic and versatile style that can suit a variety of ages and face shapes. With the right styling and hair care techniques, you can maintain this style and keep it looking sharp for weeks to come.
