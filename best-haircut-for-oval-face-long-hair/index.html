Best Haircut For Oval Face Long Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jun 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face and long hair, you might be wondering what the best haircut is for you. This article will provide you with all the information you need to know about the best haircut for oval face long hair.

Description:

An oval face is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin and a face that is longer than it is wide. This face shape is considered to be one of the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. Long hair is also a great canvas for a variety of hairstyles. However, not all long hairstyles are suitable for oval faces.

How to:

The first step in finding the best haircut for oval face long hair is to identify your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three are about the same width, you have an oval face.

Once you have identified your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement it. The best hairstyles for oval faces are those that balance out the proportions of the face.

When it comes to long hair, the best haircuts for oval faces are those that add volume and texture to the hair. Layers are a great way to achieve this as they create movement and add depth to the hair.

Step by Step:

Here are the steps to follow when getting the best haircut for oval face long hair:

Step 1: Identify Your Face Shape

Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three are about the same width, you have an oval face.

Step 2: Determine Your Hair Type

Determine whether your hair is thick, thin, curly, or straight. This will help you choose a hairstyle that works best for your hair type.

Step 3: Choose a Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair type. Layers are a great way to add volume and texture to long hair.

Step 4: Consult with a Hairstylist

Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type. They can also give you tips on how to style your hair at home.

Tips:

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting the best haircut for oval face long hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or blunt as they can make your face appear longer.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle as they can help balance out the proportions of your face.
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for oval face long hair:

What is the best haircut for long hair?

The best haircut for long hair is one that complements your face shape and hair type. For oval faces, layers are a great way to add volume and texture to the hair.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oval face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or blunt as they can make your face appear longer.

Can bangs work with long hair and an oval face?

Yes, bangs can work with long hair and an oval face. They can help balance out the proportions of the face.

Hair Care:

To maintain your best haircut for oval face long hair, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, the best haircut for oval face long hair is one that complements your face shape and hair type. Layers are a great way to add volume and texture to long hair. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you and follow these hair care tips to maintain your hair's health and shine.
