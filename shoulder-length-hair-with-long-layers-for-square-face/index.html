Shoulder Length Hair With Long Layers For Square Face Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of having the same old hairstyle? If you have a square face, you might be wondering which hairstyle suits you best. Luckily, shoulder length hair with long layers can be a great option for you! In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this look, step-by-step, as well as provide some tips on how to care for your hair once you have it. Let's get started!

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair with Long Layers for Square Face

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to consider the length you want to achieve. Shoulder length hair is flattering for most face shapes, including square faces. To add some dimension to your hair, you can opt for long layers.

Here's how to achieve shoulder length hair with long layers:

  1. Start by washing your hair and drying it completely.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back.
  3. Start by cutting the back sections. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the top and moving towards the bottom. Repeat this process for all the sections in the back.
  4. Move on to the front sections. Take a small section of hair and hold it at a 45-degree angle. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the top and moving towards the bottom. Repeat this process for all the sections in the front.
  5. Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it as desired.

Remember, it's always better to start with less hair and cut more if necessary. You can also seek the help of a professional stylist if you're unsure about cutting your hair on your own.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've achieved your desired length, it's time to style your hair. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your new haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add some volume to the roots.
  • Use a curling iron to add some waves or curls to your hair.
  • Apply a texturizing spray to add some definition to your layers.
  • Don't overdo it with styling products. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What other hairstyles suit square faces?

Aside from shoulder length hair with long layers, other hairstyles that suit square faces include long bobs, side-swept bangs, and layered pixie cuts.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 8-12 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

Absolutely! Shoulder length hair with long layers can still be styled in a ponytail. You can also experiment with different updos, such as buns and braids.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have your new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray first.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get enough sleep to reduce stress and promote hair health.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Congratulations! You now know how to achieve shoulder length hair with long layers for square face. With these tips and tricks, you can experiment with different hairstyles and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. Remember to take care of your hair, and it will reward you with a beautiful, voluminous look!
