Description

Zero and double zero haircuts are two of the most popular buzz cut styles that men choose. These haircuts are short, low-maintenance and can suit any face shape. However, there is a difference between the two haircuts that you should know before choosing one.

How to

The zero and double zero haircuts are clipper cuts that require no scissors or comb. You can either go to a professional barber or do it yourself at home if you have the right clippers. However, it is always recommended to go to a professional for the best results.

Step by step

Here are the steps to get a zero or double zero haircut:

Step 1:

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or oil from your scalp.

Step 2:

Use a towel to dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3:

Set the clippers to the desired length depending on whether you want a zero or double zero haircut.

Step 4:

Start from the side of your head and work your way up to the top. Use the clippers in an upward motion to create an even cut.

Step 5:

Repeat the same process on the other side of your head and the back.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind before getting a zero or double zero haircut:

  • Ensure that your hair is at least 1/4 inch long before getting a buzz cut.
  • If you have a sensitive scalp, apply a pre-shave oil or cream to reduce irritation.
  • Use a mirror to check whether your haircut is even from all sides.
  • Do not use the clippers on wet hair as it can damage the blades.
  • Maintain your buzz cut by trimming it every 2-3 weeks.

FAQ

1. What is the difference between zero and double zero haircut?

The zero haircut is the shortest clipper cut that leaves the hair 1/16 of an inch long. The double zero haircut is slightly longer than the zero haircut and leaves the hair 1/8 of an inch long.

2. Which haircut should I choose?

It depends on your personal preference and face shape. The zero haircut is suitable for men with a round face shape, while the double zero haircut is better for men with an oval or angular face shape.

3. Can I do a zero or double zero haircut at home?

Yes, you can do it at home if you have the right clippers and experience. However, it is always recommended to go to a professional barber for the best results.

Hair Care

After getting a zero or double zero haircut, it is essential to take care of your scalp and hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Apply a moisturizer or oil to your scalp to keep it hydrated.
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair as it can cause heat damage.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins and minerals for healthy hair growth.

Now that you know the difference between zero and double zero haircut, choose the one that suits you the best and enjoy your new low-maintenance hairstyle.
