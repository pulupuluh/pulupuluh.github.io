How To Get Thick Wavy Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 23, 2023 · 9 min read

Do you envy those guys who have thick, luscious, wavy hair? Do you want to know how to get the same look for yourself? Well, you’re in luck! In this article, we’ll be sharing our top tips and tricks for achieving thick, wavy hair for guys.

How to Get Thick Wavy Hair: The Basics

Before we dive into the specifics, let’s start with the basics. The key to getting thick, wavy hair is to have healthy hair to begin with. This means keeping your hair clean, hydrated, and well-nourished. Here are a few tips to get you started:

Tip #1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly is essential for keeping it clean and healthy. Be sure to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. For guys with thick, wavy hair, we recommend using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated.

Tip #2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Leave-in conditioner is a great way to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. It can also help to tame frizz and make your hair more manageable. Simply apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair after showering and comb it through.

Tip #3: Avoid Over-Styling Your Hair

While it’s tempting to use a lot of product to achieve the perfect look, over-styling your hair can actually damage it in the long run. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools like blow dryers and straighteners, and use a minimal amount of product when styling your hair.

How to Get Thick Wavy Hair: Step-by-Step

Now that you know the basics, let’s get into the specifics of how to get thick, wavy hair for guys. Follow these steps to achieve the look you want:

Step #1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

The first step to achieving thick, wavy hair is to start with clean, dry hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair, and then towel dry it gently. Avoid using a blow dryer, as this can damage your hair and make it frizzy.

Step #2: Apply a Thickening Product

Next, apply a thickening product to your hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or styling cream that is designed to add volume and texture to your hair. Work the product into your hair from root to tip, using your fingers to tousle your hair as you go.

Step #3: Use a Diffuser

To create waves in your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to distribute the heat evenly throughout your hair, creating a more natural-looking wave. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step #4: Scrunch Your Hair

As you dry your hair with the diffuser, use your hands to gently scrunch your hair. This will help to create more defined waves and add volume to your hair.

Step #5: Finish with Texturizing Spray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a texturizing spray. This will help to add even more texture and hold to your hair, giving you that thick, wavy look you’re after.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair

Now that you’ve achieved the perfect look, it’s important to maintain it. Here are a few tips for keeping your thick, wavy hair looking great:

Tip #1: Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

While it’s important to keep your hair clean, over-washing can actually strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Try to limit your hair washing to every other day, or even less if your hair is particularly dry.

Tip #2: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

To keep your hair hydrated and healthy, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. This will help to nourish your hair and prevent damage from heat styling and other environmental factors.

Tip #3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims are essential for keeping your hair healthy and preventing split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few frequently asked questions about getting thick, wavy hair for guys:

Q: Can I get thick, wavy hair if my hair is naturally straight?

A: Yes! With the right styling products and techniques, you can add volume and texture to even the straightest of hair.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from becoming frizzy?

A: To prevent frizz, use a leave-in conditioner and avoid over-styling your hair. You can also use a smoothing serum or oil to help tame flyaways.

Q: How do I style my hair if it’s really short?

A: If your hair is very short, you may not be able to achieve a full, wavy look. Instead, try using a texturizing product to add volume and texture to your hair.

Conclusion

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve the thick, wavy hair you’ve always wanted. Remember to take good care of your hair, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
