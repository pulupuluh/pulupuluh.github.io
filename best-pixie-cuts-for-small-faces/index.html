Best Pixie Cuts For Small Faces

Written by Aprilia Jun 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a small face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. Luckily, there are plenty of pixie cuts that are perfect for small faces. In this article, we will discuss the best pixie cuts for small faces, how to style them, and some tips for maintaining your new look.

Description

Pixie cuts are short hairstyles that are typically cut close to the scalp. They are a popular choice for women with small faces because they can help to accentuate your facial features and give your face a more defined look. Pixie cuts are also low maintenance and easy to style, which makes them perfect for women who are always on the go.

How to

If you are considering a pixie cut, it is important to find a hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair. They will be able to recommend the best style for your face shape and give you tips on how to style your new cut. Here are some general steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a pixie cut, it is important to consider your face shape. If you have a small face, you may want to opt for a shorter cut that is closer to the scalp. This will help to accentuate your facial features and give your face a more defined look.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your pixie cut, you will need to wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type to ensure that your hair is clean and healthy.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a styling product to help hold your hair in place. You may want to use a gel, mousse, or hairspray depending on your hair type and the style you are going for.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair into the desired shape. You may want to use a blow dryer to help shape your hair and give it more volume.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new pixie cut:

- Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy - Use products that are designed for short hair - Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts:

Q: Will a pixie cut work for my face shape?
A: Pixie cuts can work for any face shape, but it is important to choose the right cut for your face shape. Q: How often do I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?
A: It is recommended to get your pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for maintaining the health and beauty of your hair. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type - Avoid using heated styling tools too often - Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated

Conclusion

Pixie cuts are a great option for women with small faces. They are low maintenance, easy to style, and can help to accentuate your facial features. By following the tips in this article, you can find the perfect pixie cut for your face shape and maintain a healthy and beautiful hairstyle.
