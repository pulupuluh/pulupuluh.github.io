Best Hairstyle For Round Face Over 40

Written by Eveline Jul 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our face shape changes, and it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters our features. If you have a round face and are over 40, you might be wondering what the best hairstyle is for you. In this article, we'll discuss the best hairstyles for round faces over 40, as well as provide tips on hair care and maintenance.

Description

A round face is characterized by a wider forehead, fuller cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that creates the illusion of length and adds volume to the crown of your head. The best hairstyles for round faces over 40 are those that flatter your features while also being low-maintenance and easy to style.

How To

The first step in choosing the best hairstyle for round face over 40 is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are roughly the same width, you have a round face.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that flatter your features. Some of the best hairstyles for round faces over 40 include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bob
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Long bob

Step by Step

If you've chosen a hairstyle that requires styling, follow these steps to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the crown of your head to add volume.
  4. If you have bangs, use a flat iron to straighten them.
  5. Use a curling iron to add curls or waves to the ends of your hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling your hair:

  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and flat iron.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

FAQ

What if I don't want to cut my hair?

If you don't want to cut your hair, you can still add volume and length with the right styling products and techniques. Use a volumizing mousse or spray and blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

A high ponytail can elongate your face and make it appear less round. However, avoid a low ponytail, as it can emphasize your round face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for round face over 40, you can choose a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
