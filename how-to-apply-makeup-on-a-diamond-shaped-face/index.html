How To Apply Makeup On A Diamond Shaped Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jun 19, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you may find it challenging to apply makeup that accentuates your best features. But don't worry! With the right techniques, you can create a flawless look that highlights your cheekbones and balances out your jawline. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to apply makeup on a diamond shaped face.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Apply Makeup on a Diamond Shaped Face
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Face

How to Apply Makeup on a Diamond Shaped Face

The key to applying makeup on a diamond-shaped face is to create balance and symmetry. You want to enhance your cheekbones and soften your jawline. Here are some basic steps:

  • Start by applying a primer to even out your skin tone.
  • Use a foundation that matches your skin tone to create a smooth canvas.
  • Apply concealer to cover any blemishes, dark circles, or redness.
  • Use a contouring palette to enhance your cheekbones and create a more defined jawline.
  • Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to add a pop of color.
  • Use an eyeshadow palette to create a natural look that complements your skin tone.
  • Apply mascara to your lashes to create volume and length.
  • Finish off with a lipstick or lip gloss that complements your skin tone.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed guide on how to apply makeup on a diamond-shaped face:

Step 1: Prime Your Face

Start by applying a primer to your face to create an even base. This will help your makeup last longer and prevent it from smudging or creasing. Use your fingers or a brush to apply the primer all over your face.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone and apply it all over your face using a brush or sponge. Make sure to blend it well, especially around your jawline and hairline.

Step 3: Conceal Blemishes and Dark Circles

Use a concealer that matches your skin tone to cover any blemishes, dark circles, or redness. Apply it to the affected areas using a brush or your fingers and blend it well.

Step 4: Contour Your Cheekbones and Jawline

Use a contouring palette to enhance your cheekbones and create a more defined jawline. Apply the contour powder to the hollows of your cheeks and blend it well. Then, use a highlighter to accentuate your cheekbones and brow bone.

Step 5: Apply Blush

Choose a blush that complements your skin tone and apply it to the apples of your cheeks. Smile to locate the apples of your cheeks and apply the blush in a circular motion. Blend it well so that it looks natural.

Step 6: Apply Eyeshadow

Choose an eyeshadow palette that complements your skin tone and apply it to your eyelids. Use a light shade on your lid and a darker shade on your crease to create depth. Blend it well so that there are no harsh lines.

Step 7: Apply Mascara

Apply mascara to your lashes to create volume and length. Start at the base of your lashes and wiggle the wand upwards to the tips. Repeat for a more dramatic effect.

Step 8: Apply Lipstick or Lip Gloss

Finish off your look with a lipstick or lip gloss that complements your skin tone. Choose a shade that works well with your outfit and apply it evenly to your lips.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to apply makeup on a diamond shaped face:

  • Use a highlighter to accentuate your cheekbones and brow bone.
  • Use a lip liner to create a more defined lip shape.
  • Choose a blush that complements your skin tone and apply it to the apples of your cheeks.
  • Use a brow pencil to fill in sparse areas of your eyebrows.
  • Use a setting spray to make your makeup last longer.

FAQ

What is a diamond-shaped face?

A diamond-shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin.

What makeup should I avoid if I have a diamond-shaped face?

Avoid using dark or bold lipstick shades that can make your chin look more pointed. Also, avoid applying too much blush to the apples of your cheeks as it can make your cheekbones look too high.

What are some hairstyles that work well with a diamond-shaped face?

Hairstyles that work well with a diamond-shaped face include long layers, side-swept bangs, and bob haircuts.

Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Face

Along with makeup, your hairstyle can also enhance your diamond-shaped face. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that balances out your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your jawline.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find what suits you best.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to prevent damage.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a flawless makeup look that enhances your diamond-shaped face. Remember to experiment with different shades and styles to find what works best for you!
