Medium length haircuts are perfect for oval faces because they accentuate the natural shape and symmetry of the face. In this article, we'll go through the best medium length haircuts for oval faces, how to style them, and some tips for hair care.

How to Choose the Right Medium Length Haircut for Oval Faces

When it comes to choosing the right medium length haircut for an oval face, you want to focus on styles that don't add too much volume to the sides. Oval faces are naturally balanced and symmetrical, so you don't want to throw off that balance with a haircut that adds too much width to the sides of the face.

Some great medium length haircuts for oval faces include:

  • Shoulder-length blunt cut
  • Long bob with side-swept bangs
  • Layered mid-length cut
  • Asymmetrical bob

Step by Step: How to Style Medium Length Haircuts for Oval Faces

Styling medium length haircuts for oval faces is relatively easy, as the natural shape of the face lends itself well to a variety of styles. Here's a simple step-by-step guide to styling your medium length haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add body to your hair without making it too poofy.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting at the roots to add volume. You can also use a root lifter or volumizing spray for added lift.
  3. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls to your hair. For oval faces, it's best to avoid tight curls or ringlets, as these can add too much volume to the sides of the face.
  4. Finish off your look with a light-hold hairspray, and you're good to go!

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Haircuts for Oval Faces

The key to maintaining medium length haircuts for oval faces is to keep your hair healthy and well-nourished. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look flat.

FAQs About Medium Length Haircuts for Oval Faces

Here are some commonly asked questions about medium length haircuts for oval faces:

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with an oval face?

A: Yes! Oval faces look great with straight hair, as long as you don't add too much volume to the sides.

Q: What kind of bangs should I get with an oval face?

A: Side-swept bangs are a great option for oval faces, as they add a little bit of asymmetry to the face without throwing off the balance.

Hair Care for Medium Length Haircuts for Oval Faces

When it comes to hair care for medium length haircuts, it's important to use products that will help nourish and protect your hair. Here are some great products to try:

  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Root lifter or volumizing spray
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Light-hold hairspray

With the right medium length haircut and a little bit of styling know-how, you can create a beautiful and flattering look for your oval face. Just remember to keep your hair healthy and well-nourished, and you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go!
