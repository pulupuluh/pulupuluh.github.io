Fuller Figure Medium Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins

Written by Frank Apr 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a fuller figure, a fat face, or a double chin, you may be looking for medium hairstyles that flatter your features. The good news is that there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there are hairstyles that can help accentuate your best features while minimizing the appearance of any areas you may be self-conscious about.

Description

Fuller figure medium hairstyles for fat faces and double chins are designed to complement your natural features and enhance your overall look. These hairstyles can be curly or straight, layered or blunt, and can be customized to suit your personal style and preferences. Whether you have thick or thin hair, there are options that can work for you.

How to

To achieve a fuller figure medium hairstyle for a fat face or double chin, it's important to work with a stylist who is experienced in working with different hair types and face shapes. Your stylist can help you choose a style that complements your natural features and enhances your overall look.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a fuller figure medium hairstyle for a fat face or double chin:

  1. Start by determining your face shape.
  2. Look for hairstyles that flatter your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair type and texture.
  4. Choose a length that works for you.
  5. Decide on layers or no layers.
  6. Bring pictures of styles you like to your stylist.
  7. Ask your stylist for their opinion on which style will work best for you.
  8. Get regular trims to maintain your style.
  9. Experiment with styling products to find what works best for you.
  10. Enjoy your new look!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a fuller figure medium hairstyle for a fat face or double chin:

  • Look for styles that add volume to the top of your head to draw attention away from your chin.
  • Avoid styles that are too long or too short, as these can make your face look wider.
  • Layers can help add dimension to your hair and soften your features.
  • Consider highlights or lowlights to add depth to your hair.
  • Play around with different parting styles to see what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fuller figure medium hairstyles for fat faces and double chins:

Q: What are the best hairstyles for a fat face?

A: The best hairstyles for a fat face are those that add volume to the top of the head and create a more angular look. Layered hairstyles can also help soften the face and add dimension.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a double chin?

A: You should avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look wider. You should also avoid hairstyles with blunt bangs, as these can draw attention to your chin.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a double chin?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair straight if you have a double chin. However, you should consider adding volume to the top of your head to draw attention away from your chin.

Hair Care

To keep your fuller figure medium hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Use styling products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

With these tips and some inspiration from the many fuller figure medium hairstyles available, you can find a look that enhances your natural features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
