How To Cut Shoulder Length Curly Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to save some money and cut your shoulder length curly hair at home, you've come to the right place. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a beautiful, professional-looking haircut without ever leaving your house. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut shoulder length curly hair at home, including tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

How to Cut Shoulder Length Curly Hair at Home

Before you get started, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It's also helpful to have a handheld mirror so you can see the back of your head.

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you get a more accurate cut, as wet hair can be harder to work with.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections

Use your comb to divide your hair into sections. Start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom section. Clip the top section up and out of the way. Then, divide the bottom section of your hair into two or three smaller sections, depending on how thick your hair is.

Step 3: Start cutting

Take one of the smaller sections of hair and comb it straight down. Use your hair cutting scissors to trim the ends, starting with just a little bit at a time. Remember, it's always better to cut less than you think you need to, as you can always go back and trim more later.

Repeat this process with the other sections of your hair, working your way up to the top section.

Step 4: Trim the top section

Once you've finished cutting the bottom sections of your hair, unclip the top section and comb it straight down. Use your scissors to trim the ends, being careful not to cut too much.

Step 5: Check for any uneven spots

After you've finished cutting your hair, check for any uneven spots. If you notice any areas that need to be trimmed, go back and do so carefully.

Tips for Cutting Shoulder Length Curly Hair at Home

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a beautiful haircut:

  1. Remember to cut less than you think you need to. You can always go back and trim more later.
  2. Use your comb to keep your hair straight and even as you cut.
  3. Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member, especially when it comes to cutting the back of your head.
  4. Take breaks as needed. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so don't hesitate to take a break if you need one.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting shoulder length curly hair at home:

Can I cut my own hair if it's very curly?

Yes, you can cut your own hair even if it's very curly. Just be sure to comb your hair straight down as you cut, and cut less than you think you need to.

How often should I cut my shoulder length curly hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain healthy, well-groomed locks.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Now that you know how to cut your shoulder length curly hair at home, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  2. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and prevent breakage.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage.
  4. Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can dry out and damage your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful, professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Happy cutting!
