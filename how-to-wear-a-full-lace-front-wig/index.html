How To Wear A Full Lace Front Wig A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle without committing to a major cut or color change, a full lace front wig can be a great option. They offer versatility and convenience, and can be styled just like your natural hair. But if you're new to wigs, the process of putting one on can seem daunting. That's why we've put together this step-by-step guide to help you wear a full lace front wig with confidence.

How to Wear a Full Lace Front Wig

A full lace front wig is a type of wig that has a sheer lace panel at the front of the wig that extends from ear to ear. The hair is hand-tied to the lace, giving the appearance of a natural hairline. Here's how to wear a full lace front wig:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step to wearing a full lace front wig is to prepare your hair. If you have long hair, you'll need to braid it close to your scalp. Short hair can be slicked back with a wig cap. Make sure your hair is as flat as possible, as this will help the wig sit more securely on your head.

Step 2: Put on the Wig Cap

Next, put on a wig cap to help keep your hair in place and provide a smooth surface for the wig to sit on. Make sure the wig cap is snug but not too tight, as this can cause discomfort later on.

Step 3: Apply Adhesive

Apply a small amount of wig adhesive around the perimeter of your hairline. Be careful not to get any adhesive on your hair or scalp, as this can be difficult to remove.

Step 4: Put on the Wig

Carefully place the wig on your head, starting at the front and working your way back. Adjust the wig so that it sits comfortably on your head and the hairline looks natural.

Step 5: Secure the Wig

Once the wig is in place, use a comb or your fingers to gently press the wig into the adhesive. You can also use bobby pins to secure the wig in place.

Step 6: Style the Wig

Finally, style the wig as desired. Full lace front wigs can be styled just like your natural hair, with heat tools and hair products.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a detailed step-by-step guide to wearing a full lace front wig:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by preparing your hair. If you have long hair, you'll need to braid it close to your scalp to create a flat surface. Short hair can be slicked back with a wig cap. Make sure your hair is as flat as possible, as this will help the wig sit more securely on your head.

Step 2: Put on a Wig Cap

Next, put on a wig cap. This will help keep your hair in place and provide a smooth surface for the wig to sit on. Make sure the wig cap is snug but not too tight, as this can cause discomfort later on.

Step 3: Cut the Lace

Cut the lace along the front of the wig. Be careful not to cut the hair, as this can ruin the wig. Leave a small amount of lace in front of the hairline to help create a natural look.

Step 4: Apply Adhesive

Apply a small amount of wig adhesive around the perimeter of your hairline. Be careful not to get any adhesive on your hair or scalp, as this can be difficult to remove.

Step 5: Put on the Wig

Place the wig on your head, starting at the front and working your way back. Adjust the wig so that it sits comfortably on your head and the hairline looks natural. Use a comb or your fingers to press the wig into the adhesive.

Step 6: Style the Wig

Finally, style the wig as desired. Full lace front wigs can be styled just like your natural hair, with heat tools and hair products.

Tips for Wearing a Full Lace Front Wig

Here are some tips to help you wear a full lace front wig with confidence:

  • Choose a wig that matches your natural hair color and texture for a more natural look.
  • Trim the lace carefully to avoid cutting the hair.
  • Use a wig cap to keep your hair in place and provide a smooth surface for the wig to sit on.
  • Apply adhesive sparingly to avoid a sticky mess.
  • Secure the wig in place with bobby pins if necessary.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a full lace front wig:

How long can I wear a full lace front wig?

You can wear a full lace front wig for up to six weeks with proper care and maintenance.

Can I swim with a full lace front wig?

It's not recommended to swim with a full lace front wig, as chlorine and saltwater can damage the hair and adhesive.

How do I clean a full lace front wig?

Clean your wig every two to three weeks with a wig shampoo and conditioner. Gently comb out any tangles before washing, and let the wig air dry on a wig stand.

Hair Care Tips for Full Lace Front Wigs

Here are some hair care tips to keep your full lace front wig looking great:

  • Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig.
  • Avoid using heat tools on wet hair.
  • Protect your wig from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down the hair.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear a full lace front wig with confidence and style. Experiment with different looks and enjoy the versatility and convenience that a wig can offer.
