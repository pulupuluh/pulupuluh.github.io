How To Make Super Straight Hair Curly In 2023

If you have naturally straight hair, you may have always wondered how to make it curly. Luckily, there are several methods you can use to create beautiful curls that will last all day. In this article, we will show you how to make super straight hair curly using a few easy steps.

How to Make Super Straight Hair Curly

There are several methods you can use to make super straight hair curly. Here are a few options:

1. Use a Curling Iron

One of the easiest ways to make super straight hair curly is to use a curling iron. This tool comes in various sizes, so you can choose the one that will give you the type of curl you want.

To use a curling iron, start by dividing your hair into sections. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding it in place for a few seconds. Release the hair and repeat until all of your hair is curled.

2. Use Hot Rollers

Another option is to use hot rollers. These are heated rollers that you can use to create curls in your hair. To use hot rollers, start by dividing your hair into sections. Take a roller and wrap a small section of hair around it, securing it in place. Repeat until all of your hair is curled.

3. Use Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are another tool you can use to create curls in your super straight hair. These are flexible rods that you can wrap your hair around to create curls. To use flexi rods, start by dividing your hair into sections. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the rod, starting at the end and rolling it up towards your scalp. Secure the rod in place and repeat until all of your hair is curled.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to making super straight hair curly:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Choose your preferred curling method (curling iron, hot rollers, or flexi rods).
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the tool. Hold it in place for a few seconds.
  5. Release the hair and repeat until all of your hair is curled.
  6. Once all of your hair is curled, use a hairspray to set the curls in place.

Tips for Making Super Straight Hair Curly

Here are a few tips to help you make super straight hair curly:

  • Use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Choose the right size tool for the type of curl you want.
  • Don't curl your hair when it's wet - this can cause damage and won't hold the curl as well.
  • Use hairspray to set the curls in place.
  • Don't forget to brush out your curls for a more natural look.

FAQ

Can I make super straight hair curly without heat?

Yes, there are several ways to make super straight hair curly without heat. You can use hair rollers, braid your hair, or use a hair tie to create curls. However, these methods may not last as long as using heat.

How long will my curls last?

This will depend on your hair type and the method you use to create curls. Generally, curls created with heat will last longer than those created without heat. You can use hairspray to help set the curls in place and make them last longer.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

If you want to keep your curls looking their best, here are a few hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your curls from becoming dry and frizzy.
  • Avoid brushing your curls when they are dry - this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your curls, as this can damage them.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain your curls.

By following these tips, you can keep your curls looking their best and prevent damage to your hair.

Conclusion

There are several ways to make super straight hair curly, whether you choose to use a curling iron, hot rollers, or flexi rods. By following these methods and tips, you can create beautiful curls that will last all day. Remember to take care of your hair by using a heat protectant, choosing the right size tool, and following proper hair care techniques.
