Best Drugstore Shampoo And Conditioner For Aging Hair

If you're dealing with aging hair, you know how frustrating it can be to find the right products. The good news is that there are plenty of drugstore shampoos and conditioners that can help keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best drugstore shampoo and conditioner options for aging hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Description

As you age, your hair goes through a lot of changes. It can become thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. That's why it's important to use products that are specifically designed for aging hair. Drugstore shampoos and conditioners can provide the same benefits as high-end products, without breaking the bank. Here are some of the best options:

How to Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

When choosing a shampoo and conditioner for aging hair, look for products that are designed to add moisture and volume. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, as these can strip the hair of its natural oils. Look for ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken the hair.

Top Drugstore Shampoos for Aging Hair

1. Pantene Silver Expressions Shampoo

This shampoo is specifically designed for gray and silver hair. It helps to remove yellow tones and enhance the natural shine of your hair.

2. OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo

This shampoo is packed with biotin and collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken the hair. It's also free from sulfates, parabens, and other harsh chemicals.

3. L'Oreal Paris EverCreme Sulfate-Free Moisture System

This sulfate-free shampoo is designed to add moisture to dry, brittle hair. It's made with natural ingredients like apricot oil and botanicals.

Top Drugstore Conditioners for Aging Hair

1. Herbal Essences Bio:Renew Birch Bark Extract Conditioner

This conditioner is made with natural ingredients like birch bark extract and aloe vera. It can help to strengthen and moisturize the hair.

2. Dove Nutritive Solutions Daily Moisture Conditioner

This conditioner is designed to add moisture to dry, damaged hair. It's made with a blend of natural oils and proteins, including keratin.

3. Garnier Fructis Curl Nourish Conditioner

If you have curly hair, this conditioner is a great choice. It's made with coconut oil, which can help to nourish and define curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Shampoo and Conditioner

1. Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.

2. Apply a small amount of shampoo to your scalp and massage it in with your fingertips. Don't use too much shampoo, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils.

3. Rinse the shampoo out of your hair with warm water.

4. Apply a small amount of conditioner to the ends of your hair. Avoid applying conditioner to your scalp, as this can weigh down your hair.

5. Leave the conditioner in your hair for a few minutes, then rinse it out with warm water.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Healthy

1. Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as these can damage your hair.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.

3. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

4. Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, which can help to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

FAQ

What causes hair to age?

As you age, your hair follicles produce less melanin, which can cause your hair to gray. Your hair can also become thinner and more brittle as you get older, due to hormonal changes and other factors.

Can drugstore shampoos and conditioners be as effective as high-end products?

Yes! Many drugstore shampoos and conditioners are just as effective as high-end products, and they're often more affordable.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care Routine for Aging Hair

If you're dealing with aging hair, it's important to take good care of it. Here's a simple hair care routine that you can follow:

1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo, like the L'Oreal Paris EverCreme Sulfate-Free Moisture System.

2. Condition your hair with a moisturizing conditioner, like the Dove Nutritive Solutions Daily Moisture Conditioner.

3. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add extra moisture and shine to your hair.

4. Avoid using hot tools on your hair, and let your hair air dry whenever possible.

5. Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right products and a little bit of care, you can keep your aging hair looking healthy and vibrant. Try out some of these drugstore shampoo and conditioner options, and see what works best for you!
