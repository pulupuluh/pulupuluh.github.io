What Hair Color Suits Chubby Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hair color that flatters your chubby face? Look no further as we have gathered all the information you need to know to achieve the perfect hair color that suits you. In this article, we will discuss the best hair colors for chubby faces, how to choose the right hair color, and hair care tips to maintain your new hair color.

Description

Having a chubby face is not a bad thing, but it can be a bit challenging to find the right hair color that complements your face shape. Choosing the right hair color can enhance your features and make your face appear slimmer. But with so many hair colors to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to go for.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

When it comes to choosing the right hair color, there are several factors you need to consider. Firstly, determine your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, go for hair colors with warm tones such as honey blonde, caramel, or copper. If you have cool undertones, opt for hair colors with cool tones such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or brunette with cool undertones.

Consider your eye color as well. If you have light eyes, go for hair colors that compliment your eye color. For instance, if you have blue eyes, try ash blonde or platinum blonde. If you have brown eyes, opt for warm hair colors such as golden brown or copper.

Another factor to consider is your natural hair color. If you want to go for a drastic change, you might need to consider the maintenance and upkeep of your new hair color. For instance, if you have dark hair and want to go blonde, you might need to bleach your hair several times to achieve the right shade, which can be damaging to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Determine your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.

Step 2: Choose a hair color that compliments your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.

Step 3: Consider the maintenance and upkeep of your new hair color.

Step 4: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the right shade and technique to achieve your desired hair color.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color that suits your chubby face:

  • Consider the shape of your face when choosing a hair color. Some hair colors can enhance your features and make your face appear slimmer.
  • Experiment with temporary hair colors before committing to a permanent hair color.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dye to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use hair care products that are specifically designed for color-treated hair to maintain the vibrancy of your hair color.

FAQ

What hair colors should I avoid if I have a chubby face?

Avoid hair colors that are too dark or too light, as they can make your face appear wider. Stay away from bold hair colors such as bright red or neon colors as they can draw attention to your face.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it is possible to dye your hair at home, it is recommended to seek the help of a professional hairstylist, especially if you want to achieve a drastic change in hair color. A hairstylist can recommend the right shade and technique to achieve your desired hair color.

How often should I dye my hair?

The frequency of dyeing your hair depends on several factors such as the color of your hair, the type of dye used, and the level of maintenance required. On average, hair dye should be reapplied every 4 to 6 weeks to maintain the vibrancy of the color.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain the vibrancy of your hair color. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid hot showers and use cool or lukewarm water instead.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect hair color that flatters your chubby face. Remember to choose a hair color that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
