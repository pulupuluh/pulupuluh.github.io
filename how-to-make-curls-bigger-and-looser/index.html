How To Make Curls Bigger And Looser

Written by Daniel May 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you know that sometimes your curls can look a little too tight and small. If you want to give your curls a more voluminous and relaxed look, here are some tips on how to make curls bigger and looser.

Description

Having big and loose curls can give you a more effortless and natural look. However, not everyone is blessed with this type of hair. If you have tight curls or ringlets, achieving big and loose curls can seem like a daunting task. But don't worry, with some simple techniques and products, you can achieve the look you want.

How to Make Curls Bigger and Looser: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and conditioned. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's specifically formulated for curly hair. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair while it's still damp. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place. You can choose from a variety of products such as curl creams, gels, or mousses. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a great tool to use when you're trying to achieve big and loose curls. Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and use it to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and give them more volume.

Step 4: Twist Your Hair

Twist small sections of your hair around your finger to create bigger and looser curls. You can also use a curling iron to create larger curls. Make sure to hold the curling iron vertically and wrap your hair around it. This will help to create a more natural and relaxed look.

Tips

  • Don't overdo it with product. Too much product can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain your curls overnight.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Brushing your hair can cause breakage and frizz.

FAQ

Can I use a straightener to make my curls bigger and looser?

Yes, you can use a straightener to create bigger and looser curls. Simply wrap your hair around the straightener and twist it to create a more natural look.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

What's the best way to maintain my curls throughout the day?

Use a hairspray or hair oil to help maintain your curls throughout the day. You can also carry a small bottle of curl enhancing product with you to touch up your curls when needed.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your curly hair to prevent damage and maintain its health. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. And always use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve big and loose curls that will give you a more natural and relaxed look. Remember, it's all about finding the right products and tools that work for your hair type. Experiment with different techniques and have fun with it!
