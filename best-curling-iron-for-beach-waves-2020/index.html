Best Curling Iron For Beach Waves 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 17, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking to achieve the perfect beach waves for that effortless, carefree look? If so, you need the right curling iron for the job. But with so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for your needs. In this guide, we’ll break down the top curling irons for achieving those beachy waves, along with tips for how to use them and maintain your gorgeous locks.

Description

Beach waves have been a popular hair trend for years, and they’re not going anywhere anytime soon. This relaxed, effortless style is perfect for any occasion, whether you’re headed to the beach or a fancy event. But achieving those perfect waves can be tricky, which is why it’s important to have the right tools in your arsenal.

How to

To achieve beach waves, you’ll need a curling iron that’s designed for the job. Look for a curling iron with a tapered barrel, which will create a more natural-looking wave. You’ll also want to choose a barrel size that matches the length of your hair – smaller barrels for shorter hair, and larger barrels for longer hair.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

2. Divide your hair into sections, starting at the bottom.

3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds.

4. Release the curl and repeat throughout your hair, alternating the direction of the curls for a more natural look.

5. Finish with a texturizing spray to give your waves that beachy, tousled look.

Tips

Here are a few tips for achieving the perfect beach waves:

  • Don’t use too much product – it can weigh down your hair and make it harder to curl.
  • Alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural look.
  • Don’t hold the curling iron on your hair for too long – this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a texturizing spray to give your waves more volume and texture.
  • Experiment with different barrel sizes and shapes to find the perfect curling iron for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about curling irons for beach waves:

What size barrel is best for beach waves?

A tapered barrel between 1-1.5 inches is ideal for creating natural-looking beach waves.

Can I achieve beach waves with a straightener?

Yes, you can achieve beach waves with a straightener, but it can be trickier than using a curling iron. Look for tutorials online to learn how to do it properly.

How do I maintain my beachy waves?

Use a texturizing spray to maintain your waves throughout the day, and avoid brushing your hair too much – this can flatten your waves.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it’s important to use high-quality hair care products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed for your hair type, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

With the right curling iron and a few tips and tricks, you can achieve gorgeous beach waves that will have everyone asking for your hair secrets. So go ahead and embrace that effortless, carefree style – your hair will thank you for it.
