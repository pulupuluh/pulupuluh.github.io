How To Style Unruly Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Aprilia Jun 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Unruly hair can be a real challenge to style. It’s often difficult to manage and can be frustrating to deal with. But fear not! With the right tools, techniques, and hair care, you can transform your unruly hair into a gorgeous style that you’ll love. In this article, we’ll share our top tips and tricks for styling unruly hair, along with some essential hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Unruly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Style Unruly Hair

Styling unruly hair starts with understanding your hair type and texture. It’s important to use the right tools and products that work best for your hair. Here are some general tips to get started:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or detangler to help smooth your hair.
  • Use heat protectant spray before using styling tools.
  • Choose a hairstyle that works with your hair type and texture.

Now that you have a general idea, let’s dive into the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your unruly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or detangler to your damp hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the tips.
  5. Section your hair and use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Make sure to use heat protectant spray before using the flat iron.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your unruly hair:

  • Use a hair serum or oil to tame frizz and add shine.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls or waves.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling unruly hair:

Q: What can I do to tame frizz?

A: Use a hair serum or oil to tame frizz. You can also try using a leave-in conditioner or detangler to help smooth your hair.

Q: Can I use a straightening brush on my unruly hair?

A: Yes, you can use a straightening brush on your unruly hair. Make sure to use heat protectant spray before using the brush.

Q: How often should I wash my unruly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go a few days without washing. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips to keep your unruly hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or detangler to help smooth your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can transform your unruly hair into a gorgeous style that you’ll love. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right tools and products that work best for you. With a little patience and practice, you’ll be a pro at styling your unruly hair in no time!
