Good Short Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by Christine Jul 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect haircut for your round face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing some of the best short haircuts for round faces. Whether you prefer a pixie cut or a bob, we have got you covered.

Description

Round faces are characterized by their soft, curved edges and lack of prominent angles. The goal of a good haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and to add angles to the face. A short haircut is perfect for achieving this look, as it draws attention away from the roundness of the face and towards the angles of the haircut.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it is important to consider the length and shape of the cut. A short bob that falls just below the chin is a great option, as it adds length to the face without overwhelming it. A pixie cut with long, side-swept bangs is also a good choice, as it adds angles to the face and draws attention away from the roundness.

Step by Step

1. Choose a haircut that adds length and angles to the face.

2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and shape for your face.

3. Consider adding layers to your haircut to create more movement and texture.

4. Style your haircut with a volumizing mousse or spray to add even more angles to the face.

Tips

When styling your short haircut, consider using a round brush to add volume to the top of your head. This will create the illusion of height and length, which is perfect for a round face shape. Additionally, consider using a texturizing spray or pomade to add dimension and movement to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear bangs with a short haircut for a round face?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs are a great option for a round face shape. They add angles to the face and draw attention away from the roundness.

Q: Are layers important for a short haircut for a round face?

A: Yes, layers can add movement and texture to a short haircut, which is important for creating the illusion of length and angles.

Hair Care

When caring for your short haircut, consider using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Additionally, consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add shine and prevent damage.

In conclusion, a short haircut is a great option for a round face shape. By choosing a cut that adds length and angles to the face, you can create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best cut for your face shape, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styling products to achieve the perfect look.
