How To Cut A Pixie Bob Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle without breaking the bank, cutting a pixie bob yourself can be a great option. Not only is it easy to maintain, but it's also trendy and stylish. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut a pixie bob yourself, along with some tips and hair care advice.

Description

A pixie bob is a short and layered hairstyle that's longer than a traditional pixie cut but shorter than a bob. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice among women of all ages. Cutting a pixie bob yourself can seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, it's totally doable.

How to Cut a Pixie Bob Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, clips to section your hair, and a mirror.

Step 2: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting. Wet hair can be misleading, and you may end up cutting off more than you intended.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use clips to section your hair. Start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom section. Clip the top section of your hair out of the way.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Begin cutting the back of your hair first. Start by cutting a small section of hair and gradually work your way up. Use your fingers as a guide to determine the length you want to cut, and cut in a straight line.

Step 5: Cut the Sides

Once you've finished cutting the back, move on to the sides. Take small sections of hair and hold them between your fingers, cutting at an angle to create layers. Repeat this process until you've cut all the hair on the sides.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Finally, unclip the top section of your hair and cut it to your desired length. You can add texture by point-cutting the ends or using thinning shears.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a pixie bob yourself:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting off too much at once
  • Use your fingers and a comb as a guide
  • Take breaks if you need to
  • Don't cut too much off at once - you can always go back and cut more later

FAQ

Is it easy to cut a pixie bob yourself?

Cutting a pixie bob yourself can be challenging, but it's doable with the right tools and technique. Take your time and start with small sections of hair.

How long does a pixie bob last?

A pixie bob can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks depending on how fast your hair grows.

Can I color my hair after cutting a pixie bob?

Yes, you can color your hair after cutting a pixie bob. However, it's best to wait a few days after cutting to give your hair a chance to settle.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type, and avoid using heat tools too often. To keep your pixie bob looking fresh, visit a salon for regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

Conclusion

Cutting a pixie bob yourself can be a fun and affordable way to switch up your hairstyle. By following our step-by-step guide and taking the proper precautions, you can achieve a trendy and stylish haircut from the comfort of your own home.
