Short Hair For Long Face Over 60 Tips And Styles

Written by Robby May 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're over 60 with a long face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, short haircuts can be a great option for this face shape, as they can help balance out the proportions of your face and make you look more youthful. In this article, we'll share tips and styling ideas for short haircuts for long faces over 60.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Long Face

Before getting a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Long faces tend to have a high forehead, long chin, and narrow width. To balance out the proportions of your face, consider a short haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face and crown.

If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to consider a layered bob or pixie cut that adds texture and movement. For thick hair, a short blunt cut or a graduated bob can help reduce bulk and create a more flattering shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Long Face Over 60

Styling short haircuts for long faces over 60 doesn't have to be complicated. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a chic and effortless look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, using products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, then apply a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. If you have thin or fine hair, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots to add lift and body.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls to the ends of your hair.
  6. Finish the look with a lightweight hairspray or serum to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Long Face Over 60

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and stylish:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQs About Short Hair for Long Face Over 60

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a short haircut for a long face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a short haircut for a long face. However, make sure they are long and side-swept to balance out the length of your face.

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: No, a short haircut can actually make you look more youthful and stylish. Just make sure to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

Q: What hair color is best for short haircuts for long faces over 60?

A: Any hair color can work with a short haircut, but natural-looking shades like blonde, brown, and gray are popular choices for mature women.

Conclusion

Short haircuts can be a great option for long faces over 60, as they can help balance out the proportions of your face and make you look more youthful. By choosing the right haircut and styling products, you can achieve a chic and effortless look that fits your lifestyle and personality.
