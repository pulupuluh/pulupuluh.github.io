How To Make Straight Hair Curly Permanently Black Girl

Written by Aprilia Apr 20, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Do you dream of having bouncy, curly hair, but struggle with your naturally straight locks? If you're a black girl with relaxed hair, you might think that permanent curls are out of reach. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the curly hair of your dreams. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to make straight hair curly permanently black girl.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the how-to, let's discuss what permanent curls actually are. Permanent curls, also known as a perm, are a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair. The treatment involves applying a solution to your hair to break down the bonds that give it its shape, then reforming those bonds to create a new, curly shape. The result is hair that is permanently curly, and will stay that way until it grows out or is cut.

How to

If you're a black girl with relaxed hair, you'll need to take some extra steps to make your hair curly permanently. Here's what you'll need to do:

1. Choose the Right Perm Solution

First and foremost, you'll need to choose the right perm solution for your hair. Look for a solution specifically designed for relaxed hair, as these solutions are formulated to be gentler on your hair than regular perms. You'll also want to choose the right strength of solution, based on your hair type and the level of curl you want to achieve.

2. Prepare Your Hair

Before applying the perm solution, you'll need to prepare your hair. This involves washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup, then drying it thoroughly. You should also apply a protective cream around your hairline to prevent the perm solution from touching your skin.

3. Apply the Perm Solution

Next, it's time to apply the perm solution. Follow the instructions that come with your perm kit, and be sure to wear gloves to protect your hands. Apply the solution to your hair in sections, making sure that each section is saturated with the solution. Be careful not to apply the solution to your scalp, as this can cause irritation.

4. Process the Perm

Once the perm solution is applied, you'll need to let it process for a specific amount of time. This time will vary depending on the strength of the solution and your hair type, so be sure to follow the instructions carefully. After the processing time is up, you'll rinse your hair thoroughly to remove the solution.

5. Neutralize Your Hair

After rinsing out the perm solution, you'll need to neutralize your hair. This involves applying a neutralizing solution to your hair to stop the perm process and restore your hair's natural pH balance. Follow the instructions provided with your perm kit, and be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly after neutralizing.

6. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is neutralized, you can style it as desired. Keep in mind that your hair will be fragile after the perm process, so be gentle and avoid using heat styling tools for at least a few days. You may also want to use a curl-enhancing product to help define and hold your new curls.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to making straight hair curly permanently:

  1. Choose the right perm solution.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing and drying it.
  3. Apply a protective cream around your hairline.
  4. Apply the perm solution to your hair in sections.
  5. Let the perm solution process for the recommended time.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly.
  7. Apply a neutralizing solution to your hair.
  8. Rinse your hair thoroughly.
  9. Style your hair as desired.
  10. Enjoy your new curly hair!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when making your straight hair curly permanently:

  • Choose a perm solution that is specifically designed for relaxed hair.
  • Be sure to follow the instructions carefully when applying the perm solution.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools for at least a few days after the perm process.
  • Use a curl-enhancing product to help define and hold your new curls.
  • Be gentle with your hair, as it will be fragile after the perm process.

FAQ

Can I make my hair curly permanently without a perm?

No, if you want to permanently change the texture of your hair from straight to curly, you will need to use a perm solution.

How long will my new curls last?

Your new curls will last until your hair grows out or is cut.

Will a perm damage my hair?

A perm can be damaging to your hair, especially if it is not done correctly or if you have previously damaged hair. However, if you follow the instructions carefully and take good care of your hair after the perm process, the damage should be minimal.

Hair Care

After making your straight hair curly permanently, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your new curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right techniques and products, making your straight hair curly permanently is possible, even if you're a black girl with relaxed hair. Follow the steps outlined in this article, and take good care of your hair after the perm process, and you'll be rocking your new curls in no time!
