How To Shape Long Hair For Guys

Written by Oliver Aug 11, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you a guy with long hair looking for ways to style and shape your locks? Look no further! In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of shaping long hair for guys, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Shape Long Hair for Guys

Shaping long hair for guys can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and tools, it can be done easily. Here are some steps to help you get started:

1. Determine the shape

The first step in shaping long hair is to determine the shape you want. Do you want a straight cut or something more layered? Do you want a fringe or bangs? Once you have an idea of what you want, you can move on to the next step.

2. Gather your tools

You’ll need a few tools to shape your long hair, including a comb, scissors, and hair clips. Make sure your scissors are sharp and suitable for cutting hair.

3. Start cutting

Begin by sectioning your hair into manageable parts. Use hair clips to keep the sections in place. Start cutting from the bottom section and work your way up. Take small sections of hair and cut them to the desired length. Be sure to cut slowly and carefully.

4. Blend the layers

If you’re going for a layered look, you’ll need to blend the layers together. Use your scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, creating a soft, layered effect. Blend the layers by cutting into them at different lengths.

5. Finish with styling

Once you’ve shaped your hair, it’s time to style it. Use a hair product that suits your hair type and style. You can use a hair straightener or curling iron to create a more polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to shape long hair for guys:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair, making it easier to work with.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too hard. This can cause damage to your hair and create frizz.

Step 3: Comb your hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will make it easier to section and cut your hair.

Step 4: Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable parts using hair clips. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward.

Step 5: Cut your hair

Using sharp scissors, start cutting your hair from the bottom section and work your way up. Take small sections of hair and cut them to the desired length. Be sure to cut slowly and carefully.

Step 6: Blend the layers

If you’re going for a layered look, use your scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, creating a soft, layered effect. Blend the layers by cutting into them at different lengths.

Step 7: Style your hair

Finish by styling your hair with a hair product that suits your hair type and style. You can use a hair straightener or curling iron to create a more polished look.

Tips for Shaping Long Hair for Guys

Here are some tips to help you shape your long hair:

  • Invest in a good quality pair of scissors that are suitable for cutting hair.
  • Cut your hair slowly and carefully, taking small sections at a time.
  • Use hair clips to keep your hair in place while you’re cutting.
  • Blending the layers is key to achieving a natural, soft look.
  • Choose a hair product that suits your hair type and style.

FAQs

Can guys have long hair?

Yes, guys can have long hair! Long hair on guys is a popular and stylish look.

How often should I cut my long hair?

It’s recommended to cut your long hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and in good condition.

What is the best hair product for long hair?

The best hair product for long hair depends on your hair type and style. Generally, a lightweight, non-greasy product is best for long hair.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for caring for your long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and in good condition.

Shaping long hair for guys can be a fun and rewarding process. By following these steps, tips, and hair care advice, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect shape and style for your long locks. Remember to take it slow, work in small sections, and use the right tools for the job. Good luck!
