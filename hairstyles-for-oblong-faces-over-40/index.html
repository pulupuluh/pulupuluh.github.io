Hairstyles For Oblong Faces Over 40 Tips Ideas And Hair Care

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle to complement your oblong face? If you're over 40, you might be wondering what styles will work best for you. In this article, we'll give you tips and ideas for hairstyles that will flatter your face shape and make you look and feel confident. We'll also provide some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

What is an Oblong Face?

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a straight forehead and a pointy chin. It's also known as a rectangular face, and it's one of the most common face shapes.

If you have an oblong face, you should avoid hairstyles that make your face look even longer. Instead, you should choose styles that add width and volume to the sides of your face.

Hairstyles for Oblong Faces Over 40

There are many hairstyles that can flatter an oblong face, but some are better suited for women over 40. Here are some of our favorite styles:

1. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for oblong faces. It adds volume to the sides of your face and draws attention away from your forehead and chin. You can style it straight or add some waves for a more textured look.

2. Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut is a great option for women who want a short hairstyle. It adds volume to the top of your head and creates a more rounded shape. You can also add some layers around your face to soften your features.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to draw attention to your eyes and add some width to your face. They work well with medium-length or long hair and can be styled straight or wavy.

4. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option if you have long hair and want to add some volume and movement. They help to break up the length of your face and create a more balanced look. You can style them straight or add some curls for a more textured look.

How to Style Your Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that flatters your oblong face, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and create a more rounded shape.
  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls or waves to your hair.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a sleek look.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your oblong face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Choose styles that add volume to the sides of your face to create a more balanced look.
  • Avoid center parts, as they can make your face look longer.
  • Consider adding layers or bangs to soften your features.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for an oblong face?

The best hairstyle for an oblong face is one that adds volume to the sides of your face and creates a more balanced look. Shoulder-length bobs, layered pixie cuts, side-swept bangs, and long layers are all great options.

Should I avoid center parts if I have an oblong face?

Yes, you should avoid center parts if you have an oblong face. They can make your face look longer and more narrow. Instead, opt for a side part or no part at all.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and ideas, you're sure to find a hairstyle that flatters your oblong face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
