How To Tell If A New Hair Colour Will Suit You

Written by Eveline Sep 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hair colour but unsure if it will suit you? It can be a daunting task to change your hair colour as it can completely transform your appearance. However, with the right guidance, you can confidently choose a new hair colour that will enhance your features and complement your skin tone. In this article, we will guide you on how to tell if a new hair colour will suit you.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in determining if a new hair colour will suit you is to consider your skin tone. Your skin tone plays a vital role in selecting a hair colour that will complement your features. If you have warm undertones, you should consider hair colours with warm undertones such as chocolate brown or honey blonde. If you have cool undertones, you should consider hair colours with cool undertones such as platinum blonde or ash brown.

The next step is to consider your eye colour. Your eye colour can also play a role in selecting a hair colour that will suit you. If you have blue or green eyes, you should consider hair colours with warm undertones such as golden blonde or copper. If you have brown eyes, you can experiment with hair colours with warm or cool undertones.

You should also consider your natural hair colour. If you have dark hair, it may be more challenging to achieve a lighter hair colour without damaging your hair. If you have light hair, you may have more options when it comes to changing your hair colour. It is important to seek professional advice when considering dramatic hair colour changes.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to tell if a new hair colour will suit you:

  1. Determine your skin tone
  2. Consider your eye colour
  3. Take into account your natural hair colour
  4. Research different hair colours
  5. Consult with a professional hairstylist

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when selecting a new hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance your new hair colour will require
  • Use online hair colour simulators to get an idea of how you will look with a new hair colour
  • Start with subtle hair colour changes before going for a dramatic change
  • Consider the season - lighter hair colours are typically favoured in the summer, while darker hair colours are more popular in the winter

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about selecting a new hair colour:

Will a new hair colour damage my hair?

It is possible that a new hair colour can damage your hair, especially if you are going for a dramatic change. It is important to seek professional advice and use high-quality hair care products to maintain the health of your hair.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it is possible to dye your hair at home, it is recommended to seek professional advice. Professional hairstylists have the knowledge and experience to achieve your desired hair colour while maintaining the health of your hair.

Hair Care

After selecting a new hair colour, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Use high-quality hair care products that are specifically designed for coloured hair. Avoid using hot styling tools frequently and protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.

Now that you know how to tell if a new hair colour will suit you, it's time to experiment and find your perfect hair colour. Remember to take into account your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, and seek professional advice to achieve your desired look.
