Easy Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Frizzy Hair

Written by Eveline Apr 03, 2023 · 8 min read

Having thick, wavy, and frizzy hair can be a challenge when it comes to styling. It can be difficult to tame those unruly curls and keep them looking great all day long. Luckily, there are many easy hairstyles that you can try to make your hair look fabulous without spending too much time or effort. In this article, we’ll show you some of the best easy hairstyles for thick wavy frizzy hair that you can try today.

Description

Thick, wavy, and frizzy hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right hairstyles and techniques, you can make it look great. This article will show you some of the best easy hairstyles for thick wavy frizzy hair that you can try at home. We’ll also include tips on how to care for your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.

How To

If you have thick wavy frizzy hair, the first thing you need to do is find the right products that work for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed for curly hair, as well as styling products that can help tame frizz and define curls. Once you have the right products, you can try these easy hairstyles:

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for thick wavy frizzy hair. To create this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around itself to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place and pull out a few strands to create a messy, effortless look.

2. Half Up, Half Down

The half-up, half-down hairstyle is another easy and stylish option for thick wavy frizzy hair. Simply gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail or bun, leaving the rest of your hair down. This style is perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face but still show off your curls.

3. Braids

Braids are a great option for thick wavy frizzy hair because they can help to tame frizz and keep your curls looking defined. Try a simple braid down the back or a more intricate fishtail braid for a stylish and trendy look.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating these easy hairstyles:

1. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun in place with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands to create a messy look.

2. Half Up, Half Down

  1. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail or bun.
  2. Leave the rest of your hair down.

3. Braids

  1. Choose the type of braid you want to create.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Braid each section and secure with a hair tie.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you manage your thick wavy frizzy hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make frizz worse.
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz while you sleep.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing thick wavy frizzy hair:

Q: What type of products should I use for my hair?

A: Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed for curly or wavy hair, as well as styling products that can help tame frizz and define curls.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. Some people with thick wavy frizzy hair may find that they only need to wash their hair once a week, while others may need to wash it more frequently.

Q: How can I prevent frizz?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your thick wavy frizzy hair looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and trying out some of these easy hairstyles, you can keep your thick wavy frizzy hair looking beautiful and stylish.
