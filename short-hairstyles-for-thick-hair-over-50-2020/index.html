Short Hairstyles For Thick Hair Over 50 2020 Perfect Hairstyles For Mature Women

Are you over 50 and looking for a short hairstyle that suits your thick hair? Look no further because we've got you covered. Short hairstyles for thick hair over 50 2020 are perfect for mature women who want to look youthful and stylish. In this article, we'll discuss the best short hairstyles for thick hair over 50 2020, how to style them, and tips for hair care. So, let's get started.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse, especially when you're over 50. On one hand, you have a lot of hair to work with, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage. That's why short hairstyles are perfect for mature women with thick hair. They're easy to style, require minimal maintenance, and look great on any face shape.

How to

The first step in getting a short hairstyle for thick hair over 50 2020 is to choose the right cut. Depending on your face shape and personal style, there are several options to choose from, including a pixie cut, bob, or lob. Once you've chosen your cut, it's time to style it.

Pixie Cut

For a pixie cut, start by applying a volumizing mousse to your damp hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create texture. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add more texture and definition.

Bob

For a bob, start by applying a heat protectant spray to your damp hair. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, curling the ends under for a sleek look. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Lob

For a lob, start by applying a volumizing mousse to your damp hair. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, curling the ends under for a bouncy look. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add more definition to your curls. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a short hairstyle for thick hair over 50 2020:

  1. Choose the right cut for your face shape and personal style.
  2. Apply the appropriate hair products for your hair type.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to smooth out any frizz or add more definition to your curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle for thick hair over 50 2020:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or barrettes, to add some flair to your style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for thick hair over 50 2020:

Q: Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

A: No, a short hairstyle can actually make you look younger and more stylish.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, you can still style your short hair if you have thick hair. Just make sure to use the appropriate hair products and tools.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry or oily.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful short hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming too dry.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to prevent breakage when brushing your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and let it air-dry whenever possible.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from becoming tangled while sleeping.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Short hairstyles for thick hair over 50 2020 are perfect for mature women who want to look youthful and stylish. Whether you prefer a pixie cut, bob, or lob, there's a short hairstyle that will suit your face shape and personal style. Just remember to use the appropriate hair products and tools, and to maintain your hair with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments. With the right care, your short hair will look beautiful and healthy for years to come.
