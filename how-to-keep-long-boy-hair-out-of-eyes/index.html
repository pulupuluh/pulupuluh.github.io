How To Keep Long Boy Hair Out Of Eyes A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Mar 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair can be a blessing and a curse, especially for young boys. While it can look stylish and trendy, it can also be a hassle to keep it out of the eyes. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks to help you keep long boy hair out of the eyes.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Keep Long Boy Hair Out of Eyes
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

How to Keep Long Boy Hair Out of Eyes

The first thing you need to do is find the right hairstyle that suits your child's face shape and hair texture. There are many different hairstyles that can keep long hair out of the eyes, such as:

  • Ponytail: This is a classic hairstyle that can work for both boys and girls. You can either tie the ponytail at the back of the head or on top of the head.
  • Man bun: This is a trendy hairstyle that can keep long hair out of the eyes while also making your child look stylish.
  • Side part: If your child has wavy or curly hair, a side part can help keep the hair away from the eyes.
  • Undercut: This is a popular hairstyle for boys that involves shaving the sides and back of the head while keeping the hair on top long.

Once you have chosen the right hairstyle, you can use the following tips to keep the hair out of the eyes:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to style and may not hold its shape as well.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as gel or mousse, to the hair. This will help keep the hair in place and prevent it from falling into the eyes.
  3. Use a comb or brush to style the hair into the desired shape. Make sure to comb or brush the hair away from the face.
  4. If necessary, use bobby pins or hair clips to secure the hair in place.
  5. If your child is playing sports or doing any physical activity, consider using a headband or sweatband to keep the hair out of the eyes.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help keep long boy hair out of the eyes:

  • Use a hair tie or elastic band to secure the hair in a ponytail or bun.
  • Consider using a hair accessory, such as a hat or bandana, to keep the hair out of the eyes.
  • Trim the hair regularly to keep it from getting too long and falling into the eyes.
  • Teach your child how to style their own hair so they can keep it out of their eyes on their own.

FAQs

Q: Can I use hair gel or mousse on my child's hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair gel or mousse on your child's hair, but make sure to choose a product that is gentle and non-irritating.

Q: What is the best hairstyle for long boy hair?

A: The best hairstyle for long boy hair depends on your child's face shape and hair texture. Some popular options include the ponytail, man bun, side part, and undercut.

Q: Can I use hair accessories to keep long boy hair out of the eyes?

A: Yes, you can use hair accessories such as headbands, hats, or bandanas to keep long boy hair out of the eyes.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Keeping long hair healthy and shiny requires proper care and maintenance. Here are some tips to help you take care of your child's long hair:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, on the hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle the hair gently.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on the hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep long boy hair out of the eyes while also keeping it healthy and stylish. With a little bit of effort and care, your child's long hair can be a source of pride and confidence.
