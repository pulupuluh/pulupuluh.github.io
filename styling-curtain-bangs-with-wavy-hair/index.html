Styling Curtain Bangs With Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Apr 26, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking to add some flair to your wavy hair? One of the hottest trends in hairstyling right now is curtain bangs. These face-framing bangs are perfect for those with wavy hair, as they add volume and texture to any look. But how do you style curtain bangs with wavy hair? In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

How to Style Curtain Bangs with Wavy Hair

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, let’s go over the basics of styling curtain bangs with wavy hair. First, you’ll want to wash and condition your hair with products designed for wavy hair. Then, while your hair is damp, apply a styling product to enhance your waves. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the waves.

Next, it’s time to style your curtain bangs. Use a round brush and a hairdryer to blow dry your bangs. As you blow dry, use the round brush to smooth out your bangs and create a slight curl. Finally, use a flat iron to touch up any areas that need it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect curtain bangs with wavy hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for wavy hair.
  2. While your hair is damp, apply a styling product to enhance your waves.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the waves.
  4. Blow dry your bangs with a round brush and a hairdryer.
  5. Use the round brush to smooth out your bangs and create a slight curl.
  6. Touch up any areas that need it with a flat iron.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs with Wavy Hair

Now that you know the basics of styling curtain bangs with wavy hair, let’s go over some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

Use the Right Products

Using products designed for wavy hair can help enhance your waves and make them more defined. Look for products that contain ingredients like sea salt, which can help add texture to your hair.

Blow Dry Your Bangs

Blow drying your bangs with a round brush can help smooth them out and create a slight curl. This can help your bangs blend in with the rest of your wavy hair.

Touch Up with a Flat Iron

If you have any areas that need touching up, use a flat iron to smooth them out. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Experiment with Parting

Curtain bangs can be parted in the middle or on the side. Try both to see which looks best with your face shape and hair texture.

FAQs About Styling Curtain Bangs with Wavy Hair

Can I Wear Curtain Bangs with Any Length of Wavy Hair?

Yes, you can wear curtain bangs with any length of wavy hair. Just be sure to adjust the length of your bangs to suit your hair length.

Do I Need to Use a Round Brush to Blow Dry My Bangs?

No, you don’t need to use a round brush to blow dry your bangs. However, using a round brush can help create a slight curl in your bangs that can help them blend in with the rest of your wavy hair.

How Often Should I Touch Up My Curtain Bangs?

How often you touch up your curtain bangs will depend on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your bangs to be. As a general rule, you may need to touch up your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Finally, it’s important to take care of your wavy hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

Use Products Designed for Wavy Hair

Using products designed for wavy hair can help enhance your waves and keep your hair looking healthy.

Avoid Heat Damage

Using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair. Try to limit your use of these tools and always use a heat protectant spray.

Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning can help nourish your hair and keep it looking healthy. Try deep conditioning once a week.

Avoid Over-Shampooing

Wavy hair can be prone to dryness, so avoid over-shampooing. Try washing your hair every other day or every few days.

In conclusion, styling curtain bangs with wavy hair is a great way to add some texture and volume to your look. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look, and be sure to take care of your wavy hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
