What Snacks Were Popular In The 60S

Written by Robby Mar 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome back to our blog! Today, we're taking a trip down memory lane to explore the popular snacks of the 60s. From sweet to savory, these snacks were enjoyed by many and are still beloved today. Let's dive in!

What Snacks Were Popular in the 60s?

The 60s were a time of change and innovation, and the snacks of the era reflected that. Here are some of the most popular snacks:

1. Popcorn

Popcorn was a beloved snack in the 60s and remains popular today. It's easy to make and can be seasoned in a variety of ways. Some popular seasonings included butter, salt, and cheese.

2. Chips and Dip

Chips and dip were a go-to snack for parties and gatherings. Potato chips were the most popular, and dip flavors ranged from onion to ranch.

3. Jello

Jello was a popular dessert and snack in the 60s. It was often served in fun shapes and colors, and could be mixed with fruit or whipped cream for added flavor.

4. Pigs in a Blanket

Pigs in a blanket were a popular appetizer at parties and gatherings. They consisted of small sausages wrapped in dough and baked until golden brown.

5. Twinkies

Twinkies were introduced in the 1930s, but gained popularity in the 60s. These sweet, cream-filled cakes were a favorite among kids and adults alike.

6. Chex Mix

Chex Mix was invented in the 50s but gained popularity in the 60s. This savory snack mix consisted of Chex cereal, pretzels, and nuts, and could be seasoned with spices for added flavor.

7. Pudding

Pudding was a popular dessert and snack in the 60s. It could be served in a variety of flavors, including chocolate, vanilla, and butterscotch.

How to Make Some of These Snacks

Popcorn:

To make popcorn, you'll need a pot with a lid, oil, and popcorn kernels. Heat the oil in the pot, add the kernels, and cover with the lid. Shake the pot occasionally until the popping slows down. Season with salt, butter, or cheese.

Chips and Dip:

To make chips and dip, you'll need your favorite chips and a dip of your choice. Some popular dip recipes include onion dip, ranch dip, and guacamole.

Pigs in a Blanket:

To make pigs in a blanket, you'll need small sausages and refrigerated crescent roll dough. Roll the sausages in the dough and bake in the oven until golden brown.

Tips for Enjoying These Snacks

Here are some tips for enjoying these snacks:

- Experiment with different popcorn seasonings, like garlic powder or chili powder. - Try making your own dip for chips using sour cream, cream cheese, and your favorite seasonings. - Serve pigs in a blanket with mustard or ketchup for added flavor.

FAQ

What other snacks were popular in the 60s?

Other popular snacks of the 60s included cheese and crackers, deviled eggs, and cocktail weenies.

Are these snacks still popular today?

Yes, many of these snacks are still popular today! Popcorn, chips and dip, and Twinkies are all still enjoyed by many.

Conclusion

That's a wrap on our exploration of the popular snacks of the 60s. We hope you enjoyed this trip down memory lane and maybe even found some inspiration for your next snack time. Thanks for reading!
