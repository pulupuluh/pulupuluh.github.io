What Type Of Haircut Would Look Good On Me A 2023 Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to change things up but don't know where to start? Choosing a new haircut can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this guide, we'll go over what type of haircut would look good on you and give you tips on how to maintain it.

Description

The first step in choosing a new haircut is to consider your face shape. Certain haircuts look better on certain face shapes, so it's important to know what yours is. The most common face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking at different haircuts that would complement it.

How To

Before you head to the salon, do some research on different haircuts that would look good on your face shape. You can search online, look through magazines, or even ask friends for recommendations.

When you're at the salon, make sure to communicate with your stylist. Let them know what you're looking for and ask for their professional opinion. They may have some suggestions that you haven't considered.

After you've gotten your new haircut, make sure to ask your stylist for tips on how to maintain it. They may recommend certain products or styling techniques that will help your hair look its best.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape

2. Research different haircuts that would complement your face shape

3. Communicate with your stylist

4. Ask for tips on maintaining your new haircut

Tips

  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a new haircut. If you don't have a lot of time to style your hair, choose a low-maintenance haircut.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new. Hair grows back, so if you don't like your new haircut, it's not permanent.
  • Bring pictures of haircuts you like to your stylist. This will give them a better idea of what you're looking for.

FAQ

Q: What if I don't know my face shape?

A: There are a lot of online resources that can help you determine your face shape. You can also ask your stylist for their opinion.

Q: What if I don't like my new haircut?

A: Don't panic! Hair grows back. Talk to your stylist about what you don't like and see if they can make any adjustments.

Hair Care

After you've gotten your new haircut, it's important to take care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. If you're going to use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first. Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

In conclusion, choosing a new haircut doesn't have to be a scary experience. By considering your face shape, doing some research, and communicating with your stylist, you can find a haircut that will make you look and feel your best.
