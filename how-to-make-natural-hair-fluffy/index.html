How To Make Natural Hair Fluffy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're tired of your flat, lifeless hair, you're not alone. Many women want to know how to make their natural hair fluffy without using harsh chemicals or heat. Luckily, there are plenty of ways to achieve volume and bounce without sacrificing the health of your hair. In this blog post, we'll share our top tips for making your natural hair fluffy and gorgeous.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prep Your Hair
  • How to Style Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Fluffy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Prep Your Hair

The first step to achieving fluffy hair is to properly prep your strands. This means starting with a clean, moisturized base. Here's how to get your hair ready:

1. Shampoo and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo. This will help remove any buildup that could be weighing down your strands. Follow up with a moisturizing conditioner to hydrate your hair and prevent breakage.

2. Deep Condition

To really give your hair a moisture boost, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Apply the treatment to your hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Detangle

After washing and conditioning your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your strands. This will help prevent breakage and make it easier to style your hair later on.

How to Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is prepped and ready to go, it's time to add some volume and texture. Here are some tips for styling fluffy hair:

1. Use a Volumizing Product

Apply a volumizing product, such as a mousse or root lifter, to your roots. This will help give your hair lift and bounce. Be sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down, lifting your hair as you go. This will help create volume and prevent your hair from falling flat.

3. Tease Your Hair

If you want even more volume, use a teasing comb to backcomb your hair at the roots. This will create texture and lift, but be careful not to overdo it, as teasing can cause damage to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Fluffy Hair

Once you've achieved your desired level of fluffiness, it's important to take steps to maintain it. Here are some tips:

1. Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and flatness. Try to wash your hair no more than once or twice a week, and use a dry shampoo in between washes if necessary.

2. Sleep on a Satin Pillowcase

A satin pillowcase can help prevent tangles and breakage, which can lead to flat hair. Plus, it's gentler on your skin, too!

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, embrace it! These textures are perfect for achieving fluffy, voluminous hair without too much effort.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve fluffy hair without using heat?

A: Yes! Try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without causing damage. You can also try air-drying your hair and using a volumizing product to add texture.

Q: Will teasing my hair damage it?

A: Teasing can cause damage if done too often or too aggressively. Try to limit your teasing to special occasions, and be gentle when backcombing your hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy, hydrated hair is key to achieving fluffy locks. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top shape:

1. Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Deep Condition Weekly

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you do use heat tools on your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve gorgeous, fluffy hair without sacrificing the health of your strands. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find what works best for you!
