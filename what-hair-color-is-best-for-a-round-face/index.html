What Hair Color Is Best For A Round Face

Written by Oliver May 15, 2023 Â· 9 min read

When it comes to choosing a hair color, it's essential to consider your face shape. A round face can benefit from different hair colors that complement and enhance its features. In this article, we'll discuss what hair color is best for a round face, how to choose the right shade, and hair care tips to maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hair Color for a Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Description

A round face has soft features and a circular shape, with a width and length that are almost equal. The goal of choosing a hair color for a round face is to create the illusion of length and balance by elongating the face. The right hair color can also highlight your best features and complement your skin tone.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color for a Round Face

When choosing a hair color for a round face, consider the following factors:

  • Skin tone: Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, and chestnut brown. Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, and blue-black.
  • Eye color: Consider your eye color when choosing a hair color. Blue and green eyes look great with golden or honey blonde shades, while brown eyes look great with warm brown or red shades.
  • Face shape: Choose a hair color that elongates your face and creates the illusion of length. Highlights and lowlights can add dimension and depth to your hair, making your face appear longer.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

Before coloring your hair, follow these steps:

  • Consult with a professional: If you're unsure which hair color to choose, consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose the right shade and ensure a smooth application.
  • Prepare your hair: Wash and condition your hair before coloring it. Avoid using any styling products, as they can interfere with the coloring process.
  • Protect your skin: Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline and ears to protect your skin from staining.

Once you're ready to color your hair, follow these steps:

  • Apply the color: Follow the instructions on the hair color box to apply the dye evenly to your hair. Use a brush or applicator to ensure even coverage.
  • Wait: Leave the dye on your hair for the recommended amount of time, as specified on the hair color box.
  • Rinse: Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear.
  • Condition: Apply the conditioner that comes with the hair color kit and leave it on for the recommended amount of time. Rinse your hair with cool water.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

To keep your hair color looking vibrant and healthy, follow these tips:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner: Use products specifically designed for colored hair to prevent fading and damage.
  • Avoid hot styling tools: Heat can damage colored hair, so avoid using hot styling tools like straighteners and curling irons.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can fade your hair color, so wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time outdoors.
  • Get regular trims: Keeping your hair healthy and trimmed can prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair appear dull and lifeless.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about choosing a hair color for a round face:

  • What hair colors should I avoid if I have a round face? Avoid hair colors that are too dark or too light, as they can make your face appear wider. Also, avoid hair colors with too much volume or curls, as they can add width to your face.
  • What hair colors look best on a round face with fair skin? Lighter hair colors like platinum blonde, ash blonde, and light brown can look great on fair skin with a round face.
  • What hair colors look best on a round face with dark skin? Darker hair colors like black, dark brown, and burgundy can look great on dark skin with a round face.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires special care to maintain its vibrancy and health. Here are some tips for caring for colored hair:

  • Wash your hair less frequently: Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils and cause your hair color to fade. Instead, wash your hair every other day or use dry shampoo in between washes.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid chlorine: Chlorine can cause your hair color to fade, so avoid swimming in chlorinated pools or wear a swim cap.
  • Trim your hair regularly: Regular trims can prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair appear dull and lifeless.

Choosing the right hair color for a round face can enhance your features and make you feel confident and beautiful. Consider your skin tone, eye color, and face shape when choosing a hair color, and follow these tips for maintaining your new look. With the right care, your colored hair can look vibrant and healthy for weeks to come.
