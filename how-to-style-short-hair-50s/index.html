How To Style Short Hair 50S A Relaxed Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to channel your inner Marilyn Monroe or Audrey Hepburn, you may want to consider styling your hair in a 50s-inspired look. While the hairstyles of this era may seem complicated, they're actually quite simple to achieve. In this guide, we'll show you how to style short hair 50s style in a relaxed and easy way. Here are the steps:

How to Style Short Hair 50s: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that your hairstyle lasts longer.

Step 2: Prep your hair with products

Apply a small amount of hair mousse or styling gel to your hair to give it some hold and texture. This will also help your hair stay in place throughout the day.

Step 3: Create volume at the crown

To create the classic 50s look, start by creating volume at the crown of your head. Use a fine-tooth comb to tease the hair at the top of your head, backcombing it gently to create volume.

Step 4: Smooth out the sides

Once you've created volume at the crown, use a brush or comb to smooth out the sides of your hair. You can also use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to help keep your hair in place.

Step 5: Curl the ends

To create the signature 50s look, use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair outwards. You can also use hot rollers or a flat iron to achieve this look.

Step 6: Pin the hair in place

Once you've curled the ends of your hair, pin them in place with bobby pins or hair clips. This will help your hairstyle last longer and keep it looking neat and tidy.

Tips for Styling Short Hair 50s Style

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect 50s-inspired hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or scarves, to add some extra flair to your look.
  • Consider trying a shorter haircut, such as a bob or pixie cut, to make styling your hair easier.
  • Be patient and take your time when styling your hair to ensure that it looks neat and polished.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Styling Short Hair 50s Style

Q: Can I achieve a 50s-inspired look with naturally straight hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a 50s-inspired look even if you have naturally straight hair. Simply follow the steps above and use hot tools to curl the ends of your hair outward.

Q: How can I make my 50s-inspired hairstyle last longer?

A: To make your hairstyle last longer, be sure to use a strong-hold hairspray and avoid touching your hair throughout the day. You can also touch up any curls that have fallen out with a curling iron or hot rollers.

Hair Care for Short Hair 50s Style

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, be sure to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. You should also avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Additionally, be sure to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a 50s-inspired hairstyle that's both easy and stylish. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, the 50s never go out of style.
