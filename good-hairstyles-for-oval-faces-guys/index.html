Good Hairstyles For Oval Faces Guys The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Feb 25, 2023 · 9 min read

If you’re a guy with an oval face, first of all, congratulations! Your face shape is considered to be the most versatile and balanced one. However, it can still be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape. In this article, we will give you a complete guide on the best hairstyles for oval faces guys, including how to style them, tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Good Hairstyles for Oval Faces Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Style Oval Face Hairstyles
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Oval Face Hairstyles

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oval faces guys, it’s essential to determine your face shape correctly. Here is how you can do it:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Use a tape measure to measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down the measurements.

Step 2: Determine the Shape

Compare the measurements of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If the measurements are roughly the same, you have an oval face shape.

Good Hairstyles for Oval Faces Guys

Now that you’ve determined your face shape let's take a look at the best hairstyles for oval faces guys.

Short Quiff

The short quiff is an excellent option for oval faces as it adds volume and texture to the hair. Keep the sides and back short, and the top should be longer, styled in a quiff.

Slicked Back

The slicked-back hairstyle is a classic look that suits oval faces very well. It’s a versatile style that works for both formal and casual occasions. Use a pomade or gel to slick the hair back.

Side Part

The side part is a timeless look that looks great on oval faces. It’s a low maintenance style that requires a bit of length on top and short sides. Use a comb to create a clean side part.

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a great option for guys who want a low maintenance look. It’s a classic style that works well for oval faces as it emphasizes the face shape.

Messy Textured Crop

The messy textured crop is a popular hairstyle that looks great on oval faces. It’s a low maintenance style that adds volume and texture to the hair. The sides should be short, and the top should be longer, styled in a messy crop.

Step-by-Step Guide to Style Oval Face Hairstyles

Now that you’ve chosen your hairstyle let's take a look at how to style it.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt and oil from your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to dry your hair until it’s damp. Don’t rub your hair vigorously as it can damage the hair.

Step 3: Apply Hair Product

Apply your preferred hair product to your hair. Use a small amount of product at a time and work it through your hair evenly.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair. Follow the specific instructions for your chosen hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep your hairstyle looking great:

- Visit a barber regularly to keep your hair in shape. - Use high-quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type. - Avoid over-styling your hair as it can damage the hair. - Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. - Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for an oval face?

The best hairstyles for oval faces guys are short quiff, slicked-back, side part, buzz cut, and messy textured crop.

How often should I visit a barber?

It’s recommended to visit a barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

What hair products should I use for my hairstyle?

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and chosen hairstyle. Pomade, gel, and wax are popular choices for styling men’s hair.

Hair Care for Oval Face Hairstyles

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

- Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner. - Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair. - Avoid using too much product on your hair as it can weigh it down. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy. - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

In conclusion, there are many good hairstyles for oval faces guys. The key is to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and is easy to maintain. Use the tips and step-by-step guide in this article to ensure your hairstyle looks great every day.
