Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Youtube How To Guide

Written by Robby Jun 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for easy hairstyles for medium length hair? Look no further than Youtube! With countless tutorials at your fingertips, you can create a variety of hairstyles from braids to updos in no time. In this guide, we’ll show you step-by-step how to achieve some of the best easy hairstyles for medium length hair on Youtube.

Description

Medium length hair is versatile and can be styled in many ways. Whether you have straight or curly hair, there are numerous hairstyles you can try. Youtube is a great resource for finding inspiration and tutorials on easy hairstyles for medium length hair. From messy buns to beachy waves, there’s a hairstyle for every occasion.

How-To

The first step to achieving easy hairstyles for medium length hair is to find inspiration. Browse through Youtube for hairstyles that catch your eye. Look for tutorials that are easy to follow and use products that you already have on hand.

Once you’ve found a hairstyle you want to try, gather the necessary tools and products. This may include a comb, hair ties, bobby pins, and styling products like hairspray or mousse.

Follow the tutorial step-by-step, pausing and rewinding as needed. Don’t be afraid to make mistakes – practice makes perfect! Once you’ve mastered one hairstyle, move on to the next.

Step-by-Step

Here are some easy hairstyles for medium length hair Youtube tutorials you can try:

1. Messy Bun

Start by gathering your hair into a high ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun and secure with bobby pins. Pull out a few pieces of hair to frame your face and use hairspray to set the look.

2. Beachy Waves

Start by applying a texturizing spray to damp hair. Divide hair into sections and wrap each section around a curling wand. Hold for a few seconds, then release. Once all sections are curled, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

3. Dutch Braid

Start by parting your hair down the middle. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and divide it into three sections. Cross the right section under the middle section, then cross the left section under the middle section. Continue braiding, adding more hair to each section as you go. Once you reach the nape of your neck, braid the remaining hair into a regular braid and secure with a hair tie.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying easy hairstyles for medium length hair Youtube tutorials:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use products that work best for your hair type.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment and try new styles.
  • Watch tutorials all the way through before attempting the hairstyle.
  • Have fun!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for medium length hair Youtube tutorials:

1. How long does it take to style medium length hair?

It depends on the hairstyle you’re trying. Some styles may take only a few minutes, while others may take up to an hour.

2. Can I achieve these hairstyles with curly hair?

Absolutely! These hairstyles can be achieved with any hair type.

3. What if I don’t have all the necessary tools and products?

Get creative! Use items you have on hand to achieve a similar look. For example, instead of a curling wand, try using a straightener to create waves.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

With these easy hairstyles for medium length hair Youtube tutorials, you can achieve a variety of looks in no time. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles!
