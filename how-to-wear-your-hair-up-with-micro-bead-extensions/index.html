How To Wear Your Hair Up With Micro Bead Extensions

Written by Robby Mar 13, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of wearing your hair down all the time? Do you want to switch up your style and wear your hair up? If you have micro bead extensions, you might be wondering if it’s possible to wear your hair up without showing the beads. The answer is yes! In this article, we’ll show you how to wear your hair up with micro bead extensions.

Description

Micro bead extensions are a popular type of hair extension because they’re easy to install and remove. They’re also comfortable to wear and give you a natural look. However, one of the downsides of micro bead extensions is that they can be difficult to hide if you want to wear your hair up.

How to

Here’s how to wear your hair up with micro bead extensions:

Step 1: Brush your hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. This will make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into two parts: the top half and the bottom half. Use a hair tie to secure the top half in a high ponytail.

Step 3: Create a bun

Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Make sure to cover the micro bead extensions with your hair.

Step 4: Release the bottom half

Release the bottom half of your hair and brush it to remove any tangles. Use a hair tie to secure it into a low ponytail.

Step 5: Wrap the ponytail

Take the ponytail and wrap it around the bun, securing it with bobby pins. Make sure to cover the micro bead extensions with your hair.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finish off the look with a spritz of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing your hair up with micro bead extensions:

  • Make sure to cover the micro bead extensions with your hair to avoid them showing.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place and keep the style looking neat.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing your hair up with micro bead extensions:

Can you wear your hair up with micro bead extensions?

Yes, you can wear your hair up with micro bead extensions. It just takes a little bit of extra effort to make sure the beads aren’t visible.

What hairstyles can you wear with micro bead extensions?

You can wear a variety of hairstyles with micro bead extensions, including ponytails, buns, braids, and updos.

Do micro bead extensions damage your hair?

Micro bead extensions are a safe and non-damaging way to add length and volume to your hair. However, it’s important to have them installed and removed by a professional to avoid any damage.

Hair Care

When wearing your hair up with micro bead extensions, it’s important to take care of your hair to avoid any damage. Here are some tips:

  • Don’t wear your hair up too tight, as this can cause tension on your scalp and damage your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage to the extensions.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair and extensions healthy.

Now that you know how to wear your hair up with micro bead extensions, you can experiment with different hairstyles and switch up your look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great!
