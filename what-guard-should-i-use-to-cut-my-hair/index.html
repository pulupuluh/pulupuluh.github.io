What Guard Should I Use To Cut My Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby May 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to save money on haircuts, cutting your hair at home can be a great option. However, with so many different guards available, it can be overwhelming to determine which one to use. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about guards and how to choose the right one for your hair type and desired length.

Table of Contents

  • What is a Guard?
  • How to Choose a Guard
  • Step-by-step Guide to Cutting Your Hair
  • Tips for Cutting Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

What is a Guard?

A guard is a plastic attachment that fits onto the clippers and determines the length of the cut. Guards come in different sizes, ranging from 1/16 of an inch to 1 inch or more. The smaller the number, the shorter the cut.

How to Choose a Guard

The right guard for you will depend on your hair type and desired length. Here are some general guidelines:

  • For a buzz cut, use a #1 or #2 guard.
  • For a shorter fade, use a #3 or #4 guard.
  • For a longer fade or textured cut, use a #5 or #6 guard.
  • For longer hair, use a #7 or higher guard.

It's important to note that these are just general guidelines and may not work for everyone. Experiment with different guards to find the right one for your hair.

Step-by-step Guide to Cutting Your Hair

Before you start, make sure you have the following:

  • A set of clippers with guards
  • A comb
  • Scissors
  • A mirror
  • A towel to catch the hair

Follow these steps to cut your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose the guard you want to use and attach it to the clippers.
  3. Start at the sides and work your way up, using the clippers to cut the hair to the desired length.
  4. Use the comb to blend the hair between the different guard lengths.
  5. Move to the back of the head and repeat the same process.
  6. Use the scissors to trim any longer pieces that the clippers missed.
  7. Check the length and make any necessary adjustments.
  8. Clean up any stray hairs around the ears and neckline.
  9. Shower and shampoo your hair to get rid of any loose hairs.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down. You can always go shorter, but you can't go longer.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut against the grain for a closer cut.
  • Make sure the clippers are oiled before you start to prevent pulling.
  • Trim the hair around the ears and neckline with scissors for a more polished look.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair wet or does it need to be dry?

It's best to cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly and may cause the clippers to pull.

How often should I cut my hair?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how short you like to keep it. As a general rule, most people need a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Can I use the same guard for my beard?

Yes, you can use the same clippers and guards for your beard. Just make sure to clean them thoroughly between uses.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Shampoo and condition regularly to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut your hair at home like a pro. Remember to experiment with different guards to find the perfect length for your hair, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
