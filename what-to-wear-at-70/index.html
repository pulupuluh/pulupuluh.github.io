What To Wear At 70 Tips For A Comfortable And Fashionable Look

Written by Aprilia Apr 28, 2023 · 7 min read

As we age, our fashion choices may change. But that doesn't mean we have to give up on style and comfort. Whether you're going out for a walk, running errands, or attending a social event, there are plenty of options for what to wear at 70 that are both comfortable and fashionable. In this article, we'll share some tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice on how to look and feel your best at any age.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose Comfortable and Fashionable Clothes
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Dressing for Different Occasions
  • Fashion Tips for Women Over 70
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Women Over 70

How to Choose Comfortable and Fashionable Clothes

When it comes to choosing what to wear at 70, comfort should be a top priority. But that doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect balance:

  • Look for clothes that fit well and flatter your body shape. Avoid clothes that are too tight or too loose.
  • Choose fabrics that are soft, breathable, and easy to care for, such as cotton, linen, and jersey.
  • Stick to classic styles that never go out of fashion, such as tailored pants, blazers, and trench coats.
  • Add pops of color with accessories, such as scarves, jewelry, and handbags.
  • Invest in quality shoes that are comfortable and provide good support.

Remember, fashion is all about expressing yourself and feeling confident in your own skin. Don't be afraid to try new things and experiment with different styles.

Step-by-Step Guide to Dressing for Different Occasions

How you dress depends on the occasion. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you dress for different events:

Going for a Walk

  • Choose comfortable, breathable clothes such as leggings, joggers, or shorts.
  • Add a lightweight jacket or sweater in case it gets chilly.
  • Wear comfortable shoes such as sneakers, loafers, or sandals.
  • Accessorize with a sun hat, sunglasses, and a tote bag.

Running Errands

  • Choose comfortable clothes such as jeans, pants, or a skirt.
  • Add a comfortable top such as a t-shirt or blouse.
  • Wear comfortable shoes such as flats or slip-on sneakers.
  • Accessorize with a crossbody bag and a scarf or necklace.

Attending a Social Event

  • Choose a dress or pantsuit in a flattering color and style.
  • Add a jacket or shawl in case it gets chilly.
  • Wear comfortable shoes such as low heels or flats.
  • Accessorize with a clutch or shoulder bag, and jewelry such as earrings or a necklace.

Fashion Tips for Women Over 70

Here are some fashion tips to help you look and feel your best at any age:

  • Invest in good quality, classic pieces that never go out of style.
  • Accessorize with scarves, jewelry, and handbags to add pops of color and personality.
  • Choose comfortable shoes that provide good support.
  • Experiment with different styles and don't be afraid to try something new.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should women over 70 avoid wearing?

Women over 70 should avoid wearing clothes that are too revealing or too tight, as well as clothes that are too trendy or youthful.

Can women over 70 wear leggings?

Yes, women over 70 can wear leggings as long as they are comfortable and paired with a longer top or tunic.

What are some good brands for women over 70?

Some good brands for women over 70 include Eileen Fisher, Chico's, J.Jill, and Lands' End.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 70

As we age, our hair may become thinner and drier. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling tools such as blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Consider getting a shorter haircut that is easy to style and maintain.
  • Try using a volumizing spray or mousse to add body to your hair.

By following these fashion and hair care tips, you can look and feel your best at 70 and beyond. Remember, fashion is all about expressing yourself and feeling confident in your own skin. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles and have fun with your fashion choices!
