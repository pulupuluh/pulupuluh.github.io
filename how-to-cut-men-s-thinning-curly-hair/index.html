How To Cut Men s Thinning Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank May 22, 2023 · 8 min read

As we age, our hair often becomes thinner and more brittle, and this is especially true for men with curly hair. If you're struggling with thinning curly hair, don't worry – there are plenty of ways to cut your hair to make it look thicker and fuller. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut men's thinning curly hair in a way that looks natural and stylish.

Description

Men's thinning curly hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right haircut, you can make it look fuller and more voluminous. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut men's thinning curly hair in a way that flatters your face shape and makes the most of your natural texture.

How to Cut Men's Thinning Curly Hair: Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in cutting men's thinning curly hair is to choose the right length. For most men with thinning hair, a shorter cut is usually the best option, as it makes the hair look thicker and fuller. However, you don't want to go too short, as this can make your hair look sparse and thin. Aim for a length that is short enough to make your hair look fuller, but long enough to maintain some of your natural texture.

Step 2: Decide on a Style

Once you've chosen a length, it's time to decide on a style. For men with thinning curly hair, a layered cut is usually the best option, as it adds volume and movement to the hair. You can also consider adding some texture to the hair, either through a razor cut or by using texturizing scissors.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Now it's time to cut the hair. If you're cutting your own hair, start by sectioning it into small sections and cutting each section individually. Use a sharp pair of scissors and cut the hair at an angle, rather than straight across, to create a more natural-looking finish. If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, consider going to a professional stylist who has experience cutting men's thinning curly hair.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Once you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. For men with thinning curly hair, a light styling product like a mousse or a light gel can help to add volume and texture to the hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, using your fingers to scrunch and shape the curls.

Tips for Cutting Men's Thinning Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for cutting men's thinning curly hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair before cutting it.
  • Be gentle when cutting your hair, as curly hair can be prone to breakage.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • If you're not sure what length or style to choose, ask your stylist for advice.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my own thinning curly hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to be careful and use sharp scissors. If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, consider going to a professional stylist.

Q: What styling products should I use on my thinning curly hair?

A: Light styling products like mousses and gels are usually the best option for men with thinning curly hair. Avoid heavy styling products like waxes and pomades, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner.

Q: How often should I cut my thinning curly hair?

A: This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how quickly it starts to thin out. Generally, it's a good idea to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Hair Care for Men's Thinning Curly Hair

Finally, it's important to take good care of your thinning curly hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like hair dryers and flat irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Consider taking a hair growth supplement to support healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and using the right haircut and styling products, you can make the most of your thinning curly hair and keep it looking stylish and healthy.
