Hair Products For Over 60 A Guide To Healthy And Beautiful Hair

Written by Christine Mar 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair changes along with our body. We may notice that our hair becomes thinner, drier, and more brittle than before. However, with the right hair products, we can still achieve healthy and beautiful hair even in our 60s and beyond. In this article, we will discuss the best hair products for over 60 and how to use them for optimal results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hair Products for Over 60
  • Shampoo and Conditioner for Over 60
  • Hair Oil for Over 60
  • Hair Mask for Over 60
  • Styling Products for Over 60
  • Hair Care Tips for Over 60
  • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to Choose the Right Hair Products for Over 60

When it comes to hair products for over 60, it is important to choose products that are specifically formulated for aging hair. Look for products that contain ingredients such as biotin, keratin, collagen, and hyaluronic acid, which can help promote healthy hair growth, strengthen the hair strands, and hydrate the hair. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals such as sulfates, parabens, and alcohol, which can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause more damage.

Shampoo and Conditioner for Over 60

A good shampoo and conditioner set is the foundation of any hair care routine. For over 60, look for a gentle shampoo that cleanses the hair without stripping its natural oils. A moisturizing conditioner is also important to hydrate the hair and make it more manageable. Some of the best shampoo and conditioner sets for over 60 include:

  • L'Oreal Paris EverStrong Thickening Shampoo and Conditioner
  • OGX Biotin and Collagen Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Pureology Hydrate Shampoo and Conditioner

Hair Oil for Over 60

Hair oil is a great way to add moisture and shine to aging hair. Look for lightweight oils such as argan oil, jojoba oil, and coconut oil, which can penetrate the hair shaft without weighing it down. Apply a few drops of hair oil to damp hair before styling or use it as a finishing touch to tame flyaways and add shine. Some of the best hair oils for over 60 include:

  • Moroccanoil Treatment Light
  • Ouai Hair Oil
  • Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser's Invisible Oil

Hair Mask for Over 60

A hair mask is a deep conditioning treatment that can help nourish and repair aging hair. Look for a mask that contains ingredients such as shea butter, coconut oil, and vitamin E, which can help hydrate the hair and reduce breakage. Apply the mask to clean, damp hair and leave it on for 10-20 minutes before rinsing it off. Some of the best hair masks for over 60 include:

  • Briogeo Don't Despair, Repair! Deep Conditioning Mask
  • It's a 10 Miracle Hair Mask
  • Living Proof Restore Mask Treatment

Styling Products for Over 60

Styling products can help enhance the natural texture and volume of aging hair. Look for products that are lightweight and provide hold without weighing the hair down. Some of the best styling products for over 60 include:

  • Bumble and Bumble Thickening Spray
  • Living Proof Full Dry Volume Blast
  • Oribe Grandiose Hair Plumping Mousse

Hair Care Tips for Over 60

In addition to using the right hair products, there are some other tips you can follow to keep your hair healthy and beautiful in your 60s and beyond:

  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can cause damage to aging hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature on low.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, which can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water, which can help keep your hair and scalp moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can I use the same hair products I used in my 30s and 40s?

A: It is best to switch to hair products that are specifically formulated for aging hair, as these products contain ingredients that can help promote healthy hair growth and strengthen the hair.

Q: Can hair products for over 60 help with hair loss?

A: While hair products cannot cure hair loss, they can help promote healthy hair growth and reduce breakage, which can make the hair appear fuller and thicker.

Q: How often should I use a hair mask?

A: It is recommended to use a hair mask once a week to help nourish and repair aging hair.

In conclusion, using the right hair products and following a healthy hair care routine can help keep your hair looking its best in your 60s and beyond. Remember to choose products that are specifically formulated for aging hair, avoid harsh chemicals, and follow the tips mentioned above for optimal results.
