How To Cut Hair With A Double Cowlick

Written by William Aug 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a double cowlick can be challenging when it comes to cutting your hair. It can be frustrating to try and style your hair and have it not cooperate due to the cowlicks. However, there are techniques that can help you achieve the hairstyle you desire. In this article, we will go through the steps on how to cut hair with a double cowlick.

Description

A cowlick is a section of hair that grows in a different direction than the surrounding hair. This can be caused by the natural growth pattern of the hair or by the way it is cut. A double cowlick occurs when there are two sections of hair that grow in different directions. This can make it difficult to style your hair and can require specific techniques when cutting.

How to Cut Hair with a Double Cowlick

Cutting hair with a double cowlick requires careful consideration and attention to detail. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine the Direction of the Cowlicks

Before you start cutting, it is important to understand the direction of the cowlicks. Use a comb to separate your hair and identify the two sections that are affected by the cowlicks. This will help you determine how to cut the hair to make it look even.

Step 2: Wet the Hair

Wet the hair thoroughly before cutting. This makes it easier to section and cut the hair without causing damage or breakage.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Use clips to section the hair into manageable sections. This will help you work on one area at a time and ensure that you do not miss any sections.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Using your scissors, cut the hair in the direction of the cowlicks. This will help you maintain the natural growth pattern of the hair and prevent it from standing up or looking uneven. Be sure to cut small sections at a time and use a comb to guide the hair.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

After cutting each section, check for evenness. Use a comb to compare the length of each section and make any necessary adjustments. It is important to take your time and ensure that the hair is cut evenly.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting hair with a double cowlick:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Cut the hair when it is dry to see how it will fall naturally.
  • Be patient and take your time to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.
  • Consider using a hair product to help style the hair and keep the cowlicks in place.

FAQ

What is a cowlick?

A cowlick is a section of hair that grows in a different direction than the surrounding hair. This can be caused by the natural growth pattern of the hair or by the way it is cut.

What is a double cowlick?

How do I cut hair with a double cowlick?

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with a double cowlick, it is important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Consider using a hair product specifically designed for cowlicks to help keep them in place.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can successfully cut your hair with a double cowlick and achieve the hairstyle you desire. Remember to take your time and be patient, and your hair will look great!
