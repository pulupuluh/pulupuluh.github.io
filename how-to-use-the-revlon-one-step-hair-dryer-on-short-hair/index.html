How To Use The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer On Short Hair

Written by Frank Jul 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have short hair and are looking for an easier way to style it, the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer can be a great tool to invest in. This dryer is designed to combine the power of a hair dryer and the styling ability of a brush, making it a convenient and time-saving option for those with short hair. In this article, we will go through everything you need to know about how to use the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer on short hair.

Description

The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer is a hair tool that simplifies the hair drying and styling process. It has a unique oval brush design that helps to create volume and shine, while the ceramic coating distributes heat evenly to prevent damage to hair. The One Step Hair Dryer has three heat settings and a cool shot button to set your style in place.

How to Use the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer on Short Hair

Step 1: Wash and towel dry your hair

Before using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer, ensure that your hair is clean and towel-dried. This will help to reduce the amount of time needed to dry your hair and allow the dryer to work more efficiently.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

It is essential to protect your hair from the heat of the dryer. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 3: Section your hair

Divide your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that all sections of your hair are dried and styled evenly.

Step 4: Start with the roots

Begin by placing the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer at the roots of your hair. Use the brush to pull your hair upwards, creating volume and lift. Move the dryer down the length of your hair while continuing to brush through your hair. Repeat this process on each section of your hair until it is dry.

Step 5: Use the cool shot button

Once your hair is dry, use the cool shot button to set your style in place. This will help to keep your hair looking smooth and shiny all day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer on short hair:

  • Use the lowest heat setting that works for your hair to prevent damage.
  • Work in small sections to ensure that all parts of your hair are evenly dried and styled.
  • For added volume, use the brush to pull your hair upwards while drying.
  • To create curls or waves, wrap small sections of hair around the brush and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

FAQ

Can the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer be used on wet hair?

Yes, the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer can be used on wet hair. However, it is recommended to towel dry your hair first to reduce the amount of time needed to dry your hair and prevent damage.

How long does it take to dry short hair using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer?

The drying time will depend on the length and thickness of your hair. However, using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer can significantly reduce the amount of time needed to dry your hair compared to using a traditional hair dryer and brush.

How often should I clean my Revlon One Step Hair Dryer?

It is recommended to clean your Revlon One Step Hair Dryer after every use to prevent buildup and ensure that it works efficiently. Use a soft cloth to wipe down the brush and remove any hair or debris.

Hair Care

Using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer on short hair can be a great way to simplify your hair styling routine. However, it is essential to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using the highest heat setting on the dryer to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer on short hair can be a game-changer when it comes to styling your hair. By following these tips and steps, you can achieve a salon-quality look from the comfort of your own home.
