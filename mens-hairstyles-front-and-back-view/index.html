Mens Hairstyles Front And Back View A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Apr 03, 2023 Â· 10 min read

In this year 2023, men's hairstyles have become more diverse and experimental. From buzz cuts to long hair, there are many options to choose from. However, when deciding on a new hairstyle, it's important to consider both the front and back view. In this guide, we'll explore various mens hairstyles, their front and back view, and provide tips on how to achieve them.

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles are low maintenance and perfect for men who want a fuss-free look. The front and back view of short hairstyles are usually the same, but there are some variations to consider.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic style that looks great on most men. It's a simple, short haircut that's easy to maintain. The front and back view of a crew cut are the same, with the hair being cut short all around the head.

Spiky Hair

Spiky hair is a popular style that's great for men who want a more edgy look. The front view of spiky hair is usually styled upwards, while the back is kept short. To achieve this look, use a strong-hold hair gel or wax and run your fingers through your hair to create spikes.

Medium Hairstyles

Medium hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in many ways. They're great for men who want a more polished look without the commitment of long hair.

Side Part

The side part is a classic style that looks great on most men. The front view of a side part is styled to one side, while the back is usually tapered. To achieve this look, use a comb to create a side part and style the hair with a strong-hold hair gel or wax.

Quiff

The quiff is a stylish and modern hairstyle that's great for men who want a more formal look. The front view of a quiff is styled upwards and back, while the back is usually tapered. To achieve this look, use a hair dryer and a round brush to create volume and hold.

Long Hairstyles

Long hairstyles are bold and require more maintenance than short or medium hairstyles. They're great for men who want to make a statement and stand out from the crowd.

Ponytail

The ponytail is a classic style that's great for men with long hair. The front view of a ponytail is usually styled back, while the back is tied into a ponytail. To achieve this look, use a comb to create a sleek look and tie your hair into a ponytail with an elastic band.

Man Bun

The man bun is a trendy hairstyle that's great for men with long hair. The front view of a man bun is usually styled back, while the back is tied into a bun. To achieve this look, use a hair tie to secure your hair into a bun on the top of your head.

Fade Hairstyles

Fade hairstyles are a popular style that's great for men who want a clean and polished look. The front and back view of a fade hairstyle are usually the same, but there are some variations to consider.

Low Fade

The low fade is a subtle and stylish look that's great for most men. The front and back view of a low fade are usually the same, with the hair being tapered towards the neck. To achieve this look, use a clipper to gradually fade the hair from the sideburns to the neck.

High Fade

The high fade is a bold and modern look that's great for men who want to make a statement. The front and back view of a high fade are usually the same, with the hair being tapered towards the top of the head. To achieve this look, use a clipper to gradually fade the hair from the sideburns to the top of the head.

Curly Hairstyles

Curly hairstyles are a great option for men with naturally curly hair. The front and back view of curly hairstyles are usually the same, but there are some variations to consider.

Curls with Fade

Curls with a fade is a stylish and modern look that's great for men with curly hair. The front and back view of curls with a fade are usually the same, with the hair being tapered towards the neck or top of the head. To achieve this look, use a clipper to gradually fade the hair from the sideburns to the neck or top of the head.

Curly Undercut

The curly undercut is a trendy hairstyle that's great for men with curly hair. The front view of a curly undercut is usually styled upwards and back, while the back is usually tapered. To achieve this look, use a hair dryer and a round brush to create volume and hold.

FAQ

What's the best hairstyle for men?

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. The best hairstyle for men depends on their face shape, hair type, and personal style.

How do I choose a hairstyle?

When choosing a new hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. It's also important to consider the front and back view of the hairstyle.

How do I maintain my hairstyle?

To maintain your hairstyle, use quality hair products, get regular haircuts, and follow a healthy hair care routine.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and stylish hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair health

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle for you, you can achieve a stylish and polished look that's perfect for any occasion.
