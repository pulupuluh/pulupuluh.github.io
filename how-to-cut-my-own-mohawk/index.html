How To Cut My Own Mohawk A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you ready to rock a bold and edgy hairstyle? A mohawk might just be what you need! Mohawks have been around for decades and are still popular today. The good news is, you don't have to go to a salon to get one. With a little patience and some tools, you can cut your own mohawk at home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process step by step.

How to Cut My Own Mohawk

What You'll Need

Before you start cutting, make sure you have these tools on hand:

  • Clippers or scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Now, let's get started on cutting your own mohawk:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair. Use a comb to divide your hair into three parts: the sides, the top, and the back.
  2. Clip or tie the side sections to keep them out of the way.
  3. Decide on the width of your mohawk. Use the comb to part the top section of your hair down the middle.
  4. Use clippers or scissors to cut the sides of your hair short. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you reach your desired length.
  5. Now, it's time to cut the top section. Use the comb to lift the hair straight up and cut it to your desired length.
  6. Gradually blend the top and sides of your hair together. Use shorter guards as you get closer to the sides.
  7. Once you're satisfied with the length, use the clippers or scissors to clean up any stray hairs.
  8. Remove any hair ties or clips and style your mohawk as desired.
  9. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.
  10. Congratulations, you now have a DIY mohawk!

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect mohawk:

  • Start with longer guards and work your way down to avoid cutting your hair too short.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure everything is even.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and time-consuming.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

FAQ

Here are some common questions people have about cutting their own mohawk:

How long does a mohawk last?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you trim it. On average, a mohawk can last anywhere from 2-6 weeks.

Can I cut a mohawk with scissors?

Yes, you can cut a mohawk with scissors. However, clippers are recommended as they make it easier to achieve an even length.

Do I need to use hair gel to style my mohawk?

No, you don't have to use hair gel. You can use pomade or wax to style your mohawk.

Hair Care

Now that you have a mohawk, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your mohawk's shape.

Now that you know how to cut your own mohawk, it's time to grab your tools and get to work! With a little patience and practice, you'll be rocking a bold and edgy look in no time.
