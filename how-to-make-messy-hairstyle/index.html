How To Make A Messy Hairstyle A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 15, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to add some edge to your look? A messy hairstyle may be just what you need. Messy hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, whether you're going to a casual brunch or a fancy party. In this article, we'll show you how to make a messy hairstyle in easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make a Messy Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Messy Hairstyle

How to Make a Messy Hairstyle

A messy hairstyle is all about texture and volume. It's a style that looks effortless but requires a little bit of work. Here's how you can achieve it:

1. Start with clean hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to make sure it's clean and free of any product buildup. If you have naturally oily hair, use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil before styling.

2. Apply a texturizing product

Apply a texturizing product, such as a sea salt spray, to your damp hair. This will help create volume and texture. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair while blow-drying it to create more volume.

3. Tease your hair

Tease the crown of your head with a teasing comb to create more volume. Be gentle to avoid damaging your hair.

4. Create messy waves

Use a curling iron or a straightener to create messy waves. Don't worry about making them perfect – the messier, the better! Use your fingers to break up the waves and create a more natural look.

5. Add some texture

Use a styling wax or pomade to add some texture to your hair. Rub a small amount between your palms and apply it to the ends of your hair, working your way up.

6. Finish with hairspray

Finish off your look with some hairspray. Choose a flexible hold hairspray to avoid the crunchy look. Spray it all over your hair to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to making a messy hairstyle:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a texturizing product to your damp hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair while scrunching it with your fingers.
  4. Tease the crown of your head with a teasing comb.
  5. Use a curling iron or a straightener to create messy waves.
  6. Break up the waves with your fingers.
  7. Apply a styling wax or pomade to the ends of your hair.
  8. Finish with a flexible hold hairspray.

Tips for a Perfect Messy Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hairstyle:

  • Don't be afraid to use products. Texturizing products, styling waxes, and hairspray can all help you achieve a messy look.
  • Use a teasing comb gently to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, to find what works best for you.
  • Don't worry about making your waves perfect. Messy waves are the goal!
  • Use your fingers to break up any clumps and create a more natural look.

FAQ

Q: Can I make a messy hairstyle with short hair?

A: Absolutely! Messy hairstyles work well with any hair length. Just adjust the amount of product and the size of your waves to suit your hair length.

Q: How long does a messy hairstyle last?

A: A messy hairstyle can last all day if you use the right products and techniques. Use a flexible hold hairspray to ensure your style stays in place.

Hair Care Tips

Making a messy hairstyle can be fun, but it's important to take care of your hair as well. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it over time.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to make a messy hairstyle, it's time to try it out for yourself! Remember to have fun with it – the messier, the better.
