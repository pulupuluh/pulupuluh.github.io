Short Haircuts For 60 Year Old Woman With Glasses A Guide

Written by Petter Mar 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a 60-year-old woman who wears glasses, you may be wondering what hairstyles would work best for you. Short haircuts are a great option because they're easy to maintain and can be styled in many different ways. In this guide, we'll go over some of the best short haircuts for 60-year-old women with glasses.

Description

Short haircuts are a popular choice for women of all ages, but they're especially great for those over 60. As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose volume, which can make longer styles look limp and lifeless. Short haircuts, on the other hand, can add volume and texture to thinning hair, making it look fuller and more youthful.

How to

The first step in getting a short haircut is to find a style that suits your face shape and hair texture. Some popular short haircuts for 60-year-old women with glasses include the pixie cut, the bob, and the layered haircut. Once you've chosen a style, it's time to find a hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want.

Step by step

1. Choose a short haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture.

2. Find a hairstylist who has experience with short haircuts for older women.

3. Show your hairstylist pictures of the style you want.

4. Discuss any concerns you have with your hairstylist, such as thinning hair or a sensitive scalp.

5. Let your hairstylist work their magic!

Tips

When getting a short haircut, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure you choose a style that complements your glasses. For example, if you have thick frames, a short pixie cut might not be the best choice. Second, consider adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth. Finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: No! Short haircuts can actually make you look more youthful, especially if they add volume and texture to your hair.

Q: How often do I need to get my short haircut trimmed?

A: This depends on the style of your haircut and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should plan on getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

Q: Can I still wear my glasses with a short haircut?

A: Absolutely! In fact, short haircuts can be a great way to show off your glasses and make them a statement accessory.

Hair Care

Once you've gotten your short haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. If you have thinning hair, consider using a volumizing spray or mousse to add body and texture.

Overall, short haircuts are a great option for 60-year-old women with glasses. They're easy to maintain, can add volume and texture to thinning hair, and can be styled in many different ways. So why not give it a try and see how a short haircut can transform your look?
