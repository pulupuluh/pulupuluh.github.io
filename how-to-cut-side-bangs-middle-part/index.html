How To Cut Side Bangs Middle Part A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 18, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to switch things up? One easy way to do that is by cutting side bangs with a middle part. This look is chic, trendy, and perfect for those who want to frame their face without committing to a full fringe. In this post, we’ll guide you through the steps of cutting your own side bangs with a middle part.

How to Cut Side Bangs Middle Part: Step by Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, you’ll need to gather some essential tools. You’ll need a comb, sharp scissors, and a mirror. Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid any snagging or pulling of your hair.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Start by parting your hair down the middle. Use your comb to create a clean and straight part.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Use your comb to create a triangle shape by starting at the top of your head and bringing the comb down to the middle of your forehead.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Hold the hair you’ve sectioned off between your index and middle finger. Use your scissors to cut at an angle, starting from the center of your forehead and moving towards the outer corner of your eye. Be sure to cut in small increments to avoid making any mistakes.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you’ve made your initial cut, release your hair and check your progress in the mirror. If you’re happy with the length, you’re done! If you want to cut more, repeat steps 4 and 5 until you’re satisfied.

Step 6: Style Your Bangs

Once your bangs are cut, you can style them however you’d like. You can blow dry them straight, curl them, or leave them natural.

Tips for Cutting Side Bangs Middle Part

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you cut your side bangs with a middle part:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always cut more, but you can’t put hair back once it’s cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid any snagging or pulling of your hair.
  • Take your time and cut in small increments to avoid making any mistakes.
  • Don’t cut your bangs too short. It’s better to have longer bangs that you can trim later than to have bangs that are too short.

FAQs About Cutting Side Bangs Middle Part

Can I cut my own side bangs with a middle part?

Yes, you can! With the right tools and technique, cutting your own side bangs with a middle part is easy and doable.

How do I know how much hair to cut?

Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always cut more, but you can’t put hair back once it’s cut. Also, consider your hair texture and face shape when deciding how much to cut.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my bangs?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic! You can always trim your bangs later or go to a hairstylist for help.

Hair Care After Cutting Side Bangs Middle Part

Now that you’ve cut your bangs, it’s important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to keep them clean and fresh.
  • Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your bangs as desired.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to avoid split ends and maintain their shape.

By following these tips, you can keep your side bangs with a middle part looking great and feeling healthy.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to cut side bangs with a middle part, you can switch up your hairstyle and create a trendy new look. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow our tips for the best results. Happy cutting!
