How To Keep Nappy Hair Straight Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have nappy hair, you know how challenging it can be to keep it straight. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the sleek, straight look you desire. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to keep nappy hair straight.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Keep Nappy Hair Straight
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Keeping Nappy Hair Straight
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Nappy Hair

How to Keep Nappy Hair Straight

The key to keeping nappy hair straight is to use the right tools and techniques. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup that might interfere with straightening.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from hot tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to straighten it out as much as possible.
  4. Use a flat iron on small sections of your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a comb to help guide the flat iron through your hair.
  5. Once you've straightened all of your hair, apply a smoothing serum or oil to keep it looking sleek and shiny.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed guide on how to keep nappy hair straight:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow-dry it first.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.
  3. Apply a heat protectant to each section of hair.
  4. Plug in your flat iron and set it to the appropriate temperature for your hair type. If you have fine or damaged hair, use a lower temperature setting.
  5. Take a section of hair and clamp the flat iron down at the roots. Slowly glide the flat iron down the length of the hair, using a comb to guide it if necessary.
  6. Repeat this process on all of the sections of hair until all of your hair is straightened.
  7. Apply a smoothing serum or oil to your hair to keep it looking sleek and shiny.

Tips for Keeping Nappy Hair Straight

Here are some tips to help you keep your nappy hair straight:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates for even heat distribution.
  • Don't use too much heat on your hair. Use the lowest temperature setting that will straighten your hair.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf at night to prevent frizz and preserve the straightness.
  • Avoid getting your hair wet or sweating excessively, as this can cause it to revert back to its natural texture.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping nappy hair straight:

Can I straighten nappy hair without using heat?

Yes, you can use a hair straightening brush or a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to straighten your hair without using heat. You can also try a keratin treatment or a chemical relaxer to permanently straighten your hair.

How often should I straighten my nappy hair?

It's best to limit heat styling to once a week to prevent damage to your hair. If you want to keep your hair straight for longer, wrap it in a silk or satin scarf at night to preserve the straightness.

What products should I use to keep my nappy hair straight?

You should use a clarifying shampoo, a heat protectant, a smoothing serum or oil, and a silk or satin scarf to keep your nappy hair straight.

Hair Care for Nappy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your nappy hair:

  • Wash your hair once a week with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Deep condition your hair once a month to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf at night to prevent breakage and preserve moisture.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your nappy hair looking sleek and straight. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage and preserve its natural texture.
