How To Get Volume While Straightening Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Straightening hair is a great way to achieve a sleek, polished look for any occasion. However, sometimes straightening hair can leave it looking flat and lifeless. If you're looking to add volume to your straightened hair, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to achieve the look you want. In this article, we'll go over how to get volume while straightening hair, as well as some common questions and tips for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Volume While Straightening Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Adding Volume to Straightened Hair
  • FAQs

How to Get Volume While Straightening Hair

There are a few different methods you can use to add volume to your straightened hair. Here are some of the most effective:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

One of the easiest ways to add volume to your hair is to start with the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed to add volume and body to hair. These products typically contain ingredients that help plump up the hair shaft, making your hair look fuller and thicker.

2. Use Root-Lifting Products

Another way to add volume to your straightened hair is to use root-lifting products. These products are designed to be applied to the roots of your hair, where they help lift the hair away from the scalp. This creates the illusion of fuller, more voluminous hair.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

When you blow dry your hair upside down, you're essentially flipping your hair in the opposite direction of its natural fall. This can help create volume by lifting the hair away from the scalp. To do this, simply flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.

4. Use Hot Rollers or Velcro Rollers

If you're looking for a more long-lasting solution to flat hair, consider using hot rollers or Velcro rollers. These tools can help create volume by adding curl or wave to your hair. Simply roll your hair onto the rollers and leave them in until your hair has cooled completely. When you remove the rollers, you should be left with bouncy, voluminous hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get volume while straightening hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a root-lifting product to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair upside down using a round brush to add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, section it off and straighten it using a flat iron.
  5. If you want even more volume, use hot rollers or Velcro rollers to add curl or wave to your hair.
  6. Finish with a volumizing hairspray to help hold the volume in place.

Tips for Adding Volume to Straightened Hair

Here are some additional tips for adding volume to straightened hair:

  • Don't use too much product. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to add volume.
  • Blow dry your hair in sections to ensure that each section gets enough volume.
  • Set your hair with hot rollers or Velcro rollers to add more volume and bounce.
  • Use a volumizing hairspray to help hold the volume in place.

FAQs

Q: How often can I straighten my hair?

A: It's generally best to limit straightening to once a week or less to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How do I prevent heat damage when straightening my hair?

A: Use a heat protectant spray before straightening and make sure to use a lower heat setting on your flat iron.

Q: How can I keep my hair straight for longer?

A: Use a hairspray to help hold your style in place and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve voluminous, straightened hair that looks healthy and polished. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep it looking its best.
