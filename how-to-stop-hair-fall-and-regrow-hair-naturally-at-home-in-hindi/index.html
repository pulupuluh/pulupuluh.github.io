How to Stop Hair Fall and Regrow Hair Naturally at Home in Hindi

Hair fall can be caused by a variety of factors such as stress, poor nutrition, hormonal imbalances, and genetics. While there are many commercial products available in the market that claim to prevent hair fall and promote hair regrowth, they often come with side effects and can be expensive. Instead, you can try these natural remedies to stop hair fall and regrow hair at home.

1. Amla

Amla or Indian gooseberry is a rich source of vitamin C, which is essential for hair growth. It also contains antioxidants that help prevent hair fall and premature greying. You can mix amla powder with coconut oil and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for an hour and then rinse it off with a mild shampoo.

2. Onion Juice

Onion juice contains sulfur, which helps improve blood circulation to the scalp and stimulate hair growth. It also has antibacterial properties that can help prevent scalp infections that can lead to hair fall. Extract onion juice and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then wash it off with a mild shampoo.

3. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is rich in fatty acids that nourish the scalp and promote hair growth. It also contains lauric acid, which has antimicrobial properties that help prevent scalp infections. Massage warm coconut oil onto your scalp and leave it on overnight. Wash it off with a mild shampoo in the morning.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to use these natural remedies to stop hair fall and regrow hair at home:

  1. Mix amla powder with coconut oil and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for an hour and then rinse it off with a mild shampoo.
  2. Extract onion juice and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then wash it off with a mild shampoo.
  3. Massage warm coconut oil onto your scalp and leave it on overnight. Wash it off with a mild shampoo in the morning.

Tips for Hair Care

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall and promote hair regrowth:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in protein, iron, and vitamin C.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair and limit the use of hair products that contain harsh chemicals.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQs on Hair Fall and Regrowth

1. How much hair fall is normal?

It is normal to lose 50 to 100 strands of hair per day. However, if you are losing more than that, it could be a sign of hair fall.

2. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress can cause hair fall by disrupting the natural hair growth cycle.

3. Can hair regrow after falling?

Yes, hair can regrow after falling if the hair follicles are still intact.

In conclusion, you can stop hair fall and promote hair regrowth naturally at home by using these simple remedies and following a healthy hair care routine. Remember, patience is key as it may take some time to see results. Follow these tips consistently and you will have healthy and lustrous hair in no time!
