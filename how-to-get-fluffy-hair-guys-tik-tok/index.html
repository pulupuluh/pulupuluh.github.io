How To Get Fluffy Hair Guys Tik Tok

Written by William Mar 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Fluffy hair has been a trend for a while now, and TikTok has been a hub for all the latest hair trends. If you're a guy and want to achieve the perfect fluffy hair look, this step-by-step guide is for you. In this article, we'll be sharing tips and tricks to help you get that trendy fluffy hair. So, let's get started!

How to Get Fluffy Hair Guys Tik Tok:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to getting fluffy hair is to make sure your hair is clean. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product build-up from your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Use a Blow Dryer

After washing your hair, towel dry it gently. Then, use a blow dryer to dry your hair while brushing it with a round brush. This will help to add volume to your hair and create that fluffy look. Make sure you use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair with the blow dryer.

Step 3: Use a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or hair wax. This will help to hold your hair in place and add texture to the hair. Make sure you distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

To achieve the fluffy hair look, you need to tease your hair. Use a fine-tooth comb to gently backcomb your hair at the roots. This will create volume and lift at the crown of your head.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your fluffy hair in place, finish with a strong-hold hairspray. This will help to hold the style in place and prevent it from falling flat.

Tips:

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra volume to your hair.

- Don't overdo the teasing as it can damage your hair.

- Try different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ:

Q: How long will the fluffy hair last?

A: The duration of the fluffy hair depends on the type of hair you have and the quality of the styling products you use. However, the fluffy hair look can last up to 24 hours if you use a strong-hold hairspray.

Q: Can I achieve the fluffy hair look without using a blow dryer?

A: It's difficult to achieve the fluffy hair look without using a blow dryer. However, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to maintain your natural curls while adding volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care:

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture. Here are some hair care tips:

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

- Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to get fluffy hair guys Tik Tok, it's time to try it out for yourself. Follow these steps, and you'll be rocking the trendy fluffy hair look in no time!
