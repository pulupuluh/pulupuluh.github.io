How to Cut Short Hair at Home Video

Before we begin, make sure you have the proper tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also want to have a mirror and a towel handy.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. It is important to have clean, dry hair before you begin cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back. Clip each section out of the way.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the back section first. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the scissors vertically and make small snips. Work your way around the head until you have cut all the back sections.

Next, move on to the front sections. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the scissors horizontally and cut the hair straight across. Repeat this process until you have cut all of the front sections.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting your hair, check for evenness. Use your comb to comb your hair straight and check for any uneven sections. If you find any, make small snips to even them out.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You may want to use a hair straightener, curling iron, or other styling tools to achieve your desired look.

Tips

  • Start with small snips and work your way up. It is easier to cut more hair than it is to fix a mistake.
  • Always cut your hair when it is dry. Wet hair can be misleading and make it difficult to cut evenly.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • If you have curly hair, be cautious when cutting. Curly hair tends to bounce up and may appear shorter than it actually is.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my hair with kitchen scissors?

No, it is not recommended to cut your hair with kitchen scissors. Kitchen scissors are not sharp enough and can cause damage to your hair.

Can I cut my hair without a comb?

No, it is recommended to use a comb when cutting your hair. A comb helps to section your hair and ensures an even cut.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly to maintain a healthy look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Cutting your hair at home can be a great way to save money and maintain your hair's health. With these tips and step-by-step guide, you'll be a pro in no time!
