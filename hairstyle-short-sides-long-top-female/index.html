Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top Female

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle that's easy to maintain, the short sides long top female hairstyle might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years, especially among women who want to experiment with their hair.

Description

The short sides long top female hairstyle is characterized by short hair on the sides and long hair on the top. This hairstyle can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, making it a versatile option for women of all ages.

How to

To achieve the short sides long top female hairstyle, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist. They'll be able to help you determine the best length and style for your hair. Once you have your hair cut, you can experiment with different styling techniques to achieve the look you want.

Step by Step

Here's how you can style your short sides long top female haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the top section.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and rub them together.
  4. Run your fingers through the top section of your hair, pulling it up and away from your face to create a messy, textured look.
  5. Use a hair clip to pin back the sides of your hair, keeping them short and sleek.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short sides long top female hairstyle:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, or scarves to add some extra flair to your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short sides long top female hairstyle:

1. Is the short sides long top female hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit any face shape.

2. How often do I need to visit the salon to maintain this hairstyle?

You should visit the salon every 4 to 6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

3. Can I style my short sides long top female haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can experiment with different styling techniques to achieve the look you want.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your short sides long top female hairstyle looking its best. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to minimize damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach and hair dyes, which can weaken your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
