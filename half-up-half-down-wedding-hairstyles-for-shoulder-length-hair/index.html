Half Up Half Down Wedding Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Eveline Mar 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have shoulder length hair and are looking for the perfect wedding hairstyle, half up half down may be just what you need. This versatile style allows you to show off your length while still keeping your hair out of your face. In this article, we will go over some tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect half up half down wedding hairstyle for shoulder length hair.

Description

Half up half down wedding hairstyles are popular because they provide the best of both worlds. You get the elegance of an updo with the freedom and length of wearing your hair down. Shoulder length hair is perfect for this hairstyle because it's long enough to style, but not too long that it becomes unmanageable. Whether you want a sleek and sophisticated look, or something more relaxed and bohemian, there's a half up half down wedding hairstyle that will suit your style.

How to

To achieve a half up half down wedding hairstyle for shoulder length hair, you will need a few basic styling tools. These include a brush or comb, bobby pins, and hair elastics. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing your hair and applying your favorite styling products. Blow dry your hair or let it air dry, depending on your preference. For added volume, use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves.

Step 2: Section your hair

Take a section of hair from each side of your head, starting at your temples. Pull the sections back and secure them with a hair elastic at the back of your head. This will create the half up portion of your hairstyle.

Step 3: Add volume

For added volume, gently tease the crown of your hair with a comb. This will create a subtle bump and add height to your hairstyle.

Step 4: Pin it up

Take the two sections of hair you pulled back in step 2 and twist them together. Pull the twist up and over the hair elastic, securing it with bobby pins. Make sure to hide the elastic and pins for a polished look.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To ensure your hairstyle stays in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will also add shine and hold to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect half up half down wedding hairstyle for shoulder length hair:

  • Experiment with different twists and braids to create a unique look
  • Use a hair accessory, such as a flower crown or crystal hair clip, to add some sparkle to your hairstyle
  • Consider adding extensions if you want more length or volume
  • Practice your hairstyle a few times before the wedding day to ensure you get it just right

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about half up half down wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

Q: Can I wear a veil with a half up half down hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can wear a veil with a half up half down hairstyle. Simply attach the veil to the back of your hairstyle using bobby pins.

Q: How do I choose the right hairstyle for my wedding?

A: Consider your dress style, wedding theme, and personal style when choosing your wedding hairstyle. It's also a good idea to consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best for your wedding day, be sure to follow a consistent hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using heat protectant when styling, and avoiding harsh chemicals and treatments. You can also consider using a deep conditioning treatment or hair mask once a week to keep your locks nourished and hydrated.

Conclusion

Half up half down wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair are a beautiful and versatile option for brides. With a little bit of practice and the right styling tools, you can achieve a stunning hairstyle that will last all day. Remember to take care of your hair leading up to your wedding day, and don't be afraid to experiment with different twists, braids, and accessories to make your hairstyle your own.
