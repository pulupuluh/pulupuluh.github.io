How To Make Your Face Look Thinner With Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are looking for ways to make your face look thinner, you might be surprised to know that your hair can make a significant difference. By choosing the right hairstyle, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face. In this article, we will discuss how to make your face look thinner with hair.

Description

When it comes to making your face look thinner, it's all about creating the right balance. You want to create volume on top of your head while keeping the sides of your face sleek and slim. The key is to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face as this will only make your face look wider.

How To

Here are some tips on how to make your face look thinner with hair:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Avoid blunt cuts and instead opt for soft layers that frame your face. This will help to create a softer, more feminine look.

2. Side Part

A side part can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Part your hair on the side and let it fall naturally over your forehead. This will create a diagonal line that will draw attention away from the width of your face.

3. High Ponytail

A high ponytail can help to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. Pull your hair up into a high ponytail and tie it with a hair elastic. This will lift your hair up and away from your face, creating a more elongated look.

4. Bangs

Bangs can help to frame your face and draw attention away from the width of your face. Opt for side-swept bangs that create a diagonal line across your forehead. This will help to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Step by Step

Here are the steps on how to make your face look thinner with hair:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. This will help you to choose the right hairstyle. If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. If you have a square face, you want to soften your features.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Opt for long layers, a side part, a high ponytail, or bangs. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Step 3: Use Hair Products

Use hair products to create volume on top of your head. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots. This will help to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair according to the hairstyle you have chosen. Use a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head, or use a straightener to create sleek, straight hair. Use a hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to make your face look thinner with hair:

1. Avoid Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can make your face look wider. If you have a round face, opt for a longer hairstyle that falls below your chin.

2. Highlight Your Cheekbones

Highlight your cheekbones to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Use a highlighter or bronzer to accentuate your cheekbones.

3. Tie Your Hair Back

Tie your hair back to create a sleek, slim look. Use a hair tie to pull your hair back into a ponytail or a bun.

FAQ

Q: Can hair color make my face look thinner?

A: Yes, hair color can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Opt for highlights or lowlights that create depth and dimension.

Q: Can I make my face look thinner with makeup?

A: Yes, makeup can help to contour your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face. Use a bronzer to contour your cheekbones and temples.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using a quality shampoo and conditioner. Use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools, and avoid using too much product. Keep your hair healthy and hydrated by using a hair mask once a week.

With these tips, you can make your face look thinner with hair. Remember to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape, use hair products to create volume, and style your hair according to the look you want to achieve. With a little practice, you can create a more elongated, slimmer look.
