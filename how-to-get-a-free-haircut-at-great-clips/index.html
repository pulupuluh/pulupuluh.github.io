How to Get a Free Haircut at Great Clips

Great Clips is a hair salon that offers affordable prices for men, women, and children. However, you can get a free haircut at Great Clips by taking advantage of their promotions and special events. Here are some ways to get a free haircut at Great Clips:

  • Download the Great Clips app and sign up for their rewards program. You'll receive a free haircut for every nine haircuts you get.
  • Check their website or social media pages for special promotions and events, such as their annual "Thank a Veteran" event where veterans can receive a free haircut on Veterans Day.
  • Participate in their charity events or donate to their partner organizations. Some of their events, such as their "Back-to-School" event, offer free haircuts for those who donate school supplies.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to Great Clips and want to know how to get a free haircut, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Download the Great Clips app from the App Store or Google Play.
  2. Sign up for their rewards program by creating an account and providing your email address and phone number.
  3. Check the app for any available rewards or promotions.
  4. Visit a Great Clips salon and show your rewards or promotion code to the stylist when checking out.

Tips for Hair Care

Getting a free haircut is great, but taking care of your hair is important too. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a free haircut at Great Clips:

  • Can I get a free haircut without signing up for the rewards program?
  • It's possible to receive a free haircut through their special promotions and events, but signing up for their rewards program is the most reliable way to receive free haircuts.

  • How often can I receive a free haircut through the rewards program?
  • You'll receive a free haircut for every nine haircuts you get.

  • Do I need to bring anything to the salon to get my free haircut?
  • You'll need to show your rewards or promotion code to the stylist when checking out.

  • What if I don't have a smartphone or can't download the app?
  • You can still sign up for the rewards program online and receive rewards through email and text message.

Now that you know how to get a free haircut at Great Clips, you can save some money on your next haircut without sacrificing quality. Don't forget to take care of your hair by following our tips for hair care, and enjoy your new look!
