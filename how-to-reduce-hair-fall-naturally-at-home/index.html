How To Reduce Hair Fall Naturally At Home A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Hair fall can be a nightmare for many people, especially when it leads to thinning hair and bald spots. While there are many hair fall treatments available in the market, they can often be harsh on your hair and scalp, leading to further damage. Fortunately, there are natural remedies that you can try at home to reduce hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. In this article, we will guide you through the process of reducing hair fall naturally at home.

Table of Content

  • How to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally at Home
  • Hair Care Tips to Reduce Hair Fall
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally at Home

Here are some natural remedies that you can try at home to reduce hair fall:

Egg Mask

Eggs are a rich source of protein, which is essential for healthy hair growth. To make an egg mask, beat one or two eggs and apply the mixture to your hair and scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then rinse it off with cold water. Use this mask once a week.

Aloe Vera

Aloe vera has natural moisturizing and soothing properties that can help reduce hair fall. Apply fresh aloe vera gel to your hair and scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it off with water. Repeat this once a week.

Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a great natural remedy for hair fall as it contains fatty acids that nourish and strengthen hair follicles. Massage warm coconut oil into your hair and scalp and leave it on overnight before washing it off with a mild shampoo. Do this once a week.

Onion Juice

Onion juice contains sulfur, which is essential for healthy hair growth. Apply freshly squeezed onion juice to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo. Repeat this once a week.

Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek seeds contain protein and nicotinic acid, which can help strengthen hair follicles and reduce hair fall. Soak fenugreek seeds in water overnight and grind them into a paste the next morning. Apply the paste to your hair and scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with water. Use this once a week.

Hair Care Tips to Reduce Hair Fall

Here are some tips to help you reduce hair fall:

Eat a Balanced Diet

A balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to include plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein in your diet.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Avoid using these tools as much as possible.

Use a Mild Shampoo

Using a mild shampoo can help reduce hair fall by keeping your scalp clean and free of dirt and oil. Avoid using shampoos that contain harsh chemicals.

Limit Chemical Treatments

Chemical treatments like hair dyes and perms can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Limit these treatments as much as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How long does it take to see results?

A. It may take several weeks or months to see the results of natural remedies for hair fall. Consistency is key, so make sure to stick to your hair care routine.

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall. Try to manage your stress levels through exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.

Q. Are there any natural supplements that can help reduce hair fall?

A. Yes, supplements like biotin, vitamin E, and iron can help reduce hair fall and promote healthy hair growth.

Reducing hair fall naturally at home requires patience and consistency, but the results are worth it. Try incorporating these natural remedies and hair care tips into your routine to achieve healthy, luscious locks.
