How To Know If You Have An Oval Face A Guide To Identifying Your Face Shape

Written by William Aug 15, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking to determine your face shape, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing how to know if you have an oval face. This face shape is considered to be one of the most versatile and balanced, and it's important to know if you have it so you can choose the right hairstyles and makeup to enhance your features.

  • Hair Care for Oval Faces

Description

The oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions and gentle curves. It's longer than it is wide, with a slightly wider forehead and a gently rounded chin. This face shape is considered to be one of the most versatile, as it can pull off a wide range of hairstyles and makeup looks.

How to Determine if You Have an Oval Face

The first step in identifying your face shape is to pull your hair back away from your face and examine your features in a mirror. Look for the following characteristics:

  • A face length that is roughly one and a half times the width of your face
  • A gently rounded hairline
  • A slightly wider forehead than jawline
  • Cheekbones that are the widest part of your face
  • A gently rounded chin

If you have all of these characteristics, you likely have an oval face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Identifying Your Face Shape

If you're still unsure about your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back away from your face.
  2. Measure the width of your forehead at its widest point.
  3. Measure the width of your jawline at its widest point.
  4. Measure the distance from your hairline to the tip of your chin.
  5. Compare your measurements:
    • If your forehead and jawline are roughly the same width, and your face length is about one and a half times the width of your face, you likely have an oval face shape.
    • If your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your face length is longer than one and a half times the width of your face, you likely have a heart-shaped face.
    • If your jawline is wider than your forehead, and your face length is longer than one and a half times the width of your face, you likely have a pear-shaped face.
    • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width, and your face length is longer than one and a half times the width of your face, you likely have a square face.
    • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width, and your face length is about the same as the width of your face, you likely have a round face.

Tips for Enhancing Your Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck – this face shape is considered to be one of the most versatile, and you can pull off a wide range of hairstyles and makeup looks. Here are some tips for enhancing your features:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles – oval faces look great with long or short hair, and you can play around with bangs or layers to enhance your features.
  • Choose makeup looks that enhance your natural features – oval faces look great with bold lip colors or dramatic eye makeup.
  • Consider wearing statement jewelry – oval faces look great with statement earrings or necklaces that draw attention to your features.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles look best on oval faces?

Oval faces can pull off a wide range of hairstyles, from long and straight to short and curly. Consider trying out bangs or layers to enhance your features.

What makeup looks best on oval faces?

Oval faces look great with bold lip colors or dramatic eye makeup. Play up your natural features with a pop of color or a bold eyeliner look.

What jewelry looks best on oval faces?

Oval faces look great with statement earrings or necklaces that draw attention to your features. Consider trying out bold geometric shapes or bright colors.

Hair Care for Oval Faces

If you have an oval face shape, you'll want to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained to enhance your features. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible – if you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and hair products to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.

Now that you know how to identify an oval face shape, you can choose the right hairstyles and makeup looks to enhance your features. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
