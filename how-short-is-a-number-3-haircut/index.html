How Short Is A Number 3 Haircut

Written by Petter Mar 15, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a short and low-maintenance haircut that's easy to maintain, a number 3 haircut might be just what you need. But how short is a number 3 haircut exactly? In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this popular haircut style.

Description of a Number 3 Haircut

A number 3 haircut is one of the shorter haircuts you can get. It typically involves using a clipper guard that's 3/8 of an inch long, which results in hair that's about 3/8 of an inch long all over the head. This is a versatile haircut that can work for many different hair types and face shapes.

How to Get a Number 3 Haircut

If you're interested in getting a number 3 haircut, you'll want to visit a barber or stylist who's experienced in using clippers. Here's how the process usually works:

  1. The barber will start by washing and drying your hair, if necessary.
  2. They'll then use a clipper with a number 3 guard to trim your hair all over your head.
  3. They may also use scissors to clean up any stray hairs or to add texture to your hair.
  4. Finally, they'll style your hair using a product of your choice.

The entire process usually takes around 30 minutes, depending on the complexity of the cut and the barber's experience.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Number 3 Haircut

If you're more of a visual learner, here's a step-by-step guide to getting a number 3 haircut:

  1. Choose a barber or stylist who's experienced in using clippers and ask for a number 3 haircut.
  2. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  3. Sit in the barber's chair and explain exactly what you want.
  4. The barber will use a clipper with a number 3 guard to trim your hair all over your head.
  5. If necessary, they'll use scissors to clean up any stray hairs or add texture to your hair.
  6. Once the cut is complete, the barber will style your hair using a product of your choice.

Tips for Maintaining a Number 3 Haircut

Once you have a number 3 haircut, you'll want to keep it looking fresh and neat. Here are some tips for maintaining your cut:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your cut.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • If your hair tends to be oily, consider using a dry shampoo between washes.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

FAQs About Number 3 Haircuts

Here are some common questions people have about number 3 haircuts:

Q: Is a number 3 haircut good for thick hair?

A: Yes! A number 3 haircut can work well for thick hair because it's short enough to keep the hair manageable, but not so short that it exposes any scalp.

Q: How short is a number 3.5 haircut?

A: A number 3.5 haircut uses a clipper guard that's 7/16 of an inch long, which is slightly longer than a number 3 haircut.

Q: Can I get a number 3 haircut with a fade?

A: Yes! A number 3 haircut can be combined with a fade on the sides and back for a stylish and modern look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts like a number 3 can be easy to maintain, but they still require some care to keep them looking their best. Here are some additional hair care tips for short haircuts:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • If you're experiencing hair loss or thinning, talk to your doctor or a hair specialist to explore your treatment options.

Conclusion

A number 3 haircut is a short and low-maintenance haircut that can work for many different hair types and face shapes. It's easy to get and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for people who want a simple and stylish haircut. Whether you're looking for a fresh new look or just want to keep your hair neat and tidy, a number 3 haircut might be just what you need.
