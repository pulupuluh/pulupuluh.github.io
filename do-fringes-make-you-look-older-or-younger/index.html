Do Fringes Make You Look Older Or Younger

Written by Robby Sep 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering getting a fringe but wondering if it will make you look older or younger? The truth is, fringes can do both! It all depends on the type of fringe you choose and how you style it. In this article, we'll explore the different types of fringes and how they can affect your overall appearance.

Table of Contents

  • Types of Fringes
  • Fringes That Make You Look Younger
  • Fringes That Make You Look Older
  • Tips for Wearing Fringes
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Fringes

Types of Fringes

There are many different types of fringes, including:

  • Straight fringe
  • Side-swept fringe
  • Curtain fringe
  • Wispy fringe
  • Long fringe
  • Short fringe

The type of fringe you choose can have a big impact on your overall appearance. Let's take a closer look at which types of fringes can make you look younger or older.

Fringes That Make You Look Younger

If you're looking to appear younger, there are a few types of fringes that can help:

  • Side-swept fringe - This type of fringe is universally flattering and can help soften facial features, making you look younger.
  • Curtain fringe - This type of fringe is long and parted in the middle, framing the face. It can make you look more youthful and playful.
  • Wispy fringe - This type of fringe is light and airy, giving a youthful and effortless vibe.

If you want to go for a youthful look, choose one of these types of fringes and pair it with a relaxed hairstyle.

Fringes That Make You Look Older

If you're worried about looking older, there are a few types of fringes you should avoid:

  • Straight fringe - This type of fringe can emphasize forehead lines and wrinkles, making you look older.
  • Short fringe - This type of fringe can make your face look wider and more mature.
  • Long fringe - This type of fringe can hide your face and make you look older and more serious.

If you want to avoid looking older, stay away from these types of fringes and opt for a more relaxed hairstyle.

Tips for Wearing Fringes

If you decide to get a fringe, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a fringe that flatters your face shape.
  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a fringe.
  • Make sure your fringe is cut to the right length - too short or too long can be unflattering.
  • Style your fringe to suit your face shape and personal style.
  • Invest in good quality hair products to keep your fringe looking great.

FAQs

Can fringes hide wrinkles?

Straight fringes can emphasize wrinkles, but side-swept or wispy fringes can help to hide them.

Can fringes make my face look wider?

Short fringes can make your face look wider, so if you have a round face, it's best to avoid them.

Do fringes require a lot of maintenance?

Fringes do require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. You'll need to get them trimmed every 4-6 weeks and style them daily.

Hair Care for Fringes

If you have a fringe, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your fringe clean and fresh.
  • Use a small amount of shampoo and conditioner on your fringe to avoid weighing it down.
  • Blow-dry your fringe in the direction you want it to sit.
  • Use a small amount of styling product to keep your fringe in place.

With the right care and maintenance, a fringe can be a great way to update your look and make you feel younger and more stylish. Just make sure to choose the right type of fringe for your face shape and personal style.
