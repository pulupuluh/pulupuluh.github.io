Best Way To Slick Your Hair Back

Written by Daniel Jul 01, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle to complete your look? Slicking your hair back has been a timeless classic that can give you a sleek, sophisticated and stylish look. In this article, we will guide you through the best way to slick your hair back and provide you with some tips on how to maintain this hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Your Hair Back
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Slick Your Hair Back

The first thing you need to do is choose the right product. You can use a gel, pomade or wax, depending on the type of hair you have and the desired level of hold. A gel provides a strong hold and is best for thicker hair, while a pomade can give you a medium hold and works well on all hair types. Wax provides a softer hold and is ideal for thin or fine hair.

Once you have chosen the right product, you need to ensure your hair is clean and dry. Apply the product to your hair by starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. Use a comb to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to slick your hair back:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply the product to your hair.
  3. Use a comb to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.
  4. Use a brush to slick your hair back.
  5. Smooth out any bumps or lumps with your hands.
  6. Use hairspray to set your hair in place.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Use a comb to ensure the product is evenly distributed throughout your hair.
  • Brush your hair back in sections to ensure a smooth and even look.
  • Use hairspray to set your hair in place and prevent frizz.
  • Consider using a hair dryer to help set the product and achieve the desired look.

FAQ

Q: How long will the slicked-back style last?

A: With the right product and hair care, the slicked-back style can last all day.

Q: Can I slick back curly hair?

A: Yes, you can slick back curly hair, but you may need to use a stronger hold product and straighten your hair first.

Q: Is it necessary to use hairspray?

A: No, hairspray is not necessary, but it can help set your hair in place and prevent frizz.

Hair Care Tips

Proper hair care is essential to maintain a healthy and stylish look. Here are some hair care tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy look.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Now that you know the best way to slick your hair back, it’s time to give it a try. With the right product, technique and hair care, you can achieve a sleek and stylish look that will turn heads wherever you go.
