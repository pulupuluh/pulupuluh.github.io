Can You Cut The Sides Of Your Hair With Scissors

Description

The answer is yes, you can cut the sides of your hair with scissors. However, it's important to note that cutting your own hair can be risky and may not result in the outcome you desire. If you're confident in your ability to cut your hair, then go ahead and give it a try!

How to Cut Your Hair Sides with Scissors

If you've decided to cut your hair sides with scissors, here are a few things you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before cutting it.

2. Part your hair in the middle and clip one side away from the other.

3. Use a comb to separate a small section of hair from the side you want to cut.

4. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut the desired length. Remember to cut a little bit at a time and check your progress.

5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you've cut all the sections of hair on that side.

6. Unclip the other side of your hair and repeat steps 3-5 on that side.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Sides with Scissors

1. Start with small sections of hair and cut a little bit at a time.

2. Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

3. Cut your hair when it's completely dry to ensure accuracy.

4. If you're unsure about cutting your hair, seek professional help.

FAQs

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

A: It's not recommended. Regular scissors may not be sharp enough and can cause damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care after Cutting Your Hair Sides with Scissors

After cutting your hair sides with scissors, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage.

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to your hair.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush to avoid breakage.

4. Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Now that you know how to cut the sides of your hair with scissors, go ahead and give it a try! Just remember to take your time and be cautious when cutting your own hair. If you're not confident in your ability to cut your hair, it's always best to seek professional help.
