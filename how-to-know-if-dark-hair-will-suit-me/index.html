How To Know If Dark Hair Will Suit Me

Written by Eveline Feb 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Dark hair is always in style. It gives a classic and sophisticated look that is hard to beat. However, not everyone can pull off the look. In this article, we will discuss how to know if dark hair will suit you. We will provide you with useful tips and tricks to help you make the best decision for your next hair color change.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

There are several factors to consider when deciding if dark hair will suit you. The following are some of the things you need to take into account before making the change:

1. Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair color. If you have a warm skin tone, then dark hair will look great on you. Warm skin tones usually have yellow or golden undertones. If you have a cool skin tone, then dark hair may not be the best choice for you. Cool skin tones usually have pink or blue undertones.

2. Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining if dark hair will suit you. If you have brown or hazel eyes, then dark hair will look great on you. If you have blue or green eyes, then you may want to consider a lighter hair color.

3. Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also affect how well dark hair will suit you. If you have naturally dark hair, then you should be able to pull off the look. If you have light hair, then you may want to consider a gradual change to a darker color.

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to determine if dark hair will suit you:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

There are several ways to determine your skin tone. One way is to look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, then you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue, then you have a cool skin tone. Another way is to hold up a piece of gold jewelry and a piece of silver jewelry to your skin. If the gold looks better, then you have a warm skin tone. If the silver looks better, then you have a cool skin tone.

2. Consider Your Eye Color

Think about your eye color and whether it will complement dark hair. If you have brown or hazel eyes, then dark hair will look great on you. If you have blue or green eyes, then you may want to consider a lighter hair color.

3. Consult a Professional

If you are still unsure whether dark hair will suit you, then consult a professional hairstylist. They will be able to provide you with expert advice based on your unique features.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you determine if dark hair will suit you:

  • Consider your personality and style. Dark hair can provide a dramatic look that may not suit everyone.
  • Look at pictures of people with similar features to yours who have dark hair to see how it looks.
  • Consider the maintenance involved in maintaining dark hair. It may require more frequent touch-ups than lighter hair.

FAQ

Q: Can anyone pull off dark hair?

A: No, not everyone can pull off dark hair. It depends on your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.

Q: Will dark hair make me look older?

A: Dark hair can provide a sophisticated look that may make you look more mature. However, it does not necessarily make you look older.

Q: How can I maintain my dark hair color?

A: To maintain your dark hair color, use color-safe shampoo and conditioner and avoid excessive heat styling.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you decide to go with dark hair or not, it is important to take care of your hair. Use quality hair care products and avoid excessive heat styling. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

In conclusion, deciding whether dark hair will suit you requires consideration of various factors. Follow the tips and tricks outlined in this article to help you make the best decision for your unique features. Remember to take care of your hair regardless of the hair color you choose.
