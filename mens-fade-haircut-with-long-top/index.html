Mens Fade Haircut With Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel May 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, then the mens fade haircut with long top is the perfect choice for you. This haircut is a modern take on the classic fade haircut, and it features a longer top that can be styled in various ways. In this guide, we are going to cover everything you need to know about the mens fade haircut with long top, including how to get it, style it, and take care of it.

Description

The mens fade haircut with long top is a popular hairstyle that features a gradual fade on the sides and back, while the top is left longer. The fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on your preference. The longer top allows for more flexibility in styling, and you can choose to wear it slicked back, messy, or textured. This haircut is suitable for all hair types and face shapes, making it a versatile choice for men.

How to Get Mens Fade Haircut with Long Top

The mens fade haircut with long top can be achieved by following these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Fade

The first step in getting the mens fade haircut with long top is to choose your fade. You can opt for a low fade, which starts just above the ear and tapers down to the skin, a medium fade, which starts halfway up the sides and back, or a high fade, which starts near the top of the head. The choice of fade depends on your personal preference and face shape.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of the Top

The next step is to decide on the length of the top. The longer the top, the more styling options you will have. You can choose to have a longer fringe, or you can opt for an all-around longer top that you can style in various ways.

Step 3: Start with the Fade

Once you have decided on the fade and the length of the top, your hairstylist will start with the fade. They will use clippers to create a gradual fade on the sides and back, blending it seamlessly into the longer top. The fade can be as short or as long as you want, depending on the style you're going for.

Step 4: Trim and Style the Top

After the fade is done, your hairstylist will trim and style the top according to your preference. You can choose to have it slicked back, messy, or textured. They will also use scissors to add texture and shape to the top, giving it more volume and movement.

Tips for Styling Mens Fade Haircut with Long Top

Here are some tips for styling your mens fade haircut with long top:

- Use a matte styling product for a natural, textured look. - Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go for better control. - Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and movement in the top. - Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Is the mens fade haircut with long top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the mens fade haircut with long top is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I get a mens fade haircut with long top?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the fade grows out. Generally, you should get a trim every 2-4 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Can I style my mens fade haircut with long top differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can style your mens fade haircut with long top differently for different occasions. For a casual look, you can wear it messy or textured, while for a formal look, you can slick it back or style it into a side part.

Hair Care for Mens Fade Haircut with Long Top

To maintain your mens fade haircut with long top, you should:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Use a nourishing hair oil or serum to keep the top hydrated and healthy. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair and cause split ends. - Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

By following these tips, you can keep your mens fade haircut with long top looking stylish and trendy.
