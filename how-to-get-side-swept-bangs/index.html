How To Get Side Swept Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Side swept bangs are a timeless hairstyle that can transform your look in an instant. Whether you have short or long hair, this style can add a touch of elegance and playfulness to your overall appearance. In this tutorial, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to get side swept bangs at home.

How to Get Side Swept Bangs

Before we jump into the tutorial, let’s go over some of the tools and materials you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • Hair clips
  • A hairdryer

Now that you have everything you need, let’s get started!

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

The first step is to prep your hair. Wash your hair and dry it fully. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to part your hair to the side where you want your bangs to be.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, use your comb to section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Clip the rest of your hair out of the way so that you can focus on the section you’re cutting.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Now it’s time to cut your bangs. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle. Start by cutting a little bit at a time until you get the desired length. Remember, it’s easier to cut more hair than to try and fix hair that’s been cut too short.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once you’ve cut your bangs, it’s time to style them. Use your hairdryer to blow dry your bangs, using a round brush to help shape them.

Tips for Getting Side Swept Bangs

Here are a few tips to make sure you get the perfect side swept bangs:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. It’s easier to cut more later than to try and fix hair that’s been cut too short.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to get a clean cut.
  • Make sure your hair is dry before you start cutting. Wet hair can be deceiving, and you may end up cutting your hair too short.
  • Use a comb to part your hair to the side where you want your bangs to be.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to help shape them.

FAQs

Can I get side swept bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can! However, it’s best to get your bangs cut by a professional stylist who has experience working with curly hair.

How often do I need to trim my side swept bangs?

You should trim your side swept bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

Once you’ve got your side swept bangs, it’s important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your bangs every other day to prevent them from getting greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to help shape them.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners on your bangs.
  • Use a light hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Now that you know how to get side swept bangs, it’s time to try them out for yourself! Remember, it’s always best to start by cutting less hair than you think you need, and you can always cut more later. With a little patience and practice, you’ll have the perfect side swept bangs in no time.
