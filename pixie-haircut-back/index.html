Pixie Haircut Back How To Pull Off This Classic Style In 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 05, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that still looks chic and stylish, a pixie cut is the perfect choice. The pixie cut has been around for decades and has been worn by some of the most iconic women in Hollywood, including Audrey Hepburn, Twiggy, and Mia Farrow. In recent years, the pixie cut has made a comeback and is now one of the most popular hairstyles for women of all ages. In this article, we'll show you how to pull off the pixie haircut back, and give you some tips and tricks for keeping your new look fresh and stylish.

What is a Pixie Haircut Back?

A pixie haircut is a short, cropped hairstyle that typically features short layers on top and closely-cropped sides and back. The pixie cut has been a popular choice for women for decades, and it's a classic style that never goes out of fashion. The pixie haircut back is a variation of the classic pixie cut, where the back of the hair is cut even shorter, creating a more dramatic, edgy look.

How to Get a Pixie Haircut Back

If you're ready to take the plunge and try out a pixie haircut back, there are a few things you should know before you head to the salon. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect pixie cut:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Before you head to the salon, spend some time browsing through magazines and online galleries to find inspiration for your new look. Look for pictures of women with similar face shapes and hair textures to yours to get an idea of what your new haircut will look like.

Step 2: Choose Your Stylist

Not all stylists are created equal when it comes to cutting short hair, so it's important to find a stylist who has experience with pixie cuts. Ask friends and family members for recommendations, or search online for reviews of local salons.

Step 3: Consult with Your Stylist

Before your stylist starts cutting, it's important to have a consultation to discuss your goals and expectations for your new haircut. Be honest with your stylist about your lifestyle, your hair texture, and your styling preferences so they can create a look that works for you.

Step 4: Get the Cut

Once you and your stylist have agreed on the perfect pixie cut for you, it's time to get the cut. The stylist will start by sectioning off your hair and cutting it to the desired length, typically starting with the back and working their way up to the top.

Step 5: Style Your New Look

Once your haircut is complete, your stylist will show you how to style your new look. Depending on your hair texture and styling preferences, you may need to use a styling product or two to create the perfect look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Haircut Back

Now that you've got your new pixie haircut back, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

Tip 1: Keep Up with Regular Trims

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, you'll need to schedule regular trims every four to six weeks. This will keep your hair looking neat and tidy and prevent split ends from forming.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your pixie cut looks and feels. Look for products that are specifically designed for short hair, such as texturizing sprays, pomades, and waxes.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Styles

Just because you have a short haircut doesn't mean you're limited to just one style. Experiment with different looks, such as adding a side part, spiking up the front, or slicking your hair back.

FAQs

Q: Will a pixie haircut back suit my face shape?

A: Pixie cuts can be flattering on almost any face shape, but it's important to work with a stylist who can customize the cut to suit your features.

Q: Will a pixie haircut back work with my hair texture?

A: Pixie cuts can work with a variety of hair textures, but if you have very curly or thick hair, you may need to have your hair thinned out to avoid a bulky look.

Q: Can I still style my pixie cut for special occasions?

A: Absolutely! With the right styling products and techniques, you can create a variety of different looks for special occasions.

Hair Care for Pixie Haircut Back

When it comes to caring for your pixie cut, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for short hair. Look for lightweight shampoos and conditioners that won't weigh your hair down, and use a texturizing spray or wax to create volume and definition. To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
