How To Fashionably Wear A Beanie In 2023

Written by Bardi Aug 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Beanies have become a staple accessory for both men and women. They are versatile and can be worn in different styles to suit any occasion. However, not everyone knows how to wear a beanie fashionably. In this article, we will give you tips on how to style your beanie to look stylish and trendy.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Beanie
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Beanie
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Wear a Beanie

There are different ways to wear a beanie, depending on your style and preference. Below are some popular ways to wear a beanie:

Slouchy Style

The slouchy style is a popular way to wear a beanie. It involves pulling the beanie back slightly to create a slouchy effect on the top. This style works well with casual outfits and looks great on both men and women.

Fitted Style

The fitted style involves wearing the beanie snugly on your head. This style is perfect for colder weather as it keeps your head warm. It looks great with a coat or a jacket and works well for both casual and formal occasions.

Cuffed Style

The cuffed style involves folding the bottom of the beanie to create a cuff. This style looks great with a bomber jacket or a leather jacket and works well for casual occasions.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to wear a beanie fashionably:

Step 1: Choose the Right Beanie

Choose a beanie that suits your style and preference. Consider the material, color, and design.

Step 2: Determine Your Head Shape

Determine your head shape to find the best style of beanie for you. If you have a round face, go for a slouchy beanie. If you have an oval face, go for a fitted beanie. If you have a square face, go for a cuffed beanie.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Prepare your hair by combing it back or tying it up. This will ensure that your hair does not interfere with the beanie.

Step 4: Put on the Beanie

Put on the beanie, making sure that it covers your ears. Adjust the beanie to create the desired style.

Tips for Wearing a Beanie

Here are some tips to help you wear a beanie fashionably:

Tip 1: Consider the Occasion

Consider the occasion when choosing a beanie style. A slouchy beanie works well for casual occasions, while a fitted beanie is more suitable for formal occasions.

Tip 2: Coordinate with Your Outfit

Coordinate the color and design of your beanie with your outfit. This will create a cohesive and stylish look.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Styles

Experiment with different beanie styles to find the one that suits you best. Try a slouchy beanie one day and a cuffed beanie the next.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a beanie with long hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a beanie with long hair. Tie your hair up in a bun or ponytail before putting on the beanie.

Q: Can I wear a beanie with glasses?

A: Yes, you can wear a beanie with glasses. Choose a beanie style that does not interfere with your glasses.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a beanie can cause your hair to become flat or static. Here are some hair care tips to prevent this:

Tip 1: Use Hair Spray

Spray some hair spray on your hair before putting on the beanie. This will help keep your hair in place.

Tip 2: Use Dry Shampoo

Sprinkle some dry shampoo on your hair before putting on the beanie. This will help absorb excess oil and prevent your hair from becoming flat.

Tip 3: Condition Your Hair

Condition your hair regularly to prevent it from becoming dry and static. Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to wear a beanie fashionably, go ahead and experiment with different styles. With the right beanie and styling, you can look stylish and trendy while keeping your head warm.
