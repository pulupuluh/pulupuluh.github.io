How To Cut Curly Hair To Make It Curlier A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Apr 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting curly hair can be a daunting task, and you may be wondering how to cut it to make it curlier. Curly hair can be unpredictable and difficult to manage, but with the right techniques, you can enhance your natural curls and achieve the look you desire. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut curly hair to make it curlier. We will also provide you with some tips and tricks to keep your curls looking healthy and vibrant.

How to Cut Curly Hair to Make it Curlier

The first step in cutting curly hair is to make sure that it is dry and detangled. This will help you see the natural curl pattern and prevent any tangles or knots from getting in the way. Once your hair is dry and detangled, follow these steps:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place. This will help you work on one section at a time and prevent any confusion.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want to cut your hair. Remember that curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so it is important to cut it slightly longer than you want it to be.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting with one section, take a small section of hair and twist it around your finger. Cut the hair at an angle, following the natural curl pattern. Continue doing this for each section until you have cut all of your hair.

Step 4: Check for Balance

After cutting your hair, check for balance by comparing each section. Make sure that they are all the same length and that the curls are even. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair accordingly.

Step 5: Add Layers (Optional)

If you want to add layers to your hair, start with the top section and work your way down. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle, following the natural curl pattern. This will add volume and dimension to your curls.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair to Make it Curlier

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your curly hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends and frizz.
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more if needed, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. This can cause shrinkage and make it difficult to see the natural curl pattern.
  • Don't cut your hair too often. Curly hair tends to be dry and fragile, so cutting it too often can cause damage.

FAQ

Can cutting curly hair make it curlier?

Yes, cutting curly hair can make it curlier. By following the natural curl pattern and cutting at an angle, you can enhance your curls and add volume and dimension.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It is recommended to cut curly hair every 3-4 months to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Can I cut my curly hair myself?

Yes, you can cut your curly hair yourself. However, it is important to follow the proper techniques and use sharp scissors to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Once you have cut your curly hair to make it curlier, it is important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause damage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair, as it can cause frizz and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can cut your curly hair to make it curlier and keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Remember to take care of your hair and embrace your natural curls!
