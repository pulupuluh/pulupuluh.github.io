How To Get Tousled Hair For Guys

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to get tousled hair for guys! If you're looking for a laid-back, effortless hairstyle that still looks put-together, tousled hair might be just what you need. In this article, we'll go over the steps to achieve this look, as well as some tips and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Tousled Hair for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Tousled Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Tousled Hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to achieve tousled hair for guys:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that work for your hair type. This will help remove any buildup and ensure that your hair is healthy and ready for styling.

Step 2: Apply texturizing product

Apply a texturizing product, such as a sea salt spray or a styling cream, to your damp hair. This will help create the tousled effect by adding volume and definition to your hair.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Use a blow-dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair, using your fingers to tousle and scrunch your hair as you go. This will help create the messy, voluminous look that you're going for.

Step 4: Use a styling product

Apply a small amount of a matte-finish styling product, such as a wax or clay, to your hair. This will help add texture and hold to your hair, keeping the tousled look in place throughout the day.

Tips for Tousled Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your tousled hair:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Use your fingers to style your hair, rather than a comb or brush.
  • Don't worry about getting every strand of hair in place - the tousled look should be a bit messy and undone.
  • Experiment with different texturizing and styling products to find what works best for your hair type and desired look.

FAQ

What hair types work best for tousled hair?

Most hair types can work well for tousled hair, although those with thicker, wavy, or curly hair may find it easier to achieve the look. If you have fine or straight hair, you may need to use more product or spend more time styling to get the desired effect.

Can tousled hair work for formal occasions?

Tousled hair can work for some formal occasions, such as a casual wedding or a dressy dinner party. However, if you're attending a more formal event, you may want to opt for a sleeker, more polished hairstyle.

How often should I wash my hair to maintain the tousled look?

It's generally best to wash your hair every other day or every few days, depending on your hair type and how quickly it gets oily. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it harder to achieve the tousled look.

Hair Care for Tousled Hair

To keep your tousled hair looking healthy and stylish, make sure to take care of your hair with regular washing, conditioning, and trimming. Use products that work well for your hair type, and avoid over-styling or using heat tools too often.

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to achieve the perfect tousled hair look for guys. Whether you're going for a casual daytime look or a more dressed-up style, tousled hair can add a touch of effortless cool to your overall appearance.
