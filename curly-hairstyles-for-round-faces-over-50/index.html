Curly Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50

Written by Frank Jul 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a woman over 50 with a round face and curly hair? If so, you may be looking for some hairstyle inspiration. Curly hair can be challenging to style, but with the right cut and products, you can create a beautiful and flattering look. In this article, we will share some tips and ideas for curly hairstyles for round faces over 50.

Description

As we age, our hair texture and thickness change, and our face shape may become more round. It can be challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters our features and makes us feel confident. Curly hair can be especially tricky, as it tends to add volume to the hair and face. However, with the right cut and styling techniques, you can create a beautiful and youthful look that enhances your natural beauty.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect curly hairstyle for a round face over 50 is to consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair. They can help you determine the best cut and style for your face shape and hair texture. Some general tips for curly hair include avoiding heavy layers, using a diffuser when blow-drying, and using products that enhance your curls.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair.
  2. Determine the best cut and style for your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Avoid heavy layers.
  4. Use a diffuser when blow-drying.
  5. Use products that enhance your curls.

Tips

When it comes to curly hairstyles for round faces over 50, there are a few tips to keep in mind. Firstly, avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for styles that add height to the crown of your head, such as a curly bob or a layered cut with volume at the top. Secondly, consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair, as this can create depth and dimension that flatters your face shape.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face with curly hair?

The best haircut for a round face with curly hair is a layered cut that adds volume at the crown of your head, such as a curly bob or a shaggy cut. Avoid heavy layers that add volume to the sides of your face.

What products are best for curly hair?

The best products for curly hair are those that enhance your natural curl pattern without weighing down your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil, which help to moisturize and define your curls.

Hair Care

Caring for curly hair can be challenging, but there are a few tips to keep in mind. Firstly, avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and frizzy. Instead, try washing your hair every 2-3 days and using a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for curly hair. Secondly, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet, as this is when it is most fragile. Finally, avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair over time.

Curly hairstyles for round faces over 50 can be beautiful and flattering, as long as you take the time to find the right cut and style for your face shape and hair texture. By following these tips and tricks, you can create a look that enhances your natural beauty and makes you feel confident and fabulous!
