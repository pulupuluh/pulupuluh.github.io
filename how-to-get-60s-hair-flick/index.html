How To Get 60S Hair Flick

Written by Bardi Apr 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a fan of the iconic 60s hair flick? This hairstyle has been popular for decades and is still a favorite among women today. Getting that perfect hair flick can be tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this look effortlessly. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about how to get 60s hair flick.

Description

The 60s hair flick is a classic hairstyle characterized by a voluminous crown and flipped-out ends. This look was made famous by celebrities like Brigitte Bardot and Twiggy and has remained popular ever since. The key to achieving this style is to create volume at the crown of your head and flip the ends of your hair outwards.

How to Get 60s Hair Flick

To get the perfect 60s hair flick, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Begin by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and use a blow dryer to add volume to the roots.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into two parts, with the top section being larger than the bottom. Clip the top section of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Curl the ends of your hair

Using a curling iron or hot rollers, curl the ends of your hair outwards. Take small sections at a time and wrap the hair around the curling iron or hot roller, making sure to curl the ends away from your face.

Step 4: Add volume to the crown

Unclip the top section of your hair and backcomb the roots to create volume at the crown. Use a comb or brush to smooth the top layer of hair over the backcombed section.

Step 5: Flip out the ends

Using a round brush, brush the ends of your hair outwards to create the flick. Hold the brush at the ends of your hair and roll it upwards, away from your face. Use a hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect 60s hair flick:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Invest in a good quality curling iron or hot rollers to ensure your curls hold their shape.
  • Backcomb your hair lightly to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a hairspray to set your hairstyle in place and prevent flyaways.

FAQ

What hair length is best for the 60s hair flick?

The 60s hair flick can be achieved on hair of any length, but it looks best on hair that is shoulder-length or longer.

Can I achieve the 60s hair flick without using hot tools?

Yes, you can achieve the 60s hair flick without using hot tools. Simply section your hair and twist the ends outwards before setting the style in place with hairspray.

Hair Care

To maintain your 60s hair flick, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Conclusion

The 60s hair flick is a timeless hairstyle that can be achieved with the right tools and techniques. Follow our steps and tips to get the perfect hair flick and maintain your style with proper hair care.
