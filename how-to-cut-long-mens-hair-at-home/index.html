How To Cut Long Men s Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting your own hair at home can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can save you time and money. In this guide, we will show you how to cut long men's hair at home. Whether you're looking to tidy up your current style or go for a completely new look, we've got you covered. Read on for our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

Tools You Will Need

The first step in cutting your own hair at home is to gather the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Clippers with various guard sizes
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Mirror

Preparation

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to prepare it properly. This will ensure that you get the best results possible.

  • Wash your hair and towel dry it. Wet hair is easier to cut and style.
  • Comb your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.
  • Decide on the length and style you want to achieve. It helps to have a picture or reference to guide you.

Sectioning Your Hair

Sectioning your hair is important to ensure that you cut it evenly and don't miss any sections. Follow these steps:

  • Divide your hair into four sections: top, sides, and back.
  • Clip each section to keep them separate.
  • Starting with the back section, unclip a small section of hair and comb it straight down.
  • Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length.
  • Continue working through each section until all of your hair has been cut.

Cutting Your Hair

Now that you have sectioned your hair, it's time to start cutting. Follow these steps:

  • Begin with the longest guard on your clippers and work your way down to shorter guards until you achieve the desired length.
  • Use the clippers to cut the back and sides of your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a comb to guide the clippers.
  • For the top section, use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Start by cutting small sections and work your way through the whole top section.

Trimming Your Hair

Once you have cut your hair, it's time to trim any uneven or stray hairs. Follow these steps:

  • Use your scissors to trim any longer sections that you missed.
  • Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it is even and balanced.
  • Trim the hair around your ears and the back of your neck.

Styling Your Hair

Now that you have cut and trimmed your hair, it's time to style it. Follow these steps:

  • Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as gel or wax.
  • Style your hair using your fingers or a comb.
  • Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it looks the way you want it to.

Tips for Cutting Long Men's Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results when cutting your own hair at home:

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Take breaks and step back from the mirror to avoid getting too close and cutting too much hair.
  • Use a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair wet and easier to manage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long men's hair at home:

  • Can I cut my hair without clippers? Yes, you can use only scissors to cut your hair, but it may be more difficult to achieve an even cut.
  • How often should I cut my hair? This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it becomes unruly. As a general rule, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame.
  • What if I make a mistake? Don't worry, mistakes happen. If you accidentally cut too much hair or make an uneven cut, try to blend it in with the rest of your hair or wait for it to grow out.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner regularly.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and avoid split ends.

Cutting long men's hair at home can be a challenging but rewarding experience. With the right tools, techniques, and care, you can achieve a great haircut without leaving your house. Follow our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice to get the best results possible.
