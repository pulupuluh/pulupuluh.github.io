Why Is My Hair Getting Curly

Written by Aprilia Aug 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have noticed that your hair is getting curly, you may be wondering why. There are several reasons why hair can become curly, and it is important to understand the underlying causes so that you can take steps to manage your hair effectively.

Description

Hair texture is determined by the shape of the hair follicle. Straight hair has a round follicle, while curly hair has an oval-shaped follicle. When hair is relaxed, the follicle is temporarily straightened with chemicals, but over time, the follicle can become damaged, and the hair may revert to its natural curly state.

How to

If you want to maintain your straightened hair, it is important to take good care of it. This includes using a deep conditioner regularly, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a silk pillowcase to prevent friction while sleeping. If you want to embrace your natural curls, you may need to adjust your hair care routine to accommodate your new texture.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can take to manage your curly hair:

Step 1: Use a gentle shampoo

Curly hair can be dry and prone to breakage, so it is important to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Condition your hair regularly

Curly hair needs moisture, so make sure to use a deep conditioner at least once a week. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated throughout the day.

Step 3: Use a wide-tooth comb

Curly hair can be easily damaged by brushing, so it is best to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for managing curly hair:

Tip 1: Don't overwash your hair

Curly hair can become dry and frizzy if it is washed too often. Try to wash your hair no more than twice a week.

Tip 2: Use a diffuser

A diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help to enhance your natural curls and minimize frizz.

Tip 3: Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools can damage curly hair, so it is best to avoid them if possible. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

FAQ

Q: Can stress cause hair to become curly?

A: No, stress cannot change the shape of the hair follicle. However, stress can cause hair loss or make existing hair appear thinner.

Q: Can pregnancy cause hair to become curly?

A: Hormonal changes during pregnancy can affect hair texture, but it is unlikely to cause permanent changes in hair texture.

Q: Can medications cause hair to become curly?

A: Some medications, such as chemotherapy drugs, can cause hair to fall out or become curly temporarily. However, once the medication is stopped, the hair should return to its normal texture.

Hair Care

Whether you want to maintain your straightened hair or embrace your natural curls, taking good care of your hair is essential. By using the right products and techniques, you can keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
