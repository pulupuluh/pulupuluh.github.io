How To Thin Out Short Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 03, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have short curly hair, you know how hard it can be to manage. It can be thick, unmanageable, and difficult to style. One way to make it easier to manage is by thinning it out. Thinning out your hair can help reduce its bulkiness and make it easier to style. In this article, we’ll show you how to thin out short curly hair.

Why Thin Out Short Curly Hair?

Thinning out your hair can have many benefits. It can make it easier to style, reduce its bulkiness, and make it look more natural. Thinning out your hair can also help reduce the amount of time you spend styling it.

How to Thin Out Short Curly Hair

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start thinning out your hair, you need to gather the right tools. You’ll need a pair of thinning shears, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Wet Your Hair

Wet your hair thoroughly before you start thinning it out. This will make it easier to get an even cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into several parts. Use hair clips to keep each section separate. This will make it easier to thin out your hair evenly.

Step 4: Start Thinning Out Your Hair

Take one section of hair and comb it straight. Use the thinning shears to cut the hair about an inch away from the scalp. Cut small sections at a time to ensure you get an even cut.

Step 5: Move to the Next Section

Once you’ve finished thinning out one section of hair, move on to the next. Repeat the process until you’ve thinned out all of your hair.

Tips for Thinning Out Short Curly Hair

Thinning out your hair can be tricky, so here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with small sections of hair.
  • Use the thinning shears sparingly.
  • Don’t thin out your hair too much.
  • Be patient and take your time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I thin out my hair at home?

Yes, you can thin out your hair at home. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

How often should I thin out my hair?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how thick it is. It’s best to consult with a hairstylist to determine how often you should thin out your hair.

Will thinning out my hair damage it?

Thinning out your hair shouldn’t damage it if done correctly. However, if you’re not sure how to do it properly, it’s best to consult with a hairstylist.

Hair Care for Thinned Out Short Curly Hair

Once you’ve thinned out your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Don’t use too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy.

Thinning out your short curly hair can be a great way to make it easier to manage and style. With the right tools and techniques, you can get a natural-looking, even cut. Follow these steps and tips to get the best results.
