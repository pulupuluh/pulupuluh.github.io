Medium Length Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50

Written by Petter Apr 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair can start to thin and lose its luster. This can be especially true for those with round faces over 50. But fear not! Medium length hairstyles are a great way to add volume, texture, and style to your hair. Here are some tips and tricks for finding the perfect medium length hairstyle for your round face over 50.

Description

Medium length hairstyles are perfect for those who want to keep their hair long enough to style, but short enough to manage. They can be layered, straight, curly, or wavy, and are versatile enough to work for any occasion. For those with round faces over 50, medium length hairstyles can add a youthful touch and accentuate your features.

How To

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for a round face over 50, it's important to consider the shape of your face and your hair texture. Here are some tips for finding the perfect style:

1. Consider Layers

Layered hairstyles can add volume and texture to your hair. If you have thinning hair or want to add some movement to your locks, opt for layers that frame your face and add dimension. Soft, wispy layers can be especially flattering for those with round faces.

2. Choose the Right Length

Medium length hairstyles can vary in length, but generally fall between the shoulders and collarbone. When choosing a length, consider your face shape and hair texture. If you have thick hair, a longer length may be more flattering. If you have thinning hair, a shorter length can add volume and fullness.

3. Embrace Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some interest to your hairstyle. For those with round faces, side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes. If you have a high forehead, consider straight across bangs to balance out your features.

Step by Step

Here are step-by-step instructions for creating a medium length hairstyle for a round face over 50:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before styling. This will help your hair hold its shape and style better.

2. Apply Product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair. This will help add volume and hold to your style.

3. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to style and ensure that each section is properly styled.

4. Style Your Hair

Using a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tool, style your hair according to your desired look. Be sure to add volume and texture to your hair to create a flattering style.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your style in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for finding the perfect medium length hairstyle for a round face over 50:

1. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to try out different styles and looks to find the perfect one for you. Experimenting with different styles can help you find what works best for your face shape and hair texture.

2. Seek Professional Help

If you're unsure about what style is right for you, seek the help of a professional stylist. They can help you find the perfect style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair texture.

3. Consider Hair Color

Adding highlights or lowlights to your hair can add dimension and texture to your style. Consider adding some color to give your hair a boost.

FAQ

Q: What are some good medium length hairstyles for round faces over 50?

A: Some good medium length hairstyles for round faces over 50 include layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and shoulder-length styles with volume.

Q: How can I add volume to my hair with a medium length style?

A: To add volume to your hair with a medium length style, consider adding layers or using a volumizing product, such as mousse or a root-lifting spray.

Hair Care

When styling your hair, it's important to take care of it to ensure it stays healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to remove buildup and keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use Conditioner

Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized. This will also help protect your hair from damage.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

When using styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay and maintain the health of your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect medium length hairstyle for your round face over 50. Experiment with different styles and care for your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
