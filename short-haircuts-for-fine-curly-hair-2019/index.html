Short Haircuts For Fine Curly Hair 2019 How To Get The Best Look

Written by Bardi Jun 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have fine curly hair, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that will accentuate your curls while adding volume and texture. Fortunately, there are plenty of short haircuts for fine curly hair 2019 that can help you achieve the look you want. In this article, we will show you how to get the best look with the latest styles for curly hair.

Description

Short haircuts for fine curly hair 2019 are all about embracing your natural texture. By keeping your hair short, you can add body and volume to your curls, making them look fuller and more defined. From pixie cuts to bobs, there are plenty of options to choose from.

One of the benefits of short haircuts for fine curly hair is that they are easy to maintain. You don't have to spend hours blow-drying or straightening your hair, which can damage your curls over time. Instead, you can let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to enhance your natural curls.

How to

Getting the best look with short haircuts for fine curly hair 2019 starts with choosing the right style. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Consult with a stylist

Before you make the leap to a short haircut, it's important to consult with a stylist who specializes in curly hair. They can help you choose a style that will work with your unique texture and face shape.

2. Consider your lifestyle

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you lead an active lifestyle, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to spend on your hair, you can choose a style that requires more upkeep.

3. Experiment with different lengths

There are plenty of short haircuts for fine curly hair to choose from, so don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths. From super-short pixie cuts to chin-length bobs, there are plenty of options to choose from.

Step by Step

Once you've chosen a short haircut for your fine curly hair, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Start with clean, damp hair

Before you style your hair, make sure it's clean and damp. This will help your curls hold their shape.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product

To enhance your curls, apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your preference.

3. Use a diffuser

One of the best ways to style curly hair is with a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help enhance your curls without causing frizz.

4. Finish with a hairspray

To set your style, finish with a light hairspray. This will help your curls hold their shape throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for getting the best look with short haircuts for fine curly hair 2019:

1. Invest in a good haircut

A good haircut can make all the difference when it comes to styling curly hair. Make sure to invest in a cut that works with your texture and face shape.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb

When detangling your curls, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid causing damage or breakage.

3. Embrace your natural texture

Don't be afraid to embrace your natural texture. Curly hair is beautiful and unique, so embrace it!

FAQ

What short haircuts are best for fine curly hair?

Some of the best short haircuts for fine curly hair include pixie cuts, bobs, and shags.

How do I style short curly hair?

To style short curly hair, start with clean, damp hair and apply a curl-enhancing product. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls, and finish with a light hairspray.

How do I keep my curls from frizzing?

To keep your curls from frizzing, avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to blot your hair dry.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause dryness and frizz. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your curls hydrated.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help restore moisture to your curls, making them look healthier and more defined.

3. Protect your hair while you sleep

To avoid damaging your curls while you sleep, use a silk or satin pillowcase.

By following these tips and choosing the right short haircut for your fine curly hair, you can achieve the perfect look in 2019 and beyond.
