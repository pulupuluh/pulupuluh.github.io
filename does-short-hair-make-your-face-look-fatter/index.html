Does Short Hair Make Your Face Look Fatter The Truth Revealed

Written by Robby Jun 28, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you considering cutting your hair short but worried that it might make your face look chubby? This is a common concern that many women have before taking the plunge. In this blog post, we will explore the truth behind the question "does short hair make your face look fatter?" and provide you with tips and tricks to help you make the most of your short hairstyle.

Description

The notion that short hair makes your face look fatter is a myth. The length of your hair does not determine the shape of your face. Your face shape is determined by your bone structure, muscle mass, and fat distribution. Therefore, cutting your hair short will not necessarily make your face look rounder or chubbier.

However, the way you style your short hair can affect the appearance of your face. Choosing the right haircut and styling it in a way that flatters your face shape can make your face appear slimmer and more balanced. On the other hand, choosing the wrong cut or style can accentuate the wrong features and make your face look wider or rounder.

How to Determine What Short Haircut is Right for You

The first step in choosing a short haircut that flatters your face is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Then, trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner. Compare the shape to the following descriptions:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: Prominent cheeks and a circular shape.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of equal width.
  • Heart: Wider forehead and cheeks with a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Narrow forehead and jawline with wider cheekbones.
  • Oblong: Longer face with a narrow chin and forehead.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a short haircut that flatters your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval: Almost any short haircut will suit an oval face shape. Consider a pixie cut or a bob.
  • Round: A short bob with layers can help slim down a round face. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline.
  • Square: A short bob with soft, wispy layers can soften the sharp angles of a square face.
  • Heart: A chin-length bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can balance out a heart-shaped face.
  • Diamond: A short bob with volume at the crown can add width to a narrow forehead. Avoid styles that add volume at the sides.
  • Oblong: A chin-length bob with layers can add width to a long face. Avoid styles that are too short or too long.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Cut Short Hair

If you have decided to cut your hair short, here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. Use a heat protectant if you plan on using heat styling tools.
  2. Section your hair. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  3. Start cutting. Begin at the back of your head and work your way towards the front. Use a comb and scissors to create the desired shape.
  4. Trim the sides. Use your comb as a guide to create a straight line on each side.
  5. Create layers. Use scissors or a razor to add layers and texture to your hair.
  6. Style your hair. Use a blow dryer, curling iron, or straightener to style your hair as desired.

Tips on How to Make Your Face Look Slimmer with Short Hair

Here are some tips on how to style your short hair to make your face look slimmer:

  • Side-swept bangs can create the illusion of a narrower forehead.
  • Volume at the crown can add height and balance out a wider face.
  • Soft, wispy layers can soften sharp angles and create a more feminine look.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they can make a round face look wider.
  • Avoid styles that add volume at the sides, as they can make a diamond-shaped face look wider.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and add depth to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions About Short Hair and Face Shape

Q: Can short hair make your face look longer?

A: Yes, a chin-length bob with layers can add width and volume to a long face, making it appear shorter and more balanced.

Q: Can short hair make your face look wider?

A: It depends on the cut and style. Blunt cuts that end at the jawline can make a round face look wider, while styles that add volume at the sides can make a diamond-shaped face look wider.

Q: Can short hair make your face look younger?

A: Yes, a short, layered haircut can create a more youthful and energetic look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and use a heat protectant when you do use them.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

In conclusion, the length of your hair does not determine the shape of your face. However, choosing the right short haircut and styling it in a way that flatters your face shape can make your face appear slimmer and more balanced. Use the tips and tricks in this blog post to help you make the most of your short hairstyle, and don't be afraid to experiment with different cuts and styles to find the one that works best for you.
