How to Get the Mens Haircut Short on Sides Longer on Top

The mens haircut short on sides longer on top is a classic style that is easy to achieve. To get this haircut, you will need to visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Decide on the length of your hair on the top and sides. Typically, the top is left longer, while the sides are short.
  2. Ask your barber or hairstylist to use clippers to cut the sides of your hair. The length can vary from a skin fade to a short buzz cut.
  3. Ask your barber or hairstylist to use scissors to cut the hair on the top. They will usually cut the hair at an angle to create texture and movement.
  4. Style your hair with a product of your choice. We recommend using a pomade or wax to create a matte finish.

Once you have your new haircut, it's time to learn how to style it.

Tips for Styling the Mens Haircut Short on Sides Longer on Top

Styling the mens haircut short on sides longer on top is simple and easy. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Apply a small amount of product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips.
  • Use your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  • Try different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy, textured look or a slick, polished finish.

Now that you have some tips for styling your haircut, let's take a closer look at how to style it step-by-step.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling the Mens Haircut Short on Sides Longer on Top

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling the mens haircut short on sides longer on top:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of product to your hair.
  3. Use your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  4. If you want a messy, textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume.
  5. If you want a slick, polished finish, use a comb to smooth your hair back and create a neat part.

That's it! With these simple steps, you can style your mens haircut short on sides longer on top in any way you like.

FAQs About the Mens Haircut Short on Sides Longer on Top

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut short on sides longer on top:

Q: How long should the hair on the top be?

A: The length of the hair on the top can vary depending on your preference. We recommend leaving it 2-3 inches long for a classic look.

Q: What type of product should I use to style my hair?

A: We recommend using a pomade or wax to create a matte finish. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Q: How often do I need to get my haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the style loses its shape. We recommend getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining the Mens Haircut Short on Sides Longer on Top

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your mens haircut short on sides longer on top:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your mens haircut short on sides longer on top looking great for longer.

Overall, the mens haircut short on sides longer on top is a classic and stylish haircut that is perfect for any occasion. With the right products and styling techniques, you can create a variety of looks that suit your style and personality. We hope this guide has been helpful, and we wish you all the best in your hair styling journey!
