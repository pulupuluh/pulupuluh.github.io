If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, mens haircuts short sides longer on top are the way to go. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and is now one of the most popular haircuts for men. In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, how to style it and hair care tips.

How to Get the Haircut

The mens haircuts short sides longer on top is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personal style. The key to this haircut is to keep the sides and back short, while leaving the top longer. Here's how to get the haircut:

  1. Start by choosing the right haircut for your face shape and hair type. Consult with a professional hairstylist for advice.
  2. Ask your hairstylist to cut the sides and back short. You can choose the length depending on your preference.
  3. Leave the top longer and ask your hairstylist to add layers or texture to create volume and movement.

Remember, communication with your hairstylist is key to achieving the perfect mens haircuts short sides longer on top.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Styling mens haircuts short sides longer on top is easy and requires minimal effort. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement.
  4. Use your fingers to style your hair into your desired shape.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling mens haircuts short sides longer on top. Use a small amount of product and work it into your hair to avoid a greasy or heavy look.

Tips for Maintaining the Haircut

To keep your mens haircuts short sides longer on top looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Wash your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much styling product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that suits you best.

By following these tips, you can maintain your mens haircuts short sides longer on top and keep it looking great for weeks.

FAQs

Can I style mens haircuts short sides longer on top with curly hair?

Absolutely! Mens haircuts short sides longer on top can be styled with any hair type, including curly hair. Use a curl defining product to enhance your curls and create volume.

How do I know if mens haircuts short sides longer on top is right for me?

Consult with a professional hairstylist for advice on whether mens haircuts short sides longer on top is right for you. They can recommend a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.

How often should I trim my hair?

Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for trims and touch-ups to keep your mens haircuts short sides longer on top looking fresh and stylish.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your mens haircuts short sides longer on top looking healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner to remove dirt and oil.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much styling product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your mens haircuts short sides longer on top looking healthy and shiny.

Conclusion

Mens haircuts short sides longer on top is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personal style. By following our guide, you can get the perfect haircut, style it with ease and maintain it for weeks. Remember, communication with your hairstylist and proper hair care are key to achieving the perfect mens haircuts short sides longer on top.
