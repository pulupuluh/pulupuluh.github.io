If you're looking for a way to save money on haircuts, cutting your own hair with clippers is a great option. With the help of YouTube tutorials, you can learn to cut your hair like a pro in no time. In this article, we'll go over the basics of how to cut men's hair with clippers for beginners YouTube. We'll cover everything from the equipment you'll need to the step-by-step process of cutting your hair.

Equipment

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather a few supplies. Here's a list of everything you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Barber cape or old towel
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle of water

When purchasing clippers, make sure to get a high-quality pair that will last. You can find a variety of clippers on Amazon or at your local beauty supply store. You'll also need to choose the right clipper guards, which come in different sizes. Generally, a #3 or #4 guard is good for the top of the head, while a #1 or #2 guard is good for the sides and back.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers for Beginners YouTube

YouTube is a great resource for learning how to cut men's hair with clippers. There are countless tutorials available, so you can find one that suits your needs. Some popular YouTube channels for hair cutting tutorials include:

  • Alpha M
  • 360Jeezy
  • Barber Josh
  • SharpFade

Watching these tutorials will give you a good idea of what to do and what not to do when cutting your hair. Just make sure to follow the steps carefully and take your time.

Step-by-Step

Now that you have your equipment and have watched some YouTube tutorials, it's time to get started. Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting men's hair with clippers:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Put on your barber cape or old towel to protect your clothes.
  3. Attach the appropriate clipper guard to your clippers.
  4. Start at the back of your head and work your way up, using the clipper guard to trim the hair to the desired length.
  5. Move to the sides of your head, using the same technique.
  6. Trim the hair around your ears using scissors.
  7. Blend the sides and top of your head using the clippers without a guard.
  8. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs on the top of your head.
  9. Style your hair as desired.
  10. Clean up any stray hairs using a comb and scissors.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if needed.
  • Use a spray bottle of water to dampen the hair before cutting.
  • Always cut against the direction of hair growth for a more even cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush.
  • Practice makes perfect, so don't be discouraged if your first attempt isn't perfect.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with clippers:

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and style.

How do I clean my clippers?

After each use, remove the clipper guard and brush out any hair. You can also use a disinfectant spray or wipe to clean the blades.

What if I make a mistake?

If you accidentally cut your hair too short, don't panic. It will grow back. You can also try to blend the shorter hair with the longer hair using scissors.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and style.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your own hair with clippers like a pro. Just remember to take your time, follow the steps carefully, and practice often.
