How To Cut Undercut Haircut Step By Step Guide In 2023

Written by Frank Jul 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and edgy hairstyle, the undercut is a great option to consider. This style features short hair on the sides and back, while the top is left longer and styled in a variety of ways. If you want to learn how to cut an undercut haircut, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide to help you achieve this trendy look.

Description

The undercut is a popular hairstyle that's been around for decades. It was first popularized in the 1920s and 1930s, and it's still a popular choice for both men and women. The style features short hair on the sides and back, while the top is left longer and styled in a variety of ways. The undercut can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type, making it a versatile option for anyone looking for a new hairstyle.

How to Cut Undercut Haircut: Step-by-Step Guide

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather a few tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and some hair clips. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Use the hair clips to separate the top section of your hair from the sides and back.

Step 2: Choose Your Clipper Guard

Choose a clipper guard that's appropriate for the length you want to achieve. Start with a longer guard and adjust as needed.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin by using the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the clippers to create an even cut.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the hair on the sides and back, use the clippers to blend the hair into the longer hair on top.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Next, use the scissors to cut the hair on top. Start at the front and work your way back, taking small sections of hair at a time. Use the comb to hold the hair in place as you cut.

Step 6: Texturize the Hair

To add texture to the hair, use the scissors to point-cut the ends. This will create a choppy, edgy look.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use a variety of products, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to achieve the look you want.

Tips for Cutting an Undercut Haircut

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting an undercut:

  • Start with a longer guard and adjust as needed
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time
  • Use the scissors to point-cut the ends for added texture
  • Style your hair as desired using a variety of products

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I cut an undercut at home?

Yes, you can cut an undercut at home. However, it's important to have the right tools and to take your time to ensure an even cut.

2. What face shape is best suited for an undercut?

The undercut can be customized to suit any face shape, so it's a great option for anyone looking for a versatile and edgy hairstyle.

3. How often should I get an undercut haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the style grows out. Generally, you should plan on getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look.

Hair Care for Undercut Haircut

To keep your undercut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a blow dryer or straightener, too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished
  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain the style

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut and maintain your undercut haircut with ease. Remember to take your time and have fun with your new look!
