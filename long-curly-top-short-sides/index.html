Long Curly Top Short Sides The Trending Men s Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Christine Mar 23, 2023 ¬∑ 8 min read

If you're looking for a bold and stylish haircut, the long curly top short sides hairstyle might be for you. This haircut has been trending for a while now and is becoming increasingly popular in 2023. It's a versatile hairstyle that works well with different hair types, including curly, wavy, and straight hair. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to get this haircut, some tips on maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions about it.

What is Long Curly Top Short Sides Haircut?

The long curly top short sides haircut is a hairstyle that features long and curly hair on the top of the head, while the sides are cut short to create a contrast. It's a modern take on the classic undercut, with the top section left longer and more voluminous. This hairstyle is popular among men who want to make a statement with their hair and stand out from the crowd.

How to Get Long Curly Top Short Sides Haircut?

To get the long curly top short sides haircut, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist. They will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair short, using clippers or scissors. The length can vary, but it's usually around a #2 or #3 guard. Then, they will leave the top section long and curly, cutting it to your desired length. You can ask for layers or texture to add more volume and movement to your hair.

Once the haircut is done, you can style your hair in different ways, depending on your preference. You can use a hair dryer and a brush to create a sleek and polished look, or let your curls loose for a more natural and relaxed vibe.

Step by Step Guide to Style Long Curly Top Short Sides Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your long curly top short sides haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a styling product that works well with your hair type, such as a curl defining cream or a pomade.
  4. Use a hair dryer and a brush to style your hair in the direction you want.
  5. For a natural look, scrunch your curls with your fingers and let them air dry.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or a hair oil to add shine and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Long Curly Top Short Sides Haircut

Here are some tips on how to maintain your long curly top short sides haircut:

  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, instead of a brush.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs about Long Curly Top Short Sides Haircut

1. Is the long curly top short sides haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the long curly top short sides haircut works well with different hair types, including curly, wavy, and straight hair. However, the texture and thickness of your hair may affect how the haircut looks on you.

2. How often should I get my hair cut for this hairstyle?

You should get your hair cut every four to six weeks to maintain the shape and length of the haircut. However, if you prefer a more relaxed and messy look, you can wait longer between haircuts.

3. Can I dye my hair if I have the long curly top short sides haircut?

Yes, you can dye your hair to add some color and dimension to your hairstyle. However, it's important to use a hair dye that is safe for your hair type and to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Long Curly Top Short Sides Haircut

The long curly top short sides haircut requires some extra care to keep your curls healthy and defined. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair mask once a week to keep your curls moisturized and defined.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow drying your hair to enhance your curls' natural shape.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz and disrupt your curls' pattern.

The long curly top short sides haircut is a bold and stylish hairstyle that can make you stand out from the crowd. With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can rock this look with confidence. So, go ahead and give it a try!
