Best Haircut For Long Rectangular Face A Comprehensive Guide

If you have a long rectangular face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing the best haircuts for long rectangular faces, how to achieve them, and some tips for maintaining your new look. So, let's get started!

Description of Long Rectangular Face

A long rectangular face is characterized by its length and narrowness. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, creating a rectangular shape. This face shape is also known as an oblong face. The goal of a haircut for this face shape is to add width and volume to the sides of the face to balance the length.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Rectangular Face

The best haircuts for long rectangular faces are those that add width and volume to the sides of the face. Here are some things to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • Layers: Layers add volume and texture to the hair, which can help balance out a long face.
  • Side-swept bangs: Bangs that are swept to the side can help create the illusion of a wider forehead, which can help balance out the length of the face.
  • Bobs: Bobs that hit at the chin or above can help create the illusion of a wider face.
  • Soft waves: Soft waves can help add volume and texture to the hair, which can help balance out the length of the face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Best Haircut for Long Rectangular Face

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the best haircut for a long rectangular face:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist: A hairstylist can help you determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose a haircut that adds width and volume to the sides of the face: Look for haircuts with layers, side-swept bangs, bobs, or soft waves.
  3. Get regular trims: Regular trims can help maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.
  4. Style your hair: Use styling products and tools to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Regular washing can help prevent buildup and keep your hair looking healthy and clean.
  • Use a conditioner: Conditioner can help nourish and hydrate your hair, making it easier to style.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often: Heat styling tools can damage your hair over time, so try to limit their use.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: The sun can damage your hair, so wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen when spending time outdoors.

FAQs

What if I don't want to add volume to the sides of my face?

If you don't want to add volume to the sides of your face, you can try a haircut with a side part or a center part. This can help create the illusion of a shorter face.

Can I still wear my hair long if I have a long rectangular face?

Yes, you can still wear your hair long if you have a long rectangular face. Just be sure to add layers or soft waves to help balance out the length of your face.

Hair Care for Long Rectangular Face

Here are some additional tips for caring for your hair if you have a long rectangular face:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb: A wide-tooth comb can help prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles: Tight hairstyles can pull on the hair and scalp, causing damage over time.
  • Get regular scalp massages: Scalp massages can help improve circulation and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet: A healthy diet can help promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know the best haircut for long rectangular faces, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look. Remember to choose a haircut that adds width and volume to the sides of your face, get regular trims, and use styling products and tools to maintain your new look. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you'll be able to embrace your long rectangular face with confidence.
