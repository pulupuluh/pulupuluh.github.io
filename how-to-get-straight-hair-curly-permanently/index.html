How To Get Straight Hair Curly Permanently

Written by Daniel Apr 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to add some curls that last? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss how to get straight hair curly permanently. Whether you are looking for a subtle wave or full-on curls, we have got you covered.

Description

Getting curly hair permanently can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect curl. There are many ways to get curly hair, including chemical treatments, heat styling, and natural methods. In this article, we will cover the most effective ways to get curly hair permanently.

How To

The best way to get curly hair permanently is by getting a perm. A perm, short for permanent wave, is a chemical treatment that alters the structure of your hair, making it curly. It involves applying a solution to your hair, which breaks down the bonds that hold your hair in its current shape. Once the bonds are broken down, your hair is wrapped around rods and a neutralizer is applied to set the curls.

If you are not interested in chemically altering your hair, there are other ways to get curly hair permanently. One way is to use heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or hot rollers. These tools work by heating up your hair and reshaping it into curls. However, heat styling can be damaging to your hair, so it is important to use a heat protectant and not to overdo it with the heat.

Step by Step

If you decide to get a perm, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine if a perm is right for you.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  3. Select the size of rods you want to use to create your curls.
  4. Apply the perm solution to your hair, making sure to saturate each section evenly.
  5. Wrap your hair around the rods, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  6. Apply the neutralizer to your hair and let it sit for the recommended time.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  8. Apply a deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture to your hair.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting curly hair permanently:

  • Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best method for your hair type.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques to find the best one for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curly hair permanently:

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on how well you take care of your hair.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

It is best to wait at least 2 weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair to avoid damaging it.

Can I wash my hair after getting a perm?

It is recommended to wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair after getting a perm to allow the curls to set.

Hair Care

After getting curly hair permanently, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools and use a heat protectant when necessary. With proper care, your curly hair can last for months to come.
