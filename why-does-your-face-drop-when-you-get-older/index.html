Why Does Your Face Drop When You Get Older

Written by Robby Apr 03, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’ve ever looked in the mirror and noticed your face looks different than it did a few years ago, you’re not alone. As we age, our faces naturally start to sag and droop. But why does this happen? In this article, we’ll explore the science behind why your face drops when you get older and what you can do about it.

Table of Contents

  • What Causes Your Face to Drop?
  • How to Prevent Your Face from Dropping
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Facial Exercises
  • Tips for Maintaining Youthful Skin
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Aging Hair

What Causes Your Face to Drop?

As we age, our skin loses elasticity and collagen, which are the building blocks that keep our skin tight and firm. Additionally, the fat pads in our face start to shrink and descend, making our cheeks look hollow and our jowls more pronounced. Our facial bones also start to resorb, causing our jawline to soften and our nose and chin to appear more prominent.

All of these factors contribute to the overall droopiness of our face, which can be exacerbated by gravity, sun damage, and other environmental factors.

How to Prevent Your Face from Dropping

While everyone’s face will eventually start to droop as they age, there are things you can do to slow down the process and maintain a more youthful appearance:

  • Wear sunscreen every day to prevent sun damage
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Get plenty of sleep and manage stress levels
  • Consider non-invasive treatments like Botox or fillers to plump up sagging areas

Step-by-Step Guide to Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can also help to tone and lift sagging skin. Here’s a step-by-step guide to a simple facial exercise you can do at home:

  1. Sit in a comfortable position with your back straight and your shoulders relaxed
  2. Pucker your lips together as if you’re going to kiss someone
  3. Hold the pucker for 5 seconds, then release
  4. Repeat this exercise 10 times

Other facial exercises include smiling, raising your eyebrows, and opening your mouth wide.

Tips for Maintaining Youthful Skin

In addition to the tips mentioned earlier, here are some other things you can do to maintain youthful-looking skin:

  • Moisturize your skin daily
  • Exfoliate regularly to remove dead skin cells
  • Use anti-aging products that contain retinol or hyaluronic acid
  • Avoid harsh skincare products that can strip your skin of its natural oils
  • Get regular facials to deep-cleanse and rejuvenate your skin

FAQs

Q: Can facial exercises really make a difference?

A: While facial exercises can help to tone and lift sagging skin, they won’t produce dramatic results on their own. Combining facial exercises with a healthy lifestyle and skincare regimen can help to slow down the aging process and maintain a more youthful appearance.

Q: Is surgery the only way to fix sagging skin?

A: No, there are many non-invasive treatments available that can help to plump up sagging skin, including Botox, fillers, and laser treatments. These treatments can produce dramatic results without the need for surgery.

Hair Care for Aging Hair

As we age, our hair can also start to become thinner and more brittle. Here are some tips for taking care of aging hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, which can damage hair
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent further damage
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment to nourish and hydrate your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid harsh chemicals like bleach or hair dye, which can further damage hair

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can help to maintain a healthy, youthful appearance as you age.
