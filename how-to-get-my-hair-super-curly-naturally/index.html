How To Get My Hair Super Curly Naturally A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 03, 2023 · 7 min read

Do you want to achieve super curly hair without using any heat or chemicals? Look no further than this guide on how to get my hair super curly naturally. From tips to techniques, we cover everything you need to know to achieve beautiful natural curls.

How to Get My Hair Super Curly Naturally

There are several natural ways to get super curly hair without damaging it. Here are some ways to achieve curls naturally:

1. Braid or Twist Hair

One of the easiest ways to achieve natural curls is by braiding or twisting your hair. To create this look, wash and condition your hair, then apply a styling cream or gel. Next, section your hair and braid or twist each section. Leave the braids or twists in overnight, and in the morning, unravel them for beautiful curls.

2. Use Rollers

You can also use rollers to achieve natural curls. After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a styling product, section your hair, and roll each section onto a roller. Leave the rollers in overnight or for several hours, then remove them for bouncy curls.

3. Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunching your hair is another technique to achieve natural curls. After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a curl-enhancing product, then scrunch your hair with your hands. This technique creates defined curls without using heat.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get my hair super curly naturally:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start with clean, well-conditioned hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a cream or gel, to your hair. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to create curls and ensure that every strand is covered with product.

Step 4: Braid, Twist or Roll Your Hair

Braid, twist, or roll each section of hair, depending on the technique you choose.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair. Avoid using a hair dryer as it can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Step 6: Unravel Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, unravel the braids, twists or rollers for beautiful, natural curls.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using a brush, as it can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about natural curly hair:

Q: Can I achieve natural curls without using any products?

A: Yes, you can achieve natural curls without using any products. The techniques mentioned in this guide, such as braiding and twisting, can create curls without using any products.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, natural curls without damaging your hair. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and techniques to keep it healthy and hydrated.
