How To Slick Back Short Hair Black A Comprehensive Guide

If you have short black hair and want to achieve a sophisticated and sleek look, you might want to try slicking it back. This hairstyle has been popular among men and women for decades and never goes out of style. In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to slick back short hair black and provide tips and tricks for maintaining this hairstyle.

How to Slick Back Short Hair Black

Slicking back short hair is relatively easy, but it requires some effort to make it perfect. Here are the things you will need to accomplish this hairstyle:

  • A comb or brush
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hair spray (optional)

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect slicked-back look for your short black hair:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. It is easier to work with hair that is not greasy or oily.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your fingertips and rub them together.
  3. Run your fingers through your hair starting from the front going to the back, distributing the product evenly.
  4. Using a comb or brush, smooth out your hair, making sure there are no bumps or lumps.
  5. Once you have achieved the desired slicked-back look, you can add hair spray to keep it in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Start with a small amount of product, and add more if needed. Too much product can make your hair look greasy and heavy.
  • Use a comb or brush to create a parting that suits your face shape.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to add some texture to your hair by using a matte finish product instead of a shiny one.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back short hair black:

Can I slick back my hair if I have a receding hairline?

Yes, you can still slick back your hair if you have a receding hairline. However, you may want to use a matte finish product instead of a shiny one to avoid drawing attention to your hairline.

How often should I wash my hair when I slick it back?

You should wash your hair every two to three days to avoid product buildup.

Can I slick back my hair if it is curly?

Yes, you can slick back curly hair, but it may require more product and effort to achieve the desired look. You may also want to use a curly hair gel or pomade to help define your curls.

Hair Care

To maintain your slicked-back hairstyle, you should take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood circulation and stimulate hair growth.

Slicking back short hair black is a timeless and stylish hairstyle that anyone can achieve with the right products and techniques. Follow our step-by-step guide, use our tips and tricks, and take good care of your hair to maintain this look.
