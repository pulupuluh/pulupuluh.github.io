Does Darker Hair Make You Look Lighter Discover The Truth Here

Written by Daniel Jul 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone who loves experimenting with your hair, you may have heard that going darker can make your skin look lighter. But is it really true?

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Consider
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The idea behind darker hair making you look lighter is based on the principle of contrast. When you have darker hair, it creates a more defined contrast against your skin, which can make your skin appear lighter in comparison. This is especially true for people with paler skin tones.

However, it's important to note that this isn't a hard and fast rule. The effect of darker hair on skin tone can vary depending on a variety of factors, including your natural hair and skin color, the shade of dark hair you choose, and your overall style and makeup.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're interested in trying out darker hair to see if it makes you look lighter, there are a few things you can do to achieve the look:

1. Choose the Right Shade

When it comes to going darker, not all shades are created equal. Some shades of dark hair may be too harsh against your skin, while others may complement your skin tone perfectly. To find the right shade, consider your natural hair and skin color, as well as your personal style and preferences.

2. Add Some Highlights

If you're not ready to commit to a full head of dark hair, you can still achieve the contrast effect by adding some highlights to your existing hair. This can create a similar effect of lightening your skin tone without the commitment of a full dye job.

3. Experiment with Makeup

The effect of darker hair on your skin tone can also be enhanced with the right makeup. Consider playing around with different shades of lipstick, blush, and eyeshadow to find the perfect complement to your new hair color.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the look:

1. Consult with a Professional

Before making any major changes to your hair, it's important to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the right shade of dark hair for your skin tone and recommend any additional treatments or services to achieve the look you want.

2. Prepare Your Hair

Before dyeing your hair, make sure it's in good condition. Use a deep conditioning treatment to help strengthen and nourish your hair, and avoid washing it for a day or two before your appointment.

3. Dye Your Hair

Once you're ready to dye your hair, follow the instructions on the box or have your stylist apply the dye. Make sure to wear gloves and protect your clothing and skin from any potential staining.

4. Rinse and Style

After dyeing your hair, rinse it thoroughly and style it as desired. Consider adding some volumizing products to create a more defined contrast against your skin.

Tips to Consider

Here are a few additional tips to consider when trying out darker hair:

  • Consider your natural hair and skin color when choosing a shade of dark hair
  • Add highlights or lowlights to create dimension and definition
  • Experiment with makeup to enhance the contrast effect
  • Use hair care products specifically designed for colored hair to maintain the vibrancy of your new shade

FAQs

Does going darker make you look lighter?

Yes, going darker can create a more defined contrast against your skin, making your skin appear lighter in comparison.

What shade of dark hair should I choose?

The right shade of dark hair will depend on your natural hair and skin color, as well as your personal style and preferences. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best shade for you.

Hair Care Tips

To maintain the health and vibrancy of your new dark hair, consider the following hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently to prevent color fading
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently to prevent damage

Overall, going darker can be a great way to create contrast and enhance your skin tone. By following these tips and consulting with a professional stylist, you can achieve the perfect shade of dark hair for your unique style and personality.
