How To Cut Short Curly Hair Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Mar 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have short curly hair, you know that finding the right haircut can be a challenge. But with the help of Youtube, you can easily learn how to cut your own hair at home. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut short curly hair Youtube style, as well as tips on how to care for your hair after the cut.

Description

Short curly hair can be tricky to manage, but with a good haircut, it can look fabulous. In this guide, we will show you how to cut your short curly hair using Youtube tutorials. We will provide you with all the information you need to get started, including tips on how to prepare for the cut, how to choose the right tools and techniques, and how to care for your hair after the cut.

How to Cut Short Curly Hair Youtube Style: Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step in cutting your short curly hair is to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair and allowing it to air dry. Comb your hair to remove any tangles, and then divide it into small sections. Use hair clips to secure each section, making it easier to cut.

Step 2: Choose Your Tools

When cutting short curly hair, it's important to use the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also want to invest in a pair of thinning shears, which can help to thin out your hair and create a more natural look.

Step 3: Watch Youtube Tutorials

Youtube is a great resource for learning how to cut short curly hair. Start by searching for tutorials that are specific to your hair type and desired style. Watch several tutorials to get a sense of the technique and tools used.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Once you feel confident in your technique, start cutting your hair. Use your comb to separate a section of hair, and then cut it using your scissors. Work your way around your head, cutting small sections of hair at a time. Use your comb to check for symmetry and adjust as needed.

Step 5: Finish the Cut

When you're happy with the length of your hair, it's time to finish the cut. Use your thinning shears to remove any bulk and create a more natural look. Comb your hair to check for symmetry, and make any final adjustments as needed.

Tips for Cutting Short Curly Hair Youtube Style

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting your short curly hair Youtube style:

  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, so it's important to go slowly and carefully.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use your comb to check for symmetry. This will help you to create a more even and natural-looking cut.
  • Watch several Youtube tutorials to get a sense of the technique and tools used. This will help you to find the best approach for your hair type and desired style.

FAQ

Can I cut my own short curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your own short curly hair using Youtube tutorials and the right tools. However, it's important to be patient and take your time to avoid any mistakes.

What tools do I need to cut short curly hair?

You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also want to invest in a pair of thinning shears, which can help to thin out your hair and create a more natural look.

How do I care for my short curly hair after the cut?

After cutting your short curly hair, it's important to keep it healthy and hydrated. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. You may also want to use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and manageable.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep your short curly hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

Conclusion

Cutting your own short curly hair can be a challenge, but with Youtube tutorials and the right tools, it's possible to achieve a great look. By following our step-by-step guide and tips, you can learn how to cut short curly hair Youtube style and keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
