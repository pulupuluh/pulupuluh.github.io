What Should A 50 Year Old Bride Wear

Written by Christine May 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Getting married is a special moment in anyone's life, regardless of age. If you're a 50-year-old bride, you may be wondering what to wear on your big day. You want to look beautiful and confident, but you also want to dress appropriately for your age. In this post, we'll explore some ideas and tips for what a 50-year-old bride should wear on her wedding day.


Description

Your wedding day is a day you'll never forget, and finding the perfect dress is an essential part of the experience. As a 50-year-old bride, you may be looking for something that's elegant, sophisticated, and comfortable. You want to feel confident and beautiful, but you also want to dress in a way that's appropriate for your age.


How to Choose the Perfect Dress

Choosing the perfect dress as a 50-year-old bride doesn't have to be complicated. Here are some tips to help you find the right one:

  • Consider your body shape - Choose a dress that flatters your figure and accentuates your best features.
  • Think about the venue - Your dress should be appropriate for the location of your wedding.
  • Don't be afraid to be unique - You don't have to stick to traditional wedding dresses. Consider wearing a suit or jumpsuit if that's more your style.
  • Comfort is key - Make sure you choose a dress that you feel comfortable in, both physically and mentally.
  • Accessorize - Add some accessories to your outfit to make it feel more special.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the perfect dress:

Step 1: Determine your budget

Before you start looking for a dress, determine how much you're willing to spend. This will help you narrow down your options and avoid overspending.

Step 2: Consider your body shape

Choose a dress that flatters your figure and accentuates your best features. If you're unsure of what style would suit you best, consider consulting with a stylist.

Step 3: Think about the venue

Your dress should be appropriate for the location of your wedding. If you're getting married on the beach, for example, you may want to choose a dress that's lightweight and flowy.

Step 4: Choose a dress that you feel comfortable in

Make sure you choose a dress that you feel comfortable in, both physically and mentally. If you're not comfortable in your dress, you won't be able to enjoy your special day.

Step 5: Add some accessories

Add some accessories to your outfit to make it feel more special. This could be a beautiful necklace, a pair of earrings, or a stunning pair of shoes.


Tips for Choosing the Right Dress

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right dress:

  • Consider choosing a color other than white - Ivory, champagne, or blush can be beautiful options for a wedding dress.
  • Choose a dress with sleeves - If you're self-conscious about your arms, consider choosing a dress with sleeves.
  • Choose a dress with a high neckline - A high neckline can be a beautiful and elegant option for a mature bride.
  • Consider a dress with a train - A dress with a train can add a touch of drama and elegance to your wedding day look.
  • Don't be afraid to try on different styles - You may be surprised at what looks best on you.

FAQ

What colors are appropriate for a 50-year-old bride?

While white is a traditional color for a wedding dress, ivory, champagne, and blush can also be beautiful options for a 50-year-old bride.

What style of dress should a 50-year-old bride wear?

There's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. Choose a dress that flatters your figure and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Can a 50-year-old bride wear a jumpsuit?

Absolutely! Jumpsuits can be a stylish and comfortable option for a 50-year-old bride.

Should a 50-year-old bride wear a veil?

Whether or not you choose to wear a veil is entirely up to you. If you do decide to wear one, choose a style that complements your dress and overall look.


Hair Care for a 50-Year-Old Bride

Finally, don't forget about your hair! Here are some tips for hair care leading up to your wedding day:

  • Start a hair care routine well in advance of your wedding day to ensure your locks are healthy and shiny.
  • Consider getting a trim a few weeks before the wedding day to get rid of any split ends.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your dress and overall look.
  • Consider getting a professional hair stylist to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Don't forget to accessorize your hair with a beautiful hairpiece or veil.

Overall, choosing the perfect dress as a 50-year-old bride is about finding something that makes you feel beautiful, confident, and comfortable. By following these tips, you're sure to find a dress that's perfect for your special day.
