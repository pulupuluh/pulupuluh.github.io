Best Short Haircuts For Wide Faces

Written by Bardi Jun 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you someone with a wide face and looking for a new short haircut? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best short haircuts for wide faces that will make you look and feel great. Read on for our tips, how-to, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts are a great way to show off your face shape, and wide faces can look great with the right cut. When choosing a short haircut for a wide face, it is important to choose a style that will balance out the width and create a more proportionate look. Our list contains a variety of short haircuts that are on-trend and perfect for wide faces.

How To

When choosing a short haircut for a wide face, it is important to consider the shape of your face. A wide face looks great with a cut that adds height and volume to the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back shorter. This helps to create a more balanced look and draws the eye up towards the top of the head.

Step By Step

Step 1: Start by deciding on the length you want for your new short haircut.

Step 2: Choose a style that adds height and volume to the top of your head.

Step 3: Keep the sides and back shorter to balance out the width of your face.

Step 4: Consult with your stylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair type.

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for a wide face, it is important to consider your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can help to control the volume and create a more polished look.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for a wide face?

The best short haircut for a wide face is one that adds height and volume to the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back shorter to balance out the width of the face.

Can I still have bangs with a short haircut for a wide face?

Yes, you can still have bangs with a short haircut for a wide face. However, it is important to choose a style that works with your face shape and hair type.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

You should get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your cut.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it is important to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, there are many great short haircuts for wide faces that can make you look and feel great. By choosing a style that adds height and volume to the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back shorter, you can create a more proportionate look that balances out the width of your face. Consult with your stylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair type, and remember to keep your hair healthy and nourished with regular trims and a gentle hair care routine.
