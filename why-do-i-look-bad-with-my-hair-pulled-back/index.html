Why Do I Look Bad With My Hair Pulled Back

Written by Christine Aug 15, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Have you ever pulled your hair back into a ponytail or a bun and felt like you looked terrible? You're not alone. Many people, especially those with long or thick hair, struggle with this issue. There are several reasons why you might feel like you look bad with your hair pulled back. In this blog post, we'll explore those reasons and offer some tips on how to look your best when you're rocking a ponytail or a bun.

Table of Contents

  • Reasons Why You Might Look Bad with Your Hair Pulled Back
  • How to Look Your Best with Your Hair Pulled Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Pulling Your Hair Back
  • Tips for Making Your Hair Look Great When Pulled Back
  • FAQs About Pulling Your Hair Back
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Reasons Why You Might Look Bad with Your Hair Pulled Back

There are several reasons why you might feel like you look bad with your hair pulled back. Here are a few:

1. Your Face Shape

Some people simply don't look good with their hair pulled back because of their face shape. If you have a round face, for example, a tight ponytail can make your face look even rounder. On the other hand, if you have a long face, a high bun can make your face look even longer.

2. Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also play a role in how you look with your hair pulled back. If you have fine hair, a ponytail can look limp and lifeless. If you have curly hair, a bun can look messy and unkempt.

3. Your Hairline

Your hairline can also affect how you look with your hair pulled back. If you have a high hairline, a tight ponytail can make your forehead look even larger. If you have a widow's peak, a high bun can accentuate it.

How to Look Your Best with Your Hair Pulled Back

Just because you feel like you look bad with your hair pulled back doesn't mean you have to avoid it altogether. Here are some tips for looking your best:

1. Experiment with Different Styles

There are many different ways to pull your hair back, so don't be afraid to experiment. Try a low ponytail, a messy bun, or a half-up, half-down style. You might find that one style looks better on you than others.

2. Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can add some flair to your pulled-back hairstyle. Try adding a headband, a scarf, or some hair clips to elevate your look.

3. Consider Your Outfit

Your outfit can also play a role in how you look with your hair pulled back. If you're wearing a casual outfit, a messy bun might look great. If you're dressed up for a special occasion, a sleek ponytail might be the way to go.

Step-by-Step Guide to Pulling Your Hair Back

If you're not sure how to pull your hair back, here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. This will make it easier to pull your hair back and create a smooth look.

2. Choose Your Style

Decide whether you want to pull your hair into a ponytail, a bun, or another style. This will help you determine where to position your hair.

3. Position Your Hair

Pull your hair back into the desired style and position it where you want it on your head. Use a hair tie, bobby pins, or other accessories to secure it in place.

4. Smooth Out Any Bumps

If you notice any bumps or lumps in your hair, use a comb or your fingers to smooth them out. This will create a sleeker look.

Tips for Making Your Hair Look Great When Pulled Back

Here are some additional tips for making your hair look great when pulled back:

1. Use Products

Use a little bit of hairspray or pomade to keep your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

2. Don't Pull Your Hair Too Tight

Pulling your hair back too tightly can be uncomfortable and can cause damage to your hair. Be gentle when pulling your hair back.

3. Consider Your Hair Color

Your hair color can also play a role in how you look with your hair pulled back. If you have highlights or lowlights, they might be more visible when your hair is pulled back.

FAQs About Pulling Your Hair Back

1. Can pulling your hair back damage it?

Pulling your hair back too tightly or using hair ties that are too tight can cause damage to your hair. It's important to be gentle when pulling your hair back and to use accessories that are designed to be gentle on your hair.

2. Can I pull my hair back if it's short?

Yes! Even if your hair is short, you can still pull it back into a half-up, half-down style or a small ponytail.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Finally, here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a shampoo that is gentle on your hair and doesn't contain harsh chemicals. This will help keep your hair healthy and strong.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage.

3. Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat tools on your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can look great with your hair pulled back. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to find what works best for you. With a little bit of effort and practice, you'll be rocking a pulled-back hairstyle in no time!
