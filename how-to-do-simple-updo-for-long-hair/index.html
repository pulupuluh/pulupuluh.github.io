How To Do Simple Updo For Long Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of wearing your long hair down all the time? Do you want to try a new hairstyle that's easy to do and looks great? Then you're in luck, because in this post, we're going to show you how to do a simple updo for long hair. Whether you're going to a fancy event or just want to change up your everyday look, this updo is perfect for any occasion. So, let's get started!

Description

A simple updo for long hair is a hairstyle that involves pulling the hair up and securing it in place. It's a great way to keep your hair out of your face and show off your neck and shoulders. This updo can be done in a variety of ways, from a messy bun to a sleek chignon. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion.

How to

Here's how to do a simple updo for long hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a loose bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to hide them under the hair.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to frame it.
  5. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep it in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do a simple updo for long hair:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Use a paddle brush or comb to make sure your hair is smooth and free of any snarls.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a loose bun. Use your hands to twist your hair into a bun, making sure it's not too tight. You can also use a hair tie to secure the bun if you want.

Step 3: Secure the Bun

Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to hide them under the hair. Use bobby pins that match your hair color to keep them hidden. Insert the pins at an angle to keep the bun in place.

Step 4: Pull Out a Few Strands of Hair

Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to frame it. Use your fingers to pull out a few strands of hair around your face to soften the look. You can also use a curling iron to curl the ends of the strands for a more polished look.

Step 5: Spray Your Hair

Spray your hair with hairspray to keep it in place. Hold the can of hairspray about 10 inches away from your head and spray a light mist over your hair. This will help keep your updo in place all day.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your simple updo for long hair look even better:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair before you start.
  • Wrap a strand of hair around the base of the bun to hide the hair tie or bobby pins.
  • Use a hair accessory, like a clip or headband, to dress up your updo.
  • Experiment with different bun styles, like a braided bun or a twisted bun, to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a simple updo for long hair:

Can I do a simple updo for long hair if I have layers?

Yes, you can still do a simple updo for long hair if you have layers. Just gather your hair into a ponytail and twist it into a bun, leaving the layers out. You can secure the layers with bobby pins or let them fall naturally.

How long does it take to do a simple updo for long hair?

A simple updo for long hair can take anywhere from 5 to 15 minutes, depending on your hair length and the style you choose. It's a quick and easy hairstyle that can be done in a pinch.

Can I wear a simple updo for long hair to a formal event?

Yes, a simple updo for long hair is a great hairstyle for a formal event. You can dress it up with a hair accessory, like a sparkly clip or headband, to make it more formal.

Hair Care

To keep your long hair healthy and looking great, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like flat irons and curling irons, on a daily basis to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to do a simple updo for long hair, you can try it out for yourself. It's a quick and easy hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. So, go ahead and give it a try!
