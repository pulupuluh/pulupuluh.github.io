How To Keep My Relaxed Hair Straight

Are you tired of your relaxed hair reverting to its natural state? Keeping your relaxed hair straight can be a challenge, especially during humid weather or when exercising. But fear not, with the right techniques and products, you can maintain straight, sleek hair until your next salon visit.

How to Keep My Relaxed Hair Straight

The key to keeping your relaxed hair straight is to avoid moisture and protect it from heat damage. Here are some tips to help you maintain straight hair:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, make sure you apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help prevent heat damage and keep your hair straight for longer periods of time. Look for a heat protectant that contains ingredients like keratin or argan oil.

2. Avoid Humidity

Humidity is the enemy of straight hair. If you live in a humid climate, try to avoid going outside during peak humidity hours. You can also use an anti-humidity spray to help keep your hair straight.

3. Use a Straightening Serum

Using a straightening serum can help keep your hair straight and smooth. Apply the serum to your hair before blow-drying or straightening your hair. Some serums also contain heat protectant properties.

4. Wrap Your Hair at Night

When you sleep, your hair can get tangled and frizzy. To prevent this, wrap your hair at night with a silk or satin scarf. This will help keep your hair straight and smooth.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to keep your relaxed hair straight:

Step 1:

Wash your hair with a straightening shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2:

Apply a heat protectant to your hair.

Step 3:

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, pulling your hair straight as you go.

Step 4:

Use a straightening serum on your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 5:

Straighten your hair with a flat iron, working in small sections.

Step 6:

Wrap your hair at night with a silk or satin scarf.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help keep your relaxed hair straight:

  • Use a ceramic flat iron, which distributes heat evenly and reduces heat damage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute natural oils throughout your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, which can make your hair look frizzy.

FAQ

How often should I wash my relaxed hair?

You should wash your relaxed hair once a week to prevent it from getting dry and brittle.

Can I use a leave-in conditioner on my relaxed hair?

Yes, you can use a leave-in conditioner on your relaxed hair to help keep it moisturized and healthy.

Can I swim with relaxed hair?

You can swim with relaxed hair, but make sure you wear a swim cap and rinse your hair thoroughly afterwards to remove any chlorine or salt water.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your relaxed hair straight. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for relaxed hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a month to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using chemical treatments on your hair, such as coloring or perming.

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your relaxed hair straight and sleek until your next salon visit. Remember to take good care of your hair and avoid heat damage and moisture.
