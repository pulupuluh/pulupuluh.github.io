Best Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair 2020

If you have thick wavy hair, you know that it can be both a blessing and a curse. While it's great to have voluminous, bouncy hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style. That's why choosing the right haircut is so important. In this article, we'll go over some of the best haircuts for thick wavy hair in 2020.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Wavy Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing the right haircut for thick wavy hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to consider the shape of your face. Different haircuts work better on different face shapes, so it's important to choose one that flatters your features.

Another factor to consider is the texture of your hair. While thick wavy hair can be beautiful, it can also be difficult to manage if it's too long or too short. A good haircut for thick wavy hair should be able to balance out the volume and create a more manageable style.

Some of the best haircuts for thick wavy hair in 2020 include the long bob, the shag, and the layered cut. Each of these haircuts is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Wavy Hair

Now that you've chosen the right haircut, it's time to learn how to style your thick wavy hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for thick, wavy hair. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and manageable.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to define your waves and prevent frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, you can add some additional texture by using a curling iron or wand. Hold the iron vertically and wrap small sections of hair around it, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Finish off your style with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair

Maintaining thick wavy hair can be a challenge, but there are a few tips that can help:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush that's designed for thick hair, as this will help to detangle your hair without causing damage.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

FAQs

Q: What's the best haircut for thick wavy hair?

A: Some of the best haircuts for thick wavy hair include the long bob, the shag, and the layered cut.

Q: How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day or every two days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

When it comes to hair care for thick wavy hair, it's important to choose products that are designed for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are formulated to hydrate and nourish thick hair, and avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

In addition to using the right products, it's important to take good care of your hair by avoiding heat styling too often, using a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and protecting your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.



In conclusion, choosing the right haircut is essential when it comes to managing and styling thick wavy hair. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you'll be able to create a beautiful, manageable hairstyle that shows off your natural texture and volume.
