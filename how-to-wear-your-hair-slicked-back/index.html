How To Wear Your Hair Slicked Back A Relaxed Guide

Written by William Jul 16, 2023 · 6 min read

Slicked back hair has been a popular hairstyle for men and women for years. It’s a versatile look that can be worn for any occasion, from a casual day out to a formal event. In this guide, we’ll show you how to wear your hair slicked back in a relaxed way. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Wear Your Hair Slicked Back
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing Slicked Back Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Wear Your Hair Slicked Back

The first step to wearing your hair slicked back is to have the right hair length. Your hair should be long enough to slick back, but not too long that it becomes difficult to manage. The ideal hair length for this style is about 4-6 inches on top.

Next, you’ll need some hair product. A good quality pomade or hair gel is essential for creating a slicked back look. Make sure to choose a product that works well with your hair type.

Once you have the right length and product, it’s time to style your hair. Start by applying a small amount of product to your hair and comb it through from front to back. Use your fingers to smooth down any stray hairs.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product to your hands and rub them together.
  3. Work the product through your hair, starting at the front and moving towards the back.
  4. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting at the hairline and working towards the crown of your head.
  5. Smooth down any stray hairs with your fingers.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 until you achieve your desired look.

Tips for Wearing Slicked Back Hair

Here are some tips to help you wear your slicked back hair with confidence:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use a comb with wide teeth to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don’t use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Accessorize with sunglasses or a hat to complete your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing your hair slicked back:

Q: Can I wear my hair slicked back if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to use a hair product that works well with curly hair and use a comb to help smooth your hair down.

Q: Can I wear my hair slicked back if I have a receding hairline?

A: Yes, you can! Just be careful not to pull your hair back too tightly, as this can make your receding hairline more noticeable.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy while wearing it slicked back:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid product buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as from a blow dryer or flat iron.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to wear your hair slicked back in a relaxed way, you can rock this stylish look with confidence. Whether you’re heading to work or out on a date, slicked back hair is a timeless hairstyle that is sure to turn heads.
