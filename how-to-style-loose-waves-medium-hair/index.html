How To Style Loose Waves On Medium Hair

Written by Robby Aug 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Medium hair length is a sweet spot for many women, as it offers versatility in styling without the commitment of long locks. One popular look for medium hair is loose waves, which can be achieved with a few simple steps. In this blog post, we will explore how to style loose waves on medium hair, including tips, FAQs and hair care advice.

How to Style Loose Waves on Medium Hair

Before we dive into the styling process, it's important to note that loose waves can be achieved on any hair texture. However, it's best to start with clean, dry hair to ensure the waves hold their shape. Here's how to style loose waves on medium hair:

1. Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that work for your hair type. Once your hair is clean, apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

2. Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to curl each section and ensure that you don't miss any parts of your hair.

3. Use a Curling Iron

Using a curling iron, curl each section of hair away from your face. Hold the iron in place for a few seconds before releasing the curl. Continue this process until all sections of your hair have been curled.

4. Brush Out the Curls

Once you've curled all of your hair, use a soft bristle brush to gently brush out the curls. This will create soft, loose waves instead of tight ringlets.

5. Set Your Waves

Finally, set your waves with a hairspray or texturizing spray. This will help them hold their shape throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're still not confident in your ability to style loose waves, here's a step-by-step guide to follow:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray
  3. Divide your hair into sections
  4. Curl each section away from your face with a curling iron
  5. Brush out the curls with a soft bristle brush
  6. Set your waves with hairspray or texturizing spray

Tips for Styling Loose Waves

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect loose waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel for looser waves
  • Don't curl your hair all the way to the roots for a more natural look
  • Experiment with different products to find the perfect hairspray or texturizing spray for your hair type
  • Try adding a few face-framing layers to enhance your waves

FAQs

Can loose waves be achieved on short hair?

Yes, loose waves can be achieved on short hair with a smaller curling iron and shorter sections of hair.

How often should I wash my hair when styling loose waves?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days when styling loose waves. This will help the waves hold their shape without getting weighed down by excess oil or product buildup.

Hair Care for Loose Waves on Medium Hair

To keep your loose waves looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for loose waves on medium hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or UV protection spray
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style loose waves on medium hair like a pro. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best, and don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find the perfect style for you.
