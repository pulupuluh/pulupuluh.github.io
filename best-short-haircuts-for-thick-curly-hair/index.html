Best Short Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Apr 29, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect short haircut that suits your hair type. It can be frustrating when you want to try something new, but you're not sure if it will work for your hair. Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short haircuts for thick curly hair. So, let's get started!

Description

Thick curly hair can be gorgeous, but it can also be challenging to manage. When it comes to short haircuts, you want to choose a style that will show off your curls while still being easy to maintain. The right haircut can make all the difference in the world, so it's essential to choose the right one for your hair type.

How to

Before choosing a short haircut, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. You want to choose a style that complements your features and makes you feel confident. If you're unsure which style to choose, consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair. They can help you find the perfect cut for your hair type and face shape.

Step by Step

Once you've chosen a short haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can be harsh on curly hair, so it's essential to use a shampoo and conditioner that's gentle on your strands. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help hydrate your hair and reduce frizz. Apply a small amount to your hair after washing and conditioning.

3. Use a diffuser when blow-drying

A diffuser can help enhance your curls and reduce frizz when blow-drying your hair. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser.

4. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry

Brushing curly hair when it's dry can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your thick curly hair:

1. Deep condition your hair once a week

A deep conditioner can help nourish your hair and keep it healthy. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair once a week and leave it on for at least 30 minutes.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools

Heat styling tools can be damaging to curly hair, so it's essential to limit their use. If you must use a heat styling tool, use a heat protectant spray to help protect your hair.

3. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

A silk pillowcase can help reduce frizz and prevent breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

What short haircuts are best for thick curly hair?

Some of the best short haircuts for thick curly hair include the pixie cut, bob, and lob. These styles can show off your curls while still being easy to maintain.

What is the best way to care for thick curly hair?

It's essential to use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, apply a leave-in conditioner, and use a diffuser when blow-drying. Additionally, deep condition your hair once a week and avoid using heat styling tools.

Hair Care

Taking care of your thick curly hair is essential to keeping it healthy and looking its best. By choosing the right short haircut and following a proper hair care routine, you can show off your curls and feel confident every day.
