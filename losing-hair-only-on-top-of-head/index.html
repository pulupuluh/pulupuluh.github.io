Losing Hair Only On Top Of Head Causes And Solutions

Written by Eveline Mar 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are experiencing hair loss only on the top of your head, you are not alone. Hair loss, also known as alopecia, is a common condition that affects both men and women. However, hair loss can be more noticeable in men due to male pattern baldness. In this blog post, we will discuss the causes of hair loss on the top of the head and provide some tips on how to prevent and treat it.

Causes of Losing Hair Only on Top of Head

1. Genetics: One of the most common causes of hair loss on the top of the head is genetics. Male pattern baldness, also known as androgenetic alopecia, is inherited from your parents and affects up to 70% of men.

2. Hormonal changes: Hormonal changes due to pregnancy, menopause, or thyroid problems can cause hair loss on the top of the head.

3. Poor nutrition: A diet lacking in essential nutrients such as protein, iron, and biotin can lead to hair loss on the top of the head.

4. Stress: Stress can cause hair loss by disrupting the hair growth cycle and pushing hair follicles into a resting phase.

5. Medications: Certain medications such as chemotherapy drugs, blood thinners, and antidepressants can cause hair loss on the top of the head.

How to Prevent and Treat Losing Hair Only on Top of Head

Prevention

1. Eat a balanced diet: Eating a diet rich in protein, iron, and biotin can help prevent hair loss on the top of the head.

2. Manage stress: Practicing stress-reducing techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises can help prevent hair loss on the top of the head.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles: Wearing tight hairstyles such as braids, cornrows, or ponytails can cause hair loss on the top of the head.

4. Use gentle hair care products: Using gentle hair care products such as sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners can help prevent hair loss on the top of the head.

Treatment

1. Medications: Prescription medications such as finasteride and minoxidil can help treat hair loss on the top of the head.

2. Hair transplant: Hair transplant surgery can help restore hair on the top of the head.

3. Low-level laser therapy: Low-level laser therapy involves using a device that emits low-level laser light to stimulate hair growth on the top of the head.

Hair Care Tips for Losing Hair Only on Top of Head

1. Avoid heat styling: Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage hair and cause hair loss on the top of the head.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb: Using a wide-tooth comb can help prevent hair breakage and hair loss on the top of the head.

3. Massage your scalp: Massaging your scalp can help improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth on the top of the head.

FAQs

Q: Can hair loss on the top of the head be reversed?

A: Yes, hair loss on the top of the head can be reversed with the right treatment. Prescription medications, hair transplant surgery, and low-level laser therapy are all effective treatments for hair loss on the top of the head.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss on the top of the head?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss on the top of the head by disrupting the hair growth cycle and pushing hair follicles into a resting phase.

Conclusion

Losing hair only on top of the head can be a frustrating experience, but there are ways to prevent and treat it. Eating a balanced diet, managing stress, and using gentle hair care products can help prevent hair loss on the top of the head. Prescription medications, hair transplant surgery, and low-level laser therapy can help treat hair loss on the top of the head. If you are experiencing hair loss on the top of your head, consult with a dermatologist to determine the best course of treatment for you.
