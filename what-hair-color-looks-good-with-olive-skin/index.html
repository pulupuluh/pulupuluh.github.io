What Hair Color Looks Good With Olive Skin A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hair color but unsure about what shade would complement your olive skin tone? Look no further! In this guide, we'll go over the best hair colors for olive skin, how to choose the right shade, and tips for maintaining your new color.

Description

Olive skin has a unique undertone that can range from cool to warm. It's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone to create a harmonious look. The right shade can enhance your features and make your skin look more radiant.

How to Choose the Right Shade

When choosing a hair color, consider your skin's undertones. If your olive skin has cool undertones, opt for cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, and cool brown. If your skin has warm undertones, go for warm hair colors like golden blonde, caramel, and warm brown.

You can also consider your eye color and natural hair color when choosing a shade. If you have dark eyes and hair, you can experiment with bold shades like red and burgundy. If you have light eyes and hair, go for lighter shades like honey blonde and light brown.

Step by Step Guide

1. Determine your skin's undertones. You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones.

2. Choose a hair color that complements your undertones. Refer to the chart below:

Cool UndertonesWarm Undertones
Ash BlondeGolden Blonde
Platinum BlondeCaramel
Cool BrownWarm Brown

3. Consider your eye color and natural hair color. If you have dark eyes and hair, you can experiment with bold shades like red and burgundy. If you have light eyes and hair, go for lighter shades like honey blonde and light brown.

4. Consult with a professional hairstylist to achieve your desired color. They can help you choose the right shade and ensure that your hair is healthy enough for the coloring process.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Color

Once you have your new hair color, it's important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy. Here are some tips:

- Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.

- Avoid hot showers and excessive heat styling, as heat can fade hair color.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

- Schedule regular touch-up appointments with your hairstylist to keep your color looking fresh.

FAQ

What hair color looks good with olive skin and brown eyes?

If you have brown eyes and olive skin, warm hair colors like caramel, honey blonde, and warm brown can complement your features.

Can olive skin tone pull off blonde hair?

Yes, olive skin can pull off blonde hair, but it's important to choose the right shade. Cool undertones can go for ash blonde or platinum blonde, while warm undertones can opt for golden blonde.

What hair color should I avoid if I have olive skin?

If you have olive skin, it's best to avoid hair colors that are too dark or too light. Shades like jet black and platinum blonde can wash out your skin tone.

Hair Care

When coloring your hair, it's important to take care of it to prevent damage. Here are some tips:

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

- Avoid using hot tools too often, as heat can damage hair.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

- Use a heat protectant spray before heat styling to prevent damage.

By following these tips and choosing the right hair color, you can enhance your olive skin tone and create a stunning new look!
