How To Curl Hair Outwards With Wand A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Feb 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old curling routine? Want to switch things up and try something new? Look no further than curling your hair outwards with a wand. Not only does it add volume and dimension to your locks, but it also creates a flirty, playful look. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to curl your hair outwards with a wand, complete with tips and hair care advice.

How to Curl Hair Outwards with Wand

Before we get started, here’s a list of items you’ll need:

  • Wand (1 inch)
  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Brush or comb your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Set Your Wand

Turn on your wand and let it heat up for a few minutes. Once it’s ready, wrap a small section of hair around the wand, starting from the middle of the strand and wrapping it away from your face.

Step 3: Hold and Release

Hold the hair on the wand for 10-15 seconds, then release it gently. Repeat this process with the rest of your hair, making sure to wrap each section away from your face.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you’ve finished curling your hair, apply a light coat of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Hair Outwards with Wand

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect outwards curl:

  • Use a 1-inch wand for tighter curls
  • Start curling from the middle of the strand for a more natural look
  • Wrap each section of hair away from your face to create an outwards curl
  • Don’t hold the hair on the wand for too long to prevent damage
  • Apply hairspray from a distance to avoid weighing down your curls

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about curling hair outwards with a wand:

Can I curl my hair outwards with a straightener?

Yes, you can achieve outwards curls with a straightener by twisting the hair as you glide the straightener down the strand.

How long do the curls last?

The longevity of your curls depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, curls should last anywhere from 2-3 days.

Can I curl my hair outwards without a wand or straightener?

Yes, you can use foam rollers or flexi-rods to curl your hair outwards without heat.

Hair Care for Curled Hair

After curling your hair, it’s important to take care of your locks to maintain their health and integrity. Here are some hair care tips for curled hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent stripping it of natural oils
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and repair your curls
  • Avoid using heat styling tools everyday to prevent damage

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to curl your hair outwards with a wand like a pro. Give it a try and switch up your hair game today!
