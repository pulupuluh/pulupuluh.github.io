Medium Hairstyles For Naturally Curly Hair Over 50 A Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 04, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're over 50 and have naturally curly hair, you may be wondering what hairstyles will work best for you. While there are plenty of options out there, medium length hairstyles are a great choice for many women. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the best medium hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50.

Description

Medium length hairstyles are a great choice for women over 50 with naturally curly hair because they offer plenty of versatility. Not only can you experiment with different cuts and styles, but medium length hair is also easier to manage than longer hair. Plus, it's a great way to show off your gorgeous curls!

How to

If you're considering a medium length hairstyle for your naturally curly hair, the first step is to find a hairstylist who is experienced with curly hair. They'll be able to help you choose a cut and style that works best for your hair type and face shape. From there, you can experiment with different styles and products to find the look that's right for you.

Step by Step

1. Find a hairstylist who is experienced with curly hair.

2. Discuss your options for medium length hairstyles.

3. Choose a cut and style that works best for your hair type and face shape.

4. Experiment with different styles and products to find the look that's right for you.

Tips

When it comes to styling your naturally curly hair, there are a few tips that can help you achieve the best results:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your hair when it's damp to help define your curls.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.

FAQ

What length is considered medium for curly hair?

Medium length hair is typically considered to be between the chin and shoulders for women with curly hair.

What are some good hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50?

Some good hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50 include layered cuts, shaggy styles, and curly bobs.

How can I keep my naturally curly hair healthy?

To keep your naturally curly hair healthy, be sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. You should also avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible and apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your hair when it's damp.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your naturally curly hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, as curly hair is more prone to breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, on your hair.
  • Be sure to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Consider using a satin or silk pillowcase to help reduce frizz and breakage.

With these tips and some experimentation, you can achieve a beautiful medium length hairstyle for your naturally curly hair over 50. Embrace your curls and have fun with your new look!
