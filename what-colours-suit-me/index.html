What Colours Suit Me A Guide To Finding Your Best Colours

Written by Christine May 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on what colours suit me! If you’ve ever wondered which colours look best on you, you’re in the right place. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about finding your best colours, from how to determine your skin tone to which colours to incorporate into your wardrobe. So, let’s get started!

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding your best colours is determining your skin tone. Your skin tone is the natural colour of your skin, and it falls into one of three categories: cool, warm, or neutral. Here’s how to figure out which one you are:

Cool Skin Tone

If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink or blue hue, and your veins will appear blue or purple. You may also burn easily in the sun.

Warm Skin Tone

If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow or golden hue, and your veins will appear green. You may also tan easily in the sun.

Neutral Skin Tone

If you have a neutral undertone, your skin will have a mix of pink and yellow hues, and your veins will appear blue-green.

Which Colours Suit Me?

Once you’ve determined your skin tone, it’s time to figure out which colours suit you best. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • If you have a cool skin tone, look for colours with blue or purple undertones, such as navy, emerald, and lavender.
  • If you have a warm skin tone, look for colours with yellow or orange undertones, such as coral, peach, and gold.
  • If you have a neutral skin tone, you can wear a wide variety of colours, from pastels to jewel tones.

Of course, these are just general guidelines, and there may be specific shades within each colour family that work better for you. The key is to experiment and see what looks best on you!

Tips for Incorporating Your Best Colours

Now that you know which colours suit you best, it’s time to start incorporating them into your wardrobe. Here are some tips:

  • Start small. If you’re not used to wearing a certain colour, start with a small accessory, such as a scarf or a piece of jewelry.
  • Don’t be afraid to mix and match. Just because a certain colour is your best colour doesn’t mean you have to wear it head to toe. Mix it with other colours to create a unique look.
  • Consider your hair colour. Your hair colour can also play a role in which colours look best on you. For example, if you have red hair, you may want to avoid wearing too much pink.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what colours suit me:

What if I don’t like my best colours?

While it’s important to wear colours that flatter you, it’s also important to wear colours that make you feel good. If you don’t like your best colours, try incorporating them in small ways, or experiment with similar shades that you do like.

Can my best colours change?

Your skin tone may change over time, which can affect which colours look best on you. Additionally, your personal style may change, which can also affect which colours you prefer. It’s a good idea to reassess your best colours every few years.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some hair care tips to help your best colours shine:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner. This will help prevent your hair colour from fading.
  • Avoid heat styling. Heat can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade more quickly.
  • Protect your hair from the sun. Just like your skin, your hair can be damaged by the sun’s rays. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your locks.

And there you have it – everything you need to know about what colours suit me! With these tips, you’ll be able to find your best colours and incorporate them into your wardrobe and beauty routine.
