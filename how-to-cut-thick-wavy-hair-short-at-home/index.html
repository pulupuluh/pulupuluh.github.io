How To Cut Thick Wavy Hair Short At Home

Written by Christine Apr 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of dealing with thick and wavy hair that's difficult to manage? Cutting your hair short is a great solution for easy maintenance and styling. However, visiting a salon can be expensive and time-consuming. Luckily, you can cut your thick wavy hair short at home with the right tools and techniques. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your hair short at home.

Description

Thick wavy hair can be challenging to manage, but cutting it short can make it more manageable. Cutting your hair short at home may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done effectively. This article will provide you with a guide on how to cut your hair short at home, specifically for thick and wavy hair.

How to

Cutting your hair short at home requires a few essential tools, which include scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle of water. It's crucial to have sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut. Before starting, it's best to wash and dry your hair, so it's easier to manage.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by parting your hair in the middle or to the side, depending on your preference.

2. Begin cutting the back of your hair by sectioning it off and holding the hair straight out from your head. Trim the hair to the desired length, keeping in mind that hair often appears shorter when it dries.

3. Move to the sides of your hair and cut it to the same length as the back.

4. Move to the front of your hair and cut it to the desired length, keeping in mind your face shape and features.

5. Blend the hair by cutting small sections at a time and checking for any unevenness.

6. Wet your hair with a spray bottle and comb it straight down. Check for any unevenness and trim as needed.

7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

- Start with small cuts and work your way up.

- Use a comb to separate your hair for a more even cut.

- Invest in a good pair of scissors.

- Take breaks to avoid fatigue and ensure accuracy.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair if it's wet or dry?

A: It's best to cut your hair when it's dry because wet hair can appear longer and may result in an uneven cut.

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

A: No, regular scissors can cause split ends and damage to your hair. It's crucial to invest in a good pair of scissors specifically designed for hair cutting.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Regularly washing and conditioning your hair can prevent dryness and breakage. Avoid using heat styling tools, and instead, opt for air-drying or using a diffuser.

Cutting your thick wavy hair short at home can be a simple and cost-effective solution for easy maintenance and styling. By following the steps in this guide and taking care of your hair, you can achieve a stylish and manageable look.
