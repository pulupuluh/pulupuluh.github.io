Why Does My Hair Only Curl Underneath

Written by Daniel May 05, 2023 · 6 min read

If you've noticed that your hair only seems to curl underneath, you're not alone. This is a common issue that many people experience, and there are a few reasons why it might be happening. In this article, we'll explore why your hair might only curl underneath and what you can do about it.

Description

When we talk about hair that only curls underneath, we're referring to hair that is straight on top but curls or waves on the bottom. This can be frustrating for those who want a consistent curl or wave throughout their hair, but there are ways to work with your hair to achieve a more even look.

How to

If you're wondering how to get your hair to curl more evenly, there are a few things you can try. One option is to use a curling iron or wand to create curls throughout your hair. Another option is to try a different styling technique, such as braiding your hair while it's wet to create waves. You can also experiment with different products to see what works best for your hair type.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair to remove any buildup or residue. This will help your hair hold a curl better.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or curl enhancing cream. This will help your hair hold a curl and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Use a curling iron or wand

Section your hair and use a curling iron or wand to create curls throughout your hair. Be sure to curl the top sections of your hair as well as the bottom to create a more even look.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Finish your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your curls:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair from styling tools.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques, such as wrapping your hair around the curling iron or using a wand.
  • Don't overdo it with styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it difficult to hold a curl.

FAQ

Q: Why does my hair only curl underneath?

A: There are a few reasons why your hair might only curl underneath. It could be due to the natural texture of your hair, the way you style it, or the products you use.

Q: Can I train my hair to curl more evenly?

A: Yes, you can train your hair to hold a curl more evenly over time. This might involve experimenting with different products and techniques to see what works best for your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means washing it regularly, using a conditioner to keep it hydrated, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

Remember, every head of hair is different, so what works for one person might not work for another. Be patient and experiment with different techniques and products until you find what works best for you. With a little trial and error, you can achieve the perfect curls you've always wanted.
