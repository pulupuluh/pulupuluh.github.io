How To Make Your Hair Curl Inwards Naturally

Written by William May 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curls are a classic hair style that can add volume and dimension to any hair type. While there are many ways to achieve curls, one of the most natural and long-lasting methods is to make your hair curl inwards. In this article, we will discuss the step-by-step process on how to make hair curl inwards naturally.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Hair Curl Inwards Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Maintain Your Hair Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Curling your hair inwards gives a natural and bouncy look to your hair. By curling your hair inwards, you can add volume and texture to your hair. This style works best for those who have naturally straight hair or have hair that falls flat. With a few steps, you can achieve this look without the use of heat or chemicals.

How to Make Hair Curl Inwards Naturally

Before you start curling your hair inwards, you need to make sure that your hair is clean and dry. It is best to wash your hair a day before so that your hair has enough time to dry naturally. You will need a few tools to achieve this look:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Clips
  • Curling cream or mousse
  • Plastic wrap or hair rollers

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect inward curls:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair. Divide your hair into two sections by parting it down the middle. Clip one section to keep it out of the way.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the other side and comb it through with a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Apply a curling cream or mousse to the section of hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  4. Take a small strip of plastic wrap or a hair roller and wrap it around the section of hair. Roll the hair inwards towards your face.
  5. Secure the plastic wrap or hair roller in place by twisting the ends together or using a clip.
  6. Repeat steps 2-5 with the rest of your hair.
  7. Leave the plastic wrap or hair rollers in your hair for a few hours or overnight.
  8. Remove the plastic wrap or hair rollers carefully.
  9. Gently run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls and give it a natural look.
  10. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips to Maintain Your Hair Curls

Here are some tips to keep your hair curls looking great:

  • Avoid brushing your hair too much as it can cause frizz and make the curls fall out.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep it looking shiny and healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and keep your curls looking great.

FAQs

Q. Can I achieve this look without using plastic wrap or hair rollers?

A. Yes, you can use bobby pins instead of hair rollers or plastic wrap. Take a small section of hair, twist it inwards towards your face, and secure it with a bobby pin.

Q. How long will the curls last?

A. The curls can last up to a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use to maintain them.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your hair.

Now that you know how to make hair curl inwards naturally, you can achieve the perfect bouncy curls without the use of heat or chemicals. Follow these steps and tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
