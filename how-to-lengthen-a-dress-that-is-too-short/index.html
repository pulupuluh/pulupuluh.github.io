How To Lengthen A Dress That Is Too Short

Written by Bardi Aug 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having to wear your favorite dress with leggings or pants because it is too short? Don't worry, you don't have to say goodbye to that dress just yet. In this article, we will show you how to lengthen a dress that is too short. Follow these steps to make your dress longer and enjoy wearing it once again.

How to Lengthen a Dress That is Too Short

Step 1: Choose a Fabric

The first step is to choose a fabric that matches the dress. You can go for a contrasting color or a complementary one. Make sure the fabric you choose is lightweight and flows well with the dress. You can choose from cotton, silk, chiffon, or any other fabric that suits your dress.

Step 2: Cut the Fabric

Now, measure the length of the dress and cut the fabric accordingly. Make sure you add an inch or two to the length to give room for hemming. Once you have cut the fabric, fold it in half and iron it to remove any wrinkles.

Step 3: Sew the Fabric to the Dress

Now, it's time to attach the fabric to the dress. Pin the fabric to the dress, making sure it is aligned with the hemline. Sew the fabric to the dress using a sewing machine or by hand. Make sure you use a thread that matches the fabric and the dress.

Step 4: Hem the Dress

The final step is to hem the dress. Fold the fabric under and iron it to create a clean hemline. Sew the hem using a sewing machine or by hand. Make sure the hemline is even and matches the rest of the dress.

Tips

  • Choose a fabric that matches the dress in color and texture
  • Make sure the fabric is lightweight and flows well with the dress
  • Iron the fabric to remove any wrinkles before sewing it to the dress
  • Use a thread that matches the fabric and the dress
  • Try the dress on after attaching the fabric to make sure the length is right

FAQ

Can I use any fabric to lengthen my dress?

No, you should choose a fabric that matches the dress in color and texture. The fabric should also be lightweight and flow well with the dress.

Do I need to hem the dress after attaching the fabric?

Yes, you need to hem the dress to create a clean hemline. Fold the fabric under and iron it to create a neat hemline. Sew the hem using a sewing machine or by hand.

What if the fabric I choose doesn't match the dress?

You can choose a fabric that contrasts or complements the dress. However, make sure the fabric you choose is lightweight and flows well with the dress.

Hair Care

While lengthening your dress, don't forget to take care of your hair. Keep your hair clean and use a conditioner to keep it soft and manageable. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat-styling tools. Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

Now that you know how to lengthen a dress that is too short, you can wear your favorite dress with confidence. Follow these steps and tips to make your dress longer and enjoy wearing it once again.
