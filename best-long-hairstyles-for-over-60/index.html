Best Long Hairstyles For Over 60

Written by Robby Mar 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle, you might be wondering what the best options are. While there are plenty of short hairstyles that are popular among older women, there are also many long hairstyles that can be flattering and stylish. In this post, we'll take a look at some of the best long hairstyles for over 60 and provide tips on how to achieve them.

Description

Long hair can be a great option for women over 60, as it can help to soften the face and create a more youthful look. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that will suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. There are many different long hairstyles to choose from, including layered cuts, bangs, and updos.

How to

If you're considering a long hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure your hair is healthy and well-maintained, as long hair can be prone to damage and breakage. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can help to keep your hair looking its best.

Next, consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a long hairstyle. If you have a round face, for example, you might want to opt for a layered cut that helps to create the illusion of length. If you have fine hair, on the other hand, you might want to add some volume with layers or bangs.

Finally, think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair. While updos can be elegant and sophisticated, they can also be time-consuming to create. If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle, a simple layered cut might be a better option.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating some of the best long hairstyles for over 60:

Layered Cut

1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp.

2. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles.

3. Use a pair of sharp scissors to create long, angled layers throughout your hair.

4. Finish by blow-drying your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape.

Bangs

1. Blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry.

2. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

3. Part your hair to the side and create a deep, sweeping bang that falls just above your eyebrows.

4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Updo

1. Start by pulling your hair back into a tight ponytail at the crown of your head.

2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

3. Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head to create volume.

4. Finish by spraying your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting the most out of your long hairstyle:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to fine hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to create different looks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long hairstyles for over 60:

What is the best long hairstyle for a round face?

A layered cut with long, angled layers can help to create the illusion of length and balance out a round face.

Can I wear bangs if I have fine hair?

Yes, but you may want to opt for wispy, side-swept bangs rather than full, blunt bangs. This can help to add some texture and movement to fine hair.

How can I keep my updo in place?

Use bobby pins and hairspray to secure your updo in place. You may also want to use a hair net or hair pins to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care

Long hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your long hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently remove tangles from your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.

By following these tips and experimenting with different long hairstyles, you can find a look that flatters your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Whether you opt for a layered cut, bangs, or an updo, long hair can be a great way to look and feel your best at any age.
