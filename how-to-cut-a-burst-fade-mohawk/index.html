How To Cut A Burst Fade Mohawk A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle that will make a statement, a burst fade mohawk might be just what you're after. This style features a dramatic fade on the sides and back of the head, leading up to a strip of longer hair in the center. It's a daring look that requires some skill to achieve, but with the right tools and techniques, you can create a burst fade mohawk that will turn heads.

Description

A Burst Fade Mohawk is a hairstyle that features a dramatic fade on the sides and back of the head, leading up to a strip of longer hair in the center. This style is edgy and bold, perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hair. It requires some skill to achieve, but with the right tools and techniques, you can create a burst fade mohawk that will turn heads.

How to Cut a Burst Fade Mohawk

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the tools you need. You'll need clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have some hair clips on hand to help you section off the hair.

Step 2: Section Off the Hair

Start by sectioning off the hair on the top of the head. Use the comb to make a part down the center of the head, then clip the hair on either side out of the way. This will help you focus on the sides and back of the head first.

Step 3: Create the Fade

Using your clippers, start at the bottom of the head and work your way up, gradually increasing the length of the guard as you go. Focus on creating a smooth transition between the shorter hair on the sides and back and the longer hair on top. You can use scissors to blend the hair if needed.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Once you've created the fade, unclip the hair on top and comb it straight up. Use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length, making sure to keep the center strip longer than the sides. You can also use the scissors to texturize the hair and add some movement.

Step 5: Blend Everything Together

Use your clippers and scissors to blend everything together, creating a seamless transition from the fade to the longer hair on top. Make sure there are no harsh lines or uneven patches.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Finally, use your clippers to clean up the neckline and create a clean, polished finish. You can also trim any stray hairs around the ears and temples.

Tips for Cutting a Burst Fade Mohawk

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect burst fade mohawk:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles
  • Take your time and work slowly and carefully
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're not confident in your skills

FAQ

How long does a burst fade mohawk last?

A burst fade mohawk can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the style.

Can I cut a burst fade mohawk on my own?

While it's possible to cut a burst fade mohawk on your own, it's recommended that you seek the help of a professional stylist if you're not confident in your skills.

Hair Care for a Burst Fade Mohawk

To keep your burst fade mohawk looking sharp and fresh, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Apply a styling product to keep your hair in place
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of the style

With a little bit of practice and patience, you can create a burst fade mohawk that will make a statement and turn heads. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools and techniques, and take good care of your hair to keep the style looking fresh.
