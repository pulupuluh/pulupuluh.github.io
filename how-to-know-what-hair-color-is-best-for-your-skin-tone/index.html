How To Know What Hair Color Is Best For Your Skin Tone

Written by Eveline May 12, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're considering changing your hair color, it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we'll go over the basics of how to know what hair color is best for your skin tone.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips After Coloring

Description

Your skin tone plays a key role in determining which hair color will look best on you. Understanding your skin tone can help you choose a shade that will enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident.

There are three main categories of skin tone: cool, warm, and neutral. Cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, while warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of both cool and warm undertones.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

There are a few different methods you can use to determine your skin tone. One way is to look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you likely have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you likely have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you may have a neutral skin tone.

Another method is to consider how your skin reacts to the sun. If you burn easily and have a hard time tanning, you likely have a cool skin tone. If you tan easily, you likely have a warm skin tone.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you've determined your skin tone, it's time to choose a hair color that will enhance your natural beauty. Follow these steps to find the perfect shade:

  1. Consider your current hair color. If you're looking to make a big change, you may want to choose a shade that is no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural color.
  2. Look for inspiration. Browse hair color ideas online or in magazines to get an idea of what shades you like.
  3. Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule or don't have time for regular touch-ups, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hair color.
  4. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone. Cool skin tones look best with shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, or burgundy. Warm skin tones look best with shades like golden blonde, caramel brown, or auburn. Neutral skin tones can pull off a wide range of shades, but may want to avoid extremes like very light or very dark colors.
  5. Consider your eye color. Certain shades can enhance your eye color, so choose a color that will make your eyes pop.
  6. Consult with a professional. If you're unsure which shade to choose, it's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • If you have warm undertones, avoid shades with blue or purple undertones, as they can make your skin look sallow.
  • If you have cool undertones, avoid shades with yellow or orange undertones, as they can make your skin look washed out.
  • If you have a lot of redness in your skin, avoid shades with red undertones, as they can make your skin look even redder.
  • If you have a lot of pink in your skin, avoid shades with pink undertones, as they can make your skin look too pink.

FAQs

What hair color looks best on cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, or burgundy.

What hair color looks best on warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with shades like golden blonde, caramel brown, or auburn.

What hair color looks best on neutral skin tones?

Neutral skin tones can pull off a wide range of shades, but may want to avoid extremes like very light or very dark colors.

Hair Care Tips After Coloring

After you've colored your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your color.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Use hair masks and deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and choosing a hair color that complements your skin tone, you'll be sure to have a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
