How To Cut Short Hair With Scissors

Are you looking for a way to change up your short hair without going to a hair salon? Cutting your short hair with scissors can be a great way to give yourself a new look. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps on how to cut short hair with scissors, provide some tips, and answer frequently asked questions about cutting short hair at home.

How to Cut Short Hair with Scissors

Cutting short hair with scissors is not as difficult as it seems. However, it does require some patience and practice. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you achieve an accurate cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections. Start by dividing it in half down the middle, then divide each half into three sections: one on each side and one in the back.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Start with the back section. Comb it straight down and hold the hair between your fingers. Cut straight across, making sure to cut all the hair in the section to the same length.

Step 4: Cut the Side Sections

Move on to the side sections. Comb each section straight down and hold the hair between your fingers. Cut at an angle, starting at the back and cutting towards the front. This will create layers and add texture to your hair.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Finally, move on to the top section. Comb the hair straight up and hold it between your fingers. Cut straight across, making sure to cut all the hair in the section to the same length.

Step by Step

Here's a recap of the steps on how to cut short hair with scissors:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair.
  3. Cut the back section.
  4. Cut the side sections.
  5. Cut the top section.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting short hair with scissors:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. Regular household scissors can damage your hair.
  • Practice on a mannequin head or on a small section of hair before you cut your entire head.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and stressful, so don't be afraid to take a break if you need to.
  • Start with small cuts. You can always cut more if you need to, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with regular scissors?

No, you should use hair cutting scissors to cut your hair. Regular scissors can damage your hair and make it difficult to achieve an accurate cut.

How often should I cut my short hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, you should aim to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. It's just hair and it will grow back. You can always go to a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care

After cutting your short hair with scissors, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hair.
  • Consider using hair masks or oils to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Cutting your short hair with scissors can be a fun and empowering experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a new look without leaving your home. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and practice before you start cutting your entire head of hair.
