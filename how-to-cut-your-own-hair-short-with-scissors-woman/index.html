How To Cut Your Own Hair Short With Scissors Woman A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 23, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking to freshen up your look or save some money on a trip to the salon, cutting your own hair can be a great option. While some cuts are more difficult to achieve at home, cutting your hair short with scissors can be a relatively easy task. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve a chic and stylish short haircut from the comfort of your own home.

Description

Before we dive into the step-by-step process, let's talk a bit about what you can expect from cutting your own hair short with scissors. This type of cut is typically a bob or pixie style, which can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. While it may take some practice to achieve a professional-looking cut, with the right tools and techniques, you can create a stylish and flattering short haircut.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short with Scissors Woman: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting your own hair is to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp as you cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you begin cutting, it's important to start with clean, dry hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, and then blow dry it until it's completely dry. This will make it easier to see the natural shape of your hair and ensure a more even cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into four equal parts: two on the top of your head and two on the bottom. Clip each section out of the way, leaving only the bottom section loose.

Step 4: Cut the Bottom Section

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, use your comb to create a straight line across the back of your head. Make sure the line is level and even, and then use your scissors to cut along the line. Be careful not to cut too much at once, as it's easier to trim more hair off than to add it back.

Step 5: Cut the Sides

Next, unclip one of the side sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut a straight line, angling the scissors slightly towards your face. Repeat on the other side, making sure the length is even on both sides.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Using your comb, comb through the top section of your hair and blend it with the bottom section. This will create a layered look that adds texture and movement to your hair. Use your scissors to trim any longer pieces, taking care to blend the layers evenly.

Step 7: Add Fringe (Optional)

If you want to add fringe to your haircut, now is the time to do it. Comb your hair straight down and then use your scissors to cut small sections of hair at a time, working your way across your forehead. Take care not to cut too much at once, as fringe can be difficult to fix if you cut it too short.

Step 8: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, take a look in the mirror to make sure everything looks even and balanced. If you notice any uneven spots, use your scissors to trim them until you're happy with the final result.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair Short with Scissors Woman

Now that you know the basic steps for cutting your own hair short with scissors, here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Invest in a quality pair of scissors to ensure a clean and precise cut.
  • Take breaks if you start to feel tired or frustrated, as this can lead to mistakes.
  • Watch tutorial videos or read other guides to get a better sense of the cutting process.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair short with regular scissors?

While you technically can cut your hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Regular scissors may be dull or not sharp enough to achieve a clean cut, which can result in uneven or damaged hair.

How often should I cut my short hair?

How often you cut your hair will depend on your individual hair growth and style. In general, short haircuts should be maintained every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh and polished.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair Short with Scissors Woman

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and empowering experience, but it's important to take care of your hair afterwards to ensure it stays healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for your hair after a DIY cut:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and soft.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

By following these tips and practicing your cutting skills, you can achieve a beautiful and stylish short haircut without ever leaving your home. Happy cutting!
