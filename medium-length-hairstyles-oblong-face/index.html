Medium Length Hairstyles For Oblong Face Shape

Written by Frank May 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to find a style that flatters your features and accentuates your best qualities. Medium length hairstyles are a great option for oblong face shapes, as they can help to balance out your features and create a more proportional look. In this article, we will provide you with tips and ideas for medium length hairstyles that will complement your oblong face shape.

Description: What is an Oblong Face Shape?

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long, narrow shape with a high forehead and a pointy chin. This face shape is often referred to as rectangular, and it can be challenging to find hairstyles that flatter this face shape. The goal is to create a balance between the length of your face and your features, so you want to avoid hairstyles that elongate your face even further.

How to: Choosing the Right Medium Length Hairstyle for an Oblong Face Shape

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for an oblong face shape, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create volume and width at the sides of your face to balance out the length. This can be achieved through layers, curls, or waves. Second, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make your face look even longer. Finally, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too straight or flat, as they can make your face look even more elongated.

Step by Step Guide:

1. Start by determining the length of your hair. Medium length hair is typically between the shoulders and the collarbone.

2. Choose a style that adds volume and width at the sides of your face, such as layers, curls, or waves.

3. Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make your face look even longer.

4. Avoid hairstyles that are too straight or flat, as they can make your face look even more elongated.

5. Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle, as they can help to shorten the length of your face.

Tips and Ideas for Medium Length Hairstyles for Oblong Face Shape

There are many medium length hairstyles that can complement an oblong face shape. Here are some tips and ideas to get you started:

- Layers: Adding layers to your medium length hair can help to create volume and width at the sides of your face. This can help to balance out the length of your face and create a more proportional look.

- Curls and Waves: Soft curls and waves can help to create volume and dimension in your hair. This can help to make your face look wider and more balanced.

- Bob Hairstyles: A bob hairstyle can be a great option for oblong face shapes. A shoulder-length bob with layers can help to create volume and width at the sides of your face.

- Bangs: Adding bangs to your hairstyle can help to shorten the length of your face. Consider adding side-swept bangs or curtain bangs to your medium length hair.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have an oblong face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have an oblong face shape. However, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can make your face look even longer. Consider adding layers or waves to your hair to create volume and dimension.

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut if I have an oblong face shape?

A: While a pixie cut can be a great option for some face shapes, it may not be the best choice for an oblong face shape. Pixie cuts can create a very elongated look, which can make your face look even longer. Instead, consider a medium length hairstyle with layers or curls.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hairstyles for Oblong Face Shape

When it comes to maintaining your medium length hairstyle for an oblong face shape, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, as this will help to prevent breakage and split ends. Second, you want to avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Finally, you want to schedule regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

Medium length hairstyles can be a great option for oblong face shapes. By choosing the right style and maintaining your hair properly, you can create a look that flatters your features and accentuates your best qualities.
