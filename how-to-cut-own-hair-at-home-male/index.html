How To Cut Your Own Hair At Home Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money on expensive haircuts? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair at home? If so, you’ve come to the right place. How to Cut Your Own Hair at Home (Male)

Before we get started, it’s important to note that cutting your own hair can be difficult, especially if you’re not used to handling scissors or clippers. However, with a little patience and practice, you can achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair is more difficult to cut, so make sure your hair is completely dry before you begin.

2. Choose the right tools. You’ll need a pair of scissors, clippers, and a comb. If you have longer hair, you might also need hair clips to keep your hair out of the way.

3. Decide on the length you want. Use your comb to lift a section of hair and trim the ends with your scissors. Continue doing this until you’ve reached the desired length.

4. Use your clippers to create a fade. If you want a fade, use your clippers to slowly blend the hair at the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you’ve achieved the desired look.

5. Trim your sideburns. Use your scissors to trim your sideburns to the desired length. Make sure they’re even on both sides.

6. Clean up the back of your neck. Use your clippers to trim any hair at the back of your neck. Go slowly to avoid any mistakes.

7. Style as desired. Use a little bit of hair product to style your hair how you like it.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

- Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down to a shorter one. This will help you avoid any mistakes.

- Use hair clips to keep your hair out of the way while you’re cutting.

- Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so don’t be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.

FAQs

Q: How often should I cut my own hair?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your hairstyle. As a general rule, most men cut their hair every 4-6 weeks.

Q: What if I make a mistake while cutting my own hair?

A: Don’t panic! Mistakes happen, and hair grows back. If you make a mistake, try to blend it in with the rest of your hair or visit a professional barber to fix it.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Make sure you wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a little bit of hair product to keep it looking its best. If you’re not sure what products to use, ask your barber for recommendations.

Cutting your own hair at home can be a great way to save money and time, but it’s important to take your time and be patient. With a little practice, you can achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home.
