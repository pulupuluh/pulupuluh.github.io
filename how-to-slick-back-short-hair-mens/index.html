How To Slick Back Short Hair Mens A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 25, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to slick back short hair for men. Slicking back short hair is a timeless hairstyle that exudes class and sophistication. It’s a great look for men who want to look sharp and put together. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the step-by-step process of achieving the perfect slicked-back look. We’ll also give you some tips on how to maintain your hairstyle and answer some frequently asked questions. Let’s get started!

How to Slick Back Short Hair Mens

Slicking back short hair is a simple process that only requires a few products and tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • A comb
  • A hair dryer (optional)
  • A hair product (such as pomade or gel)

Step 1: Start with Clean, Wet Hair

The first step in slicking back short hair is to start with clean, wet hair. This will make it easier to apply the product and comb your hair back. You can wet your hair by taking a shower or using a spray bottle. Make sure your hair is completely wet before moving on to the next step.

Step 2: Apply Product

Next, apply your hair product of choice. We recommend using a pomade or gel, as these products provide a strong hold and a shiny finish. Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. Rub the product between your hands to warm it up, then apply it evenly throughout your hair. Be sure to get the product all the way to the roots of your hair.

Step 3: Comb Hair Back

Using a comb, start at the front of your hairline and comb your hair straight back. Use the comb to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair. You may need to apply more product at this point if your hair is not staying in place.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you’ve combed your hair back, you can style it as desired. You can leave your hair slicked straight back, or you can part it on the side for a more formal look. Use your fingers to adjust your hair until it looks the way you want it to.

Tips for Slick Back Short Hair Mens

If you want to achieve the perfect slicked-back look, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with clean, wet hair to make it easier to apply the product and comb your hair back.
  • Use a hair product with a strong hold, such as pomade or gel.
  • Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to get it all the way to the roots.
  • Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about slicking back short hair:

Q: How long should my hair be to slick it back?

A: Your hair should be at least 2-3 inches long to achieve the slicked-back look.

Q: Can I slick back curly hair?

A: Yes, you can slick back curly hair. However, you may need to use more product and a stronger hold to keep your curls in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have a slicked-back hairstyle?

A: We recommend washing your hair every 2-3 days to prevent buildup of product and oils.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup of product and oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools (such as blow dryers and straighteners) too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

That’s it for our guide on how to slick back short hair for men. We hope you found this helpful and that you’re ready to rock the slicked-back look. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t be discouraged if it takes a few tries to get it right. Good luck!
