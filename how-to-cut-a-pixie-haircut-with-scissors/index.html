How To Cut A Pixie Haircut With Scissors Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that's both chic and edgy, a pixie haircut might be just what you need. Pixie haircuts are perfect for women who want to show off their facial features and express their personality through their hair. If you're tired of your long locks and want to try something new, this step-by-step guide on how to cut a pixie haircut with scissors is for you.

Description

A pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that's usually cut above the ears and around the nape of the neck. It's a popular hairstyle among women who want to make a statement with their hair. Pixie haircuts can be styled in different ways, such as messy, slicked back, or with bangs. Cutting a pixie haircut with scissors is a great way to achieve a personalized look that suits your face shape and hair type.

How to Cut a Pixie Haircut with Scissors

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools, including sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut a pixie haircut with scissors:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the sides

Starting with the sides, comb your hair down and cut it with scissors. Cut your hair at an angle, following the shape of your head. Use the first side as a guide for the second side to ensure they're even.

Step 4: Cut the back

Next, move to the back of your head and cut your hair in a straight line across. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 5: Cut the top

Finally, cut the top section of your hair. Comb your hair forward and cut it at an angle, creating layers. Use the hair in the front as a guide for the length of the rest of your hair.

Step 6: Style your hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it the way you like. You can use a hair gel or wax to create different looks, such as a messy or slicked-back style.

Tips

Here are some tips to ensure you get the best results when cutting a pixie haircut with scissors:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Make sure your hair is dry before you start cutting.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head to ensure it's even.

FAQ

What face shapes suit a pixie haircut?

A pixie haircut suits most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for women with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I cut my pixie haircut?

You should cut your pixie haircut every four to six weeks to maintain its shape and length.

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair, but it's important to be careful and start with small cuts. If you're not confident, it's best to go to a professional hair salon.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is important to maintain its health and vitality. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

Now that you know how to cut a pixie haircut with scissors, it's time to try it out for yourself. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you'll have a stylish and edgy haircut in no time.
