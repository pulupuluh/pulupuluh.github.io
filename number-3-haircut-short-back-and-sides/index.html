The Number 3 Haircut Short Back And Sides A Complete Guide

If you're looking for a versatile and easy-to-maintain haircut, the number 3 haircut short back and sides is a great option. This classic style is perfect for men who want a neat and tidy look without spending too much time on their hair. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the number 3 haircut, including how to get it, tips for styling, and how to care for your hair.

How to Get a Number 3 Haircut Short Back and Sides

The number 3 haircut short back and sides refers to a style where the hair is cut to a length of 3/8 inch, or 9.5mm. This length is typically achieved using clippers, and the sides and back are cut shorter than the top. To get this haircut, you'll need to visit a barber or hair stylist who is familiar with this style.

When you go to the barber, be sure to bring a picture of the style you want or describe it in detail. It's important to communicate clearly with your stylist to make sure you get the look you're going for. Also, keep in mind that the number 3 haircut may not be suitable for everyone, depending on your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a number 3 haircut short back and sides:

  1. Sit in the barber's chair and discuss the style you want.
  2. The barber will start by using clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to a length of 3/8 inch.
  3. Next, the barber will use scissors or clippers to cut the hair on top of your head to the desired length. This can vary depending on your preference, but typically ranges from 1-2 inches.
  4. If you want a more tapered look, the barber can use scissors or clippers to blend the sides and back with the top of your hair.
  5. The barber will then use a comb and scissors to trim any stray hairs and make sure the cut is even.
  6. Finally, the barber will style your hair using product if desired.

Tips for Styling Your Number 3 Haircut

The number 3 haircut short back and sides is a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of styling. However, here are some tips to make the most of your haircut:

  • Use a small amount of hair product to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • If you want a more polished look, comb your hair to one side or slick it back with a small amount of pomade.
  • For a more casual look, tousle your hair with your fingers and let it air dry.
  • If you have thick or curly hair, consider getting a little more length on top to make styling easier.

FAQs

  1. Is the number 3 haircut short back and sides suitable for all face shapes? While this style is versatile and can work for many face shapes, it may not be the best choice for those with very round or square faces.
  2. How often should I get a number 3 haircut? This depends on how fast your hair grows, but typically every 4-6 weeks is a good timeframe to maintain the length.
  3. Can I style my number 3 haircut with a side part? Yes, you can part your hair to the side and style it with product for a more polished look.

Hair Care Tips for Your Number 3 Haircut

To keep your number 3 haircut looking sharp, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim any stray hairs or split ends in between haircuts to keep the cut looking neat.
  • If you're going to be spending time in the sun, wear a hat to protect your hair from UV damage.

With these tips, you can rock your number 3 haircut short back and sides with confidence. Whether you prefer a polished or casual look, this classic style is sure to keep you looking sharp and stylish.
