Best Hair Color For 60 Year Old Woman A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As women enter their 60s, they often look for ways to update their hairstyle and hair color to suit their changing lifestyle and appearance. Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially when you have so many options to choose from. In this article, we will guide you through the best hair color for 60 year old woman, how to achieve it, and tips to maintain it.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Before choosing a hair color, it is essential to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color. As you age, your skin tone may change, and it is essential to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. For example, women with warm skin tones may opt for golden blonde, honey blonde, or caramel highlights, while women with cool skin tones may opt for ash blonde, platinum, or cool brown shades.

It is also essential to consider your natural hair color. If you have naturally dark hair, it may be challenging to maintain a light hair color, and vice versa. It is best to choose a hair color that is no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair color.

Lastly, consider your eye color. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colors such as golden blonde or copper may complement your eye color. If you have brown or hazel eyes, cool hair colors such as ash brown or platinum may complement your eye color.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Best Hair Color for 60 Year Old Woman

1. Consult with a professional hair colorist to determine the best hair color for your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color.

2. Prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or impurities.

3. Apply a hair color base to protect your hair from damage and ensure even application.

4. Apply the hair color to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.

5. Leave the hair color on for the recommended amount of time, depending on the brand and desired color.

6. Rinse your hair with cool water to seal the hair cuticles and prevent color fading.

7. Apply a color-safe conditioner to moisturize your hair and protect the color.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

1. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair color and prevent fading.

2. Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause color fading.

3. Protect your hair from the sun and heat styling tools by using a heat protectant spray and wearing a hat when outdoors.

4. Schedule regular touch-ups with your hair colorist to maintain your hair color and prevent root regrowth.

FAQ

Q: Can women over 60 still dye their hair?

A: Absolutely! There is no age limit to dyeing your hair. However, it is essential to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color.

Q: Is it safe to dye your hair at home?

A: Dyeing your hair at home can be safe if you follow the instructions carefully and use quality products. However, it is recommended to consult with a professional hair colorist for best results.

Hair Care for 60 Year Old Woman

As women age, their hair may become thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. It is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for mature hair.

2. Avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair.

3. Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.

4. Schedule regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

5. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize and nourish your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair color for 60 year old woman can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color, and consulting with a professional hair colorist, you can achieve a hair color that complements your appearance and lifestyle. Remember to take care of your hair by using quality products, avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools, and eating a balanced diet to maintain its health and beauty.
