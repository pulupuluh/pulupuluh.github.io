Is the Heart Shaped Face the Most Attractive?

A lot of people believe that the heart shaped face is the most attractive face shape. In fact, many celebrities and models have this face shape. But is it really the most attractive? Let's find out.

Description

The heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. It is called a heart shaped face because its outline resembles the shape of a heart. This face shape is generally considered to be very feminine and attractive.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure whether you have a heart shaped face or not, here's how to determine your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a lipstick or an eyeliner to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape that you traced.
  4. If the shape is wider at the top and narrower at the bottom, you have a heart shaped face.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle for Heart Shaped Faces

Here's a step by step guide to choosing the right hairstyle for heart shaped faces:

  1. Consider getting a side-swept fringe that will balance out the width of your forehead.
  2. Avoid hairstyles with too much volume at the top as this will make your forehead appear even wider.
  3. Opt for hairstyles with layers, as this will create movement and soften your jawline.
  4. Consider getting a bob or a lob, as this will draw attention to your cheekbones.
  5. Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as this will elongate your face even more.

Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

Here are some tips for heart shaped faces:

  • Wear earrings that are wider at the bottom, as this will balance out your chin.
  • Avoid wearing chokers or necklaces that sit too high on your neck, as this will make your face appear even longer.
  • Use contouring makeup to define your cheekbones and soften your jawline.
  • Avoid wearing hairstyles that are too slicked back, as this will accentuate your forehead.

FAQ

Q: Can heart shaped faces pull off bangs?

A: Yes, heart shaped faces can pull off bangs. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs are a good option as they will balance out the width of your forehead.

Q: Can heart shaped faces wear short hair?

A: Yes, heart shaped faces can wear short hair. In fact, a bob or a lob is a good option as it will draw attention to your cheekbones.

Q: What hairstyles should heart shaped faces avoid?

A: Heart shaped faces should avoid hairstyles with too much volume at the top, as this will make their forehead appear even wider. They should also avoid hairstyles that are too long, as this will elongate their face even more.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces

Here are some hair care tips for heart shaped faces:

  • Avoid using heavy products that will weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the roots.
  • Use a curling iron to create loose waves, as this will add movement and soften your jawline.
  • Avoid using a straightener to create poker straight hair, as this will accentuate your forehead.
