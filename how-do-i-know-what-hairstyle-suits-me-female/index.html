How Do I Know What Hairstyle Suits Me Female

Written by Petter May 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and want to switch things up? Choosing a new hairstyle can be overwhelming, especially if you're not sure what suits you best. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to know what hairstyle suits you as a female.

Table of Content

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

Before choosing a new hairstyle, consider the shape of your face. Different hairstyles complement different face shapes. Here are the most common face shapes and the corresponding hairstyles that suit them best:

  • Round face: Long layers, side bangs, and textured pixie cuts.
  • Square face: Soft layers, layered bob, and side-swept bangs.
  • Oval face: Any hairstyle suits this face shape, but avoid heavy bangs.
  • Heart-shaped face: Face-framing layers, side-swept bangs, and long bobs.

Consider your hair type as well. If you have thin hair, a short bob or a pixie cut will add volume. If you have thick hair, long layers or a shaggy cut will thin it out.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to determine what hairstyle suits you best:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair type.
  3. Decide on the length you want.
  4. Look for inspiration in magazines or online.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist.
  6. Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to visualize the hairstyle.
  7. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have for styling your hair.
  8. Make the cut!

Tips

  • Consider your skin tone when choosing hair color.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new.
  • Be realistic about the amount of time you have for styling your hair.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personality and style.

FAQ

What if I can't decide on a hairstyle?

If you're having trouble deciding on a hairstyle, consult with a hairstylist. They can give you recommendations based on your face shape and hair type. You can also try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to visualize the hairstyle.

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you don't like your new hairstyle, don't panic. Hair grows back, and you can always try a different style next time. Communicate with your hairstylist and let them know what you don't like about the hairstyle. They can make adjustments or suggest a different style that suits you better.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a new hairstyle, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Conclusion

Choosing a new hairstyle can be exciting, but it can also be overwhelming. By considering your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can determine what hairstyle suits you as a female. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
