How To Put Waves In Fine Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 12, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have fine hair, you might think that achieving waves is impossible. But with the right tools and techniques, you can create beautiful, beachy waves in no time. In this guide, we’ll show you how to put waves in fine hair, from start to finish.

How to Put Waves in Fine Hair

Follow these simple steps to achieve gorgeous waves in your fine hair:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have any tangles, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting from the bottom. Use a hair clip to secure the top sections of your hair, leaving the bottom section loose.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling wand or curling iron. Hold the curling tool in place for about 10 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 4: Create Beachy Waves

To create beachy waves, use your fingers to gently tousle your hair. You can also use a wide-tooth comb to create more defined waves.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your waves in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray.

Tips for Putting Waves in Fine Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best waves possible:

  • Use a curling wand or iron with a smaller barrel for tighter waves, or a larger barrel for looser waves.
  • Don’t curl your hair too tightly, as this can cause your waves to look unnatural.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  • Let your curls cool completely before tousling them with your fingers or a comb.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and definition to your waves.

FAQ: Putting Waves in Fine Hair

Can I put waves in my fine hair without heat styling tools?

Yes, you can achieve waves in your fine hair without heat styling tools. Try braiding your hair while it’s damp and leaving the braids in overnight. In the morning, remove the braids and tousle your hair with your fingers for beachy waves.

How often should I put waves in my hair?

It’s best to limit heat styling to once a week or less to prevent damage to your hair. If you want to wear waves more often, try using non-heat styling methods like braiding or using a texturizing spray.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

Fine hair can be more delicate than other hair types, so it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for caring for your fine hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won’t weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look flat and greasy.
  • Don’t brush your hair too often, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.
  • Limit heat styling to once a week or less to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful waves in your fine hair. Have fun experimenting with different styles and finding the perfect look for you!
