How To Wear Your Hair Over 60 A Guide For Relaxed Style

Are you looking for ways to style your hair over 60? Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance style or something that will make you feel confident and stylish, there are plenty of options available. In this guide, we'll go over some tips and tricks for wearing your hair over 60 in a relaxed, effortless style.

How to Wear Your Hair Over 60

When it comes to styling your hair over 60, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your hair type and texture. Second, think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair. Finally, consider your personal style and what looks best on you.

Short Hair

Short hair is a great option for a low-maintenance, relaxed style. A pixie cut or a bob can be a great choice, and there are plenty of variations that can work for different face shapes and hair types. Short hair can also be a great way to showcase your natural texture and color.

Medium-Length Hair

If you prefer a slightly longer style, consider a medium-length cut. This can be a great option for women with fine hair who want to add volume and body. A layered cut can also be a great way to add movement and texture to your hair.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, consider adding some layers to add movement and dimension. Long hair can be a great canvas for experimenting with different styles, from braids and updos to loose waves and straight styles.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving a relaxed, stylish look:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or cream to add body and texture.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement.
  4. If you have bangs, use a flat iron to smooth them out.
  5. Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or straighten your hair.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling your hair over 60:

  • Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use products that are designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Don't be afraid to add some color or highlights to your hair.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, or scarves to add some extra flair to your style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair over 60:

What is the best hair color for women over 60?

The best hair color for women over 60 depends on your skin tone and personal style. Some popular options include highlights, lowlights, and natural-looking shades like blonde, brown, and gray.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing mousse or cream, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, or using a curling iron to add waves or curls.

What hairstyles are best for thinning hair?

Hairstyles that add volume and texture to thinning hair can include layered cuts, pixie cuts, and bobs. Adding highlights or lowlights can also help to add dimension and make hair appear fuller.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often to avoid stripping the natural oils.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a relaxed, effortless style that looks great and makes you feel confident and stylish. Whether you prefer short, medium-length, or long hair, there are plenty of options available for women over 60.
