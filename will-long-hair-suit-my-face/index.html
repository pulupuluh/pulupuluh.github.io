Will Long Hair Suit My Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 27, 2023 · 8 min read

Long hair is a popular hairstyle that many people love. While it suits some faces, others may not be as lucky. If you're wondering whether long hair will suit your face, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about long hair and face shapes. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine if Long Hair Will Suit Your Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Long Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Description

Long hair can be a great way to express your personality and style. However, not all face shapes are compatible with long hair. Choosing the right hairstyle is essential to ensure that you look your best. Before you decide to grow your hair long, it's important to determine whether it will suit your face shape.

How to Determine if Long Hair Will Suit Your Face

The first step in determining if long hair will suit your face is to identify your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you know your face shape, you can determine whether long hair will suit you.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck. Almost any hairstyle will suit your face, including long hair. This face shape is the most versatile and can pull off any length of hair.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, long hair can be a great option. However, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or short layers. Instead, opt for long layers or a side-swept fringe.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, long hair can soften your features. However, you'll want to avoid blunt bangs or straight hair that falls flat against your face. Instead, opt for soft layers or waves.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, long hair can be a great option. However, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a bouffant or quiff. Instead, opt for soft layers or a side-swept fringe.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, long hair can be a great option. However, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a bouffant or quiff. Instead, opt for soft layers or a side-swept fringe.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, long hair can be a great option. However, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add length to your face, such as a straight bob or blunt bangs. Instead, opt for soft waves or layers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, here's a step-by-step guide to determine whether long hair will suit you:

  1. Identify your face shape
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness
  3. Think about your lifestyle and how much time you can dedicate to styling your hair
  4. Look at pictures of people with similar face shapes and hair types
  5. Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion

Tips for Choosing the Right Long Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right long hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you can dedicate to styling your hair
  • Choose a style that complements your face shape
  • Consider adding layers or a fringe to add dimension to your hair
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

FAQs

Is long hair suitable for all face shapes?

No, long hair isn't suitable for all face shapes. It's important to consider your face shape before deciding to grow your hair long.

Can I still wear long hair if it doesn't suit my face shape?

Yes, you can still wear long hair even if it doesn't suit your face shape. However, you may want to consider adding layers or a fringe to create a more flattering look.

How can I maintain my long hair?

To maintain your long hair, it's important to regularly trim split ends, use a nourishing hair mask, and avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid over-washing your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Trim split ends regularly
  • Use a nourishing hair mask once a week

In conclusion, long hair can be a great hairstyle for many people. However, it's important to consider your face shape before deciding to grow your hair long. By following the tips and guidelines in this article, you can determine whether long hair will suit your face and choose the right hairstyle for you.
