How To Cut Ladies Hair Short At Home

Written by Luffy Jul 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts at the salon? Do you want to save money and learn how to cut your own hair at home? If you're considering cutting your hair short, you're in the right place! In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut ladies hair short at home. We'll also provide you with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure your new haircut looks amazing.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather some supplies. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties (optional)
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape
  • Spray bottle with water

Once you have all of your supplies, follow these steps to cut your hair short:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry completely. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use a comb to section your hair into four sections: top, sides, and back. Use hair ties to secure each section of hair.

Step 3: Start cutting

Take one section of hair and comb it straight down. Use scissors to cut across the bottom of the hair, making sure to cut it straight across. Use the first section as a guide for the other sections, making sure to cut them at the same length.

Step 4: Cut the sides

Take the side sections of your hair and comb them straight down. Use the length of the top section as a guide for the sides. Use scissors to cut the sides, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

Step 5: Cut the back

Take the back section of your hair and comb it straight down. Use the length of the sides as a guide for the back. Use scissors to cut the back, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

Step 6: Blend the hair

Use scissors to blend the hair together, making sure there are no harsh lines where you cut the different sections of hair.

Step 7: Trim the ends

Use scissors to trim any uneven ends.

Step 8: Use clippers (optional)

If you want a shorter haircut, you can use clippers to cut your hair even shorter. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you reach your desired length.

Step 9: Clean up

Use a towel or cape to brush off any hair that fell on your clothes. Clean up any hair on the floor.

Step 10: Style

Use a styling product to style your new haircut.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter if you're not sure how short you want your hair.
  • Use a mirror to make sure your haircut is even.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it will appear shorter when it dries.
  • Don't cut your hair too often as it can damage your hair and cause split ends.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with kitchen scissors?

No, it's not recommended to cut your hair with kitchen scissors as they are not sharp enough and can damage your hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. Just make sure to start with a longer guard and work your way shorter until you reach your desired length.

Can I cut my hair short without clippers?

Yes, you can cut your hair short without clippers. Just use scissors to cut your hair to your desired length.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet as it can cause breakage.

Now that you know how to cut ladies hair short at home, you can save money and have a new hairstyle. Just remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to ensure your haircut looks amazing.
