Is Layered Hair In Style For 2021

Description

Layered hair has been a popular hairstyle for years, and it doesn't seem to be going out of style anytime soon. In fact, layered hair is still in trend in 2021. Layered hair can add volume, texture, and movement to your hair, making it look more dynamic and stylish. Whether you have long or short hair, layered hair can give you a trendy look that will make you stand out.

How to

If you're thinking of getting a layered haircut, there are a few things you need to consider. First, you need to decide how many layers you want and where you want them. You can have long layers, short layers, or a combination of both. You can also have layers around your face or throughout your hair. It all depends on the look you're going for and your hair length.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to get a layered haircut:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back.
  3. Begin cutting the back section of your hair by cutting it straight across at the desired length.
  4. Gradually cut layers into the back section of your hair by holding the hair straight up and cutting into it at an angle.
  5. Move on to the side sections of your hair, following the same process as the back.
  6. Cut the top section of your hair by holding it straight up and cutting into it at an angle.
  7. Blend the layers together by cutting into them at an angle.
  8. Finish by trimming any stray hairs and shaping the layers to your desired length.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a layered haircut:

  • Bring pictures of the layered haircut you want to show your hairstylist.
  • Be clear about the length and type of layers you want.
  • Ask your hairstylist for their opinion on what layers would look best with your hair type and face shape.
  • Be prepared for some maintenance. Layered hair may require more upkeep than a simple, one-length haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about layered hair:

What type of hair is best for layered hair?

Layered hair can work for all hair types, but it's especially suited for those with fine or thin hair. The layers can add volume and texture to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

Will layered hair make my hair look shorter?

Layered hair can make your hair look shorter, but it depends on the length and type of layers you get. If you get long layers, your hair may not look much shorter. If you get short layers, your hair may look slightly shorter.

How often should I get a layered haircut?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how much maintenance you're willing to do. On average, it's recommended to get a haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and layers of your haircut.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your layered hair:

  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the layers and make them look frizzy.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with soft bristles to detangle your hair, as harsh brushing can damage the layers.
  • Consider using a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and layers of your haircut.

In conclusion, layered hair is still in style for 2021 and can give you a trendy and dynamic look. If you're thinking of getting a layered haircut, be sure to communicate clearly with your hairstylist and bring pictures of the look you want. With proper maintenance and care, your layered hair can look great and make you feel confident.
