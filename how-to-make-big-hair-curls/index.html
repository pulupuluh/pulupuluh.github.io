How To Make Big Hair Curls A Step By Step Guide

Do you want to achieve voluminous, big hair curls? You're in luck! In this article, we'll give you step-by-step instructions on how to make big hair curls. Plus, we'll include some tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Big Hair Curls
  • Tips for Big Hair Curls

  • Hair Care for Big Hair Curls

How to Make Big Hair Curls

Before we get started, make sure you have the following items:

  • Curling iron
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Sectioning clips

Now, let's get started:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron. Divide your hair into sections using sectioning clips.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, starting at the base of the hair near your scalp. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat with each section of hair until your entire head is curled.

Step 3: Tease Your Hair

Using a comb or brush, gently tease the roots of your hair to add volume. Be careful not to overdo it and create a tangled mess.

Step 4: Add Hairspray

Finish your look with a generous amount of hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Big Hair Curls

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect big hair curls:

  • For tighter curls, use a smaller curling iron.
  • For looser curls, use a larger curling iron.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly or it will look unnatural.
  • For more volume, tease your hair at the roots.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't forget to use hairspray to hold your curls in place.

FAQs about Big Hair Curls

Q: Can I make big hair curls without a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use hot rollers or a hair straightener to create big hair curls.

Q: How long will my big hair curls last?

A: Your curls should last all day if you use enough hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much.

Q: Can I make big hair curls on short hair?

A: Yes, you can create big hair curls on short hair using a smaller curling iron or hot rollers.

Hair Care for Big Hair Curls

Now that you've achieved the perfect big hair curls, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your style.

  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make big hair curls like a pro. Don't be afraid to experiment with different curling techniques and products to find what works best for your hair type. Happy styling!
